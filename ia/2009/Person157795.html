Michael Breunig’s Summary

Thirty years of providing quality technical services includes engineering graphics, vocational education, information technology, technical writing, construction project management and facility management. Goal measurements, project control methods, practical technical writing and software tools are combined to provide communication options that clearly illustrate specifications, tasks, costs, quality and performance issues. Complex systems are organized into logical categories and condensed to appropriate levels of details.

Communication capabilities include navigating complex environments such as joint venture teams and public works projects. Collaboration is accomplished with calm persistence and enthusiasm. Available resources are maximized to complete project tasks. Observations and decisions are logical, stated directly and clearly.
