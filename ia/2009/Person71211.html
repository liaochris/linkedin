Elena Acoba’s Summary

I provide business communications support for corporations and organizations on a contract basis.

I help clients present clear, concise and engaging information to their audiences for websites, newsletters, corporate reports and materials for marketing, employee communications, public relations and media relations.

What else I do:

Write features and other articles for newspapers and magazines on assignment.

Teach writing skills, including a "Writing for the Web" workshop for staff and faculty of Pima Community College and, on a volunteer basis, basic skills to elementary school-age children.

Serve as co-president and long-time board member of the International Association of Business Communicators, Tucson Chapter.

Volunteer as a mentor to industry students and as a judge in programs awarding best practices in communications.

Goals: Teach writing to elementary school-aged students in community education settings. Publish a series of books with at-home educational activities that parents can do with their children.

Elena Acoba’s Specialties:

Topics: Employee and business activities, destination marketing, family activities, health, fitness, home and garden, food, cooking, personality and company profiles, history, education, community issues, attractions, events, arts.

Writing coaching, publication management, board leadership, public speaking.
