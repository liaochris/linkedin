B2B Marketing and Communications professional

Katrina is a marketing professional with broad marketing expertise (both B2B and B2C) with post graduate qualifications (Masters of Business Marketing).

Katrina has more than eight years of extensive experience as a marketing professional in reputable multinational companies such as Canon, Fuji Xerox and AVON.

Katrina's experience within these environments has led her to develop strategic skills to successfully achieve KPIs in complex corporate environments.
