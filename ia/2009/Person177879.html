Igo Geurtjens’s Summary

Worked for NLnet (Worldcom/ UUNET) between 1996 and 1998. Co-founder of @MSTERDAM (1998), VirTube (2000) and since 2003 founder of the company OGI 1. Also co-founder of Deebmedia since 2006, a high tech startup in next generation rich media software - Cinematic interactive video; and partner at digivip (2009) - digital personal branding & digital branding for companies.

Entrepeneur, consultant and a powerful creative with strong commercial and strategic skills. Enthusiasts people based on knowledge, desire and an impressive drive to create the best. Is able to brainstorm in a wide range but will diverge and converge in time. Creating brand formats which enables clients to reach their targets.
Has his own style of management. Is flexible and adapts quickly to the culture of an organization, and circumstances.
