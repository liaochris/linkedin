Rachel 'Words that Sell' Henwood’s Summary

Following my original training in the UK as a graphic designer, I have had more than fourteen years’ experience, working both in the advertising industry and as a freelance writer. I have worked with a diverse and international clientele, including Gulf Air, Sony, Coca Cola, Nescafe, Nivea, BMW, Proton, Porsche, McDonalds, Sheraton Hotel Group, Standard Chartered Bank,The British Army, The British Navy, Tescos and Energizer.

My time spent living and working around the world in countries including England,Bahrain, Oman, Singapore and Australia has not only given me an invaluable insight into many different countries, cultures and religious ideas, but it has also exposed me to a vast array of ideas, attitudes and often unusual life experiences, from which I draw much of my creative inspiration.

I have won awards for my work at the London International Advertising Awards and
The New York Festivals and am a published author and features writer.


FIRSTLY, WHAT IS COPYWRITING?

Copywriting is best described as the art of finding the right words to introduce, promote or publicise a person, product or business. It can be used across a range of medium from print, press and the Web, to TV and radio.
