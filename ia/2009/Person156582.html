Matt Pasternak’s Summary

Matt Pasternak has made a career of observing and successfully targeting leading waves and opportunities that emerge at the locus of the entertainment, advertising/media, and technology industries. From his early experience in the Motion Picture industry – where he accurately forecasted the boom in home video cassettes and failure of videodiscs - to his present involvement in expanding the value of the Internet as a powerful and effective advertising medium, he has consistently capitalized on this technological vision. His entire career has been to direct this vision into strategically and tactically leading intra- and entrepreneurial ventures in new technology to market dominance ahead of, or against, competitors.

Pasternak gained much of his sales, marketing and strategic development seasoning while working on new ventures with such blue chip organizations as IBM, RCA, Columbia Pictures and ABC Television. He has designed and implemented the competitive strategy and built and led dedicated, elite teams for seven cutting edge projects, successfully positioning all for market dominance.

A persuasive strategic tactician, Pasternak is skilled in communicating complex topics with equal effectiveness to technical and non-technical audiences in English and Spanish. As a US expatriate, he is and experienced international professional, speaks fluent Spanish, and has a solid understanding of international cultures and mindsets.

Matt Pasternak’s Specialties:

Taking under-leveraged, funded business entities or departments and turning them around into profitable, marketable organizations. Revenue objectives are measured against realistic competition, business strategy and culture, market need and strategy, sales strategy, project development, and manpower. From these an accurate strategic and tactical plan is developed and implemented. Pasternak has successfully accomplished this task for seven separate entities over last 15 years.
