Vinod Nair (vinod@enzentech.com)’s Summary

Vinod is a Fulbright Scholar and an alumnus of Indian Institute of Management (Bangalore) and Carnegie Mellon University (USA). Prior to co-founding Enzen Group, Vinod has led technology consulting, systems integration and programme management engagements with leading global consulting firms. His experience covers a number of industries including Energy, Utilities, Engineering & Financial Services spanning Europe, US and Asia.

Enzen Global is India's largest and fastest growing full-service Energy & Utility Solutions company. Enzen offer a range of solutions covering business consulting, operations and maintenance, technology / software and turn-key services for Power, Gas, Water, Green Energy & Environment companies globally. Our Green Energy & Environment business groups work in tandem to offer an entire range of solutions ranging from strategic advice on sustainable solutions to setting up, operating and maintaining renewable energy generation units. Our Green Energy Group offers turn-key solutions in Solar, Wind, Hydro, Geo-thermal, Bio Mass and Waste to Energy. We work with several top rated utilities in the UK, European & Asian markets. In India, we have recently partnered with the Indian Government on a US $ 10 Billion program to bring in significant savings from energy efficiency improvements and loss reductions. For more details please visit www.enzenglobal.com

Enzen Technologies is a premier global technology services and solutions provider, leveraging its strong technical expertise and domain insight to provide insightful solutions and enabling business transformation in organizations. For more details please visit www.enzentech.com

Based out of Bangalore, Enzen’s footprint marks markets across United Kingdom, Continental Europe, South East Asia and the Middle East.

Please contact me at vinod@enzentech.com for more details.
