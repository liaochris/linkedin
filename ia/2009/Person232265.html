Alexandra Howson’s Summary

Thistle Editorial provides research, writing and editorial services for business professionals, academics and graduate students. At Thistle Editorial we are passionate about enabling clients to create clear, crisp communications. We deliver genuine value by tailoring our approach to match your needs. By working closely with you we enable you to produce results that reflect your best efforts. Our expertise includes:

Research
• Information Retrieval
• Fact Checking
• Support for conducting in-depth interviews, focus groups, ethnographic
observation, image/textual analysis

Writing
• Coaching/Workshops
• White Papers
• Manuscripts
• Grant Proposals
• PowerPoint Presentations
• Annual Reports
• Curriculum Materials/Training Manuals

Editing
• Developmental Editing
• Manuscript Critique
• Copyediting
• Proofreading

We offer over a decade of experience in producing high caliber research, writing and editing. Thistle Editorial is owned and operated by Alexandra Howson Ph.D., who has held faculty and visiting scholar positions in world class universities - including the University of Edinburgh and UC Berkeley. In addition to authoring books, she has won grants and scholarships, written academic articles and reports, supervised and edited scores of theses and papers, reviewed manuscripts, designed curriculum materials and produced PowerPoint presentations.
