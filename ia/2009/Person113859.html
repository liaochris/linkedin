James Keppel’s Summary

THIRTY THREE THOUSAND FEET

With nearly 30 years of experience in a wide range of design disciplines, I bring a unique combination of skills to the 33k' team; a passion for leading-edge transdisciplinary design, a thorough knowledge of construction and manufacturing technologies, a practical approach to the economics of a project, and an ability to communicate the “story.”

Prior to forming 33k’, I was the founding principal of Ellipsis..., a design firm located in the San Francisco Bay Area.

Signature projects include Walt Disney Concert Hall in Los Angeles, Guadalupe River Park & Gardens in San Jose, California, and the San Jose Arena (HP Pavilion), along with Westfield San Francisco Centre and Sheridan Ridge Private Reserve.

I serve on the Board of Directors of the Society for Environmental Graphic Design (SEGD), and I am a member of the Urban Land Institute (ULI), the American Institute of Architects (AIA), the Boulder Green Building Guild (BGBG), and the International Council of Shopping Centers (ICSC).

I am a regular contributor to the SEGD publication Messages (www.segd.org), examining the process behind developing and implementing complex architectural and EGD programs and has spoken at the organization’s annual conference numerous times.
