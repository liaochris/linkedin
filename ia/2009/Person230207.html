Ted Moreno’s Summary

I've been in private practice since 2003 with offices in South Pasadena and Glendora/Covina.
I'm an honors graduate from the Hypnosis Motivation Institute, the nation's first accredited college of hypnotherapy and a member of the American Hypnosis Association.
I have worked successfully with clients to help them quit smoking, lose weight, release fears and phobias, increase self esteem and self confidence, increase motivation, become more confident and competent public speakers and achieve greater business success.
My mission is to empower my clients to be the cause of transformation in their lives for greater health, happiness and fulfillment.

Ted Moreno’s Specialties:

I specialize in smoking cessation, managing/releasing anxiety and stress, and helping people reach greater levels of success in their busines and personal lifes, including public speaking. I am also available for public speaking engagments. I have presented to San Dimas and South Pasadena Rotary Clubs, Pasadena Chamber of Commerce, BNI Rosebowl Chapter, Cal State Los Angeles, and lectured at the Conscious Living Fair held in Pasadena. Check out my website at www.TedMoreno.com
