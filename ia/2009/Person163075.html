Michelle Munson-McCorry RN,CDP’s Summary

Believe the focus in home health care should always be on Person-Centered Planning: defined by Michigan Mental Health Code MCLA 330.1700 as the following: Modernizing Michigan Medicaid Long-Term Care
Person-Centered Planning is a process for planning and supporting the individual receiving services that builds upon the individual's capacity to engage in activities that promote community life and that honors the individual's preferences, choices, and abilities. The Person-Centered Planning process involves families, friends, and professionals as the individual desires or requires.Person-Centered Planning is what the state of Michigan is moving towards and is the future of home health care. Giving that quality throughout life's journey.At Complete Compassionate Care, I firmly believe that compassion is the very foundation of providing care, and compassion is what guides this agency. At Complete Compassionate Care, we strive to treat everyone the same, by treating them different. At all times, our goal is to improve our clients quality of life. I have instilled this key value within my company in the hopes of making a difference. Realizing that I'm only one small part of LTC (Long Term Care)I have served or currently serving on: Michigan Direct Care Workforce MDCWI; Co-chair Leadership for Change: looking at Culture Change, MI LTCSS:Workforce Development Work-Group of the LTCSS Commission ;Sub Work Groups incuiding , Data, Health-Care coverage for health-care workers ,LPN training, CNA Curriculum. Michigan Dementia Coalition ; Chair Dementia Competencies Workgroup that released the Guide" Knowledge and Skills Needed for Dementia Care A Guide for Direct Care Workers In Everyday Language" guide pub 2007, with has a self assessment tool to be released in spring. The dementia training manual that compliments the guide Pub.2008 all grant funded and available free of charge. All to try to help in the culture change in our state for LTC.
