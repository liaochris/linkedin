Andrew Graham’s Summary

Andrew Graham, a copy writer and media contact in New York, works with companies in the energy, capital markets, real estate, manufacturing, and technology sectors to produce meaningful content and get expert sources in front of the journalists who seek them.

He was previously a reporter and editor at a small-market newspaper in Illinois, where he accumulated more than 400 bylines and left as editor of the outlet’s special supplements, and spend one year managing the production of a quarterly newspaper for the public relations field that continues to be published by a nonprofit organization, the Public Relations Society of America.

He earned his undergraduate degree from Illinois State University and attended NEGOCIA, an international business school located in Paris. His writing has been syndicated by the College Media Network, and he has contributed to Mediabistro’s PRNewser blog.

Andrew Graham’s Specialties:

New media/blogs, global affairs, speeches/public addresses, media evaluation and measurement.
