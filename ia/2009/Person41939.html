Chef J. Hugh McEvoy’s Summary

Originally a professional “restaurant” chef; Chef J now writes a - syndicated - weekly newspaper column on food and fitness in Chicago.

He also teaches Culinary Science and New Product Development at prestigious Kendall College in Chicago.

J. is the President of the Chicago Research Chefs llc and President – Emeritus of the Chicago Nutrition Association. He is the winner of the prestigious RCA 2002 President’s Award for outstanding contributions to Culinology (TM) and was again named co-winner in 2003.

The chef grew up in a family of professional chefs, restaurateurs, and bakers. Most recently J helped found – and sits on the board of - a new chapter of the American Culinary Federation, the Windy City Chefs. Recently he has been inducted into the Chaine des Rotisseurs – formed in Paris in the year 1248 – the Chaine is the world’s oldest and most prestigious culinary society.

Chef J. Hugh McEvoy’s Specialties:

A graduate of Johnson & Wales University and numerous military and trade schools, the chef is a recognized expert in culinary art, new product development, both bakery and food science.

He completed classes at AIB, in Manhattan, Kansas in 1984. Currently J is finishing his MBA with Walden University. He is an internationally recognized Food Product Developer, Culinary Trend Expert and Food Writer.
