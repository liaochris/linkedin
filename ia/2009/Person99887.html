Tatsuzo Ishigami, Ph.D., FACB’s Summary

Tatsuzo Ishigami, Ph.D., FACB is working with two companies, STII (consultation) and WVP (VC). The principal contact is STII at there you can reach by sending e-mail to info@ stii.co.jp.

STI International is the consultation firm in Biotechnology and Medical technologies. The worldwide clients are able to enjoy its excellent comprehensive consultation services on business development and technology/product development, bridging of alliance, strategic research, and finance. STII's services are available through client-specific task force consisting from it's extensive network of world excellent consultants.

WVP is a special independent venture capital dedicated to "Bio" and is focusing to provide hands-on investment to early stage bio ventures.

Tatsuzo Ishigami, Ph.D. has the extensive experience in the businesses relating to the clinical diagnostics(IVD, POCT, MDx, PGx, etc), clinical laboratory testing(esoteric testing, multi-variate assay, etc.), pharmaceuticals, medical devices, and medical/bio informatics. Also Dr. Ishigami has the expertise in regenerative medicine, personalized medicine, and microarray/MEMS/Lab-on-a-chip businesses.
