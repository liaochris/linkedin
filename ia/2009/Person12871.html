Rolien Andaman Sea Club’s Summary

I have always enjoyed doing lots of different things in a project kind of way. As such i have travelled the world quite extensively. I have worked in 28 countries as tourmanager for artists, tourleader for tourist groups and diving instructor.
I have always come back a few times a year to my home city, Amsterdam. To relax in between jobs but also to do one of my favourite jobs ie working in the organisation of music events, mainly artist handling, tourmanaging and working for several tv productions.
As for now, i have found the destination where i want to stay.
Together with my man we have set up business on Phuket Thailand, the Andaman Sea Club, a sailing charter company.
Sailing the Andaman Sea and all the 7 regatta's in the area.
Our goal is to promote Thailand to the world as an excellent and upcoming sailing area. For cruising as well as racing in one of the 7 regattas in the area.
