Ajay Mahajan’s Summary

I have had ample opportunities to be involved in creating niche new concepts built around innovative requirements. My involvement in these projects has largely been focused on Conceptualizing, Technology definition, Development, Integration, Deployment & Management.

The above has been in the following sectors:
Transport – Banking – Defense – eGovernance –
Identification – Retail – Education

In the past few years – I have had the opportunity to lead some large and extensive projects in the eGovernance space. Most of these projects were multi-location and required ground-up management responsibilities. While my role in these projects has been that of leading and initiating these – the involvement across these projects would vary from project to project.

Besides this I have also been involved in the following activities:
:
• Was instrumental in setting up a country-wide network of alliances and project operations offices for these projects.
• During the course of my career as a Technology, Marketing and Management professional, was also a Domain Consultant to 3 International Smart Card Forums
• Served as a Technology Consultant to the country’s leading private banks for defining and architecting private banking, electronic purse & micro finance solutions with smart cards
• Have been a part of the core technology team constituted by the Registrar General of India (RGI) for defining the smart card technology solutions for the MNIC National ID card.
• Was also a part of the core technology team constituted by NIC for defining the SCOSTA Operating System specifications.
• An honorary Technology Consultant to the QMG branch, Army Headquarters.
