Rick Gallant’s Summary

I am an aggressive middle manager, capable of functioning at any level. I bring to the table a strong work ethic, a very diverse experiential background, with specific attachments to warehousing and distribution, transportation, customer service, supplier management, human resources, and manufacturing.

I began with Chrysler as a warehouse worker at the Mississauga national parts distribution centre. Most recently, I held operations responsibility for the inbound transportation and logistics network of the Mopar division of Chrysler LLC. I have also managed automotive inventory warehousing, distribution and customer service. For most of my career at Chrysler, I have been involved in supplier and employee training, related to materials control, logistics and transportation.
