Trevor Hammond’s Summary

Trevor Hammond is the owner of Makena Financial, a Financial Education company designed to Educate, Empower, and Inspire families and individuals to greater Financial Freedom and Safety.

Trevor has developed unique and powerful coaching processes to help clients gain greater clarity, direction, and confidence with their money. Trevor's ultimate goal is to help every family transform their relationship with money, and guide them on a journey that makes Financial Independence a reality. This leads to more freedom of both time and money, and a life of greater fulfillment.

*Trevor is also the creator, and CEO of MyFinancialIndependenceCoach.com, an online financial coaching program designed to help families simplify the path to financial independence. The Affiliate program allows other businesses, financial institutions, and professionals to license and provide to their clients as a completely turn-key way to change lives...with a personally branded website, and the flexibility of making money or giving away for free.(http://www.MyFinancialIndependenceCoach.com/mfs)

*The Financial Independence Journey workshop is a Group Coaching program that provides the tools and education on how to define and achieve true Financial Independence to lead a life of greater fulfillment. It is a 60-day "Bootcamp" to transform attendees financial future...and life.
This workshop is available for public and private groups.
(learn more at www.makenafinancial.com)


For more information regarding personal coaching or to attend an upcoming educational workshop, email trevor@makenafinancial.com.
