Jennifer Clark’s Summary

I have been a Strategic Attraction Coach for the past 7 years faciliating over 6000 consultations for people who are sincerely interested in expanding their human potential.

As an Agent of Change, I intuitively offer my clients personally tailored tools for change that provide successful results.

As a Life Strategist, I support people who are in transition by providing action plans to better navigate their personal and professional lives.

As a Creative Catalyst, I offer my clients unique, innovative and inspiring ideas and solutions that creates expansion and optimum results.

As a Canadian Certified Risk Manager and Turn Around Specialist, my intuitive and concrete business advice allows my clients the tools, information and strategy to turn any situation around for the better.

As a Professional Spiritual Teacher, I allow my clients to understand the metaphysicals laws of the cosmos so that they can fully understand the energy of money, love, wellness, and success allowing them to fully express their human and spiritual potentials.

I am the co-creator of Mimi The Angel, a loveable inspirational children's symbol of hope and positive action. I am a children's book author having co-written two books, Mimi The Angel Gets Her Wings and Mimi The Angel Visits The Hospital sharing messages of empowerment and hope.

For 7 years, I have hosted and produced a radio show called "The Namaste Show: Good News For A Change" on CKCU FM radio interviewing inspirational authors like Mark Victor Hansen, Bob Proctor, T. Harv Eker, Wayne Dyer, David Suzuki, Dr. Deepak Chopra, Doreen Virtue, Debbie Ford, James Redfield, James Twyman, Neale Donald Walsch and others.

May 2008, I am launching an engaging and dynamic internet talk show called Divine Contact TV interviewing inspirational authors, practitioners and healers in order to de-mystify the mystical and reveal the secrets of the magical to inspire humanity to its pure potential.
