Yana Berlin’s Summary

After juggling a career, family and aspiring to be an artist, I learned early on that the only way to achieve my goals was through the support of family and girlfriends. After learning that not all of the women were as fortunate to have many girlfriends, I embarked on a journey to create a social network for women forty and over. Creating a social network for women that catalyzes its members to celebrate and embrace their life became my passion. Since the launch of www.fabulously40.com I have been connecting and supporting women all over the world.
