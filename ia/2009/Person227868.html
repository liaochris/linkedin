Stephen Webster’s Summary

Stephen Webster is SVP of Channel Sales at SRC, a leading provider of business intelligence technology delivering geographic business intelligence solutions to Fortune 500 companies. SRC delivers applications for data storage, retrieval, management, reporting and analysis of both spatial and non spatial data.

Stephen has 25 years in direct marketing, market research and consumer packaged goods experience with industry leading companies. Stephen's career has centered on key leadership roles in sales, marketing, marketing research, customer service and operations.

Before joining SRC, Stephen was President at AccuData Integrated Marketing where he delivered a an 80% improvement in EBITDA performance

Prior to AccuData, Stephen was President of MarketModels, a division of AccuData Integrated Marketing, where he was responsible for the strategic, operational and financial aspects of the business. .

Prior to MarketModels, Stephen was COO for Tactician Corporation a national provider of integrated sales and marketing solutions to develop and execute geographically targeted resource and investment decisions

Prior to Tactician, Stephen was the Division President of Information Resources, a national provider of innovative business intelligence solutions to the consumer package goods (CPG) industry.

Stephen's career began with Pepsi where he held positions in finance, marketing, strategy development, sales and general management.

Stephen earned a BBA in accounting and an MBA in Financial Management from Iona College. He is a board member of the Boxford Cable TV non-profit organization in his home town of Boxford, MA.
