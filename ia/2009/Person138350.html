  • Owner & Production Consultant

    streamLineD

    (Events Services industry)

    July 2008 — Present (1 year)

    Laurie Davis launched her production career at Cramer, a digital marketing and event solutions company headquartered in Massachusetts, as a staff associate producer. She learned the ins and outs of the industry at an accelerated pace: by doing. In just two months, she was producing regional commercials and industrials on her own. She was quickly promoted to producer and her work evolved, encompassing national commercials, web casts, large-scale corporate conferences, live satellite broadcasts, and tradeshows. Laurie's passion transcends production; she also prizes her intellectual curiosity for exploring new businesses and industries. Accordingly, her clients spanned verticals and included: PricewaterhouseCoopers, The Gillette Company, Bayer Diagnostics, Bose, Business Objects, Idenix Novartis, EMC, AVID, Enterasys, Infor, and Citizens Bank.

    In 2006, Laurie left Cramer to pursue freelance opportunities in New York City, and focus on the projects about which she was most passionate. Laurie continues to freelance as a producer, and has added a new trade to the mix: production consultant. As a production consultant, Laurie joins a company's internal marketing team for live events. She supervises all production elements and manages the vendor. In conjunction with her client, she manages the budget, media, scripts and content, speakers/entertainment, contracts, and project timelines. She develops programming, writes content, and takes care of all the odds and ends that surface during a large-scale production!

    Laurie is based in New York, though many of her clients are not. She works remotely as a consultant, traveling as required. This flexibility allows Laurie to best serve the project and maintain a valuable, big-picture view often lost in the day-to-day interaction of an on-site team. Above all, Laurie fuses her knack for production with her marketing savvy to help her clients achieve an indelible event.
