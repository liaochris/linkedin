Tim Bailey’s Summary

Space advocate and education specialist creating opportunities to use space exploration as a catalyst for personal growth and development in educators, students, and young professionals.

Continually working to create and expand "space culture" through various channels including clothing design and media.

Crew for parabolic "weightless" flights for the public and researchers in the US.

Open to collaboration and consulting opportunities around aerospace events.
