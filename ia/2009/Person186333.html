Pete Walter’s Summary

I have a day job and a night job.

My day job is running the media production company Tap Bang (tapbang.com). We specialise in making video for the web. We're a young company but already we've clocked up credits for major food brands, cutting edge charities and award winning PR companies.

I make it my business to keep up-to-date with the very latest advances in web video which I blog about at tapbang.wordpress.com. I'm also the founder of the 'Web Video for Business Group' here on linked-in.

Personally I have a unique range of production experience from 6 six years spent in the television industry. I produced factual entertainment, documentaries, drama and new media and have credits for the BBC, Channel 4, Five and MTV amongst others. During my time in television production I was selected by Broadcast Magazine as one of the top people under 30 to watch in the TV industry.

I am a fully trained self shooting director and an experienced video editor.

My night job involves showing off. I'm a singer songwriter (www.myspace.com/petewaltermusic) and a history-specialist TV presenter.
