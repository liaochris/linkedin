María Feijoo’s Summary

Working at Antevenio Communications Department, my role is the development and execution of communication plans; writing and editing articles, press releases and other corporate documents, media pitches, response to media requests; etc. I have a particular focus on corporate issues and the development of new media as personal and corporate communication channels.

The company provides top-tier advertisers in Spain and Italy with innovative online brand-building, customer acquisiton, and customer loyalty solutions through four services: advertising, e-mail campaigns, lead management, and affiliate marketing.
