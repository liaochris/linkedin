Suzette West [LION]’s Summary

An entrepreneurial spirit is the common thread that runs through the years of my professional experience. I have always felt driven towards making a difference. Whether it means bringing balance between buyer and seller representation in the real estate industry or making a way for music entrepreneurs to grow sustainable music enterprises in the new digital age.

In summary, my experience includes:

Twelve years of real estate experience specializing in buyer agency, and five years dedicated to exclusive buyer agency.

Thirty years experience as a musician, five years experience as a music entrepreneur.

Overall, my passion in life is to nurture the growth and development of entrepreneurship in the recording industry, promote buyer representation in the real estate industry, seek and pursue continual personal development and education, and build a network of accomplished professionals.

Suzette West [LION]’s Specialties:

On the music side: Music entrepreneur, production, live performances, and songwriting for my band Legend Heart, as well as co-producer of the True Independent Music Show podcast.

Commercial buyer agency, market area analysis, pre-purchase consulting and planning, negotiating and money-saving strategies, seamless transaction management, buyer representation, as well as site selection facilitation and assistance for development projects.
