Colin Bertie’s Summary

GENERAL EXPERIENCE
Colin is an experienced Accredited Coach who also specialises in working with groups in a variety of settings. These include the facilitation of ‘Action Learning’ sets (150+ meetings so far), Outside Learning Environment (OLE), Coaching Support Groups and skills assessment within both Diploma and MBA programmes. He has published Learning Guides on ‘Action Learning’, ‘Introversion at Work’ (strategies for Introverts and Extraverts working more effectively), and ‘Communicating Bad News’..

His early experience was as an engineer with IBM and the BBC, followed by a series of Regional Service roles in the office equipment arena. He then switched focus to a number of People Development roles. He spent over 8 years in Nepal as a Cross-Cultural Management Developer of 100+ Nepali and 100+ expatriates from 19 nationalities. He completed his MSc in Training and HRM from Leicester University by distance learning while living in Nepal. He was a co-presenter at both the 2007 (Stockholm) and 2008 (Prague) European Mentoring and Coaching Council's (EMCC) annual conferences on research into critical moments in coaching. See http://www.emccouncil.org/eu/

SPECIFIC EXPERIENCE OF COACHING
As an Accredited Coach, he has found himself working with over 700 people in a variety of coaching relationships, at all organisational levels within the private, public and voluntary sectors. He has a client-centred, integrative, relational coaching approach informed by both Appreciative Inquiry and Solution-Focus. He is qualified and experienced in the application of a wide range of psychometric instruments, including MBTI, INSIGHTS, FIRO-B, SDI, OPQ32 and a variety of 360 degree instruments, and adheres to a Code of Conduct for Coaches. He is also licensed to use Career Counselling Services (CCS) materials.

Regularly meeting with other trained professionals to maintain his grown in best practice. He aims is to model effective behaviours for his clients.

Colin Bertie’s Specialties:

Coaching (Executive, Careers, All-round, etc), Group Facilitation, Action learning, Cross-Cultural Learning, Accredited Use of Psychometric Instruments (MBTI, FIRO-B, ECI, etc. and a range of 360-degree tools)
