Richard Wilson’s Summary

I am a hedge fund consultant, guest speaker and writer. I run the Hedge Fund Group (HFG), HedgeFundBlogger.com and CHA Designation Program. My background and current work is specialized within the areas of hedge fund capital raising, prime brokerage and startups..

I am currently finishing a thesis for my second graduate degree. This program is at Harvard University where I am earning a degree in the Psychology of Influence & Persuasion. My past education includes financial modeling and an MBA with a focus on Finance & Marketing.

I have written two books. The first was completed while earning my MBA and it is focused on Negotiation, Relationship Cultivation and Sales (Rainmaker.ws). The second is a hedge fund blog book that is a work in progress but can be downloaded right now at http://hedgefundsbook.com.

Please visit my blog at http://hedgefundblogger.com or http://richard-wilson.blogspot.com. You may also join the Hedge Fund Group (HFG) for free at http://HedgeFundGroup.org.
