Gregg Sauer’s Summary

I have 25 years in the mechanical engineering and manufacturing field and the disciplines I have worked diligently most recently is dust control, sheet metal design, material handling, structural design and process equipment. I have a thorough knowledge of manufacturing and machining processes.
