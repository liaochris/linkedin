Syarul Izam Sarifudin’s Summary

A year and half experienced, since Oct 2007, with Federal Agency Authority (Iskandar Regional Development Authority – IRDA) in managing Federal Government projects under 9th Malaysia Plan (2006-2010). IRDA is directly under the Prime Minister Office, Government of Malaysia.
Works had now comes to the implementation stages with RM2.0billion (USD 0.7billion) worth of projects had been awarded.

I have been 9 years in the Ports and Maritime industry in Malaysia, with managing the maritime engineering projects from light beacon, jetties, marina, dredging and reclamation works, port development and infrastructure works.
Highly experienced in managing complex maritime projects which involving full project management from business analysis to design, cost control, planning, construction and completion with track record of on-time and on-budget.
