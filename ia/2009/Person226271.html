Michael Friedman’s Summary

I am a process analyst and solution designer with direct experience in the commercial, legal, and publishing industries.

I focus on:
BUSINESS PROCESS REENGINEERING: working with businesses to analyze, document, design, and improve existing processes. This often involves merging process with technology via standards-based methods and automation.

SINGLE-SOURCE CONTENT DESIGN: designing answers to common process issues on how to create content once and deploy it to multiple media markets (print, online, etc).

OUTSOURCE MANAGEMENT: managing and driving the interactions between local or customer needs and offshore development teams to achieve process goals.

Michael Friedman’s Specialties:

I specialize in process design and technology integration design for publishers. Customers tell me I see their "world" very quickly and have practical designs which can be implemented well. I am a strategic, abstract thinker.
