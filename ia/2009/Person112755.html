Jill Tirone’s Summary

VAMatrix is your Virtual Business Partner! We Make Your Business Successful Without You Having To Do All The Work!

We provide virtual marketing and administrative support to dance studios, fitness centers, dance companies, and small businesses. We help our clients reach their goals by providing assistance during busy seasons as well as with the day-to-day operation of their businesses.

Based in Buffalo, New York, this virtual assistance practice is owned and operated by Jill Tirone.

For over 20 years, Jill has built a solid reputation as a dedicated, hard-working professional. Combining a B.A. in Dance Education, an A.A.S. in Office Technology, and years of teaching, marketing, and administrative experience, she has amassed significant knowledge and practical experience.

The blending of her artistic and administrative backgrounds gives her a unique advantage. As a dance teacher, Jill knows what it takes to schedule classes, run rehearsals, and direct shows. She also knows how hard it is to handle all the details during those times.

Jill’s expertise includes, but is not limited to, creating and executing marketing plans, design and implementation of websites and marketing materials, human resources management, and general business management and administration.

Through her attention to detail, strong organizational skills, and get-it-done attitude, Jill has helped numerous companies and individuals reach their goals.

Jill Tirone’s Specialties:

We specialize in providing virtual marketing and administrative support to dance studios, fitness centers, dance companies, and small businesses.

Areas of expertise include Brochures, Business Cards, and Flyers; Website Creation, Updates & Maintenance; Blog Creation, Administration, and Maintenance; Advertisements; Press Releases; Montly Newsletters; Recital Theme & Logo Design; Recital & Performance Programs; and More!
