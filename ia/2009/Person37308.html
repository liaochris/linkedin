Carin Campanario’s Summary

Flash(Lite)Developer + UI Designer + Product Manager, with over 14 years’ experience in creating compelling products for e.g. TV, mobile, web, desktop and CD-Rom, for renowned brands like Sony, BBC, Orange and Nokia, in 5 European countries (the Netherlands, Spain, Germany, France and UK).

Proven full life cycle knowledge and multi-disciplinary skills.

Passionate about new mobile interaction methods (with the accelerometer, augmented reality, etc.) and playful products (by preference for children) that are useful, reliable, easy and accessible.

Enthusiastic, inquisitive, creative, versatile, resourceful, reliable, analytical, meticulous, sociable, frank, Dutch and Spanish, London based.

Now seeking next rewarding job opportunity in a successful and fun London team.
