Colin Davies’s Summary

I have over 14 years experience in advising and managing organisations at a senior level across a multitutde of sectors throughout South Yorkshire and East Midlands. I have a passion to see, and be a part of ensuring, positive change and sustainable growth in those organisations within which I work. I am pragmatic in my approach and results oriented in my outlook.
I now work with various organisations and with social enterprises and the third sector. I work mainly with senior management and teams to plan and create the appropriate business infrastructure for today's success and future sustainability.
