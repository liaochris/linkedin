  • Director, Client Services

    IQ Partners Inc.

    (Partnership; Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    October 2009 — Present (1 month)

    We founded IQ Partners on the belief that our business could be done better, smarter and with greater professionalism. We started as a small executive search firm building exceptional teams for companies in marketing, communications, media and technology. We developed a strong reputation of success and we became extremely good at identifying and assessing 'smart people'.

    More than just head-hunters.

    Of course we still do Executive Search - but we've grown. From Resource Planning and Managed Recruitment to Contract HR and Outplacement, we now offer a full range of services to help entrepreneurial organizations manage their people more effectively. Not only can we help you improve your team, we can often save you up to 50% of your traditional resourcing costs. And because we interview thousands of people each year, we have the insight to provide you with counsel on everything from organizational design to market positioning and bus-dev strategies.

    Experts at Qualification & Assessment.

    We specialize in people. Our consultants come from corporate backgrounds and each has strong knowledge of their industry sector - but our real strength is our understanding of "people." Each of our consultants interviews over 1,000 individuals per year. This gives us a unique perspective. We can tell you who's good, who's not, and why. And we're savvy to the intangible soft-skills that aren't always evident on a resume. Our clients find our objectivity and insight invaluable and we utilize formal assessment tools for additional perspective.
