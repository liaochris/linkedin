Jim Jackson’s Summary

Jim Jackson is a speaker, author and coach who lives and teaches sales professionals the Harley Attitude… going as hard as you can, with all you can, as far as you can. It’s all about Confidence, Belief & Performance. He teaches salespeople how to exceed their quotas, how to work smarter in penetrating their market, and how to be focused on bottom-line results. Period!

For more than 25 years, Jim has gone into and coached a variety of businesses. He has helped average sales people turn into sales leaders and disorganized groups into performance teams. His message, along with time-tested sets of skills and techniques, can give you and your people the leadership tools necessary to thrive in this ever-changing global economy.

Jim is currently president of the Temecula Creek Learning Center, a corporate teambuilding and leadership development facility. Prior to Temecula Creek Learning Center he was the Vice President of Sales and Marketing for the Pecos River Learning Center in New Mexico. Throughout his career, and since 1994, Jim has been a lead sales performance coach.
