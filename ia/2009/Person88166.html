Stephen Goldsmith’s Summary

With over 20 years of experience in the Media, Entertainment and Internet industries, have held management level positions in sales and marketing as well as completed a variety of successful consulting engagements. I have a background in the creative side of the M&E vertical and have marketed and sold hardware, software, services and internet specific brands into this space. Career objective is to further the integration of new technologies into the M&E vertical, creating new work flows and efficiencies, driving revenue and creating opportunities for multiple revenue streams. Having experience that covers the gamut, from acquisition to distribution, has served to help realize many of my career goals.

Stephen Goldsmith’s Specialties:

Managing sales teams/departments while creating and executing convergent digital workflow models to enhance sales.
Working with channel partners to deliver results when applicable.
Product development, marketing and implementation.
Understanding how disparate elements can fit together to create elegant solutions.
