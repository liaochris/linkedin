Michelle DaPolito’s Summary

Michelle joined FX Solutions in October 2007. Her team is responsible for eMarketing and analytics for FX Solutions' 9 websites which are geographically targeted. She works closely with the SEM / SEO and display agencies to maximize ROI across top tier search engines and industry specific / contextually relevant websites.

Through internal & external analytic sources, Michelle manages the agency partnerships and campaign performance, fine tuning media placements and creative. All the while, keeping an eye on growth opportunities.

Prior to FX Solutions, Michelle was the Director of Marketing at TACODA. For a short period of time, Michelle was a Senior Marketing Manager for Lending Tree and before that she worked over three years as the Sales Promotion Manager at NYTimes.com where she developed big idea pitches, produced client facing events and helped in analyzing overall sales performance. She received her Bachelors in Marketing from Rochester Institute of Technology in Rochester, NY.

Michelle is an energetic, dedicated, hard-working individual that is always full of ideas. She finishes projects on time and on budget with a smile. Bringing tremendous energy to any job, Michelle keeps both her internal team and stakeholders excited about working on the project at hand.
