Khrys Vaughan’s Summary

Khrys Vaughan is the founder of Her Startup, the company that helps women launch, grow, and give back, and Executive Producer of Launch Notes, a radio show highlighting diverse women business owners. Khrys is a Certified PMP and has worked with various Telecommunication companies since 1987. Khrys' volunteer service includes Web Coordinator for the Hampton Roads Civic Ballet, Board Member of The Bread Ministry, an organization feeding 18,000 people each month, and more recently Connections to Success, Women Build-St. Louis, and the SBA Small Business Week-St. Louis. On a personal note, Khrys enjoys racing cars, is married and has four children.
