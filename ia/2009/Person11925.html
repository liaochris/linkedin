Amruth B R’s Summary

I once thought I would sit and crack equations to unify different concepts we know in Physics. Guess I lost myself in the joy of unifying patterns in all the mysteries we know of the human mind. I'm now riding my soul as a Cofounder of Vita peracta, a startup which offers virtual personality solutions in recruitment and employee management. We work on a cool algorithm we built to duplicate the decision making pathways in the human brain. Our business revolves around using this technology to offer a very different and efficient solution to the HR problems in today's industry.

I've normally been extra enthusiastic to take up roles of developing new ideas and implementation schemes, as well as corporate negotiations to sell our idea. But like in any startup, the best part has been learning to do a whole world of things ranging from operations to fund-raising.

Before Vita Peracta, I was the President of Center for Entrepreneurial Leadership [CEL], BITS Pilani - one of India's top 5 entrepreneurship cells and the Cofounders of National Entrepreneurship Network [NEN]. It was one of the most memorable experiences I've had with almost 4 years of constant exposure to building professional networks, conceptualizing and implementing new initiatives & strategic development of professional events. Did I mention corporate negotiations!

Once upon a time, was also into developing new prototypes in different things like shock absorbers, magnetic levitation circuits and near field dipole interactions. Have won NASA award and quite a few National Research Fellowships for those things [KVPY, IAS, NIUS et al]. Right now putting all those ideas to do some creative stuff at Vita Peracta.
