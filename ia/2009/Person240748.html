Uwe Jerg, CMII’s Summary

Strategic forward-looking electro-mechanical manufacturing professional focused on making a difference and delivering results that have an immediate impact on the bottom line. Respected, influential, and decisive business manager who fosters a corporate wide approach to team building. Reducing costs, improving profit margins, increasing quality and accuracy, exceeding customer expectations, proactively communicating with customers, and meeting delivery requirements are all specialties of mine, which ensure customer satisfaction is upheld and repeat follow-on business is realized. Eliminating waste, identifying and deactivating non value-add activities, and lifting processes out of the corrective action mode by focusing on what is important to run and grow the business.
