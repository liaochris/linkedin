Stephen A. Parisi’s Summary

PARICON INC. is an innovator in the arena of engineering and IT personnel consulting, but our services reach far beyond placement. Our hands-on approach to consulting and recruiting is committed to connecting our clients with the engineering and IT personnel that have common needs, goals, and interests in mind. This approach provides our clients with employment security in a industry where such fundamental ideals have been long forgotten.

Our services include:

• Permanent, Contract-To-Hire, and Consulting engagements specializing in the Engineering and IT fields, and working extensively in the Aerospace realm, mostly associated with Department of Defense contracted corporations.

• Outsourcing for business needs, such as Employer/Employee benefits.

Stephen A. Parisi’s Specialties:

Paricon Inc. has advanced experience in recruiting, networking, alternative dispute resolution (mediation, arbitration, negotiation), and revenue saving outsourcing.
