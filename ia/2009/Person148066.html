Manuel Quezon III’s Summary

I was born in 1970 and currently host a weekly program on the ANC cable news channel, “The Explainer.” I have been an opinion and editorial writer and editor since 1994. At present I am a columnist for the Arab News Newspaper; head of the Speaker’s Bureau, columnist, and an editorial writer for The Philippine Daily Inquirer newspaper; and Assistant Managing Editor and editorial writer for The Philippines Free Press weekly news magazine. I have received various awards for my professional work, including being named “Opinion Writer of the Year” in 1994 and 2005 by the Rotary Club of Manila’s Journalism Awards, the oldest journalism awards in the country.
