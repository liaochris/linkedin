Michael Malloy, Ph.D.’s Summary

A lifetime of diverse experience, in international settings and in the use of Internet technology, places me at the crossroads of the Global, Internet, Economy in the 21st Century. This allows me to pursue my passion, and "mission", to help people achieve their personal and business goals in today's globalized, Internet-driven era.

Currently leading a team of motivated, experienced executives who are taking IntellaCore around the world. We are consultants and service providers in the area of international business and the use of Internet and communications technology. We work with business leaders to transform their companies into globally competitive businesses.

International: Extensive experience traveling, studying, and working around the world my entire life.

Internet: Started participating in internet services companies in 1997. Owned and grew a telecommunication and internet solution company.

Managed and supervised two academic centers for international studies and business outreach.

Participate in US government affiliated Non-Profit, the District Export Councils, to help businesses export and to be globally active.

15 years of experience and training to train and to advise business owners.
