Kelley Connors’s Summary

All healthcare. All health and wellness. Pharma, medical device, life science, wellness, public health and assisted living marketing and communications coach and consultant with 20-year history in healthcare industry. I use my passion for helping clients as fuel for resourcefulness, initiative, creative problem solving to ignite media, PR and marketing campaigns.

If you have a new business idea and need to create a marketing package around it, or are looking to re-invent your services to meet an aging population, the demands of women as chief health officers, or shift the healthcare paradigm towards wellness, we need to talk.

My strengths are program planning, facilitating workshops, brand storytelling, media contacts, industry know-how and real world marketing savvy. Success comes with tangible media, customer experience and business results.

Prior to forming my healthcare marketing consultancy, my experience focused on serving pharmaceutical company clients included Berlex, Sanofi Winthrop, Kodak, Eli Lilly, Serono, Bausch & Lomb, & Pharmacia in both domestic and global capacities.

Contact me via Linked In to see how I can help your business succeed and thrive in this new environment.
