Victoria Generao’s Summary

At present, I work with a plaintiff/personal injury solo practitioner (medical and legal malpractice case management). My past law work experience includes criminal prosecution and jury trial experience with the Solano County District Attorney's Office in Vallejo, CA, and experience handling and litigating domestic violence issues arising in family law matters involving indigent battered women and molested children in San Francisco. I have been commissioned as a California Notary Public since 1979.

In 2007, I consulted on human resources management, administrative procedures and contracts administration concerns for a creative inventor's coop company based in Northern California (Solano County).

I continue as a practicing Ordained Minister and Certified Reiki Grandmaster-Teacher focused on providing individualized counseling as well as offering ministerial services on a one-on-one basis as well as within a corporate culture. I specialize in work-related stress, womens’ issues and depression. I am interested in academic teaching work and advising corporations on alternative healthcare modalities for their employees.

Victoria Generao’s Specialties:

Grassroots community organizer; activist and fundraiser for non-profits specializing in education and human services; public relations; networking; communication (oral and written).
