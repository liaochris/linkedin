Niamh Pedreschi’s Summary

Director at Pedreschi Electrical & Alarms Limited since the company was established in 1993. I take care of the adminstration and telesales side of the business.

We are a small family run company, we take good care of our biggest asset - thats our customers. RECI Contractor of the Year 2009, Domestic Section, Dublin Region.

We carry out the installation and maintenance of electrical and alarm installations. We are keeping up to date with new technology and sustainable engery in order to give the very best service to our customers, both new and existing.

We are EQA Certified, PSA licenced and are fully insured. We are Approved Installers of the Smarthomes range of products.
