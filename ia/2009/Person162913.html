Michelle Clarke’s Summary

Based in Cape Town, South Africa, I deliver successful coaching projects both locally and internationally.

I work with High-Achieving Business Owners, Coaches and Executives, delivering international-best-practice bespoke coaching interventions designed to help my clients to focus, hone and develop their personal and professional identities. I combine my knowledge and experience of coaching, personal leadership, yoga and astrology to provide coaching partnerships that provide a real opportunity to explore the whole person.

Together, client and coach design and re-engineer sustainable and impactful personal and professional strategies. Real Results = Real Behavior Change.

Backed by a Diploma in Practitioner Coaching and Master Coach qualification with the Behavioural Coaching Institute of Europe, my personalized coaching meets the needs and aspirations of my clients; goal-driven and committed High-Achievers.

Presently, I also head up the National Marketing for COMENSA (Coaches & Mentors of South Africa). I am a member of the South African Chamber of Commerce and The International Coaching Council (ICC)
