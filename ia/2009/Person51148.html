Corinna Steeb’s Summary

Corinna Steeb is the Executive Officer of BioAngels Inc and the Managing Director of Rubys CBS.

Rubys CBS specialize in executive contracting, project management, business advice and strategy implementation and business growth. Rubys team of professionals has extensive expertise across many Industry sectors incl. Life Sciences, Technology, Food & Beverage, Tourism, Pharmaceuticals & Neutraceuticals, Cosmetics, Engineering & Manufacturing. With a strong career background in executive management our core competencies are general business management, development and commercialisation of technology in the Life Sciences space and engineering, system development and implementation, new product development and value added products and services delivery. We assist start-ups and work with privately owned small to medium sized business on growth and expansion
