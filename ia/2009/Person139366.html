Eduardo Legatti’s Summary

System Analyst, Database Administrator, and Configuration Manager in a wide variety of business applications. Particularly interested in client/server, 3 tier applications and relational database design using Oracle.

I've been working with Oracle Database since version 8.0.5. In my point of view, to get an Oracle certification is a valuable and important credential.

Basically, I got the certification because I really like to work as a Oracle DBA, managing Oracle Databases. Currently, I have a blog site at http://www.eduardolegatti.blogspot.com/ (For a while in Brazilian Portuguese) not only to spread Oracle Database technology in my city, country, but also to share and expose what I have learned in all these years.

In addition, I'm a active member of OTN Forums at http://forums.oracle.com that is a great place for help other people and share experiences and knowledges with other members. I'm also member of Oracle Community at http://www.oraclecommunity.net/profile/EduardoLegatti
