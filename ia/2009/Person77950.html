Faren Inglett’s Summary

I am highly invested in science, health, and helping people, ultimately striving towards a global medical career. I currently work with an amazing start-up company: www.curetogether.com that bridges the gap between the medical community, researchers, and patients. Through open-access information such as treatment trials, anonymous patient databases, symptom charting, and correlation tools, we are opening up the world to further medical advancement.
I have a broad-range of international experience in a diversity of settings from biotechnology to public relations, social campaigning, environmental services, and community outreach. I am motivated by developing business with a dynamic team and inspired by the success of change. I bring a high level of ambition, innovative thinking, and leadership skills to the table.
I'm an entrepreneur at heart, and ultimately one day plan to field my own businesses surrounding some of the most immediate and life-threatening challenges our world faces: Health/malnutrition, extreme poverty, and education.
