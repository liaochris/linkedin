AmiLynne Carroll’s Summary

WHAT IS COST SEGREGATION? Cost Segregation is an IRS-approved application by which commercial property owners can accelerate depreciation and reduce the amount of taxes owed.

Our Engineers are singularly FOCUSED on cost segregation studies; our goal is to support your CPA or tax advisor with the most comprehensive & accurate cost segregation study results so you can realize maximum savings and increased cash flow.

We offer a COMPLIMENTARY CONSULTATION for your commercial property or commercial renovations.

AFFORDABLE Whether the cost basis is $150,000 or $180 Million, we utilize a method that is affordable for your commercial property application.

COMPLIANT Our engineering background allows us to provide the best possible results; bolstered and supported by out staff of CPAs, tax attorneys and real estate professionals ensuring strict adherence to all IRS guidelines and recommendations. RUI/CSSI is classified as an independent engineering specialist meeting the exact specifications stated by the IRS in regard to commercial property owners applying cost segregation applications.

PARTNERING OPPORTUNITITES Working with a variety of CPA Firms and other real estate professionals nationwide, we offer a variety of Partnering Opportunities.

FOCUS ON MARKET EDUCATION From basic to advanced application, RUI / CSSI works with individual building owners as well as offering CE programs and workshops . RUI has been honored to speak for a number of local, regional and national associations and continues to focus on each opportunity with our education first philosophy.

There is no investment that allows for a greater or a more immediate return on investment than the application of COST SEGREGATION.
