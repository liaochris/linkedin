Jason Fielding’s Summary

The Sound Campaign is a music partnership and production agency - we bring brands and music together in content, TV, online, live events. current projects and Clients include Jack Daniels and The JD Set, Future Entertainment and Warner Music. We also promote events and promote Sidetracked, a music festival at the Australian F1 Grand Prix.

Prior to setting up The Sound Campaign, I set up the music divisions of Octagon in London and Sydney. Octagon is the world's largest sports marketing agency, specialising in sponsorship consulting and events. at Octagon, I was involved with Vodafone and Manchester United, I was the TV producer for the F1 gig in the streets of London and was executive producer of the F1 drive on Sydney Harbour Bridge.
