Scott Hacker’s Summary

Let us help you reach today's active youth between the ages of 6-13 years old. We will take your brand to youth sporting events around the United States and interact face-to-face with the players, family members, and fans. Our traveling FANFEST interactive mobile tours reach millions of kids each year during the baseball, soccer, and football seasons, creating long lasting impressions and direct sales. Our sponsors include some of the best in the business.

Some clients include: ABC Network / Disney Channel (3rd year), Major League Baseball Players Association (4th year), Topps (4th year), Upper Deck (4th year), Sports Illustrated KIDS (2nd year), Airheads (2nd year), Backyard Sports (3rd year), Louisville Slugger (3rd year), Disney's Wide World of Sports (2nd year), Major League Soccer (1st year), California Cuties (1st year), Disney XD (1st year) and Activision / Little League World Series (1st year).

Scott Hacker’s Honors:

Community Project: Currently building the Baseball Youth Dream Park nestled near the Daniel Boone National Forest and Cave Run Lake, Eastern Kentucky’s major tourism attraction that draws over a million visitors a year. Youth baseball teams from all over the nation are able to participate in tournaments, camps and clinics. When completed, the facility will feature three unique youth size fields, one coach pitch/teeball field, and training facility. Field 1 is already completed, while fields 2-4 are scheduled to be complete in 2009.

The Baseball Youth Dream Park will allow today’s youth an opportunity to play on professional looking youth fields in Eastern Kentucky, an area in need of more youth baseball fields. To learn more, visit www.baseballyouthdreampark.com.
