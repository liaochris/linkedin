Genevieve Anton’s Summary

Anton Communications’ approach to media relations is based on 20+ years of award-winning experience in newspapers, television and radio. We create promotional and media opportunities for our clients, instead of waiting for them to happen. We know how to find what's interesting in what you do and turn it into a story for any type of publication or program. We have developed excellent working relationships with producers, editors and reporters across the country who have come to trust our standards and reliability.
Our track record speaks for itself: Since its inception in 2000, Anton Communications has placed more than 2,750 client stories in major media outlets worldwide ranging from The New York Times and USA Today to Cable News Network (CNN) and the British Broadcasting Service (BBC). Our work has won numerous awards for media relations, consumer awareness, issues management and creative writing.
Best of all for your bottom line, as independent consultants we provide expertise when and where you need it most. We are available 24 hours a day, seven days a week, to assist you with issues, crisis management and other urgent needs.

Genevieve Anton’s Specialties:

Anton Communications Inc. is a California-based strategic public relations firm serving a broad list of clients regionally and nationwide. Our specialties include real estate development, land use & environmental issues, public affairs, finance & investment, hospitality & tourism, litigation support, small business and professional services.
