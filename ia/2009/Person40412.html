Cezanne Huq’s Summary

Cezanne is Director of Search Marketing at Experian Interactive Media where consumers are connected with products and services that improve the way they live, learn and manage their finances.

Prior to Experian Interactive Media, Cézanne served as Senior Director of Vertrue, where he oversaw Online Marketing, Paid Search and SEO initiatives for all Vertrue businesses. Based in Norwalk, CT, Vertrue (VTRU) is an Internet Marketing Services company that leverages its products and partners to market via the Internet using cutting edge online marketing solutions.

Prior to joining Vertrue, Cezanne lead the Search Optimization Services group at DigitalGrit where he developed world class SEO and SEM services geared towards Fortune 500 clients that included ADP, Intuit, Sothebys International Realty, Reuters, etc.

Cézanne was formerly senior vice president of Connors Communications. He has eleven years of strategic planning in emerging technology start-ups to enterprise level experience, and ten years in Public Relations. At Connors Communications, Cézanne leveraged his expertise in technology, PR, Search Engine Marketing as well as broader online marketing solutions, overseeing Client services through hands on advice and counsel in the areas of online acquisition, competitive awareness, due diligence technology efficacy, and ensuring the best possible quality and results are met for Connors Communications’ clients. With regards to online marketing, he has conceived and developed solutions such as SEO, Personalized Search Engine pages, Corporate Weblogs, micro-affiliate networks, mini-sites and other grassroots programs geared to maximize online public relations initiatives that has served clients such Asera, XOsoft, GoTo (Now Yahoo Search Marketing), Educational Testing Service (ETS), Priceline.com, Powerllel Corporation, Vonage, Amazon and Evolution Robotics, just to name a few.
