Dieter De Duytschaever’s Summary

As a Master in Interactive Media I see myself as a creative and ethusiastic person with a large skillset computer-whise and a multimedia all-rounder.

I'm especially interested in Flash Development (AS 2.0, AS 3.0, Flash Lite and streaming server technology) because I believe the role of Flash applications and projects will only grow larger with very limited restrictions.

Apart from that, I'm also very interested in Augmented and Virtual Reality as well as non-computer interaction such as working with the Arduino (ThinkerIT).

At the moment I'm improving my AS3 skills by learning Design Patterns and I'm also learning Cocoa in order to develop Macintosh applications.
