    - Worked directly with CUTEC leadership to plan and execute their first conference in June 2004 (weekly videoconferences and four trips to Cambridge, UK); and
    - Stayed out of the way when necessary.

    The TVC is now profitable and the club organizes a numer of other events throughout the year to accommodate student demand. I continue to advise the current CUTEC leadership as requested.
