Natalia Real (natalia@nataliareal.com)’s Summary

I am a native English speaker also fluent in Spanish; I work comfortably in both languages.

I have a BA in literature from New College of Florida, the Honors College of Florida, and have written and copyedited for many publications, including magazines, newspapers, and blogs, in both English and Spanish.

I have worked with texts about law, nutrition, the environment, politics, and music, among others.

My considerable journalistic experience and literature degree both ensure excellent grammar, spelling, and syntax, as well as attention to detail, in my work.

I am painstaking, thorough, engaged, and have advanced researching skills. I hold high standards for myself and always deliver quality work on time.

Language is my passion, whether I'm delicately translating ideas into another language, crafting a narrative, or enhancing somebody else's.
