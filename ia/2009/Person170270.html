MyBlack Networks’s Summary

1) What is My Black Networks, LLC?

My Black Networks, LLC (MBN) is a vertical social media network company, headquartered in Austin, TX, whose primary service is an ecosystem of informational websites targeted to unique segments of the African, African-American and Caribbean heritage communities (i.e., the African Diaspora).

2) What is the mission of My Black Networks?

At MBN, we believe that one's identity is analogous to an infinite series of concentric "social circles." For this reason, we adopt, the motto "Because Individual Consciousness Begins with Social Connections.SM" MBN contends that self-awareness and self-determination are symbiotic outcomes of the complex interplay of one's social networks. MBN hopes to provide a platform and feedback loop for this journey of individual and communal discovery.

3) What are the objectives of My Black Networks?

MBN promises to be a platform by which individual expression and identity can be fostered, and cultural commonalities can be reaffirmed. MBN will deliver on its promise by evolving into a global brand that facilitates and enhances the following: 1) the manner in which people of African descent and heritage communicate with each other, 2) the method by which they find and access culturally relevant information, 3) the opportunity to celebrate present day individual and community accomplishments, and 4) the awareness of the importance of giving homage to significant historical traditions.
