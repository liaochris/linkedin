Jessica Bledsoe’s Summary

I am a Graphic Designer with more than 10 years of experience. I began with Graphic Design in 1997 when I started my internship with The National Catholic Reporter Publishing Company. Since that time I have received a lot of hands on experience with design and layout in a PC and Mac environment.

Since entering the work force in 1997 I have proven that I am a designer with a creative mind, as well as the abilty to multi-task, pay great attention to detail and work with very tight and demanding deadlines. And I am able to create original art and help guide clients to utilize the most efficient marketing plans.

1997 - 1998 Graphic Design Intern, The National Catholic Reporter Publishing Co.
I worked with design and layout of a variety of published pieces.

1998 - 2001 Production Artist, Charolais Publications
I was a full-time designer for the 4 color cattle magazine. I was also in charge of layout and preparation for the printer. I worked directly with the advertisers.

2001 - 2004 Ad Designer, The Kansas City Star
I worked with both creative and digital advertisements, including trouble shooting. I often worked directly with the advertiser on layout and design issues. Attention to detail was required as we were expected to copy check each others work before they printed in the daily newspaper. The ability to work as a team was necessary at The Star.

2004 Graphic Designer, The Olathe News
Create advertisements that run in the daily newspaper, as well as spec ads and flyers that sales executives can provide to their clients. Also laid out and send the daily newspaper to be output to film.

2005-2008 Graphic Designer II, Associated Wholesale Grocers, Inc.
Create advertising pieces for the grocery industry. Lead artist on the Country Mart account. I worked closely with the store owners and ad coordinators, as well as the printing company I was responsible for sending electronic files to be output by our printing company.

Adobe Illustrator, Indesign & Photoshop & Quark in CS3
