Ilham Shebani’s Summary

Build Self Group expertise:

1) Business Development and Enterprise Education, Training, and Coaching: Aimed at Business Owners, Business and Social Entrepreneurs, People Management Professionals.

2) Impact Coaching to improve Youth Personal and Academic Performance: To motivate students to set goals, gain greater confidence and leadership skills.

3) Entrepreneurs Business Networking and Coaching: Build Self Group want to provide a forum and specific workshops to enable and empower entreprneurs working in different sectors to focus on building their business.

About Us:

Build Self Group was founded by Ilham Yousef Shebani who is a trained professional trainer, educator and coach with extensive experience in the business and education management arena in both the UK and UAE. Educated to Masters level in International Relations, International Law, Political Science and Education Management in the UK. Ilham Y Shebani is keen to provide a professional coaching, training and mentoring service to entrepreneurs and to SME businesses that are committed to their own professional development and wanting to prepare their staff to change, improve performance and raise their brand profile.
