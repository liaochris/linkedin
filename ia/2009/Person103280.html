Jamie King’s Summary

Having worked on console video games for the last 13 years, my focus is now centered on 3D Internet gaming, social networking and virtual commerce. I was a founder of Rockstar Games until 2006 and now I'm president of a start up that is a web based service provider of digital entertainment across a digital distribution delivery platform. The focus of the new company is to create games for a global market across multiple age demographics. As an individual in the games industry, I have worked on every aspect of business development, game design, pre and post production, marketing, distribution and sales of video game titles. Previous to the video game industry, I lived and worked in London, specifically in the film and music video industry. As a founder of Rockstar Games, I was awarded a BAFTA for overall contribution to the video game industry.

Jamie King’s Specialties:

Developer / Publisher game contracts, game design, (code, gameplay mechanics, art assets, testing,) game production, (research, motion capture, live action shoots and events, voice over, editing, content licensing, co-promotions and branding, etc,) Film Finance Agreements, Music Licensing Agreements, Talent and Usage Rights, The Console Industry, 3D Social Networks and MMO's.
