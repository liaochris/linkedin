John Fay’s Summary

Hodges & Hicks is a General Contractor providing construction services to selected clients. These services include traditional general contracting for a lump sum, cost plus a fee or guaranteed maximum price, pre-construction services, construction management and consulting services. The company is pursuing opportunities in healthcare, religious, private school, office building, and data center markets.

The company started in October, 2004; however, the principals have been employed by one of the southeast’s leading general contractors since its inception in 1985 and have played an integral role in the success of the company. The principals have been senior members of the management team that has taken R.J. Griffin & Company from a startup company in 1985 to a top ENR 100 general contractor with 2004 revenues totaling over $500,000,000. Both Mr. Hodges and Mr. Hicks served on the Executive Committee and were members of the planning team, which was responsible for the incredibly successful growth plan for the company.

We are in our fifth year and we have achieved success in every facet of our business. We were able to exceed our planned results in revenues and profitability, but more important, we have established an excellent reputation with clients and architects and we have been able to attract the key personnel that will ensure our success for a long time to come.

We will continue to position the company for sustained, controlled growth. We have secured a surety line that will allow us to build single jobs in excess of $35,000,000 and an uncompleted workload of approximately $70,000,000. Our company strategy is to target key markets that the principals are knowledgeable in and, at the same time, be as diversified as possible. These markets include healthcare, private schools, churches, retail, public work, residential and office buildings. All of our work is managed out of the office in Roswell, GA.

John Fay’s Specialties:

Pre-construction and construction management throughout healthcare, religious, private school, office building, and data center markets.
