Josef Meyer’s Summary

As President and Founder of ATN Promo my responsibilities were to do anything and everything to help run the company. I have recruited in the smallest markets for the smallest events and staffed those events with great success. As the company has grown over the last 6 years my responsibilities have evolved. Not only to help manage our team by providing more tools to make them more efficient but by increasing our client roster.
My goals in the future are to increase ATN's capabilities, revenues and continue to be a leader in the promotional staffing industry.
