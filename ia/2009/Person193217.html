Ramesh Shanmuganathan’s Summary

Ramesh is a dynamic, multi-faceted, experienced Business cum IT Executive with a very good comprehension of the ICT industry. He has over 20 years of experience in the ICT industry both in Sri Lanka and USA, with the last 10+ years in the C-Level(CXO).

Ramesh possess a very strong track record both academically and professionally. He is a Hayes-Fulbright Scholar; a Phi Kappa Phi as well as a First Class Honours Graduate. Has been amongst the top 1% of his class consistently and has the rare distinction of being appointed the Vice President / CEO of the IT subsidiary of John Keells Holdings PLC, Sri Lanka at the age of 33 and as the Group’s Executive Vice President/ Group's Chief Information Officer at the age of 39.


Ramesh's career objective is to be in a strategic and executive leadership position in a progressive and dynamic multi-national organization that would provide him with an environment full of challenges together with new vistas of growth and development in a regional context.

Ramesh Shanmuganathan’s Specialties:

Commercial experience and strong track record in strategic planning, operational management, business restructuring, process reengineering, change management, sales & marketing management, project & delivery management, technology & portfolio management, human resources & personnel management, etc in Information Communication Technology & related Services industry in which his experience spans over 15 years.
