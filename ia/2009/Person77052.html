Jim McGregor Jim@jobsearchmanagers.com’s Summary

Jim has been an executive and hiring manager at Hewlett Packard, Thompson Software, Apollo Computer, and Hughes Aircraft Company. He has over 20 years of computer industry experience, and holds an electrical engineering degree from North Dakota State University - Cum Laude and an M.B.A. from UCLA.

Jim McGregor Jim@jobsearchmanagers.com’s Specialties:

JSM places Senior Executives, Middle Management, Sales and Marketing Executives, and Technical Professionals. Our Financial Services clients are leaders in Private Wealth Management.
