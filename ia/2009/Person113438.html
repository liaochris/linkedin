Jim Spaulding’s Summary

I am an experienced analyst with extensive management, engineering and teaching experience and with special expertise in the application of computers to real business problems.

My participation in client projects solves problems by

- Utilizing internal/external research to identify needs in data collection and/or computer applications using focus groups, surveys, and interviews to obtain hard data that defines costs up-front.

- Creatively designing complete computer systems from hardware and software to application.

- Implementing systems with management that keeps projects running smoothly

- Producing documentation, training and roll/out that users are satisfied with and that works the way it is supposed to.

My extensive teaching career keeps me fully involved in and knowledgeable about the cutting edge of computer systems, languages and technology.
