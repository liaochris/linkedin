Ahmed Nuaman’s Summary

Hi, I'm Ahmed Nuaman BA (hons), an interactive creative designer.

I started designing web sites over 7 years ago and began running freelance business with over 27 clients. Since then, my skills have progressed and I am now working for a successful studio in Kent.

I have a passion for producing simple ideas and solutions for problems laid out by briefs. I am interested in creating rich applications and interactions that embrace the end users' needs but doesn't overwhelm them with complexity. I believe in keeping things simple, not making the user think, and fulfilling the brief beyond the client's expectations.

I have a strong work ethic and believe that nothing is impossible; all of the work I produce houses nothing but my full expertise and passion. In 2007 I graduated from Ravensbourne College of Design and Communication with a first class degree in
Interaction Design.

My work experience ranges from creating Mac and Yahoo! widgets and microsites to fully fledged e-commerce applications, innovative fund-raising platforms - all with 3Dsecure integration, customer relationship suites, and content management system.

Ahmed Nuaman’s Specialties:

My skills include expert knowledge of PHP, JavaScript, XML (+XSL/T), HTML, CSS, SQL, Actionscript 3; and proficient knowledge of Flex framework, ASP (+.NET) (C#), and Ruby. I confidently work with a range of software suites including the Adobe Creative Suite (3) and open source alternatives such as GIMP, Inkscape, Nvu, Textwrangler, and TextEdit.
