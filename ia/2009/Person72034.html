Ellen Lichtig’s Summary

Communications professional, producer, and strategist with an interest in creative marketing, coaching and global opportunities.

Industry experience has been largely in technology, with secondary emphasis in healthcare, international work, and the nonprofit sector. I've worked as a producer in the areas of blogging, print, video, interface design, email setup, content management, DVD creation, web and e-commerce implementations, community managing and new media.

Defining personal qualities are curiosity, a tenacity in research, a love of international travel and cultures, a passion for products that work well, an ability to listen deeply to others, and a good design sense (See photography portfolio at workingcommunity.com and Hire Me Section at creativelyrising.com).

Ellen Lichtig’s Specialties:

Listening, research, writing (proposals, feature stories, specification documents, team presentations, marketing materials,scripts), editing (print and video), coaching, technology training (DVD's, blogs, online marketing, social media), computer systems setup abroad, project managing and small group facilitation.
