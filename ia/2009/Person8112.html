Alex Francis’s Summary

Web software engineer, architect, designer, team lead and manager with more than 8 years experience building applications for the web. Experience with the public sector in the UK, accessibility and compliance, publishing sector and online recruitment. I've grown up with the web as a software engineer and my preferred role is helping small teams acquire maturity and maintain agility. Language and environment neutral with enough experience that I can pick up anything required, but lots of experience in perl, java, c#.

Experience introducing agile methodologies including Scrum and XP to organisations in which there is resistance to the agile adoption. Sector experience with publishing firms and recruitment firms. Involved in industry networking events and organisations including SkillSwap and BarCamp.
