Sriram R’s Summary

“If at first the idea is not absurd, then there is no hope for it.”
– Albert Einstein

Sriram’s passion for books was serendipitous & led him to establish bookstores across India - he started with Landmark in 1988, set up Walden in Hyderabad in 1990 & then the Crossword chain.

He co-founded Crossword with Ms. K. Anita in 1992 & helped build it into India’s leading bookstore chain. He arranged for the buyout of Crossword by the current owners Shoppers’ Stop Ltd. in 2000 from the initial investor India Book House Ltd.

Sriram stepped down from Crossword in August 2006 to co-found “Next Practice Retail” that offers business design, incubation & consulting services for consumer & retail businesses. He is President Elect of The Indus Entrepreneurs (TiE) Mumbai, the global not-for-profit organization that fosters entrepreneurship & is an Independent Director on the Boards of Camlin Ltd. & Gini & Jony Ltd.

He serves on the Board of Advisors of Pratham Books, the non-profit that publishes affordable books for children in multiple Indian languages, & SNEHA - the non-profit Society for Nutrition, Education & Health Action for women & children in urban slums.

He is a visiting faculty at IIM A & a guest lecturer at IIM L & other business schools & a speaker at seminars as it helps him keep learning.

Sriram was part of the Jury of the HSBC Indo-British Awards 2005 & Business for Social Responsibility Awards 2005 to 2008. He curated the Kala Ghoda Literature Festival from 2005 to 2009. While at Crossword, Sriram established the Crossword Book Awards, now known as the Vodafone Crossword Book Awards, to annually recognize & reward the best of Indian writers & books.

He advises, coaches and mentors individuals & firms, & is particularly interested in customer based innovation, business strategy & incubation, entrepreneurship & next practices.

He remains a student & book evangelist.

Sriram R’s Specialties:

Next Practices, Business Incubation, Retail strategy, Retailing, Customer based Innovation, Customer Experience Management (CEM), Customer Managed Relationship (CMR), Customer Relationship Management (CRM), Bookselling, Publishing, Strategic Marketing, Franchising, Entrepreneurship & Social Entrepreneurship.

    SNEHA - Society for Nutrition, Education and Health Action

    (Hospital & Health Care industry)

    March 2009 — Present (8 months)

    SNEHA believes Every Woman and Child Counts.
    ...in Sanskrit it means “Love”
    ...it stands for Society for Nutrition, Education and Health Action

    A voluntary, secular, non-profit organization, SNEHA addresses the special needs of slum women and children in Mumbai by working to improve their health.

    * Over 54% of Mumbai lives in extremely crowded slums
    * 100,000 babies die before their fifth birthday
    * 1 in 2 children are malnourished in Mumbai
    * 66% of women face physical & mental harassment in their own homes
    * 450 mothers die in every 100,000 births

    SNEHA was born on November 27, 1999 in Dharavi, Mumbai to address and prevent these concerns. It was founded by a group of dedicated doctors and social workers led by neonatalogist and former dean of Lokmanya Tilak Municipal General Hospital and Medical College (LTMGH & LTMMC), Sion, Mumbai, Dr. Armida Fernandez.

    SNEHA is registered under the Bombay Public Trust Act 1950 and under the Foreign Contributions Regulation Act for receipt of foreign donations.

    Our Vision

    We dedicate our energies, expertise and resources to ensure quality nutrition, education and health care of women and children.

    Our Mission

    We impact quality of care and influence urban health policies through innovative solutions to problems in nutrition, education and health in urban communities. We build sustainable and replicable models of intervention and partnerships that empower women to change their lives and those around them.
