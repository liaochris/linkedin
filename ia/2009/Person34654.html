Bruce Culver’s Summary

Bruce Culver is a serial entrepreneur, angel investor, business owner and executive. He is Founder and CEO of IdealHire, Technologies Inc. of Scottsdale, AZ. He has 20 years experience in the staffing industry. In 1985, he found Lab Support, Inc. and raised 2 rounds of venture capital to fund and develop the business. The Company went public on NASDAQ as “On Assignment, Inc.” (symbol ASGN) in 1992.
In 1990 Mr. Culver Co-founded Professional Staff, plc., a UK specialty staffing business. He served as Chief Executive, Chairman and Director. Professional Staff went public on NASDAQ in 1996 (symbol PSTF) and was privatized in 2003.
He owns California Distribution, Inc. a 3PL logistics, warehouse, distribution and transportation company.
As an active angel investor, he has invested in 12 different start-ups. He provided the angel investment funding and is a director for Taser International, Inc. (symbol TASR) of Scottsdale, AZ

Bruce Culver’s Specialties:

Executive management, product development, business strategy, software as a service (SaaS), marketing, public relations, start-ups, team development and motivation and operations.
