Tom Motzel’s Summary

Tesserae Talent Strategies is a National Search Firm dedicated to Enterprise Content Management (ECM). Our clients include ECM Software Providers, System Integrators and Value Added Resellers.

We deliver pre-qualified ECM candidates, not stacks of resumes for you to sort through. We are not in the business of duplicating ‘Job Board’ sourcing efforts. Our goal is to develop a partnership based on the knowledge and value of our network.

Our definition of ECM is broad and grows along with the industry trends.
• Document Management (DM)
• Web Content Management (WCM)
• Records Management (RM)
• Collaboration
• Social Networking
• Semantic and Web 2.0 Technologies
• Enterprise Search
• Taxonomy
• Business Process Management (BPM)
• Digital Asset Management (DAM)
• Digital Signage
