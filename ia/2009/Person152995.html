Professional Summary

Excellent communicator, coordinator, and facilitator with ability to keep focus on the "big picture." Proven ability to provide comprehensive and consistent direction, expertise, and vision to business, project management, development, and testing personnel in (primarily) defense, telecommunications, and biotechnology industries, delivering consistent project success.
