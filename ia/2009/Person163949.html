Mike Beardshall’s Summary

I am a problem solver, enabling businesses to resolve complex business and technical issues to increase their ROI. My core skills are analysis, risk management, strategic planning and process re-design.

I formed Portfolio Software back in 2002 with my business partner Mitul. He is the true technologist with me providing the business solutions, together we have worked with many companies both in the UK, US, Europe and Asia. Portfolio provides the management and bespoke software solutions to both multi-nationals and start-ups.

In 2007 we formed Epsilica Consulting, specialising in business consultancy for companies seeking independent and cost efficient advice and guidance.

With over 20 years experience covering MOD, Government (local and central), logistics, finance, media and other sectors I am able to translate my experience into viable and cost efficient solutions.

My business career started in 1987 as a business analyst, closely followed by technical and data analysis. This progressed into business modelling and management.

I don't rely on single methodologies, there are just too many and a hybrid fit-for-purpose solution generally works best whether it be waterfall or agile, spiral or V-Model I use whatever works. My career started with formal methods but more recently our clients tend to use agile methods to speed the whole process.

I spend most of my time working with our clients to help them achieve better returns through small investments in technology concentrating on the core systems and processes, a little bit of technology strategically placed can work wonders for the ROI.

The remaining time is spent with Portfolio and Epsilica, helping to ensure we constantly meet and excel at what we do. Both companies have consistently achieved over 90% of repeat business, something I am very proud of.
