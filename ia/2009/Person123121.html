Jud Hampson’s Summary

Jud is a Senior Project Manager in Aurecon's Newcastle office. Jud has 9 years of project management and engineering experience as an Army Officer in the Royal Australian Electrical and Mechanical Engineers and as a consultant.

Jud has a background in capital acquisition project and program management with the Australian Defence Organisation. Jud has extensive experience in maintenance operations, Reliability, Availability and Maintainability testing and the Systems Engineering process. Jud also has experience in energy management systems and low-carbon technologies.
