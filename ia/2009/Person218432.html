Vinod Singh’s Summary

Vinod has over 20 years in International IT industry for Software Business Development and delivery in USA, UK, Singapore, Germany and India. Vinod has rich experience of working with some of the market leaders and has helped build offices, teams, and delivery centres for outsourcing off-shore delivery centres. These centres were built on Captive basis as well as third party delivery centres.

Vinod has helped in acquiring software technology businesses and then actively led the merging of IT unit operations in Europe and India.

Vinod has also helped in setting up different relationships based on services, Build-Operate-transfer, and acquisition for UK and Japan based companies in India.

Vinod has successfully delivered many large outsourced software projects from US and UK to India. Vinod has leadership experience of product development, organisation setup, post merger acquisition, operations and team integration.

In the current responsibility as the Technology Business Leader, Vinod is responsible for the performance improvement to Board, development, implementation, and operations of unified Online and Back office systems, cross functional and department alignment, team recruitment, mentoring and development.
