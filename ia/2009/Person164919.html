Mike Thatcher’s Summary

Over twenty years in the field of POWER WASHING and PAINTING, including;
Over fifteen years New Construction, Industrial, Commercial, Residential, Exterior and Interior, and CUSTOM PAINTING.
Use of HVLP, pressure pot, electrostatic and airless sprayers.
Master of brush and roller. Use of epoxies, special finishes, sealers, glazing, fillers, spackles, color matching, mixing, and custom colors.
Experienced and knowledgeable use with high pressure washers, drum, palm, and rotary sanders, paint burners, strippers, and chemicals of the field.
Worked for many contractors and projects throughout Northern Virginia throughout career; including running crews of painters and helpers to complete projects such as hospitals, military bases, schools, and Historical landmarks.
Licensed in Virginia, Insured, and registered with the BBB of Washington D.C.
Specializing in House & Deck Power Washing, & Custom Interior Painting.
