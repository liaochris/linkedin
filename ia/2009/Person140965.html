Lilach Bullock’s Summary

My name is Lilach Bullock and Im just a normal working mum who had a burning desire to change my circumstances.

Im lucky, I have now found what I was looking for, something that has changed my life completely.

Until recently I ran my own successful business, asklilach Ltd. I set it up from scratch whilst looking after my 2½ year old. I absolutely loved it but it wasnt perfect I was spending a lot of time working when I felt as though I should have been spending quality time with my daughter.

So, I decided to take the brave step and sell it (the business that is, not my daughter!) so I could spend more time with my family.

But I still felt as though I needed something more, but something that would fit in around my family. Because of my business success, I have an excellent understanding of the demands of todays complex business environment.

My contribution was recently recognised when I attended the Best Mumpreneur of the Year Award at Downing Street.

Having trained and mentored over 600 people I realised what it was that I wanted to do. After a bit of net surfing and due diligence I discovered the opportunity Id been looking for. Now I enjoy a fabulous income, run a business that I love and still have plenty of time to spend with my family.

If you would like to know about me or my online business check out http://yournetbizsecrets.co.uk or http://www.whoislilachbullo...

Always happy to chat and see if I can help you in any way.
