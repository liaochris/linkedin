Dani Kollin’s Summary

I'm a go-to writer who's used to hitting the ground running. I've pitched for fortune 500's, zipped off tons of movie trailers, gotten websites up and running in a few days and have created award winning GUI's for major educational institutions. I'm obsessed with word flow and I swear paragraphs, if written well, can have their own musical meter.

My goal is simple and straightforward; Take my client's ideas and craft them into words that deliver strong ROI.
