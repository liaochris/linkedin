Anna Shallenberger’s Summary

My career path hasn't been a straight line -- nor do I remain one place. What really intrigues me is problem solving. Information can be the end -- or it can be the means.

I possess an unusual mix of skills and interests. While I am exceptionally goal oriented and can be very competitive -- I am above all things a team player and service focused.

I don't necessarily feel the need for titles & hoopla -- but I do expect my contribution to be appreciated and to be compensated accordingly. I've typically been fortunate to work in a great team environment with interesting, knowledgeable colleagues.

Objective:

A strategic role that is instrumental in:
• Delivering enhanced understanding of markets, competitors, and customers
• Expanding current markets & opening new markets
• Improving relationships with existing customers
• Implementing process improvements and optimizing resources
• Generating efficiencies, innovation and higher profits.

Qualifications:

Business professional with progressively responsible positions utilizing strengths in research, creative problem solving, project management, team building, and strategic analytics. Demonstrated abilities to assess needs, develop processes, and build relationships necessary to execute effectively and successfully. Created knowledge portal. Established human intelligence networks. Initiated research ethics standards. Developed actionable output. Leaders, clients and other colleagues see me as a results-driven, customer-focused team leader who understands the big picture as well as the details.

Anna Shallenberger’s Specialties:

Market, Competitive & Human Intel; Secondary & Primary Research; Knowledge Management; Data Collection, Manipulation & Synthesis; Analytical Models like SWOT & 5 Forces; Strategic Planning; 6Sigma

Presented at & moderated trade association meetings. Published, mostly anonymously. Reader I don’t know said… "It speaks so eloquently ... I'd very much like to share it with (fellow professionals in my local trade group.)"

Charitable fund-raiser, drawing on organizational & motivational skills.
