Nick Halverson’s Summary

Approximately four years of real estate development, sales and management in Costa Rica. Our company is putting in the first golf course in the entire Southern zone of Costa Rica.

I also assist my father periodically with his consulting business - D. Halverson & Associates.

Previously, I spent ten years in international business development, marketing, and a small stint in sell side mergers and acquisitions.

Improving my spoken Spanish skills.
