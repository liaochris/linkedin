David Komurek’s Experience

  • Founder

    WANTED

    (Design industry)

    2001 — Present (8 years)

    WANTED was conceived as the first ever DVD magazine featuring never seen films from Gus Van Sant, Jonas Mekas; music videos from Terry Richardson, Mike Mills to rare interview between Johnny Depp and Harmony Korine.

    WANTED continued to realize the creative efforts of artists, film makers and musicians while integrating them with brands and creating original projects. In 2003 WANTED was responsible for Harmony Korine's last major art show at Agnes' b galerie du jour and in 2005 contributed to Sanrio’s three year worldwide celebration of Hello Kitty’s 30th birthday as an artist and producer of the likes of Alfredo Martinez, Bob Partington, Dan Colen, Donald Baechler, Kenneth Cappello, Malcolm McLaren, Sean Lennon, Surface To Air and United Bamboo

    WANTED has also done illustration and merchandise for Tom Petty’s Last Dj as well as Creative Direction, Photography and A&R for Japanese musician Fayray to past branding and design efforts for Sony’s mp3 Walkman.
