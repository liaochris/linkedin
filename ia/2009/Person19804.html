MY 3 PRINCIPLES OF TRADING:
=========================

#1. Trading/Investing is a ZERO-SUM Game. Yes - I know it's obvious, but why do people insist on believing that they can apply any strategy they consider "sensible" to the markets and make money?

#2. The economy and financial markets are INCOMPREHENSIBLE and UNPREDICTABLE. It is not necessary to make accurate predictions about the future in order to make money in the market. In fact, it is COUNTERPRODUCTIVE to make any predictions. The only thing WORSE than not knowing something, is not knowing that you don't know it!

#3. Assume nothing and TEST EVERYTHING! This follows from the 2 previous principles. Since you are competing against some very smart people, and you can't truly understand the underlying "Pandora's Box" called the market, then you must empirically verify everything (i.e. extensively back test your trading system).
