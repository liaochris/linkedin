Cathy Puett Miller’s Summary

TLA's mission is to light the spark and fan the flame of literacy with schools, communities, families and children.

That leads us to:

conduct educational research,

present, train and offer keynotes at a variety of speaking venues,

write extensively on the web and in print (Cathy has a new book for preschool parents coming out in the spring of 2009 with a companion book for teachers).

Consulting with family-friendly schools and organizations to design literacy initiatives is also a key part of TLA's efforts.

Cathy Puett Miller’s Specialties:

Designing, directing & evaluating literacy initiatives, school curriculum & preschool environments

Experienced author of professional content for teachers & educational content for children,

Design of content for parents, including improving readability of text,

Facilitating focus groups/staff development groups

Presenter, motivational speaker, trainer, writing coach.

Engaging professionalism, insight, & passion for the world of literacy are our trademarks.
