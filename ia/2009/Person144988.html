Luis Alberola’s Summary

I am an expert, manager and business developer in the Human Capital field.

I have a passion for helping organizations succeed in both developing and engaging their key people. I work with my clients helping them attract, engage and retain these key talents in the EMEA region.

I am convinced that understanding and developing Human Capital is the next frontier, both for organizations and society.

I have developed an experience in many of the fields needed to succeed in this development : HR consulting, eHR software development, social networks design and management, executive search, general management ...

I am always looking for new challenges and new issues in which my past experience, drive, and skills can be of value in pushing this frontier further.

Luis Alberola’s Specialties:

- Designing social networks (alumni, communities of practice, ...)
- Developing talent management practices (recruitment, anual review, ...), choosing softwares and leading implementation
- Developing leadership programs
- Developing Human Capital scorecards
- Designing recruitment strategies, channels, training recruiters, conducting recruitments
- Managing and training an HR team towards a more oriented "business partner" and "strategic decision maker" status
