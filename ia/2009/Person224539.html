Steven Ward, SPHR’s Summary

Steven serves as Director of Human Resource Services for Brooklyn College in New York. Brooklyn College is an innovative liberal arts institution with a history of academic excellence in both undergraduate and graduate studies. Steve's role with the college includes overseeing employment, recruitment, classification, performance management, benefits, and payroll. Steve is also serving as a HRM lead on CUNYFirst, a University-wide implementation of the Oracle/PeopleSoft ERP system that will focus on Human Capital Management (HCM), Finance, and Student Administration (Campus Solutuions).

Steve has over 8 years of hands on experience in all functional areas of HR in a variety of industries including higher education, high-tech, manufacturing, and software services in both the public and private sector.

• Human Resources Executive with technology background
• Risk management and best practices
• Classification & compensation design and implementation
• Employee relations and discipline
• Strategic business partner with operational and revenue focus
• Project management, systems design, and implementation
• Skilled communicator with proven record achieving operational goals through strategic partnerships.
• Strategic approach to problem solving and tactical implementation skills
