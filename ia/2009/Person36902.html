Candace Avalos’s Summary

I am aspiring to become a high school Spanish and English as a Second Language (ESL) teacher. I have been a Psychology major for 3 years, and in my final year I have switched to Spanish. I am interested in joining Teach for America right after I graduate and possibly doing service in Washington, D.C. I also wish to pursue a Masters in Education at the same time through a program that TFA offers. After that I plan on doing service for the Peace Corps in a Spanish-speaking country and hopefully teach English or any other service work. When I return, I want to start my career off in the Northern Virginia area as a teacher, and possibly work my way up the education system to one day become a Principal or Superintendent. I am interested in ultimately obtaining a PhD in Education.

I am interested in the lives of America's Youth and will be working to serve them in any capacity, even if that means something other than teaching. I am willing to work for the Department of Education or non-profit to helping students achieve success and seek higher education and better paying jobs and careers. I am particularly interested in the Latino population and could also see myself working for Immigration or the Hispanic College Fund or any other non-profit organizations that aid this population.
