Víctor Raúl Ordóñez’s Summary

My interest in Marketing and Branding has enabled me to participate in dozens of projects in a wide variety of sectors -- consumer products, services, health care, government agencies and politics, among others. My focus has always been a deep understanding of the role these projects can play in helping clients discover their essence as well as implementing the most suitable solutions to promote greater brand recognition as well as maintain a mutually satisfactory dialogue with the customer.

My principal goal is and will continue to be to build brands as bridges of communication and knowledge and to help companies create closer relationships with their stakeholders. In so doing, they not only improve the bottom line, but also share in the emotional and practical benefits that their marketing efforts deliver to customers.
