Brian Morin’s Summary

I excel at finding solutions. Sometimes I build them myself, other times I prototype and hand it off to others with more in-depth knowledge than myself. I have successfully made use of Open Source technology in every job I've been in, and in just about every case saved money on projects or even open up business opportunities using Open Source software.

I have a strong background in networking and telecommunications; in 1997 I was wiring up houses with first generation cable modems. I have since developed teams of engineers to build carrier class email systems as well as ISP infrastructures.

Currently I oversee PHP applications development and management of a hosting facility.

Brian Morin’s Specialties:

I have engineered two ISP businesses in my career, that experience has taken me across a landscape of technologies including telecommunications, software development, enterprise networking and security. Using this experience I make connections of technology to solve business goals. Since my first Apple ][+ , technology has been my passion and continues to this day to drive my career.
