Raghu Dodda’s Summary

I lead the design and development of internal and client-facing applications at Amedisys, a leading Home Health Care provider. Presently, I am working on a health care portal that allows doctors to collaborate in new ways and enhance the quality of care for the patients.

Prior to building .NET applications for the health care industry, I led medium size teams at BearingPoint, a Big 6 IT Consulting Company, to deliver both flagship products and custom solutions to high-profile clients (such as U.S. Navy and New York City).

Although, .NET and SharePoint have been my specialty for the last few years, I have developed and maintained applications in a variety of programming languages for several scientific research projects such as the LIGO project at Caltech.

I am very adaptable technically and personally and am equally comfortable in project lead, designer or developer roles. As a big believer in the philosophy that includes Continuous Integration and Test Driven Development, I constantly update my technical skills and often built POCs to substantiate my designs.
