Dr.Kumar Padmanabh’s Summary

Dr. Kumar Padmanabh is working as a researcher in Software Engineering and Technology Labs of Infosys Technology Limited, Bangalore India. He is leading wireless sensor network research group. He has been the main architect of Elixir, a framework for rapid application development in wireless sensor network. He has published more than 20 research papers in international conferences and journal of repute and invented 5 patent pending technologies in sensor network and related areas. He is listed in Marquis World’s Who is Who-2007 and was the finalist of Indian Mathematics Olympiad.

Dr. Kumar Padmanabh has expertise in new emerging wireless technologies, such as IEEE 802.15.4, Zigbee, Wireless Personal Area Network. He largely deals with network, transport and application layers of wireless networking in above mentioned wireless technologies. His area of interest is in Application of optimization, control, algorithmic and AI techniques in optimal resource allocation problems, Performance Analysis and Modeling of networking. Application of control theory, algorithmic techniques and detection/estimation techniques in network dynamics, network optimization and stability in wireless network in general.

Dr. Kumar Padmanabh earned his PhD degree from Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur in 2006 in wireless sensor network area, Master of Technology in Digital Systems from Moti Lal Nehru National Institute of Technology, Allahabad, India in 2003 and Bachelor of Engineering degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering in 2001 from MJP Rohillkhand University, Bareilly, India and. He has worked in Bhabha Atomic Research Center as a research/project trainee and as a lecturer in Mody Institute of Technology and Science, Laxmangarh, Rajasthan (no mitsunivversity).

Dr.Kumar Padmanabh’s Specialties:

Research with real life sensor nodes including, development of mesh networking, energy optimization, query processing, aggregation and reliability especially with crossbow motes. This also includes middleware development and deployment strategy of WSN motes using the programming in nesC, tinyOS, and JAVA and Microsoft dot net.
