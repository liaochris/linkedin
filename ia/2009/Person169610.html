johannes muecke’s Summary

I have been drawing my whole life. Visual conception and creation have always been my greatest passion. After high school I started to study Architecture to get a broader understanding of design theory, space and art.
After I had finished university and worked in an Architecture office in London I decided to turn back to fine art and pop culture. As the lead concept artist for Rockstar Vienna I had my first real contact with the video games industry.
At the moment I am working for the Vienna based, independend games developer Sproing Interactive Media as art director. I also have an employment as University assistant at the Architecture University in Vienna.
