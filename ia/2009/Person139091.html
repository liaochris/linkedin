Lee Bladon’s Summary

I am a self-employed Interim Manager and Technical Consultant to the food industry. I have a first class honours degree in Food Quality Management, 12 years experience and a proven track record in the food industry.

I have worked for a diverse range of food manufacturers and have experience of many different food groups including: meat, seafood, ready meals, dairy, bakery, sauces, food ingredients, ready-to-eat, ambient, chilled and frozen.

Clients include: Grampian Country Pork (Vion), Cumbrian Seafoods, Cavaghan & Gray (Northern Foods), R&R Ice Cream, Pro-Pak Foods, Uniq Prepared Foods, RF Brookes, Hazlewood Foods (Greencore), Merck, Avance Bakeries (Primebake), Manor Bakeries and Ryvita.
