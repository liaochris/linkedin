    Outsource Technical, founded in 1998, attributes its' success to forming iron-clad relationships with top West Coast Information Technology talent. We believe recruiting is about knowing the candidates, and not just matching a set of skills from a resume. Starting with a face to face meeting at one of our locations, we build a mutually beneficial partnership and get to know what that candidate wants in their career! Outsource Technical uses a patented recruiting process for selecting the best that the Information Technology industry has to offer. The “5 Steps to Source” process involves qualifying our client’s job requirement, reviewing our proprietary relationship database for potential candidates, networking with our contacts to find potential candidates, unleashing our trained recruiters to perform external candidate sourcing and thereby finding the ideal candidate.
