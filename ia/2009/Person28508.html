Bill Gottfried’s Summary

GOTTFRIED INTERNATIONAL, INC. is an independent and international management consulting, executive search, and project development firm active in global oil and gas, renewable energy (biofuels, biopower, and energy crops), petroleum and energy marketing, agribusiness, plant science, biotechnology, space and satellite technology, and real estate.

We work with our clients on the search for and selection of senior management executives and Board of Director candidates.

We provide professional expertise to clients in the U.S., Mexico, Canada, Europe, Russia, Central Asia, the Caspian Region, and in the Middle East Arabian-Persian Gulf Region, including Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Bahrain, Qatar, UAE, and Yemen. We have offices and representation in Houston, Washington, D.C., London, Paris, Manama, and Almaty.

Bill Gottfried’s Specialties:

Former U.S. Navy Supply Officer (Lieutenant) stationed in the Middle East Arabian-Persian Gulf responsible for petroleum inspection and tanker scheduling in Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Iran, Bahrain, and Yemen; based in Awali, Bahrain; managed 200,000 b/d of product shipments in the Arabian Gulf.

Graduate of U.S. Naval Officers Candidate School, Newport, Rhode Island; U.S. Navy Supply Corps School, Athens, Georgia; U.S. Navy Petroleum School, San Diego, California. "Who's Who In World Oil & Gas".
