Priit Kallas’s Summary

I'm the founder and CEO of Sevenline, an Estonian internet marketing company. I am responsible for the everyday management as well as for the long term vision of the company. I manage strategic planning, key client and partner relations, marketing, training, consulting and a other activities.

I have been managing various internet projects for more than ten years. I have been involved in internet marketing since 1999 – helping Estonian companies to understand and use internet for reaching their clients in Estonia and abroad. After establishing Sevenline, I helped to start up Altex Marketing, the first Estonian company that was solely focused on internet marketing.

I have been involved in organizing Eturundus, the first series of internet marketing seminars in Estonia and also participated as a speaker. I have helped Tallinn City Government with their Mentor Club project, educating new entrepreneurs about the possibilities of internet marketing. I have introduced the practical side of internet marketing to the members of Estonia Trade Council. I have been speaking on many seminars about internet marketing held by the Estonian business newspaper Äripäev. In addition to the marketing-related material I have also helped businesses to understand the technical side of the internet in Äripäev's seminar about content management systems. On the more academic side of things I have been a guest lecturer in Estonian Business School.

My articles have been published in largest Estonian newspapers (Eesti Päevaleht, Postimees, Äripäev, Director) and my internet marketing blog is one of the largest sources of internet marketing information in Estonian.

I have participated in a wide array of software and internet projects from content management systems to server software. I have years of project management experience from the fields of sales, marketing and advertising. I have worked with such brands as OnOf, HP, IBM, Compaq, Subaru, Hotell Viru, Ehituse ABC and Delfi.
