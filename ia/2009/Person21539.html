Ashwin Rajan’s Summary

Information architect and interaction designer skilled in interpreting opportunities/constraints in business, technology, culture to inform design solutions. Experience delivering within corporate, consulting and startup environments. Industries worked in include education, IT services, finance, automotive, heavy industry, energy, government, non-profit, medical & health, aerospace & defense.

Ashwin Rajan’s Specialties:

User-centered design methods including contextual enquiry, scenarios, personas, experience prototyping and visualization.
Solid analytical, research and writing skills. Comfortable tackling complexity.
Education in psychology and sociology helps bring a human-centered perspective to projects.
Strong communication, presentation and client-facing skills.
A participatory approach to leadership.
Strive to leverage design as a strategic tool to drive decisions within the c-suite.
