Ken Yeung’s Summary

I have had an interesting career path in a number of different industries and functional areas. My experience spans across strategy, finance, marketing, real estate, merchandising, store planning, supply chain and corporate communications. I have worked at organizations of various sizes, from a private partnership at a five-person office, to mid-sized companies of several thousands to the world's largest company of two million associates. I have also dealt with a wide range of complex business issues and had the privilege of advising and learning from senior executives with decades of experience in their respective industries. Many of these career opportunities also culminated in my unique cultural experiences from having lived in diverse places such as Hong Kong, Los Angeles, Iowa, West Virginia, Arkansas and New York City.

I am currently a Class of 2010 MBA candidate at Columbia Business School in New York.
