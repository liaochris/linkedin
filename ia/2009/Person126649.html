Kari Quaas, PHR’s Summary

After college and the requisite trip abroad, I started my 11 year career in the cruise line industry as a Tour Guide / Bus Driver. This lead to positions recruiting, hiring and training Driver/Guides for Seattle and the State of Alaska. With the help of my now co-worker, Kathi at CoolWorks.com, I developed the first on-line job website for Holland America's Gray Line of Alaska division focused on the seasonal jobs in Alaska.

In 2001, I moved to Princess Cruises & Tours where I continued my career in safety and training. Within 6 months, I moved to human resources, my long time goal, in a benefits administration role. This lead to my eventual title of Assistant Manager of Human Resources where I continued to administer the benefits, but also provided support to Alaska operations and the growing Seattle office with supervisor training, employee relations, harassment investigations, recruiting, ATS support, etc.

In the spring of 2007, I joined the virtual recruiting team at CoolWorks.com as their west coast branch based in Seattle, WA. I help employers post their seasonal and career job opportunities, sell and provide support for our CoolWorks.com created ATS called Staffing Center, write blog posts for our seasonal human resources managers, our job seekers, and our social network "tribe," and seek out new social media avenues for promoting CoolWorks.com.

Current Goals - Support my CoolWorks.com clients and get our name out there, build partnerships to grow the business, and hopefully to grow my little photography business on the side.
