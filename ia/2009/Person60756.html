Declan Conway’s Experience

  • Advisory Board

    Green Microfinance

    (Telecommunications industry)

    2008 — Present (1 year)

  • Founder and Executive Chairman

    Kinsale Mobile Ltd

    (Telecommunications industry)

    January 2005 — Present (4 years 2 months)

    As Founder and Chairman, Declan leads Kinsale Mobile (www.kinsalemobile.com) to be competitive enabler and provider of Mobile Virtual Network Operators (MVNO's) focusing on reducing costs and generating revenue streams for community groups, such as charities, business groups, sporting and other affinity organisations. Kinsale has carrier agreements established globally to allow it offer services in all major markets. Kinsale has secured tier 1 brands and corporates to deploy it's solutions. In particular, Kinsale operates the Call4care/Roam4care network on not for profit basis in partnership with charities including Red Cross, Amnesty International, etc

  • Co-Founder, Director

    Flint Telecom Ltd

    (Telecommunications industry)

    July 2004 — Present (4 years 8 months)

    Flint Telecom Ltd (formally Activesky Technologies Ltd) was founded in July 2004 by Declan Conway and Vincent Browne and funded by Avant in 2005 to address the emerging VOIP global market opportunity. Flint has sucessfully deployed solutions globally and went public in 2008 in the US with 2009 revenues approaching 100m$, following strategic acquisitions. Flint is well postioned to be a leading player in this exciting market sector with many white label resellers of it's solutions. Flint's channel partners address over 16m customers in the US alone and market symbol is FLTT

  • Founder and Director

    Encircle Foundation

    (Fund-Raising industry)

    2003 — Present (6 years)

    Encircle Foundation Ltd deploys and leverages corporate social responsibility solutions to help organisations achieve a triple bottom line - ie profit, social and environment objectives. Encircle creates and deploys consumer propositions that are designed to reduce global poverty and impact climate change. The Foundation currently operates in the telecoms, financial and energy sectors and has launched social enterprises that enable users to support social causes selected by the consumer (see www.call4care.nl). Encircle is involved in projects ranging from supporting community Biofuel production in Haiti (www.peopleandplace.org) and Uganda to protecting rainforests in Papua New Guinea. Encircle acts as an advisor to www.traidlinks.ie, www.greenmicrofinance.org and has developed close relationships with other NGOs supporting the UN MDG's

  • Managing Partner

    Avant Capital Partners

    (Investment Management industry)

    January 2002 — Present (7 years 2 months)

    Avant Capital Partners is a privately held investment/management partnership focusing on telecoms, finance and energy sectors. Avant, an incubator, establishes companies after a detailed market analysis, hires the management team and provides seed capital to launch and attract industry partners to succeed. Avant has a successful track record building companies with global operations and recognition including Openet Telecom Ltd (www.openet.com - founded 1997- Kinsale Mobile -founded 2005, Flint Telecom founded 2004)
    Avant's portfolio include

    Openet Telecom Ltd
    Kinsale Mobile Ltd
    Flint Telecom Ltd (FLTT)
    Openlec Ltd
    Digital Knight Ltd
    Carbon4care Ltd
    Encircle Foundation/4care

  • Chairman & President

    Openet Telecom

    (Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Telecommunications industry)

    1997 — 2002 (5 years)

    Declan Conway founded Openet Telecom in 1997 and exited the company in 2002. As President and Chairman of Openet Telecom until he exited in December 2002, Mr Conway secured it's first mediation contract in 1998, hired the executive team in 1999 including Joe Hogan, Philip Hogan, Stuart Kelly Barry Murphy and Ger Curtin and led Openet Telecom through 3 investment rounds, led by Avant (formally Taltan), Benchmark Capital, Orange, SAIC, Cross Atlantic & PWC and AIB. The company secured major carriers clients globally starting with AT&T in 2000 and Openet is now a recognised world leader in Charging, Billing Mediation and transactional intelligence. Openet has won many industry awards (http://www.openet.com/news-events/awards.html) and the founder was recognised as finalised in the Entrepreneur of the Year 2001 for his endevours founding the company (www.eoy.ie)
