Jarrette Atkins’s Summary

With proficiency in creative design, online advertising, print media design and website design, I am uniquely qualified to lend my expertise in the development of corporate identities and branding campaigns, as well as web design and development. My talents are, but not limited to: online banner advertising, multimedia CD/DVD, newspaper and magazine advertising design. I have a broad knowledge of traditional techniques, combined with a strong understanding of the latest technical advances. As a leader in my field, I am also proficient in project management, print production, staff training, and providing creative support to sales teams.
