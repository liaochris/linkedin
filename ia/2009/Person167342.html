MohanaKrishnan K R’s Summary

19 Years of experience in IT spanning domains of Insurance & ERP and various roles including Product development , Delivery management, Sales management, Operations management & General Management

Currently Founder Director of Beyontec Solutions. Was Vice President – Insurance at 3i Infotech, Middle east, Africa, Russia and CIS responsible for the Insurance delivery across the region. Started as a project manager with INSYST (which was subsequently acquired by ICICI Infotech in 2002, now name changed to 3i Infotech) in 1996 (over 12 years experience) with 3 insurance customers at that time, have been pivotal for the growth of the business with over 10 M $ annual revenue from the insurance stream as of 2008 with over 100 insurance customers spanning 25+ countries. One of the founder members to conceive, develop & launch the product PREMIA (in 1996), an enterprise class insurance solution.

Managing customer projects across multiple countries requiring extensive travel and working across multiple time zones and leading a team of over 200 professionals stationed over 20 plus countries at any point of time.

Have been instrumental to conceive, define, implement and continuously improve standard process of Implementation of Products for customers. This has enabled successful deployment of solution across continents and paved way to establish the solution as the undisputed leader in many markets.

Have played a key role as a process owner for the Dubai Quality Awards (DQA), a EFQM excellence model, which lead 3i Infotech to be awarded with the Dubai Quality Appreciation Programme (DQAP) Award.

Evolved an effective onsite – offshore engagement model to reduce cost and improve profitability.

Contributed to various process initiatives and improvements across functions like Admin, HR, and Finance. Played a key role in the conceptualization to implementation, review and improvement of Internal systems for effective operational management of the business.
