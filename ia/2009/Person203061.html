Roma Sur’s Summary

My biggest asset is my diverse writing portfolio. During my nine years as a professional writer I have written press ads, television spots, PSAs, scripts and pitches for reality shows, feature stories - on food, arts & culture, people and lifestyle - in leading magazines and ezines, and animation/live action scripts with original songs for childrens' television. I have recently authored my first book titled ‘The World of Manick Sorcar: Where Art becomes Magic.’

CREDITS: Discovery Channel's 'Surprise by Design,' HGTV's 'Offbeat America' and 'Dream House,' Food Network's 'Rachael Ray's Tasty Travels,' Denver's 5280, LA's 'Valley Scene Magazine,' India's 'Film India Worldwide,' 'Good Housekeeping' Magazine, 'Storytelling Today' - an 18 minute documentary and a 210 page book titled 'The World of Manick Sorcar: Where Art becomes Magic.'

Roma Sur’s Specialties:

Casting for reality shows, scripts for reality tv, scripts for children's television - live action & animation, pitch treatments, magazine and ezine feature stories, full-length biography, lengthy format research papers, brief tv spots and PSAs, proven research skills, copy-editing & proof-reading.
