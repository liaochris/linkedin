Manjit Saggu’s Summary

Successfully managed numerous high value projects with companies such as IBM/Compaq, developing prototypes for products such as wireless communications, laptops and mobile phones, which required excellent personal skill levels, the ability to develop, lead multi-skilled teams and add value by innovation and lateral thinking.

Designed, delivered a range of technical training events relevant to ongoing projects, both on job and classroom based, to technical and non-technical staff to ensure that skill levels were developed to an appropriate level and that completed projects maintained momentum and achieved desired objectives on an ongoing basis.
Established savings of 70% on networking costs by developing alternative solutions when setting up and delivering from marketing stands throughout Europe, Middle East and Africa.
Significantly contributed to the achievement of £8M worth of solution sales for IBM, including hardware, software and services, by utilising excellent marketing skills coupled with highly honed technical ability and the confidence to communicate clearly and effectively at senior management level.
Demonstrated significant analytical skills and innovation when awarded two patents, on behalf of IBM, connected with mobile phone technology which will contribute to the company’s ongoing success in the marketplace.
