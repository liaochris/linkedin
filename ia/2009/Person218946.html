Scott Krinsky’s Summary

I have extensive senior level management experience that includes innovative and influential software start ups, two successful IPOs, and the transformation of ideas into operationalized products. As a Senior Product Manager at MetaCreations I managed a portfolio of 3D products that pushed the boundaries of user interface design. These included Macworld Eddy award winners KPT Bryce, Poser, and RayDream Studio. Later I helped launch Viewpoint, the company behind some of the most innovative visual experiences on the Web. During my tenure as Vice President of Product Management Viewpoint’s market penetration grew to surpass QuickTime's with the help of leading clients such as America Online, Sony, General Motors, and Hewlett Packard.

My experience leading cross-functional teams to launch successful products includes market analysis, assessing market requirements, defining business objectives, scheduling resources to support business objectives, prioritizing and planning projects, budgeting and, pricing and licensing models.

In writing product requirement documents, feature specifications, product roadmaps and marketing plans I have demonstrated excellent written communication skills. In addition, I have presented to venture capital groups, participated in industry panels, and been a guest speaker at notable trade shows such as Macworld.

My passion for technical innovation helped to produce products that exceeded customer expectations. And my strong analytic and decision-making skills helped deliver game changing, award winning and profitable products on schedule. This has translated to a proven track record of growing revenue and market share for new ventures and existing products. Under my tenure the Sonopia online subscription conversion rate surpassed AT&T, Verizon and Sprint, the Viewpoint Media Player market penetration grew to surpass Apple's QuickTime in less than three years, and revenues for KPT Bryce grew 250 percent in less than eighteen months.
