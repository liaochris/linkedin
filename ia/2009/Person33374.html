Brian Gunshor, JD, CBB’s Summary

During his 24 years of private practice in business and transactional law in New York City and Beverly Hills, Brian Gunshor represented numerous business clients in both the private and public sectors. He has also served on the American Arbitration Association's Board of Commercial Arbitrators and as a Superior Court judge pro tem, presiding over, resolving and formulating innovative solutions for dozens of business disputes.

From 2000 to 2004, Brian owned the Riverside County and Greater Palm Springs franchised offices of Sunbelt Business Brokers. He joined Sunbelt's Torrance Office in 2003, which as of September 2007 has merged with Business Team (part of the BTI Group) and become its Los Angeles office and the largest independent business brokerage in the State of California.

During his career as a business broker, Brian has facilitated the sale of such diverse businesses as as Art Galleries, Advertising Agencies, Real Estate Agencies, Staffing Agencies, Plastic Injection Mold Manufacturers, Fixed Base Operators, Cabinetry Manufacturers, Home Improvement, Kitchen, Flooring and Design Firms, Roofing Contractors, Messenger Services, Janitorial Contractors, Furniture Retailers, Driving Schools, Check Cashing Services, Beauty Salons & Day Spas, Restaurants and Night Clubs, Bakeries and Fast Food Franchises, Medical Transport Companies, Durable Medical Equipment Dealers, Personal Fitness & Training Facilities, Aftermarket Auto Equipment Manufacturers, Powder Coating Firms, Recreational Equipment Distributors, Garment Finishing and Distribution Centers, Postal, Shipping & Photocopy Centers, Assisted Living Facilities, Check Cashing Services, Staffing Agencies, Stationers and Gift Shops and Fire and Water Damage Cleanup franchises. He is also actively involved in conducting retained acquisition searches on behalf of private equity groups and major foreign companies seeking strategic acquisitions of domestic B2B and B2C enterprises to gain entry to U.S. markets.

Brian Gunshor, JD, CBB’s Specialties:

Multidisciplinary practice focused on working with entrepreneurs to value their privately-held businesses, and marketing and representing businesses for sale, facilitating negotiations and due diligence to reach agreement and close the transaction.
