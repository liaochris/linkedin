    Virus Concerti

    (Events Services industry)

    April 2005 — March 2007 (2 years)

    I have been working for two years as a Press Office - Production Assistant and Marketing Manager at Virus Concerti (Pordenone, Italy). The Agency organized national and international tours for Italian/international artists like Linea77,Montefiori Cocktail, The Hormonauts, Tre allegri ragazzi morti, Corveleno, and many more. Moreover the agency organized local festivals (Barcolana, Music in Village, Isola della Musica, Fiera della Musica, etc) and live concerts (DepositoGiordani) of national and international artists like: Placebo, Morrissey, Lou Reed, Philip Glass, Echo and Bunnyman, Mike Patton, Gutter Twins, Buzzcocks, Sick of it all, Caparezza, Max Gazzè, Negramaro, Afterhours, Marlene Kuntz and many more.
    My duties included: organization of the promotion and communication plan, staff supervision and report to the Event Coordinator; management of logistic aspects, including monitoring the event time-line and schedule to ensure everything is delivered appropriately; Artists pre and post event assistance; catering organization, spectator services; press accreditation; linguistic assistance; information service and ticketing.
