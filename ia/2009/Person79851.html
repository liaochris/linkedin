Florence Walker’s Summary

Nearly 50 years of experience with the design, construction and management of various housing communities, including mobile home parks, campgrounds and apartments. Past companies that I have owned and run include an Airport Transportation Center, a chain of Sunoco gas stations, a pay phone company, a rental car company, a furniture store and an interior decorating business and a gravel pit. My primary goals are to continue running and maintaining Tilton Terrace, a mobile home community near the Atlantic City Airport, and to begin taking some time to travel the world. I currently reside in Delray Beach, FL and Galloway Township, N.J.
