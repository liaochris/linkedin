Robin Ann Peters’s Summary

Information Technology Artist providing special services in Media esp. Photography, Writing/Journalism, Voice and Graphic Design.

As a Journalist, Robin Ann Peters' has an extreme passion to find, confirm and publish the truth. Research is no stranger to Robin; before approaching a subject, many hours of research are compiled even before setting into an interview, and especially before a finished publication. Robin takes the time to create interesting stories - worthy of publication, and Robin is sensitive to the needs of her subjects, if any.

While reporting daily news reports and commercials and promo's for WSMN 1590 Nashua's News & Talk radio station, Robin also freelances on Voice123.com for Voice-Overs.

Take a step behind the camera, and Ms. Peters shines in her ability to create distinctly unique and professional images. RA works extremely well with People in Flash Photography and especially with Available Light. Her stongest photographic styles include Lifestyle and Executive-level Portrait Photography, Photojournalism, Fine Art and Architecture.

While not in the field, and while at her computer working as a Webmaster and Graphic Artist, Robin Ann can change, enhance or transform a personal or company image swiftly and professionally, with or without client direction. Photoshop is one of her best friends and working with a digital color pallette is a real turn-on.
