PRIMARY INDUSTRY: Media/Publishing | Marketing
SECONDARY INDUSTRIES: Manufacturing | Supply Chain | EHS | Design | Engineering | Contracting
Employer: Penton Media, 1300 E 9th Street, Cleveland, OH 44109
Phone: 216-931-9438 | Email: james.cowart@penton.com

My professional experiences are extensive in the development of marketing audiences to better position publishers in a competitive marketplace.

In the past six years, I have experienced a company divestiture and two company names changes in the publishing industry. We were first Primedia Business Media, then Prism Business Media and now Penton Media. Prior to that, I held various leadership roles for Not-For Profit or political organizations, and I worked for five years at BPA Worldwide, where I conducted audits and internal audits of business publications, consumer publications and tradeshows and handled member relations management issues.

In 2008 I completed my MBA program at Baker University in Overland Park, Kansas. Since then, I have expanded my base of knowledge that I learned in marketing, statistics, finance and HR areas to help me make stronger, more effective decisions in the media/publishing industry.
