Gene Tognacci’s Summary

Voice Over Artist and Media producer. Extensive experience in commercial voiceovers and training/sales narrations. Also extensive audio restoration and archival experience on oral histories.

I have a full digital studio with staff and all the bells and whistles to make your production sound great!

Seeking clients who are looking for a warm friendly believable presentation for their Bank, Hospital, Church or Retail Commercials.

Seeking training clients who want a believable and trustworthy voice spokesman for their power point or flash presentation, DVD, or website.

Looking for Historical Societies, Museums and other Archives seeking to transfer oral histories and vintage recordings onto cd.

Always looking for clients that want to have FUN! in telling their message.

Gene Tognacci’s Specialties:

I specialize in same day turnaround of large projects (up to 4 hours of finished narration per day) I also do excellent dialogue and real person narrations.

Our restoration projects have been used by numerous museums and historical societies, as well as in high profile legal cases.
