Arthur Charles Van Wyk’s Summary

I currently work as the Brand Evangelist for Sytech Supplies in Durban, South Africa. Sytech Supplies is an international signage supplies, graphics solution and display systems distributor and reseller.

After studying toward a BCom degree at the University of the Western Cape, I...

..worked as a creative consultant to the Cancer Association, where I developed material and content to promote various CANSA campaigns.

..worked as Head of Creative Solutions Development at Ticketweb South Africa, where I was involved in web strategy, content management and online brand evangelism..

..relocated to Durban, where I spent a number of years in telecoms, during which I conceptualised and constructed the first phase of the internal customer portal of MTN and was part of the team that launched the Data Helpdesk at Cell C KZN.
