Susie Blackmon’s Summary

Raised in Hawaii by parents who were born and raised in the Hawaiian Islands. Moved to Maggie Valley, NC from Hilo, HI in December 2006, and obtained my North Carolina Real Estate Broker's License.

Have also lived in California, Florida, Santa Fe NM, New York City, and Kansas City.

My experience is extensive - approximately 12 years as controller/administrator for the largest independent tire dealers in Hawaii, numerous years as a real estate paralegal, corporate and labor law legal secretary, flight attendant with TWA, caterer, retailer of high-end western boots, apparel and tack, and a passionate owner and breeder of Quarter horses while living in Sarasota, Florida.
