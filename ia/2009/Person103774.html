Janet Hipkiss’s Summary

Corporate Angels was created to help ease the pressure of administration for small/medium sized businesses.By outsourcing administration needs, our clients to have more hours in the day to develop their business, and have a life!

Our Virtual Assistant Corporate Angels offer business support services custom-built for our clients on an ad hoc or regular basis including telephone answering, secretarial services, sales and marketing, web site services, bookkeeping and database management, to name but a few. Our services allow our clients to have their work completed remotely from our own offices. This reduces overheads as we do not need to be based on site and we only get paid for the time that we are working. We can also offer on site support if required.

Corporate Angels also offer Event Organisation from Wedding planning through to Corporate Events. Due to the flexibility of how we work, Corporate Angels can do as much or as little as you need - from offering advice, sourcing venues and suppliers to organising and managing the whole thing!

For more details, check out http://www.corporateangels.net. We can also be followed on facebook: http://www.facebook.com/janet hipkiss

and twitter: http://www.twitter.com/CorporateAngels.

Lets face it, everybody needs an Angel!!!
