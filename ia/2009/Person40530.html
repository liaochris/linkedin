Charles Glommen’s Summary

Today, businesses must be data-driven to succeed. But being data-driven is not enough. The product in front of the data must be creative, useful, and cost-effective. My experience allows me to be effective in all these areas.

Creativity is at the root of creating a product that sets itself apart from the competition. Finding the right blend of traditional customer-driven input and internally-driven ingenuity will yield consistent success. As a Product Architect, this blend is at the core of my focus. I work closely with the business managers and analysts to understand client needs. Additionally, I leverage my product design experience to help determine how best to apply the technology that produces a creative and useful product.

The SaaS product must also be scalable. As a veteran of SaaS in the web analytics industry, I understand the enormity of the challenge behind such platforms. As an early and core developer of WebSideStory's flagship product, HBX, many of my contributions to its original concepts and designs not only helped develop a massively scalable SaaS architecture, but also shaped the analytics industry. Additionally, as a Cloudera Certified Hadoop Profressional, I employ powerful technologies that ensure a business has a solid foundation for pervasive business intelligence.

With this experience, coupled with my creativity, I strive to produce measurable, profitable, and scalable platforms that customers need.
