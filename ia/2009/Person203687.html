Ronald W Stevens’s Summary

Co-founded Mingleverse and started/CEO of Mixpo and Digital 5. Was an early FAE then VP in 4 other successful consumer silicon and software start ups: iCompression, Arithmos, S3 Inc. and LSI Logic Canada.
Raised over $50M in tech financing.
Dual citizen residing in Vancouver Canada and SF California.
Past Chairman for New Media BC's Digital Media Fusion Forum.
NACD- National Association of Corporate Directors
OACETT - Ontario Association of Certified Engineering Technicians & Technologists

History:

MIxpo Portfolio Broadcasting Inc.
Original Investor, former BOD and President & CEO of MIXPO

EIR at Yaletown Venture Partners

Digital 5 Inc. -Founder and CEO - Developer of Netplay. Customers were major CE OEMs worldwide. 1st streaming wireless audio video technology for PC to CE home networks. Currently found in all retail CE stores. Funding provided by major VC's , Intel, Philips and TI. Company sold and now part of Macromedia.


iCompression Inc. - VP Sales/Marketing/Bus.Dev for leading a CE and PC chip Encoder technology for digital video recorder industry (Tivo). Sold to Globespan now Conexant.

Arithmos - VP Sales and Bus. Dev - Digital display semiconductor company sold to ST Micro.

S3 Inc. - Managing Director of WW Channel Sales and Marketing and International Operations - various other positions from beginning through IPO over 7 years. S3 was the leading developer of 2D/3D graphic chips for PC industry.

LSI Logic - Design Center Manager for LSI Logic Canada (Montreal). The 1st developer of custom ASIC silicon in Canada, early management through IPO.

Ronald W Stevens’s Specialties:

Experienced start- up entrepreneur from $0 to $50M with emphasis on engineering,sales, executive management and board level experience. Focus on consumer facing digital media software, hardware and communications.
