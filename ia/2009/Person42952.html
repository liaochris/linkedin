Chris Bovi’s Summary

A job that I started as a student employee at Ithaca College has become a career for me in a very unique industry. I began my professional career as the assistant to the textbook manager at The Bookstore. I learned about the textbook industry; new and used books, interacting with faculty, publishers and textbook wholesalers. The unique aspect is the relationship between the bookstores and the textbook wholesalers, where they are both suppliers to one another in used books.

After leaving Ithaca College I had a brief tenure with a local credit union before re-entering the University/College retail segment. I joined MBS Textbook Exchange, a privately held wholesaler of used and new textbooks. I worked for several years in the wholesale division, growing textbook sales to my customers and prospecting for new buyback opportunities to bring used books back to MBS.

In 1998 I left the Wholesale Division for the Systems Division at MBS, which is responsible for providing retail technology solutions to our customers as a value-add to the textbook relationship. We provide front-end POS solutions intergrated to back-office inventory management, accounting applications, web-commerce and campus host interfacing. My territory annually generates over a million dollars in sales to my division.

I enjoy the unique aspects of the College Retail Industry and traveling the country. I get to work with a wide variety of customers, both current and prospective, which continually provides new challenges and varied sales processes.
