Stephen Ahern, CPA/PFS, CFP®’s Summary

Stephen co-founded and was named President of Wealth Management Advisors, LLC. He is also a shareholder of the firm Sullivan Bille, P.C., a diverse New England regional Certified Public Accounting firm. Stephen came to the Sullivan Bille Group of companies after several years at a “big five” international accounting firm where he served as leader of Corporate Executive Programs and Individual Asset Management Consulting Services for the Northeastern United States.

Stephen is a licensed Certified Public Accountant (CPA), a Certified Financial Planner™ practitioner (CFP®) and has been designated a Personal Financial Specialist (PFS) by the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants. He is also a Registered Financial "Fee-Only" Advisor with the National Association of Personal Financial Advisors. He received a Bachelor of Science degree in Business Administration concentrating in Accounting and Computer Science from Providence College, a Master of Science degree in Taxation (MST) from Bentley College and graduate studies in the Program for Financial Planners at Boston University.

Stephen served as Chairman of the Massachusetts Society of Certified Public Accountants (MSCPA) Personal Financial Planning Committee, an active member of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA) Personal Financial Planning Division and the Financial Planning Association (FPA) (a national professional organization of Certified Financial Planners). He also served as Chairman, President and Treasurer of the Board of Directors for the Financial Planning Association of Massachusetts.

Stephen Ahern, CPA/PFS, CFP®’s Specialties:

Stephen has provided individual financial, investment, estate and tax planning and small business consulting to a diverse base of clients. His clients include key top-level executives, high-net-worth individuals, business owners, venture capitalists and entrepreneurs. He has delivered numerous presentations on financial, investment, retirement and tax planning to corporations and professional groups and written a number of articles on investment, education and estate planning.
