Christie "CJ" Johnson’s Summary

I have been working in Advertising for 3 years and absolutely love it... and hate it. Here's why: The truth is that advertising is a constant hustle. I'm always racing the clock with daily deadlines, while having to be on the ball (and friendly) with clients & media outlets, proofing ads, managing budgets, buying/selling media, laying out ads, billboards, landing pages, having anything & everything ready at the drop of a dime (esp for my boss)... always looking at the small details, all while still finding time to be creative! I love it because the day goes fast, I'm good at my job, I love some of the people I work with & I feel extremely accomplished at the end of the day (Try my high pressure job for ONE day and you'll understand). The only reason I don't like it is that the stress can really put a toll on you, but... I have grown a thick skin!

There's so much I want to do... I want to go into Event planning someday & be the person who sets up some of the greatest weddings, corporate events & amazing parties... I'm so damn good at it and people seem to connect with me well. I would love to be a producer for either a radio or television station b/c I always wanted to know what it was like working behind the scenes (I basically do it in Print ad form and in writing radio/cable, but I'm ready for the NEXT STEP!).

With my 3 years of advertising/media buying experience, I have learned an unbelievable amount of information... I know what sells and what doesnt, I know what media works for certain clients while not working much for others, I can single handedly run an ad campaign sucessfully and I look forward to moving up in this advertising/marketing/hardcore industry and utilize the many talents & skills I've gained over this time!
