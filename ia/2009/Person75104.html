Erik de Groot’s Summary

My most relevant working experience starts with the function of “Assistant Import Manager”.
In 1995 I was hired as an Assistant Import Manager for an International Courier Service.
In April 2000 I started as Coordinator Administration Office at Getronics.
My initial task was to set up a Logistic Administration Office with initially 8 Logistic Administrative Officers. The Administration Office was to become an internal Logistic Customer Service Office with all non logistic departments as clients.
Next to an integration process for two recent acquired companies, Getronics also wanted to centralize their logistics. The warehouse in Amsterdam was to become the central warehouse; therefore my staff was doubled to 16 fte’s.
I also cooperated in developing new processes and procedures and helping with the implementation of these new processes and procedures.
After a year I was offered the position of Supervisor Administration Office witch I accepted. My direct report was to the Warehouse Manager
As I spend more and more of my time on Quality management and all operational logistic actions where outsourced, I was offered the position of Quality Consultant witch I accepted on April 1st 2004.
