Duncan Yelds’s Summary

Duncan married IN 1988 & has a six year old son; he has been a registered nurse since 1986. At age 21, Duncan invested in his first business, and at age 31 reached the highest position in the nursing field as DON (Hospital CEO). During this time, he set up multiple businesses in the health industry including joint ventures in physiotherapy practices.
At age 20, He bought his first investment property, & has since successfully negotiated upwards of 50 contracts, & renovated more than 25 properties, both for himself & others, in Australia & the USA. Duncan first invested in the US market in 2004. He also has experience in Land Development and Building Design.
Duncan has since leaving the comfort of boarding school in country NSW has taken on many & varied roles from being a “garbo” on Sydney Harbour to selling casual nurses.
Throughout this time, Duncan acquired extensive experience in setting up multiple structures for investing both in real estate and in business.
Duncan has an extensive intrinsic knowledge of conducting the business of real estate in the US.
In 1997, Duncan undertook his first joint venture, and has coached joint venture partners and business partners since that time.
In 2008 Duncan commenced working with the Positive Real Estate Coaching and Mentoring Porgram that they run Nationally in Australia - Duncan now owns the NSW Franchise of the Business and currently is responsible (with his team) for the nearly 200 clients going through their lifetime program.
Since 2006, Duncan has co-ordinated events for multi-national corporations, and performed as a speaker, presenting and educating for a range of investment seminars. Duncan has also performed voluntarily for various educational seminars and in a coaching capacity.
Duncan’s passions, along with his family are – Creating, mentoring, Educating, Presenting and Coaching and or course investing.
My primary goal in my business life to "enroll and empower all those to come into my life"
