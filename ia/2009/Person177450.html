Nicolò Wojewoda’s Summary

I mix things. Just like a DJ (which I am) or a chef (which I would like to be, someday). The only difference is, my mixes are much more abstract. I see what’s out there, and more specifically what’s around me, and try to connect ideas with resources, people with other people, skills with problems, tools with solutions.

In nonprofit jargon, you could say I strategize and mobilize for the common good. Big vision, careful planning and committed action are my signature traits. I enjoy building self-sustaining groups who can grow autonomously when it's time for me to move on to the next step in my career.

I'm currently looking for a new exciting project, to energize me and drive my commitment to scale up my impact.
