James Waddell’s Summary

I have developed a broad based background in many different industries from manufacturing to consulting to executive management. I have even served in local government as a town councilman.

My specific expertise ranges from Human Resource Management to Personnel and Organizational Development to internal and external Consultancy operations to Executive Management.
