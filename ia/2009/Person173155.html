Christine Lowe Woolard’s Summary

In 2006, I established my own line of natural bath and body care products with an eye toward reducing the use of chemicals in my family’s skin care regimen and to finding a natural treatment for my daughter’s eczema. As the owner of this company, I continue to research and implement the use of essential and natural oils in my formulations. All my products are at least 95% natural.

As a small business owner and exemplary writer, with a strong knowledge base in small business practices, I am a featured blogger on sistapreneurs.ning.com as well as maintaining my own blog which featured other small business owners. In addition, as a former educator with three children of my own, I have established a blog at neosoulmama.info which focuses on information about raising black children and other parenting issues.

If you are looking for a ghostwriter of short (300-500) word articles on parenting, discipline, natural cures, benefits of natural oils/butter, K-8 education and mathematics or technology education, please feel free to message me and I will be more than happy to discuss your needs.
