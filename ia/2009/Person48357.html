Colin Renouf’s Summary

Well, I've been specialising in all things IBM WebSphere Application Server (WAS) (middleware for the uninitiatited) and AIX for the last few years, in addition to my general infrastructure, sales, statementing, integration, workflow, and portal duties. I've worked closely with IBM on new products and facilities, written for IBM's systems magazine and have finally got into writing and editing books!

If you want to know how WAS 6.1 works, check out my "Under The Hood" work in the following book:

http://www.redbooks.ibm.com/redpieces/abstracts/sg247347.html?Open

Note IBM WebSphere architects and management who have signed it off in the acknowledgements. Hopefully that means it is right!

Also, look at the Case Study in Enterprise Infrastructure Architecture in the RedPapers as I hope this explains how to best deploy WebSphere products on AIX.
