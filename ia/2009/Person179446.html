Tom O'Rourke’s Summary

President/CEO of O’Rourke Hospitality Marketing, LLC — with nearly two decades of strategic marketing experience as a successful entrepreneur.

Prior to founding O'Rourke, Tom partnered in the growth and development of two successful and innovative enterprises, honing on the ability to move a concept to fruition with dramatic results by applying solid business practices — market research, product development, sales and marketing resource deployment — with a high level of creative energy.

Throughout his career, Tom has taken the marketer’s approach, listening to the client, investigating how they were perceived in the marketplace and then defining a creative brief that ensured the end result aligned with the their objectives.

O’Rourke is an award-winning hotel marketing firm headquartered in Newburyport, Massachusetts, serving clients worldwide.
