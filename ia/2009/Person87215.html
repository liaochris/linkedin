Giuseppe Aiello’s Summary

I'm a multimedia designer professionally born in 2001. I started designing and developing websites with simple HTML+Javascript and ASP pages but quite soon I started to study and use Flash Actionscript for my internet and CD applications.

I worked with different agencies in Milan and Madrid, always as an interactive and multimedia designer and developer. In 2003 I created the makkiadesign project in collaboration with Milos Milosevic.

So far, makkiadesign grown up and learned a lot about internet industry. See our portfolio for more info: http://www.makkiadesign.com

Giuseppe Aiello’s Specialties:

Ruby on Rails, Actionscript, CSS, XML, XHTML, Javascript, PHP, ASP, cross browser compatibility
