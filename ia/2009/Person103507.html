Jan Arendtsz’s Summary

Over 14 years of industry experience including Software-as-a-Service (SaaS), Integration-as-a-Service (IaaS), SOA, Enterprise Integration, Consulting, and Professional Services.

Intimately involved in all facets of SaaS for the last six years. Spent three years at NetSuite in product management and professional services, and founded Celigo--a startup delivering products and services for the SaaS industry.

Enterprise Integration Specialist with a deep understanding of SOA and Web Services. As the Integration Product Manager at NetSuite was responsible for launching and evangelizing NetSuite's Web Services platform.

Business development with an emphasis on ISV relationships. Experience in building an ISV/Developer program, structuring complex business models, and managing vendor relationships.

Entrepreneur with experience co-founding start-ups.
