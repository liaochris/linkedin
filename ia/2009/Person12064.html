Amy Day’s Summary

When I join a team, I do it for long term success. I carefully choose organizations based on their culture and my passion for their mission. The Walt Disney Company and CME Federal Credit Union are two such companies. Fresh out of high school, I joined Disney as a part time employee and quickly earned the respect of management and the team through my work ethic. At CME, I quickly advanced. Regardless of the position or duty, I passionately pour myself into the role. I have great experience in cash handling, sales, balancing, organization, inventory, product placement, new accounts, lending, problem resolution, reconciliation, conflict resolution, performance evaluations, position creation, human resources, training, compliance, payroll, and more. I love what I do, and I do it to facilitate the success of my organization. Ultimately, my husband and I would like to start a consulting business to aid credit unions in compliance, process improvement, and strategic planning.
