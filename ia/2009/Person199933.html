Robbin Long’s Summary

OBJECTIVE and Immediate Goal: To enter a long term relationship as a sales account manager with a progressive industrial sales company.

ACCOMPLISHMENTS

Negotiate, sell and manage numerous commercial & residential construction projects with both individual clients and corporate clients such as Ted’s Escondido Restaurants, Braum’s Ice Cream & Dairy Stores, Anderson Sales, Precision Instruments and KSBI Television. I successfully closed over 4.2 million in construction contracts.

Develop two separate steel building manufacturing sales territories, one on the East coast & one in the central United States. I closed over 5.5 million in sales between these two territories.

Develop a high degree of computer literacy by mastering programs such as Works, Word, Quick Books, Excel, Desktop Publishing, AutoCAD, Key-CAD and MBS Building Estimating.

Build and maintain a steel building construction company where I sold and delivered over 200 projects and erected more than 50 of these.

Promote & secure investors for eight oil & gas projects over the last four years, raising over 2.5 million dollars.

Compose & negotiate numerous participation agreements with other oil companies including Anadarko Petroleum, Ra-Jac Oil Company, EXCO and Ward Petroleum.

Oversee, manage & complete numerous oil & gas development projects.

Become one of four worldwide qualified specialist in horizontal logging equipment & operations in a new & emerging technology.

Own and operate an oilfield service company where I managed sales, employees and operations. We specialized in drilling mud and well engineering from site preparation to pipeline hook up. I also sold, contracted and built over 40 complete tank battery systems. We did over 4.5 million in gross sales over a six year period.

Excel in & complete a four month long specialty chemical school in drilling mud technology.
