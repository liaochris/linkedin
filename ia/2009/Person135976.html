Kyle Ramkissoon’s Summary

IJC Partners provides recruitment and manager selection services within all major investment functions, including research, portfolio management, and structuring roles. Our executive search professionals work with single-manager hedge funds, funds of hedge funds, multi-manager complexes, and broker-dealer proprietary trading groups.

As career recruiters with a commitment to hedge funds & the alternative family – including REITs and private equity – and in-house analytical expertise, we’re proud to deliver a thoughtful but aggressive service to this increasingly significant sector as it redefines the landscape of global markets.

Our practices can engage the varying complexity of your needs via:

· Retained search execution

· Contingency fulfillment

· Consulting and contract projects

· Organizational development and succession planning
