Kirk Petersen’s Summary

Versatile communications professional with extensive experience in consulting, speechwriting and other writing, employee and HR communications, publication management and website content development. Has built Intranets at two Fortune 40 companies, and served as speechwriter for the CEO of one of them. In addition to 15 years in financial services, has consulting experience in industries including health insurance, pharmaceuticals, consumer healthcare, entertainment and retailing. Adept at clarifying complicated topics and tailoring written material to diverse audiences.
