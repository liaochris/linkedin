Norbert Herrmann’s Summary

I am a catalyst for positive change, spending the last 15 years focusing on driving innovation and delivering commercially sound results through a balance of business goals, customer/user needs and delivery platform capabilities.

This proven approach has yielded excellent results and maximized 'Return on Investment' for products, services, client business and internal operations.

I have first-hand experience in small, rapid growth start-up environments including my own business (which I grew and managed until its acquisition in 2005) through to large corporations working from Melbourne, Sydney, Brisbane, San Francisco, New York and Toronto.

Some of the brands and businesses I have worked with include; Open Text Global, Telstra, Red Cross Australia, Medibank Private, QBE Insurance, Nissan Australia, State Library Victoria, Australia Post, NRL, Ansell, Crown Casino, Foster's Group and multiple government departments.
