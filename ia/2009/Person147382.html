Mary Ann McCarty’s Summary

Mary Ann McCarty, MS, is degreed in Accounting and Psychology with over 10 years of Accounting experience. She is detail-oriented and organized, able on solitary projects as well as in a team, and is loyal and dedicated to her field. She is especially knowledgeable in cost tracking and audit preparation.

Some gifts Mary Ann brings to the table are an understanding of the human condition (a previous job was at a suicide prevention house), a wide range of experiences, flexibility and an aptitude for learning, professional communication skills and a cheerful willingness to help. These traits make her an asset wherever she is.
