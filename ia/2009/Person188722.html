Atul Chaudhary’s Summary

Program Manager for a wide variety of Telecommunication services. Particularly interested in Data Centres, VPN, VoIP, CCT, IPT, DWDM technologies. While migration projects are routine, I am always interested in challenging projects, those starting with many unknowns on the leading edge of technology involving lab developments, testing or proof of concept.
