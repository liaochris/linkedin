Stephanie Rountree’s Summary

EdenSong Essentials is a woman-run business, producing all natural organic skin care and body care products for people just like you. Early on, it was obvious that the natural ways were going to follow the founder, Stephanie Rountree.

Starting with her first pregnancy over 30 years ago, Stephanie began using only naturals and organics products -- be it food, gardening, skin care, clothing, etc. Aside from her university education in English and nutrition, she is mostly self-taught through constant research, studying and experimentation -- the best teachers! Her passion for organics has grown into a business, wherein she is always striving for perfection in the products.

In that perfection is a note of simplicity. Seeking only the purest ingredients (never using any synthetics, parabens or fillers), all products are of the highest quality, organic, natural, fair-trade, wild-harvested, cruelty-free, ethical, sustainable, 100% chemical-preservative free and locally grown when possible. These ingredients must be fresh in order to retain the vital, energetic life-force of the botanicals and other unprocessed elements, working in harmony to be the most effective with outstanding results.

We turn your affection for luxury into a better quality of life. From our product offerings to our core philosophy behind EdenSong Essentials, we believe everyone can feel alive and strive for their best. To that effect we support progressive concepts that will make a difference in the lives and self-esteem of everyone, because we are all beautiful and deserve to feel wonderful, inside and out.

Purity, Quality, Effectiveness. It's really quite simple.

Go organic! Your body will love you for it.
