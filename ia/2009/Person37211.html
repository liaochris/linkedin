Randi Evans’s Summary

PRESIDENT, Career Counselors Employment Agency based in Long Island, New York.

Randi Evans had a vision when she opened Career Counselors in 1986 - to make the right employment connections based on people's real needs and goals. Commensurately, she has been successfully connecting hiring companies with qualified employees ever since!

A "boutique" firm, Career Counselors provides uniquely personalized and diverse staffing solutions to our varied clientele, including direct hire (permanent placement), temporary/contract/temp-to-hire, and executive recruiting.
The goal at Career Counselors is to identify the right talent for the right professional opportunity, something we've been doing honestly and professionally for over 20 years!

STAYING POWER...
Still recruiting for the very first client she ever obtained in this industry over 20 years ago, Randi continues to manage and grow a company based in honesty and integrity, with her finger on the pulse of what hiring corporations and job seekers want and need, and with the skillful ability to satisfy those needs quickly and correctly.

ACADEMIC BACKGROUND
Randi Evans began her academic career at Emory University and completed her studies at Hofstra University, earning a Bachelors degree.

With great value for the education she received through academia, her greatest learning has come from overcoming the on-going challenges in managing and growing a small woman-owned business. It is in facing and overcoming these very challenges that Randi has become a respected and valued partner to her clients, the hiring executives, and her job seeking candidates
