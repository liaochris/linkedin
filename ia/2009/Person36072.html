Barbara Weltman’s Summary

Tax and law expert for Inc.com and a contributing editor of New York Enterprise Report and PINK magazine.

Author of top-selling books: "The Rational Guide to Building Small Business Credit" which features a foreward by Dun & Bradstreet; "J.K. Lasser's Small Business Taxes" and "The Complete Idiot's Guide to Starting a Home-Based Business."

Founder of BarbaraWeltman.com, a comprehensive business resource center for small businesses.

Publisher of Big Ideas for Small Business®, a free monthly online newsletter providing entrepreneurs with the information on issues and concerns that matter most to the small-business community, and "Idea of the Day"sm - <http://barbaraweltman.com/resources/iotd/>. The online publication addresses the unique needs and interests of the small business community and explores market-impact issues such as health insurance, energy costs, and employee retention.

Host of a weekly, hour-long radio show, Build Your Business (BYB), providing business owners with the information they need to start, run and grow a successful business. The program airs Mondays from 3pm to 4pm Central Time on the WS Radio network.

Media commentator featured in The New York Times, The Wall Street Journal, The Washington Post, Reuters, Forbes.com, Marketwatch.com, WABC-TV, Fox News, CNNRadio, and CNBC.

An adjunct professor at Manhattanville College, teaching Principles of Entrepreneurship, and frequent lecturer at the Learning Annex.

Barbara Weltman’s Specialties:

Providing a small business resource center covering all facets of building a successful small business -- planning, launching, financing, marketing, managing and protecting a business, tax law expert, leading speaker on business-related topics
