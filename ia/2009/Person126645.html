Karin Witzig Rozell’s Summary

I combine my writing, coaching and health counseling experience to create powerful websites and marketing materials for wellness professionals. I translate my clients' unique talents into words that distinguish them from the pack.

I started as a health and nutrition counselor in 1999, as a graduate of the Institute for Integrative Nutrition. Within three years of business, I was saddened to see my friends and colleagues drop out of private practice because they failed to attract enough business.

They did not fail because they were not good at what they did. They failed because that they did not have the right marketing pieces to support their talents.

I always loved marketing and so I started offering my friends my help. They really liked what I created and I before I knew it, the Marketing Materials Maven was born!
