Paul Jacobs’s Summary

Recruitment strategy / HR specialist with a particular interest in bleeding-edge recruitment technologies and employment communications.

Co-founder of TribeHQ, a web-based conversational platform for different occupational groups that encourages global kinship, and sharing of ideas and knowledge across borders. Offers a completely new approach to online recruitment.

Excited by the opportunities that emerging technologies and social media offer to jobseekers and employers.

Founder of Recruitment 2.0 Asia Pacific (http://thoughtleaders.ning.com), an online community of professionals in the Asia Pacific region interested in recruitment innovations, recruitment technologies, social media etc.

Paul Jacobs’s Experience

  • Co-founder

    TribeHQ Limited

    (Online Media industry)

    December 2008 — Present (9 months)

    TribeHQ is a web-based platform that:
    1. Enables occupational tribalism – members select the tribe or tribes applicable to them – e.g. nurses, engineers, teachers, .Net developers
    2. Encourages conversation between tribe members, using micro-blogging as the foundation
    3. Re-invents recruitment advertising - think interactive job board!

  • Founder

    Recuitment 2.0 Asia Pacific

    (Online Media industry)

    June 2007 — Present (2 years 3 months)

    Recruitment 2.0 Asia Pacific is a community of professionals who share an interest in the evolution of recruitment within the APAC region.

  • Owner

    Engage

    (Privately Held; Public Relations and Communications industry)

    April 2006 — Present (3 years 5 months)

    A consulting services business. Engage works with employers and recruitment agencies to target and engage with their active and passive jobseeker audiences.
