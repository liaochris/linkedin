Rick Bacchus’s Summary

Rick Bacchus is a very talented and accomplished songwriter, arranger, composer, and producer who brings magic to music with his natural ability to feel the emotion of what he is playing, having never taken a music lesson in his life. Now President & CEO of Bacchus Music, he began his career in the music industry over twenty years ago and has collaborated with many multi-platinum artists and musicians. He was also awarded a Double Platinum Album for writing a hit song for the Australian Idol. He is currently introducing his two new recording artists, Chris Sheppard (www.myspace.com/chrissheppardnyc) and Mike Grabow (www.myspace.com/MikeGrabow.com) to the world, who are currently being championed by top music executives. Prepare yourself for the next generation of superstars, courtesy of our musical genius, Rick Bacchus. -Kyra Wiedenkeller For more information visit www.bacchusmusic.com.
