John Dye’s Summary

John is the President of Fluid Studio, an integrated marketing and advertising firm located near Salt Lake City, UT. Under his strong leadership abilities, Fluid has grown from a tiny start-up to a viable graphic design studio and advertising firm that has more than tripled in size. He has been published in a number of trade journals and publications.

Prior to joining Fluid, John worked for First Health as a policy and procedures analyst.

John served for over seven years as an adjunct faculty member teaching English and Communications courses at multiple colleges and universities, including Brigham Young University, Utah Valley University, Salt Lake Community College, Utah Career College, and Stevens Henager College.

John has been involved in community organizations for several years and currently serves on the board of the Salt Lake Chapter of the Cougar Club. He is also a member of the Utah Advertising Federation, AIGA, Salt Lake and Davis County Chambers of Commerce, and BYU Management Society—Salt Lake Chapter.

John is a graduate of Brigham Young University where received both his Masters and a Bachelor of Arts in English. In his spare time, he enjoys running, reading, and spending time with his wife and three children.
