Pawan Murthy’s Summary

Pawan Murthy has a unique background of analytics and creativity that provides proven and successful marketing leadership for startups of all sizes.

In 2001, he founded Stellarwind, LLC, a firm dedicated to bridging the gap between the technical and marketing worlds. In addition to working with over 60 tech startups, Stellarwind has helped larger companies such as Nationwide Insurance, LensCrafters, and Novartis.

From 2003 to 2006, Pawan was Director of Marketing at Swapalease.com, the world's largest auto lease marketplace. With his skills in usability, Internet marketing, and design, Pawan helped better convey Swapalease’s process and value proposition. Additionally, he significantly increased conversion rate. Within one year, Pawan's strategies helped triple revenue in this company.

Currently, Pawan is at BMW Financial Services - in charge of marketing for their two startups, Alphera Financial Services and up2drive.com. These companies provide automotive lending for all makes and models (through the dealer and online). Pawan is responsible for building these brands from the ground up by forming key business partnerships and cost-effective advertising campaigns.

In 2008, Pawan founded The PEP Talks, a quarterly speaking engagements with the mission to help students find their passions/careers by connecting them with local inspiring professionals. Pawan is also available for public speaking engagements to high schools and universities.

In 2009, Pawan published his first book, "Race to the Starting Line: Succeed In College by Leaving Grades Behind." The book stresses the importance of networking, attitude and work experience as a path to success in addition to grades. The book can be found on Amazon.com.
