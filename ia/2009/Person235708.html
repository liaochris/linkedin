Tom Capps’s Summary

I am the Operations Manager for a Healthcare startup in Santa Clara, CA. I supervise two full time staff for HR and Staffing and approximately 165 employees. In addition to the zillion other hats I wear, I am now the social media marketing guy so I am learning how to do all this.

On my writing, I am currently writing two novels simultaneously, one an update of one of my earlier books and the second a little classic movie from the 1960s called "To Sir, With Love."

In my own book, the first African-American President is reelected by an overwhelming majority of the voters. "Inaugural Event" is about these three women soldiers who desperately try to save President Obama and his family during the absolute worst terrorist attack in the history of the United States.

While the story centers around the attack on the President, it is really about the courage, determination, and willingness to make the ultimate sacrifice of these three women as they desperately try to save the President and his family and how they become the first women, since the Civil War, to win the Congressional Medal of Honor.

On "To Sir, With Love," I am updating it to the present, bringing it to America, and writing it with someone like Will Smith in mind, though who will actually play the part remains to be seen. Sidney Poitier became the first African-American to ever win an Academy Award for his role.

This will not be Sidney Poitier's movie however. It will be brutal and in your face about racial prejudice, violence against women and sex. Still, a real tear jerker. I want everyone in the theater crying by the time this thing is over. I want them crying every time they even think about it.

If I can pull it off, this will be the most talked about movie of the year.

I already pretty much have the entire story worked out and just need to get it down on paper.
