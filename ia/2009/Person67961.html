David Stelle’s Summary

I am a technology savvy entrepreneur/business individual. Over the years I have trained my focus on building and working with companies that leverage the Internet to sell goods and services as well as provide all necessary levels of support. My success hinges on the ability to collaborate with other entrepreneurs/business professionals in an effort to synergize our respective expertise and maximize growth potential.

I am direct and transparent in my communications, always striving to maintain the highest level of integrity. I believe that business is all about people. My goal in all my relationships, both professional and personal, is always to help others succeed.

My strengths are:

1. Internet Marketing. I have a command of internet marketing concepts, tools, and resources that help companies and professionals find customers, generate business, and support their products/services online.

2. Sales. I am a people person. I enjoy connecting products and services with the individuals and companies that need them most; creating new sales channels, motivating, and challenging co-workers to realize their full potential.

3. Customer Service/Client Relations. I believe that business is all about people, and keeping clients/people happy is a goal I thrive to achieve on every project.
