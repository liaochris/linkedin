Allan Glass’s Summary

My focus is towards investment related real estate both commercial and residential. My group has handled over $1 Billion in real estate transactions representing developers, investment funds, loss mitigation teams and REO departments at banks and private lending institutions.

I am currently working with financial institutions and private equity partners to manage and sell distressed assets and REO portfolios.

With my RE Urban Group, I am raising capital for bulk purchases of notes and real estate assets to buy, renovate and resell to FHA insured borrowers in the Los Angeles marketplace.

Allan Glass’s Specialties:

Real Estate acquisitions, dispositions, syndication, REO dispositions, Urban Development, both commerical and multi-residential brokerage, BPO Services
