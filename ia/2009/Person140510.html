Leyla Hur’s Summary

Leyla Hur International is a company which focuses on guiding, teaching, empowering, and inspiring individuals and large groups find success by effecting positive changes, and achieving success by over-coming self-esteem and fear issues.

Leyla Hur International has a world-wide following, and has aided a wide number of individuals and companies attain success. With clients ranging from major celebrities to entrepreneurs to housewives, Leyla Hur International has a program that will work for you and your schedule.
