Pascal-Louis Perez’s Summary

I've been working on web applications for the past 10 years. I am passionate about distributed systems and the scaling challenges of high trafficked web applications.

Currently, I lead the engineering at KaChing. Prior to this position, I was working at Google on the JavaScript compiler which makes Gmail and other Google applications fast. In this position, I was a member of the ECMA committee working towards a standardization of ES4, the evolution of AS3 and ES3.

At Stanford, I specialized in software theory, especially on compiler construction and optimization, type theory and computational theory. My thesis focused on type inference for object oriented programming languages such as JavaScript.

In my early years, I created a company developing a CMS available as a service and co-authored a book about entrepreneurship.
