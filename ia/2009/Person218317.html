Lorraine Bell’s Summary

Lorraine Bell is a CSR expert and has helped many companies to implement CSR into profitable business planning and change and leadership management areas.

Career started in Community Relations at London City Airport and developed with Business in the Community and the Brighton & Hove Business Community Partnership prior to starting SIMPLY CSR.

Lorraine has assisted companies to win awards for their CSR policies including The BITC Community Mark.
See Octink - announcement - One of Nine national winners
http://www.bitc.org.uk/community/communitymark/news_events/communitymark.html

Octink - CSR programme - http://www.allsignsgroup.com/articles.cfm/article_id/54/category_id/4/CSR-corporate-responsibility

She was recently invited by the Thailand Government and Chamber of Commerce to present on CSR to 200 businesses and deliver CSR workshops.

Lorraine is enthusiastic & ambitious with a confident and approachable nature, comfortable communicating on all levels from individuals through to boardrooms.

She has impressive business acumen and understanding of how CSR impacts all areas of business and confidently can demonstrate return on investment. Strengths lie in excellent training and facilitation, business development and her extensive business networks which ensure successful project delivery.

Lorraine is a Vice-President of Brighton & Hove Chamber of Commerce and represents the business community on the City’s Sustainability Partnership.
