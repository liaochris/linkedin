Tricia Basanyi’s Summary

I'm a full-time American female voice talent with my own home studio, and i love what i do! I get to work with clients all over the U.S. and overseas doing radio and tv commercials, website audio, phone messaging, real estate virtual tour narrations, podcasts and more. Flexible hours and a GREAT turnaround time (many times 24 hours or less) have helped me build a great client base over the past 10 years. Select credits include Trump Plaza and Trump Taj Mahal in Atlantic City, The Delaware Lottery, Dole, Wal-Mart, Costco, 7-Eleven and Garmin GPS navigation devices. Also the voice on the audio tours for Miami MetroZoo and several other zoos and museums in Florida!

Voiceover samples are available below!
