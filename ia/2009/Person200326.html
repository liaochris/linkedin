Robert E. Diaz’s Summary

My company specializes in providing its clients custom tailored solutions for their business and personal financial needs. VFM can bring to bear decades of business experience in various industries to help clients synthesize their goals into viable action plans. I deeply enjoy working with all levels of stakeholders in developing and implementing bold vision objectives based on providing the highest quality products and service. Reality based positivity can produce powerful results in the expansion of market share, improvement of system efficiencies, and exceeding customer/client expectations. Understanding the customer/client is key.

Whether in the world of personal and business financial consulting, or in consumer trade, many variables affect the outcome. It is in maintaining an understanding of those variables and designing solutions to them that differentiate success from mediocrity.

Since events and conditions are constantly changing, it then becomes imperative to apply historical knowledge with planned vision in order to achieve short to long range goals. This is why I believe in being quick to acquire new information, vet it carefully, then work it into the action plans in place already.
My past experience includes management of international distribution businesses doing trade with Europe, Asia, and the Americas.
