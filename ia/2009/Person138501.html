Laryssa Wirstiuk’s Summary

Once upon a time, an interviewer said, "You have a degree in creative writing? That's nice - a lot of our employees have hobbies."

For those of you who don't know, my Master of Fine Arts degree is more than just an indication that I like to write stories and read books.

My degree signifies my ability to think outside of the proverbial box and deliver ideas that will make you swoon.

I wear a scarlet letter "A" for ambition. My closest friends and family members will tell you that I am one of the most stubborn, determined, and resourceful people they know.

Honestly? I think my attitude and perseverance intimidate a lot of employers. I am seeking a full-time job because I would like financial independence, but I would ultimately like to work for myself.

In the meantime, I am working every day to achieve my own goals as a writer and a new media mover and shaker.
