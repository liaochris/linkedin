Pierre-Philippe Martin’s Summary

Professional with 26 years experience - mostly in Asia-Pacific - in the areas of asset management, infrastructure and acquisition finance. Striving to further contribute to the development of the Asian region while enhancing the lives of my family, teams, clients and "end-users" of my clients' projects / products, through (1) the financing of essential infrastructure in an ethically responsible manner, (2) facilitating the development of businesses via innovative financing structures, and more generally (3) a transparent yet value added approach of the banking business.

--------------------- Recent Awards of our team ------------------------------

Calyon won EuroWeek's Asia Pacific Project Finance House of the Year 2007 award.

Calyon was Mandated Lead Arranger in the following Project Finance Magazine Deals of the Year for 2007:
- Asia Power - Crimson Power;
- Asia Transport Ports - Blue Ocean;
- Asia Petrochemicals - HMC;
- Asia Acquisition - Tata Corus.
