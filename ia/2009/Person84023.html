Gaurang Desai’s Summary

Gaurang is one of those rare leaders who is an innovator yet an effective manager due to his team skills and organizing cum execution capabilities. He has successfully worked with Indian and Multinational organizations. His adaptive nature and high nervous energy has made him a success with startups as well as steady state organizations.

In working with organizations like National Stock Exchange, Reliance group, National Commodity Derivatives Exchange and Dubai Gold & Commodities Exchange in the last 12+ years, Gaurang has met with success at all places due to his ‘Ethics, integrity and hard work have no alternative’ motto.

Gaurang is a person who provides the vital spark. He is imaginative and original and produces lots of ideas. He is independent, unorthodox and forthright. Due to the above he was able to contribute in large measures in the success of the organizations he worked for.

His pioneering work in area of corporate strategy, process automations, quality control and methodical approach to solve business problems are still widely used in the organizations he worked with.
