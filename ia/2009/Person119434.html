Jon Jones’s Summary

I'm an Art Outsourcing Manager working on a very exciting title at an unannouncted studio. I can't wait to announce it. :)

Most recently I worked with NCsoft Austin where I built and managed an almost completely outsourced art department. The project was Dungeon Runners, a casual free-to-play MMORPG that scored 89% in PC Gamer, 8/10 in Games for Windows and consistently ranks highly among the web's top free games.

I came on board to build and lead an entire art team composed solely of external contractors, of which there are roughly 15 - 30 at any given time, peaking out at over 60. My responsibilities encompass everything related to art production, including the following: art direction (jointly with lead designer), hiring and managing external artists, negotiation, putting together contracts, budgeting, scheduling, documentation, and ingame implementation, and even find time to do my own art!

I currently outsource the art production of the following: Concept art, modeling, texturing, rigging, animation, particle effects, world building, engine integration, documentation, organizational documents and video tutorials.

I run a blog detailing my experiences in running an outsourced art team. Half of the site is focused toward art outsourcing managers. For them, I detail the lessons I’ve learned about management, production and dealing with artists. I also detail my favorite best practices, tools I use, and styles of thought that have helped me learn and develop my skills.

  The other half of my site is focused toward artists learning how to better themselves in terms of cultivating a positive attitude, keeping a high level of motivation and becoming more effective professionals. It also gives them an insight into the management side of this business of art to provide insight into high level decisionmaking processes they’re rarely privy to.

The blog is called “Jon Jones, smArtist” and is located here: http://www.thejonjones.com

Jon Jones’s Specialties:

All aspects of 3D game art, art outsourcing, managing remote contractors, art development best practices, business development, technical consulting, managing client accounts, developing marketing campaigns, and brokering art.

I'm experienced with Maya, 3D Studio MAX, Photoshop, Lightwave, Deep Paint, and Deep UV, as well as Microsoft Project.
