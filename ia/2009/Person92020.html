Hanna Torp’s Summary

Highly experienced in e-learning, active in the field since 2000. Apart from designing and teaching online courses, involved in several national and international projects in e-learning. "Dual role" at TAMK University of Applied Sciences: partly working as e-learning specialist, partly as lecturer of English mainly focusing in professional English for electrical, mechanical and computer engineering students.

Hanna Torp’s Specialties:

Online language teaching, pedagogical quality of e-learning, mobile learning, learning strategies in an online learning environment.
