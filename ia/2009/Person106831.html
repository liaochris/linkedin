Julien Chiquet’s Summary

PhD recently defended, entitled " Modeling and estimating degradation processes with application in structural reliability". it mainly deals with the use of stochastic processes to describe the time evolution of the characteristic degradation level of a given structure, also discussing the estimation issue from real data sets.

Currently working as a assistant professor, developing statistical and random-graphs-based models with application to the inference of biological regulatory networks from microarray analysis.

Julien Chiquet’s Specialties:

Probability and Statistics, Network inference, Random graphs, Computer Science, Estimation of processes, Markov / Semi-Markov Chains, Reliability
