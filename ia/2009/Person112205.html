José F. Ugarte’s Summary

José is VP Commercial for Iken solutions.
José worked at CTI Móvil – www.cti.com.ar -(GTE, Verizon and actually hold by América Móvil) expanding international roaming agreements and launching SMS services and content business (1998-2000) in the Marketing department. During 2001 he joined Telefónica Movistar’s – www.movistar.com.ar -business development department in Argentina where he worked on the launching of new services, coordinating the development of the new company´s website, defining premium content strategy, managing commercial relationship with aggregators, national and international licensors. During 2003 he got elected for a Corporate exchange program with Movistar Spain – www.movistar.es -, where he joined the Spanish business development team. In 2006 José joined Itegra - www.itegra.com.ar - a technology business development company, with headquarters in Buenos Aires. Itegra developed different technology businesses in Argentina since 2001, such as TopKash International - www.topkash.com - a mobile payment company, Mobile2biz - www.mobile2biz.com and www.futmovil.com -, bought by Netpeople International -www.net-people.com - in 2006 and Cellent Américas. On November of 2008 Cellent was bought by Iken Solutions.

Jose is also involved in the following organizations:

• Mobile Monday Buenos Aires • Member of Altergaia´s Borad of Directors • Member of Itegra´s Board of Directors • Member of Orugga´s Board of Directors
