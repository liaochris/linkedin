Vaibhav Raizada’s Summary

I introduce myself as a professional Usability Analyst, Creative Visualizer and Designer, transversely involved in the dynamic domain of web and graphics, with an aim to render creativity at its best!

For the past 6 years I have worked passionately towards some of the finest creatives, making them highly effective and efficient to use. These user-friendly solutions have been appreciated by the industry. The majority of my work profile has been in the domains of Product Software, Software Services, Web Design, Online Applications, Portals, E-Learning, Print, Online Media

My expertise lies in outlining creatives of various forms viz. Web based UI & GUI Solutions, Corporate Visual Brandings, Conceptual Advertising, Static Graphic Art, and Content Development.


COMPETENCIES
--------------------------

I insist on doing the "front end" UI as I feel its hard to do good usability if the focus is on codes.

I follow the conventional Design Process: Design Brief, Specification Analysis & Research, Testing, Evaluation, and Redesign if required.

The process is more cognitive and iterative when I work on Human Computer Interaction Design. Requirement Gathering, Contextual Inquiry, Information Architecture, Lo-Fi/Hi-Fi Prototypes, User Interface Design, Visual Design, User Testing sums up my profile.

I welcome opportunities to push the boundaries of creativity when proven effective and appropriate and enjoy familiarizing myself with various design methodologies.
