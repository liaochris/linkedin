Bob Butler’s Summary

Highly successful, energetic senior business and technology executive with broad industry experience in Fortune 500 B-to-B companies as well as small business and start-up enterprises. Experienced in multiple industries including financial services, information services, office products, high tech equipment and software, manufacturing and distribution. A record of achievement in implementing ERP and CRM business solutions, leveraging outsourcing services within IT organizations, building client partnerships with key vendors and transitioning IT organizations through company consolidations, spin-off’s and acquisitions. Highly skilled at project management, analyzing processes, creative problem solving and developing innovative solutions. Extensive experience working for Global organizations with North American responsibility at various times for IT services in the US, Canada and Mexico.
