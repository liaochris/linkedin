Emma Ozsen’s Summary

On completing my postgraduate studies at University College London, I worked as an Environmental Consultant for the last three years, on a variety of major international buildings and masterplans in Europe. My role focused on passive design for energy, thermal comfort and daylight, and Low and Zero Carbon technologies.
I have also worked extensively on preparing documentation for planning applications in the UK, including undertaking energy and sustainability assessments. Being a licensed BREEAM assessor and LEED Accredited Professional, I provide consultancy services towards achieving certification using those environmental benchmarking schemes.

Emma Ozsen’s Specialties:

Dynamic thermal modelling and simulation, Environmental Benchmarking, Site-wide environmental strategies, Water consumption analysis, Renewable technology feasibility studies
