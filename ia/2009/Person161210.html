Michael Steiner’s Summary

Accomplished Leader, Manager, Designer and Project Manager with over 20 years of experience in Computer Operations, Call Center and Data Center design and support. Extensive and progressive knowledge in infrastructure hardware, equipment cooling, equipment power and network cabling. Innovative Data Center support to over 10,000 users nationwide, earning a solid reputation for productivity, complex problem resolution and professionalism. Excellent communication and diagnostic skills, consistently solve problems and rarely escalate issues.

Provided first level support for all nationwide IT infrastructure, including HVAC, UPS and telecommunications cabling supporting over 10,000 employees in 140 diverse locations throughout the US.

Wrote new corporate “Telecommunications Infrastructure and Cabling Standards” that improved the speed of data over the network, improved the reliability of the network infrastructure and improved the delivery time for cabling services for new installations and modifications made to existing facilities. Standards took into account new buildings as well as older facilities that require a flexible approach.

Wrote new corporate “Critical Power and Computer Room Air Conditioning Standards” that improved on the delivery of services increasing reliability of system cooling and emergency power. Standards included all documentation required for maintenance and upkeep. Standards resulted in an increased system uptime and reliability.

Over a ten year period managed a team responsible for building out new office locations including the design and coordination of the installation of all infrastructure and voice data systems at over 250 new and existing locations including the installation of all equipment enclosures, HVAC systems, UPS’s , PDU’s, Diesel Generators, ATS’s, routers, switches, servers, associated support systems and cabling for over 10,000 workstation jacks.
