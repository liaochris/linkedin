Linda C. Flores’s Summary

The Elite Technical Group is a Southern California Talent Acquisition Solutions and Consulting Firm. We provide contract staffing, contingency professional and executive level search solutions for companies looking for contract, contract-to-hire and direct hire talent in the engineering, IT, and scientific communities. We match brilliant candidates with great companies!

In January of 2007' Linda Flores launched Elite Technical Group, Inc., a women and minority owned business. Our mission is to meet the strategic business goals and staffing objectives of our clientèle by providing talent acquisition solutions service offerings. We are committed to developing long term business relationships with our esteemed clients, professional and executive level candidates, delivering a more personalized service executed with the highest level of integrity.
