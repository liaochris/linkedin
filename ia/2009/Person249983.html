Yiying Lu’s Summary

- Creator of Twitter's Fail Whale Illustration - featured in CNN, New York Times Magazine, NPR and Wired Magazine.

- Named one of 100 Emerging Leaders in Innovation of Weekend Australian Magazine's Next 100 series.

- Awarded 1st place in the Design category of the Shorty Awards, held in New York. The awards recognised the best producers of short content across 26 categories across the social networking website.

- Graduated from the University of Technology Sydney with 1st-Class honours in Bachelor of Design Visual Communication

- Design work "Magic Bean" with JWT was shortlisted in Cannes 2008

- Bilingual interpreter & translator for 2008 Shanghai Biennale and Shanghai Tongji University, for international students from more than 30 countries

- Bilingual interpreter & translator for 2006 Shanghai Biennale Hyper Design for Australian Embassy (Australian Education International), for academic staffs from 5 prestigious Australian Universities

- Can do various voices including: Eric Cartman, Borat Sagdiyev, Andy Pipkin, Carol Beer, Daffyd Thomas, Sebastian Love and Emily Howard (!)
