Joel Andrews, MS, ACC’s Summary

Joel is a professional coach and team builder. As a founding partner with Triscendance, LLC, he is committed to helping leaders and teams innovate to deliver exceptional results by building, maintaining and restoring trust in key relationships and throughout their organizations. "I will introduce you and your team to a language of trust and teach / coach you to break old nonproductive habits and replace them with practices that deliver exceptional results.” The values guiding his work are authenticity, maximizing human potential, continuous learning, playful curiosity, courageous conversation, and making meaningful contributions.

Joel has held a broad range of management and leadership roles in, Learning and Organizational Development and Change, Leadership Development and Talent Management, Human Resources, Customer Service and Sales Support, and he has worked with organizations in various sectors including Financial Services and Banking, Health Care and Pharmaceuticals, Education, Consumer Products, Specialty Chemicals, Media, Hospitality, and Manufacturing.

In addition to holding a Master of Science degree in Industrial Relations and a Bachelor of Science degree in Economics from the University of Wisconsin – Madison, Joel is a graduate of coaching programs at Georgetown University and with the Newfield Network, and has been awarded certification from the International Coach Federation (ICF)

Joel lives in Glen Allen, Virginia with his wife of 25 years Gigi, and his 15-year-old daughter Kailee. He is a practitioner of aikido and currently holds the rank of Nikyu (Brown belt).

Leadership and Career Coaching; Team Building and High Performance; Strengthening Key Relationships by Building, Strengthening and Restoring Trust; Enabling Leaders and Teams to Innovate and produce sustainable results
