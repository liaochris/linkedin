Antonio Fiorello’s Summary

I have over 20 years of management experience in the new home and construction industry. Recently I held the position of Vice President of Sales for DR Horton's Corona Division.

Currently I am involved with three start up companies:

Structure Safety Group-Owner. We provide environmentally friendly products and services that prevent fire, mold and viruses in every application from residential housing to cruise ships.

TerraStone Investments-Owner. A real estate company that handles all levels of real estate sales, transactions and investments from single residential sales to large multi-unit residential and commercial properties.

TerraStone LLC-Partner. We specialize in green technologies and environmentally friendly solutions for all industries. Currently we are working on a Pyrolysis recycling system that converts organic materials into many useful products worldwide.
