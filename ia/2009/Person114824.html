Joanna P. Basile’s Summary

Basile is excited to be working as part of the VIP team at Angel Management Group. You can find her this season at Wet Republic serving as your VIP Host.
Basile has been consulting the Molasky Group on their latest hangar project, Las Vegas Aviation. She is a current member of the Nevada Chapter of the National Concierge Association, which has proved invaluable in attaining an edge in her market. Basile was previously appointed Director of Outside Charter Sales for a Los Angeles charter company, focusing on the entertainment industry. She was once based in Chicago/New York with Jet Aviation, managing private and corporate aircraft. She also worked in client services and sales/marketing, where she served the who’s who of Vegas. She enjoys working closely with high-end clientele, and has forged great relationships with her outstanding customer service skills.

In her past life, Basile managed a world-class recording facility, and was an associate in new business/consulting for the biggest independent record promoter in the country. She spent a few years as project manager with the GRAMMY Awards/The Recording Academy. She still stays plugged into the music business, consulting for a Chicago-based, record producer/engineer.

Basile fights amateur Muay Thai on Master Toddy’s Lion Heart team in Vegas. She also trains with Norm Turner, conditioning coach to professional fighter Forrest Griffin and the like. When she’s not busy ramping up for a killer pool season, selling hangar space or training for upcoming fights, Basile can be found watching her favorite bands, checking out art/photography or cooking at home with friends. As a published poet, Basile also loves to write. She recently worked on a successful Presidential campaign. Always philanthropic at heart, she runs competitive 5K charity races, and donates time to Las Vegas' homeless. Basile shares a home in the Black Mts with her 90lb shepherd-mix rescue Duke.
