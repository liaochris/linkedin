Jackie Holmes’s Summary

Very experienced Prince 2 and ITIL qualified Project Manager with line management experience, which includes sourcing (as customer and service provider), service management, transition and transformation, infrastructure, web development and software implementation projects. Technical background with hands on IT experience within retail, financial, dealing room environments, the media, the oil industry, pharmaceutical, legal and insurance sectors. Recent projects include voice based stock control, service implementation of a nationwide high bandwidth network, transition of sensitive and political projects, desktop and server deployment strategies for up to 30,000 devices, outsourcing of call centre and banking operations processes to India. Experience includes all aspects of project planning and management including setup of project office processes and procedures, business requirement analysis, budgeting, desktop and application packaging strategies, decommissioning, development of software solutions, technical documentation, and quality reviews. Good understanding of modern technologies, virtual environments, data storage and manipulation, packaging and networking skills, PC applications and mobile computing.

Jackie Holmes’s Specialties:

Good communication skills at all levels, strong grasp of business principles, excellent management skills and good team leadership ability. Thrive in busy, demanding environments. Very business, delivery and customer focused; specialist at managing difficult relationships.
