Judith Florentinus’s Summary

Having worked as an Executive Assistant on different levels, and for several international companies for over 8 years, I decided to utilise my experience as an interim assistant. I am a driven person who sees it as my goal to ensure that my manager can be successful in his/her job. I will not limit myself to a strict job-description, rather I will “do what it takes” to get the desired results. I have gained experience within national and international companies, privately owned as well as publicly listed, and within different departments (Board of Directors, Legal Department, Finance, Personnel). I have a good understanding of the various internal processes, I enjoy being challenged by a high workload, complicated calendar management, deadlines and exciting projects. In April 2007 I have successfully completed a course as Legal Secretary and in June 2008 I have gained my certification as Professional Organizer. I choose to invest in my further education yearly as I believe that it helps me to become a better professional.

Judith Florentinus’s Specialties:

Professional Organizing both in office and home situations; Facilitating the transition between departing and starting secretaries; organizing workspaces; general office support and advice.
