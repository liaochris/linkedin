Thea Weijers’s Summary

Creative policy maker and researcher. I have alternated between research and policy making. The main theme in my career has been innovation, and particularly the way people and organisations incorporate technologies in their day to day routines. Within this theme I have worked on a broad range of subjects from telework to smart living and technology for the elderly.
I am currently doing two things. I am putting my theoretical knowledge to practical use as project manager Carefree Living and I am improving my scientific knowledge by writing a thesis on the diffusion of public infrastructures to private homes, with particular interest in the role of public interests and standardization.

Thea Weijers’s Specialties:

Creation of new insights and identification of relevant new trends through the combination of existing trends and theories, creation of awareness and support for new policies, different methods for qualitive research.
