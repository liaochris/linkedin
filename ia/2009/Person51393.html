Curtis Palmer’s Summary

Product Management, Development, and Marketing Executive for Hyper-Growth Efforts and/or Strategic Turnarounds

'Coach/Player' with a proven track record specializing in ‘new venture’ startups and regenerating excitement in stagnant initiatives, moving from idea to product deployment, transforming management’s vision into technologically sound business solutions consistent with corporate strategy and market conditions.

I have extensive experience directing cross-functional teams in multi-mission matrix organizations, such as TechBirmingham, WebMD, and Atlanta’s Olympics, where results were achieved via spheres of influence rather than by direct control, achieving results with extremely low churn.

What I do best is developing and executing strategies, moving from idea to product deployment, and transforming management’s vision into technologically sound business solutions consistent with corporate strategy and market conditions!
