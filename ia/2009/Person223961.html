Steve Hartel, MBA’s Summary

Building on a successful background in corporate finance, investment management, and consulting, I am now seeking a senior leadership position in the Boston or NH area where people skills and teaching ability can be combined to grow long-term mutually beneficial relationships between an organization and its clients. I lead by taking the initiative to solve problems. My leadership style focuses on team building, mentoring, and empowerment. In addition to a team builder I am also a team player. I use my excellent interpersonal skills, especially my ability to discern people's needs, to cultivate strong relationships between an organization and its customers, client, or donors. I analyze both issues and numbers in a way that gets to the heart of the matter. I like to extract deeper meaning from the results and then use that to solve problems.

In the vernacular of sales, I am a "farmer" rather than a "hunter". Give me an established group of clients and ask me to make them happy campers who value their relationship with my firm and want to grow their business with my firm, and that's a model for big success.

I am a successful client relationship manager with recent software implementation project management experience. I have a proven ability in positions requiring a combination of detail-oriented analytical skills and broad interpersonal relationship building. I have significant expertise in communicating complex material to non-technical audiences. I have a demonstrated track record of problem solving.

I have my Series 7 and 63 licenses and my MBA. I would be interested in getting my CFP. My most recent experience was with Fidelity Investments in their Family Office Services group, helping them develop and implement tools for use by family offices in the ultra-high net worth marketplace.
