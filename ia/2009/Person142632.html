Liz Chuday’s Summary

In addition to running an image marketing/pr consulting business and working with Highlands Green Energy, I've had a parallel career as a free-lance journalist in both print and broadcast arenas.

Have also been cast as an extra in feature films including Clint Eastwood's "Absolute Power" and Nicole Kidman's "The Visiting."

Liz Chuday’s Specialties:

    Chuday Communications and Highlands Green Energy

    (Environmental Services industry)

    November 1984 — Present (24 years 9 months)

    Started Chuday Communications in 1984. Clients have ranged from Comcast Cablevision, CBS Television, the University of Maryland Cancer Center and Continental Realty Corp. to Enterprise Homes, the breast cancer lifestyle support non-profit The Red Devils and Harford Habitat for Humanity.

    In addition to Chuday Communications, joined Highlands Green Energy and its sister company, Highlands Production Co., in November of '08 to promote green/alterative energy, working on new business development deals with customers seeking to reduce their carbon footprint, from universities and hospitals to real estate developers. Also represent inventors, raising venture capital both in the U.S. and abroad.
