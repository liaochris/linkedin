Stefan Bertschi’s Education

  • Universität Zürich

    PhD , Sociology , 2006 — 2008

    Grade: insigni cum laude. My thesis concerns the logic of social interaction. In order to reveal this logic an analytical ‘in-between’ of the individual and society is developed and outlined. This intermediate construction provides a workable model for qualitative (and consequential quantitative) evaluation both in the social sciences and in strategy as well as corporate development. The explicatory efficacy of underlying ‘meaning’ can be transferred to any domain involving communication, interaction and the transfer of knowledge (e.g. economics, policy, culture, society).

  • Universität Basel

    MA , Sociology, Communication and Media Studies, German Literature , 1997 — 2003

    Grade: magna cum laude. This thesis was concerned with the relation of future studies and sociology and how this relation can be applied to and made fruitful for broader social interactions and finally for application in corporate and private settings, i.e. foresight, insight and planning.
