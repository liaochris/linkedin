    PRINCE®, which stands for Projects in Controlled Environments, is a project management method covering the organisation, management and control of projects. PRINCE® was first developed by the Central Computer and Telecommunications Agency (CCTA), now part of the Office of Government Commerce (OGC), in 1989 as a UK Government standard for IT project management.

  • Defence Academy, Shrivenham

    JOCC , Leadership, Management, Communication, Finance , 2005 — 2005

    The JOCC is a 3-week residential course conducted at the Defence Academy Shrivenham.

    The aim of the JOCC is: "to enhance the command and staff competencies required by junior officers in station and staff appointments."

    Officers successfully completing the JOCC, and who registered with the Chartered Management Institute (CMI) are eligible for the award of a Certificate in Management.

    The syllabus includes Defence studies, Air Power studies, Command, Leadership and Management skills and techniques, Communication skills, Personnel management and Financial management.

    Tuition is mainly through lectures, syndicate tutorials, discussion groups and seminars with the emphasis on practical application.

    Lecturers from King's College London provide an academic input to the Defence and Air Power modules of the course.

  • Defence Academy, Shrivenham

    ISSC , Managerial Communication Skills , 2004 — 2005

    The ISSC is a 51-week distance learning course conducted by the Joint Services Command and Staff College (JSCSC), located at Shrivenham.

    The aim of the ISSC course is to produce: "an officer who can analyse, persuade and direct effectively by means of accurate and concise written communication."

    The ISSC has 3 phases, each involving guided study, self-assessed exercises and a tutor-assessed exercise. The Course culminates in an End-of-Course Check, conducted at examination centres world-wide, wherever students are serving. The syllabus includes instruction on Use of English, Defence Writing, (using specific formats common throughout the MOD), Agendas and Minutes, Briefs and Point Briefs, Problem Solving and the handling of Classified Documents.

    Completion of ISSC leads to Diploma in Management, Chartered Management Institute.
