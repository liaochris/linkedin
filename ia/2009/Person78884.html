Fernando Espinosa (LION 2.4M)’s Summary

• Senior Managing Partner & Co-Owner of Qualifind, Inc. based in our corporate-owned offices in Chula Vista, CA (suburb of San Diego).

• First credentialed member of The Pinnacle Society from Mexico. Membership in The Pinnacle Society is limited to 75 of the highest performing recruiters in North America. Membership is attained only by meeting an exceptionally high standard of recruiting production and after a thorough review and approval process by its Board of Directors.

• Member of IRC Recruitment, a leading global alliance of executive search firms. IRC selected our company Qualifind as its exclusive member firm in Mexico and San Diego, CA. With Qualifind, IRC Recruitment’s global reach extends to 55 offices across the world, providing greater flexibility for its clients to engage IRC Recruitment’s executive search services in almost every major business location in the world.

• More than fourteen years of search experience in the manufacturing market in the U.S. and Mexico. Prior to joining Qualifind in 2000, led a Mexico-based search practice focused on Mexico's maquiladora industry. Since 1994 involved in assisting manufacturing clients from Asia, Europe and the United States solve a variety of recruiting, outplacement, cultural sensitivity and other human resource related issues in their Mexico operations.
