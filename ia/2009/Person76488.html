Charles de Moffarts’s Summary

Europe Translations does specialize in high volume and complex translations.

We implement a rigorous procedure to ensure that even very complex information is conveyed clearly and effectively:

1. We only select certified translators who are native speakers of the target language and who have specific industry expertise.

2. We use two additional industry-specific translators to edit and to proofread each file. Web designers, software engineers and desktop publishers are also available if required for the project.

3. For final verification, we assign subject matter experts to ensure the correct use of industry terms. This procedure enables us to guarantee the quality of our translations.

If you send a description of your project to contact@europetranslations.eu specifying your delivery time requirements, we will send you a free quote via email.

Our rate per source word will include our three step "quality assurance process" (involving 3 separate native translators) of translation + edition + proofreading. We also offer a 100% satisfaction guaranteed.
