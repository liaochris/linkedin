Jermaine Barnes’s Summary

Over five years of accounting experience, preparing and reporting client company management accounts to year end financial statements and analysis. I have proven abilities in defining company direction, achieving targets and developing new and repeat business. Use intellectual experience and focus to manage clients’ working capital, payroll, accounts payable, special projects and cash management activities to increase profit margin and cash flow objectives. Currently taking CPA exams.

JB Financial Services

In 2004 I started doing taxes and providing accounting services for business owners, working with company owners to cut expenses, develop budgets and forecast to meet long term projections, manage cash flow and implement detailed accounting systems and procedures. I manage working capital so current assets exceed current liabilities a minimum of 3:1. Using a good understanding of the company’s business and direction, I manage client’s cash flow to prevent shortage and liquidity issues. Discover available financing options, prepare clients financials for taxes, capital financing and continued solvency. I look at the company’s financial structure for realistic management forecasts to gain a better understanding of their financial needs, if they have an adequate record keeping system, policies and procedures in place that clearly describes their financial position and can support future budgets that show a good return on capital and supply their desired profits. As a consultant I work with clients to improve their gross revenues, cover expenses, drive down cost, taxes and meet their target projections. Also, prepare income tax returns.
