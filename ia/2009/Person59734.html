David Green’s Summary

Experienced Internet Director with track record of successfully leading complex enterprise-level projects.

Responsible for leading web publishing and e-marketing of 153 websites in 108 countries worldwide for Deloitte, a US $27 billion professional services firm.

Managing all aspects of global web operations & Internet marketing, including online brand protection and monitoring.

Oversees global team with responsibilities for the definition, management and execution of online market strategy, including email campaign management, search engine marketing, online media and promotions. Responsible for third party & supplier account relationships globally.

Instrumental in developing global common processes for web publishing, e-marketing, quarterly management reporting and support issues.

Created global e-marketing community, where none existed before: functional skills training, knowledge and competency development, regular internal communications and regional web workshops.

Deployment of an Enterprise Content Management system worldwide, supporting 29 languages - creating consistent user interface and branding. Deloitte.com has received four accolades in the past year from organisations such as Forrester.

David Green’s Specialties:

Web operations: Web content management, enterprise search, usability testing, RSS, editorial
e-Marketing / internet marketing: email marketing, search engine marketing, web analytics, brand identity
Talent management: recruitment & candidate interviews, performance reviews and appraisals, training & development plans, acting as a mentor providing counselling.
