Adrian Lai’s Summary

Born in Singapore, raised in HK and educated in the States, I currently call NYC home. During the week, you can find me at R/GA producing digital communications for Nike. Outside of that, I'm usually riding my bicycle (her name's Angie) exploring some rough and dangerous part of town. I'm into old school hip-hop, street culture, business, design and technology.

My past experience includes working on brands like Converse, Ugly Dolls, IFFBoston and Panasonic, at places like OgilvyOne (Beijing), Arnold Worldwide, Modernista!, Renegade Marketing and Honest.
