Pat Boyle’s Summary

I have just recently moved to Denver from Indiana. I am seeking work, freelance to full time, as a videographer, camera operator, editor, and/or production assistant who is willing to travel. I received a Bachelor of Arts degree from Indiana University last December with a double major in Film & Television Production. During school I was the technical producer for “Button Mashers” that received the 2009 Webby Honoree Award. I am a member of the Colorado Film & Video Association, the Colorado Professional Videographers Association, Denver Final Cut Pro Users Group, and the Denver Film Society. Currently, I am working on a documentary about the Denver Kickball Coalition, and was the assistant transport coordinator for the 2009 Starz Denver Film Fest.

As of recent, I edited a skateboarding segment for “The Captain and Casey Show” that will air on Fuel TV this month. I shot studio camera and more for WTIU, a local PBS station that broadcasts to 20 counties in south central Indiana. I have also shot hand-held camera for the NBA Pacers in-house production, and camera and instant replay for Indiana University Department of Intercollegiate Athletics basketball and football. I have worked for two local companies called VS Video Productions and ISPN.

I am an excellent people person with strong communication and team building skills. Being well organized and comfortable in the professional environment, I use my critical thinking background to solve problems quickly as they arise. I have great instinct, an excellent eye, and most importantly I take pride in my work.
