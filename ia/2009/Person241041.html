Valeria Gentile’s Summary

Writer and Proofreader for Fabio Bertazzioni's books (travel literature); Web editor and Writer for his website "Viaggi Estremi".
Blogger and Blog Manager for the italian branch of Enormo, the international network about real estate.
Web content consultant at Bitness srl, for Virgilio.
Tutor blogger for BlogLab (blogging at University).
Photographer and Chief of Staff at the International Journalism Festival (Perugia, 1-5 april 2009).

Owner of "Creativity and Communication" (blog about creative writing, since 2006) and "Altri Occhim i grandi reportage" (blog about journalism and photojournalism since 2007).

I've worked as Web editor, writer, journalist and tutor for foreign stagists at Citylife Spa (Florence, 2006). I also worked as Photo lab technician: photoretouching, developing, printing and delivery (Nuoro, 2008). I've been also Reporter and Photoreporter for LaSpecula.com and i've written for various magazines, blogs and sites.
