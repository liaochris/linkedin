Douglas Wirnowski’s Summary

I am skilled in identifying underlying unmet needs of the customer and in leading the specification and development of whole solutions that delight the user. These solutions are based on market and customer research, strategically driven, developed through innovations in software and hardware, highly focused on simplicity and an overwhelmingly positive user experience, and ultimately aimed at improved corporate profitability. My work experience spans several markets including consumer electronics, Internet media/ecommerce, biotechnology, and retail/POS.
