David Carrithers’s Summary

The competitive state of business today is like nothing in history. Technology, timing, international players, quality and knowledgeable customers are all key influencers. At the same time the lines between competitors, suppliers and partners have grayed, making it difficult to know where and how to attack the market.

The only constant is the need to gather information quickly, assess the situation with insight and make calculated calls to action. Then as you move forward watch the results and change direction if necessary. Tapping into capable, trusted, outside individuals is valuable to fast results and action.

To be a business leader and have a sustainable competitive advantage in today's world you need honest insight, feedback and ideas. You need the ability to cut through the corporate lines and strata to come to conclusions fast.

David Carrithers works with a company's board, management, outside resources or key partners to develop a plan of action and gain buy-in, direction and support by all those involved.
