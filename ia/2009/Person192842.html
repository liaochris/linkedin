Rajiv Venkatesh (MVP, CEH)’s Summary

Rajiv is working in Enterprise Security team which belongs to the Systems Integration Unit at Infosys Technologies Ltd. He is part of solutions group which is focused on providing Enterprise level consulting to clients.

Rajiv is engaged in IT security review and consulting since 2006. He is involved performing analysis and assessments of corporate webapplication and is specialized both in Blackbox and Whitebox testing.

Rajiv has hands-on experience with various commercial security tools like Cenzic Hailstorm, Ounce Labs tool, IBM Appscan, Fortify SCA, WebInspect etc. His expertise in the field of application security aids him in successfully minimizing the false positives/negatives which are usually reported by automated tools. He has a good understanding of best coding practices, standards & processes in the areas of Information Security.
