Catherine Chung’s Summary

Recent graduate of the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications at Syracuse University with a B.S. in public relations. Currently managing PR/events on the West Coast for Allkpop, the most trafficked Korean pop culture Web site reaching over 700,000 unique visitors a month. Most recently served as the Communications/PR manager for Kollaboration New York, a major talent show that empowers the Pan Asian American community through entertainment and brings Asian American talent to the forefront. An alumna of Alpha Chi Omega, Lambda Chapter who held various positions: formal chair, vice president of membership development, PACE (promoting alpha chi everyday) chair, historian, and Junior Panhellenic Council delegate. Former sales associate at Oakley, a high-performance and luxury apparel and accessories company specializing in eyewear. Former PR and media internships at CBS Television Distribution, European Vibe, Nancy Seltzer & Associates and Seychelles & BC Footwear.
