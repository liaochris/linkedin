Lee Pollak’s Summary

DoublePositive Marketing Group is located in Baltimore MD specializing in live transfer marketing. Specifically we have developed a real-time platform for acquiring Internet sales leads in data form, qualifying and scoring those leads using a proprietary algorithm, delivering the data to one of our call centers in order to be contacted, qualified, and ultimately transferred LIVE to our network of sales professionals.

DoublePositive has the ability to deliver live transfers in an any industry. We have focused primarily on delivering live transfers mortgage, debt settlement, insurance and education.

My role at DoublePositive, as a marketing shrink, is to aquire, and nuture, new and existing accounts.
