Dorian Mersini’s Summary

Born in Durres (Albania) in 1980. Graduated in Justice in 2005 from the University of Genoa (Italy) with a degree in the Maritime Law, personal publication: “I Contratti di Utilizzazione della Nave (Charter - Party)”, "Leasing Contract in the Maritime Transport". "Albanian Sea Ports under ISPS Code Regime", "Letter of Credit", "Incoterms in the International Trade".

In 2005 assistant in the Department of International & Maritime Law at the University of Genoa. In 2006 expert for the International Law at the European Integration Directory, Ministry of Interior in Albania. In 2007, Chairman of Administration Council in the Port of Shengjin; Head of Maritime Security Department (ISPS Code), in the Ministry of Public Works, Transport and Telecommunication.

November 2007, Specialization Course for the Port Management, organized by Ministry of Commerce (China).
May 2008, Specialization Course for the Port Security Policy, organized by State Department (United States).

Admitted to Albanian Bar in 2007. Representative Partner to Law Firm “AS&T Legal Albania”, Meditlegal Group.

Spoken languages: Albanian; Italian; English.

Dorian Mersini’s Specialties:

Activities Sectors: transportation, shipping and air law, insurance liability, commercial law, international commerce and the competition law.
