Gregory Layne’s Summary

I have more than eight years of experience as an editor, including nearly six years as Managing Editor of Print Periodicals for a nonprofit, member-based, public-benefit organization. The material I was responsible for ranged from highly technical engineering and architectural copy to "human interest" stories to "advertorial" copy: a critical aspect of my success in this capacity being the carefully considered balance of these sometimes disparate elements to the overall benefit of our readers.

I am currently employed as a temp-to-hire Marketing Editor at STV Incorporated's Rancho Cucamonga, CA office. STV is a multidisciplinary planning, architectural, engineering environmental, and construction management firm with more than 1,500 personnel in 30 offices.

Gregory Layne’s Specialties:

Editing, writing, technical editing, copyediting, copywriting, proofreading, fact-checking, research, production management, document management, client relations, member services, and support for public relations, government relations, marketing, professional education, and product development.
