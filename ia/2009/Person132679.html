Kip Piper’s Summary

Kip Piper – a top authority on Medicare, Medicaid, and the pharmaceutical industry – is a consultant, author, and speaker. A skilled business and policy strategist, Kip has advised Fortune 100 companies, top federal officials, governors, members of Congress, foundations, and foreign leaders.

Kip Piper is:

• President of the Health Results Group LLC, a boutique strategic consultancy.

• Senior counselor at Fleishman-Hillard Inc., the world’s leading public relations and communications firm.

• Senior consultant at Sellers Dorsey, the top Medicaid and health reform consultancy.

• Editor of the Piper Report, a popular health care blog.

• Author/editor of forthcoming books on Medicare and Medicaid.

Kip Piper's 25 years’ experience includes senior advisor to the administrator of the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS), Wisconsin state health commissioner, CEO of the multi-billion dollar Wisconsin Medicaid program, advisor to Fortune 500 employers, general manager at WellPoint, vice president of AcademyHealth, a senior Medicare budget officer at the White House Office of Management and Budget, director of major foundation-sponsored projects, presidential management fellow, and local elected official.

A frequent speaker, Kip Piper has testified before Congress and negotiated federal legislation. He’s been widely quoted in the press, including BusinessWeek, USA Today, Modern Healthcare, The Hill, HealthLeaders, Investor’s Business Daily, and Associated Press.

Kip Piper is editor of the Piper Report, a popular blog on Medicare, Medicaid, and pharma issues. He is author of forthcoming books on Medicare and Medicaid and business and policy editor for the journal American Health and Drug Benefits.

Board certified in health care management by the American College of Healthcare Executives, Kip holds a master's degree in public administration from the University of Wisconsin, Madison.
