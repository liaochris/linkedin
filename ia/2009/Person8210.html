Alexis Rhodes’s Summary

I am a marketing professional with a love of all things interactive and a passion for PR. I am currently an AAE for a fast-growing direct marketing agency.

I assisted in developing, organizing, and implementing digital media campaigns for Broadway, Las Vegas, Los Angeles, and touring shows, interacting with vendors and clients, and assisting with promotions and social networking initiatives.
