Harold Zimmer’s Summary

I am a professional with 40+ years of experience in global companies of fast moving consumer goods. Worked with multi functional/company teams to deliver results that added value to the company and address the needs of the appropriate market segments depending on the positioning of the brand. Recognized for being an excellent team worker, innovative, flexible and supportive with a persistent work style. Proven negotiation skills and ability to create rapport with internal and external clients.

March-2000 to February-2009: Account Manager for Precise Technology/Rexam, responsibilities for both current business and new business opportunities. My role included working with the customer's in a technical capacity from a project management perspective. In addition, I was responsible for sales, strategic account management, business development, product pricing and new business proposals.

1990-2000: Procter & Gamble, Engineer - Beauty Care Packaging Development R&D with responsibilities to develop & qualify new packaging concepts which would provide a delivery system for new formulations.

1985-1990: Procter & Gamble, Chemist - Beauty Care Formulation R&D, with responsibilities to develop new formulations for upstream antiperspirant brands.

1970-1985: Procter & Gamble - Process Engineer - Ivorydale Manufacturing, in the Crest Dentifrice and Secret/Sure Deodorant departments.
