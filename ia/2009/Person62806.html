David Mandell’s Summary

As a Founder and Vice President of Marketing for OneRiot, I was responsible for the company’s competitive positioning, brand development, and all marketing and communications, including all direct and creative campaigns designed to develop, engage and maintain an ongoing relationship with OneRiot’s user community as well as all external audiences. Critical to this function is a strategy of open and frequent user and external communications and tools that enable rapid and direct feedback on all product features and functionality as well as assistance with future direction and development.

Prior to founding OneRiot, David served as the Managing Director, Global Communications for Deloitte Consulting. As the first marketing hire for Deloitte Consulting, David helped launch and manage the global brand development and marketing communications organization and strategy following Deloitte Consulting’s separation from Deloitte & Touche. Using aggressive and unexpected communications, the comparative strategy succeeded in distinguishing the brand from its competitors and facilitated the company’s rise to one of the top-three brands in the industry.

David also served as the Executive Vice President, General Manager for Cohn & Wolfe, N.Y., a mid-sized public relations agency, where he was responsible for several major practice groups, including Technology, Professional Services and Public Affairs. While at Cohn & Wolf, David managed several large marketing campaigns, including the introduction of BMW N.A.’s Z3 Roadster in conjunction with the James Bond 007 Movie, GoldenEye.

David started his career working on communications for the CART/Indy Car racing series.
