Dr. Arun Kumar Barthakur, Advocate, Bombay High Court,’s Summary

Lawyer, Bombay High Court--specialized in all types civil and business law, drafting and pleading, banking & financial law, intellectual property laws, property laws, land acquisition, evacuee properties, town & country planning, cooperative law, contract law, constitutional law, service law and arbitration. Handling matters of financial institutions, banks and businesses. Conducted and argued important matters in the civil courts, Debts Recovery Tribunal (DRT), Debts Recovery Appellate Tribunal (DRAT), High Court and the Supreme Court of India. Lawsuits, arguments, interpretation of statutes, negotiating and drafting contracts, legal research & consulting services are my forte.Reputed for zealous, thoughtful and industrious representation of clients, emphasizing dispute resolution more than winning cases.
Read my articles on contemporary legal issues on the website: www.supremecourtcases.com & PL web Journal and in the Law Journals: Supreme Court Cases, All India Reporter, Banking Commercial Law Reporter.

Dr. Arun Kumar Barthakur, Advocate, Bombay High Court,’s Specialties:

Legal research & consulting services, intellectual property laws, lawsuits, arguments, interpretation of statutes, negotiating and drafting contracts, real estate law, banking & financial law, property laws, land acquisition, evacuee properties, town & country planning, constitutional law, arbitration, and above all dispute resolution.
