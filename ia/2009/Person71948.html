    Elke M. Leeds, Ph.D., is an Assistant Professor of Management of Information Systems in the Michael J. Coles College of Business at Kennesaw State University. Her areas of research interest include media richness, the relationships between task and technology, communication apprehension, oral presentation and communication skill, and student engagement with online learning.

    Dr. Leeds is actively involved with online programs and instructional technology. She coordinates Business Information Systems Management and Workplace Communication in the Coles College for the undergraduate BBA. Dr. Leeds also teaches with the Leadership and Executive Development team for the AT&T and Executive MBA program. She leads the business communication and networking curriculum for the program.
