Susan Rosner’s Summary

Susan Rosner (MBA, CBI) is the Managing Partner at Calder Associates, specializing in the sale of privately held businesses. In addition,Susan is a business and organizational coach working with business owners, corporate executives, CEO's and non-profit boards.

Susan has been recognized in the business community for her expertise as a business intermediary and coach. Susan is a member of IBBA (International Business Brokers Association) and is the Past President of The Mid-Atlantic Business Broker’s Association, a regional affiliate of the IBBA. Susan was invited by the United States Chamber of Commerce to serve as an Executive Coach to the CEO’s of the top 100 Business Associations in the United States.

Susan is also an active member of the Lower Bucks Chamber of Commerce where she is on the Board of Directors and on the Board of Advisors for Leadership in Bucks County

Susan has an MBA in Marketing from The George Washington University Susan is a Certified Business Intermediay (CBI) and a Certified Results Coach. Susan has conducted over 200 seminars on leadership, entrepreneurship and business strategy.

Susan Rosner’s Specialties:

Mergers and Acquisitions for privatey held small to mid sized companies, Exit Planning, Business and Executive Coaching, Strategic Planning
