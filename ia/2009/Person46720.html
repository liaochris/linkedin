Claire Mason’s Summary

I am a versatile creative manager with 13 year’s experience within a design, product development, manufacturing and sourcing environment. Four years within the luxury outdoor sector, covering all areas of apparel design and development. Proceeded by eight years within Reebok, and a further two years with adidas, based in China. Significant global supply chain experience with primary focus on the Far East and Europe.
Excellent knowledge of design, product development and manufacturing processes, combined with experience of product costing, commercial negotiation, critical path management and quality assurance procedures and implementation.
Key management skills and excellent interpersonal skills with the ability to motivate and manage a team to ensure optimum productivity in conjunction with exceptional standards. Experienced team player with high self-motivation and proven track record in organisational skills and the ability to contribute to and fulfil strategic objectives.
