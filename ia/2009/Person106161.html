Jay Hershman’s Summary

Jay has over twelve years of product development and business strategy experience with several companies in the financial services and technology industries. He has helped spearhead leading companies developing solution in the Electronic Payments, Telematics, Digital Media and Consumer Electronics industries.

Jay currently serves as the Senior Vice President, Global Prepaid Solutions for Citi Prepaid Services, formerly Ecount, which is a leading provider of electronic payment solutions. Ecount deliver products and services that enable organizations to easily and electronically move cash to employees and consumers in order to motivate, compensate and reward their services.

Prior to joining Citi Prepaid Services, Jay managed the Consumer Business Unit and OEM partnership at Sapias, Inc., a California based company that delivers advanced web-enabled business solutions for managing enterprise fleets.

Previously, Jay served as Business Development Manager at Mediabolic, a digital media networking technology supplier to customers such as Hewlett Packard, Pioneer and Denon-Marantz.
