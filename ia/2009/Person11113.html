Anthony Martinez Beven’s Experience

    Raytheon Professional Services

    (E-Learning industry)

    March 2007 — March 2008 (1 year 1 month)

    Re-designed, re-focused and re-established three technical newsletters (one monthly publication and two quarterly) and two Web sites for the GM Service Technical College (GM STC). Researched and wrote original stories and edited contributor content for GM STC newsletters and sites. Contributed to Raytheon’s internal communications efforts by updating policy manuals, creating and maintaining an internal images repository, planning employee surveys, and writing for the company newsletter and intranet. Produced customer and competitive research for Raytheon’s sales strategy efforts. Aided in the research and development of customer proposals and case studies. Wrote news releases and developed marketing collateral around company recognitions. Participated in the planning and execution of customer visits.

    September 2006 — February 2007 (6 months)

    Wrote stories for the monthly newsletter on internal and external business strategy, community relations activities and new product offerings; also edited stories from employees and worked to finalize layout. Wrote daily news stories for the company intranet and edited press releases. Aided with the planning, development and execution of employee surveys, employee engagement campaigns and messages from company leadership. Worked on special projects, such as an assessment of the employee “Bright Idea” campaign focused on improving business operations and cost-cutting measures through employee suggestions. Maintained a photo database of company events, executives, senior managers and brand teams. Researched, distributed and archived media clips for executive team on a daily basis.
