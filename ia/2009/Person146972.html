Maina M. Bagwanedee’s Summary

Bagwanedee is currently seeking a mid-level marketing position. She hails almost eight years of hands-on experience in number of marketing disciplines including product launches, trade shows / event planning, public relations, branding, internal corporate communications, advertising and promotions through her work in a wide variety of industries, including consumer electronics, technology, food and beverage, consumer packaged goods, health care and tourism.

Bagwanedee recently earned her MBA degree through an international MBA program that took her to three of Europe’s top business schools: Warwick Business School (England), Copenhagen Business School (Denmark) and The University of Mannheim (Germany). She spent the last year challenging not only her cognitive skills, but also honing her teamwork and management skills.

Prior to obtaining her MBA, Bagwanedee held a Global Marketing Specialist position at Shure Incorporated, a premium sound company. Here she gained invaluable product marketing experience through developing the consumer brand and launching new products internationally.

Prior to joining Shure Incorporated, Bagwanedee spent two years gaining agency experience in public relations for a variety of clients, including the following renowned clients: United Airlines, EFFEN vodka, Jose Cuervo, ACCO office products, Potbelly Sandwiches, Morton's Steakhouse and Oberweis Dairy, etc.

Bagwanedee’s career aspirations began years prior to her professional experience, as she held marketing internships throughout the duration of her undergraduate years. Most notably, Bagwanedee won a coveted two-year marketing internship with The Coca-Cola Company, considered by many to be a top marketer in the world.

As a Chicago native, Bagwanedee graduated from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign with a Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration and a concentration in Marketing.
