Vitaly Leokumovich’s Summary

Vitaliy Leokumovich is a well known technologist whose achievements are recognized worldwide and are backed by the most prestigious awards in the industry. He is the founder of Create Interact Group, an interactive boutique based in New York.

Vitaly received his Bachelor in Business Administration form Baruch College (New York City) in 1997. While in college he became closely involved with the early phenomena of the Internet. Upon graduation Vitaly joined world-renowned interactive agency Egomedia and was one of the top developers responsible for its success.

His reputation of an outside-the-box thinker and his passion toward new technological advances made him a pioneer who was ahead of the time in a way he perceived the future of Internet.
Throughout more than a decade of involvement with World Wide Web Vitaly gained a deep expertise in interactive technology and became a contributor and active member of some of the leading resources for interactive development.

In 2000 he organized a New Media United Foundation in London that became increasingly popular among technology enthusiasts in Europe, North America and South Africa.

In 2001 he received one of the most prestigious awards in the industry, a Golden Lion in Cannes for the Egomedia interactive web-site. In addition to receiving such top honor, Vitaly was rewarded for his other work by Webby Honoree, Clio, FWA, Flash Forward Nominee and others.

Vitaly founded and leaded development of a popular online Video Bookmarking System - Yuxt as well as Liquid Reel ( Video Management Platform)

Since then he provides his expertise to prominent clients such as Ogilvy, EuroRSCG, Reuters, United Nations, Sony, Xerox, CBS and many more. He currently lives and works in New York City.
