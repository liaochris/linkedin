Jordan Lindberg, Ph.D.’s Summary

I am currently involved in corporate administration, including strategic planning, organizational leadership, transformational management to maximize stability and long-term profitability, HR, budgeting and purchasing administration, sales and marketing administration, and board membership functions.

I am also serving as managing director in a small applications development company focused on servicing the third-party logistics sector through the development of integrated web-based order management and package processing applications.

Before my corporate life, I spent almost ten years in higher education as a professional educator and researcher. My areas of specialization include epistemology, metaphysics, logic, and the history of 20th century philosophy.

I also do a little guided fly fishing in the summer on my driftboat.
