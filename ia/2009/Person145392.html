Luke Miller’s Summary

I am a creative with management skills gained through experience of developing online and offline digital content since 1999. My involvement in projects has been in various roles including Designer, Developer, Consultant, Project Manager, Studio Manager and more recently Production Director. My career has privileged me with working on innovative and exciting projects for Channel 4, BBC, DfES/DCSF, The British Museum, The Welsh Assembly, Royal Shakespeare Company, UMG and more. Covering web 2.0, CMS, multi-lingual and convergent-media solutions.

My real passion is for working with creative and innovative people on exciting and ground breaking projects.

Luke Miller’s Specialties:

Team management, process implementation, project and account management, budgeting, information architecture, usability, design, web accessibility and HTML.
