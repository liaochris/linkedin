Gavin Turner’s Summary

II am an accomplished Senior Project Architect with more than 24 years experience in the construction industry over a wide variety of sectors. My interests lie in the technical aspects of project design and delivery. In my career I have enjoyed the challenge of design development and coordination of large heavily serviced projects such as:-

• Pharmaceutical research and manufacturing
• Court projects
• Further Education
• Healthcare
• Ministry of Defence
• Primary Schools

All which I have successfully delivered as project architect at both Oberlanders and Atkins. I am now looking for office where I can develop and streamline the solid background I have on project delivery.

Gavin Turner’s Specialties:

Whilst at Atkins my technical ability has been recognised and on a number of occasions I have been invited to audit projects at various stages for other offices as far afield as Bahrain. I then have been requested to develop these projects based on the audit.
Having worked across a wide variety of industry sectors I have an extensive list of contacts that will be of use in the future. I actively used these contacts to secure commissions for a number of pharmaceutical turnkey projects.
