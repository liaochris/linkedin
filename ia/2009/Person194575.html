Raymond Rumpf, Ph.D.’s Summary

Raymond has over 14 years of industry experience in the research and development of revolutionary technologies related to microsystems, advanced packaging, electromagnetics, and nanophotonics. He has a proven track record as a leader, visionary, technology strategist, team builder, and program director. He has a Ph.D. in Optics as well as M.S. and B.S. degrees in Electrical Engineering. Raymond has been awarded over a dozen US patents, authored over two dozen peer reviewed papers, and directed over a dozen government contracts and internal research and development programs.

Raymond Rumpf, Ph.D.’s Specialties:

Nanophotonics, microsystems, sensors, computational electromagnetics, antennas, ultra-wideband radio, diffractive optics, wireless technologies, fiber optics
