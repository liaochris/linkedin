Saloni Chavda’s Summary

Fortunate to have learnt under Designer Neeta Lulla for 6 months and being a Freelancing Accessory Designer for a year, I found my love in Apparel Designing. I have my own fashion company and a label, “Soul Cherry”, which speaks of ethnic garments with a contemporary touch in forms of Bridal, Couture, Pret and Accessories for women.

I've always wanted to custom make for women, so I work according to client's budgets, undertake special orders for women's wear and bridal attires. With design experience in embroidery, richly designed sarees are our speciality where each drape has its own story to tell, each in a unique creation of its own. Not two designs are the same, and not each design is ever repeated, is the value of owning a Soul Cherry Saree.

I have a long way to go, and I've just carved a fair niche for myself in the fashion industry of Mumbai, with honest exposure from Femina Magazine India, Mtv India, Marwar Wedding Exhibition 2008 and a few articles in Mumbai's Newspapers.

I wish to grow further in Fashion, that its thirst can never be quenched. My aim of Fashion is to bring the artistry of India out in rich drapes and forms of fabrics, rich cuts in gowns and dresses. Thus creating the classic beauty of India in garments that every woman can wear with proud royalty on herself.

My fashion style is Eclectically India.
