Dan McHugh’s Summary

Partner with businesses to increase profits from unusual sources.

Expense Reduction Analysts is a tactical resource that helps business executives around the world boost profits by finding cost savings in ways others can’t. Through our network of experts we review non-core / indirect expense categories using ERA’s proprietary benchmarking data.

We do this according to our clients’ specifications and without reducing service or product quality. In 2007 alone, we added 20% more profit to our clients’ bottom line.

We are 100% success-based, therefore, if we find no savings, there is no fee.
