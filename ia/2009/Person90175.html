Greg Roach’s Summary

The significant role of technology in organisations strategic business decisions has been my main focus as a Director and consultant, providing an understanding of technology with the recognition of its profitable application to products, services, and processes.

Deploying solutions for Business Process Analysis, Management and Re-Engineering. Monitoring new technologies and assessing their potential to become new products or services, overseeing of high level development projects to insure that they have the potential to add value to an organisation.

Providing reliable technical assessments of potential mergers and acquisitions, explaining products and future plans to the trade media, and participating in government, academic, and industry groups where there are opportunities to promote an organisations reputation and to capture valuable data.

Experienced manager of a wide range of personnel, has strong influencing skills, with a measurable track record.

Described as a strategic thinker, with strong commercial acumen, combined with project and program management experience and have demonstrable experience of managing sizeable budgets.

A background of working in B2B environments, and is considered a first class relationship builder with strong analytical and conceptual skills together with experience of compiling effective business plans, presentations and tenders.

Known to be a planner and organiser with high energy levels who is vigorous in execution to drive business operations.
