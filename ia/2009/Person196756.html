Reza Zadeh’s Summary

I was born in Abadan, Iran and my family came to the states when I was very young. I grew up in Southern California and came to Colorado State Univeristy for College. In college I was a member of the Football team and also had my life altered when I met a group of people that shared with me the truth about God. I had had so many thoughts about religion and honestly I was very against any type of religion.

What these people taught me was that God wanted a relationship with me which was a foreign concept to me. Over the next 4 years at CSU I learned more and more about that relationship and over the course of the last 11 years I have been on an incredible journey of faith.

I now serve as a Young Adults Pastor and have the honor of sharing this same truth with others. I believe that we all have a purpose in life and that purpose is the reason that we were created; I love watching people strive towards that purpose and fulfil the passions in their heart.

In all I do I strive to Make a Difference in the lives of others.
