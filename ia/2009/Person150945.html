Mario Sinacore’s Summary

I am a customer focused individual who delivers quality solutions on time. Over the past 10 years I have been involved in the full life-cycle of WMS projects across a number of sectors, including Retail, Third Party Logistics, Telecoms & Manufacturing, from sales through to go-live and support. This experience combined with an on-going technical background gives me the edge when identifying and solving the problems that arise during the implementation of a typical warehouse management project.
