Eric Facas’s Summary

Eric Facas, is a digital media strategist and senior sales executive with over nine years of experience at leading Internet companies. Eric spent the last five years at Google where he played a significant role in important program launches, built new teams from the ground up, and developed sales strategies across platforms ranging from Search Engine Advertising to Social Media. As a founding member of Google's current agency team, Eric developed new initiatives and helped build a strong foundation for working with the agency community. His accomplishments include successfully running Google’s SEM and Agency Councils, growing strategic partnerships, and participating in the creation of the multi-billion dollar AdWords API program. Eric received an exclusive EMG/Founder’s award from Google’s Executive Management Group for his work on this particular product in September of 2008. Before joining Google in 2004, he spent several years with America Online. While with the AOL division of Time Warner, Eric was responsible for managing the sales programs for their largest content partners such as eBay, CBS, and Monster.com. Eric also successfully integrated Time Warner's online properties including People.com, CNN.com, Money.com and Fortune.com into AOL's interactive media offerings. Eric holds a Bachelor of Arts degree in Economics from UC Santa Barbara and also developed strong analytical skills working for Charles Schwab & Co. before pursuing a career in digital marketing.

Eric currently runs a boutique consulting firm made up of former Google employees and also sits on the Board of Advisors for several Start-ups.
