Jill Elswick’s Summary

Jill Elswick is a freelance writer and conference programmer with a background in trade journalism. Her writing experience includes news articles, corporate profiles, advertorials, market research surveys, and short executive biographies.

Among Ms. Elswick’s clients are media firms, trade magazines, insurance companies, and a pharmaceutical consulting firm. Organizations she has worked with include:

* Dodge Communications
* The Economist
* Economist Conferences
* Employee Benefit Adviser
* Employee Benefit News
* Exceptional Parent Magazine
* The Guardian Life Insurance Company of America
* International Foundation of Employee Benefit Plans
* Kikaku America International
* MassMutual Financial Group
* Modern Media Partners
* SMB Finance Magazine
* Valley Business FRONT
* Virginia Polytechnic Institute & State University
* Wyeth Healthcare Systems

Ms. Elswick’s professional experience includes:

* marketing and public relations writer
* social media promoter of publications and business conferences
* journalist for the nation’s top trade magazine on employee benefits (circulation 60,000)
* ghostwriter for leading executives in the employee benefits industry
* conference programmer for events as large as 1,000 attendees and 50 sessions
* program manager for Benefits Forum & Expo, the national conference of Employee Benefit News, since 2005
* recruiter of speakers for business events hosted by Economist Conferences
* interim editor of Virginia Tech Magazine, the alumni magazine of Virginia Tech
* designer and writer of company newsletters, flyers, and postcards

Ms. Elswick is passionate about writing and communication. She is an excellent listener and quick learner who offers her writing and editing skills coupled with experience in marketing, public relations, human resources, events, and journalism.

Jill Elswick’s Specialties:

Employee benefits, health and retirement plans, group insurance plans, disability insurance, life insurance, special needs planning, personal finance, corporate finance.
