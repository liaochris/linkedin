Marion Chapsal’s Summary

My purpose is to coach women, and men too , using my generosity, creativity and passion, to communicate with confidence and enthusiasm and achieve their goals.

I believe you can do this with fun, passion and playfulness!

I am using innovative tools which foster creativity,whole brain thinking and active learning:
- multi sensory language
- role playing
- cartoons and videos
- story telling
- metaphors and myths.

You will learn to:
- story board your presentation
- design "Presentation ZEN" like slides
- develop your PRESENCE
- find your own UNIQUE VOICE
- speak with CLARITY and IMPACT
- be SEEN, be HEARD, "TOUCH" your audience with words that move...
