Gary Alan Miller’s Summary

I am a higher education professional with 15 years of experience in a variety of student counseling and managerial roles. My focus is on student success, interactive presentation creation and delivery, and strategy development. My areas of expertise include internships, career and academic exploration, and department operations and marketing. I also have a budding interest in career decision making among first-generation college students.

I am currently assistant director for business-related internships at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. For a more complete look at my past experience, see my full resume. My current professional activities include networking among Triangle business professionals, expanding my knowledge and use of social media in career development and creating new internship programming.
