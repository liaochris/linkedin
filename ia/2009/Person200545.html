Bob Jonas’s Summary

Bob's professional experience and problem-solving skills are well anchored in his extensive knowledge of Value Growth Strategies, Marketing Techniques, Financial Analysis and Leadership Development of industrial/manufacturing/distribution businesses.

As an experienced strategist for over 30 years, Bob recognizes that knowledge is power and is the essential component in today's increasingly global and electronic arena of competition. He has helped clients develop and execute value growth strategies that succeed in competitive markets.
