L. M. Carmen Villadar’s Summary

My previous 11 year nursing profession has given me the opportunity to perfect my customer service skills in all levels. Nursing has given me extensive practice and experience with all cultures, races, social classes, biases, ages and genders that I am able to look beyond what I see and really TAP into the 'real' inside the person.

Prior to nursing, I worked for WHERE Corp. in Toronto as their New Media Coordinator. The internet was still in its early stages and I was able to grasp and understand the impact of New Technology and its affects on business, marketing,advertising, communication, interpersonal relationships and the way individuals responded to any New Media on a personal level.

Today's New Media may seem a little intimidating but my understanding of technology and its social impact can help you weave through the web, seamlessly.

Currently, I work as a Freelance Business English Trainer. My classes are animated, motivated and fun. My training style is 'unique' and very effective and I bring all my skills and experiences (professional & personal) into my classes. I believe that knowledge is power and I want to help empower people in any way that I can.

Business English Language Training is certainly a way to empower people that want to succeed in Global Business Communication.

I have also taken my Business English Skills online and inside Second Life.

I firmly believe that if English is to be the Language of Global Business, then it is not only important to 'understand and know' the lingo of Business English in all its mediums, but it is more important to understand that people are human. The only difference is the environment they grew up in, perceptions they have, preconceived judgments and so on.

This quality is invaluable when dealing with a customer.

I am known to give my 120%, to bend over backwards and to exceed expectations.
