Teicko Huber’s Summary

As owner of Top Line Growth, I put my passion into action for emerging companies experiencing sales and marketing growth
hurdles.

I approach each client’s hurdles individually using four key disciplines: market research, strategic planning, sales
and marketing consulting and sales force development. Through this scientific approach, Top Line Growth rethinks companies’ challenges and creates results.

While 50% of businesses fail to reach their sales goals, I execute a business model to ensure success. Top Line Growth’s results include increasing a manufacturing company’s sales by over 135% in a year; quadrupling profit margins of a consumer packaged goods company in only 60 days and increasing revenue by more than 100% over a year; and doubling a financial services firm’s net income and increasing representative sales by 125%.

I credit my success to simple priorities:
develop best-in-class practices for each of the
growth disciplines and tailor them to the client’s needs; engage stakeholders to ensure commitment to the growth plan; and assist clients in executing that plan.

I'm a graduate of the University of Wisconsin, Stevens Point, with a Bachelor of Science degree in Biology and an emphasis in Pre-Medicine. He is a Frost & Sullivan sales
and marketing facilitator, an Innovation Games facilitator, and Salesforce.com partner. I'm also a member of Sales & Marketing Executives International and the Hudson, Wisc.,
Daybreak Rotary.

I'm the proud papa of three energetic little girls, Sophia (5), Olivia (3) and Fiona (1), who challenge me in new ways each day. My wife, Melissa, and I are active
in our community church, art center and center for performing arts.

When I'm not helping a client grow or spending time with his family, he enjoys outdoor sports. Biking, rock climbing,
power kiting and kayaking are among his favorites.
