Sean MacGillavry’s Summary

Dyro Consult provides support and coaching to foreign technology companies, primarily from Asia and the Americas, in the development of their sales & marketing strategies for Europe or the Benelux. Additional support is provided to greenfield operations in the search and build-up of the local workforce including negotiations with local councils.

Axon Olympus is committed to helping companies compete more efficiently and cost effectively by providing Microsoft BizTalk Server solutions that allow for company-wide messaging essential to organizational processes and goals. With more than 8 years of BizTalk Server experience in-house, Axon Olympus can deliver a unique level of service excellence and expertise in the area of enterprise application integration with a wide variety of systems and databases such as Baan, SAP, Oracle and Ingres.

Sean MacGillavry’s Specialties:

Axon Olympus covers all facets linked to Microsoft BizTalk Server including Service Oriented Architecture (SOA), Business Process Management (BPM) and Business Activity Monitoring (BAM), Business Intelligence, RFID, Enterprise Service Bus, Enterprise Application Integration, Architecture and Middleware. We also have expert in-house knowledge on SQL Server, Message Queuing, WCF, WF and Performance Point Server (PPS).
