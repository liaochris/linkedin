Mark Colacioppo’s Summary

Launched latest web startup, Globalizer, LLC in June 2008. Globalizer. LLC(http://www.globalizer.com) is a full service web marketing firm specializing in Search (SEM and SEO), Display, Email and Affiliate Marketing (http://www.globalizernetwork.com) in markets around the world with a special focus on the US, Europe and Asia.

Previously founded and ran online marketing agency Hot Rocket Marketing, Inc. which was sold to Traffix Inc. in January 2005. While under the Traffix umbrella, created and ran the RocketProfit (http://www.rocketprofit.com) affiliate network.

Was formerly a co-founder of online agency Network60 Inc., which ran numerous web properties including OneStopWinning.com which was sold to About.com in 2000.

Originally was involved in web marketing in 1996 while at PCH.com.
