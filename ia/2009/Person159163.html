Melanie Votaw’s Summary

I am a professional freelance journalist who has published ten non-fiction books on a variety of subjects. I also ghostwrite books and articles, and I edit and/or book doctor for individuals and publishers. I welcome inquiries from literary agents or individuals for a ghostwriter, collaborator, or editor. I often work with doctors and speakers to help them create a book or book proposal to build their platforms. My books are very diverse in terms of subject matter, and I enjoy that diversity because I have a lot of interests. That said, my main specializations include popular business, health/medicine, fitness, sexuality, psychology, spirituality, and nature/wildlife.

I have written for such publications as Woman's Day, Travel Savvy, His & Hers, and Medical Economics, and I specialize in travel, health, nature, and lifestyle (including sexuality.)

I'm also occasionally a copywriter for businesses and individuals (when it isn't a conflict of interest with my journalism work, of course.) I have written and edited websites, brochures, speeches, press releases, sales letters, and more.

I'm also a speaker available for meetings and conferences. My favorite topic is how to access your innate creativity in your career. Read more about me and my topics on my website at http://www.RuletheWord.com.

I'm a member of the Authors Guild, the Society of Professional Journalists, Editorial Freelancers Association, Freelance Success, and a former member of the American Society of Journalists and Authors.

As a travel writer and photographer, I'm also interested in hearing from PR specialists in this area with story leads. See my image galleries taken on six continents at http://www.VotawPhotography.com (although the site is in the midst of an overhaul.)
