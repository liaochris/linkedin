Sudha Krishna’s Summary

I am currently wearing two hats
1). Senior Consultant in Digital and Social Media at HJC;

2) and Director of Cross Media Productions for The Nimble Company.

At HJC I advise and implement digital and social media solutions for the Non-profit sector - including communication, content, and fund raising strategies.

Nimble is a company where the TV and the Internet meet.

I have extensive background in Television and New Media and have developed and managed large scale cross-platform deployments.

While at Nimble I supervised the production and deployment of a music social media site, RapSpace.tv and did consulting and production work for a number of clients including CanWest Media, Citizen's Bank, Heritage Canada and various broadcast production companies.
