David Morris’s Summary

Highly accomplished business analyst consultant, with over 25 years experience successfully enabling business change through strategic analysis, business process improvement, organisational change, and a range of enterprise, e-commerce, and new media web-based solutions -- involved across the full project life cycle, including significant involvement in training, mentoring, and facilitating -- with 15 years in senior, lead, and consultancy roles.

Passionate about:
* realising business benefit by ensuring business stakeholders and technical delivery teams work collaboratively, using whatever methodology suits, with good experience of introducing more agile practices;
* professionalism of the business analysis discipline, in terms of team capability maturity, individual competencies, strong leadership, and organisational alignment; and
* effective presenting ("ban the bullet-point"), visual thinking, public speaking, training, and mentoring.

David Morris’s Specialties:

business process improvement, training, mentoring, visual thinking, requirements elicitation, information architecture, project management, usability/accessibility, user experience design, systems design, functional specification
