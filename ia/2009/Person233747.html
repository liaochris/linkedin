Tim Martin’s Summary

As a marketer and motivator, I work with entrepreneurs and senior management of emerging enterprises to help ensure the value proposition being offered is of highest relevance to today's dynamic market. With a sustainable value proposition, which more times than not in our digital world comes down to culture, we can begin the process of building brand equity. My hope is that my client will seek profitable growth, while nurturing an enviable culture within their specific industry. My goal is to replicate that experience as many times as possible.

Tim Martin’s Experience

  • President

    Newtrix

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    2009 — Present (less than a year)

    Tim Martin founded Newtrix in response to the confluence of two game-changing influences - a global economic crisis and the digital revolution - which have forever altered the business landscape and the pace of change. Newtrix is an executive consulting practice dedicated to helping transform companies by identifying incremental growth opportunities. The process involves integrating two fundamental and connected processes:
    1. A new value proposition that meets tomorrow's market needs, and
    2. Operational alignment for a smarter, smoother execution.

  • Vice President Marketing, New Product Development

    OttLite Technologies

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    2008 — 2009 (1 year)

    Facilitated the Market Power Planning process, inclusive of research and validation and authored a strategic marketing plan for a national and international branding and repositioning effort. A multi- million dollar annual budget was utilized for new logo and positioning line development to reflect articulated brand essence along with brand family package redesign, brand campaign, new ecommerce web site, in-store promotion, public relations and national media.

  • Life Coach/Consultant

    LifeExceleration

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    2003 — 2007 (4 years)

    A life change and desire to test a passion led me to RDV Sportsplex for two years where I developed individualized, systematic and progressive fitness and nutrition plans for physicians and clients. Ultimately, in an effort to offer clients a more holistic approach to optimizing performance, I opened Life Exceleration, which added the critical dimension of life coaching. In combination this co-creative process of clarifying and articulating ones vision and aligning their values with relevant and attainable goals afforded the athlete and the business professional the opportunity to be their best while creating his/her personal brand.

  • President/Consultant

    FORZA Marketing Group

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    1988 — 2003 (15 years)

    Established this marketing firm and fueled its steady growth to a 26-person agency generating $12 million in capitalized billings from a loyal, satisfied client base spanning seven states. Prepared and delivered creative and business presentations that produced 14 years of consistent growth.
