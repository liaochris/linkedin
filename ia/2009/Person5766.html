Adam Feldman’s Summary

Adam is an experienced Business Analyst, who is currently in the process of launching Bright Green Projects. Bright Green Projects is an application that allows the capture and management of Requirements, Risks, Issues, Change Requests, Meetings and Actions on IT Implementations.

Adam began his career with Deloitte Consulting as a Management Consultant and for the last five years has contracted as a Business Analyst in London.

Adam Feldman’s Specialties:

Business Analyst, Siebel CRM Specialist

    Bright Green Projects

    (Privately Held; Information Technology and Services industry)

    December 2008 — Present (9 months)

    Bright Green Projects takes advantage of the latest in Social Networking technologies to allow you to easily collaborate with the business and make sure you get the requirements right first time – quickly moving towards signed-off requirements and minimising costly rework.

    The vision of Bright Green Projects is to deliver a solution for analysts and developers that makes the process of managing requirements easier for both you and your stakeholders. By adding Risks, Issues, Change Requests and Actions and also showing dependencies between all these entities, we have extended benefits to the Project Manager, Testers, PMO, Program Manager and the CIO.

    We are already brightening up 300 organisations around the world! We have users in the US, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, India, the UK, Holland, the Middle East and South Africa. These organisations are Consultancies, Software Development Houses, Government Departments, Not-for-profit Organisations, Banks and other Corporations both large and small.
