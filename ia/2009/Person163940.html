Mike Basquez’s Summary

A business-to-business sales professional who excels at building long-term relationships by establishing trust and utilizing solid problem solving abilities. I succeed in tangible, complex capital equipment sales cycles by paying attention to and consistently meeting deadlines. My analytical strengths allow me to form solid connections with my clients as I help them think through difficult business concerns and uncover solutions. I add further value through my abilities to conceptualize huge projects by breaking processes down into smaller, more manageable tasks. By recognizing patterns and connections, I am able to more accurately anticipate stumbling blocks, leading to higher customer satisfaction. While others may shrink away from problem-solving, I actually find it energizing. I am often called upon to train staff on new products or procedures due to my ability to explain detailed concepts in simple terms. My interaction style is highlighted by creating harmonious environments where people feel they are treated equally.
