Antonello Fiore’s Summary

Results-oriented Supply Chain and Operation manager leading to top management.

Proven expert in identifying and exploiting cost-reduction opportunities in the specific business through process reengineering, Lean Production, World Class Manufacturing and Six Sigma.

My leadership style is participative. My point of strength is the determination in pursuing company objectives. I usually involve all the people to share goals and vision, giving ongoing support in solving problems and enhancing motivation.

Last experiences allowed me to develop a big picture on all the activities affecting the companies during a change management phase. I got responsibility on purchasing, production, logistics and customer service.

Joining Data Service, during a depth change management, I developed and implemented the Industrial Plan, working with CEO, CFO, unions and financial analysts.
We moved our activities towards the East Europe.
We closed non core production sites and we sold non strategic business units (trucks and printing) by means of the Due Diligence.

Joining Nilfisk, I drove the Supply Chain to reduce the Delivery Lead Time, OEM critical success factor.
Recently, the Italian division passed from the worst to the best Nilfisk division worldwide on the basis of the logistic balanced scorecards. Particularly, we accomplished interventions on
1) outbound logistics – by implementing Direct Shipment -,
2) manufacturing – by implementing the Lean Production –
3) inbound logistics – by selecting main contractors per each technology and spreading the kanban system


.
