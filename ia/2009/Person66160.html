Doron Vermaat’s Summary

Founded NewChinaCareer in January 2007 to solve a puzzling gap in China's world of online recruitment and high-end employment.

Today, NewChinaCareer lists over 15,000 career opportunities within the Greater China region for experienced industry professionals that are bi-lingual (Chinese + English) and the recruiters and employers looking to hire them.

If you are interested in advertising your career opportunities on NewChinaCareer you can contact us at sales@newchinacareer.com or +852 28159004

Prior to co-founding NewChinaCareer and my role at Matthew Hoyle International, i worked at NationaleVacaturebank.nl, a Dutch online recruitment startup.
