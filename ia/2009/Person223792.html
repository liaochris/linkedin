Steve Davies’s Summary

A 20 year veteran in managament consulting best known for business transformation, value based management and developing market entry strategies. Defined the market proposition that led to the formation of the Bluetooth consortium (whilst working with Ericsson) and instrumental in launching the O2 brand within a differentiated market proposition back in 2001.

Interacting at board level in large multi-national clients have taken part in many large transformation and business start up projects. Also experienced in deal structuring, acquisition and business integration.

Steve Davies’s Specialties:

Business Transformation - Designing and delivering complex transformation programmes, designing business architectures and directing actions to maximize shareholder value and competitive advantage. Market making – establishing new business models and developing new market niches within a services and solutions business. Client Management – account management of large global clients.
Commercial delivery – leadership and due diligence of complex commercial programmes
