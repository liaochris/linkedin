Ari Huczkowski’s Experience

  • CEO

    Otaniemi Marketing

    (International Trade and Development industry)

    June 2008 — Present (1 year)

    Otaniemi Marketing Ltd is the international marketing and area development company of Otaniemi area in Espoo, in greater Helsinki area, Finland.

    Otaniemi is the heart of Finnish hitech with Helsinki University of Technology (TKK) campus, World HQ of Nokia, Kone, Fortum and Neste Oil. Local HQ of Microsoft, MSD and more. HQ of VTT Research Institute, HQ of KCL Research institute, Geological Survey of Finland, CSC with its supercomputers. 32,000 hitech professionals and students work and study in Otaniemi every day. 600 companies.

    Otaniemi Marketing arranges visits to Otaniemi university labs, research institutions and companies for international delegations. Otaniemi Marketing also develops the Otaniemi area with many different project such as the Otaniemi Microclustering Project.

  • Project Director

    Technopolis Ventures Ltd

    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    January 2008 — July 2008 (7 months)

    In charge of Otaniemi Microclustering Project 2008-2009. The aim in the project is to create between 3 and 5 microclusters with strong technological and Otaniemi focus.

    Otaniemi is THE technology hub of Scandinavia. Nokia HQ are only 2 km away. The campus of Helsinki University of Technology with 15 thousand students is right here. Otaniemi is the home for 1000+ companies, mainly in software development, mobile technologies or other hitech hardware and systems.

    The Otaniemi Microcluster Project's microclusters will have:
    * up to 10 labs in each
    * between 15 and 25 startups and SMEs
    * 1 to 2 anchor companies.

    The microclusters will work actively to enhance all participants' business opportunities, networking, R&D and more. The aim is to create longlasting relationships between the participating organizations and companies and to create permanent, well-functioning technological ecosystems in Otaniemi.
