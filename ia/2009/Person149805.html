Margaret Roberts’s Summary

I am a 17 year veteran of the recruiting and career management fields. My experience in these fields spans the temporary staffing, high tech recruiting, venture capital, higher education and career management services industries. I have written and critiqued thousands of resumes for candidates at every stage of the career lifecycle from entry-level to seasoned executives and coached this same audience through the job search process, preparing for interviews, evaluating job offers and navigating career transitions.

My professional passion lies in helping individuals define their personal career goals and enabling their success by providing the most effective tools (resumes, cover letters, job search action plans) and skills (interview training, self-assessment), allowing my clients to achieve the success they are seeking.

On the personal side I currently enjoy managing/co-coaching our daughters’ 14U travel softball team, the Elk Grove Thunder.


Certified EQ Career Strategist (2008) - Trained and certified to coach and assess competencies in emotional intelligence.
