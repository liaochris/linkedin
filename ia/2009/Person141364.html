    Production Assistant: April 2007 - September 2008

    Responsible for project management of adult and children's audio books for libraries and retail sale.     (Public Company; Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry)

This contract consisted of working 7 days a week for 6 months to finish my college co-op requirements.
