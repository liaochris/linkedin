Greg Hamlyn’s Summary

I run the business Trades Coaching Pty Ltd together with my business partner, Kurt Hegetschweiler.

We are authors, business owners, presenters/guest speakers, and business coaches/consultants who specialize in working with Trades Industry Business Owners and Leaders to grow and improve their businesses.

We have created a range of coaching programs, books, videos and education tools including the world’s first and only on-line business coaching programs exclusively developed for Trades Industry Businesses, Owners and Contractors.

Over the past 15 years, Kurt and I have worked with businesses ranging from small start-ups to large multi-million dollar businesses. In each business, we’ve helped the owners and leaders create what we call a ‘well-oiled, systematic profitable business machine’ which has allowed them to focus on vital areas of their business such as growth, strategy and leadership, all of which provide them with consistent and increased profits and time. And the business owners have personally grown as well…we guide them onto and along the right paths…helping them to reach their potential!
