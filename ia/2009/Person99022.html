Suzanne Miller’s Summary

Suzanne has worked entrepreneurially for most of her career, either owning a business or being part of a new business launch team. In 1992, she began Intrinsic Design and successfully grew a one-person graphic design studio to a total team of seven. Intrinsic Design created both print and web designs for technology and pharmaceutical clients. In 2000, Intrinsic became part of Omnient Corporation, an Internet professional services firm. As art director, Suzanne was responsible for developing strategic branding for Omnient, setting creative vision for client projects, and building goals for the creative department.

For the past five years, Suzanne has worked closely with Rich Masterson at Masterson Development. She acted as creative/marketing director for many of Masterson’s international commercial and residential real estate projects, as well as other technology-based entrepreneurial businesses. She gained vast experience in search engine marketing, web 2.0 marketing media tools, lifestyle design, brand positioning and user-interface experiences.

Currently, Suzanne manages a New Media team for Yell Adworks, Inc. in developing new online products such as video ads, websites, and landing pages for Yellowbook.com advertisers. Yell works with an exclusive commitment to one customer, Yellow Book USA.
