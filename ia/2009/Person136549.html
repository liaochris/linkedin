Lance Cross’s Summary

Delivers general management consultancy and Investors In People-related advice. Having helped many organisations grow and improve as a Business Adviser on Business West’s advisory services team, he has fifteen years’ experience in helping organisations with their planning and strategy, focusing on performance management and leadership skills. He is a qualified trainer, a registered Investors In People practitioner and national level Business Excellence Award assessor.

His clients range from small businesses to public authorities, taking in large corporates and charities along the way, across a wide variety of industry sectors.

Previous roles include managing major improvement programmes at NatWest, Business Link Somerset where he was Supplier Network Manager responsible for setting up the local service provider network and General Manager of Excellence South West for two years of its infancy. He is a member of the Institute of Business Consulting.

Being Bristol born and bred, Lance has built up an extensive network of contacts for the sharing of learning and expertise across the South West. His main background and passion is facilitation aimed at helping senior managers to help themselves and their people to achieve better business performance.

Lance Cross’s Specialties:

business skills training, industrial relations, staff and customer research, customer care improvement programmes, performance management, recognition schemes, TQM, Business Excellence.
