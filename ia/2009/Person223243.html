Stephen Giavara’s Summary

Functional Air Force military pay expert responsible for High Level Design Analysis and System/Subsystem Specification Analysis of the Defense Integrated Military Human Resources System (DIMHRS). Once implemented, DIMHRS for Personnel and Pay (Pers/Pay) will result in the largest, fully-integrated human resources information management system in the world servicing over 4 million military customers. Key participant in five Enterprise Program Management Office (EPMO) Joint Requirements Reviews to facilitate development of DIMHRS Pers/Pay core requirements that meet the needs of the Army and Air Force. Converted 120 Functional Change Requests (FCRs) that are essential for DIMHRS Initial Operational Capability (IOC) to EPMO Requirements Change Requests (RCRs); submitted the RCRs to EPMO for action. Delivered Requirements Change Requests to EPMO to ensure DIMHRS (Pers/Pay) conformance to changes in Basic Allowance for Housing, Family Separation Housing Allowance, and Overseas Housing Allowance resulting from the Fiscal Year 2006 National Defense Authorization Act. Provided P&R IM response/comment for 84 Air Force - High Level Design (HLD) packages. Reviewed and provided recommendations/comments concerning the impact of described functionality on Business Area core requirements for DIMHRS in Air Force gap documents presented in the Final Air Force Requirements Review. Provided POM support to Headquarters, USAFRICOM in Stuttgart, Germany. Created an Army Management Decision Package (MDEP) informational briefing for top AFRICOM leadership on all Army MDEPs associated with AFRICOM funding, clarifying sources of current year funding and insuring funding is properly programmed in future years (FY10-15).
