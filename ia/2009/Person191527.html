Travis Stone’s Summary

Travis became the track announcer for Louisiana Downs in March 2006, home of the Super Derby, fulfilling a lifelong dream to call the horse races which took him from the announcer booth at Suffolk Downs in Boston, Massachusetts to the roof at Saratoga Race Course. In his first year he was named an All-Star Announcer and called a horse race on Breeders' Cup weekend at Churchill Downs, home of the Kentucky Derby. He was also named an All-Star Announcer in 2007. In April of 2007 he began instructing a course he designed called, "Horse Racing: Finding Winners" to a sold-out crowd of eager community members wanting to learn more about horse racing.

Career goals and amibitions include calling the races at Saratoga Race Course and announcing the Breeders' Cup World Championships. Additional goals include the development of various online web sites geared towards, but no limited to, the sport of thoroughbred horse racing.

Travis Stone’s Specialties:

Rapid memorization and announcing of a live horse race, public address, writing, internet technology, PHP/MySQL programming.
