Eveline Wu’s Summary

Eveline is an experienced mind/body practitioner who uses touch, movement and guided listening in an integrated approach to help people reconnect to their own awareness and resolve chronic pain, stress, and neurological conditions. Her work is based in the Feldenkrais Method, a unique system for improving mobility, efficient sports performance, and freeing the body from mental blocks.
For the last 10 years, Eveline has practiced professionally, from the Bay Area to Alaska. She has taken her knowledge with special needs children and become an adaptive ski instructor and an expert level certified ski instructor.
She is working in the San Francisco Bay Area, where she is deepening and honing her knowledge of the mind/body connection, to serve with sensitivity and skill those who desire greater resiliency and potential in their lives, on all levels.

Eveline Wu’s Specialties:

Certified in Feldenkrais Somatic Education, the Anat Baniel Method for Children.
Training includes influences from Inner Relationship Focusing, and a wide range of somatic therapies including Hakomi Integrative Somatics, Psycho-Physical Therapy, Body Mind Centering, and Intentional Touch.
