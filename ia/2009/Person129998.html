Kenneth A. Barton, PMP’s Summary

Accomplished Construction Manager with proven experience overseeing both interior and exterior commercial glazing projects. Distinctive and valuable range of knowledge concerning project management, operations management, fabrication, and installation in commercial and architectural glazing industries; highly developed ability to identify and successfully implement innovative solutions to meet diverse challenges. Proven history directing numerous projects exceeding $12MM to include curtain walls, storefronts, panels, windows, entrances, and tenant fit-outs.

Highlights of my qualifications include:
• Dependably managed and delivered high quality projects ahead of schedule and within budget.
• Creative and determined troubleshooter with strong base of industry knowledge and innate ability to envision cost-saving solutions.
• Developed solid, well-trained project teams which resulted in first-rate work through every project phase.
• Deep product knowledge developed through field and project management experience; interface well with wide range of individuals from upper management to field personnel.
• Excellent history of customer service and client retention complemented by steady flow of referral business.
• Superior organizational abilities complemented by expertise in prioritizing tasks and resolving potential problems to minimize profit loss.
• Keen understanding of project lifecycle from bidding through scheduling and close-out; familiar with local building codes and dedicated to on-site safety.
• Excellent product knowledge that facilitate clear communication with fabrication and installation professionals.
