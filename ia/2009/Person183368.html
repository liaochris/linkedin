I am a Computer Engineering Junior at Santa Clara University with a concentration in Information Assurance through NSA-approved coursework. I enjoy keeping up with the latest security threats & proof of concepts in my spare time and have industry experience dealing directly with security-centric topics. I plan on graduating in 2010 and receiving a MS in Computer Engineering by 2011 through Santa Clara University's five year masters program.

Some highlights:
- Extensive experience with web application security (XSS, SQL injection, RFI, etc).

- Professional experience with all major operating systems including extensive customization and use of Windows 95 through Vista, Mac OS X & 9 and Linux.

- Excels at designing and building custom solutions: from automating temperature-sensitive window blinds to designing media centers systems; from building corporate Exchange-compatible systems to automating deployment of dual-boot OS X & Windows XP images to Macs in a university environment through the use of custom scripts.

- Proficient leadership skills (Eagle Scout, past managerial work experience)
