Lindsey Toth’s Summary

My passion is translating the science of nutrition into actionable advice for the general consumer, and communicating nutrition information through various media outlets. I graduated from Michigan State University's Honors College in 2008, with a bachelor's degree in both nutritional sciences and dietetics.

Currently, I am a Dietetic Intern at Tufts Medical Center, as well as a master’s degree candidate at Tufts Friedman School of Nutrition Science and Policy - the only graduate and professional school of nutrition in North America. In order to further enhance my skills as a health communicator, my concentration there is in Nutrition Communications.

After completing my dietetic internship in May 2010, I will be seeking an internship opportunity in the communications field for the summer of 2010. I am interested in nutrition as it relates to writing, public relations, and the food industry, but am open to other related positions, as I am always looking for new and interesting ways to express myself through health and nutrition communication.

Lindsey Toth’s Specialties:

Lindsey is proficient in Nutritionist Pro, SPSS, Stata, and Microsoft Office; She is also a certified Primary Group Fitness Instructor, and has certifications in HACCP and CPR. Lindsey possesses basic HTML and CSS coding knowledge, and speaks basic French.
