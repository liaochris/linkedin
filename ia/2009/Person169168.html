Scott Davis’s Summary

As Managing Partner and BluWater's founder, Scott brings over 20 years of experience in IT and professional services, with expertise in program management, planning, systems integration, business development and technical analysis. As President & CEO, Scott led BluWater to achieve recognition as Washington State's Fastest Growing Private Company achieving 1,526% growth in 2006 and as the 181st Fastest Growing Private Company by Inc. Magazine with 1,143% growth in 2007. He serves on the Board of Directors for the Association of Washington Businesses. Scott is also an Ernst & Young Entrepreneur of the Year nominee (2008, 2009).

Prior to BluWater, Scott served as a consultant and acting Vice President of Professional Services for his client One2One Dynamics (a Cendent subsidiary), where he developed the service delivery process for the company. He also served as Regional Director at Inteliant Corporation, leading engagements and building solutions for Fortune 500 companies. Scott earned a Bachelor's degree from Gonzaga University in Economics and Political Science, with minors in MIS and Computer Science.
