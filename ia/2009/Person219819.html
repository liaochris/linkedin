Sofia Woloschin, PMP’s Summary

IT Project Manager Professional (PMP, PMI credential), with over sixteen years of extensive work experience as a functional Business-System Analyst and Tester and later managing Implementation Software, Development and Process Re-Engineering and Quality Assurance Projects. I have developed my career working for diverse Customer Channels and different industries. I have participated in complex project environments with teams over 200 people, in both vertical and consultancy companies such as Telecom de Argentina S.A., Philip Morris de Argentina, PriceWaterhouse and others. I also have over three years experience in working within virtual teams.

My lastly acquired experience as a Quality Assurance Manager provided me with an interesting background in formal Quality methodologies. In this position I was accountable of:
• CMMI SE-SW-IPD-SS – V.1.1 Level 2 implementation, for which the company obtained the CMMI Level 2 Certification in July, 2007.
• Training and coaching of teams’ leaders on hard and soft skills, which reflected in a substantial improvement in performance and Customer satisfaction.
• Implementation of several organizational changes which conducted to an increase in revenues due to savings in logistics costs related to Customer Support activities.

Nowadays I am running the start up of my own business, gathering a superior team of professionals as well as opening new business horizons.
