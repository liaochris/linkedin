Open my and your mind: Improve business thinking and transform the results in added value. To reach this in a leading function is my professional objective. Therefore I am looking for and offering experience shares in order to wide up my professional horizon.

Mission

My mission is to enhance myself. High self-motivation and passion are base of my work. The take-over of responsibility and the conviction it is possible to move things are my drivers. Good by better to replace means at times strong resistances.
Teamwork with a clear role understanding is for me key factor for success and lead to active involvement of all roles and responsibilities within project management.
Inter human and social competency as well as the (suppose of) understanding of peoples acting support the efforts invested.

My experience shows the following attributes are essential and base of my work:

• Professional qualification

• Ability to exchange experiences

For me the potentials are impressive which can be uncovered by process management. Pragmatically means to point out exact these advantages. But also to understand this as a base for management which means to improve leading and steering in a company.
