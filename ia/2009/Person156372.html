Matt Jones’s Summary

Possess 6 years business development experience building relationships with Fortune 1000 companies in order to provide them recruiting services for top IT talent.

I recently started with Vaco, an Information Technology and Accounting consulting company, which was formed in 2002 by 3 industry leaders. Bestowed onto me was the opportunity to open the 25th office for the "Vaco Nation" located in Chicago, IL.

Prior to taking on my new adventure with Vaco, I worked for a Top 8 IT Staffing company where I helped grow revenue from $83M to $400M nationally in 5 years while working in 3 offices. My success in Chicago gave me the opportunity to pioneer the opening of the Milwaukee Office which at the time made me the youngest Managing Director in the company.

I'm excited to be back working in Chicago within a market that has unlimited potential in the staffing industry. I'm also pumped to work for a company that was named to the INC 5000 list as one of the fastest growing companies in the country (INC 5000 #33 in 2007 and #320 in 2008).

For those clients I strive to gain trust and a partnership with, if you're not satisfied by the efforts of your current staffing vendor please feel free to reach out to me. I can guarantee that you will never work with someone who is as honest and passionate about helping connect quality people with quality companies and having the best time accomplishing the connection.
