Silver Cuellar’s Summary

Silver Cuellar is a Conceptual Arsonist/Art Director at Firehouse in Dallas, Texas. He specializes in strategic brand building, art direction and facial hair. Working at agencies smaller than 20 people helping to build their numbers to more than double that, and also at Agencies larger than 1000 employees with offices Worldwide. Silver's work has been recognized by Communication Arts, The One Show, Hatch Awards, Effies, New York Festival, Clios, Lürzer's Archive, One Screen Film Fest, Adfreak, Local and Regional Addys, Killed Ideas Vol. 1; but never by his folks as they still have no idea what he does for a living.

Silver Cuellar’s Specialties:

Ideation. Execution. Spontaneous combustion.
