    - The CEL helps individuals with developmental disabilities become self-sufficient in all aspects of life.

    - The CEL is committed to providing support to people with developmental disabilities so that there are more choices open to them; including where they live, work, and play.

    - The CEL believes that because someone may have a disability, it does not mean that they are extraordinary, special or courageous (unless of course they do something that is extraordinary, special or courageous). Nor does it mean that they are afflicted, suffering, victims, or unfortunate.
