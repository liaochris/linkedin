Sandeep Agarwal’s Summary

I worked in the textile industry for over 13 years(specially the DENIM Industry) .I l became an entrepreneur in 2005 . Starting with casual browsing of the net, I got deeply involved and interested in the Internet related businesses and activities. My interest made me evaluate internet from the perspective of a layman and made me aware of some facilities which others like me would like to have to be able to use the net better. The thought led to the development of a portal .It is a portal which enables anyone to create a FREE HOMEPAGE and control their web activities from there. It also has a vast directory related to Indian Industries and enables one to find decent Indian companies under various categories in a very easy way.
