Wayne Crumpley’s Summary

Pacific States Capital Corporation was founded on January 7, 1994. The major purpose of the corporation was to do business consulting work with small and mid-size corporations with the goal of "going public." Along the way, there appeared a demand for consulting services from private business entrepreneurs wanting their first corporation, or an "aged" corporation with some credit history, or wanting to do a reverse merger into a public company. Over the last 15 years we have done it all!
Along the way we have gathered a portfolio of securities. We are continously buying and selling stocks for our own portfolio. Some of our securities are in "shell" companies or "developing" companies. Some are publicly traded. At the present time our largest single holding is in Clean Coal Technologies, Inc. (CCTC), a publicly traded corporation. Clean Coal Technologies, Inc. (CCTC) is in the "green energy" business. The company is the owner of a patent-protected technology that provides clean energy at low costs through the use of the world's most abundant fossil fuel, coal. The company's technology utilizes controlled heat to extract and capture pollutants and moisture from low-rank coal, transforming it into a clean-burning, more energy-efficient fuel, prior to combustion. This is a "pre-burn" process. We have more information with regard to Clean Coal Technologies, Inc. (CCTC) on our Website at: www.pacificstatescapitalcorp.com, or at Clean Coal Technologies, Inc.'s Website at: www.cleancoaltechnologiesinc.com.
For the next 10-20 years we are planning on taking large positions as "early investors" in "green energy" stocks, and infrastructure building companies. The world may be headed towards renewable alternative energies, but that is a long ways off. "Clean Coal," "Coal Gasification," "Smart Instrumentation," and the general infrastructure to do things smarter is the "near future" (10-20 years) path to get wealthy.

Wayne Crumpley’s Specialties:

We consult small and mid-size corporations with "going public" on the horizon. Some companies we start by consulting; others may need a new corporation; others may want an "aged" corporation; others may want to do a reverse merger. We have a number of Registered Agent clients. For some clients we write annual minutes, officially call, notice, and hold director and shareholder meetings, and act as offical monitors for elections. See our Website at: www.pacificstatescapitalcorp.com.
