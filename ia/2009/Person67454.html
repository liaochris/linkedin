Lawrence Levy, Psy.D.’s Summary

Now, along with growing my Psychology Practice in Boca Raton, I am working with The marquin Group doing Medical Recruiting. We find top-notch MDs and Osteopaths for great positions in prestigious hospitals. I am also interested in getting CVs from MDs.

I still am seeing patients at my office in Boca Raton, Florida. I am a Florida Licensed Psychologist. My degree is a Psy.D., Doctor of Psychology degree from Nova Southeastern University, 2002.

My resume is available at my website http://www.LevyPsychology.com and http://resume.LevyPsychology.com

In my private practice in Boca Raton, Florida, I treat adolescents and adults for anxiety, depression, relationship issues, phobias, confidence, shyness, couples/marriage therapy, and job stress. I also use hypnosis, short term psychoanalytic psychotherapy, and CBT, Cognitive Behavioral Therapy. My office is close to Ft. Lauderdale, Deerfield Beach, Delray Beach, Coral Springs, Pompano Beach and Boynton Beach.

I completed my undergraduate work at Harvard University, School of Extension Studies in 1993.

Before returning to school to become a psychologist, I worked as a software engineer in various companies in Massachusetts, along Route 128 such as Wang, Labs, Inc, Xyvision, and Graphic Horizons, Inc.
