Rob Edmund’s Summary

I am an experienced labor and employment attorney who recently left private practice as a partner with the law firm of Porter Wright Morris & Arthur to advance education and healthcare reform at the Ohio Business Roundtable.

Rob Edmund’s Specialties:

As a labor and employment attorney, I had extensive experience defending discrimination and wrongful discharge lawsuits; I also routinely counseled clients on updating and drafting handbook and management policies, executive and employee terminations and discipline, FMLA and other leave issues, background checks and FCRA compliance, and a wide range of labor law issues.
