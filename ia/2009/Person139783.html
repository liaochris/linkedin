Mohan Krishnamurthy’s Summary

I am a Network & Security Consultant handling large Networking, Security and IT Infrastructure projects in Almoayed Group.

I have co-authored the following books published by Syngress:
1. Designing & Building Enterprise DMZs
2. Configuring Juniper Networks NetScreen & SSG Firewalls
3. How to Cheat at Securing Linux
4. How to Cheat at Administering Office Communications Server 2007
5. Microsoft Forefront Security Administration Guide
6. The Real MCTS/MCITP Windows Server 2008 Configuring Applications Infrastructure Exam 70-643 Prep Kit
7. CompTIA Network+ Certification Study Guide

I am open to invitations to work on Computer technology books.

Presently I am working with Almoayed Group, Bahrain as General Manager - Network Security. Nature of my work involves providing technical consulting in enterprise LAN/WAN, campus and Metro networks, Information Security (Firewalls, VPN, IPS, Authentication, Content Filtering Solutions) and Physical Security (CCTV, Access Control, Biometric, RFID) projects. I also offer consulting services in Microsoft products (Windows 200x, SharePoint, Office Communications Server, Exchange, SQL etc).

I hold various vendor certifications such as Juniper, Microsoft, Novell, Nortel, Cisco, Check Point, Watchguard, Citrix etc., vendor neutral certifications such as BS7799, OPSA, OPST and some trainer level certifications.

I have traveled across 15 countries, delivered lectures and projects in most of these countries.

During my free time I engage myself in writing short stories, novels, and articles. I like music, watching movies and long drives.

More about me at http://www.leomohan.net
