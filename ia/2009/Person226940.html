Susan Detwiler’s Summary

With more than 30 years of business and marketing experience in for- and not-for-profit organizations, I guide not-for-profit organizations in their marketing, fundraising, and strategic planning efforts. Leadership development is often a component in increasing board effectiveness in all these areas.

Nonprofit organizations that can strategically focus their efforts are better able to weather these challenging economic times. With extensive experience owning a small business combined with experience leading nonprofits I can offer a cross-fertilization of ideas between the for-profit and not-for-profit industries.

Susan Detwiler’s Specialties:

Nonprofit and/or not-for-profit board development, fundraising, financial development, capital and endowment campaigns, planned giving assessments and campaigns, strategic planning, strategic marketing, small business development.
