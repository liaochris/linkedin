Alvaro Perez’s Summary

I have studied at Universidad de La Coruña in Spain, where I have received my MSc in Computer Science. Currently I'm studying a Masters in European Business at the ESCP-EAP.

Formerly I have been working in London where I have developed Ruby on Rails applications for media company Aegis Plc., for the Sainsbury Laboratory in Norwich, UK, developing a Ruby on Rails application for retrieving biological data from large datasets and for the Juan Canalejo Hospital developing a J2EE application for maintaining a diabetic clinic history.

I speak fluent English and Italian, and some French, Portuguese and German. My mother language is Spanish.
