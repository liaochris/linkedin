Derek Yu’s Summary

Principal of Law Offices of Derek K. Yu (“LODKY”), a law firm based in San Francisco, California with a multi-disciplinary approach that insures that its local, regional, national and international clients receive excellent, innovative and cost-effective representation in the large variety of matters required to do business in today's market and in the future.
