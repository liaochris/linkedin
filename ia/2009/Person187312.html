Phil McAndrew’s Summary

I'm an illustrator and cartoonist living in Syracuse, NY. In 2007, I graduated with a BFA in illustration from Daemen College, where I was awarded a portfolio-based four year scholarship. I started publishing mini comics and webcomics as a high school student. In 2004, I co-founded Found Hat Press, a collective of talented young illustrators and cartoonists. At the age of 22 I won the 2006 Syracuse Press Club Professional Recognition Award for best art illustration. I am currently attempting to write and draw my first graphic novel while pursuing freelance illustration work.

Illustration clients include King Magazine, Cosmic Pictures, bp Magazine, Baltimore Magazine, Ani DiFranco/Righteous Babe Records, Boston's Weekly Dig, New Reliable Press, The Syracuse Post-Standard, Moo Cow Fan Club Magazine, The Wit's End Players, Daemen College, 100 Damned Guns, Set To Easy, Weather Graphics, and a bunch of unsigned bands.

Phil's comics have been published by New Reliable Press, Terminal City, Serializer.net, Plotz, Almost Normal Comics, Life Meter, and Sour Josephine. He has done coloring work for Scholastic's Graphix imprint.
