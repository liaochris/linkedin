Real Social Dynamics’s Summary

GOLD STANDARD OF THE DATING INDUSTRY
Real Social Dynamics was founded in 2002. Previously, there were many individuals who made an occupation of giving advice on how to meet women, but no one who was willing to walk the talk and put it their credibility on the line by demonstrating it in real life. The growth of “Real Social Dynamics” is attributable in part to this unique selling point.

As the industry leader, the dating coach trends have followed the exclusive innovations and standards of Real Social Dynamics. A great deal of the terminology, theory, and even teachers that can be found in the industry today can be traced back to Real Social Dynamics and a few exclusive individuals that it has been tied to. Real Social Dynamics has also been the go-to company for a number of high profile and celebrity clientele, for its privacy and long-held position of leadership in the industry.

MOST SKILLED, SUCCESSFUL, CHARISMATIC INSTRUCTORS
The Real Social Dynamics crew is a small, exclusive group of only the most talented in the world.

The goal of Real Social Dynamics is to provide student’s access to guys who are extremely good with women, and to structure an opportunity for them to watch them up close and model their skills. It is one thing for an instructor to tell a student to approach and to give feedback, and another for an instructor to also have the skill himself and be available to show it off. Instructors very much walk their own talk, and use their collective knowledge of what is essentially a “mastermind group” to continually develop and streamline their own skills. They are held to the highest standards of the industry – the art of charisma, attraction, and social dynamics is the business of Real Social Dynamics.
