Karen S Musselman’s Summary

My General Transcription Business:
Northern Berks Transcription is my general transcription company based in Mohrsville, PA. I own and operate the company as a sole proprietor out of my home office.

I've been in the administrative field for over 20 years, beginning as a secretary and eventually learning transcription in college and on the job.

I'm currently operating this business as a part time venture while purusing my writing career more fully.

My Writing Businesses:
In January of this year I decided to take my writing more seriously and launched two information websites as an Infopreneur, which is information publishing on the web; building websites that are content and information rich and earning a revenue with those websites.

My first information website is Low Budget Fairy Tale Weddings, a site I've developed from my knowledge of planning weddings on a low budget.

My second information site, The General Transcription Business Guide, is a site I've developed to help others get into the business and find work in home-based general transcription.

Both of my information sites kick off my new venture in finally earning a living as a writer.

My goal - retire from transcription completely and concentrate on my writing business.
