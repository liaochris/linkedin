Ouke Arts’s Summary

The future is small. Large diversified and listed companies will not be able to adapt fast enough. Entrepreneurial energy and innovative products, technologies and markets vibrantly create new networks of local competitors serving clients globally. Digital and design make the invincible combination. Digital meaning less physically bounded. Design being an inexhaustible source of competitive advantage. Competition becomes less predictable and employees demand more independence. Managerial strengths have to be combined with authentic workmanship in order for organizational leaders to remain effective. At critical growth stages, organizational leaders are in need of creative intelligence and independent strategic advice. This is where I like to work. This is my professional arena.

I am a Certified Management Consultant focused on business strategy and strategy execution. I use my analytical skills, creativity, communicative skills, knowledge and ambition to help realize my clients goals. These clients are predominantly known for their design and technology competences. It is my ambition to become one of the best strategy consultants working with design and technology companies. I can take the pressure and enjoy learning from people with other backgrounds and in multidisciplinary teams. I appreciate intelligence and skill. I am analytical, creative and objective next to decisive, clear and assertive. I specialize in working with private companies. And add value by putting theory to practice and delivering results. I am interested in strategic management, organizational design, business planning, competitive analysis, entrepreneurship and leadership.

Ouke Arts’s Specialties:

Strategy, growth, profitability and private companies.
