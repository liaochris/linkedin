Colette Turbeville’s Summary

I focus on communication, BIZ Dev, outreach, partnership building, social media, marketing and technology.

Currently I am working on a green metrics project that intersects educational institutions, the fortune 1000, and the public.

In the past::
As Marketing + PR Director for West Coast Green, oversaw coverage of national and international media outlets such as: Sunset magazine, Kron4, NBC11, HGTV and over 200 other media outlets. Collaborated on the development of cross promotional marketing material with sponsorship partners such as: PG&E, Sunset Magazine, and Michelle Kaufmann designs.

Oversaw the relationships of over 400 outreach partners. Managed partner relationships with the AIA, City of San Francisco, Google, and Build It Green.

Worked as Art Director in creating the look and feel of West Coast Green's promotional material for over 200 print and media outlets.

Before joining West Coast Green, I worked as a consultant to major educational, financial, medical and hi-tech companies. I also worked with a variety of start ups in bringing products to market.
