James Ferguson’s Summary

My career path has focused on the strategic sales development and preservation of Western Australian & Australian Grocery Products and other Products and Services within the Corporate/Small Business Sectors. Scores of highly successful product sales campaigns structured around Western Australian & Australian Historical / National Community Events were created by myself on a voluntary basis over several decades.
For almost 25 years these very unique campaigns were supported by many hundreds of Industry Colleagues, across all job responsibilities within the Western Australian Grocery Industry. Tens of thousands of consumers took advantage of these individual targeted strategies by purchasing the various products in ever increasing numbers.
Career Highlights; include; having the privilege of working with very high profile and quality Western Australia and Australian FMCG products; launch of Project Craft 24/07/99 - 15 year campaign of personal achievement; representing Australia, specifically the Australian Manufacturing Industry within "Lifetime of Achievement One Hundred," 2005, published by The International Biographical Centre, Cambridge, England. Also representing Australia within the, "500 Greatest Geniuses of the 21st Century," inaugural edition in the field of “Mental Creating,” published in 2009, by the American Biographical Institute, Raleigh, North Carolina, USA. Induction into the American Hall of Fame - 2006 , authorised by the American Biographical Institute for creativity and enterprise.

"The most practical thing I discovered during my journey towards Project-Achieved in all dimensions. Is the art of mental creation and how most individuals over time with the correct understanding of the power of the mind. Can craft creative circles, which may produce outstanding results. Leading to personal initiative and a switching on the achievement unlocked focus within their own chosen field of expertise.”

James Ferguson.
