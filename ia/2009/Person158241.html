Maarten den Braber’s Summary

My passion is connecting technology, healthcare and events. Currently I'm self-employed and organizing TheNextHealth 2009 conference to provide inspiration in innovating healthcare. This is done with the non-profit Nexthealth collaborative focused on connecting people with people in healthcare, online and offline.

Other activities of Nexthealth I'm involved in include services (workshops, consulting), innovation (writing, publishing, presenting) and events (Health2.0 unconference, Open Hospital sessions).

As freelancer with the Event Engineers we're providing event infrastructure (VoIP, internet, PaymentOverIP, power) and event management.
