Steve Collins (scollins076@gmail.com)’s Summary

Steve is a dynamic global business leader who provides C-level strategic planning and execution for animal health, pet care and biotechnology companies. His expertise includes performance improvement, P&L optimization, turnaround, restructuring and M&A due diligence.

Steve has extensive experience driving animal health/pet divisions to deliver and surpass revenue targets. In addition to his financial acumen, he is noted for his ability to establish a shared vision, build and motivate high performing teams around that vision, and leading organizations through critical transitions. He is highly adept at delivering organic growth from existing lines, while simultaneously managing the integration and expansion of acquired businesses. His listening and collaborative skills, along with in-depth cultural understanding, enable him to excel in both domestic and international markets.

Steve has a strong entrepreneurial flair, bringing creativity, passion, and competitive insight to his businesses. He is fluent in Spanish, and globally mobile.
