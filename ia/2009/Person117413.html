John Kolchak’s Summary

Originally with a background in literature, anthropology and literary history, I have been involved in the motion picture industry in one way or another for the past fifteen years. As a screenwriter and filmmaker, I have written numerous scripts, shot and directed several short films and completed a "no-budget" feature.

After a change of creative direction and a move to Los Angeles, I am in the process of getting several projects off the ground. My work concentrates on provocative subject matter dealing with both contemporary and perennial issues cloaked under the guise of myth, history and fantasy, a bit like fairy tales for adults or the Twilight Zone for the third millenium. I am currently at work on producing my screenplay "The Messiah", a highly controversial historical drama which shows, among other things, that the current situation in Iraq and the Middle East had a sensational historical antecedent which is related to the founding of the Christian church.

I am interested in talking with producers, managers and like-minded creatives, both here in the US and abroad.
