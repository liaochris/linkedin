Jennifer Kenny’s Summary

I apply Systems Thinking to business problems. Since the late 1980’s I have been involved in both international and local projects applying the discipline of Systems Thinking to solve problems for corporations. I have focused my work on Human Centered Processes, Information Systems Design, Human Collaboration – both online and offline, Quality Control, New Product Introduction and Organizational Design and Change. I have had the pleasure of meeting and working with some of the leading thinkers in the field.

My current focus is the creation and exchange of value in online communities - ‘Systems Thinking for Money’.

Jennifer Kenny’s Specialties:
