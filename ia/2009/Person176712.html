Nicholas O'Neil’s Summary

Logicworks exclusively delivers enterprise hosting and cloud computing solutions to savvy, high-growth customers in need of high-availability for their most critical applications and content. Clients can reduce capital expenses, operating overhead and multi-vendor management by relying on Logicworks to be an extension of their technical operations.
