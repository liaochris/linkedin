Douglas Brush, CFC’s Summary

Douglas A. Brush, CFC, Forensic Scientist & Examiner

Douglas has over 20 years of computer and technology experience. An adept and knowledgeable computer forensic examiner, Douglas prides himself on his thorough dedication to any situation. Verbal and written communication with your staff and a professional attitude make him valued by his clients. Douglas offers not only a clear comprehensible technical interpretation but in-depth care and understanding of your needs. He has built a reliable reputation by providing professional services to some of the world’s largest businesses.

He founded Computer House Calls in 1995, a firm built to provide end-user computer and technology support and training. In 2002, he started TechKnowlogy taking the success of Computer House Calls IT support and training to Fortune 500 firms and providing tailored IT solutions and project management. His depth of technical knowledge, professional attitude and client-centered approach to business has driven his success in providing clients with the IT solutions.

Douglas applies the knowledge and experience gained as a computer forensic examiner for the clients of The Digital Forensic Group. As an excellent communicator of technical concepts he is committed to providing unparalleled professional services to legal, law enforcement and corporate client investigations. Douglas has provided reports and given computer expert witness testimony in large Federal cases.
