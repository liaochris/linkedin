Andre Luis da Silva’s Summary

Member of Rotary International and Deliberative Body Member at ESPRO - Associação de Ensino Social Profissionalizante - 2009-2010.

Business Director at HCor - Hospital do Coracao - Sao Paulo from may, 2007 to september, 2008.

From 2005 to 2007, in charge of São Luiz Hospital’s Commercial Superintendence, managing a group of 15 direct employees; Negotiating and Dealing with Health Companies; Reporting directly to the company’s CEO; Involved in São Luiz Hospital’s Planning and Strategic Decisions.

Experience in Corporate Account Management, Major & Global Accounts, International Negotiation, Technological Outsourcing, Services, Systems Analysis, Information Technology and Microcomputing; Enterprise Management, Administration, Sales and Large Projects.

Participation in Internal Xerox Projects: Lean Six Sigma Project, trained by George Group/USA, with Green Belt Certification; Solving Consulting Project, aiming at levering the revenue with outsourcing projects.

As a Marketing III representative, responsible for presenting, negotiating and developing Xerox services outsourcing projects to the main customers in the regions of Santos and Paraiba River Valley.

As a systems analyst, responsible for giving technical support to Sales/Pre-Sales Team, and supporting and developing applications for large customers – Post-Sales.

Development of commercial applications for small and medium companies; specialization in Payroll Systems, Inventory Control, Sales and Invoicing, Condominiums and Accounting.
