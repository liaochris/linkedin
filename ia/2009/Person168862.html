Mark Richman’s Summary

Mark Richman helps some of the world's top companies find new ways to drive substantial, profitable growth. We know that great results don't just happen. That is why lasting performance improvement at our clients is how we measure our own success.

We work with our clients to: 
- Develop creative solutions to challenging strategic issues
- Achieve rapid, sustainable profit growth
- Develop robust and compelling strategies for long-term success
- Create specific growth initiatives that quickly drive the bottom-line
- Transform outline concepts into high-performing initiatives
- Establish alignment, accountability and engagement for successful change

Our clients include some of the world's top companies in their sector. They are successful organizations that are looking to drive future profit growth. We work across a wide range of sectors and have particular experience in E-Business and technology. We tend to work with CEOs, Directors of IT and Strategy Directors as our main client contact.

Mark Richman’s Specialties:

I generally target SME organizations of approximately 5 to 50 employees, or $250k to $5 million revenue. In practice, I have some clients who fly solo, and some with over 100 employees. I work with clients to maximize returns on their technology investment.
