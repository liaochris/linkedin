Zachary Leacock’s Summary

Feel free to email me at info@itsartic.com.

My love of music has taken me on a path that has allowed me to learn a variety of skills, including audio production, graphic/web design, video production, and online marketing.

I am currently an Audio Production major at Art Institute of Washington. I transferred from Howard University, where I was an Audio Production major and Graphic Design minor. I am a recording artist (rapper) myself and record myself, promote myself, and design and maintain my website, itsartic.com. As a result of my efforts, my music is often featured on many popular websites, online radio shows, and podcasts, and I am frequently booked for shows up and down the east coast. For more information on me as an artist, check out itsartic.com

I also currently record and co-produce Jabari Johnson's video interviews for HipHopGameTV on hiphopgame.com. This experience has helped me to develop a strong interest and ability in video production.

The different experiences that I have had have made me a very flexible individual, as I can flourish behind the boards at a studio, in front of Adobe Design suite, in meetings on how to effectively promote online, and on stage in front of thousands.

I would like to secure a position at any type of creative media company, whether it be audio, video, or web, as well as continue to develop my career as a recording artist.
