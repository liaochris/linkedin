Mike Maddaloni’s Summary

I am president and founder of Dunkirk Systems, LLC and have over 18 years of experience in high technology, most of it as a consultant. I have a broad business and technical expertise, and have worked in all aspects of software design and development, including as a developer, team leader and project manager. I have delivered solutions for AT&T, KPMG, GE Healthcare and many small and medium sized businesses, as well as non-profit organizations.

Mike Maddaloni’s Specialties:

I take a comprehensive, big picture approach when working with clients. I offer them Internet business strategy, Web design and development, Internet instruction, Web hosting and SSL certificates.

I consult with clients on domain name registration and manage their domain name portfolios, ensuring they continually select domain names that represent their products and services.
