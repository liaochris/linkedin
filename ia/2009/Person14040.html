Andrew Balint’s Summary

I have been lucky enough to work in an incredibly diverse set of industries all with a marketing and brand focus and increasingly exciting online expertise. I started off running my own small business while completing my Masters of Commerce degree. Then I singled out a well-regarded management development program at Qantas which fitted perfectly with my passion for travel. After four distinct roles at Qantas, I decided to through myself in the deep end and try something completely different. That took me to eBay, one of the best organisations to work for. I followed this with nearly two and half years at News Digital Media (Senior Marketing Manaer). Building up a high performance team was one of the most rewarding aspects. I am currently working at Optus as their Group Brand and Marketing Communications Manager, giving valuable insights into yet another completely different industry.

I am striving for continuous improvement in business generally and marketing specifically, so as to be the best possible business professional. Through ongoing training and challenging work environments I plan to build on my strengths as an effective communicator, manager and businessman. I aim to ensure that my work environment is highly productive, challenging and rewarding, surrounding myself with like-minded people that in turn inspire me to better myself. It is critical that this is done in an enjoyable environment that allows me not only to work hard but to enjoy work and my diverse non-work related interests and activities.

Andrew Balint’s Specialties:

I am able to combine my entrepreneurial flair, skills and experience gained while running my own business with broad and extensive marketing expertise, particularly in brand management and online marketing built up over ten years in successful, high profile organisations. My particular strengths are my interpersonal, management and communication skills as well as my creative and innovative approach to business challenges and situations.
