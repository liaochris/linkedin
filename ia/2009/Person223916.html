Steven Goble’s Summary

Marketing and Sales professional with extensive background in product management, marketing, new-product development and sales. Adept at working with multi-disciplined teams and C-level officers to achieve revenue growth and increased market share; leads personnel through consultative- and solution-selling strategies in domestic and international environments. Has a passion for developing innovative demand-generation projects, so that prospects and customers are motivated to respond via inquiries, and product managers are excited to implement.

Has developed a number of highly successful teams at ABB and Cooper Bussmann, to implement at all levels of these organizations strategic marketing and business planning, complex cutting-edge integrated promotions, communications strategies, and product development. Managing new-product development teams has allowed him to appreciate the art of successful product introductions, beginning with market research and ending with manufacturing implementation. He also recognizes the importance of customer-relationship building and account development.
