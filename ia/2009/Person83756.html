Gary Keens’s Summary

After graduating from Bradford University with a degree in Chemistry, I worked in the pharmaceutical industry for 3 years.

In 1988, aged 24, I set up ASK as a small translation agency with my £50 life savings and a credit card!

20 years later, I am really proud that ASK is a leading business to business provider with clients including the European Central Bank, Airbus, Norwich Union and Tesco.

We have 2 UK sites, 8 divisions, representation in 10 countries, 50 staff and a regional franchisee programme giving all clients a local ASK representative.

I am currently working hard to turn ASK into a worldwide brand. Check out my blog for progress! www.askgroup.co.uk/blog

A huge James Bond fan since I was knocked over by a car aged 11 on my way to see Live and Let Die, I finally realised my ambition to own an Aston Martin in 2007, nearly, making all the hard work worthwhile!

And I’m aiming for the Casino Royal DBS next year!
