James Head’s Summary

Primarily Printed Circuit Board PCB Designer with the IPC Certified Interconnection Designer CID Qualification, also involved with Electronic Circuit Design.

Working toward IEng. and CEng. status.

Experienced user of Pulsonix, Cadstar, and Mentor Graphics SFX-RE PCB Design CAD systems, and Autocad for 2D drafting.

Experienced in PCB manufacture and CAM Engineering processes, Design for Manufacture DfM, Design for Test DfT, and modern electronic assembly processes.

James Head’s Specialties:

Printed Circuit Board (PCB) Design, Design for Manufacture, Design for Test, IPC Certified Interconnection Designer (CID) Qualified, Electronic Circuit Design, Autocad 2D Drafting, Six Sigma Green Belt, Internal Auditing to ISO 9001, ATEX, IECEx and Marine Equipment Directive (MED), Labview.
