Monika Koul’s Summary

I am a freelance Architect providing design and drafting services to my clients mainly in USA for projects based in USA, Middle East and India as a work share consultant.

I am well versed with local design Practices including Wood Construction technology and have worked on various projects in Residential, Commercial and Hospitality.

I also provide other related services viz. File Conversion of Raster image files to CAD drawings and have working knowledge on Xref (External reference) files.

Through this forum, I am looking for opportunities to obtain more work and am open to Freelance / Contract projects / Part time associations with other architectural firms.
