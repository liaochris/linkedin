Erik Kallevig’s Summary

I'm a web designer specializing in usable, accessible and attractive websites. My goal is to create intuitive web interfaces that are accessible to wide audiences so all users can find and do what they want quickly.

My work is founded on the belief that good interface design empowers users to quickly and intuitively achieve objectives regardless of platform, disability or familiarity.
