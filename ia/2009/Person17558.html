Amit Parekh’s Summary

Amit is a highly technical Information Security Professional having more than 5 years of experience in Vulnerability Assessments (VA) at Application and Network levels, Report writing and Team management. He is working as Assistant Manager in KPMG has played the role of Team Lead in last two companies.

He has performed more than 50 VA and PT assignments for clients belonging to Aviation, Publishing, Banking, Insurance, Pharmaceutical and Finance sectors and reported several high-risk vulnerabilities in websites, applications and corporate networks. He has contributed in the mitigation of reported vulnerabilities by closely working with Developers, QA and Upper Management.

He is passionate about Information Security and wants to establish himself as a Security Guru, managing a team of security pros and handling diverse clients. He is highly adaptable to change and could mold himself to new roles and challenges. He has excellent written and spoken business communication skills. He has honed Project Management skills by working with Indian and UK clients.

Amit Parekh’s Specialties:

Application Security, Vulnerability Assessments, Penetration Testing, Internal Audits, Team Management,Security Training, Secure Code Reviews, Report writing,Account/Client Management and Proposals.
