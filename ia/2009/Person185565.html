Peter Flick’s Summary

Peter co-founded Local Planet in October 2008 through Red Wolf Holdings, an investment partnership that he formed in 2007 to invest in online media and information service opportunities. Prior to Red Wolf, Peter worked in Boston for Spectrum Equity Investors, a $4bn private equity firm focused on investing in online media and information service companies. Before Spectrum, Peter worked in the Leveraged Finance Group of Citigroup in New York and was an analyst at Graham Partners, a middle-market LBO firm based in suburban Philadelphia. Peter graduated from Villanova University in 2002, with a B.S. in Business Administration. While at Villanova, Peter played Division 1 lacrosse, started and ran an industrial cleaning company and co-founded the Villanova Entrepreneurial Society. Peter lives in Strafford with his wife, and two amazing kids!

Peter Flick’s Specialties:

Passionate about helping create products that make purchasing experiences better. Online media, information services and web applications. Strategic thinker, enjoys leading early stage teams and strong business development/deal partnerships skills supported by background in investment banking and private equity. background.
