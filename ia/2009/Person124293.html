Julius Solaris’s Summary

I have a passion for connecting individuals both online and offline.

I started to work in events at 16, mostly in entertainment. I kept going with my passion even during my BA in Communication and Media.

After graduating, I became marketing manager for large conferences in Italy. In 2005 I moved to Australia where I obtained an MBA in Marketing with focus on e-Business and Online Marketing.

My first job after the MBA was in corporate Marketing Communications with Natuzzi, Italian no.1 furniture manufacturer. I shifted toward consulting and online marketing at the beginning of 2007 collecting several successes with the Event Manager Blog and Linkedin.

Who do I work with?

I now work with top web 2.0 startups as well as with popular global brands to define successful strategies in Social Media.
