Dr. Jose Esteban Garcia’s Summary

Dr. Jose Esteban Garcia is at the CEO and Chairman's Office (Gabinete de Presidencia) of Telefónica S.A.

Previously to his actual work at the Chairman's Office, he was with Telefónica I+D leading the Intelligent Environment Services group devoted to research and development in future technologies for enabling new customer-oriented services.

In his previous job he was also in charge for technology transfer at Nanophotonics Technology Center in Valencia. He has also held positions at University of Hannover as lecturer assistant during his 5-year placement in Germany.

He has published over 20 scientific articles in books and proceedings, covering areas such as communications, routing protocols, positioning services and technology transfer. He has been in the technical programm committee in several workshops and conferences.

Dr. Jose Esteban Garcia is a Ph.D. (2005) in Electronic Engineering from the University of Hannover (Germany) and MSc. in Telecommunications from Universidad Politécnica de Valencia (Spain).

He holds an MBA Junior Executive from the prestigious EDEM Business School in Valencia (Spain).

He also holds the CAE (Certificate of Advance English) and the BEC Level Higher (Business English Certificate) from the University of Cambridge.

Dr. Jose Esteban Garcia’s Specialties:

PhD, MBA, CAE, BEC Higher, company management, project management, IT expert, financial, business administration, adviser, assistance to the CEO.
