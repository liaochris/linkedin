Jon Meredith [TopLinked.com]’s Summary

A former colleague suggested that I work in employment recruiting as I am good on the phone and like to meet different people/personalities. This colleague was right! This field has really worked well for me and allows me to learn about different industries, businesses, and technologies.

My strengths are in relationship building and connecting with people from all walks of life. My phone is my friend and I have a knack for reaching people on the phone; passive and active.

Our firm is a full service firm but we go beyond generic job board searches. We identify activities and contacts through networking and referrals and fulfilling the client’s and candidate’s needs.

Prior to executive recruitment I worked as a college recruiter for a multi-campus statewide University . I worked with prospective students to help them determine which career path best suited their needs. I really enjoyed working with students but found that unless I was going to become an educator there was no long-term future.
