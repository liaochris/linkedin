Gary Matthews’s Summary

In 1995, Gary Matthews left a lucrative job in corporate America to embark on a total new career in ethnic and diversity communications, as well as develop internships for students of color in the sports media relations field. As a former sports reporter Gary feels minorities need to become more involved in providing information about ethnic market. “I feel long-term success of African-Americans as well as other ethnic groups depends on our ability to build a team as well as a future that reflects togetherness as it relates to information on our community and in sports media services.”

Gary is an experienced speaker on the subject of reaching and utilizing multicultural media.(See the below links).

http://www.blackspeakers.com/profiles/gary_matthews.html
http://www.blackexperts.com/profiles/gary_matthews.html

Gary is always teaching and helping the Mass Media out with his experts. Giving of his time is what brings Gary Matthews so much joy. For example; Gary served as The 2008 Associated Black Charities SPORT GALA Sponsorships Chair. Also, Gary currently serves as Board Member and stock holder Metro Sports Center, LLC.

Gary Matthews served in the past a Board Member of Brown’s Community Outreach which is a non-profit 501 (c) organization. The mission is to provide year-around overnight emergency shelter and homeless supportive services to men, women and families with children in Baltimore City without discrimination. We have been serving the communities of Baltimore City metropolitan area since 1986. The 7,000 sq. ft. facility with a capacity of 85 emergency beds is located in the Northwest Corridor of Baltimore City. Brown’s Community Outreach is the only emergency shelter in the Baltimore City metropolitan area, which accepts intact families with children experiencing economic and housing crisis.
