Joe Hoyle, ASLA’s Summary

Joe has been a registered Landscape Architect since 1999 and has practiced in Atlanta, Georgia. He has a comprehensive background in multiple public and private projects throughout the Southeast and has established himself as a professional with strong ability to lead innovative and diverse projects. His public project work includes greenways, active and passive parks, garden design, and parks master planning. His private project experience includes preparation of community and neighborhood master plans, community entrance designs, community amenity area designs, custom estate design, construction detailing, hardscape, and landscape planting and irrigation design. Joe has developed and strengthened his practical knowledge of all aspects of landscape architecture, having completed a variety of design / build projects throughout his career.
