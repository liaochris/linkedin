Stephen Brown’s Summary

I have 14 years experience working in the Adventure Industry in a variety of outdoor activities through Ireland, the UK, Europe and USA.

Over time this has evolved into a strong appreciation of Team, Social and Personal Development.

My business goal is to make "adventure" accessible to everyone. Adventure has different meanings to everyone and can be taken at various levels, I aim to facilitate experiences that allow individuals and groups to explore "their own sense of adventure".
