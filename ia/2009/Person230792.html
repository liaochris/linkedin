  • President

    (Internet industry)

    January 2007 — November 2009 (2 years 11 months)

    Ability Commerce is a leading provider of online marketing solutions and software. Other products include Ability Amazon Interface that helps marketers sell more on Amazon. Ability Financials also helps direct marketers improve their accounting and business analysis functions.

  • President

    G. Neil Companies

    (Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Human Resources industry)

    May 1997 — March 2003 (5 years 11 months)

    GNeil was the largest catalog retailer of HR related Products in the country selling labor law posters, HR forms, employer compliance materials, safety compliance materials, motivational and greeting card products. As President I grew the privately held company to $60MM in sales with over 400 employees and positioned it for sale to a private equity group in October 1998. I continued to run the company until 2003 when it was sold to Taylor Corporation.

  • President

    Misco Computer Supplies NA

    (Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; Computer Hardware industry)

    November 1989 — April 1994 (4 years 6 months)

    I ran the Misco computer supply catalog business in USA and Canada, reporting to the President of Misco worldwide who was based in the UK. The company had approximately 250 employees and $50MM in sales. Misco at that time was a wholly owned subsidary of Electrocomponents PLC a publicly traded company on the London Stock exchange, one of the largest electronics distributors in the world with operations in more than 50 countries. Misco had operations in 14 countries. The Misco operation was sold in 1994 to Global Computer/Systemax (SYX)
