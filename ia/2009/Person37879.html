Carlos W. Wilson, Sr.’s Summary

In addition, I have held several positions of responsibility and leadership throughout my professional career, both military and civilian.

My goal is to join the management team of a prominent organization and assist with overall profitability by expressing my honesty, unselfish demeanor and self-discipline by doing what is right and giving to others by utilizing my education, my military leadership skills and corporate expertise.
