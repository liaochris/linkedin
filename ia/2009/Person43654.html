Chris Melissinos’s Summary

Technologist with a focus on interactive and connected experiences, specifically for the entertainment industries. While most of my work revolves around video game technologies, I am often called upon to discuss consumer technology trends and solutions such as device/network connectivity, branding and experience, DRM and usage issues. Currently, I hold the title of Chief Gaming Officer for Sun Microsystems' Game Technologies Group, a position and market focus I created for myself within Sun. My long term goals are to continue to help drive gameplay, service and device innovation through the application of cross platfrom and web based technologies.

Currently I sit on the Game Developers Conference Mobile advisory board and am a member of the Transmedial Access Consortium.
