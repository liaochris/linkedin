Charmaine Mills’s Summary

Advance Image & Etiquette Consulting offers consultation sessions that are insightful, powerful and life-changing. They illuminate the pathway for those who are interested in fully realizing their authentic self.

Whether you are a CEO, sales representative, entrepreneur or graduating student, we can help you develop a winning image that will help advance you.

A winning image represents success, knowledge and credibility. Your image is your personal resume and at work your image is your personal logo.

We will show you how to master the art of first impressions with a mastery of image management strategies and techniques.

To address the needs of our international clients, in the New Year we will be offering a series of live and highly interactive telelasses on a variety of image and etiquette topics. Private coaching by phone and online will also be available.
