Evelina Molina’s Summary

OUR MISSION is to provide training, job placement, and retention of low-income, unemployed, or underemployed persons for a career in green collar jobs, to include technologies having to do with sustainability in the environment, energy conservation, and green (non-carbon based) energy production.

YOUTH GREEN JOBS SONOMA (YGJS) is a program of NBIGT committed to promoting alternative energy education and awareness, resource conservation, and employment readiness for the underserved young people in our community.for the green collar jobs industry.

Evelina Molina’s Specialties:

WE ARE COMMITTED to lift people out of poverty by uniting with and supporting those who advocate for job training, job creation, a family living wage and enterprise activities in the emerging green economy.

OUR PRIMARY FOCUS this summer will initally be young adults and their families from the greater Santa Rosa area. However, we plan to leverage the success of our summer pilot program to garner the needed resources so that we can expand our model countywide in Sept. 2009.

Evelina Molina’s Interests:

I have over a decade of experience as a human rights activist, visionary and community organizer specializing in innovative workforce advocacy and development. If there is a saying that would best describe the sum of my "activism" it would be the words of Ernesto 'Che' Guevarra, "Let me say at the risk of seeming ridiculous, that true revolutionary is guided by a great feeling of love...We must strive every day so that this love of living humanity will be transformed into actual deeds, into acts that serve as examples, as a moving force."
