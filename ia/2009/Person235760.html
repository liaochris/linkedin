Tom Craig’s Summary

Real world supply chain practitioner and visionary. Provide supply chain management consulting and develop solutions that work. Improving operations, cost reduction, cycle time compression, inventory improvement, outsourcing, lean implementation / training and more. Solutions that work,

Also provide 4PL / 3PL service for small-medium companies.

Real world supply chain experience with major corporations such as General Electric, Commodore Business Machines, Abbott Laboratories and 3M,

I have written over 50 articles on supply chain management, outsourcing and lean (many of these are posted at the website).. In addition, I have spoken at conferences worldwide, including London, Singapore, China, Hong Kong, Panama and Africa. I conducted a master class program in supply chain management in China for Chinese companies.

Work with companies as to their supply chains, including international, to improve the performance, compress cycle time, increase inventory velocity and reduce waste and cost. Most companies struggle with the supply chain they designed and deserve.

Also work with non-US companies to assess, market, design marketing program and implement and manage operations in the US.
