Sean Webster’s Summary

I currently have 24 years experience in Law Enforcement and Investigation within the South African context. I am a Commissioner of Oaths within South African and I am an associate member of the Association of Certified Fraud Examiners. I have extensive investigative training both whilst in the employ of the South African Police Services and in the private sector. I currently own a business which is based in Durban and which renders our services on a national basis to our blue chip client base and to certain Governmental departments. This business specialises in Forensic Investigative Services, which is managed by competent, experienced professionals who make judicious use of an extensive network of experienced associates, who are all experts in their specific fields, to provide a wide spectrum of tailor-made services to our clients. Our services include and are not limited to:

Forensic Investigation, Technical Surveillance Counter Measures, Other Forensic Services, Other Specialised Services and Training & Workshops. These services are explained as follows:

Forensic Investigation (Criminal, civil, corporate and industrial investigations - Covert (under cover) investigations - Copyright and patent - Copyringement investigations - Insurance Investigations)

Technical Surveillance Counter Measures (Electronic counter surveillance - The management and deployment of under cover operatives - Mobile and static surveillance - Covert camera installation)

Other Forensic Services(Forensic Auditing - Forensic Video Analysis - Computer Forensics )

Other Specialised Services (Customer service analysis - Periodic test shopping and probing - Tracing of individuals and assets - Debt recoveries -Confidential Assessments)

Training & Workshops (The presentation of workshops & seminars - The conducting of specific in-house training sessions)

Sean Webster’s Specialties:

Investigation of Fraud / Corruption / Theft / Insurance Fraud / Counterfeiting
The conducting of surveillance (mobile & static)
The conducting of confidential assessments (Due Dilligence)
Training in the Investigation of Crime (Criminal & Civil)
Knowledge of the South African Criminal Law, Law of Procedure and many South African Statutory Acts that govern certain areas that I have utilised during my investigations
A thorough knowledge of the South African Criminal/Judicial system
