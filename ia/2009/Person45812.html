Chrystal O'Jon’s Summary

Chrystal Allen O’Jon-Bio

Chrystal Allen-O’Jon comes from a long line of family singers. Chrystal attended Pacific Palisades High School and was in a Theater Group there with the now famous actors Forest Whitaker and Roy Fegan.

Chrystal’s love for music, entertainment and advertising was developed into a premier Marketing Director position at LA Focus Newspaper, owned by Lisa Collins of Billboard & Jheryl Busby, formerly of DreamWorks Records and Motown Records. She advanced to Marketing Director for Eye on Gospel Music Magazine, also owned by Lisa Collins.

Currently, she is Co-Owner of ESeats & URE LLC where she (part time) establishes and directs most aspects of the companies Entertainment Relations activities, is responsible for building the music lifestyle brand for the Next Great Drummer Show. Additionally, she develops and implements strategic marketing and promotional partnerships with key music industry, corporate, and media partners creating brand awareness opportunities. She helps expand the Next Great Drummer shows presence in the California market by developing and implementing joint marketing campaigns with media, record labels and entertainment related partners, and at large community events.

Mrs. O’Jon is a resourceful leader adept at promoting entertainment projects. Creates integrated strategies to and build brand/product evolution and media endorsement and possesses strong and persuasive interpersonal skills.

Her education was at San Diego State University, with an emphasis in Journalism/Communications, but has since transferred to University of Phoenix – to pursue a degree in Marketing/Advertising.
