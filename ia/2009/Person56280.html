Dave Lawlor’s Summary

David Lawlor, Corporate Vice President of Strategy and Corporate Development with PCTEL, is responsible for the company's overall strategic planning and corporate development activities.

Previously, Lawlor was the Chief Financial Officer & Chief Operating Officer with PCTEL Maryland. In that role, he focused on integrating PCTEL’s acquisition of Dynamic Telecommunications, Inc., and oversaw improved growth in revenue and profitability with greater financial and operational effectiveness. He achieved all stipulated five-year goals in less than three years.

Prior to joining PCTEL Mr. Lawlor spent seven years with Hewlett-Packard & Agilent Technologies in the telecommunications sector. He held a variety of roles from Division Controller, to Senior R&D Manager, to Business Unit Manager. While with HP, he led the transfer of its microelectronics business to Penang, Malaysia, the acquisition and integration of several wireless businesses valued at $770 million and the creation and management of multiple international joint ventures.

He began his career in investment banking subsequently holding senior financial roles in commercial banking, treasury management and international controllership roles with both public and private companies. He was selected to participate in Agilent’s Advanced Development Program based on performance ranked in the top one percent of senior management.

Lawlor holds a bachelor of science degree in Finance from California State University, Hayward. He is a member of the Washington, D.C., chapter of the Financial Executives Institute. He has served recently with the American Electronics Association as their representative to the SEC and PCAOB on the impending revision to Sarbanes-Oxley Section 404. He is also a member of the Institute of Electrical & Electronics Engineers.

Dave lives with his wife and four children in Maryland.
