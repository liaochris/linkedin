Stephen Johnson’s Summary

Experience architect. Social technology evangelist. Community catalyst / strategist. Founder, Mosaic. 13 years in new media working with global brands. Professional focus toward open innovation and industries in transition, inspiring businesses of the future.

Student of life, still learning. Led by a deep conviction to raise awareness for those with limited voice. Passionate about mobilising communities to action in support of social and environmental change. Social media advisor to Thankyou Water and UNICEF New Zealand.

1999-2004 held lectureships as an industry-based educator in post-graduate and Masters Degree programs, Royal Melbourne Institute of Technology and Swinburne University.

Experienced presenter both locally and internationally. Keynote events include London Festival 'Redesign 4D Innovation Green', the Australian Marketing Institute (AMI), PR Institute of New Zealand (PRINZ), and Marketing Now.

Career highlights include Al Gore's 'Alliance for Climate Protection', member strategic council for 'Live Earth - The Campaign For A Climate In Crisis', 2007.

Professional goals include continued participation in global knowledge networks, open innovation, thought-leadership in sustainable systems for living, and social ecology.

Stephen Johnson’s Specialties:

Virtual communication. Collaborative media. Design of methodology and systems for influence mining and engagement.

Change from the inside-out. Strategies which foster advocacy and community.
