Pressley Stacks’s Summary

I have been in IT Administration for eight years with a focus in networking and troubleshooting. My first 17 years of employment were spent in healthcare as a Masters Level Exercise Physiologist at Huntington Physical Therapy. On an as-needed basis, I was responsible for maintaining the company network and user support in conjunction with patient-care duties. The IT responsibilities grew into a full-time position. I spent the last 4 years there as the IT Administrator, managing the network and practice management software environment.

I left Huntington Physical Therapy in 2005 to take a position at Hess, Stewart & Campbell, PLLC Certified Public Accountants as the Information Systems and Data Services Manager. In addition to Hess, Stewart & Campbell, I provide IT support for several area companies - including law firms, medical practices and industry.
