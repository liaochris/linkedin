Bo Sandine’s Summary

Product management professional with 15 years of marketing experience and two master's degrees, including a Kellogg MBA. Has marketed across industries, both B2C and B2B, including sports (NBA’s Miami Heat), electronics, real estate, telecommunications, restaurant and event services. Improved internal and external customer relationships (i.e. Nike, Federal Reserve Bank of Chicago, AT&T and Gatorade) while increasing market share and profits for both established and start-up businesses. Outstanding problem solver and communicator who can be counted on to lead and motivate, having directed teams of up to 850 employees. Bridged cultural and departmental divides at organizations in order to boost on-time delivery of new products and services. Launched two of alma mater's largest alumni clubs.

Bo Sandine’s Specialties:

"Bo Knows" strategic marketing, segmenting/targeting/positioning, product launching, brand building, marketing research, cause marketing, cross-functional project management, marketing communications, speaking and leadership.
