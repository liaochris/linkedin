Toni Marie’s Summary

Talent Acquisition is my Passion! In Life - you NEED to be a magnet for what you love! I have spent the last 23 years recruiting talent across the country and absolutely love what I do. This passion is emulated throughout our entire company. PARAGON is a change agent that understands the significance of attracting & engaging the right talent for our clients. We are passionate about partnering within the industry to make great connections! Once we engage you – we are here to help you succeed. Our Coaching Programs & Career Assistance will bring out the best in you!
At Paragon – I run with the Restaurant & Hospitality Division – where - YOU Are OUR Concept! The cornerstone of our methods and standards is our passion for the restaurant industry & the professionals we meet on a daily basis. No hunting off the job boards here…..we actively socialize and network within the industry to discover top talent – wherever you are!

Our aim is genuine…Partner with our clients to build their restaurants, by filling them with people who excel at and enjoy their work, and have the capacity to develop others to their highest potential. By collaborating with our clients, we are able to accurately identify, select, & present the talent that has achieved the right type of track record of results that will impact their bottom line. Our attention to culture & details is key in selecting the right talent who will execute the values and goals of our clients. The results...by partnering in this manner we are able to assist our clients to become even more profitable restaurant operating companies. We recognize that talent equals profits.
