    OpenTreatment

    (Partnership; Computer Software industry)

    July 2008 — Present (1 year 6 months)

    OpenTreatment is a web-based physicians portal that addresses important needs of patients, doctors, and hospitals. For patients, OpenTreatment empowers them to easily find and schedule doctor appointments online. For doctors, OpenTreatment reduces office-operating costs, diminishes missed appointments, and creates new revenue sources. In addition to appointments, patient’s insurance eligibility reports will be directed to doctor’s office immediately once an appointment is scheduled. Patients will be able to complete an online intake form for all doctors with information already saved onto the system thus eliminating time spent filling out forms for each doctor.
