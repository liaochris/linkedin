Nicole Mayraz Bernstein’s Summary

I have over ten (10) years experience in strategic planning, management, business development, corporate marketing, product marketing, consumer marketing, mobile marketing, project management and product management.

Former clients include: Wyndham Resorts, Disney’s SOAPnet Channel, Dell’s Alienware Systems, Chase Bank, My Network TV, Up Against the Wall (national hip hop retail chain), Macy’s, The Sharper Image, Delta.com, Cheaptickets.com, Hallmark.com, ESPN Shop, HBOFilms.com, Maverick Records, The Firm™, The Backstreet Boys, Limp Bizkit, Vibe Magazine, FilmFour.com and more.

I am originally from Texas and studied Behavioral Psychology and Literature the University of Texas at Austin. I am a member of Forbes Entrepreneur Group, Springboard Women Entrepreneurs Alumni and have lived in 17 cities across three (3) different countries and speak two (2) languages fluently (English and Hebrew). I currently reside in Tel Aviv, Israel.
