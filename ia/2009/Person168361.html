Guy Moskowitz’s Summary

Q: Hello Guy, what can you tell us about your experience?

Guy: Well, I have been in the telecommunication and Internet industry from its early stage, for more than 17 years. My focus was always on multi discipline: Strategy\Marketing\Sales\Deployment, and large scale management.

Q: From your profile I can see that you have been for many years in Amdocs, Ness and now Gilat which are international and public companies, what were your primary goals?

Guy: Typically, I was responsible for line of business, managing groups of more than 200-300 people. My main achievements were building a very sharp proposition, and to turn the business into more than $150M, industry leading offering.

Q: What did you enjoy most in those companies?

Guy: I think that the thing I enjoyed the most was to build the team and offering around the products. I strongly believe that the sales team and the offering are the most important face of the company, and it takes a lot of time, experience, knowledge and human relations to create real teams, and the organization around them.

Q: So you had a lot of experience in wireline and wireless operators (Amdocs), and in corporate/business as system integrator (Ness), what makes your current position in Gilat so interesting?

Guy: Excellent question. In Gilat, we really do good for the people. We bring communication and internet to places that without us where at the stone-age. Seeing people at villages working on the internet at places that has no electricity, and the impact that it does - this is really doing good for humanity. (...and I get to see places that otherwise I would never been able to...)

Q: last question, as EVP of sales, where do you spend most of your time these days?

Guy: Currently I am sharing my time between Latin America, US, China and Asia, were our most important customers are located. I strongly believe in having good connection and "hands on" to the customers executives.
