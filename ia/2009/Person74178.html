Eric Edmeades’s Summary

BUSINESS LIFE

I started my business life in 1988 as a salesman, sales manager and personal manager for Kirby Vacuums in Halifax and Victoria, Canada. During this time I was introduced to Zig Ziglar, Brian Tracey and then, in 1989, to Anthony Robbins.

In 1990 I joined Ryzex as the first full-time employee. Over the next several years I helped the country grow from a start-up business to a multi-national IT company with 100+ employees in three countries. In 1996, at 25, I was transferred to Europe to start Ryzex's European operations. A year later, following a disagreement over promised shares, I resigned my position and started a competitive company, the ITR Group, in Bristol, England.

Over the next 9 years I grew the company from my living room floor to a major supplier and maintainer of barcode, data capture and wireless networking equipment supplying products and professional services to leading retailers and logistics companies including Debenhams, Grattans, United Airlines, JD Sports and several others.

In 1994 I started the Success Express Journal, the first internet-based business and personal development e-zine. The magazine started as a bit of fun when I sent it out to 15 of my friends. 2 years later we had 15,000 subscribers in 70 countries.

In late 2006 I sold my majority shareholding in the ITR Group and retired from the data capture industry in order focus on my passion for writing, speaking and inspirational speaking.

I have written a number of books and a movie screenplay.

I now spend my time as an inspirational speaker, corporate consultant and seminar leader. I have created a series of Adventure Seminars(TM) that take my clients to exotic locations for the perfect balance of education and experience.

I also teach a number of seminars and Adventure Seminars around the world.
