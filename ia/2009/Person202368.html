Rod Weiss’s Summary

Executive Profile and Demonstrated Abilities:

Progressive transformation through inspiring innovation and teamwork. Achieve ambitious corporate objectives leveraging technology with close stakeholder collaboration.

Teams I've led have achieved significant goals improving the following; worldwide literacy and bridging the digital divide, international cross-cultural business performance and corporate productivity, national law enforcement training programs, air travel safety and school safety certification, teacher and parent preparation programs, and most recently development and construction of programs and services to advance affordable housing. I have enjoyed opening profitable new market segments for my past four employers.

- My emphasis is on listening, studying, researching then pursuit of sales relationships with customers, suppliers and partners. New markets were penetrated with design, development and deployment of four innovative new lines of online products and services. I am recently exploring positions in the scientific and medical community.

- A decade in International Director-Level roles with P/L responsibility, presentation ability, including coordination of producers, curriculum and content experts toward executing programs that serve marketing, sales, and support teams.

- Led IT systems, application development, and procurement for the measurement and monitoring IT components of the $500m Baggage Screener Training Program, a federal contract post 9/11.

Rod Weiss’s Specialties:

Specialties in IT, sales and recruiting, managing and coaching staff into teams aligned with corporate goals. Delivery of presentations, adept at initiating and building international relationships, partnerships, and innovating new products and services for penetration of new business. Expertise in eLearning 2.0, online marketing, online sales training, leveraging innovations in information technologies platforms, mobile and wireless communications.

Our systems trained and certified 21,000 airport screeners. Program completion concluded by the deadline and finished under budget.
