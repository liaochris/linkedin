Daniel DeYoung’s Summary

I have 12+ years of very diverse marketing and development experience, and a proven track record were I have been a catalyst in growing the companies I have worked for by more than 100% a year over the previous year.

In my opinion this is all made possible because frankly… I love what I do!

I thrive to take ownership, and I see problems as opportunities brilliantly disguised.

I live outside the proverbial box, and I’m right at home in results-oriented environments, because of my love for analytics.

Being driven to continuously learn is also one of my greatest assets. I belong to many special interest groups (FDMA, AIMA and the DRM to name a few) that help me stay abreast of current best practices and new techniques.
