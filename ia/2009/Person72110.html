Ellie Jurado-Nieves’s Summary

Ellie Nieves is a highly accomplished, award-winning executive with progressive experience developing and implementing highly effective leadership development, relationship management, employee relations and diversity strategies. Versatile background in law, politics, and business with experience contributing to high-profile national and local external relations campaigns. Expertise in public speaking, team building/leadership, and managing complex projects to drive optimum results.
