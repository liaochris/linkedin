Michael Flagg’s Summary

Project manager or assistant engineer on a variety of projects that involve radioisotope analysis and production at the University of Missouri Research Reactor.

Assist with the client services unit to give a technical perspective on business issues and aid in new business development.

Current projects include:

1. Project Manager for the Type B Package design and development to 10 CFR 71 standards

2. Project Manager for development of GMP grade radiochemical product (API) to ICH Q7A Standards and FDA Part 11 compliance

3. Technical liaison to the Los Alamos National Laboratory and the International Atomic Energy Agency on a safeguards training course for 2009

4. Research in collaboration with Advanced Medical Isotope Corporation on a radioisotope production device (co-inventor of the associated IP)

5. Variety of in-house analytical projects and custom equipment development.

Career prior to nuclear industry included six years as a computer programmer in the financial industry. Worked as a team lead for a support group that was available 24/7 for critical fixes to transaction software.
