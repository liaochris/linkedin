Susan Silver’s Summary

Strategic marketing consultant with 20+ years of experience building businesses ranging from well-established $100M+ brands at international consumer packaged goods companies to much smaller brands at regional, more entrepreneurial companies. Clients range from smaller food companies, to B2B ingredient manufacturers, to services companies to large associations. Procter & Gamble is firm's largest client. While at Kraft Foods, led 13 successful advertising campaigns, created ethnic marketing group for $3B division, and developed award-winning, multi-brand Kid’s promotions. Spearheaded alliances between Kraft and Procter & Gamble, Dole Foods, Tupperware and Upromise. While at Merisant, developed ground-breaking turnaround strategy for Equal sweetner.

Cross-functional experience includes sales at Procter & Gamble, and B2B market research for MeadWestvaco.

Susan Silver’s Specialties:

Marketing strategy expert for small to mid-sized businesses (~$3M-$150M). Brand Repositioning, Food and CPG, Kids' Marketing, Multi-cultural Marketing, CRM, Corporate Alliances, Advertising, Integrated Promotions, Turnarounds. B2B Marketing, grocery products, leading marketing teams, outsourced marketing resource, timeshare VP of Marketing (TM). Growth through new products, leading growth initiatives, bridging organizations to execution of new branding direction, touchpoint training.
