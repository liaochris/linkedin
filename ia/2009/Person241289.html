George van Brugge’s Summary

Experienced entrepreneur in the software, Software as a Service and IT Managed Services market. Founder and owner at Portland Europe, founded in 1998. Portland was nominated five times in a row ('04-'07 and in 2009) by CRN as "Best Software Distributor of the Year". Started with Kaseya in 2006 and setted up Kaseya offices and distributors in the Benelux, France, Sweden, Italy, Spain and Germany.

Particularly interested in companies that wish to expand from the US to Europe to develop and deliver new products, services and technology.

George van Brugge’s Specialties:

Business development and marketing. Succesfully introduce and market international software vendors into benelux and other european markets as well as creating and setting up sales and marketing teams and offices in and around Europe.

George van Brugge’s Experience

  • SVP Europe

    Kaseya

    (Privately Held; Computer Software industry)

    January 2007 — Present (2 years 8 months)

    Kaseya, headquartered in Lausanne, Switzerland, is changing the way IT is delivered around the globe. Kaseya is a global provider of IT automation software for IT Solution Providers and Public and Private Sector IT organizations. Kaseya’s IT Automation Framework allows IT Professionals to proactively monitor, manage and maintain distributed IT infrastructure remotely, easily and efficiently with one integrated Web based platform. Kaseya’s technology is licensed on over three million machines worldwide. As of the end of December 2008 the company has 230 employees, still growing at around 100% each year, despite the state of the global economy.

  • Founder and owner

    Portland Europe

    (Computer Software industry)

    May 1998 — Present (11 years 4 months)

    On-line software distributor for IT professionals. Portland is an exclusive Benelux distributor for a number of software suppliers. As well as handling the distribution of affordable and user-friendly quality-based solutions, we also assist IT Service organisations , resellers and customers by offering support and training. Portland is permanently committed to finding the best distribution channels for suppliers, the most attractive margins for resellers and the best, most affordable and user-friendly products for users.

    Resellers play a key role in Portland’s sales policy. They are a crucial link in the chain from the supplier up to and including the end user. We acknowledge their importance by offering them attractive margins, product and sales training. Thanks to this close cooperation, the resellers are given a reliable springboard from which to successfully market all the products in our range.
