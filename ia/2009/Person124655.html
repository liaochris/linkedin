Justine Lopez’s Summary

Marketing strategies and campaigns for small businesses, non-profit organizations, and “Solopreneurs” Our 12-Step Online Marketing Program is geared to help “business minded individuals” such as yourself stay profitable and self-sufficient in the realm of marketing, lead generation, and sales; establishing a framework and system that works for you within your specific business, industry, and target market(s). HOW WE DO IT: The 12-Step Marketing Program is structured online as an on-going organizational and communication tool, this format allows us to answer questions specific to your business, find solutions, evaluate issues, and implement systems that affect your overall business goals through a focused series of weekly ACTION steps; custom analysis; 24/7 mentoring, and discussion forums. We never take a cookie-cutter approach when building you a comprehensive marketing strategy, we design, mentor, and create turn-key marketing, sales, and lead generation programs that will keep you on track.
