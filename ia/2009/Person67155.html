Drew Cashmore’s Summary

Drew Cashmore has a background in online sales strategy, business development and account management through the implementation of a sales strategy for two global Internet companies. Coupling his passion for computers and technology with his strong communication and presentation skills, Drew has bridged the gap between marketing, product, business development and sales to provide his team and the client with collateral, information, and direction for their future buys. In his most recent role, Drew’s business analysis and custom concepts have directly contributed to a 22% YOY revenue growth for MySpace Canada and made the Internet portal a relevant advertising destination in Canada.

Drew is currently managing the launch and ongoing efforts of the A Million Tweets to Remember (#1Mtweets) Project to raise awareness for Alzheimer's disease.
