Steve Harvey’s Summary

Cleantegrity helps cleantech companies bring better products to market faster and more cost-effectively.

For our first client company, when you call their office and press 1 for sales or 2 for support, and when you send them an email or web inquiry, those all route to Cleantegrity.

We've successfully handled over 2,000 cases during the past 9 months for this client, stimulating pre-sales action and resolving post-sales situations alike. And, our case analysis enables them to make product design and manufacturing process changes which help increase sales, improve the installation experiences, and reduce the ratio of support calls per units shipped.

By partnering with Cleantegrity, they're able to save money and focus their internal resources on technology and product development. We work with the cleantech channel and customers every day, and we concentrate on making every interaction positive and productive.

Cleantegrity - helping make the world a better place
