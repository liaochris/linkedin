Arpad Gerecsey’s Summary

WunderWorks BV delivers on-location and on-line interactieve collaboration and participation solutions. Allowing a multitude of participants to work in one application at the same time is a unique solution development by WunderWorks.

As CEO of WunderWorks, and in previous positions, Arpad is at his best during growth, crises and transitions. Between idealistic commitment and a a keen sense of business, he combines a sense of pragmatism and a clear eye for opportunities with communicative skills, stamina and humor. Combining the human scale of things, and global strategy are his strenghts. Clear focus on original mission and result are his primary goals.

Called a networker, entrepreneur and analyst who has great respect for the idealism of the organizations and people he works for.

  • CEO/Owner

    WunderWorks

    (Privately Held; Computer Software industry)

    August 2006 — Present (2 years 9 months)

    WunderWorks developed the WunderWall, a multi-user-single-location group interaction system. It's best known product is WunderPoint, a PowerPoint plug-in, that enables a presenter to create interactive PowerPoint presentations in which every member of the audience can participate in the presentation by giving feedback, responding to questions etc. A small wirelesss handheld device is used by every particpant.

    Other application areas include interactive education, events&fairs, group entertainment etc. See full details at www.wunderworks.com
