Tom Brown’s Summary

Understanding and applying new technologies especially chemicals for the electronics and semiconductor industry is my specialty. In addition developing extraction and use of materials found naturally in both plant and mineral applications is an interest and passion. Alternative energy such as bio-diesel and solar is both a business and personal interest.
