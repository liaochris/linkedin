Naomi Reiter’s Summary

As Editor of min's b2b I produced a weekly executive-level newsletter offering news, profiles and analysis relevant to b2b publishing. I also contributed to Minonline.com and min Magazine, and represented the publication at industry events.

As Senior Copy Editor/Copywriter at Grow Marketing I ran the daily operations of editing documents associated with the national Pepsi/Tava Program.

As Copy Editor for Street & Smith's Sports Business Resource Guide & Fact Book I checked for accuracy all copy in the 2008 edition, developed strong media and sports industry relationships to conduct and verify research and generated indexes and formatted files for the Fact Book using InDesign.

As Staff Writer and Photographer for the Naples Sun Times, I wrote an average of eight news and profile articles per week using InDesign, including a weekly night life column. I photographed major events and personalities throughout Southwest Florida and exercised relationship management skills to develop successful networks throughout the community with which to access events and break stories. I assisted with production, page layout and photo editing using Photoshop.

I wrote and illustrated a children's book, Sandy the Stingray, which is currently available on Amazon.com. ISBN # 1-4241-8619-6

My marketing experience includes acting as the Special Events Coordinator, Marketing Associate and Assistant to the President at SmartMarketing, where I acted as support for the Company President, wrote and edited copy, networked and recruited clients at nationwide trade shows and developed individual marketing campaigns tailored to specific clients.

I also worked as a Freelance Marketing Consultant for Brenda Ruth, a John R. Wood Realtor, for whom I created and distributed a bi-monthly real estate e-newsletter, updated and maintained a Web site and conceptualized and implemented a marketing campaign proven to increase Ruth's business.
