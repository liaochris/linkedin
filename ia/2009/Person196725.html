Reynald Lemaire’s Summary

After 6 years in the Business consulting division of Andersen/Deloitte and 4 years in Media as CIO of the Belgian multimedia group IPM (La Libre Belgique, La Dernière Heure, Cinebel,...), I am now evolving in Software Services as partner of ANAIS, with a strong focus on the media industry.

In further details :

At IPM,
As CIO, I was responsible of the IT and technology related issues, for the whole group :
- IT infrastructure (data rooms, LAN, HW), IT systems and applications, telecommunications (IP-Tel, WAN, Internet, fixed and mobile communications), user workstations, ...
- daily maintenance of production systems
- IT related projects, including development of new software solutions with in particular the internet websites

During my 4 years of activity, my major achievements were
- Entire IT Inrastructure redesign and optimisation (including Telecommunications and IP-Telephony for which I was elected IT Manager of the Year by Datanews)
- Reorganisation of the IT Organisation (internal and external resources) and its management tools
- Development of a Project management culture
- Building move
- Editorial system implementation
- Internet websites development

At Andersen-Deloitte,
I was member of the CRM group, where I have been involved in
- business analysis and reengineering,
- software solutions design and implementations (package or custom solutions), from functional & technical design, to solution selection and the coordination of the implementation. Areas covered: ERP, CRM, telephony in multi-media Customer Contact Centers (CTI,IVR).
Industries : especially Insurance, Fleet Management, Air Traffic Control, and Waste; but also bank, medical funds, metallurgy, High-tech, ...

Reynald Lemaire’s Specialties:

- Program/project management
- Business analylsis and reengineering, with strong competences in the Media industry (press and internet)
- Manage any aspect of the "CIO agenda" (IT strategy, ICT infrastructure management, ICT resources mgmt int/ext, negotiation, Interaction with Senior Executives, ...)
- Major areas of experience : Editorial systems, Web CMS (high traffic sites), IP-Tel/Communication convergence), ERP, CRM, Call Centers (CTI,IVR), ...
