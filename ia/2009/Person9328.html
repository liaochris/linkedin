Alisan Atvur’s Summary

A valedictorian graduate from the University of Georgia and a former 4.00 graduate student in psychotherapy at NYU, Mr. Alisan Atvur is an award-winning writer and an experienced public speaker. He has been a guest lecturer at New York University and the University of Georgia where he discussed communications theory, Apple technology and mobile media. He has experience in technology sales with consumer and business clients, and his extensive research in behavior provides him with an empathic grasp of client and company needs. His 3+ years of experience in editing and managing internationally-circulated journals have resulted in increased subscription rates, and his communication and design consultation for international ﬁrms has effected dramatic increases in sales. He is a senior editor at iPhoneCTO.com, the authority on the iPhone in enterprise.
