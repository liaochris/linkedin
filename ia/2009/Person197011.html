Dan Rhodes’s Summary

I am a seasoned high tech professional with experience managing large teams developing embedded wireless products utilizing CDMA, GSM, WiFi, and proprietary connectivity. I also have experience developing and managing projects and teams with significant firmware and software components. I have a proven track record of improving product quality, decreasing product cost, and streamlining development processes.
