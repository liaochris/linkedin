Oliver Reichenstein’s Summary

Started as a computer kid in the early 80ies. Taught MS Office and rhetoric while I studied Philosophy in Basel, Paris, New York. Working with the Internet since 1995. Helped building up the interactive team of Interbrand Zintzmeyer&Lux. In return I got a 5 year training as a brand consultant (1999-2003). Moved to Japan in September 2003 and started from scratch. Learned Japanese, got a job, married, started my own company. My goal is to build a team that sets new standards in interaction design.
