Allan Stevens’s Summary

Allan Stevens offers a diverse, 15 year technical and business management background. As President of Metropolitan Audio Video, Inc., a Seattle-based smart home integration company, Allan designed, engineered, and sold complex integrated software and hardware systems to clients such as Lebron James, Gary Payton, Matt Hasselbeck, Jamal Crawford, Dale Chihuly, and other Fortune 500 executives. His work has been featured in magazines, on television, and in other industry publications. In 2005 Allan successfully led Metropolitan through an acquisition by Bellevue, WA based Home Technologies, Inc. where he assumed a role in Business Development prior to founding Wild, LLC. Allan has developed long-standing professional and personal relationships with many of his high profile former clients, several of which are involved with his current ventures.
