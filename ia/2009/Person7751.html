Alexandre J. Gruss’s Summary

After my studies of sinology and economics and receiving a masters degree i started working for a German pharmaceutical company which was about to create a business cooperation and production site in form of a JV in Zhuhai, southern China. Tasks included assisting with the negotiations, creating a feasibility study. I lived in Beijing and Germany temporarily.
In 1997 relocated to Shanghai. Main involvement in pharmaceutical Market Research and strategic Marketing for the region.
SMA hired me in 2001 as Market Research Manager for SEA, specializing in pharmaceutical studies.

I then Co-founded Abacus Corporation Business Consulting with 2 partners, specialization in consulting SME's from Europe. This included the whole range of MR, Due Diligence, Location analysis, HR, Restructuring, Headhunting etc.

Afterwards I was appointed GM with Dashofer Management Consulting since Aug 2004: Dealing in Printing, Publishing, Seminars and a professional Semantic/Social Network Engine on and for the the Chinese Market.
Team was 20people strong.
We offered highly-specialized 12 free newsletters for professionals. Sales of job related products for hands on daily use. Also offered In-house and open seminars for various specialization in Chinese and English.
I was responsible for managing all aspects of the business which covered product development, production, all finance, hr matters and quality assurance.

Being a Networker I speak Chinese German, English and French on a professional business level.

I carry the proven ability to manage and maintain the highest efficient work flow standards whilst also minimizing costs, being a good communicator and mediator.
