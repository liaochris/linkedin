Chris Stone’s Summary

I'm the Director of Animation for a new studio owned by Activision named SLEDGEHAMMER STUDIOS. We are currently starting development on a new unnamed project. As Animation Director I always find myself wearing many hats. Having a full team of animators whom I direct and guide on a daily basis to ensure the visual quality, game compliance, and to assure a homogeneous quality relative to the character upon which we are working. I also work closely with other development departments to tailor all animations produced to the proper design specs and engineering needs. Furthermore I plan and supervise all the Mocap shoots, directing the actors/stunt people shot in our Motion Capture sessions, and even occasionally suit up myself in a pinch to get just the exact motion I need. Additionally I do all I can to find time to do as much animation as I can on all projects I work on. I don't think one can direct without leading by example. I the future my goals include taking a larger studio role, helping define what our next, NEXT gen games will look like and play like. I want to do what I can to push the boundaries of animation in our games and try to blur the line even more between what is real, and what is in game. Games have come a long way in the 14+ years I have been in the industry, but I think the next few years, with what is now possible on our consoles, will be more exciting than all the previous years combined. I look forward to the future and helping lead Activision to produce the most fun, best looking games we can possibly make.
