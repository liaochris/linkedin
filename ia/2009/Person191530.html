Nicholas Ferguson’s Summary

Racer Cartage & Logistics can be your next door to door courier. Why pay that high fee to ship a package that might take a few days to get there, when you can call and count on Racer Cartage. When you call Racer Cartage & Logistics, you know your package will get to the right recipient, it will be on time, and it will be in a safe set of hands.

All of our employees will show up at your door professionally dressed and ready to handle your every need. Our employees go through a rigorous background check and all of our drivers have a clean criminal background. If you need to, just ask, all of our drivers carry a Photo ID and proper insurance forms in their vehicle at all times.

We specialize your deliveries based on the size of the packages to ensure maximum savings to our customers. Just have a small package? We will send a fuel efficient vehicle to cut down on mileage costs. Need a straight truck or cargo van? We also offer delivery or pickup in larger vehicles that will easily bring your merchandise to you in one trip.

We offer same day deliveries and an accurate pickup time.
