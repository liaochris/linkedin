Kevin Carr’s Summary

I am a Television Production Specialist who works in both broadcasting and as an Internet Content Creator.
Having done television production at the network level, in the Los Angeles television market for over twenty-five years, I am currently the Creative Director, Producer, Shooter and Editor for "Green T with Tamara" with his partner, Tamara Henry, "Green T".

I have been working as a freelance Studio Camera Operator and Technical Director for NBC's "Access Hollywood" for the past five years and I am a member of the Motion Picture Editors Guild. On the Internet, I won first place and the video grand prize for my work as the Director, Camera Operator and Editor of the music video, "I'm Too Old for American Idol" , on a site called OurStage.com. in June 2008. I am an Emmy Award Winner (Technical Director 1999) and have worked as a Technical Director and Camera Operator in both news and entertainment production for ABC, NBC and FOX, as well as others. I also worked as a Writer/Director for Rockwell International's, Rocketdyne Division, creating marketing and training videos for the aerospace industry.
