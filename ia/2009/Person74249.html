Eric Burke’s Summary

I am the VP of Marketing at SolutionsIQ where I work closely with Sales and Professional Services to position SolutionsIQ for continued growth in Agile development and consulting.

Prior to joining SolutionsIQ I spent seven years with Pacific Edge Software (acquired by Serena Software) where I led the development of an innovative, maturity-based approach to implementing project portfolio management (PPM) that helped distinguish Pacific Edge as a leader in the IT PPM software market. Prior to Pacific Edge, I worked as an IT management consultant with Deloitte Consulting and before that I was a software engineer with Northrop Grumman.

My IT management experience spans more than fifteen years including work in IT governance, portfolio management, strategic planning, systems integration, and application development. I have a Bachelor of Science degree in Engineering from Harvey Mudd College and an MBA from the University of Washington.
