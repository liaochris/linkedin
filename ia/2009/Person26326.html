Barry Benjamin’s Summary

I have been a professional photographer for over 30 years starting as a in-home family portrait photographer, then moving into commercial and product phtography on location and in studio.
I also have published and contributed to several books including the award winning 'Isles of Eden' by Harvey Lloyd.
Most recently I have developed web sites for several hotels and environmental companies. I have also been the sales and marketing manager for a family owned small chain of hotels in The Bahamas. During that time I took the hotels from being pseudo environmentally aware to having a EMS that produces significant savings on a yearly basis for management and better working conditions for the staff.
Recently, I have been given the opportunity to help people go 'green' by hosting and producing a cable TV show called Sustainable Life.
I am looking to expand into Environmental management systems for all types of companies, saving them money and helping to provide a cleaner environment for my children and thier peers.

Barry Benjamin’s Specialties:

Negotiation, translations between industries, visionary, Out of the box thinker, photographer, talker, marketing and sales trainer.
