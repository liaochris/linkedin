Michelle Ransom’s Summary

Michelle Ransom leads projects to improve operational quality, while reducing cost. She has 18 years experience in healthcare, financial services and government in the US (Kaiser Permanente, Pricewaterhouse Coopers), Canada (Cancer Care Ontario) and the UK (Foreign Affairs). She identifies and collaboratively resolves critical issues, setting vision and focus.

Her academic experience – Bachelor of Commerce, Masters of Organizational Psychology – integrates the operational and human aspects of organizations. She has volunteer and board member experience with United Way-Madison, Fort York Food Bank & Community Agency, Voluntary Services Overseas, and the Canadian National Institute for the Blind.
