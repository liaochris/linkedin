Product Marketing Manager

Accomplished marketing professional with MBA and technical degree with diverse experience in product marketing and marketing communications. I have worked closely with engineering groups to implement specified products, marketing communication groups to promote the products and with sales people to insure their access to needed tools and training. Accustomed to working across national borders.

Kenneth Pomper’s Specialties:

My experience includes the complete product life cycle: addressing customer needs, formulating product requirements, producing promotional campaigns and managing relationships. As an MBA graduate, I know how to financially analyze and deliver profitable products. As a former engineer, I have an understanding of the development process.

I have experience marketing products to technology workers, mainstream retail customers, and blue collar workers.
