Pam Kennebrew’s Summary

Mission Statement
To provide Information, Empowerment and Self-Mastery for Women in, approaching and beyond the Fabulous Fifties enabling them to live better lives and to contribute to their communities and the world. ™!!

Vision
Fit For F.I.F.T.Y envisions mature women who are life long learners, who are fit physically, financially and emotionally and who are sharing their life experiences with others to improve their communities thus building a better world.

Overview
While others approach 50 with trepidation and regret, Pamela decided to turn a ‘mid-life crisis’ into a transition that would change the rest of her life. “I decided to leverage all the experiences of the past 50 years, both good in bad, and use them as a foundation for the next 50 years” says Kennebrew.

Believing that we are Triune Beings, that is we are Spirit, we live in a Body and we possess a Soul, she believes that in order to be truly successful all these areas must be in alignment. In the stages of life prior to age 50, we have a tendency to focus on one particular area more than the other. The maturing process is about gaining a balance in those areas. Fifty, or the beginning of ‘mid-life’ is the time to bring all those things together, not just to regret the past, but to see how you can benefit others. When we look at ourselves we can get caught up in what might have been, but when we turn outwardly and get the focus off ourselves we see those years as valuable assets that can be converted to cash or whatever else we may need.
