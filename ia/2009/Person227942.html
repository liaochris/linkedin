Sue Wilson, RN, MBA/HA’s Summary

In response to the increasing cost of healthcare insurance combined with decreasing benefits, individuals and business owners will need to combine a higher deductible medical plan with an income protection policy that pays cash directly to the policyholder.

These policies help offset out of pocket medical expenses, but MORE IMPORTANTLY, help pay expenses like rent/mortgage, car and other debt payments, and replace lost income.

As an independent insurance agent and business owner, I specialize in Aflac benefits and services, and consult with a network of brokers who specialize in medical benefits.
