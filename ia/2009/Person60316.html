Debbie Russ’s Summary

Design Director with extensive strategic design management and project leadership experience with expertise in consumer goods branding, global package development and visual communications. Proven record of increasing sales, reducing development costs, implementing increased efficiencies and minimizing design iterations. Significantly increased profit margins, created strategic management systems and executed strategies for management of evolving technologies. Fostered successful client relationships by strong communication between clients and creative team. Able to thrive in fast-paced, high-volume dynamic environments handling multiple simultaneous projects from initiation to completion. Skilled in training, leading, supervising and motivating multi-level global and domestic staffs. Problem-solver who’s passionate about the design process and branding; self-directed and results-oriented professional who understands both consumer and retail distribution channels with proven short and long-term planning successes.
