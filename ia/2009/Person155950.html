Matthew Lloyd’s Summary

Matthew M. Lloyd
Matthew is a proven public relations and marketing communications leader with over seventeen years of experience delivering results driven strategic campaigns for Fortune 500 and emerging companies in the US, UK and Europe. As the president and the principal consultant at M2L2, Matthew plays an integral and hands-on role in developing effective and result driven programs that involve corporate positioning, branding, media relations, product launches and issues leadership for his clients. He has led traditional, digital and social media public relations efforts for a variety of leading technology companies including both emerging and established brands.
