Jeris JC Miller @dakini_3’s Summary

Jeris JC Miller's East-Meets-West synthesis perspective emerged during the 1980’s while working with Jerome Kagan, a developmental psychology pioneer and Principal Investigator of the Harvard Infant Study who also acted as Director of the New England Node of the John D. and Catherine T. MacArthur Foundation Network on Human Development during her tenure.

She continued working in cutting edge research and technology environments including Bolt, Beranek and Newman (BBN Technologies; BBN Communications Group) which installed the first network node between UCLA , UC Santa Cruz and the University of Utah (DARPA, ARPANET project); with Dr. Lynn Margulis, Co-Author of the Gaia Hypothesis (in conjunction with James E. Lovelock), and acting as a Program Manager for Rapid Deployment Web Development projects at Intel, and as a Business Analyst for Microsoft before shifting her focus East.

She currently acts as Executive Producer for Social Media Applications and Programs and developed and administrates the Facebook and Twitter @SeedsCAN microblog sites for the Compassionate Action Network (CAN). She also serves as the Seeds of Compassion Ambassador for Seattle Children’s Hospital, who co-sponsored the Seeds of Compassion Conference which hosted His Holiness the XIVth Dalai Lama and His Holiness Arch Bishop Desmond TuTu to Seattle, Washington, April 11-15, 2008.

Ms. Miller is a Yoga Alliance registered Yoga Teacher, is certified as Cancer Exercise Specialist and volunteers by teaching Shamatha (Calm Abiding) Seven-Point Mind Training at Sakya Monastery and has also done youth outreach teaching yoga at the Vera Project.
