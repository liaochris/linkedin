stephen graham’s Summary

Mr. Graham has experience in criminal defense on case ranging from misdemeanors such as DUI /DWI to such felonies as homicide cases. He is licensed to practice in the local State and Federal Courts and is licensed in the Colville Tribal Courts. If you are in need a Stevens, Okanogan or Ferry County attorney, please consider my office. Steve Graham has been doing jury trials since 1994, and is a former elected prosecuting attorney. Mr. Graham also has experience representing municipal entities and has considerable experience in the field of land use, environmental regulation, and governmental regulation.
