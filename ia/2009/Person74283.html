Eric Greenspan : : Technology CEO’s Summary

Technology guy, 3 startups, currently CEO of Make It Work, Inc., the technology leader of residential computer services, board member of the Technology Council of Southern California and the Teddy Bear Cancer Foundation; guest speaker at numerous universities including UCSB, SBCC, UCLA Anderson School of Business; USC Marshall School of Business and USC Center for Telecom Management; guest speaker at numerous other functions, businesses and events including: RIM/Blackberry, Anacapa School, the Center for Technology Convergence; formerly a member of the Citrix iBusiness Board of Governors; current member of the Santa Barbara Chamber of Commerce Business Leaders Council.

My goal is to lead a balanced life of family and work, achieving success in each. With family, I wish to be involved in daily activities of my wife and children. In business, I simply wish to create one of the greatest service companies in the world measured by delighted customers.
