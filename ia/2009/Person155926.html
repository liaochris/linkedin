Matt Klempner’s Summary

I have a great deal of supervisory and leadership experience. I was co-editor-in-chief of my award winning high school newspaper. I worked as a writer and editor at two different college news papers, served on the executive board of the Student Union Board in two different capacities. I was films coordinator for the Student Union Board where I was responsible for all aspects of running a campus film program including training and supervising volunteers, and negotiating with film distributors. As Technical Services Coordinator, I was responsible for the operation of professional sound and lighting equipment, and negotiating contacts for the rental of the equipment and services by other student organizations.I lived and worked in Japan for 3 years where I was responsible for teaching over 500 students English and Western culture. I was also responsible for recruiting, training, supporting and managing volunteers for the Barack Obama campaign in Ohio as well.

I am a very hard working, well-rounded person with a passion for making a positive difference in the world. I work equally well unsupervised or as part of a team. I am currently seeking a fulfilling and meaningful position where I can bring many of my diverse skills and experiences to assist others.
