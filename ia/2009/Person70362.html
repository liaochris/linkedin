Emre Ersahin’s Summary

Almost 18 years of progressive marketing career starting at an int’l ad agency & reinforced at a multinational FMCG company. Always pushing the envelope in marketing technology, also led the 1st local Internet advertising company. Self-motivated, benevolent entrepreneur co‐founded the 1st youth marketing agency in the Eurasia Region. Recently joined Turkcell for managing content services in the marketing department.

An expert on adding-value, keen to pursue innovative international marketing practice building on brand management, marketing communications, web 2.0 and GSM knowledge. Now better equipped to tackle the future with recently developed skills in sociometry, influencer networks and launching web mash‐ups.
