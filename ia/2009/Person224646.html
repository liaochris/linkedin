Steven MacDonald’s Summary

Mr. MacDonald is the founder of StudioHDV, a brand strategy and social media consulting practice that promises to help companies stop wasting their marketing dollars. We help companies that work hard at their marketing, to having their marketing work hard for them. This is done through the combination of brand strategy, what Harvard Business Review calls every company’s greatest strategic resource, and social media which has changed the entire dynamic for how business can effectively reach customers locally and globally on a budget.

Over a 23+ year career, Mr. MacDonald has served as a founding partner in an advertising agency, VP, Director of Strategic Planning for Mazda Motors of America, EVP of a regional marketing firm and CEO of a dot.com. He has recently been appointed to the Thought Leadership Board of the Marketing Executives Network Group (MENG) to create a series of special reports to be shared with the national business press. He has spoken at organizations such as US Airways, been published in business journals, and worked with some of the smartest marketing minds at Nike, Absolut, P&G, VISA and Miller Brewing.

Brand Strategy - If there were one significant competency that should be called out, it is the classic training in brand strategy development that he received from the former head of the Coalition for Brand Equity and CMO of McDonalds. Mr. MacDonald believes in the same premise as Business Week magazine, that marketing without a brand strategy is a waste of money.

Social Media – Great brands have no meaning unless they can be infused into marketing campaigns on a tactical level. And for the same reason that Inc. magazine said the fastest growing private companies are using social media, is why it is a must have tool for today’s marketers. Mr. MacDonald has spoken on social media panels, been Director of Client Service at a top 10 interactive firm and has developed Social Media Services that can be accessed by companies of any size.
