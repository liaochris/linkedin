Samuel Mazin’s Summary

I am a postdoctoral fellow in the Radiological Sciences Lab at Stanford University. My PhD focus was on the design of a new CT system. I have thought of a better way to perform radiation therapy for cancer treatment, and have recently taken a fantastic entrepreneurship program at the Stanford Graduate School of Business which has enabled me to pursue this new venture.

Samuel Mazin’s Specialties:

medical imaging (CT, PET, MRI), medical device design, Monte Carlo simulation, reconstruction algorithms
