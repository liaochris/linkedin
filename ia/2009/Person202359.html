Rod Solar’s Summary

Rod Solar has been helping his clients sell and serve better more for over a decade.

He is a managing director of LiveseySolar Practice Builders, a company he founded with Laura Livesey in 1997. Rod is passionate about learning & teaching the secrets of influence & interpersonal dynamics to his clients and their staff.

Based in London, England, Rod works as a management consultant and trainer focusing on sales and customer experience.

His clients include private medical practices, clinics, and hospitals specialising in elective health care (i.e. laser eye surgery, plastic surgery, dental surgery, chiropractic, etc...) across the UK, EMEA, and the US.

Rod has the ability to inspire and motivate his clients and their teams, help them get more from their resources, enable them to overcome seemingly insurmountable challenges, and coach them to higher and higher levels of personal and professional success.

Rod has been a professional business to consumer and business to business salesperson, a business consultant, a college lecturer, an industry leader, a published writer and an executive coach.

Rod is Canadian by nationality, Chilean by birth, and is currently based out of the London, UK. He grew up in Vancouver, Canada, where he earned a degree in Psychology from the University of British Columbia.

After completing his degree, he pursued a further 3 years of study in Leisure & Sport Management, Instruction & Coaching.

Rod has spent most of his leisure time traveling to over 20 countries over the last 10 years. He enjoys snowboarding (a healthy addiction after spending a year living in Whistler, BC), drawing, and roller-hockey.

His professional and personal motto is: You can have everything you want in life if you help enough people get what they want.

Rod Solar’s Specialties:

sales, customer experience, customer service, partnership development, marketing, training and development, coaching
