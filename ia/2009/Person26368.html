Ben Wheeler’s Summary

Nearly 20 years delivery experience in recruitment marketing and HR resourcing roles.

Worked with (and for) major commercial and public sector organisations where projects have included candidate attraction and resourcing strategy, employer branding, career sites, assessment and psychometric testing, workforce planning, graduate recruitment and organisational research.

CIPD qualified and BPS level A&B trained, MA in Human Resource Management and Diploma of the Chartered Institute of Marketing.

5 years in business development (1997-2002) with extensive success in building productive and enjoyable HR relationships and generating revenue.

Currently working on a freelance associate basis with Bristol based consultants AcademyHR on a mixture of business development, HR event management and assessment work.

Also interested in additional freelance and/or interim work opportunities.

Ben Wheeler’s Specialties:

Resourcing strategy, assessment and psychometric testing, workforce planning, online recruiting, career sites, career coaching, employer branding, organisational research, staff surveys, sales and marketing, presentations, tenders, event marketing, revenue generation, business development, the south west business region.
