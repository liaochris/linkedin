Steve Fors’s Summary

At this particular point in my career, I’m interested in applying my usability, interaction design, and healthcare domain expertise in a fast-paced, team-centric environment that shares the common goal of producing best in class software and web applications. The specific skills I’m able to bring to such an organization include:

12 years of enterprise web and application design experience, with emphasis on sophisticated, visualization-rich data display;

Expertise in conducting on-site behavioral research and workflow analysis, distilling complex data into manageable use cases and system requirements;

The proven ability to bring new technology paradigms to hardware and software, as evidenced by 13 healthcare patents and over 50 applications in the queue; and

A history of developing and teaching healthcare usability software engineering processes and practices to development, systems, and quality assurance engineers in a global engineering organization.
