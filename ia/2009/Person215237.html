- Streamlining Client Servicing support during initial days of operations;
- Employing win-win collaborative approach with clients, suppliers and peers alike, developing confidence and dependence on the new team; and
- Motivating and retaining new recruits, orienting them into the S.E.Asian culture of the company

Currently aiming for new leadership role that offers challenging opportunities to develop & implement growth strategies for the brand / business.
