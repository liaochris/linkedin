Brian Rideaux’s Summary

Brian Rideaux is a results-driven Telecommunications and Information Technology professional with proven ability to successfully direct information technology, telecommunications, and world wide support services. His extensive experience in project and program management consists of provisioning, design, development and implementation of voice, enterprise mobility (wireless), satellite communications, and data network solutions. Brian has also managed large telecommunications projects which consisted of planning, requirements, analysis, design, engineering, and implementation of new and existing communications infrastructures in the United States, Singapore, United Arab Emirates, Mexico and Brazil. His current and previous experience includes a service line operational role providing infrastructure, application management, and Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) /Customer Care services which allows him to gain detailed understanding of the various service level agreements and the Key Performance Indicators required for managing agreements and the relationship with vendors effectively.
