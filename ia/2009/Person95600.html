Tony Williams’s Summary

I have experience as a software developer under Unix and Mac systems, managing an IT department, writing, editing and consulting on systems for small and medium business. I have a great deal of experience developing in FileMaker Pro.

I completed a 3 year course in Psychodrama, Sociodrama and Role Training that has added to my interpersonal skills in both group and individual situations.

I have 2 major strengths that set me apart from others. Over many years experience with many systems and languages I have demonstrated an ability to quickly learn and adapt my skills to new areas and technologies. My other major strength is my ability to communicate, both verbally and with the written word. My technical writing while I was at Australian Macworld was praised for its clarity while imparting a strong technical coverage. I have lectured on database systems and network design and taught classes in basic computer literacy. My verbal communication and personal skills have also allowed me to be an effective mentor to other staff.

I'm looking for opportunities to write for online and offline media. I am also deeply involved in writing about Macintosh technology. I am currently working on a book of my own, a book chapter for another author and have already had several articles published online.

I have also had many book reviews published on Slashdot. You can see them on my review site (http://books.honestpuck.com).

I'm also looking for opportunities to gain further experience in developing using PHP or (preferably) Perl. I will work on open source projects and for volunteer organizations. I have been known to write code for total strangers just for the heck of it so drop me a line if you'd like some code cut for something interesting.

To get a better idea of what I'm currently thinking about visit me at http://honestpuck.com/wordpress/
