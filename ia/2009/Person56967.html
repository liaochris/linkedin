David Bulkin’s Summary

IT Executive with 20+ years experience in architecture, business process re-engineering, development, program/project management, IT alignment, infrastructure, integration, support and strategic planning.

Worked with companies of all sizes, from startup to the Fortune 50.

Have effectively managed large, cross functional, cross geography teams.

Experienced practitioner of modern approaches including Continuous Flow, Critical Chain, Scrum, Web 2.0, XP and Lean.

Strength is combining skills in architecture, process and people to collaborate with stakeholders across business and technology to develop unified visions, lean processes, and simple solutions that drive cost savings and revenue growth.
