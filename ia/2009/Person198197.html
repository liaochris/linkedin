Rich Dolce’s Summary

Rich Dolce is a senior technology executive with twenty four years of strong technology management and strategic planning experience including systems analysis, design, development and implementation of mission critical national and global applications, specializing in Strategic Technology Planning, Business Systems design & deployment, Information Reporting and Decision Support applications.

Rich earned a BS in Information and Decision Science and an MBA in Management Information Science/Finance from Fordham University.
