Andy Kantor’s Summary

NorthStar Partners (www.northstarpartnersinc.com) helps companies accelerate topline growth in three ways:

(1) Innovation (developing and commercializing new products and services -- including a full range of services from strategic road-mapping to idea generation, project incubation, technology completion, business plan modeling and product launch).

(2) Business Model Optimization (identifying new targets; strengthening the competitive value proposition; expanding channels; extending brands into new categories).

(3) Business Representation (developing and executing growth strategies for early-stage ventures; finding commercial partners for emerging technologies).

Our clients include global, consumer-focused businesses, as well as early-stage businesses and B2B companies.

My previous experience includes the following:

Strategic marketing professional with 15+ years of increasingly responsible experience gained with L&F Products, Colgate-Palmolive and Playtex Products.

Directed the marketing efforts and maintained P&L responsibility for some of the country’s best-known brands, including Lysol, Irish Spring, Softsoap, Wet Ones, Baby Magic, Mr. Bubble and Playtex.

· Expertise in marketing strategy, branding, positioning, segmentation and targeting, innovation and new product development, consumer insights and market research, marketing plan development, advertising and media, consumer promotion, packaging design, channel development, pricing and trade promotion.

· Broad consumer products marketing experience, including skin care, feminine care, household products and infant products categories.

· Successful track record of growing sales and profits, increasing market share, strengthening brand equity and developing new products.

· MBA from Kellogg School of Mgmt. (Northwestern), JD from Georgetown and BS from Cornell.
