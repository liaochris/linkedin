Michael Mäder | Direct HR China’s Summary

Born in China, German by origin and with combined work and study experience in 6 countries spread over 2 continents, Michael is a true global citizen dedicated to cross-cultural understanding. After gaining a solid working record in the machine building, automotive, and consulting industry Michael has co-founded the Direct HR Network, which is specialized in recruiting senior professionals with a technical background in China, and the renowned international recruitment site iTraineeship.com.

In China Michael has advised Western firms on their recruitment in China and successfully filled senior professional and management positions (e.g. Chief Engineer, Head R&D, Sales Engineer, Senior Software Engineer, Quality Engineer, Senior Sales Manager, Branch Manager, Sourcing & Operations Manager, Chief Representative).

He holds a B.Sc. in International Business from Maastricht University and graduated Cum Laude with a M. Sc. CEMS in International Management from Rotterdam School of Management. You will find Michael Maeder constantly concerned about the perfect talent-opportunity fit and the integration of global best practices into Direct HR’s services.
