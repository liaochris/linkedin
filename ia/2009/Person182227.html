Patrick Wagman’s Summary

Currently, I'm involved in establishing a Program Management Office and improving and streamlining the Project Management process for large scale efforts within Enterprise Information Technology Services (EITS). EITS is the central IT department at the University of Georgia. As part of my responsibilities, I'm the functional expert and system administrator for CA Clarity r8.1. EITS utilizes Clarity as the organization’s Portfolio and Project Management tool.

I also teach Computer and Information Systems online for Park University.

My overall background is a mix of technical, operations, training and evaluation. I started in the Air Force as a computer manager on UNIX and Windows systems. After completing enough education, I attended Officer Training School and moved into the Space and Missile operations arena.
