Loren Evers’s Summary

I have extensive experience in manufacturing systems, with an emphasis on biomedical manufacturing and micro molding.

My specialties are training and molding technology involving silicone rubber and thermoplastic materials. My current focus is on micro molding technologies using silicone rubber in biomedical applications.

My Masters Degree in Manufacturing Systems makes me uniquely qualified to work on all aspects of product lifecycle.

I am also an Education Professional and have completed my coursework to become a certified PostSecondary Teacher.

I have many years of training experience, and I am available for lectures and adjunct faculty positions. I am qualified to teach in areas of technology and engineering. My specialties are Online Collaboration and Problem Solving as well as Micro Molding and Manufacturing technologies such as 5s, Lean and Six Sigma.
