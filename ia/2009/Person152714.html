Mark Stoner’s Summary

Mark Stoner is Regional Vice President and Mortgage Planner with Milestone Mortgage Partners. Milestone is a large mortgage brokerage headquartered in the Los Angeles area.

Mark provides innovative residential and commercial real estate financing for clients in California and nationwide. His clients include home buyers, developers, small business owners and real estate investors.

More well known lenders represented include Citibank, Wells Fargo, HSBC, as well as lenders that do more creative financing or that will provide loans to people with less than perfect credit or higher loan to value investment loans.

Mark’s background includes broad experience in real estate, financial services and technology. More well-known companies he has worked for include PricewaterhouseCoopers, Accenture and France Telecom.

As a real estate investor and property owner in San Francisco, Mark is well versed with San Francisco's rent control laws and landlord/tenant issues as they affect development, residential investment properties and mixed use commercial. My team includes top real estate agents, financial planners, CPA's, contractors and other industry professionals to deliver complimentary services and expertise to home buyers, real estate investors and developers.

These added value services help clients make better-informed decisions about the right property, loan structure, and tax position and in selecting the right people to work with from Realtor, Title Company, insurance agent to contractors for any renovations.

Please contact me for information on loan programs and rates available for purchasing or refinancing residential and commercial real estate.
