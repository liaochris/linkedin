Loren Burnett’s Summary

Loren Burnett, President and CEO

Loren Burnett is a senior executive with 28 years of management experience. Prior to founding StackSafe in December, 2005, where he serves as President and CEO, Loren was the COO and CFO of Astral Brands, a consumer brand management company. During his tenure, he oversaw the growth of Astral’s revenue base in excess of 100 percent over a 15 month period. Prior to Astral, Loren served as the President, CEO, and Chairman of the Board of Celcorp, a provider of enterprise integration software and services.

Prior to his work with Celcorp, Loren served as Senior Vice President and CFO of Riptech, Inc., a leading provider of managed security services. He was instrumental in the sale of the firm to Symantec, Inc. for $145 million cash in August 2002. Shareholder profits from this transaction exceeded $100 million. Loren was co-founder, COO and CFO of FutureNext Consulting, which grew to $72 million in annualized revenue. He also served as CFO at The Hunter Group, where he led the IPO initiative. Subsequently, Renaissance Worldwide Inc. acquired Hunter, which resulted in profits to Hunter’s shareholders in excess of $150 million.

Loren has held additional senior management positions with NTS, MAXM Systems Corp, MCI and Coopers and Lybrand. He has also served on the board of directors and as Treasurer of The Make-A-Wish Foundation of the Mid-Atlantic.

Loren Burnett’s Specialties:

Corporate and financial executive leadership, venture capital financing, IPO, Board of Directors
