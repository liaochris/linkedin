Michael Makin’s Summary

As President and CEO of Printing Industries of America, I am responsible for the overall management and direction of the world's largest graphic arts trade association with more than 12,000 members worldwide.

I have spoken extensively throughout the world and am a regularly featured speaker at affiliate events and conferences. I have also written extensively in trade journals.
