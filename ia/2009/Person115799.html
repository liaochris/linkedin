Joel Oleson’s Summary

Joel is a SharePoint evangelist. At Quest Software he is guiding the strategy of the SharePoint group and is working on new designs for tools to help IT administrators.

Joel previously worked at Microsoft as a senior technical product manager for IT professionals around SharePoint. He designed the first Microsoft Intranet global deployment of SharePoint, as well as the extranet collaboration and hosted SharePoint deployments at Microsoft. Leading up through the release of SharePoint 2007, Joel helped guide analysts and various customers get the guidance, critical governance information and best practices they needed to upgrade and achieve scale.

Before his 7 years working for Microsoft, Joel was a senior web engineer at a large telecom organization where he architected multi-tiered web applications and multi-staged publishing environments. While a contractor at Microsoft he managed the development and test environments for Slate.com a pioneering political webzine.

Joel’s well-read blog, sharepointjoel.com is one of the top IT blogs and is the number one SharePoint IT blog. It is consumed daily in over 102 countries.
