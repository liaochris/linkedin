Tisha Dore’s Summary

Freelance PR Professional, Tisha Dore, has 15 years experience in integrated marketing communications. Her work spans consumer, technology, finance and non-profit. As a freelancer, she can maximize results on any budget. Tisha strongly believes in this model and the value it brings to her clients and agencies. It also gives her the freedom to dedicate time to higher-purpose projects including disaster relief and protecting our planet. Tisha's strong writing skills and press relationships have been instrumental in the successful launch of numerous companies and products. She also works closely with clients to provide strategic counsel, plan targeted campaigns, build industry leadership. Tisha has secured media coverage with major media outlets including ABC, AP, BusinessWeek, CNET, CNN, Dow Jones, Financial Times, Fortune, FOX, MSNBC, Newsweek, Network World, Red Herring, Reuters, USA Today, WSJ. She holds a B.A. in Communication from University of the Pacific.

Tisha Dore’s Specialties:

Consumer, technology, finance and non-profit. Tisha also enjoys working with start-ups and on higher-purpose volunteer projects. See bio for more!
