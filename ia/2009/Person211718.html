Scott Lustbader’s Summary

Scott Lustbader is co-founder and chief executive officer of Roving Beauty Advertising. Prior to founding the company, Scott was advertising his own inventions. First a line of computer accessories which sold all over the world, and then a line of toys which are currently selling domestically.

Before that, Scott had considerable success in the advertising industry. He started as a copywriter for Arnold Advertising in Boston, and then quickly became a creative director working for top advertising agencies in New York, such as Kirshenbaum Bond, Ammurati Puris Lintas, Bates and others. Over the years Scott created campaigns for Snapple, Intel, Hallmark, Foot Locker, McDonald's,CVS, Delta Airlines, Showtime, Budweiser, and MasterCard. Scott’s major industry awards include One Show, Art Directors Annual, Caples and Hatch.

Scott is a graduate of Northeastern University where he studied journalism and advertising. He lives with his wife and dog in Manhattan.
