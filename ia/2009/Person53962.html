Daniel Mathis’s Summary

I am currently an associate attorney with Curnutt & Hafer, L.L.P., a civil trial firm in Arlington, Texas. Curnutt & Hafer, L.L.P. is dedicated to providing the highest quality legal services available.

Prior to attending law school, I had a career in the Telecommunications Industry, working for three of the largest telecommunications companies in the world, including WorldCom, GTE, and Sprint. There, I served in a variety of roles, ranging from providing financial analysis of key business opportunities to developing and launching new products. Most notably, as a Marketing Manager for Sprint, I was responsible for managing a product line of Internet Services with a revenue stream in excess of $1 Billion, and was named a Sprint "Distinguished Contributor" for outstanding service in 1999. Now, I hope to leverage the skills I developed in the boardroom to be a zealous advocate in the courtroom.

I am admitted to practice law in all state courts of Mississippi, Tennessee, and Texas, with my primary practice located in Texas. I am also admitted to the U.S. District Court for the Northern District of Texas, both the Northern and Southern Districts of Mississippi, and the United States Fifth Circuit Court of Appeals.

Daniel Mathis’s Specialties:

My practice is dedicated 100% to civil litigation, primarily in the areas of Oil & Gas Litigation, Business/Commercial Litigation, Personal Injury, Wrongful Death, and Insurance Litigation.
