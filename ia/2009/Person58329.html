David Montgomery, MSF’s Summary

Before I became a full-time Realtor I spent 18 years in the banking industry. During that time I functioned in various areas of bank operations including charge services (credit cards) as a collector; installment loans as a collector and loan officer; bank investments and retail bank operations (branch management). I have formal training in commercial and mortgage lending through the bank's loan officer training program.

After leaving banking I had the opportunity to manage a mortgage brokerage company from 1994 through 1996 quadrupling the company's gross revenues during that time.

In the real estate sector I've experience with the premarketing, listing and sale of REO properties. Additional experience in HUD, VA, Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac REO property sales; experience with multiple banks in the listing and sale of their REO properties rounded out my foreclosure marketing expertise.

As a result of operating in this segment of the market I work with several REO managers, BPO providers, investors and private home buyers and sellers I have developed relationships with area lenders and mortgage brokers.

David Montgomery, MSF’s Specialties:

Marketing and sale of REO properties, loss mitigation services. Business startup and consultation, personal income tax preparation.
