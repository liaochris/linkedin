Drew Osterman’s Summary

Drew has successfully led large financing and M&A activities for several clients and employers. Additionally, he has a lengthy success record in building and managing Fortune 500 and Early Stage startup businesses, developing successful business strategies, new product creation and development, managing innovative design, engineering, marketing and manufacturing groups. He has also driven large increases in sales (some as high as 9 figures) and built brand awareness, through the acquisition of new customers and improved customer service levels. A true "Rain Maker" and Change Agent.

Drew has been a featured key note speaker at dozens of industry events around the world.
