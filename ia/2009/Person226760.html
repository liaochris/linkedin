Suresh Kabra’s Summary

Suresh Kabra has 20+ years experience in the field of communication and software. He did his BE from BITS, Pilani (1987), MS from Concordia University (1990) and Executive MBA in International Management from NUS-Stanford, SIngapore (2004). After working in telecom industry in senior sales and general management position, Suresh founded Samadhan Systems in August 2007.
An enterprenuer by nature, Suresh likes to associate with other enterprenues and provide with all the help and assistance to grow their venture successfully. This is also the business model offered by Samadhan Systems. Samadhan (means solution) works with early stage start-ups and helps them to leverage from a globally connected workplace to scale-up their R&D at minimal costs and thereby be more effective in competing against the established players.
Suresh's other interests include particle physics and cosmology.

Suresh Kabra’s Specialties:

Effective team building, project management, communications network architectures, web2.0, business planning, career consulting, funding of start-up businesses.
