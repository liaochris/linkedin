Greg Blomberg’s Summary

FSC Preparation and general cost and process consultant to medium and large woodworking, millwork, cabinet plants, sawmills print shops from the midwest through upstate NewYork. Combining management success with hardwood lumber resource knowledge and strict cost recognition. Initiating in depth cost accounting measures to ensure products are valued on true replacement costs for repeatability at the time of product conversion. Providing objective analysis and de-bottlenecking to enhance flow and accuracy. Smoothing flow and improving team morale is easier in a clean, safe, and lean facility.

Implementing Lean Six Sigma in the form of 5S, Kanban, and VSM to build into manufacturing facilities during this slow period. It is much simpler and will yield far greater results to improve your plant now, rather than waiting until the market has made a recovery and your plant is too busy to be bothered with doing it right!

Failing to plan in this market is planning to fail. Let me help you lead your business to become all that it can be through continuous improvement techniques. Available to consult by phone, skype, and plant visit. Guaranteed results or you don't pay.
