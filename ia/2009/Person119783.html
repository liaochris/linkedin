Joost Galjart’s Summary

The BR-ND Kitchen project

Over the last 10 months the BR-ND Kitchen is being developed. A new approach to strategy driven branding by combining multiple sources of intelligence. See it as a collaborative cooking session together with top chefs. Cooking brands in this kitchen involves all your senses.

Based on the scientific findings of the 23plusone research, a core development team consisting of Alexander Koene, Kim Cramer and myself are pushing the concept of 'strategy driven branding' in to new frontiers.

Different from conventional strategy development, in this kitchen we incorporate multiple sources of human intelligence. While vigorous rational analysis still is at the core of scenario development, here it is combined with visual, kinaesthetic and social intelligence. Convinced we are that brand creation involves all our senses. While rational analysis demands a lot of our conscious intelligence, the visual and movement elements ignite parts of our unconscious creative intelligence. And the gaming platform eliminates social phenomenon like politics, rank and motivational downers to a great extend. And fun simply is something we cannot do without. No fun no glory.

The BR-ND Brand Kitchen now is in its final stages of prototyping and a full scale physical set is expected to be ready for launch by the spring of 2009.
