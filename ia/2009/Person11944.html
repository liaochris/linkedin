António Mota’s Summary

Upon concluding the 12º grade in 1980 at age 17, I opted for immediately start a technical course of computer programming, with the duration of 16 months, that I finished as the better student, with classifications of 95% in COBOL Programming, 98% in Assembler/360 Programming and overall final classification of 95%.

I then initiated my professional career as a COBOL Programmer in icro-computers based in CP/M, still in the age pre-PC/MS-DOS, successively having worked as programmer, Analyst/Programmer, Analyst and Consultant in several languages and platforms like COBOL, BASIC, xBase/Clipper, VisualBasic and VBA, Lotus Notes / Domino, JAVA (J2SE, J2ME, J2EE), Web (HTML, DHTML, JavaScript, DOM, CSS/CSS2), etc..

In the last few years I have exercised the functions of Consultant, Analyst, Programmer, Project Manager and Team Coordinator in two distinct areas; Development and Systems Integration under the platforms Java EE and Web 2.0 Application Development in AJAX, with strong incidence in XML/XSLT, CSS/CSS2 and some advanced object-oriented JavaScript techniques (like Closures, Prototype and Classical inheritance, Private and Private Static members, DOM Manipulation and so on) and web services based on the REST architectural style.

Presently I’m looking to further my career in more business-aware areas like Solutions Architecture and Systems Architecture, covering the full cycle of software development from Business Analysis to Acceptance Tests, notably n Requirements Specification and Systems Analysis and Design, where I have both recent and past significant experience.
