Arti Shah’s Summary

Having graduated in Business, this gave me an insight to many areas of the business world. I started off my working life within advertising sales at Carlton/ITV. I then moved on with my career to a challenging role within media recruitment at Reilly People. Where I worked for almost 3 years.

My background of Advertising Sales for almost 6 years, gave me an insight as to what an ideal Advertising Sales candidate is, both in terms of personality and work ethic.

My knowledge and experience in this field moved me to develop and head the online and broadcast division at Reilly People. I started this with no recruitment knowledge, but sales experience and sheer determination to become a successful recruiter.

Through my professional yet personable attitude I guaranteed long term business and good relationships with both clients and candidates.

With me, it was not a numbers game, wasting clients time was not the way I worked, it was quality not quantity. Which guaranteed long term business and excellent retention. Much of my business came from word of mouth.

I am now in a position where I am working on a number of projects such as my make-up work www.artimakeup.com and acting work www.TinyArti.com, however I am still utilising my recruitment expertise and working on a freelance basis. Still recruiting within the media industry, which I am sure, many of my clients will be happy to hear. Still working with superb candidates who appreciate my honesty and advice. If you are in need of recruitment help within your company or are looking for a new challenge, please contact me on 07956 663 640 or email me on arti@profectusconsulting.co.uk.

Best Regards
Arti Shah
