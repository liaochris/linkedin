Michael Procino, MD, PhD’s Summary

*Always welcoming inquiries for contract/consulting opportunities in healthcare IT. Not interested in fulltime employment at this time.*

Senior executive physician with unique range of experience that brings visionary thinking, global perspectives and clinical insight together to shape strategy and drive successful execution of healthcare IT initiatives.

Results oriented situational leader who drives value creation by accelerating innovation from strategy to operational practice, charging ahead of the technology curve, and leading cross-disciplinary multi-national teams through complex projects. Recognizing that there is no "one size fits all" solution, I work with my clients to select the right systems, and implement them successfully.

As a CMIO, healthcare IT consultant and speaker, I have worked with healthcare providers to streamline their clinical, financial and technical operations, and demonstrate how to best apply technology to improve the quality of patient care. I work to build a successful working relationship between clinicians and the CIO to improve patient safety and successfully deploy transforming technologies.

Ten years of clinical practice as a medical oncologist. Three years as a CMIO of a major specialty hospital.

Solution oriented expertise includes, Clinical Decision Support, Electronic Medical Records, interim executive leadership, and engaging clinicians to adopt new technology.
