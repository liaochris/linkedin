Dr. Charles Suscheck’s Summary

Creative thinker with a proven track record of leadership, mentoring staff, and sound technical decision making.

Skilled in Agile and RUP based process, use case development and application, leading teams, and product visioning. I'm able to act as equally well developer, architect, business analyst, project manager, coach, trainer, and product owner.

I have experience mentoring and training fortune 500 companies as well as over 20 publications on software development. My industry experience ranges from telecom, military applications, manufacturing, finance, health care, marketing, casino gaming, shrink-wrapped software, banking, and sales.

I'm an OMG Certified UML 2.0 Professional, Certified SCRUM Master, Certified Scrum Pratitioner, and an IBM Certified Specialist for both Rational Unified Process and use case development with RequisitePro.

I have tool experience with CaliberRM, Rational Rose, RequisitPro, Enterprise Architect, Clear Case, Clear Quest, Eclipse Process Framework, SoDA, DOORS, and many other process tools. I'm also experienced in architecture and development using a wide variety of technology including XML, XHTML, Relational and object databases, C#, ASP.net, Delphi, Java, PHP, and mainframes.
