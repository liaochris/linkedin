Jennifer Maggiore’s Summary

"Jennifer Maggiore will invariably bring perspecitive, insight and value to your company. She gets marketing, social media and networking. She is refreshing, authentic, professional and energetic." Sherry Azzarella, former ASBA Exec, Founder of non-profit Upwardly Noble

My background includes experience with small business entrepreneurship, business start ups, and both the corporate and non-profit sectors for national organizations including Nordstrom, M & I Bank, the Valley of the Sun YMCA, various real estate organizations, the automotive aftermarket, restaurants and more. My expertise, and passion, includes Social Media marketing, ROI/tracking success of campaigns, branding, integrating web, Television, radio and print campaigns, experiential event planning and management. I grew up in South Florida and moved to the Phoenix area in 2000. After attending the University of Florida and Arizona State University, I graduated from the University of Phoenix in 2005 and hold degrees in Journalism and Marketing. We are members of the Arizona Small Business Association. My blog is featured at Alltop.com, I am regular presenter at the AZ Small Business Association and have been interviewed for FuelNet Monthly and The Phoenix Business Journal.
