Brad Olinger’s Summary

Personalized Freight Forwarding Services

I look at problem solving through the eyes of Orville & Wilbur Wright in their journey to achieve sustained and controlled flight.

They did it by systematically pin-pointing the problems they faced and solving them, one problem at a time.

What is most important in business development is how your model works, is it fast, is it efficient, will it last, does it fly.

I help my clients by establishing strong relationships, reliable freight services, transportation management systems, and least cost routing.
