Nicolai Andler’s Summary

Nicolai (37) has a particular interest in career/life and business coaching. He is highly skilled at identifying the bigger picture or system and facilitating the alignment and integration of the individual employee within the system.
Originally a management and IT consultant, Nicolai brings in the strategic view, including business sense and customer perspectives. He is familiar with the ‘loneliness at the top’ symptom of senior managers that require a competent and credible sounding board.

Since 1999, he has been involved in management training, large business transformation projects, CRM initiatives, classic management consulting and IT consulting projects.

Equipped with a Masters in Chemical Engineering, a Masters in Commerce and a French MBA, management consulting projects in various industries in South Africa and many internships in Germany, Nicolai is well positioned to be able to relate to the organisational and business challenges of the employees and managers.

Nicolai has founded the Ignite Group and runs his own coaching practice (the Life Coaching Centre) in Cape Town since 2004.
He was involved in leadership development programs and organisational coaching including executive coaching, Peer Learning Groups, personal development and Emotional Intelligence.

He is also the author of a book on management tools: http://www.consulting-handbook.com
and the creator of www.Nicanto.com - the online Consultant | Tools | Community resource.
