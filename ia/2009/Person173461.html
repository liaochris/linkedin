Deana Riddle’s Summary

I am the founder of New Vision Media with imprints that include: Community Press (traditional publishing company), Cereb Press (a self-publishing service) and Publisher Assistance (helping writers become their own publishers).

My latest venture is Writer Watchdog and the Writer Watchdog Self-Publishing Directory. Writer Watchdog provides self-publishing writers with options, tools, and advice from publishing industry professionals.

I am here to assist writers with everything from book preparation and publication to distribution and promotion.
