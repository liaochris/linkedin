Paul Fetterplace’s Summary

Ten years as a specialist retail recruiter is simply an adjunct to much that preceded it. Medical radiography, land use planner, furniture retailer and product designer are the things I did before recruitment. It serves to demonstrate that even some of us baby boomers had multiple careers before it became almost compulsory for Gen X and Gen Y.

Paul Fetterplace’s Specialties:

Now I hone my skills with senior level roles in retail across merchandise management, VM, space planning & management, merchandise planning, buying, marketing and retail operations. There's a lot to know about retail behind the scenes; many levers that need to be pulled to make the right things happen. Talk to a recruiter who understands the space you work in. Someone who can suggest a different way forward, perhaps.
