Ray Nugent’s Summary

A visionary leader with nearly two decades in high technology developing innovative products.

Most recently as the Founder and CTO at start-up Cloud-based software company.

Nearly 12 years at Cisco starting as Software Development Manager working on Data Center management systems and, finally, spearheading a major initiative to launch an IPTV platform for consumer applications.

Two years at Tandem (won President’s TOPS award) and eight at IBM (ROLM) as an individual contributor responsible for design of software products that created a competitive advantage.

Established creative onshore/off-shore teams and implemented disciplined Agile software development methodologies with meager resources and in challenging conditions, delivering customer-centric products on time for an exceptional experience, not merely what was expected.
