Jonathan Natiuk’s Summary

I believe that media, especially video, has the power to convey ideas with incredible power. Consider a feature-length movie, such as a documentary. Where else can you capture such a long stretch of someone's attention span? You're lucky to get a minute with a website or email.

Ideas may be the most powerful force in the world. However, when it comes to ideas and business or a product, they're not enough. In the whole process of taking ideas to market, I've heard others place the importance of the idea anywhere between 1 and 10%. But no one has given the idea more than 10% of the overall importance.

In my ideal world, I would love to master the process of turning ideas into products or businesses. There's too many barriers and pitfalls that prevent a great idea from going where it needs to. Worse among them are the companies who prey upon the uninformed.
