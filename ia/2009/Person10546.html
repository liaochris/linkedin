Alyssa Miller’s Summary

Having been a record label publicist for such artists as, Jamiroquai, Britney Spears, NSync, Moby, and the White Stripes, Ms. Miller has translated her entertainment background and networks into some of the most innovative and award winning campaigns at 5W. Whether executing campaigns for entrepreneurs, or small & large cap companies, Ms. Miller understands how to produce multi-tiered PR programs that resonate with the media & garner maximum ROI for clients and their goals. Her roster includes former FEMA Director Michael Brown, Phillips-Van Heusen (PVH), Urban Retail,The Frick Estate, Brainstorm Cell Therapeutics, Vertical Branding, Ladies Who Launch, and Lower Manhattan Cultural Council, etc.

Furthermore, Ms. Miller manages all the lifestyle and business PR for Elite Model Management, Spotlight Live (www.spotlightlive.com) the only interactive karaoke restaurant in the world, and Wolfgang’s Vault (www.wolfgangsvault.com), the premier online shop for legendary impresario, Bill Grahams’ rock and roll memorabilia.

Ms. Miller is recognized at the firm for her ability to break exclusive news due to her wide array of contacts in a variety of industries. She consistently secures placements in top-tier outlets such as, the Wall Street Journal, New York Times, Reuters, Inc., Crain’s, Time Magazine, CBS Sunday Morning News, and CNBC, etc. Other responsibilities include client counsel, coordinating press conferences, photo shoots, red carpet appearances, serving as the clients spokesperson, in addition to writing all press materials.

Recently she procured celebrities such as Christian Slater, Katharine McPhee, Richard Belzer, Kristen Cavalleri, to name a few, to endorse PVH's first ever cause marketing campaign for the Arrow brand to help Save Ellis Island (www.WeAreEllisIsland.org).

She has also been an integral player in coordinating such high-profile events, such as Lower Manhattan Cultural Council’s Benefit honoring Senator Hillary Clinton, among others.
