Sharon Thomas DeLay’s Summary

Credentialed and certified career coach and career transition specialist (MBA, CPRW, CPCC) with over 10 years in business as a hiring manager and leader of 2 to 250 employees. Author of Career Boot Camp, a self-directed guide through career transition. Professional speaker on career, HR and social media topics.

Works closely with individuals to help them find jobs they’ll love and love the jobs they have, focusing on practical approaches, including networking skills and interview coaching, as well as the development of a full complement of career marketing strategies.

In addition to private coaching, I work with companies to assist in outplacement services; employee development opportunities, including sales networking, career advancement; and critical career advancement skills such as professional speaking and business communication, and DISC®.

Education and credentials include: MBA, B.S. in Human Resource Management, B.S. in Education, and B.S in Communication. Certified Resume Writer (CPRW), Certified Career Coach (CPCC) and Senior Professional In Human Resources (SPHR). Certified to administer and assess DISC by Inscape Publishing.

Member of Ohio Department of Development’s Women’s Business Advisory Roundtable. Published in JobNews, EZine articles, CollegeRecruiter.com, and C-Bus Magazine, and has been interviewed for articles in the Atlanta Journal Constitution and The Cleveland Plain Dealer.

Teach technical writing at Franklin University, and HR and career courses at Hondros College.

Follow me on Twitter: career_coach

Sharon Thomas DeLay’s Specialties:

Career coaching, career and business networking, interview skills, resume writing, training development and delivery in faculty development and soft skills.
