    April 2008 — Present (1 year 6 months)

    After working at Gear4music for less than a year I was offered the position of Marketing Manager. This position has afforded me the opportunity to have a detailed insight into the music industry, allowing me to communicate directly with other professionals. I manage a medium-sized, growing department, and ensure effective organization and preparation of tasks by myself as well as by my staff. Crucial to my role is ensuring coordination and coherence with other departments within Gear4music, as well as many external bodies such as manufacturers and advertising agencies.In twelve months I have pushed Gear4music's online growth forward, with a 96% increase in the organic sales generated through Google, contributing well to the 100% growth Gear4music achieved over the period April 2008 - April 2009.

    Our overall web traffic is up 100% on the previous year as a result of developments I have made in this position. I have a drive to keep Gear4music at the cutting edge of the industry. I have handled the development of an extremely popular news channel that has been successful in delivering our brand to a wider audience.

    I have also founded a Gear4music YouTube Channel, that receives over 3,000 visits daily from worldwide YouTube users. These platforms allow for direct communication with our customers, and provide me with a unique insight into the needs and wants of the music industry consumer. As these concepts have been new to Gear4music, I have had to develop a database of documentation to allow future users, as well as my current staff, to have a thorough understanding of how social media, as well as Gear4music's marketing in general, works and can move forward. In addition I manage a monthly email to over 130,000 unique users, over twenty international magazine adverts, as well as an ever growing Google Adwords program.
