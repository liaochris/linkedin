Craig Fournier’s Summary

I am a Realtor with Century 21 Access America in Southington CT, the number one Century 21 office in Connecticut. Access America offers 13 offices in CT & RI and over 500 agents, making no property or project too big. You want your home sold, we can do it. My team and I specialize in New Construction and Residential sales. We have the ability to market our property and/or project in a way no other company can offer. Our goal is to meet or exceed any and all expectations of our clients.

Being a retired Police Detective, I come from a very diverse background and have tremendous communication and interpersonal skills. Since 1985, I have been investing in real estate and have experience with property rentals and management. So, if you or someone you know is looking to buy or sell real estate, contact me and give me and my team the opportunity to represent you in a way that no other agency can offer.
