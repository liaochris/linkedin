Madeline Paterson’s Summary

I help people to achieve greater satisfaction and success and to make positive changes to their working lives. With extensive consulting and business development experience and numerous successful projects, I've earned a strong reputation.

~ "Extremely sharp when it comes to motivating, developing and leading staff. She has real business nous and the integrity and sensitivity needed to get the best from people" (S.Bradley, VP at Elsevier)

~ "Excellent consultant, learning designer, ideas person, always reliable" (A.Smith, Business Development Director Tribal Group)

~ "Creative thinker, confident presenter, technically strong, excellent client skills (P.Craig Group Assurance Director at Logica plc)

~ "Working with Madeline gave me a lot of focus" (Director at Open University)

~ "Intuitive skilled coach with a light touch, yet serious intent" (P.Bluckert, MD of PB Coaching)

I combine a disciplined approach to business analysis with a personal approach to managing assignments.
