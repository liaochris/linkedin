Duong Nguyen’s Summary

Within the Office of Strategy & Planning in Cisco Services, a $7B+ annual revenue generating organization within Cisco, Duong is responsible for supporting the Cisco Services Executive Board PMO, Services Strategic Initiative Planning, and the Services Councils & Boards Governance. With Services PMO, recognized as a Top 5 PMO in the industry, Duong is a business liaison for three multi-million Services-level strategic initiatives. Duong is a primary contributor to the Services Strategic Planning process where she quickly ascertains business changes and incorporates these perspectives into strategic initiatives’ strategy & plans. The Services Governance Apprentice role also enhances Duong’s awareness and understanding of the future strategy and direction of Cisco Services.

Prior to Cisco Services, Duong was a Program Manager for Cisco’s Global Supply Chain Logistics where she program managed Direct Ship, a $100M Transportation & Logistics top transformational initiative, to enable global supply chain rationalization and strengthen predictable delivery with improved visibility for customers.
