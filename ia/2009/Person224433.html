Steve Jenks’s Summary

Result driven sales & marketing management professional, with 15-plus years of healthcare supply chain experience. Successful experience leading and managing teams and selling solutions to health industry decision makers. Consistently exceed goals while converting new customers to my organization's product and services portfolio, analytics and e-commerce tools. Developed and maintained provider and supplier relationships across the industry.
