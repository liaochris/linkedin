Ruben Brache’s Summary

Following a 25-year career in information technology, media and Internet marketing, Ruben Brache pioneered global theatrical online investment as founder of eBroadwayPlays.com, a community of theatre aficionados that includes commercial and nonprofit theatrical producers, performers, patrons, benefactors, theatre professionals and investors.

eBroadwayPlays is becoming the first and only FINRA-registered brokerage firm in the world that deals exclusively in theatrical investments.

He founded eBroadwayPlays.com to usher in a new age of theatrical investment and philanthropy by allowing qualified investors and theatre patrons worldwide to invest in commercial productions, and to subscribe or donate to nonprofit theatre organizations using the Internet.

His current projects include a new musical for Broadway, targeted for a Broadway opening in the 2010-2011 season, and the opening of London and Shanghai offices for eBroadwayPlays.com

He founded eBroadwayPlays.com:

1. To infuse the theatre industry with investment capital and charitable contributions from previously untapped sources by using the Internet

2. To publish information on developing theatrical productions in order to pioneer online investment and to spur charitable contributions to nonprofit theatres

3. To develop turnkey online application solutions for the theatre industry's sustained long term profitability and growth

4. To create an interactive marketing consultancy by providing both tactical and strategic interactive solutions for theatrical productions on Broadway and in London's West End

Ruben Brache’s Specialties:
