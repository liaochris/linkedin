Andrew Chang’s Summary

http://www.7hourschoolweek.com

http://www.andrewbchang.com

http://www.healthwealthtruth.com and http://www.protectioninmovementfoundation.org/

My mission is to discover, create, and distribute the proven principles, programs, and products in health, wealth, and truth.

I am an author, trainer, and entrepreneur in health and in peak performance education. I am also a Producer, Marketer, and Trainer for Protection In Movement (free people from musculoskeletal pain and dysfunction), as well as a marketing and sales consultant for businesses. I am always interested in ways to create a world where everyone lives with purpose, passion, and peace.

When the five companies are established, franchised, and licensed by 2009, I will start the national health franchise, the global health e-commerce, and the wellness centers. I am also involved with real estate, stocks, trade, travel, hotels, retirement villages, manufacturing, mass customization, Web 2.0, mobile communication, affiliate marketing, and sustainable energy.

I was an obese, troubled, and timid youth until I found martial arts, philosophy, and peak performance psychology at 12 years old. I transformed my body and went after my dream of being a professional fighter. At 18, just when my career was about to take off with national sponsorship and show requests, I went through severe injuries due to overtraining.

Realizing the need for systems that could succeed without my constant involvement, the warrior became an entrepreneur. Within two years of college, I traveled the world; lead seven clubs; worked five diverse jobs (marketing and sales, personal training, executive search research analyst, student success coach, and freelance writer); did a prototype for a health franchise; overcame severe injuries; transformed relationships; found love; and studied business in one of Asia’s top universities, as well as finance, sociology, and psychology in UCSD.
