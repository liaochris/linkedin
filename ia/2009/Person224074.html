Steve Kloiber’s Summary

Steve Kloiber is the wetland monitoring coordinator for the Minnesota DNR, where he oversees an effort to update the National Wetland Inventory. He has twenty years of experience in the water resources field with a special focus on geospatial analysis and environmental informatics. Steve has authored or co-authored more than a half dozen peer-reviewed journal articles or book chapters. He did his graduate work in environmental engineering and water resource science at the University of Minnesota and his undergraduate work at St. Olaf College in chemistry and computer science.

Steve Kloiber’s Specialties:

remote sensing, geographic information systems, environmental informatics, statistics, environmental computer simulations
