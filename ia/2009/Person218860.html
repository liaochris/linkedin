Skip Andrew’s Summary

A serial entrepreneur, I have been self-employed for 40 years, mostly consulting, coaching, and business development under the rubric of AH&A – sometimes as a solo practitioner and other times in collaboration with trusted colleague-experts and ad hoc teams.

My passions are learning, freedom, relationships, high level wellness, uniquely effective marketing strategies, and the opportunity-rich challenge of environmental sustainability and this new economy.

Our work seeks to implement these principles:

1. INTEGRITY–As in “a well built bridge has integrity because it’s safe, functional, right, and aesthetic.” Thus, everything we co-create with a client must reflect the unique core values and mission of our clients, our client’s unique place and value in the marketplace, and the unique needs and desires of our clients’ employees, customers, vendors, and environment.

2, INTEGRATED MARKETING – As in “every act of every person in every department of the client’s organization affects marketing.” Thus, we co-create marketing programs that bring the target-demographic-relevant elements of a client’s culture and productivity to prospects and customers – always with the intent of creating positive buzz and validation in social networks on and off line.

3. PROFIT – As in “we create value, satisfaction, and money at the bottom line, and on way there.” Thus, every campaign we co-create with our clients must create revenue-generating tests to bring measurable results in targeted market segments most lucrative for our clients – and take our clients a step closer to their desired bottom line results.

GUARANTEE. All of our work is always done with a simple money back guarantee: “AH&A does not keep your money until you are 100% satisfied that we have delivered as promised.”
