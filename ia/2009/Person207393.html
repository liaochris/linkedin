Sally Kuhlman’s Summary

Hi, my name is Sally Kuhlman and I am the owner of Virtual Simplicity. I live in the San Francisco Bay Area.

Here is my most recent bio:

Sally Kuhlman is entrepreneurial to the core. She started her first business in 2002, Virtual Simplicity. Sally helped her clients, other entrepreneurs, save time and money as she managed their projects remotely from her home office in the Bay Area. Her niche is in the area of social media and her business has evolved in to a well-oiled social networking support machine. She helps her clients enhance their web presence and in many cases, Sally has a hand in helping them go viral.

As Sally began working with more and more new business owners through Virtual Simplicity, she realized she had a real passion for coaching and mentoring fledgling entrepreneurs. She added a new service to her business in 2006 consulting with her clients though brainstorming sessions. Since then, Sally has helped many struggling business owners focus their energy and she holds them accountable the entire time, making it easier for them to reach their goals.

She has been introducing her clients to the world of social networking and teaching them about the importance of using it to grow their businesses. In doing so, she discovered that social networking is what she loves to do! It’s what she is best at and it’s what many business owners need to learn most.
For more information about the Social Networking services Sally provides visit: www.virtualsimplicity.net
