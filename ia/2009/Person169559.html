Michiel Trimpe’s Summary

I am a driven, creative all-round programmer who puts his skills to good use working for companies that want to unlock and share information and enable new ways of interacting.

In my free time I am busy trying to wrap my mind around various programming paradigms (currently mostly functional programming, DSL's, interpreters, monads, purity, lambda-calculus and the likes in Haskell, Clojure, Common Lisp and Erlang).

My main goal is to find a way of semantically expressing and unify all (programming) languages and information and to create a rich multi-dimensional environment for exploring and interacting with these representations.
