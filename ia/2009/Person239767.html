Umberto Torresan’s Summary

Global marketing manager with strength in business growth and new business development. Previous experience in financial media also gives me hands-on understanding of finance, business communications and economic market dynamics.

I thrive in creating new business opportunities in unusual, unexpected ways. "Go where they are not" is my market development mantra. "Make it happen" is my business growth mantra. Because I have changed career and worked in different industries, I can connect the dots that many one-industry-only veterans tend to miss out on.

I am especially passionate about projects related to all aspects of sustainability (energy from renewables, renewable raw materials, waste management, water treatment and material recycling).
