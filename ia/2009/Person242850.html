Victor Okigbo’s Summary

Articulate and expressive visionary generalist with deep skills in diverse technical fields. Okigbo has over 20 years of experience in Software Development, Sales and Marketing, Marketing Research, Sales Force Automation and Systems Management.

Okigbo started writing computer programs in his early teens, publishing code listings in popular computer magazines as a schoolboy in England.

Whilst obtaining a degree in pure mathematics he merged Founded Matrix Consulting with partners and Image Graphics which he later merged with his brother to create InfoSoft Nigeria: the country’s foremost software development, company.

During his time at InfoSoft he oversaw the development of the server and communications applications for Paycard Nigeria’s first electronic payment system, Online 2000, the first online banking application and X-Trac the leading share registration management application.

Following British American Tobacco’s merger with Rothmans, he took up a marketing position in the newly formed British American Tobacco, serving as the head of special projects, Trade Development Manager, head of Strategy, Planning and Insights and Africa Middle-East Menthol Brand Manager.

He developed RIVER the company’s first sales force automation system and management system, piloted the largest retail census in the company’s history and unified the companies sales forecasting and planning system.

He later joined a re-invigorated Microsoft Nigeria as head of Marketing for Anglophone West Africa where he managed the company’s image and the biggest product launch in Microsoft’s corporate history.

He also serves on the boards of various Nigerian Companies including The Quantic Trust, Torch Publishing, No Surprises Events, Eventage Nigeria and InfoSoft Nigeria. Okigbo is a certified PRINCE2 Practitioner and a Microsoft Certified Professional.
