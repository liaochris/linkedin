Claude Valin’s Summary

I have over 16 years of experience in the information technology industry including more than 11 years in project management and business analysis. I have managed custom software development, infrastructure projects, change management and non-IT projects.

I’ve managed software development projects in R&D and for business applications, in the retail industry, e-commerce, communication, defense and marine navigation. I’ve implemented project management methodologies and SDLC for a number of companies and provided training on the subject. I have a PMP certification from PMI since 2002.
