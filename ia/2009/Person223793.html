Steve Davis’s Summary

A strong, visionary leader with 25+ years of demonstrated success and consistent achievement in implementing business growth initiatives, leading organizational change and facilitating strategic conversations throughout complex health care organizations.

I find opportunities in every problem and have focused my career around issues of strategy, business development and innovation in health care, always seeking solutions to the complex question of how organizations find “separation” in a generally “me-too” world.

I love spending time on the strategic frontier, seeking a deeper understanding of the pathways to delighted customers, heightened performance and compelling visions. I have proven abilities to grow businesses and organizations, and an in-depth knowledge of trends and challenges in the Chicago and national health care marketplaces.
