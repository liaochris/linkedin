Jamie Ali FIRP Cert RP’s Summary

Converge Recruitment with over 12 years of specialist professional Property, Construction and Engineering recruitment experience have become experts in this field with high service delivery of permanent, contract and temporary staff looking for career opportunities in the UK’s & International Public and Private Sectors. We provide professional construction recruitment solutions and supply many of the design, contracting companies and professional advisors with quality staff nationally and internationally at the most competitive price.

This company has been formed by many client and candidate recommendations and has an excellent reputation for delivering a service totally in tune with the needs of clients and job seekers alike.

We are corporate members of the REC (Recruitment & Employment Confederation) and take our responsibility of service to our candidates and clients seriously.

As we partner with our clients, we have gained a compelling track record in filling permanent and contract job opportunities, ranging from graduate level positions to director opportunities. We also have a good track record in managing sole or preferred agency campaigns for many levels of permanent, temporary and contract recruitment through to total client satisfaction. This arrives from our close business partnering philosophy, problem solving and creating tailored and bespoke solutions abilities.

We consistently work closely with all our clients and candidates to maintain long-term, mutually beneficial relationships, while reacting rapidly to our clients’ needs using our specialist industry expert knowledge.
