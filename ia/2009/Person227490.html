Suzanne Lainson’s Summary

For 20 years I have been involved in marketing, PR, research, and writing for the sports, music, and high tech industries, applying, when appropriate, an integrated marketing strategy to the projects I have developed.

I was one of the first to write a column on online marketing (in 1993 for Apple's OneNet). My sports marketing and management newsletters were widely used as curriculum materials in college sports marketing and management programs. I wrote a monthly column about women's sports business for Oxygen Media. I covered the Colorado high tech/venture capital scene for three different publications.

Before that I was a freelance writer for a number of national magazines and an author of a business book which was featured as a Fortune Book Club Alternate Selection.

I am particularly interested in sports and entertainment as branding strategies.

Suzanne Lainson’s Education

  • University of Colorado at Boulder

    MA, Integrated Marketing Communications, 1992 — 1993

    This was the first year of University of Colorado's Integrated Marketing Communication masters program. It was the second IMC program being offered anywhere. (Northwestern was the first.
