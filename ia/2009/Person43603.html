Chris Murray’s Summary

As a Producer/Technical Director/Project Manager clients hire me to assist with the planning & coordination of Audio Visual for Meetings, Events and Conferences. I book the crew, the equipment and work with the Client, Crew and facility to limit the number of surprises when we arrive onsite. Onsite I work with the Executive Producer to execute a flawless event. I coordinate the AV load-in show schedule and call cues for the Event.

I also own and operate an Audience Response System. ARS allows the audience to participate and interact with the presentation by using credit card sized keypads to interact with the presentations.

Other Responsibilities
- Manage all equip. & labor
- Create technical budget.
- Technical drawings & site plans.
- Contract vendors
- Create production schedules.
- Coordinate technical requirements w/venue.
- Locate and Hire on site support labor.
- Manage vendors/crew for setup, rehearsals, show/strike.
- Create show cue sheet & call show.
