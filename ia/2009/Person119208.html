Jonathan Booth’s Summary

Games programmer with 9 years of experience in the industry. I've worked on a series of games published on the leading platforms of the time including PC, PS2, Xbox, PS3 and XBox360. I've experience of working on nearly all aspects of game development from the tool chain through to different engine modules like AI, physic, core technologies and graphics where I've been specializing.
I find myself also interested in mobile games development - this is a interesting and entertaining hobby and learning experience although something I could persue as a career. I've also spent some of my own time becoming familiar with OpenGL and other tookits that I haven't used directly in my career to date.

Jonathan Booth’s Specialties:

Graphics engines, DirectX, HLSL and assembly graphics shaders, code optimization.
