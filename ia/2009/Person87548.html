Glenn Gemmell’s Summary

Glenn Gemmell is an award winning advertising Art Director / Creative Director, working in the Mid-Atlantic region of the United States. During his career he’s won a CLIO for packaging design, ECHO’s for direct response, Gold and Silver ADDY’s, and PICA awards for print ads and brochures. His work has been featured in: The Creativity Annual, USA Today, Advertising Age, Illustrators Showcase, The Black Book, The Print Annual, Packaging Digest, The Baltimore Sun and Art Direction Magazine.

His commercial client base is vast, including, small companies, national and international corporations and associations. He was trained at The Maryland Institute College of Art.
