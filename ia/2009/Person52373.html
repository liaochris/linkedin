Damian Reeves’s Summary

• Responsible for building and operating a responsive, high throughput, low-latency, reliable, resilient platform for Quantcast’s services. Quantcast serves over 6 billion web pages every day, reaching 1 billion unique people every month.
• Created clustered DISC (Data-Intensive Supercomputing) platform (thousands of cores, PBs of storage), innovating upon ideas from Google-style map/reduce, GFS, sawzall computation styles to implement state-of-the-art petascale data-mining algorithms in the audience measurement and online advertising space.
• Outside of Google or Yahoo, run one of the largest map-reduce clusters in production.
• Over 13 years of technical & entrepreneurial experience in extreme performance Internet infrastructure.
• Prior to joining Quantcast, I founded Zeus Technology, a leader in application traffic management and high-performance web infrastructure. As CTO & GM of Zeus, I invented the company’s core intellectual property and product set, ran the US operations and raised extensive venture capital. Zeus was cited by Red Herring as one of the top 50 private companies in the world (May 2000).
• Inventor of several patents in application traffic management, distributed systems & high-availability. Hold a Masters Degree (double first) in Computer Science from the University of Cambridge, England
