Bo Lora’s Summary

I've been involved with the Internet since 1987. I've worked on the hardware as well as the software side of the Internet. I've designed web sites as well as written many programs. Then I realized that while I had fun doing all that, some few years ago I missed a boat, a couple actually. I didn't buy Yahoo or Google stock and there I was trading hours for dollars like the rest of us. That's when I got serious about changing the way I look at work.

I decided to leave the comfort of a steady job and dive into consulting. I figured that I could leverage my web user and process engineering experience and look for a way to work with different companies as a consultant to help them maximize their work to create functional and compelling web applications.
