Gav Thorpe’s Summary

Over the fourteen years I spent with the company I worked in many roles, including staff writer, copywriting, games developer, project management and a couple of years as a department manager. Over this time my primary work has been as a writer and Games Designer/ Developer working on science fiction and fantasy miniatures wargames.

Working as part of a design studio comprising miniatures designers, artists, writers and sales support (marketing, in-house publishing and so forth) I have filled the role of Lead Developer on many projects over the years, contributing written and design material and co-ordinating the production of books.

In that time I occupied the position of management over the Warhammer tabletop games system and its developers. However, it is in a creative lead role that my greatest interest and skills lie and so I have concentrated on managing relationships between creative team members and those managerial skills required for effective project co-ordination.

In a freelance capacity I am also a published fiction writer, currently authoring my ninth novel through the Black Library imprint and with over a dozen short stories to my credit. I also wrote the script and voice over dialogue for the Mark of Chaos computer game.
