Vince Beeton’s Summary

I want to work with influential and innovative companies who deliver insightful and passionate content. I want clients to employ my 360 degrees skill set and British TV programme-making expertise to generate productions or campaigns that engage and entertain their target audiences.

My personal, practical knowledge of production means my management has insight and empathy. I instill both professionalism and harmony and pursue excellence. I also get the best performances out of people in front of the camera by helping them to enjoy the monumental moment of performance.

Running my own production companies and working as a freelancer in the UK, West Africa and the USA, gave me the opportunity to film factual entertainment TV series and observational documentaries for the BBC, Discovery and Sky.

My experience working with one of the UK’s top digital media agencies means I take an integrated approach to communication solutions. I can offer expertise to produce anything from a thirty-second animation to raise brand awareness; to an integrated and international web based thought leadership campaign. I also produce live events including conferencing and webcasts and my clients are public sector, professional services and multi-nationals.

I believe knowledge gained should be imparted and I always take the time to teach whenever I can.
