Christopher Enright’s Summary

Visionary, strategic, and results-driven executive with more than eight years of experience as the CTO of a $30 million digital solutions provider and a total of more than 20 years in the IT arena. Proven ability to develop and leverage state-of-the-art technologies, build highly effective development teams, and manage project engagements averaging $1 million in size for Fortune 500 clients. Articulate communicator and resourceful problem-solver with a strong talent for generating win-win results. Fully bilingual in English and French.

Led the technology design of a unique “social retailing” application for the New York designer Nanette Lepore, driving 300% revenue growth for her, which has received extensive coverage in TIME Magasine 10 best inventions of 2007, The New York Times and other major media and allows a shopper to share real-time video images and conversation with others via the Internet while in a unique mirrored dressing room. Principal author on pending patent for this application.

Developed a store-of-the-future leveraging RFID and interactive technologies for Prada. Managed the design and development of numerous other innovative solutions for clients that have included MasterCard, Nestlé Waters, Sony, and Valspar Paint. Developed new offerings in the areas of business intelligence, CRM, and marketing campaign management. Drove a 400% revenue growth over five years from $2 million to $8 million in annual revenue for NY office.
