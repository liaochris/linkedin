Claude Martin Rainaud’s Summary

Senior Freelance International Consultant with a broad range of competence in the field of:
• Computer Supported Collaborative Learning and Knowledge Management.
• Learning Systems: policy and strategies development, planning and assessment.
• Learning Design: pedagogy and didactic aspects, Lifelong Learning and Competence-based approaches, e-Learning, distance learning, multimedia, broadcasting,
• Planning and management of elaborated and extensive training programmes
• Promotion of entrepreneurship in vocational education
• Development and production of training material
• Design, planning, monitoring and evaluation of development co-operation projects.
• Advocacy, networking and partnership building for access to education through Open Educational Resources and Free/Open Source Software.

Claude Martin Rainaud’s Specialties:

• Standards-based Learning Objects and Units of Learning systems design
• Knowledge Management Systems Projects Design

• Free Open Source Software and Open Educational Resources use and promotion
• Usability design and testing
• Web-based applications design (Zope, Plone…).
• Videoconferencing integration in Distance Learning and e-Learning projects.
