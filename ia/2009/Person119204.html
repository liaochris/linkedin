Jon Block’s Summary

I have been working in the IT, new media and online gaming industries since 1999.

I am an enthusiastic development lead who has been leading development teams since 2004 on projects for high profile media clients such as the BBC, ESPN, The Sun and The Guardian.

I am also a part-time entrepreneur with experience in product definition and development, project management, technical analysis, front-end design and user experience.

  • Developer

    ITV

    (Public Company; Broadcast Media industry)

    March 2008 — Present (1 year 2 months)

    At ITV I work on the technology team developing the ITV.com website. Recently I have been leading a team of 4 developers on the next release of ITV’s internal CMS.

    Since early 2009 I have been involved in a small football strategy brainstorming team including the MD and key members from all departments. As a result of this I have been responsible for initially suggesting and single-handedly negotiating a relationship with a third party social media platform that ITV intend to use in an upcoming event.

The system was optimised to be able to handle 250,000 new registrations per hour.

    Also in 2008 I worked as the sole developer integrating ITV.com with KickApps social networking technology. Most of the work here involved liaising between the business sponsors at ITV and the KickApps technical specialists to make sure that the KickApps product met our requirements.
