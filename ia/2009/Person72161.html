Elliott Garms (elliott@swiconsultants.com)’s Summary

My brother and I decided that it was time for a change in the Chicago land area. With over 10 years of experience in the recruiting industry between the two of us, we are embarking on changing the bad name "recruiter" is getting here in the Chicago tech community. We differ by actually getting to know all of our candidates, and not just sending them out on every interview we can find. Instead we act somewhat as their life long career agent and work with them even when its of no benefit to us. We take serious integrity in everything we do and try to connect with everyone we meet on a personal level rather than just from a business standpoint. I am always looking to network with like-minded professionals from any walk of life, feel free to email me anytime.
