All I can do in my profession is from my personal experience, not from the university or from the books.

Usually I do following for my clients at textviruslab.com:

1) Feasibility studies of textual documents, based on their representation as a sequence of structured wordings of actions:

In practice not all the actions are carefully formulated in the textual documents. It causes various damages in management. From the feasibility studies the client can see and understand the risks and opportunities, which usually are not seen before structuring the textual documents, make well-grounded decisions in time. The feasibility studies can be represented graphically and without any special terminology.

2) Consulting:

Making the documents feasible can be the next step to normalize the activity. It may require the revision of the activity ideas.

Sometimes it is unclear for the client what documents or even reality to consider. A dialogue with the client usually eliminates this uncertainty.

The clients should remove the confidential information from their documents before getting the services. Work language is Russian, but can be another if the professional translating/interpreting is possible.
