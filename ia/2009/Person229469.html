Tasha Bovain’s Summary

As a professional writer and creative strategist, I can help you capture the attention of your target audience and humanize your brand in a variety of fresh and exciting ways.

Specializing in creating values-led communications for small businesses and non-profit organizations, I produce persuasive and powerful copy for websites, brochures, proposals, press releases, newsletters and more to help you attract more clients and enhance your professional image.

Using a “marketing from the inside out” approach, I take the time to understand your distinctive business needs and can quickly step into your world to craft compelling marketing copy that reflects your company’s unique personality and connects more customers to your brand.

My mission is to help you effectively communicate your value proposition to potential customers so that you can set yourself apart from the competition and shine in your industry.
