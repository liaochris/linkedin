Ron Reuven’s Summary

Mr. Reuven began his career in the securities business as an understudy of a NYC branch manager for 1st Union in 1999. There, he began developing his own research and investment philosophy, while learning more about office administration & operations in the financial industry. In June 2001, he was recruited by Raymond James Financial, one of the largest financial firms in the US. At RJF, he started to build his own clientele and grow through the implementation of his own investment philosophy. During one of the most difficult market environments in history, Mr. Reuven continued to build his business, and became the 3rd largest producing broker in the US for RJF by 2003.

In March 2003, Mr. Reuven decided to launch Reuven Enterprises, Inc. as a result of his decision to begin building a true full service boutique firm. He first made a leap that year into the independent brokerage sector by joining an independent broker/dealer based out of Florida. After building his own team of advisors, Mr. Reuven’s progression culminated with the founding of his own Member FINRA firm, Reuven Enterprises Securities Division, in Nov. 2006. Reuven Enterprises offers financial planning and investment advice to High & Ultra High Net Worth Individuals, Corporations, and Pensions.

Mr. Reuven now oversees his growing firm and its divisions, while maintaining a cultivated book of business and managing his own Activist Hedge Fund. His personal clientele are comprised of Business Owners, C-Level Executives, as well as other High Profile Clients. Mr. Reuven is also a NFL Players Association Registered Player Financial Advisor. The unique philosophy and track record shown by Mr. Reuven has been recognized by the media, CNBC, BLOOMBERG as well as the Wall Street Community. Mr. Reuven holds the following securities licenses: Series 7, 63, 24, 53 and is also a licensed Life Insurance Agent.
