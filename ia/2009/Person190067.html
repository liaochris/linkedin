Guy Harris’s Summary

I worked for 17 years in technical management/leadership positions. In that time I built, lead, and worked on teams of various sizes and for various purposes. About 1996 I began to study and apply leadership, communication, and team dynamics principles in my business life. This study and practical application led me to start my own business focused on the application of these principles. I now train, consult, coach, and speak on leadership, communication, and team dynamics issues.
