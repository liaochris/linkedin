Todd Bouldin’s Summary

I am an entrepreneur and teacher by personality and heart with an expertise in casting vision, creating strategic alliances to advance vision, developing communications strategies, and communicating complex ideas in understandable and inspiring ways.

I am an attorney licensed in the State of Tennessee, but I put this expertise at work in the classroom, in designing business strategies, and in creating new organizational possibilities. I currently am a professor of government relations and political science at Pepperdine University, and serve as a coordinator of new academic efforts in the arts and media fields, as the creator of a Master of Fine Arts in Screen and Television Writing, and as Director of the Lilly Endowment grant at Pepperdine. My expertise is in American politics, Congress and federal government relations.

I also own my own company, Vocare LLC, which provides corporate and training services, with a specialty in the luxury hotel market and entertainment. I currently serve as a consultant and corporate trainer for Starwood Hotels and just completed a project as a political consultant for the film "Iron Man 2".

I have over ten years of experience in public affairs, government relations and federal elections and campaigns. I provide strategic and political advice to business clients and campaigns.

Finally, I have three years of corporate and general business legal experience, and over ten years of experience in managing political operations, nonprofits and religious organizations.

Todd Bouldin’s Specialties:

University administration, government relations and strategic planning. I also have developed expertise in hotel guest relations and guest relations services. Academic specialties include American government, church and state, and law. I have excellent marketing and public relations instincts with a specialty in branding and viral marketing.
