Maita Lester’s Summary

Maita Lester is an accomplished administrator and manager. With a background in social work and program planning, she is known for her caring attitude for both employees and clients.

She is able to delegate with ease and has a keen awareness for getting projects completed on time and right the first time. Lester's first-hand working knowledge enables her to understand client needs in detail and her grasp of what's needed and what sells helps her excel in marketing.

Lester earned her B.A. degree in Social Science from the University of California Berkeley. Upon graduation, she was employed full time with the University as a program coordinator for the extension division in Criminology and Health Sciences.

Lester moved to San Francisco and worked in the Bank of California's Trust Department, later being transferred to the Los Angeles main office where she administrated Testamentary Trusts and probated estates.

She has served as an office manager and grant writer for Family Support Services, as a Volunteer and Program Coordinator for Allied Housing, and as Director of the Singles and Senior Departments for the Jewish Community Center of Denver, where she created numerous programs and planned major events and fund-raisers.
