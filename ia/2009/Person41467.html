Charles Sujansky’s Summary

A strategic HR professional who participates in senior level annual planning sessions to evaluate corporate strengths and weakness and setting 5 year goals. Works closely with business leaders to ensure accomplishments of business objectives. Has experience in both union and non-union environments with employee populations ranging from 350 to 24,000 employees.

- Designed and implemented an automated merit planning system;
- Conducted internal investigations and responded to EEOC discrimination charges;

- Selected and implemented on line assessment used for hiring sales consultants;
