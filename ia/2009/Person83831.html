Gary Nichols-Roth’s Summary

Gary Nichols-Roth is the Commercial Director of Government projects at Sovereign Publications a recently acquired publishing house by Ten Alps PLC. He was born in January 1964 and is a Psychology graduate from the University of Leicester.

He joined Sovereign in July 2007 from a specialist media agency, specifically to work on Government based titles starting with Climate Change “addressing the challenge”. An industry lead annual publication which was heralded a great success working with the G8 leaders, which was distributed in April 2008. He left Sovereign in May 2008 and returned in November 2008 in the same capacity. He has had over 16 years experience of combined publishing, conferencing and the media industries.

Gary has worked on, produced and created several government publications and topical address titles including Britain in Europe 1 and 2 - Tony Blair ' s presidency of the EU, the Celebrated Ireland 2000, European Businessman and European CEO.

He began his career as a white collar senior civil servant working in County Hall, The former ILEA (Inner London Education Authority).
