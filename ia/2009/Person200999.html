Robert Schmid, PE, LEED AP’s Summary

I have successfully worked and managed technical teams on simultaneous tasks with tight budgets and accelerated schedules on numerous projects such as:
-Electrical Power Substation Designs in various voltage classes including Smart Grid implementation
-Short Circuit/Coordination/Arc Flash studies
-Communication Network Design For Control & Monitoring Systems including Fiber Optic Cable Specification and Design
-Computer Room/Data Center Designs/Studies
-Generator/ATS/Static Switch Designs
-Power System Design
-Supervisory Control And Data Acquisition (SCADA) Systems Design
-Graphic User Interface Development

I have developed short circuit, breaker/relay coordination, and arc flash studies for numerous clients and facilities,using the latest software suites including SKM Power Tools software.

My hands-on experience extends into the latest design & analysis of:
-Low, Medium and High Voltage Power Distribution Systems
-Communication Systems
-Traction/Transit Power
-Mission Critical Applications
-Process Control and Indication Systems

I have participated in numerous facility energy studies, involving the analysis of beneifts versus costs for investments in energy efficient equipment, lighting, and lighting and HVAC controls. The created reports detailed whether the investments would satisfy client specific project goals, such as return on investment, lower maintenance costs, and added systems performance/reliability.

Robert Schmid, PE, LEED AP’s Specialties:
