Geoff Hetherington’s Summary

An Industry Agnostic, Geoff is an energetic internationally experienced senior executive who has over 30 years experience in Retail, Pharmacy, Sales, Marketing, Construction and Telecommunications. A Certified Practicing Marketer, he has a passion for General Management, Marketing and Strategy.
In February 2000 Geoff returned after 5 years in Indonesia where he was responsible for a multitude of Operational & Strategic roles for The Gemala Group, The Rajawali Group & most extensively Jardine Matheson’s Retail arm - Dairy Farm International.
In Australia Geoff has worked as a senior executive for companies such as Safeway, Venture, AMCAL, Coles, Digicall, Vodafone, Fone Zone, Leading Edge Group, Camera House, MBS & Telstra.
His lead in negotiating, saw Leading Edge Group move into the New Zealand market by acquiring 15 Telecom NZ company owned stores & with a negotiated contract which gave Leading Edge the right to develop TNZs dealer network.
An engaging public speaker, he spoke on Next Generation Marketing as the Keynote speaker at the ‘Customer Service Masterclass’ Conference in Kuala Lumpur, He was a speaker at a recent Business Continuity Conference in Sydney where he spoke of his Indonesian experiences.
At Telstra he won the 2007 Most Effective Marketing Program Award for the Experiential Next G™ Roadshow program.
With an entrepreneurial background, Geoff is nonetheless bottom line focused, & believes that businesses should be proud & unapologetic about making sustainable profits.
Carrying a wide-ranging Operations background as well as strong risk management experience, Geoff is used to getting results through people in highly competitive & challenging circumstances.
Living at Bateau Bay, Geoff is married with 4 children the youngest of which, Michael, he adopted whilst in Indonesia. A skilled Martial Artist he teaches classes when he can, & is concluding his Certificates III & IV in Personal Training.
