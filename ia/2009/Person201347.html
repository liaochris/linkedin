Robin Byers’s Summary

Most recently VP, E-Commerce at GTP Travel Group, LLC, Robin has 8 years of experience in Operations in the Travel Industry and 15+ years in Data Management and Analysis. The most recent challenge of turning around a newly acquired company was a perfect fit for Robin's skills of process improvement, operational excellence, dedication and drive.

Prior to GTP, Robin worked as a senior consultant in Brulant's Search Practice gaining a tremendous amount of knowledge of Search Engine Optimization and Search Engine Marketing. Her core objective for the practice was to create centralized Data Management and Reporting improving the ability to provide ROI to clients, measure improvement, and pinpoint opportunities to adjust strategy. This was accomplished within three months and created the foundation for the practice to double in size over a year's time.

Robin's hospitality experience began at PlanSoft (later merging to OnVantage and finally StarCite) where she began her career as a part-time project manager and within a year became Manager, Data Management and Analysis. This was a direct result of being proven to drive operational excellence in the face of the challenges brought to the travel industry caused by the devastation of 9/11. Despite these challenges, she was able to develop and build relationship and grow sales by providing customized KPIs and consistent reporting to key clients like Hilton Hotels Corporation and Wyndham Hotels.

Robin's work at Lubrizol supporting a worldwide market intelligence system allowed the company to define the oil additives market and gauge market share to determine strategy as the oil industry went through record-breaking mergers and acquisitions dramatically changing the competitive landscape. Responsible for all aspects of system design, development, support, documentation and training, including traveling to worldwide sales offices to gain commitment and ensure the success of the system.
