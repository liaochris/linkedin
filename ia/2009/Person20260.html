Gigi Greco’s Summary

Owner/President - Art Attack Promotions, a full service PR, Marketing and Promotions firm specializing in entertainment marketing, A pioneer in the Internet marketing field, Latin media and promotions, grassroots and lifestyle marketing solutions, street teams, and new media. Past and current clients include Los Lonely Boys (Epic), John Lee Hooker, Jr. (Telarc.), deSoL (Curb Records), Rabanes (Sony Latin), Gian Marco (Sony latin) Canibus (Kent), Booya Tribe (Kent), Blowing Trees (Glassnote), Jackson Taylor (Smith Entertainment), Kinky (Netwerk), Bulldog Communications (London), Market Development NYC, Xenon Pictures, Batanga, Dr. Pepper, SXSW Music Conference, and more.

Owner/President - Art Attack Productions, a full service production company specializing in music videos, television pilot and series development, commercials, multimedia content and distribution, and production management and coordination. Produced over 500 national and regional ad spots, directed radio spots for television, created advertising and marketing campaigns, produced numerous music videos for major labels and indie artists, produced several live DVD's and CD Rom DPK's, produced multimedia content and live concert shoots for corporate branding, produced/licensed music for branded CD compilations.

Executive Producer and Creator- All Access Live, a nationally syndicated 30 minute weekly live concert series which ran consecutively for three seasons and aired on all the major networks including CBS, ABC, NBC, WB, and UPN. Pioneer in what has since been deemed "Grassroots Syndication."

Vice President - Swamp Dog Productions, a multimedia production house specializing in online media solutions, interactive events, and live concert productions including Winterfresh Snocore Tour for EVB Agency.

17+ years experience in music, entertainment, public relations, marketing, promotions, and media production fields.A pioneer in the field of Internet marketing and new media promotions.

  • Founder and Visionary

    The Digital Arts Consortium

    (Media Production industry)

    January 2009 — Present (10 months)

    Conceived in Austin, TX in 2009 in the Art Attack Promotions' Go-Go Garage, the Digital Arts Consortium is a multifaceted collaborative corps comprised of graphic artists, filmmakers, musicians, DJ's, audio engineers, gaming developers, and programming, visual, performance, technical, conceptual, and spoken word artists alike which make up Austin's rich and eclectic creative community.

    We recognize the potential impact that Austin’s vibrant and diverse palette of art and technology could have on the world’s cultural canvas IF these industries could understand the assets of their creative contemporaries and open their lines of communication. Austin is the cornerstone that the D.A.C. plans to build upon as we recruit and empower more and more cities from across the United States to form a resource network of unique talent promoting pure expression, creative freedom, and mutual respect for art in any form.

    The mission of the D.A.C. is to bridge the gap between physical art and technology so that artists -- from all areas of expertise -- can construct and help grow new forms of expression through education, exploration, cooperation and collaboration, cross promotion, and pollination while employing the *Creative Commons ideology and standards to build a network of innovation and trusted resources one city at a time.
