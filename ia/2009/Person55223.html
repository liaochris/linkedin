Petri Darby’s Summary

PR and communications strategist and writer with more than a dozen years of experience spanning the agency and client sides, in non-profit and corporate arenas.

darbyDarnit employs a virtual agency approach, bringing together specialists in various disciplines (writers, designers, crisis management specialists, video production experts, Internet marketing professionals...) according to each client's unique industry and/or communications needs. Without the overhead associated with benefits and office space for full-time employees, clients get senior-level talent for a price comparable to what large agencies would charge for entry-level people to handle your account.

Representation has included:
Law firms, commercial and residential real estate entities, health care organizations and medical device companies, nonprofits, political campaigns, PR/marketing agencies, entrepreneurial ventures, utilities, environmental consultants, manufacturing facilities, software companies, and others.

darbyDarnit is a member of PR Consultants Group, Inc., a national affiliation of independent public relations firms.

We also offer several specialty solutions, including:
- Market Research
- Social/digital media programs
- More than 600,000 promotional products
- Online newsroom content management system
- Flash video for the Web
