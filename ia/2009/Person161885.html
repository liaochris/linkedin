Michael Nugent’s Summary

I have written or co-written several books, including two Christmas bestsellers, and a successful sellout comedy musical drama.

I Keano (2005-2008) is a sellout comedy musical drama about footballer Roy Keane leaving the Ireland World Cup squad. It is written as a mock-epic tale set in ancient Rome.

Dear John - The John Mackay Letters (1993), was a Christmas number one bestseller. It was a series of prank letters to famous people, and their replies.

Ireland on the Internet (1995) was the first book to be published in Ireland about using the Internet. Following this, I worked as an Internet consultant before returning to writing in 2003.

That's Ireland - A Miscellany (2003) was also a Christmas bestseller.
