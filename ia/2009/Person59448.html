Dawn Stott’s Summary

My career began with side jobs as a gourmet food Retailer, vintage clothing Shop Assistant, and Barista. I later gained on-the-job training as a Legal Secretary, working my way up the ranks into Accounting and Administration. Armed with a background in Business Administration and Food Service, I helped open a 24-hour Espresso Bar in Austin, Texas from 1994-1996. Upon my return to California, I went back to school to learn digital Photography with the desire to bring my art to the computer. Upon the advice of my school counselor, I switched my focus to Multimedia and Interaction Technologies. I am well-rounded when it comes to business and Project Management, bringing a clear understanding of the big picture and bottom line as they relate to creating success.
