Stacy Pursell’s Summary

The Pursell Group LLC dba the VET Recruiter ® is a premier direct hire search firm specializing in Executive Search, Management Recruiting, Veterinary and Sales Force recruiting within the Animal Health, Veterinary, Veterinary Pharmaceuticals & Biologics, Animal Nutrition, Pet Products, Agriculture & related Industries. Some of the areas of expertise include Veterinary Technical Services, Pharmacovigilance, Sales Management and Field Sales, Marketing, Regulatory Affairs, Research and Development, Drug Discovery and other areas. We also place veterinarians and boarded specialists in clinical practice.

Stacy Pursell is recognized world wide as one of the top search consultants in the Animal Health, Veterinary and Pet Specialty Industries. Stacy has personally assisted many of the major Animal Health, Pet Specialty and Veterinary companies in recruiting senior level management personnel, middle management and other professionals and has been told by clients that we have one of the highest sucess rates in the industry. Placements include General Manager of US Companion Animal Business, Chief Scientific Officer, President of Animal Health Company, Director of Marketing, Director of Sales, Director of Technical Services, Product Manager, Brand Manager, Associate Director of Vaccine Design Group, Director of Biologics, Specialty Account Veterinarian, Senior Director of Regulatory Affairs, Technical Services Veterinarian, Medical Director, Chief of Staff, Regional Sales Manager and Veterinary Sales Representative . Stacy has also assisted startup animal health and pet product companies in building their entire teams. She is very knowledgeable of the Animal Health, Pet Specialty and Veterinary Industries and recruits exclusively within those and closely related industries. She has a proven track record, is widely respected and is a trusted expert when it comes to professional search and placement within the Animal Health, Veterinary and Pet Specialty Industries.

Stacy Pursell’s Specialties:

Insider Knowledge of the Animal Health Industry growth and hiring trends, company cultures, compensation packages, competitive advantages and organizational structures.

Executive Search, Management Consulting, Veterinary and Sales Force Recruiting in the Animal Health, Veterinary, Animal Nutrition and Pet Specialty Industries, identifying top talent for hard to fill positions, contract negotiation, salary negotiation, new business development and research.
