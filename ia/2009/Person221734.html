Stacy Nelson, CSA (TopLinked.com)’s Summary

I've been in Reverse Mortgages for 5 years and I realized last May or so that it just wasn't where I wanted to be anymore. I started talking people out of doing the loan, not because it's not a great product but because I didn't think that the home values would make it beneficial to them. And when you start talking people out of buying what you sell then you should probably get a different product right?

So I thought that adding in regular mortgages to my repertoire would be the way, but it was never my passion so it didn't really stick. That's when my husband and I decided I would take the summer off and get a "Real Job" in September. As it turns out, lots of other people thought the same thing at the same time. With 'mortgage' anywhere on my resume, employers weren't even looking at my resume let alone calling me in for interviews.

Are you still reading because here's where it gets exciting?

I finally decided to trust that I would be led down the right path when the time was right. The moment I did I got a phone call. Hugh VanHorn, Mortgage Broker with TMG, called and asked if I would be willing to help out a couple of his Realtors from the desert with a Foreclosure listing they just took in Temecula. I called my other good friend Sue Kelley with Keller Williams and asked for her help in my transition which she gladly gave.

Anyone that knows me well knows I don't make quick decisions - I evaluate and make a plan and then jump in. Sue and Hugh just laughed and pushed me off of that cliff and Sue keeps to her mantra "This is going to be fun". I met with Arlis at KW on Monday at 11:00 and by 2:00 I was gainfully employed at Keller Williams. I look good in Red.

Now it's official: I'm in the Real Estate business.

Please be sure to invite me as one of your links! I love having new relationships and there's nothing better than networking online.
