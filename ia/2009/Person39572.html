Cynthia B. Illingworth’s Summary

We touch millions of lives everyday. We help individuals and companies prepare for the road ahead. Trusted financial services representatives realize the importance of their role and take pride in what they do. The feeling we get from empowering people has been our driving force for more than 155 years.

Since 1851, through wars and major economic shifts, we've learned how to adjust to a changing world while we help others do the same. Being a company consumers can trust has allowed us to adapt and grow in the financial services industry. As part of an experienced and stable family of companies we make a difference in the lives of our customers and employees. It is our goal as a Fortune 100 enterprise to build upon our strength – to continue helping others.

What Sets Us Apart?
- Our position as one of America's top organizations as evidenced by our #99 ranking on the Fortune 500* list and our ranking among America's "Most Admired Companies" in our industry by Fortune magazine**.
- Our member companies offer diverse products in all 50 states, DC, Puerto Rico, and across the globe. See our annual report for recent accomplishments.
- We are a mutually-owned financial protection, accumulation and income management company, which allows us to maintain our independence and make sound long-term financial decisions.
- Our dedication to our community begins at the Home Office and continues through to our agencies and into the local communities
