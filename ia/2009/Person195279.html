Rebecca Cooley’s Summary

I have over 30 years of Women and Children's experience as a nurse, manager, and director in both union and non union hospitals. I have enjoyed interim assignments for the last 5 years and am currently accepting interim roles and am also looking at permanent employment. I have numerous recommendations from each assignments to share. The recommendations come from administration, physicians, nurses. I've managed operating ,capital, and productivity budgets in hospitals with births from 350 to 5000 per year. I am knowledgeable of current healthcare trends, as well as regulatory, accreditiing and legislative mandates. Over the years I have created, developed and implemented programs as well as strengthened and enhanced established programs, Other strengths include: Mentoring nurse managers to become more knowlegable and skilled in management, empowering/encouraging staff to use evidenced based best practices, a strong committment to high quality consumer friendly services, and excellent communication and interpersonal skills to communicate collaboratively with staff, physicians, and other colleagues.
