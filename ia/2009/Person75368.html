Erin Adams’s Summary

Erin Adams is both scientist and design innovator who is able to see flexible, textile properties in every piece of material. Using cutting edge technology, and an intuitive aesthetic sense, Erin Adams Design creates high-design wall and floor coverings for the vast surface materials market. Always on the leading edge, the company's unique surface materials range from modern glass mosaics, traditional ceramics, to non-traditional recycled skateboards and aluminum. Erin Adams Design is currently on the brink of distributing new products from several important artisans around the country. The company is reaching out to new markets such as the mid-range and green sectors of the industry.

Erin Adams’s Experience

    Erin Adams Design

    (Design industry)

    1989 — Present (20 years)

    In business and profitable since 1989, Erin Adams Design designs and manufactures wall and floor coverings for commercial and residential interiors. Erin Adams Design is an excellent resource for surface materials in several mediums including LEED compliant. With the capacity for limitless volume, their wall and floor coverings embrace the structure of the built environment. The company works on large commercial and small residential projects; and also offer tile installations, design consultations, and event space rental in their high-design showroom.
