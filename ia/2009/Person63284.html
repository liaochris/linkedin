Diane Kane’s Summary

For over 19 years I've worked in various aspects of the Entertainment Industry. Recently I've specialized in working with entertainment based companies, leading their Marketing and Brand awareness efforts. I'm also responsible for, and very involved with, the development of new business ventures/contacts. I've successfully coordinated the efforts to market our products, and build new relations, at several of the top industry trade shows including the Electronic Entertainment Expo (E3), NY Toy Show. I develop relationships with Companies in order to help them with every aspect of distribution.

Diane Kane’s Specialties:

I am a Outside Rep for McFarlane Toys and for Imagination Games as well as many others. I also work on getting companies set up for Dropship Programs for Overstock.com and Six Flags and most of your second tier distribution channels. My core philosophy is to find uniform, global solutions to the entire entertainment and media industry. I foresee how it is possible to combine existing channels, markets and connections to meet and develop the needs of new business ventures.
