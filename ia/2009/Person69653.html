Ed King’s Summary

At this unprecedented time in business where customer retention is critical, and customers are leaving in droves, I help companies keep more of the customers they have and garner their long-term loyalty. In addition to converting customers into raving fans, my clients also enjoy the added benefit of growing their business via new customers (and at a much lower cost of acquisition) due to positive word-of-mouth.

I’ve developed creative, yet measurable programs for multi-million up to multi-billion dollar companies, helping them to retain more customers using my skills in:

• Brand positioning and alignment
• Employee communication and engagement
• Customer experience architecture
• Customer service innovation

Contrary to most consultants who look for what’s wrong, then try to fix it, I believe each organization (and each individual) has a core genius. I help organizations and their people find their unique strengths, and convert those strengths into lasting value for customers and employees alike. This results in:

• Higher customer retention
• Lower employee turnover
• Increased positive word-of-mouth

There is an obvious connection between having engaged employees and delivering a superior customer experience. And, a connection between engaged employees and superior business performance - in the form of loyal customers.

I welcome the opportunity to help your organization develop a cheering section of its own.
