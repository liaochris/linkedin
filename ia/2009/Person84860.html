Tracy Renee’s Summary

Gene’s Auto Repair has proudly been serving Solano and Napa County since 1994. Gene's Auto Repair is open Monday through Friday from 8am to 5pm.

Family owned and team operated, Gene’s Auto Repair is a full automotive service and repair facility specializing in foreign and domestic vehicles. Our 9,000 square foot facility is located in Vallejo two blocks from the Mare Island water front.

Gene's Auto Repair earned the prestigious Diamond Certified® award, Independently Rated Highest in Quality.
ASE Certified Technicians, AAA Approved Auto Repair, and we are a Certified Gold Shield Station. Licensed with the Bureau of Automotive Repair (Automotive Repair Dealer, Smog Check Station, Official Brake and Official Lamp Station)

Tracy Renee’s Specialties:

Our ASE certified technicians are trained and certified in engine repair, suspension and steering, brakes, electrical/electronic systems, heating and air conditioning, engine performance, and automotive advanced engine performance. All of our technicians are certified smog technicians licensed to perform emission inspections, diagnostics and smog repairs. We assist consumers with emission related repairs through the Consumer Assistance Program--need more details, visit our website.

    Tracy has been in the Automotive Industry over 25 years and through her desire to bring a better product to the automotive customer, Gene's Auto Repair was formed in 1994. As a woman owned and run facility, Tracy knew she had her work ahead of her, but through diligence, perseverance and dedication to her customers, Gene's Auto Repair has serviced the community for almost 15 years. While she is very involved in the Automotive Industry, as a member of ASCCA, ASA, CSSARA, it’s sometimes hard to understand where she finds the time for anything else. As a wife of 25 years and mother to five, plus assorted animals it's hard to see where she finds the time for business too, but she does. “Weekends are for family, friends and relaxing, while weekdays are for work”, which is were you’ll find her for what seems like 24/7. And when she is not doing family things on the weekends, Tracy attends many different events supporting and effecting the Automotive Industry. In effort to improve her customer and business skills, she even found the time to attend a two year business program through the University of Phoenix which helps her deal with the day-to-day operations of her business. She is a ESI certified service advisor, is AMI accredited in several areas dealing with automotive management, and has attended and continues to attend many educational courses throughout the last 15 years relating to the Automotive Industry, put on by: AMI, Elite and ESI. Tracy's commitment to excellence is shown everyday in her compassion for her customers, their vehicles and all the members of “TEAM” Gene's Auto Repair.
