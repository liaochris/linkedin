Andrzej Krawczyk’s Summary

Native Polish, fluent English and good Russian speaking qualified attorney-at-law with full court certificate running his law firm in Warsaw, Poland. More than 11 years of professional exprenience in several fields of law. Focused on civil, corporate, commercial law, sophisticated business law, several aspects of EU law, litigation, debt collection, VAT recovery based to 8th/13th EU Directive. Acting for and on behalf of companies and individuals before the courts, tax authorities and other bodies including comprehensive assistance for non Polish clients in respect of establishing clients' commercial activity in Poland. Ensuring professional legal assistance on Russian jurisdiction (together with top tier Russian law firm), ensuring alternative finacing on LSE/AIM (together with Nomad affiliated on LSE). Close cooperation with law firm from Cyprus and advisory firm from Duchy of Liechtenstein in respect of an offshore and tax planning.
Publications and lectures: “Management Board obligations in capital companies by the end of the financial year” - Companies Law (2000), “Country jurisdiction - acknowledging and executing foreign courts’ decisions in European Law (civil and commercial matters) by the rules of Council resolution from 22.12.2000” - European Law In Practice (2003)
“Country jurisdiction - acknowledging and executing foreign courts’ decisions in European Law (marital matters and matters between children and parents) by the rules of Council resolution from 29.05.2000” - European Law in Practice (2003), “Insolvency proceedings in EU” - European Law in Practice (2004), “Public contracts in EU Law” - European Law in Practice (2004), „Did Management Boards have to inform about currency speculations?” – “Shareholder” (2009), „If not currency options, so what now? Protection instruments in practice” – (2009), “Business Pulse”, „A way for capital” (2009), “Business Pulse”, Warsaw Stock Exchange Conference
