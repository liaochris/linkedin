Tim Maholm - CPT’s Summary

Human Resource Professional with industry experience in Professional Services, Financial Services, Insurance, and Manufacturing. Comprehensive experience in all phases of Talent Management. Client focused provider of creative solutions and consulting related to Human Resource Management. Experienced in consulting with Senior Management on Human Resources strategy and compliance. Comfortable setting strategy for organizations and large-scale project management. Accomplished in coaching on human resource matters at all organizational levels. Certifications include Certified Performance Technologist (CPT), assessments, and coaching.

Some of my accomplishments include:

- Managing the Performance Management process to a 93%
appraisal completion rate in 2008 from 78% 2007 at Aleris (paper-based system).

- Increasing Training attendance in 2008 by 350% at Aleris with no increase in expenditures by using web-based training to reach employees and managers in 24 plants throughout the United States.

- Participant satisfaction increased by 20%, Manager satisfaction increased by 30%, and attendance increased by 100% due to systematic approach to needs analysis and redesign of Manager training curriculum at Westfield Group.

- Managed $1 million development of 18 eLearning to on schedule delivery (even though development was 2 months behind schedule when I started position) by using project management principles to increase efficiency and reduce scope creep at Complient.

- Decreased development time of all eLearning by establishing QA, testing, and performance evaluation methodologies at Complient.

- Reduced Merger training expenses of Key and McDonald Investments Traders, Brokers, Assistants, and Operations staff by $500,000 through the innovative use of eLearning, Teleconferencing, Video Conferencing to decrease travel and Classroom training time.

- Managed department to a $1 million profit center at Macola Software.
