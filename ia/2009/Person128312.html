Kaushik Srinivasan’s Summary

12 years of experience in Organization, Change, People and Performance consulting. Areas of interests include Organization Design, Talent Management and HR Processes. Kaushik has worked with numerous and across industry clients in providing Human Capital solutions.

Kaushik, is currently a Senior Manager with PricewaterhouseCoopers (PwC)'s People and Change practice. He is the regional head for the West region focusing on building the market, managing client relationships, ensuring projects are effectively executed and People Management.

In his experience with Deloitte, Kaushik has played numerous roles including start-up and leading a service line within Human Capital in Deloitte Consulting (India).

Well-versed with working across global organizations and business cultures, Kaushik brings forth strong technical and project skills.

Kaushik Srinivasan’s Specialties:
