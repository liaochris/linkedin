Barry Johansen’s Summary

Experienced Human Resource Development (OD, T & D) professional; both internal and external consultant. Author of ~20 popular and scholarly publications. I have a PhD in Training & Organization Development, a M.Ed. in Adult Education, as well as other earned and honorary credentials. I have demonstrated skills and taught others the full educational program development process, calculating cost/benefits of training, strategic planning, human resource development, organization change management, and a variety of other areas.

I have over 16 years professional experience, both an external and internal consultant and have demonstrated skills in needs assessment, design, development, implementation, and evaluation of training and OD programs. I have conducted many management development programs on topics such as coaching, effective meeting management, performance management, maintaining a respectful work environment, generational differences in the workplace, and preventing workplace violence. My OD experience includes leading cultural change efforts, managing TQM efforts (including training others in the “quality tools” and philosophy) socio-technical change, team building, strategic planning in HR, and organizational restructuring. I have worked closely with HR departments on a variety of projects including developing legally defensible interviews, job/task analysis, performance management systems (at all levels including executive), the implementation of HRIS systems (PeopleSoft), and the development of policies and procedures. Recently I led a large corporate culture survey/action planning process, corporate employee performance assessment program, eLearning, succession planning, regulatory compliance (as it applies to training), and other related programs.

I'm seeking consulting, part-time, or telecommuting opportunities within the areas of training and development, OD, and using computer/technologies for learning (WebCT, Articulate, Second Life, etc.)
