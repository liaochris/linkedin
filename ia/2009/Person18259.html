Antonio Navas’s Summary

I was born in sunny Malaga south of Spain, alter finishing my engineering studies in electronics and robotics (1997) I moved to Madrid an worked as an international consultant in the telecommunication industry. I worked in several countries (Spain, Poland, Germany…), having different roles as Technical project manager and Product manager (I was responsible for launching new 3G product and services in Vodafone Germany from 2002 to 2005).

In 2005 me and my family moved back to Spain from Germany and started my own company with other partners. This company was dedicated to consultancy services and product development, always in the telecommunication industry. We worked for all the mobile operators in Spain. During this period of time we started developing a new concept of “mobile internet”, we believed that the internet mobile experience is different form the standard internet experience. Following this idea we created a product named Kimia and in June 2006 we did a spin off from the previous company and created Kimia dedicated to software product development in the internet mobile area.

Currently we are 25 professionals working in Kimia and we have deployed our technology in 5 tier 1 operators and expanding our concept and business in more than 20 other operators.
