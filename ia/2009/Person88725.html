Grace Cherian’s Summary

    Activities and Societies:
    I loved studying philosophy, psychology, political science and sociology. My absolutely favourite subject was "Religious Concepts in Imaginative Literature." We studied T.S.   • McMaster University

    B.Sc. (Partial) , Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Algebra, Genetics , 1974 — 1975

    Activities and Societies:
    I automatically studied physics, mathematics and sciences in my first year of university. As an East Indian, I realized that I was just trying to fulfill my father's expectations that I become a doctor.

    As soon as I became aware of this, I switched studies the following year.

  • Clarkson Secondary School, Mississauga

    Mathematics, Physics, Biology, Chemistry 1972 — 1974

    Activities and Societies:
    I arrived in Canada in September 1972. Because my teachers in Malacca had laid an excellent foundation, I did extremely well in school so much so that in June 1974, I won an Honors' Scholar Award for Five Years of Outstanding Academic Achievement. But I was in that school for only two school terms.

  • Convent of the Holy Infant Jesus, Malaysia

    English, History, Science, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, English, Malay 1965 — 1972

    It was very special attending the Convent of the Holy Infant Jesus (CHIJ) in Malacca. My classmates and I were together in the same class from age six to 17 when I immigrated to Canada.

    The nuns (most of whom came from Ireland) who taught us dedicated themselves to laying an excellent foundation for our education.

    It was because of them that I did so well at school when I arrived in Canada.

    Activities and Societies:
    Choral Society, the Literary & Debating Society.
