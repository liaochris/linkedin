Sassy Golf’s Summary

This Sassy Golf glove is created for you, by me, a real golfer. I‘ve been playing golf for 15 years, love spending the day on a beautiful course, and am always challenging myself to improve my game and have fun out there!

Basically, I grew tired of wearing a man's golf glove sized for a woman. Originally, the gloves were created for my own enjoyment until other ladies took notice and wanted one too! Think of this accessory as your favorite new glove/bracelet and, tried and true, it will not affect your game. The velocity of the swing keeps the featherweight charms in place and you won't feel a thing -- except Sassy!

I hope you will enjoy your Sassy glove as much as I do and be prepared to hear from other women “Where did you get that?” They truly are stylish and each glove is handmade and one of a kind, just like you. Thanks for visiting the site and I would love to hear from you.

Best Wishes,
Elizabeth
