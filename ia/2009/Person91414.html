Gwyn Headley’s Summary

Committed to opening access to all areas and to breaking down barriers of class, prejudice, bias and closed shops. Founded fotoLibra, the first open access image library, which has democratized the €1.2 billion picture library business. Active in publishing, property, photography, typography and heritage conservation. Has written five books on architecture, two on London, two on graphic design (The Encyclopaedia of Fonts and Fabulous Fonts) and three guide books.

Gwyn Headley’s Specialties:

We have a high speed, high quality and robust proprietary Digital Asset Management system backed up with an ecommerce engine for secure multiple sale and deliveries of images and documents from aggregated sources. The entire system can be white labeled for use by a variety of corporations and organizations.
