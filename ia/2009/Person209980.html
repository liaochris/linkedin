Sara Iqbal’s Summary

DESIGN is everywhere. HUMAN is the best example of "Good" Design where form follows function in its most lucid fashion.

Where God has been able to create, solely, Good Design requires a collaboration of minds, hands and eyes of CLIENTS, conceptual designers, strategists, artists, writers, web developers, printers, and a host of other vendors.

Above all, it needs good AESTHETICS, which I have tried to integrate in all my projects.
