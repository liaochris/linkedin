Deb Neuman’s Interests:

I am all about anything to do with small business. I have owned my own and counseled many! I truly enjoy helping folks who want to start their own businesses or need assistance with an existing business. I have a background in journalism and enjoy "getting and telling the story". I once wrote feature articles for the Bar Harbor Times and have been published in several magazines. Currently, I write a small business column for The Maine Edge... see: www.themaineedge.com Broadcasting and performing is also a lifelong passion. I was all set to pursue acting or broadcasting professionally, when the entrepreneurship bug really hit me! Now it feels like my paths and passions are finally coming together!
