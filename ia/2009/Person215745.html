Shaun Ritchie’s Summary

Shaun co-founded his current venture, Neutron Interactive in 2007 after selling a direct placement recruiting company he co-founded in 2002 called The DriversAgent Network. Neutron Interactive is a rapidly-growing online marketing and lead generation company focusing on the career development and education industries. Before launching these companies, Shaun worked in Mexico City for the American Chamber of Commerce of Mexico as a marketing manager where he oversaw marketing and placement efforts of its monthly magazine, Business Mexico, one of the top English-language business magazines in Latin America. Shaun has served as a volunteer associate for the University Venture Fund, an $18 million student-run venture capital organization, and as a liaison for a UVF portfolio company. Shaun earned a BA in Marketing and Advertising from BYU, where he won Best in Show and First Place (Print Category) at the university-wide advertising contest in 2001. He received his MBA in finance and accounting from the University of Utah in May of 2008.

Shaun Ritchie’s Specialties:

Marketing and advertising strategy, small business accounting and back office, internet marketing and lead generation, financial modeling and analysis.
