LA Sports4Kids’s Summary

LA Sports4Kids supplies alternative sporting, fitness and anti obesity products and solutions to the education sector.

Our aim is to encourage children to become more physically active, with particular emphasis on children marginalized by traditional school sporting activities.

Our ethos is "Play in Mind, Exercise in Body", which we complement with a range of innovative solutions and products designed for use in primary school, secondary school and further education environments. We are proud to work in partnership with the Government's Change4Life programme and the National Obesity Forum.

With ever increasing concerns over childhood obesity and the related health issues, effective new ways of combating this national problem are imperative.

LA Sports4Kids brings a fresh approach to solving these issues with non-competitive, non-gender or age specific, clinically proven ways to involve children in calorie burning exercise. Most importantly, we feel children enjoying exercise is the key factor.

Our Mission Statement:

"To supply high quality sports and related products that have an immediate impact on the targetted sector, at prices the sector deems cost effective."
