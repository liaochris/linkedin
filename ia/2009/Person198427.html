Rick Balsiger’s Summary

Rick is President of Balsiger Partners, a national consultancy specializing in Executive Search, Organizational Design and Brand Integration.

Prior to forming the company in 2003, Rick spent 25 years on the corporate side in executive leadership and senior management roles and has extensive experience in all aspects of marketing, advertising, brand integration, sales management, strategic & product planning, organizational development, corporate restructurings--all within diverse organizational environments.

He has been fortunate to have worked and consulted for Top Fortune 500 companies as well as smaller more entrepreneurial organizations and has coached and counseled hundreds of candidates.

Rick's primary executive search focus ranges from positions in General Management, Marketing and Sales, to Engineering, Manufacturing and Supply Chain--from C-level to Senior Manager.

Rick has authored numerous articles on organizational development, brand integration and job search.

He has also recently written a book for job candidates entitled "Branding You, Marketing You: A Guide to Maximizing Job Search Success" published by Xlibris and available at Barnes & Noble.com and Amazon.com.

Here's what Dave Opton, Founder & CEO of ExecuNet had to say about the book:

“I could list at least ten reasons why I would recommend this book, but to steal a line from Letterman, the #1 reason is because it’s REAL. In just 120 very readable pages Rick Balsiger helps the job seeker attack the search process the way it should be – the marketing of a product - and given his unique background as both a marketing/branding expert as well as an executive search consultant the knowledge comes together smoothly, easily and powerfully. There is no ego trip here, just very sound advice and the ‘hows’ to go with it."
Dave Opton, Founder & CEO of ExecuNet
