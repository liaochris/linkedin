Neil LaChapelle’s Summary

I've had 7 or so years of full-time study in philosophy, plus 10 years experience in web-based course design for higher education, almost entirely in management-related subjects. What my employers didn't know is that they were paying me to continue my studies, since I learned as much from the courses I designed as from the courses I took!

My understanding of the design disciplines (rhetorical, graphic, experiential, narrative, informational, instructional...) and of management studies has all been integrated with the breadth and depth a philosophical education affords. My professional goal is just to apply this capacity to advance worthy goals.
