Jahna Jacobson’s Summary

Creating an authentic storytelling experience expressing the core message of business within the context of current events and media needs through research, tracking and cultivated relationships with the media.

I will go into core of your business, your story and your goals to find the elements that will connect with an audience. After developing this profile, I will monitor the news, current trends, seasonal opportunities and local events to find a place for your business to tell its story.
Sometimes you will have a product or service in place that will be an excellent fit for an opportunity. At other times, I may be able to suggest a marketing idea based on the news and events related to your business. It may be information that comes from the inside of the business, or it can be more general information or insight based on industry, seasonal or local events branded with your company’s name and energy.

Jahna Jacobson’s Specialties:

Reporting, story development, research, copy writing, script writing, photo shoot and illustration production, pop culture analysis, trend watching, making the big picture small and vice versa.
