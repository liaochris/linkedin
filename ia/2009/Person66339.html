Doug Cloud’s Summary

--Logo-Identity--
I have a special knack for developing logos and brands. I can see what the company or individual needs and then relay that insight into a finished design. Slogans are also one of my specialties - I can envision a tag line or slogan to match the brand and the essence of what the company or individual is about.

--Character Design--
I started drawing characters when I was young, inspired by the comics of Charles Schultz and Walt Kelly. I developed my own comic strip called Little Chicago in which life revolved around a group of critters who lived in a park in the middle of Chicago. The comic never went anywhere, but this experience helped me to draw my own characters for clients later on.

--Illustration--
I learned about illustrating while working as a technical illustrator for a couple years. The technical drawings were limiting as they had no personality. When I started drawing my own illustrations later for web and product designs I gave them their own unique character. The technical aspects of a product are fine, but once you infuse the design with personality it becomes more memorable.
