Meghana Kamdar’s Summary

Entrepreneur and startup newbie.
In 2009, I started an online business, www.OneEasyVisa.com, your one-stop resource for all your travel requirements, visas, passports, and travel health.

Building my own company is my greatest adventure yet. In a short time, I learned how to setup and design my own website, monitor website traffic, create a marketing plan, and develop a successful monetization theme (that is currently being implemented). I have greater plans of introducing ways to bring people together through group travel and social media.

Previously, I have worked in the non-profit and public sectors extensively. I plan to continue to work in philanthropy and foster positive social change - one day bridging my city planning/ economic development background with my desire for conscious business and entrepreneurship.
