Steve Ward’s Summary

Having spent over 15 years within the Commercial & Professional recruitment industry across the South East and London, I have set up the Cloud Nine Recruitment Group to create a network of independent, professional and highly experienced recruiters to provide a service in the way I have learnt clients wanted be treated - and offer them the flexibility, accessibility and a tailored consultancy that cannot be wholly achieved from a corporate recruitment firm.
It allows all of us to possess the life balance and professional balance that I craved - ensuring the focus is on individual client needs and not the corporate numbers game.
I am keen to work with businesses with ambition and potential for excellent jobseekers and develop strong ongoing business links and partnerships.

Cloud Nine predominantly covers London and the South East, however will undertake national/regional recruitment campaigns on a project by project basis. We are also looking for like-minded recruitment professionals to work as part of the Cloud Nine network across the UK.
