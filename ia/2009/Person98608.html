  • Chief Operating Officer

    Ideactive Consulting

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Information Technology and Services industry)

    January 2000 — July 2007 (7 years 7 months)

    Ideactive was an out and out Internet applications and solutions provider and from a humble beginning the company steadily grew to 40 odd people. As a founder member of a startup my role was multifaceted:

    - Overall Project management and delivery responsibilities
    - Led the HR division by setting up the entire recruitment process right from sourcing thru screening, evaluation to training and induction.
    - Driving new relationships with offshore partners/customers
    - Handling day to day operations

    Somewhere along the way the downturn hit, we downsized, scraped thru the financial crisis, did some organisational restructuring, and started all over again!

  • Senior Partner

    Cynosure Media Solutions Pvt. Ltd

    (Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    November 1995 — December 1999 (4 years 2 months)

    This was an interesting concept and probably a little ahead of its time at that point of time. We put up electronic hoardings with full motion video capability in public marketplaces. It was a great idea in the beginning as corporates and media houses jumped onto the idea and we had our hands full. And trust me, these hoardings were truly the Cynosure of all eyes at these places.
    My primary role in Cynosure was to head sales and marketing while driving relationships with televisions channels, media buying houses, advertising agencies and corporates.
    The start was great and the rise meteoric but by the end of 1999, we were facing too many operational potholes and had to wind up the business. It was around the time when the Internet was emerging as a strong force in India and we decided to make a switch and jumped onto the Internet bandwagon.
