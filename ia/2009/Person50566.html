Cristen Thrasher’s Summary

Refractive surgery coordinator with three years prior experience as a certified ophthalmic assistant. I have worked directly with most current ophthalmic diagnostic technology. Focus on preliminary examinations and calculations for LASIK, PRK, CK (conductive keratoplasty), and RLE (refractive lens exchange) candidacy and treatment.

I am also Public Relations Director for an annual entertainment and educational convention held in Knoxville, Tennessee, and Accounts Manager for a local computer and gaming store in Crossville, Tennessee.

Due to my achievements in my own personal financial endeavors, I also consider myself a natural at personal finance for young adults, and am currently authoring a book on this subject.
