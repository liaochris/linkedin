Angela Walter’s Summary

With a desire to be an entreprenuer, I have taken my passion for silver jewelry and applied my "book knowledge" through an educational background in communications & business and skillsets learned through Corporate America, including General Electric, to grow a successful jewelry business that blesses other women by building self-confidence with fashion, fun, and financial freedom.

Coined Silpada Designs, my company is the original and fastest-growing direct-seller of stunning sterling silver jewelry in the industry. This unique, hip-classic collection is highlighted by the use of .925 sterling silver and semi-precious stones, and covers a range of designs, from classy and casual to sheer elegance! Every piece is hand-crafted and comes with a lifetime guarantee.

- Have friends over & earn hundreds in free jewels
- Host simple fundraisers to earn cash for your organization
- Work part-time hours & earn thousands in free jewelry & cash, tropical vacations, and national recognition as a rep for just $199 start-up.
