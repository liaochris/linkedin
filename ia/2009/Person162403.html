    Bruker Daltonics, a Bruker Corp.company, (NASDAQ: BRKR), develops and provides novel life science tools based on mass spectrometry. Diverse technology platforms integrate MALDI-TOF, MALDI-TOF/TOF, (Q-q-) FTMS, FTICR, ESI-Ion Trap, & ESI-TOF mass spectrometers with sample processing systems and productivity-enhancing software for life science applications like proteomics, metabolomics, metabonomics, and small molecule research.
