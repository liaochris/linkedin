Lisa Salters’s Summary

Capital Concierge, based in Tampa, Florida is a firm which specializes in offering corporate and executive concierge services to individuals and corporations.

We offer a variety of programs that can be enrolled into a company's current benefits package. Several of these programs are at no-cost to the employer. Services such as errands, dry cleaning pick-up and delivery, shopping, and waiting for the cable man are just some of the services offered.

"Studies have shown that it takes an employee nearly two hours to take care of personal business on company time." (Circles 2001). "Lending a hand to assist employees with their personal business will allow them to focus on projects and deadlines which will in turn maximize a company's profits", says President and Founder, Lisa Salters.

Capital Concierge also provides event planning services. With over ten years of event planning experience combined with the best hospitality connections in the Bay area, we'll help you create an event that your guests will still be talking about next year!

Capital Concierge has served the Tampa Bay area for more than three years. We are fully licensed, insured and bonded and a member of ICEA (International Concierge and Errand Association). For more information, please contact Capital Concierge directly at (813) 376-3350 or visit our website at www.capital-concierge.com.

Lisa Salters’s Specialties:

In today’s competitive work force, employers are faced with the challenge of finding new ways to recruit and retain quality employees. When conducting job searches, employees seek employers that offer an attractive benefits package. By offering a tailored concierge program as part of your employee benefits package, employers will help reduce stress in the lives of their employees. Your employees will become increasingly more productive, more loyal and employee morale will soar!
