Michael Van Cise’s Summary

After graduating Magna Cum Laude from the University of Georgia School of Law, I joined Scroggin & Co., a boutique estate, business and tax planning firm located in historic Roswell, Georgia. While at Scroggin & Co., I have assisted clients with estate planning needs ranging from simple wills to complex estate plans.
I am knowledgeable about federal transfer taxes (gift, estate, and generation skipping transfer taxes) and routinely assist clients in minimizing transfer tax cost and helping clients meet their goals for supporting family and charitable causes, and minimizing post-death conflicts.
I have assisted numerous clients with non-citizen spouses in establishing estate plans to address the special rules which apply to transfers to non-U.S. citizen spouses. I have also assisted many clients in second marriages who wish to support their spouse, but ensure that assets pass to their children (from a previous marriage) at the spouse's death.
Assisting clients with the disposition of retirement plan assets is another part of my daily practice. With the special tax rules that apply to retirement plans, it is important that clients understand their options and understand the ways to defer taxes while still satisfying their overarching goals for family. I have drafted conduit trusts, assisted clients with completion of beneficiary designation forms, and assisted clients establishing trusteed IRA's.
My practice also includes a considerable amount of probate and estate administration work. I have represented clients in standard probate procedures as well as with year's support petitions, petitions for no administration necessary, and other probate matters.
I aspire to be an excellent and trusted advisor and resource to my clients at all times and to add value to all my client relationships.
