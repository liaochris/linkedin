Phil Tapsell’s Summary

TechVenture Solutions operates at the heart of entrepreneurial life in the UK offering business critical services to technology investors and entrepreneurs. The company provides strategic consulting services to new technology companies, working with them to identify and secure new customers, build profitable revenue streams and generate strong ROI.

TechVS's success is measured by the value they add to the enterprise projects they work with in terms of their ability to survive and grow thereafter.

The primary client base for TechVS are venture capitalists and other investors who have funded technology companies and their products.

Such investors often need to deploy a highly qualified business resource, such as TechVS, into the businesses they are funding so as to maximise the chance of good return on their investment. Such core clients are normally sophisticated, savvy, wealthy and technology literate.

A secondary client base for TechVS are technology company CEOs and managing directors who need rapid access to the powerful sales and marketing skills and expertise provided by TechVS.
