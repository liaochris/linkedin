Judy Stadt’s Summary

People consider me funny, intelligent, witty, complex & stylish, my writing is about real situations poignantly delivered with life changing, hope filled & uplifting messages.

As a child of the 40s, my exposure was to fabulous torch songs and period piece films. Aware of my body as a tool from the age 5, started dancing and elocution lessons, studied characterization, the way actors talked, walked, carried themselves, dressed, danced, sung, and even laughed. Continue every day to work on how characters grow and change and how to incorporate grace, intelligence, beauty/comedy into my own life.. to dazzle audiences by design. I consider my being able to act in more than 80 plays, portraying all those different characters a gift and this gift has given birth to a dream. Audiences think of me as The American Judi Dench and I aspire to realize that dream.

Judy Stadt’s Specialties:

Skills include: Acting, singing, producing, directing, playwriting, graphic design & public speaking. Comedy added to my vocal presentations was a natural for me, as a designer, designed sets, costumes, jewelry and accessories etc . Marketed shows and successfully created the first Dinner Theatre in the Afternoon! in Rockland County, NY and entertained thousands of seniors throughout the tri-state area.

Networking, marketing, public relations, sales.
