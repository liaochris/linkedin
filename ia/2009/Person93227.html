Che de Vega’s Summary

I have pioneered and supervised for 5 years the first corporate based Metabolic Syndrome Program in our country. We were recognized as One of the Top Health and Nutrition Programs in the Philippines in 2005.

The program was initiated to keep a healthy and productive workforce and to cut-down on the cost of hospitalization by minimizing the incidence of lifestyle diseases (cancer, coronary artery disease and stroke which are the top cause of morbidity in the institution) and other associated co-morbidities of obesity, through healthy eating habits and proper nutrition and exercise program.

Che de Vega’s Specialties:

Corporate based nutrition consultancy ; main advocacy is the prevention of the Metabolic Syndrome
