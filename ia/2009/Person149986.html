Maria Espino, SharePoint Intranet Architect’s Summary

Most companies start an intranet deployment project with programmers and technology executives sitting around the room guessing what may be necessary to employees. Although that may get you a deployed system that works flawlessly within the requirements of I.T., it may fail miserably if you do not populate it with what users truly need, rather than guessing. What you need is an advocate for your employees that has the technical and usability skills to define what your employees really need and deliver a beautiful, usable intranet with content structure, departmental responsibility, and ease of use along with a working infrastructure. You shouldn't settle for one or the other. The importance of good internal communications systems and processes cannot be overlooked if you intend to grow your business nationally, or even further, worldwide.

I am an Intranet Implementation Architect and User Experience Specialist based in South Florida that can travel anywhere to help your organization design, plan, and deploy an intranet solution based on Microsoft Office SharePoint that will increase overall productivity as well as the collaborative efforts across functional groups. But to be successful, it must be done right. I can guarantee that the job will be done right and within your given timeframe and budget. Having over 12 years of experience in usability and design coupled with 5 years of information technology experience in intranet system architecture, project management, deployments, and extensibility makes me one of only a handful of experts in the entire country to make the dream of a seamless, successful intranet for your employees a reality. I really can do it all!

I invite you to tour my profile and send me emails with your questions and comments. I look forward to meeting other professionals looking to expand the way they do business from within, starting with the people that are the heart and soul of your business --- your employees.
