Greg Prior’s Experience

  • VP Engineering, CTO, etc.

    Greg Prior, Optics Consultant

    (Research industry)

    January 2009 — Present (8 months)

    Consulting in optics design, especially for projection TV, curved mirrors, thin chassis TV, and similar.

  • Chief Optics Scientist

    Silicon Optix

    (Privately Held; Semiconductors industry)

    April 2003 — October 2008 (5 years 7 months)

    R&D work to create new and unusual applications for the Geo and other eWarp chips. Created four patents for ultra-thin rear projection systems, and invented a method of creating curved mirrors to accomplish making these thin systems. These systems are capable of thicknesses of 1/10th the diagonal (6" thick for 60" diagonal) and thinner.

  • VP Engineering

    Digital Reflection

    (Consumer Electronics industry)

    1997 — July 2001 (4 years)

    Created, planned and budgeted up to $14 million per year in technology projects for this  tradeshow-driven startup. Directed the development of five internal engineering development teams—mechanical engineering, optical design, ASIC design, microdisplays, electronics—as well as external groups. Established strategic product development plans to deliver working prototypes to nationwide customers in tightly compressed time frames.
    Capitalized on an existing projection engine to deliver a prototype HDTV in three months. Interacted with team members, potential customers and suppliers in Korea, China and Japan. Drove market and customer attention to new technologies under development.

    Authored/co-authored about 20 patents which were not followed up on after the company closed. Co-authored electrode-less lamp which was granted.

  • VP Engineering
