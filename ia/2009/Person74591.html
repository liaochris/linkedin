Eric McNeal’s Summary

I am a licensed architect in the state of Colorado with approximately ten years of experience. I've worked on a myriad of project types, but I am most passionate about residential projects and anything with a focus on sustainability.

My professional mission is to transform S7g Architecture into an architectural firm that incorporates sustainability practices into every facet of the firm and lives by the philosophy of the triple bottom line - people, planet, and profit.

Eric McNeal’s Specialties:
