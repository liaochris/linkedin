Dale Eller’s Summary

Serves as the Executive Director for the following associations:
• Pennsylvania Burglar & Fire Alarm Association
• New York Burglar & Fire Alarm Association
• Installation Quality Certification Program
• Pennsylvania Association of Fire Equipment Distributors
• Association of Women in Security Electronics
• Erie Youth Soccer Association

Serves as the Director of the NBFAA Education and Standards Department.

Past Chapter Chairman of the Lake Erie Chapter of ASIS. Past ex officio member of the Board of Directors of the NBFAA.

Featured writer for the special Security Technician supplement for Security Dealer & Integrator magazine from 2007 to 2008, and became a regular columnist for Security Dealer & Integrator in April 2009 with the launch of his column "In The Field", focused on issues of concern to installing dealers and integrators.

Authored the following industry related courses / seminar sessions:

• Understanding the New PA Statewide Building Code (1 hour webinar)
• Understanding the PA Uniform Building Code for Fire Alarm Systems (2 hours)
• Understanding the NY State Building Code for Fire Alarm Systems (2 hours)

• Quality Control Initiatives for Improving Your Business – “Profiles from the Installation Quality Certification Program” (2 hours)

• Are You Certifiable?? “Understanding Certification Programs in the Security Industry” (1 hour)

• Troubleshooting Applications from Soup to Nuts – “Understanding The Troubleshooting Mindset” (1.5 hours)

• NBFAA NTS Video System Technologies Course (14 hours) – Course Development Committee Member / SME

• NBFAA NTS Level One Certified Alarm Technician Course Update (22 hours) - Course Development Committee Member / SME

• NBFAA NTS Troubleshooting, Service & Maintenance Course (14 hours) - Course Development Committee Member / SME
