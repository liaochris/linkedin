Neil Campbell’s Summary

Neil has always had a passion for sales, marketing, training and personal development and has used and developed these skills throughout his career.

He has a wide and varied career after leaving university he worked as a gamekeeper and steel erector before moving to Germany where he worked in security and facilities management with the British Army and he also worked in international coaches between Germany and the UK. He then travelled through Europe and South East Asia to Australia where he worked in sales in the microfilming industry.

On returning to the UK he continued his sales career selling vacuum cleaners, photocopiers, washroom hygiene, contract services (security, cleaning and catering), industrial cleaning and maintenance equipment.

He then joined the pharmaceutical industry and has worked for a number of blue chip companies in a wide variety of therapy areas engaging with customers from GP surgeries right through to the Scottish Government.

This made a natural progression through to Healthcare IT. A lot of the challenges faced by the Health Services are the ones about having the right information at the point of care. Improving patient care through technology is not just a nice idea. It is the only way we are going to tackle the demographic time bomb.
