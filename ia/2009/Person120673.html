Joe Escobedo’s Summary

Joe Escobedo is an up-and-coming marketing expert for the Chinese market. He has been published in numerous business magazines and high-tech industry publications where he frequently offers advice regarding marketing, trade, and entrepreneurship.

Presently, Joe is a marketing manager at Boxinves Consulting Company and editor at Business Tianjin Magazine, which is one of the leading business publications in North China. His main responsible is to manage the marketing, IT, and media team create growth opportunities.

Joe has interviewed over 100 MNC senior executives and industry leaders. He also possesses conversational abilities in Mandarin Chinese.

His current projects include:
- Co-writing a business plan on natural gas compressors
- Consulting local companies on growth strategies
- Acting as a liaison between US producers and Chinese buyers
- Assisting US companies source Chinese suppliers.
