Larry Scott’s Summary

Legal recruiter with over 25 years of experience in law, accounting, and international human resources. My focus is the placement of attorneys and practice groups at law firms. I also work with corporations on in-house positions. As a former practicing attorney (with a national firm and as in-house counsel) and as a Principal with a global accounting firm, I bring a unique perspective to the hiring and recruiting process. In addition to my work in legal recruiting, I am the chair of the Houston International HR Roundtable, one of the largest international human resource educational and networking groups. See http://www.hrhouston.org/displaycommon.cfm?an=13. My international experience includes assignments in Hong Kong and Hanoi, Vietnam. I speak basic Vietnamese.
