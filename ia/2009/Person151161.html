Marja Ruigrok’s Summary

Ruigrok | NetPanel is a full service research company, certified with a Research Keurmerk [based on ISO 20252 & 26362]. In our vision, we focus upon the three P’s, Pleasure, Personal and Pragmatic. Pleasure for the client, who’s able to use the results directly and pleasure for the respondents and for us. Our strategy is personal: focused on a close cooperation and results that are suitable for immediate implementation into daily practice. We are an innovative agency. We believe in the continuously evolving possibilities of online research. Our expertise is in online and qualitative research.

Marja Ruigrok’s Specialties:

Our core business is market research and communication. We specialise in qualitative research and research using the internet.
