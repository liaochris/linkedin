Chad Wilson’s Summary

A mystery to some, understood by few, and recognized by many, Chad Jonathan Wilson remains true to his purpose in life. Chad J. Wilson has become a valuable contributor to inspiring the lives of many and his own. He says that his "true empowerment has come from accepting all of the opportunities God, his parents, mentors and true friends have afforded" him. He says that by finding and understanding God's purpose that he has set before him, to inspire the lives of others, has truly grounded and set him forward on a course of success that will ultimately benefit all of mankind alike.

Currently, Chad engages in educating and cultivating his mind with exploring his various talents that he has been blessed with. By studying Electrical Engineering at Tuskegee University in his hometown of Tuskegee Alabama, a nexus of talent in the black community, Chad believes that he has found a subject that has challenged all of his mind, body and spirit to finally become a "master" of his craft. Finishing his studies at Tuskegee University, he is now pursuing a career in Music & Film to become a producer/director and exhibit his visions of life, love, and loss while inspiring others to become their own vehicles of success and experience. He says that he has always had a "soundtrack" to his life and views his life as an ongoing "movie" and wants to share this movie with all who want to view it.

Chad has now returned to school studying Non-Profit Management with hopes of obtaining his MBA. Coupled with his high passion for education and entertainment, his ultimate goal is to become a philanthropist and become a Foundation for all who desire and dream to become "exceptional beings" and help others to realize and reach their fullest potential that God has created them for. He wants to use his experiences in the industry to help young at-risk individuals have a chance to show the world their true talents and not allow them to be products of their environment.
