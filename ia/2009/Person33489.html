Brian Holmes’s Summary

Brian Holmes is a Senior Software Engineer Consultant currently working for Universal Mind. His background is in developing large enterprise level applications. Most notably, he helped architect an internal ERP system for the Woodward Governor Company from 2005-2007. Brian is the founder of SmashedApples which is a cloud computing development platform that aims for seamless integration with cloud computing technologies such as Amazon Web Services and Google App Engine. His goal is to help companies continually leverage technology to bring down costs and increase revenue.

Brian Holmes’s Specialties:

Flex, ActionScript, Cloud Computing, BlazeDS, WebORB, PyAMF, Java, C#, Python, Cold Fusion, Amazon Web Services, Google App Engine, MySQL, Oracle, SimpleDB.
