Joshua Waldman’s Summary

I specialize in helping job seekers use social media to become the obvious choice for their next employer. Social media is simply a tool we use to achieve a purpose, and for job seeking, that end is an interview, which leads to a better career.

It is far too easy to fall in love with the technology for it’s own sake, so I guide job seekers through an effective job search process involving online reputation building, confidence building, resume building and interview preparation.

For recruiters, I help their candidate stand out from the crowd by empowering clients with a powerful online strategy and inner confidence. My clients are able to turn an interrogator into an instant best friend by becoming more trustworthy and genuine both online and offline.
