Alexandra Zack’s Summary

My name is Alex and I am a recent graduate at Rochester Institute of Technology. I graduated with honors from RIT with a BFA in New Media Design and Imaging, and had a liberal arts concentration in American Sign Language. In the New Media program at RIT I learned interactive design, interface design, graphic design, 3D, motion graphic design, actionscript, HTML, and CSS. I am experienced in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, After Effects, Cinema 4D, Dreamweaver, and Flash.

Outside of school, I interned at MTV during the summer of 2008, doing their on-air graphic design for TRL, and assisting in the production design. From September 2008-2009 I worked at IBM, doing interface design and user experience at the RIT Collaboration Lab on campus. There I worked with a team of developers helping develop various plug-in's and programs for IBM's Lotus Software.

Aside from school I was an active member of Alpha Sigma Alpha for four years. During that time I have attended various leadership conferences, participated in multiple councils, and was president in 2007. During my time as president I gained not only leadership experience, but time management, communication skills, and how to work with an organization of over forty girls who were very diverse. Not only that, but during my time as president, we received the President's Cup from RIT, one of the most prestigious Greek Awards, acknowledging exceptional success in all aspects of greek life.

Now that I am graduated, I am looking to obtain a job doing interactive design. I would love to work in a team environment, so I can not only contribute something to a company, but be able to continuously learn new things, and have new experiences in both design and life!
