Sonya Smith’s Summary

I am an energetic person who spends long hours in the newsroom and on the dance floor. I began my journey by starting a newspaper in fifth grade - publishing for open house and back to school night at Gompers Elementary. Through middle school, high school, college and now my professional career I have kept a passion for providing accurate information to the public through journalism.
