Brace Barber’s Summary

Bringing new core armor technology for military and police to market. Working with industry leaders for refinement and customization of proprietary material technology and processes. Pre-IPO

Company has developed advance compression molding technology which makes ultra-high end composites possible. By combining materials which have never before been possible, the strength to weight ratio for our composites far exceed existing solutions. We are currently looking to the automobile and aerospace industries for incorporation of this paneling into their products in order to reduce weight and increase fuel efficiency.

Continuing to apply leadership in the non-linear business environment. Incorporating lessons garnered from West Point, 11 years of active service and multiple entrepreneurial endeavors.

Brace Barber’s Experience

  • President/CEO

    Decipherst, Inc.

    (Privately Held; 11-50 employees; Management Consulting industry)

    July 2006 — Present (2 years 10 months)

    Licensing new Ballistic Armor Technology to defense contractors. First new core technology in the armor industry in over 20 years. Decipherst has successfully raised funds and begun the strategic alliance effort.

    Decipherst was organized around the idea that there is abundant opportunity for dramatic technological improvement across multiple industries; armor being one of the first. Our goal is to identify those opportunities and design products that far exceed the performance of the legacy systems.

    The Decipherst design team starts with the proverbial clean sheet of paper, giant white boards, animated discussion and lots of markers to begin the design process. Decipherst’s solutions often bare no resemblance to the current products, but perform in ways and with sometimes surprising advantages that the former line of thinking could never produce.

    All of our work is geared towards helping our clients create significant competitive advantages.
