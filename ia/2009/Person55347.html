Dario Faggioli’s Summary

My current research topic is to investigate in the area of "open systems", i.e. systems made up of hard, soft and non real-time activities.
In particular, I'm presently focusing on soft real-time applications, e.g. video players and multimedia, and in providing some Quality of Service guarantee to them.

I'm interested in exploring existing techniques, and developing new ones as well, in the field of single and multiprocessor real-time systems with the capability of mutual exclusive resource sharing.

I care about both theoretical and real implementation related issues, focusing in particular on using and modifying the Linux kernel.
