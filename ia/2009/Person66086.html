Dorian Burrows’s Summary

The Marketing Help Line is a new development in marketing support delivering a cost effective, alteative option to employing full time marketing professionals yet offering a high level of marketing expertise.

Dorian Burrows is a Quality Assured supplier on the Yorkshire and Humber Suppliers Register

Dorian’s varied experience has encompassed numerous industries giving him a wealth of expertise in different areas. Having worked as Head of Marketing for a major retail company for five and a half years, he set up his own business in 2007 as Owner of a successful Marketing Consultancy.

About the Marketing Help Line:
To deliver a successful business solution we first need to know what you want to achieve, your business goals both short and long term and how you think you can achieve them. We then work with you to define your marketing strategy to achieve those objectives

Developing a successful marketing strategy is all about preparation... by understanding your needs, and the needs of your customer we ascertain the strategic direction that helps to stimulate and facilitate customer demand that builds success

Successful companies build value through nurturing innovation, developing relationships and creating good customer service. Communicating those benefits to the custome to become a 'top of mind' commodity.

How do we achieve 'top of mind' By getting the communication mix right, making sure that each and every contact point you have with your customers reinforces your brand, delivering the positive image attributes that generates a willingness to buy into your philosophy

The Marketing Help Line's objective is a simple one... To provide our customers with the resources they need, when they need them. To deliver effective marketing solutions that generate success and deliver a real return on their investment.
