Mary Halperin’s Summary

Top performing Interior Designer who can design and sell.

Over 25 years experience in Interior Design, Sales & Facilities Design & Management. Skilled in use of 20/20 & AutoCad. My meticulous attention to detail while developing creative designs has prevented costly errors.

Three years of experience in Kitchen Design with high end clients, custom and semi custom kitchen cabinetry (Bertch Custom, Bertch, Legacy, Medallion, Diamond and Kraftmaid). Projects included both renovation and new custom homes.

Seven years experience in design of Custom Cabinetry & interiors for Opticals.

Sales experience in both commercial and retail furniture, carpeting, ceramic & stone tile and hardwood flooring.

Commercial sales experience includes systems furniture (Steelcase, Herman Miller, Knoll, Allsteel); Facilities Management (GE); Facilities Design (Hoecst-Celanese).

Twenty plus years interior design experience in commercial and facilities design, including work with architectural firms, design firms and commercial furniture dealers.

Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
