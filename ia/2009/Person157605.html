Mark Baker’s Summary

A commercially astute interim manager, highly effective operating in an account management / interface role between business users and IT functions to drive business benefit through the exploitation of existing IT services and new capabilities.

With a specific focus on business intelligence and with substantial proven project management experience in leading the delivery of large, complex IT and change management projects gained over 13 years in the telecommunications, financial services and property sectors.

A strong leader, line manager and coach who is able to promote effective cross-functional working between customer-facing and support areas.

Now working as an independent contractor/consultant through own company.
