Marcos Hack’s Summary

Between 1998 and 2005 my professional focus was on project, implementation and administration of TCP/IP networks, mainly WAN and Internet. In that period I worked with networking infrastructure equipments such as switches and routers of medium to large sizes, as well as application layer systems such as firewalls, VPN, DNS, mail and web servers among others. From 2003 I started to focus on Linux and open-source software based solutions.

In the last five years I have worked on the development of an IP Centrex system over Linux and Java EE platforms, working as Network Architect and Software Engineer, projecting and implementing network and servers infrastructure as well as developing and maintaining software components including non-functional requirements such as performance and high-availability.

My professional goal for next years is to improve my knowledge and experience in software architecture and development for distributed computing systems based on open standards and technologies, with special interest in VoIP, Cloud Computing and Service Oriented Architectures (SOA).
