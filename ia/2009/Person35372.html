Joanna Bryson’s Summary

As an engineer, I provide integration, structure and action selection for intelligent & cognitive systems. These include robotics, intelligent environments, virtual reality and video game agents. My engineering research focus is on making human-like AI easier to build and understand. I've developed Behavior Oriented Design (an AI development methodology) and POSH action selection.

As a scientist, I specialize in understanding distributed intelligence and interacting learning systems, for both individuals and societies. I started out modelling individual intelligence, where I specialize in modular learning systems, everyday action selection and goal arbitration. Recently I have extended this interest to include social cognition and culture --- the evolution and distribution of intelligence through a population.

Joanna Bryson’s Specialties:

Systems design; group leader; education; mentoring; dissemination and exploitation; review and critique.
