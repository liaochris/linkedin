Joy Kennelly’s Summary

The Joy Writer PR & Marketing firm has over 10 years experience publicizing individuals, small companies, producing events and copywriting.

The Joy Writer PR & Marketing services include award-winning online marketing, copywriting and collateral material development, special event production, media relations and strategic planning.

Previous clients include the World Animation Celebration, Penton Media’s Media & Entertainment online film festival, Dallas World Series of Video Games Presented by Intel, Hermosa Beach Christmas Village and more.

Additional experience includes Cinemax and HBO Television Networks while working at Kahn & Jacobs, Sony Pictures Imageworks, Fine Artist Gali Rotstein, Photographer Arlene Bogna, Wealth Manager Steve Bloch of MHB Financial, among others.

Corporate accounts have included The Beverly Center, Acura, the Los Angeles Convention & Visitors Bureau, and the American Institute of Architects.

Joy Kennelly’s Specialties:

Event planning; media relations; marketing materials including press releases, bios, websites, specializing in Los Angeles. Enjoy working with entertainment, travel, art, architecture and consumer products clients.
