Carl Hallard’s Summary

I began working in the digital marketing field 5 years ago.
I had the opportunity to work on both the operationnal aspect (running campaigns, teams, on a day to day basis) and the strategic aspect (marketing audits and recommendations : KPIs, ...).
As a digital marketer, I believe part of what makes a company successful is the equation between both of these aspects.

I graduated from an european business school, HEC School of Management (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HEC_School_of_Management) in partnership with the engineering school, Telecom Paris, (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Telecom_Paris), and I am currently living in France.

I previously obtained a master's degree in IT project management.

I had the opportunity to work at PayPal, in France, being responsible for customers' acquisition through search engines (SEM) and several marketing campaigns.

I had a consulting experience for Orange, where I worked on the conception of a new service (remote access to multimedia data from a cellphone).

I also interned twice in California : for Convera, as a consultant, where I worked on the conception of a new BtoC search engine, and for BravoBrava, where I was in charge of ergonomics and visual projects.

Carl Hallard’s Specialties:

Project Management, Digital Marketing, Performance Marketing (Acquisition, SEM, SEO...), Product Management.
