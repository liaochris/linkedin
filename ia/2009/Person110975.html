Jerome Bertrand’s Summary

Founded GeoGames http://geo-games.eu in 2007 to accommodate the growing market for integration of serious games (light-weight) with 3D web virtual worlds, geo-information and mapping services (aka Mirror Worlds).

Services and products includes: design of mirror worlds geo-browsers, game and simulations design, 3D web navigation (indoor and outdoor 3D routing) for corporate premises, facilities management and resorts presentation. Add ons: located transaction sites (e.g. Hotel booking in 3D scenery), real time tracking, mapping services, mobile and RFID location intelligence.

GeoGames creates and implements premium web 3D solutions and geo-services for large to mid-large companies under the motto: Playing Your World. Co-owner CEO, with marketing, sales and creative direction responsabilities.

Has lead teams as (senior) visual designer at Dutch user interface consultancy http://www.informaat.com. Responsabilities include team growth and in-house coaching of 12 expert co-workers.

Past positions as art director at RSCG/ BIKKER, in emerging new media (CD-rom production and internet websites)

Self-empolyed in designing interactive Medical training titles on the CDi platform for MSD, Bayer.

Had a number of all-round graphic design positions in multimedia, corporate design, packaging and audiovisual design projects.

Lifetime interests and hobbies
Architecture (photography) and audio visual media (art, cinematography and serious gaming) , documentary film making, 3D simulation, Human Computer Interaction, Interaction design, e-learning, music and sailing.
