John Kilbride’s Summary

John started his first web-based business over 15 years ago and considers himself a marketer first, and an entrepreneur second, but both monikers seem to follow him around.

John is an expert in developing marketing strategies for small and medium businesses (SMB's) that are focused, practical, successful, and affordable. He does so along with his team at New Prodigy Marketing Group (http://www.newprodigy.ca).

John believes that every company, home based to enterprise, should have access to the same level of expert marketing resources and enabling technologies such that all businesses have a chance to succeed.

John understands the relationship between sales, marketing, and business development, and is also a leader when it comes to bringing web-based technologies to small businesses, and showing his clients how to avoid "re-inventing the wheel" when it comes to best-practices in today’s new media world.

Who benefits most from John's expertise?

If you are a start-up, home-based, small, or medium business (SMB) owner, principle, c-level executive, director, marketing manager, or sales manager then your company will benefit from John's expertise and the services of New Prodigy (http://www.newprodigy.ca).

If, at a minimum, you want to understand the competitive value of professional marketing and online marketing best-practices then you should review the services provided by New Prodigy (http://www.newprodigy.ca).

John and his team will show you how to market your business so that you win new customers and don't lose existing customers to the competition.

Learn more about how New Prodigy Marketing Group can help you at www.newprodigy.ca.
