Roberto Carraro’s Summary

I'm a New media Designer and a multimedia producer. Direct experience with digital video productions (more then 100 published DVD titles) virtual reality, motion graphics, Web 2.0, Interactive Design. Long experience in New media publishing (more than 200 published CD ROM titles ). Focus on digital culture and learning multimedia application.
Particularly interested in Digital Art and Contemporary Art.

  • Telecom Design srl

    (Internet industry)

    1999 — Present (10 years)

    Roberto Carraro has been working in new media since the 1980's, participating at the first "art planetary network" of XLII VENICE BIENNALE in 1986.
    Roberto Carraro planned important exhibitions on technological innovation for the Smau in Milan, from 1991 to 2001, including the "Information Piazza".
    Since the 1990's Roberto Carraro has published over 100 works on CD ROM and DVD, translated into various languages in many countries and distributed in millions of copies all over the world.

    International acknowledgements include the EMMA award at the Frankfurt book fair of 1995, with the Gospel on CD ROM and Reunion Island in 2005 with "Wonders of the 21st century".

    Now Roberto Carraro works in Telecom Design, an Internet Company He founded with Gualtiero Carraro and Alberto Barzaghi.
    Our core business is multimedia platforms and production for industrial networks. Another important business area is multimedia geo mapping.
