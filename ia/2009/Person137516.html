Laura Fairchild’s Summary

Laura Fairchild is currently the Risk Management Supervisor for 3M’s Motor Vehicle System (3M MVS) group. Laura is in the process of justifying the implementation of a project management office (PMO) in the MVS group to upper management. 3M MVS has many concurrent projects for several state motor vehicle departments and is in need of a PMO. Because of downsizing in 3M MVS Laura is also assisting in writing responses for RFP’s for work in the motor vehicle systems arena. Prior to this position, Laura was a business analyst on the design project for the 3M Driver Record and Issuance Verification Solution (3M DRIVS) product. Laura also managed the implementation for this product at a state motor vehicle department. Laura managed the data conversion, training, and user acceptance testing over a 1 year time frame.

Laura brings to 3M over 15 years of IT experience. Prior to joining 3M, Laura spent over 7 years managing projects while working for IBM on the Xcel Energy (AKA Public Service Company of Colorado) account. Laura managed several high-profile big dollar projects while with IBM. Laura was fortunate to experience a fair amount of success while managing these projects and received numerous awards from both IBM and our client Xcel Energy. Prior to being a project manager at Xcel Laura was a team leader and programmer.

Laura was born in Detroit, MI and raised in Colorado. She received her Bachelor’s in Computer Applications in Chemistry from the University of Colorado in Boulder. Laura received her certification as a Project Management Professional from the PMI Institute in 2002 and currently maintains this certification. Laura is currently working on getting her ITIL certification.
