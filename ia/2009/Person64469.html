Jamie Stanton’s Summary

A designer, developer and SEO consultant based in Dublin, Ireland, I have working on an eclectic variety of projects including government and corporate Intranets, recruitment agencies, car dealers, surgeons, NGOs, music labels, nightclubs, software developers and retailers in Ireland, the UK and internationally. I have worked on over 100 web projects.

Jamie Stanton’s Experience

  • Lead Designer

    Evolution Internet Ltd

    (Internet industry)

    June 2007 — Present (2 years 6 months)

    My primary role at Evolution-Internet is meet with clients and ascertain their requirements for web projects of all scales and propose solutions to their business needs. These included identifying the goals of the site or web application, devising an interface that will lead to the highest number of conversions, and building a design around this.

    My role also involves fine tuning the code of the site itself to be accessible and comprehensible to major search engines, while remaining ethically sound.

    I regularly audit sites and made recommendations of the best course of action for SEO, sometimes involving undoing “black hat” SEO techniques implemented by competitors. Other aspects of my role include project management and client relationship management.

  • Web Designer/Developer (part time)

    Lothian and Borders Fire and Rescue Service

    (Civic & Social Organization industry)

    April 2005 — May 2007 (2 years 2 months)

    At LBFRS I was responsible for conceiving, designing, developing and implementing the Intranet and Internet sites.The intranet system I developed allows departments to upload, edit and manage their own documents.The system was rooted in the 150 year old traditions of the Brigade. I designed a range of XP style icons to simplify the process of finding information. I also wrote help files and training packages to ease the transition between the new and old systems.
    Both sites were developed with accessibility in mind.
