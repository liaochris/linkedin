Pam Ellis’s Experience

  • Creativity coach

    Kaizen Inspired Life

    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    January 2009 — Present (7 months)

    Creativity coaching honors the non-linear process of creativity. It works well with people who are new to coaching as well as with those who have been turned off by goal oriented life coaching in the past. It is a completely different approach.

    IT'S FUN...Coaching creatively

    Emphasis is placed on the pure enjoyment of the process allowing for unlimited possibility in the product.

    It is a gentle, nurturing experience, that fully takes into consideration the rebellious nature of the creative spirit within all of us.

    The process is geared toward all areas of life!

    Although it is highly beneficial for those pursuing a specific creative venture and for those wishing to re-visit an old hobby or passion, and for those who wish to pursue a new creative outlet,

    It is not limited to the arts.

    Every human being is creative...each in our own unique way.

    Painting, Gardening, Cooking, Music, Business, Writing, Pottery, Playfully enjoying Life...the list is endless!
    What is your way?

    Throughout the coaching, focus is placed on: Awareness, Tools and Education, all within the spirit of playful discovery. Emphasis is placed on continual growth!

    Visible blocks are met and hidden ones unearthed. Both are overcome through a gentle approach of compassion, play and awareness building activities.

    You will gain wonderful new tools that you can use in both your specific creative pursuit and in your life as a whole. Because…

    Your life IS your ultimate creation!

    The Kaizen practice of asking small questions, thinking small thoughts, taking small actions, solving small problems, giving yourself small rewards, and identifying small moments is an integral component that is utilized throughout the process. This effectively helps to bypass fear and encourages continuous, steady growth.

    All coaching packages include specialized guided imagery sessions which are tailored specifically for you. These sessions may be taped for you to use whenever you’d like.

  • Owner, Artist, Illustator

    P.A.Ellis Vision Art Studio

    (Fine Art industry)

    January 2004 — Present (5 years 7 months)

    I live and create in my home on Hatzic Island.

    My experience with painting began as a spiritual exercise, but has since grown into an absolute passion, and a wonderful way to express to the outer world what I experience on the inner. It has provided me with tools that I can use in my daily life to create happier, more fulfilling experiences.

    All of my artwork is produced during contemplative meditation. In the right state of mind, visions will appear before me and within me, on the canvas or paper as well as on the screen of my inner sight. I paint what I see with my heart. I know that I am on the right track when the sensation of childlike wonder, inner excitement and the feeling of freedom engulfs me. It is vitally important at this time to completely set aside my ego's critical judgment, so that I may for a moment, step outside of myself and with an open heart and an open mind, allow the spirit to flow freely.

    It is an amazing journey that has connected with other people as well. There is an energy to the images that are produced in this way. If one can truly connect with the spirit of the painting, it results in ones own creative energy being drawn to the surface. The only thing greater than experiencing the internal flow of creative spirit moving through me, is when that same spirit touches someone else through a connection with one of my paintings, inspiring them to express their own creativity in their own individual way.

    If you would like to explore your own creative potential in a deeper way please visit my Coaching / Workshop website at:
