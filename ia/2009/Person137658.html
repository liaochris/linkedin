Laura Lykkegaard Selander’s Summary

Laura Lykkegaard Selander is an entrepreneur, pathfinder and business developer with more than 15 years business experience in South Africa.
With her passion for entrepreneurship, realisation, management and development Laura Lykkegaard Selander has since 1993 established and developed four companies.

Her knowhow, transparent and accessible management style, and business experience has - also incorporating dealings in other parts of Africa - shown to be an attractive asset to Scandinavian companies and investors, who are considering establishing themselves on the South African market.

Laura Lykkegaard Selander is today a sought-after resource and capacity familiar and comfortable with the cultures and customs of South Africa and Africa.
