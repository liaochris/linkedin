Mark Taylor II’s Summary

Often regarded as a prodigy and known for being a jack-of-all-trades, I am a December 2008 graduate of public relations from Florida A&M University. My current goals are to work at a medium or large Public Relations agency, write as a freelance journalist and to write a book. Areas of public relations that I'm passionate about include corporate social responsibility, technology, restuarant/culinary services, branding, project development, event planning and business-to-business endeavors. I enjoy giving back to the community, challenging businesses to do the "right" thing, and make a committment to listen effectively to consumers for organizations I've worked for and am a part of. Challenging myself is something I always strive to do.

Mark Taylor II’s Specialties:

I excel at strategizing and creating innovative concepts. I am knowlegeable in using the internet and identifying social networking Web sites, to include Facebook.com. I also have proficiency in Microsoft Word and PowerPoint. I have a basic understanding of Quark express and Adobe Suite applications. I also have a conversational level of competency in Spanish.
