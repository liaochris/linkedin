Jim Browning’s Summary

Jim Browning is a business executive dedicated to helping people improve their business, networking and leadership skills. As a leader of leaders, I assist people become better in networking, communication and sales.

I have extensive consumer & customer knowledge and I am an avid networker with connections all over the world.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
My career has been focused in apparel & fashion and the consumer goods industries, primarily focused in beverages, including positions as Director of Chain Retail Division, Director of Business Development, Director of Sales, Sr. National Account Executive, Manager ECommerce, Manager Customer Information Services and Category Manager.

I have a reputation for being highly energetic with excellent organizational, time management, leadership, interpersonal, and problem solving skills. I am a highly motivated self-starter, with team player abilities.

Extensive consumer & customer knowledge in many retail channels of business: Supermarkets, Convenience, Mass Merchandiser, Limited Assortment, Drug, Club, Traditional Grocery and Hypermarkets.
