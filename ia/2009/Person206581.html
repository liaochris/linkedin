Robert Zabel’s Summary

Robert Zabel is a Banking Solutions Executive within IBM’s Worldwide Banking Center of Excellence. He has significant domestic and international experience within retail financial services. In this current role he is advising banking clients around the globe and partner ISVs with core systems transformation in an effort to reduce costs, enable growth and improve resiliency. Prior to this role, Mr. Zabel was an Associate Partner within the Global Business Services, Strategy and Change (S&C) Practice for Financial Services and the S&C America’s Lead for Business Process Management (BPM) offering. Prior to joining IBM, he worked at American Express where he focused on Internet business development efforts.
