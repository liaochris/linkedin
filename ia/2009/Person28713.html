Bill Ronan’s Summary

The irresistible allure of free enterprise and the arts drew me to the business of persuasion 15 years ago. Since, I’ve led an advertising agency, a motion design company and two ad music production companies. I relish participating in the vital enterprise of selling the goods, services and ideas that underpin our economy.

My goal is to do great work – defined as: strategic, targeted, cost-effective, artful, engaging, client-centric, collaborative, and enjoyable.

Ultimately of course, no matter how pretty the pictures or sweet the sounds, work is “great” when it sells what needs to be sold.
