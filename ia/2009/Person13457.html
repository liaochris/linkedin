Andreas Dzumla’s Summary

Multilingual Internet business specialist. Currently working for the Spanish Internet start-up Goa Internet Services S.L. as country Manager for nuroa Germany (www.nuroa.de) and Spain (www.nuroa.es), a vertical search engine for real estate listings.

What I love about working for a start-up is the thrill of making something grow from the start, being able to have a real impact as well as the flexibility to react quickly on market changes and new strategic insight. On the other hand, having worked for bigger companies, getting used to working with fewer resources is definitely a challenge as well. But as always, whether in a big, medium size or small company, what's makes it easy getting up in the morning and sometimes staying late in the evening is a great team.

Latest projects in nuroa have been the overhaul of the whole internal structure and the specialization of the buy and rent search engine (buy houses at http://hauskauf.nuroa.de and http://comprar-piso.nuroa.es, rent flats at http://mietwohnung.nuroa.de and http://alquilar-piso.nuroa.es). Our newest additions are a specific flatshare search engine (find flats to share at http://wg-zimmer.nuroa.de and http://compartir-piso.nuroa.es) and for Spain a search engine for new developments (http://obra-nueva.nuroa.es)

Besides that, I am always interested to hear about other innovative and ambitious projects and products who want to make a difference, provide a new way of doing or experiencing things - not necessarily only Internet specific.
