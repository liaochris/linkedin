Dave McVeigh’s Summary

I've written, directed, edited 100's of hours of documentaries, network specials, DVD features, promo campaigns, commercials, interstitials, webisodes and EPK’s for clients like Fox, Universal, HBO, Cinemax, FX, Warner Bros., New Line, ABC, TNT, etc. I have an eye for fast-paced pieces that are motivated first and foremost by a strong storyline.

With over 15 years experience communicating and collaborating with network executives, I understand all sides of the creative process, from concept to storyboard, from shoot to final edit. As a creative director, I understand that deadlines and great work are never mutually exclusive.

Dave McVeigh’s Specialties:

Creative Directing, Editing (Avid, Avid Liquid, Final Cut Pro), Writing (spots, long-form, features, documentary), Directing (docs, first looks, spots, etc.), Music Supervision, Post Production, deadline supervision.
