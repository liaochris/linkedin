Alicia Kennedy’s Summary

I’m Alicia Kennedy, a literature nerd living on Long Island. Professionally, I’m a writer and copy editor completely obsessed with linguistic minutiae.

You can usually find me reading, working on my Spanish, or intently tapping away at my laptop. Aside from these pursuits, prolific songwriters, auteur filmmakers, and Apple are my key obsessions.

I am looking for opportunities to write or blog about books and film, as well as for copy editing work in the publishing field.

Go to alicia-kennedy.com for my portfolio, blog, feedback from clients, and more.
