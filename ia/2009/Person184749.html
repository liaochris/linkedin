Pekka Pekkala’s Summary

10 years of online development, project management, concept design and organisational forward-thinking in the oldest and largest newspaper in Scandinavia. I managed the transition from the old Helsingin Sanomat newspaper website to HS.fi, one of the leading news portals in Finland. My latest project was upgrading the mobile HS.fi site, which grew into the biggest mobile site in Finland.
