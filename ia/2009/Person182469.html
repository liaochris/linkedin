Paula deWitte’s Summary

As of December 2008, I have completed my J.D. studies. I am taking the February Texas bar. As part of my legal studies, I studied in St. Petersburg, Russia, in the Summer Law Institute administered by the University of Arkansas. I have been published once in a journal, once in a law magazine, and am submitting two additional publications to law journals. I am interested in opportunities that bridge both the areas of technology and law.

I have extensive experience both in owning my own business and running businesses for investors. I have a wide range of experience: I have led research teams, been a Principal Investigator or Project Manager on countless efforts, and performed consulting for both government and commercial entities. I have developed commercially successful products from concept through research, product development, market testing, commercialization, and building services around the product. I have developed partnerships world-wide which has led to unique experiences of briefing military audiences in Turkey and extensive lectures in Japan and Korea. I am now finishing my law degree with the goal of building a new career based on my technical experience and expertise. I have used my law school education to focus on research and writing in areas of the law relating to technology. I have also begun writing a book on integrating physical and electronic security.

Paula deWitte’s Specialties:

New business development; making oral and written presentations; product commercialization from applied research and marking through technology development and product testing and into fielded operations and support.
