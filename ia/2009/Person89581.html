Greg Losacco’s Summary

Information Technology Sales professional with close to 20 years of experience in the industry. With a BS degree in Computer Science, worked as an IT Consultant for 3 years before becoming entrenched in the staffing side of the business. From a successful track record as a Sr. IT Recruiter, to maintaining a $5M + book of business as a Managing Director and Sr. Business Development Manager he now is working to bring his experience and knowledge to candidates and companies through his firm R-3 Recruitment. Mr. Losacco has constantly been a leader in all positions being #1 every year at a branch level when performing as a Recruiter or Business Development Manager / Account Manager. He has also been the recipient of annual awards, trips and bonuses exceeding his quota every year.

He enjoys working hard and playing hard. From seasonal golf and bowling leagues, to raising his only son, he is also an avid hunter, tours the concrete pathways on his Harley Fat Bob, snowboarding and snowmobiling and thrives on self improvement as part of his everyday life.
