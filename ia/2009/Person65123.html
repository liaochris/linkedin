David Wittenberg’s Summary

Dave Wittenberg helps companies lower their spending and increase their profits by showing them how to make sound decisions when the time comes to invest in operations and growth.

His satisfaction comes from helping leaders get the right information every time. This downturn has exposed a number of common errors that led to poorly made decisions and right now there is a lot of regret out there. With capital so scarce these days, we can’t afford to repeat these errors.

Other firms usually provide support only for big, one-time events like acquisitions. Dave equips the business with tools and techniques that produce winning decisions for both big and small investments.

Dave has a unique perspective as a value-focused leader who is skilled in strategy and capital deployment across diverse industries. He has had extensive responsibilities in capital investments, strategic analysis, acquisitions, divestitures, joint ventures, business planning and investor relations. He has a strong reputation for solving problems and fostering rich, effective relationships under rapidly changing conditions.
