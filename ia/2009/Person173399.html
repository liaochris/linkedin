Bob Erwin’s Summary

NewInk Marketing Services is a full service Internet marketing and web development company that can help companies’ better service existing customers, and find new customers through the Internet.

We can help companies with an existing Internet presence through traffic development programs and web site optimization services that drive new prospects to a site. Internet programs can also service companies' existing customers through online programs that provide them a more satisfying company experience.

For companies that have no Internet presence, we can help develop a fully integrated web program. We work hand-in-hand with the client from concept to a final web presence with a fully integrated Internet marketing program.

NewInk Marketing is led by Bob Erwin, a veteran of 20 years in high tech marketing. The company was formed in 2002 to help companies leverage the Internet for the many advantages and cost savings it has to offer.

Our name “NewInk” stands for the new ink of the digital age, digital communication and commerce, which is the primary focus of our business.
