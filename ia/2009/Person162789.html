Michele Wells’s Summary

Michele is a versatile editor and writer with over 13 years of experience in publishing, both in print and online. She has a strong acquisitions list to her name, and she is skilled in conceptualizing and developing content. Currently specializing in trade nonfiction with a focus on business self-help books, she previously edited lifestyle books (primarily focusing on entertainment/pop culture, foreign language, travel, cinema/film, food/wine, music, humor, technology, and light reference) and also has extensive experience in series publishing, as well as children’s and YA books. Michele has a proven track record in managing multiple deadline-driven projects, and has a reputation for delivering quality projects on time and within budget. She continually strives to maintain a strong network of author and agent relationships.
