Tom Martin’s Summary

Econiq dramatically improves the sales and service effectiveness of frontline staff in branches and call centers.

Econiq’s LightSpeed™ technology and comprehensive tool sets automates, monitors, measures, and improves the behavior of frontline staff.

As well as directly improving the effectiveness of frontline staff Econiq allows management to understand exactly what frontline behavior maximizes successful outcomes. Leveraging this insight and using the Econiq tool sets frontline behavior can be improved further to drive sales and enhance customer service outcomes.

Econiq is particularly effective in generating referrals and sales from customer service interactions.

Econiq users benefit from:
• Dramatically increased referrals and sales and improved operational efficiencies: fast.
• Better informed management and improved sales maturity across the whole organization,
• More empowered and motivated staff,
• Higher levels of customer satisfaction.

Econiq’s LightSpeed™ technology has been developed to ‘listen’ to multiple operational and third party systems. Because we only ‘listen’ there’s no need for a time-consuming fully fledged integration into your existing systems. This means that deploying Econiq is very fast and does not impact mission-critical systems or costly staff resources. Typically, we connect to a system in under a week and can have the first phase rolled out with measurable improvements in a couple of months. Econiq has been specifically designed to be operated by business users and apart from initial setup little or no technical IT involvement is required.
