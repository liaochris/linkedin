Scott OBrien’s Summary

Meet Scott OBrien...
Holland, NY, United States

Manager & Sales Professional…

Scott OBrien has been in Sales, Management & Leadership positions for over 27 years of his professional career. Scott started out of High School as a Union Carpenter & specialized in Floor Covering. As his 4-year apprenticeship ended, he decided he didn't want to work for someone else anymore & started his own floor covering business out of the basement of his home.

Over the next 20 years, Scott's hard work & determination took his business to over one million in annual sales.

Network Marketing…

In 1996, Scott OBrien was diagnosed with cancer. Scott’s 4-year battle with cancer took him to cancer centers all over the USA. In 2000, Scott was blessed with cancer remission. Scott was happy to be a stay at home dad & take care of their kids & home, but Scott also had a desire to find a way to make an additional income from home & ventured into Network Marketing. Scott will tell you it wasn't instant success, but anyone can be successful if they have the desire.

Mentor & Trainer…

Scott OBrien has become an Expert in Network Marketing and enjoys helping others starting out in business & offers training & mentoring to learn how to build a successful home business.

To Learn more visit: www.HomeBusinessMarketingPro.com
