Flavio Patrono’s Summary

ILHA BRASIL (Brazil Island) is the Brazilian Pioneer Metaverse Company that specializes in connecting companies and entertainment artists/content with online communities, such as virtual worlds and social networks. We also design and develop branded virtual worlds from the ground up.

Clients include: Philips, Volkswagen, Nokia, Fiat, COB (XV Pan American Games), Bradesco, Itaú, SKY+, Globo.com, GloboSat (GNT/SporTV), JB, among others.

Designer and virtual world evangelist, particularly interested in architecture and the built environment, augmented design collaboration, and virtual building design.
