Charlie Ellibee’s Summary

Charlie Ellibee is a freelance graphic designer based out of Burlington, VT. He got his start growing up in Traverse City, MI before moving out to live on lake Champlain in majestic northern New England. He entered Champlain College, fall of 2003 in the Multi-Media and Graphic Design program.

In 2006, the beginning of Charlie's senior year at Champlain, he traveled almost halfway across the world to Melbourne, Victoria in southern Australia to study at Monash University's Art & Design school. He experienced the design style of another country while he studied, worked and traveled through out Australia for 7 months in a study abroad program. He then returned to the United States to finish up his bachelors degree in Vermont.

In Spring of 2007 Charles Rodger Ellibee IV graduated from Champlain College's multi-media and graphic design program with a Bachelors of Science. He has since been doing freelance for several individuals and companies alike. He has worked in a Apple store gaining valuable experience with Mac computers and all the software involved as well as sharing the responsibility of in-store signage design with a fellow co-worker. He has worked at a marketing and media company that catered to the medical industry. Most recently, he was employed at a local Vermont vitamin and supplement company. His responsibilities included overseeing of all things creative design oriented on 2 of the companies 6 product lines. He did anything from label designs and printing, to catalog layout and output, marketing and advertising material design and trade show support materials (posters, banners, flyers, etc.),

He is currently working on several personal projects but is looking for that ideal job with a Advertising, Marketing or Design Studio to further his development as a graphic designer as well as to share his creative passions with the industry. His background in layout and print design would also be ideal for magazines and newspapers.

Charlie Ellibee’s Specialties:

Adobe Creative Suite, QuarkXPress, Mac based software, design output, 4-color process, pre-press, print production, branding and identity
