JuanCarlos Puente’s Summary

I lead the Business Productivity solutions and products business for Microsoft in Latin America.

Business Productivity entails all the solutions that make business information workers at companies more effective and productive and companies love to have a reduced workforce that performs at it's peak. New technologies like social computing, portals and enterprise search lead this trend however a complete business productivity platform at a company also provides capabilities like business intelligence, enterprise content management and unified communications. The business productivity platform is driven by SharePoint Server and early next year I will launch SharePoint 2010 in Latin America as well as a new subscription (hosted) model for Business Productivity Online Services on the cloud so companies of all sizes can rent software from Microsoft at a low cost each month. Check this www.microsoft.com/online/es-es

For greater detail please see my interview at the bottom of this page and check out my blog www.EmpresaProductiva.com, my new Web2.0 website with super cool easy to understand videos www.EmpresaDosCero.com and my main product page www.microsoft.com/latam/sharepoint.
