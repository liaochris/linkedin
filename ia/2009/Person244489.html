Vivien Godfrey’s Summary

I have been fortunate to live and work in 5 different countries and in several different industries. After starting my career with McKinsey & Company, I worked for Diageo for almost 10 years. I then ran my own small company for 9 years and am now beginning my fourth career as CEO of MilkPEP. I am excited to be back in the food industry and reconnecting with many former friends and colleagues.
