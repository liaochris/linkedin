Charisse Marie Colbert’s Summary

After experiencing a successful career as a business development officer in the banking industry and as a sales agent in residential new home sales, I decided to pursue my desire to work in a more creative field. I followed my burning desire and went back to school and obtained a degree in interior design.

My goal is to serve my clients by enhancing their surroundings with beauty while being prosperous in business by continually growing, being adventurous and creative in my profession as a passionate interior design professional.

Professional interior design services to include, new home construction, remodel, custom color scheme consultation, material selection, space planning on AutoCad, purchasing services, staging and redesign.

What I can do: Design tile layouts for floors and walls Design window and bedding treatments Measure and draft a space on AutoCAD Prepare a lighting plan on AutoCAD Prepare a rendering of the space Prepare a space plan of the furnishings on AutoCAD Prepare elevations of the space on AutoCAD Prepare materials and finish presentation board Select art and accessories Select cabinetry, appliances, plumbing fixtures and lighting fixtures. Select furnishings and fabrics Select paint colors, materials for floors, walls, countertops, and ceilings.

Charisse Marie Colbert’s Specialties:

I am a problem solver that finds solutions you cannot solve or do not want to solve yourself. It is up to me to create the kind of interior that best meets your needs and enhances your environment. This is done by determining life style, function, space requirements, environmental factors, psychological factors, economic factors, and codes.
