Carlos Ezquerro’s Summary

More than 12 years of experience in different management positions in Marketing and IT areas. In most cases in charge of national and international projects related with E-business, Internet Portals, CRM, Content Management Systems, Clasified Ads and Customer Care with a high technical component on each ones.

Senior expert in Call-Contact Centers and Mobile technologies (Wireless, SMS, MMS, GSM, UMTS, ...). during theses years, involved in the definition and developments of technical architectures and services using platforms based on Content Management Systems, Web 2.0, WebTV, Mobile services and Web Portals.

Used to work in big international companies (VOCENTO, TELEFONICA, TERRA) being part of the Management team in the Corp. area and also used to work in business units with responsability on P&L statements always with a high capacity to lead teamworks.

In 2005 Carlos joined VOCENTOs Group in order to develop and lead On-Line Business within New Technologies Division. VOCENTO has 13 online local newspapers, 4 clasiffied sites (Autocasion.com, sacacasa.com, Infoempleo,com, tusanuncios.com), several social networks sites (dalealplay.com, ozu.es), 2 online national newspapers (www.abc.es, www.que.es), 7 vertical portals (hoycinema.com, finanzas.com, laguiaTV.com, hoytecnologia.com, hoysport.com, hoymujer.com, hoymotor.com) an e-commerce platfom and a lot of spanish web pages. In 2.005 Vocento had 5.000.000 online unique visitors, in 2.008 Vocento's Web sites were visited by more than 19.000.000 spanish unique visitors per month.

Since May 2008, Carlos is the new IT DIRECTOR at GPS (Bertelsmann Group). He's in charge of Informatics, technical infrastructure, system platform, developments (Web and print business) supporting G+J Spain and Motorpress Iberica companies.
