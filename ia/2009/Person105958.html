Jackson Carson’s Summary

Currently, I am Senior Web Art Director at Gaiam, Inc. where I design web sites, Flash apps, web application UI and much more. I have two decades of electronic media experience and have run my own successful new media and traditional creative design business (Wavelength Media) for fifteen years.

Prior to Wavelength Media, I taught graduate level 3D and 2D animation at the University of Massachusetts. During that time, I designed the revolutionary event-based Sequencer animation interface used in Specular Infini-D, awarded Best 3D Software for Macintosh in 1992. My timeline interface has become the industry standard and has widely adopted in such popular animation applications as Adobe AfterEffects, Swift 3D, Lightwave 3D, Cinema 4D, Anark Studio, and many others.

In addition to user-centered application UI design, I have designed easy-to-use web interfaces since the dawn of the World Wide Web.
