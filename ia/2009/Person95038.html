Daniel Ward’s Summary

Daniel Ward has 25+ years of CEO-level leadership experience in commercial enterprises, non-profit organizations, schools, colleges, and government agencies. He has created profitable businesses, started successful non-profits, and saved failing businesses by directing successful organizational turnarounds.

Mr. Ward directed marketing for a multinational web software company, created several mental health treatment programs, ran an inter-urban bus company, wrote advertising for the Dow Chemical Company, investigated felons as an adult probation officer, built the largest private dental clinic in Oregon, taught high school and college...and more.

"I highly recommend Mr. Ward to you. He is an entrepreneurial visionary and a gifted communicator with extensive teaching and public speaking experience. With an unusually wide range of talent, he has demonstrated an uncanny ability to rapidly acquire and transfer skills from one business field or industry domain to another."
