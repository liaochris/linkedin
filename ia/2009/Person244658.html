Vlado Delic’s Summary

Professor, researcher, and project manager, particularly interested in speech signal processing and speech-based human-machine communications. He has been introduced a distinguished lecturer program at UNS and leads the Laboratory for Acoustics and Speech Technologies. He established a R&D group named AlfaNum (www.alfanum.ftn.ns.ac.yu) working on R&D in the area of speech technologies and co-founded AlfaNum Ltd. (www.alfanum-global.com) that is the leader in applications of ASR&TTS in South Slavic languages. More than 100 scientific publications. Experience in leading teams, contributing to project planning and estimation, conducting design and code reviews, and mentoring. He was bestowed several prestigiuous awards for his creative work and contribution to innovations in the region.

Vlado Delic’s Specialties:

Extensive knowledge and considerable experience in:
• teaching speech technology subjects (DSP, Acoustics, ASR&TTS)
• building and managing highly motivated and focused teams
• speech technology applications design and implementation
• launching innovative speech-based products in West Balcan countries: anReader, voice portals, on-line audio library, e-mail reader, advertising monitor
