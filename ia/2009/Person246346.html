Ben Weeks’s Summary

As a Senior SharePoint/MOSS Consultant and Technical Architect I have a range of experience across a number of industry sectors such as government and insurance. Over the past few years I have specialised in providing expertise in the full lifecycle of Microsoft SharePoint/MOSS. Tasks I am often involved in are: requirements analysis with key stakeholders, writing business cases, specification design, defining roles and responsibilities, producing training documentation as well as technical guidance and management on project delivery. I also have experience of the pre-sales process such as demonstrating SharePoint/MOSS to prospective clients, responding to Pre-Qualification Questionnaires, and writing tenders.

Running my own company has provided me with a number of additional skills such as accounting, marketing, sales, customer management (including customer facing) and other issues related to running a small business such as management (I have an accountant and a number of sub-contractors who also work for me). It also means I am involved in the whole lifecycle of projects. In addition, I use Microsoft technologies in-house such as SharePoint (for a company Intranet that includes an extensive knowledgebase and project tracker), Microsoft Dynamics CRM 3.0 (for dealing with relationships with customers), Microsoft Exchange, SharePoint 2007 and Microsoft Small Business Server.
