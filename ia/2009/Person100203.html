Victor Brierley’s Summary

Gosh, it's been nearly a quarter of a century now. In that time I've been a van driver, production assistant, PR face in a concert venue, writer, account director, ad traffic manager, business development director and managing director. It's not been boring. What I can bring to the table is experience (obviously) but also the ability to join-up all the strands, to pull-together a compelling marketing strategy.

I've cherry-picked a bunch of the best people in the industry to work with. Most of the best professionals now work freelance or as part of a small and dynamic organisation, because it's fun, they can be cost effective and things are changing, all the time. Advertising agencies as such, they don't exist any more. I'm mainly focusing on the digital marketing arena now but one thing hasn't changed: I can make sure that a great creative concept is a great creative concept, wherever it might appear.

I'm also getting involved in business training and Knowledge Transfer, helping SME's (and larger firms) get to grips with not just the marketing basics but also the vast digital landscape. It isn't as scary or as complex as some people on here make out though. I sure as hell don't know all the answers... but I usually know someone in my burgeoning network of clever professionals who does. Call me.
