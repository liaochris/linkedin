Sandra Kozlowski’s Summary

I am excited to see that our timing for Conchetta House is equal to the trending of the whole country. Please read the following statements from Twitter today...

"First..benchmarked K-12 standards that truly prepare students for college and careers>"

"For the first time in history, we have the resources at the federal level to drive reform....

We have governors...together to say no more to dumbing down ....standards and cheating students of a quality education...

Today we are announcing draft guidelines for states to apply for the 435 billion dollar "Race to the Top fund."----Arne Duncan

I am so glad to hear this news, yet am concerned that we could still be leaving single parents out. What Conchetta House will be doing is working hand in hand with the schools, both k-12 and colleges/training programs in every area that Conchetta House is open, to make sure that our students are progressing to a successful completion of their programs.

Our program will provide free housing for the single parent families (however that is defined by the family). We will rely on the colleges to recommend applicants who are in danger of dropping or not enrolling due to the concerns that plague single parents. By offering tutoring for the children, we are easing that burden of worry for the single parent, so they can concentrate on their studies.

We have chosen to offer free music lessons (and hopefully art) because these programs are being cut so drastically in the schools, that this important learning tool needs to be in the childrens curriculum.

Is Conchetta House the only answer to the education question? No. Is it the best answer for single parents who want the best for themselves and their families, absolutely!
