Niha Redhu’s Summary

Niha portays a multi talented personality with a strong educational background. With science being her strong point all through her academics writing was a passion that she always harbored. After working for various companies she decided to follow her passion and started full time freelancing.

When working on her articles Niha believes in simplicity. A simple, well constructed article has a better ability to convey its meaning and therefore is capable of attracting maximum audience. Her writing work has always been a true reflection of herself. She writes from her heart. She considers the ability to give words to my thoughts as the greatest gift bestowed on her.
