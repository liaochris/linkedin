Ted Hekman’s Summary

I am currently undergoing two projects. I act as COO for an online startup called ResumeBucket.com, which has yet to launch beta(soon, we promise). Primarily I act as the chief project manager, although I also handle all operations, which means I'm doing something different every day, which I love. We're very excited about the potential of the product, and can't wait for the rest of you to see it soon!

I am also the head of technical recruiting for NeoHire, a headhunting company specializing in .com startups in the Southern California region. We have a great team of about 8 and absolutely crank it for our clients. It's a great way to spend your daylight hours, I love working with people and putting together all the pieces of the puzzle in a way that makes everyone happy and profitable.

I have done, and continue to do, a little bit of everything. Shifting gears and multitasking are some of my greatest virtues.
I've done a lot over the last 10 years in the IT industry. I've managed computer chop shops in Atlanta. I built websites and managed tech teams under multiple platforms for 8 years. I've worked in the banking industry, the mortgage industry, the health care industry, the software industry, the gaming industry, the red light industry. Just kidding.

Ted Hekman’s Specialties:
