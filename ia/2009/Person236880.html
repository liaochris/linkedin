Tony Mallam’s Summary

A Chartered Accountant (SA) by profession, I have worked in mainly media related industries in the past. I was a founder-member of the Crescent Consortium, which bought KFM Radio in 1996, and then on-sold the station in 2000 to New Africa (NAIL). Since then I have invested in some IT start-ups, one notable success being Clickatell (Pty) Ltd. Together with my partners, I founded Cape Venture Partners (CVP) 4 years ago, and since then we have touched over 400 entrepreneurs in the technology space in South Africa. Notable clients have been Skyrove, Uninet, BizCommunity, Psitek and Clickatell, to name but a few. CVP has also been instrumental in developing training courses for entrepreneurs together with CITI, and more recently Cape Biotech. I have been an adjudicator for the Top Technology 100 Awards, and also mentor at the Graduate School of Business at the University of Cape Town.My skills lie in the combination of finance, media and technology, with a strong entrepreneurial thread running through.
