Pieter van der Hijden’s Summary

Management consultant on organisation & information, mainly for government and educational sectors. Works in The Netherlands and Suriname.

Pieter van der Hijden’s Specialties:

* e-learning (distance education, Moodle)
* e-goverment (strategy development, capacity building)
* gaming/simulation (policy exercises, serious games, Cyberdam)
* interactive workshops

  • Owner

    Sofos Consultancy

    (Privately Held; Information Technology and Services industry)

    1980 — Present (29 years)

    Sofos Consultancy (http://www.sofos.nl) is a management consulting firm on organisation and ICT. It was founded by Pieter van der Hijden in 1980 and operates from Amsterdam, The Netherlands. Sofos provides project management, process consultation and expert consultation services.

    Pieter van der Hijden MSc is an experienced interim-manager and consultant, specialised in E-Learning, E- Government and Gaming/Simulation. He worked for government agencies and higher education institutes in both The Netherlands and Suriname, and for UNESCO Caribbean in Jamaica, Trinidad & Tobago, Guyana and Suriname.

    Current assignments:
    * Establishing a centre of expertise for education and ICTs in Suriname.
    * Implementing a Virtual Learning Environment (Moodle).
    * Technical project manager of the project: Learning in a virtual world (Cyberdam).

  • Teacher

    (Management Consulting industry)

    2002 — 2007 (5 years)

    Part-time assignments:
    * Developing curriculum for competence based education in "interactive media"; designing a collaborative work environment.
    * Developing short training games on project management.
    * Teaching project management, production organisation, production innovation, management information, organisational health and safety.
    * Developing and testing a course on business information systems.
    * Involved in various initiatives concerning the introduction of gaming/simulation in education.

  • Consultant

    HEC - Stichting Het Expertise Centrum

    (Management Consulting industry)

    1999 — 2000 (1 year)

    Assignments in the field of organisation and ICTs with about seven Ministries and as many municipalities in The Netherlands.

  • Internal Consultant

    Ministry of Social Affairs & Employment

    (Management Consulting industry)

    1983 — 1997 (14 years)

    With the Dutch Ministry of Social Affairs and Employment Pieter was corporate management consultant for organisation and information. He took courses on management and organisational change. He was secretary of a corporate steering committee, developed a corporate information and automation policy, represented his ministry in various interdepartmental projects, was head of the departmental computer centre during a privatisation process, chair of the Planning & Control committee during the reorganisation of the mininistry, was edp-auditor “avant la lettre”, and later interim chief inspector of the organisational health and safety inspection in the provinces of Noord-Brabant and Limburg. For the top management of the ministry he did numerous assignments, organisational diagnosis, administrative reviews, coaching of departments, etc.
