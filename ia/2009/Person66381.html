Doug Hering, Ph.D., CMA’s Summary

Entepreneurial professional with cross industry experience in all aspects of financial management, customer service, and strategic planning. I am currently focused on charter schools, working for Charter School Management Corporation serving the Colorado market with reasonable cost outsourced back office support.

We focus on the back office so that school leaders can focus on the kids.

The concept is to use our economies of scale to provide superior services at a lower cost. We use state of the art software and trained professionals in Accounts Payable, Payroll, Human Resources, Budgeting, Forecasting, Business Services.

I handle the Budgeting, Forecasting and Business Services. We provide the other services offsite.

Why don't you check out our corporate web site at www.csmci.com

Please check out my charter school blog: www.charterinsights.blogspot.com

I also write a regular column for Examiner.com. I'm the Colorado Charter Examiner, and I keep up to date on Colorado charter school news and tips.
