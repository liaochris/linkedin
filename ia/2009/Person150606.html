Marilín Gonzalo’s Summary

Freelance online journalist, with broad experience in many areas of journalism and internet oriented. In Argentina I have worked as producer of radio and tv programs. I have also done research on national and international news, political and media discurse semiotic analysis.

Since I came to live to Madrid, I have been writing articles for magazines, and also connected with online media. I have worked as content provider for some websites. I had been exploring new possibilities in journalism and microblogging.

During 4 months I've been working full time as Editor in Chief at Agora News, an online press agency that produces video content for journalistic media on the internet, focusing on tech and internet news. Reporting directly to the director, I led the team and surveilled the quality of the content produced, in both technical and narrative aspects.

After this, I started being requested for some internet companies to guide them over social networks and other internet related issues. I currently work as Community Manager and Internet Consultant for some clients.

I am fluent in English, Spanish and Dutch, I also speak a bit of French.
