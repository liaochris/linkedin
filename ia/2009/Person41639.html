Charlo Barbosa’s Summary

I've been in the internet business since 1996. I started out owning my own hosting company and I called it Virtualynx. I ran Virtualynx for five years until Abovenet pretty much shut my business down because they weren't into re-negotiating my $650 / mbit contract that I had signed a couple years before that.

One of the worst endings to one of the best domains that I used to control was poker.com. At that time, poker was just starting to get hot on the internet and we licensed that domain and developed software so companies could run a poker room online.

I started Revenuegateway.com in 2005. We are happy to say that our network has a couple hundred of quality affiliates now and we have a bunch of exclusive offers that we run for our advertisers. One exclusive offer that we are going to be running is orderonlinedrugs.com.

I am also the CEO of Admanage.com, an online ad agency that buys and sells text, display and video advertising. We contact companies to resell their remnant inventory. Admanage also is an xml search feed syndication company. We also have our own direct search advertisers using our proprietary bidding engine platform. I believe that it is the easiest and best system amongst all Tier 2 search engine companies.

I am excited to be launching our new Lead Gen Coreg business, Coreggy.com. We will be able to generate quality leads for companies interested in obtaining more data for their sales departments. Check out the site for all of our distribution methods.
