Kevin Tallis’s Summary

With more than 15 years experience, Kevin has held key roles in strategic planning, marketing management, new product development and sales. Adept in collaborating at all levels across organizations, Kevin’s marketing expertise has been instrumental in launching a variety of well-known products and services, including new lines of business. During his many assignments with both small (pre-IPO) and large (Fortune 500) firms, he has managed successful product introductions primarily in the biomedical, telecommunications and technology arenas.
