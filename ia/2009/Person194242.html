Raven Young’s Summary

Experienced project manager focusing on technology and software/web development projects. 8 Year PMI member, Member Women In Project Management and author of Raven's Brain, a project management, leadership and soft skills focused blog: http://www.RavensBrain.com. Official member of Project Management Institute's New Media Council.

I have 10 years exclusive experience on technology projects and another 5+ on retail store operations and construction projects. Comfortable with all aspects of project management - able to lead, delegate, coordinate, support, facilitate, manage vendors, contractors and outsourced work. Solid collaboration and negotiation skills, as well as core PM activities and responsibilities.

My prior development experience (HTML, ASP, CSS, XML, Scripting) provides a better understanding of what programmers, testers, analysts deal with on a daily basis. I've researched emerging technologies, including Web 2.0, SaaS and SOA, as well as new processes such as Agile, Scrum, XP and more. I'm technical enough to understand most work required in technology projects but leave the coding, architecting and implementation to those who devote their careers to all things technical. I've gathered and refined requirements, prioritized items for specific releases, planned and created schedules, tracked progress, tested functionality, triaged bugs and defects, managed internal bug bashes and internal/external beta testing, and provided concise and timely status - in short I've managed technology projects through all phases of the SDLC to meet critical deadlines.

I'm extrememly organized and love what I do. The field of Project Management allows me to use my natural abilities, past experience and new skills to deliver quality projects on time and within budget.
