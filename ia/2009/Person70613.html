Edward Hardam’s Summary

CHvV BV (www.chvv.nl) specialises in the procurement of complex ICT. Servicing public and private organisations, we focus on selecting the right vendors and products for innovative and / or complex ICT components. Combining Legal, ICT, procurement, Audit and Project Management skills, we assure that this right ICT is being acquired at acceptable costs.

My strongest points: being a building of bridges between technology and the 'other' people; covering business issues as well as laws, customers and hackers.

Edward Hardam’s Specialties:
