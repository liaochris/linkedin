Matt Aimonetti’s Summary

Web application designer/developer experienced with world wide scale projects. I am particularly interested in the User Experience and simplification of processes by using "agile" methodologies.
I have always been interested in challenging projects requiring "thinking out of the box".
Internationalization, localization and media support have been reoccurring themes throughout my career.

- Attendee and speaker in conferences (US & abroad)
