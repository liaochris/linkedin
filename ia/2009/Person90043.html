Greta Franklin’s Summary

I am passionate about applying my talent and experience to effect positive and meaningful change. I am an avid traveler and love communicating across multiple cultures. I'm committed to helping companies find solutions to develop sustainable and ethical practices, specifically in the product development and international supply chain management realms.

I've always been a highly motivated person who seeks challenges and adventure. After developing a successful career in product development and supply chain management I decided to embrace these elements of my personality as I transitioned my career into a more environmentally and socially conscious realm. As a result I completed a Thru Hike of the Appalachian Trail which stretches 2,176 miles, through 14 states, taking me roughly five months to complete.

I'm passionate about communicating the profitability and sustainability of socially and environmentally conscious product development and manufacturing across multiple cultures and international supply chain. Currently I am committed to helping companies create and manage programs supporting ethical compliance within their international supply chains.
