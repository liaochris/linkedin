Chris Luther’s Summary

I have a passion for learning! For over 15 years I have been following that passion by producing and delivering skills training and science education. I am keenly aware of how learning can empower an individual and transform an organization in advancing its mission.

These days I’m focused on Training Business Process Outsourcing (both development and delivery). In my current role I am building high-performing teams and forging strategic relationships with clients and technology partners. My objective is to implement and refine best practices which will increase both the organizational impact and the ROI of the training investment.

I continue to follow my muse by writing and presenting on topics related strategic learning, e-learning technologies and rapid instructional design methodologies. I remain actively involved in ASTD at both the local and national levels, and maintain memberships in the eLearning Guild and the Society for Technical Communication (STC).

Chris Luther’s Specialties:

Training Business Management, Marketing and Channel Development, Project Management, Rapid Instructional Design, Mentoring and Professional Skills Development, Agile Development Processes, Certification Program Development, Global Sourcing, e-Learning Communities, LMS/LCMS Selection and Deployment (LMS, LCMS, SCORM, EEDO, SABA, Moodle, OLAT), Rich Media and J2EE Web Development (Flex, Flash, ActionScript, Java, J2EE, Struts, UI Design, XML).
