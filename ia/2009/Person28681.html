Bill Paul’s Summary

Bill McInerney, Chairman and Founder of Phoenix International Freight Services, in an unsolicited message to the CEO of another Freight Forwarding company said of me, "For all practical purposes, Bill invented the Freight Forwarder recruiting business. He was our Vice President and General Manager for almost 10 years and had many years of experience before joining us. He is a freight forwarder first and then a recruiter and trainer. He has either directly hired or placed 90% of our management team from Supervisors to Vice Presidents. I give him a significant part of the credit for our success."

Integrity is the most critical element of what I do. I'm not a resume broker and I am very selective about the candidates I represent and the companies I recruit for. My philosophy is simple; if I'd hire you - I'll work hard to place you but only with a company I'd be willing to work for.

I have excellent relationships with owners and key executives at my client companies and frequently make "opportunistic" placements. In other words, they might not be looking for a particular candidate but when I present someone I think is a difference maker, they listen.
