Andrew McCormick’s Experience

  • Deputy Editor

    Revolution

    (Writing and Editing industry)

    July 2008 — Present (1 year 1 month)

    As Deputy Editor for Revolution I look after all features and online content. I played a major role in relaunching Revolution magazine in October 2008 and managed the relaunch of revolutionmagazine.com in March 2009.

    Revolution is dedicated to celebrating innovation in digital marketing, while providing business intelligence and insight to those working within the UK’s fastest moving communications sector.

    Revolution is the UK's only monthly magazine dedicated to digital marketing. Since its launch at the height of the dotcom boom it has been synonymous with industry defining insight and editorial excellence.

    Revolution prides itself on standing out from the crowd. The magazine's unique blend of snackable content and longer analytical reads are backed up by comment and reviews from industry leaders.

    Following its rebirth in October 2008, the magazine's strong editorial voice, unparalleled insight and cutting-edge design ensure it is a must-read for anyone working in digital marketing.

    You can find us on Twitter @andrewmccormick @GJ

  • Senior Reporter

    Media Week

    (Writing and Editing industry)

    May 2007 — July 2008 (1 year 3 months)

    As Senior Reporter at Media Week I covered the digital media sector from search to online publishers to mobile to the rise of social media including LinkedIn. I also covered media agencies and wrote on TV, radio, newspapers and magazines. I wrote news and features to daily and weekly deadlines and edited the online section of the magazine.

  • Reporter

    New Media Age

    (Privately Held; 501-1000 employees; Writing and Editing industry)

    June 2005 — May 2007 (2 years)

    During my time on NMA, the weekly title covering business and marketing on the internet, mobile and interactive TV I reported on key areas including media (TV and radio), online advertising, social networking, sport, gambling and digital agencies. As the most experienced member of the news desk I advised others on developing stories, managing contacts and setting the news agenda. I wrote off-diary news to weekly and daily deadlines, news analyses and a wide range of features. I also advised the editor and features editor on what issues and developments NMA should be covering in depth. I have represented NMA at conferences around the world.
