Anthony Zotti’s Summary

I've been involved in electronics at the consumer and custom levels for a number of years. I was the first systems integrator in Colorado with LEED Platinum residential experience having programmed the automation system for only the third residential platinum rated home in the United States. Since then, I have continued to utilize my experience and creativity to implement what I call "ECOnvergence", the practice of utilizing smart technology to increase efficiency in a building's various systems. I am also an embedded electronics enthusiast, creating unique solutions to automation problems. I enjoy designing systems that fall outside the normal scope of automation and AV. User Interface development is a specialty and passion. I spend my down time with my family, designing with embedded electronics and programming, hardware “hacking”, DIY sustainability, art, contributing to industry related online forums and writing at www.tonynoone.blogspot.com.

Anthony Zotti’s Specialties:

Design and engineering, systems programming (vendor specific, Flash ActionScript, C++), Sales. Ultimately, I'm a problem solver.
