David Calvo’s Summary

i used to play with shadows on my bedroom's wall,
pulling my fingers on shifting figures --
like sprites

i have published novels, video games and comic books, exploring the recesses of imagination and dimensions. i am also participating in different art and video games collectives, such as anAmnésie - and Univac 1951.

With Maxime Plantady and Sylvain Trabut, i founded Ankama Play in 2007 as an indie R&D venture inside Ankama. We are currently developing Islands of Wakfu for the XLA.

i strongly believe in a sensible approach of video games.
Mimesis and Diegesis, pixels and words ;
in between, the hands.
