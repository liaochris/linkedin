Diana Kretschmann’s Summary

Ancient historian, with special interest in the history of Archaic and Classical Greece. Research focus on social identity, political ideas and self-representation. Experience in research and university teaching. Interested in Greek culture, ancient and modern.

Diana Kretschmann’s Specialties:

Reading Greek inscriptions, organising field trips with students to Greece, developing new seminars.
