Leslie Clarke’s Summary

Marketer and market researcher with over 20 years of experience in packaged goods, retail and financial services. Expertise gained at Sklar Wilton, a market research management and strategic planning firm, as well as client side positions at Wrigley Canada and Kraft Canada. Recognized as a marketer with excellent strategic skills, judgment, solutions -oriented creative thinker who drives business results. Also a big picture thinker who can manage the day to day details.

I'm looking for either a senior client side marketing, market research role, Agency strategic planning or position in a consulting firm. I would also be interested in a general management role in the not for profit sector, entertainment sector, or wireless sector.

I have been doing some freelance consulting including Arcus Group, my health club and Queen's University while I look for my next permanent position.
