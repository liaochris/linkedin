Jeff Stromberg’s Summary

Jeff Stromberg has founded and grown software companies ranging from two person boot-strapped companies up to publicly traded companies with over 500 employees. His entire career has been spent providing video management and distribution software and content management systems. He knows how to design, build, implement and sell these software and service offerings both through direct sales as well as building and running channel distribution systems. He speaks at industry events and is at the very top of the industry. Jeff has a Master’s degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of Minnesota.

Jeff Stromberg’s Specialties:

* Running and building software companies or business units
* Strategic acquisitions in digital media
* Product development and product launch strategies
* Software product launch, including PR, Marketing, Sales Strategy
