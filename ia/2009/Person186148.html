Peter Sylvester’s Summary

GENERAL OVERVIEW:
My aim is simple : I want to add value to others by solving the people puzzle and ensure that OUR business, helps YOUR business, DO business in the current economic challenges ...!

As a Senior Vice-President, I spent many years in the automotive financial services business on an international basis with the major brands of Harley-Davidson and BMW, I am now actively finding ways to "add value" to businesses and individuals in and around the West Midlands, UK.

Our family-run people solutions business is Solihull-based Outlook Unlimited, who work on Quality, not Quantity when it comes to recruitment.

We also provide talent development through our "Success Unlimited" division.

At Business Unlimited, OUR business is to help YOUR business DO business - through networking and marketing in the most cost effective way through our "Business Unlimited" online marketing solutions.

See us at www.business-unlimited.co.uk .

In addition to these businesses, I am
- A Professional Speaker, Coach and Mentor to MD's and Boards
- An Active Networker through my BNI Birmingham Area Director role
- Non-Exec director and advisor to other companies.

I am an active member of the IoD, and like to give something back by mentoring to students at Coventry University

Peter Sylvester’s Specialties:

Individuals who want to make a positive change in their life and business ...!

New start up businesses, senior level coaching and mentoring for main board members, and go-to-market strategy solutions for businesses of all sizes.
