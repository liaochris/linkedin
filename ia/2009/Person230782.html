Terry Hassett’s Summary

I provide professional services to residential real estate clients for real estate located in Northern Colorado. I have sold real estate in Northern Colorado for 30 years. I have taught real estate courses, as well as trained and managed other brokers. I am knowledgeable in many areas of real estate including, residential re-sale and investment properties, new construction and project marketing, land and acreages, corporate relocation, REO/foreclosure properties, short sale negotiations and Sec. 1031 tax deferred exchanges. My expertise lies in negotiating real estate contracts, marketing, negotiating and Colorado real estate practice. I work to bring a unique level of knowledge and experience to each transaction. My focus is paying special care and attention to achieving desired results for my clients in helping them achieve their individual goals.
