Stephanie DeGraw’s Summary

We Strengthen businesses through effective marketing. We research your business and demographics to create a marketing plan that delivers results. Then the plan is put into action, from producing the ads, TV programs or mobile billboards, to placing them in media outlets.
We have expanded to Power Media ENTERTAINMENT and ARE WORKING WITH TALENT WHO WANT TO TAKE IT TO THE NEXT LEVEL!! We will BRAND you, your music!! NEED A MUSIC VIDEO? Need some Public Relations for your company or next album, festival or project? We specialize in getting you known! We write, distribute press releases and follow up to see that they are printed. We BOOK Concerts, TV and Event appearances. VISIT www.myspace/powermediastudio
Email info@powermedia.us for more details.

We also have a TV and radio studio. Power Media has filmed the Weber State Coach's Corner and football games for the past three years. The programs were picked up by FOX College Sports Network FCSN and broadcast nationwide.

DeGraw has 25 years experience in public relations, advertising, journalism and broadcasting. She has a Bachelor of Science from Weber State University in Broadcasting & Journalism.

During the Olympics, she was the TV Station Manager of ch.16 the ION Network (formerly PAX) in Salt Lake City. She recently served as Vice President of the Historic 25th Street Association, is on the Arts Festival board and in Women in Business. She was just inducted into the LOGSTAR group of local leaders that work with Hill Airforce Base in Utah.

Looking for clients that need assistance in marketing or media buying. Stephanie is available for Talent for corporate videos, TV & Radio ads, Talk Shows, and more.
