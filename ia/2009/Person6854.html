Al Doran, CHRP’s Summary

Phenix Management Int'l is a Toronto-based management consulting company specializing in facilitating the effective management of human resource information. We assist you in the evaluation, selection, and implementation of effective solutions to support your business functions. Our professional assistance can assist you in the making the right vendor choice for your specific needs and reduce your overall project costs as well as shorten the timeline for a successful implementation.

Al Doran, CHRP’s Specialties:

Human Resources Management Systems (HRMS) aka HRIS is our area of speciality.
We assist organizations choose effective software to manage their HR and Payroll information.
Frequent Key Note Speaker.
Co-Author of "Human Resources Management Systems: A Practical Approach".
