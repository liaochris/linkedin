Henry Hansch III’s Summary

I’ve had a successful career in business development, customer service and management. This encompasses a strong track record of outstanding achievement starting with creating/owning Hansch Landscaping. I gained valuable hands on experience running a small business with entrepreneurship flair to stay ahead of the competition and continually grow.

While operating Hansch Landscaping, I gained experience working within a governmental agency by becoming a volunteer fire fighter in a very high energy dynamic environment with the Whippany Fire Department which I still continue to do today.

After selling Hansch Landscaping in 2001, I went on to study at the University of Newcastle (NGSB) in Australia to gain a better international business prospective and obtained my Master of Business Administration and Master of Marketing degrees.

I’m currently utilizing my international exposure, experience and education working at International Flavors and Fragrances. Through my experiences I’ve always been exceedingly involved and made it my goal to try and experience as much of the organization as I could, and strive to work my way up through the ranks. By doing so, I continually learn and better not only myself, but the organizations that I’m involved with.
