Social Media Strategist. Speaker. Educator. New Media Guru.

Jennifer is an Emmy-nominated Web Executive with a one-two punch: extensive senior-level experience in editorial management, social media/Web 2.0 as well as technical product expertise. She bridges the gap between ideas and implementation.
