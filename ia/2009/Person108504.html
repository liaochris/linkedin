Jeff Ross’s Summary

Not only is Jeff (J.J.) a gifted, award winning graphic designer, but he also illustrates, animates, translates, and frequently oxygenates. You will usually find him glued to his Apple product of choice, or with a pen in hand, emptying his brain onto paper in the form of shapes, scribbles, letters, and creatures with more than two eyes. Jeff has a BFA in Graphic Design, and his projects resonate simplicity of design that is useful and creative. And he feels that if your senses do a jig while exposed to his work, he's done something right.

Jeff Ross’s Specialties:

Identity/Brand Design, Collateral/Print Design, Web/UI design, xhtml/css Web Production, Flash Animation, Pen & Ink/Digital Illustration
