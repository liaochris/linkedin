Jon Fisher’s Summary

Oh you know... a little of this and a little of that.

I'm a wheeler and dealer by nature, but not in a scummy way.

Most of my contacts are private here on LinkedIn, so I can't flaunt my coolness for being so well connected here. Shame. I was really looking for that extra ego boost.

My goal is to continue defying the predictions of my grade school and high school teachers who told me I would never amount to anything if I didn't pay attention in their classes, get a bad "final grade", and continue to be a smart ass in their class. Well, I'm still waiting for that long division problem that we spent so much time learning and practicing to be the final decider in a major life changing decision like we were warned about! What a let down!

On a more serious and truthful note though. I spent many years of my career being very serious and not very vocal. In 2006, that all changed. I decided, that since I had been pretty successful in my two previous ventures, a pioneer in the early dotcom days, and a survivor of the dot-com bust, then I was just going to start being vocal about things, actions, people and companies that bugged me, and to tell it how it is, no matter what. I also insist on using humor in almost everything, as it continues to amuse and entertain me and the people around me. I'm a big fan, for sure. I'm also trying to just soak in as much material from a never-ending self educating experience via the internets. Finally, I just want to be able to retire someday and look back and feel proud and accomplished. I don't want to cure any diseases, or figure out how to bring peace to the world. We have plenty of do gooders for that. I just want to have that ultimate sense of accomplishment, and pride for the many hours, dollars and chats I've spent over the years perfecting the art of influential marketing techniques, however unique or out of the box, or even sometimes radical.

That's it. Take it how you will. I'll invoice you later.
