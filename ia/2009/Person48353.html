Colin Powers’s Summary

I have worked in newspapers for the past dozen years, including several in management both with a smaller paper (about 50,000) and with a big city daily. I have worked on the content side — with writing experience and as an assignment editor working with a team of staff reporters, freelancers and clerks — and on the production side. I have designed pages, written headlines, edited copy for grammar and style, selected and edited stories from news wires, booked sections and overseen a universal copy and design desk.

Colin Powers’s Specialties:

• My specialty is putting together a complete news or features package — making both word and visual work together. I can pick wire stories for a page, edit them, write headlines, pick photos and lay out the page.
• Working with entertainment, pop culture and food coverage.
• Putting together exciting front pages, whether breaking news on deadline (I put together my paper's covers for 9/11 and the Iraq invasion, among other big stories) or special projects.
