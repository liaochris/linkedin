Keith G Kennedy’s Summary

In 1978 I studied electronics and have been involved in the technology industry ever since.
I started my first company on Prince Edward Island 1n 1982.
Worked in Charlottetown and surrounding communities as a journeyman carpenter and electrician and landscape designer .

I founded and contributed to the development of the Adventure Group. As executive director I completed many projects such as the ROPES Course and the learning trails located next to the QEH and the Charlottetown Skate Park in Charlottetown.


My goals are to create learning tools for people to understand and appreciate the impact of learning throughout all stages of our lives and to fully explore the opportunities within our history and heritage for future generations.

I create tools for future solutions and networking using social networking programs.

For me it all about legacy and the role we play to help our children grow.

Most important to me is my family who I love very much.

I am very blessed to live in York Prince Edward Island with my partner Lynn and our daughter Katelyn and son Jesse.

Currently I am working on The Prince Edward Island Railway.
I am working to create new search engines and content location tools for students and Island communities to both learn and market our wonderful cultural heritage.

MODE new media learning searches will market our cultural content to the world.

I am intuitive and I like people .

I am a creative person .
I like to create and develop operational sustainability.
