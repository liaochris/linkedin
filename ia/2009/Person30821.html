Bonnie Browndorf’s Summary

Bonnie Browndorf has spent her entire career in the creation and development of start up businesses. In using her entrepreneurial attributes she has designed ventures in wholesale distribution, thoroughbred racing, real estate and the title industry.

Ms. Browndorf devoted many years to the running of a of a thoroughbred breeding and racing business while raising her children , continuing in college and creating and growing a wholesale manufacturing company which she solely developed, managed and marketed.

Bonnie Browndorf is Director of Marketing for Browndorf PEM, LLC, a private equity management company and pending registered investment advisor, with a focus on niche venture capital markets and distressed real estate markets. Mrs. Browndorf has an extensive background in Market Development and has spent her entire career in the creation of start up businesses.

Ms. Browndorf obtained a Bachelor of Arts degree in Psychology from Temple University, Philadelphia, Pennsylvania and was the author and originator of the pre law society for a Temple campus.

She has over 22 years experience in real estate and the title industry with 16 years as the broker and owner her own real estate company and 12 years as the owner of her own title company. Articles published: Unique Homes-Mid Atlantic Real Estate, Philadelphia Inquirer-Real Estate –Conditions and current market evaluations. Mrs. Browndorf also earned the additional real estate Designations and Degrees GRI (Graduate Real Estate Institute) and CRS (Certified residential Specialist).

Ms. Browndorf has applied her innate talents for marketing and sales to each of her ventures. She is skilled in the analysis of market presentation and development.

Bonnie Browndorf’s Specialties:

negotiation, start up, innovations, creative resolutions, artistic design, contract solutions, entrepreneurial skills and processes.
