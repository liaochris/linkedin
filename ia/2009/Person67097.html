Kali Kunkle’s Summary

I am an ex-Paramedic and LPN (or LVN, depending on your state), a Domestic Engineer, a Super Mom, and an Entrepreneur. In 2008, we opened DreamWorthy Gifts LLC.

****************** http://www.DreamWorthyGifts.com ******************

DreamWorthy Gifts is a Web store with thousands of products including giftware, collectibles, candles, bath & body, home & kitchen decor, garden & outdoor items, toys & games, seasonal items, religious home accents, men’s and women’s accessories, virtual gift cards, gift certificates and more.

For products that we don't offer yet or where we have limited offerings, we have a DreamWorthy Gifts | Amazon.com Associate Store, with books, music, DVDs, computer & video games, software, electronics, video on demand videos, cell phones & service, magazine subscriptions, apparel, accessories, handbags and jewelry.

We are dedicated to providing a safe, productive and enjoyable online shopping experience for everyone with a computer, an Internet connection, and a need to shop for something for someone for some reason (including themselves, and hey, even if the reason is "just because!").

We are serious about running a sound business built on a solid foundation of integrity and common sense.

We are much more than business people behind a computer monitor. We are also family members, Moms, Dads, brothers, sisters, sons, daughters, co-workers, neighbors, and everyday real people. We pay too much for gas, are far too busy with work and life, and sometimes don't know what to get that special someone, when it's time to show we care.

That's why we started DreamWorthy Gifts LLC. We want to help regular people like us find the right gift, at the right time, at the right price... but also without a few things, too... without driving and wasting gas, without the hassle of crowded stores and long lines, and without losing so much of our precious time.
