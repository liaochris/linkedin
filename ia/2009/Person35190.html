Bryan Thompson’s Summary

Mr. Thompson is an Oracle Consultant with 8 years functional and technical implementation experience within the Manufacturing, Oil & Gas, Life Science, Publishing and Education sectors. With an expertise in Oracle Order to Cash, he has participated in several global implementations of the Order Management, Advanced Pricing, and Shipping Execution modules where his duties encompassed business process discovery, scope and design, module configuration, client training, and go-live support. Mr. Thompson also has assisted with the support of other Oracle modules that include Inventory, Costing, Telesales, Project Manufacturing, Purchasing, Account Receivables, Advanced Collections, iPayment (Payments) and System Administration. In addition to his functional skills, Mr. Thompson has extensive technical experience with custom Oracle forms and reports development, PL/SQL programming, and data analysis for the Oracle Enterprise Suite, SCT Banner and other Oracle-based systems.

Bryan Thompson’s Specialties:

Primary Expertise in Oracle R12 & 11i Consulting, ERP Consulting, Order to Cash Business Processes, Systems Integration, Systems Consolidation and Relational Database Development.

Secondary Expertise in Web Development, Database Development, eCommerce, and Web Hosting Management.
