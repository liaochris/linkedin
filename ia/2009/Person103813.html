Janet Palma, AICP’s Summary

At present I serve as a Project Manager for small to large environmental studies for a variety of programmatic and project plans. I manage subconsultants and clients for projects such as General Plans, Specific Plans, Annexations, LAFCO work, tranportation, landfills, school districts, energy, and other land use development projects. My goal to be a leader in climate change, green energy, and sustainability issues on nationwide issues.

I have also now renewed my involvement in the real estate market with the hope of turning around the ability of people to own homes and real property investments. I am also a notary public.

Superior customer service is my goal. I feel that all these abilities, knowledge, trainings can work in conjunction to assist my clients and customers. Check my recommendations on LinkedIn or request a reference not posted from a former client.

I was the moderator for a session on the Water Utilities Climate Adaptability research program in March 2009 at the California State AEP Conference in San Francisco. Speakers on the panel were from the SFPUC and SDWA. This group is seeking new ways for utility providers to adapt to climate change.

Janet Palma, AICP’s Specialties:

CEQA/NEPA, permitting, land use planning, energy, transportation, infrastructure, school modernization and real estate.
