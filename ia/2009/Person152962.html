Mark R. Weaver, LD’s Summary

I am always looking to be in a position where working with the assembled team is as advantageous as standing out within it.

Working with ABCNEWS for nearly seven years and then with Disney's ESPN exposed me to a fast-paced, high-pressure environment, involving personnel management, production services and operations. I've also maintained strong business relationships with all levels of personnel which has furnished me with a vast foundation to bring to any relationship.

My construction management experience with Cirque du Soleil for their first ever showroom in Las Vegas and Lincoln Center's Avery Fisher Hall's historic 1992 renovation gave me a uniquely rounded background.

The broadcast talent I have most enjoyed working with includes; Barbara Walters, Hugh Downs, Peter Jennings, Jim Mckay, David Brinkley, Spenor Christian, Charles Gibson, Diane Sawyer, Chris Cuomo, Debra Roberts, Lara Spensor and Pat O'Brien.
