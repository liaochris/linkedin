Franz Neumeier’s Summary

Basically, I'm a journalist and editorial expert - print and online -, having started at daily newspapers. Experienced in print magazine publishing, editorial concepts and change management, print-online convergence.

From being editor-in-chief of Internet Professionell and Internet Magazin for some years now as well as from very successfully runnning my private website steamboats.org I've got a lot of experience in webdesign/webdevelopment and SEO as well as Web 2.0 technology and online trends.

From the different positions as Editor-in-Chief of PC Professionell, Internet Professionell, Internet Magazin and Computer im Mittelstand I'm experienced in leading quite big editorial teams as well as very small teams.

Having managed successfully an international project (editorial convergence and joint testing facilities of several computer magazine all over Europe) at VNU, I can also offer some expertise in international business co-operation.
