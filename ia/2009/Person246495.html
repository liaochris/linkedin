Steve Wells’s Summary

I am interested in helping organisations achieve their goals through creating real-time strategies, effective collaboration and sustainable change.

Working with clients to help them achieve their goals I focus on:

Strategic Support - Strategy Development & Strategic Planning. Helping clients to make sense of the external environment through horizon scanning, external and internal stakeholder engagement, analysis, planning and learning.

Collaboration - Collaborative Working & Partnership Development. Supporting clients in developing and implementing effective collaboration strategies.

Training & Development - Training & Facilitated Learning. Developing client capability in strategy development and collaborative working.

I am experienced in strategy development, strategic and operational planning, collaborative working, partnership development, and workshop facilitation.

With over 25 years experience in the pharmaceuticals industry, my most recent roles were as an internal consultant in Partnership Development at Pfizer where I was responsible for developing partnership strategy, business partnerships with NHS organisations and supporting the development of colleague capabilities. Prior to this, I was Strategic Development Manager where I led a variety of projects including supporting the consolidation of the business post-merger, introducing new approaches to environmental scanning and strategy development.

I have presented on strategy development and horizon scanning at various cross-industry conferences, business schools and NHS organisations and is an active member of the Horizon Scanning Centre's Futures Analysts Network.
