Sam Trujillo’s Summary

Over 20 years experience U.S./Global; consumer and pharmaceutical marketing, department leadership, strategic planning, new product commercialization, life cycle management, field sales (specialty sales and managed care management) with Fortune 100 companies such as Bayer, Novartis, Warner-Lambert and Procter & Gamble and start-up companies such as Shire (originally Richwood Pharmaceuticals).

Track record of success serving on or leading teams for many well-known brands. Over a dozen product launches, four of which have reached blockbuster status. Over two dozen lifecycle and product extensions implemented.

Experienced in pharmaceutical and consumer product marketing, globally and in US across several therapeutic categories (oncology, neurology, cardiovascular, psychiatry, anti-infective, respiratory, gastroenterology, allergy, primary care, women’s health, pediatric, dermatology, health & personal care, etc.).
