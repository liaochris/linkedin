Andrew Brenner’s Summary

Andrew has over 5 years of experience in wireless industry business development and product management at both venture-backed startups and public companies.



Andrew is particularly interested in the areas of rich client interfaces, declarative user interfaces, and digital cameraphone imaging.

In addition to his experience in wireless software, Andrew spent six years as an intellectual property attorney, working on IP litigation, technology licensing, and venture financing deals.
