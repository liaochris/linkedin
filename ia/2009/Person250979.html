Zack Davis’s Summary

The saying "Jack of all trades" can sum up my experience. At first I saw this as a weakness. I could never be #1 in my school.

But then as I began to experience more, I saw that my knowledge of other trades were greater than most of my peers who were studying our skills. I used this to my advantage and began to work harder on know other technologies that could increase my design skills. Being a jack of all trades is a strength now, I am a quick learner and with my knowledge of many trades I am able to excel.

My goals are to be able to manage a interactive department bridging the gap between the designers and programmers and create new and exciting sites. I am constantly trying to learn new languages and train myself how to code in different ways with different technologies. I believe that the only way to truly immerse the viewer into your project is to use all the technologies available to create a diverse interactive experience.
