Anthony Guerra’s Summary

Over the past 10 years, I have been a lead editor at three publications in three different industries. As such, I have been exposed to a number of management styles, adopting those which fostered success, eschewing those that produced results to the contrary. I have happily settled on the fascinating field of healthcare IT, targeting the CIO-level executive (and aspirant). I am a strategic thinker, one who understands the importance of setting a vision, then embarking on the tactical work necessary to make it a reality. I enjoy strengthening the brands I lead by narrowly defining a target audience and serving that audience relentlessly, intelligently and, above all, with the highest journalistic ethics. I most enjoy creating online communities for my readers, allowing them to not just receive information from the Brand, but interact with each other. I am currently developing and refining strategies for leveraging social networking applications, such as LinkedIn and Twitter, to facilitate reader interaction and support.
