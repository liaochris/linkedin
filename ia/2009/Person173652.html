Noel Hartshorn’s Summary

I am a highly experienced technical writer with an excellent track record producing technical documentation, developing training content and consulting on content strategies, processes and best practices. I am particularly interested in new technologies and their scope for improving documentation development, delivery and reuse.

I am hard working, focussed and possess strong communication skills. I have been described as having a refreshing approach to tasks undertaken, showing interest and enthusiasm towards the projects in which I am involved.

More recently I have been focussing on iPhone development, having taught myself Objective-C and Cocoa Touch. I am currently developing and marketing iPhone apps, in partnership with an illustrator.

Whilst I have my own current projects, I am interested in opportunities within the IT industry, particularly with blue chip companies offering interesting and permanent/full-time work with opportunities abroad. I am willing and keen to relocate to the United States.
