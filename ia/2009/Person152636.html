Mark Sherrill’s Summary

In my 15+ years of professional experience, I am fortunate to have worked for such a wide spectrum of clients. I have provided creative services in the areas of identity /branding, web design, and interaction design to both for-profit and non-profit organizations. My focus is working closely with clients to fully understand their business goals and then providing creative, engaging, and innovative design solutions.

I have worked in an agency environment, spent considerable time as a freelance professional, and have been a managing partner of my own firm.
