Laura Nathan’s Summary

Writer, editor, copywriter, and blogger for magazines, websites, books and companies addressing health, fitness, nutrition, photography, travel, education, women's issues, and books/literary culture.

Clients include Redbook, Cooking Light, Photo District News, The Writer's Chronicle, Thomas Riggs & Co., Encyclopedia.com, Mother Jones, Paste, Bitch, Barnes & Noble, The Travel Channel, and the Community Health Foundation of Western and Central New York.

Laura Nathan’s Specialties:

Article and blog writing and editing; copy writing; project management; creative writing; content aggregation; planning meetings and events; web branding and marketing; social media; SEO; PC and Mac proficient; Dreamweaver; Photoshop; InDesign; Sharepoint; Access; content management systems such as Wordpress, TeamSite, Joomla, Content-Link, and Stylus; EmailLabs; highly proficient in HTML; familiar with the Associated Press Stylebook and the Chicago Manual of Style.
