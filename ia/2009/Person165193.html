Mike Levin’s Summary

I view the field of search engine optimization (SEO) as an extension of Peter Drucker's opinion that the mission of any company is to get and keep customers. SEO is core to every business, because it (along with social media) is today the first line of attack in getting and keeping customers.

Creator of HitTail.com, a business intelligence product named one of the most innovative of the year by BusinessWeek magazine, my passion since '99 has been search engine optimization. I've also enjoyed many aspects of business, specializing wherever needed, as demonstrated at Scala where I created their first search-friendly website, lead-capture system, unique user tracker/identifier, sales force automation system, multi-channel management system, web store, and order management system. I also wrote press releases, evangelized the old Amiga user base, blogged, beta tested software, built/administrated mission-critical servers, and played a key role in creating the new industry of digital signage.

But with SEO as my passion, I followed my dream (and Adam Edwards) to New York to develop a suite of search marketing products for the PR firm that launched Amazon.com. So August 2005 I went agency-side with Connors Communications. One of the products I created became the BusinessWeek acclaimed HitTail keyword research tool. Later, in my time with Hachette Filipacchi Media, the publishers of Car and Driver and Elle magazine, I refined my skills for New York media publishers where the traffic is the product. Today with 360i, I regularly consult on search strategy and enthusiastically tackle the biggest natural-search problems in the industry.
