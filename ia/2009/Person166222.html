Mitchell Yow’s Summary

I have worked primarily in the automotive aftermarket industry for over twenty years. First as an ASE certified automotive, truck and bus mechanic, then as an automotive and Diesel instructor for Greer and Lincoln Technical Institute and then I went into the sales side of the automotive aftermarket. For the past twenty years I have enjoyed a growing career in sales, marketing and management and more recently as a business development consultant.

For fun I have enjoyed many motorsports over the years. I built and campaigned a 1/4 mile D-modified production camaro, helped build and wrenched on a short track ASA super stock mustang, hot rodded a 57 chevy, built and had loads of fun with a radical 914 Porsche along with a few others. Along the way I have helped many friends and clients with their project cars and racing passions.

Mitchell Yow’s Specialties:

Ideal client profiling, product and concept branding, business plans and strategies, Internet marketing, effective marketing campaigns and promotions, sales training and general management. In short, I specialize in helping performance oriented automotive companies grow.
