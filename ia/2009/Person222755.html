Stephanie Barton’s Summary

I divide a fairly equal amount of my time between writing and editing. I write articles on topics I never imagined I'd cover, such as reducing your taxes or giving a good wedding toast. I like discovering where I can put a little cheek into something like personal finance. I also develop marketing materials for businesses and nonprofits--but I don't get cheeky with those clients.

All of my editing work is in the nonfiction arena: I edit books, journal articles, manuals, and annual reports. Again the subject matter is eclectic: software, health, law, education, and finance. I'm an aggressive editor but I try to temper it by being really friendly.

Recent clients include Wiley & Sons, ForbesTraveler.com, Investopedia.com, Aloha United Way and U.S. Air Force Public Affairs.
