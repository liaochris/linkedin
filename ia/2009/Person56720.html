David Porter’s Summary

Blog Consulting, LLC was organized in April of 2009, out of a deep love of building communities in organizations.

In April of 2006, we sold our Michigan based financial services company, and moved to Scottsdale, Arizona. If I had to put forth the one thing that we did which allowed for our success, it would easily be our ability to create trust, and life-long relationships with our patrons.

While flying home from a business seminar, in 2005, I was reading an article in Business Week, which claimed that blogging would change my business.

I caught the vision, and quickly built the nations #2 blog, in my particular niche.

Since selling our company, I started and built another blog, in a far narrower niche, and am enjoying success there as well.

Because of my love for this medium, and our obvious ability to connect with people, we decided to take this skill from hobby, to a living, breathing business.

As a business owner, I painfully understood how expensive it was to communicate with my clients. Furthermore, unless I was willing to sit in my clients e-mail in-boxes, surrounded by junk mail, I needed to change my ways.

A business armed with their own social media site, essentially has at their disposal a TV station, a radio station, a magazine, and a newspaper, all rolled up into one technology.

Now businesses, and organizations have the ability to create powerful video, audible podcasts, or simply text blog posts.

Nonetheless, these tools are very inexpensive, they deliver your messages immediately, and most of all, they provide a function to allow your patrons to give you feedback.

We are here to help you build your own social media site. We will use blogging tools, Facebook, Twitter, and what ever else might come down the line in the future.

One you build your site, and begin to use the tools, you will wonder why you didn't make the communication switch months, and months ago.
