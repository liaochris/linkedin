Jay Amato’s Summary

Until founding PersonalScreen Media (personalscreen.com) recently nominated for a 2008 Emmy Award, Jay Amato had been President and CEO of NASDAQ traded Viewpoint Corporation, an interactive media company, providing cutting-edge online video and interactive rich media advertising for leading companies such as DreamWorks, Nextel, Polo, Pepsi, Toyota, MGM and Paramount.

Recruited to Viewpoint in August 2003 to engineer a turn-around and in that short period managed to introduce two major new product offerings, leading the company to growth and profitability for the first time in its fifteen-year history.

As President and COO of NYSE traded Vanstar Corporation, I led the company through dozens of acquisitions and rapid organic growth from a $25 million retail computer franchiser (ComputerLand Corporation) to a leading network integrator serving the corporate marketplace. The company grew to nearly $3 billion in revenues.

Following my tenure at Vanstar, I led the formation of the Technology Access Action Coalition/TAAC, a Washington-based congressional lobbying organization comprised of technology lead-ers including Microsoft whose mission was to promote continued innovation and growth within the technology sector.

Jay Amato’s Specialties:

Demonstrated excellence in executive leadership in high growth environments, large scale financing, successful management of acquisitions, business re-design and strategic/organizational transformation. A proven change agent with competencies focused in:
• Overall company vision development and execution
• The ability to communicate that vision to employees, investors and partners
• Technology deployment to effectively measure, improve and leverage backend efficiencies
