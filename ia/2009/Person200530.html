Robert Kehoe’s Summary

Robert has broad entrepreneurial experience, including 10 years as CEO/founder of a highly profitable, 46-employee software development firm, plus the launching of 5 additional (all very different) businesses. Also, he worked 2.5 years as director of a non-profit business incubator, where he led a successful financial turnaround, and counseled 100's of would-be entrepreneurs. Robert also has had short stints as a political candidate and business professor, and has served on 20+ non-profit boards.

Robert returned a year ago from 2 years in Beijing where he studied the Mandarin Chinese language and culture, and authored two language textbooks. His language skills are best described as advanced Beginner or lower Intermediate, which is enough to be credible in business transactions. He has similar conversational skills in Spanish, but with a greater ability in reading Spanish.

Recently completing consulting assignments, Robert is now seeking opportunities for service in a broad range of business areas. He would prefer to remain in Columbus, Ohio, but is willing to relocate (even internationally) for the right opportunity.

Robert Kehoe’s Specialties:

Startup counseling, strategic planning, consultative sales, project management, financial turnaround, fundraising, public speaking, teaching, writing.
