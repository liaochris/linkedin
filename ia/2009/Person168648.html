  • Production assistant, Public Speaking Coach & adjudicator for ET1 broadcast 'Logomahies'(Λογομαχίες)

    ERT S.A. HELLENIC BROADCASTING CORPORATION

    (Public Company; Broadcast Media industry)

    August 2005 — July 2006 (1 year)

    Logomaxies ("Λογομαχίες" - "Speech-battles") was a TV-show that aired 12 times during Spring of 2006. It was essentially a university student debating tournament with eight 2-member teams that debated against each other on various controversial topics. Each team had three speeches: a 2' introduction, a 2 1/2' rebuttal and a 1 1/2' summary. There was a panel of 5 judges (which consisted of university professors, an actor and rhetoric trainers) that commented on various aspects of the debate and voted for the winning team.

    The debates were recorded as if live (i.e. no edits afterwards) and were broadcast by ET1 on Saturdays at 10-11pm. The students learned of the topic one week before recording. The side that they were going to defend was determined by draw one day before recording.

    My responsibilities entailed:
    a. recruitment, training and support of the university debate teams
    b. recruitment and management of the panel of judges
    c. assisting the argumentation and facts research for each topic and the creation of a 2-4' introductory video
    d. editing the script
    e. participating as a judge in 5 debates
