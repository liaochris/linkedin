Andrew Simes’s Summary

Andrew has over nineteen years experience successfully delivering strategic business and technology programmes and projects in a wide range of industry sectors. Over the last twelve years, he has furthered his management experience and capability through providing management consulting advice and skills to the executive and senior management of a number of New Zealand, Australia, and China’s foremost organisations.

As a programme and project manager, Andrew brings a strong, persuasive yet resilient management style to ensure that all projects he is involved with are focused upon delivering their defined benefits within their stated constraints. As a management consultant, Andrew is purposeful in managing and delivering to the expectations of his clients. He is an experienced people manager, effective at leading and managing teams to succeed within challenging environments and cultures.

Andrew has a Master of Project Management (MPM) degree from the University of Technology, Sydney. He is a certified PRINCE2 Practitioner and has additionally been trained in, and worked with, a number of formal programme, project and risk management methodologies including MSP, TCM, PMM, ITPM, SIIPS, P-Plus, and PRISM. Andrew’s Myers-Briggs type indicator is ENTP.

In addition to managing business change through roles and assignments in programme and project management, Andrew maintains an extensive network of relationships with clients, associates and other consultancies where he utilises his entrepreneurial skills to develop proposals and bids for competitive procurement opportunities. This work has supported the sustainability and growth of A. M. Simes Limited over the ten years that the business has been operating.

Andrew Simes’s Specialties:

Project Management, Programme Management, IS/IT Strategic Planning, Management Consulting, Economic Analysis, Risk Management, Mentoring & Coaching, Systems & Solutions Integration, Enterprise Architecture, Business Continuity Planning, Disaster Recovery Planning
