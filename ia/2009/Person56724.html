David Antonio Wagner Toledano’s Summary

John DiJulius is regarded as one of the leading authorities on customer service, organizations across America use his philosophies and systems for creating world class service. Best-selling author of TWO books, he has worked with companies such as the Ritz Carltons, Lexus, Nordstroms, Starbucks, Hallmark Cards, Panera Bread, Cheesecake Factory, Progressive Insurance, US Bank, Nemacolin Resort, Seattle’s Best Coffee, Chick-fil-A, and many more, to help them continue to raise the bar and set the standard in service that consistently exceeds customer expectations. John has pioneered dozens of revolutionary customer service concepts and techniques that are easy to use.
