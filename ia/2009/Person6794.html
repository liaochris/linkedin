Al Cavallo’s Summary

Art Director for NJ based Adcomm Advertising specializing in Direct Response packaging, TV and print. This profile focuses on my fine art and music and I hope to build that aspect of my work. As a fine artist, most work has been digital as of late using procreate Painter or Photoshop. And as a musician, currently working on my &holly (or andholly) project - &holly is in it's 8th year of existence. Without playing out live as much, I turned my attention to recording more of my ideas. The result has been &holly.
