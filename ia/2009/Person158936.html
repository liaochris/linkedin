Mehmet Akten’s Summary

Welcome. My name is Memo (Mehmet) Akten and I'm a musician / creative developer / mad scientist based in London, UK. I work through my company MSA Visuals Ltd. - the Mega Super Awesome Visuals Company - and have worked with corporate brands such as Apple, Rolls-Royce, Texas Instruments, Adidas as well as arts / music organisations such as Ars Electronica, Minitek music + innovation festival, The Glastonbury Festival, Aldeburgh Music, The BBC Philharmonic.

I entered this world of interactive audio visuals in my early teens, when the demo scene was quite big, playing about on the BBC Model B, Apple IIe, Amiga500 and various early PCs (8088, 80236, 80386 etc.).

After spending a few years (1995 - 1997) developing database driven apps for the corporate world (banks, telecommunications companies etc.) using VC++ / VB / SQL Server, I started a job making video games (1998 - 2003) and spent the next 6 years slaving for the industry: building / texturing 3D models and writing modules in C/C++ and x86 assembly (physics, particles, visual effects, 3D plugins). After 4 titles (7 SKUs) released on PC, PSX, PS2, XBox, and GameCube [and a DreamCast port that never got released :( ] I had enough of the monopolization of the video games industry and decided to go freelance in 2003. Since then I have worked on a wide variety of projects ranging from commercial / corporate promos to music videos to PHP / MySQL sites and full Flash apps.

While a lot of my earlier commercial work is generally pre-rendered video created in AfterEffects, Cinema 4D, Flash, Combustion, Shake, 3DSMax, Motion etc; lately I have moved more into (and my biggest passion is) interactive generative / programmatic audio & visuals - and thats mostly what I focus on now - Human-Computer Interactions, Computer Vision, Processing.org (& Java), OpenFrameworks ( & C++), Quartz Composer, VDMX, ActionScript 3.0 and related subjects.
