Miles Aronnax’s Summary

Going back to school after working in the IT industry for several years has provided me with a solid working background in computer technology--what they can do and what they can't. Additionally, my programming experience has made me meticulous and allowed me to develop an ability to quickly and logically breakdown systems and data for analysis. I believe these skills will be a great benefit to a research career in physics.

Overall, my goal in life is--quoting Carl Sagan--to "reduce the error bars". Beyond this, I find physics to be a very fulfilling in another way: it provides a deeper understanding of the universe in which we live. Now when I look at a wall, I see it not just as a divider or support, but also as a system of several molecules in thermal equilibrium, held together by electromagnetic interactions, constantly interacting with incoming photons in such as way as to reflect some and absorb others. To think of it as just a wall is somehow to belittle the reality.

On June 3rd, 2008, I had my name legally changed from "Luke Matthew Henry Wilson" to "Miles Vincent Aronnax".
