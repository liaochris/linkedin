Angel Vera’s Summary

Attended a Stage and Dramatic Arts School in Tijuana Baja California, Mexico
at "Centro de Artes Escenicas del Noroeste" (CAEN) 3rd generation.
Performed as a swinging trapeze artist, contemporary Dancer,
actor and acrobat at a Circus/Night Club (“ZOOLL”) in Baja California Mexico for 3 years.
Trained and coached aerial acrobatics at "GTC" (Gymnastics Training Center) from 2004 to 2006.
Coached an aerial acrobatics class at the “Royal Academy of Dance”
on Saturday mornings in 2006.
Trained and coached aerial Acrobatics at "Bounce California" gymnastics training facility in Poway, CA from 2006 to 2008.
Trained at "Trapeze High" in Escondido California.
Coached boy gymnastics and aerial acrobatics to children and adults at "Gym Ventures, Inc." a gymnastics facility in Pacific Beach, California from 2007 to 2008.
Member of the San Diego based troupe "Cirque for Christ."
Member and Co-Founder of the San Diego based troupe "Aerial Revolution Entertainment" and "Aerial Revolution Academy."
