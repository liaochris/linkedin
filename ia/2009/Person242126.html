Vered Sharon’s Summary

• At Calyx, my advisory services extends from providing fund-raising and business development assistance with a focus on industry-defining companies in natural resource-saving technology, biotech, and broadly-defined technology companies, including VoIP and new media.

• Have enjoyed spending my career building emerging-growth companies in various capacities: business development and marketing, raising capital for well over 70 companies from both private and public equity markets (Top-tier seed-stage VC round to IPO), investing, providing investment banking services, and serving on Boards as Director.

• As an investment banker, I was fortunate to be the 1st Israeli, technology-focused investment banker to raise funding on Nasdaq for Israeli-founded companies (initially with F-1; and overwhelming majority subsequently with S-1 filing and headquarters in Silicon Valley). At the time was with H&Q/JPMorgan.

• While at H&Q my business development and sales efforts resulted in over $350 million additional firm-wide revenue, and well over $5 billion raised for emerging growth companies. I went on to serve in a bus dev and marketing executive position at WebGlide (now RichFX), an Internet shopping application company.And invest in seed-stage and later stage investments in consumer Internet companies as a VC with Veritas Venture Partners of Herzeliya, Israel, and investment fund co-manager at Private Equity Partners of New York, NY.

• Finally, bring an extensive and deep network of leading fund, CEOs, and generaly speaking, individuals of high net worth.
