David James Nielsen’s Summary

David James Nielsen is a composer for Film, Television, and Multimedia. He has composed original music for 6 independent feature films, television series, shorts films, and theatrical plays, for various production companies. Some of his credits as a composer include RECLAIMING THE BLADE (documentary), TALES FROM BEYOND (feature film), HAUNTING VILLISCA (feature film), SMALL TOWN FOLK (feature film) ,THE LITTLE DOCUMENTARY THAT COULDN'T (feature film), and ANIMAL EXPLORATION WITH JAROD MILLER (TV series).

David also has worked in various roles as an additional music composer, orchestrator, and synth programmer for the popular TV series STAR TREK: ENTERPRISE, and SUPERNATURAL, the National Geographic Special EDEN AT THE END OF THE WORLD, and the television movies, THE TENTH CIRCLE, FLIGHT 93, and FAITH OF MY FATHERS

David James Nielsen’s Specialties:

-Conducting, Orchestrating, MIDI Synthesizer Programming
-Knowledge of creating music on Macintosh and PC computers
