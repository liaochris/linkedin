Somnath Baishya’s Summary

Somnath has had a rounded HR exposure covering areas of Organization Development, Compensation & Benefits, Recruitment, Employee Relations and Communications. He has had led HR in Regional offices as well has been in the Corporate set up.

Somnath enjoys the challenges of conceptualizing and designing new programs and practices in the HR arena aligned to the organization's need in an environment of high growth and complexity. He has led various talent branding interventions internally as well as externally with Stanford, Harvard, SHRM etc. Key focus has also involved competency and capability development at a strategic level and driving productivity and efficiency through outsourcing and automation.
