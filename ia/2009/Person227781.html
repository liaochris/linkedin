Stacy Montgomery’s Summary

I have built my career around my passion, which is embracing my own unique beauty and healthy, plus-sized body. I have always been full-figured, and I am a great looking size 16. I am healthy, beautiful, and proud to be me. With this uncommon self-esteem for a woman my size, I was invited to be in charge of sales for SWAK Designs. SWAK is unlike any other company I've seen. The fashions are young and exciting, filled with color and loaded with vibrant confidence. At SWAK Designs we cover everything from "the little black dress" to everyday fashions and outerwear.

Stacy Montgomery’s Specialties:

Color specialist. Spokesperson and fashion expert for the plus-sized clothing industry. Advocate for the full-figured young woman of today. At SWAK Designs, we seek to inspire, encourage, affirm, and celebrate women everywhere.
