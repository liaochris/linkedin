Jan Libby’s Summary

Jan created the popular indie Alternate Reality Games - Sammeeeees & Wrath of Johnson (Sam II). After Sammeeeees, she spent nearly a year working with LG15 Studios (on the Lonelygirl15 Series and the Jumper ARG) as a Writer and Interactive Designer. Jan has been a speaker and panelist for ARGfests and the NATPE LATVfest. She also works as a New Media/Interactive consultant to Media Companies and Agencies. Having wrapped on GMD's latest installment of the horror/sci-fi Eldritch Errors, Jan is now working with W & K and Levi's on the Go Fourth Fortune.

Jan Libby’s Specialties:

highly immersive and interactive storyworlds, crossmedia experience design, alternate reality game design, innovative use of social media platforms, community building
