Paul Dietenhofer’s Summary

My goal is to address and support the need for time critical high end rigid printed circuit boards for commercial and military applications. For over 30 years now I have been associated with some of the best board fabricators in North America.

Technology continues to drive smaller footprints and higher speeds which are areas that I and MEI have stayed ahead of the curve. A recent expansion provides a truly state-of-the-art facility. I am able to address your prototype and small to mid volumes domestically and can work with you to support a successful transfer to offshore volume fabrication.

Paul Dietenhofer’s Specialties:

Rigid printed circuit board fabrication to 50+ layers
BGA footprints as small as .25 mm
Blind / buried / micro / stacked / staggered / filled vias
18:1 Aspect ratio, 5 mil drill, 4 mill laser
High speed, RF and Polyimide laminates
RoHS and Lead-free applications
IPC-6012 Class 3 and ITAR & JCP Registered
Commitment to Quality through PCQR2 Benchmarking
