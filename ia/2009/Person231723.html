Nonglak Thanarak’s Summary

Nonglak acquired several years of experience in the hotel and fast moving consumer goods industries and spent over a decade working in the area of executive recruitment of fast trackers and high caliber executives at Diageo. In executive search, she has experience with MERC, PricewaterhouseCoopers, The Wright/Manaco Consulting as Principal Consultant.

With several years of experience in executive search, she plays a key role in establishing a Singapore based GSI Executive Recruitment in Thailand. Nonglak specializes in search for executives across industry sectors, particularly Pharmaceuticals, FMCGs, Luxury Products, Retail and Manufacturing industries.
