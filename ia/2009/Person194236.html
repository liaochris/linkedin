Paula Werne’s Summary

As PR Director at Holiday World & Splashin' Safari, my primary raison d'etre is media relations. I also keep our website updated and post regularly on our HoliBlog. It's a great job and I plan to be a "lifer." In my spare time (ha!) I am on the board of directors of the Lincoln Boyhood Drama Association, which will open a new Lincoln drama in June, 2009.
