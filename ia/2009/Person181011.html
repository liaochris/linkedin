Richard Papík’s Summary

Starts and continuing practice in scientific, technical and economical information, or STM - scientific, technical and medical information; and step by step in positions of information specialist or information manager in online retrieval and online services, reference services, R & D information support, decision making information support, project management, research. Lecturing and research at university, also practice and coaching in a variety of institutions and companies are complementary each other. Motto relevant for an information profession and my work can be "Knowledge is of two kinds: we know a subject ourselves, or we know where we can find information upon it." (Dr Samuel Johnson). Motto for teaching of students can be "There are two ways of spreading light... to be the candle, or the mirror that reflects it." (Edith Wharton)

Richard Papík’s Specialties:
