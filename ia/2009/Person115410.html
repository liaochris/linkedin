Joe Cortez | Web & Social Media Professional’s Summary

I am currently keeping busy as a professional freelance writer & reporter. My work has recently been featured on CNN, and you can read much of my writing on several different websites across the internet.

I've worked the last five years in news -- in television, I've grown ratings of a early morning newscast in Columbus, Ohio as a producer, and directed the long-term goals of weekend news programming at a TV station as Weekend Managing Editor in Santa Maria, CA. As a radio producer, I have grown the reach and ratings of every radio station I have reported or produced for, including growing relationships with advertisers and making them sound stronger on the air. As a writer, I've approached my stories with truth to all sides. Turning my attention to what the community needs has grown the reach of every outlet I've written for -- not just in traditional forms such as newspapers and television, but online as well.

I've produced, shot, and edited television reports, written, recorded and edited radio reports and commercials, and written newspaper news and feature stories for a weekly paper. I have a background in supervising journalists, bringing together fantastic elements for a variety of newscasts, managing projects and deadlines from start to finish, and bringing together all the right pieces to turn dreams into reality. My only question is how can I help your message get out to the right people today?
