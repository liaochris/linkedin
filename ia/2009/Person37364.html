My own life intention informs all that I do. I love the adventure, and exploration of all terrains. This journey has bought me to a place, through EVOLVE and NoMad Meetings, where I am very clear about who I want to work with and what we can do together: I thrive on being part of this passionate team helping organisations and influential leaders build relationships of credibility and trust with their communities. We do this through innovative facilitation, meeting and public participation approaches.

If you fall into any of the following four areas, then we should talk:

***
YOU ARE an aware person ready to 'seize the day' and make the most of this life. Then lets talk about Evolve’s Intentional Living Program: Reveal your clear and grounded direction and life purpose through this unique coaching program, bringing you clarity and vitality. .
***
YOU ARE an innovative organisation or individual genuine about seeking peoples ideas and input. You are also practical and need results. Then lets talk about Evolve’s Meeting Marketplace™ an innovative way to bring large groups of people together to exchange information and ideas, taking the best from our long tradition of ‘going to market’.

***
YOU ARE an organisation or individual interested in consistently delivering top quality community engagement processes. Then lets talk about Evolve’s Training: Become an exceptional and inspired community engagement practitioner and facilitator, being trained and mentored with our internationally recognised experts.
