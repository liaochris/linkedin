Don Shapiro’s Summary

Management and marketing consultant for over 24 years. Worked with profit and non-profit organizations in 30 different industries on strategy, structure, marketing and leadership.

Currently writing a book "Life Is A Fork In The Road." Ever followed your inner voice or feelings to make a choice with surprising results? Ever do that with little or no facts to support the path you took? Don is collecting 1000's of stories from all walks of life as research for the book. Share your story, read others' stories and offer comments at www.lifeisaforkintheroad.com

Very enthusiastic and passionate about helping people and organizations grow. Always interested in learning new things and exploring new opportunities. Known for his innovations in the marriage of strategy and structure, strategic marketing, how customers buy, customer value and managing change.

A desire to make this world a better place, make a difference in people's lives and promote real change drives Don's energetic and uplifting approach toward life.

Don Shapiro’s Specialties:

Creative strategies, how structure supports strategy, strategic marketing and sales, how customers make decisions, how people think and how that affects results, leadership, systems thinking and theory applied to organizations, changing cultures to support strategic initiatives, and needs based selling
