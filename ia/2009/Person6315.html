Alan Mokbel’s Summary

My career has set me to the path of water and wastewater treatment with a specialty in membrane technology, which includes reverse osmosis, nanofiltration, ultrafiltration, microfiltration, membrane bioreactors and membrane contactors. My expertise lies in technical solution development, membrane treatment design, project management and sales.

:: A BLURB ABOUT GRUPO AKVOTEK ::

Grupo Akvotek, S.A. de C.V. is an enterprise dedicated to the development and integration of Water Management Solutions, which involves the practices of planning, developing, treating, distribution and optimum utilizing of water resources under defined water norms and regulations. We are a young team of professionals with a specialty and experience in:

* water management and optimization programs
* clean water conditioning
* seawater desalination (reverse osmosis)
* surface water drinking water plants
* boiler and cooling tower water treatment
* wastewater reclamation (including greywater and industrial wastewater)
* municipal and sanitary wastewater treatment

Our systems offer the following benefits:

* low energy and chemical consumption
* small footprint
* modular units easy to operate and maintain

Our services include:

* development and implementation of cost-effective solutions
* development of a water management program
* assessment and optimization of existing water treatment systems
* engineering and process design
* project management
* technical staff training and courses
* water treatment courses and seminars

All our solutions offer water of high quality that meets all the stringent norms from Mexico, United States and Canada.
