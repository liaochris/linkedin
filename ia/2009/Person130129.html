Kenn Parks’s Summary

If I had to describe (only) one attribute about myself; one that would sum up- much of who I am...it would be that- I am passionate in all things, at all times!

Life is a gift; an endless opportunity, how we live it; how we impact others & the planet; our gratitude, the legacy we leave behind- are all the "Actual Measurement of one's success!

Staying active in all facets of life, I seek to continually learn, seek discover and share all that I acquire! I am a father of 4 incredible little boys, an avid outdoor/adventure enthusiast, yachtsman, artist, cook, environmental-social change advocate, philanthropist and ardent fan of history, martial arts, philosophy, world culture, classic literature & film!

I am a passionate, open minded, perpetual student and experienced practitioner in:
• Active Awareness and Pursuit of Sustainable Social, Environmental and Cultural Responsibility, Impact and Change
• Architecting: Original, Innovative & Creative Solutions to all Challenges
• Creating and Maintaining Strategically Aligned Relationships
• Engaging in Effective, Open, Respectful Communication Practices
• Establishing, Benchmarking and Maintaining High Service Levels
• Being Accountable, Goal- Driven and Approachable
• Possessing Perspective, Integrity, Balance and a Sense of Humor
• “Truly” Leading by Example, in all Facets of Leadership

I am privileged to have worked in a multitude of market sectors, industries, influences and leadership capacities; possessing over 20 years experience in: Strategic Planning and Management, Marketing, Public Relations, Communications, Design, Technology, Non-Profit, Logistics and Program/Project Management assignments.
