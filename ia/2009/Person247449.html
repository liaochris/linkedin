Bill Cunningham’s Summary

After receiving a 5 year Masters of Architecture from the nation’s largest private art school, I moved to the Orlando area and I have worked as an architectural and production designer in the Entertainment and Hospitality field for 15 years.
I have spent my career as a Planner and Designer.

I have worked in the Hospitality field developing plans for site of Timeshare and hotel development. I this area I have worked with many of the larger Hotel and Timeshre groups in the United States taking open lots and helping to see the development potential of potential projects. I have taken this though the permitting process and into construction.

The other half of my career has been spent in the Entertainment field of Theme Parks and Cultural Entertainment Parks. I have worked for owners, suppliers and design firms I believe this gives me an insight into all the disciplines of theme park development. I have worked on all aspects of parks including blank paper concept, master planning, architectural Design, ride development, show development, and graphic signage.
