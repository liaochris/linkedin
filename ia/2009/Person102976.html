James Saldanha’s Summary

At Whitech Software Solutions I was responsible for group sales and marketing activities, managing key accounts within the channel, heavily involved in strategic planning and directional setting of the business.

Launched and positioned management point of sale software, photo kiosk and photo online products as market leaders within Australia in the fast changing photographic market.

Actively played a key role in Whitech's emergence as an award winning Australian IT company that has managed to develop exceptional technology within the photographic industry. Whitech won the prestigious National 2005 Telstra and Australian Governments Business of the Year award. Over a dozen other awards have been won in both business and for our range of software products since 2003.

In July 2007 Fujifilm acquired 35% of the business as part of its strategic road map. I was involved in the acquisition process, which presented both companies with unique business opportunities.

After taking 8 months off to travel around India with my wife, I rejoined Whitech in July 2008. The focus was to successfully launch Imagine Online software in October 2008 in conjunction with Fujifilm and one of Australia's major retail chains, Big W. Imagine is a Rich Internet Application that allows customers to upload and order over 300 products including prints, photobooks, calendars, canvas art and photo gifts online. Sales from Imagine exceeded the forecast by10 times for Big W during the first 3 months. Other major retailers are queueing up to utilise the platform. The likes of Harvey Norman, Fairfax media, Teds, WOW have already turned on the application taking it to the number one online platform in Australia.

I moved to San Francisco in July 09 and am working as a business consultant for Whitech USA LLC from September 09 on a term contract sharing my knowledge and experience with the US team.
