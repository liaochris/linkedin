Daniel Crispin’s Summary

With over 2.5 years' recruitment experience in the IT industry, I have developed a strong understanding of the technology, roles, and most importantly people in this field.

I recruit across the infrastructure and support sectors filling positions from 1st Level Helpdesk through to Infrastructure Managers and specialist Consultants. I also recruit IT Sales candidates from Sales Support through to Senior Sales Managers.

Why candidates work with me:

I always provide an honest, consultative approach and invest time in understanding what they are looking for from their next role and where they wish to get to in their career and then advise them on the next step accordingly. I continually strive to build trusting relationships with candidates that I have both placed and those that I have not had the opportunity to yet, and as a result I have developed a strong network of highly skilled individuals to work with both now and in the future.

Why clients work with me:

I pride myself on offering an outstanding service which can save both time and money in the recruitment process. I do not believe in the ‘scattergun’ approach when it comes to CV submittals, and hence when I am given a role I will interview a number of candidates and produce a shortlist of two or three exceptional candidates to submit to the client who I believe are suitable for a role. I believe that to be able to do this successfully it is very important to spend time getting to know the type of individuals that will prosper in each and every one of my clients’ environments and so I work hard to get to know both the company culture and the particular nuances that will accompany a particular role and team.
