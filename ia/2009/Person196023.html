Remco Timmermans’s Summary

Having worked in different management roles within international organization design, process management, off-shoring, leasing, financing and IT for more than 12 years, Remco can be seen as an expert on the crossroads of these areas.

After succesfully finishing University, Remco joined Digital Equipment as information specialist. He then rapidly climbed the management ladder within Digital and later Compaq Services until reaching the role of European IT Manager at Compaq Asset Management Services and Remarketing.

In 2000 Remco moved into IT Leasing and Financing, focusing on organization design, business process improvement, change management and business process outsourcing (BPO), and became a member of the worldwide HP Financial Services leadership team. In 2006 he moved into a global process management role at Oracle Financing, the worldwide industry leader in software financing.

In 2008 Remco joined Sense Sourcing Management, a new fast growing consultancy company, specializing in sourcing solutions and new organizational forms. He plays a pivotal role in building the methodology this company provides to its customers, and acts as interim manager at customer projects.

Next to this, he co-founded and owns Expedition Factory, a team building company that combines industry standard psychological type methodology with arctic expeditions. This combination of deep psychology and extreme conditions in special locations provides customers a profound and lasting team building experience.

Remco holds a Master’s degree (MSc/drs) in management sciences and Bachelor’s and Engineering degrees (BBA/ing) in business administration and mechanical engineering. In 2002 Remco received the Compaq Financial Services living legend award. More recently he went through the Project Management Professional (PMP) and six sigma Green and Black Belt curricula. In 2008 he became a qualified psychological type coach per the Myers-Briggs (MBTI), MTR-i and ITPQ methods.

Remco Timmermans’s Specialties:

Organization Design, Globalization, BPO (Business Process Outsourcing/Offshoring), BPM (Business Process Management), Visionary and Strategic Management, Six Sigma Black Belt and Green Belt certified, PMP (PMI Project Management Professional), Marketing (Dutch marketing degrees NIMA A + B), Team Building (certified MTR-i and ITPQ teacher)
