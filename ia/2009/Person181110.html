Mika Parikka’s Summary

Deep-fried software business professional with very broad knowledge on all aspects of software business: managing projects, programmes, specialist teams and businesses; defining, designing, marketing and selling products; running operations, change projects; consulting both line organizations and top management; running non-profit organizations and organizing large (10.000+ people) events; designing advertising for different media and doing guerrilla marketing.

Able to analyze and find discontinuities in business plans from all points of view: Markets, Customer, Business logic, Process, User, Design, Technology, Platforms etc.

Goal: To be at least nationally recognized authority on running and growing software business and advisor to best software-related companies.

Mission: Create efficient and productive work environment for people around me by understanding and defining the discontinuities in strategy&logic and advise people to fix those by themselves.
