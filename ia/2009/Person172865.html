Neil Edwards’s Summary

Formed Pyramid Consultancy in 1995.
We now stand as a market leader in the recruitment of high calibre professionals to the printed communication + BPO sectors.
We operate on a Global basis and whilst the UK is still of paramount importance to us, our International coverage continues to develop and grow with pace.

We have an extensive, satisfied Client list that return to us, time and time again.
