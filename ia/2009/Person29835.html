Maciej Galecki’s Summary

Over 6 years of professional experience in search engine marketing industry - from search engine optimization & PPC management in the most competitive areas to be a consultant focused on building and implementing SEM strategies which are a part of company's global marketing plan.

Fascinated with search engines, I try to understand why search behaviour has become so popular nowadays. That's why our company is not focused on SEM area only rather than analyzing, understanding and improving people's "search experience" on our clients' websites as well.

As an individual - Google Advertising Professional and expert in SEM of the classified and media business area.
