Lori Shapiro’s Summary

Lori Shapiro has over 15 years of corporate experience helping companies in industries such as the toy, automotive, software development, corporate travel and non-profit sectors to ask the proper marketing/business communications and consumer research questions, and then answer them in an effective, actionable manner.

As owner/consultant of By All Writes, Lori's primary focus is business writing, consumer and secondary research, creative writing, presentations and public speaking. She aspires to build a clientele in need of such services both locally (NJ/PA/NY/DE) and nationally, based upon her professional work ethic, flair for creative as well as analytical writing, and strong desire to forge meaningful, long-term client relationships.
