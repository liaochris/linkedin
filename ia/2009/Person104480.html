Jared Robb’s Summary

Broadcast Media via Radio Advertising is ultimately the business of bringing buyers and sellers together. I have many years of experience in every aspect of the radio industry from Programming and Promotions to Sales and Management. My ultimate goal is to expose my client's products / services to an audience of over 1.4 million people each week that listen to our powerful cluster of 6 radio stations in the Kansas City Metro via on-air advertsing, internet advertising, promotions / events, and sports marketing. I have consistently used a very simple formula to assist clients in gaining results utililzing the power of radio..... If you advertise consistantly enough with enough reach and frequency, you will gain mindshare which in turn equals marketshare! This simple approach combined with compelling creative will leave a long lasting impression on these listeners (your potential customers) thus building brand awareness and making your business "top of mind" in your given verticle which ultimately means more customers!
