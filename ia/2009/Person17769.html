Anthony Mills’s Summary

I am an IT professional of 15+ years experience. I have worked at all levels of IT in the infrastructure and support side of the shop. Having been a help desk technician, desktop support person, network administrator, engineer, manager and Director, I have recently become a CIO.

This growth through the ranks has given me insight into how difficult and rewarding each role can be. I try to manage and mentor my staffs to service our customers as well as we can. I believe that having been in their shoes helps in earning respect from my staff.

In my current position, I have moved into a new industry and the most senior role in my career. The challenges and rewards in front of me are exciting to the point of intoxication. I will work together with the other senior staff at Forrest and Swiss Post to make our companies as successful as possible.

Anthony Mills’s Specialties:

I am an aggressive negotiator and often get discounts no one else can.

I am loyal to my people and push them to deliver, but reward them for those deliveries. I have an exceedingly low turn over rate.

I also have strong business skills. I built up a company from zero to $2MM in sales in 18 months, and sold the company recently for a healthy profit.
