Niki Atherton’s Summary

Summary
Ashley Ellis LLC. is contingency based Information Technology Recruiting Firm, who is committed to connecting highly-talented IT Professionals with reputable organizations. We provide job seekers and employers with efficient service, confidential representation, and expert knowledge of the demanding job market.

Our clients range from multi-national conglomerates to start-up ventures. As a matter of process, we conduct very thorough, in-depth, structured interviews of both our candidates and clients before our match making process begins. We ensure that the job seekers career goals and the goals of the organizations we represent are inline to ensure a fulfilling, long-lasting connection.

As a privately held company, Ashley Ellis is driven to create win-win relationships and provide value to our clients and job seekers instead of focusing on shareholder value. With this philosophy, we have a high level of personalized service while servicing a broad spectrum of requirements.
