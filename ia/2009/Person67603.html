    (Motion Pictures and Film industry)

    April 2008 — Present (1 year)

    After shutting down nearly all my activities to recover from chronic fatigue, inspiration struck on April 6th, 2008 and the concept of a movie titled, "The Vacuum Law: Supercharging the Law of Attraction" was born.

    The following 4-6 months were spent in voracious research to put "meat" on the concept's "skeleton." Once formulated, potential interested participants were approached. Thanks to CEO Space, this concept has developed to the point of acquiring management, board and advisory team members. It is ready to finish development in the pre-production phase.

    Clear Voice Studios was acquired in December, 2008. Subsequently in January 2009, subsidiary HyperNova Films, LLC and The Vacuum Law, LP were formed.

    The major themes of the movie will be to engender greater acts of charity/service, unconditional forgiveness of self and others, increasing abilities to be more valuable for others, taking "leaps of faith" pursuing individual unique abilities and passions, and removing resistance such as fears, self-limiting beliefs, doubts, etc..

    The mission is to influence and impact lives of millions around the globe to become empowered with better lives by becoming truly valuable human beings to society.

    The vision is to create a "Franchise Brand" with many topical themes to follow in the years to come.
