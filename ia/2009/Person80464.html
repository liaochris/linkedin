Francesco Prandoni’s Summary

Extensive experience in business planning and operations in global Corporations as well as business development as entrepreneur.

CEO and owner of DOC International, a logistic and meeting planning company with offices in Somerset, NJ and Florence, Italy, providing meeting planning and logistic to Pharmaceutical, Biotech, Medical, Sports Companies.

Board Member and co-owner of SKY BLUE WOMEN'S SOCCER, an organization based in Somerset, NJ, running the New Jersey/New York franchise of WPS - Womens Professional League starting in 2009.

More than 15 years as executive in the business and operations in a variety of industries, including retail, consumer goods, food and IT industries. Among his significant previous positions, he worked with Bed Bath and Beyond, M&M/Mars, Masterfood and IBM, Olivetti in US and Europe.
