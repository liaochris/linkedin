Karen Kaufman’s Summary

Karen Kaufman's talents have flourished as a Fine Art Printer and Photoshop professional. "It is quite thrilling and an honor to work with the most renowned artists in the State of Hawai'i." With years of professional scanning, color manipulation and editing, Karen has an ability to create any image or design, and to enhance that image for small or large scale printing.

Her passion for actualizing "outside the ball" concepts must have originated from birth. Karen recalls some thoughts from childhood, however, in keeping with her "motto: "Tomorrow I will be better at what I'm doing than I am today," she continues to press forward.

  • Owner/President

    Kinaole Group Ltd. dba Mo Media (Fine Art Reproduction)

    (Printing industry)

    September 2002 — Present (7 years 1 month)

    Mo Media is an exclusive Fine Art Reproduction service. Some of Hawai'i's very best artists call on the "Mo" for their high quality reproductions.

    When you purchase a Fine Art Print from a gallery in Hawai'i and you see the Mo Media Mark on the print, you can be assured that: It was created using the finest archival inks and materials available anywhere. Mo Media uses old world, time honored techniques along with the latest digital tools to recreate the artist's work down to the brush stroke.

    The traditional (Hawaiian) definition of kina'ole is: "Doing the right thing in the right way, at the right time, in the right place, to the right person, for the right reason, with the right feeling, the first time."
