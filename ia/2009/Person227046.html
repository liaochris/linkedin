Sue Kohr’s Summary

York County PA Realtor since 1987

I've specialized in New Construction with an emphasis in Condominiums, i.e.: legal formation, sales specifics towards the "lifestyle" aspect especially as it relates to providing a comfortable transition to seniors making a lifestyle change. Taking lead involvement in marketing and sales have been my primary roles. My creativity and imagination team up to create and impliment unique marketing strategies. I enjoy the marketing side of sales because the sales take care of themselves when the marketing is right on the money.


AS A FAUX FINISHING ARTIST...
I studied with Martin Hersch in Louisville KY at his Faux Finishing School "Faux Effects". His unique finishing designs gained him world wide aclaim as one of the finest and most talented faux artists in the industry today. The opportunity to have been taught by a master faux finisher has been invaluable, not only through just the experience of learning from him, but it provided me with THE most unique finishes that only I am now able to offer in this part of the country.

Faux Finishing is a passion of mine and, just like any piece of art created by an artist, it is extremely personal and I leave a piece of myself behind in every home I have the opportunity to be welcomed into. (cont. in specialties below)

I have an extensive portfolio and it only takes being asked once for me to share it with anyone who expresses an interest. Contact me anytime you would like to learn more of the faux services I provide.
