Jackie Wetzel’s Summary

The Phoenix Business Journal is the Valley's number one source for local business news and the only newspaper with proven growth over the past five years of 18%. My daily goal is to effectively brand that reputation to our prospective clients.

The Business Journal newspapers, in 40 cities across the country, are a unit of Advance Publications Inc., which also operates Conde Nast Magazines, Parade magazine, Fairchild Publications, the Golf Digest companies, Newhouse Newspapers and cable television interests.

President Elect of the American Marketing Association Phoenix Chapter. The American Marketing Association is a professional association for individuals and organizations involved in the practice, teaching and study of marketing worldwide. We are also one of the top industry organizations in the Valley of the Sun with nearly 400 members and 3,000 other professionals that attend our networking and educational events. Members include CEO’s, marketing and advertising executives, business owners, salespeople and marketing representatives from a wide variety of industries.

Project strategy. Taking ideas/concepts and formulating a plan of action behind the campaign. The detail aspect of project development is one of the areas that I allocate a lot of attention towards before putting the wheels in motion.
