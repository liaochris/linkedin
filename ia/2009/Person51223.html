Chris Tomlinson’s Summary

Chris is currently MD of Friend Digital Ltd. An agency specialising in Social Influence Marketing, Online Reputation Management and Online PR.

Chris setup friend in April09 with other leading Social Media experts to help businesses in the West Midland and beyond build equity in this emerging marketing space.

Chris was formally Head of Digital at WAA, one the UK’s top 50 Advertising Agencies*. I was responsible for all aspects of WAA’s digital offering and for advising clients on their digital strategies. In recent years, I have worked with clients including Habitat, House of Fraser, B&Q, Intercontinental Hotels, Marketing Birmingham, Willis, NEC Group, Birmingham International Airport, and the Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors.

In 1995, founded Internet consultancy, WebXpress. Tracking the Internet's evolution into a mainstream marketing medium, I transformed WebXpress from a simple ISP to a digital marketing agency, culminating in its acquisition by WAA in 2005.

Prior to this spent nine years in sales at Sun Microsystems in the UK and Middle East.

I am a prolific blogger on the subject of using Internet technologies to engage with customers and have a weekly column in The Birmingham Post, the Midlands’ leading business daily newspaper.

Chris Tomlinson’s Specialties:

Digital marketing strategies, online brand engagement, search and social media, website design/development, email marketing, online display advertising, Online PR, Social Influence Marketing.
