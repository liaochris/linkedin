Simon TATE’s Summary

An influential, dynamic and visionary senior operations and engineering executive repeatedly awarded for decisively mastering complex problems and delivering innovative, practical solutions. Combines critical intelligence with exceptional leadership ability, first class communication and influencing skills and the determination to succeed whatever the obstacles.

A senior project manager with KBR, delivering smart solutions for clients in the infrastructure and defence sectors in the UAE.

Background as an aviation engineering professional in the Royal Navy Fleet Air Arm. Highly experienced in many areas of operations and engineering, having held a variety of roles at various levels from squadron engineer through to senior positions in headquarters staffs. Very experienced in operations, procurement, support and corporate functions in addition to professional, academic and technical training and flight research.
