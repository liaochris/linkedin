Maggy Sterner’s Summary

Matchmaker and translator in the Web world: I support clients by acting as the project manager for online projects and help them choose the best available talent for their specific Web jobs.

- Web site usability analyst focusing on identifying, setting, and realizing online business goals by maximizing the user experience. Put succinctly: If you make it about them and not about you, everybody wins.

My mission: To support people and businesses to identify what's broken - not by providing the answers, but by asking the right questions.

Maggy Sterner’s Specialties:
