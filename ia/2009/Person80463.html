Frederic Ponzo’s Summary

Frederic Ponzo is a founding member of NET2S in the Square Mile. Today, he focuses on providing leadership, direction and vision to NET2S in the UK, ensuring we offer our clients best in class services, whilst leading the company's business development efforts.

Frederic joined NET2S Paris in 1998 as a consultant specialising in Capital Markets technology. Based in the City since early 2000, he has taken on a wide range of consulting and operational roles. Frederic's consulting experience in banking technology spans over 30 financial institutions including BNP Paribas, Credit Suisse, JPMorgan Chase, Merrill Lynch, Nomura, SG and UBS.

Frederic Ponzo became Managing Director of NET2S Ltd in 2004. He graduated from the National Institute of Telecommunications (INT-Telecom) in Paris.
