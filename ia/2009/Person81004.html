Frances M. (Fran) Ferguson’s Summary

Ms. Ferguson blends analytical strength with creativity to advance the entire organization's ability to compete. She has served in-house at one of the nation's top-ranked corporate law firms; one of the top three international accounting firms; and the largest full-service real estate firm in the South and Southwest. In addition, as an independent advisor or interim executive, she has shaped business plans for raising capital and represented young companies in their search for funding; and influenced program direction and development for four international environmental or conservation initiatives.
