Nicole Gottleib’s Summary

A December 2007 graduate of the University of Wisconsin-Madison, with a B.A. in Theatre and Drama in the Acting Specialist program, as well as in Communication Arts; also gained certificates in Women's Studies and LGBT Studies. I am currently pursuing a career in theatre in Chicago. I have worked on a variety of productions, community and professional, since I moved to the city in September of 2008, as well as during the 5 years I lived in Madison, WI. I also have experience working as a leader in multiple organizations, such as with my current position as the Call Center Recruiter with Total Attorneys, along with my two-year position as Involvement Coordinator in a residence hall at UW-Madison. Such leadership skills and experiences flow seamlessly into my work in the theatre, while my experience on stage and researching/developing roles also seeps into my work style with businesses and organizations. Ideally, I am able to be in a position that allows me to combine the above skills with my passion for theatre and social awareness/justice, either with the roles I take on or with the activities/opportunities I bring to my company.
