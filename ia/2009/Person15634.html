Angela Goldsmith’s Summary

I specialize in helping clients and business partners recognize and define their software application needs. With a background in social services/non-profit, telecommunications, distribution, recycling, business ownership, human resources, software implementation and consulting, I am able to generalize and leverage my diverse experiences to identify with what my clients are trying to define in their application needs, identify key processes and determine attainable goals.
Throughout my career I have found that I love the challenge of learning about and working with so many industries. It is my passion and my goal to continue to learn new things across all areas of my life. My current areas, sometimes recurring areas, of study are: leadership, project management, new technology and new software both for work and entertainment.
As I often tell our clients, the only limit to what we can do is imagination and money. My job is to help you imagine the possibilities and then together we can decide just how much we want to do today and what we want to do tomorrow.
