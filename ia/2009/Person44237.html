Christian Bason’s Summary

Christian is passionate about transforming the public sector's ability to meet the needs of citizens and society. He has worked for more than a decade with leadership, innovation, and development of the public sector. Building on a career as a management consultant working for government clients, he now heads MindLab, a cross-ministerial innovation unit. MindLab wants to be a world leader in the process of transforming understanding of the realities of citizens and businesses into new social solutions. Christian is the author of three books and numerous articles on innovation, leadership and management. He is a frequent guest speaker in Denmark and abroad, and writes regular columns, blogs and articles about innovation and leadership. He is censor at the sociology institutes in Denmark.

Christian Bason’s Specialties:

Citizen-centred innovation; leadership in the public sector; performance management; strategy, organisation and HR.
