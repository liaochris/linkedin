Andrew Kornuta’s Summary

I started in IT as a software engineer. Through the years I realized that I could be most effective in an organization if I continue to move in the direction of leadership while keeping my feet firmly planted in an understanding of new and existing technologies. Even though I no longer write software I still understand and participate in low-level technical discussions.

As I began to move in a leadership direction I started to take classes regarding Project Management and achieved certification from PMI. I have a passion for process improvement. I am very interested in the strategic role of IT in modern organizations and how that role is changing and developing. Professionally I am looking to continue to grow in my management role, applying these strategic and tactical understandings.

I am starting to become much more involved in the "business side" of IT, examining situations for real efficiency opportunities, analyzing the cost of the work that we do and trying to find ways to bring value to the business. My current work is centered around Enterprise Document Management and Business Process Management. These are areas of great interest to me and I think they are technology corner stones of the modern enterprise.

Andrew Kornuta’s Specialties:

Service Oriented Architecture (SOA), software architecture, Enterprise Document/Content Management, Business Process Managemenet, process improvement, Project Management
