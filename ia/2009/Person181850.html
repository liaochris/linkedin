Patrick Collman (patrick.collman@esptalent.com)’s Summary

A Co-Founder and Director of ESP Talent, I’m a seasoned recruitment professional with an extensive background in delivering quality staffing solutions for the Spanish operations of transnational clients, predominantly - but not exclusively - within the Technology sector.

The ESP Talent team has invaluable experience in handling a broad spectrum of roles - from the purely technical right through to CXO level. Our recruitment professionals, each with an international business background and related specialist expertise can, through their far-reaching networks, find exactly the candidates you need.
