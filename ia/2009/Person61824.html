Dennis Yu’s Summary

I have been in fashion industry for more than 7 years mostly in the field of sales/marketing. Started out in the industry as West Coast Account managers for 2 denim brands and slowly built up the retail relationship to launch the Natural Selection branding agency.

We recently expanded the ability to offer not only sales/merchandising service but private label opportunities through our oversea manufacturing facilities. Range of facilities are equipped and experience to handle from small orders to mass quantities with QUALITY as priority.

We represent industry leader brands as their image liason while placing products in appropriate sales distribution through meticulous planning and strategies.

We understand each brand has a different personality/DNA just like how an individual is. Thus our goal is to continue branding and growing lines just like babies.

We are always looking for new partnership through brands, retailers, manufacturers and just plain old good people!

Dennis Yu’s Specialties:

Developing fashion brands from start to finish with knowledge/experience/contacts in design, manufacturing, sales and marketing.
