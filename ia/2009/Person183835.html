Paul Tyler’s Summary

Producer Director for Digitial Media company Gameware Media / Cross Media Mentor on the German ‘Akademie für Kindermedien 2007/08/09’ / Juror for PRIX JEUNESSE Interactivity Prize 2008 / Interactive Mentor on the 2007/08 European, MEDIA course ‘Pygmalion Plus’ / Producer for Danish State Broadcaster DR Saturday night game-show 'Førstevælger'/ Originated, developed and produced BBC’s innovative, cross-media entertainment game-show format, BAMZOOKi / 12 years at BBC working through all production roles from runner to multi-camera studio gallery-location Director and Producer, working across family entertainment, drama, magazine, live music performance / Leader in youth work for 8 - 15 year olds / Theatre Stage manager for a mixture of classical work, new plays/adaptations, contemporary theatre, storytelling and interactive ‘Theatre in Education’ / Studied Physiology and the History and Theory of Art and Design / Born 1968 in England

Paul Tyler’s Specialties:

Production, development and mentoring for Television and cross-media/interactive media.

Paul Tyler’s Experience

He's a quick thinker and do'er, able to generate ideas on the fly whilst always open to the ideas of others. He's someone who's never satisfied with the status quo, always on the look out for ways to develop & self-develop & with an instinct-driven sensibility to discover errors in the systems you work in & the talent to transform them to potentials.” Thomas Hailer, Programme Manager, Berlin International Film Festival.

  • Interactive Mentor

    (Broadcast Media industry)

    June 2007 — Present (2 years)

It collaborates with the strong conviction that it is essential to create specialized professional development programmes for those who want to make original work in media for children.

  • Producer, location & multi-camera studio Director

    BBC

    (Public Company; 10,001 or more employees; BBC; Broadcast Media industry)

    May 1994 — December 2006 (12 years 8 months)
