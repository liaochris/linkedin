Wendy Juergens’s Summary

Currently an independent distributor for Reliv International, a manufacturer of pharmaceutical grade nutritional supplements, I help others to maintain their health and to build home-based businesses.

In 2002, my husband and I founded Pump Grump, Inc., a septic pumping company that has grown to over 2,200 customers. Our goal is to educate the consumer and build a loyal customer base.

I am currently helping the Tri-Town Chamber of Commerce in their membership efforts. I particularly like helping people maximize their chamber networking opportunities.

I always considered myself to be a good networker until I worked as an Assistant Director in BNI, where I learned that there is much more to networking than meets the eye. As a member of BNI, I continue to fine tune my skills as a networker. I consider myself a "connector," connecting people and businesses who can help each other succeed in this challenging economy.

From 1986 to 1998, I held a number of positions with Groundwater Technology / Fluor Danield GTI, which provided me with experience and knowledge of business and international operations. These positions included executive assistant, human resources and health and safety. I traveled to Europe, Australia and Canada.

As a very young woman, I had the opportunity of working with my parents who were business owners in Sharon, MA. They taught me the importance of integrity, facing daily challenges, and using humor.

Wendy Juergens’s Specialties:

Sales, networking, training, team building, presenting, writing and editing.
