Mike England’s Summary

A SELF-MOTIVATED, ADAPTABLE, HARD-WORKING AND PROFESSIONAL EVENT DIRECTOR with an excellent record of achievement in sales management, new business development, key account management and event development within focused B2B technology exhibitions and conferences.

Commercially astute, has focused on establishing and implementing strategies for exploiting existing and new markets and maximising the potential from existing shows. A resourceful team player capable of working with all levels of management and also able to act on his own initiative. Excels at working with existing marketing teams to add further value and shape messaging. A good communicator with first class interpersonal skills and history of staff management and motivation. Quickly established a significant level of achievement in meeting and exceeding sales quotas through careful balance of sales team management/account management/business/product development.
