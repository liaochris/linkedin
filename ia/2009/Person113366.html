Jim Porzak’s Summary

Mission

Marketing Analytics for Informed Decision Making – Evangelize the use of analytics to make informed strategic and tactical marketing decisions.

Direct Marketing Optimization - Make profitable predictions to optimize the return on your direct marketing investments using the latest data mining and machine learning methods.

Open Source Analytics – Leverage open source community for latest and best tools and methods to deliver actionable customer analytics to the business.

Skills

* Business: Discover information needs and translate into data, query and reporting requirements. Design easy to understand reports fulfilling stakeholder information needs. Find a common language for reporting across the organization and to its clients. Evangelize metric driven business concepts.

* Analytics: Apply latest statistical methods for exploratory data analysis, modeling and data mining to business problems, especially customer analytics and direct marketing optimization using R - the open source data analysis and visualization platform.

* Data Housing: Apply star-schema principals to design data warehouses and datamarts for holding report-ready data. Implement using Microsoft DTS and other ETL tools. Design and develop OLAP cubes to deliver “packaged answers” to business users and analysts. Automate routine processing on a scheduled basis.

* Data Quality: Define quality measures. Build quality checks into data ports and reports. Apply data profiling methods. Give feedback to data creators. Evangelize data quality principles throughout the organization.

* Data Herding / Information Harvesting – Corral, cull, control, combine and house data so it is harvest ready. Create user-friendly and statistically sound reports that meet business needs. Perform predefined and ad hoc data queries for internal and external users. Enable do-it-yourself information harvesting by users. Automate information delivery to all stakeholders.

Jim Porzak’s Specialties:

Marketing Analytics, Predictive Methods, Customer Analytics, Business Intelligence, Database Marketing, Direct Marketing Optimization, R, OLAP, SQL, Data Warehouse/mart design (of Kimball school), Visualization, and Open Source.
