Andy Wiseman’s Summary

I am the New Business and Marketing Director for the Transport Operations and Technology (TOT) Business Unit. I am a member of TOT’s Leadership Team and am responsible for sector planning and marketing strategy and for growth and development in new business areas and partnerships. My technical background is 20 years as a communications engineer in Intelligent Transport Systems. My managerial background is 10 years as a Project Manager and a Divisional Director.
