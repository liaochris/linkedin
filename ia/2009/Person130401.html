Jim Kenzig’s Summary

Jim has over 28 years of IT experience having previously worked for Boeing and Bendix Field Engineering at NASA Glenn Research center. Jim is a Citrix Technology Professional, on the board of directors for the Ohio Public Library Information Network (OPLIN) and a member of the Citrix Customer Advisory Council. Jim has several technical websites, blogs , forums, and has moderated the THIN Discussion list now at freelists.org for 14 years.
