He started to tell me about some of his friends on how they made their living from the internet, how they had all the time in the world to do whatever they wanted, had all the nice things in life, like the big house, nice cars, freedom and always seemed to be happy.

He put me in touch with one of his close friends. He asked me what I wanted in life, I said more time to myself and not have to worry about money. It was then he introduced me to his business.
He showed me how he made his living, then told me that I if I wanted the same I could have it.
It was up to me to take action and change my life.

I carried out some research into this business opportunity and was totally shocked by what I had discovered.
