Sandy Kalik’s Summary

I am the newest member of the Media AWAKEN team, a social media PR consultancy supporting non-profits and socially responsible companies.

Public relations is the technical classification of what I do, but I don't ascribe to the smile and dial philosophy of cramming, begging, stalking the media until you meet your goals. Relations implies a relationship, so I prefer to see myself as a connector--bringing together people, businesses and ideas that make sense.

Additionally, I write a column for Media Bullseye (http://mediabullseye.com) called Familiar Marketing and blog at sandying.blogspot.com.

Recently, I've begun teaching yoga at Prana Power Yoga, Harvard Business School and Beacon Hill Athletic Clubs.
