Ralph Pampino’s Summary

BMAC Spectrum Therapies is now providing services in the Puget Sound Area

Each child's current functioning level is assessed and an individualized program is developed to meet his/her needs in the areas such as; functional communication, social skills, self-help skills, pre-academic skills, academic skills and maladaptive behaviors.

Our *NEW* Flexible Service Model is available: In the student’s home, on site at the student’s public or private school or in our *NEW* BMAC Learning Center in Mountlake Terrace

Each student’s Educational Program/Curriculum is: individualized, comprehensive, and flexible to meet ongoing needs, rooted in evidence-based practices such as Applied Behavior Analysis [ABA], tailored to his or her specific needs and abilities, designed and supervised by a Board Certified Behavior Analyst [BCBA], implemented by trained and experienced ABA tutors and regularly measured and adjusted based on the student’s progress
