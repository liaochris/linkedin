Adrian Rasburn’s Summary

Worked as a freelance graphic designer since the mid nineties (whilst studying design).

Degree educated up to MA level, in 3D Computer Animation (from the National Centre of Computer Animation).

Own and run my own web design and hosting company Million Monkeys Ltd.

A professional web designer since 2000. Multiple roles from standard web designer to senior interactive designer.

I was in charge of the creative process for the Merlin CBT project commissioned by the MoD / Navy undertaken by Lockheed Martin. There for two years, I both initiated and implemented efficient design management processes and was the primary animator for the project. At the time I believe it was the largest or second largest CBT project in the world with over 100,000 assets.

I've spent two years contracting at one of the largest (if not *the* largest), and certainly the best Recruitment advertising agencies in London / UK. At Barkers I worked as an interactive designer building websites, online advertising and presentations for pitches, primarily using Flash.

I am now looking to move overseas and work as a designer/interactive designer around the world. First stop hopefully New York.
