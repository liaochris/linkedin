Nouman Siddiqui’s Summary

Key Qualification: Started career with Kausar Abbas Bhayani Securities (KABS) as Research Assistant way back in 1994. Joined Arif Habib Securities Ltd. in Equity Research and Corporate Finance Departments as Investment Analyst in 1998. Later, promoted as a Senior Investment Analyst, Vice President & Head of Research & Corporate Finance. Presently working as Vice President Capital Market Operations.

During the period enhanced the Company’s operations on the Equity Research and Corporate Finance side. Ranked among the most prominent investment analysts of the country. Prepared large number of analytical reports, market commentaries and updates on various sectors in general and leading companies in particular.

Written reports have regularly been published in prestigious business magazines and newspapers such as Pakistan & Gulf Economist, the Dawn, The News, and Business Recorder.

Regularly appears on electronic media, Business Channels, to provide updates on Stock Market behavior in general and Companies in Particular. Besides this has also immense on hand experience in many listing issues, IPOs, Offer for Sale, Underwriting and consortium arrangements etc.
Job description:

As a Senior Investment Analyst work under the direction of the Head of Investments team. Responsible for portfolio analysis of current and potential investment, day-to-day administrative tasks for the plan (i.e. monitoring of guideline compliance, direction letters, etc.), creation of presentations, and other special projects.
