Jeff Pinegar’s Summary

I am a corporate event management and experiential marketing professional. I develop, design, and execute high-profile events, product launches and brand immersions for the World’s largest and most respected brands.

I started my career in marketing at the international accounting firm of Grant Thornton where I worked on their Shared Professional Services program. Following Grant Thornton, I went on to work as a freelance logistics coordinator for companies such as American Express, Maritz Travel, Compaq, and Sun Microsystems.

In early 2001 I joined Imagination USA, a fully integrated communication, design and project management agency in New York City. While at Imagination, I worked on a range of communications projects including: Ford National Dealer Meetings in Philadelphia, Orlando and Las Vegas; Unilever Investor Relation Programs; Canadian National Rail Annual Shareholder Meetings; and the European and North American Aston Martin Vanquish launches.

After spending four years working as a project manager on corporate marketing and public relation events in New York City, I moved back to the Detroit area in May 2005 where I worked on large scale Ford Truck consumer exhibits.

From June 2006 to July 2007 I worked with FUSE Communication, a luxury markteting and communications agency based in Birmingham, MI managing a national dealer marketing initiative for Bentely Motors.

Currently, I am the Director of Events Production with the Cleantech Group. We produce five global conferences yearly with the flagship event taking place in San Francisco, CA. The remaining four conferences take place around Europe, the East Coast of North America, India and China.

Jeff Pinegar’s Specialties:

I specialize in the logistics and production of events around the world. From the initial planning phases including the art direction of event graphics and collateral, creating the floor plan design through to the on-site direction and management of the main stage, multiple breakout rooms, registration process and venue liaison including food and beverage.
