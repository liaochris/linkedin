Michael Kirkham’s Summary

As a Product Manager my primary goal is to create the strategic roadmap that ensures successful product growth. I continuously talk to customers, support agents, sales and marketing to understand needs of the industry to set tactical priorities. In order to prepare proposals for executive management, I gather, analyze and prioritize market information and statistical data. I also use this data to create product requirements for development teams. During the entire product lifecycle, I work closely with the engineering, QA and market roll-out teams to manage the delivery of the product.

I have product management experience in the following areas:
+ Mobile applications (BREW, J2ME, WAP)
+ Voice Recognition Software
+ Local Search Engines
+ Internet Advertising (PPC, Paid Inclusion)
+ Automotive Information Software (desktop, internet)
+ Technical Manuals
