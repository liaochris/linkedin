Ilja van Roon’s Summary

Corporate communication specialist with 10+ years of international experience. Solid interpersonal and advisory skills, honed by working with business leaders and their teams. Entrepreneur with proven ability to envision and build a profitable business. Broad understanding of current business, political and social issues.

Polyglot with masterful command of English. Incisive, optimistic, trustworthy, quick to build rapport. Driven by curiosity, love of adventure and the desire to contribute to the growth of others. Passionately devotes waaaay too much time practicing the martial art of Aikido.
