Anne Kiely’s Summary

Kiely & Associates was established in Ireland in 2007. We specialise in increasing the visibility of small businesses; many of our clients are sole traders or start-up businesses. We also provide services to community and voluntary organisations, offering a discount on our usual good value prices.

Through PR, networking and event management, we can increase the number of your clients/customers, by helping you tell people about what you do. We have lots of ideas about how to benefit from linking with other businesses, using social media, sponsorship and other items from the PR and marketing toolboxes.

Whether you want press releases, proof-reading, building of contacts lists or just advice on how to raise your profile, contact us for a chat about how we might help you. If we cannot help you, we will know someone who can!

We also provide business support. We can organise your office and paperwork and provide information about regional or national support for businesses.

Kiely & Associates manages the Kerry Businesswomen's Network. We also co-ordinate the Kerry Enterprise Network on behalf of Kerry County Enterprise Board.

Anne Kiely’s Specialties:

Promotion of SMEs, including networking, press releases, copywriting, proof-reading, media relations, newsletters and e-zines. Business support including research
