Ozzie Diaz’s Summary

My business insight and prowess stems from kicking off my early career in the field...in front of customers, partners, government agencies, and carriers where you could not hide but MUST address the issues in realtime. I've taken this seasoning into all of my subsequent roles at Motorola, Cisco, Bivio Networks, AirFlow Networks, Firetide, and the Wireless Subject Matter Expert to the U.S. Northern Command and BP to demonstrate a laser customer focus.

If you Google "Ozzie Diaz", you'll see in almost all of the first 20 hits references to my market and wireless technology subject matter expertise. Wireless is my passion and my passion is evangelism of new, exciting technologies as part of solutions to real-world problems!

The Internet is a vast resource in an ever-evolving society and how to utilize it effectively is the reason why so many companies such as Google, Yahoo, Cisco Systems, Microsoft, Motorola, Nokia, and so many others exist or are constantly reinventing themselves. Whether it is mobile content being delivered by a service provider to an 18-25 year old demographic, the infrastructure that needs to be deployed to cost effectively deliver and scale with the needs of the market, or the back-end systems that enable many ventures to build their customer base and make money, I understand the issues and how to drive a company to succeed.
