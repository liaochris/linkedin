Jeff Perry’s Summary

A Recruitment Advertising Executive by day and a professional, conservatory-trained musician by night.

10+ years experience as a Recruitment Advertising Executive at StarTribune specializing in talent acquisition tools and strategies.

Published articles on recruitment advertising, diversity and innovation in publications and websites including; Mpls/St Paul Magazine, ERE.net, HR.com, The Journal of the Hong Kong Institute of Human Resources Management, and more.

StarTribune, startribune.com, and vita.mn offer both traditional and non-conventional ways to reach and acquire talent including: print and online job postings, a large local resume database, email newsletters, SEM (search engine marketing), mircosite career page design, and more. We can reach passive talent and Gen X & Y (via vita.mn) in ways that job boards cannot.

Musician/Guitarist/Composer

Called "Fabulous - one of the Twin Cities best" by the Dakota Jazz Club and recognized by the John Lennon songwriting contest and Grammy-winner Dave Holland for compositional skills. Original music featured in the UK documentary "Accidents Can Happen". Specialize in Cool and smooth jazz, swing, bossa nova, pop, rock, soul, R&B and Gospel. Have played for 3 US Senators, the Mayors of Chicago, Minneapolis, and St. Paul, the co-founder of Best Buy, Fortune 500 companies including Target, International Dairy Queen and C.H. Robinson, and many other companies, law firms and private individuals.

Trained at Berklee College of Music, Boston, MA, and the American Conservatory, Chicago, IL. Among other standouts, studied guitar with Bobby Broom and arranging with Frank Mantooth.

A presenter of "Jazz: America's Original Diversity Success Story." Clients have included the Federal Reserve Bank and the City of St. Paul.

Provide everything from solo jazz guitar up to an 8-piece dance band for:

Company parties/events
Holiday events
Open Houses
Anniversaries
Weddings and more
