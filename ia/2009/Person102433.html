Jim Rea, CMA’s Experience

  • Chief Operating Officer / Chief Financial Officer

    Ardea Beverage Company

    (Consumer Goods industry)

    2002 — 2009 (7 years)

    The makers of Nutrisoda, a Lifestyles of Health and Sustainability (LOHAS) nutrient-enhanced carbonated beverage. Part of the company since it was an idea in a basement, through the acquisition by and integration into PepsiAmericas. Responsible for Finance & Accounting, Supply Chain & Logistics, and Information Technology.

    • Designed and managed all Finance & Accounting, internal and external reports, analysis and recommendations
    • Developed and administered operating budgets and departmental expenditures
    • Established national/international supply chain including raw materials suppliers, co-packers, transportation partners, multiple 3rd party warehouses
    • Managed logistics nationwide, including customers such as Wal-Mart, Sam's Club, Costco and Target. Achieved a perfect Target "Green Scorecard" rating
    • Partnered with Amazon.com, increasing their Nutrisoda sales over 1,000%
    • Managed growth as high as 400% / year
    • Reduced COGM over 10% each year, reduced key ingredient cost over 60%
    • Reduced benefits costs by 17%, IT development costs by 80%
    • Implemented and upgraded all Logistics, Supply Chain and IT infrastructure
    • Implemented all internal IT systems; Accounting, Order Management, Inventory Management, CRM, Route Management, Promotion Management, Forecasting & Sales Management
    • Created B2C and B2B eCommerce, Intranet and Extranet sites Nutrisoda.com, pop.Nutrisoda.com, SodaPup.com, Ardea-HQ.com

  • Principal

    (Public Company; IBM; Information Technology and Services industry)

    1996 — 2002 (6 years)

    As Lead Principal, recruited to grow the Upper Midwest Retail, Consumer Packaged Goods, Manufacturing and eCommerce consulting group.

    • Established go-to-market strategy including identifying targeted client base, defining services to be delivered, constructing sales and staffing approach, while leveraging the capabilities of the then $84B company.

    • Hired and developed a high-performance team of 15 direct reports, 45 indirect reports, and hundreds of contractors.

    • Led or directed projects as large as 100,000+ person-hours.

    • Grew consulting group revenue to $47M within 4 years, over 70% of total local office revenue.
