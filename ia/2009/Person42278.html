Chesley Rafferty’s Summary

Mr. Chesley Rafferty was born in 1979 in Perth, Western Australia. He attended Chisholm Catholic College from 1992 to 1996, where he was highly active and involved in all aspects of the school. Rafferty was a member of the Senior Debate Club, was one the Lead Role in the School Production of “Wizard of Oz”, the school’s Student Exchange Representative to France in 1995, and on the Swimming and Hockey teams. Mr. Rafferty also received special recognition in TEE (Tertiary Entrance Examination), where he received the highest external TEE Score in the school for Discrete Math. After High School, Rafferty was accepted to attend Murdoch University. In 1999, he received his Bachelor’s degree in Commerce and Social Ecology. In 2000, Chesley began at Notre Dame University in Fremantle, where he studied for his Bachelor of Law. Today, Mr. Rafferty serves as the Director for “CPR Marketing” located in Perth. He is in charge of all of the marketing of the company, some duties including advertising, management of design and production, creating media sponsorship, and market research. Before beginning with CPR, Mr. Rafferty worked for “Messages On Hold Australia” as the Sales Manager, where he was in charge of managing ten sales account executives. Rafferty showed a natural fervor in his work and easily shined with the company, where he was honored as the Employee of the Year. Mr. Rafferty devotes his energy and focus on many things aside from solely his professional career. He loves to read, watch movies, and cook, and works out 4-5 times a week. Rafferty is also a generous contributor to “Surf Life Saving Australia”, an organization whose mission is to provide a safe beach and aquatic environments throughout Australia. Each year, SLSA is responsible for savings thousands of lives. You can also often find Mr. Rafferty volunteering and serving meals to the hungry and disadvantaged with the charity kitchen “Manna Industries”.
