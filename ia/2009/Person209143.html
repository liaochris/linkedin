Sanj Sukerkar’s Summary

Sanj Sukerkar is the VP of Commercial Loans for Access Commercial. A veteran of 5 years, Sanj cut his teeth in the industry as a loan officer successfully originating and closing hard money loans for residential and commercial real estate properties. He specializes in distressed properties, mainly un-bankable deals. His specialty lies in motel and retail centers. At Access Commercial, he is now responsible for originating and underwriting permanent and bridge financing for such property types.

In 1995, Sanj graduated from Northern Michigan University with a Bachelor of Science degree in engineering and special interest in Finance. Sanj successfully started several E-Commerce enterprises. He understands the business-owners’ desire for capital and their growing pains.

He has been a resident of Las Vegas since 1998. He is an active member of LV Asian Chamber of Commerce, Commercial Marketing Group and several other special-interest marketing groups.

Sanj Sukerkar’s Specialties:

Financing private money deals usually unconventional financing. Like to work in retail, medical facilities and land development.
