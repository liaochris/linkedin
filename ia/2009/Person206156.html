Ryan Donovan’s Summary

Experienced corporate communications and agency PR professional with 19 years of combined experience as business group AR-PR leader, corporate spokesperson for company, crisis counselor, agency GM, business development leader and team manager.

Currently oversee all SanDisk corporate and product communications and web properties for this $4bn publicly traded flash memory leader.

Previously, spent five years at HP, three of which as primary corporate spokesperson, media strategist and two in leadership positions in the $30BN+ enterprise group.

In addition, spent three months in 2007 in Singapore as APJ region media relations director, overseeing regional PR program, traveling to meet with country PR teams and identify insights and opportunities for HP. Spent six months as Interim Director of Corporate Analyst Relations in addition to my News duties.

Enthusiastic dedicated professional with extensive experience working in Silicon Valley, Singapore and Chicago markets. Have worked with many leading brands including HP, IBM, BEA, SGI, SUN, Accenture, Quaker Oats Co. and Sara Lee Corp.

Ryan Donovan’s Specialties:

Corporate positioning, media strategy and relationships, crisis communications, workplace/HR issues, digital/social media engagement, competitive PR, C-level executive counsel and extensive team management and motivation skills of up to 50pp.
