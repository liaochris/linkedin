Thomas Marzano’s Summary

My professional goals
As a designer my goal has always been to create memorable experiences through imagination and design. I firmly believe that putting people at the center of the imagination is the only sustainable way to create a meaningful experience.

For the future?
Designing for the total experience of a brand at every touch point with it's consumers is what I find the most inspiring and challenging.

Experience and skills
During my ten years as a professional designer I have developed many skills and gained a wide experience in different design disciplines such as interaction design, product design, strategic & innovation design, communication design, branding, UI graphics, web design, video and sound design.

A few projects
At Philips Design I have been privileged to contribute to some of the award winning projects such as Living Memory, La Casa Prossima Futura and most recently Next Simplicity.

Thomas Marzano’s Specialties:

Design for Innovation, Communication Design, Design for the total Brand Experience
