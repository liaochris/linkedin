Eilidh Milnes’s Summary

When you laugh ... you Learn! Many of her clients call Eilidh Captain Positive... others the Confidence Coach....
Eilidh has spoken to nearly 20,000 people in UK and Europe in recent years and has coached clients from Aberdeen to Abu Dhabi and Australia.
As an inspirational speaker, Eilidh has skill in working with people to facilitate change, reduce stress and increase the happiness factor in their lives. Her dynamic training skill has generated outstanding testimonials from her extensive customer list: BT, Compass, public sector in particular educational establishments around the world.
