Ron Cheli’s Summary

I have several years of experience in Management, Financial Analysis, Purchasing & Forecasting, and Inventory Management & Planning. I started off my career as an Assistant Buyer at Ahold USA, where I was put in charge of a $700 million dollar budget. I left Ahold USA to become a Buyer at Speedline Technologies, which is a high tech manufacturing company. I was in charge of a $10 million budget. After a year at Speedline, I decided to join my family's company which was involved in the Healthcare Industry, primarily Skilled Nursing Facilities & Nursing Homes. I worked as a Project Manager for the company where I traveled to various locations and looked for opportunities where we could make our locations more profitable, scouted out potential locations by conducting analyses in different areas of the country, and also was involved in the actual purchase and turnaround endeavors of operating units we purchased that were in financial turmoil. When my family sold their company in 2007 I joined First Transit as a General Manager. I traveled extensively with First, troubleshooting issues at various locations and getting contracts up to speed with their operations and finances. I recently joined MV as an Operations Manager.

My ultimate goal would to be a Management Consultant either in the Transportation, Retail, or Procurement sector. I like helping organizations reach their goals and I love to travel. I really enjoy what I currently do and would like to move up in the Transportation industry but I'm definitely open to pursuing a career in other industries if my skill set was a good match for a position I was being considered for.
