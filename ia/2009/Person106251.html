Jayme Shelton, MBA’s Summary

Jayme Shelton is a recent MBA graduate from the Marshall School of Business at the University of Southern California. She has many years experience working for small companies, as well as in the corporate environment. She can bring the wide range of experience and creativity necessary to excel at any company.

The combination of Jayme's practical work experience and solid education has made her able to:

• Understand business objectives and see the value of people.

• Interact and influence effectively with her superior communication and interpersonal skills.

• Quickly adapt to change and able to embrace new challenges.

• Think outside of the box and come up with creative and innovative solutions.

• Build and maintain strong relationships with team members, vendors and the community.

• Work proficiently with Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Publisher and Outlook.
