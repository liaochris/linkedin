Tim Corrigan’s Summary

A self-motivated, highly effective full lifecycle recruiting professional with a track record of success in a variety of industries for a wide array of skill sets. Proven ability to recruit for skill sets in the IT, software engineering, supply chain, logistics, manufacturing operations, analytics, legal, finance and sales/marketing professional environments. Has recruited Director-level, program managers, project managers, engineers and software developers in the Defense industry, Federal Government, Telecommunications, Financial, Retail and Business Consulting, Has recruited globally throughout the U.S., Mexico, South America and China.

Implements staffing strategies to fit customer’s specific recruiting needs. Utilize full compliment of recruiting resources to source, screen, recruit and close candidates. Highly skilled in partnering with hiring managers and Human Resources to ensure the most efficient and cost effective recruiting focus. Wide range of experience in behavioral interviewing, college recruiting/university relations and cold calling candidates.
