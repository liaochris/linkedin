Darren Haas’s Summary

I started off a Cisco Trainer, then moved into web and wireless application development and management. Later, I found interest in Financial Systems and ASP.NET implementation while managing international deployment for Euronet Worldwide (RIA/AFEX). After the acquisition by Euronet Worldwide, I helped start Change.org with some great guys. During my career I have aided youth through mentoring and career development and founded YoungJobs.com, a career platform, which is implemented in schools across America.

In late 2007 I started consulting for SRI International (Stanford Research Institute) in the AI department and ended up at an SRI International spin off, Siri Inc., which I love and am fortunate enough to be employee #1.
