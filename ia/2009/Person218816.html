Stephen Furner’s Summary

Stephen is a qualified accountant with 20 years experience as a Finance Director and Non-Executive director within both UK listed groups and venture capital backed MBO’s. During that period he has completed 20 M&A deals and successfully raised more £50million of equity and debt finance.

Stephen is also a Supervisory Director of G. Snel B.V. (Netherlands based, pan-European logistics business); Non Executive Director of Luxco Investment Holdings S.A. (Luxembourg based holding company) and Non-Executive Director of Digital Window Ltd (London based digital marketing business).

Stephen Furner’s Specialties:

Corporate finance services including exit planning, preparing companies for sale, transaction support during sale process, acquistions and funding.
