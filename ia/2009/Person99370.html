Matt Rappaport’s Summary

- IP Checkups is a boutique patent analytic firm providing companies and investors with competitive patent landscape analysis. We assist companies to develop superior patent portfolios and IP strategies.

- We combine qualitative analysis with sophisticated software tools to dig deep into competitors' patent portfolios to understand how companies are positioned relative to each other in particular technology areas.

- IPC delivers in-depth due diligence and competitive intelligence reports aimed at evaluating licensing opportunities, identifying M&A targets, and assessing R&D initiatives.

- We offer custom services and off-the-shelf sector overviews, company portfolio profiles, valuation, and alerting services among others.

- IPC strives to increase company and investor success rates by analyzing the intangible assets that underlie companies' products.

Matt Rappaport’s Specialties:

Competitive Patent landscape analysis for Cleantech, Biotech, Pharma, and Med-tech companies and investors.
