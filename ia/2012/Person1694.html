Susan Heim's Summary

Susan M. Heim is an author and editor, specializing in parenting, multiples, Christian and women’s issues. Her published books include "Chicken Soup for the Soul: All in the Family," "Chicken Soup for the Soul: Devotional Stories for Women," "Chicken Soup for the Soul: Twins and More," "It’s Twins! Parent-to-Parent Advice from Infancy Through Adolescence," "Oh, Baby! 7 Ways a Baby Will Change Your Life the First Year," "Twice the Love: Stories of Inspiration for Families with Twins, Multiples and Singletons," and "Boosting Your Baby's Brain Power." Her articles and essays have appeared in many books, websites, and magazines, including "Angels on Earth" and "TWINS Magazine." Susan is the founder of TwinsTalk, a website for parents of twins and multiples, at www.twinstalk.com. She blogs on parenting at http://susanheim.blogspot.com. The mother of four sons, Susan can be contacted through her website at www.susanheim.com.
