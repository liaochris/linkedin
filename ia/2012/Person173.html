Ben Sanson's Summary

My name is Ben Sanson. I am a fully qualified Graphic Designer with industry experience since the year 2000.

I pride myself on being an effective graphic designer of brochures, logos, corporate identity, marketing campaigns, web design and development, exhibition displays and advertising.

I can take your project from the brief stage to the delivery of the final outcome and help you access funding to help pay towards the costs.

Recently I have had heavily involvement with social media, setting up numerous accounts and setting them up for companies to increase their digital footprint across the internet and reach people they thought would not be possible. I have been using such platforms as WordPress, Facebook, Twitter, Flickr and YouTube.

I have worked with some large clients such as PD Ports, Typhoon International, LV Shipping, Redcar & Cleveland Council, Middlesbrough Council, Stockton Borough Council and Hull Council.

As well as large organisations I work with sole traders and smaller organisations. All clients are treated with the same level of respect and professionalism.

I handle the projects from taking a brief right through to the final delivery of the project and maintain a down to earth and personal approach to my clients. I am always on the other end of the phone and go that extra mile for my clients.

Specialties

I offer a lot of services, ranging from designing brochures, corporate identities, literature, posters leaflets right up to brand creation, logo redesign, web development and SEO strategies. I perform particularly well with start up companies, and companies in the early stage using our industry experience. With my wide network of peers, I can also facilitate projects involving photography, PR, Videography and more.
