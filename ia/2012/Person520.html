Jesse Stay's Summary

I am a Social Media technologies consultant and strategist. Recently named by Mashable and Entrepreneur Magazine as one of 10 entrepreneurs to follow on Twitter, and also by Mashable as one of 20 developers to follow on Twitter, I specialize in Facebook, Twitter, Google+, and other technologies that enable you as a business to better reach your customers in a viral manner, leading to more targeted ad and marketing positions, leading to faster adaptation of your brand through the social networks.

I have firm experience developing and providing quality applications, Social Media Accounts (Facebook/Google+ Pages), and websites that have already had strong successes. I have both developed and consulted for successful social networking applications, some of those in the top 10 of their targeted platforms. My clients include Facebook, Myspace, and others. I help strategize very large corporations, as well as small businesses in the best way to merge "social" into their overall business strategy and see effective results.

I write on and have written about these topics through my books, my own blog StayNAlive.com, LouisGray.com, InsideFacebook.com, AllFacebook.com, and others. My writing has been featured on Techmeme, TechCrunch, Mashable, ReadWriteWeb, Venturebeat, and elsewhere. I understand the blogosphere and know how bloggers think (as I am one), and can help your business gain the exposure it needs, and can take that to the very grass-roots level, leading to greater pageviews, longer time on site, and more customer loyalty and sales.
