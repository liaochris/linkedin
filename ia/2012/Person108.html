Anneliz Hannan's Summary

Professional nursing experience provides me with the skill set of preparedness and alacrity during crisis and continual change. Additionally, it is my versatility in the executive roles of marketing, corporate communications, government and public relations that elevates my business value for developing strategic and integrated communication programs for the diverse stakeholders of the health care community.

With strong advocacy qualities and analytic acumen, I am discerning, tactful and persuasive in communicating about complex health care issues from clinical settings to executive and media boardrooms, and through the halls of Congress.

As a relentless and tenacious voice for the brand, I have chartered the course for independent companies, non-profit organizations and corporations by building strong partnerships and constituencies.

• Launched two biomedical research organizations during an extended period of public and legislative scrutiny. Fundraised $3M for startup of national organization.

• Designed an award winning outreach campaign for a multinational corporation deluged by a decade of public protests. Protests ceased with positive media coverage and a 27% increase in public support in just two years.

• Established and directed department of public affairs for a medical device corporation in Washington DC. Positioned CEO as national ‘thought leader’ resulting in a private meeting at the White House.

• Restructured communications department for a Chapter 11 corporation. Company emerged from bankruptcy.

Lasting relationships are nurtured through credible and reliable sources requiring critical reasoning to craft timely, trusting and understandable messages that engage the socially networked and multi-channeled communities of today's market.

Strategic direction, integration of resources and critical assessment of complicated health care policies and issues defines my competitive edge for identifying, engaging and retaining these relationships.
