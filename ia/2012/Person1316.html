Nellie Huang's Experience

Editor-in-Chief

WildJunket Magazine

February 2008 – Present (5 years) Worldwide

WildJunket Magazine is a digital travel magazine with a focus on outdoor adventures and sustainable travel. The bi-monthly magazine is published as an online flipbook and available through Magzter and Zinio. We cover in-depth stories on unconventional destinations and extraordinary experiences around the world. As the magazine's co-founder and editor, I write several features, articles for our regular departments, gear review and edit contributions from our writers. I am also responsible for the editorial calendar, liasing with writers, marketing the magazine via social media and other platforms as well as negotations with advertisers.

Our website is ranked as one of the world's best travel blogs, coming in as #8 on Google US Top 100 Travel Websites and #12 on Google UK. We've won several awards and our articles are syndicated by Lonely Planet blog as well as Travel Avenue and Tips from the T-List. Our articles range from destination-focused blog posts to photo essays and quirky top 10 travel lists. I am the main blogger of the site and take charge of editing all content.
