Annelies Verhelst's Summary

So you probably got confused by my headline and got to here, my summary. Good thing! I'll try to explain to you what I'm about.

I am a freelance copywriter. But, I do that next to my 32 hour job as a Social Media & PR Executive. It doesn't mean I don't have time for assignments, or that I'm unreachable. You can always e-mail me and chat with me via Skype. During the day I will answer your e-mail and chats close to immediately.

As I have a job as a Social Media & PR Executive for four days a week, you can best contact me if you have got anything that doesn't take up too much of my time. But, experience tells me that most of the time we can work something out, as long as the deadline is a bit flexible. I use my full network to help you the best I can.

But what exactly do you do, you may ask? Well, I do a fairly good job at writing press releases, webcopy (SEO-friendly), blog articles or reviews. That means I can be your freelance journalist, PR-assistant or copywriter.
Also, I know a lot about using social media for your business and I know a lot about SEO. I could advise you, with the risk of documents ending up under a pile of more documents, but I'd rather put some people together and make a workshop out of it, preferably Dutch though.

Why so many different angles in your work, would be your next question. Well, I just love to see a thorough strategy for anyone's business, and that can only happen when everything is connected the right way. My passion goes to providing good copy that explains all you want to the right audience. PR-agents and journalists both have their own ways of delivering content to their audience. I like both ways, when done correctly. Can't help it, can't deny it. It's all about dynamics for me and this it.

Contact me if you're interested!

More about my passion and work can be found at my personal blog (Dutch) www.anneliesje.nl and my experience and expertise can be found at www.insperide.nl.
