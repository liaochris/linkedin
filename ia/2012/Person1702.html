Tess Whitty's Summary

Tess Whitty is an English-Swedish-English translator with the comprehensive know-how, language skills and knowledge of Swedish culture you need to save your business time and money. Tess specializes in IT/software, business communications and medical software/equipment. Driven, ambitious and highly organized, Tess is highly skilled in translating, proofreading/editing, copywriting and localizing English documents into credible, persuasive Swedish communications.

Based in Park City, Utah, Tess is a Swedish expatriate with two Masters Degrees: a Master of Arts in Business Communication and Public Relations and a Master of Science in Economics. She is fully bilingual in Swedish and English, has nearly nine years of full-time translation experience, and has lived in four different countries. By combining her expert language skills with the keen eye of a trained marketer, she will help you reach Swedish markets and help you develop culturally appropriate communications that resonate directly with Swedish consumers.

She is also a proud member of several professional translation associations where she is involved in representing and enforcing their high standards. She is also currently the Chair of the English-Swedish Language Certification Committee for the American Translators Association and was responsible for launching the certification program for the Swedish language.

For more information about Swedish Translation Services, please visit www.swedishtranslationservices.com or visit Tess’ blog at www.swedishtranslationservices.blogspot.com to learn more about professional translations and how you can work effectively with professional translators.
