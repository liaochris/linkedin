Sheila Scarborough's Summary

I'm a writer, speaker, trainer and consultant specializing in tourism, travel and social media.

As the co-founder of the Tourism Currents online learning community, we teach tourism, hospitality and economic development professionals how to make sense of social media for their destination marketing.

A certified Navy Master Training Specialist, I'm also a former Associate Professor of Naval Science at the University of Florida, a member of the National Board of Directors for the AWC (Association for Women in Communications) and an NHRA drag racing fan.

Specialties

I write and blog about general travel, usually with a cultural twist and I really enjoy showing individuals and companies (particularly tourism organizations) how to navigate social media and use the Web to enhance their professional and personal lives.

My motorsports expertise is in NHRA drag racing because there is one winner, one loser and no whining.
