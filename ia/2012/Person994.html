Linda Pollock, NCC's Summary

I'm an ENFP and my top strengths from strengths finder are
1. connectedness, 2. woo, 3. individualization, 4. positivity, 5. communication
I love working with students from all backgrounds and skill levels. I want to be a "student" my whole life because everyone has something to teach. My time is spent teaching career development at UNCG. I tell people I majored in "reframing" in graduate school because so much of resume development, interview preparation and self-confidence in the job search comes from how you articulate it to someone else. So I use this skill everyday with the student population in the Bryan School of Business and Economics and am hopefully doing some good in the process.
I am passionate about encouraging others to their potential and helping them reach their dreams. Social justice, equity, change and effective communication are all topics to get me talking for hours.
