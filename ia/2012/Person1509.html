Richard Michie's Summary

I’ve worked in Marketing for a long time. In that time I’ve worked in lots of different industries from Retail to Travel, Digital to Mail Order. In that time, I’ve learnt a lot and achieved some amazing things.

I’m proud to be Director of Marketing and Operations for Global Lingo a transcription, translation and interpreting company. The whole company is passionate about delivering high quality language services to our clients to world over. As well as Leeds, Global Lingo also has offices in London and Singapore.

I’m also the co-organiser of Bettakultcha a series of presentation events based in and around West Yorkshire, where people speak for 5 minutes about their passions.

Helped organise the hugely successful TEDxLeeds in Feb 2012. This brought speakers from around the world to the Leeds Corn Exchange to discuss the subject of money and alternative currencies.

Happy to talk to journalists about how language services can help businesses improve their sales and profitability.

Experience of a vast array of sectors, Leisure and Tourism, Mail Order, Localisation, Local Government, Health and Safety, Business to Business, Retail, Entertainment.

Specialties

Business Change, People Management, Social Network Marketing, Events, Search Engine Optimisation, Marketing Strategy, PR, Brand, Presentations, Copywriting, Design, Photography
