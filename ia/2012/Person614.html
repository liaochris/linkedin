Internet media in practice.

I have been working in online media 5 years now - as an online editor in the very beginning till head of online news product now.

I was involved in creating some good internet products like gazetopedia.pl, wysokieobcasy.pl and redesign of wyborcza.pl what gave me an opportunity to learn how internet media work in practice.
