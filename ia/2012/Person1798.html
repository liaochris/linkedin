Vivian Stewart's Summary

Vivian Stewart has a wealth of multi-disciplinary business experience. He has co-founded several successful companies and a not for profit, sits on the Board of ASX-listed telco BigAir, and is an experienced corporate adviser, having executed many successful transactions over the years. Vivian also worked with NASDAQ-listed Intel Corporation in Hong Kong in a regional marketing role. Vivian is an ICT industry veteran. He is a well-travelled, proactive, broadly experienced businessman and an outstanding communicator with a keen interest in strategy, building businesses in general, and assisting others to understand their capital strategy options - from capital raising through to exits.

Vivian joined Corporate Advisory firm Hall Capital as a Director in 2005. Since this time, he has worked on a number of successful transactions and assignments for public and private companies including capital raisings, trade sales and strategy.

In 1999, Vivian co-founded early-stage investment group TiNSHED, which specialised in the Internet, media and telecommunications sectors. During this time, he assessed several hundred businesses on behalf of high net worth individuals, led marketing and business development efforts, forged alliances with global organisations, negotiated agreements and presented deals to very high-net worth individuals on a regular basis.

In 1993, Vivian co-founded and was an executive director of pioneering Australian Internet Service Provider - Magna Data. In 1999 Magna Data was sold to publicly listed Davnet.
