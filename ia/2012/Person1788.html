Victor Jasin's Summary

Financial
During various projects I was involved in raising venture capital and seeking equity participants. I was an equity/owner in Tripak, V.Jasin Consulting, V.Jasin International Consulting Inc. Personal venture capital procurement ranged up to $125,000 individual investment. Banking presentations were made on behalf of numerous clients including international banking arrangements with the World Bank for international trade development in PRC. Preparation of financial statements and various risk, cost and market analysis were performed as well as budgets and forecasts.

Business Development
Various small business clients regarding start-ups planning and strategy, business plan preparations, banking presentations, marketing assessment and sales development were performed. Various U.S. clients seeking business presence in Canada were also represented on a variety of issues.

Sales and Marketing

Legal and Compliance
