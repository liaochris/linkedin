Surekha Pillai's Summary

I am currently working as an independent consultant advising select clients. My current long-term projects include working with Edelman India to head the transition on the Tata Group business and working with the Water and Sanitation Program of the World Bank for a project with the Kerala state government.

In the past, I have also undertaken projects for a boutique consulting firm for their key clients, an energy major entering India, and a trade council division of a European country.

I have over 18 years of experience in the PR and communications field.

2000 - Jan, 2008: General Manager, Northern Region and Branch Head, Delhi (IPAN HQ) reporting to the President.

As GM, the responsibilities ranged from overall management of the operations and supervising key businesses of the agency. Managed accounts including ITC, Hitachi, Hyundai, Nikon, ESPN, Turner, Continental Airlines and Wizard (a GE Money company), among others. Managed clients such as Ford, HCL, Beam Global, Cisco Systems, ISB and POSCO in the past as IPAN’s Client Servicing Head.

My role in client management included:

Strategic Planning and counsel, Competition track and strategy, Perception and image audits, Issues and crisis management, Brand property creation, Policy track, Trade and industry body liaison, Advisory panels, Media counsel, Integrated communications (360 degree)

My areas of interest: Developing specialist communications tools and products, Web communications and social media, CSR

About IPAN: A pioneer and industry leader in public affairs and public relations in India. The two-decade old IPAN is part of India's largest communications conglomerate and is a sister unit of JWT, India’s largest advertising firm. It is also part of three leading international networks in PR: Hill & Knowlton; Eurocom Worldwide and Cohn & Wolfe.

Specialties

Public affairs, Issues management, Stakeholder management, Entry strategy for MNCs, Corporate Image Building, Strategic communications, Crisis communications
