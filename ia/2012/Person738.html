Jarrod Williams' Summary

I am a keen and hardworking PR professional, with a flair for creative campaigns and digital strategies. I have a broad range of PR skills, with experience in internal communications, media relations and events management. I am also highly skilled in audio production.

I'm dedicated to my own professional development, and strive in all projects i'm working on. I am extremely proud to be CIPR Midlands Student of the Year & overall PR Winner of the 2011 B Hive competition. I constantly work to enhance skills through CIPR workshops, networking events and other courses & hve presented papers on the link between students & the PR industry at conferences. I also hold a First Class Degree from one of the UKs top Schools of Media.

I have a strong knowledge base, both through theoretical academia & practical public relations experience, having had some fantastic opportunities to work in a range of media environments. From agency experience with Willoughby PR, McCann Erickson and Grayling (amongst many others), to in-House experience with the NHS, as well as placements with Kerrang Radio, the work I have undertaken has been diverse & engaging, allowing me to gain first-hand insight into various areas of the PR and media industry.

Heavily active on social networking sites such as Twitter & with a grasp of new media digital strategies, as a student I already clock up a Klout score of 56, with a current peerindex of 53. I have achieved press coverage for a wide range of 'freelance' and university project clients, including achieving 37 positive print mentions for one air cadet unit.

I have managed internal communication audits for NHS trusts, been instrumental in providing consultancy for consumer campaigns on digital platforms & have spearheaded promotional and media relations strategies for youth charities.

My outlook is to be the next CIPR Young Communicator, and I hope to achieve this by continuing to build on my skills to become a highly productive and innovative PRO.
