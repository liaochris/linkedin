Jeff Cobb's Summary

Through my writing, speaking, and consulting I help organizations in the business of lifelong learning maximize the reach, revenue, and impact of their offerings.

My next book, Leading the Learning Revolution will be published by AMACOM in january 2013. More at http://www.learningrevolution.net.

I have written extensively about the use of learning technologies by trade and professional associations. My publications include:

- Association Learning + Technology: State of the Sector
- Association Learning Management Systems
- Learning 2.0 for Associations
- Association Virtual Conferences

More about these publications can be found at http://www.tagoras.com/catalog/.

I also write and speak about the broader impact of technology on business, education, and society in general. I am the co-author, along with futurist David Houle, of ShiftEd: A Call to Action for Transforming K-12 Education, published by Corwin in April 2011. (http://www.shiftedtransformation.com)

I have more than a decade's experience as an entrepreneur, executive, and marketing professional in the field of Web technology and education.

I have served on the Professional Development Section Council of the American Society of Association Executives and am an adviser to Philantech, provider of the PhilanTrack™ online grant proposal, reporting and management system.

Finally, I blog weekly at http://www.tagoras.com/blog (for organizations) and http://www.missiontolearn.com (for individual lifelong learners).
