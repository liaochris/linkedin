John Fox's Summary

Software industry professional with a successful leadership background and strong technology foundation.

Currently Vice President of Marketing at Siemens PLM Software, responsible for the Velocity Series portfolio -- a set of out-of-the-box software tools for the mainstream product lifecycle management (PLM) market. Velocity Series software products include Solid Edge® for computer-aided design (CAD), Teamcenter® Express for product data management (PDM), Femap™ for finite element analysis (FEA) and CAM Express for computer-aided manufacturing (CAM).

Specialties

Business development, financial modeling and analysis, product strategy, product management, product roadmap planning and development, competitive analysis, marketing strategy, public speaking at industry and customer events. Subject matter expertise includes: innovation management, Product Lifecycle Management (PLM), Product Data Management (PDM), design for environment, product stewardship, sustainability, product environmental compliance, REACH, RoHS, ELV, WEEE, ErP.
