David Taylor's Summary

Writer in print, film and the web, of character-driven narratives that explore culture, science and history. Work has appeared in Smithsonian, Outside, The Village Voice, The Washington Post and The American Scholar. Wrote Ginseng, the Divine Root (2006, Algonquin), an adventure through the medicinal plant trade and Soul of a People (2009, Wiley) about a pivotal moment in the 1930s. Wrote and co-produced the award-winning television documentary also titled Soul of a People, which premiered on the Smithsonian Channel in Sept. 2009, as well as the script for Worlds of Sound: The Ballad of Folkways, another Smithsonian documentary that premiered in 2009.

Specialties

history, environment, science, trade, innovation, culture, travel, documentary
