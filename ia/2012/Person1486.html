Senior Vice President

Clients include Global 5000, SMEs, VC-funded startups, and privately held. Emphasis on consumer and enterprise IT. Campaigns centered on thought leadership, social networking, advertorial, inbound marketing, lead generation, and sales enablement.

Host/Producer

PC Talk Radio

1994 – 2012 (18 years)

Host of the longest-running computer industry talk radio program, broadcasting on terrestrial radio since 1994 (as Computer Talk and later as PC TALK) and on the Internet since 1997. PC TALK features live, unedited interviews with IT industry personalities on the topics, technologies, and business issues of the day. As heard on some of America's greatest radio networks, including Public Radio Exchange (PRX), WPHT 1210 AM "The Big Talker", KYW Newsradio 1060 AM, WPEN 950 AM, and hundreds of stations through coast to coast syndication.

Editor-in-Chief

North American Publishing Company

Editor-in-Chief of three leading national trade magazines produced by one of America’s oldest publishers: PrintMedia, BookTech, and Graphic Design & Business. Led redesign of two publications, launched a third. Increased circulation and ad pages by reinventing magazines from fawning trade pubs into must-read periodicals, using hard-hitting news and analysis, compelling features, and explosive cover stories to grab and hold readers.

Custom Content Editor

Drove creative; conducted all reporting; produced editorial; directly interfaced with and managed CMP's clients and partners, including Microsoft, hp, Dell, Intel, and others.

Senior Editor
