Georgia Bullen's Summary

I recently completed a masters degree in Urban Planning at Columbia University. I focused on intelligent transportation systems and how the prevalence of hand held technology affects our relationship with the urban environment.

I also do freelance design and usability work. A simple, well designed user interface can make all the difference with how a product is received, how it's used, and how successful it is.
