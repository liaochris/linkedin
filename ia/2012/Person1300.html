Myra Binstock 973-783-6006's Summary

I have been the principal of Myra Binstock Legal Search since 1986. Prior to that I headed the intellectual property search team at Wells International and Audrey Golden Associates, both search firms located in New York City. I have been placing patent attorneys and agents for the past 26 years.

My prime goal is being available to speak by phone or in person to candidates and be available to them. I'm a child of the 20th century so my approach to success in placing candidates in the right jobs requires hearing a voice and meeting a person, not just relying on email.

Specialties

Negotiate the placement of intellectual property attorneys and agents, as well as others, in law firms and corporations. I handle mergers of large and small firms. I enjoy discussing, suggesting and enabling personal career growth with newbies and more seasoned legal professionals.
