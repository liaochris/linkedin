Niamh Tumelty's Projects

  • 23 Things for Professional Development

    • January 2011 to Present
    Team Members: Niamh Tumelty, Annie Johnson, Jo Alcock, Katie Birkwood, Claire Sewell, Isla Kuhn, Helen Murphy, Maria Giovanna De Simone, Meg Westbury, Charlotte Byrne

    23 Things for Professional Development (CPD23) is an online programme consisting of a series of 23 blog posts, each focusing on a different element of professional development. The programme first ran in Summer 2011 and was a huge success, having over 700 registered participants and being awarded a 'highly commended' runner up certificate in the Credo Reference Digital Award for Information Literacy 2012. The programme is now running again over Summer 2012.

  • Cambridge Library TeachMeet

    • July 2010 to Present
    Team Members: Niamh Tumelty, Katie Birkwood, , Isla Kuhn, Chris Barker, Jo Harcus, Liz Osman, Jenni Kuziw, Clare Aitken

    TeachMeets are informal gatherings of (originally) teachers, designed to allow the sharing of ideas. Cambridge Library TeachMeets offer the opportunity for information professionals from all sectors an informal and fun way to share their 'techie tools and teaching tips'. Two TeachMeets have been held so far, in September 2010 and March 2011.
