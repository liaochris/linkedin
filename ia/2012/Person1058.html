Martijn van der Meulen's Summary

I am an experienced Game Producer who originally started working at Electronic Arts over ten years ago. My career at EA took me from Online Marketeer responsible for setting up their very first websites, to Producer on the award-winning The Simpsons Game (http://goo.gl/SEjeu). About four years ago I moved to the Sony PlayStation London Studio. This studio is a pioneer in the social gaming genre with the BAFTA award winning EyeToy and SingStar franchises. I was one of the key members of the team behind the successful online social gaming platform PlayStation Home (http://goo.gl/07c2P). In my last year at Sony I was responsible for setting up and managing the pre-production process on Wonderbook: Book of Spells, which is based on the $15+ billion Harry Potter franchise, for PlayStation 3. Additionally I spent several months within Sony R&D creating the ‘Magnet Demo’ (http://goo.gl/ss8Z0) - eight unique augmented reality concepts for the PlayStation Vita.

Currently I work at Relentless Software in the UK. Together with Microsoft and National Geographic we are creating Kinect Nat Geo TV, a unique Kinect experience aimed at a younger audience. It’s pretty amazing. I enjoy creating remarkable products that give people new experiences and brings them together. People say that having a videogame making someone cry is the ultimate goal - but for me, personally, it is to use the medium in a way that makes people laugh and have fun.

Specialties

• Social gaming, social interaction and collaboration / competition in video games.
• Agile project management (SCRUM) and other methodologies (GTD / Action Method etc)
• Managing relationships with external IP holders
• Managing and inspiring teams to get the most out of themselves
• Structuring and streamlining development approaches
• Game Design, Game Flow, User Experience
