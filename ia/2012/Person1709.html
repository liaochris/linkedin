Tanveer Naseer, M.Sc.'s Summary

Tanveer Naseer is the Principal and Founder of Tanveer Naseer Leadership, a leadership coaching firm that works with managers and executives to help them develop leadership and team-building competencies to guide organizational growth and development, while ensuring they remain focused on what creates a fulfilling sense of purpose in what they do.

Tanveer credits his background and experiences in the scientific field for his keen understanding of the nature of interactions in business, as well as being able to help organizations break down and improve complex processes which are holding their teams back.

Tanveer’s writings on leadership and workplace interactions have been featured in a number of prominent organization and media publications, including Canada’s national newspaper “The Globe and Mail”, Human Capital Institute (HCI), American Management Association (AMA), Hallmark Business Connections, “Floral Management” and “Incentive” magazines, as well as in numerous SmartBrief industry and International Spa Association “Touch Points” e-newsletters.

Tanveer is the recipient of several awards and recognitions as one of the leading writers in the leadership sphere, including being recognized as one of the best 21st Century Leadership Blogs, earning a #3 ranking on HR Examiner’s Top 25 Online Influencers in Leadership, voted the top Canadian Business blog (as well as one of the best-written Canadian blogs) in the Canadian Weblog Awards, and ranked as one of Top 25 Leadership Bloggers.

You can read more of his writings on leadership and workplace interactions on his award-winning blog at TanveerNaseer.com. You can also follow him on Twitter - @TanveerNaseer.
