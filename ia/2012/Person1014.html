Lynsey Sweales' Summary

Helping businesses of all sizes with all their SEO, social media and content marketing requirements.

With a wealth of experience in social media and online marketing Lynsey is extremely well placed to give your business consultancy, training or managing how to have a real impact in the online arena.

An expert in her field many of her clients have been and continue to be extremely pleased with fantastic SEO and social media results.

Although Norfolk based, Lynsey's clients span the UK and her successes for her clients worldwide.

Social media training and consultancy is a key part of Lynsey's expertise. She provides training through her own business, but also white labels her services.

A sample of clients who have used Lynsey's social media and SEO training services include O2, De Beers, Emirates, The Retail Trust, The Natural History Museum, ASDA, Wilkinson, the Welsh Rugby League, The Carbon Trust and the NHS. More examples can be provided on request.

Many of Lynsey's clients also include large marketing agencies who tap in to Lynsey's wealth of social media knowledge to provide their clients with the full content marketing experience.

Lynsey can help her clients really utilise social media to maximum effect by tapping in to her knowledge and experience of successful social media strategies alongside her SEO consultancy.

Whether you simply require training or would rather outsource your social media, SEO or content marketing work Lynsey can help you.
