Scott Hendison's Summary

With 15+ years of retail management experience in the 1980's and 90's, for this century, Scott's specialty has been search engine placement, as demonstrated by his own typical front page search results for phrases like "internet consultant" and "search engine expert".

Scott closed a computer & software store in 2002 to pursue independent consulting service for businesses wanting to be found on Google and elsewhere.

In 2004 he founded Search Commander, Inc. and in 2009, released SEO Automatic, a suite of white label WordPress SEO tools.

If you're going to connect on Linked in with Scott (or anyone, really) then it's best to say who you are and where you know him from, since many default requests that say "I'd like to add you to my network..." can get ignored.

Specialties

Organic search engine optimization, WordPress, keyword research, competition analysis, conversions, affiliate marketing, analytics, and automation. Work smarter not harder.
