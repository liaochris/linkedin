Seth Fiegerman's Summary

Seth Fiegerman is a business reporter at Mashable, where he covers startups, tech stocks, marketing and media. Before joining Mashable, Seth covered all things Apple as a reporter at Silicon Alley Insider, the tech section of Business Insider. He has also worked as a staff writer at TheStreet and as an editor at Playboy Magazine.

Seth's articles have been featured in dozens of publications, including Newsweek, NPR, CNN, Kiplinger and The Huffington Post. You can e-mail him at seth@mashable.com, or get in touch through Twitter @sfiegerman.

Specialties: Reporting, business journalism, social media, HTML, editing.
