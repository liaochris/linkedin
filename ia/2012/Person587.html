The Healing Power of Music for Men and Women Living with Alzheimer's and Related Dementia

Therapeutic Music Listening in Dementia Care

October 2000 – July 2010 (9 years 10 months)

In October 2002 I wrote and submitted an in-depth proposal to the Sony Corporation to introduce the use of Therapeutic Music Listening as a valuable tool in the daily care of men and women living with Alzheimer's and related forms of dementia. In 2005 I expanded the proposal to include the technological world and submitted it to Apple, Yahoo, RealRhapsody, XM Satellite Entertainment and AOL. My mission, in conjunction with that of the American Music Therapy Association in Silver Spring, Maryland, is to educate the public, home caregivers and the dedicated dementia care community about the life and health giving benefits of therapeutic music listening in the lives of men and women living with Alzheimer's. An idea whose time has certainly come, this is a program of caregiving that has potential to be expanded worldwide.
