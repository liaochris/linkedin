Daniel Graeber's Summary

I am writing on contract for United Press International for their premium services. I contribute several pieces daily on political and general developments in the Levant and broader Middle East. I also cover the energy sector, focusing predominately on Eastern Europe and Central Asia. I've also supervised a native Arabic speaker who translated articles from Iraqi newspapers into English.

My goal is to expand beyond media outlets to provide sound analysis regarding political developments in the Levant. My particular areas of interest are Iraq and Iran.

Specialties

I hold a graduate degree in international relations from Norwich University. I've published several pieces outside of UPI, including a text-book feature on U.S.-Israeli relations. A pending feature deals with the U.S. policy of containment during the Cold War. I also contribute to the Foreign Policy Association writing on war crimes and international law.

My strengths lie in my knowledge of Iraq and Iran, while my graduate focus was Afghanistan and international relations theory.
