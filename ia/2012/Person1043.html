Mark Cooper | Financial Planner | IFA's Summary

Mark Cooper is a Retirement Planning Specialist at Surrey based Independent Financial Advisors Clarity Financial Planning Ltd.

He works with individuals and small business owners with a key focus on building a financially secure, comfortable and fulfilling retirement.

Accruing wealth and capital for future income, demystifiying the world of Pensions, choosing income options at retirement and investing with the tax-breaks still available are a core part of the advice delivered, in a clear, concise, no-nonsense way.

Mark has 26 years of retail financial services experience and has entered his seventh year of offering independent financial advice.

The choice of company name “Clarity” reflects the very clear way in which our advice is delivered, the use of charts, graphs and pictures to illustrate the implications, risk and opportunity of our recommendations and the open affordable way in which we charge for our services.


Regulatory Statement: Clarity Financial Planning Ltd is an Appointed Representative of Financial Ltd which is authorised and regulated by the Financial Services Authority.

Disclaimer: Any opinion/views expressed are the personal views of Mark Cooper and do not represent those of Financial Ltd.

Mark Cooper | Financial Planner | IFA's Experience

Retirement Planning Specialist IFA

Clarity Financial Planning Ltd

January 2010 – Present (2 years 6 months)

I help business owners and private individuals to plan a comfortable, secure and fulfilling retirement with truly independent financial advice.

Not just an 'order taker' for financial products, I help people reach decisions on what they wish to do with the rest of their life, what it will cost and how to achieve it.

My specialist area is retirement planning, from accruing sufficient assets, including consolidating and revitalising existing arrangements and use of the fund to provide income in the most tax efficient way possible.

In addition, I advise on savings and investments, financial protection (the 'safety net' for businesses, individuals and their dependents) and tax planning.

With 26 years industry experience I can offer clarity, experience and affordable advice.
