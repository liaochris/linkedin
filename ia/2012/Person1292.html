M Sarah Wickham's Summary

Responsible for the development and management of Archives & Special Collections service; supporting teaching & learning, research and engagement with heritage; strategic direction of records management including the implementation of a University-wide electronic document & records management system; information governance at strategic and operational level.

A regular trainer with a reputation for facilitating learning in inclusive and supportive environments, and working successfully with a wide range of colleagues.

My professional technical expertise includes information governance (Data Protection, Freedom of Information and Copyright Law), records management from creation to final disposition, resource discovery and imaginative interpretation and access. I have a longstanding interest in digitisation for accessibility and was particularly delighted to have an association with the Digital Image Archive of Medieval Music over several years which combined many of these interests (see http://www.diamm.ac.uk/access/partners/collaborators.html)
