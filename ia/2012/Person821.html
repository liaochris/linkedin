John Denny's Summary

My digital marketing experience dates back over two decades, to my work leading the ad agency team for the Prodigy Online Service. In 1996, I had my first experience with the revolution that is search, heading up the AltaVista Search Engine business at DDB advertising. In the late ‘90’s, I worked for one of the first ad networks pioneering behavioral targeting- CMGI’s Adsmart. Later at Winstar Interactive Media I led monetization strategy for sites including the search engines Ask.com and Northern Lights. Currently I’m VP Marketing for Advance Digital, part of Advance Publications / Conde Nast (the 12th largest U.S. Media company according to AdAge). I head up a 17 person group with teams focused on Search Engine Optimization, Social Media Optimization, Paid Search, Analytics, Consumer / B2B marketing, and email marketing for 12 U.S. local media sites comprising over 20 million unique users. In addition, I founded, built, and head up our Advance Search & Social unit that provides SEO and Social Media Optimization services to local and regional businesses.

Specialties

Digital Marketing and advertising, Search Engine Marketing (SEM), Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Local Search, Social Media Optimization (SMO), Content Marketing, Paid Search (PPC), Web Analytics, Usability Research, Digital Product Development, Digital Sales Team Training, Vision and Strategic Plan development for organizations.
