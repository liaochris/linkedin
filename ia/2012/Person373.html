Dave Chaffey's Summary

Hi!

> ABOUT ME

My passion is sharing and educating on the latest best practices to help businesses compete through digital marketing.

I'm a specialist in using Digital Marketing to improve company performance. I'm fortunate to have been involved with the fantastic opportunities available for marketing since 1997 as a bestselling author, speaker and commentator.

My latest book editions are in the panel below.

> CONNECT WITH ME!

Feel free to connect if you'd like like to say "Hi" or ask me a question. Let me know if we've met on a course, talk or you've used my books.

> ABOUT MY BUSINESSES

My main business is SmartInsights.com, an online publisher which helps marketers get the most from their digital marketing through our digital marketing advice portal, strategy and analytics consulting.

I also work for ClickThrough Marketing, the search conversion specialists as Insights Director, where I work with our team and clients to develop processes and reporting to get the best results from search marketing.

Finally, Marketing Insights Limited is a company I formed in 2001 used for independent consulting and training to help companies improve their Internet marketing.

I also lecture on Ecommerce and Emarketing at leading business schools.

> ACCOLADES

Dave was recognised by the CIM, in 2004, as one of 50 marketing ‘gurus’ worldwide who have shaped the future of Marketing.

I'm also proud to have been recognized by the Department of Trade and Industry as one of the leading individuals who have provided input and influence on the development and growth of E-commerce and the Internet.

In 2010 I was elected an Honorary Life Fellow by IDM in recognition of my work in educating marketers on best practice in digital marketing.

For Dave's latest insights, go to http://www.smartinsights.com

Specialties

Internet marketing training, E-marketing courses, Digital marketing consulting. I consult and train on: Internet marketing planning & Strategy, Web analytics, Search engine marketing, E-mail marketing, social media and persuasion marketing - all the tactics that work best to get results!
