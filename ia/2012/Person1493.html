Remco Timmermans' Summary

I dedicate my career to developing a sustainable future for society. In my previous life I worked on great projects, with great people all over the world, learning a great deal about how things work. I also learned that some things matter more than other, and that I can accomplish great things in teams of people with a clear sense of passion, purpose and urgency.

It is my ambition to work on a sustainable future for a new global society. I want to explore the benefits of technology and social media, new organization models, globalization, human wellbeing through tourism and leisure and keeping humanity's pioneering spirit alive through new discoveries and exploration of space.

I consider myself an expert in social media, space flight, aviation, new organization models and the future of tourism. In old-world terms I am an MSc in Business Studies and have a Mechanical Engineering Bachelors degree. I am a Lean Six Sigma Black Belt, an ERP system and Leasing processes expert. I have 15 years of experience in IT system design and implementation, financing, business process reengineering, process outsourcing and management consulting.

Recently I decided to turn my passions into businesses. I am the co-founder of arctic expedition and corporate training company Expedition Factory, and co-own Future Matters, a sustainable tourism consulting and training firm. In addition I work on public outreach for the space industry through social media and I am a professional space blogger.

Specialties

Social media experimenter, space blogger, spacetweetup organizer, space flight expert, tourism futurologist, author. As a person I am highly analytical, an eternal student, too widely interested, but dedicated to everything about improving our common future.

My acronyms: MBTI (step I and II), LSS Black Belt, PMO/PMP, ERP, BI, ITIL, BPR/BPM, BPO, CSR, MVO, AI
