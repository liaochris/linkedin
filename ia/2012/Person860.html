Jessica Safir's Summary

I graduated from University of the Pacific in May 2010 where I earned a BS in Business Administration with a concentration in Marketing from the Eberhardt School of Business. During my years at Pacific I participated in NCAA Division 1 Athletics, worked on campus as an event manager, held executive positions in campus organizations, was selected to join multiple honor societies, and led Pacific's chapter of the American Marketing Association as Vice President. I also gained experience working as a Marketing & Communications Intern at Good Samaritan Hospital and Regional Medical Center in San Jose, CA.

Upon graduation I was selected to receive the 2009-2010 Eberhardt School of Business Outstanding Student Award in Marketing. I was also chosen as the Greek Woman of the Year based on my accomplishments within my sorority and the Greek community.

I spent three months after graduation living with a family in northern Spain. After moving back to the Bay Area, I began working in campus marketing at Kno, Inc. then transitioned to my current role at Google as a University Programs Coordinator.
