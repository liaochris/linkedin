Damian Radcliffe's Summary

I have 16 years' experience in the UK's commercial, public, regulatory and non-profit media and communications sectors.

My roles have encompassed all platforms: Radio, TV and Online, as well as creative, strategy and policy functions.

I have worked in digital media since 1999. Digital take up, inclusion and preventing digital exclusion, have been frequently recurring themes across these different roles.

Since 2009 I have developed a recognised specialism in hyper-local media, with an emphasis on online digital communities.

Outside of my day job, I am increasingly interested in the role new technology can play in supporting the international - and domestic - development agendas.

Specialties

* Knowledge and experience of working in the UK's media and communications industries.
* Expert in hyper-local, community media and digital inclusion issues.
* Research, report writing, fundraising applications, marketing materials and presentations.
* Partnership / Stakeholder management, from relationship building to true collaboration.
* Business Development, from start-ups to more established propositions.
