Shira Abel's Summary

Shira Abel is the CEO of Hunter & Bard - a turnkey marketing agency for startups, small companies, and intrapreneurs. She is a thought leader in Israel in the relationship marketing space and works with companies to build and implement smart marketing strategies that accomplish company goals. Ms. Abel has experience speaking worldwide at conferences and universities on marketing for startups and social media marketing, and mentors at several startup accelerators. She also works with companies to build the marketing into the product. MBA from Kellogg School of Management.

Specialties

digital marketing, marketing strategy, relationship marketing, social media, business development, crisis management, customer relations, marketing communications, management, technical marketing, community development and management, account management
