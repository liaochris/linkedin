Jen Baptist's Summary

Ms Baptist is an environmental geologist by profession and an avid outdoors woman exploring in the northwest region of the United States. One of her many passions is capturing the beauty of the world she experiences in photographs. Her photos are 'jen'uine, without artistic doctoring. To view her art work please visit her blog at justjenuine.com.

Professionally Ms. Baptist holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Geology and has 10 years of experience in the environmental consulting industry, including Phase I and Phase II Environmental Site Assessments, field sampling of various media (soil, groundwater, porewater, stormwater, etc, including off-shore sampling), beneficial water use studies, and chemical injection methods. Her background in stormwater management and regulatory compliance includes preparing Stormwater Pollution Prevention Plans and Spill Prevention, Control, and Countermeasure Plans.
