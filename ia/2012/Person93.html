Andrew Horton-Hall's Summary

I'm a corporate communications professional with over five years of editorial experience and three years as a public relations practitioner at a Providence, R.I.-based non-profit, where my work was featured nationally. I currently serve as a marketing professional at an international architectural firm dedicated to designing environments that enhance people's lives around the globe. Being around some of the brightest minds in healthcare design is a thrilling experience.

In addition to marketing duties, I am also an MBA in Sustainability student at Antioch University New England in Keene, N.H. At Antioch, I am learning new ways of applying environmental and social sustainability measures at the corporate level, helping executives envision savings and marketing potential as they embrace a "triple bottom line" philosophy.

My career goal is to one day create an international consulting team dedicated to helping corporations better market their sustainability practices through an enhanced approach to corporate social responsibility (CSR).

Specialties

web design, graphic design, editorial experience, public relations, branding and identity, marketing, communications, sustainability, green, consulting, project management
