Alfonso López Manzano's Summary

Computer software professional devoted to the analysis and management of projects related to the areas of administration, finance, commercial, quality, safety, operations and human resources, specialized in business intelligence tools, and experienced in project implementation or evolution of ERP applications, CRM type business management, corporate portals and custom applications.
