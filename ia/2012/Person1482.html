Rayanne Thorn's Summary

I am the Marketing Director - North America for Broadbean, Inc., with some social marketing responsibilities for our Asia Pac operation. Broadbean is perpetually perfecting and evolving the technology of job posting distribution and automated candidate sourcing. I love that I get to share with the world what we do. Broadbean Technology provides global job distribution and candidate response analytics to the World's Largest Staffing Companies, Recruitment Advertising Agencies, Technology Vendors, Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO) businesses, as well as Major Employers / Corporations. Our ROI metrics and job board analysis allow recruiters and hiring professionals to fully understand where their money is going and if they are benefiting from that spend.

Additionally, constant innovation takes us to the next level: social employee referral, mobile phone apps, and the most popular social media channels! We understand their affect on business today and the importance of creating products that benefit current and future clients.


Additionally, I write a daily featured post called Bonus Track (BonusTrackDaily.com) at Blogging4Jobs.com. Go to http://www.bonustrack.us for a back catalogue of the past 600+ consecutive posts that were published at RecruitingBlogs.com.

I have written over 850 total posts...

I enjoy assisting small businesses in establishing an online presence through the use of Social Media, as well as consulting in Search/Recruiting & Strategic Business Practices.

My varied background (sourcing, direct recruitment, marketing, sales, health care, new tech development, start-ups, and social media) rounds out my knowledge base and has provided the experience necessary to assist companies through recruitment processes, alpha & beta testing, funding, launch, as well as business & marketing plan development.
