Russell Tewksbury's Summary

Internet strategist, writer and technical consultant. Lectured at conferences in Europe, the Caribbean and throughout North America. Listed in Marquis' Who's Who in the World (1999 – present) and Marquis' Who's Who in America (2000 – present).

Owner of MarketWorks, LLC, a professional network management and Internet marketing services company. MarketWorks provides custom cloud-based application support and Internet optimizations and marketing services for small to mid-sized businesses.

Specialties

- Google Apps For Business.
- Cloud Services Planning & Integration.
- Litigation Support and Internet Consulting.
- Online Advertising and Marketing Services.
- Search Engine Marketing (SEM).
- Search Engine Optimization (SEO).
- Search Engine Reputation Management (SERM)
- Technology and Vendor Selection.
- Website Management Services: Design, Development and Hosting.
