Craig Kanalley's Summary

A native of Buffalo, New York, I'm a tireless worker and self-starter who pays close attention to detail. I enjoy writing, telling stories and connecting with people online. I'm a big believer in a commitment to innovation, experimentation and creativity to achieve success.

I'm passionate about the intersection of technology and media. I studied the impact of the real-time, social Web on journalism, focusing on Twitter, while completing a Masters degree in journalism (online/new media) at DePaul University in Chicago.

I get excited about social media, real-time news, networking, building communities and engaging online. I love being a team player and communicating with others, though can thrive working independently as needed. I'm a huge hockey fan and genealogist in my spare time.

Specialties

Breaking news coverage, social media, writing, blogging, liveblogging, copyediting, community engagement, analytics, SEO, sports/news reporting, content partnerships, building traffic
