Alan Reynolds' Summary

I am now an independent consultant, following an extensive career in UK public service. So I believe I can bridge the gap between public and private sectors ethos.

I have experience in how public bodies promote foreign trade or investment. So I can assist organisations with either developing such services or with reviewing how current services operate.

I have a long standing interest in information management, and how data is found, collected, stored, kept upto date and made available to those you need it.

Budgeting and managing money was a responsibility when in Civil Service, and subsequently in the Trust running the housing estate where I live. I am interested in management of resources.
