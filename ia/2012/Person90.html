Suupohja Living Lab is part of the European Network of Living Labs. Its concept and actions are part of the Regional Development Center Programme for the District of Kauhajoki, which is managed by the Federation of Municipalities for Economic Development in Suupohja region (Finland).

IST - Information Society Technologies is one of the thematic priorities in the European Union Sixth Framework Programme for research and technological development. The research activities funded in this framework address both technical aspects (hardware, software, knowledge) and societal challenges (communication, collaborative working, education) of Information Technologies.
