Michele Osella's Experience

Researcher & Business Analyst

ISMB

Nonprofit; 51-200 employees; Research industry

March 2010 – Present (2 years 5 months)

I hold a Junior position in the Business Model & Policy Innovation Unit, supporting policy makers and managers in using ICTs as a leverage to favor socioeconomic development and competitiveness. My research and consultancy activities mesh the evaluation of ICT related policies with the design of innovative business models based on new digital technologies. I’m currently involved in a number of national and international projects, developed in private and public sectors.

Research intern

ISMB

Nonprofit; 51-200 employees; Research industry

June 2009 – January 2010 (8 months)

My internship was focused on the conceptualization, formalization and validation of business models in the Infomobility&Infotainment industry. Business scenarios that I depicted were centered on embedded on-board IT platforms devoted to the seamlessly provision of a variety of services (e.g. advanced satellite navigation, m-commerce, georeferenced marketing, business directories, automotive CRM, multimedia entertainment, augmented reality, ITS solutions) using alternative technological paradigms. Taking advantage of business model ontologies, such as BMO and e3-value, I outlined innovative business models based on new value propositions compliant with emerging enabling infrastructures.

Research intern

ISMB

Nonprofit; 51-200 employees; Research industry

June 2007 – September 2007 (4 months)

During my placement I had been involved in studies which were carried out on behalf of the Regional Observatory on Information and Communication Technologies. I focused on the analysis concerning adoption and usage of ICTs as a driver of economic development in Piedmontese enterprises. My activities encompassed the assessment of enterprise capabilities to align ICTs with the organizational business mission and strategic objectives, figuring out various barriers which requested Government intervention orientated to adopt appropriate policies.
