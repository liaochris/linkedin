Ola Rynge's Summary

Through The Rynge Group I work with funding and starting up new ventures, where I have as goal to start at least one idea every two years where I am part of the management team. While focusing on running one venture, I invest and work strategically with other portfolio companies through the board of directors.

I am currently involved in Relevant Relations Layer as founder and CEO and at the same time I am working with helping inventors to realize their ideas as a consultant for the governmental companies ALMI Företagspartner and Business Region Göteborg. 

My experience in the start-up area includes companies in medical devices and food stuff processing, but lately my focus is on e-commerce and mobile marketing.

Please let me know how I can help you or if you would like to pitch an idea for us to work on together. Note that I will only get involved in projects where I will be shareholder and that I believe I can add a lot to the equation.
