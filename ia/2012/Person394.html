David Kingham's Summary

After 12 years in the Architectural field I decided to follow my true passion of Photography. Working directly with Interior Designers in this time has given me unique insights to their needs and a greater understanding of how fine art nature photography can aid healing of patients in healthcare settings with evidence based design.

I have hundreds of pieces installed in healthcare facilities around the country with extraordinarily pleased clientele.

I sell unframed archival prints on paper and canvas.

Specialties

Art for hospitals, Evidence-based Design, Evidence-based Art, Nature Art for Healthcare, Positive distraction, Stress reduction, Canvas prints, Archival Fine Art Paper prints, Flower Pictures, Tree Pictures, Landscapes, Panoramas, Native Plants, Wildflowers
