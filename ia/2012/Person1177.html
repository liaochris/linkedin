Mission statement:
Get Europe on the tech map as the largest economy of the world. if you are an European you will be aware that Europe is technologically very capable, but hardly visible on tech blogs which are dominated by US based companies.
Europe has very capable engineers, an excellent education system, the best digital infrastructure in the world and a long tradition of outstanding creativity, but all of this goes mostly unnoticed.
