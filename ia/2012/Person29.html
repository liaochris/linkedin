Ahmed Zeeshan's Summary

Diversity has been at the core of my academic and career development over the years. Beginning from my primary school in Pakistan, I learned quickly that people should have a broad mix of skills. As such I've always endeavoured to not limit myself in anyway.

Therefore, even though I am a certified Electrical and Computer Engineer, I've decided to combine Project Management with my core engineering expertise to create a more interesting combination. Currently I am employed as a consulting Web Projects Manager at Via Röper Communications in Bremen, Germany. My main client is Jacobs University. The job has allowed me to gain substantial experience in project management. I've worked with some of the best in the market which has been an invaluable learning experience.

My engineering and scientific academic background led me to write two research papers during my undergraduate degree. The first one examined efficient electrical distribution systems that would eventually lead to net zero-energy residential buildings for colleges and universities. The second one provided a detailed comparative analysis of different photovoltaic modules to explore simple cost-effective solar energy solutions for the modern day home.

I understand that today the world wide web in general and social networks in particular are the most effective mediums of communication. Hence, a shift in focus to social media as a communications platform could bring in immediate and significant rewards for an organization.

Core skills and specialities:
Web design and development, project management, team collaborations, event planning, community and social media, IT, electronics and photovoltaics, photography and video editing. Highly proficient in the drupal content management system, sql, php, html, css, android, iOS, microsoft windows and office, adobe creative suite.
