Lizzy Nephew's Summary

Lizzy Nephew provides online marketing expertise, strategy development and campaign implementation for clients. She finds opportunities for brands to engage with consumers, provide online customer service and build community loyalty.

As a lover of challenges and adventures, Lizzy finds excitement in the fast paced nature of digital marketing and emerging media. She enjoys researching and analyzing trends that assist her clients in reaching their online target audience.

Lizzy graduated cum laude with a B.A.J. from The University of Georgia's Grady College of Journalism and Mass Communication with a New Media Certificate and an English Minor. In her free time, Lizzy enjoys hiking outdoors, attending rock concerts, trying to be artistic and reading any book that meets her eyes.

Specialties

Digital Integration, Social Media, Online Strategy, Analytics, Blogger Outreach, Influencer Outreach, Branding, Metrics, New Media, Community Moderation, Research, Graphic Design, Writing, Editing and Layout, Content Management, Campaign Management
