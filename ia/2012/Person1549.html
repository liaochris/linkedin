Ron Cornell's Experience

Director, Membership Development & Marketing

Center for Health Value Innovation

Health, Wellness and Fitness industry

January 2011 – August 2011 (8 months)

The Center for Health Value Innovation is a non-profit organization chartered as an educational 501c3, with the social mission to educate the market on improved health quality through the use of value-based benefit designs. The Center functions as a forum for analysis and exchange of innovation concepts that accelerate the value of health investments by plan sponsors and service providers. Because the basis of a value-based benefit design is the focus on outcomes and the predictable cost trend reduction, the value-based benefit design is one of the foundational tools of the Center. Our membership is comprised of all of the stakeholders in the health value supply chain, from employers and plan sponsors, to physicians and hospital systems, to health plans, brokers, consultants, technology companies, pharmaceutical and device manufacturers, academia and policy makers. It is a collaborative for developing tools, strategies and recommendations that achieve greater value from benefit design, sharing the findings and challenges of improving health quality. Our goal is to accelerate the adoption of benefit strategies that improve the health of the people, the organizations, and the communities of America.
