Owen Marcus' Summary

Men aren’t broken. We’re not bad. We were just never taught what we really needed to learn. Sure, we can change the oil in a car, run a business, manage our investments, but can we truly connect with our wives? Our children? Our friends? Ourselves? We’re doing the best we can with the limited real-life lessons we got. No one taught us how to manage our own emotions, let alone deal with other people’s.

With all the economic and socio-political shifts in our world, men of all ages are searching for new answers. Men are re-discovering the emptiness of material success. Men want to move past “good enough” to Remarkable.

The old men’s movement rode the coattails of the women’s movement. That movement is dead. It did not work for one simple reason: men and women are different, and need different tools to get where they want to go.

So now people are looking for what’s next. The soon to be release book: Grow Up, Guys: Your 9-Step Path to Releasing the Remarkable Man Inside You - gives each man a playbook to teach him exactly how to get where he wants to be in his life—in his relationship with his partner, his children, his friends, his parents, and most importantly, his relationship to himself. Men learn how to lead and coach themselves.

We live in a time where so much of our interaction is virtual, fast and not personal – we need real interactions. Through developing a micro-community you give yourself what our ancestors had in their tribes, friends that support you beyond your limitations. I teach men and women how to create groups that are intimate communities of sponsorship.
