Rob Sheffield's Summary

With over 11 years experience working within the digital, creative services and advertising industry I have built and managed permanent, contract and executive search recruitment teams covering a wide range UK and International clients.

These have ranged from above the line, Integrated and Digital Agencies through to Corporate/FTSE 100 and Government organisations. My understanding of the digital space and channels clients and consumers are using to create and develop digital solutions is coupled with an in-depth knowledge of digital marketing, brand, delivery and the integration of multi-channel agency offerings has enabled me to build high performing teams delivering a range of consultancy based services from recruitment solutions, fixed cost project work, change management, requirements analysis and advising a wide range of clients on budget control for recruitment on a local and global level.

I have always worked closely with clients to turn their current objectives into creative, measurable online solutions whether as part of employment, contract campaigns and stand-alone projects. This has involved managing outsourcing agreements and PSL requirements for a global and UK based corporations. I have worked with project teams to ensure the right solution is achieved for the clients on budget, on time and on brief.

This has ranged from preparing and managing specialist teams of recruiters, costing, margin and quality control throughout a range of digital projects. I believe that any service or work we participate in should maximise quality and profitability for all of our clients.

Specialties

Freelance, contract, permanent recruitment of digital specialists ranging from CEO’s, Technical directors, Creative directors, Operational specialists, project managers, producers, Web Designers, Web Developers planners, user experience professionals, content managers, account managers, creative’s, & front end developers. Building project teams, PSL and Retention strategies, change management, fixed cost project work, requirements analysis, proposal & bid writing, budgeting, project management.
