Beth Kanter's Summary

Beth has worked in the nonprofit sector for 32 years. She is the 2012-2013 Visiting Scholar for Social Media and Nonprofits for the Packard Foundation. Beth is the co-author of the Networked Nonprofit and author of Beth's Blog: How Nonprofits Can Use Social Media, one of the longest running and most popular blogs for nonprofits. Her next book, "Measuring the Networked Nonprofit, will publish in October, 2012. In 2009, she was named by Fast Company Magazine as one of the most influential women in technology and one of Business Week's "Voices of Innovation for Social Media."


Please see my bio at http://www.bethkanter.org
