Merilee Schillinger's Summary

Drawing from over 20 years experience in the high-tech industry, my career roots are in human resources. I steered my career focus to technical recruiting after discovering a passion for connecting high-tech professionals with great opportunities in an ever-evolving industry.

Five years as an agency recruiter paved the way to becoming an “in house” recruiter for great companies like Unicru, Intel, Sabrix, Tripwire, iovation and now Ensequence. In 10+ years as a recruiter I have filled a diverse variety of high-tech industry positions. In primarily contract recruiting roles I work in house at software companies to fill specialized positions during periods of increased hiring. As a consummate networker I strongly advocate for the increasing use of social networking as a primary tool in recruiting, job seeking and career development.

I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Industrial/Organizational Psychology from Western Washington University.

Specialties

Software Industry, Recruiting Life-Cycle, Technical Recruiting, Interviewing, References, Recruiting Best Practices, Job Based Candidate Assessments, Human Resources, Recent College Graduate Recruiting, Applicant Tracking Systems
