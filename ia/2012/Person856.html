  • The Sedona Conference Commentary On Information Governance: Finding The Hidden ROI In Information Assets

    • The Sedona Conference
    • March 28, 2011
    Authors: Joshua Kubicki

    The purpose of this paper is to provide an approach through which an entity can better identify, calculate and leverage the “hidden” value or return on investment (ROI) of the information it creates. The option value approach emphasizes the importance of recognizing the long-term value of an entity’s information, and provides a method whereby that value can be measured to help justify an investment in information governance schemes.

With each new service acquisition LPOs disrupt the business and profit models of firms.
