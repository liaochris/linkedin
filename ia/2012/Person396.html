David Nykanen's Summary

I am a real estate and business attorney in Southeast Michigan. On the real estate side, I have been focused on assisting developers, owners, landlords, tenants, and municipalities in real estate matters for my entire career. I also have special expertise in property tax appeals. On the business side, I have represented small and mid-size businesses in addressing the legal issues that arise in the founding, growth, operation, and sale of a business.

Specialties

commercial real estate transactions, business transactions, zoning, land use, property taxation and appeals, property acquisition, leasing, development, financing, and commercial litigation.
