John Bradford's Summary

Working with advanced engineering companies in the South West through the AAE iNET, I help them become more innovative & competitive through new partnership projects with Universities and other research organisations.

As Chair of the Bristol & Bath Branch, British Science Association, I'm believe that we should have a society in which people from all walks of life are able to access science, engage with it and feel a sense of ownership about its direction. The regional branch is key to achieving this by connecting research, industry, and society through our programme of events & activities.

I launched jbsh 3 years ago to build on over 10 years experience in working with companies and research institutions on launching new products/services. Principally focusing on identifying market opportunities and business models to exploit new IP. We also deliver research and consultancy reviews on policy frameworks.

I started as a mechanical engineer and have migrated through systems theory (via a brief career as a research academic) in to project focused partnership building. I've stayed within high technology (engineering, electronics, mobile & software) but have a strong interest in social enterprises and the social impact and opportunity provided by science.

I've organised OpenCoffee Bristol for over 2 years, we now have around 200 members and fortnightly meetings.

You can contact me on johnb@jbsh.co.uk or +447739705578

Business case drafting, partnership building, project initiation, PRINCE 2, Project evaluation & review.
