Tim Caravia's Summary

Tim has more than 12 years of experience in customer service and 10 years of solid experience in the field of web & graphic design. Tim has vastly expanded his design abilities and talents over the last five years as an eLearning developer for Teksoft Ventures, an experienced ERP training partner. From Day One at Teksoft Ventures, Tim helped choose the eLearning tools that are still being used today to create eLearning courses and material including Adobe Captivate and Lectora Publishing Suite with assistance from Photoshop, Flash, and HTML. After his first year of course development with Teksoft, Tim assisted in researching the purchase and configuration of a Learning Management Systems (OutStart) for the delivery and tracking of customized eLearning courses for training. As an eLearning developer, Tim now creates the courses and places them into customized and branded LMS sites for our clients. Since 2009, Tim has been installing RWD's uPerform at client sites and using the tool to develop SAP training documents and eLearning courses.

Specialties

Web Design, Graphic Design, Email Marketing, Search Engine Optimization, eLearning Course Development, and Brand Development (Logo, Website, Positioning, Delivery, Differentiate)
