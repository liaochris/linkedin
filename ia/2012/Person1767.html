Tony Manolatos' Summary

An experienced communications strategist with more than a dozen years of experience in news media and media relations, Tony excels at developing, managing and implementing successful communication and public policy plans that promote businesses, non-profits, individuals, positions and educate and improve the lives of San Diegans.

Unique blend of journalism and public policy/political experience shapes the planning of effective and creative strategies, building of public and stakeholder relationships and response to crisis situations.

Tony's media training has benefited numerous leaders and communicators throughout San Diego. An award-winning journalist, Tony is a versatile writer able to quickly distill complex issues into engaging op-eds, speeches and blog posts that people respond to.

Specialties

Strategic Communications - Media relations - Policy Advisor - Government Relations - Project Management - Crisis Communications - Video Production - Executive Media Training - Speech and Op-Ed Writing - Social Media Management - Campaigns - Messaging - Branding - Research - Editing - Web Development
