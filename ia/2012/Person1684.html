Steve Richards' Summary

With ten years of Online Marketing Experience, I have a passion for Business & Social Media,

I started freelance internet marketing in 2003 via affiliate programs, forums & SEO, I quickly moved into corporate internet marketing working at a local web design company Planned Sites responsible for SEO & link building.

2007 went to work with Rippleffect.com a leading UK digital marketing agency clients include NHS, Peel Group and Everton Football Club... Worked on finding Everton Football Club relevant demographic businesses to advertise with on a CPM, CPC or sponsorship bases, Interaction & management with clients such as Sony, Coca Cola, Gillette, Tesco, Marks & Spencer & The RAC etc…

From there I went onto a Yahoo backed business called White Box now known as Affilyon after I signed up tens of thousands of users to their "paid to search program" via social media and online marketing. (see more about Affilyon & the great work they do below)

In 2010 I created my own social media consultancy SRI Int with a focus on branding & developing businesses on Twitter & Facebook, locally & nationally I am involved in many Twitter & Facebook accounts, I get to know products & provide interested & engaging followers and fans, I usually run accounts for approx. 16 – 26 weeks, but some I’ve been running for 3 years. Social Media for business is and should be a time consuming process as very rarely in life do you buy or get to really know someone first time around, you build a friendship or working a partnership, I take my time with businesses making sure I place them in the right demographic, after having my own personal twitter account for 4 years I've made great friends that have turned into great business & consumer contacts via numerous networks & Social Media accounts I both manage & engage with.

Many thanks for viewing my profile, if I can help or advise you or your business with Social Media please get in touch on linkedin or Twitter - https://twitter.com/SteveRichardsUK
