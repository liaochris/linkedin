Christina Trapolino's Summary

My passion for online interaction has been nearly lifelong; as a teenager, I spent most of my time connecting with others online and building digital communities. In fact, before even reaching my twenties, I was responsible for leading a digital community of over 100,000 users. What I learned from that experience is that communities are powerful -- and when they're connected via mouse and keyboard, there is no limit to their passion or their collaborative spirit.

Today, I spend most of my free time trying to help consumers understand and leverage the power of their own networks. I believe that social media empowers us to directly influence brand behavior -- I also believe it empowers us to grow as people.
