Connor Murphy's Summary

Connor is the CEO and Founder of Datahug.

Datahug unlocks, measures and manages the hidden network of 'who knows who' within every organisation. Automatically. In 2 minutes.

He is an experienced product lead, software developer and management consultant. He is passionate about customer centric design, big data, automation and making life easier for professionals. He has worked with several Fortune 500 companies, venture start-ups and government organisations.

Connor's pre-Datahug career highlights include;

(Copenhagen, Denmark) Led the agile development of an online banking application system for Denmark's leading commercial bank. His team pushed the product to live within 100 days, beating previous estimates by 9 months.

(Kingston, JAMAICA) Led the ICT strategy review for Country's Fire Brigade Service. Connor also worked with the Ministry of Education of their e-learning strategy.

(Dubai) First team member on the ground to design and build a KM solution for a leading Dubai World Real Estate Company

(Washington DC, USA) Connor was responsible for the data storage & communications network architecture of a major Smart Grid project.

(London, England) Designed and ran usability testing for a leading Global Airline. Lead architect and developer on Europe's largest E-Learning project. He built a predictive model on traffic flows and user journey times for the Highways Authority.

(Dublin, Ireland) Developed, tested and ran the regression testing harness for Sun Microsystems Solaris 8 and 9 releases.

(NY City, USA) Worked with the Chief Information Security Office of a 30,000 person organisation to track and manage IT Security (i.e. ISO 27001 and PCI). Advised a leading Financial Services company on the design and roll-out of a best in class e-learning platform to 200,000 end users.

Connor holds a first class honors (1H) degree in Computer Science and is originally from Cork, Ireland.
