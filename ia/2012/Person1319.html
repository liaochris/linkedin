Alan Gray's Summary

► ► ► I'd be happy to connect with you ◄ ◄ ◄

NewsBlaze is an independent media news source. We publish US and world news and press releases to an increasing audience.

The mainstream media puts the same color lipstick on a new pig every night. If you give us the same 15 minutes you will get a range of stories that tell you what is going on in the world, that we think you might care about.

NewsBlaze delivers news from 200+ writers and 3 editors, to our readers. You can add facebook comments and share your news using the social media buttons.

As a business person, I know you have a great story to tell. Please connect to me and send me your story (not a sales pitch) and we will check, edit and publish it.

I willingly accept all feedback and tips.
