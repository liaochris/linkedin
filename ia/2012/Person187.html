bkmacdaddy designs is a full-service web & graphic design company founded in 1998. Specializing in custom web and print media, SEO, social media marketing & integration and more, President and founder Brian K. McDaniel (the ‘BKM’ in ‘bkmacdaddy’) provides a hands-on, personal approach to customer satisfaction. A lifelong student and teacher of numerous practices, Brian loves to capture a person’s or organization’s spirit and vision, then translate them into identity packages, websites, and more. bkmacdaddy designs challenges the status quo in all areas of media and strives to bring new ideas and technologies into the most unlikely but strategically fruitful instances.

Some - but not all - of the services that bkmacdaddy designs provides include:

* Custom Joomla, WordPress, Magento & other CMS themes & templates
* Completely custom websites built from the ground up
* Logo design & corporate identity packages
* Print graphics
* Social Media Management
* XHTML, CSS, JavaScript, MooTools, PHP, mySQL and more!
