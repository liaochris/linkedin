Kevin Bradshaw's Summary

Kevin has an exceptional record of product innovation with start-up and high-growth digital media and technology ventures, with a focus on direct to consumer mobile/social services.

Kevin's early ventures in large part created the mobile entertainment industry.

Over the last decade, across a series of successful ventures, Kevin has driven leading-edge technology platform and product development, as well as creating original and new format content for some of the world’s most successful IP franchises, including EA Sports FIFA Soccer™, Star Trek™ and many others.

■ Lead delivery of a series of innovative "rich social" media services including buzzd.com, zendit.com and beerdog.com.

■ Captured market leadership with launch of playphone.com service. Drove the development of 10,000+ unique content offerings supported by 500+ wireless devices to assure PlayPhone’s place as one of the largest global distributors of mobile games, ringtones, wallpaper, videos, and other personalized content.

■ Founded and piloted i-play (Digital Bridges), the first pure-play mobile game publisher, as CEO. Designed and executed business and product development strategy, operations, and business model. Recruited tier one executives and Vice Presidents, the majority of which remained with the company until acquisition.
