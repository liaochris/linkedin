Denise Graveline's Summary

I call my company don't get caught because it's a framework for communicating effectively. If you're prepared, knowledgeable, trained and ready, you won't get caught by surprise, a clever interviewer or an irate audience member. People get caught when they're communicating every day--mainly because they haven't taken the time to come up with a plan and prepare for what they're going to say and how they're going to interact with an audience, whether that audience is live and in front of them or scattered and on social networks.

You're in the right place if you need strategies for your communications or social media efforts or the training to put them in action, including training for public speaking, media interviews or social media skills. I also facilitate staff and board retreats, primarily on communications issues. I help many clients with media relations strategies or training in effective pitching and building relationships with reporters.

My clients include major nonprofits, such as the American Association for the Advancement of Science; corporations, such as Wrigley; conferences, such as TEDMED; government agencies, such as the U.S. Department of Homeland Security and the Centers for Disease Control; and more.

My approach is all about preparing to communicate clearly and well. I've been attracted to complex issues and complex organizations, and while I don't work exclusively with scientists, I have worked with researchers in every discipline--an unusual communications skill that I put to use in training workshops on communicating science to public audiences.

I'm passionate about making art and making music: I exhibit my assemblages and collages at Washington, DC's Touchstone Gallery, and I'm learning guitar on a marvelous Martin D-18GE. I work with clients in many locations, which helps fuel my yen to travel, and I'm delighted that there's a village in France, Gravelines, where I am introduced as Mademoiselle Graveline-sans-s.

Specialties

Communications strategies, social media strategies, speaker training, presentation training, media training. Facilitation for communications retreats. Training scientists in public communications skills.
