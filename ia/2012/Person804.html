Jon Russell's Summary

I am Asia editor at global technology news site The Next Web, having started in October 2011.

My role at TNW saw me return to writing full-time after a stint as a social media consultant for a multinational, travel technology company.

My background is varied and I have worked as a journalist - on staff and freelance - and blogger having previously held marketing and PR consultant roles both agency-side and in-house in Asia and in the UK.

Writing, and blogging in particular, has been a big passion of mine since I moved to Thailand in 2008. I avidly follow technology, internet and digital news in Asia: having blogged for Asian Correspondent and ZDNet Asia on these topics.

I began my working life in PR and marketing in London before expatriating. My time in the UK saw me work in the technology sector, specialising in mobile technology, the internet and digital marketing.

Specialties

writing, blogging, journalism, PR, social media, digital marketing, mobile, technology, internet, Asia, Thailand, Southeast Asia
