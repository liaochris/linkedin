Jerry Kinard, MS's Summary

Jerry Kinard is the Network Leader and founder of Growth Ministries an urban house church network whose mission is to help advance the Gospel of the Kingdom by making and multiplying disciples house to house. He is also a Clinical Research Professional who is particularly interested in therapeutic areas that seek to improve health disparities within the African-American community. He holds a Master of Science (MS) degree in Clinical Research Administration from Walden University and is currently pursuing a Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in Integrative Medicine from Kingdom College of Natural Health and a Master of Divinity (M.Div) in Biblical Counseling from The North American Reformed Seminary.
