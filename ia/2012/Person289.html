Chris Winfield's Summary

I am the Co-Founder and Chief Marketing Officer of BlueGlass Interactive, Inc. BlueGlass is an Internet marketing company that creates unmatched software and provides extraordinary results for our clients.

At BlueGlass I am responsible for setting the overall direction of and leading the marketing efforts for the company. With the goal of changing the online marketing landscape with scalable services and products, I work hard to streamline the company's processes, services, and production capabilities on a daily basis.

Before co-founding BlueGlass, I was the President of New York based 10e20, one of the leading boutique social media marketing agencies in the world. I have helped many of the world’s largest companies increase traffic, develop natural links, and increase profitability. I have taught global corporations, such as Viacom, NBC Universal, Thomson Reuters, Intuit, HomeAway, and AT&T, to leverage social media in innovative ways.

I have been featured in The Wall Street Journal, The New York Times, USA Today, Investor’s Business Daily, The Boston Globe, Entrepreneur Magazine, and more than 200 other media outlets.

I am a regular speaker at numerous Internet marketing conferences including SES, SMX, Pubcon, DPAC, LegalTech, DM Days, RMC, Elite Retreat, and IBM 2.0.

I graduated from the State University of New York at Albany while spending one semester as a Congressional intern in Washington D.C. and one year in London attending the University at Westminster.
