Jerry Silfwer's Summary

Jerry Silfwer (b. 1979) is the Chief Operations Officer at Whispr Group, a data-driven social media agency with offices in New York and Stockholm. The agency was founded in New York by Joakim Leijon who brought Jerry onboard as the single co-owner in 2010. After a year stationed at Whispr New York, Jerry relocated from Manhattan back to Stockholm in late 2011 to set up Whispr Stockholm.

Besides administrating, recruiting and leading, Jerry’s creating highly visible social media campaigns, interactive websites and applications as well as strategies and digital programs for international companies in fashion, beauty, travel and lifestyle. He also trains the Whispr team in blogger relations, social media strategies and account management.

Prior to Whispr, Jerry was the Head of Social Media and Key Account Manager at Springtime PR, the largest owner-led agency in the Nordics. At this time, the agency gained acclaim as one of the most prominent Nordic PR agencies in social media, combining it with expertise in Public Affairs, Internal Communications, Corporate Communications and Investor Relations. During a period of this time, he was also outsourced part-time as Nordic PR Manager for Adobe.

His past and present client list include H&M, Oriflame, Oracle, Procter & Gamble, Disney, Saab Group, .SE (The Internet Infrastructure Foundation), TeliaSonera and Groupon.

Jerry has Bachelor Degrees in Public Relations and Linguistics from Mid Sweden University and were awarded scholarships by PRECIS (Public Relations Agencies in Sweden) and DIK (Union for Communication Professionals in Sweden).

In 2008, Jerry founded the no. 1 PR community in the Nordics, PR of Sweden and he is the author of Doktor Spinn, a blog on digital PR ranked amongst the top 100 blogs on PR in the world by Invesp.

Jerry was also Communications Advisor for the Swedish Pirate Party throughout the EU election campaign in 2009, which resulted in 5,1% of the votes and two parliament seats for the party.

Specialties

Digital PR, Social Media PR, Issues Management, Change Communication, Media Relations, Blogger Outreach, Community Management.
