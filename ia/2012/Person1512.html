Richard Hoefer's Summary

Though I have been an Information Architect & Usability Designer since 1995, my UX Design skills are rooted in a non-Computer Science past. My interdisciplinary media messaging background of Hollywood TV and writing, Ad & Marketing Design, and Interactive media, were all focused on engaging consumers and holding their attention. I am particularly strong in first-stage conceptualization and structure of the user experience; later in detecting obstacles that interfere with clear decision-making.
