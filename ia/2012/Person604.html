Grant Crowell's Summary

I am a freelance media consultant and digital media strategist. I am considered to be an influencer and trusted source of information around online video solutions for business and professionals, including social video and video SEO strategies. I help companies share their stories and messages, get them seen by the right people, and convert to real-world ROI. What makes me special is I don't just talk about what I recommend to others, I actually do it!

I've been covering SEO and the rest of the online marketing industry since 1996, and the online video/social media space since 2006. Before then, I was a professional talk show radio host and editorial cartoonist out in Hawaii.

I enjoy working with companies, organizations, and individuals that value educating people on community values around the responsible use of new media.

Specialties

• Analyst in multiple online marketing fields, including video, SEO, social media, web usability, news/pr, and e-commerce.

• Building content marketing and online community management strategies along with custom training solutions for small-to-large enterprises.

• Conduct private media training/strategy sessions for corporations, non-profits, and entrepreneurs.

• Editor of professional writers' own content marketing programs.
