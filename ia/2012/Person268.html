Charlie White's Summary

Online editor and journalist with three decades of experience as an Emmy Award-winning television producer, director and video editor

Specialties

Edits and writes about tech, internet, social media and gadget-related topics, making them accessible and entertaining. Specializes in topics that attract tremendous traffic numbers.

Thousands of articles published on high-traffic websites and respected magazines. Wrote Bloggers Boot Camp, a book published by Focal Press in 2011.

Other specialties: live multicamera TV directing, music videos, quick turnaround video editing, voice-over performance and promotion/commercial video creation.
