Sandra Frank, Ed.D., RD, LDN's Summary

Registered Dietitian and Licensed Nutritionist with experience in journalism, education, web design, recipe modifications and analysis, menu planning and grant writing.

Goals.
1. To Editors, Cookbook Writers, Recipe Designers and Publishers: I am an experienced researcher in nutrition analysis with over 20 years experience working with international, national and local publications.
2. Provide current news, events and resources in Nutrition, Food, Health, Food Recalls, Safety, Disability Rights and Environmental Issues.
3. Encourage awareness and inspire ideas for Journalists, Educators, Consumers and Health Professionals.
4. Provide employment opportunities to idividuals with special needs, such as Cerebral Palsy, Autism, Down syndrome, Muscular Dystrophy and other disabilities.

Dietitians-Online Goals:
Dietitians-Online was created to acknowledge the contributions of the Dietitian (RD and APD) on the Internet.
1. Provide and network with the media, consumer, health professional and educator reliable on-line food and nutrition articles and materials created by the online dietitians.
2. Dietitians-Online submits articles with national or worldwide appeal to major networks and government health agencies in the USA and worldwide. (All submissions are subject to approval. You will receive full acknowledgement with your submission.)
3. Construct an on-line resource of Dietitians' Blogs, News and Services.
4. To assist the Dietitian in planning and creating an on-line presence. Web and Blog Design services are available.

Specialties

Nutrition Analysis; Web and Blog Designs; Consumerism and Education in the following areas: nutrition, food, health, weight control, food recalls, food safety, food savings, disability rights and environmental issues.

Sandra Frank, Ed.D., RD, LDN's Experience

Editor

Dietitians On-line

January 2005 – Present (7 years 8 months)

Dietitians-Online is a website dedicated to acknowledging the online contributions of the Registered Dietitian (RD) and the Accredited Practising Dietitian (APD, Australia) by highlighting blogs, websites and social media pages written by dietitians.

The website includes: Online Dietitian Database; Blogs; Daily News Updates from the American Dietetic Association and Dietitians; Featured Dietitians; Quotes and Books by Dietitians; Links to State Dietetic Associations; Dietetic Practice Groups; Health & Medical Associations; Government Agencies; Trade Associations & Corporations; CPEU Providers and Wellness News.

Our goal is to highlight the work of the dietitians, in order to encourage good nutrition and a healthy lifestyle.
