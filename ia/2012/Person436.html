Dirk Puehl's Summary

Monster Consultancy Services

Sales, concept development and implementation of complex HR marketing and branding projects on the WWW - focus on Monster with strong targeting and remarkable, SEO landing pages with target group specific, tailor-made content.

Concept and implementation of dedicated micro pages and career micro sites to get a holistic view on a company - designed with taking the viewpoint of potential candidates - engineering, IT, finance, sales, marketing, etc. bearing in mind their professional expectations (e.g. what makes engineers proud to work there, what is it like to work in IT, what state-of-the-Art software is in use, why are projects cutting-edge, etc.) and allowing for regional and age-specific demands.

Strong Web 2.0 approach, e.g. establishing an employer brand on Facebook and counseling companies on the how-to's in the Social Media - from blogs to Xing and linked-in.

Media planning on the internet with display ads as well as Google Adwords / Adsense.

Team Manager of 3 sales, 2 project consultants and web creatives and copywriters, leading project teams, additional agencies and other service providers.
