Damien Patton's Summary

As a four-time entrepreneur, Damien has built his career on making ideas a reality. Damien has spent the last year building Banjo, a leading social discovery mobile service where users can discover and connect with the people and events around the corner or around the world. Banjo is known for being one of the most sophisticated and powerful location based technologies.

Banjo is one of the fastest growing companies in the location space with over 3 million users in their first 17 months. The company has been Apple’s App of the Week in more than 70 countries, and has been the featured app on Google Play three times. In 2012, Google named Banjo a top mobile developer. Banjo was awarded the 2011 Ruby Innovator Award by Matz, the creator of Ruby, as well as featured as a Heroku success story for Banjo's work in Ruby on Rails and MongoDB. With Banjo's fast growth and innovative technology, it has been recognized and used often by the biggest names in news: Fox, CNN, HLN and Univision.

Damien's passion for problem solving is at the hallmark of everything he does. He has put his talents to use in some vastly varied disciplines. He served in Desert Storm, was the Chief Mechanic of the Lowe’s racing team in NASCAR and even dusted for prints and collected DNA as a crime scene investigator while working full time in the role of COO for a technology company.

Banjo is growing and looking for talented and passionate individuals to join the team. To discuss career opportunities, contact Damien directly at damien@teambanjo.com.
