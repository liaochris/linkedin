Doreen Rainey's Summary

With a mantra of "Get RADICAL", Doreen LOVES to shake things up - daring her clients to tap into their gifts, talents and purpose to maximize this opportunity called life. As a speaker, life coach and management consultant, her goal is not to make friends, but to urge my clients to have the guts to get out of their own way to get the results they want - professionally and personally.

Described as optimistic, motivational, inspirational and insightful, Doreen challenges individuals to "Get Radical" by providing tools, tips and information to her clients to achieve self-defined success.

With her no-nonsense style, Doreen provides practical information in a manner that supports action. Her message of self-defined success challenges people to tap into their gifts and talents – ultimately moving them into their purpose and passion.

With over 17 years of corporate HR experience, she also lends her expertise in performance management, effective communication skills, and managing conflict to corporations and organizations through keynotes, training and workshops.

Doreen is also an adjunct professor. She received her undergraduate degree from Spelman College and her M.S. in Conflict Negotiation and Conflict Management from the University of Baltimore.
