Stephanie L. Schierholz's Summary

An award-winning communicator, Stephanie brings 10 years’ experience leading strategic communications, media relations, and public outreach efforts to her role as Raytheon's first social media manager. She has been instrumental in creating and leading successful Tweetup events that take online engagement with fans and followers to the next level through behind-the-scenes, in-person experiences. Stephanie is recognized as a leader in effective use of social media.

Stephanie's experience includes coordinating and implementing strategic communications and media plans; writing and editing; on-camera interviews and news conferences; and speechwriting for executives.
