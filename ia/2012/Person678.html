Jack C Crawford's Experience

Sr Director, Planning, Strategy and eCompliance

Allergan

Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; AGN; Pharmaceuticals industry

September 2007 – Present (5 years)

Member of the Allergan Business Practice Management organization. Focus is on compliance of interactions with Healthcare Practitioners (HCPs) and Healthcare Institutions (HCIs). Our team is responsible for reporting of aggregate spend for HCPs and HCIs across all business areas.

Lean Six Sigma Black Belt supporting business process transformation and improvement efforts in various areas at Allergan.

Prior to this role, was the project manager for a cross functional team which designed and developed business process management (BPM) automation for healthcare compliance (Project BEACON). This effort was a multi-phased project to automate critical business processes and provide reporting of healthcare practitioner (HCP) aggregate spend.

Business liaison for sales and marketing divisions in North America. Managed technology portfolio for sales force automation (SFA), supply chain logistics and customer relationship management (CRM). Also responsible for projects supporting payor contracting and government pricing, reimbursement services, customer and consumer marketing, medical affairs and global marketing. Initiated global salesforce automation strategy, consolidating eight providers into two strategic partners.

Senior Manager

Gilead Sciences

Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; GILD; Biotechnology industry

October 2006 – September 2007 (1 year)

Led team responsible for regulatory compliance of IT infrastructure, regulatory submission management, pharmacovigilence/safety and sales force customer relationship management (SFA/CRM) systems. Managed Gilead's first qualification of server virtualization in full compliance with GAMP methodology. Validation manager for pharmaceutical product docket system. Validation lead for drug safety data warehouse to support advanced analytics and signal detection. Principal author for new custom software development standards and SOPs. Participated in multiple SOX, regulatory and security audits.

Founder and President

Managed Ventures LLC

2000 – 2006 (6 years)

Founded Managed Ventures, with a mission to provide custom software applications to firms and research organizations engaged in drug discovery. Implemented research information systems for molecule registration and library management, combinatorial plating operations, assay data management for high throughput screening and structure activity visualization. Provided solutions to pharmaceutical and biotechnology firms in North America, Japan and Europe.

Chief Technology Officer

Xdrive
