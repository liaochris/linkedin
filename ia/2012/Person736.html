Jared Cloud's Summary

I am an attorney, a litigator with a practice focused on fiduciary litigation: trust and estate litigation and family business disputes. I also have experience in corporate, commercial, product liability, media defense, copyright and trademark litigation.

Specialties

Trust litigation, estate litigation, and family business disputes: I have represented fiduciaries and beneficiaries working to resolve their disputes without formal litigation (including through mediation) as well as in litigation through trial. Typical matters include claims of breach of fiduciary duty, will and trust construction and reformation, contested guardianships, and claims against probate estates, and may involve multiple jurisdictions in the U.S. and internationally.
