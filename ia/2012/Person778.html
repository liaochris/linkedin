jerome davis' Experience

Filmmaker

Lord Of The Fly Films

February 2001 – Present (11 years 11 months) Los Angeles

Lord of the Fly is an independant media out let, focused on breaking new creative ground.

Jerome Davis is legend among his peers. Starting in the 1990's as wanted graffiti artist, he has built a network that spans the globe. His work in music videos is both hard edged like broken glass on the pavement, but with a beauty in the composition that rivals the hellish visions of Bosch or Dali. Having worked in guerilla filmmaking for countless artists much to chargrin of their labels at times. He directed "Raw Heat" video for Bronx legend Percee P, a banned promo for Freestyle Fellowship, "Casualty" for new Stones Throw signatory Dave Dub.

His style comes from a child hood running the streets, in foster homes and juvenile facilities. There is a "Beautiful Chaos" a famed artist Eugene Rodriguez once put it. His art work has appeared in Ruth Tunstall Grants works on foster youth. He has created commsioned murals for Bill Wilson Center, Silicon Valley Debug and graphic design for several "now defunct" bay area punk bands.
