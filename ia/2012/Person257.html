Catherine Hall's Experience

Owner

Catherine Hall Studios

January 2007 – Present (5 years 7 months)

Though my mother isn’t a photographer, she was my first photography teacher. When I was growing up, people always told me how comfortable they felt around her. I have always admired her anticipation of subtle moments and personal interactions. It’s instinctive to her; she knows what is important to people.
Over the years, I learned this empathy from her. It soon became my most important photographic skill.

I fell in love with photography at sixteen years old, and have used this incredible medium as a way to observe the world. Photography has provided me with opportunities to meet new people, experience different cultures, and explore my surroundings. I enter all environments without judgement and cherish the bond that is created with the persons I photograph. I feel incredibly fortunate that my subjects trust me enough to be vulnerable and invite me into their world in an honest way.

I respect my artistic vision and always aim to document each subject’s hidden beauty, complexities, and uniqueness. I relish in taking personal and photographic risks to create images saturated with raw emotion within interesting environments.
