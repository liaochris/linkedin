Gwyn Jones' Summary

VistaPrint is a billion dollar company with over 25 million e-commerce customers, co-founded by four INSEAD alumni. A case study in MBA startups, it is one of the most successful start-ups from a top European business school. I also co-founded Serif, a leading software developer of low-cost desktop publishing and graphics applications. Over 20 successful years, Serif has created an award-winning range of products, including the market-leading PagePlus, with millions of users around the world. I have over 20 years experience as a senior (C-level or VP) entrepreneurial executive, and as a company director in both private and public companies.

Specialties

Interim Manager, Mentor/Advisor to start-ups, Board of Directors, "Freemium" model & marketing, fund-raising, Speaker on Entrepreneurship, Freemium, Product Development
