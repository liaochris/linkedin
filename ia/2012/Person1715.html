Ted Rubin's Summary

I am a marketing, business development and sales professional, and have been building, turning around and growing companies for many years.

I have a deep online background beginning in 1997 working with Seth Godin at Yoyodyne, which was acquired in Q4 1998 by Yahoo! More recently I was the CMO of e.l.f. Cosmetics, EyesLipsFace.com where I built an award winning social media presence. In December 2009 I became a member of the OpenSky (OpenSky.com), Board of Advisors, and as Chief Social Marketing Officer, from 2010-2011, took an active day-to-day role building their social media presence, engaging and interacting with the blogging community, and working with other worthwhile potential partners in the media and publishing communities. In addition was a member of the Executive team and an integral part of the strategic shift/pivot OpenSky made in their social commerce strategy in 2011.

On May 1st, 2011 I announced leaving OpenSky and accepting the position of Chief Social Marketing Officer at Collective Bias, a company I worked closely with for two years since meeting the Founder, John Andrews, through the blogging community and whose Advisory Board I joined a few in January 2011.

I am on on the Advisory Boards of Blue Calypso, Collective Bias, OpenSky, SheSpeaks, TapTank, Zuberance, and Crowdsourcing Week.

I am a native New Yorker and a graduate of Cornell University. I am divorced, have two teenage daughters, and live in Long Island.

Specialties

I am a leading social marketing strategist & in March 2009 started using and evangelizing the term ROR (hashtag #RonR), Return on Relationship™.

Many people in the social media world know me for my enthusiastic, energetic & undeniably personal connection to people. I am the most followed CMO on Twitter. ROR is the basis of my philosophy…It’s All About Relationships!

I understand team dynamics as well as bringing a sense of urgency to the sales/marketing process & the organization as a whole.
