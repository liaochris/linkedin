After taking the position as System Manager the challenges included managing an outside plant build-out, headend build-out (including contract negotiations with programmers and broadcasters), community relations (governmental entities and general public) and the design and management of the high-speed product/service (in addition to the typical network engineering required, designed and built a provisioning and subscriber management system from scratch as budgetary constraints prohibited the purchase of an off-the-shelf solution).

As System Manager the responsibilities also included supervising all employees and contractors, which included the management a true crisis--a near-fatal construction accident.
