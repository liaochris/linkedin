Michael Taylor's Summary

Founder of Star Talent Inc., a boutique entertainment production company representing the event industry's premier roster of customized Music Services, Dance Bands, Classical Ensembles, DJs and Celebrity Headline performers for events throughout the NY Metro area.

“I have spent my professional career listening carefully to corporate event planners, hotel catering directors and brides & grooms, providing each with the finest level of musical talent. Industry trend setting and innovative performances that are always produced with complete integrity and an unsurpassed level of customization for each client’s musical style. The way I see it, event production is complex enough. Entertainment buyers should have a reliable and uncomplicated relationship with their music services firm.”

Over the past 24 years, created and produced thousands of turn-key entertainment performances nationwide for major corporate, private and non-profit organizations including 10 New Year's Eve Galas at The Waldorf-Astoria, Performances for Three US Presidents, G. W. Bush Family Event at The White House, Microsoft, The Walt Disney Company, Coca-Cola, Greenwich Capital, Pfizer, The World Economic Forum, The Belmont Stakes, American Cancer Society, NASCAR and other Fortune 500 Firms, Sporting Events, Charitable Organizations and private individuals.

Extensive experience booking and producing Celebrity Performers for Corporate events. Recent productions have included performances by Tony Bennett, Sheryl Crow, Sting, Natalie Cole, Rihanna, Lionel Richie, Frankie Valli, Earth, Wind & Fire, The Doobie Bros., Dana Carvey, Bon Jovi, KC & The Sunshine Band, Ashford & Simpson, Taylor Dayne, Neil Sedaka, Paul Anka, Frank Sinatra Jr, Jewel, Blues Traveler, Jay Leno, Robin Williams, Darrell Hammond across a variety of industries including Pharmaceutical, Banking, Jewelry, Law, Trade Associations, and Charity Galas.

Recently appointed the musical director of the Trump Soho Hotel in New York City.

Specialties

Also a professionally trained Opera and Symphony Conductor who studies privately with Maestro Vincent La Selva of the New York Grand Opera and at The Juilliard School. Regularly attends Master Classes in Conducting given by notable conductors from Rome, St. Petersburg and London Music Conservatories.
