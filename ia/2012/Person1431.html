Peter Elst's Summary

Peter Elst is a certified Flash instructor, Adobe Community Professional and manager of the Pondicherry Adobe User Group.

As a freelance Flash platform consultant and respected member of the online community, Peter has spoken at various international industry events and published his work in leading journals. Most recently Peter was the lead author of Object-Oriented ActionScript 3.0 (Friends of ED, July 2007)

Speaking engagements include:

- The ActionScript Conference, Singapore
- Flash on the Beach 2008, Brighton - UK
- Scotch on the Rocks, Edinburgh - UK
- 2M08, Kortrijk - Belgium
- on AIR tour Europe - Amsterdam, Brussels
- FITC 2008, Amsterdam - the Netherlands
- Adobe MAX Barcelona 2007 - Spain
- Adobe MAX Chicago 2007 - USA
- Flashforward 2007 Boston - USA
- MM07, Kortrijk - Belgium
- Flashconference.de, Stuttgart - Germany
- Flash on the Beach 2006, Brighton - UK
- webDU 2006, Sydney - Australia
