Paul Simbeck-Hampson's Summary

After finishing post graduate studies I set up my first company delivering 1-1 and small group training for senior management on the broad topic of ICT. After some years I successfully sold the company and headed East to explore opportunities in SE Asia.

A change of career focus ensued and I became a PADI scuba diving instructor and have now certified over 400 divers. I also managed a number of dive schools in both Thailand and the Philippines; this blog post captures the day I dived with a whale shark - a very special day! http://bit.ly/awesome_day.

After some years I changed continents and returned to Europe. I married the girl of my dreams and we set up our family home in Bavaria. For near-on ten years I helped my father in law with his rather unique photography business and as a result learnt fluent German and travelled frequently.

During the last few years I've heavily focused on my core passion, learning and technologies, and I'm fortunate to collaborate with some of the worlds' great minds on topics like enterprise collaboration, social business, organisational learning, knowledge management, collective intelligence and cultural transformation.

I'm currently providing 'social business' consultancy to UNIT4 Business Software as they 'Embrace Change'. I also work closely with my brother who specialises in design, marketing communications and brand management. I share what I've learnt and the insights I've gleamed, and those of others, via my blog and on social media channels.

Take a moment to review my profile, see the recommendations from those I've worked and collaborated with, feel free to email me questions. I'm always happy to take advantage of serendipitous encounters whether it be to discuss consultancy projects, partnerships, opportunities or just to drink tea and talk whale sharks and sunken ship wrecks!

Thanks for taking the time to read this short summary, hope to connect with you in the not so distant future...
