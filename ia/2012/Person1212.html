Michael Meehan's Summary

Michael is a senior technology executive and advisor to startups and investors. Michael has built and managed software businesses in several markets including supply chain, energy performance management technologies, carbon/sustainability reporting systems, application performance management, HR systems, and SaaS technologies. As a serial entrepreneur and the inventor of several technology patents, Michael has founded several software companies and has led teams in both large and small-cap ventures in the USA, Canada, and the UK. In addition to advising state and federal US governments, startups, and investors, he was voted one of the "Top CEOs to Follow" by BusinessWeek magazine, is a frequent guest lecturer in both corporate and academic circles, writes regularly for several publications including Forbes and others, and has made several guest appearances on television to discuss cleantech issues including ABC News and PBS.
