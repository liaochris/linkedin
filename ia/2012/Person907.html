Kenneth Darryl Brown (Ken@eWeb2Sales.com)'s Summary

eWeb 2 Sales & Profits is a B2B sales 2.0 training, profit and business development company that specializes in showing how to leverage the web and technology to increase sales and profitability. Sales 2.0 B2B Sales-Web Experts.

Kenneth was selected as one of the Top 25 Influential Sales Leaders by InsideView this year. His show, “The Passionate Entrepreneur “ was picked as one of the best podcasts for small business by Anita Campbell's Small Business Trends for 2010 and 2011. Ken is a sales expert blogger-contributor for Growth University and Address Two. Ken is called, a “Sales, Web and Profit Evangelist”. He has appeared and published over twenty-five commentaries and articles in Atlanta Journal Constitution, Atlanta Tribune, Business to Business, Catalyst and Emerge Magazine, Creative Loafing, Champion Newspaper, Ezine and Impact Articles.

Specifically, we show how companies can improve their sales & profits by incorporating the web in their sales & profit system, enhance consultative selling skills (how to connect, engage and enhance the client experience), upgrade their web presence, website, social and new media efforts. Also, we show how to create new content to improve web presence, company brand and online reputation..

Since 2006, eWeb 2 Sales has created over 50 B2B sales 2,0 training coaching programs, developed mastermind and networking groups online, launched an online social network for his clients and strategic partners.

We offer a no cost, no commitment complimentary Sales, Web & Profit Jump Start Session for B2B companies that want to improve their sales and profitability.

Since March 2006, over 2,500 people have participated in our online business coaching and networking sessions. eWeb 2 Sales has connected over 4,000 businesses since 2007!

eWeb stands for Entrepreneurship -- Empowerment -- Evolution -- Enlightenment -- Engagement -- Education -- Enhancement and E-Commerce.

Specialties

Sales 2.0 Training, B2B Sales Coaching & Training, B2B Sales Training Programs, Web Business Coaching, Consulting & Training, Social & New Media Coaching, Consulting & Training, Web Content Coaching, Training & Consulting, Business Processing Outsourcing (BPO): Human Capital Development, Human Resources, Technology, Financial and Business Services.
