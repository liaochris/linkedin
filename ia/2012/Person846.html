Joseph A. Santoro's Summary

Joseph A. Santoro > joe@politicojoe.com > 202.730.9885

Over the past decade Joseph A. Santoro has consulted and collaborated on over 60 political, public relations and media campaigns throughout the United States. He is a clear-eyed strategist, forceful creative voice, thorough analyst and disciplined campaigner with wide-ranging public relations message development experience. Beyond Policy, in the private sector this requires a great degree of organization and marketing talent to juggle multiple variables and maintain long, prosperous relationships.

Having worked in progressive politics, Congress, non-profit organizations and foreign governments, Joseph brings a strategic, detailed eye to projects of all stripes; those that present to voters candidates who are real, make issues vivid and bring messages to life. He has created new methods, tools, and applications to promote his clients interests, hone their messages, navigate through the complex channels of Washington to obtain their ultimate objectives. Some of his accounts have included The Sierra Club, The National Education Association, The DNC, The Ohio Campaign for Change, Labor Unions like AFL-CIO, IBEW and ATU, a wide range of officeholders like Senator Mark Begich and President Barack Obama, as well as ballot initiatives and issue platforms varying from healthcare reform, education, pensions, college grants, land conservation, a living wage, biotech, transportation, and immigration reform.
