Brian Wey's Summary

I'm a product and project manager with over 10 years experience building a wide array of large-scale consumer applications. I've worked on everything from consumer finance applications—photo sharing, webmail, social networks, user-generated content, and payments—and have a proven history of successful execution of product strategy and rollout.

I've been successful at execution in large organizations and small—at Yahoo! Finance, I worked on personal finance applications, such as bill pay, credit management, and portfolio and expense tracking. As a project manager for Apple's .Mac group, I did successfully launched .Mac Web Galleries, a photo sharing service, and a redesign of our flagship product, webmail.

After that, I joined the startup world. I served as the sole Product Manager for Cake Financial, where I helped rebuild and rebrand a social network for investing with portfolio management tools. I was Director of Product for Zong, a payments company, where I executed our international product roadmap, as well as our design, user experience, and marketing.

Zong was acquired by PayPal (eBay) in the summer of 2011. I am currently a Senior Manager for Product Management in the PayPal Mobile division, focusing on carrier billing and digital goods.
