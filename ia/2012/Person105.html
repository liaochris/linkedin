Anita Li's Summary

With experience in broadcast, print and online media, Anita's past positions have included: on-air reporter for CTV Ottawa, research assistant for The New Yorker's Beijing bureau, reporter/copy editor for The Globe and Mail and reporter/photographer for The Toronto Star. In addition, Anita has freelanced for various publications across Canada. She currently works at Mashable, the world's largest independent website dedicated to news, information and resources for the connected generation.

Anita holds a Master of Journalism from Carleton University and an Honours Bachelor of Arts in international relations, Asia-Pacific studies and English from Trinity College at the University of Toronto.

With interest in issues of local and global governance, Anita has also served on the board of directors for the Chinese Canadian National Council (Toronto Chapter) and as a member of the G8 Research Group. She is currently the Digital Communications Lead for TEDxToronto 2012.
