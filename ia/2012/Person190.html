Blair Rorani's Summary

Blair helps businesses improve the way they learn, work, think and communicate in the new world of social business.

In his career he's helped clients in the energy, banking and finance, health, motor vehicle and telecommunications industries, a range of government departments and non-profit organisations.

He asks the right questions and is a skilled teacher and communicator. He uses a highly visual, collaborative approach to designing solutions.

His mission is to help businesses use new tools and techniques to improve performance.

Specialties

Performance consulting for social business, game-based / social / mobile learning solutions (and instructional design), collaboration (ways of working and technology platforms), creative problem solving and brainstorming, visual communication and storytelling.
