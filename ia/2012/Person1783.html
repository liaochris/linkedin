Valmay Young's Publications

  • FIBIS Fact File 5 - Getting the best out of the FIBIS Website

    • Families In British India Society
    • September 2010
    Authors: Valmay Young

    FIBIS Fact File No. 5 is the latest in the popular series of handy guides to sources being produced by the Families in British India Society. It is aimed at people who are new to, or unfamiliar with, the FIBIS website and would like to know how to get the best out of its content. The guide also contains several handy tips and tricks as well as advice.
