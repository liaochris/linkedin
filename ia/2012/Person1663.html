Sommer Poquette's Summary

Sommer Poquette is a highly motivated, organized, diverse and educated woman. An entrepreneur that believes with the economy today a person must be diverse in their opportunities and experiences. A mother who is emerged in many projects but remains devoted to her family’s happiness and well-being.

A professional blogger, children’s book author, Internet and affiliate marketer, early childhood advocate, social media consultant and digital strategist specializing in blogger relations, program development and implementation. Poquette has a fascination for metrics and connecting with bloggers on a professional and personal basis. Utilizing her Master’s in education Poquette hones in on connecting with her audience and valuing needs and personal stories. A people person at heart who believes in karma and doing well by others.

Specialties

Poquette’s specialties and experience include blogger relations, training and campaign strategy, metrics and implementation. In addition, blogging for business and social media training for companies small and large. Consultation and public speaking on digital media and blogging with a passion for early childhood advocating and helping those in generational poverty and need, a strong advocate for 0-5 education!
