Nate Oostendorp's Summary

Between being a co-founder for Slashdot and a primary architect for SourceForge.net, I've worked for over a decade developing collaborative online spaces. During this time I have focused on developing online communities used by millions to build and discuss open source software and technology.

I have worked in many different roles as a software engineer, architect, interaction designer and project manager to develop complex online properties from concepts to robust high-capacity community systems.

My 13 years of professional experience combined with a rich educational background in computer science, information science, computer/user interaction, give me a unique set of skills for the creation and management of software tools used for collaboration.

Specialties

Open Source Strategies, Website Scalability and Performance, Software Architecture, Engineering Management, Legacy Project Refactoring, User Interaction Design, Data Analysis, Machine Vision, Online Media Strategy, Technical Writing and Lecture
