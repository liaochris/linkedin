Michelle Mangen's Summary

Virtual Assistant that specializes in Bookkeeping, WordPress maintenance, Aweber and Social Media Marketing.

My first job was at about age 11 picking strawberries. From waitressing to front desk at a hotel, my most recent work experience resulted with six years in upper management at a Property Management / General Contractor company.

I've always had the entrepreneurial spirit and finally decided that if I "didn't strike out on my own now" I never would. Being a Virtual Assistant allows me to assist many business owners, rather than just one as an employee.

If you want ideas, new insights and committed high quality virtual services I encourage you to contact me.

Detail oriented. Highly motivated. Ability to learn and adapt quickly (excellent memory). Ability to understand, utilize various applications with little or no training.
