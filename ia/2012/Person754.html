J.C. Kendall's Summary

My name is J.C. Kendall. I am the CEO of the TekPersona Corporation, where we specialize in helping businesses of all sizes maximize their performance through sound Branding & Marketing Principles, combined with the intelligent usage of Information Technology. You might already be great, but we can make you better!

The rules for a successful business have not changed in a century. We understand at TekPersona, that Social Media is only another business tool, and its about what you do with it that matters.

I consider myself on a mission against Marketing Jargon and Buzzwords. My business is not about impressing you with lingo and conceptual drivel, it's about helping your business succeed.
