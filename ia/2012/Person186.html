Bjørn Toft's Summary

I am an independent sport & exercise medicine practitioner working as a sport performance coach in charge of multiple consulting services within Physioinnovation. The consulting services involves bike fitting & biomechanics, golf coaching & mental training as well as organizing training camps in the alps. The unique virtual coaching platform virtualsportscoaching.com offers sport performance coaching virtually through skype and TrainingPeaks.

Personally I am an ambitious road cyclist (license races) but also a skilled cross country skier (long distance races, Vasaloppet <5 hours) In other words I am a multi-athlete whom have been residing and working in Chamonix Mont Blanc, France from 2000-2011, from where I have my experience within extreme sports as well.

I work with athletes, no matter their level, based upon a throughout consultation. This is conducted through an in depth investigation of capacities and goals through a specific developed sports medicine screening survey.

Sport performance methods
- Mental training and the PETTLEP method (have a look at my blog)
-- Individual designed training programs through virtual consultation on skype - virtualsportscoaching.com
- Consulting within biomechanics as bike fitting, XC skiing and running analysis
- Coaching within sports nutrition and diets such as the paleo and modified paleo methods
- Individualized training programs designed through the best traininglog ever www.trainingpeaks.com
- I focus on cyclists, triathletes, cross country skiers, runners, golfers, adventure racers, ultra runners, skiers and mountaineers.

One of my main specialties within sport performance consulting is altitude training & hypoxic training (TrainHighLiveLow method) for endurance athletes.

I offer these services on a consulting basis, workshops or public speaking for private athletes, as well as organizations, teams, shops or clinics all over Europe. Furthermore I offer these services in the alps, look at alpcamps.com.

Specialties

Manual therapy, training, exercise, kinetic chain based coordination rehabilitation, injuries, rehabilitation, biomechanics, nutrition, individual training programs, sports psychology, (PETTLEP), bike fitting, triathlon, cycling, cross country skiing, virtual training programs, paleo diet, strength training for the endurance athlete, altitude training, virtual sports coaching, stress management techniques, self talk techniques, motivational goal setting techniques

physioinnovation

June 2010 – Present (2 years 3 months) Scandinavia and Europe

Physioinnovation offers consulting within sport performance, both virtually and physically for individuals athletes, groups and teams. At our base in the Alps, we offer personalized coaching for cyclists, trailers, cross country skiers and other endurance athletes.

Physioinnovation focuses on sports psychology and mental training methods, such as the PETTLEP method (have a look at my blog). We always include Individual designed training programs through virtual consultation (Skype). Our niche is consulting within biomechanics and human movement analysis as bike fitting and running analysis. To optimize performance and bring every aspect together, we offer coaching within sports nutrition and different approaches to the broad specter of diet methods as well as linking it to recovery methods. Our individual designed training programs through are made through TrainingPeaks.com

Personally as a sport performance consultant, I mainly focus on triathletes, cyclists, cross country skiers, runners, adventure racers, ultra runners, skiers and mountaineers.

Physioinnovation offers consulting in altitude training as well as renting out a machine replicating up to 6000m altitude (endurance athletes and mountaineers). I am specialized in altitude training methods.

Sport performance services are offered on a consulting basis for private athletes, as well within organizations, teams or clinics. Many of these specialties can be done from distance through virtual meetings.

Independent physiotherapist

February 2008 – July 2010 (2 years 6 months)

As a continuity from my former job at the hospital, I started as a private sports physiotherapist working with French Osteopaths in Chamonix, France. I had many of the same skiing accidents and surgeries that I had seen from the hospital, but here I saw them at a later stage, which required more complex rehabilitation. The rehabilitation phases where often longer and I had more time with my patients to follow them through their rehabilitation protocol to start working as mountain professionals again (guides, alpinists, skiers etc.).

The most developing sides of this job was the injuries or patients coming in from the street without any doctors referral. This gave me a lot of skills in diagnosing, clinical reasoning as well as independency. I chose to specialize my diagnostic skills with my Post Graduate Diploma in Sport and exercise medicine, which gave me a further lift in evidence based sports medicine based consultation.

This job helped me develop a highly independent nature and sharpened my clinical skills alongside with my specialization in sport and exercise medicine.
