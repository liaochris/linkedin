Keith Lim's Experience

Marketing Director & Co-founder

Bio Essentials International Group

November 2002 – Present (9 years 10 months) HQ in Singapore

Bio Essentials International is a dynamic, leading edge company that is in the forefront of marketing, developing, distributing & retailing premium grade nutraceutical & health supplements through their extensive distribution networks that span the region. Specializing in complementary health products, the company has expanded into the foray of functional foods, organic clinics, therapeutic gadgets & organic pharmacies.

In its first year of inception alone, the company grew rapidly to become one of the leading nutraceutical companies in Singapore & Asia Pacific. With innovative solutions for the booming health industry, what sets the company apart, is the company’s sheer ability at deciphering very quickly what the market requires & demands, being able to fulfill that gap & the essential premise to offer products of the highest international quality standards & award-winning formulas, usually manufactured by privately held global leading labs & directly sourced from around the world.

With a commitment to excellence, personalized service & superior quality products, the company requires that all products sourced, to be subjected to a stringent process of an 8-step elimination, which would qualify a product to be a part of the coveted range under the Bio Essentials International’s line of Nutraceutical Products. The company's management believes that the performance of any nutraceutical supplement is determined by several key factors: raw materials, formulations, delivery systems (absorption rates), safe, credible manufacturing practices, coupled with a uniqueness & beneficial effectiveness of each product chosen.

Additionally, the company’s highly skilled team of professionals and employees has applied the company’s philosophy of dedication, honesty, integrity, personalized service & care in dealing with & delivering the best to its consumers, clients & in expanding this concept with all like-minded principals & partners of the business.
