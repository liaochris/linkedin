Rebecca Gill's Summary

Rebecca Gill has a well-rounded business background and over fifteen years of experience in sales and online marketing. Her love for website design and her strong belief in the user experience is equally matched by a fascination with search engine optimization, blogging, and social media marketing.

As an early adopter of blogging, her online marketing efforts have been highlighted by media outlets such as Wired Magazine, Ziff Davis, and IT Toolbox. Her writing has been featured in variety of publications that range from technology outlets such as Backbone Magazine, Social Media Today, Sphinn.com, and Search Engine Roundtable to industry specific magazines such as Contract Manufacturing & Packaging, Food Manufacturing, and Progressive Distributor. She has appeared on Detroit2020 and has spoken at the University of Toledo and Grand Valley State University.

As President of Web Savvy Marketing, Rebecca is able to utilize her love for marketing, technology, and sales to assist small and mid-marketing B2C and B2B organizations achieve long-term business success through website design and search engine optimization.

Rebecca manages Web Savvy Marketing’s day-to-day activities, is an active blogger, and teaches three workshops including Social Media Boot Camp, SEO Boot Camp, and Blogging Boot Camp.
