Kevin Reamy's Summary

I've been in HR for the past 20 years, previously as a hands-on senior business partner, now leading the HR team that supports LinkedIn's technology organization. My best work is in dynamic environments, including start-ups, high growth situations, mergers/acquisitions, restructurings and turnarounds.

Along with focusing on delivering practical, everyday HR solutions to business problems as a Generalist, specific expertise includes management coaching & mentoring, performance management, organization diagnosis & improvement, employee relations, and talent acquisition & retention.
