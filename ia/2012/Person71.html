Allan Schoenberg's Summary

I am an accomplished public relations executive with a successful track record in all aspects of business-to-business communications with an emphasis in brand management, public relations and social media. My approach is the communication is a strategic business function and I continue to focus on seeking information and metrics to improve results and to build internal consensus. My strengths are that I am detail-oriented and an energetic self-starter with a proven ability to manage multiple priorities at once and effectively lead teams and large, complex projects. With my academic focus in economics, I often look at the business and behavioral aspects of communications, which has been a key component to my success.

I am an experienced public speaker and have presented at Blogwell, Business Marketing Association, Finextra, FIA Expo, Ragan Communications, University of Chicago and other events, discussing communication strategies and achievements.

I was an adjunct professor with DePaul University and Loyola University Chicago from 2005 to 2010 teaching many public relations disciplines in the graduate and undergraduate schools.
