Kimberly Hayworth's Summary

Kimberly Hayworth is an Academic Technology Specialist and manages the Academic Technology Lab (ATL) at Stanford University. She consults with faculty from all disciplines on strategies for integrating technology into teaching, learning and research. Kimberly specializes in instructional design, podcasting and digital video.

Specialties

Multimedia project management and consulting, curriculum development, video production, Final Cut Pro, iLife apps, DVD authoring, podcasting, audio editing
