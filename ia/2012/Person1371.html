Patricia Kotze's Summary

Diversified Risk Management, Inc. is a nationwide provider of corporate investigation and internal risk mitigation consulting services. DRM, Inc. assists employers and the law firms that represent them in identifying, preventing, mitigating and responding to risks through a comprehensive and integrated suite of professional services.
DRM, Inc. specializes in solving complex business problems, increasing profits, reducing risk and improving morale. Today, the Company's business services are organized into six specialized practice groups:

Employment Background Screening

CCTV Security Systems

Compliance Training & Educational Services

IT Security & Computer Forensics

Litigation Support & Due Diligence Investigations

Physical Security Armed & Unarmed Services
These six business groups enable DRM, Inc. to provide its nationwide base of clients with comprehensive, single source, risk consulting and investigation services.
