Jon K. Brent's Summary

Jon's a dynamic multi-awarded multimedia journalist and leader that drives and manages growth for broadcasters from coast to coast. Jon has proven time and again to bring outstanding news sense and impactful story telling, community based journalism, leadership, knowledge and expertise of the digital space, inspiring the performaces of others, business development and visual innovation on all media platforms.

Specialties

Community based multimedia journalism, simplifying complex stories, attracting consumers, innovation, public information, social media, collaborative management, leadership, strategic thinking/planning, concept/design, executing strategy, identifying & developing talent, research analysis, news anchoring, investigative reporting, credibility, honesty, enthusiasm, technical savvy, staying connected with the community and having fun!

Jon K. Brent's Experience

Multimedia News Anchor/Reporter

KION CBS46; KCBA FOX35; KCOY CBS12; KKFX FOX11

June 2012 – Present (3 months) Monterey / Salinas / Santa Cruz, California

News anchor and investigative reporter. KION/KCBA/KCOY/KKFX are the CBS and Fox affiliates for the Central Coast from Santa Cruz to Santa Barbara. Our broadcasts are the local news on these affiliates in this region and is known as 'Central Coast News'.

Multimedia News Content Manager

BrentCom Media

1997 – Present (15 years) Fresno/Monterey, California

Manage multimedia journalism, marketing and news reporting business for media contacts at TV, Web and Radio.
*2011 Start up News Manager for KVHF 4, Fresno CA. Led creation of community based news department and budget which included an innovative content and news gathering plan.
*2010 covered the breaking Tsunami Warning on the Big Island of Hawaii for mainland media contacts.
*2009 to present-daily web host/writer for “World News Now with Jon K Brent” Facebook and Twitter news blog with over 2450 followers. Regional, state, world news with topical health, consumer and entertainment news included.
*1999 to present- News content presenter for media contacts.

Multimedia News Anchor/Reporter/Leader

KSEE NBC 24

2003 – 2010 (7 years) Fresno, CA

Primary news anchor/reporter and managing editor for evening newscasts. Leadership included managing/writing/inspiring nightside news team in strategic planning and innovative reporting of relevant daily and breaking news.
* Organized and produced an investigative series and telethons on KSEE 24 News called ‘Save Your Home’. The series helped thousands in the Central Valley get free foreclosure information that they didn’t know existed and help saved their homes. Efforts led to being awarded the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development’s 2009 Media Award.
*Developed the confidence of suspected murderer Dave Hawk prior to his arrest and during his local incarceration in his nationally recognized murder case. I was the only local reporter that he spoke to through the media in person and through jailhouse letters.
*Extensive social media outreach/community service with Central Valley Crime Stoppers, the Housing Council and the City of Fresno.
*Creator-producer-reporter of ‘Central Valley Focus’ that grew our nightside local content efforts by 50%.

Multimedia News Anchor/Reporter/Leader

KBTX CBS 3

2000 – 2003 (3 years) Waco-Bryan-College Station, TX

News anchor/reporter for primary newscasts and nightside managing editor and strategic planner/innovative thinker. Experiences included covering health/medicine as reporter/producer and grew this segment by 300% during my tenure.
*One of several projects produced was a 3 part series around the first experimental Abiocor Artificial Heart implantation: ‘Your Heart: Past, Present and Future’. Lead source was world renowned Heart Surgeon Dr. Denton Cooley, the first to perform a Heart Transplant and Artificial Heart Transplant in America in the 60’s. Dr. Cooley led consultation on the Abiocor Artificial Heart program at the Texas Heart Institute, Houston, prior to the first implantation in July of 2001.

Show Host

KCRA 3

1999 – 2000 (1 year) Sacramento, California

Hired from the Radio industry to host and co-produce half-hour regional issues show on KCRA/KQCA. Involvements lead to my hiring at KBTX.

Manager of Programming/Personality

KHYL Cool 101.1

1993 – 1998 (5 years) Sacramento, California

Managed and led staff of 40 air talent in music and news, promotions, marketing and support staff. Led research and strategic plan of redesigned format and programming focus and created the unique ‘station-ality’.
1993-1998-Achieved top 4 25-54 Adult Arbitron ratings 17 of 20 quarters, with several #1 market victories on the station and in various day parts.
1998-National Association of Broadcasters-Marconi Award Nomination: “KHYL Sacramento- Station of the Year”.
1996-Led creation of the new morning show ‘The Breakfast Club with Sam, Jim and Jen’ that achieved top 2 25-54 ratings and #1 35-54

KOQL-FM KOOL 102
