Michael Greer's Summary

I have worked in most parts of the UK as a troubleshooting store manager for some of the biggest retail chains on the high street. A move into head office followed where I discovered a talent for public relations. Wishing to develop this passion further, I accepted an offer to manage all PR for the struggling Hammicks Bookshops (now part of Waterstones), and helped to turn the business into the most dynamic and proactive chain of bookshops in the UK.

My PR campaign credits include:

* Publicising and managing personal appearances by: JK Rowling, Jamie Oliver, Sir Alex Ferguson, Terry Pratchett, Bill Bryson, Sir Peter Ustinov, Barbara Windsor, Francois Pienaar, Ian Botham, Carl Fogarty and Prime Ministers John Major and Edward Heath.

* Promoting the films: Lord of the Rings, Harry Potter, Pride and Prejudice, Saving Private Ryan, Babe, Perfect Storm, The Grinch That Stole Christmas and Star Wars.

* Managing eight store openings and launching thebookplace.com, the UK's largest independent Internet bookshop.

* Celebrating Paddington Bear's 40th birthday, National Poetry Day, Children's Book Week and National Schools Film Week.

* Establishing World Book Day as the biggest literary event in the World.

* The merger and acquisition of Hammicks Bookshops by Mega International, the largest media group in the Southern hemisphere.

* Counteracting bomb threats and attacks by animal rights activists against Clarissa Dickson Wright at book signings.

* CSR fundraising for Oxfam and Action Medical Research.

Specialties

Creative publicity, managing celebrities, news generation, brand development, event management, marketing, retail promotions and nurturing talent.
