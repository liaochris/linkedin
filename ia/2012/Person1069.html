Matt Griffiths' Summary

An inspirational leader with a can do approach, Matt’s career has spanned twenty years with a focus on creative industries, education, community regeneration and social enterprise. His collaborative skills, enabling style and natural ability to work in partnership are particular strengths, as is his ability to set up new organisations and projects delivering outstanding results. Throughout his senior management career, Matt has successfully generated income from a variety of public and private sources, with his total tally now standing at over £6m. A skilled communicator, people focused, Matt connects well with everyone he works with and has a natural, optimistic approach that motivates those around him. His appetite to make things happen, together with his hard work, dedication and tenacity make him a real asset for anyone he works with.

In June 2012, Matt was appointed Executive Director of Youth Music.
