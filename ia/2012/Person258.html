Catherine Francis' Summary

As a copy editor, layout sub-editor and proofreader, I have worked regularly on some of the UK's biggest selling magazines, including: Fabulous (News Of The World), The Sun TVMag, Star, New!, Woman, Woman’s Own, Chat, Good Housekeeping, Health & Fitness, Family Circle, Living Etc, First, Shape, Perfect Home, Bliss, Mother & Baby, Pregnancy & Birth, OK! Hotstars, Here’s Health, Tesco Healthy Living, It’s Fate, Zest, Celebrity Bodies, At Home series (Dr Hilary Jones, Nicky Hambleton-Jones, Lorraine Kelly), In Style, Look, Good To Know Recipes, Slimming, and customer magazines for John Lewis, Waitrose and Homebase.

As a writer, I have produced hundreds of articles for publications including: Health Plus, Slimming, Health & Fitness, Pregnancy, Here’s Health, Natural Health & Wellbeing, Tesco Healthy Living, Mother & Baby, Perfect Home, At Home series (Lorraine Kelly, Nicky Hambleton-Jones), Ask The Doctor with Dr Christian Jessen, The Apprentice, Careers With James Caan, Local Government Manager, The Enforcers, Woman Alive, Inspire, Christian Herald, UNISON publications.

I also write and edit advertorials/marketing materials for a range of clients, which have included Waitrose, Co-op, Homebase and John Lewis.

I undertake short-term publicity projects for smaller clients such as churches, local arts projects/festivals and independent musicians/artists. This includes producing press releases, managing local press campaigns, and editing/proofreading marketing materials and brochures.

I also offer editorial consultancy services for smaller specialist magazines and publications for business customers/charity supporters etc.

Contact me on cathfrancis@btinternet.com

Specialties

As I writer, I mainly cover health (nutrition, fitness, complementary medicine, slimming, pregnancy); relationships, psychology and lifestyle; the environment; religious issues; politics, human rights and law enforcement; music and culture; celebrity and real-life interviews.

As a sub-editor and proofreader, I'm known for my scrupulous attention to detail and house style, while maintaining the bigger picture - the arc of the story, take-away value for the reader, and the needs of the client.
