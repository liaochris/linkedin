Ken Kaminesky's Summary

Specialties

Commercial lifestyle photographer with broad experience in travel, scenic, HDR, people and lifestyle image creation. Working with major stock photo agencies including Corbis, Jupiterimages and Getty Images. Adept at working closely with art directors and crews including assistants, make up artists, hair stylists, wardrobe stylists and producers. Works locally in Montreal and anywhere that cars, trains, boats and airplanes travel to.
