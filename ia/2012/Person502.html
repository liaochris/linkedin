Eric Bryn's Summary

As Vice President, Digital Innovation at Baird & Warner Real Estate, Eric will work closely with Baird & Warner Marketing, IT, and Residential Sales to develop and introduce new technology solutions that will continue to keep the Baird & Warner sales team on the industry's cutting edge, ensuring that agents have access to the very best digital marketing strategies, innovations, and tools available both now and into the future as they build their business and assist their clients. Eric has advised numerous real estate firms, executives, and agents on Internet marketing, social media strategies, website design, database analytics, and legal issues with respect to social media. He has spoken at several industry conferences—including Leading Real Estate Companies of the World, NAR, and Inman Connect—on Web 2.0/3.0, blogging, legal and risk management issues with respect to social media, and interactive/multichannel marketing strategies. Eric was named by Inman News as one of the top 100 Most Influential Leaders in Real Estate for 2009.

Specialties

digital media strategy and advice, internet lead management, internet marketing, training, public speaking, blogging, client relationship management, client lifetime value theories and practices, business development, strategic partnerships, entrepreneurship, helping other entrepreneurs
