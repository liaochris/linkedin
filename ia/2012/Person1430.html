Peter Bourne's Summary

Peter Bourne is a senior executive with leadership achievements spanning all company operations including P&L, sales and marketing, and R&D. He has spent 10 years in CEO, COO, EVP and GM roles at both public and private companies, from early stage through revenue to profitability and exits. Peter's key strengths include establishing successful company strategies, building and resizing organizations, and motivating teams. He possesses M&A experience as both target and acquirer from initial contact through final integration, including complex and international transactions.
