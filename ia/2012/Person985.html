Lisa Sullivan's Summary

Former educator turned digital media marketing specialist...or as I like to refer myself as...paid social butterfly. No really. That's kinda what I do! And I love it! I love telling stories about people - who they are, what makes them experts in their respective fields, the causes they support, etc. - and I do it all through digital media. I believe it's these stories that help people find you, build awareness for who you are, your brand, and ultimately, connect you to them.

Oh and by the way...I ENJOY MAKING CONNECTIONS HERE, HOWEVER (and yes, I'm screaming to get your attention!) if your Connection request doesn't fully explain why you are asking me to connect with you, I may not accept it. Don't be generic. Be real. It's not about the number. It's about the quality of the connection. What can I do to help you? Tell me and perhaps we'll connect! Thank you.
