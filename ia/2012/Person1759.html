Tom Simnett's Summary

Tom comes from a long history of development, especially in the advertising industry and with web based technologies. As an Open Source advocate, he is a contributor to a number of open source projects, and revels in technology developments.

Initforthe is an online production house aimed at taking the stresses and strains of project builds away from the creative agency, allowing them to concentrate on what they do best - have creative ideas and put those ideas in front of clients. By providing developers, designers, project managers (all of whom are PRINCE 2 Practitioners, the workings of which we use every day), we work from brief and deliver completed projects to budget, on time, every time.

Specialties

Deep understanding of web technologies, corporate document management systems and web content management systems (Documentum), online marketing, application architecture
