Deborah Teramis Christian's Summary

Deborah Teramis Christian is a science fiction & fantasy novelist published by Tor Books. She is the founder of the World Building Academy, and is president and managing designer of Storybones Publishing, an rpg design company and small press imprint. She is also a writer of non-fiction articles, a political/social commentary blogger, and works on certain projects as a sociologist and independent scholar.

Past experience includes systems analysis, design and consulting for clients including NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory, the City and County of San Francisco, and Kaiser Permanente. As CEO of ISIS Management Group, an IT consulting corporation, her areas of focus were information services and delivery, business process improvement, and organizational change management. Teramis has also done copious amounts of technical and business writing over the years, as well as business development and marketing management, and is an experienced teacher and public speaker.

Specialties

For technical career specialties, see above. She no longer works in that field.

In the writing/editing, game design and publishing areas she has these specialties:
- fiction, tech and business writing
- senior-level editing and managing editor skills
- document production and publishing
- game development and rpg theory
- detailed game design (paper and computer-based)
- novelizations
- voice acting
- German translation
