Muhammad Yasin's Summary

Muhammad Yasin is the Social Media Strategist for an international travel medical insurance company. His social media marketing campaigns reached over 2 million people in 2011. Muhammad’s innovative use of social media in the healthcare industry was nominated for a Mira Award in 2010 as well as being featured as a case study in the book Search Engine Optimization: An Hour a Day. He was recently recognized as a Rising Star by the Indianapolis Star and by the Indianapolis social marketing community as the most influential Up and Comer of 2011.

As an evangelist for the use of social media, he regularly publishes tips at www.muhammadyasin.com as well as making appearances on the topic.

Specialties

Social Media, social media strategy, online marketing, online brand management, contact center infrastructure, project management.
