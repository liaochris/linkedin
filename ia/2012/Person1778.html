ESE, European School of Economics' Summary

The European School of Economics (ESE) is a British private College of Higher Education, recognised as an Educational Institution accredited by the British Accreditation Council (BAC), offering bachelor, master, MBA degree programmes in international business validated by the University of Buckingham (UK).
ESE has centres in London, New York, Rome, Milan, Florence and Madrid.
ESE is a school without borders, based on a new concept in higher education, combining academic excellence, internationality, and pragmatism to prepare a new generation of leaders and entrepreneurs.
