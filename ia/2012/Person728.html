Jamie Bellinger's Summary

Jamie Bellinger a second-year BA Creative Music Technology student at Anglia Ruskin University in Cambridge, England. Filled with a passion for music from a young age, Jamie has played jazz saxophone for many years, and has taught himself to play the drums and piano. Jamie has always had an entrepreneurial flare, which began with the self-motivated startup of a one-man mobile disco business in his early teens, charging £25/hour to provide a disco service for parties and weddings. He now sells his own electronic music productions online, as well as enjoying amateur photography and running digital student magazine 'The Flick'.
