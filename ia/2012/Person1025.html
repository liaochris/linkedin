Marco Calzolari's Summary

DOING INFORMATION ARCHITECTURE AND WEB PROJECTS
I use several techniques, such as Wireframing and other Architecture Information methods: software or traditional tools (paper, pencil, markers and post-it). I design the user interface and functionalities and I coordinate the site building between the customer and the staff that actually develop it.

I DEAL WITH WEB PROJECT MANAGEMENT
I have worked on web activities which range from programming to multimedia design. I have been specializing in Architecture Information ever since its origins and over the years I’ve taken on diverse tasks and management roles.

CURRENTLY
I coordinate the Customer Care and Accounting Department at Intesys Web Agency in Verona. This position requires a solid understanding of the client’s needs. It also requires the ability to create prototypes and carry out feasibility studies, evaluate and choose software and technology solutions and implement them with the help of the relevant partners both internally and externally.

I love my customers. I do my best to take care of them and they trust me (sometimes they waiting a lot...). We work together and we see our projects grow and the satisfying results they give.

In the meantime I collect and listen to a lot of music, read, and I sometimes write. I enjoy experimenting and joking around on the web, and I consider my family as the best gift that life has given me.

Specialties

Information Architecture, Prototyping, Web Project Management, Accounting, Customer retention, Team Building, Managing and Caring Customer, Conflict Resolution, Sales and presales of web project
