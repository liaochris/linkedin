David Eckoff's Summary

My name is David Eckoff, and I specialize in developing and launching game-changing online consumer businesses. My goal is to develop consumer products that are important to the daily lives of millions of people.

I've defined go-to-market strategies and led teams in companies ranging from startup to Fortune 500. My passion is bringing innovative ideas from the drawing board to market, then growing those businesses.

Currently, I am co-founder of Pickoff Sports. We develop web and mobile applications for sports fans to interact, compete and have fun with their friends. Our first game, Pickoff, launched in Alpha in December 2011. Predict game winners and challenge friends in a fun social game for sports fans.

I’m also an advisor to media & technology companies, helping take revolutionary ideas from the drawing board to market. I've worked with companies included Kleiner Perkins backed Zazzle.com, live interactive broadcasting platform Ustream.TV, 3D virtual world innovator Kaneva and more.

I co-founded Spitter, Inc. an early stage company dedicated to aggregating the explosion of news and discussion on the web and filtering and organizing that content by niche topics of interest to passionate fans.

Previously, I was Vice President, New Products & Innovation at Turner Broadcasting overseeing the development, evaluation and launch of new products.

Before joining Turner, I was Senior Director at RealNetworks Inc. While at Real, I led the live online hit show “CBS Big Brother 24/7″; invented an online network for the long tail of video, a forerunner of YouTube; developed live online sports webcast products; and revitalized and repositioned the company’s flagship consumer online product, SuperPass.

Before joining Real, I was Senior Vice-President at Rivals.com, the online destination for the world's most passionate sports fans.

Prior, I was at IBM in various financial management positions.

I’m also an Eagle Scout. Once an Eagle Scout, always an Eagle Scout.

Specialties

Online strategy, product management, social games, business development, consumer online marketing, negotiating.

Speaking: "I have had the privilege of speaking at numerous events, as a keynote & panel speaker. I'm very comfortable with large audiences having spent years as an Instructor for the Dale Carnegie Course. Favorite topics include innovation, trends in media & technology, bringing ideas to market, and customer experience. To schedule me to speak for your group, please contact me."
