Duncan Johnson's Summary

I have been actively promoting websites since 1997. After running some personal websites including a HTML humour ezine whose membership grew over 11,000 I drifted into 6 years of affiliate marketing.

I then took up a full time roll in a couple of Web Agencies of which I was a director and gained 9-5 experience of dealing with International Brands and a host of Small to Medium Enterprises.

I set up Yodelay in August 2010 as a Social Enterprise that will give 70% of it Net Profits to charity. I have openly stated that my staff and my salaries will be fixed inline with market rates with a performance related bonus.

The Yodelay business model represents a win-win relationship between Business and Charities. Businesses get a world class service in Web Design, Development and Digital Marketing and Training and Charities get a sustainable source of income from businesses.

The Yodelay Business Model removes the traditional "Give" relationship with charities and now business can "Get" something in return in the way of Digital Business Growth.
