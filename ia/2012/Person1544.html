Roland van Rijswijk's Summary

I'm an experienced Internet innovator and passionate evangineer (engineer + technology evangelist). I love to immerse myself in new Internet technologies, learn from them, extend them and apply them in innovative ways. I speak regularly at international conferences on Internet technology.

My current work involves research and operations of innovative middleware services for higher education and research. My main focus areas are identity management, mobile identity, mobile application platforms, general security and DNSSEC.

In my work, I'm responsible for both initiation and management of innovation projects as well as operations and contract management for several SURFnet services.

Specialties

Innovation, Evangineering, Internet technology, Mobile Identity, Mobile PKI, DNSSEC, identity management, identity federation, PKI, applied cryptography
