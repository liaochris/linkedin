Vlad Sapozhnikov's Experience

Founder, SVP

Sapo Group, a division within Prudential Douglas Elliman

Privately Held; 5001-10,000 employees; Real Estate industry

May 2011 – Present (1 year 2 months) Manhattan, NY

Real estate entrepreneur, broker and business leader with more than a decade of professional, intuitive sales and marketing experience.

A founder of the Sapo Group, a division within Prudential Douglas Elliman which comprises a trusted real estate resource for an ever-expanding international ethnic marketplace. The Sapo Group’s highly experienced multi-lingual staff focuses on integrity and performance, deploying extraordinary marketing strategies, unique technologies, expert project management, and high level strategic planning and execution. Vlad's first New York real estate venture was the establishment in 2001 of the award-winning New York Business Group (NYBG) - a boutique brokerage designed to market luxury residential developments to both overseas and local investors. In 2007, Mr. Sapozhnikov served as Founding Partner and executive officer of Bid on the City (BOTC), New York's first-ever online real estate trading platform, in which top worldwide properties are bought and sold in a live auction format. He developed the concept, designed the business plan, managed operations, created unprecedented marketing technologies, and tailored breakthrough social media and selling approaches.

Vlad's creativity and novel strategies have made him a sought-after real estate expert by media including The New York Times, The New York Observer, Crain's, CBS MoneyWatch, Bloomberg and many more. He is also well known for daily real estate news reports for several ethnic New York radio stations.
