Ray Toler's Summary

The bulk of my experience is in marketing communications and creative services including collateral production, marketing strategy, advertising campaign development and production, promotions, branding and identity, music composition and production, voiceover and acting, video production, multimedia presentation development, and leveraging emerging technologies to meet both internal and external corporate communication goals.

I have also provided managerial and operational consulting services covering such areas as organizational structure, social network mapping and analysis, change management, and effective communication of executive and corporate goals to employees.

Additionally, I have significant IT experience including project management, architecture and planning, enterprise applications, web and content production, management information systems, and systems administration.

I earned my MBA with a concentration in Executive Leadership from the University of Houston in 2007.

Specialties

Creative concept generation, branding and identity, art direction, strategic marketing and corporate communications. Client interaction and customer service. Intra/Entrepreneurial development; operational process evaluation and optimization.
