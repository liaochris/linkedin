Gordon Makryllos FAICD's Summary

B Eng (Elec. Eng.) , M.B.A., M.Org.Coaching, FAICD

Gordon is Managing Director of Orange Business Services, Australasia and is an active Fellow of the Australian Institute of Company Directors (AICD) and the International CEO Forum; as well as a Director on the Board of The French-Australian Chamber of Commerce & Industry (FACCI).

With extensive experience in the leadership of diverse global organisations, Gordon's success includes executive roles with HP, IBM, Telstra, APC and Orange Business Services. Gordon has demonstrated a successful track record of leading the transformation of teams into the market leaders of the Region.

With a focus on Strategy, Innovation, Culture and Employee engagement, he has led & coached teams that have exceeded market expectations. Gordon is passionate and can talk with authority about transformational leadership and innovation based on his experience in leading organisations for the achievement of superior market share and superior employee engagement.

Gordon can talk with authority on the challenges faced by CEOs & CIOs in managing their ITC Infrastructure needs and he is at the forefront of the latest developments and innovation in Telecommunications, Cloud Computing & Data Centre Solutions.

Gordon has a Bachelor of Electrical Engineering from the University of New South Wales; and an MBA plus a Master of Organisational Coaching from the University of Sydney. His Director accreditation includes graduating with a GAICD from the Australian Institute of Company Directors and awarded Fellow accreditation as FAICD.

On a personal note Gordon is married with three teenage children and one dog. He loves the theatre, film, travel, history, art and museums of all kinds.

The French-Australian Chamber of Commerce & Industry (FACCI) primary objectives are to promote the development of commercial, industrial, and economical bilateral links between France and Australia.
