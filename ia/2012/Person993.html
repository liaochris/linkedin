Lindsey Jarrell, FACHE's Summary

Lindsey is an accomplished healthcare leader with over 18 years of experience and an exceptional record of achievement in consulting, clinical transformation, strategic planning, contract negotiation and CIO leadership in a large integrated delivery network.
He is currently a Partner with PwC and serves as the lead for application consulting in the healthcare industry.
In 2009 Lindsey received the CHIME-AHA Transformational Leader of the Year award while he was Chief Information Officer at BayCare Health System.
Lindsey's experience in fast growth technology firms, large integrated delivery networks, and in a world leading professional services firm provides a solid foundation for him to continue his passion of improving healthcare through technology.
