Sherilynn Macale's Summary

Professionally, I am a blogger and social media manager currently available for hire. My most recent experience includes roles as the social media strategist for Identified.com, the lead social media news editor for The Next Web, and contributing editor to Virgin.com's entrepreneur blog (among others). I've also previously written for industry media sites such as G4TV and Destructoid all while maintaining my own personal brand. I regularly set out to exceed my own expectations on a daily basis and consistently employ both unique and trending marketing strategies into my overall technique.

I have over 196k subscribers on Facebook, 8k+ followers on Twitter (@heycheri), 26k+ subscribers on YouTube spanning various channels, am in 29k+ circles on Google+, claim one extremely varied professional background in community management, news, editorial, marketing and social media -- topped with one kickass personality -- and regularly exude a strong desire to live and breathe video game, tech, Internet and beauty/style culture. Needless to say, you should probably hire me while you can.
