Marc P. Bernegger's Summary

Marc, Master of Law UZH, is a serial entrepreneur and angel investor based in Zurich, Switzerland.

Aged 20, Marc founded the party website usgang.ch (trade sale to the listed media company Axel Springer in 2008) and he is a founder of amiando (awarded by the World Economic Forum as Global Technology Pioneer in 2010; trade sale to the listed business platform XING in 2010).

In 2010 Marc was awarded as "Newcomer of the Year" by SwissICT (biggest association of the ICT industry in Switzerland).

Marc is partner of the listed investment company Next Generation Finance, founder & partner of the crowdfunding platform c-crowd, member of the board of the Pioneers' Club PCU and angel investor in several startups.
