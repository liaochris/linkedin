Welcome! I help SME Businesses in Pembrokeshire and Wales, and the Welsh Border Counties get 'seen' more with targeted online marketing, and social media marketing strategies.

It's a fact that whether your customers are local, or from further afield it's no longer enough to be doing traditional offline marketing - leaflets, adverts, Yellow Pages etc

Your customers expect to be able to find you via a range of online routes such as social media, mobile and google search, with an engaging web presence.

I offer training, and done-for-you services to make sure your business doesn't get left behind as your competitors snap up all the top online spots

The Outcome:

Understanding social media in a simplified way
Greater visibility for your business
Easier targeted lead generation
More new business inquiries
More potential new customers
Better engagement with current and past customers
More sales


Excellent marketing is a very different skill from being great at what your business is (your products or services) – but with the right guidance and support there’s no reason why you can’t have BOTH!
