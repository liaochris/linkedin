Susan Tournour's Summary

My passion lies within print design, particularly publishing and book design. However, I also enjoy designing for the web. My love of design began with the world of photography. There's nothing like looking through the viewfinder of a camera. It allows a different view of the world around oneself that helps to create a good eye for layout, which is something I pride myself on.

In my past and current positions, I have gained experience in both the print world and the web. While my dream would be to work in publishing, I am currently open to using my design skills in other industries as well and am available for freelance and remote contract work.

Specialties

Print Design, Web Design, Web Banners/Advertising, Identity Branding, Pixel Illustrations, Writing and Editing, Multi-tasking
