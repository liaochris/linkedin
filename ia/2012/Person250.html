Carol Dodsley TheHow2Girl's Summary

With over 15 years experience in training, management, facilitation, mentoring & consultative roles, Carol is a leading and sought after professional mentor and creative ideas strategist, helping solopreneurs and service sector business owners attract more clients, be more creative and see more results than ever before.

A highly recommended International Author, workshop presenter & speaker, Carol shows you how to create the right mindset and marketing strategies so that you will stay in front of your customers and ahead of your competition.

Carol takes you by the hand and inspires you as you find your way through the confusion & overwhelm often associated with building and growing a sustainable and profitable business.

Carol is the creator of TheHow2Girl TV and spends her time seeking out some of the worlds most successful entrepreneurs to invite to join her on the How2Girl.TV and share their immense knowledge and ideas to inspire and add value for solopreneurs and service sector business owners who are committed to building a successful business and looking for the right information, ideas and strategies that will take them from where they are now to where you want to be.

Carol is also a Social Media Trainer and Facebook marketing specialist w& has founded How2GetSocial, to offer a range of professional Social Media Training programmes, including the highly recommended Facebook Business School.

As an international Author, Carols books and programmes are highly recommended by business and self development experts such as: Jonathan Jay, John Assaraf & Peter Thomson.
