Kyle Mortensen's Summary

Leading New Science is CEO / Creative Director, Kyle Mortensen. His 18-year career has carved a creative niche in the youth market. Mortensen is a former executive of EDM bible URB Magazine and VP of Marketing for social media agency StreetWise. His campaign work includes the launch of Scion, Call of Duty: Modern Warfare, Coca-Cola, Sony Pictures, and Live Nation.

The company is based in Playa Del Rey, CA. Current clients include Square Enix, Ubisoft, and THQ.
