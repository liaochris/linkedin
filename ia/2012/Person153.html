Michael Beale's Summary

Want to improve your performance? I help you, and where appropriate your team, achieve great results and personal fulfillment through generative changes in how you think, communicate and act. This includes working through anything that might be holding you back.

Current and past clients include Accenture, Argos, AstraZeneca, Barclay's, BT, BAE Systems, British Sugar, CSC, Centrica, HBOS, ODPM, Oracle, Petroleum Development Oman, Roche, PM's strategy office and the main public services.

I specialise in senior level 1:1 NLP training and executive coaching.

In addition I use my NLP, 30 years business experience and the latest neuroscience research to train NLP, contribute to company talent programmes, speak at conferences and develop podcast MP3s for on-line learning.

I run the monthly CoachingCloud Tribal Meetings in London, Mayfair and Milton Keynes and use CoachingCloud internationally to enhance my programmes. CoachingCloud is an enabler for individual, team and company coaching and mentoring worldwide.

The business was started 10 years ago when I left corporate life and trained directly with the co-creators of NLP in the US. My practitioner, coaching practitioner, business practitioner and master practitioner courses are NLP Co-founder Richard Bandler and The Society of NLP (SNLP) certified.

Specialties

Open accredited NLP courses with a maximum of 6 delegates in the UK at the Kents Hill Park Training Centre, near Milton Keynes. The location has good motorway and airport links

NLP Master and Business Practitioner modules include:

Executive coaching
Sales success
Managing organisational change
Leadership
Presenting impact
Meditation, relaxation and hypnosis for performance, focus and health
