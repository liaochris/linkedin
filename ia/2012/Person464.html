Duncan Watson's Summary

Trainee solicitor at TV Edwards LLP specialising in criminal law with a specific interest in financial and regulatory offences. Over 2 years experience as an accredited police station representative and broad litigation experience, including fraud and confiscation proceedings. A developing legal expertise is built on a strong commercial grounding.
