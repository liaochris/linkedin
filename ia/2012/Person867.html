Julie Myers' Summary

Dedicated to marketing the small business … helping you put the pieces together to develop a roadmap for your business.

JM Marketing Solutions … Formulates strategies for the challenges faced today and tomorrow. Guides the creative process, keeping it customer-focused and targeted. Offers a balance of right brain and left brain thinking.

MARKETING CONSULTING
What are your goals? How do you break through the clutter? Where do you go when you need help putting the pieces together … figuring out a roadmap for your business? I work with you to put together an actionable marketing plan specific to your business’ needs.

BUILDING YOUR PRESENCE
Where can consumers find you? Today consumers go online … Facebook. Twitter. LinkedIn. Website. … But don't discount traditional marketing methods either. Every touch needs to build on one another. Online or off. In person or on the phone. My job is to help you get found using the right tools so you can focus on running your business.

MENTORING
Are you on Facebook, Twitter, Google+ or somewhere else? What’s your next step? Or do you just need a little guidance? I help both the individual and the business learn to use these relationship-building tools to increase their online presence and credibility.

JM Marketing Solutions … helps you get found … helps you put the pieces together … helps increase your reach and visibility … so you can do what you need to do to run your business.
