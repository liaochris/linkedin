Simon Anderson's Summary

Simon is CEO of DreamHost, a US-based web hosting company with a hip personality; plus over 300,000 customers and 1.2 million domains hosted! Simon joined in early 2011 to take the already strongly growing founder-run DreamHost into the clouds. DreamHost is the principal sponsor of Ceph, the open source massively-scalable distributed storage system, and an active member of the Openstack community. DreamHost plans to launch public cloud storage and compute services during 2012 based on Openstack and Ceph. DreamHost is Simon's fifth executive role at a web start-up or growth venture in the US. He came over to running things from the dark side of venture capital investing in the early 2000's, where he'd been poached from his calling as a weekend surf lifesaver in Sydney, Australia. He now lives in Los Angeles where everyone wants to live, but nobody wants to admit it.

Specialties

Web and cloud hosting, SaaS, analytics, executive management, product definition and go-to-market strategy, direct and indirect sales management, strategic business development, complex deal negotiation, partner management, fund raising & M&A.

Simon Anderson's Experience

Privately Held; 51-200 employees; Internet industry

DreamHost is a leading web and app hosting company based in Los Angeles with over 300,000 customers and more than 1.2 million domains hosted. It's also a very cool place to work. The company operates "green" data centers and is one of the top 50 most democratic workplaces according to www.worldblu.com, alongside Zappos, GroupOn and Hulu. Ceph was created by Sage Weil, co-founder of DreamHost, and incubated at DreamHost since 2007. The new venture is initially funded by DreamHost and has attracted talented engineers to the cause in LA, San Francisco, the Mid-West and Europe.
