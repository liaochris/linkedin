shel israel's Summary

I have helped hundreds of companies tell their stories more effectively by writing content and as a presentation coach.

I am author of four books and am working on a new one valled "Age of Context" with my friend Robert Scoble. Forbes columnist. I've contributed editorially to BusinessWeek, Dow Jones, Fast Company, Business Intelligence, American Express Open Forum and other publications.

At the core of my consulting is the belief that business will do better by telling their stories in engaging ways, than with "talking bullet points."

I work with companies of all sizes.

I'm currently working on my fifth book with my friend Robert Scoble. It is about the coming Age of Context which will change your life and work even more than social media has done.

I've been a keynote speaker many times, addressing audiences on five continents usually on topics related to tech innovations.
