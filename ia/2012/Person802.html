Joseph K. Levene | Joseph K. Levene Fine Art, Ltd.'s Summary

Joseph K. Levene Fine Art, Ltd., established in 1991, is a Dealer of secondary market Post War & Contemporary #Art featuring paintings, sculpture, photography and prints & multiples by Contemporary Masters.

Joseph K. Levene, an Andy Warhol and Jasper Johns authority, and Robert Grunder, Director, together, have over 3 decades multifaceted Fine Art experience, as Art Dealer, Collector, Art Executive and Internet Art Sales.

A Wharton School graduate, Joseph K. Levene is an Internationally known Andy Warhol and Jasper Johns authority. Before establishing Joseph K. Levene Fine Art, Ltd., Mr. Levene was previously President/COO, Petersburg Press, Inc., the International Fine Art Publisher; Director e-commerce, artnet.com; an Invited Charter Member, Sothebys.com.

In 1999, Mr. Levene established the Joseph K Levene Fine Art Ltd, eBay Store where all Fine Art offered and sold is guaranteed Authentic.

Vigilant regarding Authenticity and Condition, Joseph K. Levene writes about relevant Art Topics on The Fine Art Blog; Art Topics, Online & Off.
