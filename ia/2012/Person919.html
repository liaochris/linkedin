GLOBAL MANAGING DIRECTOR

Established to trigger meritocracy for the top tier of worldwide polo, long structured for rich amateur players to secure playing spots over more talented athletes.

• Raised more startup capital than all other executives and business partners combined; initiated major cost cutting, generating greatly improved operations; significantly expanded assets, market research/analysis, sponsor and government affiliations, favorable public image; built advisory board that included highly accomplished members from several world regions

• Earned the nickname "Kevin the Conqueror," the title of a PACE (European polo magazine) cover story about ProPolo's leadership

• ProPolo's plan has been successfully adopted in key world regions. In a story on the initiative, Forbes noted that it appeals to "a vast global viewership...to bring polo to its greatest audience in the history of the game...to make the game more spectator friendly." Added player and Ralph Lauren model Nacho Figueras, professional polo's most popular player, "ProPolo...is the future of the game."
