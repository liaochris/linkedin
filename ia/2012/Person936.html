Krista Sanders' Summary

Hi. I’m Krista Sanders, a design strategist and user experience pro. I have more than 10 years experience in graphics and user experience planning and strategy.

As a design junkie, I like taking on challenges that push me to redefine my creative edge, and I actively seek out like-minded colleagues. Over the years, I have racked up experience points in online and mobile interaction design, system design, video, print, and I love running around with my camera pretending to know what I’m doing.

Energizing my work is a relentless passion for making things useful and simple, a determined spirit, and a commitment to bold, courageous, unbound thinking.

Recently, I took on the challenge of reinventing email with the AOL team, and I'm really looking forward to our public launch. Stay tuned!
