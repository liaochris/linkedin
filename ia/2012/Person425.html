Derek Ross' Summary

Derek Ross has been providing professional Information Technology services to the greater Huntingdon area for nearly a decade. Derek’s area of expertise includes providing award winning customer support, system integration, network architecture, network administration, business and consumer consulting, cost saving solutions for small to large businesses, as well as building and managing many large scale networks; including many local government offices.

Being a supporter of economic growth in Huntingdon County, Derek sits on the Board of Directors for the Huntingdon County Chamber of Commerce, graduated from Leadership Huntingdon County in 2006, and is a member of Rotary International.
