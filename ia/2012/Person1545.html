Rolf Larsen's Summary

Rolf holds qualifications in computer engineering from 1988. He has started and headed several IT-related businesses. Rolf was the CEO and founder of the ISP RiksNett in 1995, later acquired by Telia and renamed Telia Internet. He was the CEO and founder of Active ISP/Active 24, a pan European web hosting company. Rolf was the CEO of NetConnect Systems AS, a Web Conferencing company that was sold to Premiere Global Services. He was he CEO and founder of NetConnect ASA, a company offering a SharePoint App Store platform. Rolf is currently the CEO of CloudNames AS, a provider of Top Level Domain (TLD) services.
