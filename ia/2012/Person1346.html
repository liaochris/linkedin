Niv Singer's Summary

Niv Singer is a co-founder and CTO at tracx where he is responsible for leading the architecture and development of big-data collection and processing in distributed and scalable systems. Niv has over 15 years of experience in cutting edge technologies and has been writing code since he got his first computer at the age of thirteen. This passion for technology spurred him into a professional programming job when he was sixteen and he has not stopped programming since then. In the past 12 years he has been working in a number of companies, always being a cornerstone - both socially and technologically.

Prior to tracx, Niv was the System Architect at DVTel, responsible for the planning, management of the development team and the implementation of DVTel's real-time media archiver from the ground up and the establishment of DVTel's R&D branch. Prior to that, Niv was a Software Engineer at Nice Systems and NiceEye. Niv served in a high-tech IDF Intelligence Unit and took part in "Mamram" programming Course (Israel Defense Force), graduating with excellence.
