sharon martin's Summary

Welcome to Beck Valley Books

We are a family run business, based in Billingham, Cleveland, located near the picturesque nature reserve of Billingham Beck Valley.

Our passion for books started many years ago, going to as many locations as we could to find more books for our ever expanding collection. Sharing this passion to other booklovers seemed the next best possible step. Finding hard to find titles, unusual titles, lovely old books and even modern titles was a bookworms dream......There's nothing better than holding a lovely old book in your hands, knowing it has its own history.

Selling over 5,000 second hand, rare and out of print books in a variety of subjects online at www.beckvalleybooks.co.uk all competitively priced. All books are sent out in new padded envelopes and come with a free Beck Valley Books bookmark. Stock is increased and changed frequently, so please visit us often.

We are committed to providing each customer with the highest standard of customer service. We aim to deal with all enquiries personally as soon as possible, either by email or phone. You can always rely on our friendly service.

You can also find us on facebook and twitter.

Thank you for visiting.
