Jock Busuttil's Summary

Jock is a highly capable product and line manager, who is passionate about getting the most for his customers. He is a proficient problem-solver and seen as a safe pair of hands. His manner is assertive, collaborative and reasoned. Jock is motivated most when his team are fulfilling their potential and by a job done well.
