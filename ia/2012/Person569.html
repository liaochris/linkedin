Gaynelle Grover's Summary

For 20 years I've been directing big brand projects for companies with big brands: Apple, Google, AT&T, Logitech, Bell Atlantic, Delta Air Lines, IBM, Intel, just to name a few. During this time I've also worked with not-so-big companies, brand new companies, and taught branding classes at Stanford University’s Professional Workshops.

Specialties

- Brand strategy, architecture, repositioning, extension
- Competitive analysis, positioning
- Messaging strategy, architecture
- Visual and verbal brand system development
- Brand audits, research design, customer experience mapping
- Creative direction, creative team management
- Brand seminars, worksessions
