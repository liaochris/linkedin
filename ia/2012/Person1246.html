Mitone Griffith's Summary

I am a picture of zen...a cool balance between Star Trek Nerd and Hippy Artist. When I was younger I dreamed of squirreling away in a cabin writing long novels and literary missives like those of the greats I had read with such passion.

Unfortunately my attention span probably translates more like a Pollack painting...lots of stuff splattering quickly all over a huge canvas!

So when I discovered HTML and taught myself how to write a little code, I learned my words can turn into pictures with the magic of computer languages! So both halves of me had a happy little vulcan mind meld and my inner nerd and outer artist joined to make a happy me who loves to design and develop websites for business owners!

My main customers have been those in the tourism industry: county and city tourism agencies, bed and breakfasts, realtors, and similar groups seeking assistance in getting attention from outside our immediate service area.

I have been designing websites and print collateral in the Southwest Virginia region since 2003. My current big tourism project is a complete overhaul of the Carroll County VA tourism website. Our scheduled release date is September 30, 2011. We are making a complete business directory with mapping capability and interactive social media. And so much more! Stay tuned!

The Fancy Free Years

1996 – 2000 (4 years) Mount Airy, NC, Garden City, KS

When I graduated with my degree in English, but no certification to teach, I admit I was rather lost!! What is an English major to do besides write or teach? Well, the writing did not pan out, and as most 23 year olds will soon realize, one needs a steady job in order to eat and pay apartment rent. I accepted a position with American General Finance as a Financial Management trainee. Boy was I ill-suited for that career track!! I found happiness a year or so later as a part time cake decorator while I still attempted to have a normal career in finance. Soon my cake decorating skills and creativity led me to a full time position at a grocery store bakery in Garden City, KS as the cake decorator extraordinaire! Having my artistic talent recognized only fueled my desire to do something even more challenging so I taught myself HTML and web design and found a part time position with a marketing agency as their web consultant and ad layout girl. That's where I learned a lot about marketing, advertising and graphics! So, please disregard the jumble of positions I held as I discovered my calling during these formative years!
