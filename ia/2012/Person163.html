Benjamin Tseng's Summary

Benjamin Tseng is an Associate at DCM, a leading venture capital fund based in the Silicon Valley, Beijing, and Tokyo, managing over $2 billion in assets where he is responsible for analyzing new investment opportunities as well as assisting on financing, M&A, and strategic matters for DCM’s diverse set of portfolio companies.

Prior to DCM, Benjamin was a Senior Associate Consultant with management consulting firm Bain & Company based in the Bay Area where he worked on a number of client projects across the technology industry evaluating new markets, strategies, and acquisitions

Benjamin graduated Phi Beta Kappa with highest honors in Biochemical Sciences and a minor in Mathematical Sciences from Harvard University where his studies focused on understanding complex biological systems using a combination of mathematical and experimental approaches.

His interest areas lie in disruptive opportunities that exist at the intersection of business and science & technology and include Android, semiconductor/software components, hardware, next-generation mobile/web platforms, Big Data, and the intersection between traditional life science/healthcare and technology

Specialties

Tech strategy, management consulting, venture capital, semiconductors, components, Android, mobile devices, biochemistry, immunology, Big Data, digital health, web platforms, genomics
