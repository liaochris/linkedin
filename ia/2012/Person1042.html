Mark Asquith's Summary

I am a dedicated and somewhat geeky digital professional, having started work in the digital domain in 2001.

Since then I have been driven to educate myself, and others, in digital technologies. My past experience includes training the Ministry of Defence on digital recruitment, Morrisons PLC on digital integration and a long stint developing digital solutions and training strategies / delivery for the NHS. I am also a specialist TPP SystmOne trainer with experience in business change & project management.

Since 2003 I've been designing and building websites and continually keep myself up to date on coding practice through avid reading and continual development of personal projects.

Since 2005 I've been heavily involved in the web and digital / online marketing; in 2009 expanding my marketing skills to more integrated campaigns and business growth strategies through effective planning, tactical implementation & measurement.

I am the proud co-owner of DM SQUARED, a design & marketing studio with a small yet perfectly formed team of specialists working together to create award winning work.

I am a TAP accredited trainer & IDM certified marketeer and have a dog called Pete.
