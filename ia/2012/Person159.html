Ben Fortney's Summary

A hard working, results driven idealist, I'm fascinated by the unique intersection of technology, sociology, information, and design, found on the web. My goal is to use these constantly evolving aspects of online communication to produce positive results on spreadsheets and create positive change in the world.

After a decade of both professional and academic work within online environments, I look to continue focusing on the transformative effects of networks and the complex relationships that they can form with users. With every project, I look to engage the audience with an online experience that is both meaningful and appealing, while transmitting a concise and focused message.

Specialties

web administration, online identity, social media marketing
