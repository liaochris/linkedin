Istvan Vincze's Summary

What I do:

* Interpreting requirements into UI concepts through sketching, wireframing and prototyping to illustrate user interactions, and high-detail Photoshop mock-ups.
* Building custom sites from scratch, or on top of WordPress, Joomla and e-commerce sites using Magento and LemonStand.
* Projects for clients ranging from small business to state government, including information sites as well as web-based tools and applications.
* Good grasp of on-line marketing and SEO practices and experience in setting up Google Website Optimizer tests and Analytics.

What I know:

* HTML5, CSS3/LESS and jQuery, support down to at least IE7, with polyfills where necessary.
* Building sites and email templates with broad user-agent compatibility.
* Using third-party frameworks.
* Experience with responsive design, but strongly focusing on this; currently building a responsive e-commerce site.
* PHP knowledge to understand, modify and work with existing code, editing and creating simple plug-ins, and using APIs in both procedural and MVC environments.
* Mercurial, Git and SVN.
* Solid Photoshop experience producing easily maintainable files.
* Working in a team or autonomously.

Interests and activities:

* User/customer-centred solutions and design in everyday life.
* Reading, podcasts, seminars, conferences, workshops and online training.
* Attending events such as WSG, UX Australia conference, Mobile Monday, Melbourne Web Developers Meetup, UX Melbourne.
* Information architecture, creative thinking, psychology, design techniques and principles, online and general marketing.
* Trying out different frameworks, tools and other forms of development, such as Android apps using Appcelerator.

Specialties

Wireframing and designing UI concepts. Front-end development: standards-oriented and semantic CSS/LESS, HTML (HTML5 and XHTML), Javascript/jQuery.
