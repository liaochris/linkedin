Jacqueline Button's Summary

I am a commercial property solicitor at SA Law LLP, a market leading law firm based in St Albans.

I have 15 years experience in the commercial property sector in both the City of London and the home counties and advise on all aspects of transactional property law, development, landlord and tenant and property finance.

I joined SA Law in April 2010 bringing the expertise I gained in the City to a team of dedicated, dynamic lawyers most of whom have also worked at City firms. We focus on really understanding our clients’ businesses as well as providing top quality legal advice at a fraction of City firms’ prices.

My particular interests are mixed use development and the retail sector where I have acted for numerous high street brands. I am also familiar with the specialist requirements of health and sporting occupiers. I have acted on a variety of PFI transactions and have dealt with both sale & leaseback and stamp duty saving schemes.
