Mark John Buenconsejo's Summary

I am the CEO and a co-founder of Caresharing. Our mission is to make a positive impact in people’s well-being. We build software solutions that help us and our customers achieve that.

I started my programming career a little early than usual, at 17 years old. I started as a systems administrator and a freelance programmer, and became an entrepreneur which led me to a few startup ventures, like LearningToGo and SimpleTeq. Other projects i’m involved in are BuhayStartup, Runroo, TweeTiTow, CodrSchool, CoderDojo Cebu, Startup Weekend Cebu and TechTalks PH.

I am passionate about using technology and software in helping solve the world’s biggest problems in healthcare, social and education.

I love mountain biking, trail running and similar endurance sports.

More info: http://markjeee.com

Specialties

Since 1998 i am both a programmer and a manager, managing everything in a small ISV company. As a programmer, i develop using C, C++, Ruby, PHP, Perl, Visual C++, Visual Basic, on these platforms, OS X, Linux, Palm OS, Pocket PC and Windows.

I also specialize in bootstrapping a startup from zero, and scale that to profitability.
