Mike Levin's Summary

Please "View Full Profile" so recommendations show—or visit http://mikelev.in/about/

The mission of any company is to get and keep customers. That's the lens through which I see my field of search engine optimization (SEO). It is core to every business, because reaching out to self-directed searchers at the moment of inquiry is not merely an objective, but rather the duty of every company—and must be part of your very information architecture. Strong leadership with a finger on the pulse of evolving SEO strategy, tactics and methodology must understand and promote these concepts throughout the organization, supported by robust technical knowledge.

Today with 360i, I regularly consult on search strategy and enthusiastically tackle the biggest natural-search problems in the industry. Whatever the situation, I work with an entrepreneurial spirit and mindset. Feel free to reach out to me for a chat ( miklevin@gmail.com ).

Specialties

I speak the language of sysadmins and CMOs with equal ease—making me uniquely suited solve the intractable problems that frequently plague organizations, blocking natural search excellence. I develop on the cloud, directly administrate hardware, and manage teams. My personal platform today is Linux/Python on pocket-size ARM micro-servers, which I use as secret weapons for fancy and unexpected API integration that yield competitive advantage. I strive to master both trends and the fundamentals.

Mike Levin's Experience

Director of SEO Strategy & Inventor of 360iTiger

360i

Privately Held; 201-500 employees; Marketing and Advertising industry

July 2008 – Present (4 years 2 months)

Created 360iTiger, a potentially game-changing tool that crawls websites directly into Google Docs for SEO and social media audits—created to extend the capabilities of SEO Managers and Technologists, and now made available to the public at http://tiger.360i.com. Devising and aiding in the implementation of SEO strategy for Fortune 500's, including THE top name-brand tech company on the planet. Oversaw the lifting of the most notorious Google penalty in history for a top retail brand. Even aside from these most singular accomplishments, the basic goal of optimizing big name-brands for high traffic generic terms is one of the biggest challenges in this field, because such companies have simultaneously the best assets and the most constraints. I effectively navigate the politics of stakeholders and their priorities, including the most technical of techs, to get the job done.

Inventor and Creator

Helped thousands of people incorporate algorithms into their content platforms, thereby creating a large group of followers, or "tribe", that lasted for years, and became a recognized thought-leader on SEO. Executed every aspect of this endeavor, from conceptualization to implementation to a half-decade of hardware system administration and maintenance. Aided with transition to new owner, ensuring smooth server migration and keeping the tribe intact.
