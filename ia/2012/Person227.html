Bryan Foss' Summary

My career includes continual personal development through complex systems, enterprise sales, product and market development, board executive, non-executive and risk & audit chair roles.

Having worked globally for many years within IBM, one of the largest and most transformed companies in the world, I am able to challenge and assist the directors and executives of companies both large and small, whether supplier, clients or investors.

Since mid 2005 I have proven my ability to transfer these same skills to listed company boards, central government and other complex client and supplier organisations beyond my IBM career successes.

I am extremely well networked and always work cooperatively with others to achieve shared goals. I continue to build a flexible portfolio of non-executive, interim and permanent (part time or near full time) roles.

I also provide voluntary time to emerging SME's, professional and academic bodies, also charities related to executive skills development and sustainability.
