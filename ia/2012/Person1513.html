Richard Vetstein's Summary

I am a Massachusetts attorney, often recognized by my peers and the media for my expertise in real estate matters. However, I have a diversified practice, assisting clients with anything from commercial litigation, trademark applications, business start-up formation, to condominiums.

As a business and real estate litigator, I'm not afraid of a street fight. I've successfully tried cases to verdict in every Trial Court branch of the Commonwealth. I also have significant appellate experience in the Appeals Court, Supreme Judicial Court, and First Circuit. I am often found litigating real estate disputes such as adverse possession, boundary disputes, zoning appeals, condominium disputes, breach of contract, title and landlord-tenant cases. I am also well-versed in alternative dispute resolution and mediation, where a majority of cases are settled these days.

As a real estate conveyancing attorney, I founded TitleHub Closing Services LLC, which does full service settlement agent work for national, regional and local lenders, as well as individual borrowers and sellers.

I love writing about real estate issues on my blog and other publications. I'm grateful to have published or contributed to articles in the Wall St. Journal, Boston Globe, Banker & Tradesman, Financial Times, Bloomberg News, and the Associated Press.

But what gets me out of bed each morning is the variety and "spice" of life experiences which comes with the practice of law. Every day there is a new set of problems to solve, new personalities, and it's never boring!

You can reach me at rvetstein@vetsteinlawgroup.com or at 508-620-5352.
