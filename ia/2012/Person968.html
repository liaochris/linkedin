Lee Smallwood's Summary

Lee is a digital comms consultant with specialist knowledge in integrated online direct, data and digital marketing techniques. In addition to providing overall support to the account management, creative teams, agencies and internal comms, Lee works to explore, develop and implement the most effective ways of reaching those 'hard to reach back teeth' – their target audiences.

Lee has worked on projects and campaigns for:
