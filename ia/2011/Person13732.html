Enrico Bianchi's Summary

My expertise comes from years of involvement and experience in the video post production and the computer graphics arenas. I began in the early 80s as a film and video editor for commercials, then for the following 6 years as a leading European technical support and demo artist for the Italian subsidiary of TechExport, an American firm distributing and supporting high-end 3D authoring systems in Europe.
In 1990 I co-founded the Italian subsidiary of Softimage, a Canadian company leader in 3D Authoring applications later acquired by Microsoft Corp.
While with Softimage Italy I founded a software development group that later became part of the Softimage Special Project Team in Los Angeles. With Softimage Special Project Team I had the opportunity to work with the major special effects and computer animation companies such a Disney, Digital Domain, DreamWorks, Centropolis FX and others.
In 1997 back to Italy I co-founded Phoenix Tools in Milan, together with part of the Special Project engineers and specialists, developing solutions and providing worldwide high-end consulting for the special effect and videogames industry.
I've been involved in public founding researches in the field of advanced technologies for the realistic integration of real and digitally generated imagery, working with International broadcasters and important European and North American Universities.
Since 2002 as expert in mutimedia platforms and architectures I'm a senior consultant for large mobile phone companies in taly Milan area (Vodafone and H3G). Starting from 2008 at H3G Italy, I'm the Team Leader of the "DMS International Project" following the Streaming Platform adopted worldwide by all H3G Opcos.
In June 2010 I've started a cooperation with Studio Azzurro Produzioni for renewing their multimedia framework based on Free Open Source Software.

Specialties

Computer Graphics, Computer Animation, Editing and Compositing, Multimedia and TV Broadcasting, Audio/Video Compression and Streaming, Multimedia asset management, Mobile TV platforms, Web Architectures, Optimization
