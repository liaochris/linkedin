James Cooper's Summary

Social Media Advising and Training:

As Social Media Co-ordinator at Brock University, I regularly give social media advice to clients -- staff and faculty members. Specifically, I show clients how to use social media to improve their marketing and communications results.

Along with giving advice, I conduct regular training in social media and blogging best practices for clients.

And, as a continuing education instructor, I teach social media classes to business professionals and the general public. I focus my classes on how to use Facebook, Twitter, YouTube, LinkedIn and Flickr in personal and professional life.

Social Media Monitoring, Development and Management:

I monitor and track online "buzz" about Brock University on a daily basis. The web marketing team, which I'm a member of, uses this information to decide when and where to take actions of greatest benefit to the university's brand.

I also manage and develop the university's main accounts, blogs and communities on social web sites, including Facebook, Twitter, YouTube, LinkedIn, Flickr, Tumblr and Foursquare. In doing so, I have multiple responsibilities: community management and engagement, content creation and repurposing, performance metrics and benchmarking, and cross-platform marketing.

Web Content Training, Support and Development:

I have a vital role in training clients around campus to use the Drupal web content management system. I hold regular hands-on sessions in which I train clients to create web pages and add content with search engine optimization and usability in mind.

Beyond training, I provide clients with ongoing web support, troubleshooting, editing and design solutions. And, in the process, I get many great opportunities to create and develop microsites within the university's web architecture.

If you want to find out more about what I do at Brock, go to http://brocku.ca/marketing-communications/social-media

Specialties

Social Media Optimization (SMO), Search Engine Optimization (SEO), RSS, web marketing, web metrics, social media metrics, Google Analytics, Drupal content management system, HootSuite team management, web community management, emerging media, word-of-mouth marketing, experiential marketing, integrated marketing communications, public relations, science and research communications, wine and beverage marketing, business development and relationship management.
