Vamsi Seeta, PE, LEED AP, BCEE's Summary

Over 11 years of experience in design of water and wastewater treatment facilities with emphasis on sustainability and renewable energy efficiency, which includes project management, master planning, feasibility studies, proposals, pre-design reports, water & wastewater survey, audit reports, detailed design engineering and cost estimation.

Project managment experience includes managing and monitoring subconsultants, project scope preparation and contract negotiation, preparation of invoices and processing, budget projection, monitoring and analysis, preparation of project schedules, resources allocation and management.

Specialties

* Wastewater Treatment - BNR -simultaneous NDN, MBR, SBR
* Water Treatment - Municipal, Ion Exchnge, Membrane - Reverse Osmosis
* Digester Gas - Purification systems - H2S, Siloxanes, Halides, waste gas flares
*Renewable Energy Systems - IC Engines, Fuel cells, PV systems
* Trenchless Sewer Rehabilitation - Non-circular large diameter sewers
* Odor Control Systems - Chemical, Biological
* Preselection / Pre-purchase - Bid documents for water/wastewater equipment
