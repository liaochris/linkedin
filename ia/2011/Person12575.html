Diane Hamilton, Ph.D.’s Summary

Welcome to my LinkedIn page. I focus on giving people information to help them improve their lifetime potential. I have experience in many industries including online education, software/computers, pharmaceuticals, banking and real estate. I have trained corporations in areas such as time management, emotional intelligence and Myers Briggs. I currently work as an online professor for 6 different universities. I teach mostly business-related courses to bachelor-, master- and doctoral- level students. I am a contributing writer to several websites including Forbes Media's site: Investopedia.com. My daughter, Toni, and I wrote the book The Young Adult's Guide to Understanding Personalities. Other books I have written include: How to Reinvent Your Career and the Online Student User's Manual. Future books in process include: Ten Things the Young Adult Needs to Know to be Financially Savvy. For more information regarding my books, check out www.drdianehamilton.com or see my blog at http://drdianehamilton.wordpress.com/.

Diane Hamilton, Ph.D.’s Specialties:
