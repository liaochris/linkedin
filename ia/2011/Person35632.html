Not to do anything by halves, by time is divided between 3 core businesses;

Organiser

TWiSTldn - This Week in Startups London

Venture Capital & Private Equity industry

July 2010 – Present (8 months)

I organise TWiSTldn, a meetup group for entrepreneurs and investors in the London area. We hold monthly meetups, currently at The Collective, Camden Town.

The format of the meetup is to network among peers in a laid back and casual atmosphere. The core element of the event are the 5 or so pitching companies who present their business or idea.

Those presentations & businesses are then rated by the attendees. The winning pitch then goes forward to represent TWiSTldn and pitch live on the popular Internet Show This Week in Startups, flagship show of the This Week In network, hosted by Jason Calacanis.

Global Meetup shows pit the best ideas from cities around the world, including London, Paris, Madrid, Rotterdam, Sydney, Male as well as many of the main US Cities.

To apply to pitch at a TWiSTldn event, visit www.twistldn.com.
