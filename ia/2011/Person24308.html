Kerry Thompson’s Summary

I am an expert consultant with over 11 years of experience with online brand building, creating web strategies and developing engaging user experiences for nationally recognized financial institutions, cause-based companies, and political organizations. Services include strategy development, online marketing, UX design & web design that gets measurable results.

Currently working as the Web Marketing Manager at SchoolDude.com. In this role I am responsible for our company’s web strategy and leadership for our overall web presence, including development and execution of the company web marketing plan to drive business and measurement of the plan’s effectiveness.

My duties primarily revolve around identifying web-related opportunities that will increase revenue and successfully plan, internally sell and implement these strategies as well as execute and increase inbound and outbound marketing effectiveness, improve websites for lead conversion, and manage paid and organic search strategies.

Additional past and current clients include Contactology, Triangle Art Works Inc., Carilion Labs, ActionAid USA, BBYO, Triangle Technical Recruiters Association, EMILY's List, Dancer Publishing, Fidelity Investments and GlaxoSmithKline. Recently partnered with hesketh.com as the Creative Director for a complete overhaul for the Southeast's largest, privately owned banking corporation.
