Jackie Jaeger’s Summary

My many years as Private Events Director (Pops For Champagne / Gordon) coupled with decades of experience in the Hospitality Industry (PRA Destination Management, Grand & Wells Tap, Harry's Cafe, Hotel 21 East, Hilton & Towers) are now focused on Voon de Var. What sets Voon de Var apart from other Private Events planners is our "partnership" with Chicago-land restaurants, Night Clubs, and Venues. These relationship allow us to offer our clients the ideal location for their occasion, demographic and budget. Whether a corporate function (meetings and conventions, dine arounds, team building, transportation, entertainment) or social occasion (weddings and receptions, showers, memorial services luncheons, birthdays, bar mitzvahs, bachelor dinners) we plan and execute to the smallest detail with a fresh and creative approach.
Voon de Var is a preferred service provider for HelmsBriscoe.
