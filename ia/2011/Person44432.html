Tom Williamson’s Summary

My passion for engaging people was around long before the social media craze took the world by storm. Spending time in the UK & US working in restaurants & on cruise ships, I realised I had a knack with engaging people and converting them into regular guests who would follow me one restaurant to another.

In 2009, I was appointed the Business Development Manager of The Highway hotel. Quickly developing their social media strategy to include a strong and successful presence on Twitter, Facebook, Foursquare & a WordPress blog, The Highway’s turnover increased & they soon saw how effective social media is in comparison to traditional print, radio & TV media, leaving a sizeable gap in their marketing budget.

At the AHA Awards in 2010, The Highway were awarded Best Marketed Hotel in SA – General Division. Their submission was based on their social media presence. Judges were impressed by over 10,000 users engaging with them on Facebook, over 1300 Twitter followers and 100′s of Foursquare ‘check ins’.

After receiving news an unexpected move to Sydney in April 2011, I founded Twillyon, a social media consultancy business, with an aim to teach small businesses you don’t need a big budget to run a successful social media campaign & teach all businesses how to engage & form relationships with their guests successfully.

I takes my years of experience and apply them to your business, wherever you are in your social media strategy. Twillyon offers real life, achievable solutions to further expose your brand, create brand frequency and increase your ROI.
