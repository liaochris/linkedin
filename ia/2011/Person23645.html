Kate Wickham’s Summary

My art career began after I turned 50 and realized it was time to quick thinking, "one of these days" and take action.

Color is the inspiration and dominant attribute in my work. I have always been drawn to vibrant organic abstract and impressionistic works, particularly and passionately to water colors; they flow, merge, push, explode, granulate, gradate, lift, and stain.

Actively nurturing my creativity has opened a channel for an abundant flow of ideas and solutions in every aspect of my life.

Eclectic Expressions is a cell of artists' studios with gallery space and art classroom space. Additionally various instructors and faciltators will hold classes and workshops of community interest.

Eclectic Expressions is located across the street from the National Historic District in Arlington, TX. It is in and adjacent to the lowest income areas of Arlington. A non-profit organization is being created to finance activities and materials for those who would not otherwise have access to such resources.

Kate Wickham’s Specialties:
