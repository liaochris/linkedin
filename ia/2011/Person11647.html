Lindsay Hill's Summary

Lindsay Hill Design.
What we’re all about. As a partner, what this means to you.

We’re all about you.

At Lindsay Hill Design our mission is to provide you with substance, style & satisfaction. And we take this seriously. But we don’t take ourselves so seriously that we can’t have a few laughs along the way. “Having fun” isn’t a policy around here, yet we believe that if you’re enjoying your work, your work will communicate it. That’s why we say, “SUBSTANCE, STYLE, SATISFACTION + a few laughs, guaranteed.”

Lindsay Hill Design takes pride in working as a team with and for our clients to deliver a product best suited to meet your needs. Teamwork has been vital to our success: we partner with each client to create solutions that are unique, and reflect your vision and distinct personality. While our staff is fully dedicated, every project receives the personal attention of Lindsay Hill, Principal + Creative Director.

Lindsay Hill Design is a full-service design & communications firm located in Charlestown, Massachusetts. We have been in business for over 20 years, producing outstanding, creative solutions. Our services include strategy, positioning, branding, creative development, web design, and social media.Our award-winning creative accomplishments are provided for a wide variety of corporate, nonprofit, and public sector clients.
