Jens Baltrusch's Summary

My passion for all sport related topics, the enjoyment of an active and social life which are connected with sport, as well as my desire to assist in peoples lifestyle improvement has led me finally to follow a truly global career in areas of sports & wellness. I studied sport coaching and sport management in Germany and, after finishing University, started to travel right away.

Since then, i worked versatilely in the fields of sport media, sport marketing, sport consulting, sport coaching and personal training in Germany, Australia, Turkey, Switzerland (and, of course, online) and did further studies (sport management, sport coaching)

At the moment I am working as entrepreneur in areas of multi media production, online marketing, sport seminars & coaching, personal training and sport gear development in Zurich/Switzerland.

In case you are interested in a business partnership (e.g. for online distribution of sport goods, writing services, common video or ebook productions) please contact me via jens@jensbaltrusch.com

With best wishes, stay acitve!
