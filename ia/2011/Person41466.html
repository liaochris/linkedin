Stephanie Abrams’s Summary

A graduate of Cornell University School of Hotel Administration, Ms. Abrams moved to Orlando, Florida to pursue a career in sales with Marriott International. She then moved to New York City to sell cutting edge fractional ownership property for Hilton Worldwide.

Realizing the connection between social media and business growth, Ms. Abrams decided to leave her career in sales to utilize her social networking expertise in the business world.

Combining her sales and marketing experience with her underlying passion for helping others succeed, Ms. Abrams partnered up with Deirdre Leone to develop an online marketing and social media company that would take the pressures of an ever-changing online world off the plates of successful businesses and personas. Gabbaroo and aStar Interactive, a full service digital marketing and public relations company, was born through a dream, passion and drive to help businesses thrive in an online market.

Ms. Abrams past experience includes facilitating “Relay for Life” at her alma mater, Cornell University, one of the American Cancer Society’s most profitable fundraiser at Cornell, this event raised hundreds of thousands of dollars annually for cancer research.

For more information on Ms. Abrams, please contact her at Stephanie@Gabbaroo.com or Stephanie@astarinteractive.com
