Stephanie Sansoucie’s Summary

Knowledgeable and versatile professional with a strategic and tactical user experience approach that leverages expertise in user-centered design methodology to translate user needs, business objectives and technology capabilities into satisfying user experiences for e-commerce and informational websites.

Solid background in user experience methodologies spanning the full user-centered design lifecycle, including: design strategy, task analysis, user needs analysis, competitive analysis, persona development, quantitative and qualitative research, web analytics, heuristic evaluations, surveys, interviews, benchmarks, wireframes, visual comps, prototypes, usability testing and cognitive walkthroughs.

Extensive project management experience leading cross-functional teams through the project life cycle resulting in the delivery of a solution on time while meeting and exceeding both business and customer expectations.

Expert communicator with the ability to lead discovery workshops, present solutions and motivate teams.
