Jennifer Castle's Summary

I have accumulated 20 years experience in sales both consumer sales and B2B sales, in a number of industries from cosmetics to construction to events to real estate! I have owned couple of small business and gone through all the lessons one does in managing and running a business. After years of sales training, seminars, reading and researching the greats, and working with sales teams in various organisations, I have identified areas that are often overlooked in most businesses.

One was in effective marketing and sales systems, the other in personnal development, recognising skill sets and developing individuals and teams that make a company soar.

Even though I already had a business degree, I decided to specialise and became one of the first in Australia to complete Jordan Belfort's Straight Line Accreditation Program to become a Straight Line Persuasion Sales Trainer, completed Anthony Robbin's Business Mastery Course and became an accredited StreetSmart Business Advisor. I also joined forces with Sue Aspin, whom I have worked with and known for 20 years, who brings her expertise as a pschologist specialising in personal development, mindfulness training, recruitment and business systems to the table.

Together as ESP Business Solutions, we assist businesses to find solutions that drive reults from marketing and recruitment through to motivation, negotiation and closing the sale.

Our goal is to assist businesses in achieving their visions through advanced sales pschology and persuasion techniques and strategies that allow will their results to soar.
