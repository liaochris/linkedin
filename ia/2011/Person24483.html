Kevin White's Summary

Having studied to work in the travel industry I started out working for an independent Tour Operator & Travel Agency (TV Travel Shop) in the call centre, moving into the commercial department after 1 year to gain more experience.

After 2 years at TV Travel Shop I made the choice to take a position at lastminute.com and was there for over 6 years gaining experience in product management, call centre management & account management. My last role at lastminute.com was a partnership management role dealing with key & strategic partners in the hotels sectors, flights sector, closed user groups sector and membership clubs.

As of January 2010 I have taken up a new role with a Hotel Representation company - Cara Hotel Marketing. I manage a team of 2 to provide sales to our hotels throughout Mexico & the Caribbean via our key tour operator & travel agent partners.

Specialties

Cruise Product, Account Management, Partnership Management, Strategic Partnerships, Ancillary Sales, Product Management, Commercial Negotiations, User Generated Content, Marketing, Budget Planning, Hotel Contracting, Hotel Rate Setting, Media Advertising, Marketing Planning, Incentives.
