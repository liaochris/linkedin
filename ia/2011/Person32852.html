Nikki Schiro’s Summary

Using the language of portraiture, through painting and drawing, I investigate physical, social and psychological relationships conditional to my surroundings, encounters and personal experience. I’m interested in what affects choice making, what layers and colors personality, what shapes us into who we are. My paintings and drawings are both investigations and documentation of my world, obsessive fretting, theory-making, findings, and fantasy.

Nikki Schiro’s Specialties:

portraits, figurative painting, drawing, color
