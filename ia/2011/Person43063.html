Theresa Reed’s Summary

I've been working as a professional Tarot Reader for over 15 years. I am an avid blogger and love writing and reading about Tarot. You can check out some of my tarot writing at: http://blog.thetarotlady.com

I also teach yoga and own Inner Divinity Yoga Studio - a unique yoga studio that only allows 3 -4 students at a time. This is a great place for those of us who don't like to be in a large studio. Whether you are self conscious or simply like one on one, my studio will serve your yoga needs in a fun and laid back manner.

Recently, I have begun a new side project - teaching women how to use social media networking to promote their businesses. My main target market is mature women (over 40) who are scared of technology. I am admittedly a social media junkie - and have found that it really helps my businesses to run better and more profitably. I hope to help light that same fire for other women so that they may see the computer as their friend rather than some intimidating machine!
