Matthew Pitt's Summary

I have operated at a senior level in Retail for 10 years.

3 Mobile and Vodafone Australia Merged in a 50 / 50 joint venture in June 2009 creating a new company called VHA. I am currently managing the Retail Support and Development of VHA's Company owned Stores.

I have also served as National Operations Manager - Retail at 3 Mobile for the last 6 Months, and as the National Sales Manager, 3 years prior I also held the position of Regional Manager NSW / ACT for 3 years.

I have also worked as a Regional Manager for Kodak Express Retail for a period of two Years and held various Management positions in Retail over the last 16 years.

Specialties

My core capabilities involve Sales and Retail Operations, financial management and employee development. I have a sound understanding of Retail inventory Management, Retail Category Management, and Multi Site Management.
