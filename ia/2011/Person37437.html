Robin Moore's Summary

10 years experience in online marketing and the build & management of websites. I have worked both client-side and agency-side & bring broad digital skill-set to projects.

My team provide a structured approach for web development & online marketing projects big and small. We gain a thorough understanding of a client's commercial and competitor environment before analysing and outputting; online marketing plans, information architecture & technical specs. We then guide the projects through design, build and out to final deployment.

Specialties

/ Competitor analysis & benchmarking.

/ Web & project management.
