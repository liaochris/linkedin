Akisia Grigsby's Summary

Akisia Grigsby is the Founder / Visual Brand Consultant of Akzas formally known as Atomic Designs Inc., a multi-cultural brand-imaging agency and visual communications design studio founded in 2000 to deliver creative concepts, branding ideas and design services to companies in the fashion, beauty, entertainment, sports, business/non-profit, financial, technology and political campaigns. Ms. Grigsby has a proven 18 year tract record, serving as creative director of Sean John clothing and Bad Boy Entertainment, amongst others. Due to her extensive and numerous multi-million dollar campaigns in the Urban and Hispanic markets, she has been able to successfully access the psychology of trends and the mind of the hispanic and urban communities.

Mr. Grigsby, an alumni of Parsons School of Design, is a Philosopher at Heart, an Artist by Trade and Entrepreneur by Spirit. While maintaining a successful business in the most competitive areas of the work, New York City and Jersey CIty, she enjoys her true passions: painting, drawing and designing.

Specialties

Brand Imaging, Design, Illustration, Business Management, Creative Direction, Advertising, Retouching
