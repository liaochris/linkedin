Joshua Sturgill’s Summary

After working several years as a Commercial Vehicle Operator I decided that it was time for a change. A friend introduced me to Geospatial Technology and from that point forward I was hooked. After attending classes in York, PA and a great internship opportunity I managed to secure a position with the City of Annapolis where I have worked since. As with any government entity there are several departments that each use GIS in a specific way. This has given me a unique perspective since each department could be an industry unto itself. I am currently pursuing an Associates of Science Degree in Geospatial Technology at Harrisburg Area Community College and continue to expand my knowledge and talents.
