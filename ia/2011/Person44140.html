Anthony M Turner’s Summary

Working hard and getting nowhere? Most small business owners feel this way at times - some all the time.

Our/my small business clients appreciate the way we get them off the work hamster wheel they call a business and back into their lives.

Many comment on how our combined coaching, mentoring and skills development training totally supports the integration of learning in a practical way that's relevant to their business.

They appreciate the fact our methods are simple, down to earth and relevant to small business and how we offer easy to learn and easy to use business/marketing skills drawn from our ‘real life’ experience and from the training we have undertaken in Personal Development, General Management, Marketing, Strategic Planning and OH&S.

Anthony holds qualifications in Management, Training & Assessment, is a Mentor with SBMS, Author of Dream to Reality...connecting heart & business and Hamster NO MORE...less wheel, more llife, is a registered life/business coach with CoachNetwork & ICF, a ROQUE facilitator, a Workshop/Seminar Presenter with Small Business Victoria and developer of the 'Hamster NO MORE' online training & coaching program.
