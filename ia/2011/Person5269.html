Bill Murray, MBA - Sales Trainer’s Summary

With over 25 years of business experience, I have focused the last 8 years business coaching, sales training and employee training over 100,000 individuals and companies to a new level of results. Specializing in developing sales people, leaders and managers for bottom-line and/or top line results while living a great life. I inspire and empower my clients to take practical action immediately and guide them through to self fulfilling wants and goals. With industry experience ranging from luxury travel, real estate, health and fitness to skincare and cosmetics, I have worked as an International performance management consultant and I am 'in touch' with the issues and challenges faced by real people daily. As a confident, and engaging presenter, I present my rich knowledge in dynamic ways that educate audiences to use proven tools/principles and specific ways to apply them with hands on tools.

At RSBST we are an employee owned and operated business, Rock Solid Business & Sales Training, Inc. goes far beyond the average business coaching and employee training company. Each member of our experienced team has had P&L responsibility. When we coach and train our clients for results we always consider the financial implications to their business.

We are not a franchised coaching company. Our team members bring with them relevant and varied experience whether that involves Fortune 500 companies or entrepreneurs and start-ups. You’ll quickly see from our client list and by reaching out and speaking with our partners that our programs and services have worked in each industry that we’ve entered. In fact, we have a very strong track record of exceeding expectations! Your relationship with RSBST affords a systematic approach to your sales, service, and leadership needs with verifiable metrics and results.

When you want employee training, sales training, sales management training, or leadership and management development, RSBST continually outshines the competition
