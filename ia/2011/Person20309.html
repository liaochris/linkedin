Jenny Cardwell's Summary

Jennifer Cardwell is currently the Search Specialist at MENATSA. Prior to joining MENATSA, Jennifer was previously the Head of Research for Talent Partners in the Qatar Office. She was responsible for the strategic management of the research function within the office.

Jennifer has experience with senior level and specialist searches across a number of industry sectors including; Oil & Gas, Banking & Finance, Real Estate Development, Sports Investment, Media & Aviation

Jennifer has worked at a management level in the Hospitality and Retail industries in both the UK and the Middle East.

Jennifer completed a BA Retail Management from Glasgow Caledonian University.

Oil & Gas, Banking & Finance, Real Estate Development, Construction & Contracting, Sports Investment, Media & Aviation
