Rusty Eldred’s Summary

Raised by my grandparents I've absorbed a knowledge that can only be learned from experience. I started working at the age of 13. During my years of working I've learned what it means to work hard. I relate to the man who gives his back every day to make a living. Ones success can only be judged by self-sufficiency.

In a working environment I feel a good team gives value to individual efforts. I've noticed that positive energy is a great way to help the office environment be more productive and attitudes can make or break a company. I love being challenged, creative and helpful. At the end of the day, if I've accomplished those three things, I'm pretty satisfied. I have a natural passion for what I do. Like most designers I am consumed by inspiration the minute I wake up; leaving me with a hint of childish wonder.
