Jim Kean’s Summary

Jim is presently CEO and Founder of WellnessFX™ – an online consumer wellness and longevity company focused on personalized nutrition strategies for individuals and driven by the rapid evolution of sophisticated diagnostics from the fields of blood chemistry, genomics, epigenomics, protein expression, metabolomics, etc…

In the 1990's, Jim was President and Co-Founder of Sapient Health Network (SHN). SHN pioneered the concept of online consumer health communities, building some of the largest and most loyal groups on the Web today. The company’s service was named one of the “Seven Best Websites of 1998” by Business Week. In 1999, WebMD purchased SHN for $185 million. SHN became the foundation of WebMD's Consumer Health offering.

After WebMD, Jim served as Entrepreneur in Residence with Alloy Ventures of Palo Alto. While with Alloy, he participated in the investment process of one of Silicon Valley’s oldest venture firms.

Jim co-founded the Metolian Resort - North America’s first outdoor vacation community design based on Natural Step principles of sustainable energy, water, and trash management and focused on an outdoor, active lifestyle. The State of Oregon wrote legislation for Ecoresorts based on the Metolian design.

Jim is a former Board Member of Portland Center Stage (PCS) – a highly regarded regional theatre. During his tenure as Treasurer and Chair of the Finance Committee PCS achieved recognition for developing and opening the first Platinum LEED performing arts building. An avid outdoorsman, in 1999 he hiked the Pacific Crest Trail from Mexico to Canada. In his twenties and thirties, he danced ballet and musical comedy with several regional dance companies. He graduated in 1984 from Lewis & Clark College in Portland, OR. In 1991 he received his MBA from Tuck School at Dartmouth.

Jim Kean’s Specialties:

Within consumer health Jim's concentration is on internet oriented consumer health and wellness, performance sports and nutrition. Within sustainable development his expertise is in vacation/hospitality communities, wellness amenity development, and outdoor recreation. Recently he has been speaking on longevity and consumer health.
