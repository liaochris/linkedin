Paul Burwood's Summary

I help organisations improve the efficiency and effectiveness of the sales, business development and marketing functions to ensure they meet or exceed targets.

This involves developing business models that commercialise Professional Services, Products, Services or Solutions and profitably take these to market. This includes developing and implementing relationship management approaches, routes to market, product lifecycle management and sales & marketing campaigns.

Specialties

• Setting up & structuring sales & marketing organisations
• The setting up & selling through, single & dual tier distribution channels
• The commercialisation of Intellectual Property
• Setting up and structuring a direct sales force, advising on recruitment and interviewing
• Sales force optimisation: advice on what to measure to ensure success
• Developing the sales funnel & sales funnel management
• Designing motivational sales incentive schemes
