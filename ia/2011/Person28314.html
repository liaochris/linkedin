Mark Millar's Summary

Mark is co-founder and owner of PraeMedica, with eight years in healthcare digital marketing and communications.

From it's inception in 2002, Mark has grown PraeMedica into a successful agency working with pharma clients in the UK, Europe and USA across several therapy areas.

He has hands-on experience of building brands across multiple digital channels, inc web, CD/DVD, mobile and email.

Mark leads PraeMedica in delivering engaging, targeted multimedia programmes with rich audio and video content.

Mark has an honours degree in Computing Science and a Diploma in Digital Marketing. He speaks at industry events and writes for publications on digital marketing within the pharmaceutical industry.

Mark runs PraeMedica with co-Director Douglas Dick.
