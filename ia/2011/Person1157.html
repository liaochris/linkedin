Alban Martin's Summary

Passionate about the internet and the new technologies, I have written several books about Social Network and Social Media impact on society (economy, culture & politics). I am also co-founder of the Social Media Club (Paris), and blog on a regular basis about innovation in democracy (http://cocreation.blogs.com)

After having designed serveral community services for Orange R&D, I am now in charge of Social Network Strategy for the web, mobile and TV international activities of Orange : advertising & partnership possibilites, business development through online communities. I am also teaching online communication at la Sorbonne
