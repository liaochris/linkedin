Rifa Bhunnoo's Summary

I am a freelance digital project manager with over 15 years of agency experience. I am technically minded, creative and have a marketing background. I’m also genuinely passionate about digital media and technology, I've worked on digital media/online solutions since 1993.

I am also part of the Arc Alliance: http://www.arc-alliance.com
producing creative concepts for TV, Film & Digital advertising.

I will bring: a pro-active positive personality, strong front-line project management skills, with a great eye for detail, knowledge of industry trends, best practice and emerging technologies, knowledge of MS Project,the ability to create meaningful Gantt charts and cost analysis summaries.

I get involved from initial conception to generating ideas to managing the process and I make sure that the work is delivered on time and on budget.

Specialties

I am a team leader when necessary and have proven my ability to work at director level. My skills include: defining the project scope, and the resources involved, controlling work streams, troubleshooting and identifying risks and change, ensuring all project output is of top quality, presenting work, managing client expectation and controlling change, minimising re-work through application of quality procedures.
