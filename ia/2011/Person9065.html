Colleen O'Brien's Summary

I have four years of major market experience, which was spent at KOMO 1000 Newsradio in Seattle.

I began at KOMO at the age of 19 and quickly worked my way up from an overnight editor to working as an anchor, reporter and editor for drive-time shows.

During my time at KOMO I studied at the University of Washington and earned a degree in Communications with a minor in Women's Studies.

Currently I am working at KXLY TV in Spokane as a morning reporter and web editor. As reporter I am responsible for presenting local, national and international breaking news, quick, one-minute news updates every half hour (self produced) and also a special segment called "Cool Clicks" in which I introduce our audience to a new and interesting website.

I am passionate about web journalism and at KXLY I am in charge of editing and maintaining KXLY.com. I also write stories for the website that are specifically geared towards online audiences.

I am interested in all facets of journalism and invite new ideas and opportunities.
