Stacie Hackl's Summary

Stacie is a business owner and leading expert in the area of business networking and social media. She has spent the last two years helping professionals learn how to use social media to grow their businesses and build stronger connections. She manages a LinkedIn group called Link Greater Green Bay, which has over 1,700 members and involves both an online forum and monthly face-to-face networking events. She is also president of her local HBBA networking chapter. Stacie has real world experience on how to use the latest social media tools to grow a business and make new contacts. As owner of an insurance agency, she has proven that social media can play a significant role in new business development. Over 90% of her new insurance customers are referrals as a direct result of the trusted relationships she has developed by helping others.

As a result of her business networking experiences, Stacie co-developed IntroduceMe360.com. The goal was to overcome the challenge that many business professionals have limited time to spend on social media and that they could benefit from a more automated way to make new contacts. The new website is free and has been embraced with enthusiasm. www.IntroduceMe360.com
