Nick Barcio’s Summary

I am currently employed full time by PNC Financial Services as a Senior Loan Support Specialist in the Consumer Loan Center. Here I work directly with both customers and branch representatives to update and close a variety of consumer loan products.

Also, I am a Slippery Rock University of Pennsylvania alumnus holding a B.S. in Resort Recreation Management in which I was awarded with both Outstanding Junior and Senior of the Year certificates. To further my education I completed a minor in Business Administration.

Concurrently my senior year I presided over the Slippery Rock Student Government Association, Inc. and held a seat on the Board of Governors for the Pennsylvania State System of Higher Education.

I have held many officer positions throughout my tenure as a college student and constantly work on my leadership and communication skills.

I understand how important education is to personal and company success which is why I plan to continue with a Masters of Business Administration after a couple years of professional experience.
