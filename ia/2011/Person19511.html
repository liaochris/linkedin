Javier Tomé’s Summary

For more than 16 years Javier has been involved in training and development, with university groups to business professional development, in Venezuela. For the last 10 years has dedicated to business consulting, developing knowledge and experience in different fields and industries.

As Systems Engineer of the Universidad Metropolitana in Venezuela Javier started his young career as a software developer. Thanks to his drive, thinking style, leadership, and expertise development he was promptly promoted to management positions.

Obtained a Master’s degree at Universidad Complutense de Madrid in Spain, in Management Consulting, knowledge that allowed him to develop career helping business owners and executives to develop their business to their fullest potential throw Organizational Modeling and People Development.

Passionate about food, restaurants, and hospitality obtained a Culinary Arts’ Degree from Le Cordon Bleu College Miami to introduce himself to the Hospitality Management Industry, where he had the chance to work restaurants and hotels in the city of Miami, and today is helping professionals in the industry to become more productive and successful through best practices.
