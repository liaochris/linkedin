Claire Harbeck’s Interests:

For the past year, I have served as an ambassador for the National Marrow Donor Program’s “Be the Match” registry, whose purpose is to recruit peripheral blood stem cell and marrow donors and solicit financial donations. I became involved with this organization in the name of my good friend and former professor, Carl Stam, who is battling an aggressive form of Non-Hodgkins Lymphoma. Since January 2010, we have recruited 1,853 donors and raised $55,837 by conducting an online campaign and live drives in California, Illinois, Indiana, Kentucky, Michigan, New Jersey, North Carolina, and Virginia.
