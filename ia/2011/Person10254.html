Danny Hernandez’s Summary

I have over 11 years experience developing award-winning concepts and campaigns for marketing and advertising projects. These have included websites, e-mails, games, banners, print campaigns, online marketing efforts and guerilla advertising.

I believe all remarkable ideas start with collaboration. Whether it’s joining forces with strategic planners, account managers, copywriters and other teams… working together is the key ingredient to seeing ideas fully realized and taking creative to the next level.

My client list includes: Ameriquest, DS Waters, eHarmony, EPSON, E! Online, Paramount Pictures, Sierra, Southern California Edison, Vivendi Universal Games, Sony, Logitech, Wells Fargo and Mammoth Mountain Ski Resort.
