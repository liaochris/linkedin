Eva Kempinger's Summary

I am a professional career coach and human resources advisor with many years' experience in the corporate and public sectors. I accompanied my partner to France, Germany, Italy and the United States – and succeeded in sustaining and developing my own career whilst abroad. My personal experience of setting up a new life in different countries makes me the perfect coach for expats and their accompanying partners in Holland. I know what it means to live abroad and the challenges expats face.

Job Search or Career orientation? That's the question. Please make an appointment for a free orientation meeting at www.expatsatwork.com

Expats@Work is official partner of the Expat Center in Amsterdam (www.iamsterdam.com/expatcenter)
