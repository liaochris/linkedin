Glennie Mercer’s Summary

"Unleash Your Potential to Go Beyond Your Best!"
...that's what I want to work with you on, and it's FUN!

Whether working with individuals or teams, I work in relationship with clients to inspire and enable choice and action!

I established Glennie Mercer & Associates in 1996 and have been working with wonderful clients in the public, health care, and not for profit sectors for the past 14 years.

I DESIGN customized solutions and FACILITATE interactive, generative processes to help leaders and teams clarify directions; identify shared principles of practice; create meaningful plans; develop enhanced interpersonal skills and deepen relationships.

I COACH individuals to maximize their personal and professional potential.

Having worked extensively within the health and public sectors, I am familiar with the challenges and opportunities of leading and working in these highly complex, unionized and ever changing environments.

Enabling and supporting difficult conversations, by creating respectful spaces for dialogue is a key strength
