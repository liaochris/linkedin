Mark Hughes's Summary

I worked for several years in politics, including for a U.S. Senator, for the ACLU in Arkansas, and on many local, state, and national political campaigns. After several years of management jobs, I began a career in journalism that included online, print, and radio news writing and commentary. In Washington, D.C., I was a producer on a weekly radio program and frequent on-air news reporter and commentator.

Taking a position for a public policy lobbying organization on Capitol Hill, I began working in media relations and campaign advertising for several years, during which I started a side career writing for film and television. Some of the ads I wrote and produced aired on Air America Radio and other outlets.

I am now a full-time screenwriter, having sold an option for a screenplay as well as being hired to complete several other film projects. In addition, I am co-creator and writer for a new sci-fi television series making the rounds at several studios. I've co-created and co-written a new horror film franchise that is currently in pre-production.

Specialties

Screenwriting, television writing, editing, on-air production, Web layout, branding, criminal law, lobbying, campaign advertising, media relations, press releases, news stories, commentary
