Annelies Verhelst's Summary

Profiel is ook in het Nederlands beschikbaar (zie rechtsboven)

Since 2008 I write content for the web and advise companies about Online Marketing. My focus is on SEO (Search Engine Optimization) and usability. Social Media and Networking is also big part of my expertise.

No nonsense!
I sell no-nonsense advice, if I think something is unnecessary for your company, you will know.Honesty is a big part of my philosophy. I want to be inspiring, or link you to people that could be inspiring and useful to you. That's also why I love to think with you about new ideas, concepts. Getting you and the rest of the world on higher levels of operation is very rewarding for me. That's not always easy, I will probably tell you things you don't like.

Search engine friendly
My writings will be search engine friendly, they will be fit to you needs and will be clear and understandable for you target audience. But, I will always try to teach you how you can do certain things yourself. Writing for web and SEO are no rocket science.

Being an editor
Apart from having my own indepent business, I also work fulltime as an editor for a computer magazine. Testing all the nice hardware goodies and writing a good review about them is my main purpose. Editorials about computer-related events and new media are also part of my work.

More about my passion and work can be found at my personal blog (Dutch) www.anneliesje.nl and my experience and expertise can be found at www.insperide.nl.
