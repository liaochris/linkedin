Guillaume DEBAR’s Summary

Young development worker from France, Sciences-Po Alum, passionate about non-authoritarian ways to encourage positive social change, curious about everything.

I have an M.A. in Development and International Cooperation from the Institute for Political Studies, with a particular focus on social change through non-formal education and ICT.

My abilities include ensuring analysis is translated into action, creative problem-solving in difficult situations, effective team leadership in multi-cultural environments, and delivering measurable results.

  • Project Manager

    Tostan

    (Non-Profit; Non-Profit Organization Management industry)

    August 2009 — January 2011 (1 year 6 months)

    Jokko means "communication" in Wolof, a national language in Senegal.

    With the launch of the Jokko Initiative, Tostan and UNICEF have formed a partnership that positions them at the forefront of SMS-based innovation, focused on empowering people to harness the power of accessible mobile technology to improve their lives and those of their children.

    As Project Manager and team leader of 5, I am responsible for the national strategy of project implementation in Senegal and refinement of the model for scale-up.

    I build and maintain good relationships and networks to regional and national, authorities, other NGOs, UN and other relevant programme partner in the region. I also promote cooperation and synergies between Tostan International and national offices, local/foreign universities, potential tech partners and authorities. I develop donor strategies and help fundraise for future expansion of the model ;

    Finally I ensure that the project is in line with Tostan financial policies and strategies, with emphasis on quality, cost efficiency and impact and an efficient coordination with Tostan’s MERL Dpt. on issues related to M&E strategy and Tostan’s Communication Dpt. for external and internal promotion of the Initiative.
