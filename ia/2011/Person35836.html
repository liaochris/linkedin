Rajat Rakheja's Summary

I have been in the Human Capital Management Consulting Industry for the last five years. I am basically a training & performance solutions consultant focusing on enhancing performance outcomes through learning solutions.

Primary areas of my consulting work, so far, has been around frontline & middle management (Sales & Service) functional & skill training through learning engagements, and working on building learning as an enabler to high performing teams and as a retention tool.

I have worked with large organisations in this domain - Bharti Resources (now called Centum Learning) & NIS Sparta - to improve performance outcomes around productivity / customer satisfaction, attrition and allied indices, through my role(s) as Account Head.

I have also worked with OD Alternatives, a boutique organisation development consultancy, working in the domain of organisation transformation, change management and building high performance teams.

I am now back with Centum Learning!

Specialties

Human Capital Management, Training & Performance Coaching solutions, People Consulting, Sales Consulting
