Andy Albright's Summary

We are America's #1 Seller of Mortgage Protection Insurance. We market life insurance policies on behalf of insurance carriers and independent agents through our nationwide network. We represent Mutual of Omaha, Old Mutual, Foresters, ING, American General and others!

If you're looking for an opportunity to help people, have more freedom and earn more money than you ever thought possible, let me show you how at NAALeads.com.
ndy Albright is the co-founder and President / CEO of National Agents Alliance, arguably the country’s largest and most successful insurance marketing organization specializing in life event marketing and sales. Andy has appeared in regional North Carolina magazines and newspapers, and his company NAA was recently selected from a pool of 35,000 area businesses as the North Carolina Triad’s Fastest Growing Company by Greensboro’s Triad Business Journal. It is his following of loyal independent insurance agents, along with Andy’s aggressive drive and dissatisfaction with the status quo that makes NAA such a success. While most Americans are calling it a day, Andy can often be found making calls to his top managers during the midnight hour. His managers relish this time, and are willing to take calls from Andy at all hours of the night just to get that extra bit of personal advice and guidance.

Andy has a strong desire to see his agency managers, often his best friends, succeed. To outsiders, his rugged and raw determination can be intimidating – but to those who are closest to him, his family, his top managers and his corporate executive team, know Andy has a masterful skill of motivating people to take action.

Andy Albright is a proud graduate of North Carolina State University and the WolfPack club.

Andy also just completed his first book - 8 Steps to Success, and sales have been insane.... in the first 3 months!
