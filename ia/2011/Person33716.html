Pamela Ziemann’s Summary

What's your biggest challenge getting your message out to create more support for your cause? Email me at www.OnlineSpeakingSchool.com

"Pamela Ziemann's "Giving Voice to Your Cause" has great tips for getting the word out about what you are passionate about, whether it's a humanitarian non-profit, your business or motivating people for grassroot causes. If you are interested in showing the world how compassionate you are and conveying it with humor and fun, "Giving Voice to Your Cause" is a must read." -Laura Legere, Author, It's Not Weird Anymore

I help Nonprofit professionals and social entrepreneurs who want to articulate their message in a simple, powerful and memorable way. I help them connect with supporters to get results and make a significant difference in the world.

Want a free e-course on how to think on your feet and answer tough questions in the moment? Go to: www.OnlineSpeakingSchool.com and sign up in the left-hand column.

Here are some of the outcomes you can expect when working with Pamela:

1) Be comfortable and confident speaking to anyone

2) Trust your intuitive impulses and say what matters most

3) Learn to tell personal stories that make an impact

4) Externalize the passion you feel inside and inspire others

Working with a coach is fun and will get you there faster!
