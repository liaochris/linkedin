Chris Michael’s Summary

I write copy at Draftfcb and political humor for the Huffington Post.

Both the Sorbonne and Humboldt-Univerität took turns trying to educate me before I graduated from Bard College, with a self-made major in -- ready? -- psycholinguistics and its effect on comedy cross-culturally. (Yeah, you can imagine how that panned out, so I went to the School of Visual Arts for creative copywriting.)

Now my goal is to be a New Yorker cartoonist and the next Augusten Burroughs while I figure out how the antics that landed me in the principal's office as a child will pay for a one-bedroom apartment.
