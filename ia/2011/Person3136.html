Anne Lataillade’s Summary

In another life (10 years ago), I've worked in risk management. I stopped working to take care of my children and I've created my foodblog, Papilles et Pupilles (http://www.papillesetpupilles.fr/) in June 2005.

Thks to its success, I began to work as foodwriter for different websites.

My other blogs :
- Les recettes pour allergiques de Papilles et Pupilles (gluten free, eggs free, .. recipes.)
- Culinodates (http://culinodates.blogspot.com/) is a calendar to publicize food and drink blog events. Whether it's a one-off or a regular event, this is one of the few places to tell everyone what your food blog is up to.
- A table et compagnie (http://www.atableetcompagnie.com/) is a blog co created with 3 other foodbloggers.
