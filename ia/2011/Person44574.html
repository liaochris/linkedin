Jennifer Webb's Summary

I am the jewelry designer and artisan of Copper Reflections. We have been working very hard to create the most unique handmade jewelry in animal, wildlife and Native American themes since 1985. Using innovative techniques that we have developed over the years to craft our jewelry and gifts as well as paying attention to fine details, we may have succeeded in creating the most unusual jewelry that you have ever seen. We are looking for Wholesale Jewelry and gifts distributors.

Copper Reflections has been established in wholesale jewelry business in the United States and Canada.for over 25 years. We value your experience and would love to trade ideas and knowledge. We think this will help all of us to be more creative in business.
