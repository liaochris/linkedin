Jason Weisberger's Summary

Jason Weisberger most recently served as Chief Operating Officer at Federated Media Publishing (FM), the market leader in effective social media advertising. At FM he was responsible for day-to-day management and performance of the enterprise through its most explosive years of growth, 2006-2009.

He has led development, engineering, operations, corporate development, product management and finance organizations at companies varying in size from seed-level startups to multinational members of the FTSE 100. Over the course of his career, he has been responsible for managing aggregate product lines in excess of $800M in annual revenue and has successfully closed over $1B in M&A activity with such companies as Pacific Bell, Cable and Wireless, and Exodus Communications.

Additionally, he served as CTO and VP of Operations at SoftAware, Inc. a start-up in Marina del Rey, CA. He performed a key role in taking this start-up from $50K to $20M in annualized revenue, and leading it to a public equity sale to Digital Island (ISLD.)

His diverse array of talents provided opportunities for him to serve in a consultative role for the following organizations: Associated Northcliffe Digital, the National Science Foundation, Panorama Capital and Soros Fund Management, LLC. He has participated as a panel member and speaker at a number of conferences and events, as well as delivering Santa Monica College's year 2000 Commencement address. He also serves as Publisher of the internet’s leading independent voice on internet culture and esoterica, Boing Boing, where he also occasionally contributes photography and written content.
