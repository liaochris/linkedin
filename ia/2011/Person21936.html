John Stauffer's Summary

John is the Regional Strategy Director within the 360° Digital Influence Group at Ogilvy in Hong Kong. He works in social media across a dozen markets in Asia Pacific for clients ranging from Ford Asia Pacific, Shiseido, Ikea Hong Kong, and Fidelity.

Prior to moving to Hong Kong, John worked in Ogilvy Washington D.C. developing social media training programs for a variety of clients, both writing the curriculum as well as delivering the training across the enterprise.

While in the United States, he was a guest lecturer at Johns Hopkins University, Georgetown University and the University of Maryland speaking about the role of social media in marketing and communications programs.

He’s contributes to the 360° Digital Influence Blog, Fresh Influence, as well as the Asia Digital Map.

Specialties

social media, word of mouth marketing, china, asia pacific, science of influence, social media training, monitoring tools
