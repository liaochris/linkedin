Jessica DaSilva's Summary

I'm a law student at Florida Coastal School of Law looking for an summer associate position/internship/externship in Florida for summer 2011. My preference is criminal law.

My background in journalism and communications, coupled with my budding legal education, make me a strong law student. As a result of my experience as an account executive for Ron Sachs Communications, a student newspaper editor and reporter, and press intern for Senator Bill Nelson, I have a solid foundation in writing, editing and research. My experiences required me to research political issues to ghostwrite opinion columns and blog posts for political figures, such as the CEO of the Florida Hospital and Senator Nelson.

While serving as editor-in-chief of The Independent Florida Alligator, I oversaw a staff of about fifty student journalists and edited all of the newspaper's copy for daily publication. I had the final say in decisions regarding content and trained the staff in advanced online reporting techniques. My experience and education ensure that I will provide competent assistance to the prosecutors in your office.

Growing up as the daughter of a police officer, I was naturally drawn to criminal law. My decision to pursue criminal law solidified after I participated in an in-class mock trial as a prosecutor last fall.

I'm currently involved in the Women's Law Student Association, the Technology Law Student Association, the Criminal Law Society, and the Hispanic American Law Student Association.
