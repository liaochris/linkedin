Sr.Sales Executive

Tata Infotech Ltd.

Public Company; 501-1000 employees; Information Technology and Services industry

October 1998 – March 1999 (6 months)

TIL hired me to grow business in GIS applications and the first project I initiated for company was to map entire state for generating land records for revenue department. A very interesting ground level project where we created prototype and demonstrated to the Ministers/officials. This project was later renamed as "Bhoomi", that went on winning awards all over the world and now in wide use at several states in India.
- Bhoomi requires data from land records generated by revenue department and in local scale format (chains, guntas etc). This data is used to create 2D maps of the property along with ownership and land quality inputs (irrigated, soil type etc). Each of these maps are pieced together to form overall land mass - like a mozaic. One can present survey number and get a one page printout of his land records at any Kiosk. This project resulted in resolution of several land-right cases and not many new cases come up for boundary dispute now.
