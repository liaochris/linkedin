Bill Moller's Summary

"This was the most important class my daughter took" one parent told me when I taught my method in college. On sales or client calls few understand how to use the expressive qualities of voice, face and body. They speak poorly...especially young recruits who pepper their sentences with "like, ya know" and "uh." They slouch, mumble and don't convey confidence or authority. They lack the professional polish to leave strong positive impressions.

My boot camp program can help anyone who has the desire, break free from a lifetime of poor presentation and speaking habits.

Not only does the program make you a better sales person, CEO or manager...it'll make you a better and happier person in ALL your interactions in every department of life.
