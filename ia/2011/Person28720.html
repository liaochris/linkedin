Mary Henry’s Summary

Senior Professional in Human Resource certified through the Society of Human Resource Management with 10+ years of diversified HR experience. I am the President and Founder of HR on Demand LLC.

HR on Demand LLC our goal is to provide business owners with human resource support in the areas of strategic planning, recruiting and retention, compensation and benefits (Total Rewards), employee and labor relations, federal,state and local compliance, human resource development, and safety and risk management. HR on Demand will provide the most current and proven methods to support achieving the financial and operational goals of your company.

Mary Henry’s Specialties:

Interim human resources support. Building organizational capability, union negotiation, competency based interviewing, career development,succession planning, employee engagement projects
