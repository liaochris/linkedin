January 2007 – July 2007 (7 months)

The the members of the ACC has a very aggressive desire so see informatics technologies advance aggressively and they have dedicated a team to see what they can to from various fronts to help it to happen.

I'm impressed to see that such a large organization "gets it" and is dedicating member dollars to the effort.

Among various efforts, the ACC is one of the principal fiscal sponsors of the IHE (Integrating the Healthcare Enterprise - www.ihe.net), a worldwide effort to promote, test and certify compliance with connectivity standards such as DICOM and HL7. Part of the IHE effort is the annual "Connectathon" where vendors bring in their product and do a proof/test of the connect-ability based on various patient care test cases. The ACC is also working to develop a "tool kit" for Cardiology practices to help them spec, select and implement an EMR solution. Believe it or not, I'll be leading and working on all these remarkable efforts and more!
