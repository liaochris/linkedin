Bram Timmer’s Summary

Bram Timmer is an award-winning art director, designer, and photographer that specializes in innovative new media design. Bram applies his creativity and expertise across a variety of media, including: web design and development; e-learning and interactive media; print materials; packaging; information architecture; illustration; film and photography. Bram’s past and present clients include commercial, entertainment, financial and government institutions of all sizes, including MasterCard, Nivea, Kraft, Hershey's, SC Johnson, CTV, CFL, Hewlett Packard, AMD, Adobe, Bombardier, Applebee's, Hilton Garden Inn, TELUS, Home Hardware, EnCana, Survivorman, Alex Cuba, Meridian Credit Union, University of Calgary, Mount Allison University, Calgary Stampede, FITC, National Film Board of Canada and others.

In addition to his freelance design career under the alias Beside, directing and photography career under the alias Aside, Bram has founded and operated Dutch Blue Media and two/signed, provided inhouse design and development services for: White Iron, an award winning digital media agency in Calgary, AB; Webtek, where he worked as Director of Flash Development; and Blatant Media, where he provided animation, print, interactive and other services to support their e-learning platform.

Bram has a wide variety of interests and passions that inform his design work, including architecture, art history, digital culture, and visual arts in various mediums. In addition, Bram is Chief Editor of Digital Softbox Magazine, and contributes to a variety of design related blogs and portals. Bram also participates in a number of design and art-focused events, groups, exhibits, not-for-profit causes, and occasionally guest speaks at conferences, colleges and universities.
