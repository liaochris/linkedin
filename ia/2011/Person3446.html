Howard Amoils’s Summary

Despite a short break of 5 years the IT industry has been my chosen home for over 25 years. I have worked in South Africa, New Zealand with nearly 20 years in Australia that at times included SE Asia.

My roles have varied from Programmer, Support Manager, Systems Engineer in the early days. Since 1991 I have worked in Sales & Marketing. The focus has always been enterprise solutions for large corporates including Hardware Infrastructure Solutions, Systems Integration, Enterprise Software Solutions and Training.

In my current role as Business Development Manager at IMI, I am working with companies providing systems integration and deployment of technology services to the edge of the network i.e. PCs, servers, printers, networking equipment, wireless, POS systems and more. IMI specialises in quality deployment and systems integration that enables our clients can focus on their business while IMI's proven integration services and methodologies install and configure the equipment with minimal disruption to the end user and the client's business.

My experiences outside of the IT industry have broadened my overall business skills and understanding of the challenges experience by businesses and business owners worldwide.
