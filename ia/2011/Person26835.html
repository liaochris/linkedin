Lori Sutorius Jones’s Summary

Advertising and marketing is a personal passion-center. I have worked with my father Kit for over 20 years and have loved every minute of it. He started the firm when I was a teen and enjoyed running an entrepreneurial business out of his home office. Shortly after I began working with Kit, we landed a regional rental car agency and they requested to visit our offices and meet "the team". Again, we were officing out of my parents home and Kit and I were "the team!" The request came in on a Thursday: Friday we found office space and signed a lease; Saturday we purchased used office furniture on a shoe-string budget; Sunday we moved in and Monday we were open for business (with several family members as "fill-ins" for the empty offices). Today we enjoy an incredible staff who share our same entrepreneurial spirit!
