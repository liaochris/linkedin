Drew Sygit’s Summary

First Michigan Bank purchased Citizens First Bancorp of Port Huron from the FDIC on 04/30/10 and First Banking Center of Lake Geneva, Wisconsin on 11/19/10.

I currently lead the Shared-Loss Team that manages those assets to maximize their recovery.

Several monthly & quarterly reports are required by the FDIC for reimbursement under the Shared-Loss Agreement, I'm also responsible for creating and delivering those reports.
