Greg Miller’s Summary

Greg Miller is the Engineering Talent Manager at Ultimate Software. Greg handles the Recruiting and Talent Management for Ultimate Software's award-winning Software Development Team. Ultimate Software earned the “Best Product Development Team” award from the prestigious American Business Awards national organization in 2003 and 2008, citing recruiting top talent as one of the key points in the award submission.

Greg recruits individuals with a track record of top performance at all experience levels from interns to very senior software development professionals to join our Agile Development team.
