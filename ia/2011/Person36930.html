Richard Dyer's Experience

Ticketing Director

Skiddle Ltd

Events Services industry

May 2001 – Present (9 years 11 months)

Skiddle is The Uk What's On Guide. We provide a free facility for event promoters across the UK to list their events online and sell tickets through our independent service.

We are a primary ticket outlet working directly with promoters across the country and parts of Europe selling tickets for comedy events with a capacity of 50 people through to exclusive deals of 20k+ capacity festivals.

The site currently receives approx 650,000 unique visitors a month as people browse our local town pages to view events in their area. We add to the experience by offering nearby restaurants and hotels as well as extensive event news in their area.

We offer a self service facility for all promoters by visiting www.skiddle.com/promotion

I welcome all connections from people who are looking to promote events, sell tickets or create new online opportunities and partnerships for our users.
