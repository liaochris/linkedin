ABOUT ME:
Having 20+ years banking experience, many as a Vice President of Mortgage Lending, & 10 years as managing owner of ERA Professional, has provided an excellent & unique background in real estate, as well as the contacts and in-depth knowledge of both the real estate & banking world. This has been invaluable, as we have been able to successfully close many transactions that otherwise wouldn't have happened.
Currently, we are extremely successful, dealing with, understanding, and closing REO, Short Sale, & Foreclosure properties. Because these transactions are vastly different from "normal" real estate transactions, in-depth knowledge of is absolutely necessary. In addition to my banking, lending, & real estate background, I am certified for Short Sales/ REO properties; plus, ERA Professional brings to the table a complete solution for REO properties, including property stabilization, repair, maintenance, management, valuation, reporting, marketing, transaction coordination with our full-time closing coordinator, closing agents, & REO/Short Sale experienced, trained real estate agents.
I am originally from the Clermont area, attended Clermont High School, & have been in the business community as a professional for 28 years. I have four children, a new grand-baby, & am co-owner of both ERA Professional, and Coldwell Banker Commercial Atlantic Properties, both located in Clermont.


ABOUT ERA PROFESSIONAL:ERA Professional is a family owned company, & has been for over 20 years. We started out in 1986 as Puryear Realty, founded by Clyde Puryear, a Lake County native, and former citrus grove owner who specialized in land & development .
Today we have over 50 highly trained real estate professionals & staff, all dedicated to the client & a seamless transaction. Unlike many real estate companies, we have full-time staff responsible for marketing, customer service, & closing coordination.
