Sally Ormond's Summary

As a professional freelance copywriter, Sally currently works with companies in the UK, Europe, USA and as far afield as Japan and Trinidad & Tobago. Her clients range from SMEs to Blue Chip Companies and cover a diverse range of industries.

Working as part of their team, Sally helps save them time and money by creating powerful and compelling sales copy that boosts sales and their market visibility.

Her broad range of copywriting services cover all their on and off line marketing needs with SEO copywriting a speciality. A more recent service that Sally has added to her portfolio is voice-overs.



Sally's working life began at NatWest Bank on their Management Development Programme. After a career break to start a family, she worked in fundraising for an International charity producing brochures, posters and direct marketing letters for regional fundraising campaigns.

From 2001 to 2007 she undertook a a BA(Hon) degree in English Language and English Literature through the Open University. She graduated in 2007 with First Class Honours.

In her spare time, Sally is a volunteer wish granter with the Make-A-Wish Foundation UK.

As for life outside of work, she is a mother to 2 boys and a keen cyclist and gym fanatic. She has also writen a children's book - The Adventures of Tilly the Tractor and Freddie the Fire Engine.
