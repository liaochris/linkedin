Milan Radosavljevic's Summary

Construction Management academic leading one of the world's best Project Management courses at the prestigious School of Construction Management and Engineering, University of Reading with several years of senior industrial experience and with particular expertise in:

- team dynamics and effectiveness (e.g. circumstances and parameters that make teams succeed or fail)

- innovative behaviour (e.g. enablers and barriers to creativity and innovation in the industrial context)

- project planning (e.g. particularly detailed short-term planning including scheduling, control, resource planning and continuous improvement)

- technology implementation process (e.g. selection and successful implementation of new technologies including materials, tools and IT)

- communication (e.g. bringing down communication barriers within project teams comprised by individuals from different organizations)

The most important goal is to effectively use and transfer the knowledge into the industry either through collaborative and/or contract research, CPD and consultancy.

Specialties

Project Management, Project Planning, Chaos Theory, General Systems Theory, Autopoietic Theory, Organizational Behavior
