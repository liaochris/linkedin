Shelley Roth’s Summary

As a sales & marketing pro, I assist businesses in achieving higher levels of productivity & growth. Whether it's a solopreneur or a Fortune 500 company, I'm passionate about making a difference with people & businesses & use socal media training, consulting and speaking as a "springboard" to growth for all I work with.

From my humble beginnings as an elementary school teacher & guidance counselor in Slippery Rock, PA, to managing sales teams for Fortune 500 companies, I embarked on the next phase of my career, working with small business' seeking to learn how to sell more stuff and founded Springboard Ventures, Inc. in 1999.

As President, I have worked with many promising, emerging Houston and Austin based companies; I have trained & spoken on topics including: LinkedIn for Business Growth; Facebook for Business; Building Fabulous Facebook Fan Pages; Social Media 101; ‘Living Life Fearlessly’; and ‘Breaking Through Your Glass Ceiling’.
From my varied experiences, I am a sought after speaker, trainer and business development consultant, passionately working with business on incorporating social media tools into their marketing strategies.

I have served on the advisory boards of technology companies, & served as a mentor and member of the Rice Alliance for Technology and Entrepreneurship, the Houston Technology Center and MIT Forum, & worked with many entrepreneurial ventures in various stages of development. I am a founding member of The Greater Houston Partnership, Emerging Business Council and have served as a judge for the Rice Alliance SW Business Plan Competition and Texas A&M Technology Transfer Competition. I have worked closely with angel and institutional investors in the entrepreneurial communities of both Houston and Austin.

Beginning my career in education, I was nominated as teacher of the year, served as an assistant principal and guidance counselor and created and launched the first state mandated program for gifted students in PA.
