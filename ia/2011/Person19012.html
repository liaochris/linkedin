Jane Abma's Summary

I have had 5 years experience as an art director and illustrator in the fashion, music and publication industries and as a result I have sound knowledge of what designs youth markets respond to. During this time I have art directed cover shoots for magazines as well as taking on the roles of stylist and make-up artist.

I aspire towards becoming more involved in the music and fashion industries whilst developing my client base in the areas of web and print.

Clients include: Sensis, Gift Of Gab (American hip-hop artist), Pigeon John (American hip-hop artist), Lost At E-Minor, Pacific Magazines (Channel 7 Australia), Cornerstone Records, Australian Traveller Media, Luigi Rosselli Architects, Healthy Food Magazine Australia, KORA Organics, Deep As Funk, Rootbeer (American hip-hop group), Smart Population Foundation, Halfstar Clothing Australia, STOC Clothing Australia, Outsource Media Australia, Dan Banks Band, Independent Community Housing Organization, Holly Brisley (actress), ClubVIP Australia (music entertainment and touring), Sounds On Sunday, Exergy Energy Australia, Meso Creative Studio.

Specialties

1000 Words specializes in magazine art direction, logo design, corporate stationery design, illustration, annual reports and other small print publications, CD and vinyl art, music event/festival art as well as tour merchandise.

Also offered: web design (photoshop screen designs and/or full website design), company or personal promotional material (media packs, brochures, etc), fashion look books, wedding invitations, packaging, on screen presentations and newsletter design (print/web).

Interests:

I am a freelance art director, graphic designer and illustrator based in Sydney, Australia. I have 5 years of experience, 4 of which have been running my own freelance business as an art director and designer. I began my career designing and illustrating for a record label and music/pop culture magazine, and have since worked my way on to art directing various travel publications and photo shoots, developing Australian Super Model Miranda Kerr's skin care range KORA, and working on album designs for musicians such as USA's Pigeon John and Gift Of Gab. I aspire towards building up my own freelance studio 1000 Words, while designing and illustrating for more musicians and fashion labels along the way. I am always chasing new and exciting projects and looking forward to meeting the inspiring people behind them.
