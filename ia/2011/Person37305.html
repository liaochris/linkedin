Robert (Bob) Presser’s Summary

I am an IT and Business Consultant to companies in the New York City tri-state area who are users of IBM Midrange (IBM i, AS/400 and iSeries) computers and software.

I have worked both as an employee manager and as a consultant for companies in a wide range of industries. I have personally designed, developed, implemented, trained users for and supported many types of business applications.

I am looking for new clients in my region or for a senior IT Management position at an established organization.
