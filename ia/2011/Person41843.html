Mark Stringer's Summary

PrettyGreen is an independent Brand Incubator for entertainment, lifestyle, blue-chip and start-up brands.

We could say we’re the new breed of this and that, and that we’re unique, but isn't everyone? We'd rather say, we think we’re relatively smart, enjoy cracking strategic brand problems, love seeing work come alive, and just want to work with like-minded people.

Whether you’re looking for strategic counsel, help in developing a new business, an elusive integrated idea, or have a discipline specific brief: Media Comms, Promotions, Experiential, etc. we'd like to think that we can help.

We work with exciting start-ups like La Maison Fontaine Absinthe alongside well-established and revered brands like Red Bull, Cadbury, and Nando's - If any of that sounds interesting why not get in touch.
