FREDDIE RAVEL's Summary

Bridging his distinguished career with the preeminent evolutionary leaders and global music icons of our time, Tune UP to Success Founder and Visionary Freddie Ravel has emerged as a creative force for innovation by empowering personal and professional growth through Music.

Utterly captivated by Music since age 5, the Grammy nominated composer, pianist and educator began performing worldwide with Brazilian master Sergio Mendes by age 23 where Universal Music discovered and released three top-charting solo albums including the #1 in-the-USA hit, “Sunny Side Up”.

Driven by his passion for exposing the true roots and relevance of how music can enhance our lives, Ravel’s concert appearances span six continents from the heart of the Amazon to the Sultan of Brunei, the King of Thailand and the boardrooms of corporate America.

With a keen understanding of integrating cultures through music, Ravel’s career includes producing, recording and composing with Earth, Wind & Fire, Al Jarreau, Madonna, Prince, Quincy Jones, the Boston Pops and rock legend, Carlos Santana...the list goes on.

Destined to develop a breakthrough system that applies Music to Life, Ravel launched Tune Up To Success in 2002. With rave reviews from clients Apple, NASA and Wal-Mart to enthusiastic endorsements with colleagues Dr. Deepak Chopra and Jack Canfield, Ravel has designed a unique and powerful methodology to bring society from dissonance to harmony.
For more, please visit freddieravel.com.

Specialties

Business consulting, Corporate and Executive Training, Live Performer, Speaker, Team Builder and Edutainer utilizing a unique methodolgy based on Music as a multi-tasking power tool for life skills. Master teacher and bridge builder with clients ranging from Microsoft, Citi, Apple, NASA and Wal Mart to Madonna, Andre Agassi, Dr. Deepak Chopra and Carlos Santana.
