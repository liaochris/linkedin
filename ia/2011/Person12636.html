Kathryn Brown's Summary

I am an art historian and literary critic specializing in nineteenth-century French painting and literature, contemporary art, and aesthetics. My book, Women Readers in French Painting 1870-1886, is forthcoming with Ashgate Publishing.

Teaching responsibilities have included preparation of curricula, lecturing and tutorial/seminar teaching for courses on law and film, nineteenth-century French avant-garde painting, art and sculpture in Europe and North America from the Renaissance to the present, contemporary French cinema, and nineteenth-century French poetry and prose.

Specialties

Areas of expertise: Nineteenth-century French art and literature, aesthetics, book history

Areas of competence: British and US contemporary art, film studies, law and film
