Jeff Andrews, PGA’s Summary

I have come to realize throughout my career that the business of golf is much more than a game. It is creating experiences and memories for customers and it is maintaining the long held traditions of the game, while managing team members, facilities and financial performance to the highest standards. I have experience in start-up operations, facility conversions, construction, multiple property management and facility restructuring. I thrive on the opportunity to convert a financially struggling facility into a vibrant cash positive club. I have experience in all types club management including; municipalities, public, private, destination and semi-private. I have worked for large and small management companies and thrive in both environments. In working at multiple facilites I have also directly managed food and beverage operations with revenues in excess of $4.5 million annually.

In addition to operations, I have a strong background in the corporate management side of golf. With aquisitions and due dilligince experience, corporate wide training and direction guidance, and corporate program development, I enjoy the opportunity to help mold and shape a company into a better organization.

My professional goals are to continue to grow in the golf and hospitality industry, while making leadership decisions that lead facilities into a better financial direction while growing service standards.

Jeff Andrews, PGA’s Specialties:

Improving guest service, teamwork, overall facility performance and instituting a sense of commitment to the overall improvement and success of a property.
