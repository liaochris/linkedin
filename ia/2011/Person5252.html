Bill Harding’s Summary

Fully trained and capable of servicing your pool and spa, including opening, closing, service and maintenance. We do weekly, bi-monthly, and monthly maintenance. We specialize in equipment repairs and replacements, leak detection and repair, Loop-Loc Safety Cover sales and installation, BioGuard chemical sales and testing for your pool and spa, HelioCol Solar pool and spa heaters as well as gas heater and heat pump installation. We are a coveted Platinum Level Hayward Warranty Repair Center, and also warranty repair centers for Raypak, Tigershark, and more...

Bill Harding’s Specialties:

Our specialties include mobile computerized water testing for your pool and spa, leak detection and repair on vinyl, plaster and fiberglass pools, and also equipment pad replacements and updates.
We have been contractors for American Home Shield, Americas largest and oldest home and pool warranty company for more than 5 years.

I was the team leader to locate, excavate and protect over 300 underground utilities during the fiber optic project and the quality control of the various components of the communications infrastructure.

I remained as Utilities Foreman until the track construction was completed.

  • Field Coordinator

The experience of a lifetime.

  • Aviation Submarine Warfare Technician

    (Military industry)
