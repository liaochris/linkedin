Aniela Ruseva's Summary

I have a Lawyer degree but I am entrepreneur by heart. I am interested in Marketing, Sales, Investment, Managing, Negotiations, Outsourcing, Startups, High-Tech and IT, Software, Real Estate, New Ventures so I work in these areas.
One of my company's current projects is WebIT – eMarketing & Digital Business Expo for Southeastern Europe. Some of the keynote speakers of the conference part are key execs from Google, the Guardian, BBC, Amazon, etc.
I am part of the crew of one of Bulgaria largest new media agencies - design.bg. In design.bg I am client service director.

I am also Event director of e-Academy - a professional qualification training center for digital studies.

e-Academy provides an unique platform for the market players of the digital and internet markets of Southeastern Europe to exchange ideas and business expertise and possibilities and also for the rest of the world to enter the regional digital markets, to find partners and explore new business options.

e-Academy is organizer of one of the Europe's biggest events on e-Business & e-Marketing - Webit Expo & Conferences. Webit is the biggest event of the industry for Central and Eastern Europe and the biggest B2B event for the region. In the year of 2009 more than 4300 people from Bulgaria, Romania, Serbia, Macedonia, Turkey & Greece visited the event.

Some of the speakers at the conference were key executives from Google, Microsoft, AOL, eBay, Guardian, Coca Cola, AdMob, Bytemobile, Hurriyet, IAB Europe's President, MMA Global's Managing Director for Europe and other more than 40 world renowned and regional digital professionals. With more than 65 media and supporting partners from the region Webit is the only business platform for Southeaster Europe for investors interested to enter the market and for the regional players to explore new business possibilities within the region and rest of the world.
