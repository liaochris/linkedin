Phyllis McElroy-Dicks's Summary

Phyllis McElroy-Dicks’ account experience includes work with G-CAPP, Living Well with Yoga, Broadway Across America-Atlanta, The Mansion on Peachtree, The Atlanta Broadway Series, Concourse Athletic Club, as well as Simon Property Group in Atlanta and Charlotte. Additionally, she has assisted with The Fresh Market, Recycle Safe Services and Atlanta Medi-Care.

Prior to joining the KCPR team she flexed her creative muscles as a Marketing Coordinator at WABE 90.1FM, Atlanta's NPR station. She also contributed to the Coventry Healthcare of Georgia team in an administrative role while attending college. A Washingtonian transplant, Phyllis holds a B.B.A. in Marketing from Georgia State University.

Phyllis administers the KCPR internship program including interviewing, hiring and training incoming interns. In her account work, she generates press coverage on behalf of her clients. She also heads up the media outreach efforts for KCPR.

Specialties

I'm experienced with creating marketing collateral including brochures, sales letters, written proposals, PowerPoint presentations, and other business documents. I've recently added to this experience e-newsletter administration, blog writing, as well as editorial articles, press releases, research reports, and creative print/radio ad copy.
