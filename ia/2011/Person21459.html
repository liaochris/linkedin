Joel Welch's Summary

Joel Welch served nearly seven years as a RCMP officer with investigative experience in Alberta and Ontario. He left the Royal Canadian Mounted Police on good terms in September 2007 to attend law school at the University of Western Ontario and subsequently received his Juris Doctor ("J.D.") degree in June 2010.

He has passed both his Barrister and Solicitor examinations as administered by the Law Society of Upper Canada ("LSUC"). Currently he is completing his legal articles with an expected Call to the Bar date of September 2011.

Joel is looking forward to future challenges wherever they may arise.
