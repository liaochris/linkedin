Laura J. Schachter's Summary

Professional Placement Associates is an executive search firm specializing in recruitment of health care professionals. We are experts in the field, having focused our efforts exclusively in the health care industry since our inception in the early 1970's. Over the past 35 years we have established an outstanding reputation for providing consistently high quality service to our numerous and varied clients. Our client base includes hospitals, certified and licensed home care programs, nursing homes, medical schools, physician practices, faculty practice plans, managed care organizations/insurance companies/HMO’s, as well as any organizations that employ healthcare professionals.

Specialties

Mid and Senior level positions in managed care, home care, Human Resources, Clinical Informatics and Information Technology
My recruiters handle a myriad of other healthcare fields
