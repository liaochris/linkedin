Alfred Krautgartner’s Summary

Since I started competing in many different sports , I have still heavily pursued and immersed myself in the world of fitness and nutrition. Working with some of the biggest sports, entertainment, and corporate celebrities in the world, I have been able to help my clients obtain physical heights and as a by-product, achieve many of my own goals and dreams as well.

In my new endeavor, my goal is to provide for people the opportunity to share my experience in Nutrition and Exercise and how to implement it in their lives successfully.
