Miss Susy's Summary

Growing up a Miami native, I developed a love and affinity with my city and South Florida. With roughly 4 years of blogging and making connections via MySpace and Facebook (to name a few familiar ones); I am an experienced Social Networker.

While my primary focus for social networking is on enhancing social interactions within Miami and other South Florida communities, I also act as a corporate social networker for various companies. These companies have entrusted me with the creative development of their online identity which is consistent with their corporate branding. Everything from creative SEO-rich page content to regular blog postings and setting up effective corporate pages on major social networks such as Twitter or Facebook. More than implementing a few catchy phrases, I assist in developing their internet persona.

On a more personal level, I have a devout passion for fashion, community improvement and non-profit. Over the past few years I have developed substantial working relationships with various businesses and organizations throughout South Florida.

I believe a thriving community is a community that works together to improve quality of life, advancement and growth. My work with local businesses and organizations is not only to assist in those areas but to also increase community awareness of local issues, develop unique social experiences/opportunities within communities and help the fellow entrepreneur get on their feet.

With a strategic and effective following of over 4,000 individuals across 6 mainstream social networks and 5 blogs, I believe my voice is effective and positive.

Specialties

Developing creative web content, corporate and recreational blogging, creating positive, influential and effective presence on social networks such as Twitter, MySpace, Facebook, LinkedIn, etc., coordinating community events, fundraisers and productions and making connections.
