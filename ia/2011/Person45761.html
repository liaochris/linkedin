Andrew C. Whitelaw's Summary

I have 15 years of experience in all aspects of Film and Video Post-Production. Titles/Skills have included: 3D Modeler/Lighter, CG Coordinator, Inferno/Fire/Tape-Op, Visual Effects Producer, Systems Administrator, Compression Specialist, Roto Artist, 3d Matchmover/Tracker, Compositor, Editor, and Visual Effects Supervisor. I know what works, and what doesn't. I have empathy for artists that receive poorly supervised footage.

Though I have focused primarily on film in the past few years, my wide-ranging experience has also given me the tools to help the emerging interactive web community work with and understand the world of video production and post-production. Aware that every page on the web has the potential to become a self-contained (TV) channel, my familiarity with emerging technologies allows me to educate and coordinate with creative professionals (around what was once a static medium). Though I came up in 'multi-media' in the mid 90's, it's clear we are about to witness the birth of fully cross-medium production.

I have been able to identify many of the caveats that can arise in tapeless acquisition and web-related developing non-traditional media. I am particularly able to help in pre-visualization, working with technology savvy DP's, and careful planning of ambitious multimedia sites, and visual effects shots. I prefer to be involved from the beginning, as it allows me to help clients save money in projects that can quickly scale out of control. Pipeline tightening is my latest focus.

I have been lucky to work with some of the most talented artists in LA and San Francisco (the world?), and have great relationships with individuals and post houses that can provide pre-vis, concept design, matte painting, 3d animation (photo-real and otherwise), and delivery for a variety of media (from the silver screen to the iPhone).

Specialties

Pipeline Development/Facility Building, Compositing, 3D Tracking, Set Extension, Matte Painting Integration, Visual Effects Supervision, Supervision for Video-Rich Media Websites
