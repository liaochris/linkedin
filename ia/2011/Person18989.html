Jamin Arvig’s Summary

Distribution, marketing, and fulfillment for the biggest and best water purification manufacturers. Specializing in sales of water filters, reverse osmosis systems, water softeners, refrigerator filters, whole house water treatment, drinking water systems, and more.

  • CEO

    (Wholesale industry)

    August 2001 — Present (9 years 7 months)

    Commercial Water Distributing is a source of water (and air) treatment products to water dealers, plumbers, hardware stores, offices, universities, corporations, and the military. The customer base for CWD is global, although the majority of the customers are located within the US. CWD is a master distributor for the worlds biggest and best manufacturers of water treatment products, including Pentair, GE, Everpure, Omnipure, Brita, Culligan, and many more.
