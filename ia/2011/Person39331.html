Scott Sullivan's Summary

Professional, positive, mature, experienced closer with international sales, sales and account management with operations experience. Strong originator (hunter), superb networker, trainer, mentor and coach. Vast experience building successful sales teams in all aspects of sales: lead generation, relationship building, cold calling, follow-up, strategic selling, account management, complex sales strategy, technical sales strategy, closing and project support.

Working in energy with emphasis on the renewable space, solar (PV), fuel cell and solar thermal for the last decade. Well versed in power electronics and solar PV. (2 inverter companies and one panel manufacturer).

Expert in state and federal rebate and tax incentive programs, including all renewable and emerging efficiency programs. Deep understanding of the utility space and how the politics and money work together at utilities. Well connected in the energy business domestically and abroad, commercial and industrial market segments. Executive Level contacts.
