Krystal M. Lechner's Summary

My Definition of Beauty:
It comes from within. It is something that makes you go "ah!". It is luscious curves, beautiful lines, combination of colors, or creating your best self. It is living up to your own expectations and appreciating everything around you. It is classic, timeless, experimental, ugly, twisted, or endlessly fascinating. In other words, it is what you want it to be. Once you find what makes you feel good inside, you express that through your own beauty.

Aspirations:
Create a worldwide community of divas through Smashing Divas. After all, we are divalicious and want to be fabulous daily. Together we can empower, transform, grow, change, laugh, and love together. Life's a beautiful journey, why not rise up and be divalicious together!

Chief Diva's Makeup Artistry professional aspirations:
Understudy well known makeup artists in Europe and New York.
Work published Vogue, W, Cosmo, Elle.
Public speaker and inspirational best-selling author.
Produce creative work with an edge and concept. Work with major and experimental fashion designers.
Produce shows and workshops that turn makeup upside down. The industry needs a bit shaking up!
Launch my makeup line! :)
Have work shown in art gallery and publish a book of life's work.
Empower thousands of women around the world, and host retreats for beauty and renewal. :)

Finally, ALWAYS be divalicious!

Specialties

Airbrush makeup, editorial, high fashion, beauty, wedding makeup. Teaching, writing, branding, marketing, sales.
Chief Divaship. Creative-holic.
