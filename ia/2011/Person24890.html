Sean Koppelman's Summary

Respect, dignity and integrity are the foundation for the manner in which I conduct business and treat people.

Using every available resource, I successfully match the needs of a select group of employers with the career goals of extraordinary professionals seeking change and growth.

My communication style/sense of humor and "brutually straight forward" personality are often what candidates note as distinguishing from others providing similar services.

"Magnet for Success"

Linking talented people with the companies that need them is the mission of a company founded by Sean Koppelman.

He made a career change in 1994 to the recruitment/personnel industry. That year he assisted in launching a new division for Access Personnel. In 1996, he was recruited away by the prestigious staffing firm CTI Personnel in New York City to start a recruitment division for them. Over a six year period, he generated in excess of $30 million in revenue and successfully placed hundreds of job seekers in new career opportunities.
