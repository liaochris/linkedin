Don Tomasian's Summary

Well the votes are in - Bella Funding, LLC. is the #2 Private Money Lender in the State of Arizona - according to Ranking Arizona Magazine! Hi I'm Don Tomasian proud Founder and CEO of Bella Funding, LLC. a private equity firm based out of Scottsdale, Arizona. Bella Funding specializes in "tough to do" transactions which are generally problem solving in nature. I can lend on any type of real property including raw land, private residences and single use properties. We perform best in the $100,000.00 - $5,000,000.00 loan range but can fund as high as $10,000,000.00. We prefer short loan terms between 12 - 24 months. From loan submission to loan funding can take a couple days to just over a week. Feel free to call me to share a scenario or just to introduce yourself - most of the time I can determine if we can successfully fund the transaction from the initial phone call..

Specialties

One of our specialties is simply "speed" In addition to our pool of private investors we are "self funded" so our clients enjoy the benefit of the financial bandwidth we deliver that perhaps some other lenders may not be able to offer.
