Jeanna Fournier’s Summary

Jeanna Fournier is a highly motivated professional, coming from a line of entrepreneurs. Jeanna’s entrepreneurial-spirit, compassion, and ability to exceed expectations make her a highly qualified professional who thrives in dynamic and quickly growing environments.
