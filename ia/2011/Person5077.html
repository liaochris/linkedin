Beth Feild Pisculli’s Summary

Beth is an executive coach and senior organizational development consultant with extensive experience in the field of talent management. She is passionate about the development of high potentials and multi-cultural leadership in organizations.

Through her consulting firm, Theories in Practice, Beth combines her expertise in organization development and her skills in program management to design and implement corporate leadership development, change leadership, succession planning, on-boarding, team building, and internal coaching programs. In addition, Beth serves as a executive coach for key talent. She partners with corporate leaders to identify performance goals and objectives and collect feedback from critical stakeholders; Beth then supports these leaders with deep developmental change through on-going, leadership coaching. Beth often partners with organizational talent in preparation for new, more challenging roles. She specializes in performance coaching, on-boarding coaching, team coaching, 360 degree feedback, and coaching in environments experiencing significant change.

In the spring of 2010, Beth relocated to London with her family to gain a broader range of global consulting experience and to support Roving Coach International with international business development. Beth's current clients include companies such as Johnson & Johnson, Biogen Idec, Red Hat, Royal Bank of Canada, IBM, Roving Coach International, and Quintiles. She holds a Masters Degree in Organization Development from American University and a Certificate in Business Coaching from Duke University.
