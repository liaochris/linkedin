Norm Yustin’s Summary

Living on 3 continents, working in 20 countries, and building over 100 brands – more than 25 of them #1 in their category – has turned Norm into a 24/7 executive marketer. Succeeding in C-level positions within large multi-billion dollar client organizations and a billion dollar advertising agency, has given him a well-rounded view from both sides of the fence.
 
Whether building brands on the client or agency side – for mega brands such as Macy’s, Claire's, JCPenney, Best Buy, Procter & Gamble, Estée Lauder, Mercedes Benz, Ikea, Polo Ralph Lauren, Johnnie Walker, Martha Stewart, Nintendo, Activision and Tommy Hilfiger to mention a few – Norm is driven to express his business acumen and creativity in unexpected ways to get results.

Dynamic change in the marketplace requires dynamic change in business thinking. And this is his specialty: bringing new ideas, structures, and ways of doing business, then operationalizing them within large organizations.
 
During his career, eight marketing campaigns have been awarded Effie’s. Fourteen have been acknowledged at Cannes, including a Gold Lion for media innovation. Other awards and acknowledgments have been at the Marketing Awards, Kelly Awards, Clio Awards, and the One Show. 
 

Norm Yustin’s Specialties:

Re-inventing businesses: Retail, fashion & beauty marketing. Training & development. iPhone apps and all digital and mobile marketing applications.
