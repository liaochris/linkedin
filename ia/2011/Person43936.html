Tonya Byrd's Summary

I love helping people with their real estate needs. Whether you are buying, selling or both, I can help you through every step of the way.

I really enjoy getting to know people by listening to their wants and needs. Everyone is different and by getting to know them I am better prepared to help them through the buying or selling process.

I am motivated to serve my clients to the best of my abilities. I will work around their schedule in order to make the whole process go smoothly. When I work with a client, they are my client for life. This means I keep in contact with my client throughout the process and beyond.

I am a full time, licensed real estate professional. I am a member of the following Associations: Houston Association of REALTORS®, Texas Association of REALTORS®, and National Association of REALTORS®

I have a Bachelor of Science degree in Business Administration from the University of Tennessee. My prior experience is in the logistics and transportation industry. My experience in this field has served me well as a REALTOR®. I am able to combine my past experience with my love of helping people. This enables me to help them get the best deal possible.
