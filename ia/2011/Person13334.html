Elfie Hayes (AMP)’s Summary

Elfie Hayes is the business owner of the Mortgage Intelligence office in Oshawa. Elfie's business is located in her professional building conveniently located in downtown 1 block from the new Courthouse.

Elfie is supported by a professional team of full-time licensed mortgage agents each focused on one particular aspect of their business. Together they have grown to be one the of fastest growing mortgage services locally.

Despite the recession their business has increased thanks to the high Internet visibility that they have created through their award-winning websites and their exceptional Social Media presence.

Mortgage Intelligence Oshawa is the only local Mortgage office to provide all of the following services:

*Credit repair (specializing in post-Bankruptcy credit repair)
*Re-finance Consumer Proposal-Re-build credit after 1yr
*Debt Settlement through informal Consumer Proposal
*Construction Financing for Self and Custom Build Homes
*Land Development and Project Financing
*Equipment Financing Division
*Commercial/Investment Mortgages
*Reverse Mortgages
*Plus Traditional Mortgage Lending-Institutional & Private.

Elfie's business is now based on word of mouth and personal referral, something that she is extremely proud of. Elfie has embraced the concept of referring her business partners in return. Something she is known for in her industry.
