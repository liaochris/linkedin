Peter Videv's Summary

Peter Videv serves as an advisor for Sperry Van Ness Butler Kane LLC, specializing in investment sales and retail leasing in Manhattan. With years of commercial real estate experience and a background in construction engineering, Videv brings together his technical experience, multicultural exposure and knowledge of the Manhattan market.

Prior to joining Sperry Van Ness, Videv served as a sales associate for A&I Broadway Realty in New York City, where he represented landlords in retail transactions, developers and European funds in investment sales. Previously, Videv served as a project and construction manager for Coteba SAM in the Principality of Monaco, where he oversaw the construction of a 5 -star 300 room hotel (Monte-Carlo Bay Resort), a convention center (Grimaldi Forum), and a 12- story mixed use building among others.

A resident of Manhattan, Videv earned a Master's in construction engineering from Ecole Speciale des Travaux Publics in Paris, France. He is fluent in French, Bulgarian, Spanish and Italian.
