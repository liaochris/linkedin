Derek Phillips’s Summary

You won’t see the words “guru” or “expert” anywhere in my resume or profile. I consider myself a student; always learning and open to new ideas and perspectives. I do use my ten-plus years as a digital strategist but as a way to get the conversation started.

My sweet spot is creative strategy. It’s what I do best and I have a good track record. I work best in collaboration with others whose strengths differ from my own. It’s how I learn and it’s what inspires me. That inspiration has led to some pretty cool campaigns and digital experiences. I’m always looking for the next one.

In the last decade I've been:
- Digital Strategist at The New Group where I am developing their Content Strategy practice area.
- Director of Content for Critical Mass, named Top Interactive Agency for three years running by Forrester Research. My creative team at CM won a bunch of awards. Check out my resume to see which ones.
- A Content Strategist for the famously doomed marchFIRST
- Web Manager for the largest child advocacy organization in the country serving 6 million members.
- Co-founder of the world famous Glorious Noise, building a readership of over 100,000 unique users a month through active engagement of online communities and the creation and management of top notch rock and roll writing.
