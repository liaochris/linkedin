Gavin Barrett’s Summary

Owner/Founding Partner + Creative Director, Rao, Barrett and Welsh, (originally Barrett and Welsh).

In pursuit of big ideas, he has: nibbled on pig’s ears (not on a live pig at the time); gone elephant-back in the Thai jungle; gambled in a Macau casino.

His commercials have been shot by: Deepa Mehta, David Carson and Louis Ng.

His poems: can be found in Penguin’s anthology of 14 contemporary Indian poets, Reasons for Belonging.

His ads: have run in 35 countries.

He has accumulated: 1 wife, 2 kids, a BA (econ.), an MA (eng. lit), a murderous guppy, a scruffy cocker spaniel poodle and 69+ awards for his work.

He cannot: sing.

Gavin Barrett’s Specialties:

Disciplines: advertising, marketing, ethnic/multicultural marketing, marketing strategy, brand design, speaking or teaching engagements. Sectors/category expertise: Alcohol, Automotive, Financial Services, Media and Entertainment, Real Estate and Development, Transit, Government, Not-for-Profit/Charities, ethnic markets. Specialisation: idea-driven advertising and design.

Bookwallahs also distributed Indian magazines like Stardust in Hong Kong, through Magwallahs, its magazine distribution division.
