Greg Short’s Summary

A Law Graduate from the University of Queensland, Australia I am CEO of Electronic Entertainment Design and Research (EEDAR) based in Carlsbad, California.

Prior to working at EEDAR I worked at Sony Online Entertainment and a number of personal start-ups.

With over 15 years experience in the interactive entertainment industry I have touched almost every aspect of it: consumer media, community, marketing, PR, diligence, business development, production and senior management.

Business Development, Team Management, Strategy, Operations
