D. M. Thérèse Byrne's Summary

An entrepreneurial digital media strategist and senior business development/marketing executive, Thérèse has a proven ability to lead ground-breaking new digital media initiatives: strategies and implementation surrounding content development & distribution, product-technology solutions, marketing, partnerships and business model/plan.

Her deep, rich expertise includes: business development/partnerships, sponsorship/ event marketing, consultative sales/client relations, online/inbound/integrated marketing, social media, product-market strategy, team-building, P&L.

A career path that started as a cub reporter, has afforded her groundbreaking experience on all sides of media, technology and marketing arena (TV/video, web/digital/rich media, broadband, social media, mobile)
• Spearheading “intrepre-prenerial” ventures at big media companies (NBC News, IMG Interactive Media) and entrepreneurial Web 2.0 startups (e.g., Spire.com) and small businesses (JBC Golf).
• Advising and providing start-ups, small businesses and the health sector with digital media strategy, marketing, intelligence and business development and transformation services.
• She has worked and formed strategic partnerships (b-to-b and b-to-c) with premier brands, media and technology companies online and cross-platform.
• A passionate leader, excellent communicator with a “relationship capital” building emphasis, she has a BS in Journalism (CU Boulder), an MBA in Marketing (BC) and is a certified Inbound Marketing Partner (Hubspot).

Thérèse thrives on the cutting edge of what the confluence of media, marketing and technology enable in the digital, interactive, rich media/video and increasingly mobile, on-demand communications landscape.
