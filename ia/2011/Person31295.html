Molly Gold's Summary

Molly Gold is the president and founder of GO MOM!® Inc., wife and mother of three. After graduating from the University of Nebraska with a BA in Speech Communication, she began her career in Events Management spending 7 years in association meetings and conventions.

As a new Mom, she quickly discovered the need for time management tools targeting a Moms special needs and in 2000, Molly invented, trademarked, and launched The GO MOM!® Planner which was then successfully licensed to Mead Westvaco from 2002 to 2008.

Today Molly is a well known, nationally recognized Home Organization and Family Time Management Expert. Brands connect with Molly to leverage her skills as a video marketer, brand spokesperson, speaker, writer, and events consultant. Recent projects include Honda sponsored Road Trip to Disney Social Media Moms Celebration, Video Communications/Conference Consultant The Stow Company, Ambassador MomThink.Org, Spokesperson Sam's Club Back At It Program, and Speaker 2010 TypeAMom Conference. Molly is honored to be a regular Expert Contributor on Mom Talk Radio and Guest Blogger at Momformation on BabyCenter.com.

She is the host GO MOM!® Live on MomTV.com, Getting Organized with Molly Gold on Raleigh's NBC17 My Carolina Today, and Raleigh’s National Moms Nite Out in partnership with BSM Media. Her video blog My GO MOM!® focuses on all things Schedule. Organize. Grow.® and can be found at www.GOMOMINC.com where visitors will also find the DIY GO MOM!® Planner, the ultimate Home Management Notebook.

Molly has appeared on major network morning shows including The Today Show Weekend Edition and Good Morning America, cable network series Time Makeover on Fine Living Network, radio, local network, and online programming. Molly, her company, and her product have been featured Southern Living, Better Homes & Gardens, Scholastic Parent & Child, Parents, Parenting, Family Fun, Woman's Day, The Washington Post, Chicago Tribune, and Orlando Sentinel Journal.

Privately Held; Online Media industry

January 2000 – Present (11 years 5 months)

GO MOM!® Inc was born in 2000 as a basement office company shipping planners from Molly Gold’s parents garage. An entrepreneur with an idea to change the ways busy Moms manage their time, Molly created the GO MOM!® Planner. At its inception, there were only 3 titles on the market meeting the needs of who is now widely recognized as the most powerful consumer in the country, Moms. Licensed to Mead Westvaco in 2002, the product arrived in mass market in March of 2004, debuting in Target stores nationwide. Placed in the Dayrunner portfolio and sold in big box and office stores alike, the GO MOM!® Planner was a trail blazing product in a category which today boasts more than 100 titles.

In 2008, GO MOM!® Inc. exited consumer products moving to a consulting and services model focused on Molly Gold. Today, she is a widely recognized voice of organized motherhood and has earned the trust of the mom community which depends on her earnest and candid nature. Her work as a video blogger and lifestyle media expert is enjoyed by many and her reputation for being a talented professional, personable, knowledgeable, and creative makes her a natural extension of your progressive team. Organizations connect with GO MOM!® Inc to collaborate on brand, product, and event management, social and lifestyle media expert needs, speaking and writing opportunities and much, much more.
