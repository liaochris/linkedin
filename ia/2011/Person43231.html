Steve Thomson's Summary

Entrepreneur, business manager, and media and technology investor, having built and grown a number of businesses in these sectors.

Co-founded Thomson Intermedia in 1997, a technology play in the media monitoring industry. It was listed on AIM in 2000, and subsequently acquired billetts the media auditing business. The business grew to £20m T/O, 250 employees and a market cap of £80m operating in the UK, Germany & the US. Brought in a new management team in 2007. I remain on the board of the business now renamed Ebiquity.

In 2006 I co-founded Newsbridge, the publisher of children’s Newspaper First News which has become the widest read children’s publication in the UK.

In 2007 I founded Priority One IT, a Managed IT support, Consultancy & Hosting business, for London SMEs. It is the process of building a reputation for providing a very high quality of service, as backed up by an unprecedented client retention rate.

In 2009 I have taken a stake in and become a director of Addictive Interactive, a business with social networking technology for brands.

Looking for start-up or early stage businesses in media or technology seeking funding and advice.

Outside of work I am a keen pilot, holding a commercial licence with instrument rating, play tennis, ski & compete in the occasional triathlon.

Specialties

Application of technology in business, business strategy, operational efficiency, user interface design, motivational management, emerging technologies
