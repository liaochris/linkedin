David Geer’s Summary

Your content needs

It is not easy to find a writer who understands technology and writes effectively about it. Yet, you need a professional who is both artisan and subject matter expert.

You need a writer who knows your time is precious. You need a professional who can gather information and write a quality, finished product that adds value while you attend to your core business.

You may need to off-load white papers, case studies, custom articles and other projects to save time or meet looming deadlines.

Your solution

As an 11-year-veteran technology writer with a practical background working hands-on, in the trenches, I think and write technically and practically. I understand customer pain points and write knowledgeably about the solutions. This is evident in the samples you will find linked from this profile.

Once we discuss your project scope and come to an agreement, I will meet your deadlines and expectations.

I write about IT and data centers every month and have published in a number of data center centric publications such as Data Center Management magazine.

I cover deeply technical topics such as AI, the global sensor grid, high performance computers that generate 3D earthquake models, and NoC (Network on Chip) technology.

I cover the not-so-heady topics such as smartphones, computer support tips, and high-tech luxury products.

I create service articles that inform about broadband products and services, desktop software, and the Web.

I update those who buy, sell, fix, and use technology including enterprise decision makers, channel partners, gurus, and consumers.
