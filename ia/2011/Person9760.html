Dale Cohen's Summary

I am an internet professional with a balance of content, technical, design and commercial skills. With nearly 20 years experience in the internet industry, particularly web based media, I have run many of Australia's most visited websites.

I build websites seen by millions of people but understand the needs of the individual; why they visit and what brings them back. I enjoy the challenge of applying finite resources to deliver maximum value and shareholder return. I like innovation and creativity, matched with profitability.

Specialties

Online Customer Experience, website information architecture, website design & UI, content planning and acquisition, content management, search engine optimisation
