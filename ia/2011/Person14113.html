Erin Lumley's Summary

I have 12+ years of hi-tech/consumer public relations experience and have worked at some of the top agencies in the San Francisco Bay Area including Shandwick International (now Weber Shandwick), The Horn Group, Lois Paul & Partners (owned by Fleishman-Hillard), and overseas at n2n Communications in Sydney, Australia.

During 2002 to 2004 I started my own consulting business-GoGo Communications and worked in consumer public relations servicing Macy's West and a local designer, as well as technology PR consulting for Porter Novelli and Gallagher Communications.

Overall, I have experience in enterprise software and hardware, wireless, telecommunications, mobile, CRM, Internet marketing, security, and consumer/fashion.

Specialties

Strategic communications, media and analyst relations, client and team management. Mobile, wireless, emerging and consumer technology, and enterprise software.
