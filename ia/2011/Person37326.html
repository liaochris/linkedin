Roberto Hernandez’s Summary

Roberto is a senior manager in PricewaterhouseCoopers’ Consumer Finance Practice and has over ten years of experience serving financial services companies.. He advises clients on strategy, servicing profitability, customer retention and segmentation, loan economics and profitability analysis. As one of the leaders in the Consumer Finance Group’s thought leadership initiatives, Roberto routinely facilitates client round tables and serves as a writer as well as the co-editor of PwC’s Consumer Finance Newsletter. Roberto has authored whitepapers on Hispanic market lending and customer retention strategies and other articles on trends that affect the consumer finance industry. Roberto is a Chartered Financial Analyst and a former Corporate Finance professor at the Panamericana University in Mexico City. Roberto holds an MBA from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill and B.S. in Business Administration and Finance from Universidad Panamericana in Mexico City.

• Experience in advising clients on strategic planning, process improvement, risk and profitability analysis, loan accounting, customer retention, and servicing operations.
• Over 10 years experience in the financial services industry having worked for two of the top 10 global financial institutions
• Roberto is a frequent speaker at industry conferences: - Mortgage Bankers Association (MBA) National Convention, The National Mortgage Servicing Conference, and the Residential Loan Production Conference on topics such as Servicing Profitability, Customer Retention Strategies and the Future of Mortgage Banking.
• Author of several whitepapers on segmentation, loss mitigation, servicing profitability, loan modifications, customer retention strategies, and various Consumer Finance Newsletter articles.
• Co-editor of the Consumer Finance Newsletter
• MBA from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.
• Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) charterholder
