Dan Dulin’s Experience

  • Apartment Sales

    Kasten Long Commercial Group

    (Privately Held; Real Estate industry)

    August 2009 — Present (1 year 6 months)

    Dan graduated from Arizona State University in 1992 with a Bachelor of Science degree in Sociology. While working his way through college, he leased and managed multiple 200 plus unit apartment communities in what was historically marked as a challenging time for the commercial real estate industry due to the disposition activity of Resolution Trust Corporation (RTC). Following graduation, he worked as a commercial property manager and Director of Multifamily Operations with Sevo Miller Inc, a large commercial brokerage and property management firm based in Denver, Colorado. While in this top leadership position he was responsible for the operation of over 13,000 apartment units across eight states—representing well over a half-billion dollars in investment real estate.

    Joining Marcus & Millichap Real Estate Investment Services in 2000, Dan was quickly promoted to the apartment sales team where he proved himself as a market leader in Phoenix apartment sales by closing over 100 million dollars in investment transactions. Dan won numerous awards at Marcus & Millichap, including their coveted Sales Achievement Award and National Achievement Award for outstanding sales performance.

    Today, Dan remains exclusively focused on selling apartment properties with Kasten Long Commercial Group and has extensive apartment sales track record.
