Daniel Gingras’s Summary

Dan Gingras
Partner
Tatum LLC
www.tatumllc.com
dan.gingras@tatumllc.com

Dan Gingras is a partner in the Information Technology Leadership practice of Tatum LLC. He specializes in global ERP and CRM implementations and ROI focused IT strategy. A well known CIO, Dan is a well known author on the subject of IT strategy and has written columns for some of the major IT magazines. Is is regularly quoted by leading magazines.

Dan currently operates as an Interim CIO and consultant for organizations seeking to create competitive advantage using information technology

He has held a faculty appointment at Boston University where he taught System design, project management and Technology strategy. He also has taught part of the CIO Essential course at Carnegie Mellon's Center for Technology Leadership.

Dan’s key capabilities and related experiences include:

Developing Business and Technology Strategies: As a former VP and CIO, CTO and CEO of numerous companies, Dan focuses companies on ensuring that their technology, infrastructure, applications and, most importantly, staffing provides a measurable ROI for their investments. His work on IT strategy at Boston University and Carnegie Mellon University ensures that he stays at the cutting edge of the use of technology by modern organizations.

• Developed technology strategy for global multi-billion dollar manufacturing companies.
• Has taught technology strategy at the graduate level for more than 10 years.
* Writes a monthly column for a CIO magazine on the latest strategies for CIO's.
• Teaches a CIO Leadership Essentials course for Carnegie Mellon University Center for Technology leadership.
• Has spoken at numerous conferences on maximization of technology usage within the organization.


Education and Training:

Dan holds a Master’s Degree in Computer Science from Boston University. He also has completed doctoral work in Computer Science.
