Chris Ennis's Summary

I've worked with web-based applications and dynamic websites for the past 15 years. My experience includes projects for Ticketmaster, Excite, CitySearch, Comcast, Dell, housingwithinreach.org, sobercircle.com and docCheck. Recently, I have overseen the development of OneRecovery.com, building a product for insurance pilots, achieving Series A funding in 2010.

I am also an advisor to web startups, speak at various web related events and continue to develop numerous web and mobile products.

I'm the classic builder, constantly looking for ways to build solutions that fill a void or improve upon existing tools.

Specialties

Social software architecture, APIs, project management, user interface, database design, application development, SEO management, PPC management, open source projects (Wordpress, Drupal)

Commonly Used:
