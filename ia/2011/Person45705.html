Susan Conley’s Summary

MY CAREER ROCKED
Throughout my rewarding career inside global professional services firms, I helped partners take the guesswork out of client development and reap significant R.O.R. (Return On Relationship) with their clients. I accumulated more than 15 years of evidence. Bottom-line results came in the form of significant growth within priority accounts, global engagements saved, improved profitability and expanded client relationships & loyalty. One client CFO greeted me with, “I told them I didn’t want to meet with you.” That same CFO, 60 minutes later, wanted to continue our in-depth dialogue in the company cafeteria over lunch - a $300K engagement resulted. By connecting through the power of strategic & sincere communication, it's possible to expand the profitability & possibilities in all client relations.

ROCK BOTTOM
Severe health complications sidelined me for three years. My life seemed to unravel. The harsh reality hit me: my identity was all wrapped up in my work. A major identity crisis ensued & detox from the drug of doing began. I came to the very end of myself physically, emotionally & spiritually.

While at the bottom, I went deeper into all of my important relationships & gained an even fuller understanding of their value.

INTRODUCING ROCKbiz
The doctors released me from disability just in time for the global market meltdown. The shrinking economy left no capacity for me to return to my former position. It was the right time to launch the vision that had unfolded during those three critical years. It was time to create a brand that ROCKS. A brand that’s all about relationships. After all, relationship is where influence is deeply rooted and it's the foundation for success.

I welcome the opportunity to guide, coach & inspire you as you cultivate remarkably rich client connections. ROCKsolid relationships yield ROCKsolid growth. Let me know how I can help you expand your client relationships & your business.

P.S. You ROCK!
