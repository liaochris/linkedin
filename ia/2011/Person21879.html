John Pointer's Summary

Professional musician since high school. Synthesizes new approaches to and provides new perspectives on any problem. Constant innovation and application of creative solutions.

Multi-intsrumentalist producer, arranger and performer based in Austin, TX. Cirque du Soleil talent pool. National commercial credits include principal appearances as a beatboxer for Chili's, Schlotzsky's and Austin City Limits, Southwest Airlines, and music for Wal-Mart. Acting credits include Judas in ZACH Theater's production of Jesus Christ Superstar, Roger in ZACH's production of RENT and a co-starring role on Fox's "Prison Break".

Active in the Austin, Texas Music Scene since moving there in 1991. Produced, performed, and recorded with numerous bands and artists including Ozomatli, BoomBoxATX, Schrödinger's Cat, Woodwork, Trio Los Vigilantes, and Sixpence None the Richer. Nine Austin Music Awards. The City of Austin named January 27th, 2005 "John Pointer Day." Has ten Austin Music Awards.

Focuses on creative business solutions. Currently building the Live Music Capital Foundation with the goal of providing a place to invest tax deductible capital into Live Music. The flagship program will be the Venue Incubator, which will empower venues to fearlessly book the best talent for their establishment, guarantee the talent appropriate pay, and promote the talent and the venue aggressively and effectively. Also building the Live Music at ZACH program to create platforms for world class local musical talent with the full production power of the theatre to elevate the experience for audience and artist alike.

Founded and launched Patronism.com to empower the people who love music to connect directly with the musicians they love most in order to catalyze the creative process. Musicians Make Music. Patrons make music *happen*.

Specialties

Music messaging, business strategy, demographic analysis, psychographic analysis and publicity. Composition, arrangement, recording and performance on vocals, cello, acoustic guitar, electric guitar, keyboards, electric bass, banjo, dobro, human beatbox and drums. HTML, Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, Flash and InDesign. Fluent in Italian, conversant in Spanish, can find the bathroom in French or Japanese.
