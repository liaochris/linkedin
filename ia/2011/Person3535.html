Ardath Albee's Summary

Ardath Albee is CEO and B2B marketing strategist for her consulting firm Marketing Interactions, Inc.. She taps over 20 years of business management and marketing experience to help her clients create customer-focused eMarketing and content strategies that produce more sales opportunities.

The way customers buy has been irrevocably changed by the Internet. By recognizing how buyers’ needs are evolving, I help companies to get closer to their customers by escalating their ability to communicate their differentiating value with effective, continuous, and consistent eMarketing and sales initiatives.

Marketing needs to be implemented as a strategy that works in concert with business objectives. I teach companies how to use digital tools and eMarketing approaches to their full potential to transform the marketing and selling of complex solutions with a focus toward capitalizing on business outcomes.

I'm absolutely compelled to help companies get the results they dreamed about when they implemented marketing automation and CRM systems in the first place. My focus is on connecting the process and the people, but it's also on the creation of buyer-centric content. With those three fundamentals in place, and the software tools to facilitate marketing and sales initiatives integrated in the optimum fashion, the opportunities for marketing are phenomenal. Among my clients are Cisco, Qwest, BMC Software, Avid Technology, Silicon Graphics, and LANDesk – An Avocent Company.

Writing the Marketing Interactions blog involves me in substantial marketing, sales, industry and customer conversations. Every exchange deepens my knowledge of what companies can achieve while verifying many of my strategies. I'm honored that my blog posts are referred to often by other industry leaders and syndicated on community websites including Junta42, The Customer Collective and My Venture Pad and The B2B Marketing Zone.

Specialties

Developing eMarketing strategies with compelling content platforms to nurture and progress prospects throughout their buying process and generate sales-ready conversations.
