Jon Dessel's Summary

Successful career spanning 20 plus years experience in advanced enterprise information system design, architecture, configuration, deployment, and project management. 14 years experience in executive-level IT corporate management.

Mr. Dessel was a technological advisor to the Department Of Defense as well as provided strategic I.T. consulting services to such companies as PepsiCo, Wachovia Securities, Bank of America, Onyx Acceptance, Hughes Data Systems, Fluor Daniel, ETrade Financial, Triad Financial, Ford Motor Credit, 21st Century Insurance, First American Title, Thales Avionics, Petco, Parker Hannifin, GMAC Financial, and Hyundai.

Extensive experience in managing multiple projects, team members, and external contractors. Experience in integrating all major system types, which include ERP/CRM/HRS/MES/ EDI/PDM/ECM and custom programmed systems.

- Excellent in identifying business risks and opportunities as compared to industry best practices, then establishing a plan for business continuity and growth. Exceptional knowledge of systems used to support the ability to increase corporate communication and business productivity.

- Black Belt certified in the use and deployment of Six Sigma, as used to facilitate change and re-engineering efforts within an organization. He is a Certified Project Management Professional and certified in TQM.

- Highly experienced in creating IT system roadmaps that consider the present, near term, and long-term needs of a company. Flawless past history of deploying corporate-wide solutions on-time and within budget.

- Design and deployment of security management systems, automated backup, disaster recovery plans, backup management, and offsite storage plans.

- Development of technology standards, methods and schedules for corporate planning of Information Technology needs. Experienced with network systems, hardware, software, and ERP/MRP systems.

Specialties

Executive management, process engineering, business development, business system analysis, CIO consulting, operational improvement consulting, and design of Service Oriented Architectures (SOA).
