John Miniati’s Summary

Internet and mobile executive with experience bringing to market over 20 innovative technology products. Managerial and tactical expertise in scaling high-growth product and marketing operations and teams across companies large (Hewlett-Packard), small (Bessemer-backed startup) and midsized (pre-IPO comScore).
