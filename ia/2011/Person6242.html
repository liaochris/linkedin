Brian York, CMA’s Summary

An accomplished finance professional with 15 years of expertise in management reporting, financial planning & analysis and cost accounting with both hands-on and people management experience for the semiconductor, heavy manufacturing, promotional marketing and financial services industries. Consistently recognized for excellent analytical and organizational ability, anticipating issues and proposing direction and alternative solutions based on knowledge of business trends, collaborating well in a team environment and having an established record for building strong cross-functional relationships.
