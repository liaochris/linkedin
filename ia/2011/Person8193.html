Chris Lurix, PMP’s Summary

SYNOPSIS: Project Management Professional (PMP®) with over sixteen years experience in software development and project leadership working predominantly as a professional services consultant for companies such as Grant Thornton, PricewaterhouseCoopers, Oracle Corporation, Kana Software and Sungard Energy Systems. His experience includes 10 years working with clients involving wholesale/retail energy trading systems. He has over 16 years experience working with Oracle, SQL Server, and Sybase databases and has very strong PL/SQL and T-SQL scripting experience and data analytical skills. Chris possesses the rare ability to understand the most meticulous business needs and translate those needs into system logic. Chris has managed several teams of up to twelve members for the development and deployment of both custom and package applications.
