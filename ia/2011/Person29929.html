Michael Astor's Summary

Quantitative analyst with many years of experience developing and managing development of asset allocation and equity selection strategies, both market-neutral and long-only. My most recent research interest has been development of statistical arbitrage strategies based on non-stationary time series and conditional mean reversion.

Specialties

Strong quantitative and analytical skills. Extensive background in quantitative and theoretical equity model building. Demonstrated ability to think “out of the box”. Practical knowledge of financial statement analysis, equity valuation models, CAPM, option pricing, macroeconomics. Hands-on leadership of research teams through the full cycle of investment strategy development. Experience in presenting investment strategies and results to clients and senior management.
