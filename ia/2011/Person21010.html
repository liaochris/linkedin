Jim Roberts III's Summary

I have been in the Sons of The American Legion since January 1978. I have been afforded many opportunities within the organization including serving as National Vice Commander – South, National Membership Chairman and Vice Chairman of the National Internal Affairs Commission.

My current position with the Detachment of Florida allows me to interface regularly with members at all levels of the organization and throughout Florida. This includes serving as the Detachment Disaster Preparedness Chairman and Detachment Membership Chairman. As a Past Detachment Commander, I feel that it is my responsibility to assist our membership in learning more about our organization by instructing and presenting at our Detachment Meetings. These sessions range from understanding the administrative processes, to leadership training and disaster preparedness training. All of this is to support and mentor our up and coming leadership within the Detachment.

I think that I would be an asset to the Sons of The American Legion as National Commander because of my experiences within the organization, my job and in my personal life.

Professionally, I am employed by the Florida Division of Emergency Management as a Regional Emergency Management Coordinator. Please view my professional Linked In Profile at http://www.linkedin.com/in/jimrobertsem
