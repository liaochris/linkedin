Kate Rolston's Summary

Extremely Creative Business Professional with exceptional sales background, online media experience and innovative solution provider.

Experience in both traditional and new media, I lead teams with clear vision and a dedicated passion to accomplish our goal.
As CEO of Real Savvy Media, I was responsible for the strategic direction and day-to-day operations of the company, which included editorial oversight duties as the Executive Producer of the award-winning TV series 'Real Moms' which aired on PBS stations for 4 seasons.
We then created and sold an edited execution of the series specifically for online consumption; distributed via online video ad network and carrying the sponsors' messaging the series exceeded Double-clicks' click through rates for similar video executions.

In terms of sales, I over saw the sales and sales/marketing team and was instrumental in configuring strategic media solutions that helped close/win business.
The company offered e-newsletters, sponsorship of content & contests, and the custom creation of WebCasts or other branded entertainment webisodes.
I created all the new products at the the company which in many cases were considered 'first-to-market' in our vertical.
After 4 years, having built an award-winning library of top quality short segments and thousands of articles for our niche 'Mom' audience, we are now expanding into the broader Women's Interest area and have already booked clients.

They say "Content is King", but I believe that exceptionally high quality niche content is even better! It can be re-used, repurposed and re-monetized over a good period of time.

Real Savvy Media's 'Mom' content was acquired in an asset Purchase agreement in April 2010.
