Wesley Terrell’s Summary

Wesley is a mergers and acquisitions lawyer with AT&T, a Fortune 7 company. Wesley's team handles all international and domestic M&A transactions for AT&T, Inc. and its subsidiaries.

Wesley represents AT&T in several telecom, wireless and new media acquisitions. Wesley is also the lead lawyer for AT&T's strategic venture capital portfolio with more than 250 minority interest positions in growth companies. Since joining AT&T, Wesley has led more than 30 deals.

Wesley worked for several years at Skadden, Arps, Slate Meagher & Flom as an M&A and corporate finance lawyer. At Skadden, Wesley assisted corporate clients in private and public M&A transactions valued in the tens of millions of dollars up to $3 billion. Several deals involved international aspects in Brazil, Mexico, London and Luxembourg.

Although the bulk of Wesley's experience is as a deal lawyer, Wesley worked as commercial litigator at Vinson & Elkins law firm in Houston. Wesley had primary responsibility for the development of 50 product liability suits. While a V&E lawyer, Wesley worked on loan as a prosecutor with the Houston City Attorney's Office and tried 17 jury cases to verdict as the lead lawyer. He has examined over 35 trial witnesses and 300 civil deposition witnesses as the lead examiner.

Merger & Acquisitions Transactional, Private Equity Transactional, Corporate Finance Transactional, General Corporate Transactional, Commercial Litigation and Securities Litigation.
