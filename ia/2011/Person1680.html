Alison Morris's Summary

Alison is a recent graduate of the Boston University College of Communication and currently works at The CHT Group, a strategic communications firm based in Boston, and for The DoBand Campaign, a social media startup.

Previously, she interned in financial communications at Fleishman-Hillard London and Morrissey & Co., an award-winning public relations firm. Alison has also held internships in special events, marking and business development roles.

Alison is an avid blogger and writer, and is actively involved in the social media circle in New England. She has been featured in Mashable! and ABC Tech news for her social media accomplishments, and has been invited to speak on communications and social media panels in Boston. .

Specialties

Creative marketing and communications planning, writing and editing, competitor analyses, new and emerging media strategy and management, new business research and pitching, media relations, partnership relationship management.
