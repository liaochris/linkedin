Andrew Lovatt’s Summary

A long career in media beginning with writing - which is the core to so much media, book publishing (Andrew Lovatt, Publishers, London 1968) and film (editor FIBA: Critical Film Quarterly, 1969, awarded Best International Film Magazine, Venice), Film Consultant to John & Yoko Lennon (1969-72), Program Director at Telitalia Cable Network (US-Italy), publisher and editor of various magazines - incl, The Irish Diviner (Dublin) and the Philadelphia Energy Quarterly (USA) and consulting editor of Current History (USA). Opened one of the first "electronic design & publishing" businesses in Phila, PA, US, in late 1980s and dipped toes into the fast-emerging Bulletin Board System network (US) and the early Internet.
