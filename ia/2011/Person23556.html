Kartik V. Kothari's Summary

Kartik began his journey at the age of 18 when he launched his own fortnightly publication – Infotainment. Infotainment broke new grounds in how local area advertising media was looked at and utilised. He even started marketing consulting firm called Juxtapose for small & medium business and helped them better formulate business strategies and leverage the available resources. With the dotcom boom a new sister concern – Webkraftnmore was born. Webkraftnmore offered web-designing and E-commerce solutions to the clients of Infotainment and Juxtapose. After graduating in Business Management from Jai Hind College, Kartik joined Vodafone (then branded as Orange) in the corporate relations team and eventually moved to corporate sales. After spending nearly 3 years at Vodafone he moved to The Times Group. Kartik is currently the Product Manager for Bizxchange.in, a B2B portal from The Times of India Group. In the past two and half years that he’s been here he has handled various profiles right from strategic marketing of JLT –a youth magazine to organising business events for CXOs.
