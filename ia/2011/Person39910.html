Shannon Martinez’s Summary

Shannon Martinez is an award winning sales leader, and payroll expert. Shannon graduated with a BS in Marketing from Southern NH University. Shannon “sold” her way up the corporate ladder starting at InterPay Payroll Service in Manchester, NH as a District Manager, then Area Sales Manager, with a team of 5 district managers. She became a Regional Manager in 2000 and managed the South Florida office in Ft Lauderdale.

In 2001 and 2002, Shannon's Leadership strengths were recognized by InterPay as she was accepted into their Platinum Meadlist Club. - the elite sales program offered by InterPay. Only top performing individuals were accepted each year.

Shannon is currently employed as a Regional Account Manager at Payroll Solutions in Whitehall. Where she is responsible for sales and marketing in the Pittsburgh Area.

Shannon is a Past board member of the National Association of Female Executives, and is on the Board of Directors for the North Shore Community Alliance and the Brentwood, Baldwin, Whitehall Chamber of Commerce.

Shannon has a son, Mateo who is 4 years old; and resides in the South Hills.

Shannon Martinez works with business owners and executives who want to drive better results. A specific strength is working with the business owner who wants to save time and money and increase their bottom line. Shannon’s goal is to help companies by increasing their employees’ productivity by implementing time and attendance tracking and human resource services.
