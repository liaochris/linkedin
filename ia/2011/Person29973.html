In 1996 founded internationally renowned Lookandfeel new media and negotiated the acquisition by Barkley, the largest employee-owned advertising agency in the United States. Named a "Top Fifty Technology Leader" by Kansas City Star twice.

Successful selling projects across a broad range of clients with a proven ability to manage both technical and creative teams through the entire product development process. Currently working with a variety of emerging technologies for independent clients.

When it comes to interactive there isn't much I haven't tried, built or imagined. My history with interactive began in the CD-Rom craze of the early 90's before the web really existed.
