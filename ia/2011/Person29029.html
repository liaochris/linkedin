Matthew Rideout's Summary

I received my degree in Marketing from the University of Southern Maine in the fall of 2010. I am most enthusiastic about market research, consumer behavior analysis, and statistical data analysis. Using these tools to discover consumers opinions and latent motivations both fascinates and excites me.

During my senior year of college, I interned at revDRTV, a direct response marketing firm in Portland, Maine. It is here that I gained priceless real world experience and insight into the world of direct response marketing, as well as an opportunity to refine my web design and development skills.

I am most interested in marketing opportunities that present a need for strong primary and secondary market research as well as statistical data analysis. Other strong areas and interests of mine are internet marketing, including e-commerce systems, social media marketing and viral marketing.

I have several years of experience in web design and web application development. I have built e-commerce solutions which handle processing of payments and orders from the consumer to fulfillment houses, from scratch. I am continually refining my web design skills.

Blogging is a passionate hobby of mine. I am continuously pushing harder to see how I can attract more traffic, search engine optimize better and earn more through affiliate programs and advertising.

Specialties

My specialties include market research (and statistical analysis), brand development, campaign refinement, web design, graphics design, computer hardware and software troubleshooting, electronic records management, blogging, search engine optimization, and social media marketing.
