Genia Larionova’s Summary

I am a business owner, digital artist, photo editor and retoucher. With a background in graphics and web design, I make sure that each photograph is bright, crisp and razor-sharp.
My goal is to make sure the final image is perfect and priceless.

I am the last person to touch the photo before it goes to the client. So the work I do is crucial. However, I am always careful not to alter the image to such a degree as to make it lose its natural beauty and quality. My challenge, as that of a makeup artist for example, is to accentuate the good features, while leaving the integrity of the subject intact.
