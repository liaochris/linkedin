Financial Services industry

February 2010 – Present (1 year 2 months)

Inara Partners is an Independent Research & Financial Engineering boutique dedicated to the African MidCap/SME segment. Inara Partners aims at improving the visibility into the small & Medium size African corporations, by on the one hand, helping these corporations improve their internal financial processes & adopt Best Practices, and on the other hand by providing investors access to independent & grounded quality information.
At Inara Partners we believe that an increase in transparency in the African capital markets will have a positive effect on their liquidity, and hence increase their traction to foreign capital.
Our processes and practices are based on high integrity and Best Practices guidelines
