Pengfu Tan's Experience

Principal Metallurgist

Xstrata, Australia

Public Company; Mining & Metals industry

April 2004 – Present (7 years)

Responsibilities:

• Manage and develop the whole smelter technical team of 12 metallurgists that monitors the day to day metallurgical performance and implements the project improvements.
• Provide technical leadership to the whole metallurgical team in Copper Smelter to achieve the goals for safety, production, costs, compliance, planning and reporting.
• Develop the short term and long term improvement strategies / projects for the metallurgical performance of the Smelter, and implement metallurgical improvements to the Smelter performance, in a safe, efficient and cost effective manner.
• Provide technical and commercial analysis for the smelting improvement initiatives, budget and capital program.

Major Achievements:

• The copper loss in RHF slag (or copper smelting slag) dropped from 2.8% in Dec 2007 to 0.76% in Apr 2009. Currently the copper loss in RHF slag is about 0.9%. This improvement is worth of 93 million AUD revenue increase and 12 million AUD profits per year.
• The overall smelter recovery achieved more than 98%, and the free metal gain is worth of 30 million AUD per year.
• The smelter yield has increased more than 6% via the improvement of process control from 2008 to 2009.
• The number of foaming problem per month in the converter operations has been decreased from 35 in Aug 2008 to only one per month in 2009/2010.
• The efficiency of the crushing and magnetic separation plant has been improved 3 times.
• Technical support to the test work, trial and commissioning of the leaching plant.
• Led the technical team to develop a new flow sheet to recover all the copper, gold and silver in ESP dust. The potential extra profit is 10 million dollars per year for the fresh ESP dust treatment, and 53 million dollars for the ESP dust stockpile treatment.
• Developed the KPIs to monitor the metallurgical performance of the whole smelter.
