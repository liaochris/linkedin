Carole Baker's Summary

With a solid background in New Business Development and Account Management for Advertising and Marketing Agencies, I am now leveraging my skills into the Social Media realm as a Consultant and Strategist. I am the founder and Community Manager for Dayton's largest Social Media Enthusiast Group: New Media Dayton. I am one of the few professionals in the Midwest with advanced knowledge of:

* Social media marketing
* Community management
* Web 2.0 publishing
* User-generated content
* Online customer relations
* Blog publishing
* Social applications from photo sharing to tagging
* Web programming strategy and design knowledge
* Social media and metrics thought leadership

Anyone can learn to build a WordPress blog, launch a website, Facebook for Business and Twitter account. It's the strategy, thought-leadership and execution that separate the followers from the leaders.

I believe in creating a strategy and business model that continues to integrate traditional media with measurable, rich, social media content. Authenticity and transparency are my strongest character attributes.

I have an entrepreneurial spirit and a passion for creating new community where none has existed before. I'm passionate about connecting people and ideas. New projects thrill me. Don't be a stranger. Schedule a complimentary 30 minute consultation to see if we should pursue a working relationship. I'm open to doing things in ways that haven't been done before.
