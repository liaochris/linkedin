Diane Gracely’s Summary

I am the founder of KEWL TUBES (www.kewltubes.com). We manufacture traditional knit golf club covers for golfers around the world. We take orders from individual golfers and pro-shops, schools, etc... through our web site or by calling us.

I have 13 years of experience in internet marketing and growing a business online. I also have over 20 years of experience in accounting.

OUR GOAL is to become well-known around the world for our KEWL TUBES Golf Club Covers.
