Martin Kievit's Summary

Martin is an experienced manager with knowledge on different levels. He has experience in line manager functions as well as in project manager functions.

As a line manager he managed groups from 2 up to 84 people. He managed people as well in the same building as people with field functions. As a line manager Martin likes to coach people and help them to improve their skills. His approach is to make them responsible for a part of the work that needs to be done, in this way they find them self accountable. This leads to achieving the goals that are set for the company or department Martin is responsible for.

As a line manager Martin has experience in:
•Supermarkets
•Telecom store
•Operation department
•Online sales and service department

When Martin is working as a project manager, he works on a nontraditional way. He knows how to use the members of the project to achieve the goals and time lines that are set. He uses his people management skills to get and keep everybody on board. Martin is always aware of the mile stones that need to be reached in order to realize the main goal. Business Poject Manager Internal and External Pilot

Telfort

Privately Held; Telecommunications industry

December 2009 – June 2010 (7 months)

Within the 2.0 program we had a pilot before the Cut over moment.
In this pilot I was the Business Project Manager.
In this role I was responsible for the alignment between the Business needs and the program deliverables and time lines.

The goal of the internal pilot (Friendly User Pilot) was to see how the system and the new processes would behave when they were used as in a life situation. To test this we created 400 different scenarios that were executed by Telfort employees behaving as customers. We build a small organization to support these Friendly Users as if we were life.

The goal of the external pilot (Commercial User Pilot) was to see how the customer experience was while using this new system. We did not inform the customers that they were part of a pilot. This to make sure that they would behave as regular customers. To capture the customer experience we performed a customer survey by phone.

While we were in the pilot phase we found a lot of small bugs in the processes as well as in the system. By solving this issues we increased the quality of the program before the actual launch.

Manager Service en Diensten, MyCom Business en MyCom Direct

MyCom Nederland B.V.

Privately Held; Information Technology and Services industry

May 2008 – May 2009 (1 year 1 month)

The commercial director of MyCom asked me to set up a Service at Home concept. Both for B2B as the consumer market. As an example we took The geek squad and The tech guys.

I took this job as a project and next to that I was responsible for:
•MyCom Service a department where people can call to for technical and network questions.
•Assurance product line (we selected a new partner to work with)
•Financing products (we selected a new partner to work with)
•Telecom products

I started the project with writing the business case. After approval of the case an advertising agency started with the look and feel of the brand we wanted to introduce on the Dutch market.

At the same time I started with the selection of the required software.
•CRM (for combining online, offline and on site)
•Planning software (make it possible to make an appointment in the agenda of the mechanic)

The CRM software we created in house and incorporated in the POS software that was already there.
For the planning software we chose Ortec. With a web interface we made it possible for a customer to plan a visit online at a day and time that suits him. This software selected the mechanic, based on costs and distance. It also calculated the time that the mechanic must travel to get to the customer.
All planned appointments were sent to the navigation device of the mechanic. He just had to select his next appointment and the device showed him the way and told him what to do when he arrived at the customer.

After the software selection I hired 8 mechanics and leased cars for them.

I stared working at MyCom on May 15th 2008. We were operational live at September 1st 2008.

After the start of the “service thuis” organization I focused on the Service departments in the stores. There I reorganized the way of work and processes to make them more efficient.

In December 2008 I became also responsible for the aftercare of the web shop sales and telesales. Both for Consumer and B2B.
