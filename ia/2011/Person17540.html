Hoda Osman’s Summary

Graduate student at the George Mason University Department of Computational Social Science, completing a PhD program in Urban Development Modeling.

My current focus is on urban growth and development in developing countries with special interest in slums from the perspective of land markets and the effect of strategic policies on local economic development, security, social inclusion and other socio-economic issues. I am also interested in the recent advances of integrating GIS and agent-based methodologies in urban modeling.
