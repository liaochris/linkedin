    (Privately Held; Real Estate industry)

    1998 — Present (13 years )

    John and I opened our doors back in 1998 as a small RE/MAX franchise. Our goals were to build a dynamic business by building effective partnerships with our clients, agents and suppliers.

    This growth is indicative of the quality of service provided by our agents and the marketing provided to each and every one of our clients.

    Market Leader Saves you Time & Money
    We leverage an extensive network of vendors & contacts to provide comprehensive services that save you money.

    Other agencies talk about market leadership – our Las Vegas Foreclosure Bus Tour, Short Sale Seminars, and focused business model that saves our client’s money has achieved national and international market presence.
    We’ve been featured on the Canadian Broadcast Corporation, cover article in the USA Today, CBS, NBC and Fox affiliates, the CBS Early Show and in print via the Las Vegas Sun and Review Journal, the national Chinese Daily News, a wide number of Blogs and Newsletters and numerous secondary articles in national publications.
