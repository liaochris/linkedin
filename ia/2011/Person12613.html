Drew Stogsdill’s Summary

I am an experienced Web Designer, Content Management Systems page builder, print and online advertising artist, and I am highly recommended from my previous employers as a front-end developer / graphic artist with a focus on satisfying client objectives.  I run my own successful side job at www.sierrajavadesign.com, a business that is driven by strong passion for web, graphics, print development, and most importantly, a commitment to my client relationships. Just recently, I started a new company, Pressbriar.com, designing and developing websites using the Wordpress platform.

As an experienced web designer, I can integrate best practices towards web development from the planning stages using XHTML, CSS, javascript, image formats, Flash and JQuery modules to build advanced social networking/content management systems and standard websites that are both cross browser functional and W3C Section 508 Compliant. I have participated as an integral part of the development process on such social networking sites like Simons Foundation, Math for America, Walter-Kaitz Foundation, and The Cradle to name only a few.

In a short period of time, I grew from a production artist to Art Department management at Innovation Specialties (now called Innovation Line – www.innovationspecialties.com), using Photoshop and Illustrator for typesetting and setup of various artworks quickly and effectively in a high-volume atmosphere. My responsibilities included but were not limited to processing orders, corresponding with clients and vendors, handling equipment maintenance, ordering supplies, assisting in the implementation of new procedures and overseeing the Art Department in general. Unfortunately, I had to leave after a little more than a year, since my family and I made a decision to move to Virginia.
