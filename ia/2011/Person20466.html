Jerod Bond's Summary

I have over 15 years experience in the design and installation of large scale automation projects for automotive, process and material handling applications.

My recent contracts in Bioenergy and solar have piqued my interest in green energy. I have started a side business installing residential Grid-Tied PV systems between industrial contracts. My installed systems have qualified me to sit for the March 2011 NABCEP PV-Installer Exam after which I would like to pursue a career in green energy if it makes financial sense. If not, I will continue doing installations as a hobby.
