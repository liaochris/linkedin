Kathryn Bachhofer CTC MCC's Summary

Kathryn is an independent travel consultant with 9 years of travel & cruise experience. Working from her home office in Queensville, Kathryn is able to offer personal and confidential travel consulting services to her clients meeting with them when and where it is convenient for them. For clients further afield, she is able to work virtually via phone, email and internet. Focusing on finding the best fit for their travel requirements, Kathryn works hard to make sure they have a perfect vacation, the trip they always dreamed of taking.

Specialties

Vacation Planner - Certified Travel Counsellor; Cruise Specialist - Master Cruise Counsellor; Princess Cruises - Commodore; Sandals Certified Specialist
