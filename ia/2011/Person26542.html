Lisa Petrilli's Summary

Unique ability to not only develop a clear vision for a business, but to create and articulate bold strategies needed to bring the vision to life, and the talent to lead, communicate, motivate and successfully implement the vision.

As CEO of C-Level Strategies, Inc I work as an Executive Marketing Strategist, Social Media Concierge and Leadership Consultant to many executive clients with the ability to accept new, executive-level clients on an ongoing basis.

Passion for finding win-win solutions during negotiations and for public speaking.

Experience running a $750 million Med Device/Drug Delivery business, leading global product line expansion, negotiating multi-million dollar co-licensing/commercialization agreements with Big Pharma, and managing a team of A+ marketers.

Presented at a United Nations Environmental Conference in Geneva,Switzerland on behalf of Baxter and was Contributing Author to "Design for Environment" (McGraw Hill). Kellogg MBA.

As Global Group Marketing & Business Development Manager negotiated on Baxter’s behalf with all major pharmaceutical company partners to grow Baxter revenues in the millions of dollars and enable product line globalization via strategically structured deals.

Addressed Greenpeace’s non-PVC efforts by leading a strategically critical, cross-functional team to formulate and then implement the EnvisionTM Recycling Program that protected market share, deepened customer loyalty, drove new sales and afforded me the opportunity to present the program’s success on Baxter’s behalf at a United Nations sponsored conference in Geneva, Switzerland.

Taught executive leadership courses at the Baxter Institute to Baxter execs so they could better fulfill Baxter’s strategic vision.

As a member of Baxter's Corporate Sales & Marketing team, served as a Consultant to Baxter Partnership Hospitals, nurturing C-level relationships.

Specialties

Marketing strategy, Social Media strategy, Leadership strategy, Cross-functional team leadership; Strategic initiative visioning, development, implementation and management; Relationship development; Product and membership marketing and management; Written communications; Public speaking; Organizing large, complex events; Negotiations.
