Rick Hocker's Summary

Rick Hocker is a multimedia programmer with seventeen years experience in programming interactive CD-ROMs and web sites. Coming from a graphic design background, Rick has been a freelance programmer and consultant for over fifteen years. Rick has been a developer on many children’s educational software programs, including 10 Leapfrog Leapster cartridge games, Hello Kitty products, and a Pearson Education Math and Reading web site. Five of the Leapster games were awarded Parent’s Choice awards.

As a freelance Flash programmer, Rick prefers projects that allow him to telecommute. Games and educational projects are his preference and specialty.

Specialties

Macromedia Director, Macromedia Flash, Lingo, ActionScript, PHP, mySQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PhotoShop
