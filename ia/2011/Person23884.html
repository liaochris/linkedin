Kaz Brecher’s Summary

Now focused on cloud computing implementation, and with a broad background across media platforms, Kaz brings a unique creative perspective to her interactive work, combining film, print, broadcast, branding and new media. Beyond her role as an executive interactive producer, Kaz layers on strategic guidance to content and digital media development from 4 years of user-centered design experience. Her primary areas of interest include emerging and cross-platform development, social media, and pro-social or environmental engagement projects. And she also has experience in managing not only inter-disciplinary teams and multiple vendors and stakeholders but also direct reports for whose career paths she has held responsibility.

Kaz Brecher’s Specialties:

Story-telling and content development, brand implementation, cross-platform and emerging digital platforms, rapid prototyping and iteration, project and program management, user-centered design and social media strategy and production.
