Alex Day's Summary

Distribution of video ads online has a barrier to entry that is being solved by Webshoz with its revolutionary Videobar. The Videobar parses up the ad unit so that multiple video ad thumbnails can be inserted and displayed, play when moused-over and when clicked, provide the full version. This allows a sneak peak before committing to a click. This patent-pending solution is available for licensing free, with a revenue share on ad insertions. Any website with multiple videos can utilize the software to offer premium placement of video ads as well as receive placements from our network and earn additional revenue by offering distribution into our network to your existing customers.

I have been fortunate enough to have extensive video ad experience and understand the need for this solution to help the video ad business grow more quickly.

Webshoz, Inc. also offers FREE video ad production when advertisers buy distribution.

I am also helping out some folks with a good idea on a contract basis - web cams viewing horses complete with online horse ownership. We even created horse trading cards. Visit on the web at: www.horseofmine.com

I've also built a library of HD video clips for use in online video ads - not only for editors to have better quality to work with, but also for online automated ad-builders that need content to run on.

If you have any questions about HD - you might want to join my new Linked-in Professional Group called High Def.
