Sudhir Ahluwalia's Summary

Sudhir is a 1976 batch officer of the Indian Forest Service (IFS) with 23 years of experience in the government. IFS is one of the three All India Civil Services of India.

He now advises company's to expand their business footprint.

In 1999 he moved to Tata Consultancy Services, Asia’s largest IT services company for now over 10 years and has headed various business divisions in that company including Head of India Geography- TCS Global Consulting Practice.

He is Honorary Chairman of South Asia Consultants Private Ltd that is into business advisory and implementation of global projects in entertainment, healthcare and pharmaceuticals, social services, food and food processing, merger and acquisition management. (www.sacglobal.org)

He is also Adviser India Public Sector Datamatics (www.datamatics.com) and also to Geo One Marketing Services (www.geoonems.com). Geo One is a pharma and lifesciences focused publishing and marketing company with over 20 years of experience in publishing pharmaceutical research journals.

He was Executive Director Business Development to a management consulting firm with over 30 years of track record. In addition he assists Nanjing Jiangsu province based MRK Software and industrial parks in e government projects in that province.

He has provided advisory support either directly or to consulting teams engaged in projects in different parts of the world. An indicative list of activities during the total 30 years span has been categorized and summarized below:

1. Business concepts
2. Pure IT projects
3. Strategic consulting projects
4. Re-structuring and transformation projects
5. Operation support programs and projects


Sudhir is a prolific writer and has one book- Indian Forestry Scene and several articles to his credit many of which have been published in national and international journals.
