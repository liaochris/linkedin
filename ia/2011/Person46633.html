Zoe Temmerman **LION** zoe@avalonexec.com’s Summary

Zoe Temmerman is co-founder and partner at Avalon Executive Search, LLC. Prior to Avalon she worked on a national basis as Vice President of Recruiting. It was her dream to start a firm and unite progressive companies with enterprising candidates and have fun doing it. Luckily she met two other talented recruiters who felt the same way, and Avalon was born. She has been a Certified Personal Consultant (CPC) since 2006. Zoe is originally from Brighton, England. She has lived on three continents and moved to the United States in 2004. She lives in Dublin, Ohio, with her husband Gavin.
