John Mason's Summary

John Mason is founder and managing director of Oberon NSW Pty Limited, trading as quality.com.au.

quality.com.au is a specialist management consultancy designing, developing, implementing and supporting quality management systems based on ISO 9001.

As a businessperson, John has achieved a significant track record in commercial growth and client satisfaction with his focus being strategic planning, project management and sales.

John is a metallurgist, lead auditor in quality management systems and certified management system consultant. He is the immediate past president and current secretary of Sydney Hills Business Chamber, chairperson of Global-Mark Advisory Board and chair of Hills Schools Industry Partnership.

On a personal note, John is married with 3 daughters, enjoys physical fitness, personal development, ballroom dancing and travel.
