Paul Barnett BSc. (Hons), FStratPS, FRSA's Experience

Executive Officer

Strategic Planning Society

Nonprofit; Professional Training & Coaching industry

December 2010 – Present (4 months)

The Strategic Planning Society has been in existence for 40 years and is the oldest such body in the world. The society aims are: to be recognised as an expert voice on strategy and strategic thinking, and to promote the case for strategy as a professional discipline.

The Strategic Planning Society (SPS) fosters and promotes research and best practice in strategic thought and action, and it creates a link between the academic and practitioner worlds of strategy by keeping strategists up-to-date with developments in strategy-related research and practice.

I will be working on the administration and development of the society, including the formulation of its marketing and communication plan.
