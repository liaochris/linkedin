Denise Ganjou's Summary

Experienced advertiser and marketer specialising in the world of theatre and the arts. With extensive experience in both London and New York city, I have worked with a range of commercial and not-for-profit companies to devise and implement effective marketing and advertising campaigns to suit vastly differing budgets and timelines. I am currently looking for new opportunities / projects within the NYC cultural arena.

- Media planning / budgeting (across all media platforms including online, out-of-home, print, broadcast)
- Project managing art development projects from concept through to completion
- Direct response marketing (email / direct mail)
- Sales analysis & promotion
- Audience development
