Jeffrey L. Wannamaker’s Summary

Jeff is a Director with Deloitte Consulting LLP, in their Pricing and Profitability Management Practice, responsible for delivery of pricing strategy, consulting and emerging software package technology. Deloitte's Pricing practice is ranked the #1 Pricing consulting firm by Kennedy Research.

Prior to joining Deloitte, Jeff served as Vice President of Technical Professional Services at PROS Holdings responsible for implementation, global delivery and support of Business Intelligence technology, including a suite of Pricing & Revenue Optimization solutions. Jeff held senior manager positions for other product and services companies including Dynasty Technologies, El Paso Corp, Logica, Nynex and IBM.

Jeff has over 25 years of experience in the software industry, having directed software product development, professional services and customer support organizations. He has managed the delivery of solutions to clients in the Energy & Utilities, Petroleum, Airline, Hotel, Manufacturing, Distribution, and Healthcare industries. Jeff has led multiple efforts to develop product strategies, go to market plans, technical architecture and cross-industry solution delivery frameworks in multiple positions.

Jeff holds a Bachelor of Science in Electrical & Computer Engineering from the University of Houston and is a member of Tau Beta Pi and Eta Kappa Nu national engineering honor societies. He lives with his family in Kingwood, Texas and enjoys photography, snow skiing and is an avid fan of volleyball and soccer.
