Nadine Sharara's Summary

I am an astute and motivated online professional with full end to end ecommerce experience from platform development through to trading optimisation. I specialise in trading, helping retailers maximise their online opportunities by acquiring, converting and retaining valuable customers. I have been working at the forefront of eCommerce for the past 5 years prior to which I worked in traditional retail and wholesale marketing for large UK fashion retailer and global fashion brand.

Specialties

End to end ecommerce for retail and multi-channel, Platform Development, Budget Management, Online Marketing, Strategy, Planning, Analysis and Measurement, PPC, ECRM, Affiliate Marketing, Web Analytics, SEO, Online PR and Social Commerce.
