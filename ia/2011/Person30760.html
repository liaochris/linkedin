Mike Reed’s Summary

The global economy, and the job market has been challenging to say the least, from an employers and employees perspective for the last couple of years as reflected on my resume. In summary, I have excellent references from all organizations, and I would be happy to share these references and experiences with your recruiting team in the near future.

Recently, I was employed with Surfas Gourmet as Controller/VP of Operations ($115k), however after approximately one year, I wanted to be with a larger growing organization, so I was recruited by Cereplast ($125k) as the Director of Finance/Operations. I continued to be employed with Cereplast, until they encountered financial issues, and defaulted on their vendor and employee payments in excess of 150 days. They had asked me to stay on in exchange for stock options in lieu of wages , however I was then recruited by Performance Team ($125k) as a Corporate Treasurer. Performance Team was a great opportunity, however with changes in their market share, and current year forecasts, management wanted to consolidate and eliminate positions, therefore I was terminated with no reasonable explanation at the end of 2010.

After leaving the professional corporate world I would like to re-enter the employment market for a stable, but growing corporate entity, looking for a professional employee, with over 10+ years of solid accounting, operations, and technology experiences. In addition, I am currently in the process of completing my MBA program, with a specialization in Supply Chain and Operations Management from Northeastern University.

I have managed GAAP requirements, budgets/forecasts, corporate reporting, financial analysis, month-end closing procedures, quarterly /year end audit processes.

Also, I am well versed in ERP/accounting technologies, along with advanced Microsoft Excel skills, and I am very effective in building and mentoring various team members throughout the organization.
