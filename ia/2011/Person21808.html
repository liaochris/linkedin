John Macias’s Summary

Celebrating 20 years in the real estate industry, a skilled networker and authority in deal negotiation, John Macias has successfully mastered positions from the ground up within national title organizations, has launched several profitable business ventures and has recruited, trained and deployed a number of top-producing sales teams over the span of his career. Particularly adept in facilitating national business for all types of real estate transactions and a proven talent for architecting proprietary tools and aggregating resources that support the listing, selling, buying, financing and closing of real estate.

As Senior Vice President, National Sales, for New Vista Community Asset Solutions, Macias works to create affordable homeownership opportunities for minority and low to moderate income Americans, generating awareness of foreclosures as affordable housing options and aligning lender and servicer disposition strategies with programs that best serve the growth and stabilization of communities nationwide.

Mr. Macias serves the National Association of Hispanic Real Estate Professionals (NAHREP), a nonprofit trade organization dedicated to empowering real estate professionals to increase sustainable Hispanic homeownership opportunities, as one of ten National Board of Directors and as President of the Orange County, California Chapter. A long time minority homeownership advocate, John also serves his local real estate community as an Advisory Board Member of both the Asian Real Estate Association of America, Orange County (AREAA) and the National Association of Real Estate Brokers, Orange County (NAREB).

John Macias’s Specialties:

Consultative approach to sales, willingness to try new and innovative ways to provide exemplary service, a leader in applying cutting-edge technology to improve workflow, always working to minimize client efforts and maximize returns for all parties involved in transactions by providing the most efficient technology solutions available in the marketplace, minority and first time homebuyer advocacy.
