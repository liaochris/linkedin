Jeff Sporl’s Summary

Exploration geophysicist working primarily onshore U.S. basins at present.

Jeff Sporl’s Specialties:

Exploratory prospect generation and reserve replacement. New fields. Joint ventures. Rapid property evaluations. Short term lease sales. Deal screening. Company turnarounds. 3-D field re-developments in areas of high structural complexity or complicated velocity fields. Detailed fault/fracture mapping. Multi-objective targeting. DHI identification with AVO/AVA.

Jeff Sporl’s Experience

  • President, Partner, and Geophysicist

    Streamline Exploration

    (Oil & Energy industry)

    1995 — Present (16 years )

    Natural gas, condensate, and oil prospect generation. Non-operator.

    2-D or 3-D seismic interpretation and drilling joint ventures (developmental field studies to exploratory initiation studies). Detailed G&G interpretations from initial data load to post-well appraisals; including seismic, subsurface, structural, attribute, volumetric, risk, and targeting analyses. Conventional, directional, and horizontal wells.

    Independently licensed on Seismic Micro-Technology software since 1995.

  • Partner and Entrepreneur

    MOAM

    (Online Media industry)

    1998 — 2003 (5 years )

    Management company. Investments, start-ups, and dot-coms. Domain name aftermarket. Website development and search engine optimization. Music recording and production, MP3 distribution, and internet radio promotion. Side projects.

  • Senior Staff Geophysicist

    Apache

    (Oil & Energy industry)

    1993 — 1995 (2 years )

    3-D interpretation and development. Generated and drilled wells on 3-D seismic data.

    3-D re-development of several of the Hall-Houston acquisition properties, including the key property unitization partner study on the largest natural gas and condensate field in the acquisition.

    3-D work flow demonstrations to investment analysts on behalf of both Apache and Landmark Graphics (in Houston, TX and Beaver Creek, CO).

  • Senior Geophysicist

    BHP Petroleum

    (Oil & Energy industry)

    1991 — 1993 (2 years )

    2-D and 3-D interpretation, 3-D acquisition, development and exploration. Generated and drilled wells on 2-D and 3-D seismic data.

    Lead interpreter on two large 3-D screening projects - 25,000 acres of Chevron 3-D in one month - 100,000 acres of Shell 3-D in six months; earning a BHP Petroleum Award for the extensive multi-level mapping.

  • Geophysicist / Senior Geophysicist

    Amoco

    (Oil & Energy industry)

    1985 — 1991 (6 years )

    2-D seismic processing, interpretation, sub-salt project group, development, and exploration. Generated and drilled wells on 2-D seismic data.

    Processed and interpreted (base of salt within 100') with the new 2-D depth migration on Amoco's first sub-salt well (Pooh prospect).

    Selected as one of the lead interpreting geophysicists for an acquisition attempt of Tenneco with only two years experience. Interpreted first 3-D seismic data set and utilized time slice technology in 1987.

  • Geologist / Geophysicist

    Geo-Technical Services Inc

    (Civil Engineering industry)

    1984 — 1984 (less than a year)

    Summer intern after junior year at Penn State.

    Electrical resistivity and borehole detection of caverns for highway and airport runway projects. Water table mapping and report preparation. Harrisburg, PA.
