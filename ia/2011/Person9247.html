  • Managing Member

    Ostara Asset Partners

    (Real Estate industry)

    March 1993 — Present (18 years )

    We have two distinct areas of specialty.

    For large investors, we have a number of programs from which buyers can purchase in substantial volume for steeply discounted pricing. The assets offered include residential and commercial hard assets (REO ’ s), performing notes and non-performing notes. Several of the programs require investments ranging from a $500M minimum to over $1B. Investors at this volume of transactions are provided direct access to the owners of the product. The vast majority of product portfolios are filled from custom orders from these clients.

    For smaller portfolio investors, OAP bridges a substantial gap in the bulk liquidation of real estate assets today. While there is institutional trading of “ toxic assets, ” it remains difficult for private investors to acquire portfolios that they can channel into retail or rental ready properties. Many of the investors who are well positioned to achieve this want portfolios they can purchase at an average of around $10M to $15M. Portfolios at this level are near impossible to find, or when found, are priced too high, as they have gone down a long brokering chain. OAP fills this gap by often working with our high volume transaction clients who are looking for a disposition strategy, some of which includes selling off regions to smaller investors.

    Whether you fall into the former or latter category, we look to build relationships that are not singular transactions in nature. We want a client base that knows we are the go to company to fill their needs.

    Our principals have been investing since 1997 and currently hold property portfolios. We look forward to the opportunity to work with you.
