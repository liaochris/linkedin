Toby Barnes's Summary

I am co-founder of Mudlark, an award winning entertainment production company founded in September 2008.

I spent the last 16 years developing and launching large online projects; consulting with large brand owners and generally causing trouble. With Mudlark I continue to develop my own products through the practise as well as working with a range of content creators.

I have spoken extensively on games, transmedia, cross-platform entertainment and social gaming and is currently advising the Mayor of London, and the GLA on Digital Strategy.

Specialties

Digital design, user engagement, playful and game design across all platforms from handheld, and console, to MMO and social gaming, social media, alternate reality gaming, user generated media, transmedia, post digital projects, developing arts projects, design, product design (anything I missed)
