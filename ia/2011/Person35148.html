Pekka Kess's Summary

As a Professor of Industrial Engineering and Management (IEM) In charge of the research and teaching as well as service to the society in the area of industrial engineering and management.
Special Interest Areas include:
Strategic Management,
Knowledge Management
Change Management,
Risk Management,
New Ventures,
TQM and
Global Business Issues
An additional expert area is the eLearning and Virtual University development.

In most cases the utilization of the ICT in business processes is the key issue.

The Industries covered by research and training are
Telecommunications,
Electronics,
Software,
Basic Metals and
Wood industries.
Some new research ventures into the Service sector (both private and public) are under way.

Management of several research projects: HILO (High Tech Logistics), ELVA (TQM of electronics manufacturing networks), NIPPU (Quality improvement in contract manufacturing networks), PROELECTRONICA (Electronics Manufacturing Improvement and on its effects on the society), ELISE (Creation of a global technical service and maintenance concept for the electronics and ICT industries), CODENET (Combining design with Concurrent Engineering Process), eXcellence (Development of quality management ro university eLearning), Tekevä (Development of the agri production and logistics)

The latest area of research and expertise has been eLearning and the virtual university concepts.

Specialties

Business / industrial process management: anaysis, improvement, re-engineering
ICT applications in various processes

eLearning
