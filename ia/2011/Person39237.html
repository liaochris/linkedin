Scott Kessler's Summary

I'm a high-energy and experienced manager and investment analyst.

For more than 15 years, I've focused on themes, issues and companies in and related to the Technology, Media and Telecommunications (TMT) areas. My professional experience includes more than a decade in equity research, as well as time as a private regulatory attorney and adviser on Capitol Hill.

I've covered stocks in the Technology Sector for more than 12 years, with an emphasis on Internet and Software names. I've received internal and external accolades for my efforts and achievements, enjoy regular communications with clients, and am frequently featured by major media outlets as an industry expert.
