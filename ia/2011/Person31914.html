Religious Institutions industry

January 1983 – January 2010 (27 years 1 month)

I began teaching in the Presbyterian Church and several of its churches in PEI, Nova Scotia and New Brunswick, working with and developing leadership, ensuring the health of communities, and participated in the development of teaching materials. I also started a new church that is in Kentville, NS.

In 1995 I became a pastor and teacher in the Vineyard Church in New Brunswick until 2010.

I was a guest teacher at a college in Manila Philippines in 2000. I taught a class of over 50 young adults in leadership skills, ministry and biblical study. This experience deepened my strong appreciation of other cultures.

In 2002 I planted a church in New London, New Hampshire.

In 2010 I was invited with other bloggers to Haiti to document in writing and photography the aftermath of the earthquake. This was to sustain interest in charitable work there. I interviewed many Haitians. We were able to launch new charitable programs to aid those most affected.
