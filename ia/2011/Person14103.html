Erin Joy's Summary

Erin Joy is a third-generation female entrepreneur. As a child, she played “office” under her mother’s desk. Today, she helps other women unlock the potential in their own companies, and in themselves.

Erin understands the rewards and challenges of owning your own company. A businesswoman since 1995, Erin has more than a decade’s experience assessing and improving underperforming organizations. She also retains a large network of colleagues across multiple business disciplines. If there’s a need, she can fill it, or find someone who will!

Erin’s consulting and executive coaching services can improve all aspects of your organization:

· Business Development
· Market Research
· Marketing Plans
· Meeting/Workshop Facilitation
· Networking
· Operations Management
· Project Management
· Public Speaking
· Quality Improvement
· Work Flow

She has improved the profitability of companies in the following industries:
- Construction
- Homebuilding
- Mortgage
- Real estate
- Interior design
- Technology

Why is Erin so good at creating growth opportunities? Central to Erin’s philosophy is the concept of well-being. When a company is inefficient, employees and executives alike can feel overwhelmed and off-balance. By making the changes needed for a healthy business, employees are able to work to their highest abilities – and growth occurs.

How Erin Works:
Erin will help you reach your organization’s potential using her unique Growth Opportunity Key. An action plan will be created. Then she’ll work shoulder-to-shoulder with you to manage the project and team members to successful completion. After the transitions are made, Erin will continue to follow up with you so that your newly maximized company remains on-track.

For more information on how Erin Joy and KeyWomen Consulting can improve your business, contact her at 314-223-0000.
