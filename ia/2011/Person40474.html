Simon Pursehouse’s Summary

Worked in various areas of the Music Industry including management, promotion, radio and publishing. I have a passionate interest in music and I strive to keep up to date in what is a ever-changing and often troubled industry.

I have been working at Sentric Music since it’s conception in 2006 and have helped oversee every area of the business during its growth. My goal is continue to grow Sentric Music into a simply essential service for unsigned/independent musicians in the UK and then to look at applying the model abroad into other potential markets.

Within the broadcast media:

- We have an ongoing relationship with UK terrestrial channel Five TV, offering them music for idents and inhouse adverts
- We also have an ongoing relationship with Lime Pictures, regularly having music featured on the teen soap Hollyoaks
- We’ve had music featured on Channel 4 trailers and on BBC productions (Eastenders/Strictly Come Dancing/CBeebies)
- We’ve had music featured on various US productions (One Tree Hill, Vernoica Marrs, Cold Case, Paris Hilton’s BFF, VH1’s Celebrity Soberhouse, Burn Notice)
- We’ve had music featured on advertising campaigns within the UK and internationally (Lexus - US, De Montfort Uni - UK, Stockland Estates - AUS)

I also do a bit of music journalism on the side and I rather enjoy rambling on about various subjects in the Sentric Music Blog which is aimed at advising independent/unsigned talent about the goings on in this industry we call music. Have a gander at the blog below within the nifty little Wordpress add in! (or go here - www.sentric.wordpress.com)
