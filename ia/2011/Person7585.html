Catherine Greenland's Summary

We had no experience of running or owning a restaurant before we bought the Polmary 7yrs ago. Hit the floor running, and haven't stopped since. Have slowly built up a healthy customer base and a good reputation locally. Also have a good work force, and have created a family atmosphere both in the restaurant and the kitchen. My husband does all the cooking, and our business partner helps us in the kitchen. I do some of the baking, all of the washing up and all of the cleaning!

We aim to build on our good reputation in the years to come, and will hopefully retire whilst still able to enjoy it.

Specialties
