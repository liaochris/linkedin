Biography

Mr. Kambiz Golchin educated at Wesley College in Dublin undertook his medical studies at Royal College of Surgeons in Ireland. Graduating in 1994 he continued his surgical training in Northern Ireland. He became a Fellow of the Royal College of Surgeons of England in1998.

Mr. Golchin specialised and trained in Ear, Nose, and Throat surgery and passed the Otolaryngology and Head & Neck Surgery Fellowship examination of the Royal College of Surgeons of England in 2004. FRCS (ORL-HNS).

Having an interest in all aspects of Facial Plastic and Cosmetic Surgery, he was awarded a one-year fellowship at the Australasian Academy of Facial Plastic Surgery. Mr. Golchin trained in Sydney under Dr. Martyn Mendelsohn, a leading Australian Facial Plastic Surgeon renowned for pioneering work in modern rhinoplasty techniques.

Mr.Golchin established the “Fellow Clinic” in Sydney offering full range of both surgical and non-surgical procedures in Facial Plastic Surgery to facilitate ‘hands on’ experience for trainees in this field.

Mr. Golchin was entered into the General Medical Council UK, and Irish Medical Council Specialist Register for Otolaryngology Surgery in 2006. He is a member of the British Association of Otorhinolarygologists and Head and Neck surgeons and the European Academy of Facial Plastic Surgery.

Mr. Golchin has written several papers and presented in scientific meetings. With aview towards teaching and training Mr. Golchin completed a postgraduate Masters degree in Medical Science in Education for the Health Care Profession at Queens University Belfast.
