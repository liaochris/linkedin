Allen Bonde's Summary

Allen is a high-energy business leader and entrepreneur, with 20 years experience in product development and marketing of Web applications and services. He has a proven track record growing new businesses and creating innovative online marketing and support programs, launched Evoke CRM Partners in September 2009, and joined the founding team at Offerpop in April 2010.

Career highlights include:

As Chief Marketing Officer at eVergance, Allen helped to grow and lead a $18+ million CRM and Web services business, brought the eVergance brand into the market in the US and UK, and was instrumental in the company's sale to KANA in 2007.

As a management consultant and market watcher, Allen started studying and defining early internet computing and online advertising models at Yankee Group and McKinsey, has appeared on CNBC, FOX News and PBS, and is the author of numerous white papers, articles, and guest posts in Social Times, 1to1Media, B2CMarketingInsider, CIO.com, CRM Magazine, SearchCRM.com and other publications.

As an entrepreneur and business leader, Allen started his first company, Allen Bonde Group (ABG) in 2001, grew it to 50 clients, and merged it with eVergance in 2005. Prior to ABG, he launched new business units at Extraprise and Yankee Group. He is now co-founder of Offerpop, which recently received its Series A funding.

As an advisor to early stage ventures, Allen has worked with successful start-ups such as Jive Software, Motive (now Alcatel-Lucent) and Siperian (now Informatica), is active in the WPI Venture Forum, and is on the Advisory Board for the New England Center for the Performing Arts.

Specialties

Bridging the worlds of business, marketing and technology to bring value to each; Spotting and defining disruptive Web trends - and creating products and services to monetize them; Building practical market and growth strategies, online support programs and customer communities; Starting up and running new services businesses, forming partnerships, generating buzz and closing deals; Hiring, motivating and managing high-performance, often virtual/distributed teams
