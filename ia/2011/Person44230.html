Johnathan M. Holifield’s Summary

Described as a “Jack of many trades and master of getting things done,” Johnathan M. Holifield is founder of Trim Tab System, LLC, a business, organizational and personal development and leadership methodology, which applies innovation concepts and tools to generate exponential impact.

Prior to establishing Trim Tab System, Johnathan’s diverse interests and talents led him to serve as President/CEO of Urban League of Greater Cleveland and of the Buffalo Olmsted Parks Conservancy, and as executive vice president of Council for World-Class Communities in Benton Harbor, Michigan, vice president of New Economy Enterprise for the Cincinnati USA Chamber of Commerce and founding executive director of CincyTech. Additionally, he practiced civil litigation with the Manley, Burke & Lipton firm and the Hamilton County, Ohio Prosecuting Attorney’s Office and was a member of the National Football League’s Cincinnati Bengals.

Passionate about business, organizational and personal innovation and deeply motivated to achieve exponential results, rather than incremental outcomes, Johnathan has used the Trim Tab System throughout his career to contribute meaningful leadership, including:

▪ Winning government, education, corporate and philanthropic support for major new initiatives
▪ Master planning for technology, entrepreneurship and innovation growth and urban green-based revitalization
▪ Designing economic inclusion and community benefits strategies for projects totaling nearly $1.5 billion
▪ Establishing market-leading regional technology-based economic development organization and Leadership Council, comprised of Fortune 500 CEOs, government and university officials and technology advocates
▪ Collaboratively winning $40+ million of grant funding and forming $40+ million to spark creation of a $250 million early-stage capital network
▪ Creating 21st Century public high schools focused on information, design and health technologies
