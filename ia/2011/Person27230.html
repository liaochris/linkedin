Lynda McDaniel’s Summary

I'm a business writing coach and creativity catalyst. When we work together, I'll show you (and/or your staff) how to use an easy brainstorming technique to jump-start your business writing--and your creativity. In the process, you'll quickly overcome any fears of writing. (We all have them to varying degrees.) Through business writing workshops and newsletters, writing tips and creative inspiration, you'll learn how to fill that screen with effective business writing that converts into increased sales, productivity, and recognition.

I help you find your voice, your talent, and your natural writing rhythm so you can stand out from the rest—whether you're job-hunting, selling, or communicating at the office.

I've enjoyed a long career as a professional writer and journalist, and I know firsthand the benefits effective business writing can deliver. I now offer the fruits of my life through the Association for Creative Business Writing, my award-winning book, “Words at Work: Powerful business writing delivers increased sales, improved results, and even a promotion or two," and the e-book series, Inspiration on Demand.
