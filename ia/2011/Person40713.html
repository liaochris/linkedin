Susan M Warner’s Summary

Throughout my career I have led groups in all aspects of Manufacturing Operations, with a focus on Supply Chain Management, Materials, Inventory Management, Logistics and Lean process optimization. I enjoy working in Global organizations with talented people from different countries and cultures. I have a record of bringing diverse groups together to meet shared goals through clear communication and personal relationships. I have a high-level user knowledge of SAP (two implementations), JD Edwards and many other ERP systems. I have obtained CPIM certification from APICS.
