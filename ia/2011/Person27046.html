Lucinda Bredin's Summary

Writer and Editor for National Broadsheet newspapers and magazines.

Specialist in launching glossy magazines. One of the original team that founded The Week magazine (I am the Arts Editor). In 2004, launched a magazine for Bonhams, which I continue to edit, and in 2007, created PrivatSea, a twice-yearly magazine about yachts and lifestyle.

Interested in any new projects in the magazine industry.

Specialties

Launching magazines, assembling teams of journalists, creating a structure for a magazine, editing copy, knowledge of all areas from layout (proficient in Quark and Indesign) to overseeing repro and printing.
