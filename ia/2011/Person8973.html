Gwendolyn Allen’s Summary

"FAIREST OF THE FINEST"

Hi my name is Gwendolyn Allen aka Coach Gia, I have been recognized as a loyal and dedicated member of the Police Department of the City of New York. Having achieved 20 years of dedicated service to the citizens of the city of New York, and contributions to the “Finest” Police Force in the world, I am now enjoying my retirement, and my Coaching Practice!

"Helping others to make healthy lifestyle changes one person at a time."

My business is coaching, I am the "coach" and I hold no agenda other than my clients.
The relationship between me and my client is co-creative, meaning that we are equals and both have an active role. I am not a therapist, counselor or consultant. I am a trained coach using honed communication skills to support you as a detached thinking partner. Together we create more power for you to effect meaningful change and take dynamic actions toward your goals.

There’s nothing like coaching, to understand the value of it, experience it for yourself!
