Jason Mehmet’s Summary

I founded WhizzyDigital in 2007 after building up 10 years of web design and development experience.

Whilst working for a range of companies and industries, I began to believe that creating a fast moving business with none of the drag involved with large organisations was the way to go. So that is exactly what I did.

Through WhizzyDigital I provide freelance and contract services from Reading, Berkshire, and can help with anything from web design and development, to web hosting and internet marketing.

When I'm not working, I spend the rest of my time with my family, and make efforts to occupy the time of my daughter to varying degrees of success.
