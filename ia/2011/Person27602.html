Manuel Melo {manuel.melo@centrodecontacto.com}'s Summary

Manuel Melo, 44 years old, is a Call Center, Contact Center and Communication Center Projects Manager.

Manuel Melo started to focus his career on Telemarketing, Call and Contact Center activities in 1997, with responsibilities in creating new ways of communication, improving interaction results effectiveness and building relationships with customers and citizens. In that assignment, he became involved in developing communication operations for both private companies and public institutions and had the opportunity to take part in the largest and integrated multi-channel Call Center & Contact Center projects, working with institutions all over Iberia (Portugal & Spain).

Previously, Manuel Melo worked on Direct Marketing and Sales Communication, from 1988 until 1996.

Currently, Manuel Melo is the Manager Director of Point Center Group – an Iberia Group that provides products, services and media for Marketing and Sales operations, focused on Call Center, Contact Center and Communication operations – with two main headquarters:
- the Portuguese Headquarter, based in Lisbon, Portugal, developing services for all the Portuguese language countries and
- the Spanish Headquarter, based in Madrid, Spain, for all the Spanish language countries.

Manuel Melo launched in 2000 two professional portals:

- Centro de Contacto ( www.centrodecontacto.com : Portal for all the Call and Contact Center professional community;

- PressHit ( www.presshit.com ): Portal for all the ICT – Information and Communications Technologies professionals.

In 2004, sharing the vision and the market knowledge of the European Union, Manuel Melo managed the kick-off of four new Europe based professional forums:
- Europe Contact Center;
- Europe Citizen;
- Europe Offshoring Center;
- Contact Institute.

Actually, Manuel Melo is launching a new professional services network:
- www.centrotelefonicoderelacionamento.com;
- www.sistemasdegravacao.com.
