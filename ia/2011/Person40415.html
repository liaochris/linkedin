Simon Bock’s Summary

Senior level intellectual property attorney specializing in trademark and copyright clearance, protection and enforcement; intellectual property licensing; intellectual property litigation and licensing; intellectual property transactional due diligence; and agreement drafting related to mergers, acquisitions and IP securitization. Almost 20 years experience advising and representing both Fortune 500 and early stage companies in various fields of business, including the apparel and toy industries. Managed and implemented major international and domestic brand clearance and protection programs including worldwide brand filing programs and anti-counterfeiting seizures and training.

I continue to look for opportunities to represent additional clients in my areas of expertise either in-house or as outside counsel.
