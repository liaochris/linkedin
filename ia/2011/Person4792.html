Benita McShan’s Summary

I am a professional writer skilled at explaining and delivering complex information in a format that is easily understood by the general public. Whether you need a well crafted white paper, technical documentation, RFP management, corporate newsletter creation and production or a simple press release I'm your go-to-gal. I provide communications coordination services to commercial and nonprofit organizations. I offer freelance writing, contract management and paralegal services.

My expertise is in contracts and proposal management. I have more than a decade of experience as a regional corporate manager of contracts for a leading telecommunications and former Fortune 50 company.
