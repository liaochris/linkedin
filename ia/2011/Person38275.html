Ryan Lucia’s Summary

I am currently seeking a position in sales management with a larger organization based in Sacramento, Northern California, or the Greater Seattle area.

I recently left my position as VP of Business Development for Voyage Financial Group based in Sacramento, CA. My primary function was to recruit and develop outside branches for the organization. It allowed me to combine my consulting skills with my marketing know-how as a big part of developing branches is honing their individual marketing strategies. I was also tasked with developing Voyage's corporate identity and branding, as well as the ultimate goal, growth of loan volume.

As the Manager of Marketing and Special Projects for WebLessons.com I grew quite a bit in my creative skills and in the use of Adobe Creative Suite, Dreamweaver, HTML and even in Flash. The project management experience was also fantastic. I also learned quite a bit from observation about what to do, and what not to do as a leader and businessman. I also trained (sales skills & product knowledge), managed and supported our sales team.

I was with DHL Express (and Airborne Express) for 8 years after graduating from college. I quickly worked my way from Inside Sales to District Sales Manager through strong growth and sales. I also had some unique opportunities to help in product development and marketing. Further, I was involved with a team that developed, implemented and rolled out SFA/CRM systems. Working on these side projects helped me find that I am best at developing products and ideas and driving through implementation.

I look forward to continuing my growth as a salesman, sales leader, and in marketing. I believe it is best to always continue the development of not only your primary and known talents, but other areas of expertise.

As a manager I have learned that it is not only your job to guide and lead your team to success, but also to learn from their strengths, knowledge and experiences to best achieve SMART goals.
