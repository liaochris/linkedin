Byron Udell’s Summary

Byron has spent the last 24 years in the life insurance business. During that time, he founded AccuQuote.com, which has grown into one of the nation's largest and most respected life insurance brokerage firms. He is licensed to do business in all 50 states. Since 1986, AccuQuote has helped hundreds of thousands of people save a fortune on their life insurance. They do this by quickly and easily comparing the price, features, and financial strength of hundreds of products from trusted brand name insurance carriers such as Prudential, Transamerica, ING, Genworth, Fidelity Life, Mutual of Omaha, Lincoln, Protective, and many more. And all of this is done in just a few minutes, right over the phone, from the convenience of your home or office.
