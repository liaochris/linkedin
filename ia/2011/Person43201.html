Thomas Valenti's Experience

Founding Member

Mediators Beyond Borders

Non-Profit; Non-Profit Organization Management industry

February 2009 – Present (2 years 1 month)

Mediators Beyond Borders (“MBB”) is a nonprofit organization that brings together experienced volunteer mediators worldwide to improve conflict resolution capacity and support alternative approaches to expressing, negotiating and resolving interpersonal, political, economic, social, ethnic and religious differences.
MBB‘s members are internationally recognized experts, premier thinkers and innovators, and practitioners in dispute resolution. This broad and deep array of conflict resolution, peacemaking and capacity-building experience has heretofore never been assembled as a volunteer organization. .
It has projects in communities in Liberia, in a refugee camp in Ghana, assisting community integration of Somali refugees in Pennsylvania, and with Katrina victims in the Gulf Region of the US. Projects are currently being organized in Nepal, Zimbabwe, Israel and the Palestinian Territories, Thailand, Kosovo and the countries surrounding it, and with indigenous communities in Canada. Teams are actively researching project possibilities in Colombia, East Timor, Cambodia, Yemen, Nigeria, Kenya, and Ecuador.
