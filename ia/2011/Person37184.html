Rob Christianson’s Summary

I'm Rob, a freelance illustrator and graphic designer. I've had over 12 years of agency and in-house experience, where I've worked with clients such as Focus on the Family, World Vision, Randall House Publishing, Cinnabon, Starbucks and Microsoft.

My recent packaging for J&D Foods (BaconPOP, Bacon Ranch Dip & Dressing and Mmmvelopes) has been featured on Gizmodo.com, ThinkGeek.com and The Today Show with Kathie Lee and Hoda!

Take a look at my portfolio at http://www.RobTheDesigner.com. I have a fun and friendly style, and I'm a breeze to work with (or so I hear). Have a great day and I hope to hear from you soon...

Rob Christianson’s Specialties:

Strengths: Illustration, graphic design, Web, Email design, product packaging, poster design, etc.


Style: Wacky, fun, skewed, colorful, irreverent.


Software: Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Dreamweaver, After Effects. Macintosh evangelist and PC tolerant. ;)
