Monique Hodgkinson’s Summary

I am a resourceful, entrepreneurial, and energetic leader with over 15 years planning and executing strategic marketing solutions for Fortune-500 companies, non-profit organizations, and small business (SMB) clients. As an astute marketer, I know how to leverage the right mix of media and messages to create the greatest marketing impact. I’m well-versed in all aspects of marketing program development, including competitive analysis, survey techniques, collateral development, customer focus, and analytics. With the explosive growth in social media and how it is used in strategically marketing a message I bring an extensive experience with web and print communications, knowing well how to design and fashion the right message for the right medium and how to create synergy using both.

My most recent seven years have been as a Consultant at a boutique marketing-services company responsible to client development, client service design, and delivery for SMB and corporate clients to help their business with both developing marketing strategies based on consumer web technologies and then delivering on those strategies to achieve the desired operational outcomes. My focus is creating and delivering comprehensive marketing programs based on sound strategy and detailed planning that are anchored in client needs and my expertise. I work collaboratively with clients to maximize value and deliver the exceptional, not merely what is expected. I work with outsourced providers to help my clients achieve most value from their budget and to provide the best outcomes.

Previous three years as Senior Manager of Customer Satisfaction at Network Appliances responsible for transforming how customer experience was managed in different geographies. Transformed European, customer-facing operations to lift the C-Sat scores from the low 20s to high 80s in just one year. Transformed ops in a downturn to automate processes for efficiency.
