Steve Kapner's Summary

Steve Kapner co-founded Aquent, the global marketing and design staffing company, in 1986 while an undergraduate at Harvard University. Within 5 years Steve and his partners built the business from a dorm room start-up to the 12th fastest-growing private company in America, and today, to 45 offices across 7 countries.

Aquent finds work for close to 10,000 marketers and designers a year, at clients ranging from the emerging leaders in digital marketing to those with the world’s best-known brands. Aquent has 4 offices in Japan, where the company has operated as jinzai haken and jinzai shokai since 1997.
