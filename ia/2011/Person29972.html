Michael Chadwick’s Summary

Mr. Chadwick is an accomplished commercial real estate professional with almost 10 years of experience dealing in all property types in most of the major U.S. markets, at all levels of the capital stack. His experience includes underwriting first mortgages through the Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac multifamily programs at Berkshire Mortgage (Deutsche Bank) to structuring mezzanine & equity investments with TriSail Capital Corp. (Bank of America). Mr. Chadwick currently manages TPG’s $1.8B debt portfolio and is responsible for the due diligence and capitalization of all prospective multifamily investments. In addition, Mr. Chadwick is technically skilled and has a track record/library of developing very complex Excel models used to evaluate traditional acquisitions, first mortgage financing, equity investments (waterfall), note purchases, fund/portfolio performance.

Michael Chadwick’s Experience

  • Director of Capital Markets

    The Procaccianti Group

    (Privately Held; Hospitality industry)

    May 2007 — Present (3 years 10 months)

    Michael Chadwick is the Director of Capital Markets at The Procaccianti Group, a privately-held real estate investment company that specializes in the acquisition, renovation and management of investment real estate.
