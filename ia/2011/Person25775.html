Laurie Posner’s Summary

Laurie leads PNC Capital Advisors' (formerly Allegiant Asset Management) institutional sales and marketing within the Western United States. Throughout her seventeen year financial career she has demonstrated a successful track record of developing relationships and growing revenues to fuel business growth. Prior to PNC she has held senior level business development, sales and team management roles, from which she has built an extensive network of business relationships. She earned her B.S. in Applied Economics from the University of San Francisco and is a decorated veteran of the United States Army.
