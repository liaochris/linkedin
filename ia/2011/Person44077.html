Susan Rivers's Summary

Imaginative thought-leadership strategist for the C-suite; proven success in raising profile of individuals and organizations. Seasoned global communications professional adept with both traditional and new media; actively working with major financial-services trade groups as a thought leader on integrated communications and social media for the industry.

Award-winning journalist with more than 20 years in print media including The Wall Street Journal, Baltimore Sun, Newsday; stellar list of clips and superb project management, writing/editing/public presentation skills.

Experienced in branding and marketing for higher education (Princeton University) and financial services, conducting government relations and developing good links with federal agencies and key policy/advocacy groups.

Strong global perspective: Lived and/or worked in Europe, North Africa and Middle East; former general manager of Al Manahil women's club in Riyadh, (owned by members of the Saudi royal family.) Fluent French.
