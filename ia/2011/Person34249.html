Paul O'Flaherty's Summary

Your Web was started by Paul O'Flaherty, an Irish IT professional living in Denmark. Paul brings nearly 20 years of developing software to Your Web, as well as more than a decade of developing for the web.

Your Web is in a unique position to combine software and web developing, hardware, marketing and training expertise and deliver them to the Danish IT market from a single company.

Besides the commercial service offered by Your Web, we also maintain the OFlaherty.dk network of sites. Paul maintains technology blog and broadcasts a regular technology based internet radio show (a podcast).

These activities put Your Web on the "bleeding edge" of the internet as a consumer, producer and innovator, which gives us a distinct advantage over our competition. Being on the forefront of internet technology allows us to provide you with the best possible solution for your business at the best possible price, while ensuring that your site remains relevant now and in the future.

Specialties

SEO, Web Design, XML, HTML, PHP, SQL, Java, Software development under VB, C, C++
