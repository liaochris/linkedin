Massimo Paolini's Summary

In a world full of artificial intelligence, it’s easy to break down a web analysis consultant into small parts. Knows Google Analytics? Check. Has increased traffic on several business sites? Check.

But besides the buzz words, what do you really need?

This is where MPThree stands apart. We understand all of the relevant technology applications and use strong analytical skills to provide insights and recommendations for changes. We’ll even help you convince key company decision makers. But most importantly, you get a pro-active partner in analyzing customer behavior and using available data to influence changes on your website.

Ultimately, you’ll get a collaborator—someone who helps create a vision, turn it into individual tasks, and end up with a seamless plan to deliver your audience at your doorstep.

We don’t just deliver jargon—we provide REAL intelligence.
