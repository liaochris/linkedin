Abhilash Ravishankar’s Summary

Three years into the Oil & Gas Industry, I have loved the challenges of leading multi-national diverse teams in some of the toughest environmental conditions, being responsible for millions of dollars of equipment and being the key Schlumberger liaison for some of the biggest Oil & Gas companies in the world.

I co-founded yofa (Youth Factor), a non-profit society in India, where we work on novel projects to engage youth in social change. Through two programs - Grassroutes and Activist Circle, we have created a small, yet significant, dent in the Indian social sector by inspiring and enabling young leaders.

In previous years, I have been involved with various entrepreneurial programs ranging from web 2.0 to social entrepreneurial ventures. I have been President of one of India's top 5 entrepreneurship centers for 18 months.
