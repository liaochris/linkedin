Lisa L Galarneau's Summary

I am a researcher (anthropology), strategist, and project leader specializing in digital cultures and how people around the world use technology and access media. For my doctoral research in Screen and Media Studies, I used ethnographic and traditional methods to explore social learning and group dynamics in and around massively multiplayer online games (MMOGs) and virtual worlds, both online and offline (13 countries), including a large survey of online gamers.

In the past I have worked in a variety of roles focused on improving experiences, and have used a range of research methods and approaches to understand those perspectives. I also have a technical and design background and can function as a useful bridge on teams, as well as being acquainted with the latest research, analysis and design tools.

My view is frequently strategic but I perform tactically as well, rallying groups, marshalling resources, effecting innovation thinking and positive change.

Specialties
