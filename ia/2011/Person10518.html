David Douglas's Summary

In 1999 I founded what is now one of Dublin's leading creative digital agencies, ebow. I am also founder & Creative Director of Gallery Number One art gallery, and co-founder & partner in online clothes store Genius (www.genius.ie).

I specialise in connecting the dots between digital, and the "real world", with an ability to turn great ideas into great products, healthy sales, and happy customers.

GN1 (Gallery Number 1) celebrates the creative link between art, music & pop culture. It is housed in the ebow HQ at No.1, Castle Street. I was responsible for bringing the artistic talent of Ronnie Wood, Pattie Boyd & Bob Dylan's Rolling Thunder Revue show to Ireland. I supply art to Dublin's collectors and businesses, including the new Gibson Hotel who liked our stunning picture of the moment Bruce Springsteen first met Bob Dylan.

I have worked with : Aras an Uachtarain, O2, Entertainment.ie, Hairy Baby, The GAA, Audi, Volkswagen, AIB, Coolmore, Hector, Irish Museum of Modern Art and The Gaiety School of Acting. I have been profiled by Image Magazine, Sunday Business Post and The Examiner; with additional articles on branding and marketing published in the Irish Marketing Journal and Marketing. I judge the GAA McNamee Communication Awards and the UK's Fresh Awards. I have been on Matt Coopers 'The Last Word' and used as a case study 'Early Adopter' by The Irish Times.

I believe in allowing people do whatever it is they do best in order to create great work. What I do best is I apply relish, passion & energy to our projects because I love what it is we do. If I can't do this then I don't.

Specialties

Online Design and Marketing, Branding, Corporate Design, Art Events and most importantly I am quite the reader in my spare time....along with a weird thing for space relations.....
