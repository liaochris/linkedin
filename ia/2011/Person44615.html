Ursula Klein's Summary

Over the past 10 years, Ursula has developed and implemented successful marketing and public relations campaigns, both in the corporate and non-profit sectors. This included designing print and web-based promotional material, resource tools and corporate communications and assisting in strategically positioning organizations’ programs and services in the media and amongst stakeholder groups.

The effectiveness of these initiatives depended on continued research and analyses of industry trends, capitalizing on related marketing opportunities, ensuring consistency in branding and having a sound understanding of effective design elements and web capabilities. To further ensure the success of these activities, Ursula had to establish key partnerships and alliances with corporate partners, government representatives, media and volunteers. Her skills in these areas have led to a proven track record of an expansion of programs and an increase in enrollment, funding and volunteer participation.

In addition, Ursula has extensive experience in participating on a variety of committees and taskforces, reporting to stakeholders and setting benchmarks to monitor and evaluate all aspects of campaign and project/program performance, including budget management, media tracking and retention rates and stewardship of corporate donors, participants and volunteers. These skills are complemented by several years of working in the human resources and special events fields, including managing logistics, securing sponsorships and recruiting, training and motivating both staff and volunteer groups.

Ursula is also an avid volunteer; from actively serving on a variety of committees to working with community service providers to coordinating and participating in special events such as sporting competitions and events, charity auctions and golf tournaments, community festivals, telethons and other fundraising drives.

Specialties

Communication (writing, editing, public speaking, facilitating, presenting, training), marketing (branding, design & layout, direct marketing, promotions, public relations), program development and management, campaign planning, organization, training and motivating staff and volunteers, stewarding donors and volunteers, fundraising, research, budget management
