Stephen Ng's Summary

My best belief is what I do today will help my career tomorrow. Expanding my educational comfort zone means expanding my ability to aim higher and achieve more. My major strengths are high sense of responsibility, self-motivation and ambition.

I learnt that people who help me, who invest in me, become my life long partners, and my success is their success that relationships are symbiotic throughout my career progression. I learnt responsibility based on others' faith in me.

I have a professional background with a good mix of education and business acumen, the ability to liaise with people with all levels, the ability to anticipate questions and problems, hands on experience in implementation, enough experience to realize there is more than one solution to key business issues.

To remain competitive and to be able to fully understand how all of the facets of business inter-relate, it is important to have a diverse but sound knowledge of many business and process areas. The commitment towards life long learning prepares myself for the wide roles of management.

Learn new skills. Look at another perspective. Accept new challenges. Take risks. I dedicate my life to continuing professional development (CPD) with the following human capital investments over time:

* MA (CityU), Professional Accounting and Information Systems
* Postgraduate Diploma (CityU), Corporate Administration
* Graduate Diploma (HKU), Management Consulting and Change
* BA(Hons) (Brock University), Administration and Economics

The focus and exceptional effort I expend to earn additional advanced degrees will show corporate stakeholders that I have the ability and drive to contribute to their business at a high level.
