Elizabeth White-Dziub, PsyD's Summary

A Skilled and effective MENTAL HEALTH PROFESSIONAL with 14 years of comprehensive experience in individual, group, and couples counseling, as well as child/adolescent, and family therapy.

Noted achievements in conducting a wide range of psychological testing for social-emotional functioning, learning disabilities, and age-appropriate development.

Broad background in children and family dynamics, play and art therapy, and grief work.

Demonstrated skills in conducting psychosocial assessments, crisis interventions, treatment planning, and case management duties.

Experienced in working with severely emotionally disabled children and adolescents, foster children, mentally ill, chemically dependent, and those impacted by abuse and other traumas.

A strong emphasis in Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy, as well as in Dialectical Behavioral Therapy techniques.

An active contributor to the interdisciplinary team process, and successful working with clients of diverse ages, as well as cultural and socio-economic backgrounds.

Strong family focus, and very successful in working collaboratively with parents, teachers, children’s welfare agency, medical providers, and the juvenile justice system.

Highly organized, and well developed communication, writing and leadership skills.

Self-starter in re-inroducing psychotherapeutic and educational groups, which contributed to being the highest performer in productivity.
