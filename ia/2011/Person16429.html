Graham Hoad’s Summary

Sales Tax Resource Group represents companies of all sizes in sales tax audits before the California State Board of Equalization, as well as all other state tax agencies.

STRG also performs contingent fee based credit and refund studies, and otherwise consults on sales tax matters, including nexus, taxability studies, voluntary disclosure, due diligence, and training. We are former California sales tax auditor and former "Big 4" multistate tax consultants.

Graham Hoad’s Specialties:

We are looking to serve companies who are undergoing sales tax audits by the California State Board of Equalization, or any other state tax agency.

In addition, if the company believed it may have sales tax exposure in states where they are not registered, we are here to help.
