Ray Darnott’s Summary

Seasoned media specialist with more than 15 years of experience working in the Television and Film industries, the Internet industry as well as marketing and in Public Relations.
Specialized in the U.S. Hispanic and Latin American markets.
Currently focused in the producion of commercials and promotional spots for diverse clients.
More than 9 years of experience in the News business covering many areas ranging from editorial management, content creation, branding, marketing, promotions, reporting and team building.
Creative thinker whose ideas and content have directly increased monetizing opportunities and ratings.
Excellent managerial skills, multi-project experience and ability to motivate crews to perform at its best. Project Management trained. Effective writer and communicator, with a special strength in creating compelling advertising and promotional copy which has proven to be effective on diverse audiences. Fluent in Spanish and Portuguese.
Very well versed on new media and new trends in the communications business.
Strong technical background. Operates most of the standard TV equipment and software and proficient also with technology/software for web content creation, management and graphic optimization.
