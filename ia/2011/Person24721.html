Kirk LaPointe's Summary

I have managed in newspapers, news agencies, television and online media for 25 years. I have helped launch media and participated in revitalizing media. I have been a reporter, editor, teacher and television host. In addition to my work as the Ombudsman for the CBC, I teach at the graduate school of journalism at University of British Columbia. I write a blog on media ethics and change at www.themediamanager.com.

Specialties

Newsroom management, precision journalism, teaching, freedom of information, ethics.
