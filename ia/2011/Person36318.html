Lee Moody’s Summary

Working for Mohawk Fine Papers is a perfect fit for me because I am passionate about paper, and all styles of creativity. I love to talk to students about graphic design + I love to meet the masters of design that started it all.
I went to school in New England & NYC and was a designer with my own business in NYC for many years.
One day skipping down the path of life, I bumped into Mohawk Fine Papers, and found a home.
The paper world is like no other....every day I have the pleasure of seeing beautiful design on Mohawk's beautiful papers.
I can only equate it to perhaps living with a fantastic chef who cooked gourmet for me every night next to our own vineyard in Italy !
That would be nice....but I think I would eat too much.
I like to collect paper, books, cool stuff, and anything that makes you say, "wow" !
And in my off time, you can find me near any water swimming or kayaking, or on the slopes skiing with friends or reading a book !
And of course....showing YOU....Mohawk Paper !
