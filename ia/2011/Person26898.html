Louis Johnson IV, MBA, CPBE’s Summary

I began my career as an electronics engineering technician specializing in audio/video and RF equipment used in telecommunications and broadcast television.

Over the past 25 years I have successfully analyzed, designed and supported business systems in mortgage banking, legal, architectural and engineering vertical markets. As a former technical director in a top ten television market, I am an SBE certified professional broadcast engineer and broadcast networking technologist with an 8VSB DTV endorsement.

In addition to my technical background, I am a creative writer and developer of marketing strategies in various business fields. After recently earning an MBA, I have included business research, analysis and consulting among the many jobs I enjoy performing on a daily basis.
