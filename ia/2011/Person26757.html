Lonnie Sciambi’s Summary

Experienced small business advisor and mentor, who brings a track record of success and hard-won experience across a mix of markets, products and services. More than 30 years of successful P&L management, primarily as an entrepreneur and CEO for small businesses, both public and private, from early stage to turnaround situations. Have sold two of my own small businesses as a serial entreprenur and have advised, mentored and/or invested in dozens of small businesses. Successfully completed multiple small business turnarounds. Functional expertise in sales, marketing, finance and operations in multiple industries and vertical markets. Skilled negotiator and closer with a major sales successes, more than three dozen merger/acquisition transactions completed and more than $350 million of capital raised as executive manager and investment banker.

This background and experience provides the ability to assist small business owners in quickly assessing a situation and developing practical solutions in the shortest possible time. It also provides the basis to help entrepreneurs avoid the many pitfalls of starting and growing a business. On a very select basis, I also mentor very early stage entrepreneurs to help guide them through initial launch. I was fortunate to have been mentored early in my career and I am merely "paying it forward."

Lonnie Sciambi’s Specialties:

I assist small business owners, helping them fix short-term problems, drive long-term solutions and achieve the success they seek.We learn through our experiences, successes and failures. I’ve had many successes, but my greatest learning has come from my mistakes and my failures. There is wisdom from these invaluable lessons that few can teach.

I also provide M&A advisory for entrepreneurial exits, helping small business owners not only maximize value but find the right partner.
