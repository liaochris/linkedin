Jonathan Sadowsky’s Summary

Vaca Creek is a personalized asset management firm focusing mainly on fixed income, specifically global credit and rates, for ultra high net worth individuals, family offices and small institutions.

A top down approach is driven by the client's optimized risk and return profiles. Portfolio structuring starts with high level sector allocation and ends with security selection and curve positioning to achieve alpha in different market environments. ETFs are used as complimentary and for risk adjusting (i.e. hedging) purposes, as well as to enable investments in non-core positions such as currencies and commodities.

A separate comingled fund using primarily ETFs is also available where more liquid and less passive management, yet similar positioning and risk management, is desired.

Vaca Creek provides frequent commentaries to our portfolio management and commentary distribution clients that provides detailed macro-economic analysis and portfolio positioning recommendations.

Jonathan Sadowsky, Chief Investment Officer, a securities industry professional with 18 years experience in asset management leverags a strong quantitative background in focusing on portfolio and risk management, modeling, and analyzing the intricate structure and risks of investments in his portfolios.

Prior, he spent six years as a fixed income hedge fund portfolio manager, researcher and trader at Barclays Global Investors (BGI, now Blackrock), specializing in the area of credit and rate products portfolio and risk management.

He was instrumental in developing investment strategies, trade ideas and portfolio structuring as well as overseeing asset valuation, with an emphasis on derivatives and the creation and maintenance of detailed risk profiles of the fund’s complex holdings.

Mr. Sadowsky graduated with a BS in Material Science and Engineering from Lehigh University in Bethlehem, PA., and studied for his MBA at the Stern School of Business, NYU.
