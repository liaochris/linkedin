Helen Pengelly's Summary

I am a qualified maths and EFL teacher but left full time teaching in 2005 to pursue my dream of running my own business. I was diagnosed with coeliac disease in 2003 and was disappointed with the limited range and quality of gluten free food that was in the supermarkets. I went in search of products from smaller producers that were only made from the finest ingredients and did not contain unnecessary additives.
In 2009 due to family circumstances I decided to limit the range of products that we sell to gluten free beer and focus on internet marketing. I closed my company and launched a new website providing information and support for people on a gluten free diet in 2010.
In 2011 I am writing my book and coaching programme to help newly diagnosed coeliacs adjust to the change in lifestyle.

Specialties

healthy eating, gluten free diets, alkalising, nutrition, mathematics for business, social media, internet marketing, blogging, nutrition, singing, music theory, spelling and grammar (this should be specialities surely!)
