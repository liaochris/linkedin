Adrian Maguire’s Summary

In 1985 founded a computer consultancy specialising in customisation of marketing databases and integrated financial packages. Projects for league football clubs, petrol forecourt retailers and the Manchester Business School, provided a wealth of hands-on business understanding and experience.

Began working with a technology focused PR company, Marketing Workshop soon after its foundation in 1985. My ability to understand and translate high technology language to suit the intended audience made a positive contribution to the business. This was formally recognised by the formation of a partnership in 1987 and a name change from Marketing Workshop to Ainsworth Maguire.

Currently interested in developing a purely online PR brand, CLICKintoPR.com.
