Jacqueline Sloboda’s Summary

"Results oriented individual with a disciplined, analytical and determined demeanor.She enjoys challenge, can handle pressure and hammer the deal home. Her industrious and assertive manner reflect her strong leadership skills and confidence. She has a track record of comfortably and effectively interfacing with multiple leadership levels.. Excellent interpersonal skills and the ability to be a team player enable her success in her endeavors"(--Employment Profile- Manpower Assessment)

Jacqueline Sloboda, Founder and CEO of Flex Hour Jobs is a 20 year veteran in the Sales and Marketing arena. Her experience in Brand Management inlcudes launching several licensed apparel divisions inlcuding those for Disney Stores,ReeBok, MLB and Guess. Her sales and marketing experience has led to her entrepreneurial successes in a number of internet companies, creating Private label programs for large national chain stores and catalogue companies.

Jacqueline graduated with a Degree in Business Administration in Marketing and Organizational Development. She has a Business Project Management Program Certificate from New York University and has completed New York University's Integrated Marketing and Communications Program. Jacqueline also attended UC Berkeley's e-Commerce Program as well as post-graduate studies for Master's in Education at Cal State Dominguez Hills, CA.

Jacqueline has served as an active Board Member for the national RESOLVE organization and has provided full and part time consulting to various start-up organizations.
