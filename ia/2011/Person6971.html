Carlien Helmink's Summary

As a youngster I thought my interest in fashion was based on the glamourous image of it. During my Communications masters I realised I have a true passion for fashion - far beyond its glamour. My education has raised global and social awareness which made me choose for more than 'just' clothing. There is much to change within the fashion industry and I want to contribute to these necessary changes.

marketing, pr, eco-sustainability, communications, negotiation, copy writer
