I am working as a Clinical Instructor for various social welfare programs run by Adarsh Rural Development Society. This society is a social welfare organization under Ministry of Health and Family welfare, Government of India and is also associated with National AIDS Control Organization. One of their programs is Targeted HIV/AIDS Intervention among high risk group.We ( our team) has also completed one study .The name of the study is Community Needs Assessment Survey. It is done by Adarsh Rural Development Society under the guidelines of National AIDS Control Organization to identify the migrants who are at high risk for HIV/AIDS and need targeted intervention .The assessment tools and questionnaire was provided to us by NACO. I was involved in planning, implementation and analysis of this study after the data collection. We did a survey on 30,000 migrants and we found 5000 migrants, who are at high risk for HIV/AIDS and need Targeted Intervention.
