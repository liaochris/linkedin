JD Siazon’s Summary

JD SIAZON is an artist nonpareil. He has done large-scale community murals in Manhattan and Brooklyn, NY working alongside all age groups from children to senior citizens. At Pratt Institute, Siazon was named a Collegiate All-American Scholar by The United States Achievement Academy for his outstanding academic success during the 2004-05 school year. Currently he is a scholarship graduate Fine Arts student at the prestigious Parsons The New School for Design in New York City.

JD Siazon’s Specialties:

Creating timeless art of impeccable quality in multifarious mediums and writing enjoyable text
