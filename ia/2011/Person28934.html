Résumé de Matt Harnden

I am in the business of creating new opportunities. I am a facilitator and operations specialist. I focus on being an incubator for new brand and product concepts in the wine & spirits industry. I utilize my knowledge of marketing, social media and promotions to turn these ideas into real products for retail and on-premise locations. Bringing the best ideas to life is my goal.

More often than not, the people with the best ideas are not the same as those with the resources and know-how to make them a reality. If you have an idea, please contact us and we can work together to make it a reality.
