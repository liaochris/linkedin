Benjamin Langley's Summary

While I am completely capable on my own, I really enjoy the team building experience. I enjoy supervising, and I also enjoy working for others. I really appreciate the opportunity to build connections and working relationships.

I understand the basics of programming, but my passion is networking. It is great to know that you can keep people and machines properly communicating with one another.
