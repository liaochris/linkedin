Anthony Hewson's Summary

I began copywriting years back as a sideline to my full-time work, and continued through my time as a corporate communications and PR specialist at Kier Group plc.

In May 2008 I resigned from my position at Kier in order to concentrate on growing my copywriting business, and the last year has been hugely rewarding.

Inevitably, with a background in writing for a top ten building contractor, I still carry out a fair amount of writing for various building trade companies, whether they be architects, contractors or property developers.

The diversity I sought when I began AH Copy has been realised though; I've written copy for airports, a helicopter charter company, a law firm, a nursery, life and business coaches, retailers, a dieting company, commercial print houses, an estate agency and numerous and disparate others.

Copywriting is just a matter of understanding your clients' businesses, how they see themselves and how they want to BE seen, and of course their target audience. The advantage of working across so many sectors, B2B and B2C, is that I've picked up knowledge in the course of research that I'd never have dreamed of accumulating otherwise.
