LYNNE OCONNOR’s Summary

Lynne O’Connor is Marketing and Communications Administrator for National Association of Teachers of Singing (NATS). She brings a unique balance of creative and technical expertise to advance the promotional efforts of NATS into new media.

Lynne began working in marketing and promotions at an early age by helping her mother with the family-owned dance studio, and has had her hands in new technology since the early 1970s when her step-father taught her how to solder solid state electronic circuitry. She studied Computer Science Engineering for two years at Arizona State University and graduated with a B.S. in Marketing in 1987. In her ensuing career, she applied her ability to communicate with people across all tiers of technical expertise to bridge the gap between software developers and end-users as a "cultural liaison" by writing computer software manuals and help systems, as well as providing support and training.

In the mid-1990s, Lynne found the internet to be the perfect medium in which to exercise her aptitude in technology and promotion, and incorporate her innate skills as an artist, by developing websites and internet marketing campaigns for her employers. During the evolution of the internet into the Web 2.0 era and social media, she continued to keep her finger on the pulse of new technology and its impact on contemporary culture. Lynne started her own internet marketing firm in 2008 and became a Certified Inbound Marketing Professional in 2009.

Now, with a portfolio that includes technical and creative communication, website development, print and digital graphic design, video and audio production, and social media marketing, Lynne taps into her passion for the performing arts to help NATS with its ongoing goals to effectively communicate with its constituents. In the coming year, she will be launching expanded efforts into social media, editing an extensive audio and video library for member access, and overseeing marketing of events and programs
