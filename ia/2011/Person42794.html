Terri L Maurer’s Summary

Following completion of a B.A. degree in Interior Design, extensive training was acquired in speaking and presentation skills, leadership, coaching and mentoring skills, strategic planning, environmental scanning and future scenario planning.

More tha three decades in the interior design arena coupled with as many years in leadership positions at local and national levels with The American Society of Interior Designers culminated in election as national President for 2001. This combination of skills positioned Maurer to work with the industry/supply side of the interior design profession, assisting suppliers in situational analysis and strategy development to capture interior design market segments.

Speaking and training seminars on business management, growth, marketing and generational diversity have been developed and presented across the US and in Canada at major industry trade shows and for related organizations. Included in ASID 'Distinguished Speaker Series' 2005--2008.

Maurer has written numerous articles for national trade publications, web sites and blogs as well as being interviewed and quoted in books and magazines on issues and topics related to the interior design profession. Author and publisher of 'Designing Strategies', a bi-monthly e-newsletter and 'Designing Strategies: The Blog." Co-author of 'Interior Design in Practice: Case Studies of Successful Business Models". Contributing author of business and marketing column for Sources+Design Magazine, 2010.

Terri L Maurer’s Specialties:

Strategic planning, situational analysis, trend analysis, environmental scanning, market identity and segmentation, target marketing, personal branding; leadership development and team building; coaching, training and business seminars, keynotes, writing for print and virtual publications; expertise in generational diversity.
