Mercedes Alfaro's Summary

Mercedes Alfaro is president and founder of First Impression Management, Inc. a nationally recognized company. Ms. Alfaro travels throughout the United States and works with Fortune 500 companies, government agencies and Universities. Her corporate background spans more than two decades of experience working for IBM (including management, project management, customer service and technical writing for research and development).

Clients include: Abbott Laboratories, Anheuser-Busch, AT&T, Center for Disease Control (CDC), Deloitte & Touche, Emory University, Fidelity Investments, Florida Legislature, Heery International, Hyatt Hotels, Johnson & Johnson, Lockheed Martin, Motorola, Solvay Pharmaceuticals, Social Security Office, UBS, UPS, US Attorney’s Office NYC, Washington State Governor's Office

Ms. Alfaro has published articles in numerous business publications and has appeared as an expert etiquette adviser on various television networks including: ABC, CBS, UPN and FOX stations. Ms. Alfaro has also been quoted as an expert in business etiquette in articles published by: The New York Times, the Chicago Tribune, the Los Angeles Daily News, the Atlanta Journal-Constitution, the Miami-Herald and USA Today.
