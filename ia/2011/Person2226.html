Amy Miranda's Summary

Amy is passionate about the digital space and whether it’s Technical, Creative or Conceptual she lives and breathes it. Having worked in all facets of the industry Amy offers a unique multidisciplinary perspective which is met with an equal degree of humor, creativity & commitment to results.
She began her career in the early days of CNN.com in Atlanta. Since then, Amy has produced, conceptualized & managed thousands of large-scale online campaigns and solutions ranging from frequency programs to eCommerce sites. Current & past clients include: MINI, Telus, AOL, Canadian Tire, Amp’d , Honda, Labatt, Nike, WestJet, Molson, MSN, Bell, Bombardier, Coca-Cola, and UPS. Amy has won multiple international awards for her work producing digital.
She now draws on over 12 years of Interactive and Operations experience delivering award-winning, digital marketing solutions. She’s worked as an Editor, Designer, Technology Manager, Technical Services Director, Project Manager & Interactive Consultant at companies including iXL, CNN, U8TV/Alliance Atlantis, Activate, Henderson Bas, Interactive Production Director at Strategy & Marketing's Agency of the Year, TAXI. While at TAXI Amy led the Interactive production department delivering internationally award-winning, results driven solutions throughout the TAXI network. Amy moved on to Grip Limited where she was the Director of Production & worked to streamline & restructure to achieve greater profitability & quality of production. Amy then moved forward in executing her vision for integrated production made easy. Lunch.
Lunch is a network of companies who create high quality digital work for clients, agencies, and production companies. Lunch assists in educating & providing insight into creating award winning productions -coupled with ROI. She is also the co-founder of think tank INTER-ACTION, with the goal of continuing to innovate & communicate in the field of digital media.
