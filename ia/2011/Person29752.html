Mere Noonan’s Summary

After graduating in 2004, Mere started working as a web developer for TheAcademy.com, an internet based training company providing custom learning solutions to some of the largest organization and associations in the United States. She was promoted rapidly and often, eventually making Senior Vice President of a 23 person company by January 2010. During her tenure, she oversaw both a system and product redesign, outstanding company growth and took on increasing leadership roles. Her commitment to her team, clients and professional partners was unmatched.

In August of 2010, she parted ways with TheAcademy.com to pursue other adventures including freelance consulting work, small business marketing solutions and entrepreneurial counseling. Her small but growing clientele eventually enlightened her to her true passion and goals: providing entrepreneurial guidance, simple but cost effective solutions for start up's and motivating people to get off the couch and get busy turning their business ideas into real businesses.

In December 2010 "The Mere Idea", a site dedicated to collecting interviews and advice from entrepreneurs to be shared with those individuals seeking inspiration and motivation was conceived. The site officially launches January 10, 2011 and has already collected a large number of interviews from some of today's up and coming business owners and entrepreneurs. If you would like to share your story and advice, please visit http://themereidea.com/?page_id=118 for our proposition and a listing of our standard interview questions.
