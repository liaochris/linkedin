Ninon de Vere De Rosa’s Summary

Ninon de Vere De Rosa is prominently known throughout the world and at present in the Los Angeles area as a spokesperson, producer, show host, journalist, investor and philanthropist in connection with her many charitable activities. She is founder and president of KidsTalk Productions, a full-service television production company for fourteen years, KidsTalk Web Site, promoting KidsTalk, by giving the younger generation a voice in society and our future leader of the world, founder and executive director of KidsTalk Foundation, a global philanthropic organization. Ninon is also the creator of NinonSpeaks, a national keynote speaker, motivational speaker and inspirational speaker service. Ninon has also been in real estate as an Investment and Development Company, NDD Properties, LLC.

Originating from England, Ninon was privately educated and raised in prestigious Devon England.
