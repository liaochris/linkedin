Karen van Minnen's Summary

Since 1994, I have worked as a designer in the non-profit sector. Including backdrop and costume design for theatres, various forms of graphic material for companies, institutions, theatres, festivals and private parties. I also create and build items with and without motion on order for a number of theatres, and have designed costumes for children.
I am developing a semi-commercial children's (play) clothing/items line for boys and girls aged 2 to 7 (working title: Hidden Solutions for Small Problems) and I am working on a new light form.
In 2008 I started working as a teacher (graphic design) at the HvA (HeS Duaal).

Specialties

exhibit design / graphic design / costume design / theatre design / education / semiotics
