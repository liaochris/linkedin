Rick McCurdy's Summary

Innovative senior executive and consultant (Kellogg MBA, Harvard Ph.D.) with 20+ years expertise growing businesses through successful development and commercialization of market leading products and services.

Domain expertise specializing in energy efficient product markets especially next generation glazing and fenestration markets.

Proven success delivering profitable and sustainable growth strategies for startups through $500MM manufacturing and service-focused organizations by establishing clear linkage between business model, market positioning, and P&L performance.

Execution driven through organizational alignment by inspiring commitment to the vision using a collaborative management style combined with clear goals and accountabilities.

Excel in building strong brands and utilizing market positioning and messaging to capture the value created from innovative products and services.

Specialties

Material Science and Thin Film Domain Expertise, Formulating & Implementing Growth Strategies, New Product Portfolio Development and Execution, Team Building & Coaching High Performance Teams, Business Development, Category, Channel and Product Line Expansions

Public Company; Building Materials industry

Executive Committee member. Led New Product Development, Engineering, and R&D (2004-2009), Marketing (2004-2007) and Fiberglass Composite Window and Door Manufacturing (2007-2009).

P&L responsibility for fiberglass composite window and door manufacturing, delivering company leading growth rates in both revenue and profitability.

Created and implemented a technology and new product development strategic roadmap to drive the long range strategic growth plan. Portfolio was balanced between strategic and tactical development activities and grew new product sales to 35% of gross sales while targeted to maintain a >25% rate long term.

Business Segment Leader, Specialty Glass Products

Pilkington

Privately Held; Glass, Ceramics & Concrete industry

1988 – 2004 (16 years)

Responsible for the global sales, marketing, and profitability of the Pilkington TEC™ electrically conductive and OptiTEC™ anti-reflective portfolios for use in photovoltaic, display, refrigeration, white goods and electrochromic markets. Directed Architectural Technical Services function responsible for product applications as well as industry and governmental activities (e.g. Energy Star, IGMA, AIA, AAMA).

Previous positions held were Manager Quality and Coating Operations, Ottawa Float Glass Manufacturing (1998 - 2000) and Senior Scientist/Manager Advance Coating Technology, Pilkington, Libbey-Owens-Ford (1988-1998).
