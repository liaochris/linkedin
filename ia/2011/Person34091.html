Paul Bierker’s Summary

Paul Bierker brings the best technology to designers and manufacturers of various luxury goods in the United States.

As an account focused executive designer for over 20 years his expertise in the area of CAD/CAM, product development, merchandising and communications assures his clients the best honest, reliable solutions.

Our software offerings are as follows-

TypeEdit

The artistic CAD/CAM software

TypeEdit is the leading artistic solution dedicated to mechanical machining for 2D, 2,5 and 3D cutting, engraving and artistic modeling.

Laser Type

The professional laser CAD/CAM software

LaserType offers all the necessary functions for laser marking, cutting and engraving applications.

3 Design CAD6

The unique 3D solution for jewelry Designers

The 3D CAD solution for jewelry & accessories creation. User-friendly, parametric with amazing dedicated functions and rendering module.

Type3 CAA V5

The engraving add-on for CATIA

Type3 CAA V5 Based Solutions were developed in close parternship with Dassault Systems; to allow the inclusion of texts, symbols and graphics earlier in the product life cycle.

Our product range will cover all artistic manufacturing markets worldwide.
