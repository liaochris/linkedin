Ann Acquaviva’s Summary

Over 15 years experience in Design, Organizing and Home Staging. Founded The Creative Organizer LLC in 2010 as it was my life's calling to help people get organized and offer compassion and sensitivity through the process. Utilized many organizing skills prior in the corporate sector working on time and paper management thus increasing productivity. We partner with individuals who may be sending children to college helping them organize dorm rooms, as well as preparing them for send-off, or downsizing elderly parents due to illness or other circumstances and aid them in the packing and purging process and help prepare the home for sale. We deal with special situations including relocation, death or divorce, which can be an overwhelming process, and aid in clean-out, organizing and home staging. We partner with Realtors and offer services to help secure listings to turn over properties quickly at the best possible prices. Our compassionate and professional service will help take the stress out of life thus creating peace and harmony.
