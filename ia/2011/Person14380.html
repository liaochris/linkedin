JJ Kennedy’s Summary

2010 Update: Starting September, I will be heading up the online business for Jackson Hewitt. EGI has been good to me over the last 10 years, but the challenge at JH just seemed too cool to pass up :)

Hi my name is JJ Kennedy and I live in Sarasota Florida. I hold an MBA from the University of Rhode Island with a specialization in Marketing and Information Systems.. I began my career as a webmaster and eventually moved up the ranks as an internet marketing manager, product manager, internet director and eventaully became the Vice President of Internet and eMarketing at Pitney Bowes.

During the last few years I have been splitting my time between working as Vice President of Marketing at Action Real Estate and running my own web and marketing firm called Evil Genius Interactive responsible for much of the marketing work that sold tens of millions in investment properities in Northern Flordia. Alas, they are probably all in foreclosure for pennies on the dollar..

These days, EGI is in full swing in Sarasota, FL. The bulk of our work consists of online marketing strategy, technical development, and SEO.

If you need help with any type of marketing (from strategy to specifics including websites, direct mail, to content creation) let me know. I would love to chat about it some time!
