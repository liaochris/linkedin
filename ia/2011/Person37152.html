Roanne Parker's Summary

I love technology, gadgets and people. I started working fulltime in digital media in 1999. For the last 7 years as a Director at Jericho I've been championing the email marketing channel.

How I can help you:

1. The best team, and the best platform, for your multi-channel marketing programs starts here at Jericho. We are 30 individuals from all over the world, based in a great office with a sunny deck, and BBQ, in Auckland. Our platform SmartMail Pro, is not just another email marketing program. It's a fully smart, social media empowered, automated, dynamic platform that will run your program, and make you money.

2. Advice on digital marketing. I am an expert at email marketing programs and have helped to design and build many that boast massive ROI, week in and week out. I know the laws, the approaches, the tone and the professional approach that will put you far ahead of your competitors.

3. My clients value that I love challenging, teaching, brainstorming, trouble shooting, devil's advocate-ing. We all learn together and because I'm lucky enough to be in the middle of a lot of smartness, I'm always learning. And sharing.

4. Introductions & networking. If you'd like to say hi, talk about the bad old days when we just made most of it up, or the future that's so bright it makes me squint, I'm all ears.


My industry involvement includes past Board Member of the Direct Marketing Association and Chair of the eMN Executive, founding contributor to the development of NZ's UCEM (Spam) Act and regular attendee of local and international digital marketing conferences each year.

Specialties

Digital marketing strategy, and tactical planning. I help the marketing teams in many of Australasia’s smartest private and public sector organisations to design profitable marketing programs using social media, connected web, and specialist email design and delivery management.
