Laura Gilmore's Summary

Laura is an experienced online editor/writer/content strategist with a broad range of experience in web publishing and print. She has ten plus years of demonstrated excellence in creating and editing web content for leading technology business publishers, primarily at UBM (techweb.com) from 1999-2005.

Her other experience in the digital sector includes projects at a leading ad agency (McCann/MRM Worldwide), a tech giant (IBM), a major US retailer (Barnes & Noble, Inc.), city government (The City of New York), and the NY Federal Reserve Bank. She has 5 + years of related B-to-B and B-to-C interactive project manager, as well as content management and social media experience.

Before she became "digitized," she worked extensively in the print and media world as a contributor to The Boston Globe Calendar and VH1 (music reviews), the Boston Phoenix (contributing writer, supplements project leader, copy editor, and proofreader). Her editing skills were used in projects for companies such as Agilent, Polaroid and State Street Global Advisors, Boston.

In the media sector, her background includes independent PR and press relations work in the entertainment field, including planning a 275-attendee joint conference for a US government agency (the CDC/Massachusetts Dept. of Health/Harvard School of Public Health). Laura has also lived the performer's life, working as a singer/songwriter with numerous Boston bands in the 1990s. She is a native Californian and lives in the New York City area.
