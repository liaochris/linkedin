Chandru Shankar's Summary

Chandru Shankar is the Dynamics Offerings and Methodology Director for Microsoft Services and the Solution Architect for Microsoft Dynamics Sure Step. He is responsible for driving solutions and offerings for the Microsoft Dynamics Program Office, while overseeing the functional design and content flow in the methodology. Chandru recently authored a book on delivering Microsoft Dynamics solutions, titled "Microsoft Dynamics Sure Step 2010: The Smart Guide to Successful Delivery of Microsoft Dynamics Business Solutions." The book is available at this link: https://www.packtpub.com/microsoft-dynamics-sure-step-2010/book

Chandru has consulted for companies in a variety of industries, including Aerospace, Automotive, CPG/Retail, High-Tech, Industrial and Manufacturing. He has undertaken multiple implementations of ERP & Supply Chain solutions, in numerous capacities including Program/Project Manager, Solutions Architect & Lead Consultant. His consulting skills are complemented by several years of Manufacturing Industry experience.

Chandru has also functioned as Adjunct Faculty for a major University, where he has covered topics including Supply Chain/e-Business Design, Strategy & Architecture for MBA students & Company Executives. He has spoken in multiple internal and external conferences over the years, as well as conducted training programs around the world.

Specialties

ERP/CRM, Methodologies & Processes, Advanced Planning & Scheduling, Order Management, Demand Management, Inventory Management, Supply Chain Management

Chandru Shankar's Publications

  • Microsoft Dynamics Sure Step 2010: The Smart Guide to Successful Delivery of Microsoft Dynamics Business Solutions

    • Packt Publishing
    • September 27, 2010
    Authors: Chandru Shankar

    Delivering business solutions is more than just product play. Software is often viewed as the enabler; however, the key to success is how the solution is implemented and how the implementations are managed. With this as the background, Microsoft has developed Dynamics Sure Step as the full lifecycle methodology for the Microsoft Dynamics business solutions portfolio. Dynamics Sure Step enables you to maximize your Microsoft Dynamics investments and increase your efficiency to select, sell, deliver, operate and support your business solution.
    This book details the use of the Dynamics Sure Step methodology to enable the full lifecycle of Microsoft Dynamics business solutions engagements. Guidance is provided on the due diligence process for the customer that overlaps the solution selling cycle for the service provider, through to the solution implementation phases, encompassing on-going maintenance and upgrades.
    This book provides detailed coverage of how to use Microsoft Dynamics Sure Step to successfully deliver Dynamics solutions for your business.
    The book begins by focusing on the aspects of due diligence and solution selling– whether you are the customer or the service provider, you can learn how you work together to select the right solution to meet the organization’s needs.In the ensuing chapters, you will learn how Dynamics Sure Step can help improve the overall quality of your Dynamics business solution, including coverage of proactive and post go-live optimization offerings and a thorough explanation of how to upgrade your Dynamics solutions to the latest release of the products.
    Finally, the book discusses the essence of Dynamics projects and how to successfully deliver these business solutions, including project and change management disciplines and processes.
