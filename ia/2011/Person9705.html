Chris Young's Experience

Social Media & Technology Manager, NW Division

First American Title

Public Company; FAF; Information Services industry

August 2007 – Present (3 years 8 months)

Although First American Title is one of the largest title insurance companies in the world, their jump into the social media arena has been something entirely new and exciting. I began at First American in a sales capacity on a local level, focusing on technology and social media which soon led to a promotion, Social Media and Technology Manager.

This position began with a clean slate, creating and implementing a social media strategy for First American’s NW Division, encompassing 15 states. This strategy identifies the company’s target audience and their needs, then engages with that audience and fulfills their needs through effective social media resources such as blogging, Facebook and Twitter.

Primarily, I work with internal managers and client-facing employees, showing them how to implement the social media strategy in their area while emphasizing the importance of using social media as a listening and monitoring tool. Additionally, I use blogging as an outreach tool for internal employees to spark conversations and opportunities with external clients.

Ongoing responsibilities include regular monitoring and evaluation of progress through analytical and metric tools, constant communication with managers and clients, networking with social media leaders across all industries, and keeping current on new innovations which can be recommended for future company use.
