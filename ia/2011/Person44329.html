Terique Greenfield's Summary

Terique Greenfield is a classically trained songwriter and producer. The multi-instrumentalist has had his music used for videos, artists, albums, commercials and more. He is represented by True Distinction Music, and individual publishing company with BMI and has a library of radio ready music that may be licensed to artists, films, and labels. His musical production reigns supreme when it comes to multi-genre music with compelling melodies and unparalleled structure. With a wide range of musical and technical resources, the ability to score to picture, and specific attention to musical detail, True Distinction Music is able to create a unique sound that sets them apart.

Specialties

Logic Pro 8, Reason 4, Garageband, Music Composition, Scoring to Picture, Multi-Track Mixing, Arranging, Sound Editing, Knowledge of Digital/Analog Audio Gear, Signal Flow, and Mastering
