Rashunda Tramble's Summary

Rashunda Tramble is a passionate technophile and an award-winning journalist. Currently, she is editor-in-chief of the corporate website of Swiss Re, the world's second largest reinsurance agency.

Formerly she was managing editor of ISN Security Watch (www.isn.ethz.ch), the news service of the International Relations and Security Network at the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology in Zurich (ETH Zurich).

She has won two awards from the North Carolina chapter of the Associated Press: Rookie of the Year and Best Feature Report.

She is a member of the the National Association of Black Journalists, the Online News Association, the Radio and Television News Directors Association and Mensa International.
