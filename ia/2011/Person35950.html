Summer of Startups

Professional Training & Coaching industry

July 2010 – August 2010 (2 months)

Summer of Startups is a program that helps students create a startup rather than take a summer job (similar to SSE Labs in Stanford). The goal is to enable students to work on their idea, get coaching, and meet inspiring speakers.

It is a collaboration between student-run associations from the top 3 schools in Helsinki (Aaltoes, Hankenes and Hues) and funded by TEKES.

The program features:
- 10 selected startups (10 nationalities, 30 people) from 60 applications
- 20+ guest speakers
- pitching competition in Estonia, demo day in Finland

Summer of Startups has been noted to deliver very high quality pitches and is considered to be one of the most effective entrepreneurship programs to date in the country. It has received acknowledgment from various ministries and state organizations.
