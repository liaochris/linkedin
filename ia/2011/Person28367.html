Mark Roush’s Summary

Personal Profile:

Director and editor by day, writer by night - Mark Roush crosses into unparalleled worlds in both occupations. Residing in the Shanghai city of Portland, Oregon - he is the owner of New Adjustment Productions, a commercial and motion picture development company that produces, options and develops commercials, feature & short films and webisodic television programs.

Commercially, Mark has worked on a wide range of projects for companies such as Laika, Autodesk, Hewlett Packard, Intel, Nike, NET10, Regence, Black & Decker, Sierra Club, Char-Broil, VH1, Disney and is currently writing and directing for MDiTV.

Mark Roush and Wasteland Films LLC is also in pre-production for the relaunch of their web series, "Lady Wasteland" - a post-apocalyptic story which has amassed over a million views since its launch in Dec, 2007.

Mark is an award winning freelance commercial director and editor. He can be contacted at film@newadjustment.com.

If you'd like to view his director's and editor's reel - please visit www.newadjustment.com

Mark Roush’s Specialties:

Product placement contracts for independent films through company marketing executives. Producing and managing budgets for commercial and independent productions. The optioning of screenplays to independent development companies. Script layout / breakdown into production for day to day scheduling for feature film and commercial projects. Commercial producing and directing from concept through development into post production.
