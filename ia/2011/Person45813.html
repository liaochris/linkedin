Wil Armstrong's Summary

Wil Armstrong is an entrepreneur and small businessman. His business background includes technology, mortgage banking, and commercial banking.

As an active community member, Wil has served on many community and non-profit boards and has volunteered in numerous political campaigns.

In 2008, Wil acted on his core belief that our country needs more citizen legislators and set aside his own business interests to run in the Republican primary for Congress. His second place finish in a four-way contest was marked by his ability to inspire grassroots activists and to raise money in the political arena.

Specialties

Wil has been called a Happy Warrior and an Incurable Entrepreneur. He loves business - starting them, building things, and developing a team.
