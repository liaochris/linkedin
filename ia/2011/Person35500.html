Peter Stone, MA,CPT,MLADC,CAS’s Summary

For the past 20 years I have studied and researched the acquisition, actuation, and maintenance to cycles of anger-based hostility, entitlement-based domestic control, other self-abusive behaviors and addictions. As a result of my research, I pioneered Select Emotional Alternatives Cognitive Application Process (SEACAP), a cognative-social application which offers an efficient approach to providing pre & post assessment on the cyclical nature to relational abusive behaviors. As a cognative-social systems approach, SEACAPoffers a psychoeducational approach to re-framing (e.g., Application-Processing) lost power of control as social interactionists. Taken together, SEACAP has been field tested and exceeds the standards set for Best Practiced Evidence Based programming.

Peter Stone, MA,CPT,MLADC,CAS’s Specialties:

The uniqueness of my research into addiction and other abusive behaviors is the twelve men I learned from. These men shared their criminal addictive behavioral life-spans with me. Our goal was to discover how they lost behavioral control which resulted in their killing their partners while under the influence of substances. To further study and correlate my findings, the defining factors of influence for these twelve men were compared to an additional 40 case studies of violent offenders.
