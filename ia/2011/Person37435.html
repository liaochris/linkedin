Robin McCasland’s Summary

Creative, senior-level communication professional with 25 years of experience in strategic communication planning, employee engagement, employer branding, talent management/recruiting communication and more. Skilled in creating effective presentations, facilitating planning sessions, and in planning and directing attention-getting corporate events.

Offers strategic, creative communication solutions with the same experience as large consulting firms -- but with much more reasonable fees.
