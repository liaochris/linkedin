Patty Reagin’s Summary

I help marketers in the animal health, veterinary device and diagnostics, biomedical and medical device/software industries increase ROI, shorten sales cycles and retain more quality leads with valuable and highly-sought out marketing content.

I am an expert B2B marketer with extensive experience both in the corporate world and as a freelance copywriter. I understand intimately the stresses and strains of being a B2B marketer and I know what it takes to bring in ROI and write compelling demand-generation copy. I also understand the psychology of buyers and how to write persuasive copy without turning them off. I've worked for 12 years in various iterations of the health industry including alternative and complimentary medicine, health foods, supplements and nutraceuticals and pharmaceuticals. I also worked in a veterinary hospital for 6 years.

My experience and knowledge of how all facets of B2B marketing work, my understanding of the corporate ethos and my strong skills as a custom content copywriter make me singularly equipped to increase ROI and generate highly qualified prospects.


For more information and a copy of my FREE white paper: The Medical Industry Online: How Websites Fail the ROI Test Plus 8 Mission Critical Elements for Successful Demand Generation, visit www.medicalindustrycopywriter.com

Follow me on Twitter: http://www.twitter.com/pattyreagin

Patty Reagin’s Specialties:

white papers, case studies, sales letters, website copy, content marketing, E-books, newsletters, blogging, social media and other marketing content for the animal health, biomedical and medical device/software industries.
