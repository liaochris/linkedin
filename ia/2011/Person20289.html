Jennifer Lim's Summary

I am a New York City based photographer, photo editor, and information specialist. Having finished my MLS (Master of Library and Information Science, Archives stream), I am looking to put my many talents and skills towards helping public and private institutions digitize (and monetize) their photographic archives for the purposes of education and greater access.

Specialties

staff management, budget administration, project oversight, database and content management systems (CMS) design, knowledge organization, image licensing and copyright, photo research, photo editing, photo shoot production, film photography, web design and usability, writer, editor, presenter
