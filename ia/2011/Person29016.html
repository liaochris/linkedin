Matthew Miller's Summary

Matthew L. Miller, Esq. is currently the Business Development Manager for the Information Management & eDiscovery Practice at Forsythe Technology, an information technology infrastructure integrator. Forsythe helps F1000 complex organizations across all industries, optimize the cost and manage the risk of their IT, while improving performance.

Matthew focuses on growing margin for Forsythe and product sales for technology partners.

Previously, Matthew worked at Guidance Software, as the Product Marketing Manager for EnCase eDiscovery, the company's main product line. Prior to this role, Matthew was an Account Executive at Guidance Software, made President's Club, sold into the F100. As an attorney, Matthew worked at Peckar & Abramson, the number 1 construction litigation firm in the US, and as a consultant to the AmLaw 200 law firms.

He has written whitepapers and is a frequent speaker at industry conferences. Matthew received his BS from Syracuse University Whitman School of Management and his JD from Hofstra University School of Law.
