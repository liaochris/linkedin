Paul Templeton's Summary

I like to see myself now as a social entrepreneur in the field of education. I have had over 30 years experience in starting and turning around schools, colleges and universities I have always wanted to make education a force for good in the world.

We now have set ourselves a real challenge!!

We wish to set up an international network of global village schools which: extends the learning beyond the local school to a global community; brings the advantaged and disadvantaged together in equal partnership; helps grow the next-generation of global citizens and social entrepreneurs.

Specialties

Social Entrepreneurship, Educational leadership, mentoring and coaching, strategic planning, R&D into effective ways of teaching and learning
