Why Modded, and why now? Modded is a Social & Digital Media Managed Services Company. Modded LLC was essentially born out of necessity. Modded quickly realized that small, medium and enterprise organizations like Media, Advertising, Gaming, and Entertainment companies were paying a variety of 3rd party vendors to accomplish a single goal and that is brand awareness and recognition. Unfortunately when you have so many vendors touching your brand, you lose the opportunity to focus your brand on what you need it to do the most, and that's to drive revenue and client/customer acquisition.

Modded is designed to be the single voice, and overall technology brand enablement advisor. Modded offers a wide range of programs that consist of but not limited to viral marketing, mobile enablement, web presence, social and digital media guidance/enablement, social governance and most importantly streamlined technology service provider/custom developer. Modded’s goal is to evaluate the our partner clients current environment, social methodologies and viral market programs and help tailor them into tightly executable social and digital strategy that is cost effective for their organization.

The strength of ModdedLLC.com, rests with expert in-house talent and our global partner eco-system. We built it right, and from the ground up with the best and brightest, best of breed consultants, partners, and technology developers from around the globe to provide best in class service.
