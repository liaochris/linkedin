Massimiliano Maria Longo’s Summary

35 years old, Massimiliano have been working in the communication market since 1994. At 13 he develops his first videogame. After his studies in architecture and different experiences in communication and design, he gets a Master in New Media Communication & Strategy. Between 2001 and 2004 Massimiliano is Digital Marketing Strategist in digital@jwt. In 2008 he becomes Associate Creative Director Interactive in RMG Connect and is now working as Head of Experience Department in JWT/RMG Connect Italy. He has created and developed integrated communication plans, digital brand identity, brand experience, social marketing, viral communication, advergames and cross-media experiences for Vodafone, Enel, illy, Alfa Romeo, Wilkinson Sword, Kimberly-Clark, Johnson & Johnson, Heineken, Kraft, Nestlé, Nokia, Canon, RCS MediaGroup, DTC, Coca Cola, Trentino, Sony Ericsson, Nintendo, MySpace. He is one of the most awarded italian digital creatives. He’s member and juror at the Italian Art Directors Club.
