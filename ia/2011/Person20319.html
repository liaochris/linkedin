Jenny May's Summary

Professional singer/songwriter and musician who has performed with bands in the US and the UK in the studio, at festivals, clubs, pubs and parties with music appearing in the movie Philadelphia. Currently using Apple’s GarageBand software to record music and podcasts and has demoed GarageBand for the Oxford Mac Group at Oxford University.

Jenny May's podcast Band Weblogs Podcast Show aired on CBS Radio's KYOURadio.com and 1550 AM in San Francisco, California.

Created BandWeblogs.com in January 2005 which was the first website directory dedicated to promoting band and music blogs.

Band Weblogs has since grown and now features music news, online press distribution, videos, exclusive interviews and more.

Articles on Band Weblogs have appeared on websites such as Reuters, Austin American Statesman, Virgin Radio and NBC11.com. Bands, musicians, publicists, management and record companies promote their bands and music for free.

Specialties

Singer with live bands, studio singer, songwriter, musician, music engineer, music producer, produced own EP, recording using Apple's GarageBand software, demoed GarageBand software for Apple Mac Group at Oxford University, music publicity, online press distribution, music reviews, interviews, creating innovative music websites, experience with HTML, CSS, MYSQL, PHPNUKE, Wordpress, Drupal.
