Nick Lavezzo’s Summary

I am one of the co-founders of FoundationDB, where I manage all non development related aspects of the business.

Previously, I served in a variety of financial and operational executive roles in rapidly growing technology companies, as well as practicing as a CPA with Ernst & Young, LLP. Some highlights of my professional career include serving as one of three team members at Visual Sciences who negotiated and closed the sale of the company for $60MM to a public company in 2006, working closely with the CEO of the acquiring public company (who was actually the CEO of the Visual Sciences business unit prior to his appointment) in a successful restructuring and re-branding of acquiring company (WebSideStory, Inc) to Visual Sciences, Inc. (Nasdaq: VSCN), and serving as a key member of the team that closed the sale of Visual Sciences to Omniture in 2008 for approximately $400MM.

I continued to work with the CEO on a variety of special projects touching most areas of the business. In late 2007, i served as a key member of the team that negotiated and closed the sale of Visual Sciences, Inc. to Omniture, Inc. (OMTR) for approximately $400MM in January 2008. Omniture has subsequently been acquired by Adobe, Inc.
