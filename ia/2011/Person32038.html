Natalia de Windt’s Summary

Hi, I am a freelance digital designer and animator with over 8 years commercial experience. Throughout this time I have developed an extensive knowledge in a number of creative disciplines including Graphic Design, TV adverts & screens animation, web banner animations, video editing motion graphics, and multimedia.

Having previously study, lived and worked in Spain has allow me to develop an understanding of different ways of designing and creating for a diverse range of clients and business types.

I am passionate about creating digital work. With experience working within advertising agencies, design companies, and as a freelancer, I have a solid understanding of what is needed to produce exciting and engaging projects from conception to completion. Over the years I have produced work for various industries and business types in Aruba including fast food, telecommunications, trading companies and retail brands, ranging from small businesses to large ones.
Alongside producing commercial work I am frequently involved in creative projects including magazine designs, and photogrpahy.
