Nonprofit Organization Management industry

June 2009 – Present (1 year 10 months)

When the Rubber Meets the Road is a grassroots movement of people devoted to loving others who are dealing with a critical situation. It's a movement that believes tough times are an opportunity to come together as a community, step forward, take action and love others in a life-changing way.

Two Cincinnati families dealing with cancer inspired the group to act and it launched two inaugural events in October 2009. The When the Rubber Meets the Road Road Rally and The Burn Rubber Bash brought together over 750 people from across the Greater Cincinnati area to help raise over $30,000 to give the families freedom from the some of the worries that come with cancer.

But When the Rubber Meets the Road is about much more than raising money. It's about connecting and sharing . . . and believing that every contribution, no matter how large or small, really does make a difference in the lives of others and in this wonderful world we share.
