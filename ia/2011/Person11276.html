Debbie Miller's Summary

Debbie is the Search & Social Marketing Coordinator for HyperDisk Marketing, an emarketing firm in Orange County, CA. She’s proficient in social media, content development and strategic communication. Enhanced by her degree in English, she has strong analytical-thinking skills along with a knack for copywriting and editing.

Her current responsibilities include executing SEO strategies, optimizing content for user experience and SEO, managing social media channels for various clients, strategizing and implementing social media marketing tactics and analyzing social media platforms and their best practices.

In her previous role as Website, Social Media & Marketing Manager for the Costa Mesa Conference & Visitor Bureau (CMCVB), she maintained the CMCVB’s website and leveraged their social media presence. For Twitter and Facebook, she gathered all the content, scheduled and organized information for distribution on an ongoing basis. This resulted in nearly 50,000 brand impressions per week. The organization’s social media pages pages attracted over 3,000 followers and 1,000 fans, respectively, in 18 months, and both entities are a top-50 referral source to the website, producing substantial outreach and brand engagement.

In January 2011, she launched her own blog, Social Hospitality, which outlines the ways businesses in the hospitality realm can utilize social media for customer service, marketing, branding and more. Her goal is to educate those who may be new or skeptical about the benefits of using social media and emphasize all that can be accomplished by doing so.

She is a co-founder of ConnectOC, a group whose mission is to bring locals together through networking events and social media. The group’s two events have each drawn over 400 attendees. She is regularly active in numerous networking groups and is on the Board of Social Media Club, Orange County as well as Girls in Tech, Los Angeles.
