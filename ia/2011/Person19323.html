Jason Ring’s Summary

I build, differentiate and transform brands for the digital age.

My holistic perspective is informed by 15+ years of creative experience working across multiple touch points including print communication, online advertising, packaging, point of sale, web sites, social media, mobile and digital products and services.

My formal training in strategic design planning and innovation methods helps me bridge the divide between business strategy and customer experience to create new value. It also allows me to harness disruptive trends and technologies for clients.

I’ve worked with iconic brands like Brooklyn Academy of Music, McDonald’s, Motorola, New Museum, Sprite, Target, Visa and Yale. I’m currently a Creative Director at AKQA, one of the world’s most awarded interactive agencies.

My heroes range from Michael Porter, Al Ries and C.K. Prahalad to Tibor Kalman, Rei Kawakubo and Charles and Ray Eames. When I'm not doing something strategic or creative (or both) I enjoy martial arts training. For several years, I've studied under 34th generation Shaolin fighting monk Shi Yan Ming and I feel this experience has heavily influenced my approach to life and work.
