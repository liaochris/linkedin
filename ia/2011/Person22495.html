Josh Sears’s Summary

I have professional, proven experience in nearly every facet of the design profession. From print design, promotional materials, trade booth design, posters, postcards, business cards, stationery, branding material and of course, website design and development.

Currently I co-own a web development company with a focus on web 2.0 standards. I work alongside clients with full knowledge of their needs to deliver sites with a focus on User Experience, and a highly polished interface. Check out my portfolio here:

http://www.littlelines.com/work

I am also an accomplished Illustrator, working with clients like Perrier Water, Wolfgang Puck, Kellogg's, Random House Books, Relevant Books, and more. Along the line I have gained recognition from publications such as 3X3 magazine, HOW Magazine, and Print Regional—as well as being a winner of the American Illustrators competition in both 2005 and 2010.

I continue to develop my illustration and blog on http://www.PaperTelevision.com.

Josh Sears’s Specialties:

Over a decade of professional expertise in Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator. Focus in Web 2.0 standards in design, SEO, CSS / XHTML.

Also: Copywriting. Grass Roots and Guerilla Marketing expertise (having spent 5 years as a Marketing Manager for an Illustration Agency). Podcast production. Video editting. Flash. Coda. InDesign. Quark Xpress.
