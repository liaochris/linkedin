James Freudigmann's Summary

James Freudigmann brings over 10 years experience in residential property starting as a valuers assistant in 1999 and becoming Queenslands youngest registered valuer in 2002. After 6 years of valuation experience James worked as a buyers advocate opportunistically acquiring over twenty five million dollars of property in the Brisbane area in a period of 18 months in the low part of the cycle. He has also worked as a project manager and has headed up negotiations of over $1.2 billion of residential property throughout Queensland. James started his own personal portfolio in 2005 and has continued to build on that through the strong & not so strong times in the Brisbane market. James’ extensive knowledge of development feasibility, sourcing and acquiring high performing properties, coupled with his due diligence expertise and negotiations background, drives him to exceed his clients expectations.
