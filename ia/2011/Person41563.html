Stephen Klabnik’s Summary

I call myself a 'software craftsman' because I am devoted to producing software that is both beautiful and of a high quality.

These days, Ruby is my language of choice, though I'm comfortable in many others, having been a developer for most of my life.

I specialize in web technologies, but I also maintain a GUI toolkit for desktop applications as well. The distinction between the web and the desktop is becoming more and more meaningless with each passing day.

I'm incredibly interested in software education. My main Open Source contribution is Hackety Hack, a desktop/web application combo that teaches introductory programming.

Lately, I've been focusing on blending my interests in writing with my software and educational goals.
