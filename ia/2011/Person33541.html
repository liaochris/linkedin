Orlando Tonholi's Summary

Orlando has started his career on the communication industry in Brazil as Marketing Officer / Design Graphic during three years.
As result of his involvement with the non-profit sector, Orlando has also developed and coordinated social non-profit projects dealing with social non-profit organisations and local government, being responsible to plan, implement projects as well as improve the sponsors profile.
In 2006 redirected his career on the Mining industry as Document Control Group Leader in a large EPCM – $ 2.5 billion Hatch and Camargo Correa enterprise.
At CH2MHill from 2007 to 2008, worked as Project Management support for Infrastructure and Oil/Gas projects.
April 2008, after moving to Perth-Australia, Orlando joined Hatch as Project Scheduler on BHP Billiton Nickel enterprises Western Australia.
January 2009 moved over to SKM as Document Management Specialist on a $33 million mining conceptual studies. Relocated to Brisbane-Australia in order to move forward with the project team to run the current project phase as a large EPCM enterprise.
