Teri Pacitto’s Summary

“It’s all about service and putting the clients first.”

Setting industry standards since1988 Teri Pacitto consistently provides extraordinary customer service unparalleled in today’s industry. Teri approaches her real estate business and every transaction with integrity, honesty and a commitment to the end result… a truly exceptional experience in the representation of buyers and sellers of fine homes and estate properties. Each and every customer receives the benefit of her innovative, individualized, creative marketing skill, contract expertise and ability to negotiate.
Attention to detail and exemplary customer service is how Teri has maintained customers for life and the satisfaction of constant referrals from her previous buyers and sellers. Her business approach is to truly listen to her clients, take each day one hour at a time, do a fantastic job in every aspect of your life and business, and remember …to be successful pay attention to the way you treat everyone around you.
Teri Pacitto represents some of the area’s finest properties that are currently available and represents buyers who are looking for that perfect home. She is a preferred agent representative and works with some of the area’s major corporations relocating employees nationally and internationally.
Teri is consistently in the top 1% of Realtors nationally and internationally and has made her mark in the industry in achieving awards annually. In real estate affiliations throughout the years she has earned top industry awards; Hall of Fame, International Presidents Elite, Chairman’s Award, and continues to maintain status as one of the company’s top agents. Market conditions change, but consistency in acheiving not only her goals, but those of her client’s keep her focused and on track.

Please contact me anytime with questions, inquiries, updates on market statistics and current home value requests.

Referrals are always welcome.
