Troy Jensen’s Summary

Troy serves as Vice President of Finance and Business Development for Solar Solutions and Distribution, with offices in Denver, CO and Philadelphia, PA. Solar Solutions and Distribution is rapidly-growing full-service Clean Energy product distribution and service organization, and since 2009 has evolved quickly into one of the industry-leading Clean Energy providers in North America. Solar Solutions and Distribution has vaulted into an industry-leader by adhering to one simple but powerful time-honored business premise - unrelenting dedication to providing our clients the services and products faster than any other company in our competitive set. From training, engineering and feasibility studies, to project-level financing and industry-best Clean Energy pricing and the fastest delivery amongst our competitors, Solar Solutions and Distribution is dedicated to their client's success first and foremost.

Troy has expertise from the ground up, working onsite at at small residential solar installs, to helping broker large commercial and residential Power Purchasing Agreements (PPAs). He has spent his career in marketing, sales, finance, technology startup fundraising and monetization modeling throughout the GreenTech, Digital, Mobile and Technology sectors. His strong background and focus on high-margin gross revenue acceleration has led to the generation of over $600 million in revenue over a 13-year career. Troy has led hundreds of sophisticated digital sales and marketing campaigns and technology offerings for many Fortune 500 clients. He has also founded, co-founded, consulted and led funding efforts for several successful technology startups.

When not consumed by all things CleanTech, Troy's passion is in the air - he has his private pilots license and loves to find any excuse to get "wheels-up." Other interests include traveling, reading, golf, gadgets and emerging technology, and is a avid Dolphins, Yankees and Lakers fan.
