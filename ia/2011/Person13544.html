Eric Martin’s Summary

I am an Executive Search Consultant with Specialized Recruiting Group, a division of Express (AKA Express Employment Professionals - Professional Staffing Division, RWJ), a professional search firm specializing in mid to senior level national direct hire and contract recruiting. My niche expertise is functional vs. limited to one specific industry. It’s my belief this provides me access to a candidate pool often overlooked by those with a narrower field of view. I specialize in the areas of sales, marketing, and executive leadership across a wide variety of industries.

I have experience recruiting positions ranging from CXX to strategic sales/marketing type roles. If you work for a company in need of great people I'm the guy who can make an impactful introduction. I have networked with the best of the best sales, marketing and executive talent in the US. My job is not to find a needle in a haystack; rather my job is to locate the single sharpest, best needle in a haystack size stack of needles. I am a true headhunter.

Over the past four plus years I have been fortunate enough to help over 90 good people find better jobs with great companies. If you're the best person you know at what you do and are interested in staying abreast of a potentially stronger situation or simply just looking to network please introduce yourself. Let's have a short confidential conversation about your future.

If we are not already connected on LinkedIn, please invite me to join your network.
