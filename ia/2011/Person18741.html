James Caspell's Summary

Public sector professional with extensive experience in strategy, management and performance environments.

In the fields of housing and education I have delivered improvement across a range of services, starting from various baseline positions at local and regional levels. I have supported two ALMOs through Audit Commission inspections which have both resulted in "two stars and promising" outcomes.
