Michael Pupil's Summary

My move to Connex International was easy, as Vice President of Business Development I head the production of our Sector Meetings business model dedicated to providing our clients with enhanced networking between senior level executives in various industries. Contrary to traditional “conferences” we deliver a highly tailored and customized venue where those executive who are accountable for the strategy of their organization can meet.

I have had the pleasure and privilege of interviewing Dr. Alan Greenspan former Chairman of the Federal Reserve. I have also had the honor of working with leading executives from the PBGC including former Executive Directors Bradley Belt and Charles Millard,Professor of Finance at the Wharton School, Jeremy Siegel as well as Zvi Bodie Professor of Economics at Boston University to name a few.

In recent years I have built relationships with organizations and coalitions like the MidWest Business Group on Health, considered one of the most elite employer led coalitions on health and welfare in the industry.

In the years to come I am committed in producing not just events but help build a company that will set a new bar in the industry, one that is respected and sought after by thought leaders. It is my personal belief that once upon a time trade shows were the elite gathering of executives… followed by the typical conference model… today I believe we need more, we need to provide senior level management with a customizable itinerary that is specific to the needs of an individual while providing a more intimate setting thereby allowing those who work with us to achieve a solution(s) to most mission critical issues.

This is my commitment.

I hope to see you there.
