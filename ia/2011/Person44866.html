Nancy Ziegler’s Summary

Nancy Ziegler is the founder of Verdura Consulting, a culinary tourism consultancy in New York City that combines her fifteen years' experience in two fascinating fields. A graduate of Amherst College and the French Culinary Institute, she holds master's degrees from New York University in Journalism and in Food Studies. Prior to returning to graduate school, she was an associate producer of travel programs for public television and a culinary book editor. Nancy has snacked, sipped and supped in more than 45 countries and six continents, boosted by journeys abroad as both an experienced tour leader and passionate backpacker. She lives and cooks in Harlem.

Nancy Ziegler’s Specialties:

Culinary tourism, sustainable agriculture and tourism, tour operation, culinary education.
