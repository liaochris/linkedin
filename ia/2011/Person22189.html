Jon Kenton’s Summary

Jon Kenton is “The Strategist” and CEO of TechMarketeers, a highly experienced marketing company with a 100% focus on the Hi-Tech Industry. Jon has extensive experience, built over 25+ years working in the computing and communications industries.

Why TechMarketeers?

“While working with many of the top global technology companies on numerous marketing strategies and projects, one pattern became evident. No matter how great the marketing team, there always seemed to be more to accomplish than resources available. Bringing on external marketing resources was the logical solution but it was hard (if not impossible) to find a marketing company that also fully understood the level of technical detail required. Hence TechMarketeers.”

Why “The Strategist?”

“Each of the key principals at TechMarketeers has a special focus (Strategy, Marketing, Creative, Social Media, etc.). While I am passionate about all aspects of marketing, everything starts with a direction and strategy. One of my favorite sayings is “if you don’t know where you are going, don’t be surprised where you end up!” With a strategy and a plan you know exactly where you’re going and how to get there. I think that says it all.”
