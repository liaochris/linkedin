John Liebhardt's Summary

I have a strong background in reference, bibliographic instruction and research of databases in a variety of settings.

A recent MLIS graduate with experience at academic libraries and testing relational databases in the technology sector, I have a strong understanding of database concepts and architecture to solve information needs. My teaching experience provides solid background for reference problems, instructional sessions, database tutorials and web tutorials.

Prior to entering information science, I spent seven years as a foreign-based reporter, giving me great attention to accuracy of information provided with detailed fact checking and sourcing. I have the ability to interview people at a journalistic level to understand information needs. I have experience searching information from a variety of international sites: foreign governments, international organizations and multilingual databases. I am also versed with citizen media, digital technologies, information and communication technologies (ICTs) and social media platforms.

Specialties

• Information Science: reference services, digital content management, information architecture, metadata creation, cataloguing, collection development, information needs analysis, digital library creation
• Journalism: Reporting, writing, audio, editing
• Technology Sector: Testing web and computer-based applications, working with relational databases, writing training manuals, creating training courses
• Teaching: Information literacy, course creation, information gathering, source analysis
