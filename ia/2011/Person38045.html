Rudy De Waele’s Summary

In the last 10 years, I have been mostly active in the development of the 'open' mobile ecosystem as we know it today. Capturing mobile trends on my blog mTrends [http://m-trends.org]. Recently I co-founded dotopen.com [http://dotopen.com], a social platform for decision makers to connect & collaborate.

Often invited as a speaker and contributing writer to a number of professional publications, my thoughts and ideas on Mobile 2.0 have gone around, you can view my last presentation on the subject on Slideshare - [http://slideshare.net/rudydw].

I am the founder of MobileMonday Barcelona - [http://mobilemondaybarcelona.com] and MobileMonday Madrid - [http://mobilemondaymadrid.com], and organize the Mobile Premier Awards - [http://mobilepremierawards.com], the largest open and global startup competition in the mobile industry and the point of reference in startup premiers during the Mobile World Congress in Barcelona.

I am part of the Mobile 2.0 Organizing Committee - [http://mobile2event.com/about] and organize Mobile 2.0 Europe - [http://mobile20.eu].

You can follow my activities on Twitter here - [http://twitter.com/mtrends].

Recently I curated Mobile Trends 2020 - [http://slideshare.net/rudydw/mobile-trends-2020], a collaborative outlook including some of the key industry experts & visionaries on the mobile trends for the next 10 years.

With dotopen, I initiated AppCircus - [http://appcirc.us], a unique global traveling showcase of the most creative and innovative apps presented by their creators during some of the most influential international events in mobile/web. AppCircus is open to developers, startups and any other organization and offers each winner a nomination to the Mobile Premier Awards.

Well-connected among key players in the digital media distribution and global mobile value chain players, I’m passionate for the new and the innovative and not afraid of mixing the unmixable.

Nirvanet wass created and maintained by an international team of webdesigners based in Brussels, Belgium.
