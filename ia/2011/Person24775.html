Founder, Owner & Spokesperson

Rhabdomyosarcoma Info

Health, Wellness and Fitness industry

August 2006 – Present (4 years 8 months)

Rhabdomyosarcoma is a rare cancer that little children get in their extremities.

More rarely, as in the case of my father, adults can present with this type of cancer. My father was 1 of 6 people in North America who had this cancer as an adult.

Therefore, no research is done on this type of adult onset cancer, and people in this situation are thrown into the gauntlet as doctors try anything they can think of at them. There is no specific treatment regimen, and most doctors are at a loss.

Help us inform people about this type of cancer, and the fact that no research is done, and give these patients hope!

This organization was created in Loving Memory of my father, Gerald M. Turner, the greatest man and father I have ever known.
