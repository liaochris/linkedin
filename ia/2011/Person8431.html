Christine Lyes's Summary

PH Digital is an independent communications agency that specialises in digital design and internet based solutions.

We work in the Pharmaceutical, Health and Scientific sectors providing materials and tools to support educational and brand communications.

Owner Director at PH Digital:
I run the finance, PR and marketing, also the scheduling and resource management at PH Digital.


Additional Profile:
Freelance Finance Executive and Production Accountant.

QBE Accountant with 20 years experience. I have business experience across several industry sectors including; Digital Design, Television, Animation, Music Programmes, Drama, Documentaries, Formula 1, Sports Events, Marketing and Advertising, Exhibitions and Conferences.

I can provide ad-hoc accounting services for Individual Events and TV Productions or Company Accounts. Accounts up to Trial Balance, Management Accounts, VAT Returns, Payroll.

Accounts Software experience - Sage line 50, Sage Payroll, TAS, TAS Payroll, Quickbooks, Access, Axium Vismax Production Accounts, Pegasus, Fact 2000, Excel.

I have implemented new accounts systems and software for companies and used a range of bespoke systems for accounts and other non accounts software.

Services on a daily rate, negotiable and from your premises if required.
