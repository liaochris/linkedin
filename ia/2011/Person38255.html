Ryan Hanau's Summary

I bring over 15 years of experience to my profession and current role as the VP/Director of User Experience at Saatchi & Saatchi. My passion is creating transformational user experiences that leverage customer insights.

Over the years I have received multiple patents and numerous awards for my design thinking and execution. I have served as an adjunct professor and have lead usability research and design for a Fortune 100, Motorola, where I oversaw a multitude of large-scale international research and design efforts.

My previous experiences also include leading a private consulting practice here in NYC and founding and managing Chroma Design, a User Experience design studio in San Francisco.

Specialties

Functional Specification, Heuristic/Expert Review, Human Factors, Information Architecture, Interaction Design, Interface Design, Requirements Gathering, Research, Strategy and Planning, Usability Analysis, Usability Testing, User Centered Design, User Experience Design, Web Marketing, Wire Framing

Senior Global Manager, Usability

Motorola

Public Company; MOT; Telecommunications industry

Practical responsibilities include conducting large-scale usability testing , quantitive surveying, heuristic reviews, persona development, localization issues (28 countries/11 languages) and evolving the product lines to meet industry usability best practices.
