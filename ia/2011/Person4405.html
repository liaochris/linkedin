Lead the library’s Collection Development Team: Office of Materials Selection, Collection Resources/Inter-Library Loan, Cataloging, and Technical Processing Services. Responsibilities included: articulating collection standards for all print, non-print, and electronic content; formulating policies and procedures related to collections and their use; identifying, developing, and assuring collection management measurements for the efficient and cost-effective selection, acquisition and processing of library materials in all formats; negotiating and managing all materials vendor and value added services contracts. Supervised 55+ full time employees and managed an annual departmental budget of over 10 Million. Reported to the Deputy Director for Public Service (DDPS).

Cooperated closely with Technical Processing and Access Services, Branch Administration, Department of Programs and Special Services, Central and Business Libraries, Information Technology, Development, Finance. Reported to the Director, Department of Collection Development. Supervised a staff of 10 Full Time Employees.
