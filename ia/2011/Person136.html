David Spann - Vistage Idaho's Summary

David is known as “the corporate firefighter,” helping executives and their teams walk the ragged edge between putting out fires and starting new ones; honing decision making, increasing productivity; and delivering incredible results. In his role as a Vistage Chair, Mr. Spann provides one-on-one executive coaching, facilitation for a peer-to-peer group of chief executives, and helps each member become better leaders, make better decisions and lead better lives.

"On July 6, 1994, my life changed when 14 wildland firefighters died on Storm King Mountain in western Colorado. Because of internal communication breakdowns, politics, and peer pressure to "get it done" these 14 people (including a friend) lost their future - it's in their memory that I dedicate the rest of my life helping executives not make the same mistakes."
