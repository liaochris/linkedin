Bill Tyson’s Summary

Seasoned direct marketer, business developer, strategic planner and leader with more than 25 years of combined domestic and international experience. Proven track record in impacting corporate bottom line through skillful orchestration and execution of business plans focused on sales, marketing and account management functions by targeting diverse markets through multiple channels. Strategic cultivator of profitable partnerships, joint ventures and alliances with a successful background serving in an instrumental role in new product and service innovations serving financial services companies. Served as one of the youngest Profit Center Managers in AIG in the late 1980s. Managed 3 different call centers. Served two successful tours as an expatriate. Vertical expertise includes financial institutions, unions and affinity groups (associations and clubs). Executive Level Product expertise includes: Term life (individual and group), Accident and Health (individual and group), Personal Lines and P&C Program Business (both Professional Liability and BOP).
