Mbugua Njihia's Summary

I am a business savvy – techpreneur with over 8 (eight) years experience in the new media industry with focus on web and mobile engagements. Instrumental in the set up and operations of one of the regions inaugural value added services, and having worked at key positions in leading web and mobile service firms, he possesses unique insight that marries technology with the market place. My role is to implement the strategic goals and objectives of Symbiotic, to give direction and leadership toward the achievement of the organization’s philosophy, mission, strategy, its goals and objectives as well as seek out new business and partnerships that continue to grow Symbiotic Media going into the future.

I have worked in the new media industry (on web and mobile) for the past eight years and executed campaigns for major brands in the region - Coca Cola, East African Breweries, Nation Media, Central Bank of Kenya, Safaricom, Celtel (formerly Kencell). I have also worked with a number of NGO's - The Kenya Human Rights Commission, Transparency International, CRECO (Kenya) and Goal ICT.

Under the auspices of the Mobile Marketing Association, I will be working to craft policy frameworks to guide the industry and where necessary guide legislation to protect the mobile consumer as well as ensure growth of an important industry.

I have consulted for the Government of Kenya on M-government with special focus on consolidating efforts to take government services closer to the citizen as well as developing recommendations to ensure the sustainability of the said services.

The Mobile Marketing Association (MMA) is the premier global non-profit trade association representing all players in the mobile marketing value chain. With more than 700 member companies, the MMA is an action-oriented organization with global focus, regional actions and local relevance. The MMA's primary focus is to establish mobile as an indispensible part of the marketing mix. The MMA works to promote, educate, measure, guide and protect the mobile marketing industry worldwide. The MMA's global headquarters are located in the United States and it has regional chapters including North America (NA), Europe, Middle East and Africa (EMEA), Latin America (LATAM), and Asia Pacific (APAC) branches.
MMA members include agencies, advertisers, hand held device manufacturers, carriers and operators, retailers, software providers and service providers, as well as any company focused on the potential of marketing via mobile devices.
