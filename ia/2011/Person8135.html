Chris Heuwetter's Summary

Chris Heuwetter has implemented award-winning strategic marketing and public relations programs for an array of clients, in various industries, during his years as a professional. Before launching TwiloPR, Chris worked for M. Silver Associates, a New York City public relations agency with an office in Fort Lauderdale, handling accounts in industries that range from travel and education to financial services and non-profit organizations. His work with M. Silver Associates can be demonstrated by his dense portfolio which contains media placements he has secured for clients that range from magazine cover stories, to national AP placements, to local broadcast segments in top regional markets around the country. Chris is extremely tech-savvy and his new media expertise has allowed to help launch the new media and online reputation management practice of M. Silver Associates and introduce the new service to clients.

Prior to working with M. Silver Associates, Chris spent some time with Estrela Marketing, an interactive marketing firm located in Delray Beach learning the "ins and outs" of Web 2.0 PR initiatives, social media marketing and new media platform development. Through utilization of various Internet marketing techniques, including email marketing, online advertising and search engine optimization, Chris' tenure at Estrela proved to be an invaluable asset for clients looking to capitalize on "PR 2.0."

Chris also spent some of his early career at Cohn & Wolfe - New York City, a public relations powerhouse and subsidiary of Young & Rubicam. At Cohn & Wolfe, Chris worked with very prolific brands such as Ford and Hewlett Packard, focusing mostly on corporate communications with each company's cutting-edge technology offerings.

Specialties

Online Repuation Management, Social Media Marketing, Media Relations, Direct Mail and Email Marketing Campaigns/Design, Ad Placement and Design, Event Management, Press Release Drafting and Media Pitching, Media Interview Training, Branding, Corporate Communications
