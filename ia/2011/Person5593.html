    March 2010 — October 2010 (8 months)

    So what do I bring to this established and prestigious firm, besides my 20+ years of operations history & experience and a proven supply chain family?

    In logistics and supply chain management, the difficulties associated with getting the right product to the right place at the right time in the right quantity and condition at the lowest possible cost is the challenge. Finding the accurate balance between improving inventory turns and avoiding stock outs requires strong supply chain management. My charge is to set up a Supply Chain Management division at FIVE STAR LOGISTICS utilizing my expertise, to design a highly refined operational and cost effective supply chain, planned to suit each client’s current goals, needs, and future vision. Business expansion requires management to have the foresight to understand revenue growth will be dependent on internal cost reductions. Our clients expect reduced costs and increased services at the same time. Therefore, growth planning is contingent upon total “supply chain diligence”.

    We work closely with our clients to identify their supply chain team and logistics strategies. By creating a dialog and listening to the unique “voice of the customer” we preserve the distinctive characteristics that are inherent to their business. FIVE STAR LOGISTICS’ goal is to assess, collaborate and implement the tools and skills necessary to ensure operational excellence and successful supply chain execution. We act as an agent for our client and share the same values and customer service principles as the client themselves.
