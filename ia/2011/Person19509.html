Javier Segovia’s Summary

Founded Tap 'n Tap, a software and design firm based in Cambridge, MA bringing the best User Experience to Android Tablets. We are helping our OEM and Operator customers bring top notch tablets to markets around the world. Tap UI tablets are intuitive, fun, and engaging to use both at home and on the go.

At Mirador Tech, I led a team to pursue a Management Buyout (MBO) of a business unit of a technology company.

As the Director of Marketing & Sales for SigmaTel's Imaging Systems Group, I led the growth of our digital imaging technology business by 65% CAGR. I championed the extension of our product lines into new emerging markets such as digital photo frames, and drove the scalability of our business by developing customer support kits, training the field staff, and enabling our customers to confidently deploy our hardware and software products. This resulted in the transformation of our business model from a turnkey service provider to an embedded products company with a broad customer base. None of this would have been possible without the support of an amazing, dedicated team of product managers, BD professionals, program managers, and applications engineers.

At Oasis Semiconductor, I led the growth of our second product line, initially less than 10% of revenues, and grew it to becoming our largest and most profitable business. The role spanned end-to-end marketing & sales responsibilities: developing the marketing strategy, managing the product roadmap, marketing existing products, and closing multi-million dollar deals with customers and supporting them through the adoption lifecycle. Our product line's steady growth contributed significantly to Oasis's successful exit -- its acquisition by SigmaTel in 2005 -- yielding returns to investors of over 20x.

My career goal is to become involved with exciting technology startups in the consumer electronics, mobile tech, and software sectors, and help build profitable, sustainable businesses.

Javier Segovia’s Specialties:

High tech startups, entrepreneurial marketing, product management, business development, building scalable businesses. Track record of consistent revenue growth and extensive experience with Asian customers.
