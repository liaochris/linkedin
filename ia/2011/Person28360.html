Mark Haas CMC, FIMC’s Summary

ROM helps executives of organizations whose performance is acceptable but not exceptional to improve performance against mission by streamlined planning and disciplined execution. We work with executives and staff to create agile and profitable companies by improving strategy, operations and culture, creating realistic action plans, supporting execution and capability development, and evaluating progress. We bring a technical background and advanced analytical capabilities to advise on science, technology and R&D-focused companies. We also bring extensive nonprofit experience to support foundations, association, and public sector clients aimed at progressive social change and in healthcare. Mr. Haas is a Certified Management Consultant (CMC), expert witness, former lead Baldrige Examiner, author, invited speaker on ethics, business simulation, and decision making, advisor to national organizations, and holds degrees from Colgate and Harvard Universities.

Mark Haas CMC, FIMC’s Specialties:

Performance measurement, business process, business planning, operations, business ethics, governance, change management, organizational development, strategy consulting, technology, organizational assessment, quality management, process improvement, strategy development and implementation. Public, private, nonprofit clients in the areas of technology, entrepreneur/expanding business, healthcare and progressive social change.
