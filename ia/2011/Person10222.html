Dan Aufhauser’s Summary

14 years of experience in domestic and international music and technology business. Specialize in using web and mobile technology to create community and connect bands, brands and fans.

Talents:
business development, strategic partnerships, sales, music marketing, music branding, digital distribution, label relations, content acquisition, music licensing, music supervision/placement, artist management, brand management, marketing agency account management, new media, old-skool record label marketing, music promotions, and brand sponsorships.

Accomplishments:

Helped build and create the MIX.dj community of 650,000 members, 125,000 VIP accounts, 20,000 DJ mixes - and over 500,000 iPhone App downloads from the Apple Appstore!

Executive-produced and sold over 350,000 custom music compilation CDs and online music marketing campaigns.

Identified and secured digital distribution contracts/agreements with over 250 independent record labels worldwide.

Business Development and music marketing partnerships include: Google, eBay, Nautica, NBC-Universal, BravoTV, Wells Fargo, Pottery Barn, Station Casinos, Starwood Resorts, Banana Republic and many more.

Built and merged Globalphonic Music, LLC. - a lifestyle music marketing firm which merged with Sugo Music Agency in 2004. Created Mellow-Vibe Music, a global music-licensing library.

Entered digital music distribution industry in 1999. Worked as International Business Development Director and Worldwide Label Relations for Liquid Audio, the industry pioneer of DRM music technology and digital distribution. Managed over 250 independent and European record label relationships from the London and San Francisco offices.

Worked as an Artist Development Rep. at Interscope Records (Universal Music Group) through college, helping establish the success for many of today's most popular multi-platinum artists including No Doubt, Bush, Snoop Dogg, Dr. Dre.

Love using music, technology and relationships to do good business.
