Massimo Casagrande's Summary

In 2004 I finished my first experience university a degree in computer science: analysis new type of interaction with supermarket products using RFID.
I learned fundamental basis for general programming, relational databases, software engineering, media and communication technologies.

Look for new perceptions, I started a degree on Multimedia Visual Communications with which I added my know-all that graphics culture (paper, web, movement), color theory, psychology of perception, video, photography,visual communication,design and art: fundamental aspects for creat a complete project with h-quality.
I continued to learn new media theory and different techniques to use collaborative languages for web pages, i know softwares to create videos and digital graphics to take full control of the theoretical study.
I studid interaction design and user experience to improve the analysys of the communication towards user.

I concluded my career specialist university with a degree in 2006 analyzing a new multimedia interaction for univeristy users by defining a system using web 2.0 applications.

In 2006 experienced I collaborated with Riboli at San Marino University, finding new motivations and new ways of interacting in my working life.
The experiment repeated in 2007 at IUAV of Venice.

I'm working as a visual designer-developer where I can experience all the techniques and keeping touch with new trends and new experiences.
I continue to develop new types of projects using new media, I feel curious about the internet world so that i'm into searching and testing new web 2.0 applications.
I like to meet people and team in order to collaborate with them sharing the abilities and this happens every day.

Now my new (also old because I like to know the potential of device) passion is mobile media: design and create mobile website and application, fast communication and new interaction.
