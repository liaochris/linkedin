J. Michael Peace's Summary

J. Michael Peace is an experienced & dedicated Consultant (owner/consultant, JMP Consulting LLC) focusing primarily on working with Utility companies in North America who use or are implementing SAP software as their customer service & billing solution. Michael has a strong background in SAP for Utilities (CCS / IS-U) software and has been working with SAP IS-U and the Utilities Industry for over 9 years in many aspects, including IS-U Product Development Project Management and taking on Lead Consultant roles during entire project life cycles. Michael has also participated in pre-sales meetings with multiple utility clients & Assessment Projects with SAP customers who will soon be kicking off full scale CCS/IS-U implementations.

Michael is currently focusing on SAP Product Development Project Management, Lead Consulting roles in SAP IS-U/CCS Implementations, SAP IS-U post go-live Support (Project Management and hands-on Functional Support), SAP Business & IT Process Improvement Initiatives, and SAP Quality Assurance.

Michael's SAP for Utilities project experience includes Entergy Corporation, Reliant Energy / NRG Energy, Pinellas County Utilities, New York Power Authority, Aquarion Water Company, and Enbridge Gas Distribution, among other clients as well. His track record proves that he can provide creative solutions to complex business requirements and he ensures that solid, reliable processes & systems are in place for utilities that have chosen SAP as their Customer Service, Metering, Financing, and Billing solution.

Specialties

Strategy: Project Management & Preparation, SAP IS-U Quality Assurance & Risk Mitigation, SAP Client Development, Business Development, Business Process Improvement.

Process: SAP Software – IS-U/CCS (Industry Solutions – Utilities), Electric, Gas, & Water Utilities Industry, Billing/Invoicing/Rate Configuration, Full Project Lifecycle (full Implementations, and post go-live support).

People: Project Management, Project Coordination, Team Leadership.
