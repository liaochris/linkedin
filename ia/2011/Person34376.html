Pawel Piechowicz's Summary

Having both a degree in Architecture and Urban Design as well as over 10 years of experience in web design, I try to focus my activities on areas where these two professions overlap. While working part-time in an architecture office in London, I also run my own company Diffusion Studio (started 2009), which focuses more on virtual and experimental aspects of architecture, as well as multimedia, web and print design. I specialize in media-rich web design for the creative industries and architecture studios.
I am continuously looking for original, innovative and fresh design projects from the field of architecture and art.

Specialties

architectural design, HTML web design, Adobe Flash design and ActionScript programming
