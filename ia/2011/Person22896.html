Judy White, SPHR, GPHR, HCS’s Summary

Partnering with leaders and organizations to infuse a whole new kind of workplace that is ready to lead the competition into the future, creating long-term value and profitable growth through next generation people management consulting & coaching services.

White, an accredited people practice strategist, serving people, senior executives and organizations (domestic/international) brings seventeen years experience in change leadership, great place to work initiatives, organizational culture transformation and development, talent and performance management, proactive employee relations and rewards.

White, is a published writer whose articles on straight HR talk, strategy and business performance have appeared in HR Management Magazine, CIO Insight, Business Management Magazine, Engagement Strategy Magazine, and the Raleigh Examiner for Workplace Issues. She has been quoted in news publications and trade journals.

Discover the benefits that an adaptable 21st Century Workplace can bring to your tough challenges! We invite you to visit us at www.theinfusiongroupllc.com and explore a whole new way to engage your people strategy and move your vision forward. Or drop us a line at contact@theinfusiongroupllc.com.
