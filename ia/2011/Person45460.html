Walter Kravchenko’s Summary

Stem Cells for Hope is a company engaged in the field of regenerative medicine. This includes ongoing research, development and the treatment of patients with stem cell transplantation therapy. SCFH uses Adult Stem Cell and Cord Blood Stem Cells in the treatment of patients suffering from degenerative disorders of the human body such as Alzheimer's, Parkinson's Disease, ALS, leukemia, muscular dystrophy, multiple sclerosis, arthritis, spinal cord injuries, brain trauma, stroke, heart disease, liver and retinal disease, diabetes as well as certain types of cancer. SCFH has established license agreements with medical treatment facilities in Mexico and Eastern Europe where stem cell transplantation therapy is approved by the appropriate local government agencies.

Since SCFH provides both your own Adult Stem Cells and Cord Blood Stem Cells which are available now for the treatment of patients outside the US. Our cost per treatment is the lowest in the industry, without sacrificing the quality of our clinics medical care and still be able to provide the greatest amount of stem cells per dose available.

I welcome discussion with anyone interested in exploring a wide variety of potential wealth-building opportunities with Stem Cells For Hope.
