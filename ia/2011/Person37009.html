Rick Shadid’s Summary

As a foreclosure specialist in the real estate industry today, there is much to know, and learn as well. I am focused on staying abreast of the latest information in regards to foreclosures as well as the real estate marketplace in general. I make it a priority to evaluate each and every client individually while keeping their goals/best interest in mind. The investment into the foreclosure market is not something to take for granted. Their are various stages to the foreclosure process, each with its own unique set of advantages and disadvantages. To work with a specialist in the foreclosure market is truly the best route to take. Would you go to a pediatrician to treat your brain tumor? No, so why would you go to a residential/retail Realtor to handle your foreclosure purchase. To make a mistake in this arena could cost you thousands if not, tens of thousands of dollars. Chicago Realty Partners has over 250+ listings at any given moment, all of which are REO (Real Estate Owned), which means they have been taken over by the lending institution and are now being marketed for sale. This would be an example of the 3rd stage in the foreclosure process. DO not hesitate to write me and ask any questions you may have... To buy in real estate is now a great opportunity waiting for you to capitalize. Gone are the days of sellers dictate the prices they will recieve it is truly a buyers market, and if you are going to buy why not start your hunt within the arena that can see you save up to 25% or more off the retail value of your next home... I am a licensed sales agent in the state of Illlinois, and a member of the Chicago Association of Realtors, so in turn if you can not find what you are looking for in the foreclosure market, I can show you proven ways to get sellers to come down off their inflated asking prices to get you a great deal on any listed property in the Greater Chicagoland area .
