Jenny Simpson's Summary

I've been working in online news and marketing for approx. 10 years now, doing a mixture of editorial and commercial content production which has ranged from interviewing celebrities, writing news reports / press releases and planning promotions.

Doing PR for a web marketing agency, SEO (incorporting social media) strategies have recently become an important aspect of my work.
