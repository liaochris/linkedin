Felix Weber's Summary

Felix Weber is an award winning, independent composer/songwriter/ producer with 25+ years of experience. He worked as an exclusive writer for Warner Chappell Music for 15 years. He also worked as a writer for Sony Music. He was successful with artists such as Chaka Khan, Randy Crawford, Toni Braxton, Paul Anka, Nancy Wilson ... (see portfolio for complete list). Felix relocated from Germany to the United States where he started a small music production Company in Atlanta, GA. Felix later moved to Ohio where he continued with "Felix Weber Productions". He now works out of his studio in Columbus, OH. Felix is frequently collaborating with his longtime songwriting partner Irmgard Klarmann.

Specialties

Felix is a precise and detail oriented worker. His passion for music which he has been doing professionally for almost 30 years now, has allowed him to experience several eras and genres of music. This has given him the skills and ability to compose, produce and arrange with authenticity in many different styles.
