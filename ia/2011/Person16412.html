The Graduate Studies Office at NUI Maynooth looks after postgraduate admissions, funding and policy (both extarnal and internal). NUI Maynooth was awarded University of the Year in 08-09, due to our excellence in Research, Careers Service and top level academic training.

The Graduate School at NUI Maynooth offers postgraduate opportunities across three faculties; Faculty of Arts, Celtic Studies and Philosophy; the Faculty of Social Sciences; and the Faculty of Science and Engineering. visit our website, http://graduatestudies.nuim.ie for more information, and reignite a passion for learning!
