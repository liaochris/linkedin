Kay-Cheree Hearne's Summary

Promoting the Photographer/Artist within...

Kay-Cheree Hearne is a traditional digital photographer based on the Sunshine Coast, QLD.

Naturally gifted in a number of creative graphic applications, Kay-Cheree studied Art and Building graphics through school, with skills including but not limited to creating collages, video clips and logos for business and personal use through various software applications.

Picking up the camera from a young age, Kay-Cheree has developed her own unique photographic style for landscape, sporting and portrait photography. Preferring the candid style of ‘what you see is what you should see’, capturing the natural beauty in our land and seascapes, the energy and determination in our sporting communities, and the innocence and emotions from natural portraits.

A burning passion, Kay-Cheree’s focus in photography has been the ‘Sunrise’ aspect, applying the beauty and serenity of these images to the people, in the form of downloads, prints and gifts, but also specializing in the creative side of turning her timeless candid captures into family keepsakes by way of collage prints.

With the following of her two boys through their sporting endeavours, Kay-Cheree has become adept in sport photography, taking on the position as a club photographer for a local Nippers Surf Club.

Kay-Cheree’s nature/lifestyle photography was also noticed by a local residential developer which is now used in their advertising, leading her on the interesting path of commercial property photography for Real Estate agents and Clientele. With architectural angles taken into consideration, you shouldn’t be surprised if you find her climbing fences & trees to get that perfect shot!!

Kay-Cheree Hearne's Education

School of Life

Honours

Back in my school days I was top of the class in hands on crafty subjects like Electrical, Building Graphics, Art etc but due to a turbulent childhood I left half way through Year 11 & jumped into a 12mth Clerical Traineeship with a local Ford Dealership. Staying there for 6 years I also studied Tafe courses such as Vehicle Fitting (upholstery), Horse training & Natural Therapies. My love of animals then led me into working for a local Vet Clinic both in the office & out in the field/surgery, then married life and family led me into the Banking & Finance Industry.
I have covered many aspects of the office, have an eye for detail & a compulsion for creativity. Over the years I have dabbled in weekend workshops or learning by correspondance to improve my knowledge in any area of my interest.
Although I have never been to Uni & studied for degrees etc the world has been my classroom, and every person I meet is my teacher. In any position I give 110% effort, such is my Life! :)
