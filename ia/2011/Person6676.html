Donna Marks's Summary

Power Purchasing works with member companies ranging in size from small local offices to $5 billion companies, and specializes in multi-location businesses. Since 1996, Power Purchasing has helped more than 5,000 member companies in nearly 9,000 locations across the United States and 23 other countries.

Power Purchasing makes it easy for small and large corporations,schools, universities, medical facilities and government offices to save time and money when when buying office supplies, furniture, uniforms, tech products, promotional products, customer and employee holiday gifts, and corporate programs. By leveraging the buying power of the group and negotiating deep discounts and more customization with premier suppliers, Power Purchasing brings significant value to its members.

Through the Diversity Supplier Program, Power Purchasing’s WBE Certification allows them to meet the objectives of businesses that need minority subcontractors & supplier diversity programs in order to win large corporate contracts or government contracts.

Specialties

Discount office supplies, office furniture, uniforms, commercial printing, tech products, promotional products, bulk holiday gifts & corporate programs.
