Mike Gionta's Summary

While I still actively own and operate and multiple million dollar search firm, I began Recruiter training, Coaching, and Consulting with recruiting firm owners to show them how to properly hire recruiters, retain them, and grow their businesses.

We do this thru analyzing their current operation, and then implementing systems and strategies that make recruiting revenue predictable and consistent.

I work with recruiting firms that are not satisfied with their current situation and want more. From training products, teleseminars, a recruiting "coaching club" featuring industry all-stars to group and one-to-one coaching, TheRecruiterU.com has solutions for owners seeking knowledge and growth.

Visit www.TheRecruiterU.com for a FREE 7 part audio series on The 7 Deadly Sins Recruiting Firm Owners make and How to AVOID Them!
