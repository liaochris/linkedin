Devin James, Sr.’s Summary

The Devin James Group is pleased to submit our qualifications to your company. We believe that our first-hand knowledge of the emerging technologies, combined with our expertise developing campaigns for communities, organizations and corporations, makes us a great partner for you.

Founded in 2000 by brand strategist Devin James, The Devin James Group is a strategic marketing and communications agency that leverages a brand with unique programs that incorporate innovative PR, marketing and advertising. While serving a diverse client list that includes other advertising and marketing agencies, PR firms, large corporations and small businesses, the agency specializes in brand identity, advertising art, cutting-edge web initiatives, brand/public awareness campaigns, out-of-home advertising, event "experiential" marketing, taxi/vehicle advertising and sponsorship activation.

In 2010 the Devin James Group was selected to enter into a joint-venture with global PR firm Weber Shandwick St. Louis, as the first Minority Mentor-Protégé Strategic Partnership in company history.

Headquartered in Memphis, Tennessee with offices in Nashville and St. Louis, clients of the Devin James Group include governmental organizations, educational institutions, small businesses, large corporations and political campaigns.

Since inception, the Devin James Group has served more than 70 individual clients, not including multiple or multi-year assignments.

We guarantee you have seen our work before. This is why we don't have to brag or scream about what we've done. We let our clients do the talking because all of our business over the years has come from referrals and simply doing great work. Just in case you live under a rock and have not heard of us; please check out some of our most recent testimonials, case studies and a short client list.
