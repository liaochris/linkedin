Avi Kaplan's Summary

I’m an online marketing consultant and coach. I advise nonprofits, businesses, and job seekers on social media best practices and campaigns. I’ve worked with authors, filmmakers, synagogues, schools, activists, and small business owners and have presented workshops and webinars on social media and online community organizing.

I’m a digital native, an nptechie, and a rabble-rouser active in progressive causes and the Jewish community. I’m a South Florida native, a Washington D.C. resident, and a Harvard University alum. Recently, I was the Director of Online Community and Special Projects at Epic Change, a small international grant making organization. Now I work with clients through my company Ozen Media.
