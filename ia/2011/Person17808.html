Ian Perry's Summary

Remedy For Business Ltd works with owner run businesses and works with them to understand the issues and areas that are holding back growth and profitability, and through a nationwide network of 300 advisors and about 500 local strategic partners,provides solutions.

Providing as much or little support as business owners require, Remedy helps with a diverse portfolio of services from business mentoring to sales team training, business recovery to preparing a company for sale.

Whatever a business owner needs help with, there is Remedy - taking care of business.Remedy For Business Ltd are proud to be accredited advisors for the IBD Group.

Ian started out as a chef and restaurant manager, but went from cooking food to cooking the books - and became an accountant.Over the last 25 years, Ian has worked his way through the ranks from Assistant Accountant, Accountant, Financial Controller, Group Financial Controller and finally Finance Director.

All of his experience was gained in Small and Medium Enterprises (SME's) mainly in engineering and manufacturing companies. Sectors worked in over the years was diverse and included malting,shipping,an oil company,speaker manufacture,plastic injection moulding,protective clothing manufacture and car transporter design and manufacture.

By being from the other side of the desk as it were, Ian is in position to understand the challenges business owners face, and has developed a track record of adding value to company's and improving their profitability and financial stability.
