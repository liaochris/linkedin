Brad Waldron's Summary

An industry leader

Equally effective coaching on a 1:2:1 basis or delivering a conference message to 1000 people, Brad has a proven record of helping others to succeed.

He harnesses a rare combination of insight, humour and practical strategies to increase performance whilst enabling people to get more fulfilment from their work role.

His work has been featured in the NLP MasterClass, Financial Times, HR Managers Magazine and has authored numerous articles.

With a background in International Trade incorporating importing, exporting, sales and distribution he has led businesses in Australia, USA and the UK. His bias for results driven productivity is coupled with a balanced approach to developing the people who achieve the results.

He has worked with international speaker Anthony Robbins in the UK and USA, was an official licensee for international author Dr Stephen Covey and was certified to facilitate The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People, Principle Centred Leadership and First Things First. In 1993 he became a certified trainer of NLP through the Society of Neuro Linguistic Programming.

Brad has had exposure across a broad range of business sectors: FMCG, Banking, Financial Services, IT – Software and Hardware, Retail and Charity Services.

His clients include Diageo, Sony, Microsoft, Dame Ellen MacArthur Trust, Fujitsu, Lovells, GSK, Britvic, Welsh Water, UBS, Co-Op Financial Services, Barclays, L’Oreal, Redken NYC, Legg Mason, Iron Mountain, Kerastase, PRS for Music and KPMG.

Specialties

Leadership Development, Team Work, Personal Effectiveness Training, Solution Sales Skills, High Impact Presentational Skills, Conference ad Event Design, Planning and Facilitation
