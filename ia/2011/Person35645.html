Rabbi Jason Miller's Summary

Ordained Conservative rabbi available to officiate at Jewish weddings, funerals and other life-cycle events. Provides kosher certification to restaurants, bakeries and specialty food producers. Available for speaking engagements on a wealth of topics.

Focused on creating fun and enjoyable synagogue experiences for families and individuals. Specializing in Jewish teen initiatives, adult education, couples and individual counseling services, and the enhancement of synagogue-based youth activities. A strong proponent of offering varied programs to congregants on Shabbat. Much experience with creating meaningful and memorable life-cycle events.

Specialties

kosher certification, wedding officiation, Jewish teens programming, couples counseling, addiction counseling, conversion to Judaism, life-cycle events
