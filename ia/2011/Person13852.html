Eric Bull's Summary

12 years sales and product development experience in Online Advertising, Online Media, Lead Generation / Co-Registration, Email, Data Monetization, and Affiliate/Publisher/Network relationships. Substantial direct advertiser and agency/media broker portfolio as well as attended/exhibited at over 40 online advertising tradeshows since 1998. Benefited from 5 individual corporate acquisitions over a 8 year period.

Specialties

Generate and sell CPA, CPC, CPL based online lead generation and co-registration (custom, opt-in, and opt-out) as well as email marketing, banner advertising, and pop advertising. Additional specialties include media buying, email deployment, i-frame, pixel, sub ID, real-time debug codes, interstitials, opt to pop, tell-a-friend, ad design + messaging, data validation, and web analytics. Expertise in building and managing advertising platforms for Canada, UK, and other international markets.
