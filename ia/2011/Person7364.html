Cathlena Spencer, CIR, CDR, CSSR's Summary

I am a certified recruiting and marketing professional with an expertise in placements of mid to senior level candidates. We exclusively serve the financial activities sector which includes wealth management, banking, insurance, brokerage, credit services and more. Although we only serve the financial sector we handle most all positions (financial, operations, legal, IT, etc) within our sector.

I have been a small business owner and a Marketing Director. I have helped to create a non-profit theatre company and then taken it to the Edinburgh Fringe Festival and served on the Board of Directors for two fantastic theatre companies. I am a creative genius with fundraising and event planning; from no budget to gala affairs.

Specialties

Diversity Recruting, Social Sourcing Recruting, branding and identity, grant writing, blog writing, commerical scripts, voice overs, fundraising, creativity and resourcefulness.
