Ajit Kumar's Summary

Ajit is known for his gregarious and sociable nature. As Cofounder and Chief Operating Officer of Malhaar iMarketing Advantage, Ajit looks after the delivery systems and operations for this company.

Prior to this he established highly successful Indian IT operations for Sirius (now SSP UK). Ajit has not only brought unprecedented cost savings to the company, under his leadership Sirius has been able to migrate product development function to India, something not off-shored commonly even today.

Prior to the Indian stint, Ajit enjoyed leading technology design and product development management. Ajit has led the revamp of the core product base of Sirius’ insurance products from conceptualization to delivery.

With experience spanning about two decades, Ajit is known to bring resonance in development centres along with his vision and experience. Having served NIIT, Velocient and Sirius, organizations in training, services and product delivery, Ajit has addressed several complex service challenges through simple delivery processes.

Ajit is a fun loving person with a bit of religious and spiritual tilt and appreciates similar wavelength in his friends. Ajit strongly believes in positive aspect of all the fellow human beings and tries to see things in positive perspective. He would like to make an overall positive net impact on this planet however small.
