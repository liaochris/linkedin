Kristen Matlick's Summary

Museum educator with broad experience ranging from school tours at a national historical park to development and management of all aspects of education programming at a local history museum. Growing interest in utilizing participatory experiences and new media in museum settings, allowing visitors to contribute to the curatorial voice.

Specialties

Guided tours, "discovery boxes," outreach programs, Scout prorams, family programs, workshops, lectures, exhibit guides, exhibit development.

Waukesha County Historical Society and Museum

Museums and Institutions industry

Developed interpretive plans and materials for hands-on “Discovery Boxes,” and reinstituted outreach programs, all utilizing an inquisition-based model of learning and materials such as photographs, reproduction objects, and collections objects.
