LAUREN MIELI’s Summary

LAUREN MIELI is a conscientious and passionate financial advisor working with the high net worth market with a special focus on women in transition (divorced, widowed, job loss,e t al.) Drawing upon her extensive background in the financial services arena, Lauren provides trusted and value-added counsel to her clientele. Whereas she is fluent in all areas of financial service, her strength is in client education and offering sound strategies for wealth accumulation, wealth management, and wealth distribution.

Lauren spent considerable time in the marketing end of the financial services industry with many Fortune 50 companies. The joy she derives from working with and educating people accelerated her transition to financial advisor. Her financial services practice is predicated on collaboration and building trust.

“INTEGRITY IS DOING THE RIGHT THING EVEN IF NOBODY IS WATCHING.” ~ Jim Stovall

Lauren brands with integrity and makes a positive impact in her client’s lives. She places the greatest emphasis on the advisor-client relationship. Lauren’s approach to financial planning is warm, consultative, and thorough. She is adept at assessing an overall portfolio, diagnosing risk, and asking pointed questions to isolate specific needs. She builds a great deal of equity into every relationship. “My clients are like family to me - I look out for their best interest and want to be an integral part of their success,” she states.

Lauren is the consummate builder of trust, offering clarity and navigation through a turbulent economy and, in many cases, saving her clients financially. “I subscribe to the highest code of ethics,” Lauren contends, “I am defined by integrity; that is how I live my life.”

Lauren specializes in working professional women and women in transition (e.g.recently divorced, widowed), "Empowering women to take control of their finances is a passion of mine". She is also a great resource for the entrepreneur or “next-wave millionaire”.
