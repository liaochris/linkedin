Ralph Contreras's Summary

Hello, my name is Rafael "Ralph" Sergio Contreras III, I've been involved in the Graphics Arts field for over 10 years now, working with individuals, small businesses, corporations, and for myself. Design is my chosen profession and I really enjoy it. I remember my first computer illustrations on my Apple IIGS way back in 1988 when I was just a kid. I would stay up all night drawing my favorite comic heroes in Paintworks Plus. I've been working with design in one form or another ever since then.

I graduated from New Mexico State University in spring 2000 with a Bachelors Degree in Fine Arts with an emphasis in Graphic Design. After college my joy for comics prompted me to open and run my own successful comic book store for 5 years. After closing the doors to my comic shop I dove into the world of newsprint advertising as a graphic designer. I found myself working for fast paced small town newspapers to the even faster paced city of Las Vegas, NV. I am currently living in Santa Fe, NM

I am currently in the process of creating my own independent comic company for my own comic book.

I am very knowledgeable with both Macintosh, Pc, and Adobe software. I am a very organized person and have great experience working in fast pasted companies with multi-ad deadlines. I am very cheerful, energetic and excited to utilize my skills. I work very well in a team environment and have been a team leader for one of the papers I have worked for. I am knowledgeable with webpage upkeep, I created and maintain my own website that has my portfolio.

I believe I have the right experience and enthusiasm to be an assist to any company.

- Rafael "Ralph" Sergio Contreras III
