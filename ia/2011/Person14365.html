Eve Majure’s Summary

With over 20 years of international training and development experience, I help companies reach their financial and business goals through effective selection and development of their management staff. I have had success in the agricultural, technology, university, and non-profit sectors. My goal is to develop, implement, and measure world-class skill development programs in the US and abroad.

Through strong relationships with employees, colleagues, executives, and vendors throughout I have been able to build global understanding of new concepts and initiatives. Communication, skill development, financial acumen, behavior-based performance incentives, and recognition are key tools to my success. Used together, I have found these tools strengthen manager/learner learning, performance, and results.

My interest and education in international affairs began with overseas assignments and lots of moving in my early years. As a result, cultural and language understanding come easily to me. I speak, write and teach in Spanish. In addition, I speak some French and Italian.
