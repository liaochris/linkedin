Pete Leibman's Summary

CAREER EXPERT, SPEAKER, AUTHOR AND COACH:

CONTACT INFORMATION: Pete@IdealizeNow.com

MISSION: To help students and young professionals get their dream jobs and achieve career success of their own.

BIO: Pete Leibman has spoken in-person to audiences as large as 4,000+, and he is the creator of both The Dream Job College Tour and The Washington Wizards’ Sports Careers Day, events attended by thousands of people since 2004.

Despite being told it would be “impossible,” Pete networked his way into his dream job to work for a pro sports franchise after graduating from Johns Hopkins University in 2003.

Less than 2 years later, he was promoted into management for the NBA’s Washington Wizards (at the age of 23). He then managed and trained entry-level staff, while being ranked as the franchise’s #1 Salesperson for 3 straight years.

As President of Idealize Enterprises, Pete has been invited to share his career advice at some of the world's finest academic institutions, including Stanford University and Johns Hopkins University.

Specialties

Dream Job, Job Search Strategy, Networking, Career Development, Career Advancement, Career Advice, Career Coaching, Professional Development, Public Speaking
