Donald Ramsell’s Summary

Donald Ramsell has been an Illinois DUI Attorney for 26 years. His firm has defended over 14,000 cases since 1986. He is a highly respected leader in the field of DUI Defense, having lectured before thousands of attorneys in Illinois, Michigan, Missouri, Texas, New York, Arkansas, Louisiana and Wisconsin.

Donald Ramsell’s Specialties:

Specializing in Illinois DUI Defense, Criminal Defense and Traffic Laws.
