Marika Abuashvili’s Summary

Real Estate has been my profession since 1995. I come from teaching and literature background. When helping you through buying or selling your home, I take an approach of educating and guiding. Would it be latest market trends, net proceeds from home sale, advice on staging or understanding a contract. My goal is to inform you about each and every aspect of home sale so it’s easy for you to make right decision.
If you are a home seller – I will create custom marketing campaign for your home using the most advanced tools today’s technology has to offer. I will advise on necessary changes to exterior and interior so that your home shines on the market.
If you are a home buyer, I will introduce you to all the financing options available to you. We will explore the neighborhoods, schools and shopping areas and find most comfortable place for you. Then step by step I will guide you through the complex process of turning a house into your home.
It's a privilege to be trusted by people with life’s most important financial move. I continuously update my knowledge by staying atop of professional education. So each person I work with gets most current expert advice on every related subject.
Whether you are buying or selling I know the range of emotions that takes place. You can count on my loyalty, patience and of course – sense of humor. Because not matter how serious the work is, it can't be done right without couple of laughs.
I welcome you to contact me with your real estate needs today and look forward to getting started!
