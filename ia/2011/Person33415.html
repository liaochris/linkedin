Vahe Arabian- Search Engine Optimisation (SEO) professional. Web Analytics, Social Media & Conversion Optimisation enthusiast.

Why am I passionate about SEO? Why do I want to pursue this industry? I believe it is the foundation in comprehending search behaviour targeting.

As much as everyone knows their own business, by thoroughly understanding your: (1) Target Market, (2) Competitors, (3) The difference between being where you are now and how to get on page one (Gap analysis), (4) The ability to correctly optimise and execute SEO strategy, whilst providing unique quality content; you can develop a strategy which could not only deliver results via traffic generation and potentially higher conversion rates, but with a good user experience.

Ideal Postion? SEO/ Search Analyst or Consultant

Ideal Company? An established Search agency/company who are result driven and value contribution from employees at all levels.

Environment? Challenging and competitive internally, however working towards the same goal. Supportive on training for latest trends.

My ultimate career goal? To dominate in all aspects of commercial SEO by two years and reach to management within three. I would also like to learn and implement other aspects of online marketing i.e. social media, analytics to complement my SEO skills, hence strengthen my ability to produce great results.
