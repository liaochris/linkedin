Derek Lyman’s Summary

I studied at the University of Massachusetts where I focused on complex organizations and informational systems. While a student, I co-founded Dexrex LLC, an information technology company providing innovative communications data management software and services. I was Chief Executive Officer at the company for over three years. While in this position, Mr. Lyman has led the company from the lab to the market. He is actively involved in University entrepreneurship programs where he has received accolade from the local investment community for his leadership and support of young businesses. Looking forward, Derek’s primary focus at Dexrex is to manage the company’s long term market strategy and organizational growth.
