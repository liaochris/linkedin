Jon Tucker’s Summary

We live in exciting times...

I have been involved in interactive marketing since my high school years and have worked with both Fortune 100 companies and small businesses around the world. I've been fortunate enough to be in the middle of a rapidly changing consumer technology environment.

Mobile phones like the iPhone and Android smartphone have changed so many things. Not only has the way businesses reach consumers changed, but smartphones have changed the very way that consumers go about their day to day lives.

One of the areas of drastic change is in retail shopping, with Target and Walmart innovating along with a range of high-growth startups. However, Target and Walmart have become a threat to the grocery industry with their constant innovation coupled with a big push into the grocery product category in recent years.

Through my experience in interactive marketing technologies, the fortune of being guided by some of the smartest minds in retail, and the network of passionate technology geniuses throughout the world that I'm fortunate enough to call friends, our team has engineered a way for the grocery industry to compete with Target and Walmart and reclaim the market share that's been lost in recent years, all while delivering the most innovative and useful shopping experience consumers will see in 2011 and beyond.

This shopping experience will be available in Q2 of 2011 with select grocery retailers around the country.

Stayed tuned, reach out to us, and be excited.

Jon Tucker’s Specialties:

Interactive marketing technology, mobile phone software and apps (iPhone apps, Android, Blackberry, etc.), internet marketing, marketing analytics, conversion optimization, etc.
