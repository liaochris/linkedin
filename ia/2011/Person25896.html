Her core beliefs are that we all have choices in life. Choosing to think big, work with like minded people, be available to her family, give to others and have an abundance of wealth have all been possible due to the success of “Life Choices Now”.

When she created “Life Choices Now” she was looking for a lifestyle: time to truly raise her kids; be there on sports day and attend their after school activities and growing needs. Choosing to be an entrepreneur and choosing to be at cause and not at the effect of life is simple when you apply certain principles”.

The array of personal development products, conferences and workshops available through “Life Choices Now” will teach you the secrets of successful entrepreneurs and visionaries. The entrepreneurial educational publications, DVD's, curriculums and empowering events allow regular people to achieve success, improve their competence, enhance their self confidence and gain self reliance.
