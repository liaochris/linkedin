Bram Willemse’s Summary

I am a junior communication consultant, freshly graduated in september 2010 in Communication Systems. I live and work in Groningen, Netherlands.

As a consultant I'm focusing on communication in it's broadest sense, with a love for online communication and passion for (technical) innovation. Important for me are: creating an honest picture and realizing effective interaction between different persons or groups of persons.

Need help with a website? I can help! I'm an experienced web designer, especially in designing and building themes for the WordPress CMS. Making use of my network of designers, programmers and writers, I'd like to help you out improving your website our creating one (or two..) from scratch.

I have a passion for music, film & games, gadgetry and cars and I am an active inline skater.

Visit my website (see above or below) for my (Dutch) blog, more information about me and to take a peek at my portfolio.

Please do not hesitate to contact me for any inqueries about me or my work.
