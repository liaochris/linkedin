Melese Michael’s Summary

Melese contributes to any organization a business and entrepreneurial style well beyond his years. He is a creative thinker and is not afraid to make decisive actions. Young, articulate, professional, and as good a listener as he is a speaker. As the founder of Bubble Bunnys.com and a current co-founder of the Nterxon Corporation, Melese demonstrates an uncanny ability to bridge the gap between older and younger generations with his enthusiastic yet ambitious personality. He has worked closely with his father on mechanical engineering patents filed by companies like the Nvidia Corporation and STEPcon Global and in 2008 Melese became President of North America for VIZnMETA4 LLC.

Melese is a 2007 graduate of the National Blue Ribbon High School, Monte Vista. He was a representative delegate for the city of Antioch California for its Sister City Exchange Program with Chichibu, Japan. His many travels to Asia, Europe and throughout the North American continent, compounded with in depth studies in the field of business management have developed him into a resourceful entrepreneur and role model.
