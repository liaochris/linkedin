christine gibson’s Summary

My work career truly began in my mid-teens, determined to earn my way through college without borrowing, get a 4 year degree and make my parents proud of my work ethic. In my 4th year in college, I was offered a position as an Illustrator II with E-Systems, a contractor to the Dept. of Defense, in Warren, MI.

I greatly enjoyed 6+ years at TACOM designing, publishing and creating with a diverse team of artists, photographers and videographers working together to meet the diverse requirements of TACOM for art, exhibits, publications & presentation graphics.

In 1988, I purchased a Mac SE and Laserwriter, became one of Detroit's first PageMaker users, as well as owner/operator of Fast Forward Type & Graphics, which served me well as a full time wife and eventually, mom to five wonderful children. Our business provided desk top publishing, writing, editing and graphic art for area clients including local news and publishing agencies. I also produced, marketed and edited a monthly national tax tip newsletter for home business owners from 1992-1995 entitled, Moms, Inc.

In 1998, I began to dream about returning to my original passion - illustration. After selecting children's illustration as a star to reach for, I completed a course and portfolio for the children's market, began marketing, and entered a 5 year period of illustrating children's books and materials for companies like Modern Publishing and Highlights For Children. I provided b/w and color original illustrations and licensed art, for characters such as Little People, Rescue Heroes and Clifford the Big Red Dog. I eventually illustrated 5 books through various publishers.

Currently, I am seeking to re-enter the local publishing and graphic industry. I also would like to utilize skills gained from business ownership and years of volunteering, and interact with others on a team who work together to solve whatever challenges the day presents with a goal of helping each person around me succeed.
