Jeb Blount’s Summary

Jeb Blount is the CEO of SalesGravy.com, author of the #1 bestseller, "People Buy You" and considered one of the leading authorities on Sales and Sales Leadership.

SalesGravy.com the world's portal to all things sales and is the most popular Sales Content website on the internet (source: Alexa.com, Compete.com, Quantcast.com, Google Benchmark). Often referred to by its members as the "facebook of the sales profession" SalesGravy.com offers Sales Professionals access to thousands of hot sales jobs, business networking tools, thousands of articles, blogs, videos, audio programs, and sales training materials.

Sales Gravy helps employers and recruiters "Find Better Salespeople" offering a world-class array of Sales Talent Sourcing Tools including career advertising, pay-for-performace recruiting solutions, employer branding, matching and accessment tools, virtual job fairs and on-demand video interviewing.

In 2007 Jeb founded Sales Gravy Press a traditional independent publishing house focused exclusively on sales and entrepreneurial books. With more than 20 books in print SGP has published books by Mike Brooks, Jill Konrath and Hank Trisler.

In October 2008 Jeb launched the Reach Sales Advertising Network. Reach Sales is a network of 45 Sales Specific websites linked together and served by IAB certified ad servers. It serves in excess of 4 million ad impressions each month.

Jeb is also the author of "PowerPrinciples" and "7 Rules for Outselling the Recession" and a regular speaker to sales and business organizations. His powerful, inspiring messages and non-stop energy, keep audiences on the edge of their seats and asking for more. He has a passion for growing people and the unique ability to see potential in everyone. Over the span of his career he has coached, trained, and developed hundreds of Sales Professionals, managers and leaders. His sales audio programs have been downloaded more than 4 million times.
