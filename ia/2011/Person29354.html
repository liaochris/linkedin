Mark Bushey's Experience

Partner

IndustrialParkOnline.com Inc.

Information Technology and Services industry

January 2010 – Present (1 year 3 months)

IndustrialParkOnline was formed out of a need for companies to be able to simplify the process in procuring all their technological and communications requirements. With that, our idea of forming a 'collective' of individually competent and relevant companies and gather them together in a virtual location where we could easily provide an aggregation of services became the basis for starting IndustrialParkOnLine. IPO allows the principal owners to oversee the entire project without being actively involved and keeps the points of contact to a minimum. In a sense, we have created a one stop way of securing all your IT/communications needs. The great thing about this model is that the individual partner companies are strengthened by the capabilities of the other partners or 'the group'. The group itself is strengthened by the addition of each individual company's capabilities. This simplification is most apparent to the client, who will not see most of the back-end logistics and company combinations that were built specifically for their individual need. They will have a singular point of contact throughout the project no matter what the size. For business owners, we have made the complicated simple.
