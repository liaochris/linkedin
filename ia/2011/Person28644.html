MARTY FAIERSTAIN's Summary

If there’s one word that best captures the business story of MARTY FAIERSTAIN, it is determination. A self-made entrepreneur, Marty has built companies through sheer tenacity and uncompromising attention to detail. Known for his entrepreneurial flair and versatility, he excels in sales, marketing, merchandising, retail distribution and technology to provide strategic and visionary direction to several successful businesses. He is the President of the Licensed Sports Division at Optimum Fulfillment and Founder and Co-Owner of Sports Line Distributors.

Like many successful entrepreneurs, Marty’s extensive expertise in sales and marketing comes through hard-fought, real world business experience as opposed to formal academic training. “I’m a tireless worker,” he declares. “I have learned from mistakes and risen above challenge and adversity.” From humble beginnings in business as a video games distributor, Marty gravitated toward sales and discovered the relationship building techniques that fueled his entrepreneurial vision and provided an entrée to Sports Line Distributors in 1987. From its humble beginnings as a home-based business, the firm grew into a successful wholesale distributorship with reach into a variety of profitable industries. In 2010, Marty assumed the leadership role in Optimum Fulfillment and brings a wealth of sports merchandise marketing expertise into the venture.

Featured in numerous articles for industry trade journals, Marty is gifted at spotting trends in the wholesale distribution business. He is adept at placing products in the right light for sale. He understands the dynamics of how licensed sports merchandise fares in major metropolitan areas, studies consumer preferences and paces strategy with the shifting profitability in the licensed sports and entertainment marketplace. A natural mentor, Marty also believes in giving back. “I like to motivate by example,” he says. “I enjoy helping other people reach their business goals.”

MARTY FAIERSTAIN's Experience

President, Licensed Sports Division

Optimum Fulfillment

Sports industry

September 2010 – Present (7 months)

PROVIDING OUR PARTNERS WITH VALUE IN INCREMENTAL SALES AND A PREMIUM SERVICE TO ENHANCE THEIR ONLINE PRESENCE

Optimum Fulfillment is an Ottawa, Illinois-based E-Commerce solutions provider for product manufacturers who seek to outsource the sales and distribution areas of their merchandise line(s). Differentiated by its highly elastic technology platform, the firm worked in concert with Microsoft for over two years to construct a system geared toward ease of operation and offers a scalable, high-tech advantage for large corporations with multiple brands.

After 23 years of experience in leading a large manufacturing and distribution enterprise he built from scratch, Marty now provides strategic direction to Optimum Fulfillment. The firm is focused on the acquisition of manufacturers of licensed sports merchandise (MLB, NFL, NHL, NBA, NCAA, etc.) and offers all-inclusive, no-risk business development channels for online retailing.

Leveraging his expertise in the E-Commerce business model, Marty implements solutions that support the sales functionality of a huge catalog of items and create exceptional visibility of products without the associated hassles. “We assume all the inventory risk,” he claims. “We establish the distribution center and the automation. We know where the growth is measured and what products will sell. You can instantly fill your store. We provide the setup then pick, pack and ship.”

BRILLIANCE IN BOXES is a subsidiary of Optimum Fulfillment that provides customized distribution and fulfillment services for E-Commerce retailers at their SKU threshold or those that have limited bandwidth in managing additional vendors

Founder, Co-Owner and Shareholder

SportsLine Distributors, Inc.

Sports industry

1987 – Present (24 years)

Starting with $500 and a dream, Marty and a partner started a humble video game (Nintendo, Sega) distribution business in their shared apartment and grew it into a thriving enterprise that accounted for in excess of $250 million in sales. Marty became the guiding light of the operation, cultivating key customer relationships and orchestrating the expansion of the company.

In 1990, SportsLine entered the highly profitable trading card marketplace and, ultimately, secured placements with top grocery, convenient and drug stores for its line of cards. Concurrently, SportsLine developed a licensed sports merchandise program specifically designed for the firm's distribution channels. The company also introduced a line of fishing products and began to tap international markets – most notably, Europe and China.

In 2004, Marty actively marketed a comprehensive licensed sports collectible program that included Major League Baseball (MLB), the National Basketball Association (NBA) and the National Hockey League (NHL), which eventually propelled SportsLine to the industry leader in the distribution of such merchandise. To support this expansion, Marty negotiated retail arrangements with Walgreens, 7-11, Jewel, Dominick’s, Stop & Shop, A & P and Gulf Oil among others.

Marty’s interaction with vendors, alliances, and customers was a tour de force of relationship building and catalyzed over two decades of business growth.
