Jessica Lawrence’s Summary

Research Scientist with a background in Entomology and Botany. Research is currently focused on ecotoxicology (primarily in honey bees), consumer products, urban entomology, greenhouse testing, and agricultural pests. Rearing experience with bedbugs, honey bees, aphids, mites, tobacco splitworm, Sasajiscymnus tsugae and various other arthropods. Familiar with current software, including ARM and Excel. Have experience writing protocols, editing protocols, and writing Standard Operating Procedures. Have worked with several pest species of mosquitoes, flies, spiders, roaches, ants, ticks, mites, ladybugs, bedbugs, aphids, fleas and stored grain pests.
