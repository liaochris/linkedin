Asad Dhunna's Summary

Having graduated in German & Business Studies from Warwick University, I am currently a Junior Consultant at IncrediBull, an award winning brand communications company. I work across the Digital, Events and PR teams to provide top class marketing communications solutions to a number of high profile clients.

Asad Dhunna's Experience

Junior Consultant

IncrediBull

Privately Held; Media Production industry

July 2010 – Present (8 months)

I work across the PR, Events & Comms teams at IncrediBull. PR clients include Glasses Direct, BT and Bayer Healthcare. I assist across these teams in strategy implementation; securing press coverage, which involves writing press releases, identifying potential leads and building relationships with journalists. Coverage thus far has featured in numerous titles such as The Evening Standard, with highlights including double page spreads in The Times and the Metro.

In terms of events, I have assisted in the organisation of two media mornings, dealing with venue booking, materials preparation and logistics on the day. I have also part-managed events for Nokia Academy's management team, which included hotel liaison & negotiation, logistics planning and onsite management.

Communications-wise, I am a part of the Nokia Academy team, assisting in the communications planning and implementation. This includes sourcing content for newsletters across Europe as well as generating ideas on how to develop communication between a Europe-wide team.

I also manage the Twitter feed for IncrediBull, with the aim to increase brand awareness of our creative and fantastic agency.
