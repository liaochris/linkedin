Kimberlee Cloutier-Blazzard, Ph.D.’s Summary

Kimberlee specializes in the history of Northern European Art of the Renaissance and Baroque periods. She teaches at various colleges in the Boston area, gives public lectures and has published articles, reviews and exhibition catalogue entries. In her scholarly writing, she typically focuses upon comedy in Early Modern art, particularly its social implications.

Kimberlee Cloutier-Blazzard, Ph.D.’s Specialties:

writing, research, public speaking, visual analysis, teaching, copy editing
