Kamal Tahir’s Summary

I am a strategist focused on execution and a sweet spot at the juncture of innovation, information and implementation. I thrive in challenging environments and excel at transforming strategy into actionable steps and tangible results. Having lived in four very different countries I have a cultural sensitivity and agility to adapt to different environments.

I tend to see concepts, themes, and trends from multiple perspectives which in turn allow me to draw parallels, evaluate scenarios, and define effective actions for optimal results. This is partially a result of my work experience which includes strategy and technology consulting, strategic marketing, program management, and global product launches in several industries. Some of it comes from growing up in 4 very different countries, and studying across Europe, USA and Asia. Most of it however, comes from the excitement of discovery, and pride of leadership to materialize an idea into a winning solution.
