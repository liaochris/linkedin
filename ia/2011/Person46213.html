Jimmy Anderson's Summary

XpressPrint was recognized as the 2nd Fastest-Growing Up-And-Coming Print Company nationwide in 2008 amongst peers with less than $5 Million Annual Gross sales.

Since inception, XpressPrint has been a key contributor to the printing industry by helping develop proprietary web-based print order management systems - also known as Web-2-Print. Additionally, XpressPrint has been developing key custom branded print order management systems for major franchise chains, direct sales organizations and large corporations with many field offices providing a single location for branded marketing materials and the ability for distributors and/or reps to personalize their marketing materials while processing the order conveniently from within their own back-office.

Because we're able to increase efficiency and automate the majority of the ordering process, including some pre-press work, we're able to offer more competitive cost savings, faster turnarounds and consistent quality.

XpressPrint can print & ship nationwide from any one of our five partner distribution facilities within the same 3 - 5 business day and/or next business day turnarounds, and have a package just about anywhere in the continental United States in 2 business days thereafter.
