Matt Miller's Summary

MobileDemand, President & Founder
Matt Miller founded MobileDemand in 2003, after more than 15 years of experience in the mobile computing industry.

Now established in numerous verticals, including manufacturing and distribution, warehousing, consumer goods, field service, transportation and logistics, retail, government/defense, EMS and agriculture, among many others. Customers include Anheuser-Busch, General Motors, Walt Disney World Parks & Resorts, NASA, Pfizer, Pioneer Hybrid, Virgin America and the Federal Emergency Management Agency.

MobileDemand is poised for further growth in the coming years. With Millers’ leadership, MobileDemand ranked 148 on the 2008 Inc. 500 list of the country’s fasted growing private companies. The company also obtained Microsoft’s elite Gold Certified Partner status – an honor only the top three percent of all Microsoft partners achieve. Recently MobileDemand and Miller have garnered multiple awards. The Corridor Business Journal named Miller 2008 Entrepreneur of the Year, and the Iowa Technology Association named MobileDemand 2007 Technology Company of the Year.

MobileDemand and Miller have been featured in numerous business and industry publications, including Engadget, GottaBeMobile, The Future of Things, Rugged PC Review, Wireless Workforce Online, The Cedar Rapids Gazette and EdgeBusiness Magazine, among others. Google featured Miller and MobileDemand in a Google AdWords Success Story video.

Miller received his Bachelor’s of Science degree in Computer Science from the University of Iowa in 1990. He obtained his MBA with emphasis in marketing in 1991. He has held previous positions with Procter & Gamble and Norand, now Intermec Technologies.

Miller actively volunteers in an e-Marketing role for the Cedar Rapids Area Catholic Schools and serves on the Board of Directors for the Technology Association of Iowa. He also coaches an 8 and under boys baseball team.
