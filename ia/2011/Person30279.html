Michael Sick's Summary

Senior level marketer and business strategist has grown many well known brands including Arby's, Pearle Vision, Jack In The Box, Burger King, Pizza Hut and others.

My strong financial analysis abilities combined with a knack for developing creative alternatives has enabled me to provide measureable results for not only large companies but also smaller companies that I have consulted with in the software, pet supply, financial services, motion picture and publishing industries.

Recent clients include pet supply manufacturers, restaurants, the Army Reserve Family Programs Office, sales trainers, eCommerce companies, sporting goods, investors, distributors and others. I help clients position, brand and launch. I integrate online and offline strategies. Most of my time I spend with clients who have retained me to serve as their part time CMO with many relationships spanning multiple years. I also perform assessments for clients who are looking for an outside opinion to validate what they are doing and suggest alternatives to improve their results.

A prolific writer, Mike regular writes newsletters, business plans, web content, articles, blog posts and other online and offline content.

Specialties

My expertise is in growing brands and companies through development of new products, advertising, media, promotions and positioning. New product launch expert.
