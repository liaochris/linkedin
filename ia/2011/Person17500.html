Marcus Lim's Summary

I make history...literally.

For the last decade, my work has helped many famillies and corporations to retain their own heritage through my paintings. These paintings not only help to retain stories about their own lives that are important to the people around them, they have also become a prized legacy for their future generations.

These are their stories, immortalized for all eternity.
