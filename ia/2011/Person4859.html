Ben Landers’s Summary

As President, Ben brings over 10 years of digital marketing experience and sales leadership to Blue Corona and is responsible for developing its technology platform, tools and strategic partnerships and for helping to develop and execute the company's strategic vision. Ben oversees the day-to-day operations of the company and is focused on growing the company to be one of the leading Internet marketing and tracking solution providers in the United States.

Ben began his career during the dot-com era as an early employee at HotJobs.com (now a Yahoo! Company).. Ben helped HotJobs.com launch and grow its Midwest Sales office in Chicago, IL. Ben left HotJobs.com to become a Major Account Executive with WorldCom prior to WorldCom's accounting fiasco and then spent several years as the Regional Sales and Marketing Manager for Windy City Sports and Gen-A, an advertising agency based in Chicago. Ben ran Windy City's DC office and DC area properties and sold multi-media advertising packages to clients ranging from Cannondale Bicycle Company to WL Gore and the Marine Corp Marathon.

Prior to starting Blue Corona, Ben spent five years as the Vice President of Sales and Marketing of DrinkMore Water, an Inc. 500 regional bottled water company located in Gaithersburg, MD. Under Ben's leadership, DrinkMore Water embraced the digital revolution by launching several several new websites and an online ecommerce store. Today, SEO and Pay Per Click continue to be DrinkMore Water's top sources of new business.

Ben holds a BS in Communications and Marketing from Ohio University, is a Google AdWords Professional and a member of the Web Analytics Association.
