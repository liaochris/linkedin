Cheryl Rainey, MBA, PhD's Summary

Cheryl A. Rainey, MBA, PhD
Goals: Discovering Strengths & Resources
www.DrCheri.net
-Licensed Psychotherapist for Individuals, Couples, and Families: As a licensed psychotherapist, Cheryl Rainey, Ph.D., treats clients suffering from trauma, grief, anxiety, fear, sleep disorders, depression, low self-esteem, relationship issues, and disturbing life events. Without the use of anti-depressants or lengthy therapy, she facilitates breaking through barriers to produce immediate, natural, long-lasting relief.
-International Business Consultant: As an international business consultant, she also works inside organizations to uncover individuals’ blind spots, help them modify stress behaviors, and develop leadership strengths, as well as improve attitudes, work satisfaction, performance, and teamwork.

Dr. Rainey holds an MBA from Emory University and her doctorate in Marriage and Family Therapy from Florida State University. A prolific writer for trade publications and the author of How to Hire and Keep a Winning Team, she has presented her expertise in front of crowds as large as 2,000.

Now, along with the principles and practical applications that have made her personal breakthrough programs so successful, she’s packing her most compelling case studies from her clinical work and leadership coaching sessions into a new book: Take Back Your Life! On the Path of Least Resistance.

Specialties

Cheryl A. Rainey, MBA, PhD focuses on breaking through barriers to discover natural strengths & resources, as a Licensed Psychotherapist for Individuals, Relationships & Families and as an International Business Consultant for Leaders and Teams.

Dr. Rainey provides evidence-based care, is an EMDR Certified Therapist, and brings more than 20 years of experience in Leadership & Team Development.
