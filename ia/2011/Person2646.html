ImpactEdge Consulting Services

Neville & Rowe provide a wide range of professional consultancy services to help you deploy your business intelligence solution in record time. Our experienced team of BI consultants work to industry best-practices to ensure you get the best results every time.

Our consulting services start from an initial installation right through to full software development and implementation. We aim to keep our consulting projects short to provide you with assistance at the most relevant stage of your implementation. Our projects are delivered by experienced Neville & Rowe consultants that fully comprehend how to deliver a successful business intelligence implementation as well as how to ensure you get the most from ImpactEdge.
