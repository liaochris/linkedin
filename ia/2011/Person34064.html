Paul Allen's Summary

Paul Allen is Chairman and CEO of the Project Executive Group, a Houston-based firm providing advisory and execution services. Paul has over thirty years in project development, project management & structured project finance.

Paul served as an adjunct full professor of management at the Jones Graduate School of Management - Rice University in the Executive Education and MBA programs from 1997 - 2008.

Specialties

Specializes in strategic project development, structured project finance, risk and decision management, project management, and troubled project turnarounds in the oil & gas, power, infrastructure and engineering & construction industries.
