    APTEC Lebanon

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    January 2011 — Present (2 months)

    APTEC is a leading technology distributor with a clear vision of providing resellers in the Middle East, Turkey and Africa with a one-stop-shop for a complete range of leading IT and telecommunications products and services.

    The group distributes a wide range of products covering Services, Networking, Software, Hardware, PC Components and Telecommunication products from the world’s leading vendors in each segment. More than 400 employees and 10 subsidiaries and locations in the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Egypt, Lebanon and Turkey ensure speedy, effective and professional service to resellers throughout the region.

    Aptec plays a vital role in the value chain, linking manufacturers of technology to thousands of resellers covering wide geographical territories. The company creates sales and profit opportunities for vendors and resellers through unique marketing programs, advanced logistics services, technical support, consultancy, high-level technical consulting and outsourcing services, financial services and product delivery and distribution.

    APTEC's regional geographical coverage, extensive customer base and its value added services made the group the largest and fastest growing distributor in the region. The group’s automated processes link its suppliers and resellers through unique systems developed for this purpose.

    The group comprises a number of specialist companies to ensure a clear market segment focus. APTEC provides a one-stop-shop of hardware, software, peripherals and networking products; TDME is a distributor of Enterprise solutions working with partners like Cisco, Oracle and a range of high end server and storage products. AKS is a specialist in Kiosks and self service systems; ATS is an IT consultancy serving vendors and channel partners in the region.
