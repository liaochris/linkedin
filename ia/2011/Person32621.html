Nick Dawson’s Summary

Nick Dawson thinks big about healthcare. With more than 12 years experience in hospital operations, he understands what makes healthcare tick. He has an unyielding passion for the patient experience and believes when it is a strategic goal, revenue, clinical outcomes and excellence follow. Nick's strengths include observing trends from other industries, technology, consumers and applying those concepts to the business of healthcare.

Currently, Nick leads health 2.0 initiatives, including a meaningful use compliant regional EMR offering for non-imployed physicians for Bon Secours Health System's largest market.

As the champion of a social media success story, Nick pioneered the role of Director Community Engagement at Bon Secours Richmond Health System. In addition to those duties he served as Director on the Revenue Cycle team for a 14 hospital Bon Secours system. In the past, he has worked for other multi-hospital systems and been a consultant on staff for a major health IT and strategy vendor.

Nick is a member of the Healthcare Financial Management Association and can frequently be found posting about healthcare and cooking on his blog, twitter and Flickr. He speaks nationally and has been published on the topic of community engagement and patient experience.
