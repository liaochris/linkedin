Angus Logan's Summary

I run a partner marketing team focusing on driving adoption of Windows Live and partner offerings. I work with device manufacturers, mobile operators, service providers, and top web sites.

Prior to this I was the technical product manager for the Windows Live partner offers including but not limited to Messenger Connect, Hotmail Active Views, and Windows Live ID. During this role I participated in the development and championing of emerging standards and specifications within Microsoft.

I started my career was a professional developer focusing on Microsoft technologies such as ASP.NET, Content Management Server, and SharePoint technologies. Later I joined Microsoft in a national technical presales role for enterprise customers.

Specialties

Social media, online advertising, Microsoft technologies, online services delivery, empowering swivel chair users.
