The Passion Test for Business

As well as my personal coaching practise (see below) I also offer The Passion Test for Business with my partner Beatrice Buchser.

As a Consultant for The Passion Test for Business we focus on defining the Company's Unique Contribution - it's true purpose - and the Company Passions - what is necessary to deliver that Unique Contribution.

So often, once a business is successful it is so easy to lose sight of why it was started in the first place. Work becomes repetitive and without true purpose and many employees, and sometimes the bosses, become disengaged with what they do.

The Passion Test for Business is about turning that situation around, re-engaging everyone in the true purpose of the company and thus increasing the bottom line.

There is also a Passion Test for Business for Solo Entrepreneurs, specializing in identifying the clients unique contribution and ensuring that they are focusing all their efforts in the right direction. Once they have that information they can assess new opportunities when they arise with the confidence of knowing exactly where their company passions lie and how to follow them.

The Passion Test for Business
Enhancing profit through contagious engagement

In a Gallup poll in the USA in 2009 it was shown that only 29% of employees are engaged in what they do. Engaged employees work with passion and feel a profound connection to their company. They drive innovation and move the organization forward. It is this 29% that goes the extra mile, actively comes up with new ideas or solutions for problems. The majority - 71% - are disengaged or actively disengaged. Disengaged employees are essentially “checked out.” They’re sleepwalking through their workday, putting time – but not energy or passion – into their work. Actively disengaged employees aren’t just unhappy at work: they’re busy acting out their unhappiness. Every day, these workers undermine what their engaged co-workers accomplish. These 71% can be costing your company up to £10,000 a year.

The Passion Test for Business is about turning those statistics upside down. What distinguishes the Passion Test for Business program is that we assist companies in identifying their unique contribution to its customer, clients and to the world. We then go on to identify the company passions, and after a training wiht the employees we draw up
a Cultural Action Plan outlining action steps to implement the company's passions.

When everyone in the company, from senior management to the new trainee, is engaged in the company’s unique contribution and passions, then not only is everyone happier and more productive at work, but the bottom line improves.

There is also a Passion Test for Business for solo entrepreneurs, specializing in identifying your unique contribution and work passions. Once you have that information you can assess new opportunities when they arise with the confidence of knowing exactly where your work passions lie and how to implement them, hence improving your profits.
