Saahil Goel, PMP’s Summary

Saahil Goel is a Consultant with SDLC Partners, LP in the Pittsburgh area.

He has an MBA and a MS-MIS degree from the Joseph M. Katz Graduate School of Business, University of Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, USA.

In the past, Saahil has extensive experience with Identity and Access Management Systems, Role Based Access Control and Identity Workflow Engine, LDAP and Single Sign On. He has also worked as a Business Analyst for Cash Management System implementations.

More recently, he has worked as a founding team member and consultant for Kasper Consulting Pvt. Ltd. (India) where he still holds a part-time position.

Other experience include Wal-Mart Stores Inc. at their headquarters in the Enterprise Risk Management division as a Business Continuity Planner and Bayer Corporation- Demand-driven Service Level project to enable cost rationalization.
