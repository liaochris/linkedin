Juri Bendini, (Open Networker)'s Summary

Since the beginning of 2006 I started working as European Field Service Engineer for the Laerdal Europe TechCenter located in Munich and then as Technical Manager & Educational Consultant for Laerdal Italy. As Technical Manager I’m responsible of all Preventive Maintenance contracts about Laerdal-Philips monitor defibrillators, patient simulators and the After Sales & Services in general. I’m also responsible for the
development and implementation of Simulation Centers.

Specialties

Since the beginning of my career in Laerdal, I was also involved in Marketing Assistance, supporting the Sales Force about all the technical features of Laerdal’s products and Philips’ defibrillators. Now, I’m mainly involved in the Training & Educational Service, training customers about how to use micro and macro simulators.
