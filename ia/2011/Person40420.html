Simon Campbell’s Summary

Simon has recently founded The Sandpit; an exciting new business aimed at finding great technology products, specifically in the sales and marketing space, and taking them to market in the UK and the US. The first vertical is SEO Sandpit, based around an excellent content platform. More to come on this soon!

Simon is a multilingual entrepreneur who has set up and built five businesses over the past decade. Based in Barcelona for 8 years, Simon founded and launched successful companies into the IT, advertising, publishing and fashion sectors in both Spain and the UK, before moving back to London and into the postal sector. His key skills are based in fundraising, commercial and strategic business development.

Most recently, prior to The Sandpit, Simon built ViaPost from initial business plan through launch and into growth stage in three years as CEO, along the way raising around £2M of capital from private investors and putting the business on the map by creating a strong brand, key strategic partnerships (such as Microsoft) and numerous sales into large and small businesses.

Having managed the restructure of the business in Summer 2009 which saw final stage capital to see the business through to breakeven and the appointment of a successful entrepreneur (Scott Fletcher) as Chairman, Simon stepped down as CEO in April 2010 and took up a Non-Executive role enabling him to focus on the more strategic opportunities for ViaPost both in the UK and internationally, with Scott moving to Executive Chairman.

Simon was named in the top 100 Entrepreneurs in the Courvoisier/Guardian Future 500 List in 2008.
