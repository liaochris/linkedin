Heather Cupp [LION]'s Summary

Heather Cupp is a HR Specialist-Recruiter for Wolters Search Group- Management Recruiters International, where she develops innovative strategies that lead to staffing solutions for nationally acclaimed Healthcare Systems. Hired in December of 2009, Heather leverages her proven experience in sourcing and placing qualified candidates for the Clinical Information Systems, Project Management, Health Information Management, Revenue/Compliance, Programmer/Analyst, PACS/RIS Administrators, Hospital CPOE Systems Implementation. Heather is a passionate advocate for social networking, social sourcing and innovative recruiting strategies to recruit top talent for National Healthcare Systems.

Prior to joining Wolters Search Group, Heather’s career focus was Corporate Restaurant sales and sales management. Her 12 years in sales includes senior manager responsibility to grow a $32 million LOB while overseeing a diverse group of sales and operations management personnel. This experience has given her the foresight to understand and relate to today’s human resource needs.

Heather attended The University of Tulsa, majoring in Political Science and minoring in Communications. She has an Associate of Arts Degree in International Business with a Certificate in Chinese and an Associate of Science Degree in Political Science from Tulsa Community College. She has studied six foreign languages both in the classroom and abroad: Chinese, Japanese, French, Spanish, Russian and English.

She is a veteran of the United States Navy, where she graduated “A” School at the top of her class. She was chosen for a staff billet serving Commander Submarine Group Seventh Fleet in Yokosuka, Japan. She maintained a top-secret clearance providing voice, IP and satellite communications and intelligence support for SEVENTH Fleet. Heather’s military service provided her the opportunity to travel internationally, learning early on about intercultural diversity and communication.
