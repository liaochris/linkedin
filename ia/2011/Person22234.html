Jon Stevens’s Summary

An experienced, highly security-cleared, flexible and professional Chartered Engineer, with indepth knowledge and experience of the MoD, its strategies, policies and programmes. Future looking, embracing change and understanding organisational culture and the technological opportunities that exist for business process automation as key enablers in meeting business objectives.

KEY STRENGTHS
Management of many simultaneous high profile and complex information technology projects both in well found (UK) and in expeditionary locations (Arabian Gulf, Balkans, Falklands).

The innate ability to apply knowledge and experience to complex problems in familiar and unfamiliar situations, using innovation to provide unique plans to address planned and unplanned events.

Extremely self-reliant and adept at working to tight, demanding and changeable deadlines, under considerable pressure, producing high-quality and timely results and communicating these concisely and in the most appropriate way at all levels, including executive level.

Understanding the application and development of technology across a broad array of subject areas and to understand its impact on an organisation and its culture. Adept at understanding new concepts and equipment and exploiting them at short notice to the benefit of the organisation

An enthusiastic leader of both large and small teams with a proven ability to motivate and manage staff. Effective as leader of a team or a dedicated team member.

Proven financial management skills; planning and managing the expenditure of multi-million pound budgets, delivering value for money and implementing cost saving measures, as appropriate.
