Dan Gilliland's Summary

In my new role as Solutions Consultant at NetSuite, I am responsible for meeting quarterly and annual quota objectives working in tandem with the Sales organization. As a member of this team, I add value throughout the sales process in the areas of requirements analysis, solution development, in-depth product demonstrations, and facilitating the transition professional services.

I have over 25 years in technology including 6 years in Solutions Engineering for companies like Adobe and Instantis, and 10 years in Product Management for Sun Microsystems and Micro Focus Inc. I was Senior Product Manager for Sun’s Forte for Java Product Line and for Micro Focus’s flagship Micro Focus Workbench. I have experience managing large scale new product development projects spanning multiple geographies, languages and cultures.

My early experience includes systems analysis and financial systems programming in industries ranging from telephony to manufacturing. I studied Computer Science at the University of Kansas until I dropped out to experience adventure by hitchhiking through the Southeast US.
