Peter Tahmin’s Summary

I joined Ritz Interactive Inc. on April 1, 1999, as Vice President and Chief Operating Officer and a founding member of Ritz Interactive Inc. Responsibilities include IT, Customer Service and Distribution and Fulfillment.

Prior to joining Ritz Interactive Inc. I held the position of Director of Digital Services Integration for Eastman Kodak’s Qualex Division. Qualex is the world’s largest photofinishing company. In this role, I was responsible for the introduction of Kodak’s Picture CD, Picture Disk and the joint Kodak/America On Line program called You’ve Got Pictures!, into the Qualex organization. During my stay at Qualex, I designed, developed and launched www.Kodaklabs.com, Eastman Kodak’s first on line digital imaging Internet service.

I was with Qualex for six years through Q1 1999.

Over the past 20 years, I have specialized in launching or restructuring niche photographic. digital imaging and ecommerce businesses. These include the opening of the Washington DC bureau of Chroma Copy an international photographic and display business, Digix Imaging the world’s largest Photo CD and Digital scanning facility for Qualex , Colorfax Labs and now Ritz Interactive Inc.

I have been in the photography industry literally all of my life, professionally since 1981 and in ecommerce since 1995.

I am a graduate of Pace University’s Business School in Westchester New York, where I earned a BA degree in Marketing.
