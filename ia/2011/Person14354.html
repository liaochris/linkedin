Eva Velasco's Summary

Extensive experience in product management, brand development and key account management for pharmaceutical and professionally driven consumer brands. Broad international experience, my assignments have taken me from my native Spain to Europe and the United States, where I worked for six years as Global Marketing Manager for Alcon Laboratories driving the strategy and implementation for my brands around the world: pricing, patient modeling, market research, KOL development, CME and marketing programs to drive demand.
