Jim N. Craig, MBA's Summary

More than 18 years of marketing experience, including 15 years in financial services and 11 years in management positions. Have done just about every type of marketing, from event and sponsorship to product and promotional, through just about every channel, from print and TV to direct mail and the internet. Still pushing the boundaries and enlarging the role of Marketing in the corporate environment, I am leading the charge to develop a more meaningful customer experience and developing more emotionally-relevant marketing messages.

Specialties

Branding and identity, strategic planning, strategic marketing, retail marketing, financial services marketing, emotional branding, web marketing, promotional marketing, sponsorship marketing, business development, branch location selection, market research, direct marketing, customer relationship management
