Jim Palam’s Summary

At Jim Palam & Partners we have always believed that intelligence and simplicity are the keys to effective communication.

We believe that our relationships with our clients must be built on mutual respect – and a respect for the audiences our communications target.

We believe that advertising and design budgets are investments, not expenses.

To quote a Japanese proverb, we believe that "None of us are as smart as all of us."
