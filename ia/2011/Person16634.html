Gregory Taylor's Summary

Greg is a dedicated professional whose clients' needs are his first priority. His determination to cater to clients specific needs, attention to detail and sharp negotiating skills allows them to receive first class service with utmost integrity.

Prior to his career in Real Estate, Greg was an executive in the music business. By combining his passion for Real Estate and Entertainment, he discovered a niche that has helped him become very successful with both sales and listings.

As an internet professional, Greg uses the newest technology to sell your home. With almost 80% of all buyers starting their search online, Greg uses the internet to position properties to sell in the shortest amount of time. His marketing plans are customized, with both traditional and modern methods of promoting the property. This translates to the most exposure possible for your home worldwide.

Whether it's a person looking for their first home or a company looking to do a condo conversion, Greg has the expertise and experience to get the job done.

His goal is to exceed your expectations. His warm, sincere yet professional business style will turn you into a client for life!
