Steven Elkins's Summary

I love finding the perfect words. (Although, there’s probably a better way to say that.)

My writing career spans a broad spectrum of media platforms and writing styles. I have a reputation for a robust use of language and a knack for infusing personality into both the printed and spoken word. I enjoy creating exciting Internet, audio-visual and print materials that inform, motivate and are emotionally moving.

My experience writing for Hollywood and my understanding of drama and irony have given me some powerful tools to wield in the world of marketing and corporate communications. I like to put a little spin on the ball, disarm with humor and upset expectations. I want to grab the audience up front and hold on tight. The same rule applies, regardless of whether your goal is to inform, to sell or to motivate: Always Be Memorable.

When my fingers are not on my computer they’re on my guitar. I compose and perform with the New Edge Ensemble, a Classical/Americana fusion trio. My music will soon be featured in the upcoming West Coast premiere of Rogue Artists Ensemble’s new show, “Hyperbole: Origins” at the Ford Theater in Hollywood.

I’m always on the lookout for an exciting, challenging opportunity. I invite you to exploit my talents as a writer, project manager and media producer in the fields of marketing, training and entertainment. Allow me to help you identify your message, tailor your message to your target audience and adapt your message to the medium that suits it best.

Let me bring your idea to life.

Specialties

Video production and post production; adult education; instructor training; on-camera and voice-over skills; music composition.
