Rohan Mann's Summary

Rohan is an avid Social Media enthusiast whose mantra is to focus on the word ‘Social’ which he himself practices both online and offline. Media- after all keeps changing, and that dynamism is the exciting bit for him.

An engineer by degree, (with a brief stint in Recruitment Consulting/Headhunting) and now a digital marketer, Rohan is currently working at XM Asia Pacific (A JWT Company) as a Social Media Strategist.

Previous to joining XM, Rohan was at Euro RSCG Singapore spearheading Social Media campaigns for the country’s largest supermarket chain, NTUC FairPrice and popular convenience store chain 'Cheers' on Facebook. He has also executed a successful campaign for DBS Bank, Singapore’s biggest bank through channels like Youtube, Twitter and Flickr. Besides, he worked with clients such as Microsoft Partner Network, RS Components and EA Games marketing them digitally both in Singapore and the Asia Pacific.

Over time, Rohan aims to conceptualize successful brand strategies and campaigns for corporates on social media platforms and fulfill his dream of backpacking across the Latin American continent before he turns 30.

In his free time, he is usually found exploring new eating joints as well as online networking platforms, backpacking with books or discussing Politics, Current Affairs, Films, and Philosophy.

Specialties

Social Media. Branding. Digital Marketing. Client Relationships. Communications. Strategy.
Not to forget- Ideas. Enthusiasm. Team Work.
