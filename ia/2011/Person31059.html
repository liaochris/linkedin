Mitch Gross’s Summary

Rocketed to Earth from a doomed planet, Mitch now lives among us as a mild-mannered community manager, grass-roots marketer, product evangelist and writer who has worked for THQ, IGN, and other companies. Currently, he is the Community Manager for NASA's Moonbase Alpha game. When not chatting with fans, he splits his time between New York City, the Alpha Quadrant and the Mojave Wasteland.
