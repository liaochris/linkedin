Lucy Holloway's Summary

A dedicated and commited Legal Advisor with a will to succeed; I have a strong interest in commercial and company law and contracts and over 6 years working with various types of commercial contracts- including JCT, NEC, ICE and customer bespoke terms, in the telecomms, software and medical industries.

My commitment is to ensure that all contracts are as fair as possible to both parties, while protecting my company's interests. Honesty, openness and integrity are as much a part of the negotiation process as knowing the law, industry practice and how to apply them to the current situation.

An empathy with both sides of the negotiating table is an essential part of what I bring to a discussion, as well as a keen eye for even the most minor details and a sense of professionalism with a positive "can do" attitude.

I enjoy being challenged and endeavour to keep my skills up to date through continuing personal and professional development, in order to meet any opportunity offered to me.

Recent projects include:

Working for one of the largest IT and technology services companies in both the UK and international markets, Lucy worked as part of a three man team rolling out a new project for a contractual obligation mapping tool, for both national government and private sector contracts. Lucy required full security clearance for this role due to the sensitive nature of the contracts involved. This role required an outstanding commercial contract knowledge and included being able to critically analyse and dissect contracts in to the various obligations of all parties involved, categorise and effectively communicate and summarise these obligations for the project teams. Lucy was required to map the obligations onto a database using Sharepoint technology, provide training to the project teams on how to use the database and provide support until the teams could run the database themselves.

Working for a telecoms logistics company, Lucy provided support to the commercial team in all matters of commercial contract drafting and preparation, including liaison with internal legal and other departments as required.
