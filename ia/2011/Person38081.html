Rupen Shah's Summary

Opus Una Ventures was set up by business partners, Rupen Shah, Craig Boddington & Jeremy Harbour . These, three experienced entrepreneurs wanted to help struggling businesses they provide the fresh pair of eyes in a difficult situations that can take a struggling company to a thriving company in a very short space of time.

During the credit crunch many good small to medium sized businesses are experiencing tough times. With no bank support and suppliers squeezing credit terms the knock on effects can be devastating to shareholders, staff and customers.

Where can we help?

• Creditor pressure, too much debt.
• Personal guarantees or problems with the bank or HMRC.
• Big drop in turnover or profits
• Legal issues, or risk of insolvency.
• Cash flow issues
• Directors need a change/holiday/sleep
• 2nd round/3rd round financing
• Or even if your business is sound apart from one issue holding you back.

So, if you own a small to medium sized business who would benefit from the knowledge and expertise of Opus Una Ventures, get in touch - use our experience in helping SME's find the right solution to help turn them around.

Rupen Shah who has worked with various Large organisations (e.g. Diageo, Brakes), Medium sized organisation and New Business Startups, his partners in Opus Una include expertise across insolvency, accountancy, online business, project management and Information technology.

Projects the team have worked on cross many sectors and can bring new passion and enthusiasm to any business

Rupen says "It is such a shame to see so many great businesses with good ideas and loyal teams of people fall over just because the goal posts have moved"
