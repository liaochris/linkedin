DIVERSANT LLC is a privately-held professional services firm providing IT contingent labor and consulting services to mid-market and Fortune 500 companies nationwide. Our firm combines a wealth of practical business expertise with decades of experience in the IT and staffing sectors.

DIVERSANT’s proprietary sourcing methodologies help us accurately match highly-skilled candidates with our client's exacting business requirements. Our staffing solutions are complemented by consulting and payroll services which provide analytical insights and process controls respectively. DIVERSANT also develops and implements innovative programs that promote diversity in the workforce and the talent acquisition supply chain. We believe that diversity in all its forms leads to greater opportunities for consultants and partners, and to more innovative solutions for clients.

At DIVERSANT, we are equally interested in developing the careers of our consultants by providing them the best possible opportunities at leading corporations. We complement this service by offering generous benefits packages commensurate with tenure. Consultants working with DIVERSANT find challenging projects where their industry specialization pays off.

DIVERSANT LLC was formed when Diversant Inc. and Info Technologies merged in early 2010, thereby creating one of the largest African American-owned IT staffing solutions companies in the United Sates. The merge was part of a natural progression for both companies, which have been operating in close cooperation since 2005 when DIVERSANT Inc. was first founded.
