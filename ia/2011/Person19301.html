Jason Anderson's Summary

I began my career in the game industry as a contract computer artist. I had a number of clients, one being Interplay Productions. In 1993 I was employed by Interplay to work on the yet to be named Fallout. Within a short time, I rose to a lead position and became a key member of the Fallout team. After three plus years with Interplay, I struck out on my own, becoming one of the three founding members of Troika Games and successfully shipping three RPGs within seven years. In October of 07 Interplay hired me as Creative Director, leading a team in the creation of Project V13, a next generation MMO. I left Interplay in March of 09 to become a Creative Director at inXile Entertainment where I am working on a yet to be announced RPG.

I have a passion for the creative process and immensely enjoy working within a team environment. I pride myself on my ability to see a project through from beginning to end, to work with my team to get the job done and to jump in where needed and provide assistance to any aspect of game development. I have immense problem solving abilities, vast attention to detail and a deep pride in my work.

My many years of experience in the computer game industry has given me insight into many aspects of game development including publisher negotiations, contracts, employee management, budgeting, writing and presenting pitch documents and presentations, game design, animation, level building, scripting, sound engineering and interface design.

I have extensive knowledge of several software packages including business software, raster, vector and 3D art packages as well as a number of scripting languages.

Specialties

Creative Direction, General and Production Management, Game Development, Game Design, Technical Artist, 3D Artist & Animator
