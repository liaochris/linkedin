Liverpool Chamber of Commerce's Summary

Liverpool Chamber of Commerce was established in 1850 to protect the interests of the merchants and businesses in the city.

We are the voice of Liverpool's business community locally, nationally and around the world.

We are part of a wider network of North West Chambers as well as an accredited member of the British Chambers of Commerce (BCC).

Our international trade missions encourage global business, including to the Far and Middle East, Europe and America.

Our LinkedIn profile is an opportunity for us to connect with other business proffessionals, as well as to act as a form for discusssion. Please note, some of our groups will be invitation-only, created for Chamber Council and Committee members.

Specialties

Business support, networking, training, international trade.
