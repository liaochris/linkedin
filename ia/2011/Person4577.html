Bas Van Beek's Summary

Dutch based senior web developer and hostmaster who is expert in TYPO3, but also likes to develop from scratch.

Bas is always focusing on results and speed to be able to deliver top-quality on it's best performance. He prefers to use the TYPO3 CMS framework as much as possible to gain effective and optimized results.

Bas is also a active contributor to the TYPO3 open source community by sharing his custom made web applications to other web development companies. He is, for example, the inventor and core developer of TYPO3 Multishop, a very unique and promising e-commerce solution for TYPO3. More information can be found here: http://www.typo3multishop.com

Bas is a experienced hostmaster and has experience with hosting thousands of vhosts including high traffic web sites.

Besides of doing web development Bas is also teamleader, coacher and entrepreneur in a wide variety of projects and businesses.

Specialties

TYPO3 Multishop, TYPO3 (TypoScript), PHP, Oscommerce, jQuery, Prototype, Ajax, JSON, Linux, Apache.
