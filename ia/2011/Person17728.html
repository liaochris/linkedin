Hung Lee's Summary

Social Media is transforming the recruitment industry. Platforms such as LinkedIn, Facebook and Twitter have provided Job Seekers, Employers and Recruiters with new ways to find and talk to one another, with game changing implications for all the parties involved.

Wise Man Say Ltd is a consultancy practice providing advisory, partnering and outsourcing services to each of these actors in the recruitment space, helping them draft strategy, update skills and re-engineer process to stay competitive in the Web 2.0 era.

My role as a hands on Managing Director is to provide full spectrum analysis on your current situation, advise, design and build your jobsearch or recruitment strategy, transfer knowledge on key social media tools & techniques and provide ongoing support as you remodel your process and practice.

You may be a Job Seeker wondering how social media can improve your competitiveness. You may be an Employer looking for the best way to source, recruit & retain top talent. Or you may be a Recruiter looking to stay ahead of the curve. Whatever your situation - do not hesitate to contact Wise Man Say - I would be delighted to hear from you.
