Monica Alexander's Summary

Having spent more than 15 years in the music industry, I had the unique opportunity to orchestrated public relations campaigns that helped break or cross over numerous artists to a successful career.

In 2002, I founded PR Wizzzz Entertainment, a stylish, boutique PR agency that focuses on emerging and established recording artists, television and motion pictures personalities, professional athletes, author, along special event management; as well as other corporate and individual projects, which require unique entertainment and music-based tie-ins.

My public relations background includes experience in personal and entertainment PR as well as celebrity special event management, brand marketing and lifestyle PR. Prior to establishing the agency, I was Senior Director of Publicity at MCA Records and served as Senior Account Executive at Rogers & Cowan.

I have been a top producer since the beginning of my career in public relations & special event management; i.e. I have supervised PR accounts that have received awards from the Public Communicators of Los Angeles, Public Relations Advertising Marketing Excellence, and a Excel Awards from Weber Shandwick (Rogers & Cowan).

I consider myself a cut above the rest; and my consistent loyalty is a comfort to my clientele, I have fought hard and I continually meets their needs in an efficiently and thoughtful manner. It is my knowledge of the PR industry and passion for excellence which drives me to improve and rise above the client’s expectations.

I am an associate member of The Recording Academy, NAACP, Black Entertainment & Sports Lawyers Association, National Association of Women Business Owners, National Association of Black Female Executives in Music & Entertainment, and Cambridge’s Who Who’s Women in Business.

I am a graduate of University of Phoenix, and hold a Bachelor of Science degree in Business/Management along with an Associate of Arts degree in Legal Studies emphasis on Entertainment Law.
