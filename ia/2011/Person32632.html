AppForge Crossfire (previously "MobileVB") was a cross-platform, “write once, run anywhere” mobile/wireless application development suite that ran as an add-in to Microsoft Visual Studio. It supported PalmOS, PocketPC, Symbian Series 60 & 80, UIQ, and other platforms.
