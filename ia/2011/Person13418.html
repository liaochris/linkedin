Elizabeth Atkins's Summary

Elizabeth Atkins writes and speaks from the spectacular spectrum that she embodies as the daughter of an African American judge and a Roman Catholic priest who was English, French Canadian and Cherokee.

With books, screenplays, poems, lectures, and blogs, The Bliss Report, on www.elizabethatkins.com and Fox 2 News, Elizabeth edu-tains by exploring life, love, health and happiness.

Elizabeth's novel, Dark Secret — a sexy thriller about a biracial woman “passing” for white — is being shopping for movie rights in Hollywood.

Montel showcased Elizabeth as race expert and author of White Chocolate.

Oprah featured Elizabeth’s 100 pound weight loss!

She also wrote Twilight with Billy Dee Williams... two hip-hop novels in Black Expressions Book Club... Click! with networking guru George Fraser... and Going Full Circle for quadriplegic record company CEO Kenneth Tyson, Jr.

She wrote her master’s thesis about mixed-race Americans at Columbia University’s Graduate School of Journalism. A Diversity Speaker, the University of Michigan graduate was The Detroit News race relations reporter, nominated for the Pulitzer Prize.

She appeared on Tyra to discuss her article in Ebony.

Elizabeth’s story Take It Off! appears in the anthology Other People’s Skin.

The former Fox News writer and Toastmasters International member has lectured about race at the NAACP, General Motors’ World Diversity Day, Gannett, the University of Michigan, Beaumont Hospital, Levy Home Entertainment, Comerica Bank, the Association of Multi-Ethnic Americans and the American Library Association.

She taught writing at Wayne State University, Oakland University, Wayne County Community College District, PowerNetworking and DaimlerChrysler.

Elizabeth appeared on The CBS Evening News, Good Morning America Sunday, and many local TV shows. The former Detroit NABJ Chapter president has written for The New York Times, The San Diego Tribune, Essence and a Rosa Parks tribute.

Specialties

Writing novels and non-fiction books for myself and as a Ghostwriter, Writing Coach, Writing Instructor for adults and children, Diversity Speaker, Poet, Exercise Enthusiast, Bliss, Journalism
