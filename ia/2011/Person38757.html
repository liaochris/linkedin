Sanjoy Datta's Summary

As an experienced early entrant in Indian Real Estate Private Equity Investment scenario, Sanjoy has evolved as an expert professional, rich with knowledge on Indian Real Estate and contributed tangible inputs towards pre and post investment fund management business.

Sanjoy has 4 year’s hands on experience in Real Estate Private Equity funding and over 9 years experience as Project Manager and client representative, in India. His extensive development management experience in major cities including New Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Bangalore, Chennai and Hyderabad helped him gather knowledge of micro-markets within the fast emerging growth centers. While working with Project Management Consultants, Sanjoy has undertaken responsibility of client management, project delivery for various asst classes, team building & leading and handling multi-disciplinary positions and delivered prestigious projects for top notch Indian and multinational clients. Sanjoy has been involved in the complete greenfield project life cycle for hotels, malls, institutions, IT Parks, commercial buildings, large campuses and tall residential towers.

Sanjoy's current responsibilities include-
~Technical evaluation of potential transactions
~Market study : identification of growth areas, assessment of capital & rental values
~Engineering Due diligence of properties
~Project risk assessment & development of project execution strategy
~Facilitating Value Engineering exercise & Design optimization.
~Monitoring of Developments under investment
~Liasoning with investee companies, promoters & developers
~Direct activities of third party consultants and contractors
~Report on performance, budget variances and significant changes in market and property conditions

Specialties

Strategic planning, project development, building and driving project team, setting up monitoring mechanism, design management, procurement strategy, contracts, formalization of systems & processes, delivery management.

He has extensive experience of conceptualization & development of 5 & 4 star hotels, boutique hospitality, Institutional buildings, Commercial properties, IT spaces, Mall & Multiplexes, High rise premium residential buildings and multi-use urban community centres.
