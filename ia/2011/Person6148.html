Brian Avila’s Summary

Brian is a Certified Public Accountant who specializes in the audits of large, international multi-line insurance companies. His experience focuses on risk transfer, reinsurance, commissions, statutory accounting, catastrophe reinsurance, and Sarbanes-Oxley compliance.

Brian has extensive experience in coordinating multi-location interdisciplinary teams in delivering audit services, including specialty teams of actuaries, information-technology specialists, tax accountants, internal auditors, and shared-business process teams. In addition, Brian leads his own teams of financial auditors and is responsible for their development of industry expertise as well as oversight of their deliverables.

In addition to his client service responsibilities, Brian has worked for fair representation and inclusion of diverse groups, specifically ethnic minorities and the GLBT community. Brian founded and leads the employee resource group for Gay, Lesbian, Bisexual and Transgender (GLBT) professionals in PricewaterhouseCoopers' Florida practice offices, in addition to serving on the executive committee of the PricewaterhouseCoopers' South Florida multi-cultural affinity group. Brian is also actively involved in PricewaterhouseCoopers' campus sourcing activities.

Brian Avila’s Specialties:

Property & Casualty Insurance, Life Insurance, Reinsurance, Risk Transfer, Catastrophe Reinsurance, Statutory Insurance Accounting (US SAP), SOX Compliance
