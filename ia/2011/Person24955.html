Julie Kreisler’s Summary

The Kreisler Group is a full service marketing and advertising firm based in Southern California who's clients consist of manufacturers in the Kitchen & Bath industry, emerging national brands, local companies.

What makes us different is our hands on approach ensuring consistency in the execution of our clients marketing objectives. Our experienced staff of professionals is driven to fulfill our client’s marketing needs through strategically employing effective programs and campaigns that yield bottom line results.

Our services range from ambitious national TV campaigns to strategic public relations programs. We view our clients as partners in our business and take pride in helping them succeed.

Our agency thrives on a culture of creativity and we are constantly evolving and expanding our reach into the Internet and digital media.

TKG clients include innovative companies such as Fisher & Paykel, DCS by Fisher & Paykel, Convaid Wheelchairs, Bellerive Residences, DOMA Properties, Papa Ben's Kitchen and the Long Beach Convention & Entertainment Center to name a few.

Brand Strategy, Media planning/buying, Digital Media, Creative, and Public Relations.
