Steve Marshall's Summary

After a 4-year-attempt at becoming a Hollywood producer (and halfway through my MBA), the Lord's hand reached into my life and rearranged my priorities.

He pulled me into formation with the Eudist Fathers, a tiny religious order in San Diego. They have only 10 priests in the US, but have grown incredibly over the past two years, and now have 10 seminarians!

Lord willing, in the fall of 2011, I'll begin my masters in Theology at the major seminary. Until that point, I'm living in the House of Formation, finishing my MBA, and looking for opportunities to help the Eudists support their amazing growth.

Specialties

MEDIA: Story development, low- to zero-budget solutions for video production, and fundraising videos for nonprofits.

GENERAL: BSA leadership training, project management, entrepreneurship and business plan development.
