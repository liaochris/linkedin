Ryan Stubblefield's Summary

Ryan Stubblefield has a resume that any engineer would envy, but he’s no run-of-the-mill software jock. Since the early 90’s, Ryan’s commitment to developing the best artistic software on the market has made him a driving force behind a wide range of art and technology endeavors. Ryan spent 10 years at Adobe as an Software Engineer, Engineering Manager, and Technical Lead developing world class software for creative designers. His work can be found in everything from Photoshop, Illustrator, Flash, After Effects, Encore, and almost every other piece of Adobe software. He was also a software architect at Yahoo, where he designed highly scalable content sharing systems that integrated with core technologies across the organization.

More recently, Ryan has worked closely with several social networking startups, focusing on widget strategies and developing analytics systems to drive viral marketing strategies. Ryan’s recent projects include a Flash-based video editing suite, a discovery based business recommendation site (think StumbledUpon meets Yelp), an art distribution iPhone application, a handful of iPhone applications for children, and a Facebook virtual gifting application.

Currently, Ryan is working at ngmoco:) making social games (We Rule, Adventure Bay), and developing the ngcore gaming platform.

When he’s not developing the next great piece of software, Ryan is painting new canvases for his next art show or spinning tunes on the decks at one of his local DJ gigs.
