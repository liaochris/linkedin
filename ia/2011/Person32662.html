Nick Young FDN, CMTA's Summary

Nick Young, FDN, CMTA, is a Functional Diagnostic Nutritionist and Physician Consultant. Nick has received advanced training in Nutritional Therapy, and maintains professional memberships in the Weston A. Price Foundation, the International Foundation for Nutrition and Health, the American College of Physical Medicine, and the International Sports Sciences Association.

He is also a Certified Metabolic Typing Advisor and Personal Fitness Trainer. Additionally, he advises medical doctors and chiropractors throughout the country on lab interpretations and repair protocols that will help restore normal function and improve health. Nick’s D.R.E.S.S. for Health Success has been helping people throughout the country to get well and stay well naturally for more than 8 years; This natural approach includes: Diet, Rest, Exercise, Stress Reduction and Supplements – All are specific to the client’s unique biochemical needs.

~ Let us Add Life to Your Years, not just Years to Your Life! ~

A message from Nick -

“In our office we pride ourselves on providing a loving, compassionate, supportive environment to help our clients not only regain their health, vitality and youth, but also to maintain their health once regained. Our goal is to help you restore normal function so that medication may no longer be necessary!

We provide care for children, seniors and persons of all ages. We have taken care of some of the most notable personalities in our community and have clients who come to us from long distances due to the unique approach we have to help restore health. Here at Metabolic Solutions, we’re constantly attending advanced programs of study on the latest and most innovative methods to help our clients.”
