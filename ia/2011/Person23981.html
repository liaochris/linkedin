Keith Keller (The Social Media Strategy Coach)'s Summary

Keith is known internationally as "The Social Media Strategy Coach"

He is also the founder of SOCIAL MEDIA MONEY TREE TV and his latest project is "THE SOCIAL MEDIA MONEY TREE" is a new audio product containing interviews with successful people who are using social media extensively in their marketing campaigns (fascinating listening)

Keith is a very passionate advocate of social media which he uses extensively to market and promote his internet radio show - Career Success Radio - As a result it has now been downloaded well over 75,000 times and is listened to in 120 countries.

He also holds a Graduate Certificate in Career Education and Development from RMIT University in Melbourne and is a professional member of the Career Development Association of Australia (CDAA).
