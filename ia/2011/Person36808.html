Richard Manson-Hing's Summary

Prior to setting up ThreeSpaces, I was a founding partner and CEO of Interactive Systems Limited in 1981, an IBM Personal Computer Systems Dealer, and TCS (Trinidad) Limited, a software developer specializing in developing localized business software for the Trinidad market. While CEO of TCS (Trinidad), I managed the development of an award winning payroll business package in Trinidad. After transitioning the leadership role at TCS (Trinidad), I went to work as a consultant for a sister company in Canada - TCS (Canada) Limited which specializes in distributed banking systems.

More recently, I was a consultant based in Boston, Massachusetts, specializing in creating personal productivity, groupware, Internet applications and web services. Some of the products I have helped develop as a consultant in Boston over an 18 year period are Lotus Symphony, Lotus 1-2-3, HP95lx palm computer, Lotus Notes, Lotus LearningSpace, Silverback Information Portal for network management and the PegaSystems PegaRULES Process Commander.

Currently, as CEO of ThreeSpaces I am responsible for business strategy and development. I also operate as the company's CTO, responsible for the design and development of the company's products, where I am particularly interested in seeking innovative solutions for pervasive and ubiquitous computing, especially in the mobile device space.

I can be contacted at: rmansonh@gmail.com
