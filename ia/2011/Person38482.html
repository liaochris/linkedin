Sally Bernstein's Summary

A dynamic and experienced contact for all your copy and editing needs; I am your go-to consultant for any and everything related to the food, beverage, wine and spirits, or restaurant industries.

I have more than 20 years of experience traveling the globe to study, interview and write about, all things related to food. I can help you with interviewing, writing and editing copy related to food and drink suppliers, authors, vintners, culinary institutes, authors, restaurateurs and other food industry specialists from farm to table.

My past experience includes: food educator and demonstrator, event appearances and lectures, writing, editing, and leading tour groups. I have produced an extensive library of resources, samples and articles from which to draw. From ad copy and PR to marketing materials and website content, contact me. If I can't help you, I can tell you who can!

In 1994, even before the popularity of blogs, I conceived of, created, staffed, sold ads, collected data, wrote and edited a comprehensive, now award-winning website for all things food and beverage related. You can follow my adventures and learn more about my wide range of writing by visiting www.sallybernstein.com

Specialties

Farm to table suppliers (farm, garden, co-op, ranchers, fishermen, etc.);
Coffee and tea nuances, styles, traditions and etiquette;
California wineries and distilleries;
Product suppliers (professional and commercial food prep and serving products).
