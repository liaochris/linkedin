Jeremy Sigel’s Summary

Jeremy Sigel has a diverse project management and creative background in the media industry. Beginning his career with the New England Patriots as a designer, Jeremy later spent several years in Silicon Valley working as a Producer for RichMedia Networks and playing a key role in the launch of the Wave Magazine as Managing Editor. He has worked for Millbrook Distribution Services, one of the nation's leading service merchandiser, and most recently contributed to the revitalization of the National Hockey League's broadcast department, building organizational systems used in broadcast operations and event planning. Jeremy holds a B.A. in Government from Skidmore College, and earned a M.S. in Media Management from the S.I. Newhouse School at Syracuse University .
