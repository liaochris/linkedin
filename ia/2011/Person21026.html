Jim Stanley’s Experience

  • Vice-President, Products and Marketing

    Zoove

    (Privately Held; Wireless industry)

    December 2010 — Present (3 months)

    Zoove operates a national directory of vanity mobile phone numbers that lets brands easily connect with people – anytime, anywhere, on any mobile phone. Zoove is the exclusive national administrator of StarStar® calling numbers for the largest wireless operators in the U.S., serving more than 250 million people.

    With StarStar® calling, consumers simply call the name of brand they see being promoted – for example, **USA (or **872). Instantly, they can be pushed that brand’s mobile app, web page, coupon, videos or more, directly to their mobile phones – without the need to use bar code readers or disjointed text message short codes.

    Like branded URL names, such as Nike.com, or vanity numbers such as 1-800-FLOWERS, StarStar® numbers are easy for consumers to remember. StarStar® numbers enable people to engage with existing promotional campaigns on-the-spot – through the one device they carry everywhere.

    Most “mobile marketing” efforts focus narrowly on “search results” or “display ads” on the phone screen themselves. Zoove’s focus is to enable brands to better leverage their existing ad spends across any promotional medium. StarStar® calling numbers are designed to be promoted as part of any marketing campaign, such as: billboards, print ads, TV and radio commercials, online ads and more. Zoove provides the mobile connection brands are seeking, and the ability to measure the results instantly.
