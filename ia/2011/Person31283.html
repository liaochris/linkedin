Mike Prince's Summary

Michael is a talented, versatile and experienced artist who has been working in the graphics industry since 2000. Since then he has worked as a designer/artist in the Print, Multimedia, Film, Television & Video Games industries. He specializes in models, textures and streamlining art production. His modeling experience covers the creation of environments/props, characters & vehicles.

Michael has experience making feature film previs as well as leading a team through layout. He has the experience & confidence to manage, train and mentor others.

Specialties

High resolution polygon/subdivision surface modeling.
Displacement, normal mapping & texturing.
Advancements in UV mapping techniques.
Extensive experience and knowledge of leading edge industry software/techniques.
Technically minded with a high degree of attention to detail.
