Jean Fredrick's Summary

Jean is an accomplished marketing professional with over 15 years experience in online marketing, product development and project management. Having served as Marketing Manager for Verizon’s online Yellow Pages team in the successful creation, launch and growth of the product, Jean gained extensive knowledge and experience in product positioning, branding, pricing & sales support, vertical marketing, and product market testing. She also honed her skills building co-promotional business relationships to promote and grow the business. Jean led the marketing team at The Kelsey Group as Executive Marketing Director for their subscription and consultative services in Yellow Pages, electronic directories and local media, including advertising, PR and marketing their major conferences. Since branching out on her own, Jean has brought her strength in market research and services marketing to Stratfor, focusing on geopolitical, economic, political and military information services. Jean also continues to support YellowBrix, focusing on data analytics and contextual advertising, and Sloan Consortium, focusing on online education providing monthly marketing newsletters, weekly forum post compilation and website content support. Jean holds a BS in Business Administration from Emmanuel College in Boston .
