Damien Krebs's Summary

I am a process engineer and study manager with extensive experience in nickel processing. My greatest technical strength is in the area of hydrometallurgical process development for projects. My technical skills are complimented by business accumen and management skills. Through working in a global business I have a network of contacts throughout the world in the mining industry. The next stage of my career involves a move into Uranium processing as it has a wonderful growth profile and is a close technical analogue to nickel hydrometallurgy.

Nickel hydrometallurgy especially that of Nickel Laterites.
