Carolyn Monaco's Summary

Carolyn--a seasoned marketer with 25 years of publishing experience--began her career at Atlantic Monthly. Subsequent magazine work included consumer marketing positions with Lotus Development (now w/IBM), Cook's Illustrated, Horticulture, Utne Reader, Inc., and Fast Company. Carolyn was then appointed founding finance & marketing director for Tufts Media (Tufts University's new for-profit publishing company) whose revenues grew to $6 million through acquisitions and launches of newsletters and books. Her next position was marketing director at Harvard Business School Publishing's book division. Carolyn began consulting in 2009 to build authors' businesses through bestselling books.

Specialties

- Book publishing consulting
- Author management and platform building
- Preselling authors' books through their networks
- Book proposal development, incl detailed marketing plans
- Customized book marketing Roadmaps for all parties
