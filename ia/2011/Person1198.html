Alberto Luna’s Summary

I started my career at KPMG, LLP in San Diego, CA. During my time at KPMG, I worked as a tax preparer to the firms domestic and multi-state tax clients. I later formed a small tax consulting practice with a mission to provide quality and professional due care to each client.
