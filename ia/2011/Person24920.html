Kyle Blades’s Summary

As a person that has consciously chosen to live life on the unbeaten path, Kyle Blades has walked a diverse route. After a year of college Kyle quickly decided that four years of school would not lead to the life he wanted. In order to make a dramatic change he joined the United States Marine Corps Reserves. Almost immediately upon retuning home from his initial training, he was informed he would be going to Iraq.

While facing long, exhausting days, Kyle still found time to teach himself Arabic which was in dire need due to the lack of interpreters. Upon returning home Kyle took a marketing job with Harley-Davidson and quickly moved up the ranks of the Fortune 500 company. After several years, Kyle learned that he would once again be returning to Iraq.

After returning from his second combat tour, Kyle decided to once again go against convention and open up his own company. A few months later Rebel opened as an advertising firm that uses social media to reach consumers. For over two years, Kyle enjoyed the challenges of owning his own company but not the inability to focus solely on executing fabulous social media campaigns.

Kyle is proud that he has been at the forefront of the social media revolution and is very excited to be ushering in a new era. It is his goal to stay on the cutting edge of knowing how to reach consumers and to use that knowledge to grow businesses.
