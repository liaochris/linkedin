Andrew Margetts’s Summary

Andrew is an astute, delivery and results focused business and IT change professional, known for his ability to bring a logical and pragmatic approach to complex change projects across business functions – from Finance to IT and managing 3rd parties. A programme / project manager experienced in delivering complex change projects in financial services. Responsible for implementing a variety of business and IT solutions globally including restructuring, rationalisation, process improvement, strategic change, systems development / integration. This experience combined with strong communication, stakeholder management skills and determination to deliver, brings a valuable combination of talent, skills and knowledge to clients.
