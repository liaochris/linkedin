Samara Tauber’s Summary

I believe it is really important to give back to the world; therefore, I am apart of a team that has created a space for the cycling community to give back and support each other in many ways.

There are several aspects of BikeWear World L.L.C. One aspect is that we sell cycling clothing in a retail store on a bike path in San Diego, as well as, online. We were the first to partake in shooting video of product and displaying it on our site for commercial use.

We also have created uRide.tv, which allows users to upload race footage, wipeouts or trail rides. Because of the uploading technology available, we are becoming a resource to find bike shops, mechanics, athletes, manufacturers, race directors and many more industry leaders via video commercials.

BikeWear World L.L.C. will still strive to influence more people towards healthier, active lifestyles, emphasis on GO Green, nutrition, exercising, looking and feeling great.

    Why We Love It: Sami Tauber, 32, owner of San Diego's BikeWear World, says that what you wear is as important as what you ride. "You don't want to worry about your clothing when you're hammering," she says. "Your clothes should represent you and fit comfortably." Tauber has worked exclusively with cycling clothing for more than a decade at several area shops. BikeWear World, which opened in September 2007, carries only cycling clothes. "I have worked hard to find the best clothes on the market, especially for women," she says. "I carry the best--Campagnolo, Louis Garneau, Zoot, Adidas." Her stock covers everyone from roadies to downhillers to triathletes.
