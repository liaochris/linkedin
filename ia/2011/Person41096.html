Stacey Ann's Summary

With over 5 years of creative industry experience from both agency and client sides, specializing in online fashion retail and creating memorable branding experiences, I have been able to accomplish many realms in design for some of the world’s leading brands. In 2010, my enthusiasm for fashion, imagination, and photography has resulted in the successful launch of the Captain Apticus Clothing Line.

My career reflects my wide range of interests in art, design, photography, and fashion and I look forward to collaborating with creative individuals who understand the importance of branding, design, and its overall impact for success.

I currently oversee my own design and photography studio, and you'll often find me constantly blogging about art, travel, food, fashion, and photography.
