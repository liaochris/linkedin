Michael Heraghty's Summary

Listed below are some of the projects I have delivered for User Journeys and/or its partners since 2003:

• User personae, scenarios, stakeholder workshops, field research, usability guidelines document and wireframes for public body (subcontract, 2011)
• Usability review and A/B split testing for online dating site AnotherFriend.com (2010 -2011)
• Usability review and usability testing for Jameson Irish Whiskey (2010 - 2011)
• Field research, wireframes and storyboard for AXA call centre application and website mid-term transactions redesign (subcontract, 2010, 2011)
• Usability review of start-up EventElephant (2009)
• Requirements gathering, wireframes and direction of visual design for start-up Foleys.ie (2009 – 2010)
• Usability review of 3 Mobile website (2008)
• HTML/CSS prototype (early concept) of CRM tool for Scottish and Southern Energy (subcontract, 2008)
• Stakeholder workshops, paper prototypes, wireframes and visual design direction for AXA car insurance web redesign (2008, subcontract)
• Requirements gathering, PowerPoint and HTML wireframes, review workshops and usability testing for successful start-up ExamSupport.ie (2007 – 2008)
• SEO & usability review for Concern (2007)
• Web project management of DiscoverIreland.com (subcontract, 2005/2006)
• Usability & SEO analysis of 20+ websites for Tourism Ireland (subcontract, 2005)
• IA/card sorting workshop and wireframes of DELL intranet navigation (subcontract, 2006)
• Bespoke usability training for 02 (subcontract, 2005)
• Field research and usability review of Allianz CMS (subcontract, 2005)

Prior to setting up my own company, I worked for Vision Consulting, for whom I led design & usability projects for clients such as Intelligent Finance and Warner Music Group.

I have also lectured on usability and SEO for the DMI, NUI Maynooth, Trinity College, the IMI and the IIA.

Specialties

• Designing wireframes, flow charts and storyboards
• Preparing and moderating usability tests
• Conducting usability reviews
• Running A/B split tests
• Conducting stakeholder workshops and field research
• Modifying HTML/CSS code and/or Photoshop mockups
• Generating user personae and user scenarios
