Tom Brauch’s Summary

Executive leader in Digital Marketing, Technology and Business Strategy. Background in Usability and Project Management.

Outstanding relationship builder with proven ability to become the trusted advisor to C-level executives and to communicate exceptionally well with IT, marketing and business leaders

Leader, shepherd and coach with a natural and proven ability to hire, inspire and motivate diverse teams to produce, gamestorm and innovate in the midst of tight budgets, sketchy requirements and late-night pizza; creating a culture where people can’t wait to get to work

I have spent my career changing the prevailing attitudes and assumptions about how things should get done. Most businesses begin their organization chart with the CEO at the top. I believe this should be flipped. The most important person at the top should be the customer, the end-user, the website visitor. We tend to miss the reason we’re in business.

The customer experience is all that matters. It is about earning their loyalty. Marketing your business (social media, PR, branding, etc...) is not about creating a veneer of what should be, but about creating a reflection of what is.
