Tom Eldridge's Summary

Digital Strategist with extensive experience of the new media sector and implementing digital and social media solutions.

To date my role has been to provide leadership, budget management, roadmap definition, and governance for the roll out of social media programs.

My multi-disciplinary career has seen me take a hands on role with experience in managing stakeholder requirements, developing strategy and planning, leading a social media program forward, monitoring competitors and trends, managing a team, and measuring and reporting on Return On Investment.

Going forward my career path will be increasingly focused on driving social media programs, team leadership, and formulating business strategy within the digital and social media workspace
