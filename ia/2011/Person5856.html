Brad Monterio’s Summary

- Accomplished financial services, investment banking, marketing and communications executive.

-Hold FINRA Series 63, 7 and 79 licenses (all securities are offered through Mid-Market Securities, LLC, a FINRA/SIPC registered broker-dealer).

- Specialties in XBRL/interactive data, integrated reporting, FinTech, CleanTech, ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) sectors

- Chair of Institute of Management Accountants' (IMA) XBRL Committee

- Other specialties include: financial planning/wealth management/investment advisory, business valuation, accounting, legal, and professional services sectors.

Counsel clients in:
- Investment banking, global private placement agent services, capital introductions / fundraising, corporate finance
- Business development and strategic alliance development
- Sponsorship and fundraising
- XBRL and interactive data
- CSR/ESG/Integrated reporting

- Media relations and government affairs

I speak and write regularly on marketing, XBRL, ESG and communications topics, including "Evolved Reporting (ESG and financial information)," "Integrated Reporting," "Communicating Across the Generations,” "Sourcing Premier Clients," XBRL and Interactive Data, Green Audits, “Microtrends in Marketing – An Exponential Shift,” “Media Relations Means Money in the Bank,” "Media as a Marketing Tool," “Simple Steps to an Effective Marketing Plan,” “Using Media Relations to Build Brand Awareness and Attract Clients,” and “The Art of Media Pitching.”

Contributing author to the recently published "Trust Meltdown: the Financial Industry Needs a Fundamental Restart," along with Kofi Annan, Robert Zoelleck of the World Bank, and others.

Personal interests in socially responsible investing, corporate accountability, transparency, governance, integrated reporting, triple bottom line.
