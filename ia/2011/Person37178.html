Rob Brown's Summary

Joined Staniforth in 2008, part of the TBWA group, as its UK Managing Director with responsibility for its offices in Manchester and London.

Prior to that was at McCann Erickson for seven years as PR Director working with clients as diverse as Durex, Aldi, Peugeot and the NHS.

Fellow of the Chartered Iinstitute of Public Relations (CIPR) and a Member of the Council of the CIPR. In January 2010 joined its Executive Board.

Author of Public Relations and the Social Web (Kogan Page 2009).
