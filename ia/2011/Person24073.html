Kelly Groehler's Summary

I work for Best Buy Co., Inc., a key player in one of the most fascinating and rapidly-changing industry sectors: consumer electronics and technology. I initially joined Best Buy to help build a reputation management framework, so the company could better understand stakeholder perceptions at a time of massive business growth, expansion, and transformation.

Today, my team helps guide the actions and words of Best Buy in ways that tell our story of continued, sustainable growth and differentiation as the technology sector evolves. I also lead our team's use of consumer technology, including social media, to create, aggregate, and distribute news and information that keeps pace with the rapidly-changing global media landscape – and actively engages our employees, consumers, journalists, communities and stakeholders worldwide.

Prior to Best Buy, I spent nearly a decade with Padilla Speer Beardsley Inc., one of the top business-to-business communications consultancies in the U.S. My responsibility included industry analyst relations, employee communications, change management initiatives, labor communications, and marketing communications for such companies as Rockwell Automation, BASF, ConocoPhillips, and Coors Brewing Co.

Follow me: www.twitter.com/KellyGroehler

Specialties
