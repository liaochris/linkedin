Birgit Krols's Publications

  • Extreme Hotels

    • Tectum Publishers
    • 2007
    Authors: Birgit Krols

    ﻿Imagine spending the night in a hotel that looks like a giant dog. Or would you rather enjoy the view from an enchanting jungle tree house? Extreme Hotels takes you on an amazing journey along the world's most bizarre hotels, bed and breakfasts and rental places, from a survival capsule via a hobbit house lodging and a wine barrel hotel to an underwater resort.

  • Extreme Bars

    • Tectum Publishers
    • 2008
    Authors: Birgit Krols

    ﻿Have you ever visited a condom café? Ever been to a double decker bus pub? Or visited a beauty salon that sells more drinks than beauty treatments? No one has ever asked you to cycle a mobile café through the city streets? No worries, Extreme bars will point you to the quirkiest bars around the globe. This book takes you on an amazing pubcrawl along the world's most bizarre bars, pubs and cafés, from an ice bar and an inflatable pub to a café on a municipal water tank and a bar in which you can stare at the other customers by means of 60 cameras. Going out for drinks has just gotten a new meaning !

  • Extreme Restaurants

    • Tectum Publishers
    • 2008
    Authors: Birgit Krols

    ﻿Imagine having a romantic dinner in a tram as it is making its way through Milan city centre. Or would you rather visit an underwater restaurant? Would you even consider dining on a toilet seat and wiping your mouth with toilet paper? Or does the idea of sitting at a table suspended at a height of 50 metres seem more appealing? Extreme Restaurants takes you on an amazing journey through the world's most bizarre restaurants, from a UFO eatery and a skyscraper restaurant to a bistro in a revolving cable car and a restaurant with only one table. Grabbing a bite to eat has never been so adventurous!

  • Extreme Venues

    • Tectum Publishers
    • 2009
    Authors: Birgit Krols

    ﻿Does a reception on a historic clipper or a working warship appeal to you? Would you like to prowl the dance floor in a zoo, surrounded by gorillas? Or would you rather invite one thousand of your best friends to an intimate night out at the Sydney Opera House? 'Extreme Venues' provides the world's most unique addresses for parties, receptions, meetings and every kind of gathering. From Tower Bridge and St. Paul's Cathedral in London, to a party room in a goldmine, an ice palace at Europe's highest point or a party location on the bottom of a shark tank. With this book in hand you can organise the event of the century!

  • Accidental Inventions

    • Tectum Publishers
    • 2010
    Authors: Birgit Krols

    Did you know that the popsicle was invented by an eleven-year old who had forgotten his soda water mixture on the porch on a cold winter night? Or that LSD was discovered while trying to produce a drug that would ease the pain of childbirth?
    Call them accidents. Call them mistakes. Even serendipity. Fact is, while most good inventions are the result of a common problem and a well thought-out solution, some of them were accidental and unintentional.
    This book reveals the fascinating stories behind the world’s greatest unplanned inventions, from roller skates, penicillin and dynamite, to Coca-Cola, Post-Its and Velcro: they are all discoveries that changed our lives and the world around us.

  • Too Young To Die

    • Tectum Publishers
    • 2010
    Authors: Birgit Krols

    When death strikes unexpectedly and unfulfilled potential is nipped in the bud, that’s when myths are made. This stunningly illustrated book brings a tribute to the lives and work of more than 100 international 20th century celebrities who went before their time. From Judy Garland, Keith Haring and Eva Perón to James Dean, Florence Griffith and JFK… all the monarchs, politicians, activists, entertainers, artists and sportsmen in this book had two things in common: they inspired multiple nations during their lives and attained immortality by dying too soon.

  • 3D Street Art

    • Tectum Publishers
    • 2010
    Authors: Birgit Krols

    All over the world, trompe l’oeils, anamorphisms, lenticulars, abstract illusions and other 3D techniques have been finding their way to pavements, walls, subways, utility boxes and other public spots. The materials, subjects and results are as diverse as the artists themselves, but the works of art all have one thing in common: they trick the unsuspecting passer-by into seeing things that aren’t there.
    This book introduces you to some of the most remarkable artists from the past, present and future of 3D street art. Meet their mind boggling oeuvre and their unique vision, witness how their thrilling three-dimensional creations transform ordinary locations into alternative realities and behold the unexpected worlds that lie hidden beneath the surface of everyday street life.
    Featuring Kurt Wenner, DAIM, Blue Sky, John Pugh, Edgar Müller, Dan Witz and many more.

  • De Kus

    • Tectum Publishers
    • 2010
    Authors: Birgit Krols

    Casablanca mag dan twee van de mooiste kussen uit de filmgeschiedenis bevatten, maar één ding zag deze klassieker toch fout: een kus is nooit zomaar een kus. Eerste kussen, gestolen kussen, afscheidszoenen, moederlijke kusjes, homozoenen, kussen van verraad of spijt… elke kus heeft zijn moment, redenen en hoofdrolspelers. Dit boek belicht meer dan 150 van de meest romantische, memorabele en ophefmakende kussen uit de geschiedenis en uit de wereld van de film, tv, muziek en kunst.
    Van Rodins beroemde standbeeld over Romeo en Julia’s stiekeme geflikflooi en Madonna’s controversiële tongzoen met Britney Spears tot de lang verwachte kus van Rachel en Ross in Friends – dit boek zal elke romantische ziel in vervoering brengen.

  • In His Footsteps

    • Tectum Publishers
    • 2010
    Authors: Birgit Krols

    Simply being the son or daughter of someone famous doesn’t guarantee success, and in order to step out of the shadow of your well-known dad, you have to muster up more than good genes. Nevertheless, some celebrity fathers are so inspirational, they entice their brood to choose similar career paths.
    In His Footsteps sheds light on the most famous father-son and father-daughter combinations in the world of sports, politics, entertainment, business, science, art and literature, such as Muhammad and Laila Ali, John and Julian Lennon, George H.W. and George W. Bush, Ferdinand and Ferdinand Jr. Porsche, and Donald and Kiefer Sutherland.
