  • Director of Recruiting

    Ning

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    March 2008 — May 2010 (2 years 3 months)

I also provided detailed metrics and reporting on recruiting activity and headcount planning to Ning's Executive staff on a daily, weekly and quarterly basis.

    (Public Company; GOOG; Internet industry)

  • Recruiting Lead

    Amazon.com

    (Public Company; AMZN; Internet industry)

    2003 — 2006 (3 years )

    I managed recruiting for Amazon.com’s World Wide Discovery Platform Organization (WWD) which was responsible for all consumer facing features and products at Amazon. This encompassed search and navigation, personalization (machine learning and data mining), online communities and social networking. My small team of 4 was the highest performing recruiting pod at Amazon, responsible for hundreds of hires on a yearly basis.

    I recruited Software Engineers up to Directors and across all hiring needs for North America, Australia, Ireland and Romania.

  • Employment Manager

    Apple

    (Staffing and Recruiting industry)

    1999 — 2003 (4 years )

    I was responsible for all sourcing, screening and interviewing of candidates as well as preparing and closing all offers. I also managed University relations, this included intern and new graduate hiring. I also managed contract recruiters, vendors and recruiting resources.
