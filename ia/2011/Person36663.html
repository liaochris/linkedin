Rhonda Spellman’s Summary

Autism Awareness began when my son severely reacted to a 'required' immunization in 2001 and disappeared into a silent world for 2 1/2 years. With a DNA analysis we discovered that he had been mercury poisoned and had received over 200% of the "safe" amount of mercury. I wondered, "Can I hold BIG Pharma accountable?" (no). Big Pharma certainly contributes to our problems but I believe that autism is triggered by three things:

* Environmental Toxins: Our world is an unregulated waste dump with over 6 trillion tons of 85,000+ chemicals produced annually. 80% have never been screened for their affects on humans. Many of these chemicals simulate autoimmune diseases.
* Medical Toxins: Toxic ingredients used to suppress symptoms of illnesses further suppress our immune systems. The result: over 100 auto immune disorders (when our immune system literally attacks itself). Lupus, Crohns, and Lyme's are among well known.
* Genetic Toxins: Every year the branches in our genetic trees splinter in new and unique ways due to weaker contributing DNA. Weak links in our DNA make us more susceptible to reacting to the ever-increasing levels of toxins.

My programs for adults are designed to teach them to understand what Autism and auto immune disorders are (and aren't). I give you information, tools and a positive road map on my mission to Reach, Teach and Inspire Those with Special Abilities AND the Adults who Love Them!

My programs for children include an educational assembly 'Penguin Particulars' designed to teach them to embrace diversity. Which penguin is most like you? In groups of 30 - 300 we write and illustrate a complete 16 page book. The new authors / illustrators receive an award and take home their book that day!

I am interested in
*Speaking about Autism & Auto Immune Disorders.
*Teaching groups of children to write and illustrate educational books.
*Partnering with a publisher to make these stories I help children to write available and to create a video series.
