Teun den Dekker’s Summary

Born in 1983 in The Netherlands and always looking for new adventures. After a Bachelor in Services Management and a Master in Innovative Hospitality Management I was ready for a challenge. Something different. Working on things that matter, things that are fun. And all of this in an innovative and creative setting. In 2006 I started Co-creations with my partner in Spain and in 2007 I went 'back home' to develop Co-creations in The Netherlands.

Teun den Dekker’s Specialties:

I am always looking for answers. Putting pieces together seems to be an going process in my mind, where logic and creativy are going hand in hand.
