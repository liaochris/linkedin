Mike Moore’s Summary

Leader, teacher and motivator, I help people live and work to their personal best. I am the leader of "The Sales Revolution"!

I bring a practical perspective and humor to my keynote addresses, seminars and transformational courses.

My expertise is in new home sales, design centers, floor covering and big ticket retailing. I have been developing leadership, sales management, sales training, interior designer training and customer retention programs for these industries for 30 years.

My courses teach intentions changes that create behavioral changes to heal the dysfunctional buyer and seller relationship.

I have been researching, writing and speaking about the affects of the internet and technology on sales, marketing and customer retention since 1992. I now teach how to use technology and social media in the sales process.

In addition to my business courses I have developed personal performance and motivational life coaching courses.

I have consulted or trained for many industry leaders including William Lyon Homes, Standard Pacific Homes, Pardee Homes, Warmington Homes, DR Horton, Laing Luxury, Emaar Properties Beverly Hills, The Olson Company, Dunsmore Homes, Taylor Woodrow, Pacific Communities, John Laing Homes, Presley Homes, Trimark Pacific Homes, Spectrum Skanska Development, Chateau Interiors & Design, Shaw Ind., Mohawk Ind., Allied Chemicals, DuPont, Wundaweve, SCFCA an affiliate of WFCA, NHFA and the New York Design Center.

I have also been a featured speaker at industry events including Eliant National Roundtable, IBS, PCBC, CE Pro, Surfaces, TecHomExpo, High Point Furniture Market and BIA events.

I believe in abundance, sharing and helping others reach their personal best. I teach and believe in long term selfishness, the philosophy of, "Living your dreams by helping others live theirs".

My mission, is to live to my personal best everyday and make a positive difference in the lives of every person I interact with.

  • Owner

    Making Customers, Inc.

    (Privately Held; Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    May 2005 — Present (5 years 9 months)

    Making Customers, Inc. is leading “The Sales Revolution” with new intentions training.

    We are a full service consulting and business education company. We specialize in revolutionizing the sales process by healing the dysfunctional consumer and salesperson relationship. We implement new attitudes, skills and actions.

    Our services include education, training, consulting and live-video coaching to serve new home builders, design centers, floor covering and big ticket retailers.

    Mike Moore and Kirk Chittick met in 1996 when Kirk, as Vice President of Sales and Marketing for a leading home builder, hired Mike to speak at his company’s sales rally. After hearing Mike present his Making Customers sales strategy, it was evident they were teaching many of the same business and sales philosophies. They continued working together until they became partners in Making Customers, Inc. in 2009.

    Making Customers courses include all Mike and Kirk have learned and experienced from coaching, teaching and business over the past thirty years. They continue to update their existing courses and develop new courses to meet the needs of a changing business environment.

    Business courses include, “New Home Retailing”, “Finished Home Design”, “Floor Fashion Selling”, “Customer First Leadership”, “Customer First Sales Coaching”, “Customer First Sales Training”, “Keeping Customers”, “Selling with Technology” and “Communicating with Social Media”.

    Personal performance, motivation and life coaching courses include, “Life and Other Tough Assignments”, “When Winning Isn’t Everything”, “Personal Attitude Management” and “Responsible Communication”.

    Making Customers, Inc. is a leader in transformational business education and new business intentions training. Our keynote addresses, seminars and courses educate and reenergize employees, resulting in increased sales, higher profits, business growth, and customer and employee loyalty.
