Alex Beutler's Summary

Alex began his recruitment career with Hays Resources & Mining in Sydney in 2005, where he was responsible for professional metalliferous recruitment for appointments across NSW, Victoria & Tasmania. Recruitment assignments covered technical and management roles in the areas of mine production, maintenance, processing, HSEC and management.

In 2006, Alex established the global mining recruitment practice for CDI AndersElite, where he recruited for technical mining appointments across Australia and internationally for expatriate assignments. Destinations for expat recruitment assignments have included China, Thailand, PNG, Laos, Vietnam, Indonesia, Senegal, Botswana, Brazil, New Caledonia, Mongolia and many more.

Since 2006, Alex has specialised in the recruitment of technical services, geology and executive appointments within the mining industry for appointments across the globe. Typical assignments include mining engineering, geology, geotechnical engineering and mine management.

Alex joined Talent2 in late 2008 to continue his recruitment specialisation in executive, technical services and geoscience appointments in the mining industry, for both Australian and expatriate appointments. Alex is supported in his role by a full-time Candidate Researcher, ensuring Talent2's seamless ability to proactively source the industry's best candidates and unrivalled depth of market knowledge and networks.

He holds a BBA (Hons) and BA (Psych) from Macquarie University.

Specialties

Executive search and targeted selection recruitment assignments for Australian & international professional appointments within geology & geoscience, mining engineering and senior management.
