Kimberly Parker’s Summary

My professional and personal experiences have given me the opportunity to work in many fields with many great people. I've worked with children and adults in roles that required teaching, selling, coaching, managing, operating, writing, simplifying, executing, leading and creating.

I am happiest in my work when I use my writing skills to help someone else. I use my love of the written word to write teaching material, help my business clients clearly and creatively communicate their unique messages, and in my own personal blogging and book writing. I also write articles for magazine, web-posting and newsprint. Often these are related to Christianity, autism or business.

I am a mom to three amazing children. My oldest son has autism. My husband and I own a sales and marketing company called Red Giraffe and I am co-founder of For the Sake of ONE Ministries. My first book was released in August. It is titled Radical Love...Forever Changed. I am currently writing a book about autism, faith and parenting for which I am seeking a publisher.

I look forward to connecting with other professionals in sales & marketing, writing & editing or in fields related to autism.
