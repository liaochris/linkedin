Christian Pagh's Summary

MA, Cultural planner, Partner in culture design company UiWE (as You-I-We). Our solutions and products explore the field between culture and space, strategy and design.

I've worked with a range of projects within the field of culture, design and urban planning. The last couple of years my main focus has been cultural planning, identity, strategy and communication for both public agencies, companies and organisations. See more at www.uiwe.dk

I work with film, both artistically and as part of our company's production. My feature documentary "Battle of Rabbit Town" (selected for CPH Dox and a range of other festivals) is a personal portrait about democracy, corruption and political passion in a poor neighbourhood in Brazil. See www.battleofrabbittown.com

Specialties

Cultural planning, concept design, project management, identity, branding, filmmaking

Christian Pagh's Experience

Cultural Planner, Partner

UiWE Culture Design

Architecture & Planning industry

January 2009 – Present (2 years 3 months)

We are a team working in the field of strategic design, urban planning & social innovation. UiWE comes out of desire to make different and more integrated solutions in the borderland between communication and space, between idea and design, between values and everyday life.

Our outset is deep interest in the culture of interaction. How do the You's and I's become a We? How do we enable collective, creative solutions to everyday challenges? We believe that can we improve the way we meet and interact in cities, in organizations, in business.

As we see it, it's a matter of understanding and developing the links between people. That's why we offer a variety of cultural, architectural, artistic approaches to rethink the links: between you and your employees, users or citizens.
