L. Blake Harvey's Summary

As President and Founder of The Lawrence Blake Group,

The Lawrence Blake Group is a New York City based Event Management and Public Relations Company specializing in emerging artists and businesses. Officially operated as two individual companies, Lawrence Blake Event Management and Lawrence Blake Public Relations frequently pulls resources from both brands. As the first of it's kind, we are able to completely cater to our clients by providing creative and promotional services from preparation to execution.

Innovative creativity is the key to success in the field of Public Relations as well as Management. The Lawrence Blake Group thrives on maintaining superb relationships with clients and allows for prosperity in their individual industries by providing services that are practical, effective and affordable.

Lawrence Blake Event Management specializes in sponsorship based events, media events and tour management.

The Lawrence Blake Society for Entrepreneurship is the way The Lawrence Blake Group gives back. Founder and President L. Blake Harvey personally understands the need for programs that aid the development of business ownership ideals in teens. The Lawrence Blake Society for Entrepreneurship works directly with high school students across the country to provide them with the necessary skills and tools to start and operate their very own businesses.

Specialties

Brand Management, Strategic Marketing, Public Relations, Business Planning, Event Management.
