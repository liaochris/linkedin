Will Tyler's Summary

As CEO of a leading UK branding, design and production company I am currently keen to network with professionals from the construction industry looking for solutions which do not harm the environment. Octink have extensive experience in designing and building creative environments.

►Come and visit Octink’s manufacturing plant and project management facility◄

I welcome visitors to our ISO 14001 operation in Brentford west London to see how we are leading the industry in ◘ green production ◘ and why we recently came 6th in the Sunday Times Greenest companies for the manufacturing category.

We have a complete range of substrates, materials and products which support our R3 programme (Recycle, Reuse, Reduce). The programme is core to our proposition and as a result is attracting many environmentally conscious companies.

Octink has extensive experience in the ‘green’ production of exhibition graphics, retail graphics, marketing suites, signage, hoarding production & installation. We have recently been awarded ISO 9001; a testament to our investments.

As the problems around the world continue to impact on our customers, we are actively looking at ways to maximise their exposure by proposing new ideas, methods, and opportunities in a cost effective manner.

There are many different ways this can be achieved and we are balancing all of this whilst maintaining an ethical purchasing arrangement supported with an incredibly strong environmental agenda.
