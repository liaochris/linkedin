Nigel T Packer's Summary

Nigel T Packer
Managing Director at Business for Business Internet Marketing Ltd. Established in 1999 the company specialises in Internet Marketing Knowledge delivering that knowledge through consultancy, training and publications.

Author
'Internet Marketing - How to get a Website that Works for Your Business’ Nigel T Packer’s first book published by Constable and Robinson.
The Book is the culmination of many years of knowledge gathered through research and whilst working with clients from small, medium and some large companies. The work includes internet marketing strategies from the design of the website, to the user experience, through to the promotion of the website.
In the book Nigel takes a straightforward approach, using non-technical language. It is full of insights and real-world observations, practical techniques and actionable strategies. The book focuses on a way of thinking to meet the demands of the Digital age. It has received many reviews from business owners and the Chartered Institute of Marketing who stock it for their students to read as part of their studies.

Business leader
In recent years Nigel held posts in many organisations including the Chairmanship of the Institute of Business Advisers in South Wales. He was Inaugural Chair of the South West Wales Association of Purchasing and Supply. Currently he is sharing his knowledge and giving the benefit of his experience with Federation of Small Business in his region.

Business Speaker
Nigel has been booked as keynote speaker at Business Conferences and Seminars around the capital cities of Europe. Recently he has spoken in London, Paris, Sofia, Budapest and his home capital city of Wales Cardiff.

Each time he speaks he engages the audience with his direct approach, he educates with his insights and practical tips and he entertains with his wit and observations that make this complex subject understandable to all who attend.

Nigel T Packer's Publications

  • Internet Marketing: How to get a Website that Works for Your Business

    • Constable and Robinson
    • August 28, 2008
    Authors: Nigel T Packer

    Seventy per cent of businesses have a website. But 90 per cent of those websites don’t fulfil the expectations of their business owners and don’t provide the services their customers are seeking. Nigel T. Packer guides business owners and managers through the maze of getting a successful website. He explains the principles that website owners need to follow to provide the right service to their customers and to maximise sales, they must: Define the type of website the particular business needs. Understand what customers want from the website. Analyse problems and defects in their existing website. Select the correct keywords to target profitable customers. Enable search engines to find the website quickly and rank it highly. Make sure the website is easy to use and accessible to all. Research competitors’ websites. Find the right web developers and communicate effectively with them. Test the website thoroughly before it goes live.
