Eddie Ulrich's Experience

Creative Director / Owner

Fuzzy Duck Studios

Privately Held; Entertainment industry

2001 – Present (10 years)

Fuzzy Duck Studios is an animation and visual development resource.

Trained in the art of traditional animation and illustration, the staff at Fuzzy Duck Studios can help turn any client’s vision into a visual reality and convey that reality to the desired audience with distinctive flair and therefore added value.

Fuzzy Duck Studios provides visual development for intellectual properties as well as animated logos, opening credits, visual effects, character development, transitional elements, animated shorts and trailers.

Beyond its fee for service and collaborative projects, Fuzzy Duck Studios is also an incubator for its own character driven properties. Be sure to visit the Projects page for samples of properties currently being developed.
