David Kalman’s Summary

Senior media professional with deep experience as a chief editor, business technology expert, publisher, marketing communicator, and team leader.

I help publishers with product strategy, organizational development, sales, marketing, and more.

I help other organizations with "content marketing," i.e., using content and activities to engage customers and prospects.

Current Goal: To work with diverse clients on a mix of short- and long-term projects, for example:

* Temp publisher assignments
* Publication redesigns
* Sales management/assessment
* Media portfolio analysis
* Online and social media strategies for small business
* Custom/branded media development
* Brand development and copywriting
* Event marketing for small business

I can work by the hour, day, week, or project. I would also consider full-time employment for the right opportunity.
