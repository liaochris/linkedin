Eager to bring value and expand his own content knowledge as well as his ability to adapt and apply a diversity of consulting skills, Dennis has built experience in various knowledge areas:

Industries: Government, SMB, Banking, Utilities, Healthcare, High tech, Telco, Consulting
Management positions: Departmental, Interim and Project management
Business processes: HR, Service management, Logistics, Sales & marketing, etc.
Applications: large ERP (Oracle/PeopleSoft/SAP), point solutions and custom build

Dennis combines his experience with a no-nonsense results oriented drive, sharp thinking, a keen eye for quality and a relationship focused attitude.
