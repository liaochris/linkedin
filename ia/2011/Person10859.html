David Kmiotek's Summary

Experienced inside sales professional experienced in metals (both ferrous and non-ferrous metals) in tubing and flat rolled forms. I'm currently in a career transition for a full-time sales position with a growing company which has opportunities to advance within the company. I'm also open to relocating outside the state of Michigan.

I don't want to limit myself to just the metals industry as I feel that my skills and experience could make for a smooth transition to another commodity. I would be open to other position titles such as Customer Service, Entry Level Outside Sales, Account Management, Sales Assistant/Coordinator since my inside sales career included these types of duties that these positions entail.

If you should have any questions relative to my qualifications, please feel free to contact me. If you would like a copy of my resume in MS Word format, please feel free to contact me as well.

Specialties

Extensive knowledge of both ferrous and non-ferrous metals. Able to read blueprints and use both an AS400 & UNIX systems. Also have proficient knowledge in computers and computer software, such as MS Word, MS Excel & MS Outlook. Experience processing quotations, managing accounts (shipping, quality, engineering, accounting issues). Excellent communication and coordination with other personal within an organization (engineering, production, quality, accounting, management, etc.).
