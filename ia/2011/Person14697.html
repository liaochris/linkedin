Felix Wetzel's Summary

As Group Marketing Director of Jobsite, Felix Wetzel is responsible for the brand innovation and development of the group and all entities within its portfolio. He has overseen the Jobsite brand journey since 1999 and was most recently responsible for initiatives including the UK's largest ever recruitment advertising campaign (2008) which saw Jobsite on TV for the first time, and the integration of new media platforms such as Jobs-by-Twitter services and smartphone apps.

Felix is also an established speaker and blogger in the area of digital marketing, social media and online recruitment, and a qualified NLP Practitioner.

Prior to joining Jobsite, Felix worked for/with brands including Phillipp Morris, Beiersdorf, AutoScout 24, HypoVereinsbank, and Vh1, as well as several regional media organisations in Germany. He holds a MSc in Marketing from The University of Glamorgan and a DipM from the Chartered Institute of Marketing. He is also recognised as Member, Chartered Institute of Marketing.

Specialties

Digital Marketing, Brand Development and Building (including TV advertising), Innovation, Website developments, E-Commerce, Research, NLP.
