Mike May’s Summary

Versatile, Experienced All-round IT Professional
with a Strong Business Focus

With experience encompassing a wide range of business sectors, technologies, project types and team roles, Mike demonstrates impressive versatility. A strong business focus combined with the ability to quickly and easily acquire new skills and knowledge as the situation requires, a good communicator, self organised and with the ability to organise others, Mike is a first-rate asset to any organisation.

His past experience ranges from small companies to major multinationals, to all of whom he has brought identifiable value. Working in a variety of sectors, from retail and distribution, through utilities and telecoms to energy and financials, he has continuously demonstrated his unique ability to assimilate and fit into new business environments. Exposure to all manner of technology solutions, from desktops to mainframes, from back-office software to enterprise systems, have instilled an ability to rapidly acquire and utilise technical skills. His experiences on projects of all sizes enable him to work effectively within any type of technology effort.

A continuing desire to learn new business and technology skills as well as find new ways to apply what he has already learned in enabling the business to become more effective make Mike an innovative and imaginative practitioner of the art and science of information technology.
