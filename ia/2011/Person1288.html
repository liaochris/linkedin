Alessandro Mure''s Summary

The word I like best.

Learn.

23 word presentation.

Communicate is to bring together. I bring together by writing and public speaking on ICT and literature, and doing pr for a publisher.

Got time?
272 word presentation.

My education begins at the American School of Milan, which I attended since kindergarden. I grew up in an international environment, in close contact with people coming from all over the world. After Junior High School, I chose the Liceo Classico (classical humanities high school) and then Modern Literature at the State University of Milan.

During the years of university, I developed a deep interest for communication. I started working for a university newspaper, for which I wrote literary reviews. I then worked in a local newspaper in the southern outskirts of Milan. After two years, I have been called to work as free lance journalist for “Il Giorno” of Milan. Meanwhile, I started to work also as press officer for public authorities. This job gave me the opportunity to face increasing responsibilities and to coordinate small teams of journalists and press officers.

I then decided to deepen my knowledge: I attended post-graduate degree “Communication and Organization of Events” at the Catholic University of Milan.

After that I built and followed media relation plans in various practices (culture, hi-tech, food and beverage and others).

Currently I write on Information and Communication Technologies, on books and literature (especially science fiction). I am chairman and speaker in ICT events. I work for a publisher as reader and pr manager. Sometimes I sleep.

Years of experience have led me to love communication. Motivation derives mainly from curiosity. It’s not about instinct for news, writing ability, relation and organizational skills. I find that it is essential to possess the desire to learn, to be open to different inputs. I’m lucky because I possess this inner drive.

Specialties

Giornalismo, relazioni pubbliche, relazioni con i media, eventi, editing, public speaking, traduzione.
