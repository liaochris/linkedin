Deepak Goel's Summary

A new generation Indian entrepreneur venturing his way into specialized consulting, professional education and outsourcing by pioneering MPO (Marketing Process Outsourcing) for international geographies. He is a strategic driver of BFG Corporation.

Under the hood of BFG Corporation, He has also pioneered in bringing in industry insights to professional education sector, SMEs & Start ups by way of IIS (Industry Interface Services).An Economics graduate (from University of Delhi) with an MBA from Symbiosis (SCMHRD, Pune, India )in IT & Management Systems and International Marketing, Deepak has worked across geographies in India, US, Canada and Europe for more than 12+ years in B2B space IT, ITES, Consulting and BPO.

He is a Strategic marketing specialist has worked in India and US on number of consulting assignments related to Sales & Marketing, Project Management and CRM.

He has facilitated setting up of sales organizations in new regions and building successful Centre of Excellence (CoE) for Key Account Management, Lead/Campaign Management and Customer Services.
During his professional stint, Deepak conducted various in house trainings on areas like Strategic Marketing, Business Development, Sales Analytics, market Planning and Process Consulting.

He is much sought after for Guest Sessions/ Building Industry Interface by B-Schools and other such professional associations including various start ups and SMEs.

Specialties

Business Development ,Digital Marketing, Business Planning,Account Management, International Marketing, Marketing Communications, & Sales Operations
