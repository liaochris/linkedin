Eben Brown's Summary

I am just a storyteller at heart.

No, really. I’m a guy who likes to tell you interesting things about your world, and specifically in your community. You’d be surprised at the amount of not-so-ordinary things that go on in your backyard. What makes it a good story? That it IS going on in your backyard, and you don’t even know it!

Along the way, I have worked at WFLA/Tampa, WFLF/Orlando, Florida’s Radio Network, CBS Radio News, WHTZ/New York City, WLIR/Spring Valley, WRVO/Syracuse, and WABC/New York City. I've also popped up in the past on CNN Headline News during the Nancy Grace program, and I have been known to turn up on Glenn Beck’s radio and television shows.

Specialties

I have no real credentials to do this job other than the ability to observe and regurgitate. I grew up in New York. I have an A.A. in Humanities and Social Sciences and B.A. in Communications. Meanin: I didn’t have too many difficult classes in college.

I witnessed the September 11th attacks in New York City, was one of a long line of network war coverage producers, ran into and out of plenty of hurricanes and spent a couple of weeks meeting U.S. Troops, Seamen, Airmen, & Marines in Iraq.
