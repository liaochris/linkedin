Ed Allmann’s Experience

  • Vice President - Sales and Marketing

    Turning Stone Resort and Casino

    (Privately Held; Gambling & Casinos industry)

    March 2010 — Present (11 months)

    Responsibilities include oversight of resort and casino marketing, conference and group sales and services, entertainment and events, player services and VIP services.
    Turning Stone Golf and Spa Resort and Casino is the premier resort destination in central New York. Located in the heart of the scenic Mohawk Valley, between the cities of Syracuse and Utica, the resort comprises 1,200 acres. It is the fifth-largest tourist attraction in New York State drawing 4.5 million visitors annually.
    A distinguished roster of performers have appeared at Turning Stone, The resort’s 5,000-seat event center, adjacent to the Tower Hotel, has hosted celebrity performers such as Stevie Wonder, Alicia Keys, Fergie, Josh Groban, Michael Buble, Jay Leno, and Tim McGraw. An 800-seat showroom has presented Tony Bennett, Michael Bolton, and Kenny Rogers among other famous talent. The resort also includes a dance club called Lava which currently hosts a comedy club, as well.
    The 120,000 square-foot casino at Turning Stone has high-stakes bingo, live poker, 103 table games including blackjack, craps, roulette, pai gow, and mini-baccarat, as well as more than 2,300 cashless instant multi-games, keno, and pull tabs.
    Turning Stone has four hotels, two of which have earned AAA Four Diamond status, and features five golf courses (designed by Tom Fazio, Robert Trent Jones, Jr. and Rick Smith). Its Fazio-designed Atunyote Golf Course hosted the Turning Stone Championship on the PGA TOUR for four years.
    Other notable aspects of the resort include the Sportsplex, an all-season golf dome with two tiered hitting area and forty separate hitting stations and an adjacent tennis dome with four climate-controlled indoor courts; Skana, a 33,000-square-foot destination spa; and Ahsi, a separate day spa.
    The resort has 21 dining venues including the Four Diamond rated Wildflowers, and more than 100,000-square-feet of meeting space.
