Magnus Hellberg's Summary

I have lived in Los Angeles now for about 20 years, and just love the L.A lifestyle. It is great to be able to swim in the ocean, go skiing or snowboarding and take a trip to the desert all in one day, where else in the world can you do that.
Even though I was born and raised in Sweden I have lived and worked in USA for such a long time that I consider my self American. There are a few things I miss about Sweden, like some food dishes and my friends and family but thanks to the internet and all the social media it is so easy to stay in touch.

Just last year we started up a high-end real estate boutique brokerage and acquisitions firm and just in the first year our company was voted as one of the Best Places to Work in Los Angeles in 2010 -- we have a fun company culture and a dynamic social media program.

When I am not helping my friends finding a place to live or make a move I love:
Playing Tennis.
Snow Skiing.
Playing Golf.
Hang out with my kids.
Eating.
Going to the desert.
Among other things but the point is that you can do all those things all year around here in Los Angeles.
