Katie DeYoung, CLDP’s Summary

I have worked in the retirement planning industry for the last five years with alternative IRA and 401(k) investments, such as: real estate, precious metals, hard-money lending, and so many more unique options. After processing these transactions, I noticed that most of our clients were utilizing Limited Liability Companies in order to have checkbook control of their retirement funds, and also to minimize their investment liability. It is from there that my partner, Timothy Schubert, and I decided to go out on our own and form Blue Diamond Documents.

Katie DeYoung, CLDP’s Specialties:

I am a Ceritified Legal Document Preparer. Therefore, we can process LLC's in all 50 states per our clients' request. We specialize in LLC's with IRA's or 401(k)'s involved and work with the client's custodian to provide all the necessary documentation.
