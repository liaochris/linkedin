Gerry Tseng’s Summary

A senior member of P&G’s Global Digital Marketing team, currently integrated within Procter & Gamble's Future Works organization with focus on their MDVIP business. MDVIP is a national network of top primary care physicians who provide highly personalized care to their patients, emphasizing wellness, prevention and early detection.

Now here's my personal take on why I'm such a BIG FAN of MDVIP:

2 years ago I signed up as a patient myself to see why others spoke so highly about MDVIP. While skeptical I figured I'd try it for a year and if not convinced I'd go back to my old primary care physician. Within 30 days I was sold and haven't looked back since. Even though I'm young, fit and hardly ever sick I discovered so many benefits that supported an even healthier, more vibrant life (their promise). Among many other benefits the wait times were GONE, the ability to quickly get my AMAZING physician's help (even when traveling), and no longer worrying about life's health surprises made this a worthy upgrade.

This model is THAT good! In no time at all it felt like I stumbled onto the best-kept secret in health care. It's like going from a dial-up modem to high-speed internet, from old bulky to LED televisions, a once-you-pop-you-can't-stop experience: there's no going back once you try it.

And then I was invited to join the team in March! I couldn't pass it up to grow & advocate a model I'm deeply passionate about that works for me, my lifestyle and an OVERWHELMING majority of our patients.

MDVIP enables health-minded individuals across the US to get the very best medical care through our top-credentialed doctors in the network. The program is focused on early detection, prevention, wellness & tighter patient/physician relationships. To me it's priceless for piece of mind that I now have a great shot at a longer, healthier life expectancy without any surprises.

Visit MDVIP.com and schedule a free wellness consultation with a local MDVIP doctor to see for yourself!
