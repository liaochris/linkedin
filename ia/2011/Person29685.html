Melissa Lallo's Summary

If you can put your logo on it, I sell it. I love building brand identity, logos, marketing plans and the process of turning those things into tangible products that enhance your overall brand. I have access to millions of products that I can specifically choose to fit your needs. I love options too! So, when you ask me to help with a project, I'll give you as many options as you wish.
