Charles Chan's Summary

Charles Chan has over 14 years of experience in digital marketing and technology consultancy for many multinational clients and large scale digital projects. Charles has worked in the capacity of Digital Director of Publicis Modem Shanghai, Chief Technology Consultant of Publicis Modem Asia, Head of Digital of DraftFCB Hong Kong, Senior Vice President, Director of Technology of Digitas Greater China and Chief Technology Officer of Lemon Asia.

Charles has deep experience in digital strategy, delivery management, content management, CRM, e-commerce, loyalty program, social media marketing, search marketing and data analysis.

Client experience includes PayPal, Citi, Lee Kum Kee, Philips Lighting APR, Philips China, Sony China, PCCW / SUNDAY, Bank of China, Agriculture Bank of China, Hongkong Land, Hitachi GST, AT&T, etc.

With a first class bachelor degree in Computer Engineering and graduate diploma in management consultancy, Charles has deep knowledge in digital marketing technology, problem solving and consultancy skills.

Specialties

Digital Marketing Strategy, Technology management, Content management solutions, Search engine optimization and paid search management, Web Analytics, CRM, E-mail marketing, Social Media Marketing, E-commerce Operations Management, Qualified Individuals on Google Adwords and Google Analytics.
