Kevin Clearie’s Summary

As a Director at LSI, Kevin works directly with senior level executives and group EVP's on near and long term organizational alignment and management of the disciplines associated with running a $1B division. He successfully lead the integration team supporting global business operations during the merger of LSI and Agere Systems. Through his leadership and innovation he designed and implemented both the organizational structure and processes/systems required to integrate two complex operational business models into one.

Prior to his experience in LSI, Kevin served as the Business Operations Director for Agere systems. His contributions in this arena were focused on creating and managing the organizations responsible for flows required to load the factory and support "lean" processes and capacity management. Through his leadership Agere was able to reduce lead-times, and substantially improve customer responsiveness while driving down inventory levels. Kevin successfully lead the core business operations teams through significant organizational change required in bringing up a spin company as Agere was spun off from Lucent.

Prior to his work in Agere, Kevin was the Chief Procurement Director for the Microelectronics Divisions of Lucent Technologies. Kevin and his team were responsible for as much as $2 billion of spend in direct and indirect materials, manufacturing, and services associated with manufacturing operations in 5 countries, and over 100 service/office locations worldwide.

Kevin's diverse background also includes director level assignments in global semiconductor commodity management, international logistics and facility operations, product management and IT systems development through his 14 years at both AT&T and Lucent Technologies.

Kevin received his Bachelors of Science degree from Rutgers University, and has attended executive programs at the Univ. of Tennessee, Univ. of Michigan, and Columbia Univ.
