Steven Johnson’s Summary

ExecutiveSurf is a Global Talent Gateway.

ExecutiveSurf is a place individual executives access to plan their professional futures and to develop their career options.

ExecutiveSurf provides a solution for companies who want to snap out of dysfunctional recruiting habits and access a range of talent management services that actually address their requirements, both immediate and longer term.

ExecutiveSurf has 12 offices in Europe and Asia. We continue to develop our global web presence, and to build our sophisticated on-line toolset which allows each individual executive and client to proactively take control of their future.

With over 200,000 registered executives, we have the ideal starting point for you, whether you are a company looking for talent, or an individual executive looking to enhance your career or identify your next professional move.
