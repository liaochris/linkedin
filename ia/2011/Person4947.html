Connie Bereny's Summary

Located in southern California, our industry experience ranges from a wide variety of business, such as golf, construction, real estate, restaurants, financial, healthcare and small business. Our assignments have included corporate identities, collateral, packaging, promotions, print ads, brochures, publications, event and trade show planning, websites and public relations.

For over a decade we have built successful and lasting relationships by carefully examining our client's goals and objectives. We spend time getting to know them and their businesses. We research their products or services, their customers, their stores, their factories and their employees. Only then, after in-depth marketing strategy development, do we get our designers involved. We also recognize the value of teamwork with our clients who might already have a successful plan in place, and welcome the opportunity to provide needed support.

When presented with the marketing or design challenges of your company, we bring a wealth of experience. We've produced projects spanning a wide range of clients, services, products and markets. This depth and scope of marketing and design expertise is reflected in the quality and strength of the work we produce.

In order to provide the best possible results and the best possible service for our clients, our goal is to go beyond just being a vendor. We want to team with you and develop precise marketing strategies and materials that achieve your goals. We thoroughly familiarize ourselves with the standards, policies and personalities of your company by examining what has worked in the past and what may need a new approach. Success for our clients drives the strategy of any work we produce.

Women's Economic Ventures

Non-Profit; 11-50 employees; Education Management industry

September 2005 – Present (5 years 7 months)

Women's Economic Ventures (WEV, pronounced "weave") is a local, non-profit organization dedicated to creating an equitable and just society through the economic empowerment of women across the globe.

WEV provides opportunities for entrepreneurs in every phase of the business lifecycle: start-up, launch, grow and sustain/exit. The programs and services offered fall under the areas of training, access to capital & business services. All services are open to women and men, and offered in English and Spanish.

Journey through our 14-week Self Employment Training course and learn how to write a business plan to start, operate and expand your business.
