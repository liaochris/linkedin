Shane Kelbel’s Summary

Credit Repair Expert providing life improving solutions to American consumers through expert help is my mission!

The term Expert is used to often and without merit. My stripes have been earned with over a decades experience in the credit services industry and helping thousands of consumers fight back to improve their credit reports quickly and legally.

As the Credit Repair Expert of choice by Fox6now.com, I invite the opportunity to share my in depth credit knowledge with consumers looking for answers and lenders in need of solutions.

I strive to be a key that opens the door of opportunity for those who may have once been locked out with no way in. My decades plus of in the trenches credit experience with extensive credit law training delivers the golden key inside.

I have been accomplishing this through industries with some very challenging dispositions since 1999. I began my mission in the automotive industry and then transitioned into the Mortgage business becoming a top producer in both of these professions by delivering fast solutions to credit challenges.

My niche showed itself in each industry which was helping people overcome credit challenges to achieve their goals.

This opened my eyes when the “Credit Crisis” really started to show itself as lending guidelines tightened and doors closed.

I used my inside lending experience to educate myself with the internal working of creditors, credit bureaus, and collection companies to become a Nationally Certified Credit Repair Expert.

My focus is the credit industry continuing to study the evolution of credit and credit scoring to produce positive results for the people I encounter.

I strive to change the perceived persona's by providing life changing benefits.
