Ian Chamberlain's Summary

Ian Chamberlain – The Specialist Keynote Speaker for your Medical Sales Conference.

During his award winning career in medical sales Ian began training and coaching others in 1997. Fascinated by “what makes the best the best”, over the next 2 years Ian developed his Mastery of Customer Access System. This lead to him being one of the highest earning medical sales people in the United Kingdom.

Following his passion, Ian co-founded Fully Equipped Ltd in 2002 with a goal of energizing and inspiring pharmaceutical and medical device companies to bring a pride, passion and purpose back into their medical selling..

Today, Fully Equipped’s groundbreaking and unique workshops are used across Europe helping Medical Sales people get better access to their key customers. The programmes are known for delivering behavioural change for their participants and a spectacular return on investment for their commissioning healthcare clients.

Since 2002 Ian has run hundreds of seminars and spoken to thousands of medical sales people from over 50 countries.

Ian’s conference keynote talks draw on his specific and extensive industry experience. Tailored to his clients goals and themes they entertain, stimulate and provoke both thought and transformational change.

The leading expert in his field, Ian’s audiences include:

Abbott Laboratories, Bayer, Bristol Myers Squibb, AstraZeneca, Genentech, Roche, Wyeth, MerckSerono, Schering-Plough, Nutricia, Meda, Solvay, Chiesi, Menarini, Medtronic, Stryker, Rocket Medical, The Pharmaceutical sales Managers Group and many others.
