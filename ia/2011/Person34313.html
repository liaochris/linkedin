Paul T Ferrante’s Summary

I am an independent consultant specializing in the implementation and maintenance of SAP's Asset Module (AM). In addition to performing all the AM configuration steps needed to meet client’s requirement's, I gather business requirements, convert/reconcile legacy data, perform unit and integration testing, develop user training and configuration scripts, lead training classes and provide end user support. My background includes 8 full asset conversions and several system upgrades meeting both US GAAP/Tax and global regulatory requirements.

In addition to my SAP Asset conversion/configuration expertise, I have over 25 years of financial accounting/managerial accounting experiences and, as a consultant, have worked in the energy, telecom, automotive, pharmaceutical, aerospace A & D and retail industries.
My consulting expertise also includes Business Support Group specialist for Assets, Project Systems, Investment Mgt., FICO GL, Internal Orders and Accounts Payables, (US, Italy, Netherlands, Switzerland, Belgium, Germany, Britain) and as a Global Team lead for a full cycle redesign SAP implementation of AA, and AP along with experience in CO, GL, PS, IM and Costing.

Paul T Ferrante’s Specialties:

Configuration of CoD to meet all US Tax requirements, MM, MQ, Special Bonus Depreciation, etc. This includes configuring Asset History Versions which satisfy US Statutory requirments.
