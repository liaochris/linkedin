Heather Whaling’s Summary

An award-winning, seasoned communicator, Heather Whaling fuses strategic thinking, strong writing skills and creativity to deliver public relations, social media and marketing results. As president of Geben Communication, Heather advises nonprofit organizations and small- to mid-size businesses about new media's influence on online and offline communication -- and the value of integrating online media strategies with traditional approaches.

Heather co-moderates the popular #pr20chat, a weekly exploration of social media's influence on public relations, and recently launched pr20chat.com, a hub to discuss PR and social media trends, best practices and what's next. She is also a featured blogger for Nike Women's "Make Yourself Movement." She has been quoted in BusinessWeek.com and Inc.com, as well as a dozen of industry publications, about the influence of technology on public relations.

Thanks to nearly a decade of PR agency work, Heather has extensive experience in a variety of industries, including nonprofit organizations, professional services (e.g., law and accounting firms), health care, real estate/development, software/technology, education and public affairs. Her clients have been featured in TechCrunch, Fortune Small Business, Entrepreneur Magazine, the New York Times, Wall Street Journal, Associated Press, Washington Post, New York Daily News and the Miami Herald, as well as regional and trade publications.

Heather loves social good, sports (especially the Yankees!), her iPhone and a good glass of wine. Connect with her on her blog (www.prtini.com), Twitter (twitter.com/prtini) or via email at heather [at] gebencommunication.com.

Heather Whaling’s Specialties:

Strategic planning; media relations; interactive marketing and social media; branding; crisis communication; message development and deployment; public affairs and grassroots organizing; event planning; technical, news and feature writing; and public speaking.
