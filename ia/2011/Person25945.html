I have personally (not outsourced or hired someone else to) sold more than $1Million worth of products in the last 3 years, using the internet, the phone and selling from stage.

In 2009, while everyone else was crying about the Global Financial Crisis and the death of the seminar industry, I increased my business by 400% (without employing a single person).

I regularly pre-sell in excess of $25,000 worth of products – before I even start creating them.
