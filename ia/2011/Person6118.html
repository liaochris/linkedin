Brett Seyler's Summary

As General Manager of Union for Unity Technologies, I'm responsible for nearly every aspect of this new initiative: strategy, operations, finance, and recruitment.

Union aims to help developers earn more revenue from their Unity-based projects by bringing these projects to new platforms. Target platforms for Union will vary widely, but we have our eyes set on large audiences and markets ripe for disruption such as connected TVs and set top boxes, mobile phones, tablets, and other gaming-capable computing devices.

Developers can sign up for Union at unity3d.com/union. Platform owners and "app store" operators should reach out to me directly (use LinkedIn! :)

In addition, I also serve as Unity Technologies' VP of Strategy, where I focus on new initiatives in R&D, licensing, platform adoption, and customer acquisition.

Prior to Unity, I spent three years at InstantAction (GarageGames) running the Torque business and leading company wide business development. I also have an education and research background mathematical finance and modeling.
