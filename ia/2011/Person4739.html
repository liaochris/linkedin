Ben Barren’s Summary

After completing 3 degrees incl BA/BBus (Mkg) @ Caulf_Monash + Masters of Enterprise Innovation (MEI) Swinburne; Ben has spent 15 years working in the online industry, starting at DDB in 1995, ninemsn as a Founding Product Planner in 1997; Virtual Communities in 1999 + he helped setup Sensis MediaSmart for the Telstra Network 2001-4. For the past 5 years he has worked in Social Media Industry with the company he setup Feedcorp with the gnoos Aussie Blog Engine + Social Media advising to clients via Red Barren.

Currently as tracked at his www.benbarren.com blog + www.twitter.com/benbarren, along with his love of Mornington Peninsula golf plus Rye + MFC Demons, Ben spends his time Monitoring the Social Media Space for clients - across Blogs, Micro-Blogs (Twitter/Friendfeed), Rich Media (Flickr + YouTube) as well as Traditional Media Sources online. Ben sets up (+ can manage) the social tracking + syndication systems for clients + agencies. Contact him via redbarren at gmail dot com.

As well as Social Media Monitoring, Ben is working with clients who want to syndicate in realtime popular user generated content to their website. This can be done to a local level - Such as property, tourism or lifestyle sites wanting to publish hyperlocal content by country, state, suburb region as well as user generated reviews for Accomodation, Property, Small Businesses + Restaurants.

Ben is working with clients in the Lifestyle, Alcohol + FMCG, Classifieds, Retail, Fashion + Shopping, Travel + Tourism Spaces. As well as Social Media Intelligence + Syndication - many clients want to target + engage High Traffic/Influence Social Media Commentators. Ben does this hands on Coversational Marketing with The Community.

Ben has received favourable coverage in AFR, Sydney Morning Herald, The Age, ZD-Net and over 300 blogs globally for his work in the Social Media Space.

In the previous boom The Age/SMH listed him as one of 30 people under 30 as future succesful entreprenuers.

Ben Barren’s Specialties:

1. Social Media Intelligence : tracking companies, brands, products, topics, locations across blogs, micro-blogs (twitter/friendfeed), video (youtube), images (flickr) and social networks.
2. Social Media Syndication: Delivering user generated content across blogs, twitter + rich media eg YouTube to Publisher website in designated client verticals at a hyperlocal level.
3. Conversational Community Marketing: Identifying, targeting + delivering high traffic/influence social media commentators.
