The Institute for Environment and Sustainability is part of the European Commission's JRC and provides its research results to policy makers - the ones who make and break and european "laws" for environment and sustainability in Europe.

Its research results are not just for policy makers in Brussels but also for European citizens, other scientists, journalists, science students and many other stakeholders. To communicate research results to all the various stakeholders and audiences is our main objective.

In this role I've been contributing to the drafting and editing of three publications* along with supporting internal communication and having some very fruitful brainstorming sessions, in which I learned I'm good at coming up with ideas. Case in point I created a roadmap for our website's redesign.

Apart from this so far I've also been liaising a lot with our graphics department, printing company representatives and our own Communication team. Having done both design and communication helps me understand both sides better and get better results.

Intern (Germany)

EPO - European Patent Organisation

International Affairs industry

September 2009 – December 2009 (4 months)

This is the hub for everything concerning European Patents and it's also an important player in the worldwide patent scene along with the other major patent offices. Obviously their influence in the world of innovation and science is important.

When I was there, I was part the European Patent Academy which offered specialised courses for national patent offices (French Patent office, Italian etc. ) as a way to keep these smaller national level offices up to date with the newest tools, databases and procedures.

My role was to support the planning of these workshops/trainings for the following year; I liaised with four other EPO offices in The Hague, Berlin and Vienna to synchronize our offer and settle clear dates for the offer. I also helped draft the descriptions for our workshop programme that were meant to be displayed online so National offices representatives would know what would be their highest interest. As a side interest I also help test the e-learning platform, which was still under development; it represented a new, more efficient way to attend workshops at a distance.
