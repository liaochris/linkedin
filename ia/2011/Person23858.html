Kaya Redford www.TeamUpForLife.com's Summary

Dynamic, prosperity-producing motivational speaker, life coach, NLP practitioner, and change catalyst, Kaya Redford brings people and principles together to transform life on the planet. As the founder of TEAM Up for Life (Think Excellence, Achieve Magnificence) Kaya leads "Think and Grow Rich" mastermind training sessions and workshops around the world.

He has coached literally thousands of people to achieve the life of their dreams, offering more than 100 mastermind workshops free of charge as a gift. He has facilitated numerous Science of Getting Rich Club workshops sponsored by seminal New Thought leaders Bob Proctor, Michael Beckwith & Jack Canfield.

He has co-hosted and co-facilitated with Bob Proctor, Niurka & Mary Mannin Morrissey. Kaya is committed to supporting individuals in living lives of purpose and freedom and is always open to new ways of reaching those who are ready to hear his message.
