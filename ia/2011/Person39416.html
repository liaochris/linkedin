Sean McGinnis’s Summary

Sean McGinnis is a results-oriented leader with proven success at building and leading world-class teams for top global companies. He has been with Thomson Reuters for 14 years. He has 8+ years of experience in web site promotion including search/online marketing and over 10 years of sales/sales management experience. He has managed teams ranging in size from 3 to 37 employees and budgets from $1.5 to well over $3 million.

Sean currently serves as General Manager at MicroMash Bar Review where he is responsible for managing all aspects of the business. Prior to this role, he was Manager of SEO for Findlaw.com another Thomson Reuters business, where he was responsible for the strategic direction of all aspects of SEO for one of the largest vertically specialized digital agencies in the country. During his tenure at FindLaw, Sean grew the SEO team from 19 to 37 employees and was the catalyst behind one of the most successful product lines in FindLaw history.

Prior to that, Sean was a nationally recognized sales leader and sales manager for the firm. He managed a team of 12 sales professionals using a consultative sales process, serving customers across 7 states in the Northeast. He leveraged his business acumen and sales knowledge to create the sales training process subsequently used to train hundreds of industry sales professionals.

Upon graduation from law school in 1994 Sean passed the Pennsylvania Bar Exam. He has been a member of Search Engine Professional Organization (SEMPO), Minnesota Interactive Marketing Association (MIMA), Chicago Interactive Marketing Association (CIMA), and has been active in creating and building web properties since he founded DVD Verdict in April 1999. He has been a Web Awards judge since 2008. In 2007, he was named one of the "top in-house SEO's" by Natural Search Blog (http://www.naturalsearchblog.com/archives/2007/02/27/some-top-in-house-seos/).
