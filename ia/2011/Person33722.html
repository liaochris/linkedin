Pam McAllister’s Summary

I work with change-makers in business, public service, and nonprofit organizations -- leaders (and leaders-in-the-making) who have a burning desire to do something significant with their lives. I also work with speakers, writers, coaches, consultants, and others who use thought leadership and service-based businesses to make their contribution to the world.

To fuel their success, I share big ideas and concrete action steps on my blog -- "Making a Difference Made Possible" -- at http://www.pammcallister.com.

My main service is 1-on-1 coaching and consulting on vision, strategy, leadership, effective action, and financial sustainability — so you can thrive while you make a difference.

For a few select clients, I also offer "Get-It-Done Implementation" services to bring their ideas to life as quickly as possible. (And I pull together virtual teams to deliver on this, so I'm always looking for folks with complementary skills.)
