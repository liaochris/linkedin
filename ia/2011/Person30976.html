Brian Atkinson's Summary

Brian is a sought-after international and inspirational speaker and consultant for Online Ministry and is a Relational Leader and speaker in his church’s high School ministry. He enjoys filling guest-speaking slots whenever he gets the chance. Currently, the Director of Web Communications at American Bible Society, Brian has served as the Director of Digital Media at Gospel Communications International, responsible for the Bible Gateway. He worked as a communications director, technology manager, church-planter, radio announcer and ice cream man, and has served on the board of directors of an adoption agency. Recently, Brian was elected to the Internet Committee of the National Religious Broadcasters. Brian also runs his personal Facebook ministry, Bibleish, loves spending time with his lovely wife Sylvia and 16 year-old step-son Zeke, and just culled his t-shirt collection from over 50 to about a dozen.

Specialties

Internet Ministry, Digital Communications, Search Engine Optimization, Worship Environments, Adoption
