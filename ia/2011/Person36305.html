William J. Brown’s Summary

■ 13+ years of results-driven accomplishment leading multichannel commerce, marketing & operations, for FORTUNE 50, mid-cap & start-up companies. Financial services, hospitality & travel, consumer electronics, and mobile services.

■ 4+ years (concurrent while working in full-time roles) as a member of faculty in the MBA program at University of Miami School of Business, teaching digital business strategy and topics involving business innovation. Chaired "Executive Perspectives on E-Commerce Lecture Series" for 2 years. Over 25 journal publication credits on digital strategy since 1999; spoken or paneled at many large digital events in the United States, Canada and Europe.

■ People & process leader who has directed substantial revenue producing, customer-facing functions with direct staff accountability for as many as 300+ FTE across global locations and 13 teams. Led two e-commerce & digital marketing organizations with over $125M+ in annual sales.

■ Particular expertise in digital branding, acquisition marketing, customer engagement & retention programs. Have undertaken groundbreaking work in behavioral targeting, segmentation-based profiling, and re-marketing during all phases of the relationship cycle to drive rich engagement and conversion in high-volume e-commerce environments. Co-defined social media response strategy as part of interactive servicing leadership role at American Express.

■ Recently quoted in mainstream publications -- such as on page D-1 of The Wall Street Journal, August 25, 2010 ('I'll Have a Latte & An Outlet" link > http://on.wsj.com/9gBhtZ) -- along with vendor case studies (refer to profile below).
