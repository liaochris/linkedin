Marta Salas-Porras’s Summary

Interested in how digital media is consumed ubiquitously over different platforms and changing the way people interact with content. Using creativity and innovative thinking to re-frame the dialogue while creating new business models and sustainable revenues. Passionate about pushing the boundaries of how people engage with consuming media, storytelling and accessing information through simulation, interactivity, across multiple platforms. Helping start ups with business strategies, experiential innovations, digital communication and mobile applications. Designer, brand and strategy expert with specialization in environments, product development and new media. Holds 2 patents, one in mold making and one in the development and design of a future generation wireless inventory solution. Has worked internationally on business and design strategies- everything from digital innovation, environments, websites to smart phones to green projects to wireless and sensor networks to fashion to organic textiles to steel to organic food & creative content to green thinking to software and hardware. Ability to manage multidisciplinary teams comprised of business strategists, designers, art directors, programmers, writers, and video/photo/audio/mobile production teams with a hands on approach and a deep understanding of the disciplines.
