Nate Balcom's Summary

I've been designing web sites for over 10 years now. I started off in web marketing and moved into more of a graphic design position. When Macromedia's Flash came out I became primarily a Flash Programmer/ Designer. I began working with Action script and learned Illustrator so I could manipulate vector files. I also became extremely proficient with Photoshop and did a ton of web design and high end print work.

Later in my profession I started getting more into the heart of coding. I learned HTML, CSS, XML, DHTML and Javascript in the first few years of my programming career. I learned to code cross-browser-compatible, WC3 compliant, XHTML web sites. I began working with PHP & SQL while building user forums and CMS driven web sites and e-commerce sites.

I've been dubbed the social media specialist at the Quantum Interactive Group and am actively maintaining several large profiles for our main companies.I continue to build, design, market and maintain websites. I currently head up the web design department for Quantum Interactive Group in Auburn Hills.

In my spare time I run my blog NateBal.com. Stop by for web site design tutorials and social media marketing tips.

I'm a geek at heart. I was born a .com. I love what I do.
