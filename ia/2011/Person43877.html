Tom Northey’s Summary

Tom has worked in a variety of business development settings within and without the health care industry for the past 17 years. He has engineered the successful start-up and sale of multiple companies including a wireless communications manufacturing company, a state-wide IPA for audiologists and a long-standing, multi-office audiology practice.

Currently, Tom works for CORHIO riding the whirlwind of health information exhange, EHR implementation and assisting physicians in Colorado achieve meaningful use.

Tom has operated a strategic recruiting company working exclusively in the Audiology space (Bridgeline Associates). He has experience in coaching and developing audiologists and hearing professionals, defining what "right looks like" for employers through benchmarking and retention/selection strategies AND assembling the critical A-level talent that will implement company objectives.

Tom also worked for Centura Practice Management as their Western Regional Director for primary care physicians. Tom has experience with both SBA and investor funding, institutional lines of credit, and equity sharing models. His primary experience is with revenue generation strategies, top-line growth initiatives and go-to-market approaches.

He is particularly adept at financial and cost analysis and finding new and creative delivery models that can compete in the ever changing health care environment.

Tom earned his Masters Degree in Management from Regis University (where he now teaches healthcare marketing online) and his undergraduate degree in Speech Communications from Metropolitan State College of Denver. His wife Shana is an RN who works in pediatric care. They have two sons and enjoy all of the recreational activities Colorado has to offer.

Tom Northey’s Specialties:

Teaching others how to succeed. Business planning, go-to-market approaches, product launches, implementation specialist. Public speaking. Market strategy. Metrics analysis.
