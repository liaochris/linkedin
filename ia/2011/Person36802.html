Richard Lau's Summary

Domain Management: DomainManager.com

Named 2004 “Domainer of the Year”, Richard has generated millions of dollars in revenue in the domain industry. As part of his consulting services in domain forensic background checks (for domain purchasers) Richard was asked to investigate alleged hijackings of various domains. Now, having overseen the rescue of many high-value stolen domains such as God.com, Religion.com, Wifi.com, Iraq.com and others, Richard has become an unfortunate expert in the area of domain hijacking recovery.

Described by customers and colleagues as "a true professional", Richard is recognized for his integrity, creativity and tenacity.

Volunteer Activities: Domain Hijacking Recovery. Contributor to ICANN SSAC Releases Domain Name Hijacking Report found at ICANN.org. Returning Speaker to the Domain Roundtable Conference.
