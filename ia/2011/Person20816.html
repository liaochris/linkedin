Jhaymee S. Wilson, MSPM, PMP’s Summary

Jhaymee S. Wilson is a project management professional with several years of commercial and government experience in the financial services and IT consulting industries. She has extensive experience in strategic business development, proposal management, and business analysis. In 2009, Ms. Wilson received her Master's Degree in Project Management (MSPM) from The George Washington University, and will continue her studies in the Executive MBA Program at the Goizueta Business School at Emory University. Her inspirational leadership style, proven track record of project success, advanced education, and devotion to the profession makes her an excellent asset to any team.
