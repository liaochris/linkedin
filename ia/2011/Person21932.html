John Spilman's Summary

Transactional attorney with 25 years experience with international law firms, as in-house general counsel and legal executive, and as a sole practitioner. Core clients and prospective clients are companies strategically positioned in the value chain of centralized or distributed-scale renewable energy generation, especially wind or solar, or large scale bulk energy storage, or ground lessors hosting these facilities.

Specialties

• Legal advice, structuring, due diligence, negotiations and documentation for renewable energy project development, finance, M&A and operations, especially wind and solar.
• External general counsel services and business-minded legal counseling for small companies.

Solar Land Partners

Renewables & Environment industry

November 2010 – Present (4 months)

Solar Land Partners, Inc. (SLP) is a professional services company made up of energy utility, interconnection, real estate, permitting, solar, finance and legal professionals formed to get land sites "shovel ready" for solar EPC construction in the 3 to 50 MW size range, or larger. With our team of leading solar industry professionals, Solar Land Partners provides the know-how to create a bankable solar project.

General Counsel

Gridflex Energy, LLC

Renewables & Environment industry

October 2010 – Present (5 months)

General Counsel to Gridflex Energy, LLC and its affiliated project companies. Gridflex Energy is the leading originator of utility-scale energy storage projects, in a range from 250MW to 700MW each, that support the integration and expansion of renewable energy, particularly wind, with commercially proven hydroelectric pumped storage (PS) and compressed air (CAES) technologies. Gridflex Energy will be an active partner in the development of integrated wind-energy storage projects from conception through the day the project is energized. Early-stage services include project scoping and feasibility analysis. Gridflex Energy and its development partners will then take the project through the siting, permitting, licensing, marketing, financing, and construction process. Wind developers will be interested in pursuing storage to enhance the competitiveness and viability of their projects. Utilities will be interested in pursuing storage to create a more firm and versatile generation asset utilizing a renewable energy base. www.gridflexenergy.com
