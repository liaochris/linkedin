Mark Solvie’s Summary

My mission and privilege is to be a pioneer for leading innovative high quality power transmission manufacturers into the North American market. In this effort I am able to share with our American customers the passion for innovation, quality, and service offered by each of our design & manufacturing partners in Italy.

Our Goal is to bring TVT Group into the forefront of the minds of innovative designers & manufactures who are seeking a superior alternative to mechanical power transmission. To be the easiest partner to work with, the greatest overall value as a supplier, and to help our customers profit from their relationship with TVT.

I commit my lifelong experiences as an Inventor, Innovator, Engineer, Marketer, Sales Professional, Designer, Programmer, and Negotiator to the mutual success of my partners and our clients. There is a better way, it is called TVT America.
