David H Owen's Summary

We have been helping owner managers of businesses for the last 10 years achieve the ambitions they had when they first started out.

Most people are technicians when they start a business they are not entrepreneurs. They are technicians in that they can make something, design something, deliver a service.

However if that is what they always do they will never achieve the results from the business they want. They will be always doing it doing it doing it and never running a business.

In fact they will give up their lives to the business by always working in it and never on it.

It doesn’t have to be that way.

We help owner managers find a different way. We help them identify what they want from their business and then provide help and support to enable them to achieve their goals.

We use a combination of executive coaching and mentoring. We have 20 associates throughout the UK who deliver our programmes to accountancy practices and their clients.

Specialties

Executive Coaching and Mentoring, Business Advice and in some cases Non Exec Directors.
