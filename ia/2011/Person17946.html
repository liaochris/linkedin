Abhinav Gupta's Summary

Over the past 3 years, I have worked extensively in building products based on mobile, web and social web ensuring high User Experience to the target users. In the past year and a half, I have worked in a product based start-up with a highly focused team. I have had hands of experience in working and coordinating with teams at different stages of a product development cycle.

I believe in employing pragmatic design approach to solve design problems and I am also exposed to the standard UCD and PACT methodologies. The focus of most of the projects have been on catering the User needs and fulfilling the Business requirements; working around the technical constraints to ensure high UX.

I am experienced in conceptualizing products, translating the user and business requirements into linear flow screens, defining the design patterns for the product interfaces, and collaborating with the design and development teams to maintain smooth flow of the product development process.

Specialties

UCD, Information Design and Architecture, Social Media and News monitoring, Social Computing, Information Visualization and Information Graphics.
