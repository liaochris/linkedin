We provide computer repair services, web design, & network installation to individuals and companies in the Atlanta area. Most of our customers are people who have a home computer or laptop that is having problems with viruses, spyware, locking up, running slowly, crashing, or just not working the right way. Just about every computer will experience problems like these as time goes by if they are used on a regular basis. Similar to the way your car needs the oil changed, tires rotated, and a tune-up after a certain number of miles, your computer needs regular maintenance after a certain number of hours to keep it performing at its best.

So what can we do? We are experts at “tuning up” computers and restoring them to their maximum potential. We will examine your machine, identify the problems it’s having, disinfect it, remove unnecessary and annoying files and programs, upgrade the software & drivers, and ensure that everything is configured to operate smoothly and quickly.
