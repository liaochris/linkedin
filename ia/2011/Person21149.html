Jose Sebastian’s Summary

Jose L. Sebastian is a well-rounded Art Director, he has been developing work in many design disciplines around the world for the past years. He has experience working in Europe and United States, his capabilities expand across concept, identity, print, interactive and advertising.

Jose doesn't have a set style, he approaches each project with a fresh perspective, free of trends. His passion for Design keeps him ready for new challenges.
