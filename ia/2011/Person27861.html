Margie Torres, M.A.’s Summary

Margie Torres, MA is a burgeoning Business Owner and Artist supporting enlightened wealth building in the areas of real estate investing, creative finance and entrepreneurial ventures. She is Managing Member and Founder of several enterprises including, Creative Rim, LLC, Treasure’s Path, Inc., Somara Institute and Sole Proprietor for MargieTorresDesign.

Margie is a Creative Entrepreneur with years of explorations between architecture, creative & expressive arts, dance/movement, humanistic psychology, and more recently business, real estate investing and creative finance with ways these connections can deepen personal, professional and spiritual growth.

As a life-long learner, Margie is pursuing her doctorate degree in Psychology, holds a Masters of Arts in Humanistic Psychology, a Professional Degree in Architecture, Certification in Body-Mind Hypnotherapy & Socially Engaged Spirituality and trained as a Laban/Bartenieff Movement Analyst, Dancer and Choreographer.

Margie has attended real estate investing trainings and seminars with prominent leaders and continues expanding her knowledge in real estate investing and strategies, finance and sustainable business practices.

Margie Torres, M.A.’s Specialties:

ENTREPRENEURIAL: Real Estate Investing & Creative Financing, Building Collaborative Relationships, Residential Design & Green Architecture.

PSYCHOLOGY: Self-Awareness Development, Mind-Body Connectivity, Creative & Expressive Arts: Dance/Movement Therapies.
