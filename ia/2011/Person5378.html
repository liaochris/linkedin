Brian Linder, CISSP’s Summary

Currently a Security Engineering Manager for market-leading Check Point Software Technologies. 19 years of experience securing enterprise networks in a diversity of organizations. I hold a B.S. in computer science from Drexel University, and an M.S. in Information Science from the Pennsylvania State University.

I'm a tenured presenter on Check Point’s Western Hemisphere User Experience team, and I'm a member of Check Point’s Technology Tour presenter team.

I've contributed to graduate courseware development in security technologies, and have been a guest speaker at Villanova University.

In addition to ISC2’s CISSP and GIAC’s GSEC, I hold a number of certifications from Sun, CompTIA, Nokia, Check Point and Cisco.

I enjoy bringing high energy and enthusiasm to audiences of all sizes with my engaging and interactive presentation style. I am not above any reasonable stunt to engage my audiences...
