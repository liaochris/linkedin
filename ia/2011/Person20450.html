Jeremy Wadsack's Summary

I am a senior technologist, entrepreneur and leader interested in building self-directed technology teams focused on continuous improvement and delivering ground-breaking products that add true value to people's lives. Having launched five firms over the past two decades, I thrive in an environment of adaptability: where the future is created from the choices we make today. Equally I am a levelheaded executive and have learned to assess risks early, to measure results continually, and to strive constantly for improvement in myself, my team and my company.
