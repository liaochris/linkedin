Cyril Pierre de Geyer's Summary

After completing computer engineering studies as salutatorian at EPITA, I created web applications for a year for multiple companies as a freelance developer.

I then co-founded the web agency “Kaptive” which designed and produced highly innovative projects (including a documentary video intranet for France 3, revolutionary at the time). This led me to work on varied aspects of commerce and project management.

At the same time I created an Open Source technologies training program within a new company of mine called “Anaska.” There, I had the opportunity to focus especially on business development, business and technical networking, and on technical leadership.

Sold in June 2008, the company had 15 employees, a 1.5 million € yearly turnover and a highly efficient profitability.

My technical expertise led me to participate in numerous international conferences. I am coauthor of the book Advanced PHP 5, cofounder of the AFUP (Association Française des Utilisateurs de PHP) and I contribute to the website PHPfrance.
