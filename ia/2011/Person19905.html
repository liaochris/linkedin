Jeff Harrison’s Summary

Since starting my WSI WebSpecialist business in 2006, I have focused on developing top-notch websites that take into account important factors such as web accessibility and usability, and search-engine readiness, while maintaining the business' needs as the top priority.

One area of specialty is in helping existing web sites that are currently under-performing to reach their potential through professionally managed pay-per-click (PPC) ad campaigns, SEO and Social Media. Continual education and training in SEO techniques allows me to optimize web sites and web pages without sacrificing the critical factors that facilitate customer conversions and promote ROI.

Previous experience as an educator and customer service manager help me to offer my clients the best service and web training in the area. WSI WebSpecialist currently serves Baltimore, northern Maryland, the Eastern Shore and Delaware.
