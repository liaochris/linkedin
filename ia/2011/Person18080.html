Chloë Thomas’s Summary

Whilst at uni Barclays Bank were kind enough to employ me one summer working in their Current Accounts team on a range of projects from internal training tools to mini-websites. Post-uni they gave me a full time job again working in retail banking. After a couple of years with Barclays I moved to Retail Variations and ran the customer communications for Past Times.

Next stop was working for Ardington Ltd managing the web business for 4 mail order operations.

The Present

Right now I am the Managing Director of Indium Web Management. We look after the online marketing of a growing number of businesses. A lot in the retail trade, but also others – wholesalers, events, publishing and corporate.
