Debra Lindell’s Experience

  • Founder, CEO

    Empathic Clinical Suite,

    (Hospital & Health Care industry)

    November 2006 — Present (4 years 3 months)

    Empathic Clinical Suite, is an online service for behavioral health professionals. It is a full service, software program that has patents pending on an innovative process that is currently saving up to 90% of the time/effort therapists use to spend on administrative duties. Among other things, Empathic includes collaborative intelligence. The system becomes smarter as it is used by clinicians. It offers diagnostic suggestions. If a therapist has not offered enough information for the system to pin point a diagnosis, Empathic will ask the clinician questions until a diagnosis is found.

    Empathic is not a software people buy and are then left on their own to deal with it. It is a community of therapists they join. Therapists enter their notes into the system, click and a claim is sent to the correct insurance company. Leave your office, and your Empathic Financial Center continues to work while you spend time with your family, or even as you sleep at night. Come into your office the next day, open your computer, and Empathic tells you what you need to do that day. Look at your financial center and see what claims have been accounted for, how much each client owes, and who in the Empathic System wants to consult with you.
