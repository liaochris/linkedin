Maricela Morales’s Summary

Maricela, a Fulbright Scholar, has been a leader in executive management and consulting for corporations and non-profit organizations in the healthcare, technology, hospitality, and social services sectors. Throughout her almost twenty year professional career, Maricela has embraced innovation and collaboration using technology as a mechanism to achieve solutions to complex business and social problems. She is a strong proponent of Agile Methodology & Lean Startup.

Believing that a healthy society can be achieved by balancing for-profit, non-profit, and governmental agencies, Maricela has dedicated considerable personal and professional time volunteering for a broad range of projects in all three sectors and has even founded efforts designed to enable better interaction between the three.

Beginning in 2005, Maricela turned her technology and business skills to Internet technologies designed to facilitate collaboration. Even more recently, she has expanded her focus to cloud computing and mobile technology in emerging markets in Latin America and the Caribbean.

Originally from Panama, now holding dual citizenship in both her native country and the United States, Maricela obtained her B.A. from Linfield College, McMinnville, Oregon with dual majors in Finance and English Literature.

Fluent in both English and Spanish, Maricela brings a wealth of management and consulting experience across the Americas in a variety of fields and technologies vital to your organization.
