Chris Fuelling’s Summary

We have been doing web based software devlopment for the mortgage industry since 2006, with our first release of www.TheLoanPost.com The Loan Post is a web based platform for searching, pricing and pre-qualifying mortgage applications with 200+ wholesale lenders.

In 2008, we transitioned our software towards loss mitigation to help companies process and manage the loan modification and short sale process via www.themodpost.com

1) Online submission forms (with doc upload) integrated with your website or your affiliates websites. This allows your clients and agents to submit 24/7 online. Several versions exist depending on your business model and service offering.

2) Online Tracking- Your client and agents can track the results online 24/7 in real time. Email and SMS text message reminders are also available.

3) Document Automation allows you to create and send signature ready documents or packages with one click. See
sample docs attached. You can use the E-signature feature to get your docs signed, legally online.

4) Loss Mitigation Tools- Quickly determine HAMP qualifications (Pass/Fail Results) (see attached loanDisposition.pdf), Automated Property Value Results (see attached LPS.jpg), Auto-calculate lender losses and Net Investor Benefit (see attached Proposal pdfs), forensic Audit Reports (see attached ComplianceEase Report.pdf), and more!

5) Do-It-Yourself System- The DIY Loan Mod System is private labeled and built to allow homeowners to access your
software system and create their own Bank Ready loan mod package automatically. You pick the domain, set the price, and keep 100% of the Profit. You just have to drive the traffic to your site and get the word out. We charge one flat monthly fee. It comes with 2 user for the back office processing platform to manage your client files.
