Thornton Phister's Summary

Thornton Phister joined UMB Investment & Wealth Management in 2008 as a Vice President. Thornton provides customized credit solutions, and Private Banking deposit and investment accounts to high net worth clients with more than $1 Million of assets.

Thornton brings 18 years of experience in the financial services industry to his role at UMB. Prior to joining the bank, Thornton worked as a senior loan consultant in San Clemente, California. He has also held positions as a Premier Banking client manager and banking center manager in Missouri, Kansas and California.

Thornton currently maintains Series 7 and 66 investment licenses, and has completed the UMB Wealth Management Certification program which addresses the major financial concerns for affluent clients. He is an alumnus of the University of Missouri, and a volunteer for Junior Achievement.

UMB is a 97 year old diversified financial services company with $19.8 Billion in assets, and 136 offices throughout Missouri, Illinois, Colorado, Kansas, Oklahoma, Nebraska and Arizona. UMB has been ranked as one of the best banks in America by Forbes magazine. UMB chose not to participate in the federal government's Troubled Asset Relief Program.

Specialties

Wealth Accumulation Strategies, Retirement Planning, Mortgage Services, Customized Credit Solutions, Investment Management
