Margie Shaw’s Summary

Margie is marketing strategist with extensive experience helping companies build solid marketing programs to drive business and sales. In addition to running her own e-commerce business, she has overseen the launch and roll-out of an online gift guide for e-rewards pioneer Netcentives on a shoestring budget under tight time constraints, and overseen the launch of a Business & Investing Channel for Excite.com that resulted in a 50% increase in customers in a single quarter. She was a pioneer in the world of internet advertising as an early hire at Hotwired where she pioneered the concept of “beyond the banner” advertising by developing online promotions and co-op programs for national advertisers. While she provides solid strategic vision, she excels at managing the tactical details of program implementation.
