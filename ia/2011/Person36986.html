Rick Lochner's Summary

Everyone has leadership potential. We define leadership as the ability to set goals and achieve desired results. RPC Leadership Associates, Inc. was founded on this singular premise and is focused on helping business and non-profit organizations, individual professionals and students achieve their full leadership potential. We elevate focus on performance change to a new sustained level such that leadership becomes a way of life for lifelong professional and personal success.

What does an RPC Leadership Associates, Inc. client look like? They are organizations or individuals looking to:

• Grow the business at a faster rate than has occurred to date
• Successfully navigate the current recession and gain competitive advantage
• Manage though Change and/or Transition (merger, acquisition, recession etc.)
• Deal with dissatisfaction with the current business situation
• Counter declining sales and/or profits
• Correct issues with teamwork or effective communications within the organization
• Provide effective succession planning

Some of these scenarios might not be obvious to those in a position to refer prospects to RPC Leadership Associates, Inc. However, when an organization or individual asks:

• Why is it so hard to make a decision around here?
• We are losing customers as fast as we win them!
• Our productivity is too low!
• Sales are not meeting goals!
• Our business plan is not having the desired impact!
• Why are employees unhappy and morale low?
• This business is no longer fun!
• All we do is work, work, work; We have no life!

...then you know you are speaking to an RPC Leadership Associates, Inc. prospect.

Find out more about how RPC Leadership Associates, Inc. facilitates personal and professional leadership success by contacting us at (630) 219-3316 or through our website at www.rpcleadershipassociates.com.
