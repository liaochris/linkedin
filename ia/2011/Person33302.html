Olga Gil, PhD (olgagil@olgagil.es)’s Summary

Transmedia management. Ph.D. in Political and Social Sciences, European University Institute. Olga Gil has international experience in the corporate sector and the academia -16 years experience in Spain, the United States and Italy- and a strong backgound in institutional communication, technology and education. In 2003 she joined Ana Botin team in Banesto (Office of the Chairman --financial sector, 9,700 employees, profits 765 m. euros, national and internacional shareholders, a member of Santander Group). There she was project manager and managed instituctional communications plus edited the multimedia versions of sustainibility and social responsibility reports. Olga is a specialist on event amplification, web project management and multimedia, usability and customer experience management. She has developed the online communication strategy of the Association of Fulbright Alumni based social networking, community building and online positioning. She teaches courses on web2.0 at John Hopkins in Spain, UNIA, and the Menendez Pelayo International University. Highly trained, dedicated, efficient, empathetic, with plenty of capacity for social relationships and committed. Excellent teamworker with a strong culture of innovation and collaboration.
