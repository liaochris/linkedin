Christopher Gonzalez's Summary

Perez Gonzalez, A Professional Law Corporation was established to meet the need for effective professional counsel in areas of Corporate/Business, Intellectual Property and Civil Law as well as Immigration and Nationality Law. Perez Gonzalez continues to work with a base of long-standing clients engaged in varied industries such as major professional sports, entertainment, technology/Internet, manufacturing, health care, real estate and advertising.

Perez Gonzalez bases its commitment to client service by developing an intimate knowledge of each client's needs and objectives. The Firm seeks long-term, partnering relationships with clients, to the end of providing the best total solution to the client's multi-disciplined and industry-specific legal service needs. The Firm's goal is to be an instrumental part of each client's success.

Mr. Gonzalez serves as a member of the management team as General/Special Counsel of several Internet ventures and “brick and mortar” business organizations. His clientele ranges from individuals, start-up ventures and privately held family to international corporations, where he was engaged to serve as special counsel to corporate boards of directors and to independent committees of corporate boards of directors.

Specialties

Corporate Civil Law: Business Corporate Civil Transactions Litigation; Cyberspace Computer Internet, Intellectual Property; Individual Rights; Labor Employment; Sports Management

Immigration Law: Temporary Professional Work Visas; Professionals and Investors; Intra-Company Transfers; Extraordinary Ability; Permanent Residence Green Cards; Family Based Petitions; Labor Certifications; Employer Sanctions; I-9 Compliance; Advisory Opinions; Citizenship Naturalization; Consular Processing
