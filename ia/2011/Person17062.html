Harry Hayes's Summary

Harry has been sharing his expertise with Durham residents for almost 40 years. He embraces modern technology to market not only himself, but also his listings.

Clients enjoy having listings provided to their personal email via the property finder tools through TREB. They also embrace his virtual tours and on-line marketing of their listings.

It's a "New Day" in the Real Estate industry! Forget putting up a sign, placing an ad and saying a prayer. The Internet is where the buyers and seller go when they need professional advice. You can count on Harry's to be there sharing his experience and now his technology!

Harry has connected with both his business associates and clients using Facebook, Linked-in and more recently his blog
where he shares local news.

Residents of the Chalk Lake and Deer Creek have a source for local information. Visit Harry's wordpress blog here on Linked-in.

Specialties

Harry is focused on luxury, executive, country and waterfront real estate in the Chalk Lake and Deer Creek areas. He is also a knowledgeable real estate broker for commercial and investment property.
