Jacob Thomason's Summary

Since my foray into web technology during the first part of 2001, I have worked closely with some of the most talented individuals in the industry, delivering amazing results for clients. I have worked with mom and pop businesses, publicly traded corporations, community sites, blogs, photo galleries, ecommerce sites, and much more. I am familiar with nearly every acronym related to web technology, and have "played" with, or regularly use, most of them.
