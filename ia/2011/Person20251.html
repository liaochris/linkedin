Jennifer Nichols’s Summary

Jennifer is an HR professional offering 10+ years of dedication to Human Resources. Her emphasis is in strategic recruitment and talent acquisition, including workforce planning, employee retention, branding, employee benefits and compensation. She is recognized for developing creative campaigns that attract qualified talent. She has broad experience building benefits strategies to fit an organization and establishing competitive salary structures for organizations. Jennifer’s consulting clients include Siemens, Textura LLC, ACCO Brands, Resources Connection, AstraZeneca, R.R. Donnelly, The Popcorn Factory, Kemper Insurance, Outboard Marine, Walgreens, Allstate Insurance and Comcast.

While much of Jennifer’s experience is freelancing for Fortune 500 companies in the Chicagoland market, she has also held top talent acquisition positions in both start-up and large organizations. Her character, confidence, ethics and drive make her an excellent fit for any organization seeking change and execution of new strategies.

Jennifer is a Project Manager/Talent Acquisition for The Lennic Group, LTD . The focus of the business is to provide Talent Acquisition, Employee Relations, Organization Effectiveness, Leadership Development and Training Services based on the latest technologies and best practices on a short or long-term contract basis.

Jennifer holds a degree from Northwestern University in Organizational Behavior and Psychology. She is the Founder and chairs a Women’s Networking Group in the Northwest Suburbs of Chicago. Jennifer serves on the Board of Directors as the VP of Communications for the Northern Illinois Society for Human Resources in Management (NISHRM) and an active member of both the Society for Human Resources in Management (SHRM) and the Staffing Management Association of Greater Chicago (SMAGC).

Jennifer Nichols’s Specialties:

Human Capital Consultant with expertise in the following industries: Supply Chain Management, Retail, Information Technology, Medical, Manufacturing, Distribution, Energy and Telecommunications.

Emphasis in strategic recruitment and talent acquisition, recruitment process outsourcing (RPO) staffing and recruitment, workforce planning, employee retention and branding. Full life cycle recruiting with expertise in implementing staffing processes for anticipated talent needs.
