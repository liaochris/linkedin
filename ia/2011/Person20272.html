Jennifer Simon's Summary

Jennifer Simon is an accomplished business development professional in the digital marketing and technology services industries. Her ability to help clients solve business problems through insightful consulting engagements and the application of innovative technologies has earned her the role of trusted advisor to clients and colleagues. With 14 years of experience providing solutions to clients throughout the Midwest, Jennifer has succeeded in helping organizations meet their most complex business challenges. She is consistently invited into her client base to lead project teams that execute on business strategy while providing high value and high impact results.

Jennifer is an Enterprise Business Development Manager at Manifest Digital, a Chicago based interactive consultancy focused on creating meaningful connections between people and companies in a rapidly changing marketplace. She serves on the Leadership Team of Manifest’s Enterprise Division, helping to define the influence of digital marketing for Fortune 500 clients. Jennifer brings a strong business and technology background to this growing division within the agency.

A member of the Executives Club of Chicago, the ECC’s New Leaders Group, and Step Up Women's Network, Jennifer always embraces an opportunity for networking. She is a fitness enthusiast and enjoys running on the Chicago lakefront and competing in local charity events. Jennifer lives in downtown Chicago and appreciates the vibrant restaurants, sports, museums, and cultural events Chicago has to offer.
