    Creative design matters. Think about a time you’ve exchanged business cards and liked the look of theirs. Or how about the time you binned the flyer shoved through your letterbox because it looked like an amateur service. Think about how a PDF made that company look professional. These are the things I think about because these are the things that matter to us and to your business.

    Whether developing your brand or logo, creating promotional material with flyers or poster, even through to personal and professional invites or announcements, each project gets the attention it deserves. Who’ll be looking at it? Where will it be seen? Is there a better way to communicate your message. More questions. Luckily, the answers are also at hand thanks to years of experience, study and obsession.

    And I design websites. I don’t just mean the visual design, I mean the everything design. I take into account attention mapping (determining where you want your customer to look), navigation (layout of the menus and ease of getting around your site), I consider your customers (who they are, what they want) and I consider the point. What’s the point? Exactly.

    What is the point?

    Depending on the point and purpose of your web existence, I will design your site around these factors.
