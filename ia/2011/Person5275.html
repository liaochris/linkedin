Bill Parke's Summary

My 47 year career as an Entrepreneur spans several diverse businesses. For 20 years, I focused on the travel industry. I owned and managed several travel agencies in the greater Orlando area and was affiliated with American Express Travel as a Representative Office, providing financial services including American Express travelers’ checks. Through an aggressive marketing strategy, I gained a reputation for successfully designing and escorting group tours to Asia and Europe as well as promoting ship-based programs throughout the Caribbean. I achieved Top 50 status for many years with American Express and served as a member of their Advisory Board.

Anticipating the changes that were about to take place in the travel industry, I sold my agencies and successfully marketed an international health and skin care company, growing the business throughout the U.S., Asia and Europe. I also marketed Pre-Paid Legal Services as an employee benefit in Central Florida.

Seeing the trend towards the expansion of the real estate market, I became a real estate investor and for several years bought, rehabbed and sold homes in Central Florida. I continue to research the trends that are reshaping the way business is conducted both in the US and around the world, which has led me to the Internet and has opened a whole new world for me.

My wife and I are building internet marketing businesses that we can operate from anywhere in the world. Due to the current economic situation our focus right now is helping people save money and live better.

Specialties

Internet Shopping, Internet Reverse Auctions, International Marketing and Business Consulting, Credit Report Repair
