Kathy Sanborn's Summary

Kathy Sanborn is a musician, recording artist, and songwriter. Known for her velvet voice, Sanborn performs her own original jazz, R&B, and adult contemporary songs with an elegance that can take your breath away.

A keyboard and piano player, Sanborn composes and produces both jazz instrumentals and vocal tunes. She released her first CD, Peaceful Sounds, to critical acclaim in 2008. Peaceful Sounds, a jazzy-new age blend of philosophical songs, also features trumpeter extraordinaire, Wayne Ricci.

Sanborn's second project, Small Galaxy, released in April 2010 through the Pacific Coast Jazz label. Upbeat and inspirational, Small Galaxy garnered a #4 ranking on the Top Chart of radio station WSCA.

Blues for Breakfast releases March 2011, and is Sanborn's most ambitious project yet. It features a tune dedicated to the jazz masters of yesterday, and includes GRAMMY® nominee bassist Scott Petito, sizzling drummer Chris Carey, and soulful trumpeter Wayne Ricci.

“Kathy Sanborn is able to convey emotional truths with a sound no one else can produce--a quavering joy that works equally well over an ocean or acoustic chords of ballads. No one else sings like that.” ~John Shelton Ivany, National News Bureau

Sanborn is a voting member of the National Academy of Recording Arts and Sciences (GRAMMY® Awards), and her biography is displayed in Who's Who in America and Who's Who of American Women.

Specialties

Contemporary jazz, Latin music, smooth jazz, pop, R&B. Composes music for film and TV shows, both vocal and instrumental.
