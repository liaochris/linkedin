Melissa Cooley's Summary

Career management and job hunting are tough.

Who thought it would become so hard to determine what to do in life and, once you figured it out, to actually be able to do what you want to do?

If that wasn’t challenging enough, here’s the rub: people are changing jobs and careers regularly. The days of working for the same company for 40 years are nearly extinct. It’s not a matter of establishing your career once and finding a position; you have to be prepared to conduct multiple job searches, to redefine the direction of your career.

In addition, there’s all the expectations regarding things like personal branding, the use of social media…

It’s a lot.

But that’s why you’re here, right? To get a little guidance with your career management? Cut through the confusion?

Well, I’m here to help.

If you want to work together one-on-one with your specific concerns, contact me and we can talk about meeting your needs!

Specialties
