Johnson Koh's Summary

Johnson is currently heading Design and Visual Communications for one of Asia's leading gaming platforms - Garena, in Singapore.

Johnson works daily as an artist, designer, photographer and director. He led the web designs under SONY account for 7 out of 12 major SONY subsidiary brands at Takiguchi & Yoshimi Associates.

Before that, Johnson led the design team for Playworks, ensured the creative function operated in an efficient and profitable manner. He also planned and designed booths for the largest annual gaming event, World Cyber Games with Rapture Gaming.

His design blog, 10Steps.SG, helped to shape online design community throughout the last three years, and earned him a $50,000 grant from the Info-communications Development Authority of Singapore (IDA) to reach its next level. 10Steps.SG is currently receiving more than 600,000 monthly visitors and now become one of the most popular design blogs.
