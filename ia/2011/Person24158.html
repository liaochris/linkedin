Ken Estabrook's Summary

Over the past 15 years, I have provided leadership in building the training and development functions at two internationally known applied research and development laboratories, MIT Lincoln Laboratory in the Boston area and JHU Applied Physics Laboratory in the Washington DC area.

My primary focus has centered on creating leadership/management development programs and activities for the technical managers and staff at all levels, although I've also provided leadership over the technical training and education programs. These programs and activities have been primarily highly interactive facilitated courses and workshops, although I also have strong experience in building "blended" solutions using online learning technologies.

Specialties

Leadership and management development, executive development, coaching, program and project management training
