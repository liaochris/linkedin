Shawn Ward's Summary

Experiences:

Almost 20 years of financial services experience in both large company and entreprenurial environments. Started in back office operations to product development/management, then on to sales and partnerships, finally to business ownership.

Our goal is to build Geezeo into the largest financial destination on the web. Provide a platform for users to learn more about their financial situation, explore their options, and learn how to get on a path towards financial improvement. We are fostering a strong community where people can share their success stories and make personal finance feel a lot less painful.

Specialties

converting concept to product, creative revenue models, strategic partnerships, networking
