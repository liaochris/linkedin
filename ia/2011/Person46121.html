Diane O'Connell's Summary

Author of 5 books; I spent over 10 years working in NY publishing houses before starting my own business in 1993; have helped many authors get published, including a first-time author who got a $500,000 book deal from Bantam.

I work with both fiction and nonfiction authors, providing manuscript critiques, book development, coaching, all levels of editing, help with getting an agent and publisher, and guidance in self-publishing strategies.

I also specialize in working with entrepreneurs and business owners, helping them brand themselves with a book, then use that book to build their business.

Specialties

First-time authors, fiction and nonfiction, branding through book writing, speaking, coaching, self-publishing, e-book publishing, finding an agent, platform building
