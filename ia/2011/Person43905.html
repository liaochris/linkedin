Tom Tenney's Experience

Director & Founding Member

LOFI - League of Independents

Media Production industry

2009 – Present (2 years)

Executive Producer of the RE/Mixed Media Festival 2010

Established in 2009, LOFI is a flim/video collective started by four artists who met in NYC’s alternative performance scene and came together with a vision of an organization devoted to supporting independent film and media makers of all stripes. All proceeds from RE/Mixed Media 2010 will go towards LOFI’s mission of providing an outlet for the new breed of independent artists by creating a safe brick-and-mortar space for them to develop. Empowered by technology, more artists are creating media than ever before, but their works are often seen only on sites like YouTube and social networks. LOFI aims to create a real face-to-face community for these artists to screen their works, and to discuss and critique the works of their peers. We hope to have a permanent home by 2011.
