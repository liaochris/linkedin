Shawn R Chaput's Summary

Shawn R. Chaput is a highly regarded and well respected Information security consultant operating within Western Canada. He has over 14 years experience in IT and the Security, Audit and Compliance profession and acts as a business adviser to many large and well known organizations. As a co-owner of a Canadian PCI QSA company, his role has lead him to advise executive management how to effectively govern and manage IT risk; design enterprise security architectures, strategies & plans; develop cost-effective and sustainable security management policies and practices for governance frameworks. Shawn has also led a number of high value projects to implement and deploy security solutions. His experiences in the Information Security arena have extended from the transportation industry, telecommunications, oil and gas, natural resources, higher education, financial, health care and public sector.
He actively participates in the Canadian Advisory Committee for the JTC 1/SC27, which develops the ISO/IEC 27000 series Security Standards and has also contributed to several articles and books for ISACA, ITGI and ISC2.He was also the chair of the 2008 West Coast Security Forum on Cyberterrorism and Cybercrime. A published author on the topic of cloud computing security, Shawn has spoken on a number of occasions with respect to the topic for industry inquiries, conferences and interviews. He holds high national security clearances from government and industry agencies has been involved in various committees dedicated to the protection of critical infrastructure.

Shawn has a Honours Bachelor of Arts in Economics and Political Science from the University of Winnipeg and is currently enrolled in the MOT MBA program at Simon Fraser University's Segal School of Business. Also, he has achieved a large collection of industry certifications such as the CISA, CISM, CGEIT, CRISC, CISSP, QSA, CIPP/C, ABCP, PMP, CIA, CFE, ITIL, ISSMP, and ISSAP.
