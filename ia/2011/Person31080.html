Marilyn J. Magett, MBA's Summary

CRS Financial Management Solutions helps Business Owners and CEO’s to make sound decisions to grow and manage their business utilizing our consulting services backed up by sound financial management tools and techniques that include forecasting, modeling, access to financing, accurate and timely reporting, and clear and consistent internal accounting processes and procedures.

We help our clients to increase profitability, stabilize cash flow, secure financing, and organize internal accounting operations to support the CEO in creating his or her vision of the business as a reality.

We have a unique service offering in that our core competency is in CFO and Controllership Services, yet we support our clients in generating consistently accurate and timely financial reports by overseeing their internal accounting departments or by actually taking on the entire accounting role for them.

Our founder Marilyn Magett says..."I am passionate about assisting my clients – small and mid-sized businesses owners and CEO’s - to grow their businesses, increase their profitability, secure financing, stabilize their cash flow, and organize their internal accounting and financial management procedures. On average my clients experience a minimum of 500% return on investment based on my financial services and advice."

Marilyn J. Magett, MBA's Experience

PRINCIPAL

CRS FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT SOLUTIONS

Financial Services industry

October 2005 – Present (5 years 6 months)

Marilyn J. Magett, principal, has more than 25 years experience in financial management, reporting, planning, and control in a variety of industries. She has particular expertise in strategic financial management and the design of practical solutions for financial and operational issues.

Prior to consulting, Marilyn served as Chief Financial Officer of a bank, Corporate Controller of a technology manufacturing firm, as well as Controller of several manufacturing and distribution firms. She has experience with organizations of all sizes, from small businesses to Fortune 500 companies, in roles ranging from individual contributor to senior executive.

Marilyn received her B.S. in accounting and financial planning from the State University of New York and her Executive MBA from Baruch College, City University of New York.

Director of Finance

Molecular Imaging Corp

Hospital & Health Care industry

May 2006 – December 2008 (2 years 8 months)

Recruited by the CEO to serve as the de facto Chief Financial Officer , through the turnaround process, overseeing all financing and accounting activities and functions, supervising, training, and motivating professional and support staff of this publicly reporting entity in the Healthcare–Diagnostic Imaging industry with revenues in the $20M range.
• Identified business units generating both losses and negative cash flow.
• Packaged non-profitable business units for sale to competitor effectively monetizing accounts all non profitable operations saving $4M annually.
• Negotiated new forbearance agreements with primary creditors generating $15M positive cash flow annually.
• Reduced annual premiums for $10M Directors & Officers Insurance policy by implementing internal procedures to effectively manage risk factors saving $150K annually in premiums.
• Converted internal accounting software from QuickBooks to Microsoft Great Plains and initiated 7 day monthly close process.

Controller

Recruited by the Board of Directors to serve as the de facto Chief Financial Officer through the turnaround process, overseeing all financing and accounting activities and functions, supervising, training , and motivating support staff of this private entity of this value add computer hardware firm with revenues in the $20M range.
