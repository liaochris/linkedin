Benno Aalders's Summary

Business has nothing to do with doing a trick, business is all about passion. Anyone who has a passion for something will find his abilty to develop within this specialty.
My pasison is within the combination of marketing, communication and media. Learning everything on the subject of media and communication and being able to use this knowledge into a solid advisory for my customers, that's what it is all about for me.

Throughout the years I have gained knowledge, experience and overview of the possibilities for my clients. With that I support my clients with both an excelling advisory in media strategies but by doing so also helping my clients cutting the costs of advertising. Focussing the campaigns on the target groups that really matters through the communication channels that really work means less 'waste'. less costs and more revenues.
This way communication becomes an investment instead of an extra cost.

My background includes nearly 5 years of media advisory and account management for national publishers and Dutch associatons. Having completed both a NIMA A Marketing as NIMA B Marketing study creates a perfect theoretical base.

My employer is PSH Media Sales. The mission of our organisation is training and generating the best media advisors and account managers of the Netherlands. By attracting people who have a passion for this business and who are willing to contribute to an environment of taking responsibility for our own skills, learning and training, the organisation already is at the top of the Dutch market for years now.

Specialties

Inventising structures within an organisation, setting goals ans target groups, translate organisation goals to communication structures, researching relevant communication channels, supervising media campaigns
