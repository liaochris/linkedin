Mark Smout's Summary

A business development professional specialising in digital content creation, video production and motion graphics. Working with some of the world's leading media agencies and best known brands to deliver compelling messages through film and digital media. Specialists in creative video production, experiential & live events; producing video advertising content for a wide variety of platforms to fit a range of budgets.

Our work is varied and wide-ranging. Projects that we have handled range from delivering HD 3D motion graphics for use across the London Underground network to producing educational video content for, and filming, world leaders at energy summits. Our production department have all trained with world class broadcasters and maintain these high standards whilst continuing to push the boundaries of innovation.

We have produced content, in partnership with media agencies, for a diverse list of global brands including Panasonic, Peugeot, Virgin Media, BT, Alcatel, Puma, Clarks, Kraft, Asda, HTC Europe, and The Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi.

For more information visit http://www.hurricanemedia.co.uk or feel free to connect.
