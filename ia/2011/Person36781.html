Richard Hill's Summary

I run ECRM. We are an Internet & Direct Marketing Agency founded in 1999.

We are not a 'web design agency' nor an 'IT company'.

We believe that the web is the best marketing tool ever invented and that, properly planned and executed, almost any business can obtain significant amounts of business via internet marketing.

We offer a range of services including ECommerce, Web design, Web development, Search Engine Marketing, Search Engine Optimisation (SEO), Pay per Click, EMail and ECRM to small and medium size businesses.

We are based in Bath, Somerset, United Kingdom.

Contact me to find out more.

I also run RaDiSH, my own business and marketing consultancy.

I am a director of Innovantage.

Innovantage is the UK’s leading provider of employment market intelligence. We collect millions of online job postings in real-time, enabling powerful analysis of recruitment demand.

Our customers can quickly explore recruitment activity; generating leads,
highlighting trends in individual sectors and even individual companies. The combination of real-time and historic data is integral to the operation of many of the UK’s key staffing businesses, job boards, media owners and employers.

Recent developments are focused on the provision of market and competitive intelligence. We are the only vendor to provide accurate, unduplicated market share analysis for job boards and media owners. Recruitment Consultancies use the data to track customers, competitors as well as recruitment demand. Employers use the data to track their competitors and better understand the talent pool.

The web mining competence developed in our core business has recently been applied to other industries. Our service driven approach takes the pain away from web mining; offering customers an opportunity to completely outsource web mining in any sector. If you need to harvest data from the web and are not interested in the technology behind it, we can work with you to do this.
