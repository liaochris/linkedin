Managing Director

TBDA Ltd (The Business Development Agency)

Marketing and Advertising industry

1994 – Present (17 years)

TBDA is a marketing company called The Business Development Agency founded in 1994. Its specialism is providing targeted marketing expertise to create revenue or profitability or efficiency by making Clients' propositions more relevant and engaging to target audiences. We’re sad when we see poor marketing and we always try to push the edge of the envelope. The service proposition splits into 3 key areas:

- Data analysis, knowledge enhancement, database marketing.
- Digital customer connection online.
- Offline direct marketing fulfillment.

Markets we work in - B2C, B2B, Retail, Charity

Unless there is a specific brief after initial discussions on a client’s needs & our services, we typically start working on a ‘data fact find’ on their customers’ & prospects' behaviour. This usually crystallizes the client’s gaps & opportunities in target market penetration & retention; this in turn scopes the marketing/customer relationship management (CRM)/digital connection brief to be addressed for their business or organization in the short & medium term.
