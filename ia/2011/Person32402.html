Vicki Kolovou's Summary

With initial studies in the field of Food Technology and 16 years of experience in the publishing/advertising department of the Greek technical Wood Industry magazine- she started studying IT & Computing at the UK Open University and upon completion decided to embark on her own business. Apart from owner of netWire she is also one of the Web developers of the firm, that now employees 12 people.

netWire is located in Athens, GR with major activities, web and mobile development solutions. We specialise on retail and social shopping ideas for our customers through the development of web & mobile applications for fashion and shopping brands. We also help our customers develop their online social media presence, through training and online solutions.

netWire is the owner of ermoumag.com, the online fashion & shopping magazine and of StreetGuides which include stores of the major Athenian roads. ermoumag is a significant influence in the Greek fashion scene, offline and online, with over 45.000 monthly readers.

Vicki Kolovou was a helping member for the Girl Geek Dinners in Athens and in March of 2010 was the co-organiser of MediaCamp Athens 2010.
She started the Startegy initiative calling upon 6 other entrepreneurs, as co-founders, in the hope of motivating people to start their own venture, whether this is a new business or some other project.

Specialties

Experience in design, creativity, branding and identity awareness, Social shopping and retail solutions. Networking. Enjoys meeting and talking with people from all over the world.
