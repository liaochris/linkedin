Jason Gastrich's Summary

I'm an entrepreneur and author with many interests, but I have a passion for music and teaching it. I've taught all grade levels (elementary school through college) and now I teach private music lessons in Pacific Beach.

I've written a few books and one is on improvising. It's called, "Improvising Made Easy For Guitar and Bass". It's a great system that helps students play music in any key. You simply find the key a song is in (or you write a song), then use one of the key sheets to find the right notes to play.

Specialties

Doing a lot with a little.
