Chad Hanysh's Summary

Founded MyLoopCard.com in 2007 after seeing a niche in marketing to golf courses and providing local businesses with an advertising vehicle to reach the high profile demographic of golfers. MyLoopCard.com is a 16-page, custom designed, full color, graphical description of the golf course supported by ads from local businesses. The golf course saves money on the cost of the scorecards, golfers now 'experience' their loop of golf, and local advertisers can reach a new and untapped audience.

Specialties

Sales, Marketing, Advertising, Web Development, Franchise Development
