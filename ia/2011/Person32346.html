Neil Other’s Summary

Construction technology is my passion. Over the last two decades I have built, marketed, sold and serviced every kind of machine control system and precision positioning instrument imaginable.

Today, through The Kellogg Report I educate our industry about 3D grade control and telematics solutions and help contractors adopt this technology in order to improve their bottom line.

Successful implementation of this technology requires more than just bolting hardware to your machine. Getting the most of 3D grade control systems means embracing a new way of moving dirt. From drafting to as-built, there is more margin to be had in every step of the process.

We have only just begun to see how technology is revolutionizing the construction industry. Automation and information technology are increasingly taking center stage, helping to ensure projects are completed according to specs, on time and on budget.
