Dimitris Kalogeropoulos's Summary

I am a serial blogger and a web enthusiast. I started blogging back in 2008 in my Greek personal blog. Now, I am the editor in Chief of away.gr, I am writing in pestaola.gr and in my English personal blog. I enjoy writing about cars, social media, technology and entrepreneurship.

I am working as a social media consultant and I strongly believe that social media is a great way to transform the way companies communicate with their audience. I also believe that web2.0 should and must be as honest as possible. We are well over the era of secrets, especially in web.

What I enjoy most, is participating in Conferences about Web and Technology. I have participated twice in "The Next Web", in "LeWeb" and in "Mobile World Congress".
