Sally Franklin’s Summary

I'm focusing on fine art and although I enjoy many different mediums, much of my work is done in Watercolor and/or Prisma Colored Pencils. I was a guest author for artist, Lori McNee, when I wrote an article describing my technique. http://www.finearttips.com/2010/02/using-colored-pencil-to-create-fine-art/ I never want to say that I am satisfied with the art that I am doing because I feel there are always creative things to learn or experiment with and art will always be a big part of my life.

Sally Franklin’s Specialties:

I excel in the medium of colored pencil, Detailed drawing and illustration, Composition and Color combinations.
