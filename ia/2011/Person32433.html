Andrew Jones's Summary

Graduate of the University of Oklahoma with a degree in Advertising. I was (and still remain) the Director of Internet marketing for one of the most well known loan officer training companies in the United States for several years prior to starting my own internet marketing company; New Media Fluent. New Media Fluent focuses on new technology, social media marketing, training and social media consulting. I also worked for one of the most prestigious advertising agencies in the country, The Richards Group, working on the accounts of; The Home Depot, The Atlanta Falcons, Bahama Breeze, and Baal Dan Charity. I have also been a student of new and social media for several years now learning the in’s and out’s of how to make it work for digital promotion. I make it my job to stay on the cutting edge of all new media so as to insure you are already in the ballgame when the next big thing catches on.
