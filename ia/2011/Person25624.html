Laura Ramos's Summary

Follow me on Twitter: @lauraramos

Marketing executive and industry analyst specializing in demand generation, lead management, product marketing, social media, integrated marketing, and customer relationship management.I place a high premium on public speaking, writing, marketing effectiveness , product positioning, competitive analysis, and making the call on marketing automation winners and losers.

Currently, I run industry marketing for Xerox Services in North America. My team builds business demand for managed print services at the interface between people and the business process -- the document. We help position Xerox as a thought-leader and business contributor in the Education, Financial Services, Government, High-Technology/Telecom, Manufacturing, and Retail sectors.

Laura Ramos's Experience

Vice President, Principal Analyst

Forrester Research

Public Company; FORR; Research industry

October 2001 – August 2010 (8 years 11 months)

As a VP and Principal Analyst, I lead Forrester's B2B marketing practice. My research focuses on effective lead generation, marketing mix, demand management, sales and marketing integration, setting social media strategy, and the use of the Web to build customer engagement. I have advised F500 and high technology companies on marketing measurement/accountability, marketing strategy, Web marketing, and marketing technology investment including: Adobe, Bell Canada, CA, Cisco, Cognizant, DemandBase, Eloqua, HP (TSG), IBM, McGrawHill, Microsoft, Orange Business Services, Silverpop, Sun, T Rowe Price, TELUS, UPS, Unisfair, Whirlpool, and Xerox Global Services. I have also built Forrester's B2B marketing consulting revenue from $0 to in excess of $750,000 annually in 18 months. Prior to initiating Forrester’s B2B coverage, I advised clients in healthcare and life sciences industry. I also researched the portal and enterprise search markets and how businesses invest in these technologies to drive business change and collaboration.
