  • Group General Manager

    Kura Hulanda Resorts

    (Hospitality industry)

    August 2010 — Present (6 months)

    Hotel Kura Hulanda Spa & Casino and Lodge Kura Hulanda & Beach Club are sister properties on the sunny Caribbean island of Curacao. Kura Hulanda Resorts offers two unique vacation experiences in one sophisticated island destination. Both properties are members of Small Luxury Hotels of the World, meeting the precise and high standards the organization requires to be a part of this prestigious group. While the properties offer two different vacation experiences, they are alike in their level of luxury service and the pampering environment they provide.

    The five-star Hotel Kura Hulanda Spa and Casino offers a boutique village experience in the center of Willemstad, the island's capitol city, 20 minutes from the airport and walking distance to prime shopping, diverse cuisine, nightlife and more. Spread over an eight-block area, the resort village includes two pools, multiple restaurants and bars, shops, a world-class museum, spa, casino, courtyards, gardens and more - everything at your fingertips.

    On the western end of the island you'll find the beautiful Lodge Kura Hulanda and Beach Club nestled between the cliffs and ocean, providing a secluded, island hideaway. Situated to overlook the Caribbean Sea, the Lodge's 74 villas and rooms provide a sophisticated simplicity, furnished with crisp white cushions and linens that complement dark wood and rattan furnishings. Voted number one property in the Caribbean for snorkeling and diving in 2009 by the Professional Travel Guide.

    Both properties are members of Small Luxury Hotels of the World.

    Complimentary scheduled shuttle service provided daily between the properties.
