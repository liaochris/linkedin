Jo Ketley's Summary

Addiction Entertainment is a sister company of Addiction London, and part of the Addiction Worldwide Group. We work specifically for the music and entertainment industries.

With offices in London and New York, we are a creative agency and production company with over 12 years of experience. We pride ourselves on being able to offer all your creative and production needs under one roof: TV Commercials, Art Direction, Music Videos, Artwork, Print, Press, Outdoor, EPKs, Digital Marketing, and Bespoke Online Content. You get total consistency of creative and quality across your campaign, one point of contact to work with, and cost-efficient, competitive, up-front budgets.

Working closely with record labels and artist management, we pride ourselves on being responsive, meticulous, passionate and of course incredibly creative in all we do - striking the right balance of being creative yet very commercially aware.
