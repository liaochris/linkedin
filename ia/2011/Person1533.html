Alessandro Salvati’s Summary

In 2009 I worked for Barcelona Port Authority (strategy and development office). I worked out informations on other ports development's plans and guides for the development of crisis scenarios on global demand of freight mobility.

In October 2008 I joined a 5 months Master course on 'GIS' (Geographical Information System) and 'territorial survey' at Autonomous University of Barcelona,

I attended 2 international workshop: 'Low cost landscapes II', in Alghero (Sardinia) in April 2008 and 'Po Delta Summer School' in Rovigo area in July 2008.

I got my MA degree in 'Environmental planning and policies' (organized by IUAV University, University of Sassari and Autonomous University of Barcelona) in April 2010 with a thesis focused on environmental economy (Mark 110/110 cum laude). Thesis title: ' Fossil Economy', a both theoretical and empirical research on the relationship between 'energy regime' and society's wealth.

I earned my BA degree in 'Urban and Regional Planning' (IUAV University) in March 2007 with a thesis focused on the use of 'scenarios' as a tool for planning and public participation processes.
