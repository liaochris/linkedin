Larry Steinberg’s Summary

Larry Steinberg is the president of Steinberg Financial Group, an unique financial services firm that offers investment advisory services through Steinberg Financial Advisers, 401(k) plans through Arizona 401(k) Plans, as well as a full service insurance agency, Steinberg Insurance. SFG has offices in Scottsdale and Pasadena.

Larry’s personal clientele includes entrepreneurs, business owners, corporate executives, & the self employed, while his business client’s range in size from solo practitioners to the US operations of foreign multi-national corporations. Larry views his role for many of his client’s as that of the financial quarterback, a key member of the team his client’s rely on. To be the best team player he can be, Larry has spent the last two decades not only developing his personal expertise in all things financial, but also building a network of other professionals & contacts that he utilizes as a center of influence to the benefit of his clientele.

A veteran public speaker, Larry has spoken to groups as diverse as the IBEW Retiree Club, the foreign press, numerous service clubs, & is a regular speaker for the Information Management Network, global organizers of institutional finance & investment conferences (imn.org).

Larry received his AB with honors & distinction from Occidental College, where he played football & rugby. Larry continued his athletics after college, playing rugby for the Oxy Olde Boys RFC. Larry has earned numerous awards both in the financial services industry, in athletics, for his activity in community organizations including Rotary & the Jaycees, & from his alma mater.

Licensed in AZ, CA, CO, IA, NM & TX. Securities and Investment Advisory Services offered through Berthel Fisher & Company Financial Services, Inc. (BFCFS) Member FINRA/SIPC. The Steinberg Financial Group and its divisions Steinberg Financial Advisers, Arizona 401(k) Plans, and Steinberg Insurance are independent of BFCFS. CA Insurance License 0B19683.

Larry Steinberg’s Specialties:

investment management (asset allocation), 401(k) plans, employee benefits, business succession planning, business insurance (liability, property, & work comp incl. surplus lines), personal insurance (life, health, disability, long term care, auto, homeowners (including high value), liability incl. umbrella), financial advisor for entrepreneurs, business owners, pre-retirees and those already retired
