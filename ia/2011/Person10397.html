Roger Darnell's Summary

Roger Darnell is a leading press agent specializing in media and high-tech businesses, as well as being a publisher, a consultant, and an award-winning writer-producer working in all media.

Combining U.S. Air Force supervisory-level experience in instructional systems design with two B.A. Communications degrees and nearly 20 years of top-level production and marketing experience, Roger designs and conducts results-oriented strategic public relations campaigns for dynamic high-profile companies and their clients.

Specialties

Developing strategic marketing plans and then leading, managing and executing them, maximizing media channels.
Serving as trusted liaison with customers' high-level client and vendor contacts.
Authoring, pitching, clearing and distributing strategic news content.
Brand development.
Reputation management.
Scriptwriting film/video/multimedia content.
Content concepting and production, for all audiences, media forms and channels.
