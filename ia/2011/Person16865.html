Joseph Haddad's Summary

General Counsel Majid Al Futtaim Group.

Head of the Corporate Law Department and founder of Haddad & Associates, Me Haddad is a member of the Bar of Beirut (1979), the Canadian Bar Association (1991) and the Bar of Quebec (1992).

In diverse positions held, Joseph had put in practice transactional skills (real estate, commercial, corporate ‎restructuring, strategic funding), organizational skills (catalyst for policies and procedures and authority ‎matrix covering different articulations in corporate environment), lawyering skills (preemptive legal ‎advice rooted in his multi legal system thorough knowledge, and a multi jurisdiction litigation and ‎arbitration management expertise), managerial skills (enhancing the value add of the legal ‎department as both a service provider and a risk gatekeeper), and business skills.‎

Joseph’s vast experience of international law dates back to the beginnings of his law studies.
A graduate from St. Joseph University's Faculty of Law (cum laude), he furthered his studies by obtaining a Bachelor's of French Law diploma from the Université de Lyon in France. An Excellency Scholarship from the French Foreign Ministry led him to a Master's degree in comparative law from the Université de Paris II and a Diploma of advanced studies (DEA) in International Private Law from the Université de Paris II.

Aside from his professional obligations, he has been a teacher of two law courses at the Faculty of Law of St Paul University –Sagesse- in Beirut (Comparative corporate and partnerships law, and principles of conflict of laws international private law).

Specialties

Practical experience in private international law, property development, corporate and real estate law within MENA, Levant and GCC.
