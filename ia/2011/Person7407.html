Cristiano Betta’s Summary

In 2005 I started a small company with 3 old colleagues/co-students from university called Education Made Easy. In 2007 we decided for multiple reasons to change our plans and venture a new startup called TipIt.to.

In 2007 I left this company/idea before it was launched to go and finish my Master of Science degree in London, UK.

In 2008 I started work at an exciting social media agency in central London to get the experience. After a year I left to start my own freelance business.

In 2009 I joined MinuteBox.com as co-founder and CTO and learned to work with PayPal payments integration.

In 2010 I won PayPal's CharityHack 2010 and started a company called PizzaPowered with my fellow winners to further pursue the development of CharityBoxApp and some other technologies that we had in the oven.

Cristiano Betta’s Specialties:

Frontend (HTML,CSS,JS) and backend designer in Ruby (Rails), PHP (Symfony) and expert at PayPal payments integration and browser extension development
