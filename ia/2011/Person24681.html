Kim van Velzen's Summary

I am an optimistic epicure with a great passion for food & beverages and travelling around the globe. I have a strong believe in living together, working together and creating together. My work and private life are in perfect harmony with each other and with my passions. I am always looking for big challenges and stimulate high goals. I am an inspiration for and facilitate my team members in their work. Besides that I can be a little pigheaded but always listen to the quality of arguments.

My career has always been in hospitality. It’s the most beautiful work in the world because everyday you can be the positive difference for guests. Important values for me are: innovation and social innovation, servant leadership, fair-trade, sustainability, organic food, Make Your Own, sharing, co-creation, social media and sensory marketing.

My best personal skills are a strong guest focus, keen eye for detail, strategic insight, result driven and my servant leadership style.

Live your dream and let your passions be your guide!
