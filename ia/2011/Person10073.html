Danielle Elliott Smith’s Summary

Danielle Smith is a wife and Mommy to two sweet and sassy small people. In her ‘free time’, she balances work as the founder and primary author of ExtraordinaryMommy.com as well as hosting The Spin Cycle on MomTV.com - a show that keeps you current and helps you to balance your world as it spins around you. She co-hosts Why Moms Matter on Blog Talk Radio and is the Video and Vlogging Editor for Blissfully Domestic.com.

Danielle is making a name for herself in the hosting arena - tackling a 4 hour live event in Chicago for MomTV as well as being the go-to person for Devries PR's broadcasts from the 140 Conference at Kodak Theater in Los Angeles.

In her previous life, Danielle was a Television News Anchor and Reporter. Many of her life’s experiences have been in public speaking, writing and listening. Her career path has served to make her the author, social media addict and right-brained, creative girl she is now.

ExtraordinaryMommy.com is a website designed to remind moms of the Extraordinary work they do daily while loving and raising their children. The site provides resources and a regular blog chronicling life as a Mommy - as Danielle sees it.
Danielle makes ‘Give Good, Get Good’ a motto she lives by - aiming to teach her children to give of themselves daily.

Danielle, her family and ExtraordinaryMommy.com made international headlines this summer when a family photo - one used for their Christmas card, was taken from one of her sites and used in a life size window advertisement in the Czech Republic. It is Danielle’s social media connections that brought this story to her attention and helped people to begin the process of protecting themselves online.

In the past year, Danielle has been seen on the CBS Early Show, CNN, and FOX News, in addition to every St. Louis affiliate. She has also been on NPR and mentioned in numerous newspapers, including USA Today, and is a contributing author to The Internet Mommy and The Little Pink Prayer Book.
