David Wright's Summary

After 17 years of building a career in the Global Automotive OEM Industry I have relocated to Japan to develop opportunities in a broad range of areas.

This involves the research and development of procurement channels for specialty components for markets external to Japan and providing connections and contacts for counterparts in Oceania.

My existing and previous contracts provided professional assistance and coaching to clients at Daimaru, Cordial Financial Advisors, CERI (Civil Engineering Research Institute), Hokkaido University, Sumitomo, Continental and Auto Parts International.

In addition I am providing professional English Instruction to adults and children based on my broad experience of language acquisition and the requirements of the individual.

I am focused on providing solutions through continuous improvement and problem solving methodology using a multinational network of contacts and reliable professional resources.

I am experienced in the Automotive Industry through various facets of business, engineering and customer relations.
