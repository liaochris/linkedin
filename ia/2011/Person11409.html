Deirdre Leone’s Summary

A graduate of the Widener School of Law, Deirdre Leone worked in both law and sales in the Washington, D.C. and New York City markets. Utilizing her knowledge from these two areas of business practice, Ms. Leone became a strong asset to both her clients and business partners.

Tapping into her social networking abilities, Ms. Leone quickly realized that online relationships were a new measurable point of communication between businesses and their customers. Ms. Leone saw the impact of businesses and personas utilizing social media was a continually growing trend and decided to take her business expertise and social media knowledge to launch Gabbaroo, a social media firm, and aStar Interactive, a full service digital marketing and public relations company.

Dedicated to helping businesses and personas grow, Ms. Leone continually tracks the ever-changing digital landscape to find new tools, strategies and unique angles for her clients.

For more information on Ms. Leone, contact her at Deirdre@aStarInteractive.com.
