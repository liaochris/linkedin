Ed Primeau’s Summary

Ed Primeau is the President and CEO of Primeau Productions, Inc a full service digital video production and Internet marketing company. Ed is also an audio and video forensic expert.

Ed Primeau began his career in multimedia in 1979. As an engineer in the music industry working with renowned recording artists like Anita Baker, Bob Seger and Barry Manilow. In the early 1980’s, Ed Primeau developed a music industry marketing niche, hosting album release parties for major label record companies.

In 1984, Ed founded www.PrimeauProductions.com a full-service multimedia production company engaged in digital audio and video production, Internet marketing, publishing and social media promotion.

Video in strategic Internet marketing campaigns is crucial for business today to compete in the global arena. Video is also a powerful media for communication. Skype video and video email are part of Ed's coaching agenda. Corporate, sales training, marketing and entertainment audio and video production is Primeau Productions, Inc. foundation.

Digital publishing helps Primeau Productions clients’ transition from traditional books, Cd's and DVDs to low cost mass distributed digital products on iTunes, Amazon and Audible among other networks

Ed Primeau’s Specialties:

Video production, Internet marketing, Internet video, web 2.0 coaching, broadcast advertising, documentaries, audio and video forensic expert, expert witness, video clarification, video restoration, audio restoration.
