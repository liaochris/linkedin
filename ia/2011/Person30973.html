Juan Felix's Summary

I strongly support the idea that a business without a strong brand has no business. Successful positioning starts with listening to your prospects, clients and employees. Learn how your brand is perceived. Mirror Yourself.

That's why I love to support businesses with social media to grow their engagement with their audience. The social reflection businesses receive from their clients grows their business.

A strong position in the mindset of your clients, means high returns on your marketing investments.

That's why I founded Mirror Yourself in 2010. I offer businesses my extensive experience in marketing research to connect them with their audience.

My guiding question in business is: “Why should anyone do business with you?"

"If you want to have success with positioning, you have to be consistent." "Make sure that your personal social profile matches your positioning.

I want to help other businesses with successful positioning. I teach them how to listen to their audience and use social media reflection as their guide. In other words: Mirror Yourself.

I want to support businesses with the best quality possible, that's why I keep listening and learning every day.
