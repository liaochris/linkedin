Abigail Blackburn's Summary

An expert in communicating effectively with mass market women in a highly visual, engaging and entertaining way. I live, breathe and understand magazines - with broad experience in both the glossy monthly and weekly sectors.

Experienced Editor after spending two years at the helm of NOW, one of the UK's leading celebrity news weeklies. I have also worked on several of the biggest consumer magazine brands, including Cosmopolitan, New Woman, TV Week, Vogue Australia, Reveal and The Daily Mail's Femail.

Recently relocated to Sydney, where I've moved back into the glossy market, with a year-long stint on Cosmopolitan's Australian edition.

Specialties

Magazine development, editing, writing and managing a large editorial team. Solid experience in commissioning, interviews and print & online media team integration.

Expert commentator on women's issues related to celebrity news, careers, relationships, current affairs, fashion, beauty, health and lifestyle.

TV and radio appearances (including NBC Today Show, BBC Breakfast, BBC 5Live Radio, BBC3 shows) as a cultural commentator.
