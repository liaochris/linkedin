Annie Infinite's Summary

"I help people find their authentic voice and project it out into the world with integrity, passion and authenticity. Oh & by the way, I strategise, manage and teach social media as well. I am the Social Media Sourceress!"

Annie Infinite (Robinson) is a successful social media strategist, consultant and trainer whose clientele includes both international and Australian businesses with wide and varying interests from green businesses, jewellery, resorts, directories and social sites. Annie consults with organisations wishing to take advantage of social media using social engagement strategies and creative, intelligent use of social media profiles to disseminate brand awareness and engagement online in an individualised focussed approach resulting in more brand recognition and user advocacy.

Annie is the owner of Saucy Social Media a social media management, consulting and training company, specialising in creating social media strategic plans, social media management and education (workshops) and training (in-house) in social media for businesses. Annie is a Medical Social Media specialist with a very unique set of skills required to navigate the minefield of social media for the medical profession.

Annie presents on best practice and scope of using social media to business owners with a natural, fun and informative style which belies her deep knowledge of her subject. With her own very busy social media profiles and management of her clients profiles Annie spends every moment refining her craft and keeping up with the trends to form an encyclopedic and ever updated knowledge of her niche.

"I am so passionate about educating people about marketing in authentic ways, be true to your passions and yourself and market your business with this in mind. I want to turn the marketing world on it's head, by recreating trust in marketing, using 'word of mouse' to create word of mouth through creating connection with your client and customer base."
