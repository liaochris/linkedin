Samantha Rathling's Summary

Samantha Rathling is a dynamic and driven entrepreneur with a mission to change the way the world does recruitment, co-founder of Expect Talent. Over 90% of our business comes from the 3 R's - Referral, Recommendation and Repeat business.

With a unique approach, and innovative ways of working, we deliver results for our clients, and are passionate about WOWing our customers and candidates with exceptional service levels.

Expect Talent is proud to be working with clients such as Bentley Systems in Dublin and Amazon in Cork. These companies work with us as preferred suppliers because of our flexibility and quality of candidates.

In addition to running Expect Talent, Samantha is also the Area Director for Professional Development within BNI, working with a talented team of 14 experts on networking and referrals, upskilling our members on how to be successful through word of mouth marketing. Samantha recently joined the BNI European Training Team and now trains brand new and existing BNI Directors from across UK, Ireland and the Netherlands.

Samantha speaks regularly on the subject of Networking Skills and Referral Marketing, and has presented internationally in Holland, the USA, and in the UK and Ireland on this topic.
