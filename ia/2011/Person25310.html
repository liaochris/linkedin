Konstantine Kyros’s Summary

Pioneer in online legal marketing services. Created web based consumer platforms connecting hundreds of thousands of consumers with law firms since 1998. Owner, lead attorney in Internet technology law firm representing businesses in new media transactions and litigation in high profile cases at beginning of internet era to present.

Current practice group focusing on federal and state securities claims including class actions and derivative claims, Dodd Frank SEC whistleblower claims, Federal False Claims Act matters.

Heading mesothelioma litigation recruitment team in one of nations leading plaintiffs efforts to represent victims of asbestos exposure.

Leading expert in law firm business growth using digital marketing. Directed marketing programs for over 100 law firms throughout the United States and Europe. Developed web-based strategies for connecting clients with lawyers. Worked with who's who of the most prominent plaintiff's law firms in the United States handling pharmaceutical, patent, tax, aviation, securities, insurance, class actions and asbestos/mesothelioma litigation matters. Created and introduced internet marketing strategy for the national Law Office of James Sokolove and partner with that firm for 8 years (Firm is now the largest marketer of legal services in the US). Implemented and designed one of the most successful results oriented legal digital marketing programs in the United States.

Generated over $150 million dollars in gross legal fees for my law firm clients (over $400 Million in Settlements for consumers) (2004-2010) including $29.4 million in 2007 by connecting thousands of consumers with law firms using internet marketing. Managed an over $15 Million online budget in last 5 years with annual online marketing expenditures of over $2+ Million (2009).

Actively partnering with law firms in joint ventures to get new clients in all areas of practice.
