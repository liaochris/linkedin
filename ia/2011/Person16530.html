Greg Day's Summary

Greg Day, Director of EMEA Security Strategy for McAfee and Vice-Chair of Intellect’s Cyber Security Group, helps organisations in the private and public sector develop a comprehensive security risk management strategy. His wide-ranging practical and consultative experience at both business and technical levels gives executives access to the expert insight they need to make informed decisions about the increasingly complex threat and security environment.

Greg’s professionalism, experience and commitment to the industry has made him one of Europe’s most eminent and sought-after speakers on global cyber-security issues. He is a frequent contributor to journals, has had numerous papers published and is a keynote speaker on all aspects of information security at conferences and events.
