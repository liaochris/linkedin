December 1992 – Present (18 years 3 months)

I am a partner in Dreams and Logic, a development service consultancy. We formed Dreams and Logic about a year ago and like to sum up our offering as, "A consultancy at the intersection of Physical and Virtual worlds."

SVP Interactive/Creative Lisa Sirlin (http://www.linkedin.com/in/lsirlin) and I combine our 40+ year trenches-to-executive expertise in Interactive and Electronic Toy and Consumer product creation and team management. We’ve built an extensive network of partner agencies, industry veterans and rising stars in the creative kid and engineering fields.

Altogether these sum up to a group of people with similar passions that work very well together and have a blast while doing it. It's a consortium of colleagues that collectively, under the Dreams and Logic umbrella, offers ideation, design, development, manufacturing and sell-in expertise at any stage in a children’s or child-like-consumer product lifecycle.

Dreams and Logic (and its colleagues and agencies) can provide concept-to-shelf services for a wide variety of toys and consumer electronics; we choose to be centered on the marriage of physical and digital initiatives, encompassing:

- Web-enabled and pc-connected toys
- Consumer electronics
- augmented reality
- Toy + web online games
- Social and media experiences
- Virtual worlds and digital destinations
- Compelling kid content that tells a story
- New user interfaces
