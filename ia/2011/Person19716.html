Jason Deitch's Summary

I opened my own law practice right our of law school in 1994. May area of practice was, and has continued to be since, personal injury litigation. In 2002, after building a highly successful one man firm, I reached a crossroad. My practice had become too large to continue as it stood. I had a choice to significantly increase my infrastructure and staff, or seek a merger with a larger firm. In June of that year, I chose to merge my practice with Boone & Davis, another small firm in Ft. Lauderdale, FL. With a substantial infrastructure and staff already in place, I was able to concentrate on doing what I do best, to wit, rain making and negotiating. I handled all of our pre-litigation files from their inception through either settlement or the filing of a lawsuit. I also handled all marketing efforts for the firm. While success can be measured in many ways, that which I am most proud of is the fact that several of our most fierce adversaries (insurance adjusters and defense attorneys) have chosen to refer to us their friends and family in need of representation.
In spite of the level of success I have achieved to this point, I have just made a life changing decision. In July of 2009, I informed my law partner that I was leaving the practice to embark on a new adventure. At the time, I did not yet know where that adventure was going to take me, but I was beyond excited at the possibilities. Whatever career path I chose, whether I remained in the legal profession or tried something completely different, I knew it will be pursued with a renewed vigor and a sense of excitement that has been curiously missing for the past several years in my business life.
Fast forward to September 18, 2009. I have just launched a website called ineedapersonalinjurylawyer.com. I developed this website to make a difference for people injured by the negligence of others, by using my experience to direct them to a lawyer that I know will get the job done right.
