Michael Goldsberry's Summary

Integrated marketing communications leader with a proven background in utilizing fresh consumer insight for building holistic brand communication plans that exceed business goals. Experienced in developing multi-channel programs that engage consumers throughout their path to purchase cycle.

Strong understanding of Retail, CPG, and Private Label companies. Have worked on ALDI Foods, P&G, Kraft, Heinz Pet Foods, MSNBC, Samsclub.com, Morningstar, Northwestern Mutual Life and B2B work for Northern Telecom, Polycom and NEC Monitors and Projectors.

Experience developing integrated marketing strategies and implementing plans for multi-channel communications utilizing 'storeback' strategies. Led advertising, digital, direct marketing, public relations, search and social media, and design teams.

Strong background in digital marketing building digital teams, CMS, online advertising, SEO/SEM, social media and mobile marketing. Responsible for local, national and global account management including P/L, staffing and training, revenue growth, competitive, forecasting, technology and innovation.
