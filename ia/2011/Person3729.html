Art Fromm’s Summary

Extensive experience helping high-tech companies grow revenue, hold price, increase customer satisfaction, and optimize cost of sales through Solution-Oriented, Consultative Sales Transformation programs and services.

Also, strong specialization in Webinar best practices, conversion from in-person sessions, and teaching presenters how to deliver Excellent Webinars.

Over twenty-five years experience in roles of increasing responsibility in Engineering, Sales, Sales Management Positions, and Sales Development

Results include:
• Managed, developed, and helped facilitate Sales Transformation program for sales and sales support team of a multi-billion/year company. Level 4 evaluations show that the training program resulted the customer winning 17% NEW business within 2 years, and increasing win-rate 35%.
• Key sales, development, and delivery roles for the Consultative Selling roll-out for hundreds of Commercial and Partner Account Managers for a Key Client, resulting in up to 4x increase in pipeline, and increasing win-rate by up to 30%.
• Developed and personally delivered training sessions for hundreds of Sales Engineers and Partner Sales Engineers in the High-tech/IT/Telecoms industry, helping them achieve higher rates of Technical Win and Technical Closure, and find and manage Risk to the Deal.
• Converted numerous ILT sessions to Virtual Classroom, resulting in dramatic customer savings both in time/expenses and opportunity costs by eliminating travel and keeping personnel in the field.
• Developed "Webinar Excellence" Workshop covering the best practices for delivering outstanding presentations and training via the internet. Attendees who were already delivering Webinars rated their capabilities as 3.2/5.0 coming into the session and 4.8/5.0 after the session. More importantly the participants of their workshops should benefit from substantial increase in uptake/learning due to the focus on engaging the learner/audience.
