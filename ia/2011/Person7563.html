Christopher Hosford’s Summary

Christopher Hosford is an award-winning business and financial writer and editor based in New York City. He is former editor-in-chief of Sales & Marketing Management magazine, the multi-award-winning professional business magazine serving the needs of executives with direct responsibility for sales, marketing, and strategic development. He is East Coast Bureau Chief for BtoB magazine, and in addition has written for such publications as Architectural Record, Inc. Magazine, Multi-Housing News and Hospitality Design. Hosford has served as editor-in-chief or senior editor with Sales & Marketing Management, Meeting News, Corporate & Incentive Travel, and Life Extension. He is co-author (with Dr. Jorge Picaza) of "AIDS: The Hidden Killer," one of the first book-length examinations of the AIDS crisis worldwide.
