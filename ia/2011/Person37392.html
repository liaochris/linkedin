Robert Gedarovich's Summary

Seasoned digital publicist and social media public relations/marketing executive always interested in learning about new technologies for the ever-growing social media space.

I am a part of Kaplow PR's Kdrive social media division, providing digital strategy and thought-leadership in a cross-agency role.

I also work on several of Kaplow's technology clients, including @Foursquare and @NeroSolutions, in a traditional PR and media relations role.

Specialties

I excel at crafting social media strategies to help bring brands into the digital realm while ensuring that all online efforts tie back to their greater business goals.

Blogger outreach, social media strategy and best practices council, social content distribution, media relations, writing, event planning.
