Chris Hewitt's Summary

I am a film director, screenwriter, and editor in Austin, Texas. I am studying film at the University of Texas while working as a Walt Disney Motion Pictures Studio intern. My film-related work experience includes interning one summer in Los Angeles, CA for Hollywood Gang Productions (“300,” “Everybody’s Fine”, “Immortals”) assisting the Development Assistant and Story Editor with script coverage and other tasks. I have also worked for two summers at Channel 13 in Mandeville, LA as an Editor and Videographer.

I have five years of experience with Avid Media Composer, Avid Express Pro and Avid Mojo, in addition to more recent experience with Adobe Flash CS-5. My production experience includes work with the Panasonic AG-HPX500 P2, Bolex, Arriflex Super 16, Canon GL2, and Canon XLR cameras. My short-films and short screenplays have received awards in film festivals all over the country. I have strong communication skills and have received numerous awards in Extemporaneous Speaking and Oratory speech competitions while in school. I am an academic honor student, an Eagle Scout, and I am proficient in Spanish.
