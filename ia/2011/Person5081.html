Betsy Flanagan’s Summary

Social Media Strategist at Wells Fargo Bank with a passion for innovation, strategy, new media and online trends. Betsy has over a decade of financial services experience; previous positions at Wells Fargo include online sales and marketing, investor relations and Internet strategy. Outside of Wells Fargo, Betsy founded StartupStudio.com, a podcast featuring her interviews with some of the world’s most innovative entrepreneurs, including the founders of Southwest Airlines, Coinstar, and ZipRealty, among others. Startup Studio has been distributed via the San Jose Mercury News, AllBusiness.com, University of Virginia’s Darden Graduate School of Business and Chambers of Commerce. Prior to joining Wells Fargo, she was a wealth manager at DLJ, where she advised company founders who had recently sold or taken their companies public. Betsy also founded and sold Georgetown Learning Centers (www.learnglc.com), a college prep tutoring center. Betsy has an MBA from the University of Virginia’s Darden Graduate School of Business and an AB in anthropology, cum laude, from Harvard College.
