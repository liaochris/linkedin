Shashank Shekhar’s Summary

Shashank Shekhar is widely regarded as “Bay Area's #1 Mortgage Expert”. Shashank is the author of widely acclaimed book - "First Time Home Buying 101". He has trained thousands of Real Estate Professionals on the topic of Lending, Real Estate and Social Media. He has been featured on Pen TV, KCBS Radio, SF Examiner and Blog Talk Radio. He was recently rated among top 25 mortgage professionals in the country by National Mortgage Professional Magazine.

He has an MBA in Marketing. Shashank’s business philosophy is to “create raving fans” out of all his clients and referral partners. He achieves this by offering “legendary service” that is reflected in 100% satisfaction record of his clients.

Shashank's more than 10 years of lending business experience includes working for GE Money in mid level management and working for a start-up mortgage origination company as Director of Product Management. He is currently the CEO of Arcus Lending, Inc. a mortgage brokerage company that he founded in 2008. He is also the CEO and Co-Founder of Web Lunch Box, LLC a Social Media Training and Consulting company.
