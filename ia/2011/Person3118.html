Ann Douglas's Experience

Author, Brand Creator, and President

Page One Productions Inc.

Privately Held; Writing and Editing industry

June 1997 – Present (13 years 10 months)

Author and creator of an internationally bestselling pregnancy/parenting book brand: The Mother of All Books series. Launched in Canada in 2000 and in the US in 2002. Now available in numerous other countries as well. Audiobook format of the lead title -- The Mother of All Pregnancy Books -- is also available.

A spinoff series, The Mother of All Solutions series, was launched in Canada and the US in spring 2006 (Wiley Publishing Inc.)

Other titles focus on pregnancy and parenting, self-help, and children's non-fiction.

Contributor to a number of highly respected anthologies about motherhood, including Shari MacDonald Strong's The Maternal is Political, Andrea Buchanan's It's a Girl, May Friedman and Shana Calixte's Mothering and Blogging: The Radical Act of the Mommy Blog, and Kyra Anderson and Vicki Forman's Gravity Pulls You In.

Copywriter and Online Writer

Page One Productions Inc.

September 1992 – Present (18 years 7 months)

Specialize in writing copy for health, education, lifestyle, and non-profit campaigns aimed at female consumers. Clients, brands, and organizations served include Sunlight, Kraft, Ford, Toyota, the Quaker Oats Company of Canada, Trent University, Fleming College, Lakefield College School, Ontario Parks, Ontario Ministry of Natural Resources, Peterborough Square.

Magazine and Newspaper Columnist; Freelance Writer; Blogger; Vlogger; On-Air Columnist

Page One Productions Inc.

Privately Held; Writing and Editing industry

June 1988 – Present (22 years 10 months)

Magazine and newspaper columnist and writer specializing in parenting, pregnancy, preconception, fertility, motherhood, women's health.

Video columnist and expert for ParentsAsk.com (June 1010 to present). I contribute short videos in response to parents' questions about pregnancy and parenting-related issues.

Columnist for The Toronto Star (Sept 2010 to present), ParentCentral.ca (April 2008 to present), Yahoo! Canada (May 2007 to present), Conceive Magazine (October 2005 to present), City Parent (1995 to present), and What's Up Kids? (2005 to present).

On-air parenting columnist, Channel 12 TV (CBC affiliate station), Durham, Oshawa, Ontario (2007 to present).

Former columnist for WebMD.com, Women.com, Canadian Living, Healthy Woman, Flare Pregnancy, Glow, WeeWelcome.ca, and Urban Baby and Toddler.

Articles have appeared in Today's Parent, Parents Canada, Canadian Family, Canadian Living, Chatelaine, Cottage Life, Quill and Quire, The Globe and Mail, The Chicago Tribune, Flare, Pregnancy, ePregnancy, Junior, and numerous other publications and websites.
