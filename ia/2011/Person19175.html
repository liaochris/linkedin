Jared Garfield’s Summary

LION500 & GroupLinked Open to All Invitations: jtgarfieldjr@yahoo.com

As a top “Rich Dad Poor Dad” Trainer, Jared Garfield was hand selected to train Robert Kiyosaki’s Rich Dad Coaching Staff on identifying top cash flow markets, market entrance and exit timing, power team development, and negotiation strategy. Jared has successfully coached over 500 clients, as they built real estate portfolios.

A frequently requested speaker, Jared has addressed investment groups across the country, and done consulting for clients around the world. He is an expert in fields of building and establishing credit, and creating wealth through partnerships.

Excellent skills in acquisition, renovation, management and property re-sale allowed Mr. Garfield to develop a significant portfolio of investment properties through creative and traditional means. These methods included purchase of pre-foreclosures, short sales, foreclosures, land trusts, lease purchases, assignments and purchase through wholesalers.

Jared's excellent understanding of the utilization of bypass trusts, LLC's, estate and financial planning strategy, have allowed him to help clients protect the wealth they have built.

In 2008 Jared founded Dartnell Investment Academy, a team of real estate investment mentors that have helped investors purchase 20-100% ROI properties with significant equity in top cash flow markets. In each of these hot spots the Academy has developed an extensive variety of real estate experts.

Prior to Dartnell Investment Academy LLC, Jared developed and grew a small real estate brokerage to four offices, recruiting and training over 42 agents at two different firms.

Mr. Garfield is licensed in real estate, and has studied the Accredited Buyers Representative and GRI real estate Courses. To further his value as a consultant he has received life, variable products, health, accident and sickness, and property and casualty insurance licenses.
