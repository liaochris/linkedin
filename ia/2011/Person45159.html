Vincent Vassallo’s Summary

A catalyst for growth. Over the past 30+ years, he has demonstrated a unique ability to bridge strategic vision and tactical implementation to ignite breakthrough results (for well over one hundred businesses, in a broad range of competitive environments). His focus is on enabling environmentally and socially responsible businesses to prosper.

Vincent has started and grown three different successful companies - Each in unrelated industries.

Starting from nothing, he grew each of these three to be a leader within its niche - Each with over a $ million in profitability - Each within two years - All with successful cash-outs for everyone involved. Further, he has been called in to lead the turn-around of three different failed companies - Each one succeeding beyond all projections. Vincent has been lucky enough to have never run an unsuccessful company.

As a consultant, he has coached over a hundred clients to growth and success; and worked with an array of venture capital firms (including countless projects with one of the world's largest) to assist their portfolio companies.

Currently, he is (among other things) a senior advisor to the State of Connecticut's Clean Energy Fund, and helping to lead the start-up of a solar energy company.
