Chris Soames's Summary

Over 6 years experience covering the full digital marketing spectrum. A bulk of this time spent at Thomson / First Choice owned i-to-i Travel & TEFL Course provider. During my time at i-to-i I was involved in all digital marketing channels but with a heavy bias towards traffic acquisition. The result of my time at i-to-i saw traffic more than doubling while commercially i-to-i grew at amazing rates through one of the toughest recessions on record.

Within TUI (Thomson / First Choice) i-to-i were consistently seen as the leaders in digital marketing and we were often called on to offer advice to other members of the travel giant. Working with experts such as Dave Chaffey (co-owner of Smart Insights) and Dan Bosomworth (co-owner of Smart Insights) i-to-i led the way in digital marketing and will continue to do so for years to come.

Other experience includes working &/or advising companies such as Genesis sports, The Purveyor, Puma, Cherry Gorgeous, Specialised Covers & more recently starting a new kind of digital agency (First 10) with business partners Danyl Bosomworth & Stuart Miller
