John Schmidt’s Summary

Clear Choice LLC reflects my desire to help people find greater fulfillment in their work, so we provide tools and assistance to companies that have issues with turnover, an employee's or team's performance, the lasting implementation of process changes, absenteeism or getting everyone rowing in the same direction.

My background includes turn-arounds, distribution center re-organizations, high-risk project / program recovery and management, teaching and process improvement implementation. Prior to a focus on operational concerns, I spent time as a sales person and sales manager. I opened retail sales locations, hired and trained sales people, and had responsibility for my own book of business.
