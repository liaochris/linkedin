Author

The Graceful Divorce

Writing and Editing industry

June 2010 – Present (10 months)

This blog is my attempt to sort out my feelings publicly so as to save a few people from making similar mistakes. I hope you find this blog nurturing for your own life journey. I truly believe that more divorces can be entered into lovingly and without the mud-slinging. I think that we as a society would benefit from more conscious divorce and separations. It is so important–especially if there are children involved (I have a pre-schooler).

That is what has inspired me to take you on a journey through my emotions and behaviors. It is my attempt to spread grace to anyone affected by separation or divorce: step-parents, adult children of divorce, separating couples–really anyone looking for healing from a separation.

Separations and divorces are some of the most painful events in life, and historically they have been shrouded in shame. My goal is to have a conversation that alleviates as much guilt, fear, and disappointment as possible.

I hope you will join me!
