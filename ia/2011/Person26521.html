Lisa McGee's Summary

Lisa has spent almost two decades working in the publishing industry. She graduated from The College of Wooster in Wooster, OHIO (USA) in 1991 with a Bachelor of Arts in Art History. From there she went to New York where she spent the better part of 15 years working for The Hearst Corporation, Hachette Filipacchi Media and Meredith both as an on-staff editor and as a freelance writer and stylist. Her work has focused primarily on home and lifestyle magazines.

In 2004 she published her first book, At Home in Nantucket (Chronicle Books) which depicts 16 houses on Nantucket Island divided up seasonally and mixed in with a diverse range of recipes.

In July 2007, she re-located to Nenagh, County Tipperary, Ireland where she continues to write and produce stories for newspapers and magazines throughout the country.

In Ireland, her editorial work has been featured in Image Interiors, Improve Don't Move, Munster Interiors and ur dream home magazines, The Limerick Leader newspaper and The Guardian (in Nenagh).

In December 2009 she has branched out in a different direction and now will be handling direct sales for SPIN South West radio station, based in Limerick. Lisa covers North Tipperary, Galway, So. Laois and parts of Limerick. This young, hip musically driven station is a leader in the SouthWest region and it can be heard in Kerry, Limerick, Clare, Laois and Tipperary.
