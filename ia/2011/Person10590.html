Dave Weinberg's Summary

Creative, Design and Marketing Services - Across Media. Dave Weinberg helps businesses develop purpose-driven integrated communications for online and print-based media.

Dave is an award winning Creative Services Professional and visual communications problem solver. Consultant, Freelancer, Business Owner, In-House Team Leader, ‘outsourcerer’ extraordinaire, his cross-media industry expertise spans more than 20 years.

For 10 years, Dave served as Senior Art Director with L.L.Bean, Inc. in Freeport, Maine, where he was instrumental in advancing the design and usability of llbean.com along with fostering alignment of visual communications across web, catalog, retail and advertising channels.

He is a founding board member and current President of the Maine chapter of AIGA, the professional association for design. He currently serves as adjunct faculty at University of Southern Maine where he teaches Graphic Communications and Cross-Media Publishing.

Dave is the creator of cellphoneSketchpad™ - mobile journal. cyber comic. digital rorschach - an evolving creativity project that includes a daily blog and further fostering community engagement through social media. cellphoneSketchpad products are also available, such as note cards and high-quality fine art archival prints with more developments in the works.

An accomplished Illustrator, he holds a BFA from the School of Visual Arts in NYC.

Dave continues to explore a broad range of creative forms, both visual and performance-based. Over the years, designer, writer, art/creative director, stand-up comic, improvisational actor, cartoonist, banana-wild blueberry pancake maker.

Dave is a proud and adoring Dad who also enjoys a really good nap!

Specialties

Cross-Media Creative Services - Creative Direction, Graphic Design, Branding and Identity Across Media Channels, User Interface and Interaction Design, cellphoneSketchpad
