Peter D. Shelp's Summary

I am a firm believer in the value of education to aid in keeping current with the trends of our global economy and rapidly changing industry. After obtaining a B.S.degree from Pennsylvania State University, I attained a M.S.degree in Business and Managerial Science from Marywood University as well as several strategic advanced financial planning designations.

The value of education goes beyond keeping yourself educated. Teaching involves continued learning. I have been an instructor at Pennsylvania State University, Marywood University, and Misericordia University.

Thorough, comprehensive financial advice is what all present and future clients can expect. I welcome the opportunity to work with you as your investment partner.
