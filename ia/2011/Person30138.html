Michael Koerber’s Summary

Michael Koerber is a senior-level financial and operations professional with 22 years of achievement within the retail and healthcare service industries.

Koerber is currently a Principal in the New England practice of Tatum, LLC.

During his career, Koerber has coordinated the acquisition of a 1350-unit franchisor as CFO of Fantastic Sams International, led financial system implementations as Vice President of Finance of Edible Arrangements International, and created a metric benchmark system for 25 hospitals which led to 5% profit margin improvement as a Corporate Financial Analyst. He also served as Hospital Controller for Tenet Healthcare (NYSE: THC).

As a Tatum Principal, Koerber has served as a consultant for a $1.1B PEG owned multi brand retail comapny where he developed dashboard and flash report metric models to improve operation assessments. Koerber also served as a consultant for a commercial bank where he developed a 52-week cash flow projection and restated three years of financials for a distressed bank client. Analysis led to a restructuring plan to avoid bank work out. Assisting Tatum's healthcare practice, Koerber was a consultant for a hospital, where he analyzed systems and procedures for a revenue cycle transformation project, with an emphasis on implementation of a denial mitigation program.
