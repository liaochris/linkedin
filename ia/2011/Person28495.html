Paul R. Marobella, Jr.'s Summary

[wire] stone is an AdAge Top 50 Digital Marketing Agency and as the Managing Director of [wire] stone, Chicago, my focus is on igniting growth for our iconic brand partners, growing our influence in Chicago and ensuring our people love coming through our doors to work every day. In addition to the Managing Director role, I serve as the National Growth Director leading out on Wirestone's new business, marketing and growth efforts from coast-to-coast.

[wire] stone is a Digital Marketing Agency. Our strategies are the antidote for a new breed of fluid marketplace where the power is in the buyer's hands.

Our clients include, HP, Apple, Microsoft, Motorola, FTD, Nike, Brand Jordan, A.C. Moore, Intel, Chicago Convention & Tourism Bureau, Think Beyond The Label and Boeing.

Prior to joining [wire] stone, I was the Executive Vice President of Strategic Planning, Client Services and Business Development for the 575-person agency, Ambrosi - Responsible for overseeing Ambrosi’s clients in Chicago as well as directing the agency’s Strategic Planning practice, which included research, consumer insights and trend forecasting. The client base included, The Home Depot, The Home Depot Expo, Sears, Office Depot, Godiva, Fannie May, Wahl Clipper, Sunbeam, Mr. Coffee, Linens N Things, The Great Indoors and OfficeMax.

Before Ambrosi, I was the EVP, Brand Strategy and the Managing Director of Greco Ethridge Group's Boston office- opening the office from the ground-up for the NYC-based agency and was responsible for all leadership/recruiting, strategic guidance and business development. We grew the agency from $10MM to $100MM in billings in just three years from clients such as IBM, 3Com, Siemens, Raindance, InformationWeek and CMP Media.

I was fortunate enough to be a two-time winner of Ad Age’s Best and Brightest Strategist Award in 1998 and 2001 And, our team won Agency of The Year from Ad Age's BtoB Magazine while at Greco Ethridge Group.

Specialties

My passion is in finding new ways for brands to build relationships with people through the use of technology and digital platforms.
