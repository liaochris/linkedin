Matthew Scholtka’s Summary

Highly experienced web designer with over 12 years of user interface and Internet design experience and over 5 years experience managing design and usability projects.

Expert in developing hand-coded Hypertext Markup Language (HTML), Cascading Style Sheets (CSS), and Javascript to create fixed, fluid, or elastic site layouts and interfaces and support W3C accessibility standards and cross-browser compatibility without compromising the original vision.

Expert in designing graphics, layouts, and interfaces using the Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Flash, Dreamweaver, etc.).

Expert in providing consistent, quality branding and designs for Fortune 50 brands across a variety of mediums, such as web, television, print, and internal applications.

Excellent communication and interpersonal skills with an ability to work in a team as well as independently, as well as ability to manage multiple tasks in a high pressure environment.
