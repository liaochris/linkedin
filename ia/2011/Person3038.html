Ankit Nagori's Summary

There I worked on a project with India's second largest lighting company. After a brief stint with Milagrow, I started a Media and Advertising company with a focus on Youth Marketing. YouthPad.com was one of its succesful products, which was eventually sold off to a UK based company. After some successful projects in the domain of Social Media Marketing and Out of Home Advertising, I joined India's number one e-Commerce company Flipkart.com. Here I am currently involved in Buying and Merchandising of Books, Movies, Music and Games.
