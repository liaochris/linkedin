David Tang's Summary

Over ten (10) years of experience and expertise in Information Technology Consulting Services, IT Recruitment and Talent Acquisition. Experienced at management, technical recruitment and business development with the ability to build rapport and develop long-term relationships. Extensive track-record of success fulfilling high-end and niche skills for Fortune 100 Clients to small and medium sized organizations. In-depth knowledge and experience with most Puget Sound based companies including, but not limited to: Microsoft, AT&T Wireless (Mobility), WaMu (Chase), T-Mobile, Starbucks, Expedia, InfoSpace, Motricity, Safeco/Liberty Mutual, BECU, Northrop Grumman, etc.

Co-Founder and Managing Partner of WideNet Consulting Group. Focused on quality and successful delivery to our Clients, Consultants and Employees. We fill the gap between large firms that are focused on quantity and “hitting the numbers” and professional services firms with professional services rates. We have the ability to deliver top-caliber, highly-skilled technical professionals at competitive rates. We invest in building strong relationships which results in success and a long-term rewarding partnership with our Clients and Consultants alike.

WideNet Consulting helps organizations attract and retain talented professionals for contract, contract-hire, and direct hire needs within IT, Applications, Infrastructure and IT Leadership roles.

For inquiries, contact me at david@widenet-consulting.com

Specialties

management, technical recruiting, strategic sourcing, business development, cold-calling, relationship development, negotiations, lead generation, market and trend analysis, continuous process improvement.
