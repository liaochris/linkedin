Zania Guy's Summary

A passionate digital expert who has specialized in online marketing since 2000 and has recently returned to New Zealand after working in London for 10 years.

Zania has worked both client and agency side delivering communications solutions for some of the world’s most renowned brands including: Kimberly-Clark; Nokia; Metro International, McDonald’s; Sky; Phaidon Press; Glaxo Wellcome; Yell and The New Zealand Herald.

She has many years experience in developing and monetizing web sites, both content and ecommerce based including a focus on SEO, PPC and Affiliate Marketing and has most recently specializing in Social Media.

Zania is an engaging people manager and is incredibly efficient and organized. She aims to deliver solutions that focus on delivering tangible results.

Specialties

Global online marketing/advertising specialist; social media expert; website development - e-commerce, corporate and promotional websites; excellent communicator; commercially minded; SEM (SEO+PPC); CRM; e-mail marketing;affiliate marketing;UGC; brand management; project management; creative commissioning; media planning and buying; agency and clientside experience.
