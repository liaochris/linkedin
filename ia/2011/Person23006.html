Julie Maahs's Summary

Julie Maahs of Tampa, Florida is a single mom of two beautiful girls, Lindsay who is 15 and Courtney who is 10.

Julie has been a struggling Real Estate agent due to the challenges in our economy. The truth is, she could no longer make ends meet. “Too much month left at the end of the money.”

She was in a mess and had to do something. She had heard about this opportunity, so she had to find out the truth about the “The Trump Network Launch”. When she realized that “The Trump Network is about to change the face of Network Marketing.” she got very excited and joined. Her situation started to improve quickly.

She has already achieved the level of Regional Trainer and has become a strong and respected leader.

She has also been a major contributing factor in the development of this website and continues to set a great example for others to follow.

“My girls are the most important things in the world to me, there is nothing I would not do for them. But we were in a mess. That mess is going away thanks to this opportunity. This is why I now know “The Trump Network will be a huge success story in America and those who recognize it early will thrive.” My girls and I are living proof.” -Julie Maahs
