Mark Hindmarsh's Experience

Joint Managing Director

JiWire Europe Ltd

Privately Held; Online Media industry

May 2010 – Present (11 months)

Responsible for co-founding and operating the European Head Office of this highly successful US based company which specializes in the delivery of innovative advertising solutions to “On-the-Go” mobile audiences.

Reaching a rapidly increasing number of Wi-Fi users when they access public Wi-Fi hotspots, JiWire has built the World’s largest dedicated Wi-Fi Ad network. The company has exclusive relationships in place with 30+ global Wireless Broadband network operators enabling them to offer advertisers highly targeted “point of connection” front-page inventory when consumers access public Wi-Fi. In any given month, the company serves more than 60 million Ad impressions across more than 40,000 high-traffic, long dwell-time hotspot venue locations including; hotels, airports, cafes and wireless city networks. More recently JiWire have started to address App users on hand held devices. To date over 100 Global brand advertisers have successfully run campaigns through JiWire enjoying higher than industry average user engagement and performance.
