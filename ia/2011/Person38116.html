Russell Yardley's Summary

Joined IBM in 1978 left in 1985 to start one of Australia's first multimedia businesses and in the mid 90's became one of the first Internet developers in the country.

Strong skills in attracting and managing highly creative design teams with a real drive for innovation. In 1995 I participated in the Williamson Community Leadership Programme and have enjoyed many challenging opportunities as a Williamson Fellow.

Having spent 23 years running Acumentum and other small business interests now taking on non-executive director roles and helping the new entrepreneurial companies grow and prosper!

As chair of the AIIA in Victoria am working to accelerate entrepreneurial experience to help make Australia a Centre for Entrepreneurship.

Specialties

Small business management, innovation, entrepreneurship, team building, business planning. Government relations, IT industry networking.
