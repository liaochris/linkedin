John Barnes's Summary

Provide services for Solar PV utility scale developers of 3-20MWp farms in CA and US SW regions. Interconnection, site permitting, development services that produce "shovel ready" projects.

Development of solar system implementation plans, supplier partnership relationships, manufacturing partnerships, and energy sales plans. Management of all aspects of solar farm projects for investor/owners with the required IRR.

Co-chairman for interconnection committee of Solartech.org. Active member of FIT coalition working on legislation in California and the US. Ambassador for solar initiatives with invVEST.org, promoting government programs for massive solar investments.

Skilled in leadership of hardware/software/systems engineering, marketing, and operations teams. Proven VP level executive that has developed 40+ multi-$M volume selling complete projects (software, semiconductors and systems) for large and small companies.

Specialties

Development and Management of MW solar PV farms in California and US SW.

Detailed knowledge of CA solar incentives, solar farm siting and PUC regulations, and status of module and installation costing for MW size solar farm implementations.

Competent in solar farm siting from evaluation to permitting of the sites for optimal returns to investors. Experienced in interconnection application and approval process with Utilities for maximum solar farm size and minimal connection costs.

John Barnes's Experience

President, CEO

Solar Land Partners

Renewables & Environment industry

September 2010 – Present (6 months)

Solar Land Partners (SLP) is a professional services company made up of energy utility, interconnection, real estater permitting, solar, finance and legal professionals formed to get land sites "shovel ready" for solar EPC construction. SLP works in partnership with land owners, project developers, investor owned utilities, municipal owned utilities, agencies, cities, counties and others in order to create a bankable solar site.

Solar Land Partners has meaningful experience in all aspects of solar project development including; site control, acquisition, lease, option, management, right-of-way, environmental compliance and governmental regulatory compliance services to the renewable energy industry.

We are a trusted supplier of Certified Solar Ready sites to the leading players in the solar industry including; Solar Developers, Solar System Integrators, EPC Contractors and Project Investors
