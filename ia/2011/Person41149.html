Associate

Smart&co Ventures

De financiación privada; Sector de Ocio, viajes y turismo

2010 – Presente (1 año)

Smart&co Ventures is the independent venture capital fund of Smart&co located in Paris.

It invests from €0.2m to €25m in high-potential companies and future global leaders in a large range of stages and models in the leisure and entertainment market worldwide : travel, hotel, other hosting, restaurant, wellness, sport, culture and education...

It is particularly focused on seed and VC investments but also in house « from scratch » fundings and specific growth, LBO and distressed equity deals.

The vision is to become the first VC firm in leisure, entertainment and travel.

Smart&co Ventures' current portfolio includes:
- La Fourchette : #1 online booking platform and management software provider for restaurants in France and Spain. Rival of OpenTable.
- Weekendesk : #1 online thematic and packaged short breaks provider in Western Europe.
- Buyagift : #1 online sale of experiences vouchers in the UK, France and soon Spain and Italy.
- Gault Millau : #2 print and online guides for restaurants, hotels and wines in Europe.
- Camping&co : #1 online booking platform of camping slots.
- Lookingo : #3 highly-discounted coupons provider for local partners in France (Lookingo) and Australia (Ouffer) behind Groupon and LivingSocial.
- Balinea : #1 online booking platform and discounted vouchers provider for beauty/wellness institutes.

I have joined Smart&co Ventures:
- To make a day-to-day management of two portfolio companies: lead monthly reporting, participate in the board of directors, daily interact with CEOs and management, and support top Executives in strategic issues.
- To participate in all stages of the structuring and development of the venture capital fund.
- To boost the deal flow : identify and contact companies in need of equity financing or in disposal process, communicate with investment banks, fundraisers and private equity firms…
- To execute due diligences and lead investment executions.

- Supported Partners in the day-to-day management of companies in portfolio such as Twenga, Taptu, Open ERP, BlueKiwi and Upek.
