Albion Water Structures Limited

Privately Held; Civil Engineering industry

February 2011 – Present (2 months)

A temporary, portable, cofferdam system suitable for water depths from 10cm to 1.8m in lengths from 5m to 300m/1000ft.

Outright purchase for owner installation or use our installation and hire service.

AquaDams work well in flowing water. Dams deployed parallel to a bank, straight line deployments with river flows diverted or comprehensively over pumped are often feasible for self installation. However, flowing water does represent a more challenging installation and is often best carried out by our experienced installers.

Our Budget dams are designed as a substitute for 1 tonne bag dams. Suitable for water depths up to 75cm/30inch. Sold in standard lengths. The latest addition to the Budget Range is the Budget-Mini for water depths to 45cm/18inches. This dam is highly portable and comes in a 15m/50ft
standard length.

Our Standard range is suitable for water depths to 95cm, 1.1m, 1.4m and 1.8m and are sold to the length required.

The Flood Protection range are smaller dams designed to be deployed in long runs by 2 man deployment teams.

We're always happy to discuss your options.
