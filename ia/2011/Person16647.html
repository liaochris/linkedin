Greg Salerno’s Summary

Award-winning marketing communications professional with Fortune 500 corporate branding expertise, as well as B2B ad agency and media experience as creative director, copywriter, and publication editor.

Affinity for science- and technology-driven subjects. Subject matter expert for oil & gas exploration, drilling, completion, production and transportation. Broad background in marketing to global audiences. Experience in chemicals, plastics, aerospace, nuclear power and engineering services.

Goals include challenging work that grows the 'top line' and increases bottom-line returns -- while giving more than taking from friends, colleagues and associates.

Greg Salerno’s Specialties:

Marketing strategy, creative concepts, writing/content generation for all media, project management, public & investor relations, employee communications and channel analysis. Proven successful team-building and project-management skills.
