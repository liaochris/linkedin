Justin Kozuch's Summary

Justin Kozuch is the lead researcher for the Pixel to Product research study and the founder of Refresh Events. He works behind the scenes bringing together the most engaging and influential minds in Toronto’s vibrant digital media industry. In a previous lifetime, he spent his 9-5 as an Interactive Developer at Sapient, and worked with large brands such as RIM, the Wall Street Journal and Virgin Charter.
