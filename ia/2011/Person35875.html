Raj Mahajan's Summary

Inventing products, scaling companies, and managing large organizations at the intersection of financial services and technology best describes my professional experience. The highlights of this pursuit include co-founding Kiodex, the first company to commercialize a web-based commodity-trading platform; building the Goldman Sachs’ electricity trading business; leading the turn-around of a major division within SunGard Data Systems, the Fortune 500 software company that acquired Kiodex; and now, transforming SunGard's trading division from a silo-based organization into an integrated industry leader through a program of divestiture, acquisition, and innovation.
