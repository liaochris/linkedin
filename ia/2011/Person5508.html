Remy Arteaga’s Summary

I am currently serving as CEO of a computer vision software firm that develops best-in-class mosaic & registration solutions for critical applications. As a seasoned entrepreneur with 16 years experience, I have developed deep expertise in forming, managing, and marketing new ventures.

One of my first ventures was an Internet sports magazine, which IGN.com later acquired. I went on to launch and promote several types of new ventures including “Software as a Service”, Medical Device, and Computer Software.

During the launch of a new venture, I am generally responsible for fund raising and team building. After a launch, I am responsible for all aspects of the venture including strategy, branding, and business development.
