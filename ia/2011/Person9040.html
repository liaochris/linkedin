Colin A. Kinsella's Summary

As President of Digitas North America, Colin drives growth, innovation and collaboration across the agency’s largest region. He joins Digitas with an extensive background of more than 20 years of experience in traditional and digital advertising.

Most recently, Colin served as Global Chief Innovation Officer for Razorfish, a role he was appointed to in May 2009. Previously, he was President of the agency’s West region where he led all of the agency’s discipline areas, including analytics, strategy, delivery, media and technology to drive the development of customer experiences for clients such as Visa, Singapore Airlines, Genentech, Sony, Williams Sonoma and Westfield. Prior to this role, Colin served as general manager of both the agency’s California offices, a position he’d held since joining Razorfish in 2004.

Prior to joining Razorfish, Colin led the creative, strategy and business-to-business efforts at QuinStreet, a start up specializing in online marketing and technology. QuinStreet, now the leader in the customer creation segment of lead generation, has grown from $35MM in revenue in the year Colin joined to $300MM today.

Prior to this Colin spent 14 years at Foote Cone and Belding serving leadership roles in San Francisco, London and Chicago. In San Francisco he served as EVP Partner and member of the 6 person leadership team. In London (96-98) he led FCB’s efforts to establish a network throughout Europe, Africa and the Middle East. In Chicago he served as SVP Group Director leading the SC Johnson business. He has broad experience working with a wide range of brands such as AT&T, Coors, SC Johnson, and Gallo wines.

He holds a MBA from University of Chicago and a B.A. and B.S. from University of Dayton.
