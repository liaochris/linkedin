Ed Szymczak’s Summary

Ed Szymczak is a moving media professional with a strong background in music, video, and interactive technologies. By day, he supports the image operations of media giant, Scripps Networks Interactive. By night, Ed is the father of two beautiful girls and husband to his gorgeous wife--- and as his family sleeps, Szymczak works as Composer Support for Merritt Music Productions!
The latter brings Ed Szymczak here to LinkedIn... to meet new people and share in the latest interactive experiences that captivate the media-sharing masses. Szymczak supports Merritt Music composers as agent, administrator, and supervising sound designer.
