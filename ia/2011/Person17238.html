Heath Thorpe's Summary

I specialise in the development and implementation of systems to manage high Environmental, Health and Safety (EHS) risks associated with activities / aspects in mining and heavy industry - High Risk Control Protocols (HRCP), Major Hazard Standards (MHS), Fatality Prevention Elements (FPE) and Fatal Risk Control Protocols (FRCP). I have practical experience in the development, implementation and auditing of these systems on numerous sites in different countries and for varying clients.
I have extensive proven experience in the development, implementation and management of EHS auditing strategies, systems & protocols and in the management of auditing units / departments / companies. A sound audit strategy is a fundamental aspect of an effective EHS management system.

I am the Owner of an EHS Advisory Company – Oraeps Pty Ltd, and prior to that I operated in various senior management positions in the EHS field. I have extensive experience in the Asia, Asia Pacific and Southern Africa regions. I have over 17 years experience in my field.

Specialties

Development, implementation and management of effective EHS strategies and systems to achieve ZERO harm.
EHS High Risk Controls Protocols (HRCP, MHS, FPE, FRCP)
Development of EHS Auditing strategies, systems, protocols and auditing units
EHS Auditing (over 700 conducted); RABQSA Business Improvement Auditor - OH&S (OHSAS 18001) and environment (ISO 14001)
Management of multi-disciplinary EHS teams
Marine and aquatic ecology
Environmental Assessment of Submarine Outfalls
