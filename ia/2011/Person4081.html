Atman Kadakia’s Summary

Atman Kadakia currently works full-time for an entrepreneurial venture targeting college students. He also attends Georgetown University and is pursuing a Master of Professional Studies in Real Estate Finance. He is expected to graduate in May 2011.

Atman Kadakia graduated from the University of Southern California with a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration from the Marshall School of Business, a Minor in Accounting from the Leventhal School of Accounting, and a Master in Construction Management from the Viterbi School of Engineering.

His prior experience includes operations finance for Hilton Worldwide, operations finance and business analysis for Greens Group, construction and project management for Perini Corporation, real estate financial analysis for Creative Environments of Hollywood, and multi-family brokerage for Sperry Van Ness.

Atman Kadakia was born in Southern California and has lived in Irvine for most of his life. He is a graduate of Irvine High School where he played on the tennis and basketball team. He also played chess competitively and won more than twenty-five trophies. His other hobbies include skiing, water-sports, and hiking. In addition to excelling in the classroom, he started two successful online businesses, Bat Notes and Screw The Middle Man, and has led several campus and cultural organizations including Southern California Indo Americans and Associated General Contractors.
