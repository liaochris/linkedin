Jerome Pineau's Summary

"If you find yourself in a comfort zone, you're probably parked illegally".

Welcome to my LinkedIn profile. I am a community and social media strategist. In my spare time (yeah right) I also run and produce www.whattimeradio.com - the first live international web radio show about watches and the people who love them!

I was born in Paris, grew up in NYC, and lived in NJ then CA. I have twenty years of extensive experience as a software developer, entrepreneur, sales engineer, and technical evangelist in the United States.

Several years ago, I discovered blogging, community management, social media, and digital marketing. I fell in love and never looked back. Then a year ago, I changed industries and moved to Switzerland into the luxury watch universe as the industry's first full-fledged community manager.

Alain Silberstein once famously said "True happiness is having one's passion for a profession". I'm not sure there's another way to live.

Specialties

Brand evangelism, community building, digital marketing, web radio, journalism, writing, and social media strategy.
