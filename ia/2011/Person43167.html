Thomas Knoll’s Summary

I help businesses discover and love their customers.

Do you know the number one response I hear from entrepreneurs and engineers when I tell them what I do?
"But, talking to customers is so hard."

While I can sympathize with that reaction, I've never quite understood it. For me, talking to and understanding customers is a joy. I love to get in their head, figure out what makes them tick, understand their perspective, and help them feel heard. And, while I have a technical background, I love people more than technology, and truly believe technology is just a tool to help people make the world a better place.

So now--with my experience and passion for communication, psychology, social media, metrics, user experience, community cultivation, and evangelism--I find myself in a position to help businesses discover and love their customers.
