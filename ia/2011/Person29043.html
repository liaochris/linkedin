Matthew Solan's Summary

Accomplished professional freelance writer, journalist, and editor with 20-plus years experience. A skilled researcher and interviewer who specializes in locating the most qualified sources and uncovering the latest studies and research. Investigative reporter with the ability to translate technical and scientific data for specific audiences. Independent worker experienced at completing both short turnaround and large-scale assignments within designated deadlines. Project manager with a reputation for developing concepts and packaging multi-layered stories. Writer for consumer magazines, trade publications, books, newsletters, and newspapers.
