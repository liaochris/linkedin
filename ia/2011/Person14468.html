Fabian Danielsen’s Summary

A Visual Information & User Experience Designer, passionate about making the Internet a more logical place. A perfectionist and a pixel junkie trying to improve the quality of displayed information and experiences.

Particularly interest in existing and emerging syndicates between online interfaces and physical products and services.

During a stint of expansion beyond mouses and physical keyboards, internet is being redefined as a combined place to physical environments; the only certain will continue to be - constant rapid change. Therefore...

...determine, define and adapt how to people behave, and design according to it. Constant renewable design principles are logical consequence's.

"Users are creatures of habit"
Doug Bowman. 

Interests:

- Pixels
- User Interface Design
 - User Experience
- Information Architecture
- CrossPlatform Branding & Communication 
- Design Methodology 

HCI & NUI. In other words:

Internet, Touchscreens, Tablets, Smartphones, Open Source, Transparency, Adaptive Web, Internet of Things, Changing behavior etc.

Fabian Danielsen’s Specialties:

Huge passion for details. A sense for clean and logical visual information design and elaborated, comprehensive cross-platform branding, insighted by integrations of both traditional and new design patterns - inspired by environment, not by a genre.
