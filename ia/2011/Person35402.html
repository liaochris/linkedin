Prissi Cohen’s Summary

Trained as an RN, I have been advocating for patients for over 20 years, helping them make the best medical decisions for themselves and their families. What do you do when you are first diagnosed with an illness? How do you decide which treatment or specialist to choose? Do you need someone with you or a loved one at your next appointment? Are all of your medical records in order? Perhaps you are in the hospital and need help with an impending discharge. If you need help navigating the medical system, if you feel overwhelmed and wish you had someone helping you get the answers you want and deserve, call ProPatient Advocacy.
