Scott Clark’s Summary

Don't let questionable HR practices undermine your business. Whether your business has one or one hundred employees, Ascend HR Solutions' comprehensive and proactive HR support can help you effectively administer your work force. Let us keep up with government regulations and reporting burdens while you concentrate on growing your business. You still manage and fully control the what and how of your employees' jobs. We just take care of the red-tape.

With my extensive banking and B to B sales background, as well as having owned my own business, I can relate to the obstacles faced by today's business owners. Ascend HR Solutions is uniquely qualified to help businesses over many of these hurdles including expert and efficient administration of payroll and all related tax filings, Worker's Compensation policies and claims, unemployment claims, employee handbooks, and benefits packages. Thus putting the business owner back in a position to build the business rather than focus on internal expense categories.
