Interests:

We think you will find our seminars to be a breath of fresh air. Our "active duty" K-12 teacher trainers live "in the trenches" on a daily basis and pride themselves in being much more than just college faculty with lofty ideals and fancy theory. We have been nick-named the "Tools for Tomorrow not just Theory For Thought" company as our seminar participants receive highly practical training which can be immediately implemented when they return to their own schools. Our programs are proven in practice, and are presented by outstanding experts in the subject matter. Many thousands of educators flock to our teacher workshops and educational administration courses every year throughout the United States and Canada because they learn things based in research, but which are practical and easy to implement. We are redefining teaching and learning in schools and providing highly useful professional teacher staff development.
