Debra Mann's Summary

I am a passionate Sales & Marketing Manager and have always achieved even in the hardest of markets, my background spans from Local Press, Radio and Events where I was the first person to bring Co-op advertising into UK Radio to working in the Conference & Hospitality industry.

My passion has always been Business to Business sales and marketing and I have a way of finding opportunities to win business and my enthusiasm in achieving results has given me an opportunity to work for some of the best companies in the UK.

Variety Events organise the Keeping Business Local, The events are for anyone wishing to network, meet new customers, build business contacts, find new suppliers or just wanting to do more business locally in the Thames Valley and we have seminar speakers throughout the day, check out our website or follow us.

We are a growing company and welcome enquiries from professionals who feel they could add value to our business.
