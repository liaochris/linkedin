Christiane Philipps's Summary

Agile Enthusiast and Interim IT Manager.

More than 10 years of professional experience in web projects:
Project management, managing teams up to 20 people, budget responsibility, software development, IT architecture, software testing, scalability.

Experienced in working for eCommerce companies (B2B / B2C) and big social network services (SNS).

Currently, I am Interim CTO at Rebate Networks, with 30 countries a global player in daily deal business.

Before that, I was Interim Manager at Piku Media KK, one of Rebate Networks' daily deal platforms, located in Tokyo, Japan.

From January to August 2010, I worked as Chief Technology Officer (CTO) at DailyDeal, Rebate Networks' daily deal platform for Germany, Austria and Switzerland, located in Berlin, Germany. My mission was building a team of highly skilled developers and sysOps, shifting up code quality and performance, building a professional IT infrastructure and just making things work - "Ship it!". In order to achieve this, I implemented Scrum and it's a pleasure to see how team and results have improved more and more.

In 2008/2009 I was Head of Quality Assurance at one of Europe's biggest and fastest growing social network services (more than 15 million users).
Together with my colleagues, I was in charge of the engineering department (up to 50 people) in daily business.

My objective is to manage a team in an internationally oriented web company.

I prefer international work environments in dynamic companies with really passionate people who are into the principles of Agile or willing to change to Agile.

I really enjoy working for and with people and achieving changes and improvements together with others, in order to create a better workplace for everyone.

My profession is “Firefighting” as Interim Manager in critical projects as well as building up sustainable teams for long-term success.

Specialties

General specialties: Interim Management, IT firefighting, startup consulting, managing teams, workflow optimization, improving communication, embracing change. Agile Testing / QA, Agile Leadership.

Technical specialties: Testing, QA, Unit Testing, Acceptance Testing, Selenium, PHPUnit, Zend Framework, MVC architecture, PHP, Magento, Unix/LAMP systems, NGinx, Varnish, memcached
