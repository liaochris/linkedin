Mike Grew's Summary

Hi, I'm an HSE professional working in drilling for Easternwell Minerals. I'm based at Marandoo, in Western Australia.

Previously I had developed new RII training packages such as RIIOHS204A Work Safely At Heights and RIIOHS202A Enter and Work in Confined Spaces. My developments are highly compliant, absolutely current and well researched. They are used by some of the biggest players in the industry.

I am a current Master of Occupational Health and Safety, at ECU in Perth, WA. I'm a graduate member of the Safety Institute of Australia.

Goals for the future involve advancement in the industry, contribution to industry media and research, and self-improvement through further study.

E-learning, training, compliance, development, safety, environment, standards, legislation, behavioural safety, research.
