Justin Clarke's Experience

Chairman

Private Property Listings (Pty) Ltd

Internet industry

November 2005 – Present (5 years 5 months)

CEO

Private Property

Internet industry

June 1999 – November 2005 (6 years 6 months)

Co- Founder of Private Property, taking the company from raw concept, internet startup to major regional player in the Internet space. The model pure FSBO (for saler by owner) where the Private advertiser paid for a listing on the internet. To service the large geography, franchises were established in various localities. During the period the company pushed the envelope with new tecnologies, particularly the use of mobile. The South African, and indeed the African Internet industries were immature suffering with limited broadband and expensive connectivity. To monetise the purely web offering the company integrated vertically into Homeloan and real estate services.

Managing Member

Powerforce Projects C.C.

Real Estate industry

January 1987 – September 2000 (13 years 9 months)

Founded with the mission to develop real estate for investment purposes. The business moved through all sectors from Commercial, Docnor Medical Centre in Durban North, to Residential complexes modeled on the GASH principle, Small Townhouses in small complexes near the work centres in good locations, and finally developing Industrial spaces in the Jacobs industrial area of Durban.

Managing Director

The Blockman (Pty) Ltd

Building Materials industry

February 1985 – October 1988 (3 years 9 months)

Founded Blockyard business in Swaziland, with a collegues and family. The business expanded fast taking advantage of changing political climate in South Africa. Swaziland grew at full capacity with shortages in primary building supplies that Blockman offered. Further companies were formed, including Power and Plant (Pty) Ltd. The business was closed when the rights to the mining of riversand was revoked by the King. It was decreed that the industry should be reserved for Swazi Citizens.
