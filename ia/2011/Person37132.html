Mark Gibson’s Summary

30 years in sales, sales management, business development, marketing, consulting and sales training in the technology business in Australia, EMEA and 12 years in Silicon Valley.

Established sales and marketing consultancy business in UK in 2004 to help companies achieve four major objectives;

1. Inbound Marketing Messaging clarity to connect with buyers and drive buyer-relevant sales conversations.

2. Implementing the HubSpot Inbound Marketing platform for improved lead generation and marketing ROI

3. Blended learning to improve sales performance of customer-facing teams through communication, language, psychology, and skills training

4. Sales process alignment to match risk profile and buying behavior based on market maturity and tools to control the sale and create pipeline transparency.

Mark Gibson’s Specialties:
