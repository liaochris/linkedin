The Mountain Bike Unit (MBU). The MBU was formed in 1988 as part of the Concerned Off-Road Bicyclists Association (CORBA). Now an independent organization, the MBU works closely with the National Parks Services (NPS), California State Parks (CSP) and the Mountain Recreation and Conservation Authority (MRCA). MBU members range in age from their 20s to 70s and come from varied backgrounds. The diversity and training of the team have helped the MBU contribute to a bike-friendly environment in the Santa Monica Mountains.
