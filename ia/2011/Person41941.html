Sue Koch, CPC’s Summary

Thanks for visiting my profile! After 15 years in the corporate world I took what I loved from my past and created Soaring Solutions, LLC to help others grow, thrive, and reach new levels of engagement and success in their businesses.

My experience from start-up to divestiture, technology from development to social marketing, business intelligence, customer service & leadership development coaching provides me a unique foundation to help others doing what I love most.

Soaring Solutions is a full lifecycle Social Media Consulting & Training organization with a coaching mindset. I take you from idea through strategy, training and implementation, all while having fun doing it!

My strategy work is a blend of consultative facilitation and coaching, to ensure you bring out your best self and greatest goals in creating your online voice, brand, audience and content, all while reducing overwhelm and ensuring time management.

I provide training on the depths of Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, YouTube, Blogging and all related integration tools to support your business. You’ll learn the tools social media managers keep in their back pockets to do this in an efficient manner, and keeping it a free or low cost solution.

Soaring Solutions provides packaged or custom workshops for sales & customer service teams on building pipeline, creating strategic partnerships, reputation management and more via social media. If the cold call is not yet dead at your organization, let’s talk.

My corporate background & client base are highly diverse. I adapt to your industry and environment to customize a program and strategy to meet your needs. Soaring Solution’s clients include industries such as; advertising, boutique clothing, coaches, diversity and inclusion, entertainment, fitness, mortgage and loan, real estate, photography, print media, graphic and web design and more.

The possibilities with social media are endless, and I am excited and energized to share it all with you!
