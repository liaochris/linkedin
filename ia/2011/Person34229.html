Paul Meyers's Summary

Proven expertise in high-level business strategy and development; Asian market entry and operations; designing, building and leading regional teams; managing sophisticated development projects and managing/growing global entertainment brands in Asia.

Extensive background in strategic planning and fund raising for small, new and high growth companies, as well as M&A and exit strategies. Broad compliance experience having worked with government regulatory agencies in such countries as Korea, Vietnam, Singapore and Malaysia. Possess a significant network of professional and personal contacts built over more than 25 years in the media industries.

Now focused on the mobile, social and payment facets of the Asian digital media space.

Available for venture advisory, due diligence, Board and other advisory positions - as well as mentoring for smart new companies in Southeast Asia.

Specialties

Mobile, internet, television, payments, games, music, content, social media, entertainment, fund raising, strategy, business development, Asian market entry, Asia, Taiwan, Hong Kong, Southeast Asia, Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Indonesia, Philippines and Vietnam.
