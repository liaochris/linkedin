Ryan Riley’s Summary

Ryan is web developer specializing in .NET and a former internal control specialist with broad industry experience, especially in the construction, information technology and oil & energy sectors. His professional interests include developing web platforms to make business processes more efficient and integrate with other systems seamlessly, building and motivating teams, and educating and mentoring others. Ryan has special interests in utilizing web technologies to make business processes more efficient and enable more seamless integration between existing systems and new business requirements.

Ryan leads the Community for F# User Group and presented on F# and REST at Houston Tech Fest 2009.
