Aimee Nicholls’s Summary

Passionate, inspired communications professional. My experience includes three years in a boutique lifestyle public relations agency in New Zealand as a fashion, beauty and lifestyle specialist, working with a variety of international and local clients.

I have expertise in various areas from product promotion and publicity, to writing and branding, sponsorship and event management. I have a solid grounding in social media and marketing communications, and have been exposed to the public relations/marketing worlds in two contrasting settings.

Prior to public relations, I briefly worked for NZ House & Garden magazine (Fairfax Magazines), pursuing my love of writing and editing.

Past clients include Freemans, Samy Salons, Look Good Feel Better, Ashley Fogel (fashion designer), Crocs, Style Pasifika NZ Fashion Awards, Fairfax Magazines, Taylor's Port, Peppers Retreats, Resorts & Hotels, Electrolux. I have worked on projects such as the NZ House & Garden Tours, the New Zealand Gardener of the Year and the Look Good Feel Better Charity Dream Ball.
