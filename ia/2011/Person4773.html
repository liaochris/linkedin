Ben Fletcher’s Summary

In 2009 I started Ben Fletcher Advisory (www.benfletcheradvisory.com) to work with start up companies to assist their growth through business development, general strategy and product design consultancy. This led me to take a more hands-on role with TalentPuzzle in 2010. I remain an advisor to www.peerius.com and www.schport.com.

Since 1996 I have been growing and managing Osney Media - a business conferences and publications company owned by myself and one other shareholder with activity across sectors including Finance, TMT (Technology, Media, Telecoms) and Human Resources. I remain a shareholder and director.

At Osney, I have taken a hands-on approach to all aspects of running the business - from finance and corporate governance to sales, recruitment, marketing and business development.

In 2007 I invested in Delegate Select, a company which provides online networking software and in 2008 helped fund Oxford Global, a pharmaceutical media business.

Ben Fletcher’s Specialties:

Business Development: sales, marketing, partnerships. General business management. Growing and managing growth in businesses
