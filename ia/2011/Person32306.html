Neena Newberry's Summary

Companies hire Newberry Executive Coaching & Consulting to work with their top talent, to get results they couldn't before. We work with organizations to build and strengthen their pipeline of leaders through executive coaching, development programs, workshops, and facilitation. Our clients are national, but we have a significant market presence in Dallas and Houston.

I help high performing senior leaders and managers focus on how to have a bigger business impact, and I specialize in working with women in two-career families.

For over seventeen years, I have been advising and coaching clients in midsize to Fortune 50 companies. My credentials combine expertise in HR and coaching with a strong business background, to deliver business results.

I worked at Deloitte for fourteen years, first as a management consultant and later as an HR executive. I had been a Director there for three years when I decided to launch my business. I was involved in Deloitte's Women's Initiative since its inception in 1993 and led the development and implementation of several talent development and retention programs across the firm.

I am a Certified Professional and Executive Coach, have an MBA from the University of Texas, a Professional in Human Resources Certification, an ACC certification through the International Coach Federation, and am a graduate of the College of Executive Coaching. I am certified to administer the MBTI®, CPI 260™, Leadership Practices Inventory, FIRO-B®,and Six Seconds Emotional Intelligence (SEI) assessment tools.
