Sally Dominguez's Summary

*Professional judging: currently on air for ABC TV New Inventors, annually for Wheels Car of the Year (2005-07), Australian International Design Awards, and various private design award programs.
*Freelance writing on automobile design, sustainable design in products and housing, the process of invention and trends and innovations in rainwater harvesting. Publications include Wheels Yearbook - cars, Monument magazine - special report on water architecture and innovation, book reviews, G Magazine - report on sustainable product design.
*Product Innovation: designed and produced the award-winning Nest highchair, first of the modern, tulip stand highchairs - now held in the Powerhouse Museum, showing with the V&A around Europe, and listed in Metropolitan Home's Top 100 2005. Designed O MOON outdoor light, now licensed to Design Within Reach.
Designed Rainwater HOG modular rainwater tank, the first modular, multidirectional, reusable rainwater tank, now retailing with Design Within Reach.
Architecture: ran sole practice for 12 years with B. Architecture from Sydney Uni.
Domestic architectural work featured in various publications including the Australian book DOMAIN: INSIDE AND OUT by Cerentha Harris. Focussed on a holistic practice which encompassed landscaping, building, interiors, kitchens and bathrooms and custom furnishings.

Goals: to invent more green solutions and useful consumer products without overconsuming resources.

Specialties

An understanding of production and marketting which allows me to see a product through from concept to the end user.
Materials and construction knowledge and an understanding of material life cycles
Knowledge of child safe design standards
