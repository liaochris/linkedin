A.J. Wand's Summary

Results-driven software technology professional with expertise in product management and analysis working for companies ranging from startups to mature organizations building solutions for a broad range of industries. Strengths include understanding the big picture for a complex product -- the problems it solves, needs it satisfies, and opportunities it creates for customers -- translating requirements into a strategy and vision, creating a roadmap, and executing on release plans to build the right solution. Especially passionate about owning the product and leading cross-functional teams to make all supporting organizations in the company (engineering, ops, sales, marketing, etc.) effective and successful.


Selected Recent Accomplishments
• Revenue growth support: Managed redesign of an online video advertising platform, integrated a new video ad management system, and redesigned behavioral targeting system to support 10X revenue increase. (Veoh)
• Concept to international deployment of large-scale revenue-generating application: Led advertiser application product definition and management effort for Project Panama, a complete rebuilding of the company's paid search platform, its largest and most ambitious project to date, and responsible for approximately 50% of its $7B+ annual revenues. (Yahoo!)
• Next-generation feature build-out to support business model expansion: Managed delivery of a number of major new features including an application redesign, while managing maintenance releases to support a shift in business model and scope. (RelayHealth)
• Pre-concept to production launch of Internet B2B exchange: Defined application functionality, business logic, and website content to go from whiteboard concept to launch in six short months. (MovePoint.com)
• Migration of host-based application to an open platform: Ported retail merchandise management, data warehouse, and decision-support system to Web and Windows clients and UNIX servers. (PeopleSoft)
