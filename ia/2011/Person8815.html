Claudio Chagas's Summary

I started my higher education in the United States and finished my BA degree in Publishing in Oxford, Great Britain. The advantage in sharing a city with world-famous University of Oxford offered a perfect environment for study and social life.

I work as a freelancer providing Portuguese translation services for DTP projects, websites, blogs, video subtitling and software, or any worthy work that excites me. I have edited and proofread translation for print and on-line distribution, including press releases, sales agreements, charity's marketing materials and business reports, with translations valid for information and research purposes.

I am passionate about IT applications to publishing and social media integration for web publishing. Clear communication, insight and understanding are the merits by which I measure my success.
