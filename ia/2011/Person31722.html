Murray A. Mann’s Summary

Diversity and Inclusion Change Agent / Multicultural Workplace Strategist / Career Coach / Author / Columnist / Trainer / Consultant / Speaker

- Forging Diversity Partnerships, Building Inclusive Workplaces
- Delivering Cutting-Edge Multicultural Career Management Services

Google "Murray A. Mann" w/ quote marks; Keywords- diversity, Latinos

**Recipient of the 2010 Brillante Award for Entrepreneurial Excellence - National Society of Hispanic MBAs (NSHMBA)**

Murray’s delivers more than 30 years of diversity thought leadership and human resources management stewardship in the private, public, and not-for-profit sectors.

He is a nationally recognized columnist and co-author of Barron’s The Complete Job Search Guide for Latinos. His articles on diversity and employment issues have been featured in 30+ publications and electronic media.

Murray recently presented on diversity, inclusion and multicultural career management at the Multicultural Forum on Workplace Diversity, National Association of Minority Media Executives, the IDEAS Conference: Bringing Diversity to Madison Avenue, National Association of Colleges and Employers, International Coach Federation, Career Management Alliance, National Council of La Raza National Hispanic Business Association, the National Society of Hispanic MBAs, Women of Color in Higher Education Roundtable at Cornell University, International Career Development Conference, and several universities.

In addition, Murray is a founding board member of the Hispanic Alliance for Career Enhancement (HACE), career expert for NSHMBA, held leadership positions in multicultural professional organizations and career coach for ALPFA, HACE, LULAC, NAACP, NHBA, New York Times Salute Our Heroes, Women for Hire, etc.

He served with the IL Governor's Human Resources Advisory Council, the Joint Committee on Rules on the Illinois Human Rights Act, President’s Committee on the Employment of People with Disabilities.
