Errol Damelin's Summary

I'm a global entrepreneur and seasoned executive who loves building innovative businesses with inspirational people. I'm proud of my adaptability, having founded three major businesses on different continents and in completely unrelated industries.

I'm currently the founder and CEO of Wonga.com, a ground-breaking online lender. We provide small, short-term cash advances to UK consumers and our automated, real-time processing systems leave traditional lenders eating dust.

Wonga's mission is to provide a genuine alternative to slow, rigid and long-term sources of credit and we're committed to doing this responsibly. That means transparency, using sophisticated decision technology to ensure we lend to credit-worthy individuals and giving applicants unique control over the size, length and cost of loans.

We're also building a significant partnership with Kiva.org, a non-profit organisation doing great things with micro finance. For every Wonga cash advance we process, we're lending £1 interest-free to entrepreneurs in the developing world.

Prior to creating Wonga I was founder and CEO of Supply Chain Connect Ltd, which I developed to become a leading provider of hosted supply chain collaboration solutions. With offices in the UK and US, the company serviced over 250 blue chip customers and was acquired by ChemConnect in 2005.

My first start-up experience was in Israel where I was part of the founding team at Barzelan Ltd. The business rapidly grew to over 100 employees and became a global leader in advanced steel wire production.

I grew up in South Africa, before spending eight years in Israel. Following business school, I became an investment banker and, while helping entrepreneurs turn their dreams into reality, was inspired to switch roles.

I work hard, but in my limited free time I enjoy an active lifestyle including marathon running, mountain biking, sea kayaking and skiing.
