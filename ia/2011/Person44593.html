Upal Sengupta’s Summary

I have worked on a wide range of electronic system designs. My technical background includes board-level electronic design and embedded system applications. I have also had served in team leadership / engineering management roles, and have extensive customer support and new business development experience.

For most of the past 20 years I have focused on portable power conversion and system design, which includes laptop/notebook PC systems, "smart battery" applications, consumer product battery chargers, mobile phone systems and accessories, and power IC system definition and testing.
