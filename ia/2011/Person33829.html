Pascal Gauthier's Summary

Today, as Operation Manager of Bookassist France, my mission is to manage and develop the sudsidiary of this irish base leading company.
Bookassist is a recognised and innovative industry leader. Bookassist provides technology and online strategy for hotels, in particular developing and operating the award-winning and market-leading Bookassist booking engine for hotel reservations automation with PMS integration, channel distribution, agent/corporate interface and loyalty program capabilities. We manage online web 2.0 strategies for hotels and develop software to maximise online presence. The Bookassist engine can be embedded in hotel and hotel group websites to offer immediate bookings with credit card validation for online customers, plus it offers full reporting and yield management for the accommodation provider. Bookassist has won design and business awards and is the market leader in its home base in Ireland. Bookassist has offices in Ireland, Spain, Czech Republic, France, Scotland, Poland, Austria and Italy.
