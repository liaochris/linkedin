Alexander Rinehart, DC, CCN's Summary

Welcome!
My name is Dr. Alexander Rinehart and I am a Certified Clinical Nutritionist through the International and American Associations of Clinical Nutrition (IAACN), Chiropractor and the Owner of CoActive Health in Short Hills, NJ.

I look at how diet & lifestyle stress has affected your overall health by identifying the underlying causes and patterns behind your "dis-ease". I see practice members from across Morris and Essex Counties in North Jersey, as well as Greater NYC, and have conducted phone consultations with members as far as Las Vegas, NV.

CoActive Health focuses on conducting comprehensive nutrition consultations taking into account a full medical and social history as well as medication analysis for potential drug-herb benefits and interactions.

Location:

-Advanced Performance & Rehabilitation Center at 532 Old Short Hills Road, Short Hills, NJ 07078 (Drs. Jason Levy and Courtney Centrelli near St. Barnabas Medical Center). (By appointment Monday, Wednesday, and Friday, 8am-5pm)

Check out CoActive Health's website (http://www.CoActiveHealth.com) to read my blog, contact me for speaking engagements, or learn more about the clinical services I offer.

Feel free to contact me with any nutrition questions or topics you'd like me to cover in future articles!

For a complimentary consultation ($140 value), please call 862.210.WELL and we'll find a time that works for you!
