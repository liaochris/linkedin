Brian Danzis’s Experience

  • VP, National Sales

    Tidal TV

    (Privately Held; Online Media industry)

    September 2008 — Present (2 years 6 months)

    Brian joined TidalTV in September 2008; a tumultuous time for the US Economy, fostering great uncertainty in the advertising market. Over the next few weeks, a change in company direction was implemented and as the first East Coast Sales Manager (and bag carrying rep) he worked closely with the CEO and Head of Marketing to design a new marketplace approach, value proposition, sales presentation and collateral. Key Agency Holding Company executives, Buying Department Directors, Transactional Media Planners and Brand Marketers from the client side were identified, influenced and partnered with, and after six months - 1/2 dozen blue chip advertisers were signed on with TidalTV, generating over $1MM in revenue.

    Over the next two years, Brian built out a National Sales Force to meet increased demand, counter growing competition and compete for new opportunities as the agency model evolved: Existing relationships were nurtured and expanded, new categories were broken and strategic alliances forged to foster both. Today, TidalTV has over 100+ customers, revenue well into eight figures and is well aligned with key thought leaders in media, technology, entertainment and advertising.

    In a cluttered environment and 24/7 press release marketplace culture it’s often challenging to stand out among companies who claim to do the same thing as you- even if your technology is better. In the end, relationships matter and service make a difference. Brian’s consultative philosophy and solutions oriented approach has led to a 90%+ renewal rate and differentiated the company as one dedicated to the needs of all of her customers- each a constituent in an efficient ecosystem, powered by technology but driven by its people.
