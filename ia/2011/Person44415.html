Tutku Rüya KURT's Summary

I have attended extracurricular activities during my secondary education in Saint Benoit and higher education in Koç University. I was a licensed volleyball and basketball player. Since my first year, I was an active member at Koç University Marketing Club. In my second year, I was the president of Panel Group and in my third year the president of the Marketing Club. During this period I've initiated, planned and organized a marketing activity in the club named as "Kamp'05".  80 students from 25 different universities attended to the marketing training camp. They were trained by famous marketing experts and got the opportunity to listen and meet with the credible marketing people. Among the conferences I have organized, there were outstanding speakers like the marketing guru Jack Trout. I was also the organizer of the second camp, "Kamp'06" in my fourth year of education. This marketing event still continues and I still support them when they need. I have also organized and managed the Graduation Party and the yearbook. All these organizations not only contributed to increase my management capabilities and skills  but also I got the chance of increasing my knowledge about numerous marketing subjects and listen the real life cases from the marketing executives of the corporations.
