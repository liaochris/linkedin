Annabelle Drumm's Summary

Annabelle has a career more than 20 years working out front and behind the scenes via the mediums of corporate, theatre, fashion, entertainment, advertising, promotions and broadcast media.

She previously built two successful companies but throughout her life she's been attracted to nurturing the careers, self esteem and inspirations of others. She now takes that experience and applies it as a specialist Business and Life coach for the creative industries including ad agencies, VIP performing and visual artists, event managers, writers, photographers, fashion industry, designers, chefs, architects, digital media, film and TV production and talent.

"I find, as Creatives, we all share similar problems in keeping our jobs fresh and stimulating, keeping the ideas flowing as fast as the demand and improving life balance when we're working long hours.

"I notice there is often a common theme amongst clients of wanting the commercial work to not only be abundant but to have a greater meaning for ultimate job satisfaction. I am passionate about helping clients find what they value most so they can mould their lives and careers to match those values. Then they can truly love who they are and what they do.

"Being creative is not just about making money. It's about making a difference too. Work with me and I can help you build a legacy of which you can be proud."
