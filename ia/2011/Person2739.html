Andy Adams’s Summary

Hello! I'm Andy — I live in Madison, Wisconsin and I'm passionate about using digital media to promote arts + culture experiences. For the past five years, my projects have explored the intersection of online publishing, Web 2.0, and community collaboration in contemporary photography. In my spare time I publish FlakPhoto.com, an online art space that celebrates the culture of image-making by promoting the discovery of photograpers from around the globe. More about me at ANDYADAMSPHOTO.COM

Andy Adams’s Specialties:

• Visually literate with an excellent eye for detail; Exceptional skills in writing, editing and packaging content for the web — including blogs, email newsletters, Facebook and Twitter

• Experienced with Adobe Photoshop, digital imaging, scanners, digitizing standards, digital asset management, and quality multimedia & print output
