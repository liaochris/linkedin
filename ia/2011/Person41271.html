Sten Hallstrom's Summary

I have worked with IT for 20 years and followed technology from DOS and text terminals into the Internet revolution. I have been an entrepreneur within CD Mastering and search and retrieval technology and search engines. I held positions as: project manager, technical adviser, architect, sales, product manager, country manager (UK), account manager and IT Strategy.
My goal is to be the natural point of contact between the technology people and the clients, both the management of companies and clients. I am good at translating business requirements into technology but also vice versa. I have broad experience and like to see myself as a generalist. In the sales role I have the ability to instill trustworthiness.
I have done many system integrations and can quickly grasp, plan and drive these projects. As a product manager I took a search engine from CD ROM to an xml over http based search engine. I have created a protocol for single sign on between systems and platforms. I have worked with publishing houses in Sweden, Denmark, UK and Germany managing and developing their on-line publication platforms, financial and corporate data but also multimedia encyclopedias. The past years I have lead several integration projects with banks and custodians delivering reconciliation data to portfolio management systems. I have developed much of these integrations myself using VB, MS SQL and Power Shell to migrate from XML and SWIFT.
I have implemented and designed the distributed IT infrastructure for asset management and corporate services. It is based in Luxembourg which means taking the strict local laws into consideration and being audited by the finance authorities. The systems are based on Advent software platforms with in-house developed integrations, add-ons and wrapper applications like CRM, Fee management systems, ERP integrations, KYC and more. Last year I designed and lead the implementation of a cross office SIP based VoIP telephony system (3CX)

Specialties

Portfolio Management, financial reporting and monitoring systems, search and retrieval systems, archiving systems, automation, systems integrations.
Pragmatic and sensible approach to development and project managent. Technical assessments and advice within small to medium size IT infrastructure and software.
Technical sales, account managment and also management and deployment of smart phones.
