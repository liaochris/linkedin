Jake Fernandez’s Summary

B.F.A. and M.F.A. in Painting, with 30 years experience as a professional artist with studios in New York and Florida. I have work in public and museum collections as well as private collections, and an extensive exhibitions record. I have had a one-man retrospective exhibition at the Museum of Art in Fort Lauderdale and a recent one-man drawing retrospective at Florida International University in Miami. My paintings were purchased for the Florida State Capitol's Senate and House of Representatives in Tallahassee. I am currently pursuing large projects involving site-specific landscapes by commission, as well as producing a body of work for exhibition.

Jake Fernandez’s Specialties:

Fine artist specializing in large-scale landscapes, on linen and on wood panels. Pastels, graphite drawings, and photo-collages. Site-specific projects for public and private spaces.
