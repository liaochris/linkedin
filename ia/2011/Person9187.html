Shahzad Khan's Summary

A Gold medalist from MICA, Shahzad Khan has worked with companies like Dell, American Express and Haldirams before his PGDM and had very closely observed how brands were built. Shahzad Khan joined Quasar Media Pvt. Ltd (A WPP Company), after completing his PGDM (Communications and Entrepreneurship) from MICA-EDC, as a Manager- Social Media and Account Planning.

During his tenure at Quasar Media, he worked on some of the dream accounts including Sony, Microsoft, Delhi Daredevils (IPL), RPG, Skoda, Make My Trip, General Motors and many others. He worked on a 360 degree basis on most of these profiles. Right from the online strategy building to Client servicing, he took every project as a turn-key project. He made a record with the Delhi Daredevils project in which the members’ club website was launched in just 15 days.

Shahzad also worked as Managing Director at Conversations Digital Media Pvt Ltd which is his own brainchild. A company which integrates technology, marketing and anthropology together.

At Conversations, he has headed many successful campaigns including Microsoft's VSTS campaign, NDTV Imagine's RAKHI KA SWAYAMVAR and Delhi Daredevils' online membership club, to name a few.

Currently, Shahzad is working with east Africa's largest media house where he is heading sales and marketing for their Digital Division. Not only does he market the properties of the company (12 products each having their own website and social media presence) but also works on creative digital campaigns for his clients. He is working with clients including Microsoft East Africa, Bata Shoes, Kenya Airways, Apple Kenya, Kenya Tourism Board, Airtel Africa and a dozen of other companies.

Specialties

Social Media Marketing, Digital Marketing (Web 2.0 + Mobile), Semiotics, Brand Building, Reputation Management, Crisis Management, Identity Management, Corporate Communications and Cultural Communications.
