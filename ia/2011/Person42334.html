Tab Burton’s Summary

Over the past six years I have been developing my skills in virtually every aspect of animation and storytelling. After graduating from California State University, Monterey Bay with a degree in Teledramatic Arts and Technology, I decided to further my education at Ex’pression College for Digital Arts, where I decided to stay focused on character animation. I am an organized self-starter who understands the importance of meeting deadlines, and I work well in a fast-paced, multi-tasking environment. My B.A. and B.A.S. in the fields of animation and storytelling, with multiple academic awards, including graduating Summa Cum Laude, give me a strong foundation on which I hope to build a career in the animation industry.
