Hannah Kleyn's Summary

Hannah Kleyn is an information designer whose education in both teaching and business management enables her to approach design projects with an inherent understanding of business objectives while taking multiple learning styles into consideration. In support of sales, marketing, product, and reporting efforts, Hannah's work visually communicates product concepts and business information across both web and print media with clarity and transparency.

Hannah graduated Phi Beta Kappa from Indiana University Bloomington in 2007 with a Bachelor of Arts in Studio Art, certification in K-12 Art Education, and the Liberal Arts and Management Program in cooperation with the Kelley School of Business. Hannah worked with ChaCha Search, a Quantcast Top 100 website, from 2007 through 2009. She currently provides design services to several organizations from start-ups to Fortune 500 companies, and across multiple industries including mobile technology, SaaS, healthcare, automotive, government, non-profit, investment management, real estate, and education. Hannah's professional and academic interests focus on human-computer interaction and design thinking for positive cultural and economic impact.
