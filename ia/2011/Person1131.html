Alan W. Silberberg's Summary

Technology innovator; visionary catalyst, and incubator who is consistently ahead of the curve especially in Government 2.0 "Gov 2.0" technologies, media and apps. Digital crisis communications and brand engagements are major focuses, as well as market creation for innovative and disruptive technologies.

Silberberg Innovations, is the first Gov 2.0 merger, acquisition and tactical research and advisory company in the space, and is launching it's first Gov 2.0 M + A fund early 2011. We are partnered with Constellation Research Group, of which I am a principal analyst as well. We are also partnered with Bubbleideas, a semantic web company.

Gov 2.0 L.A. "Gov20LA" was the first major West Coast Gov 2.0 live and online streaming event. It is an annual event that brings a wide collection of technology, government, academic and finance leaders.

You2Gov was one of the pioneering Gov 2.0 citizen/government engagement platforms that paved the way for Governments in the United States and elsewhere to envision how data and communications can occur in the Government 2.0 era. I shut the site in July 2010 when most Federal agencies in the U.S. began doing what we did on their own. see this video: http://vimeo.com/9190493
