Like a “Spotify” for books
24symbols is a platform to read and share digital books.

What?
24symbols is a solution to read and share digital books, that works in any reading device with internet connection and that gives any user access to an international catalogue of a huge variety of publishing companies.

How?
24symbols uses basically a freemium model: it allows users to read for free by showing them non-intrusive contextual advertising. The premium model allows them advanced functionalities and to read without internet connection by paying a subscription fee.

Why?
Because we want to provide everyone the posibility of reading. Because reading makesus free. Because we believe in a ‘win to win’ model for readers, publishing companies and advertisers.
