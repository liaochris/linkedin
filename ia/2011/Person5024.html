Beryl Lieff Benderly's Summary

My prizewinning work has appeared in major national magazines ranging from Scientific American to Ladies Home Journal, in newspapers including the New York Times and Washington Post, in eight books, and in reports and volumes by prestigious institutions such as the National Academies. My column appears monthly on the website of Science, the nation's preeminent scientific outlet. My honors include election as a Fellow of the American Association for the Advancement of Science and national prizes for writing about cancer, genetics, psychology, ethics, information technology and more. I specialize in turning complex ideas into lucid, compelling prose and cover a wide range of subjects-- medicine, science policy, higher education, health, behavior, and religion--with expertise and versatility. My hallmark is writing that informs, illuminates, and engages.

Specialties

Articles, columns, reviews, reports and trade books that are incisive, accurate and a pleasure to read.
