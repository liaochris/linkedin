Anthony Fasano, P.E., LEED AP's Summary

Anthony Fasano AKA Your Professional Partner, CEO and founder of Powerful Purpose Associates is the author of the popular “Daily Boost From Your Professional Partner” and a nationally recognized professional coach and inspirational speaker. Known as “Your Professional Partner,” Anthony works with engineers and technical professionals to help them boost productivity and become successful beyond their expectations while maintaining balance and having fun.

With more than 15 years of experience in the engineering industry, Anthony knows what it takes to get ahead in your career. He has acted in leadership and advisory capacities for many organizations and associations.

With an appreciative awareness for the stresses and challenges of the corporate world, Anthony has dedicated himself to helping others in unleashing possibilities to create a career that is exciting, enjoyable, successful and well balanced.

Specialties

Career Development for Engineers, Engineering Coach, Professional Speaking, Professional Coaching, Inspirational Speaker, Executive Coaching
