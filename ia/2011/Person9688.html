Cynthia Jabar’s Summary

Cynthia Jabar has written and illustrated 5 picture books for children (including the one she is working on now) and illustrated over 25. Some of her picture books have been translated into Chinese, Japanese and Korean. Her "sassy, spirited" characters are licensed internationally for games, toys, stickers, cards, t-shirts, and Tabletop designs for the home. She loves to hang out with her family and friends, kayak in the ocean, surf the web, doodle, volunteer with kids and eat cookies. And not necessarily in that order. Right now she is probably busy in her NYC studio BUILDING DIGITAL BOOKS that scale, making messes and eating cookies.
