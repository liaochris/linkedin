Nancy Rose Senich's Summary

For over 20 years, Nancy Rose Senich has enjoyed leading an established high impact, high energy virtual marketing and public relations firm with substantial experience in launching new organizations and helping existing firms adopt and sustain change to meet new market conditions.

Nancy Rose & Associates leverages highly experienced professionals and best available and appropriate communications technology to achieve measurable results. NR&A is well known for achieving RAPID MARKET AWARENESS of new products and services TO DELIVER BOTTOM LINE BUSINESS RESULTS. Using a variety of tools such as end-to-end campaign planning and implementation, launch events (real and virtual), press releases, announcements, press kits, NR&A finds there is no substitute for HIGH TOUCH MEDIA RELATIONS to BUILD RELATIONSHIPS ON YOUR BEHALF.

NR&A's team can show you myriad success stories for companies involved in consumer electronics, information and defense technology, aviation diagnostics, clinical practice management, medical devices/breakthroughs and biotechnology.

NR&A is passionate about providing support to in-house talent or is also pleased to offer turn-key marketing and PR support to new management team. Nancy Rose Senich likes helping organization's establish in-house competence.

Start-ups and early stage companies/organizations/new initiatives are a specialty.
Nancy Rose Senich is a cheerleader for new initiatives and new ideas and can rapidly assess and correct resistance to new ideas.

Feel free to call her today at (202) 262-6996 (cell.) to discuss your needs!

Specialties

Publicity and marketing strategy for information technologies (federal and commercial), healthcare, defense technology and intelligence community technical services, biotech, medical practices, medical devices and equipment, advanced technology industries and non-profits.
