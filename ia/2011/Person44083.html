Tracey Taylor's Summary

I write, I edit, I blog and I sell and buy real estate.

I am co-founder of local news website Berkeleyside and a professional blogger -- until June 2010 I wrote a daily real-estate blog for the San Francisco Chronicle and I run my own independent blog, Home Girl.

I began my career in publishing working in a think-tank housed in a beautiful Beaux Arts building in Brussels. Then I moved to London to work for a number of different business magazines, including the weekly advertising title Campaign. I have also edited two books .

Today I live in the San Francisco Bay Area and work as a freelance writer and editor as well as a realtor.

Specialties

Profiles, interviews, business, homes, architecture, real estate, food, lifestyle.
