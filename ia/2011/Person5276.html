Bill Peters’s Summary

With over 20 years experience as a leader in the not-for-profit museum, science centre, planetarium and children's museum community, I have developed a network that includes the best planners, strategists, communicators and fund raisers in the business. My goal is to contribute both my own expertise and the expertise of my network to address your challenges and contribute to your success.

Bill Peters’s Specialties:

Visioning, strategic planning, community consultation, business planning, project conceptualization, sponsorship and fund development and recruiting great professionals.
