Paul Connell's Experience

Director

Woodholmes Group

Privately Held; Think Tanks industry

March 2010 – Present (1 year 1 month)

Wood Holmes is an innovation business, with offices in London, Manchester, Newcastle and Singapore. We assist a wide range of clients address complex problems.

Paul leads Wood Holmes services from the Leeds and Manchester offices. He is an Environment & Management professional with over 15 years experience working at the highest level in consulting, providing Environmental, Sustainability and Liability & Risk Management, Project and Programme Management services across many industry sectors. He is working with our ICT, Information, Knowledge and Digital Specialists on SMART CITY Concepts and projects.

Regional Director

WSP Environment & Energy

Public Company; LSE:WSH; Construction industry

2005 – March 2010 (5 years)

As Regional Director Paul was responsible & accountable for the operational delivery of WSP E&E's services from their Leeds & Manchester Offices for 5 years.

Working with the UK management team in a national Client Focus role, Paul led the development of a bespoke client relationship management approach for WSP E&E in the UK, collaborating with the Cranfield School of Management KAM team.

As part of the Global Environmental Liability Strategy team Paul was an integral part of the team that delivered the first liability transfer in the UK and was responsible for the development of this unique service in Europe where we successfully diversified the offering into a high value advisory service to liability holders, alongside the Liability Buy-Out offering.

Associate Director

WSP Environment & Energy

Privately Held; Management Consulting industry

2003 – 2005 (2 years)

Paul led a multi-specialist Business Unit based in Leeds, which latterly developed into a Soil & Groundwater Business Unit for WSP Environment & Energy, accountable for all aspects of business performance and development of this unit, Paul and the team delivered consistent and significant top and bottom line growth.

In 2004 Paul became WSP's Key Account Manager for National Grid.

Paul became part of the Global WSP Environmental Liability Transfer Team in 2005.
