Felicia Rubinstein's Summary

341 Studios is a full service marketing, graphic and web design agency, helping our clients stand out and get noticed for 10 years and counting!

We believe that strong visuals are only half of successful marketing. The other half is strategic intelligence, to make sure that your creative direction, message, and plan are the right ones for your organization.

► We've assembled an exceptional team of 16 designers, marketing strategists, copywriters, Web and e-communications specialists with a full range of expertise.

► We are a virtual office, so clients don’t pay for unnecessary overhead. Marketing budgets go directly into developing smart strategy, breakthrough creative, seamless execution, and material production.

► We’ve produced great results for a long list of clients across a wide range of industries, including health care, financial services, green technologies, political campaigns, and professional services firms, to name a few. Recent highlights:

- Marketing strategy, re-branding, Web site redesign, and collateral materials for work/life balance agency Flexible Resources

- Branding, Web site development, and collateral materials for real estate developer Baywater Properties, and start-up United Printer Technology Services

- Branding and Website design for a range of legal and investment professional services firms, such as Catalyst Investors and Kathryn Beller, LLC

- Strategic PR and advertising for boutique travel agency African Portfolio.

► Specific services include: marketing, graphic and web design, interactive media/Web site design, branding, identity/logo development, integrated marketing strategy, advertising, direct mail, email marketing, collateral materials (brochures/annual reports/trade show displays, etc.), social media strategy/implementation, and SEO.

We are committed to exceeding the expectations of every client. Please get in touch, and let us know how we can help your business grow.

Specialties

Interactive media and Web site design, branding, identity/logo development, integrated marketing and advertising, direct mail and email marketing, collateral materials such as brochures/annual reports/trade show displays, social media strategy and implementation, and SEO.
