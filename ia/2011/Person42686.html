Ted Gudorf, J.D., LL.M.'s Summary

Ted is the owner of Gudorf Law Group, LLC in Dayton, OH. He is one of the first attorneys in Ohio to be board certified by the Ohio State Bar association as a specialist in the area of estate planning, trust and probate law. He is the first lawyer in Ohio to obtain his Master of Laws (LL.M.) in estate planning and elder law. Ted is the Co-author of Estate Planning Strategies published in 2009 and is Co-author of For Ohio Doctors published in 2010.

Ted has over 24 years experience as a practicing attorney in estate planning, estate administration, business planning and asset protection.

Specialties

Estate Planning, Elder Law, Estate Administration, Tax Planning, Long Term Care, Medicaid Planning, Veterans Benefits Planning, Business Succession Planning, Probate, Trust Administration and Asset Protection
