Robert Maroszek's Summary

I am currently the acting Chief Operating Officer of Melbourne Specialist Imaging, a subsidiary of IM Medical, listed in ASX as IMI.

My professional experience runs across the IT, SCM, Luxury Markets, Corporate Governance Audit, Niche Branding and Management Consulting areas at C-level or senior management positions. In the past I was active in the American and European markets. My current projects and ventures are mostly in the medical and med-tech field, but also include distance education platforms, Capital Acquisition Strategies, Cooperating with ASEAN, and IT outsourcing.
