Erik Hagborg's Summary

I am a web strategist, information architect and UX consultant. I have extensive experience with web projects of all types and sizes, ranging from improving the user experience of an Endeca driven ecommerce app, through to helping restructure the entire web governance of a Crown Agency.

My approach is dedicated to improve how organizations use the web. It involves bridging the sometimes disparate fields of business, marketing/creative, technology and user experience to create a strategic solution that is appropriate and sustainable.

One of my passions in particular is working with heritage institutions. I have had the privilege to work with almost every major national museum in Canada and continue to focus on clients in the heritage sector.

Some of my most recent projects include web strategies for the Canadian Coast Guard, Health Canada, the Federation of Canadian Municipalities and the National Capital Commission.

Specialties

- Strategic planning, (Web Vision, Governance, Strategies, etc.)
- Stakeholder research and engagement,
- Business analysis, information architecture and content strategies.
- Heuristic Evaluations, User Experience and Interaction Design.
- Site optimization, analytics and metrics.
