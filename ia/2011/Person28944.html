Matthew Abate's Summary

I've been working in online design almost exclusively since the late 1990s , with some excursions along the way. I've shed (somewhat unwillingly at first) my pretensions as an editorial designer and embraced digital media as the future of graphic design.

My current goals are to become as knowledgeable in the areas of online and mobile media design as possible and to make the internet as pleasant as I can. I'm a Utopian modernist to a fault.

Specialties

Websites : B2C, B2B, editorial content, social networking, interactive video, mobile integration & mobile apps, and marketing oriented microsites / collateral
