Mark Nuzzo’s Summary

Mark Nuzzo is currently a Managing Partner of Creative Impact, which operates a series of companies in the Business Development, Management, Franchise, and Consulting areas of Hospitality and Vacation Ownership. These companies offer a broad spectrum of professional competencies and intellectual capital from Sales & Marketing to Property Management to Event Management. They also offer in-depth services such as Quality Assurance, Club Services, Anti-Rescission Programs, Marketing Networks and Business Consultations. Creative Impact has aligned its self with a group of quality and professional companies referred to as Business Success Partners who deliver specific resources to Creative Impact and its clients.

He is the former President of RCI New Market Development & Consulting (Leisure Sciences) and EVP & GM, RCI Global Consulting. He is also formerly President of RCI Resort Management. On April 2nd, 2001 Fairfield Communities was acquired by the Cendant Corporation. This merger created the largest Property Management Company in the industry. There are more than 175 Associations representing over 600,000 owners both Timeshare and Whole Ownership at over 75 resort locations. He was President of the Fairshare Plus Vacation Ownership Association for over 10 years.

Mr. Nuzzo was instrumental in the original development and implementation of the FairShare Plus Program, which is now the major component of the Fairfield Vacation System. He began his career with Fairfield in 1977 as the Food and Beverage Director at Fairfield Glade in Tennessee.

In 1995 he became a corporate officer of Fairfield Communities, Inc. and headed up as President of the Resort Management Services Division, which included the overseeing of the property management division, and along with the corporate operations of member services, call centers and a national rental division.
