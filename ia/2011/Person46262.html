Yannick Naud's Summary

Yannick Naud has considerable experience in derivatives & credit trading at leading financial institutions in France, Japan and the United Kingdom.

From 2002 until 2008 he was Managing Director and Head of High Yield & Convertibles at Dresdner Kleinwort in London. From 1998 until 2002, he was Head of Convertible & Credit Derivatives Structuring at Calyon, having previously been a senior team member in the Structured Products group. Prior to this, he worked with Credit Agricole Indosuez in Tokyo as Head of Proprietary Trading, from 1992 to 1998. He began his career in 1991 at Banque Indosuez, in Paris.

Yannick has a Master's degree in Business Administration and completed the Post-graduate program, both at Dauphine University, Paris.

Specialties

Convertible Bond, CB, High Yield, Credit, Callable Asset Swaps, CDS, OTC & Listed Deriatives, Prop Trading, Positioning, Market-Making, Asset Management, Structuring, Origination, Corporate Buy-Backs, Block Trading, Private Equity, Media & Technology Start-Up, Business Angel.
