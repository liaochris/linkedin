Morena Menegatti morena.menegatti[at]gmail.com's Summary

I'm a journalist listed in the Italian Albo dei Giornalisti Professionisti (Professional Journalists Register).
I have been working for 20 years in the communication field, developing important experiences on traditional and digital media (internet, sat tv, web tv, radio, newspapers and magazines), focusing on different audiences: journalists (through press offices), customers and business people.
In particular, in these last 5 years I've been developing a strong know-how on the web tv's used as means of communication both within and towards outside the companies. Furthermore, I'm cooperating with the Network Business TV held at the Università Luigi Bocconi in Milan. Recently, I've also been offering my knowledge in terms of new media and public relations in order to strengthen both Epson Italia and Centro Epson Meteo's communication, while managing a personal blog (for over 4 years, now) and attending several social networks.
Foreign languages: very fluent English, French and Spanish.

Specialties

Finding the most suitable means to powerfully support a company's communication strategy, either on line or off line.

I'm used to address the company's communication both to final customers, opinion leaders and the press. My actual focus is on Web 2.0 applications, e.g. social networking, blogs, web tv's, wiki's..., without excluding traditional means of communication, such as press office management.
Sat TVs, radios, newspapers and magazines are also part of my professional background.
