    Is This Seat Saved - Passionate About Furniture

    Is This Seat Saved is a premiere online supplier of furniture because we offer a wide selection and the highest quality at affordable prices. Couple that with our legendary customer service and you have one of the best providers in the industry.

    We Care About You

    Have you ever noticed when shopping online that the service you’re promised is rarely delivered? And when you request help after the sale, things tend to go downhill fast. So, where does that leave you - the customer? Probably feeling a little uneasy about making a repeat purchase. I understand because I've felt that same
    way…and that’s why I wanted to start an online furniture business that was different. One that actually delivered on what was promised.

    So when I founded Is This Seat Saved, I knew one of our foremost concerns had to be making sure you, our customer, were always number one. And we work very hard every day to make that goal a reality. We earn our customers trust by offering a user-friendly online store that provides exceptional-quality products backed up
    with superior customer service. It’s that simple.

    Plus, much of our furniture is manufactured from organic, renewable, reclaimed and other eco-friendly materials. We’re doing our part to reduce our carbon footprint and ensure the environment is considered in all of our business decisions.
