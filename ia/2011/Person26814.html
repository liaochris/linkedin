Lori White's Summary

Lori Elizabeth White is an award-winning digital and strategic consultant focusing on innovations consulting and online branding, content strategy and interactive product development at Electric Artists.

A graduate with honors from the University of Pennsylvania, Lori has worked to develop products with international and national public relations firms and advertising and marketing agencies, working with clients that range from St. Louis based startup MealChime.com, Bronx-based environmental justice superstar Majora Carter to national brands Bacardi USA, AT&T and The Economist Newspaper.

Her insights into the intersection of the real world and social media (as well as her resume) can be found at her personal website, http://iamlori.com.


SUMMARY OF EXPERIENCE
• Strategic consulting in developing and implementing social media innovations. Experience developing solutions-driven interactive products and partnerships, in collaboration with both client and agency creative teams.
• Focus on building the strength of brand conversations through content strategy, interactive copywriting and social media partnerships, creating vibrant communities around brands and personality, companies, products and ideals.
• Specific interests in social entrepreneurship, public-private partnerships, social media best practices and workflow, and online innovations within large civic and government institutions.

Specialties

strategic communications, strategic consulting, interactive product development and workflow, interactive content strategy, new media partnerships
