Jesse Salmon, LMT’s Summary

ABOUT ME:

I have been practicing massage since January of 2006. I love this profession and I aspire to be a big player in it as it continues to grow, flourish, and expand.

I am contracted by the City of Orlando to provide massage therapy services to the City of Orlando employees and retirees.

I give back to this profession by teaching students in the art of massage therapy. I am currently an instructor at Florida College of Natural Health.

Lastly, I am an independent contractor in this filed. I have established professional relationships with the premier national chair massage companies in the United States.

CHAIR MASSAGE:

I am an independent contractor for some of the best national chair massage companies in the United States. I provide chair massage services and I represent the individual companies when working their events.

I focus on chair massage services and events. I provide chair massage services at conventions, trade shows, expos, product launches, store grand openings, wellness fairs, small business, corporate functions, employee rewards, customer/client appreciation, and at any event where chair massage would benefit the individual and the company.

SPORTS MASSAGE:

I also work with athletes at sporting events providing sports massage during pre-event, inter-event, and post-event scenarios. I work with marathon runners, and tri-athletes.

THERAPEUTIC MASSAGE

I also offer a menu of services that range from relaxation to therapeutic massage services. I cater my table massage work to the needs of the client. I focus on results oriented sessions. Your car needs a regular oil change, likewise your body needs a regularly scheduled massage therapy session.

I do in-call massage therapy services in a beautiful office located in Winter Park near PF Changs. I also do out-calls to your home or office.
