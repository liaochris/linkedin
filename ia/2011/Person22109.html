Jonathan Tuck’s Summary

A highly motivated and resourceful consultant to the online recruitment industry working closely with a Job Search Engine, 1Job.co.uk. This site achieved HitWise top 10 ranking for Employment & Training in 2008.

Interim Managing Director for 3 months, providing Business Consultancy across the board including Sales and Marketing, Business Strategy, Systemisation, Innovation, Credit Control, and Accounting and Reporting.

Representative at Social Networking and Social Media events, and Coordinator of the National online Recruitment Awards (NORA’s).

For 10 years a Materials Scientist and Engineer with published patents and papers in all fields of specialisation: Coatings and Thin Films, Fuel Cells and Refractory Metals.

Keen to embrace the business side of operations at a higher level an MBA-type study was undertaken. This resulted in working closely with many small to medium sized businesses across the board.

Web editor and committee member for the South Wales and Western Polymer Group and the Energy Materials Group (IOM3). Owner / creator of several commercial websites, including Dating, Costume Jewellery Retail, Spiritual Healing and Classic Fords. SEO apprentice.

Seasoned traveller with experience of business cultures in the US, UK, France, Germany, Belgium, Slovakia, Bulgaria and Indonesia. Passionate about people and networking.
