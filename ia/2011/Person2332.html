Academia de Studii Economice din Bucuresti

Doctorate, Management in International Trade and Financial Relationships-Faculty of International Business

2001 – 2008

PhD thesis title: The Management of the Market Entry Risks(2008)
(Gestiunea riscului la patrunderea pe piata internationala)

It develops a new and original indicator, in an attempt to assess the risk of association with an enterprise that already activates on a certain market. Also a software application, called AnBilant (VisualBasic), was developed, in order to help the marketers to evaluate the non-public companies.
The research was made over more than 800 companies, mostly from Romania and listed at the stock exchange. A special attention was given to the gas and oil industry.

Exams on World Economics, Management of Risks and International Marketing.

Research projects on:
1) Market entry strategies (exports, franchisig, licensing, joint venture, strategic alliances, foreign investments: mergers, aquisitions, greenfields)
2) Country risk analysis
3) Risk management related to the process of internationalization.
