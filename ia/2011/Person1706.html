Ali Yildirim’s Summary

My name is Ali Yildirim and I have been working as a self employed freelance translator since 2003. I came to UK in 2001 as an archaeologist and began translating documents from and into English for Amtec Co-op, the company I worked for. In 2003, I started working for myself and setup Bosphorus Language & Translation. Since 2003, I have provided services for many companies and organisations such as Nokia, Canon, Portsmouth City Council, Defence School of Languages, Greater London Authority and for countless others. I also work for the Language Line a as a telephone interpreter. Translation, interpretation, proofreading, localization and transcription are among the main services I offer. I am a member of ITI - Institute of Translation and Interpreting. Please check the Bosphorus Language & Translation website for more information.
