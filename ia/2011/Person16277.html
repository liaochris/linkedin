Gökhan Besen's Summary

Hello,

I'm an Internet specialist with 14 years of experience.

I have found shoppio.com and Jetbul.com, first social marketplace and first vertical search engine for classifieds in Turkey.

In the last 4 years I have designed, developed and managed 2 of the top 30 sites in Turkey, driving 3.5 million visitor traffic daily.

I design websites since 1998. During this period, 5 of these sites was awarded as the "best site" of their respective categories in "Altın Örümcek", the one and only internet awards in Turkey.

My goal is to continue developing good ideas, rethink the existing ones and create better internet products for all internet users.

Specialties

Designing large scale social media concepts, SEO, analytics, managing and execution.
