Johan Farid Khairuddin’s Summary

One of the regions most experienced strategic youth business & media development experts, traveled to work across the globe with Asia's leading satellite broadcasting company (The Astro All Asia Networks plc) as the Head of Regional Youth Projects from 1999-2009.

Main role was to enhance brand positioning & enhance profitability amongst the youths through all group owned cross-media platforms, projects & business units-- worldwide.

Highlights of my 10 year tenure include speaking on Youth CSR & ICTs at the UN Global Youth Forum in Switzerland (Sept 07), the UN Meets Web 2.0 Conference in New York (March 08), the European Alternative Channel TV Forum in Barcelona (April 08), the World Congress for ICT (May 08) in Kuala Lumpur, the 1st World Radio Broadcasters Meeting (June 08) in Istabul, Turkey, the UN Global Youth Forum in Armenia (Oct 08), the 2nd Annual Middle East Corporate Social Responsibility Forum in Dubai (Nov 08) and the Annual General Meeting for the Committee for ASEAN Youth Cooperation in Manila, Philippines (Dec 08).

I was awarded by the United Nations the role of Youth Ambassador for the Asia Pacific region. Within Asia, I was appointed e-Ambassador to the Committee for ASEAN Youth Cooperation (CAYC). In Malaysia, I was the Youth Ambassador (CDC) for the Ministry of Youth & Sports-- All this in 2008.

Also as a veteran Radio & TV Show Host with more than 12 years experience in the entertainment and broadcasting industries; my hands-on and award winning work in all media including my role in music as a singer, songwriter & producer have given me an international following of fans which continue to expand my own network of global & influential decision makers.

I hold an MBA with a fellowship honor (MBA F.Inst BA) from the Trinity College & University in the United Kingdom, a Diploma in Aviation from the Australian Wings Academy, Gold Coast and an Airline Transport Pilot's License from the Malaysian Dept. of Civil Aviation.
