Harry Saul’s Summary

With over 40 years experience in the mortgage field, I have the vast knowledge and expertize to address and handle almost any lending situation.

Dellovo Capital acts as a correspondent for various HUD and Fannie/Freddie Direct Lenders focusing on Multi-Family, Assisted Living, and Hospital financing. Additional correspondent relationships exist with internationally focused lending groups.

Dellovo Capital also handles Debt Financing, Hard Money, and Venture Capital on all other types of commercial projects in a broker capacity. Funding is available from $3MM to $500MM+. Some examples of what we handle are: Land (single use or developments), Retail (stand alone or malls, anchored and not), Small Business (or bigger, if that's what you desire), Resorts (national and international), as well as other types of Commercial (manufacturing and industrial).
