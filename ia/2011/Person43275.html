Tiffany Johnson's Summary

I recently joined Spoonfed Media as a Client Manager to work on their Bullseye product. Bullseye is a digital marketing platform for the entertainment industry. Bullseye is a on-demand software tool that allows clients to create, manage and dispatch mobile and email marketing campaigns. Bullseye also provides seamless event dissemination and social media management in additional to analytics and reporting. As part of my role as Client Manager I will be responsible for CRM, product development, and research.

My previously held role as Marketing Executive at Experian Ltd was a diverse one. I gained a great deal of experience in planning, campaign management, copy writing, lead management and CRM. In particular I played an influential role in implementation of Siebel on Demand for global Experian divisions. My input to this global initiative helped shape and standardise use of the system to build an effective marketing database and CRM tool.

In addition to my professional experience I have also achieved international academic success. After earning a Bachelors degree with honours in Business Economics and Global Studies from the University of California in conjunction with McDaniel College in Budapest, Hungary, I went on to complete a double Master degree sponsored by the European Union. I received 1 of 26 worldwide scholarships to participate in the Erasmus Mundus programme for Global Studies. As a result I earned a Master degree in Global Studies from the University of Leipzig, Germany and a Master degree in Global History from London School of Economics.

Apart from my professional aspirations and academic achievements, I have an active passion for travelling, languages, and horse riding. I have visited more than 26 countries and I actively seek to improve my German, Hungarian, and Spanish language skills. I am also actively progressing through my teaching qualifications through the British Horse Society to become professional riding instructor.

Specialties

*Copy Writing and Copy Checking: excellent verbal and writing skills

*Research: strong academic grounding in research

*Campaign Management: end-to-end campaign planning, execution, and measurement
