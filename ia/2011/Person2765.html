Andy Giefer’s Summary

I'm a seasoned strategic marketing guy with a love for all things digital. I'm passionate about connecting remarkable and brands and people. I'm currently employed as Social Media Specialist for Anytime Fitness. I love my job!

Previously, I created and managed a number of successful campaigns at an integrated agency in Minneapolis agency over a six year period. These programs included PR, advertising, research, branding, direct and digital components.

In 2008-09 I embarked on a life-changing journey when I left my agency job to live in Argentina for one year. During that unforgettable trip, I taught English, studied Spanish, traveled widely and made many great friends.

Upon returning, I worked independently, providing strategic counsel, planning and implementation in social media and PR. I also blogged and championed ideals, ideas and remarkable brands.

Now at Anytime Fitness, I'm fortunate to be able to practice what I preach, building a strategic social media program from the ground up, engaging and igniting the interest of a large, customer base for a growing international company.

I love to talk shop. Feel free to get in touch!
