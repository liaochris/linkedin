Tim Reha’s Summary

I am CEO of New Media Synergy. Our clients include IDG's DEMO.com technology launch event, Wall Street Journal, Microsoft and Seattle Wine Awards. In partnership with the Washington Technology Industry Association, we host a monthly C-level social media training program.

We are experts at integrated digital marketing and social media. Organizations such as TEDxSeattle hire us for our real-time events expertise that leverage social media teams, HD video, live streaming and photography. We drive measurable results such as 300% increases in live stream viewership and 10,000 photo views up from a typical 1,000 views.

I develop both "on website" and "off website" internet operations. For on website, I develop CMS systems and content production teams that create search engine optimized content in the form of videos, photos, blog posts and web pages that are engaging and drive a direct response.

Next, I develop an integrated matrix of 35+ "off website" web services such as social networks (Facebook, Twitter), social book marks, content syndication (Digg), video syndication sites (YouTube), live streaming video (uSteam), photography syndication (Flickr) and dozens of other services. Each off site web property is customized with corporate branding, descriptions and links back to the main corporate website.

The entire ecosystem of on website and off website web services work together to create synergy for search engine optimization, direct sales, branding, social media memberships and other corporate goals.

I create internet dashboards that provide visibility for the above marketing ecosystem. The internet dashboards also feature other analytics to measure social media conversations, viral media spread, competitive intelligence and simple to use work flow tools to enable a team approach to managing a totally integrated marketing strategy.
