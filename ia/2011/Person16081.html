GIORGOS KOUTINAS's Summary

After having sound background and experience in engineering and project management, entered in the year 1992 in the insurance sector. Started with a French Insurance Group where obtained specialist know-how on insurance of business risks. Continued as an independent Insurance Expert providing insuance consulting services focused on construction projects and general liability issues. In 2002 created the "KOUTINAS SA - Insurance Brokers" company with the aim to develop business in Greece and at a next step to the nearby SEE countries. Collaborates with Greek insurance companies and the London Market. Since December 2007 is an accredited Lloyd's Binding Authority.

Specialties

Expertise in Professional Indemnity, Liability, Construction, Corporate and Special Business Risks Insurance.
