Alberto Barreiro's Summary

Digital Creative Director & Strategist with 15 years of international experience.

Alberto started his career as a digital artist, producing artworks that explore the intersection between art and technology, exhibiting in a large number of individual and group shows and getting excellent critics reviews and art prizes.
This experience in digital and web-based art helped him to join Yahoo! as a Product Designer for the european markets in the year 2000. Alberto took responsibility over the different disciplines of the User Experience and Design, creating numerous and diverse products for a very broad and demanding user base, including among his achievements, the design of the current global Yahoo! front page.
Alberto ended his career at Yahoo! as the Product Strategist for Yahoo! Mobile in the year 2006.
Briefly after leaving Yahoo!, Alberto co-founded Webjam Ltd. one of the most promising Web 2.0. companies in Europe.
Funded by a french venture firm (I-Source), Webjam is an innovative Internet start-up company based in London with the aim to give users the power of create, share, aggregate and publish personalised content through a disruptive bottom-up approach to web media publishing.
Alberto was in charge of driving the Webjam products and defining social media solution for their clients, from the creative vision to a successful development.
After the sale of Webjam, Alberto is now leading the Experience Design team at ITV, the largest commercial broadcast company in the UK.

Specialties

Digital Strategy, Creative Direction, User Experience, Social Media, Interaction Design, Information Architecture, Design Management
