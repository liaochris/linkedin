Dan Goldstein’s Summary

Your need for a top-performing creative artist who is detail oriented is an excellent match to my extensive history as an experienced and versatile artist. My specialties include 2D/3D digital design and animation. I am currently looking for work in this creative field.

In conjunction with traditional art forms such as drawing, painting, sculpting, and illustrating, I have an extensive background in modern 2D/3D digital design and animation techniques. My work experiences have allowed me to improve these skills, while also adding value to the company's projects. Contributing to several film and design projects has allowed me to gain a significant amount of experience and proficiency with various software packages for design, digital art, 2D / 3D animation, 3D modeling, and video editing.

I take pride in my work and always deliver a high-quality finished product. Working with others in this particular field is extremely important. I have been successful as a result of my ability to interact and communicate well with others.

You can view my recently updated online portfolio website and resume at www.TheDanGoldstein.com and I encourage you to contact me if you are interested in my work.
