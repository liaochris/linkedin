Shannon Yelland's Summary

Shannon, a Certified Web Analyst, has over a decade of online marketing experience. She shares her knowledge at http://seedtheweb.com.

She attends various conferences and seminars in North America throughout the year to keep up with the trends, strategies and best practices of analytics and online marketing. (See full list here: http://seedtheweb.com/about/)

Successful projects in the past cover such initiatives:

- Advanced web analytics consulting (strategy, implementation and tactics) with key stake holders and defining custom KPI dashboards
- Marketing optimization and lead generation analysis
- Search engine optimization (SEO) and audits
- Online marketing strategy, implementation and audits for all paid search engine marketing (SEM)
- Advanced pay per click strategies and implementation
- Social media strategy, management, execution and training (SMM)
- Agent of change. Evangelize for change within the company when it comes to SEO, SEM, analytics and blogging.

At ActiveState, she was responsible for analytics, driving organic search engine and paid traffic by creating the strategic vision, development and execution of key SEO & SEM programs while delivering the highest ROI. She also pioneered, trained and supported ActiveState`s corporate blogging program with thought leaders inside the company.

At Sitemasher (was a founding member and company was acquired) she was responsible for its SEO, SEM, analytics as well as the product SEO features and recommendations.

Before Sitemasher, she developed her own business - Mystic Marketing utilizing her diverse portfolio of expertise in online marketing (SEO, SEM and Social) and web development. Shannon had worked with clients from the concept through to the implementation stage of their online marketing strategies to increase their sales and revenues.

Shannon’s ability to understand Internet technologies in great depth brings tremendous value to any team. She also has numbers to back everything up!

Specialties
