James Morales’s Summary

Halito,
My name is James Morales and a member of the Mississippi Band of Choctaw Indians. I grew up in the Conehatta community and went to school there on the rez. I want to take this moment to welcome each of you to my humble page and tell you " Yahkoki ! " ( Thank you ! ) for taking the time to look at my profile.

If you would like to see, read, and learn more about Native Hoop, you can find us most anywhere online. Just do a keyword search or check on Facebook where there are many links to our shows, clients, groups, and pages.

We are working on a new non profit project called NERVA, Native Emergency Relief & Volunteer Agency. The purpose of NERVA is to create a national disaster relief organization targeting all tribes living on reservations and communites during natural and man made disasters. If you have any suggestion, thoughts or ideas you might want to share along these lines, they will be great appreciated. We also have a group here on linkedin with the same name....
