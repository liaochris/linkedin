Matt Hawkins's Summary

Hi, I'm the Founder of C4L in 2000. Winner of the South West's fastest growing tech company for the last 2 years in a row. Listed as the 12th fastest growing in the UK over the past 5 years with 3425% growth and the UK's fastest growing DC provider.

We are a wholesale Internet infrastructure solutions provider, providing white label & direct data centre space,colocation,internet bandwidth,data backup,data storage,network capacity,leased lines,wireless internet,fibre to the home/premises,interconnects,cloud,Virtualisation & VoIP.

We are Dorset's largest data centre & network provider and we have the UK's largest neutral data centre network,with over 36 data centres on-net in the UK & more in Europe.

We were the first UK company to provide 1000Mbps internet to the home,speedtest at over 900Mbps. Listed as UK's fastest ISP.

We own the South West's England's largest colocation data centre with up to 15,000 sq ft of DC space.

We specialise in 100% uptime,100% reliability Internet solutions.Using any of the following multiple data centre solutions, virtual platforms, dual site fail over,we will guarantee 100% uptime for our clients or money back.

The company has now become one of the UK's top Data Centre Internet & Colocation providers.Millions of users use our services everyday & we are estimated to currently be running about 1% of the UK's Internet.

We have created one of the UK's largest networks & an international network spanning into Europe and the USA.

We specialise in reducing clients current internet costs and at the same time improving their service. Through our economies of scale,own DC's,network & virtualisation we can reduce the costs of virtually any online presence and at the same time increase the reliability.

My other businesses include VoIP, Cloud & Wireless.

I am always looking for new developers,engineers & excellent sales staff as the business grows,so please get in touch,but please no recruiters/agencies.
