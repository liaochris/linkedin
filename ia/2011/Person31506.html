Mark Peskin's Summary

I come from a very deep scientific/academic background, and I have the strong analytical skills that that implies. I started my career as a developer, gaining extensive programming experience on C/C++, Java, and .NET platforms. I can still write code with the very best of them, but for the last few years my career has been more focused on a pure software architect/technologist role. I have reported to a the R&D director of a substantial software organization with HP (~500 employees of all types, worldwide), working on large-scale distributed software architectures that integrate multiple components across product silos to provide targeted solutions for various customer verticals. I have also directly with the business team controlling that organization to provide guidance on technology choices and deployment of development resources. In doing so, I have become well acquainted with all aspects of the software business, including R&D, marketing, sales generation, professional services and support, management of external and internal partnerships, fulfillment, and legal and intellectual property issues.

I hope to continue to leverage the breath and depth of these software industry skills to eventually contribute at a CTO level.

Unique Qualifications:

Demonstrated ability to create novel and sophisticated enterprise software architectures “from scratch”.

Analytical skills from a rigourous scientific and matematical background including statistics, optimization, advanced numerical techniques, and simulation software development.

Keen understanding of the entire software business, end-to-end and top to bottom. Not just a coder.

Unusually strong ability to very rapidly and independently learn and evaluate new languages, platforms, and technologies.

Top notch communicator with excellent writing, presentation, and public speaking skills. Can communicate effectively with customers and management as well as fellow engineers.
