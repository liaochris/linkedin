Andrew Ballenthin's Summary

As president of Engagement Marketing Cubed (EM3), Andrew's passion is branding, integrated marketing and thinking outside of the box. Andrew has worked with organizations like Assante, the University of Guelph, Dalhousie and Ryerson University, Ministry of Environment, Harley Davidson, Molson, Labatt, Volvo Group, Quebecor, Thorn EMI, Sharp, Direct Cash, Rothmans Benson and Hedges, Canadian Tire, Gateway Newstands, UPS and Texaco. Andrew's specialty is research and blending marketing with new media engagement and web 2.0/3.0.

- Presented plans for social media and pipeline integration for full service marketing agencies.

- Bogging achievements; 360+ posts, 2,100+ comments in addition to a peak web ranking of U.S. - 22,381, Canada - 19,387
