Oliver Manalese's Summary

While at University of Toronto, Oliver was compelled to leave so that he can master his sales skills. Shortly thereafter he built a successful track record in the recruitment and staffing industry as a Business Development Consultant. Over the years he helped advance the careers of many Canadians as well as advance the businesses for some of Canada’s top companies. Throughout this time, Oliver developed a strong passion for real estate investing.

Surrounding himself with successful industry professionals, he acquired and developed an expertise in Rent to own. He was ecstatic about this opportunity as he was not only investing in property, he was investing in families who were on their way to achieving home ownership. A true win/win scenario!

Oliver is Co-Founder and HIT Broker at The RentBusters, Sales Representative with Right at Home Realty Inc., and specializes in rent-to-owns and income property.

Find out what a HIT is at: www.TheRentBusters.ca
