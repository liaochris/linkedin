Dana Leigh Shafman’s Experience

  • President & CEO

    Shieldher Inc

    (Public Safety industry)

    January 2007 — Present (4 years 1 month)

    Shieldher Inc was started in February 2007 by an entrepreneur, Dana Leigh Shafman, with a mission to increase the quality of our lives by pacifying the daily fears we face as women. In order to get our message out to women throughout the USA, we decided to invent Tupperware-style TASER® Parties across the country to educate women about the benefits of personal protection with a TASER® C2. Shieldher Inc is still a woman owned and woman operated business with an even broader focus to protect all life, not just the lives of women.

    We were absolutely blown away by the positive reception of the Shieldher TASER® Party and found ourselves fielding inquiries and media requests from every corner of the world from South Africa to New Zealand to Israel. Shieldher was featured by almost every prominent national and international media outlet and we were euphoric to appear on the TODAY Show (in studio) in January 2008. In fact, our audience and clientele grew from women to men and even young adults at that point; more fathers and sons were attending our Shieldher TASER® Parties for their daughters, sisters, mothers and even themselves. We realized that we were actually making some progress in bringing personal safety and self defense to the forefront.

    As we continued to gain favorable exposure, it was evident that we needed to broaden our product suite to include products and services that were attainable by all geographic locations and economic classes. Therefore, we began an extensive search for the best Shieldher products and services that focused on self defense and personal safety. We met some great people and found some amazing products and services to aid us in our mission and with an even bigger passion to protect.

    This year, we are so energized about our new Shieldher Against… charity campaign and we are anxious to donate our time and money to many worthy causes.
