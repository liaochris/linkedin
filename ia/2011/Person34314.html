Paul Thiemann’s Summary

Building out a consulting practice that helps growing mid-sized companies elevate their marketing sophistication, with a focus on branding, online/social media and communications.

Currently working with a major medical co. and a young adult apparel retailer. But always open to hearing about a great opportunity. Contact: thiemannbrandconsulting@gmail.com or 818.304.4746.

Uniquely well-rounded, senior brand marketer; focused on the "big picture" and delivering meaningful results; extensive experience counseling C-level management and leading teams; passion for marrying research and strategy with consumer touchpoints; great love of sports and music and leveraging them to connect brands with consumers

20 years of marketing and communications experience for some of the world’s most valuable brands, including American Express, Bass footwear, Budweiser, Coca-Cola, Dockers, Hasbro and Levi's

With increasing responsibility, led numerous teams in the creation of brand-building and integrated marketing programs to support key business objectives – i.e., create brand awareness, improve brand equity measures, leverage sponsorship, increase sales, drive traffic

Broad marketing experience, including retail/wholesale, PR, sponsorship, experiential, online and advertising, across apparel, footwear, beverage and financial services categories, among others

St. Louis native and Mizzou journalism grad who left for broader horizons in ATL (Coca-Cola) and then SFO (Levi's), where I made the jump from PR to broad-based marketing; moved back to STL for family reasons; gained extensive brand strategy experience at Brown Shoe and selected to be first-ever Director of Marketing for Brown's E-Commerce division, including Shoes.com, relocating to LA; after Brown consolidated in STL in 2008, joined AEG to complete the fairly rare trifecta - client, agency and property sides; when the AEG role turned into mostly sales, I returned to my love of brand marketing and consulting

    (Privately Held; Entertainment industry)

    2008 — 2010 (2 years )

    With a strong desire to stay in Los Angeles for family reasons, I was recruited to start a new group within the $250m sponsorship division of AEG, a multi-billion-dollar sports, music and real estate conglomerate -- AEG LIVE, LA LIVE, STAPLES Center, O2 London, LA Kings (NHL) and Galaxy (MLS), Amgen Tour of California, etc. Served as the in-house strategy and marketing consultant, focused on innovative marketing/communications, retail activation and high-level business development.

    • Developed from scratch comprehensive processes and procedures, including a turnkey “Brand Audit” template, that brought rigor and strategic direction to both the sales and service teams.

    (Public Company; BWS; Apparel & Fashion industry)

    2004 — 2008 (4 years )

    • Spearheaded a major initiative to re-position the Bass brand. Worked with external agency to develop new brand position/brand identity and create integrated marketing campaign, including consumer and retail marketing elements.

    - In concert with merchandising, coordinated CPC, SEO, affliate and shopping engine activities to balance traffic/sales-driving focus with appropriate brand standards.

    - Led development and execution of Shoes.com first direct-mail catalogs, a partnership with eBags. Program exceeded expectations of skeptical senior management, proved the viability of a targeted offline activity, and the catalogs have continued to be a major seasonal sales driver.

    When Brown relocated the e-commerce division from LA to St. Louis in 2008, we declined to move back, due to the limited long-term professional opportunities for me there.
