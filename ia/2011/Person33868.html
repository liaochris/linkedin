Pat Iyer’s Summary

Legal nurse consultant with 23 years of experience assisting medical malpractice, personal injury and products liability attorneys with medical aspects of their cases. Skilled public speaker with extensive experience providing education to healthcare professionals, attorneys, and paralegals. Author or editor of over 120 medical/legal books, case studies, chapters, articles or online courses. Legal nurse consultant based in New Jersey servicing clients throughout country.

Keywords: legal nurse consulting, Principles and Practices of Legal Nurse Consulting, AALNC, American Association of Legal Nurse Consultants, patient safety, LNCC, legal nurse consultant, avoid medical errors, nursing documentation, nursing malpractice, nursing negligence, medical malpractice, risk management, nursing home litigation, medical legal aspects of pain and suffering, medical legal aspects of medical records, personal injury, products liability.
