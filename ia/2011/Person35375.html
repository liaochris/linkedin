Jacki Ralph (Press Release Etc.)'s Summary

I have been freelancing in the gift, collectible and toy industry for the past 13 years as an Editorial Assistant, Staff Writer and Contributing Writer. I have authored hundreds of articles over the years for publications such as Playthings, Collector's Edition, Teddy Bear and Friends, Teddy Bear Review, The State Journal-Register and more. You can read my press releases each month in trade publication like Giftware News, Gift & Dec, Greetings Etc., Museums and More and many others where they are printed almost verbatim in the midst of a feature story.

You may have dealt with me early on in my specialized field when I was a staff writer for Mary Beth's Bean Bag World in 1997 where I wrote for more than 150 of Ty Warner's soft toy competitors. I was the non-Ty liaison for Bean Bag World and the assistant editor on Mary Beth's Teddy Bears and More.

Needless to say, my grassroots knowledge in the editorial field has been an asset to my clients. I know exactly how to write a proper press release and when to put it on the right editor's desk in time for publication. That is the key to success with the media. I know exactly what the editors are looking for and when they need the material from your company because I am one of those editors who review your current press releases and decide if is worthy for print.

There are literally hundreds of thousands of toy and gift companies that are not getting the proper media attention they deserve, and you could be one of them. If you don't tell the media about your product then they won't print anything to help you sell your line. Thus my motto, "If you tell them, they will print it."
