Nathaniel Payne’s Summary

I am a developing researcher, writer, teacher, and marketer with over 9 years of research, teaching, writing, and coaching experience. I believe in the power of learning, and for the last 5 years have been a top ranked guest lecturer & teaching assistant at Simon Fraser University in the areas of marketing, organizational strategy, and finance. Similarly, I actively research with professors in both the Faculties of Business and Education at SFU, and have an established publishing record.

Through my various positions as a marketing & financial analyst, project manager, and consultant, I have built marketing plans & analyzed the financial, marketing, and operational strategy of businesses in the education, consumer products, resource, finance, and transportation domains. I am the former assistant manager of Future Shop's $400 million laptop business in Canada, have written a book on financial analysis, and have been involved developing and leading recruitment and marketing campaigns for a variety of clients.

I am highly creative with a passion for writing, teaching, and research. I intend on completing my graduate work at UBC's Sauder School of Business (M.Sc & PhD), becoming a leading international researcher in the areas of business strategy, operations, logistics, statistics, and applied mathematics, while also becoming a top research consultant for companies located across the globe.

Finally, and most importantly, I believe in changing the world through action & inspiration. My goal is to one day become one of the greatest philanthropists and leaders of this century, and to change the world by investing in education, health, sustainable development, and the environment. When I am not researching or teaching, I pursue one of my greatest passions - writing - and aspire to be one of the greatest literary contributors, authors, and storytellers of this century.
