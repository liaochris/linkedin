Pure Fight Gear is working to give back to the MMA community by supporting MMA’s next generation. The fans have a direct impact on the success of our mission and as such, formed our motto: You buy. You wear. You spread the word. You make the difference.

    Our mission is to make Pure Fight Gear a globally recognized brand, known for its ability to benefit the sport of mixed martial arts. Pure Fight Gear is dedicated to the sport’s future generations and believe at-risk youth can benefit greatly from learning core values; dedication, discipline, commitment and respect. We understand the character shaping values the sport provides and feel the positive aspects of MMA will benefit at-risk youth. Our mission will be achieved with the help and partnership of the sport’s athletes and organizations.

    We believe mixed martial arts organizations, management and fighters will embrace our cause when our mission is clearly communicated and when they are provided the opportunity to actively participate. We plan to provide these opportunities to participate by hosting a variety of events ranging from youth camps to hosted fundraising events.
