Jim Stapleton's Summary

Jim Stapleton is a creative, focused executive who has successfully led and sustained start ups and turnarounds at Atlanta Olympic Committee, National Linen Service, Allied Automotive Group, BENS and Ecolog USA. He holds one of the best track records when it comes to winning in high visibility, high pressure, and high profile operations. His brand is simply stated as “people first, mission always”. For the past decade he has led specialized work forces in complex organizations to accomplish unique projects. His work with the Atlanta Committee for the 1996 Olympic Games, National Linen Service, Allied Automotive Group and KBR has cemented his reputation as a “get it done” leader. Jim knows how to win in environments of uncertainty, generality and ambiguity. The success with KBR operating out of Baghdad was the ultimate win!
