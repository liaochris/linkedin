Isaac Gealer’s Summary

During my last year in High School and my years at the University of Arizona, I have worked in the music industry extensively including music journalism, recording labels, and a radio station.

I am currently Head Music Director for KAMP Student Radio, a free format college radio station with 130+ student DJs and staff. I manage six genre (2 Alternative, 1 Hip Hop, 1 Metal, 1 World/ Jazz, and 1 RPM) based Music Directors. My responsibilities include overseeing charting to a College Music Journal and receiving new music in the studio. I also host an hour long radio show called "Only For The Night." I also intern at Park Avenue Records, the University of Arizona's own recording label run through the school of music.

I also write extensively. I mainly focus on music journalism where I review and interview artists and albums. I have had many reviews and articles appear in print while the rest are on the Internet on respectable music journalism sites.

I would like to pursue a career in music management, journalism, or recording.
