I am the President of Systems Integration & Analysis, (SIA) and Vice President and Principal Consultant for Aire Expresso and Vice President and Principal Consultant for Zwerdling Technology Industries. I have over fifty years experience in systems engineering, including 25 years in the logistics, manufacturing, maintenance, electronics, procurement, supply and operation areas, and 25 years experience in Information Technology (IT) program management and consulting with large, complex Computer Aided Software Engineering (CASE) software development projects in COBOL, C/C++ and Microsoft’s Visual Studios. Along the way I have become an expert in the Sales and Marketing arena. Aire Expresso is a small disadvantaged minority business that is a Small Business Administration 8a Program Participant and a Disadvantaged Business Enterprise (DBE) with the Department of Transportation.

Aire Expresso is a General Contractor doing construction, heating, ventilation and air conditioning work.

Zwerdling Technology Industries is a Service Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business doing business in the Logistics Management arena.

I consult with small Disadvantaged businesses that want to do business with the Federal Government. I then help them to get certified in therir area of exprertise.
