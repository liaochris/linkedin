Deborah Ritchey’s Summary

Deborah's career specialty is finding unknown opportunities in the marketplace and capturing that market in a timely, profitable manner. Owner of a 25 year old Chicago based marketing company she has worked with Fortune 500 companies to growing and start-up businesses to help them develop and rollout successful marketing programs. Her approach integrates traditional brand strategies with innovative marketing techniques to build business growth. An entrepreneur at heart, Deborah selectively invests and actively participates as CMO in new ventures with promise. She continues to work with clients who want distinctive marketing with measurable results.

Deborah Ritchey’s Experience

  • Founder, Principal

    Ritchey Associates, Inc.

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    May 1984 — Present (26 years 10 months)

    Deborah founded Ritchey Associates, Inc., a results oriented marketing and communications firm. In 1994 Ritchey was one of the first internet advertising firms in Chicago. Today the focus is on integrating sound marketing strategies with both traditional and digital communications to achieve superior marketing results. Consulting services include market research, strategic planning, brand and message positioning and creation, web development, product/service launch, and producing highly effective marketing communications programs both on and off the internet. Ritchey has worked with clients ranging from Fortune 500 giants to growing start-up companies including BMW, Citibank, Kemper Financial, Four Seasons Punta Mita Residences Development, Westin, McDonald’s, Motorola, Starting Point/YesMail, CoolEmail, Lettuce Entertain You, and more. Prior to founding Ritchey Associates, Deborah was an Account Executive at the Chicago adverting agency Marsteller, Inc.

  • Co-Founder CMO

    Pied-a-Terre Living

    (Real Estate industry)

    September 2008 — December 2010 (2 years 4 months)

    A passion for luxury real estate, marketing, and travel, Deborah co-founded Pied-a-Terre Living to provide highly specialized real estate consulting services. The firm specializes in marketing and sales of luxury properties and internationally recognized Private Residence Clubs around the globe. As marketing strategist consultant, Deborah brings years of measurable marketing results to the firm’s clients by bringing them the best possible branding, messaging and marketing solutions on and off the internet to attract qualified clients. Pied-a-Terre Living operates under the prestigious global banner of Coldwell Banker, the nation’s oldest real estate organization that has made developers successful and ownership a reality for millions. Today, the Coldwell Banker brand has a global reach, with offices on six continents, 46 countries and territories, and thousands of highly qualified agents.

  • Co-Founder CMO

    Starting Point LLC

    (Internet industry)

    July 1995 — July 2000 (5 years 1 month)

    In 1995, Deborah entered the internet space in a partnership to form Starting Point, a web portal and directory. At that time, Starting Point was second only to Yahoo! As Managing Partner and Chief Marketing Officer, she defined the company strategy, created and implemented the marketing plan, and garnered significant revenue opportunities for the company. Starting Point was one of the few profitable internet companies. Later, Ms. Ritchey sold her interest to YesMail.com as part of a roll-up strategy to go public. The company was then sold to CMGI.
