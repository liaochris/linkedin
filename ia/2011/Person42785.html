Terri Cox's Summary

I'm rare… I'm a Colorado native living in Boulder! I love it here! I grew up in Lakewood, then Broomfield, and after high school I joined the United States Navy and travelled the world! I worked as an Aviation Storekeeper with my main duty station in Sicily where I was a part of the "Black Stallions" of HC-4, a helicopter combat support squadron that provided food, mail and cargo for the ships. What an experience!

After the Navy, I moved to Manhattan and worked as a bartender. I would say that’s quite a change of pace! It didn’t take long before I fell in love with the hustle and bustle of the Big Apple! But all good things come to an end so a few years later I moved back to Colorado. When I bought my first house, my mortgage lender persuaded me to work for him and that is where my real estate career began. For the next six years I worked at Automated Lending and got quite comfortable with the whole process of what it takes to get a mortgage.

But mortgage lending wasn't quite what I wanted. It was too much time on the phone and not enough personal interaction so the natural thing to do was to become a Real Estate Broker. I got my license and started working at RE/MAX of Boulder, Inc. where I’ve now been for over three years. I have to say I really love my job because I’m constantly helping people! Nothing makes me happier than to see the smile on someone’s face when I have taken them from the beginning to the end of their goal in finding the right home!

Specialties

Providing personal attention to first time homebuyers, negotiating contract, punctuality, listening to your needs and wants
