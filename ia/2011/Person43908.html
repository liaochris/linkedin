Tom Troceen's Summary

I started working with DW Simpson Global Actuarial Recruitment as the webmaster of the Actuarial Outpost in April 2005. I have since joined On-Campus Resources (on-campus.com) and recently launched 4InsuranceJobs.com.

I continue to develop a rich environment for actuaries to network online and discuss actuarial issues. If you're an actuary, check it out for yourself at http://www.actuarialoutpost.com. If you would like to take part in the discussion, you can register for an account at http://www.actuarialoutpost.com/register. We also have a large group here on LinkedIN, which you can find by simply going to www.actuarialoutpost.com/linkedin, and you will be forwarded to 'Actuary: The Worldwide Actuarial Group.'

My primary goal is to provide a forum for actuarial students to work together and pass actuarial exams. The Actuarial Outpost (aka `The AO`) is also a great resource for actuaries not taking exams to discuss their specific field in the actuarial profession. We have over 300 forums, on a wide range of topics, such as career advice, actuarial software, actuarial careers, actuarial science universities, health, P&C, pension, investments, life, and more. With well over 20,000 members and 4,000,000 posts, we are the largest social network of actuaries world wide.

Specialties

Recruitment on contingent and retained searches in health, life, annuities, property & casualty, investments / financial, pension and other actuarial disciplines.

New Media, Social Network Development, On-line Community Development, Wiki Development, GUI Design, SEO, PHP, MySQL, Linux, vBulletin, Wordpress, Mediawiki...
