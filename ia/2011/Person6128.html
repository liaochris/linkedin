Brett Wingate’s Summary

Brett Wingate, an award-winning creative leader with extensive experience in advertising and integrated marketing, is founder/president of Fear No Ad LLC, a full-service ad agency. Prior to starting Fear No Ad, he managed World Wrestling Entertainment’s Creative Services department and developed creative solutions for all initiatives globally to support the WWE brand.

A graduate of the School of Visual Arts, Wingate has been the recipient of numerous advertising industry accolades including Promax Gold and Clio’s as well as having his creative work featured in all of the advertising trade publications.

Wingate held positions as Creative Director at Saatchi & Saatchi and Grey Advertising working in New York City and London offices where he managed the creative executions for multiple global clients including Mumm Champagne, Proctor & Gamble, Diageo, Hasbro, HBO, Pepsi, Maxell, Kraft, Harper's Bazaar, Citibank and Kohler to name a few.

Wingate also worked extensively in the fashion/beauty and cosmetics categories, creating successful campaigns for L’Oreal, Calvin Klein, Pantene, Ivory and Olay.
