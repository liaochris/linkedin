Jody Ranck's Summary

I have extensive experience working with major health players in the public and private sectors advising on innovation, ehealth, strategy and foresight. I have recently become a member of the Executive Team of the mHealth Alliance and am a Senior Health Policy Advisor for InSTEDD. I have also convened major global health summits for top-tier foundations and have over 20 years of experience in global health and development with work at the Grameen Bank, post-genocide Rwanda and Southern Africa. My core interests include mobile health, social media and health, and health system transformation and innovation.

Specialties

Research, eHealth/mHealth, strategic planning, innovation, trends and forecasting, future studies, Islamic business and finance, microfinance, public health, human rights, corporate social responsibility, biotechnology, bioethics, global business, global health, scenarios, development, agriculture, mapping.
