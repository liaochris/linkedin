Justin Abernathy’s Summary

SureClick is passionate and committed to providing superior customer acquisition programs. Our clients depend on us to be an extension of their marketing departments to create sustainable and innovative programs that are designed around meeting their cost per acquisition that result in profitable campaigns. The SureClick philosophy of pay for performance aligns us with our clients' success. Our results driven mentality permeates throughout the company and challenges every team member to not just meet but exceed our clients' expectations.



Our systems and processes have been built exclusively for delivering scalable acquisitions of either leads or customers. With our staff that is trained in managing intensive acquisition programs, we have all the key elements needed to provide our clients with a successful customer acquisition program at or below their allowable cost. Campaigns are tailored to meet each clients unique needs now and as they grow in the future. Through partnering with SureClick, clients are able to alleviate the burden on their marketing resources, enabling them to focus on core competencies with the peace of mind that they are capitalizing on the opportunities in the online market.



Inc magazine ranked SureClick as the 3,628th fastest growing privately held company in the US. SureClick was also recognized by Deloitte as the 298th fastest growing company on the 2007 Deloitte Technology Fast 500.
