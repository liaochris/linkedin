Alex O'Byrne's Summary

I've spent a lot of my life trying to build useful software that is usable. I set up We Make Websites with Piers in 2009, with the aim of helping businesses and charities meet their strategic objectives better with web technologies. We do this by creating premium quality, highly usable websites.

Previously, I worked in private banking technology for a major US bank, with positions in London, New York and Geneva. During this time I discovered the lean startup movement and re-embraced my passion for creating good quality software using many of the fantastic new open source technologies available.

My offline passion is snowboarding, but I rarely get to do that now I spent nearly all my time in London.

I am also the co-founder of photography&mash, an online community of fine art photographers. Recently I've been working on building a photography gallery and studio in Dalston, it's about a month away from completion.

I studied Computer Science with Business and Management at the University of Manchester.

Specialties

Software development, specifically web (Drupal, Rails, PHP). Banking technology (front and middle office). Retail e-commerce development, specifically art and fashion. Requirements gathering and working with clients, understanding the interplay of design and technology. Trying to solve business problems with well thought through solutions. Making websites.

- We use open-source software as the foundation of the systems we build, which means no expensive licensing fees and better quality code.
- We are flexible, can work within a budget, deliver to tight timescales and are quick to react.
