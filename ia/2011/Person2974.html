Anil Pagar's Summary

Anil is the Founder, Managing Director and CEO of SpadeWorx Software Services. He is a Bachelor’s of Computer Science and Technology, followed by a Masters in Computer Engineering.

He began his career in 1989 with stints at Informatics, IBM and Hewlett Packard. With acumen of Product and Technology Development, Anil has been instrumental in churning several middle ware and back end products currently implemented at several Fortune 500 companies.

He in his career has had an exceptional track record of establishing flourishing business units from ground up. He has a strong technology foresight with the capability to employ technology to suit business climate, demands and usage.

He has a distinct ability to envisage solutions that are adaptable to the user groups and create these solutions in most contextual and relevant ways befitting and benefiting the Businesses with ever changing climates, demands, needs and landscapes.

Anil has received several awards and accolades for his innovative product ideas and IP disclosures. He has presented his papers and views on varied topics in industry consortium and seminars globally and has been a Guest Lecturer at renowned Engineering Colleges.
