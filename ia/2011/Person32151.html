Nathaniel Galimore's Summary

Nate brings a valued set of skills cultivated over the past 10 years as a technologist to any team. His expertise includes areas such as Web Server Administration, Enterprise Server Environments, Microsoft Operating Systems, MS SQL Server, MS SharePoint, and Project Management. Nate’s career began with an exploration of the creative arts through work as a Graphic Designer while familiarizing himself with other technologies

His reputation and ability to learn quickly landed him a position as Systems Administrator with a Vermont based newspaper. Nate managed the IT department budget, implementing cost-benefit analysis to optimize expenditures while improving efficiency. Leveraging advanced knowledge of and certifications in Microsoft technologies, he branched out as an independent IT consultant contracting with companies such as Merrill Lynch, General Motors and Solomon Smith Barney.

A position as Project/IS Manager with a local retailer soon followed, where responsibilities included WAN and LAN resource management and security, SQL database management, and POS, Accounting and Warehousing workstation setup and maintenance.

Most recently Nate has served as a consultant with the NYS Division of the Budget, NYS Department of Corrections where his accomplishments included high-level systems administration and applications support, implementation of MS ISA 2006 Enterprise Proxy Servers, a successful datacenter relocation and oversight of a state-wide 200+ server, 6000+ client MS Windows\Citrix Enterprise class infrastructure.

In the fall of 2008 Nate launched his own IT Consulting firm Level Nine Technology Consulting, LLC. The focus is to take his experience of technologies and IT Support at the enterprise level and provide it to the Small to Medium Sized Business Market.
