Christopher Sequeira's Summary

My international responsibilities included being Country Coordinator for South Asia and on project assignments in Germany and the United Kingdom working on Global projects. I have been fortunate to visit many cities and towns all over India during the course of my project assignments at Siemens offices and at external Siemens clients.
