Troy Angrignon's Summary

Troy Angrignon is a socially and environmentally aware entrepreneur, advisor, and consultant to emerging technology companies in both the cloud computing and cleantech sectors. His functional strengths are in business planning, sales, and strategic marketing (joint ventures, partnering, channel development).

His writings, partner deals and ecosystem map work done in concert with BC Innovation Council, RightScale and Appirio have garnered press in Backbone Magazine, Sandhill, eWeek, Information Week, Info World, VentureBeat, ReadWriteWeb, Deal Architect, and many other technology industry web-sites.

In 2009, he was guest co-chair of the “Under the Radar: Clarity in the Cloud” conference where he helped vet over 350 companies to find the top 24 new companies in the cloud computing space.

Prior to consulting in the cloud computing space, Troy was Part-owner, President, and VP Sales & Marketing for a multi-million dollar startup that specialized in building private label social networks for startups, media companies, and Fortune 1000 enterprises.

Before that, he was the Emerging Technology Strategist for Business Objects, where he did market analysis for the company's first Saas offering as well as wrote the company's draft web 2.0 / enterprise 2.0 strategy. Before that, he was an Enterprise Sales Account Manager who achieved greater than 300% of quota.

Prior to joining Business Objects, he owned a management consulting / system integration firm for ten years.

Specialties

Functions: Business Planning, Marketing, Sales, Sales Management, Partnering, Integrated Marketing & Sales.
Domains: Sustainability, cloud computing (all layers), Saas, Enterprise Software, IT
Currently studying energy (traditional and clean), carbon markets, and efficiency related industries.
