Stuart Auger’s Summary

A highly capable and motivated Senior Facilities Manager, with blue-chip corporate experience ranging from business critical office environments, technical warehouse management as well as financial services and retail, with previous experience in the hospitality and catering industry.

Strong customer-facing experience especially with regards to issue management and resolution with confident inter-personal skills. A competent knowledge of budget preparation, control, analysis and presentation.

Experienced with subcontractor and performance management, as well as auditing and report preparation for various styles of projects and initiatives.

My current role within Carillion on the Centrica contract has further allowed me expand my knowledge of contract management within the Facilities Management field, working on a managing agent contract where I have operational responsibility for a range of buildings from Midlands to West and Wales. Whilst being a regional role I also have national responsibilities for various sub-contractors and contract initiatives.

Previously held positions on their HSBC and HFC contracts.
