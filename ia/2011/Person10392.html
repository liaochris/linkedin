Darlene Quinn's Summary

By Darlene Quinn:

Darlene's first novel in the Webs Series "Webs of Power" won The National Best Book 2009 Finalist Award in Fiction & Literature: General Fiction

Her next book, Twisted Webs, is provocative &inspiring & already garnering rave reviews.

Excerpt:Twisted Webs:

A baby girl is stolen, and tightly woven webs of deception encase her and two families for 8 traumatic years

The stunningly beautiful and elegant Ashleigh Taylor gives birth to twin daughters while she’s in Long Beach visiting her father figure, Charles Stuart, the founder of Bentley's Royale. Hours later, a parent’s worst nightmare occurs: Cassie is kidnapped. Twisted Webs unravels the mystery of the abduction of Callie’s identical twin, and why. With the same drama, tension, and high style that hooked readers of Webs of Power, the author invites loyal fans and readers new to the series to step into this provocative sequel set in the 1990s. There is plenty going on to keep them turning pages all the way to the end.

Darlene Quinn probes with candor and sensitivity the shock and despair, the anger and frustration of parents suddenly missing a child and the long-term effects on a family wrestling with these emotions. Each of the novel’s complementary storylines ties to the redemptive theme of motherhood.

Readers crisscross the USA, from LA to NY, as vivid scenes of luxury, envy, retail empire power plays, backstabbing, lust, haute couture, and manipulation play out among the supporting cast of spirited, talented, and oftentimes snarky personalities—some of whom are familiar, others new.

Suspense electrifies the pages of each short, fast-paced chapter—at the close of which Quinn has crafted a knockout ending.

Darlene's newest book Webs of Fate, prequel to Webs of Power will be available 10/1/11.
