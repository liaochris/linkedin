Sabahat Azim's Summary

A trained Medical Doctor and IAS Officer, earlier Secretary to the Hon'ble Chief Minister of Tripura. Have also been a Director of Information Technology, Director Vigilance, Jt. Secretary, Personnel & Training, Jt Secretary Cabinet & Confidential, District Magistrate, Sub Divisional Magistrate, Land Acquisition Collector, Camp Director for Shikaribari Rehabilitation Camp for Surrendered militants. With IAS training from LBS National Academy of Administration, Microfinance & SHG training from NABARD and Infrastructure & Financing from IIM Ahmedabad, I founded SREI Sahaj E-Village Ltd, a pan India rural ICT Venture for the SREI Group. Currently I have set up a new Venture in Rural HEalthcare called Glocal Healthcare Systems Private Limited.

Specialties

Business Strategy, Creating new businesses & making them sustainable, Building Organizations, Entrepreneurship, Development, Information Technology, Education, Rural Development, Healthcare

Sabahat Azim's Experience

Chief Executive Officer & Founder

Glocal Healthcare Systems Private Limited

Hospital & Health Care industry

July 2010 – Present (9 months)

An ambitious venture to bring State of Art Healthcare to rural population in India through an integrated model of Block Level Comprehensive Primary & Secondary Care Hospitals, Health Insurance, Skill Development and Technology. Glocal aims to create one of the largest rural healthcare delivery systems in the world. Glocal is right now concentrating on creating the backbone that will enable a TPS based large scale intervention in Health. the construction of our prototype hospitals is slated to begin by October 2010. When the model is proved on the ground Glocal intends to start full scale rollout.
