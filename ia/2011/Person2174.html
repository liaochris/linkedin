Amy Becker’s Summary

Passionate about education and business, I have combined the two to perform as a Business Start-Up Consultant.

Using my 18 years as a Corporate and Securities paralegal and learning about proper business formation through being a business owner, I am able to help companies to start up the RIGHT way the first time.

While still involved as a Marketing Manager for Club Z! In Home Tutoring, I have been helping non profits and for-profit companies with their start-up endeavors on a volunteer basis for the past 5 years. It has now grown into a full time Consultant Business that I am so happy to see take off!

My goals are to continue to see Club Z! grow as it has been for 7 years and to be able to assist small business owners to realize their dreams as I have been able to realize mine.
