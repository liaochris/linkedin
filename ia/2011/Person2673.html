Andrew Scharf [LION]’s Summary

As the Head of the WCW Group, I am passionate about online news media and the positive impact that we can make on our international readership concerned with Global Affairs, Business, Economy, Career Management, Fashion, Culture & Lifestyle.

As the steward of this enterprise, I relish the opportunities that our challenging times represent and the constant need to channel reporting to the heart of the key issues at stake.

Helping people find a real purpose in life so that they can make a real impact lies at the heart of all the activities of the WCW Group. This ethos percolates through the strategic corporate management. The group's innovative consulting practices focuses on career management and professional development.

If you are considering an MBA or EMBA find out how we can help.

You will find the Group's online media activities such as our blog, "WCW INSIGHT" tailored to your professional & leisure interests. Our focus is leadership, while fostering human talent and aspirations through career management and enhancement.

All WCW media channels feature high quality content and expertise across pertinent topics, business issues, and diverse cultures.

We offer you a platform to share yours on how we as concerned business leaders can contribute to build innovative solutions to the problems we face as a global community of responsible professionals. I invite you to join us, to contribute, and to sound off.

There are few obstacles we cannot overcome when fostered by the willingness, integrity, and dignity to work together as catalysts of positive and productive change with respect.

For further information, I invite you to consult our websites or email me directly.
