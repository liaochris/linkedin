Dave McClain’s Summary

Senior-level Interactive Creative Director with extensive background establishing overarching creative vision and direction on numerous accounts and projects. Comprehensive experience in working with clients, leading internal teams, and delivering award-winning creative solutions.

Substantial experience building client relationships and growing accounts, including helping acquire agency of record contracts from multiple clients. Strong understanding of industry innovations, trends, and history. Highly proficient in leading brainstorming sessions and presentations of concepts, strategy, and design.

Directed/managed a team of up to 40 creative professionals. Considerable history in providing mentoring and leadership to team members. Experienced serving in a Director-level leadership role within multi-agency blended creative teams, and partnering with client-side in-house creative teams.

Directed/managed numerous projects simultaneously, with specific responsibilities including providing creative direction, resource allocation, developing scope/statements of work, internal and external presentation of creative strategies and deliverables, team mentoring, and ensuring quality control of the creative product.

Partial client list: Nike, FX Networks, IZZE, Adobe, ESPN, Intel, Activision, Microsoft, Fox, Nissan, Infiniti, West Coast Choppers, Lexus, Toyota, Mistubishi, Sea Island Resorts, Hard Rock Hotel and Casino, Outrigger Hotels, Yonex, Disney.
