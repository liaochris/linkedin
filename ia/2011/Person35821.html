Rajarshi Ray's Summary

As a seasoned general manager & consultant with experience of over 10 years, I have always been at the forefront of value creation for the customer and my company. I have consistently been able to articulate strategic direction & vision and develop & execute plans aligned with desired business outcomes. I have extensive Customer Relationship Management experience and across the following domains: Hospitality, Finance, Manufacturing, Healthcare, Retail and Telecommunications.

I am currently with RAMPgreen Technologies, an exciting IT products & services startup specializing in the customer services domain, where I have Profit & Loss ownership responsibility for its Professional Services business unit with specific focus on pre-sales & sales support, alliances & business development, solution implementation & delivery, product deployment, end-user adoption, application support & maintenance and overall client relationship & program management.

Prior to this, I was at Tata Consultancy Services Limited, Asia’s largest software services provider, where I led global cross-functional teams across multiple organizations to design & execute 12 engagements for 8 clients with 7 of them being in the Fortune 200, spending 3 years across various client locations in the North America geography. In this tenure, I was also responsible for business development & key account relationship management.

I completed my MBA in both Strategy & Leadership as well as Entrepreneurship from the Indian School of Business, a globally ranked FT 15 school. While at the ISB, I was also the president of the Net Impact chapter, which was also Asia's first chapter, whose status my team and I were able to convert from bronze to gold in under a year’s time (gold being achieved by only 7% graduate schools worldwide).

My range of leadership experience spans across diverse functions such as Business Development, Operations Management, Team Mentoring, Client Relationship Management and P&L Management.
