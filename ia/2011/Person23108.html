Justin Bedecarré's Summary

Justin Bedecarré is a commercial real estate adviser at Cushman & Wakefield, a leading global private commercial real estate firm. Justin works on the top producing office leasing team in Northern California for Cushman & Wakefield.

Justin represents technology and media based companies at every stage, from startup to fortune. He disrupts the industry with social media, technology and passion for innovation.

A sample of his clients includes large corporations like Broadcom, Qualcomm, Thomson Reuters and Hearst Corporation; Mid-market and emerging leaders like AKQA and Rhapsody; Promising startups like Kaboodle, SharesPost, AppBistro, 1Up, Thumbtack, Luxe Commerce (LilLuxe), Internet Entertainment (eBaum's World), EditRing, DevonWay, BleacherReport and Greentech Media.

A sample of his professional service clients includes Bank of America, Wells Fargo, O'Melveny & Myers, Marsh & McLennan, Delta Dental, Williams Sonoma, and Allen Matkins.

Justin is the co-founder of the Bay Area Commercial Real Estate Blog (bayareacomre.com), a blog that challenges the status quo and intersects technology and commercial real estate. He is also a contributor on VentureBeat, Silicon Valley Watcher and SF New Tech.

Justin was awarded "Rookie of the Year" for Cushman & Wakefield in Northern California. He is an active participant of CoreNet Global and established the C&W CFO Roundtable in San Francisco (www.cwcforoundtable.com).

While studying political science and economics at the University of California, Berkeley, Justin worked at the Home Builders Association of Northern California (trade association promoting home building in the Bay Area) and USA Marketing (promotional marketing firm). Justin was an active member of Beta Theta Pi fraternity and a mentor at Washington Elementary School. He currently sits on the alumni board of Beta. Go Bears!
