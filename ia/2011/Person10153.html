Daniel Rucci's Summary

Daniel Rucci is a cross-trained media producer and project manager, director and editor with ten years of wide-ranging professional experience. He manages video, photography and audio productions from idea to screen, produces in partnership with interactive teams, and has a track record of leading technicians, artists, vendors and clients to on-time, on-budget results on highly complex projects.

His clients include numerous ad agencies and production companies, for clients across the nation: Kraft, California Pizza Kitchen, JCPenney, McDonalds, Eli Lilly, MillerCoors, Whirlpool, BP, United Stationers, Wrigley, CBS, The Big Ten Network, Northwestern University, Broan/NuTone, Chicago Faucets and many more.

Genres Daniel has expertise in include: branded content, interactive advertising microsites, OLA/display ads, regional and national broadcast and preroll commercials, radio commercials, augmented reality, mobile, industrial and corporate communications, retail/kiosk displays, documentaries, training, live events, episodic television, educational and radio.

Verticals: durable goods, food, beverages, healthcare, non-profit/social issues, political, manufacturing & distribution, real estate, travel, independent film, comedy, etc.

Daniel is a hybrid project manager, producer, creative and technician. He has consulted, produced, budgeted, managed, directed, shot and edited video, audio, presentations and graphics/animation. He is deeply familiar with web technologies (HTML, Flash, Javascript, AJAX) and iOS application development.

He is a hands-on, tech-savvy and creative specialist who forges new solutions to technical problems, creates technical workflows with the vision of a director and budget-conscious producer. He has deep experience in:
• video & film and photography
• web technologies
