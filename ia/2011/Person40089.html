Shay McKelvey, RN, MS's Summary

I love everything about health & fitness. I've been a nurse and exercise physiologist forever. I'm founder of BFitSanDiego, the go-to place for fun in fitness.

In my spare time, I provide personal training & health and nutrition counseling to optimize health, function, strength, posture, balance, body weight and fitness level. My focus is on the lifestyle choices necessary for vibrant living

Specialties

Weight loss, nutrition and wellness, strength training, functional fitness, TRX, Kettelbells, P90X, Postural training....
