Steve Schulin's Summary

I read a lot, and write a little. My longstanding interest in communicating information prompted me to start up a dial-in customer service electronic BBS years before the internet had expanded beyond the .mil, .edu, and .net domains. When email was opened up to allow .com domains, I jumped right in and requested nuclear.com. It's been a real pleasure and honor to have the industry's premier domain name, with millions of eyeballs a year seeing my take on the issues. I'm sort of amazed that none of the huge companies which aspire to be the world's premier nuclear company has yet to make a serious offer on the premier domain name.

Specialties

Power reactor radiation protection, performance-based training program design and development, risk communication, public relations, spent fuel cask licensing, industry database use consultant, listener, researcher, writer, speaker
