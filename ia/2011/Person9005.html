John Cody Walton's Summary

Interactive designer with 11 years of industry experience designing and producing multimedia presentations for web, CD-ROM, and kiosk. I've worked on clients such as Diet Coke, Pizza Hut, Omni Hotels, Norwegian Cruise Line, GlaxoSmithKline, The Art Institutes, Pfizer, Unisys, Compaq/HP, Shell Oil, Baker-Hughes, BP, and more.

Specialties

User interface design and functionality for websites, CD-ROMs, and kiosks. Conceptual GUI design, pre-HTML production and cuts, implementation guides, Flash design and action-scripting, logo design, digital video editing, DVD authoring
