Martha Newman, J.D. Coach's Summary

Martha M. Newman is an Executive and Professional Coach specializing in goal-setting and action-planning on a wide array of business and personal issues that transform her clients’ careers and bring success to their lives. Having received her certification in coaching from the prestigious Graduate School of Management at UT Dallas, Martha’s coaching and workshops are outcome-oriented with a focus on positive change that produces tangible results for her clients. She was formerly a business attorney, broadcast journalist and Fort Worth Zoning Commission Vice-Chair. Her corporate work experience is in the fields of sales training, technical writing, public relations, and advertising.

Specialties

Leadership training, team building, communication skills, marketing, interpersonal skills, business development, networking, career transitions.
