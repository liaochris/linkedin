Ryan Dunning's Summary

Since 1994, I have consulted clients in commercial real estate and industry on environmental liability management, real estate due diligence, brownfield redevelopment, site investigation, and remediation. During this time I have gained some trusted clients that look to my firm for senior leadership, integrated services, and personal business relationships.

In addition to environmental consulting, my firm provides full service civil engineering and sustainable site development/redevelopment services, renewable and alternative energy site selection services, survey, wetlands/ecological consulting, waste to energy engineering, and solid waste management permitting and design.

My ultimate goal is to become your #1 trusted partner to turn to whenever you need civil or environmental consulting.

Specialties

environmental real estate due diligence, environmental liability management, site selection, brownfield consulting, site investigation, site closure, remediation, sustainability, alternative and renewable energy.
