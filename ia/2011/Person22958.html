Julia Roy's Summary

I have been working in New Media for 5 years. During this time I have created, managed and launched social marketing campaigns, as well as build a brand of my own that continues to foster and grow a loyal community of friends, followers and fans across different web platforms.

I'm a thinker, communicator and educator of digitally smart strategies. I advise corporations, brands, individuals and start-ups on how to tap into the new media market and establish community online. I enjoy observing how individuals interact with each other, in both niche and major online social platforms, and thinking about unique ways to facilitate mutually-beneficial relationships that go far beyond marketing.

My goal is to continue to connect brands and consumers through new media and experiment in the intersection of digital and social.

Specialties
