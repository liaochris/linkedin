John Charles Roe’s Summary

John Charles Roe, CPA is the principal of Roe CPA, P.C., Certified Public Accountant. The firm, which was founded in November 2002, provides complete tax services as well as accounting, bookkeeping and payroll processing and compliance services. These services are provided to a wide variety of businesses in the Atlanta Metropolitan Area.
With over 20 years of experience, Mr. Roe has built a reputation as an expert in tax and business consulting for self-employed business owners of closely held corporations.

His experience includes working as a corporate controller for several companies and as an audit manager for one of Atlanta's Top 25 Public Accounting Firms.

Mr. Roe graduated from Georgia Southern College with a Bachelor of Business Administration.He is a Certified Public Accountant in the State of Georgia.

His mission is to bring together the excellent service concepts he learned from his years working for the Ritz-Carlton Hotel and the high quality of accounting experience to one firm that is able to meet and exceed client expectations.
