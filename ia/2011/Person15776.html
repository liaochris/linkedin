Geoffrey Stern’s Summary

Inventor and creator of meaningful new ways for people and brands to exchange gifts, memories and content using audio and visual recording and playback technologies. Has developed audio chip enhancements for the toy (Build-A-Bear Workshop), imaging (Snapfish, dotphoto ..) gifting (FTD, Cheryl's), medical education (Advanstar) as well as jewelry and special education spaces.
