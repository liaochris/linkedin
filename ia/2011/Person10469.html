David Smith's Summary

David is Chief executive of GFF and an international conference speaker.

GFF has developed a reputation for being a valuable asset to government, business and the media on issues impacting our society, business and government operations.

The GFF has a network of over 9,000 future thinkers from around the world each with clear views of the short, mid and long term futures in their specialised areas of expertise which the GFF consolidates and helps business to understand.

After 30 years in business in the IT services industry serving the construction and financial services markets, there followed 5 years of leading the Unisys strategic futures think tank - 'The Global Future Forum'. Today as 'Global Futures and Foresight' David helps business appreciate and respond to the challenges the future has in store for us all.

The GFF membership is free. Sign up at www.thegff.com

Specialties

Helping business and government better prepare for the future by highlighting issues likely to impact them in the near future and preparing them to respond. International conference speaker and workshop facilitator and conference chairman. Register your interest to receive regular insights at www.thegff.com
