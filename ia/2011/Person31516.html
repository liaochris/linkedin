Morgan Wheeler’s Summary

I am first & foremost a dreamer; if I cannot make an impact, then what is the point? I am fascinated by the movement of content and information in both the business and social contexts. I believe that technology exists to enable, empower and free the human mind to focus on what is really important: the life and people that surround us.

I live to craft, deliver, track, tweak, and celebrate the reasons why people pay attention to... well, anything. I believe that all ideas, new and old, left and right, traditional or radical, have their place and audience. I believe in civic engagement, social responsibility, capitalism, community, freedom, efficiency, merit, gumption, innovation, honor, respect, love ... and above all... family.

Organizational & Business Development, Brand Strategy, Marketing Tactics, Sales, Traditional & New Media Public Relations, Advertising and Media Planning, Legislative & Public Affairs, Fundraising, Media Relations, Corporate Communications, Information Management Architecture and Enterprise Content Management Strategies.
