Toni O'Berry’s Summary

Built upon a strong consumer brand foundation, professional experience includes all points of contact with a focus on developing and maintaining brand integrity throughout the creative process: cultural studies > strategic planning > brand steward > creative strategy > creative direction and design: packaging, advertising, retail/POP, online, out of home, advertising, DVD, print collateral, sales kits and broadcast.

Experienced in building and directing creative teams in small agency and large corporate environments, with staff and independent contractors. Specialized in creative strategy direction.

Brand experience includes brand strategy and development, multichannel/multi-audience literature systems, standards manuals, conceptual testing, brand monitoring, and launch/communication strategies.

Toni O'Berry’s Specialties:

Brand Work Includes: Clarisonic Skin Care System, AHNU footwear, Einstein Wireless, Fidelity Investments, Williams Sonoma, CitiBank, Levi Strauss, 3M, and WealthVest Marketing.
