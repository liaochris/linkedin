Ralf Fletcher's Summary

Ralf, founder and MD of Digital United, has over 12 years experience in Digital on both client and integrated agency side in London, Brighton and on international projects, with a particular specialism in the Computer Games and Entertainment sectors.

Working with clients including EA, NCsoft, Sony (SCE), Nickelodeon and others in the Viacom stable including MTV, Ralf's remit has expanded over the years from being a client-side technical team leader to agency-side Account Director, Head of Digital and Commercial Director.

He is an experienced Producer, qualified software project manager and brings an appreciation of both creative excellence married to technical execution within the crucial context of delivering highly measurable results and return on investment.

Prior to founding Digital United in August 2008, Ralf was a freelance Producer and Digital / Integrated Marketing consultant working with ambitious local entrepreneurs on leading-edge techniques including an ARG (Alternate Reality Game) for Sony Computer Entertainment and the creation of 'extended realities' to increase length and depth of interaction in the era of 'continuous partial attention.'

Ralf has over 5 years experience operating freelance network-model and 'virtual agency' based businesses and applied all his learnings and proven processes and effective delivery practices in collaboratively forming DU with the core Management Team.

Digital United is neither a loose-knit network or virtual agency. It is a very real, tangible agency, but without the failings of traditional agencies. In fact, Digital United is the *first* agency to be structured as a part Worker Cooperative, giving the team a very real say in what we do and how we do it and the performance incentive of profit share on our end of year profits.

Ralf holds a degree in Computer Science studied at both the Universities of Sussex and Brighton, combined with proactive continual professional development.
