Joelle Reeder's Summary

Joelle Reeder and Kathy Scoleri (aka The Moxie Girls) are the design duo behind popular web design firm Moxie Design Studios.

They are also authors of "The IT Girl's Guide to Blogging with Moxie" (Wiley, 2007) and have spoken at events such as SXSW Interactive.

For media inquiries, speaking engagements, or literary inquiries please contact us at inquiries@themoxiegirls.com.

Specialties

website design, blog design, content management systems, small business design, CMS, logo design, Wordpress, Expression Engine, XHTML, CSS, a damn fine martini
