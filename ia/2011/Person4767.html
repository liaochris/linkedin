Ben Dorland’s Summary

It is a great thing to love your work. Every morning I have the opportunity to wake up and look forward to helping people fulfill their dreams of homeownership. Whether I am helping my first time homebuyer clients navigate their first transaction, assisting my friends/clients with purchasing their next home, or selling my investors most recent project for profit, I see it all! I have been entrusted by my clients to help them find the best deals and get the most money out of their sale in a timely manner. Over the past couple of years I have helped many of my clients purchase homes with large amounts of equity in them. With my customized selling program I have also helped many of my sellers obtain above market offers in even the toughest of times. As a Colorado native and living in the Denver Metro area for most of my life I have a deep understanding of many neighborhoods along the front range. In the past year I have taught many other real estate agents on Denver metro price trends and how specific data helps them analyze neighborhoods. I was a contributing author to a book, The 2011 Guide to Colorado Real Estate Investing, and I am currently the president of the Denver Athletic Club Real Estate Club.

Ben Dorland’s Specialties:

Negotiations, Property Analysis, Denver Area Real Estate Trends, Mapping, GIS, Remote Sensing, Property Management, Real Estate.
