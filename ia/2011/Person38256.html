Ryan Hinchey FSA’s Summary

Consultant specializing in financial risk management with a focus on variable annuity guarantees. Advise large multi nation insurance companies in the area of variable annuity hedging, pricing, and product development.

I am interested in networking with financial advisors to better understand their viewpoint on VA’s as well as help answer questions on the complexities of these products.
