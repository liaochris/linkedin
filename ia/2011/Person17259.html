Heidi Butzine’s Summary

Heidi Butzine is the founder of Bliss Discovery Group, Inc. Bliss Discovery was founded upon Heidi's desire to share with others her love of wine, food and travel. Bliss Discovery's first venture is Wineopolis - a unique combination of paperback travel guide, wine tasting companion and trip log, blended with an online community.

Leveraging Heidi's experiences travelling to wine regions across the globe and gathering first-hand knowledge about regional wine and food, Heidi has authored the Wineopolis Citizen's Guide to Wine Country Series.

Heidi's journey continues to build upon her experiences. She has studied food and wine pairing with local experts throughout France and completed wine study courses at the Culinary Institute of America in the heart of Napa Valley.

Prior to founding Bliss Discovery Group, Heidi led a successful 10-year career as a founding partner of a consulting business that achieved multi-million dollar revenue and was ultimately acquired by a Fortune 300 company. With her experience as a C-level executive and successfully taking business concepts from idea to execution, Heidi has applied her talents to her recent ventures.

As an entrepreneur, Heidi continues to call upon her experience in modern management techniques to create highly adaptable business systems, process and infrastructure to support an organization at all levels from start-up through profitability. By fostering an organizational culture that rewards creativity, Heidi has effectively led teams through significant changes (growth, acquisition, economic) which resulted in enhanced client service, talent growth opportunities and improved financial performance.

Driven by her desire to help others learn more about discovering food, wine and travel. Today, Heidi's mission is to create tools that help others learn while making it fun, easy-going and attainable.
