Matthew Bennett's Summary

I had my first encounter with Search Engine Optimisation (SEO) in 2000 when I spent the summer working as a keyword researcher for one of the first UK SEO providers. Following this I spent several years as a musician before returning to SEO in 2005. Since then I've been lucky enough to work with some great SEOs and On-line Marketers.

I am currently working at MEC Interaction (a GroupM/WPP company) as an SEO Account Director, heading up the Lloyds Banking Group account. I'm also involved with training and developing the SEO team and our abilities.

In the future I plan to keep learning, progressing and raising my industry profile as well as the profile of "respectable" SEO in general.

Specialties

Search Engine Optimisation, Social Media Optimisation, Social Media Marketing, Market Research, Usability & Accessibility, Conversion Optimisation, Copy-writing & Content Creation, Link Building, Wordpress Development & Optimisation
