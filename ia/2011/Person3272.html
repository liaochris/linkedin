Anthony Alfidi’s Summary

I invest primarily in the public equity markets, although I have made investments in startups both via cash infusion and sweat equity. I perform quantitative analysis of publicly held companies using deep value criteria along with qualitative analysis of company strategy. I make my analytical products available for free as a public service.

I also occasionally consult with seed-round startups to assist them with strategy development and social media campaigns. I will only perform this role as an investor in companies I have selected to be part of my portfolio. Entrepreneurs in logistics, defense/aerospace technology, renewable energy, and natural resources are welcome to send me their business plans.

Anthony Alfidi’s Specialties:

Investment analysis, due diligence, decision modeling, operations analysis, strategy development, knowledge management, writing for publication, public presentation and speaking.
