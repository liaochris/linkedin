Social Media Education:

I am the co-author of the book: LinkedWorking and author of LinkedIn Master Strategies. These two resources give you everything you need to maximize LinkedIn for your professional career or business.

I speak at conferences on the topic of utilizing LinkedIn for your business, and host regular webinars on LinkedIn, as well as other social media for business topics.

If you would like to learn more about either book, speaking engagements, or webinars please go to www.lewishowes.com to learn more.

Sports Business:

I founded SportsNetworker.com because I saw a hole in the sports industry. There is an old way of thinking when it comes to the sports world, and many teams, leagues, and organizations are still unwilling to dive into online marketing and social media as a way to interact with customers and fans. SportsNetworker.com is a resource that covers the best tips and practices on social media in sports. We also host regular sports networking events around the world to help connect more people in the industry.

The Sports Executives Association (SEA) is a membership site that connects the tops sports professionals together, and creates ongoing education and training to help better understand the best way to increase business for your sports organization (among other things). To learn more about becoming a member, go to www.SportsExec.net

Magnetic Webinars, Shy Networking, and More:

If you want to use webinars in your business to generate more leads, sales, and income then head over to www.magneticwebinars.com for our free report.

If you find it hard to connect with new people and leverage others networks to receive more referrals and opportunities, then you will want to go to www.ShyNetworking.com to learn more about our networking course.

For more products, resources, articles, videos and tips go to www.lewishowes.com
