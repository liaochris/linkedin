Renewables & Environment industry

Saving Energy Makes Cents!

VIS VIVA Energy and Services Inc. (www.visvivaenergy.com) offers energy saving products and services to the Metro Atlanta region. We deliver value to our client's homes, offices or buildings by reducing their energy demand and improving the health and comfort of their space.

We are certified under the leading regional and national energy saving programs such as HERS, BPI, ENERGY STAR, LEED and EarthCraft House. We are also a Home Performance with ENERGY STAR Certified Contractor and a preferred contractor of the ARRA funded Weatherization initiative in Fulton and Clayton County, GA.

We are a complete turnkey operation offering a full line of in-house services including Insulation, Air-Sealing, Heating, Ventilation and Air Conditioning (HVAC), Geo-Thermal Systems, Air-Sealing, Duct-Sealing, Windows & Doors and Health & Safety Testing (CO, Radon, Natural Gas, Lead, Mold).

We are a complete consulting and turnkey contracting firm meaning we do not sub-contract out any of our work. This fact assures our customers the highest level of quality possible.
