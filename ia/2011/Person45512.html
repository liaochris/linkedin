Interested in senior management team position or management succession scenario with another business trade association, financial services organization, or transferring my extensive experience, numerous and polished professional skill sets, adaptability, and business acumen to other corporate environments, public or private, profit or non-profit, state/local government administration or university/educational setting where I could lead/manage/mentor/coach.
