Andrew Wagner’s Summary

"Gearhead" I'm interested in all things mechanical.
I'm a very hands on individual and love working on engines, vehicles, and machines both professionally and in my personal time.

As a mechanical engineering professional, I have spent the majority of my career in the field of internal combustion engines. For the past nine years, I had the opportunity to work in a world-class product development facility and specialized in powertrain development. While working in engine development, I have built a strong background in combustion gas airflow and in performance development.
I've had the opportunity to manage multiple development projects for OE engineering, parts & accessories, and racing programs. While managing development projects, I've spent time planning and directing work in test labs, collecting and analyzing high speed combustion data, creating base engine calibrations, and following up with Homologation and COP testing.

On my personal projects, I've enjoyed repairing and building automobiles, snowmobiles, motorcycles, and even agricultural equipment. I grew up on a family farm where fixing and maintaining equipment was a daily routine. As part of my collegiate design project I was able to design and build an SAE mini-baja racecar, where I was responsible for the driveline design. In the last several years, I've been a crew chief for a sprint car competing in the Interstate Racing Association and enjoying the thrill of competition.

IC Engine Development, Cylinder Head Development, Combustion Gas Airflow Testing & Development, Dynamometer Testing, Base Engine Calibration, Valvetrain Dynamics, Combustion Analysis, High Speed Data Acquisition, Homologation & COP Testing, Emissions Collection & Sampling, AVL (Digalog, Indicom, Concerto), ATI Vision, Pro-Engineer Wildfire 4.0, Solidworks 2009.
Trouble Shooting & Problem Solving, Product Development, Project Management, Continuous Improvement
