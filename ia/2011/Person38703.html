Sandra Slavec's Summary

My business is to promote and advertise products, services and events using the power of words to bring attention to your business. Crafting copy with the right words to create interest and desire for your products or services - which leads to increased awareness and sales for your business. Proof is in the copy!

I am especially interested in forming alliances with graphic designers, web designers & developers or anyone that needs the services of a writer. Would really love the opportunity for more PR/Media Release writing.

I mainly write website content for small to medium businesses. Would very much like to broaden my experience and networks with people who have a genuine need for copywriting.

Specialties

Website content writing, articles, brochures, newsletters, press releases and email marketing campaigns.
I can write about a broad range of industries & businesses, particularly for retailers, communications providers, service providers, health & wellbeing to name just a few.
