Kevin Davis’s Summary

I am the president of TopLine Leadership, Inc., a sales and sales management training company serving clients from diverse sectors. I have 30+ years of experience as a salesperson, sales manager, sales trainer, and consultant.

My company offers a custom in-house or public seminar for corporate sales managers. Control your time/priorities, improve sales performance coaching, install greater sales team accountability with "The Sales Management Leadership" program.

We also provide custom sales training based on the "Slow Down, Sell Faster!" sales model. How to match the pace of your selling to the pace of your customer's buying process - and gain advantage over your competitors at each step.

My 2nd sales book, "Slow Down, Sell Faster! Understand Your Customer's Buying Process and Maximize Your Sales" (Amacom, Jan. 2011) is an easy-to-read practical guide featuring a counterintuitive sales model. The book shows salespeople how to help customers to buy faster by slowing down each sales conversation.

I currently reside in the Reno-Tahoe area.
