Haydar Can Taygun’s Summary

Haydar Can Taygun is a senior at Brown University, originally from Istanbul, Turkey, majoring in Political Science and Economics. Studied abroad at University College London in Spring ’10, he is now working on his senior honors thesis about the role of energy industry in the Turkey-EU accession negotiations.
