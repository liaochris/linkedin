Dave Hatton’s Summary

I am a Senior Search Executive at Adconnection in London, after previously working for Focus Integrated in Essex as an Account Executive.

I was also fortunate enough to work with the video games publisher and developer giants; SEGA, as part of a 4 month consultancy project for my masters dissertation which researched 18-25 gamers and their attitudes towards subscription, non-subscription, MMO games and gaming in general - all part of research for SEGA's 'Football Manager' brand. It was a valuable experience to me and one I appreciate fully. For this work, I was awarded a Distinction (1st) from my University.

I am a reliable, confident, enthusiastic person who is able to express ideas. I work well independently and in a team; as a leader or a member. I can meet deadlines and handle important information, working extremely well under pressure to reach required targets. I am an organised, versatile, tidy person with a keen sense of humour and a passion for technology, gaming and sport. I am confident that I can work with a wide range of personalities and individuals as I have a wide range of interests, knowledge and excellent social skills to enable good rapport. I see myself balanced creatively and academically, enjoying creative and challenging tasks thoroughly, being able to multi-task when needed and learning new traits quickly. I am a positive thinker and have a good general knowledge, with a strong interest into Digital/Online Marketing; linking my passion for technology and my academic strengths together.
