Jeanne Baer’s Summary

Founded Creative Training Solutions in 1990, with the goal of developing workplace performance skills, especially in terms of interpersonal skills.
Currently work throughout the U.S. and Europe, helping people learn to solve problems and build stronger relationships.
Have contributed chapters to 18 books published by McGraw-Hill, Harvard Business School Press, Pfeiffer International, and others. Jeanne's book, "You Can't Do It All: Delegation Skills for Supervisors," was published in 1999 by American Media, Inc.
Have had more than 180 "Managing Smart" articles published by Strictly Business magazine and other periodicals.
Founding host for "TrainingNow," a weekly TV magazine produced by ASTD-Lincoln.

Jeanne Baer’s Specialties:

Do consulting, writing, training, and speaking on topics of selling, customer service, personal effectiveness, presentation and training skills, and teamwork and team building.
