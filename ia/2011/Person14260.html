Denise Pietzsch - Director, EPIC, LLC's Summary

The "e.p.i.c." mission is to bring confidence, civility, refinement, and success to this global generation through etiquette education.

Denise presents dynamic speaking engagements, seminars, workshops and consultations for audiences across cultures and generations. She has a passion for helping collegiates, new hires, business professionals and organizations. A key segment of e.p.i.c. is dedicated to helping internationals integrate into the American culture.

WHY: Social, professional and personal manners give you the leading edge.

GOALS: Bring back basic manners and civility. Build success for individuals and businesses through their interpersonal skills.

Help others be the best they can be in any situation.

Teach the winning edge that etiquette education provides.


BENEFIT: Success and Civility

TRAINING: BA in Social and Behavioral Sciences from The Ohio State University Certification through The Protocol School of `Washington, the only accredited school of its kind. The school known world-wide for being the "experts who train the experts".

HISTORY: The concept of becoming an etiquette expert began for Denise more than 10 years ago while working at a private middle school. After much research and continued education, she was able to become certified in the etiquette education art and thus begin the business that she loves.

International protocol education has become an essential skill in our global economy. Denise teaches American dining skills to the International community in Central Ohio and beyond. She writes for the Ohio Chinese Newspaper, teaches weekly for adults learning English as a Second Language. She continues to learn about other cultures on a daily basis.

Specialties

INTERNATIONALS: Integrations into the American culture culture

COLLEGIATE and entry level corporate education

CORPORATE environment and experience enhancement

I have found my passion and absolutely love my job! I am a Certified Corporate Etiquette and International Protocol Consultant. I love conducting my workshops, seminars and speaking engagements.
