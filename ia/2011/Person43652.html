Todd Appleman's Summary

The Appleman Group is a virtual public relations consulting firm dedicated to delivering exemplary results to outstanding clients. Launched 10 years ago on January 1, 2000, we celebrated our 10th anniversary on January 1, 2010!

We also launched a new venture, Big Blue Moon Entertainment, on January 1, 2010. Already, with two new clients, Big Blue Moon, will focus on serving "once in a blue moon" talent -- which is to say, uniquely inspired artists, songwriters, musicians, directors, film makers, choreographers, and others that only come along “once in a blue moon,” and who contribute positively to the fabric of today’s culture.

The Appleman Group will continue to serve clients in the health care industry, corporate clients, professional services firms, e-commerce companies, consumer products companies, among others, and pro bono work for causes in keeping with our culture.

Our clients range in size from start-ups to international medical device and pharmaceutical companies, as well as national consumer brands, professional services firms, and other client offering unique products and solutions -- b2b and b2c.

As one of the leading virtual agencies in the country, The Appleman Group is recognized for having the capabilities of a national public relations delivered with the personal service and cost-effectiveness that you would expect from a boutique firm. For clients, this means our clients only pay for the assistance they need, and when they need it.

According to PR Week Magazine, "It's easy to see the populist appeal of The Appleman Group's offering in these belt-tightening times. Besides, why should a client fork over big money to pay for an agency's plush suites with one hand, while slashing its own payroll with the other?"

In the spirit of striving to deliver more than our clients expect, we have a passion for what we do. We work hard. We have fun. We are dynamic. We think strategically. We are creative. And we deliver results!

Specialties

Startegic Communications, Corporate and Brand Positioning, Product Launches, Special Events, Technical and Feature Writing, Video News Releases, Audio News Releases, Pod Casts, Webinars, Issues Management, Crisis Communications, Organizational Communications, and Online Communications, including viral new media/social media strategies in cooperation with some of the best minds in the business.
