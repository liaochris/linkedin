David Morgan’s Summary

Accomplished finance executive with significant senior leadership experience in multiple high-tech industries, including 11 years as the CFO with four different companies: (1) a $170 million (sales) publicly-held, global information technology services company, (2) a $100 million subsidiary of a $20 billion Fortune 50 global telecommunications company, (3) a venture capital-financed “start-up” software company and (4) a development-stage, publicly-held, cleantech/chemicals company. Excellent oral and written communication and presentation skills.
