Mike Mueller’s Summary

Greetings and thank you for visiting my site on Linked in. I am a veteran mudlogger in the oil and gas industry since the year 2000. I have logged wells in California, Nevada, New Mexico, Colorado, Texas, Louisiana, and GOM. From the Siliceous and Dolomitic Shales and Limestones and sandstone turbidite reservoirs of the Monterey Group, to the shallow marine shelf carbonates of the Permian Basin, and to the marine transgressions and regressions recorded in the Mesozoic and Cenozoic sands, silt, and shale of East Texas and South Texas I've loggged it, bagged it, and delivered it to the clients porch. I have worked geothermal in the Shoeshone Range in Nevada, I've logged Rhyodacitic Ignimbrite core at the US Nuclear Test Site near Yucca Mountain. I know a thing or two about rocks and running a logging unit. Please click my resume if if you are looking for a shale shaking geologist.

Bookmark www.needmudlogger.com and contact me whenever you are ready.

Note: I am currently employed as a Mudlogger for Marcellus Shale work.
