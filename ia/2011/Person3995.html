Allen Smith’s Summary

Goal: To network with others within the trucking industry in order to provide knowledge, products, and services of value to the professional truck driver.

I've been involved in many facets of the trucking industry for over 30 years now, including: driver, owner operator, operations manager, and local business owner.

In the year 2000 my frustration for the way the trucking industry was conducting itself led me to write, "The Truth About Trucking" which discusses the recruiting tactics, high turnover rate, low driver wages, poor CDL training, and other aspects new drivers need to be aware of.

As time allows, I submit articles for many of the article directories, including IdeaMarketers where I'm categorized as the expert for truck driver training.

My intention is to to educate students and new drivers, revealing the ways of the industry and to direct them in the right direction in order to avoid the pitfalls that I had seen so many succumb to during my 33 years within trucking.

AskTheTrucker.com blog was later introduced to share more knowledge and insight with others, including all those interested and involved in trucking. The blog is designed to allow and share thoughts and ideas as current concerns and issues of the industry are posted regularly. The blog is opinionated, but open for debate in order to open ones mind and discuss things in an intelligent and civilized manner.

In 2008 we began the Blog Talk Radio show Truth About Trucking "Live" where we discuss many of the most controversial and current topics and have included many well known expert guests as a part of the conversations and dialogue. The global audience is over 100,000.

In 2009 we introduced the first application for the trucking industry, the Apple iPhone app "TRUCKER" Drivers have frequently updated information via Trucker and it is a means for accessing many needed daily resources

Allen Smith’s Specialties:

Specialize in trucking currents events and research. Guide new drivers and students in the right direction. Discuss the in and outs of the trucking industry, including the most current issues and regulations. Network with like minded people who desire to be a part of a team where integrity is the number one priority. By doing so we can add value and respect to one another and " Raise the Standards of the Trucking Industry"
