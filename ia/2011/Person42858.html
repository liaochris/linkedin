Thad Bentley’s Summary

Thad is an IT Services executive with extensive and successful operational and client facing experience. He has managed end-to-end delivery from laptop to data center, supporting an annual TCV of $82M and a workforce of 250 employees. Highly effective leader in both internal and external cross -organizational programs. Strong understanding of IT, IT services, and ITIL best practices.

Thad began his career with HP in October 1992 just after graduation from college. He started with HP as a Customer Engineer in Columbia, South Carolina. In June 1999 Thad accepted his first management role as an Enterprise District Manager for Memphis, Nashville and Huntsville. In 2004 Thad was promoted to lead the Mission Critical business in Tennessee, Kentucky and Indiana. In early 2007, Thad accepted a Director’s role in Technology Services. In this position Thad leads the Technology Services Delivery Organization with a team of ~250 people covering six states in the Southeast.

Throughout his leadership career, Thad has focused on people development, results based leadership and operational excellence. He has a strong belief in communicating the “why” of our strategy then providing clear objectives and allowing the leadership team to use their own unique skills and talents while holding them accountable to achieve the desired results.

Outside of HP, lives in Spring Hill, Tennessee and enjoys raising horses and spending time with family and friends. Thad also has a strong interest in politics and enjoys working with local candidates on strategy and planning.
