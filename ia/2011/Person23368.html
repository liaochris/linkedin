Kara Jensen’s Summary

Bop Design's goal is to make great design accessible to small businesses. We believe that every organization, no matter how small, deserves an effective brand strategy that conveys its unique value proposition. When you work with Bop Design, you’ll experience the value and success that come from having a compelling brand identity.

Kara Jensen’s Specialties:

Bop Design makes your business look good. We design every visual aspect of your business -- your logo, website, brochure, print advertising, signage, proposals, etc. We also develop a marketing strategy to help you reach new clients. And, because having the greatest business in the world doesn’t do you any good if nobody’s ever heard of it, we boost your online visibility through SEO, SEM, email marketing and social media.
