Pete Weickert’s Summary

Pete is a real estate acquisition specialist who also provides funding for commercial properties, specializing in Bridge and Debtor in Possession financing . He is dedicated to achieving his business goals and finding great solutions for his clients.

With an educational background in both Business and Technology, his prior work experience includes 10 years in IT managing projects including medical systems deployment, new facility commissioning, and inventory system installation. He also owned and operated an international trade company.

Pete pursued specialized training in real estate investing and finance, and has actively and profitably invested for the past 21 years.

Pete has lived and traveled extensively in Europe, Africa and India.

He is a passionate sailor and skier and is always planning his next adventure or looking for the perfect property to add to his real estate portfolio.

Pete is proud to be known for his “Can do!” attitude and excellent customer service.

Current Projects:

-Providing bridge financing for commercial real estate in the US
-Providing financing for Debtor in Possession (DIP) scenarios
-Sourcing distressed commercial real estate in the US
-Flipping single family homes in the US
-Hunting for capital to expand portfolio
