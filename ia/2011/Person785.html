Andrew Thornicroft’s Summary

Aethernet Consulting was formed in 2008 by Andrew Thornicroft. Andrew is a Security Cleared (SC) engineering consultant with over 20 years of communication systems and management experience. He has worked extensively in requirements capture, system design, sales support, technical and end-user training, project management, product marketing, systems test, installation and maintenance.

Aethernet Consulting has access to a large number of very experienced SC cleared engineers and managers who are available for short and long term contract work either individually or as a team.

Please contact us with your particular requirement - if we can't help - then we probably know someone who can.
