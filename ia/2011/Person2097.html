Dr Amit Nagpal, PhD's Summary

Experience Summary-
He has been associated with Marketing, Soft skills/life skills and Media management for past 15 years. Born in Delhi, Dr Amit has worked at Mumbai/Delhi/Chennai/Dehradun/Pondicherry, and has been exposed to variety of cultures and their unique charms.
He has been associated with industries ranging from FMCG, Publishing, Television, and Training
He has worked with Cadbury India Ltd, UK, Sage Publications, USA, Sahara TV, ICFAI and SOM, Pondicherry University
He is also an inspirational speaker and has conducted workshops on Leadership, Entrepreneurship and Life Skills at Ma Foi Academy, ICFAI etc. Conducting Stress (Mock) Interviews and Business Games is another area of expertise.
He has written several articles for reputed journals & magazines and attended many national/international conferences.He is a leadership mentor for IAYSCP (International Association for Young Supply Chain Professionals), Ohio, USA.

Personal qualities-
He parcels out energy and has powerful and eloquent oration, Cross-cultural adaptability and Perceptive personality are his other core strengths.

He always tries to detect the hidden mechanisms and processes behind the phenomena he observes. He finds problems and crises especially stimulating and they bring out his personal resources best.

He is a blogger, author and guest speaker and has delivered lectures at several reputed institutions on Entrepreneurship/Leadership (through Self-Mastery) and Digital Media.

He believes, Sustainable Success = IQ + EQ + SQ +PQ
(Intelligence, Emotional, Spiritual and Physical Quotient)

He wants to help creative people create better, help managers become leaders and help students become entrepreneurs through self mastery tools
