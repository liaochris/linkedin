In 1991, Jim founded Bell Investment Advisors. Since then, his firm has enjoyed steady growth, primarily through personal and professional referrals. Jim has appeared as a guest on MSNBC Power Lunch, CNN Financial News, CNBC and has been interviewed for a variety of online and print news sources, including Money magazine, mutualfundwire.com and the San Francisco Business Times. He has also appeared on View from the Bay with his wife Bonnie, addressing financial and life planning issues for Boomers.
