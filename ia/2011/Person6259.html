Brian Kuzdas's Summary

Entrepreneur, business visionary and team builder, Brian currently owns and operates several multi-million dollar companies in the real estate industry, all of which he built from scratch. Brian is head of OFI Holdings, which owns a family of companies under one marketing arm known as National REO Relief (NRR).

NRR is a one-stop solution for banks and lending institutions to manage and liquidate their foreclosed real estate, commonly known as REOs. NRR does this with a variety of services: acquisition, auction, asset management, joint venture and/or traditional sales brokerage. NRR is in process of opening in 50 cities and 20 countries, with anticipation of becoming the largest servicer of bank REOs by 2012.

Below is a partial list of Brian's companies:

• RealEstateAuctions.com (international auction company)
• OwnACondo.com (national condo brokerage)
• BankREOs.com (real estate brokerage)
• RE Management (asset management)
• United Real Estate Holdings ( REO acquisition)

Brian began his real estate career in 1985 specializing in commercial sales. In 1990, he opened a boutique commercial office called Brookshire Investments, mainly to represent retail and multi-family projects in the Chicago metropolitan area. In 1997, Brian purchased a 30-unit apartment building to convert to condos changing the direction of his entire career. The project generated a 102% return for investors and launched the next 10 years of growth, with his team purchasing more than $350 million in Chicago real estate, converting more than 3,500 units in that 10-yr period.

In 2006, he took the sales arm, OwnACondo.com, out of the conversion company to create one of the first, "condo-only" brokerages to service the needs of the condo buyer. Today, OwnACondo.com is on path to open in 50 cities with the intention of becoming the largest condo brand nationally.
