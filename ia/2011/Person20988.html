Jimmy Lynn's Summary

JLynn Associates is a global strategic advisory firm, focused on Sports Marketing, Sports Media, Sports Retail and Teams / Athletes. Jimmy Lynn, founder and managing partner, is a veteran of more than 20 years of experience in the Sports and Entertainment sectors. Lynn is one of the pioneers in the Internet Sports space and is regarded as one of the leading relationship brokers and connectors in the sports industry.

In addition to JLynn Associates, Lynn serves as the "Visiting Professional in the Practice of Sports Industry Management" for Georgetown University's graduate Sports program.

The services of JLynn Associates are:

* Strategic Planning -- Sports-related media, marketing and retail for a diverse set of clients including teams, athletes, leagues and associations, media outlets and other businesses

* Leveraging Digital Media Knowledge and Partners -- focusing on internet and mobile properties; web design; digital marketing solutions; analytics; and mobile applications.

* Incorporate multicultural programs to reach key consumer segments -- focusing on providing multicultural marketing solutions for companies targeting the Hispanic, African-American and Asian-American markets.

* Fund Raising -- financing through strategic partners for early stage sports tech companies.

* Diversity and Inclusion -- partnering with TransAsia Lawyers to advise international businesses in Asia, particularly in the Greater China markets and Japan.

* Brokering Relationships -- leveraging one of the best rolodexes in the sports industry to advise firms on developing strategic alliances and partnerships

Specialties

Brokering relationships, Social Media, Digital Marketing, Multi-cultural Marketing, Strategic Planning and Fund-raising for the Sports industry.
