Andrew McKeon's Summary

As principal of carbonRational I am incorporating sound management theory into addressing climate change and environmental carrying capacity. To achieve the goal of a sustainable industrial economy, leaders must use a systems approach to problem solving that informs both private sector strategic planning as well as public policy making.

My entire work experience reflects this systems approach. I have a technical background (MS Mechanical Engineering) and worked as a systems engineer at Bell Laboratories. After recieving my MBA, systems theory continued to flow into my work as a new products consultant and a derivatives controller at Goldmans Sachs and Deutsche Bank respectively. When I founded OpServe LLC, the mission was a to provide a new system and a new way of accessing the derivatives markets for end-users of these financial instruments.

Climate change is the biggest systems problem that mankind has ever caused - and may ever face. It requires a systems approach to addressing it. The framework put forth by W. Edwards Deming in his Theory of Profound Knowledge is especially applicable to addressing this problem on a global basis.
