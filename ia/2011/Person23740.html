Kathy Morse’s Summary

I am strategic visual communicator who is great at implementing management directives.

Background and skills range from professional photographer (both commercial and reportage) to experienced graphic designer and printing broker. An area where I found my niche in project management was in intranet development and growth management.

Working with corporate initiatives, I began to hone my skills working in a Fortune 250 firm with $9 billion in annual revenues and two major electric utilities serving 3.1 million customers in the Carolinas and Florida for over 12 years.

While there I was able to markedly raise awareness of fundamental goals in the areas of Safety, Human Performance (HU) and Diversity. This had been measured by the amount of OSHA recordable injuries, HU mistakes made at the nuclear plant during my tenure.

I am excited to have a new avenue to communicate in at The College of Central Florida.
