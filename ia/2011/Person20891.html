Jill Vitiello's Summary

Business communication leader who has provided strategic counsel, planning and writing to CXOs at some of the world's largest corporations. Passionate about communicating for results. Successful small business owner and award-winning writer.

Specialties

Business writing and communications consulting with expertise in the following areas: Change management, strategy and implementation, employee engagement, and leadership communication. Proven track record of success in leading communications through mergers and acquisitions, and providing communication support for global information technology programs, human resources initiatives, corporate restructuring and reorganization, and corporate social responsiblity activities.
