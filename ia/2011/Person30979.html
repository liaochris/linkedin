Miranda Zukowski’s Summary

Miranda started her path in fitness at the age of 4 when her mother put her into ballet school. One thing lead to another, as it tends to do, and she has now been a successful Personal Trainer, Pilates, Yoga, Kickboxing and Sculpt Instructor all over the Northeast since 1997.

"The backbone of MirZuk Fitness is for you to challenge yourself but to listen to your body. So you push to YOUR personal edge, and when your body tells you to stop- you stop. Next time around- you'll be stronger and your edge will be further away. How about that progress?!"
