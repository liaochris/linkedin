Tijl Pieter de Zwart's Summary

CeTeau is an innovative contracting company active in geosynthetic manufacturing, trading and construction. We can provide design and execute specialized ground improvement, environmental and geosynthetic constructions worldwide. We specialize in installation and manufacturing of Prefabricated Vertical Drain (Wick drain), vacuum consolidation and geomembranes.
Thanks to our extensive knowledge, combined with the most modern equipment and expert technology, all activities are carried out with the utmost professionalism.
Please visit our website www.ceteau.com for more information.
