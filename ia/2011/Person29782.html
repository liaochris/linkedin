Meryl Weinsaft Cooper’s Summary

Meryl is a results-oriented communications professional with 12+ years of experience. She has cultivated expertise and a track record of success in all facets of public relations, digital media initiatives, positioning strategies and special event planning for arts, business, consumer brands, entertainment, media and non-profit organizations.


In addition, she is the co-author of BE YOUR OWN BEST PUBLICIST: How to Use PR Techniques to Get Noticed, Hired & Rewarded at Work (Career Press, Jan 2011 - http://tinyurl.com/3yc7mtn).

Meryl Weinsaft Cooper’s Specialties:

Digital and social media strategies, public relations and promotions, influencer and celebrity seeding, corporate communications, writing, event management, production and publicity, media training, crisis communication
