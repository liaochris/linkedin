Kartik Mithal’s Summary

User Experience design professional with 10 years experience in leading design teams, and creating great designs. Ability to build a collaborative team that excels in delivering outstanding designs and seeing them through to implementation. Attention to the details that can make or break a design. Platforms: desktop, web, mobile and TV.
