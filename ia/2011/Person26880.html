Louis D. LoPresti II’s Summary

Mr. LoPresti has been a strategic advisor to Fortune 500 Brands and their advertising agencies including direct work with senior executives from DraftFCB, Ignited, RAPP, Exopolis, WOO Agency, Creative Asylum, CKIdeas, and MUSE Communications. His client list includes ESPN, KRAFT, Taco Bell, Disney, The PGA Champions Tour, Mercedes Benz, Honda, Toyota, Charles Schwab, Wells Fargo, Bank of America, City National Bank, Blue Cross/Blue Shield, Moneygram and American Express Financial Services.

He founded RedPants in 2006, a digital think-tank dedicated to a "vast reconsideration" of market forces, including how and why consumers consume-and how the market itself is the best possible fulcrum for social change.
