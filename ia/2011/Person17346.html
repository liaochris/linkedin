Software Engineer

Technical co-founder / Early employee

SpeedAlert

Information Technology and Services industry

September 2005 – November 2007 (2 years 3 months)

I was the sole employee of the company when I joined SpeedAlert. I led the technical effort to built SpeedAlert’s mobile application for tracking speed limit in real-time. I invented few algorithms to allow the application run under low power-memory-and-storage devices such as: compression for the data, fast loading of the relevant geographic data (cold load < 30 seconds) and real-time speed query.

Besides working in the mobile application, I worked on technical integration with Sensis and prototyped the thin-client version of SpeedAlert (where the data is being queried through GPRS on real time basis).

SpeedAlert has successfully grown into 10+ people company (with 4 developers) when I left.
