Kashy Ghazzagh’s Summary

We look to find the unique residential, winery and other architecturally designed projects to build for our clients. Using our collaborative approach, our goal is to stay true to the design and vision, building the structure as it was intended. We strive to deliver the finest product possible and within the expected schedule & budget. Another one of our goals is to also make the experience a fun and enjoyable one for our client(s), architects, designers and all other consultants. We build the finest structures with a great deal of pride and attention to detail. By building trust and having a transparent "open book" approach we enjoy a long lasting relationship with our clients.
