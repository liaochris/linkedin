Kurt Baumgartner's Summary

Software security researcher focused on behavioral based security technologies with previous experience in network appliance based technologies. Interested in reverse engineering malicious code, exploitation and shellcoding techniques, and processes to identify and categorize malicious software and its behaviors.

Specialties

Reverse and analyze known and unknown malware, identify unique behaviors and static characteristics, distill large amounts of information about malicious software and identify worthwhile data and concepts. Selection, analysis and presentation of current issues at international conferences.
