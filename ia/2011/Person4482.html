Barnaby Piggott, CFA’s Summary

Highly accomplished Project Programme Manager and Business/Financial Analyst, with significant experience gained in the private equity sector.

Ability to draw on deep knowledge of alternative assets, coupled with strong business, financial and systems analysis skills, backed-up by a CFA Charter and Prince2 accreditation.

This unique combination of skills and experience enables private equity firms, hedge fund managers and fund of fund managers to continually update their operations and maintain significant competitive edge in the market.



Business & Financial Analysis:
• Experienced and skilled in carrying out the complex business and financial analysis required to understand a private equity firm’s present systems and operations.
• Strategic planning of business process improvements and the most suitable technology to achieve it which meets all requirements possible within time and budget constraints.

Consultancy:
• A wealth of experience in providing strategic advice to the senior management of private equity firms, internal project teams and third-party suppliers.

Project Programme Management:
• Business change achieved through managing expectations, producing timelines, milestones and regular reports to keep clients fully informed every step of the way.
• Core competencies in risk management, issue management, and handling of project variations and cost variation. Ensuring delivery of projects on time and within budget constraints.
• Helping client operational staff fully understand the scope of the project, and then working with them to successfully implement and manage the new systems and technology.

Internal & External Client Management:
• Presenting to senior management, explaining complex technical projects in non-technical terms.
• Working with client and 3rd-party stakeholders to keep projects on track by being always available and supportive along the way, whilst providing the strong leadership required.

    • Six month project, managing an internal project team in conjunction with Frameworks.
    • Delivered the historical data migration and business process implementation for fund accounting, fund administration, portfolio management and performance monitoring.
    Career Highlights (cont.)

    Consultant – Eastern European development bank – London (2 years)
    • Detailed requirements analysis for the Fund of Funds investment team (AUM $2.7bn).
    • Re-engineered an Excel-based solution to manage over 100 fund investments, providing complex portfolio management and performance monitoring tools.
    • Represented the investment management team on a multi-million pound change management programme to deliver a Frameworks implementation, SAP R/3 integration and support a risk management project.
    • 6 month sub-project to build a quantitative model, forecasting the portfolio’s current and future investment cash flows, based on Dean Takahashi’s Yale Model. The deliverables from this project enabled senior bankers to better evaluate funds prior to investment. In addition, the model was used to improve the risk management (particularly liquidity risk) and portfolio management business functions.
