Marilyn Gansel's Summary

Owner Fitness Matters Kent, CT
PsyD Applied Sports Psychology
Holistic Lifestyle Coach

Marilyn’s credentials include a doctorate in Applied Sports Psychology .She also has advanced degrees in education and certification by the American Council on Exercise. She holds specialty certification for seniors and youth as well as pre and postnatal exercise and aqua aerobics. She is a cancer recovery specialist and has worked with various special populations.

Her goal: to help others through her non-profit organization, The BINA Foundation, Inc. to benefit individuals needing assessments who are at high risk for heart disease, diabetes, or cancer through educational workshops and health screenings. She and her board members are in the process of creating a multi-generational, green, recycable, and sustainable playground for her hometown of Kent. To find out more, go to www.binafoundation.org.

December 2008 – Present (2 years 4 months)

Marilyn Gansel,announces the establishment of a non-profit organization, The BINA Foundation, Inc.” I created this 501(c) (3) organization to Benefit Individuals Needing medical Assessments who are at high risk for heart disease, diabetes, and cancer. The BINA Foundation, in honor of my aunt Elbina (Bina) Sabini and her brothers is an extension of a benefactor program that started in 2005 at Fitness Matters.

Each family member died of complications from diabetes, heart disease, and cancer. Early detection and medical monitoring was not an option; none of them had health insurance. .

The BINA Foundation’s mission is to provide a medically supervised lifestyle modification program to high-risk individuals who are underinsured or non-insured and cannot afford medical, nutritional, psychological, chiropractic treatment and/or fitness program. Our mottto is “Providing Healthy Futures Today!”
