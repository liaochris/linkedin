Dr. Glenn Nozek, D.C., FIACA's Summary

My name is Dr. Glenn Nozek aka 'CleanzDoc' . I'm a retired Chiropractor and Fellow of the Intl. Academy of Clinical Acupuncture, Lecturer, Educator, Executive Consultant/Trainer for the ReadyToCleanseNow System (featuring the Isagenix nutritional technology) Isagenix Top Income earner and all around pleasure seeker.

We have created a great coaching tool designed to work with the Isagenix Nutritional Cleansing & Fat Burning technology. This program was originally designed for health pratitioners but is now being used company wide by Isagenix associates. It takes the 'technical' aspect of cleanse coaching and makes you a partner with myself & Dr. Ina as she provides daily LIVE as well as video coaching for various Isagenix programs. It even includes customized fitness routines. It's turned out to be a phenomenal tool.

In my personal life, I have an amazing wife, Ina, also a Chiropractor (retired) as well as a Certified Clinical Nutritionist. We have 3 great children: Max-15, Arielle-12 & Jacob-10.

My goal is to make a huge impact on the health & well-being of those I meet while having as much fun as humanly possible. If you like to have fun, keep healthy and make a difference in the lives of others (and get paid for it!)...then we need to talk, LOL!
