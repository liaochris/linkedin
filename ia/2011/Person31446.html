Moritz Winkler’s Summary

Moritz Winkler is a German attorney, whose practice focuses on corporate/M&A, foreign investment, and arbitration. Before joining Yulchon, Moritz served as a counsel for a German “Mittelstand” company and was a legal research fellow at Hanyang University, College of Law in Seoul. During his studies, he worked for nearly five years as a student intern on the China Team of Clifford Chance in Munich. Moritz is a Member of the Board of Directors, Korean-German Association, a member of the Korean Judicial Reform Advisory Committee and a honorary reporter for the Journal of the Korean Bar Association. Moritz speaks German, English, and Korean.
