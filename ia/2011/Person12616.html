Dr. Gaby Cora, MD, MBA’s Summary

"I work with people and companies that want to be healthy while they become wealthy and with leaders who want to enjoy their success and well-being"

GABRIELA CORA, MD, MBA. For the past twenty years I have enjoyed working with C-level executives and entrepreneurs who want to experience peace of mind while making tough business decisions, who constantly deal with conflicting options, who want to avoid burnout and effectively manage stress, who want to improve their relationship with their loved ones, and who want to lead their companies to great success today and tomorrow.

CREDENTIALS

* Leadership Consultant
* Author: ExecutiveHealth.com's Leading Under Pressure: Strategies to Avoid Burnout, Increase Energy, and Improve Your Well-Being (Career Press, 2010).
* Medical Doctor - MD & Board Certified Psychiatrist
* Master's in Business Administration - MBA
* Master's in Health Administration & Policy - MHA
* Mediation Certification Training
* Media Expert: interviewed by Wall Street Journal, New York Times, Forbes, and Business Week. Guest on national FOX, CNN, & Lifetime (English), Univision & Telemundo (Spanish)

Dr. Gaby Cora, MD, MBA’s Specialties:

What can I do for you?

- Peace of Mind: Achieve your goals while avoiding burnout and minimizing stress: CEOs of privately owned companies (1M+ to 1B+.)
- Maximize your executive talent at work: Leadership Development.
- Lead Change and Transition: Succession Planning of family-owned businesses (1M+ to 1B+): Succession, Mergers, and Acquisitions.
- Create Visionary and Strategic Meetings.
- Lead and Manage people during critical times.
- Guide Women Executives to become outstanding leaders.
