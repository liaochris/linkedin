Susan Clarkson's Summary

Highly experienced Human Resource (HR) professional specialising in providing HR support and advice to small/medium sized businesses. We work mainly within West Yorkshire but are happy to travel anywhere within the UK.

Proactive and with a commercial approach, our aim is to provide the full remit of HR services to clients without them having to employ someone on a permanent basis. This ranges from recruiting the right staff, guiding owners/managers through the maze of employee related legislation, policies and procedures they need to be aware of.

In short using Apollo allows clients to concentrate on building their business with the knowledge that their people related issues are in good hands.
