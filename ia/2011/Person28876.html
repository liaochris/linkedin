Résumé de Matt Brunini

Founder of branding, marketing, communications, and public relations firm. Over 15 years of experience in developing large-scale initiatives for some of the most prominent companies around the globe across a wide range of industries, including Russell, AIG, SunAmerica, Principal Financial, WaMu, and RE/MAX.

Expert in all forms of content creation, from sophisticated investment product and process analysis, capital raising to real estate, entertainment, and consumer products. Expertise in financial and investment services at MBC Strategic (www.mbcstrategic.com).

Created and managed a long line of successful marketing campaigns and tools, with significant experience in: strategy, project/team management, plan creation & execution, copywriting, core messaging, design, art direction, websites, press releases, web content, research reports, internal/external communications, annual reports, business plans, capital raises, and material development across print, multimedia, and web.

After launching first agency in 1998, has worked extensively with firms such as AIG, SunAmerica, and Washington Mutual, as well as additional large-, mid-, and small-sized companies in multiple industries. Built an expert team that can handle projects both large and small--from logo and start-up brand development to national marketing campaigns and large-scale websites and branding initiatives.

Sample projects: online training/education brand launch, investment process white papers, AIG investment products website, Anonymous Content entertainment rebrand, www.iMemories.com website, Excel Sports Management identity brochure and advertising, real estate investment branding, www.articulationagency.com website, www.theresnofreelunchinrealesate.com, www.blackiris.tv website, www.mbcstrategic.com, www.americanrealtycap.com and full rebrand, active asset allocation white paper, hedge fund white papers, HNW investing Research Report, www.libertycapitaladvisors.com and ad development
