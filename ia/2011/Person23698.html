Kathleen McGuire, D.M.A.'s Summary

I am a conductor specializing in larger forces including orchestras, 200+ voice choirs, and opera. For 30 years I have worked in Australia, UK, and USA with professional, community, high school, and college organizations. My career highlights include appearances at Carnegie Hall, Kennedy Center, Sydney Opera House, and Soldier Field. I am also a published arranger and composer with more than 40 works listed with ASCAP. My arrangements have been performed widely by more than 100 organizations in the US and Australia. My professional experience also includes teaching music and directing ensembles at high schools and colleges.

Specialties

Strong diplomat able to bring together large, disparate groups of people who possess divergent views. Community leader and social justice advocate with a record of longevity. Additional skills include: producer (CD, DVD); public speaker (live audiences; radio and television); writer (media releases; monthly columns; grant applications); instrumentalist (bass & classical guitar; trumpet; clarinet; percussion); vocalist (three octave range: soprano, alto & tenor)

Community Women's Orchestra

Entertainment industry

August 2005 – Present (5 years 8 months)

Established in 1985, the Community Women's Orchestra (CWO) is a volunteer, all-female ensemble of 75 musicians. Since commencing with CWO in 2005, the orchestra membership has doubled in size, we have commissioned works by established and emerging women composers, produced our first CD, performed four times at Davies Symphony Hall, and toured locally and nationally. As CWO's conductor and manager I oversee all artistic areas and - working closely with the board of directors - many administrative areas. In the 2010-2011 season our goal is to increase the orchestra's budget by 50% through membership and audience growth, an individual giving campaign, sponsorship, and two annual fund raising events. In July 2010 I represented CWO as a panelist at the annual conference of the Association of California Symphony Orchestras.
