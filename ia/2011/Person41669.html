Steve Larsen's Summary

Steve Larsen’s career reflects more than two decades of experience in providing technology solutions for industry giants UnitedHealth Care and Norwest Corporation as well as small to mid-sized companies. A keen strategist and consensus-builder, Steve has created and implemented strategic plans and projects to grow revenues, reduce costs, improve quality and increase operating efficiency. For example, in his position with Zenith Insurance Company, he implemented a solid foundation of technology infrastructure, organization, and architecture that dramatically improved its credibility. He also reduced annual ongoing support costs $2 million by successfully consolidating systems and slashed IT operating costs $7 million by completely rebuilding the organization.

Mr. Larsen’s expertise includes change management, outsourcing and vendor management. He is particularly proficient in rejuvenating antiquated systems into well-aligned value-producing environments and transforming stagnant groups into high performance teams. He is well regarded for his ability to simplify complex problems and manage a large staff through rapid change in both domestic and international environments. Moreover, Steve has held budget accountability up to $15 million.

For anyone who knows Steve Larsen, his achievements come as no surprise. From his earlier days as a Programmer and Analyst with Burlington Northern Railroad, to his rapid rise to senior executive, Mr. Larsen clearly reflects the discipline, drive and confidence of a world-class leader.
