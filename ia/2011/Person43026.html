Theresa Earnheart’s Summary

Track Chic® is the only online/offline social club, shopping, charity and racing news source targeted to motorsports' forgotten 240 million female fans worldwide.

We're shifting gears in motorsports marketing... it's not just a Good Ol' Boys Club anymore!

In a sport traditionally marketed to men, Track Chic successfully fills a void and brings together and gives voice to millions of neglected female fans across the globe. One in three women in the U.S.are avid race fans; they have an average household income of $58,000. These brand-loyal, decision making females have been underserved by the racing circuits and advertisers in the industry and represent a missed opportunity to influence women's $7 trillion of consumer purchases. But not anymore!

Track Chic combines the passion of motorsports with the frivolity of Red Hat Ladies into the Divine Secret that best-selling author Rebecca Wells and women today call "Ya-Ya-Sisterhood".

We're not pink. We don't shout. We don't condescend. We treat women, long overlooked in motorsports, as the intelligent, race-savvy individuals we are.

Track Chic represents more than a website or social phenomenon – it’s a forum for women leading the way in racing information, events, fashion, charities and more. Our mission is to celebrate the female fans and showcase the many women involved both behind the wheels and behind the scenes in the industry today.

Track Chic is establishing an unprecedented trend to embrace and engage female race fans through fun trackside hospitality events, membership conventions, Girls Garage Parties home business opportunities, pampering by Track Chic's male hosts, The Lug Nuts, and fun social events.

Track Chic has now established itself as a legitimate motorsports presence, fostering strong relationships with elite track operators like International Speedway Corporation, Speedway Motorsports, Inc, and the Indy Racing League.
