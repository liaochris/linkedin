Heather Collins's Summary

I've had a very interesting life to date! From starting at 17 teaching Piano, playing in a band, working full time, organising 3day Art and Craft fairs at the local Primary School, supplying pav's and cheesecakes to restaurants, inventing a steel trolley that went into the BHP Steel Awards, inventing and building a device for my third daughter so that I could bathe and change her,discovering I could draw at the age of 40 and having an art exhibition, writing three novels and two poems, running my own business supplying logos on clothing and marketing material, running a thriving canteen at the soccer club where I'm a life member, playing numerous sports in competition and being the mother of 4 children ~ I guess you could say I'm multi-talented.

Now I work full time as a Finance/Admin. Manager for an Insurance Brokerage, and although I love the variety of my postion, I'd love to be working for myself again.

Meeting Leigh Wilson three years ago and taking coaching lessons was the turning point in my life. Firstly I thought I'd like to 'just work from home' but during the past almost three years, the twists and turns of developing my new business have enabled me to put all of my past experiences and my network of friends and aquaintences in the 'one melting pot'.

My friends and contacts in many ways have moulded my business today, and I have a vision to give back into the community both assistance in the way of offering disabled and disadvantaged people to earn extra money by becomming an 'administrator' for my business and by building and maintaining 'age-specific' nursing homes. I am in the process of becomming a 'charity' whereby 10% of all the monies that are collected through my business can be channelled into this project.

Other opportunities exist for investing into the business, to which I am targeting self-funding clubs and community groups to be the recipients.

It is my passion to help and make a difference.

I am on a MISSION!!!

Specialties

My speciality is as someone who creates ideas and develops them with the ability to be able to think outside the square. Sourcing information and assistance where necessary, and never EVER giving up on an idea.
