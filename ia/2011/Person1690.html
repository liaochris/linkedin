Alison Zarrella’s Summary

Alison Driscoll is an interactive copywriter and social media strategist with a background in advertising and marketing. She has worked with a wide variety of clients ranging from small, local businesses to international brands and is the co-author of the forthcoming "Facebook Marketing Book" from O'Reilly. She currently works as an interactive copywriter and community manager at Zipcar in Boston while running her own social media consultancy.

A self-described social media fashionista and advertising junkie, Alison enjoys writing about anything and everything, but especially geek fashion, lip gloss and cool new stuff online. She dispenses humor and advice about copywriting, Facebook, internet marketing and social media news on her blog, AlisonDriscoll.com.

Alison holds a Bachelors of Science in Advertising from Boston University’s College of Communications and is a frequent contributor to the Mashable social media blog. A self-proclaimed Facebook fanatic, Alison has often been featured on radio interviews and university guest lectures as a social media expert. She is also a frequent conference speaker, appearing at such events as Pubcon Vegas and Pubcon South, as well as local Boston meetings and TweetUps. Alison spends her days explaining Facebook and Twitter to clients and colleagues while occasionally shopping online.
