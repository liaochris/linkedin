Hans Mestrum's Summary

Hans is going for the strong and long relationships.
He believes in the idea of a changing world: coming from an Information Society and entering the experience economy and later on the transformation economy based on emotional values and stories around products and services, in which the relation between people is the real transaction, thus creating new markets.
He believes in bottom-up processes in which the user is in control, always on and superconnected.
Positive attitude and optimism are characteristic values.
Creativity and innovation are key-drivers.
