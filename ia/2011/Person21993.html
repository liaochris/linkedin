Jo Jackson's Summary

Born and bred in London’s West End, Jo was one of three original founders of Beyond the Valley – the concept store and community for up-and-coming London designers. Recognized as one of the UK’s Coolbrands for four consecutive years it is still one of the youngest companies to feature in the list. Since its inception five years ago the business helped launch over 300 talented new British designers into the fashion, art and design industries.

Jo has now moved over to join brand experience experts 'i-am' associates to run 'i-am beyond' (previously known as ‘Beyond the Valley Insight’), creating alternative marketing strategies for the Fashion and Retail sector.

She creates innovative brand awareness & advocacy campaigns, producing international pop-up stores, multi-venue events and establishing neighbourhood marketing initiative, such as the Newburgh Quarter and the Wish You Were Here exchange (http://www.wishyouwerehereswap.com)

Previous clients include Nokia, Nike, Adidas, Diageo, Ben Sherman, Fenchurch, Topshop, Eurostar, Blyk, Carnaby, Avon, Courvoisier, British Council, University of the Arts London, and New York's Lower East Side.

Jo is currently on the UK Coolbrands council, and the Art & Design ambassador for the Courvoisier Future 500 network.

Specialties

Brand advocacy, brand awareness, alternative marketing, retail strategies, pop-up stores, brand events, launch parties
