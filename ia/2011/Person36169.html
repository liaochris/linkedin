Raymond T. Hightower’s Summary

Raymond T. Hightower founded WisdomGroup in 1994. WisdomGroup creates web sites, web-based software, and software for the iPhone & iPad. Our products are intuitive to use, tastefully animated, and designed to generate revenue.

Our toolbox includes Ruby, Rails, Ajax, MySQL, Linux, Apache and other open source products.

WisdomGroup serves the open source community as:
* Supporters of ChicagoRuby, a user group for Ruby & Rails enthusiasts.
* Creators of WindyCityRails, the annual conference for all who are passionate about Ruby on Rails. Developers, designers, entrepreneurs, and investors all find value in WindyCityRails.
* Creators of WindyCityDB, a new conference for all who are excited about NoSQL databases.
