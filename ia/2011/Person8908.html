Nick Hall’s Summary

Nick is known for championing new software, identifying emerging trends and API opportunities for existing clients and prospecting purposes. He is an employee who “keeps the pulse of the industry” and the first person consulted about new, creative or “out of the box” revenue-generating ideas to serve a client’s needs or attract a prospect’s attention. He recently organized, motivated and managed a team of individuals to spend bi-weekly after hour’s sessions brainstorming, scoping and executing new business development opportunities including Peak Systems' first iPhone application.

Nick is passionate about all things web-related. His philosophy: if you do business you should be doing business on the internet - and he can help you make it happen. His goal is to explore new avenues of entertainment and marketing through emerging technologies. His insatiable desire to learn is second only to this enthusiasm. Nick is also a mentor who willingly shares his knowledge and expertise through whitepapers, blog articles and informal seminars with clients.
