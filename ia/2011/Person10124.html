Daniel Nogueira's Summary

Daniel Noguiera (1972) completed several studies: Audiovisual Design and Documentary Film Production, a post-graduate study in Television and Electronic Imaging and a bachelor's degree in Media Studies. He received the Citroen award for is graduation-film "Solitair" at the 1997 Dutch Film Festival. His second short film, "Diagonis", has been shown at National and International festivals. He started his career making bumpers for "Sport Paleis de Jong", a programme for the VPRO. The visual character of these short films soon brought him in contact with the advertising-industry. Subsequently he started as an inhouse-director for 180 in Amsterdam.
