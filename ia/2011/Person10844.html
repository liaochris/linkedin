David J. Shulman's Summary

My legal practice area has been exclusively practicing workers compensation for twenty years. My primary focus has been on the defense side of the practice. The experience I have gained allows me to represent both insurance carriers and claimants with very complex workers' compensation related issues including but not limited to major catastrophic injuries to occupational exposure scenarios.

Specialties

Workers' compensation including all phases of litigation, from pre-trial through trial.

Represented two major insurance carriers and its' insureds in all aspects of workers compensation insurance defense litigation.

Demonstrated case analysis ability with track record of proven settlement/trial skills.
