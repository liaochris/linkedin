Anthony Arther’s Summary

Anthony is the managing partner at The Arther Law Firm. Specializing and focusing on creating and negotiating international deals.

Anthony graduated New York Law School; and completed part of his legal studies at Temple University Law School in Tokyo, Japan, the only full-time ABA accredited U.S. law school in Japan.

Mr. Arther has been fortunate to work with some of the industry's top executives and personalities. Mr. Arther also has served as outside legal counsel and Director of International Sales and Acquisition for CCRE, a Japanese media company, whose parent company, CCC, http://www.ccc.co.jp/eng/, owns Tsutaya, the largest media distribution stores in Japan.

For more info and updates please visit www.artherlaw.com

Anthony Arther’s Specialties:

Deal making. Domestic and International contracts in High End Fashion, Film, Music and New Media Technologies. Purchase and sale of media properties, goods and merchandising products for and from the Japanese and world markets.
