Carrie Hemp's Summary

Cornerstone consists of a husband and wife team and experienced, professional team members; both in the field and on our service team. Six members of our team have been in the industry for 20 years! We provide timely, accurate, and competitive estimates for your projects. Get the BEST PRICES and the BEST SERVICE! We ensure each job is done right, every time.

We specialize in the following services:

- Decorative Concrete: Colored, Stamped, Acid Stained
Fun designs with lots of colors to choose from.

- Flatwork: Driveways, sidewalks, patios

- DURA-CRETE®

- Block Work

- Highly Energy Efficient ICF Foundations & Homes
As a Certified Installer of Insulated Concrete Forms and an instructor at the Builder's Association of the Twin Cities, Kelly is highly inspected in the industry as you can see below.

- In addition, our other company, Solid Ground Homes & Remodeling, specializes in remodels, additions, and building new ICF/concrete homes.

Choose Cornerstone Custom Construction, Inc. for your concrete solutions and much more. To learn more, visit us at www.cornerstoneccinc.com.

We are more than happy to provide a long list of satisfied customers. We can be reached at 763-754-3939 or carrieh@cornerstoneccinc.com. Thank you for considering Cornerstone.
