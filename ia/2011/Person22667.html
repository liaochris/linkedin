James Llorca's Summary

Well rounded with more than 25 years of practical experience in both technical and management roles in the exploration, mining and data processing industry. A goal driven, highly motivated professional who has demonstrated commitment of providing quality service to clients within budget and time constraints, together with strong supervisory capabilities, technical competence and broad aptitude in the use of modern computer technology. Always approach tasks with professionalism, efficiency, determination and dedication, understanding the need for planning, personnel and equipment safety, time management and achievement of deadlines. Skilful at training, problem solving, conflict resolution and capable of working autonomously or in a team based environment.

* Independent technical audits (due diligence), and audit (QA-strategic/QC-implementation)
* Classical to geostatistical resource estimation (JORC Compliant).
* Training and mentoring of staff and technical personnel to competency levels.
