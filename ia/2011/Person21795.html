John Lestock, CPP, CEBS’s Summary

Experienced accountant specializing in payroll and pension plans (both defined contribution and defined benefit plans). Specific experience includes reconciling numerous GL accounts (including positive pay zero-balance payroll cash account); filing payroll tax returns for numerous states and localities (including localities in MI, PA, IN, KY and MO); producing W-2 statements and T4 statements (for Canadian employees); testing proprietary defined benefit pension system before implementation (testing items such as vesting service, benefit service, final average pay, covered compensation, PIA offset, benefit formula, J&S payment options, C&L payment options, lump sum payment options, 401(a)(17) limits, disability benefits, death benefits, etc.); reconciling cash clearing account and fund balances held in trust for qualified defined contribution plans; and preparing draft financial statements for defined contribution plans.

Vice President, Rainier Chapter APA
Treasurer, Pacific Northwest Chapter ISCEBS
