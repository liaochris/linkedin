Dan Lascoskie’s Summary

At Carly G Marketing, LLC, we specialize in creating the ultimate experience for Dallas Cowboys fans all over the world. From the game weekend experiences of pep rallies, tailgate parties, current and former player appearances and stadium tours, we give our customers a Dallas Cowboys experience unlike any other. For our sponsors, we provide incredible global exposure opportunities and creative marketing ideas. Through the magic of the internet we are able to reach large pockets of fans all over the world. Our customers include Cowboys fans from Alaska, Hawaii, Germany, the UK, Switzerland, Canada and China. Over the last 12 years, we have expanded our customer base exponentially and have grown our concept into “the Ultimate Cowboys Experience” for fans all over the world. For our sponsors, we offer the most unique marketing concept and branding to create maximum exposure all over the world!
