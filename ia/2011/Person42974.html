The Network Coach Limited is the Limited company behind The Linked In Man, a business that trains, consults and mentors people on how to get more success and more satisfaction from their connections, network and LinkedIn.

The business name doesn't come from the website solely but from the passion I have for networking, linking problems with solutions and getting intimate with the needs of my network of contacts, clients and people I know so that I can continue to add value to the network.

The business came about as I was fed up of being pitched at by social media "experts" who had never used LinkedIn to promote or achieve any real career or commercial successes, but had read the book etcetera and I had an ethical issue with that.

Now I train everyone from individuals through to a room of 100+ on the essential elements engaging in relationships, networking and using the LinkedIn platform to the full.

I don't consider myself an expert on LinkedIn, I don't think anyone should do as it constantly evovles, but I share the pragmatic experiences I have had to raise over £10m additional business sales revenue and gain two jobs - just because of the small amount of time I invest in LinkedIn.
