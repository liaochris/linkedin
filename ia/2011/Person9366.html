Craig Bland’s Summary

Whether you are buying, selling, refinancing or building your dream home, you have a lot riding on the performance of your mortgage professional. Since market conditions and loan programs can change rapidly, you need to deal with someone who is on top of the game. Craig Bland can help you explore the many innovations and options to get the mortgage that is right for your family.

Craig's success can be attributed to a never say no attitude. He endeavors to find a way where others can't. Each client is his most important customer. His dedication to excellence, integrity and ability to find the very best financing solutions for his customers own personal needs are what set him above his competitors and keeps his phone ringing with referrals from Builders, Realtors, and homeowners alike.
