Cole Jones’s Summary

Cole Jones is the General Manager of Sunshine Burger & Specialty Food Company LLC, a maker of USDA certified organic veggie burgers which are gluten free, soy free and vegan. Sunshine Burger was established over twenty years ago by Carol Debberman, a practicing vegan and single mother who wanted to make nutritious whole food vegetarian meals which her five children would enjoy eating. Carol’s veggie burgers were so successful with her family and friends that she was persuaded to convert her passion into a business. Sunshine Burger is now based in Fort Atkinson, WI and distributes its products nationally to natural and organic food retailers; the company also offers its products to foodservice operators through both national broad line and selected regional foodservice distributors.

Previously, Cole was responsible for international sales and special segments at Jones Dairy Farm. In that capacity, he developed overseas markets for Jones products in Asia, Canada, Mexico and the Caribbean; he also worked closely with Costco to develop a market for Jones products in the warehouse club store format. Prior to rejoining the family company, Cole had spent two decades in Asia working in advertising, sourcing and product development for US based companies. He speaks fluent Mandarin.

Branding, Product Development, Negotiation, Sourcing. Domestic and international sales of processed foods to retail and food service customers. Asia based manufacturing of home improvement goods.
