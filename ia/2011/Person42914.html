The Conrad Hall’s Summary

From Jan 08 to Aug 09, I wrote and published 5 books on internet marketing and social media. The titles include the 2010 Social Media Directory, Writing E-Books for Fun and Profit and Starting an Internet Marketing Business after 50.

My client list includes the Ontario General Contractors Association, Early to Rise, George Brown College, Dr. Al Sears, BMO Nesbitt Burns, Accounting and Business Consultants Inc, iRecycle Computers, and ITRoadmaps.

My niche is partnering with business owners to develop their marketing plan and grow a healthy business.

The Conrad Hall’s Specialties:

Editorial Copywriting - Bob Bly says I'm the best editorial copywriter he knows. I am his first choice for new book projects.

Direct Response Copywriting - developing the advertising that sells a book, other product or service.

Marketing Performance - consulting, planning and development are just part of the process. If we work together, then let's make results.
