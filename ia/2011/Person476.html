Adam Butler's Summary

An Online Recruitment & Social Media Geek with a passion for providing exceptional service to my clients and delivering outstanding results for their business.

For a number of years I worked in Sales, Marketing and Management across the Automotive and Financial Services Sectors (yep… I was a motor finance “rep”), but one day sometime in mid 2006 I woke up in the middle of the night and thought “I want to do something different, AND exciting!”

Most people would have jumped out of a plane, or climbed a mountain but I decided to start my own business, and so with my parachute prepared and lunch box packed I set out on my journey…….

Today, my business provides one of the most innovative recruitment services in the UK. We have won a few awards, and gained some recognition along the way, but most importantly we get loads of great feedback from our loyal clients who love what we do for them.

So if you want the best people in your business, and a partner to work with that will help you find them, you may well have found someone that can help, and you’ve definitely found someone that will care.

We GUARANTEE all our work, give me a buzz sometime I’ll talk you through it, and we have a range of services including:

- Online Candidate Attraction and Response Management
- Social Media Candidate Attraction
- Search & Selection
- Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO)
- Assessment Centre Design & Delivery

Specialties

Online Recruitment, Financial Services Recruitment, Sales & Management Recruitment, Quick Service Restaurant Recruitment, Motor recruitment, Selection day recruitment, culture, aptitude, behaviour, coaching, mentoring, chief executive officers, managing directors, leaders, assessment centres, psychometric testing
