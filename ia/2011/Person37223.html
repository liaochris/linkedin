Rob Christensen’s Summary

My passion is building breakthrough products.

In my current role, I am a senior product manager responsible for Adobe AIR, a cross operating system runtime that allows developers to leverage their existing web development skills (Flash, Flex, HTML, JavaScript) to build standalone applications for desktop and mobile devices. My role includes distilling customer pain points into requirements via PRDs, speaking to analysts and press, co-managing a product backlog (we use Scrum), analyzing the market including competitors, working with engineers around the globe, presenting to internal and external executive teams, and evangelizing the product wherever possible. I am also responsible for identifying needs for enterprise customers for the Flash Platform runtimes including Flash Player.

AIR now powers many business critical applications from global companies as well as start-ups. The AIR runtime has been installed on over 300 million computers since its released. I also post regularly on the AIR team blog:
http://blogs.adobe.com/air/

Prior to my current role, I managed a product development framework used by product teams at Macromedia and Adobe. Before that, I spent several years working on Dreamweaver on the engineering team.
