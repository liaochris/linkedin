Antony Smith's Summary

Antony Smith is a driven and ambitious marketer with over 10 years of marketing and eCommerce experience. He has successfully lead and delivered across a range of industries that include Retail, Gifting, Electronic, and Leisure markets.

Antony is currently employed by The Gala Coral Group as Head of CRM and heads up a team of 14 across six brands that support the delivery of £2.5b group turnover. Antony is personally responsible for delivering significant return on his £15m group CRM budget across both remote and retail landscapes.

Previously, Antony has worked with high profile brands such as The Body Shop, Interflora, and more recently Jessops as Head of CRM and later as Head of Ecommerce and Call Centre, where he was key in improving the customer experience and business profitability.

Key achievements with Jessops also included driving 22% growth through collect@store channel and re-launching the Jessops.com website culminating in sustained double-digit sales and profit growth

Prior to Jessops, Antony led the development of Interflora digital and direct CRM capabilities, redesigning database architecture, increasing campaign ROI by 40% and tripling revenues, which lead to the successful VC ownership exit.

Antony cut his marketing teeth at The Body Shop during its resurgence at the start of 2000. There he gained 5 years retail and direct marketing experience that culminated with his leadership of the successful launch of its CRM loyalty scheme (Love Your Body Club) into the UK ROI market, driving revenues of over £2m in its first year under his management

Specialties

Extensive knowledge of CRM strategies, technologies and methodologies, Direct and Digital marketing Techniques. Commercial strategist and visionary. Senior Management experience of Online and Multi-channel businesses. Solid understanding of web marketing and web platforms development.
