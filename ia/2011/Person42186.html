Sutha Kamal’s Summary

I'm an entrepreneur and product executive who's excited about strategy and product design as well as soldering irons and writing code. I've led teams to build and ship core network infrastructure software, mobile games, and user generated content solutions for social networks in everything from a 3 person startup to a Fortune 500 company.

Recently built and ran a mobile business unit that shipped multiple games (5 SKUs) for iPhone, including a game ported from Windows, and managed multiple development teams in Canada and India to deliver these titles. They have received average 4-5 star ratings on the App Store, and have been promoted by Apple as well.

I previously founded a wireless software startup and secured trials with wireless operators and partners with a 4-person team before raising funding, launched direct-to-consumer and carrier-infrastructure products, rasied financing from a tier-1 VC, and won a Canadian VC industry "Best Early-Stage Startup" award. Ultimately moved on and joined Celtic House Venture Partners (our funding VC) as an Entrepreneur in Residence where I sat on the other side of the table evaluating deals, and led the firm's investigation into the mobile, consumer internet and social sectors.

At SOMA Networks, a next-generation wireless infrastructure startup, I managed consumer and operator facing products with up to 20 people across 4 offices and 3 timezones. Also pitched our solution to investors and large telcos, contributing to $100M in venture fundraising.

Starting out at Rogers Wireless I designed and shipped the first wave of wireless data services in a product management role (including our first mobile website and data-enabled handsets, GSM BlackBerry, in-stream data optimization and compression, and others), and took on deeper engineering tasks out of interest, including designing the data network security architecture, and the core network provisioning and authentication flow.
