Kathy Rosenblatt's Summary

A Personal Chef Service is your answer to great tasting, healthful, affordable meals enjoyed in the comfort of your home without the daily grind of shopping and cooking.

I have a passion for creative and nutritious meals. I Will travel around the Los Angeles area from Santa Monica to Downtown and from South Bay to the San Fernando Valley.

Cuisine ranges from Thai/Asian to Italian to Middle Eastern. Your tastes, dietary needs and goals will drive the menu; meals that will excite your adult palate and can be adapted so kids and seniors will love them too.

You decide how much I do for you: shop, cook, serve, clean up, or maybe just prepare the ingredients and you cook. I also give lessons.

Inspired by reading cookbooks, cooking for many years and the Italian cooking classes I took in the Chianti area and Orvieto.

email me at: kathy@kathycooksforyou.com

Specialties

I'll cook in your home or office kitchen for just one person or a small group.

Kosher -- chicken, lamb, beef and fish

Vegetarian -- lettuce wraps,eggplant parmesan, thai bisque soup, stuffed vegetables and much, much more
