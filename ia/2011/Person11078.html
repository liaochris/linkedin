David Wendt’s Summary

David Wendt has finished his Masters program in Artificial Intelligence and minor Evolutionary Biology at the Vrije Universiteit of Amsterdam (VU) in 2010.

His passion? Behaviour. During his studies he focused on Computational Intelligence and Self-organizations and he has been inspired by studying the emergence of organizational and behavioural dynamics of economical, environmental and biological phenomena.
* http://tiny.cc/un59a

David typically is an idea and people person - creating ideas and inspiring people by sharing thoughts and energizing others. He can be described as a teamplayer with a lively and creative mind with 'change' as a middle name. He can be characterized as: creative, solution-driven, enthusiastic, curious and eager to learn. David likes to be in an environment where performance is delivered by blending people, thinking and innovation.
As long he can work for a strong strategic and innovative brand where behaviour could play a central role, he is at his best.

Branches of interest are:
* Organizational Behaviour and Technology,
* Strategic Branding and Communications and
* Earth and Sustainability

Currently, he is working for Client Services - ING Commercial Banking. Contributing to improve operational excellence and client satisfaction for our ING business clients.
Right know he tends to learn more about:
* the core businesses of Banking,
* proces -and change management while respecting impact on employees,
* improving marketing -and service level towards clients.
