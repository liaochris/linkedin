Marcia Daszko's Summary

As a Catalyst for Change, Innovation and Transformation, Marcia Daszko & Associates guides organizations in developing leaders, new thinking, business models, strategies, systems and processes, M&A integration, and innovative products, services and processes. From start-up companies to the Fortune 500 organizations; non-profits; government agencies; and the U.S. military, Marcia and her associates coach and guide leaders in their personal and organizational transformations. In addition, they conduct Innovation and Leadership Seminars, deliver keynote speeches, and facilitate executive retreats.

Marcia has taught Graduate Management classes at UC-Berkeley and Fordham University and holds degrees from Santa Clara University in English and from San Jose State University in Mass Communications. She is a co-founder of the Bay Area Deming User Group (BADUG) and a Founding Board Member of the In2In Thinking Network.

She currently serves as a Board Member for San Jose State Alumni Association and Project Hired in San Jose. She served on the Golf Council for the 2009 Naval Post-Graduate Foundation's annual fundraiser in Pebble Beach, CA.
