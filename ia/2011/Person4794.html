Ben Johnson's Summary

My work experience consists of hands-on sales, marketing, business and product development. I am used to working within different cultures (lived & worked in 5 countries). At my last company I was responsible for setting up the key partnerships with the film distributors while building the UK network from scratch. I built the market to 23 satisfied paying customers that included Paramount, Universal, Sony, etc, and a network of over 300 media including most of the major online broadsheets i.e. MSN, the Guardianonline, the Timesonline, etc. This network now delivers over 100,000 film trailer views per day and provides the film distributors accurate real time reporting on the progress of their video.

I am extremely passionate about the digital video industry. There is so much exciting potential right now; we are standing on the cusp of such a huge change in the way content is being distributed and consumed.

I recently started working on my own company called Gruvi. Gruvi is a Facebook application powered by a video recommendation and event booking engine that helps marketing teams target and acquire more meaningful fans for their brand fanpages, as well as provide a clearer set of metrics about the campaign's performance.
