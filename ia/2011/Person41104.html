Stacey Homan’s Summary

Stacey Homan is a creative and analytical marketing professional with five years successfully managing the marketing communications department for AtriCure, Inc., a high-growth medical device start-up focusing on innovative cardiac surgery. Stacey specialized in the distribution of digital content and pioneered the use of unique methods to revolutionize AtriCure’s marketing department; bridging the gap between sales, marketing and compliance. In addition, she was responsible for the creation and distribution of all marketing material for a rapidly growing global sales force, including product brochures for six product lines, medical videos/animations, patient education materials, tradeshow messaging, website creation, e-mail marketing and the creation and updating of promotional procedures and systems to ensure compliance in a rapidly changing regulatory environment. Prior to her work at AtriCure, Stacey had over three years special event management while working for the Muscular Dystrophy Association, a non-profit organization dedicated to eradicating over 40 neuro-muscular diseases. Stacey holds a B.S. in history and political science education from Ohio University.
