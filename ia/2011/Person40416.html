Simon Booth-Lucking's Summary

Consultant with specialist expertise in digital strategy, ideas, user experience, social media, and agile methods in the public and private sector.

In-depth knowledge of the digital industry and associated trends in society, politics and economics.

Experience of developing and managing cross-disciplinary teams to deliver marketing and service delivery results.

In-house experience at the BBC and agency experience as a company director of a leading Brighton web agency.

Client experience includes NHS Trusts, the Home Office, Coca Cola, NSPCC, Emap, JISC Collections, Breakthrough Breast Cancer, Reed Business Information and various SMEs.

Particular passion for finding elegant solutions to complex problems, whether in the digital field or elsewhere.

Specialties

Strategic planning, proposal writing, selling and pitching, social media planning, agile methods, workshops, requirements gathering, user-centred design, information architecture, interaction design, content management, managing diverse teams and stakeholders.

Director

Privately Held; Internet industry

April 2006 – July 2010 (4 years 4 months)

An innovative web agency that employs user-centred design and agile development methods to create usable websites that get results.

Client work has included:

The end client is the Child Enforcement and Online Protection agency (CEOP). We work with social workers and law enforcement officers to improve the usability of the vital reporting and disclosure forms. I have provided interaction design and information architecture, and overseen usability and accessibility testing.

NSPCC Wishes - www.nspccwishes.org.uk
A major annual Christmas campaign for the UK's biggest children's charity, the site raises a significant figure each year. I led on the creation of the original concept and have worked closely with the client and partners to evolve the campaign since 2007.

Chichester College - www.chichester.ac.uk
A new content-managed website and e-booking system for this large FE college that has increased site traffic by 60% and seen a significant increase in applications. I provided strategic planning, requirements gathering and interaction design.
