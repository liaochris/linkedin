Michele Fellows’s Summary

I am a Director of Fellows and Associates a professional services sector recruitment and management consultancy focused on Intellectual Property worldwide. I head up the Management Consultancy division working with businesses on all aspects of strategy from cost control, to internal audit/review, to merger and acquisition.

With 15 years in finance I have extensive experience dealing with a wide range of companies from large corporations to SME’s, in a variety of industries and sectors and across a multitude of countries and jurisdictions. 4 years running a promotional company in South Africa has added to my commercial and marketing expertise.

The Recruitment arm is staffed with a highly experienced team recruiting Patent Attorneys, Trade Mark Attorneys, IP Lawyers and IP Professionals both within the corporate environment and for private practices. This includes business strategy and recruitment advertising expertise, particularly in the use of new media.
