Jack Bourque's Summary

I have more than 40 years of experience in professional recruiting, public relations, journalism, and public education. In the past quarter of a century my practice has been focused in the global RF, wireless, and wireline infrastructure market where I have provided my clients creative talent acquisition processes.

I have leveraged my extensive RF expertise into newly emerging marketplaces: video security, mobile video, RFID, wireless access control, home security and health monitoring. We have provided our clients with introductions to some of the top talent in these markets as well as expanding into new and exciting wireless application markets.

To effectively meet company and individual requirements, I seek to understand the compelling story of each. Only then can I passionately commit to developing customized career opportunities that are good for both. I believe that only through a partnering relationship that involves mutual respect can staffing success be achieved.

Clients seek my services because I qualify candidates first by expertise, then by personality using verifiable behavioral experiences, and then by applying my intuition honed through years of experience.

I have assisted companies to identify key talent in the USA, Canada, Europe, China, Taiwan, India and Mexico at all levels within an organization -- from board level positions, to senior and mid level management to entry level talent. Contacts and placements have been in Operations, Engineering, Sales, Marketing, Product Line Management, Business Unit Management.

I have been a presenter at conferences and seminars, am an award winning public speaker, and have held positions with Toastmasters International and on municipal boards and commissions.

MA in Corporate and Public Communications from Fairfield University; BA from Westfield State College.

Cellular Infrastructure, RFID, Broadband, Wireless networks, WiMax, WiFi, M2M, Security Monitoring, Access Control.

Specialties

We provide Intelligent Talent Acquisition Process for the Global Wireless and Broadband Industry. Our clients include companies designing, manufacturing, building, and selling cutting edge wireless and broadband communications infrastructure integration technology. These companies focus on components, systems, and networks that provide - either as manufactured products or services - the delivery of voice, data, audio, or video signals in a wide variety of applications. www.wirelesscareers.com
