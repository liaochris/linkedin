Bonnie O'Toole's Summary

I currently am President of Market Pursuits Inc. a business development organization helping businesses:
- Find markets
- Get funding
- Cut costs

I specialize in developing action-orientated Business Plans, market plans, funding proposals and process re-design.

I have a 16 year performance track record in marketing, sales and customer service. I have a broad range of business acumen including the development of: business plans, market plans, funding proposals, analysis, forecasts/budgets, performance metrics/scorecards, strategy development, project management, process re/design, training, facilitation, coaching, and consulting.

My education includes a Bachelor of Science degree from Dalhouse University, Master Certificate in Business Analysis, Master Certificate in Project Management, certificates in: Lean Six Sigma Green Belt, Adult Education, Advanced Facilitation, Small Business Management, Professional Selling, and Professional Consulting.

I previously worked for a large Canadian Telecommunications provider, Bell Aliant, and its predecessor companies for 14 years in senior positions as Business Planner, Strategist, Executive Advisor, Business Analyst, and Sales Manager.

Specialties

Market Analysis, Business Planning and Corporate Performance
