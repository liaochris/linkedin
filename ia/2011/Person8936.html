Chris Maxwell's Summary

I am a software manager, a developer and an architect with an extensive experience and passion in the digital music domain.

I'm an Agile advocate and have actively practice agile techniques, including Scrum, TDD and Extreme Programming. I believe strongly in domain driven design, the DRY principles and rapid development techniques, which I actively practice in my day to day development.

I have extensive Groovy and Grails experience and have architected, developed and launched three applications using Grails including the Walmart's MP3 Music Service, which is one of the most visible and largest Grails applications in the industry. Among the other applications in Grails, I have built a merchandising and promotional application as well as a help desk support system which all contribute to running the Walmart Music Service.

I also have extensive experience in building our content ingestion and management system, which is built using the Spring Framework and Hibernate. This system is designed to ingest and manage content from the music record labels and provides our users the ability to service the Walmart MP3 Music Service.

Coming from a web developer background I have extensive experience in front end development. I have extensive knowledge of CSS and HTML and have built applications using Javascript libaries such as Dojo and Prototype. I also have built Ajax applications using Dojo and DWR. At Liquid Digital Media I have prototyped and designed most of the applications as well as proof of concept designs and projects.

I also have experience developing for the Roku, where I created a prototype music and video streaming service which connected to a backend Grails application via web services. This application streamed music, videos and television shows to the Roku player, which enables the user to view the content on their televisions.
