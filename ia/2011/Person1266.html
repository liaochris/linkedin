Al Lemieux's Summary

I have a BFA in Graphic Design from Massachusetts College of Art ('94). I am a Flash designer/developer, web designer/developer, graphic designer, media producer, video/audio producer, and an instructor. I am a certified Adobe Photoshop Expert. I also develop and deliver curriculum for the Boston University Center for Digital Imaging Arts in Waltham.

Check out my blog:
Elevate Your Mind: http://lemieuxdesign.blogspot.com

Specialties

Flash, ActionScript, Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Final Cut Pro, After Effects, Peak Pro, Sound Soap Pro, All things Adobe, BBEdit, Dreamweaver. I also have expertise in making sites 508 compliant, usability focus, web standards compliant.
