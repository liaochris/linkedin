Richard H. Johnson's Summary

Its been said that Richard never misses an online advertising event. That could be why his clients, colleagues and friends describe him as “the consummate interactive advertising sales professional, with deep industry knowledge, the companies within it, and how all the competitive sets intertwine.”

Richard is an online advertising veteran having been in the industry for 10 years. His experience has taught him how to develop successful revenue strategies for digital media companies large and small. Most recently, as SVP of Media Sales with Adify, Richard built a media sales organization which contributed to the sale of the Company to COX Enterprises for $300 Million.

In early 2009, Richard left Adify to start his own consultancy called New Vision Strategy with the goal of helping early stage companies find their way to gaining a share of the digital advertiser pie. In a changing world, Richard believes it required "new vision" to develop successful digital sales and markeitng in a very crowded and dynamic digial space. Aside from working with Series A/B venture backed companies, Richard also has unique expertise to assist with larger media companies in competing in the digital space.

Early in his career, Richard was involved in the early stages of rich media advertising where in the late ‘90’s he worked with colleagues at Bank of America to create and implement interactive video and rich media campaigns with @Home and Snap! for High Speed. Richard also successfully built sales organizations for MyFamily.com, PhotoPoint, WeatherBug and ValueClick Media. Richard also worked for Tiffany & Co. in the early 1990's in a Corporate Sales role where he learned from top industry executives the value of customer relationships and the art of the consultative sell.

In his spare time, Richard has a passion for wine, his family and the great outdoors.

Specialties

Online Marketing, Media Sales, Sales Management, Sales Planning, Interactive Strategies, Vertical Networks, Wine, and Family.
