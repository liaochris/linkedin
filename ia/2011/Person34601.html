Peter Chapman's Summary

Peter Chapman BA, ACIR, is Team Lead of Soul Source Recruiting, an Independent Recruitment Firm Specializing in ENGINEERING and ENGINEERING TECHNOLOGISTS in Canada.

Specialized in the recruitment of US and Canadian Professional Engineers (P.E. and P.Eng), and Technologists for opportunities throughout Canada.

Originally from Calgary, Alberta, and a graduate of the University of Calgary, Peter has developed strong relationships with a long list of exceptional candidates as well as reputable, ethical and diverse engineering companies throughout Canada.

Peter has earned the respect of his peers as being an ethical and focused recruiter who maintains only the highest standards of integrity in his work. Peter is also a past member of the Human Resources Association of Calgary and has contributed as a member of the Awards Committee.

Peter has also received his accreditation as an Advanced Certified Internet Recruiter (ACIR) through the AIRS specialized recruitment training centre. This incredibly valuable training program teaches very unique methods of uncovering passive candidates.

Genuine due-diligence, and a clear understanding of the region, the candidates and the employers in our market is essential to our continued success.
