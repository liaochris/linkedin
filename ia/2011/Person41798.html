Keith Stiles's Summary

Keith A. Stiles, LMT, is a Nationally Certified Massage Therapist and professional member of the American Massage Therapy Association, who has fifteen years of sports, orthopedic, and clinical massage expertise. He is a graduate with high honors from the internationally-renowned Boulder College of Massage Therapy (BCMT), in Boulder, Colorado.

The 1060-hour manual therapy certificate program earned Keith a unique opportunity to work with industry heavy-weight Elaine Calenda, board member of the National Certification Board for Therapeutic Massage & Bodywork (NCBTMB). He began his work with her in 1995, assisting in orthopedic massage seminars, which spring-boarded him into a long-term teaching position with his alma mater, BCMT, as an instructor of Swedish Massage, Structural Kinesiology & Orthopedic Massage.

During his six-year tenure at BCMT, Keith also gained world-class clinical experience at Boulder Rehabilitative Centers and worked with Myofascial Release Master Mark F. Barnes, MPT, at SOMA Physical Therapy. Concurrently, Keith also maintained two highly successful massage office practices in Boulder and Denver.
In 2002, Keith was recruited to work with the international, renowned artistic entertainment company, Cirque du Soleil. For six years, he traveled extensively throughout the United States, Canada, Mexico, Japan, and Europe, working with Olympic gymnasts, acrobats, aerialists, contortionists, dancers, jugglers, clowns and actors across four traveling big-top shows, Alegría, Dralion, Quidam & Corteo.

At the conclusion of his tour, Keith joined Moore Massage in January 2009. However, by the fall of 2009 made the move to NYC to live his dream of making it in the Big Apple. Keith now maintains a private practice in Manhattan with house-calls and an office at Fluid Fitness NYC in Bryant Park. Keith continues his relation with Cirque, working with Wintuk, OVO & Banana Shpeel.

Specialties

Deep Tissue Massage, Swedish Massage, Sports Massage, Orthopedic/Medical Massage, Trigger Point Therapy, NMT
