Jeff Dodes’s Summary

Currently Executive VP, Marketing & Digital Media at Jive Label Group, a division of Sony Music Entertainment. A young veteran in the entertainment business including experiences in traditional music distribution, A&R, product management, record company management, marketing, business development, digital/mobile sales, DVD authoring and emerging technologies.

Currently, heads up Marketing & Digital Media at Jive Label Group overseeing 16 person staff that handle all aspects of the marketing and digital/mobile business for the label. Responsible for large marketing budget and significant percentage of total revenues. Core responsibities include:

- Transitioning from a traditional record company to a music based digital media and content development company
- Development & implementation of superstar artist marketing campaigns
- D2C strategy and implementation
- CRM strategy and implementation
- commercial digital/mobile sales & marketing strategy
- digital/mobile content development
- design/development of artist and corporate websites
- design/development of digital marketing tools (widgets etc.)
- search engine marketing/rich media advertising campaigns
- explore new technologies and generate new business opportunities

Received law degree in 2003, graduating with distinction from New York Law School. Jeff's article "Beyond Napster, Beyond The United States" was published in the New York Law School Law Review and won ASCAP's Nathan Burkan Memorial Writing Competition.

Prior to Jive, spent 10 years working in various positions within the music business, gaining experiences in all aspects of the creative and business process, from the recording studio to delivering music to market and everything in between. Started out working "in the field" dealing directly with retailers, radio and consumers and later was the GM of a "mini-major" record label (Volcano Recordings/Zoo Entertainment) where he oversaw 50 employees in various departments.

Jeff Dodes’s Specialties:

Commercial digital/mobile distribution/marketing, Strategic planning and marketing, Intellectual property, Technology analysis, On-line and wireless technologies, Web design, DVD video and audio authoring, Music licensing, Copyright and entertainment law
