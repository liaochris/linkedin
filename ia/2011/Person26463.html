Lisa A. Amaral’s Summary

With 21+ years of experience in the real estate and relocation service industry, most recently as Director of Business Development for the largest independent homeowner company in the Mid-Atlantic region, Ms. Amaral embodies a wealth knowledge. Prior to relocating to the Mid-Atlantic region over 8 years ago, Ms. Amaral held leadership positions with successful residential brokerages in Manhattan, NY, and Southern Connecticut.

Ms. Amaral participates in the Employee Relocation Council (ERC) as a Meritorious Service Receiptant and most recently completed her service on the CRP Certification Review Board. Lisa also obtained her GMS and has a Certificate in Achievement for Applied CRM (Customer Relationship Management) Strategy. Lisa often speaks on wide ranging topics such as Business Planning, new Business Development, Sales Associate training, Increasing Profits, and Lead Generation and Management for E-Business Development.
