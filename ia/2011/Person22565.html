Joshua Winograd’s Summary

I graduated from Georgia Institute of Technology with a BS in Management and certificates in finance and marketing.

While attending Georgia Institute of Technology, I worked for Barry manufacturing, a men's clothing chain with 40+ stores and a manufacturing presence in Costa Rica. At Barry Manufacturing, I managed the relationship with the company's outsourced marketing firm and assisted the marketing firm and Barry Manufacturing's CEO in developing the company's advertising and marketing campaigns.

After graduating I went to work for Regency Hospital Company and moved up through the company as a financial analyst, manager of financial systems and the Director of MIS. In the Director of MIS position, I worked as a liaison between the company's business units and IT. By leveraging my strong project management and business analysis skills, I have been able to, in numerous areas, help the business realize greater efficiencies through better use of technology while at the same time focusing IT on aligning its implementation and development of technology with the business goals of the organization.

I have a strong financial analysis and planning background that I have used throughout my career. I am entrepreneurial and I look for opportunities to strategize and think creatively outside the box to find unique opportunities and innovative solutions.

I am currently working as a Lead Financial Analyst for AT&T Mobility. I manage a team that is responsible for analyzing financial impacts related to technology obsolescence, acquisitions, and large equipment implementation initiatives.
