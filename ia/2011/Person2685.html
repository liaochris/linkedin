Founder and CEO

Future Value Consulting

Privately Held; Information Technology and Services industry

July 2009 – Present (1 year 9 months)

Future Value Consulting was founded by Andrew Crane in 2009 as Aptify's first domestic implementation partner. Our mission is to help organizations maximize the future value of their technology investments by pairing best-of-breed technology - Aptify AMS and AptifyRAD - with technological expertise and association domain experience. Intuitive end-to-end processes, effective staff training, sound internal controls and comprehensive project management all factor into the equation of building and maintaining an enteprise-wide solution that will help an organization grow for many years to come.

Prior to launching Future Value Consulting, Andrew served at Aptify for five years as Consulting Services Manager and led several major Aptify implementations in the U.S., Canada and Europe. Andrew has over fifteen years of software development and consulting experience, a degree in computer science, and is currently pursuing an accounting certification. He looks forward to growing his team at Future Value Consulting and expanding his service offerings to the Aptify community.
