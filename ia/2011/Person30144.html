Please contact me at mkresge@cpatucson.com or 520-321-1334 for all of your accounting & auditing needs.

I have a wide array of background in the field of accounting including public accounting in a large size regional firm, governmental accounting, and corporate accounting. I'm currently with a small local firm in Tucson, AZ specializing in audits and reviews of non-profit organization and small entities also focusing on individual and business taxes.

My career goal is to utilize the knowledge obtained while working within the different sectors of accounting to advance our firm to the next level. On the horizon is a masters in accounting.

Our firm is always seeking new clients and new opportunities in the Southern Arizona area. Please contact me (mkresge@cpatucson.com or 520-321-1334) if you have any questions or interested in our services.
