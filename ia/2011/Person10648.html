David Barrie's Summary

I run a creative consultancy and project production company that enables capital growth in cities and the improvement of people's lives.

We design and deliver projects, programs and action plans for clients who work in real estate, urban renewal, the creative, community and social sectors.

We have created and delivered initiatives valued at over £16m ($25m) and credited with leveraging over £260m ($400m) of new investment in to towns and cities.

Our work fosters public participation, creates new social ventures, wins planning applications and has been widely recognized in the U.K., Europe and Asia as best practice in urban enterprise, social innovation and place-making.

Project associates include architects, digital media producers, visual artists, project managers, cost managers, community leaders and social entrepreneurs.

Our work is often featured in the media - it has been the subject of TV series on the BBC and Channel 4 and press coverage in publications like the New York Times, Sunday Times, Monocle, Blueprint and Property Week.

In 2009, our projects won the 2009 Grand Prix, Regeneration & Renewal, the Civic Trust Special Award for Partnership and RIBA CABE Public Space Awards, sister of the Stirling Prize.

Specialties

Citizen participation, social innovation, social design, communications design, stakeholder management, project implementation, media production
