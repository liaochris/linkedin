Vijay Jain's Summary

Vijay Jain is the Founder & CEO of Astute- the award -winning, South Asia's largest Developer, Aggregator and Publisher of Mobile VAS. Astute has been working on robust and state-of- art technology platforms including Java, BREW, I-Phone, Blackberry, i-mode, Symbian, WAP, Android, Windows mobile and new media technologies. Astute has its own catalogue of 500+ games and applications.

Vijay’s management skills, Sharp Vision, Patience, Timely Decision , precise forecasting, Analytical Skills and in-depth expertise in Wireless and Telecom, made deserve to hold the position of CEO in Astute. Vijay has built up the milestones for Astute’s Success in Mobile Content Business globally. On one part, Vijay’s Management Brain helps to run the entire organization smoothly and on another part Vijay’s Technical Brain contributes and motivate New Technology and R&Ds to the company to stand ahead among the Industry Players. Therefore, within short span of 4 years Astute has achieved great height and bagged various Awards for contributing in Mobile VAS to stand at forefront.

Earlier in his carrier, he served as VP WCDMA R&D in NEC Systems with NTT Do-Co-Mo, Japan, where he was responsible for overall leadership in World's first Mobile TV phone R&D. Before joining NEC, he served as Sr. Consultant in IAP Tokyo Japan.

Mr. Jain started his career in 1999 with Zenith Infotech as a Wireless Consultant. He has held numerous positions of increasing responsibility in Wireless Network Planning, Consumer Services, Engineering and Operations, and was responsible for all New R&D in Wireless with Telecommunications Network Operations in BPL Mobile and Nokia in Mumbai.

Vijay’s knowledge was embellished with B.E. and Masters in Advance computing from most reputed Pune University.
