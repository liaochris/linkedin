Ryan M. Beshel’s Summary

Ryan M. Beshel graduated with honors from the Illinois Institute of Art-Chicago with a Bachelor of Arts in Fashion Marketing and Management in December of 2009. While studying for his degree, he completed an editorial internship at Michigan Avenue Magazine assisting the editor-in-chief by transcribing interviews, writing articles and contributing ideas for inclusion in the magazine. He continued his work in the fashion industry handling public relations, promotions and brand marketing as a PR intern and personal assistant for Image and Wardrobe Consultant, writer and mentor, Barbara Glass. Ryan is currently the Director of Runway and assistant to Marie P. Anderson, the Fashion & Beauty Director at Agency Galatea - Model and Artist Management. He works closely with models to develop their potential while fulfilling the needs of each client through efficient communication. He also scouts for new models, manages marketing materials such as comps/portfolios and books photo shoots and runway shows. In his free time, Ryan works as the freelance Chief Wordsmith for successful Chicago publicist, Julie Darling of The Darling Company. He also enjoys attending industry events and promoting Chicago fashion through his successful blog, 'The Bowtie Memoirs'. His future plans include continued work in the modeling industry, freelance writing and striving to elevate the fashion industry in the fine city of Chicago.
