Rob Smale's Experience

Director, Chemical and Environmental Services

Quality Distribution

Public Company; Transportation/Trucking/Railroad industry

February 2007 – Present (4 years 2 months)

Quality Distribution (QDI) is the largest bulk transportation company in the continent of North America, combining the largest fleet of stainless steel tanks with the industry's broadest and most complete terminal network. Quality Carriers, the main subsidiary, transports bulk chemical products in the US, Canada, and Mexico. Boasso America Corporation is a premier intermodal service company that provides tank container cleaning, heating, testing, maintenance, storage, and transportation, along with associated equipment sales. Quality Transload and Terminals specializes in railcar and stationary tank transfer and storage, warehousing, packaging, and intermodal and total distribution of bulk liquid and dry flowable commodities throughout the continent of North America. Quala Systems (QSI) operates a network of tank-cleaning facilities nationwide. QDI is an American Chemistry Council® Responsible Care® Partner Company, and has been certified to the Responsible Care Management Systems (RCMS) technical specifications.

My specialty is identifying business risks associated with bulk chemical transportation. I facilitate development of transportation rates commensurate with a product’s risk by teaming with all organizational levels, including the sales force, senior management, terminal managers, dispatchers, drivers, tank wash managers, tank washer associates, and mechanics. As the owner of the corporate product/chemical cleaning procedure repository, I guide tank-cleaning facilities on providing safe, environmentally sound cleaning services at competitive prices. I co-chair of the QDI's Responsible Care committee, and oversee the environmental aspects of the RCMS. My environmental management role includes maintaining the environmental information management system, supervising engineers and specialists, negotiating solutions to complex compliance issues with regulatory agencies and implementing strategies to meet challenges presented by proposed regulations.

Quality Distribution

Public Company; Transportation/Trucking/Railroad industry

I was corporate manager of environmental programs for QSI’s network of tank-cleaning facilities, and QC’s network of terminals. My primary responsibilities included planning, directing and managing all aspects of the RCRA, CAA, CWA, and EPCRA compliance programs, including all corresponding State and Local programs. I reduced the Corporation's impact on human health and the environment by engineering industrial wastewater treatment systems and air pollution control systems. Through a rotational training program, I managed operations and customer service at several union and non-union QSI tank-cleaning facilities. I started a new parking-area lease program to capture additional revenue, and to stipulate environmental terms and conditions. I designed and implemented a SQL database to coordinate industrial waste shipments, and transformed the database into a comprehensive environmental information management system that replaced the existing paper-based system.
