Pierre Collinet (+5000)'s Summary

Prior to joining Sterling International in October 2001 as Director of Research and Studies, Pierre Collinet was Research Associate with Russell Reynolds Associates in Brussels for 9 years creating the research function, and specializing in the sectors of Industry and Natural Resources. He began his career as Corporate Documentalist with Union Minière (Umicore) prior to joining Spencer Stuart where he remained 4 years as Research Assistant.
