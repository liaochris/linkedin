Archis Gore's Summary

Currently work on Windows Live (www.live.com) for the last 18 months.

Worked on Live Mesh before that, and being fresh out of college was an exciting and adventurous journey - to see code being written from the very first lines, to look at how scale services are written, how to tackle complex security systems, to look at failure-tolerant clients, etc.

My best ability is in solving hard problems and rapid prototyping - getting systems up and running fast, exploring the unknown, and getting around hard that involve concurrency problems, performance problems, scalable architectures, etc.

Specialties

Incubation efforts and prototyping. Designing and developing for hard problems that involve multi-threading, concurrency issues, data synchronization, native development for small form-factors (phones, low-power devices), etc.
