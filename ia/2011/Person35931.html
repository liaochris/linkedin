Alan Rambam’s Summary

Thank you so much for visiting my page. An amazing mentor of mine once told me that I needed to focus on “where the puck was going”. I later found out that he was referring to a comment Wayne Gretzky made to describe his success, “I don’t skate to where the puck is. I skate to where the puck is going to be.” I’ve read this statement hundreds of times and it has become one of my guiding life principles. So who am i?

• I am a mobile, digital and new-media guru.
• My role today is that of a successful new media strategist, technologist, and mobile marketer with a deep passion, endless creativity, and a highly respected global reputation for breaking-down my insight into easily understandable and scalable product marketing efforts, new media models, trends, mobile technologies, socialization patterns, and behavioral shifts.
• My background also includes extensive support for top VC Firms and hands-on development/production direction for large global brands in-addition to account management/planning experience.
• All of these efforts are grounded by my unique consumer first approach to marketing and transmedia for mobile, social media, and digital products/solutions.
• I have developed and built numerous mobile/emerging media platforms, influencer programs, and ground breaking digital initiatives for some of today’s top Fortune 100 companies, media properties, digital shops, youth brands, charities, and media/advertising agencies.
• My consistent record of success has helped me to become a globally respected thought-leader regularly contacted for my ability to monetize cultural, social, mobile, and digital/media trends.
• I am also a highly skilled presenter and mentor that excels at building and managing teams to drive revenues, innovation, and change for large global agencies and corporations.

Alan Rambam’s Experience

  • Founder and Chief Strategic Officer MobileBehavior and Chief Mobile Officer

    Tribal DDB

    (Public Company; OMC; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    October 2008 — Present (2 years 4 months)

    Founder, and Chief Strategic Officer of MobileBehavior and now also the Chief Mobile Officer of Tribal DDB Worldwide, which has 60 offices in 38 countries. Both companies are part of the Omnicom Group. The recent acquisition of MobileBehavior by Tribal DDB was an unprecedented move for an existing Omnicom specialty agency. The alignment marks the launch of a new center of excellence for Tribal DDB Worldwide managed by myself and my team to serve clients' growing need to seamlessly incorporate mobile communications into their overall marketing and media mix. MobileBehavior observes emerging global mobile technology, cultural, and behavioral trends, then we connect the dots to generate mobile strategy, activation and engagement programs. In addition, we gather mobile and real-life insights and intelligence that also feeds a new global cloud-based recommendation engine the we are developing for all of Omnicom called the Global Mobile Toolkit (GMT).  I successfully pitched the idea to build this proprietary platform 2 years ago to Omnicom and I am listed on the provisional patent filing.

    (Public Company; OMC; Public Relations and Communications industry)

I also spearheaded the development of their Social Media practice from the ground-up, through early relationships with MySpace, Facebook, and Mixi.
