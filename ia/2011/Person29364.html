Michael Casalino’s Summary

I began my artistic studies at the Savannah College of Art and Design. After graduating with a BFA in Computer Animation and a Minor in Art History, I began working as a freelance designer for MTV Networks, IBC Digital and various graphic design firms.

After freelancing for some time, I took a job with EA Sports. I worked at EA Sports for over 7 years as an Environmental Modeler, Texture Artist and most current, Associate Art Director. I worked on such games as MADDEN Football, NCAA College Football, NASCAR Thunder, NFL Street and Superman Returns.

I am currently working at Disney Interactive Studios as an Art Director on various Disney properties, as well as some really cool New IP.
