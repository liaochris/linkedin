Bob Gale's Summary

After nearly 30 years in Advertising and Marketing, I am most grateful to those Industry pros who took their personal time to mentor and coach me through the good, bad and off strategy. To all of those whose gift of personal time was freely given, I dedicate my practice to you. .

At Gale Executive Recruiting we follow a simple mission; give back to those who value the gift as much as you do. This is not a "job shop" it is a career coaching "safe haven"; we are here to serve the long-term career needs of our candidates first. Our Clients appreciate our high level of service and dedication to our top talent. Each client wants the best, those who work with us understand what great lengths we go to find the perfect match. Perfection lies only in attaining the top talent, shared values & respect for individuality and a balanced lifestyle.

As an exclusive firm, you must have a direct relationship with us or a client candidate. We will take the time to know you and your talent in-depth before we ever talk about specific opportunities. Every relationship we have is our long term bond, we guide and advance along with you...

Specialties

Serving the unique needs of today's top talent in advertising and marketing is but one of the exclusive offerings that attracts and retains quality candidates to Gale Executive Recruiting. Life and career coaching is our passion and just one of the exclusive offerings that attract and retain quality candidates by value of your time, providing relevant category insight, personal one-on-one career coaching, and relevant career branding program.
