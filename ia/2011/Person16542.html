Gregg Apirian's Summary

I have spent the past decade as an entrepreneur and pioneer in the digital media and technology consulting business. Throughout those 10 years I’ve co-founded and managed successful and well-respected digital agencies like BLITZ, Schematic, and Trailer Park; worked in management consulting with a heavy emphasis on technology and operational consulting; and planned, designed, built, deployed and sustained hundreds of websites and applications for many different types of clients across many industries.

As the business has evolved, so have I. My subject matter expertise has grown, I’m more technically savvy today than I ever have been before, and I understand that it is my responsibility to try and help the clients I server understand how to develop their strategies so their goals can be accomplished over time through cost-effective means.

My experience ranges from digital and operational strategy, to user experience design, to technology selections and implementations; that all likely create positive change for my clients and their organizations.

Currently, I am the founder and CEO of DVELOP, an interactive consulting firm and digital production studio where we use our experience and business intelligence to drive measurably effective design and technology solutions for our clients. We have great clients and get to work on fun projects of all sorts.

I love what I do and I cherish the people I get to work with everyday. Please reach out and connect with me as I enjoy meeting new people and finding ways to work together.
