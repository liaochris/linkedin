Andrea Feinberg's Summary

Goals? To coach business owners who wish to make more money by day & sleep better at night. To ensure no one hangs their integrity at the door when working. To teach entrepreneurs: time off from business is key for the business to succeed.

President, Coaching Insight, LLC, leveraging intangible assets for businesses. As a Wall Street exec, marketing consultant, & coach I have 30 years experience helping clients articulate & exceed goals for business or personal lives. Clients include Standard & Poor’s Corp., Sheshunoff Financial, Integ, Sandler Sales, Cameron Advertising, Provista Group, KMF Property Mgmt., Certified Payroll, Modularm Corp., Aztec Ind., Osmosis Devel., L. Britt Consulting.

A polished, responsive, powerful speaker: work/life balance, vision creation, strategy development, guerrilla marketing, intra-preneurship, stress reduction, crafting an elevator speech, more.

Facilitate custom designed, off-site retreats for those wanting to reinforce & refresh their commitment to their business.

certified Professional Behavioral Analyst; certified EQ Mentor; certified facilitator, The Alternative Board; Small Business Expert, LongIsland.com; v.p., Brookhaven Small Business Advisory Council; former president, Westbury Chamber of Commerce. Profiled in The New York Times, Newsday, Long Island Business News; published in “Business to Business”, “Creations Magazine”, ‘The Hauppauge Reporter.” co-author, an award-winning book: “The Essential Coaching Book”.

Was a gen'l manager for Standard & Poor’s Corporation, with a success record including an $8,000,000 business unit with 75% ROS. Built a wholesale enterprise, sold it, and trained for professional coach designation.

A graduate of Coach University's advanced coach training program, Founding Member of Coachville and of the Int'l Association of Coaching and Certified Strategic Business Leadership Coach. M.B.A., Marketing, from NYU’s Stern School of Business.

Specialties

business/product development, marketing planning & implementation, event planning, business writing, team building, leader development, goal setting & achievement skills. DISC Assessment tools for employee attraction, retention, development; sales success, improved communication, enhanced relationships
