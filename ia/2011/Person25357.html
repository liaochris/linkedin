Laila Sotoodeh's Summary

Laila Sotoodeh came to from Monterey California, with over 15 years of outstanding experience in customer service, sales and human resources. Her background ranges from Travel Agent to Human Resources/Recruiter for Hyatt Vacation Club, where she was also part of a spirited sales team.

While she was in the role of human resources, Laila managed employee relations, recruitment and a staff. During her sales career with Hyatt in Carmel California, and later promoted to San Antonio Texas, both of her sales teams were ranked #1 nationally for annual sales, and were continually in the top 3. As a Travel Agent, she was voted "Agent of the Year" and was also an active member of the Chamber of Commerce’s.

Laila is a self-motivated and decisive professional. Whether client or candidate, you will notice Laila's determination to achieve excellence is complemented by her level of integrity in all of her business relationships. Her strengths lies in recognizing and solving problems, allowing her to thrive in the world of recruitment.

Throughout Laila’s professional career, she has always been active in the volunteer community. Whether it was fund raising for the United Way, passing out water at the Nashville Country Music Marathon or volunteering for the Humane Society, she enjoys each experience and likes working with others on the volunteer committees.
