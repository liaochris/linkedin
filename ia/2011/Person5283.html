Bill Pringle’s Summary

Mr. Pringle has close to 40 years of full-time software experience, from programming to management, and over 30 years of part-time teaching experience. His programming experience ranges from operating system internals to applications programs, web applications, and user support. He has both Unix and Microsoft experience.

He has a great deal of experience with many aspects of software development, but themes that frequently recur are software tools, software methodologies, and what is now called data mining.
