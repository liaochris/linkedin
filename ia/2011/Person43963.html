Tony Coffey's Summary

A successful, highly experienced, Interim Head of Procurement and Interim Procurement Consultant with a distinctive blend of drive, gravitas, expertise and credibility, who is ideally suited to leading the design and implementation of transformational performance improvement.

This track record in interim procurement management and interim consultancy has led to a comprehensive client base that includes numerous unitary local authorities and a Regional Improvement and Efficiency Partnership (RIEP).

Also, in the private sector we have led the successful introduction of procurement category management and strategic sourcing at a renowned food processing company and major high street retailers. We also developed business with a key environmental body, providing advice on procurement strategy.

Furthermore, our corporate experience includes senior career procurement positions within the Management Consultancy, Financial Services, NHS, Construction and Nuclear Energy industries.

Texell is pleased to announce its continued association with Newpoint Consulting, who assists clients with all aspects of procurement competence assessment, development and improvement in procurement and supply chain management.

We are very keen to speak with organisations, large or small, that are planning to bring about performance improvement and achieve cost savings from the procurement of their works, goods and services.
