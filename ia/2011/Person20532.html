Jerry Maurer’s Summary

Jerry Maurer grew up in Brooklyn, NY with a family of 5. Two brothers and two sisters of which one brother is an identical twin.

Upon graduating from Brooklyn College, Jerry landed a position as a video producer at a NY based medical center. Video producing in a medical center is a very unique and creative role. After a few years, it was there Jerry had his first urge of becoming an entrepreneur. "I created my own company and started to video tape weddings & Bar Mitzvahs. Being the boss was a dream come true. My new business was a hit from the start as I started to get referral after referral. I learned quickly that being so busy wasn't such a good thing. I was busy 40 weekends a year, and realized he had created a rut for myself."

It was then Jerry was introduced to network marketing and started to immediately see how he could LEVERAGE more of his time. But it was not until Jerry hit the Internet did he start to achieve REAL leverage & success.

Jerry Maurer is currently the Founder of JL Net Bargains. JL is internet based and sells tools and resources (that help leverage time and make money smarter) to home based entrepreneurs and internet marketers.

Jerry Maurer is also the owner and creator of the 5K Inner Circle. "People are not duplicatable, but systems are. That is why I created the 5K Inner Circle. 5K is a system anyone can plug in to create a strong 5K residual income. It has been a huge success since launching Nov 2010."

Jerry is currently an Internet Network Marketing Mentor, a YouTube Video Marketer, a Skype Enthusiast, and a Social Media Specialist.

Jerry loves helping people change their life and to think positive about working online. His road wasn't all glory! Jerry had to pay his dues to figure out how things work online, but now that he has gone through the "process", he's working hard to build an empire and help others become successful.
