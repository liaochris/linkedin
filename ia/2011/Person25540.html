LaTisha D Styles's Summary

LaTisha Styles ia a recent graduate from Kennesaw State University’s Coles College of Business with a major in Finance.

This past summer she designed a trading strategy based upon fundamental and technical analysis to tackle the spot currency market.

She is currently guiding young adults to financial freedom with her Financial Success for Young Adults blog at www.FSYAonline.com

Her goal is to leverage her knowledge of the financial markets to create economic opportunities for her community.

She is fluent in Spanish, written and oral. She currently holds a Bachelor of Arts in Spanish Language and Culture. She also speaks, reads and writes Portuguese and Japanese, though not fluently.

She enjoys traveling and interacting with various cultures and has visited Spain and Portugal for a semester abroad, Mexico for a missions trip, and Japan, Puerto Rico, and the Bahamas on vacation.

Her community involvement also includes Mount Paran Church of God where she volunteers with autistic children.
