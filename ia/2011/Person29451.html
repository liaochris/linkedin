Md. Saifur Rahman's Summary

Md. Saifur Rahman is Account Manager of Customer Team - Business Development at Nokia Siemens Networks where he spearheads customer engagements for Telenor and Bharti Airtel.

Saifur demonstrated continuous growth, achievements, and exemplary leadership in the management of key account & project activities. Experience include developing business with global carriers while maintaining and growing company’s sales.

Prior to joining NSN, Saifur worked as Head of Technical with NEC Corporation Dhaka Branch Office to build strategy & organization management for it's start-up.Saifur held a variety of positions in Solution Sales Management with Ericsson AB across South East Asia.

Earlier in his career Saifur worked as a faculty member with leading private universities in Bangladesh- East West University (EWU) & Independent University,Bangladesh (IUB) where he taught undergraduate/graduate courses in Telecommunications Engineering.

Saifur holds Bachelor of Science in Electrical & Electronic Engineering from the Islamic University of Technology (Bangladesh), Master of Engineering (Telecommunications) from the Asian Institute of Technology (Thailand), Executive Education in Strategic Marketing from The University of Chicago - Booth School of Business (USA) and is currently studying for Post Graduate Diploma in Global Business at University of Oxford - Said Business School (UK).
