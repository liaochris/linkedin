Steve Dangerfield’s Summary

I enjoy managing projects, maintaining security, and managing IT systems within challenging constraints, resolving difficult issues, and achieving results. I want to continue performing these roles and induce the synergy gained from combining these roles in future engagements.

I am a professionally certified IT Project Manager who is experienced in tailoring PRINCE2 and PMBOK project management methodologies to suit projects of varying complexities and working those methodologies to deliver projects within constraints.

I also have a lot of experience performing Operational Systems Management, Service Delivery and Information Security Management roles.

I have a technical background and use this to great advantage in getting the best out of technical staff.

I am Security Ceared with 7 years working in classified environments.

I am experienced in the use of a wide range of tools, techniques, and the creation/management of documentation expected in the governance of projects.

I currently hold PMP PRINCE2(practitioner) CISSP.

I practice the Getting Things Done productivity methodology.
