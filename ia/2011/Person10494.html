Dave Chaffey's Summary

Dave Chaffey is a specialist in using Digital Marketing to improve company performance. I am a bestselling author, speaker and commentator.

My passion is sharing and educating on the latest best practices to help businesses compete through digital marketing.

My main business is Smart Insights which helps marketers get the most from their digital marketing through our digital marketing advice portal, strategy and analytics consulting, training and performance improvement dashboard software.

I also work for ClickThrough Marketing, the search conversion specialists as Insights Director where I work with our team and clients to develop processes and reporting to get the best results from search marketing.

Finally, Marketing Insights Limited is a company I formed in 2001 used for independent consulting and training to help companies improve their Internet marketing.

Dave has written four best-selling books on E-marketing including ‘Internet Marketing: Strategy, Implementation and Practice’, ‘E-marketing Excellence’ and ‘Total E-mail Marketing’.

Dave was recognised by the CIM, in 2004, as one of 50 marketing ‘gurus’ worldwide who have shaped the future of Marketing.

He is also proud to have been recognized by the Department of Trade and Industry (in 2004) as one of the leading individuals who have provided input and influence on the development and growth of E-commerce and the Internet in the UK over the last 10 years.

In 2010 I was elected an Honorary Life Fellow by The Institute of Direct Marketing in recognition of my work in educating marketers on best practice in digital marketing.

I also do some lecturing on Ecommerce and Emarketing at leading business schools such as the Universities of Birmingham, Cranfield, Leeds and Warwick.

For Dave's latest digital marketing advice, go to http://www.smartinsights.com

Specialties

Internet marketing training, E-marketing courses, Digital marketing consulting. Specialist in: Internet marketing planning & Strategy, Web analytics, Search engine marketing, E-mail marketing and persuasion marketing.

Find out more at http://www.smartinsights.com
