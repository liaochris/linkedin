Shannon Naylor's Summary

Having commenced my career in 1976 as a cadet with the State Energy Commission of Western Australia in power generation projects and many years experience in large EPCM projects in the materials handling area in Iron Ore, Bauxite, Coal, Gold and Diamonds from $5M to $2B; commencing from conceptual studies, feasibilities through to commissioning and handover.
I have held positions across all areas of projects, working from the bottom up over 34 years from working as a concreter, electrical, drywall, bricklaying, and automotive, house building to design drafting, Document control, Logistics, QA/QC, Project Engineering, Project Controls and Management.
I have designed developed and implemented systems with new technology in accordance with the company’s business processes and controls to ensure on time and on budget delivery to the highest standards and quality and safely. Included have been direct relationships with all stakeholders including client, consultant and contractor organizations located in supportive and non-supportive environments.
The training of local nationals in overseas engagement has played a major role in my positions held. Areas of management have been in Estimating, Budget control, Contracting strategy execution and administration. Planning and scheduling. Development of Work breakdown structures, equipment numbering and cost coding, document control and project close out and estimate preparation.
I have extensive experience across all areas on EPCM projects from Feasibility through to Commissioning, handover and Close Out. My career spans over 30 years.
My skills in management of all processes of an EPCM environment are sound. With a ‘Zero Harm,Delivery Focus,One Team’ philosophy.
My abilities and ethics are of the highest standards. I strive to achieve the best in all that I do and have strong analytical abilities. My loyalty and honesty is beyond reproach.
