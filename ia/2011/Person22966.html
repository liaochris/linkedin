Julia Swanwick’s Summary

A Bostonian, I moved to Los Angeles in January of 2006. I graduated with honors from Emerson College in Boston, where I received a BS in Integrated Marketing Communications. I have worked in marketing and public relations roles at several entertainment companies.

Working with the great team at EMI Music Publishing, I serve as Marketing Program Manager. I am also currently working as Marketing Director and North American Representative for KPM Music, a production music library. I work closely with KPM's North American distributor, APM Music, to increase awareness of the KPM brand and boost market share for the library.

Along with working full-time, I am also a part-time student in the MBA program at The University of Southern California, Marshall School of Business. I will graduate May 2012.

I am a devoted fan and supporter of the performing arts. I regularly attend productions from the L.A. Opera, musicals and plays at the Ahmanson Theater and the Mark Taper Forum, and concerts from local bands. I am also an avid museum-goer; my favorite L.A. museum being the Getty Villa in Malibu.
