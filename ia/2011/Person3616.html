Ari Tikka’s Summary

I started my career with ten years of mathematical engineering and SW development around 1985.

Since 1997 I have helped people to work together in a productive and gratifying way. I have coached big and small organizations in leadership, change, culture, process, program and portfolio management. I am happy and excited about working in the Lean and Agile revolution during the last few years.

I am offering a journey of co-learning in what is really going on - to become aware what happens and how it is interpreted. Seeing creates new mental models, new decisions and new actions. I have strong experience and personal interest in working with the organizational culture.

As the consultant and coach, I bring in knowledge, the external observer and the facilitation of the learning process. I draw from solid experience in three diverse fields:
• software development and business
• leading complex and turbulent organizations
• behavior of individuals, groups and organizations

Formal qualifications (more below):
• MSc in Structural Mechanics,
• Organizational Consultant (FINOD),
• Occupational Counseling Consultant (työnohjaaja)
• Certified Scrum Master, Practitioner, and Product Owner
• Zen meditation instructor
