Adam Levine’s Summary

Adam graduated from the Savannah College of Art and Design with a Masters Degree in animation. He is currently working as an animator/designer/art director at All Things Media LLC. His skill-sets include illustration, photography, animation and graphic design.

He has been active in the SIGGRAPH organization since 2002. He started as a student volunteer and worked his way up to volunteer team leader. As a team leader he was responsible for assisting conference organizers in leading various planned activities while directing a diverse group of student volunteers. Recently, he joined the ASIFA East organization and he is eager to become involved. Currently Adam is working on his film "Tuesday," a 3D animated short, which will be ready for screaning later this year.

Adam Levine’s Specialties:

3D Animation and modeling (Maya), 2D Animation, Digital photo retouching and enhancement (Photoshop), Motion graphics and Effects (After Effects), Flash animation, Photography (both film and digital), Video Editing, and Illustration.
