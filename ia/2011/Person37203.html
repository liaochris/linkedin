Robert A. Pabich’s Summary

Robert A. Pabich International Properties specializes in the marketing, sales, sourcing and procurement of properties worldwide.

As a real estate marketing firm, we've created a unique approach to realty, serving both localized regions as well as the international real estate market. With a shift to technology and a change in economic trends, the landscape of the industry has created a competitive atmosphere forcing the extinction of monopolized local territories, effectively opening up new and emerging markets to new agencies and opportunists.

Our firm specializes in marketing exceptional properties around the world to discerning clients across Europe and the Americas. The vision: to reach beyond what is typical; excelling at what few have ventured to create. Through our worldwide network of contacts, we enable international sellers to employ our strategic marketing paradigms to reach a global audience while allowing private buyers and institutional investors to source unique international properties for acquisition.

We work closely with key partners around the world, effectively tapping into exclusive local markets. Through this network, we've create complex multi-national business arrangements and have gained a growing real estate portfolio of properties exceeding € 500M (EUR). With this, we've created a free market approach to international properties, creating a service that allows our clients to find an exclusive luxurious second home or vacation villa, streamline the process of international relocation or find unique foreign investment opportunities. We emphasize building strong, service-oriented relationships with our clients, recognizing value, integrity and creating an approach to property sales and acquisition very few independent agents have conceptualized.
