Jack Duncan's Summary

Jack Duncan, Radiance Road Marketing Owner,

After working in radio, television and print for 13 years, Jack Duncan graduated from Spring Hill College in Mobile, AL in 1989 with a BA in Communication Arts. In 1991, the University of SW Louisiana awarded him a MS in Communications and Jack began working as a small-business marketing, advertising and public relations consultant while teaching college-level communications courses.
After moving to Florida in 1995, Jack joined the MS Foundation as National Public Relations Manager where he wrote articles and worked on developing their website. Amazed at new technologies for communicating, he served as Corporate Relations Director for Matrix Information Systems from 1997-2000 where he employed leading-edge tech for electronic publishing and recruiting on the web.
For the next six years, Jack planned, executed and evaluated marketing projects & campaigns for a variety of businesses as an independent consultant in the Space Coast area (Brevard County). Embracing change and seeking professional growth, he worked for Universal Healthcare in Sales & Marketing as a Medicare Marketing Specialist, Seminar Presenter, Regional Agent Manager and Senior Medicare Marketing Specialist for more than three years, moving to Lakeland in the process. Jack began Radiance Road Marketing to help you, the local business owner in Polk County, to get your BEST prospects to visit your website and BUY your products and services. By appointment only.
