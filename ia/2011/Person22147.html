Jon Chun’s Summary

Jon Chun is an entrepreneurial executive that works to innovate in emerging areas of business and technology. With over 15 years of diverse experience, Chun has taken an interdisciplinary approach to developing new business opportunities. He has over 10 years of business development and marketing experience in the digital media space where he has helped businesses blend together Content, Devices, and Advertising.

Chun has helped pioneer a philosophy of attuning content and devices at some of the leading digital media companies, including Intel’s Content Development Group, Sony Pictures Digital Entertainment, eBay, and THX, Ltd. (Lucasfilm). He was one of the founding members for the Academy of Television Arts & Sciences Interactive Media Group.

Most recently, Chun helped secure venture funding and launch Zadspace, an alternative media advertising network. This experience led him to the development of a new approach to marketing channels and digital media. Chun was invited to present at the DMA 2010 conference in San Francisco.

As an entrepreneur, Chun helped develop new business opportunities and led teams to implement them in digital media, online services, gaming, wireless, ecommerce, direct marketing, and consumer electronics. Chun has extensive experience with business plan development and fundraising as an entrepreneur, a consultant, and advisory board member.

As an executive, Chun has headed the marketing groups for THX, Ltd. (Lucasfilm) and Zadspace. His experience in business development comes from structuring and negotiating over 50 transactions/deals. He has held executive roles with Sony Pictures Entertainment, eBay, and Intel.

Chun earned B.S. degrees in Mechanical Engineering and Materials Science Engineering from the University of California, Berkeley. He also earned his M.B.A. from the UCLA Anderson Graduate School of Management with a concentration in Entertainment and Entrepreneurship.
