Marco Kronen's Summary

Seattle condominium and residential marketing and sales. I am an expert on urban real estate in Seattle.

For SELLERS I provide consultation for optimal pricing and home readiness to ensure the condo appeals the the largest general audience. I use the latest online media, collaboration, and direct marketing techniques to ensure my clients' homes sell as quickly as possible for the highest profit available to them.

A majority of my current business envolves me working with BUYERS of real estate in the city. I focus on ensuring clients locate a home or investment by using as little upfront capital as possible for them. In this market clients are looking for ways to buy real estate in a manner that allows them to preserve capital and save more money at the closing table. By focusing on FHA loan programs, Washingotn State bond down payment assistance programs, and pre screening and qualifiying lenders my clients can buy with less down than they often expect. This allows buyers to be more prepared and save money for emergencies, sudden change in employment, or other unexpected lifestyle changes.

What sets me apart from other real estate agents is my strong network of brokers, real estate buyers and investors, condominium site sales managers and industry players which I work extensively on behalf of my clients. This also helps my team and I stay tightly connected to market trends, optimal pricing strategies, successful marketing methods, technology advances, and our competition.
