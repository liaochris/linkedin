Zack Smith's Summary

As a consultant I have experience with Active Directory integration,and Setting up Apples Open Directory (Open LDAP) server at multiple sites with 10,000-12,000 workstations. I teach Apple's own Directory Service Integration class (among others) and have expert knowledge about most of the core technologies behind Mac OS X. I have years of experience in troubleshooting server and client systems. 
I see work as my life, and so often independently of work requirements, I push myself to constantly expand my knowledge and skill sets. Currently for instance I am endeavoring to learn many of the interpreted languages such as ruby and python, also in the long term I have plans for programming in Objective C. I have taught and consulted for a variety of different institutions including Apple Inc. , Education and Government customers. I have very rarely found something that was beyond my ability to learn. In fact in these positions I am normally learning skills at a very quick pace out of necessity of time, thus requiring me to become a very quick study. I have no problem with travel or giving presentations.
I am a fluent Mac IT developer. I have yet to find a problem across multiple clients requests that given enough time I cannot automate. I enjoy finding custom and creative solutions to complex enterprise problems, such as modifying the source of an application or creating a script to fix a production issue.
