Len Feldman’s Summary

Great product management begins with the customer's needs and wants, leads to products and services with excellent product/market fit, and results in profits and sales growth. That's what I've done for years for technology companies in Silicon Valley and around the world. Whether as an employee, consultant or co-founder, I've managed products through every stage of the product life cycle, from inception to end-of-life.

Whether you're a startup or a multinational, I have the experience to help you. The industries I've worked in include Internet software and services, eBooks, eCommerce, streaming media, computer hardware, software and peripherals.

Len Feldman’s Specialties:

Product marketing, product management, industry analysis, strategic planning, market and technology research, product launches, communication strategies.
