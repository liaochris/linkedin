Mark Jansen, MBA’s Summary

Consulted for a variety of clients independently, including universities, high schools, public institutions, publishing firms, public relations firms and advertising agencies. Advised and produced marketing and media plans targeting the 18-34 year old educated market. I have consulted for a number of for profit and non-for-profit clients: Sallie Mae - Was employed to execute a national program targeting college students who needed financial aid. Employed campus postering, media board placement, college newspapers, college web sites, promotional mouse pads and college-targeted radio. $150,000 was spent on the media placement campaign and did not include the cost of the creative or materials.; St. John’s University of New York - Developed a new student recruitment advertising campaign targeting all high schools and junior colleges; Matthews Media Group in targeting graduating medical doctors for employment; METRA Rail Service in handling all their marketing initiatives and campaigns promoting youth oriented ridership; INcard.com and INsider magazine in establishing national and regional sponsors for their youth discount card and publishing network; United Airlines in designing and constructing a multimedia presentation promoting their new international flight amenities; MediaMix Marketing, Inc. in promoting and executing the national Baywatch Search for new talent. This national talent show event was executed in twenty-five metropolitan markets on college campuses. Extensive time was invested in the organization of each event and included the execution of all marketing, public relations and advertising to promote attendance at each event. Local and national print, radio and television advertising were employed in promoting these events.; INsider Concert Series - Produced and marketed five national college music tours with 75 performances each on college campuses nationwide sponsored by Mystic Ice-T, Jensen Car Stereo and Musicland/SamGoody.
