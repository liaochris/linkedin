Philip (菲利普) Hirschberg's Summary

Mr Hirschberg is founder, CEO and Responsible Manager of boutique corporate advisory firm, Octani Capital. Mr Hirschberg has over 17 years of experience in establishing, building, improving, investing in and raising capital for public and private companies across multiple sectors. Mr Hirschberg is an experienced company director, and has been engaged extensively at board level in the areas of corporate strategy and business planning, mergers and acquisitions, and capital raising, among other things.

Across his career, Mr Hirschberg has held executive and senior management positions with Deloitte, Release Energy, Ernst & Young, and LogicaCMG, and was a Non-executive Director of a Sumitomo Corporation company until joining Deloitte.

Mr Hirschberg holds an Executive Master of Business Administration from the University of Western Australia Business School, where he is an adjunct lecturer of Strategic Management on the Master of Business Administration (MBA) program, and also a Diploma (Company Directors Course) from the Australian Institute of Company Directors. Mr Hirschberg is also currently completing a Master of Applied Finance (Corporate Finance).

Mr Hirschberg is a Senior Associate of the Financial Services Institute of Australasia (FINSIA), and a member of the Australian Institute of Company Directors (AICD) and the Graduate Management Association (GMA).
