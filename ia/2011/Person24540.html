Keith Hanks, MBA’s Summary

Through the management of over 50 SEO and Paid Search online campaigns and projects, Keith brings industry and agency experience to online marketing account services. He is regarded by clients, coworkers and partners as being high energy and results driven -- always having the client’s best interest in mind.

In his primary role, Keith is an Account Supervisor at Response Mine Interactive and a standout among client consulting e-marketing experts in Atlanta, a city that leads the south in online marketing ingenuity and talent. From a quantitative perspective, he is renowned for his commitment to continuously improving online conversion rates through improvements in online search engine visibility.

Keith works with the client to accomplish paid search optimization through bid price strategy, landing page optimization and keyword list (national and local-focus) analysis. On the SEO side, he is known for his ability to manage challenging national-brand SEO campaigns that focus on onsite web architecture analysis, content strategy and offsite challenges such as link building strategy.

Management and Leadership Capabilities: Keith is versatile in his leadership capabilities and can present campaign strategy to various C-Level executives within an organization. He is equally comfortable discussing key performance indicators and metrics with marketing, IT, or finance departments. He is experienced with building and nurturing SEO agency-client relationships, including helping bridge a client’s budgeting goals with an annual online marketing strategy around departmental deliverables and objectives.

Keith is a MBA graduate of Providence College where he authored a master thesis titled, “Non-Traditional Marketing Impacting Multi-Collaborative Networks.” He holds a BSBA from Bryant University (Smithfield, RI) with a double major in Marketing and Computer Information Systems. He resides in metro Atlanta with his wife Nicole.
