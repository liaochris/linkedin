Elizabeth McGregor's Summary

My business, Fine Lines Communications, is all about putting your ideas into words. It’s about taking the vision that you have for your business and translating it into an irresistible presentation for your clients.
I can show you how to create a language for expressing your inspirations and your aspirations. It will change the way you see your business and how others see you.

Specialties

I love words. Words are magical tools that can inform, persuade, inspire and entertain. If used skillfully, words have the power to unlock doors and open minds. I love to write and teach others about writing. My work as a copywriter, editor and speaker is never really work; it is my passion.
I enjoy meeting people who love what they do, so if you feel as passionate about your business as I do about mine, contact me and let’s chat.
