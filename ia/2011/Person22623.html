Joy Pecchia's Summary

Joy Pecchia, Peak Performance, Executive Coach for Successful Men and founder of Behind Boardroom Doors, is devoted to empowering men around the world with exclusive, proven strategies and tools, including her Speed to Advancement™ System designed for the fastest advancing leaders in today’s world. Joy’s clients come to her to achieve their business goals, and ultimately, they get so much more by helping put their entire life in balance, as every aspect of life is intertwined.

Joy has 20 years of executive experience leading small to mid-sized businesses, and has been involved with the world renowned coaching company, Robbins Research
International, an Anthony Robbins Company, since 1999 and has also been an Elite Coach with RRI since 2003.

Joy’s advanced training was with the Coaches Training Institute, Robbins Research International and the Meta Institute.
