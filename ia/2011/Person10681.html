As Northeast Regional Program Manager I am identifying BIM issues along
with implementing BIM solutions to the A/E/C/O community through VDC
(virtual design & construction). We virtually construct all projects and mitigate
risk prior to field work.

I have direct involvement with client/owner presentations, RFP and Scope
preparatation, 3D model creation at any milestone either in Design/Bid/ Build,
Design/Build or IPD environments.

Constructability reviews, Clash/Collision detections, QTO and Discrepancy reporting,
4D & 5D integration, Trade Coordination planning and implementation/facilitating,
As Built Modeling and FM/Manage Modeling to industry standards. 3D Laser Scanning
capable for current condition modeling or BIM commissioning and/or model checking
during construction.

I am fortunate to work with a team of industry leaders and professionals that take on every
BIM challenge, deliver to our clients and learn from every project that we take on.
