Brad Murphy's Summary

Brad has spent in excess of 25 years focused on product and software development innovation, near-shore Agile software development innovation centers and launching innovation start-ups.

Brad’s current focus is on helping companies transition to an adaptive, high velocity Lean Product and Software Development Innovation life cycle supported by well integrated social collaboration communities, Agile Product Management, advanced Agile Engineering and well synchronized release / fulfillment operations.

In addition to his role at Gear Stream, Brad is an angel investor and advisor to several entrepreneurial organizations, including Aurora Funds (one of the largest early stage technology venture firms in the South East) as well as several early stage technology and BPO start-ups.

Prior to founding Gear Stream, Brad was the CEO of Valtech U.S. a large scale Agile advisory and consulting firm. Prior to Valtech Brad was Founder & CEO of digitalESP, a technology outsourcing and consulting service as company later acquired by Valtech.
