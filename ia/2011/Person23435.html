Karen King’s Summary

Karen began her career in "corporate theater" with the Multi Image Group in Boca Raton in 1987, creating graphics for 40 projector slide shows on a Genigraphics computer system that output to film. Transitioning to the first Mac computers; designing theme graphics, animation, charts, maps, etc. Seasoned in big ad agency clients; her career has been exclusively devoted to Fortune 500 presentation graphics. Working either directly with the CEO who is presenting on stage or in a boardroom to a collaborative group effort; Karen possesses the knowledge and experience from storyboards to template design to graphically developing the client's story line to equipment setup and running the show behind the stage. As Karen's colleagues say, "she is grace under fire" in "an industry that craves speed."
