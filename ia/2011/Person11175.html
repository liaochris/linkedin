David Harvey's Summary

An Information Systems executive with broad experience in all aspects of developing, implementing and managing information systems for wireless carriers.

A customer relationship oriented General Manager with an intense focus on results and a proven track record in starting, growing and improving international business operations and systems.

Specialties
