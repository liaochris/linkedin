Larry Maguire’s Summary

I have spent the last 20 years in the Construction and Service Industries during which time I have provided Electrical & Audio Visual Services to both private and business customers.

Our Services include design and installation of Custom Audio Visual, Intelligent Lighting Systems, Smart Home Technology, CCTV, Gate & Door Access, Home Networks, Heating Ventillation & Air Con Control and integration of many other Electrical Systems.

Some of our customers include US Embassy Ballsbridge & Ambassadors Residence Phoenix Park, Aldo International (Retailer), Barrett Cheshire Ireland (Health Care), Kerry Foods (Food Producer), Robert Roberts (Food & Drink) and many other commercial and residential customers.

It is my business goal to contiunue to offer quality to my customers and grow the business into one that is recognised for delivering expert knowledge and installation.
