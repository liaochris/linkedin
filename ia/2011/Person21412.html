Joe Gomez’s Summary

I currently own and operate two Livonia based IT companies. Each company specializes in a variety of services and products.

Dynamic Technologies (aka DynamiTek) provides on site support for small and medium businesses who do not have staffed IT support. DynamiTek is an authorized reseller of Dell equipment. DynamiTek provides the following services, Computer hardware and software support, Computer systems sales and installation, Virus removal, Wired & wireless networking and network security, Site survey and analysis, Web Design, Search Engine Optimization, Web Advertising.


Dynamic Software Group provides web based software solutions for business needs. Our first product to launch in 2008 was DynamiChat. DynamiChat is a fully featured live chat program for websites. Having the ability to chat live and direct with visitors on your website increases sales and customer satisfaction. DynamiChat is versatile enough to fit any business, any website, and provide instant growth and results.

DSG's next project, launched in the first quarter of 2009 is BudgetChoiceComputers.com. Budget choice computers is a web store providing top quality off lease and refurbished computer equipment to the public. BudgetChoiceComputers specializes in laptops, desktops, LCD monitors, and servers. BudgetChoiceComputers was launched to help growing businesses outfit their company with business grade technology at affordable prices.
