Adam Corney's Summary

I work with the internets.

It's a pretty wonderful career to have.

I focus specifically on digital marketing & interactive advertising.

While I find technology fascinating, it's what what people do with technology that is far more interesting.

That explains why I also like working with people and culture.

People don't buy what you do, they buy why you do it.

So I like basing strategies & programs around adventures and cultural tensions and passions and play and interactivity and design and all the fun things that we do to create better experiences.

I like to explore higher concepts and how we can bring deeper levels of meaning into the experiences we create - not just for our business, but for the person on the other end of the communication.

I'm interested in everything we do. You never know where a piece of information may come in handy.
