Miguel Angel Bustamante’s Summary

I've been in the music business for about 9 years now. I've worked at larger professional recording studios and a multitude of smaller independent studios. I've done everything from picking up trash and picking up food to recording and mixing for major label and independent artists. All at its respective time.
3 years ago I ventured into online marketing and the social media world with my website, pulsobeat.com. In April 2010 I made a slight career change and co-founded ACL Management & Marketing to primarily focus on online marketing. 2010 was a big learning process for ACL with all the artists and brands that we promoted including: Placido Domingo Jr., Christopher Von Uckermann, Edwin Bonilla, and SonicProjects Records. It is uncharted territory and I'm loving every minute of it. I love a challenge and social media is what I live, eat and breathe lately. If you have an artist or a brand that is in need of social media and online exposure please don't hesitate to contact me.
