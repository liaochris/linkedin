Ann-Marie McHugh's Summary

Award winning Producer, Director with over 12 years experience creating digital media, TV shows, films, commercials for clients including: Discovery Channel, Food Network, Smithsonian Institute, NBC, PBS Chicago, Logo, MTV, JanSport Internet media, Target, etc. In addition to Media Production, Ann-Marie also offers Production and Performance Consultation.

• Emmy Award winner for creating and producing segments for the show ARTBEAT for PBS. One of the segments "Lion Taymor" is about Julie Taymor and her approach to her work.
• Strong Producer-Director who meets the goals of creating effective and enjoyable media and commercials.

Specialties

Producing, creating digital media and commercials that improve your business or organizations outgoing messages. Story and media development, casting, story editing, scheduling, crew and staff leadership and supervision, independent media production, post supervision & delivery.
