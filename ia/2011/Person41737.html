Steve Spenceley’s Summary

Career Objective
Continue to develop world-class customer experiences that are a leap ahead of the industry standard. As a creative and passionate leader with many years of product management experience I have learned to balance strong product direction requirements with the need for powerful yet easy to use and learn software.

Experience
Interaction design expertise in a wide range of web and software applications, from streaming video on the Web, financial sector solutions, digital asset management, world class 2D paint, 3D paint, award winning 3D automotive solutions, and Oscar winning 3D special effects software.

Five years consulting experience and over ten years, experience with Alias Systems leadership team holding various senior development positions responsible for product management, human computer interaction, while always being the corporate champion for design, software quality, usability, and positive customer experiences at Alias Systems, who are renowned as world leaders in interactive experiences.

Leadership
Through my leadership abilities I have achieved many ‘firsts’ in the technology industry in the area of human computer interaction design and advanced software development. We received the Academy of Motion Pictures Arts and Sciences team Oscar for technical achievement (2002) and Japan’s Good Design Award (2003).

Alias Systems developed award-winning software, custom development and training solutions for the film and video, games, web, interactive media, automotive, industrial design, education and visualization markets. During my career with Alias System I was awarded status as “Technical Director” due to special contribution on the following products; Studio Tools, Maya, and Studio Paint. As part of this experience I have had the pleasure of managing experience design and cross functional product development teams around the world to create some of the world’s best 2D and 3D interactive solutions.

Steve Spenceley’s Specialties:

Specialist in converting complex user requirements into user friendly software, by adopting strong user centered design principles. I’m responsible for the introduction of pen based gesture interaction design used by all Alias products, along with customizable shelf interaction, and art direction of over 700 icons used in Alias Studio. As lead interaction designer it’s been my honor to work on many international award-winning products.
