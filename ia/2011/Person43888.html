Tom Paul's Summary

I have a proven track record in managing large integrated and globally dispersed teams over my professional career. I’ve done this by being a strong manager and knowing the simple fact, that I am only as successful as my team makes me. Therefore, every team that I manage has my direct attention regardless of level and capacity. While I am extremely professional in my approach, I also let my proverbial hair down to connect. This is why I get results! It helps that I have a keen sense of Vision for any organization I work for, seeing the great opportunities that exist and appropriately setting the right course for action.

While managing my team successfully is an absolute, I should also state that working across functional areas is a passion. The relationship builder in me enjoys this aspect of my work. It gives me great satisfaction to bring people together from different areas to accomplish one common goal.

To be successful, one must also compliment their conceptual talent and tangible managerial skills with the proper tools to ensure an efficient critical path is established for success. I have created RACI documents, used flow charts, and compliance metrics to help achieve my teams and ultimately my goals.

Specialties

Developing multi-department synergies, team development and management (P&L, Utilization, Training & Development, Escalation process, etc), Market Research Operations (Translations, Multi-country PM, PMO vision and execution, multi-methodology knowledge (F2F, CAWI, CAPI, CASI)), Change managment.
