Emmanuel Carcassonne's Summary

Mr. Carcassonne is the founder and CEO of APECYD International, a sourcing company in China. The company offers its services to western businesses, acting as a sourcing and purchasing office for its customers and supervising their manufacturing projects in China. APECYD offers a turnkey service: from initial factory audit to final goods delivery including quality control and logistics organization. APECYD is also a platform to explore business opportunities, currently the company is working on projects such as a brand consulting offer for Chinese factories, niche products distribution in Europe, as well as importing and distributing products in China.

Prior to this he had his first entrepreneurial experience founding CAMARIN Ingénierie, a computer services company in Paris; he sold his shares to his partner while leaving for China in 2006.

He holds an International Executive MBA from IE Business School and a Master’s Degree in Computer Engineering from ESIEA Paris.

Specialties

Management of Businesses in start-up and expansion phases: including all aspects (Strategic Management, Financial Management, Marketing Management, HR Management), Operations Management in Asia, Business opportunities identification and pursuit, International trade.
