Bill VanGorder’s Summary

Bill VanGorder is one of only two consultant's in Canada who has earned both his CAE (Certified Association Executive of CSAE -- Canadian Society of Association Executives) and CFRE (Certified Fund Raising Executive of the AFP -- Association of Fundraising Professionals)
His goal is to help both for-profit and not-for-profit businesses and associations change the way they hire to assure they find and keep the best staff in these turbulent times.
Bill's 42+ years experience as a manager, a CEO and a consultant give him the background and experience to help you know about your people to make your organization more successful. That’s why he offers a process to help you get smarter about the way you hire, engage and retain the best people for your organization. Bill will hep you learn how to attract the right candidates, how to identify top performers versus poor performers, how to use assessment tools to improve organizational performance, and so much more.
