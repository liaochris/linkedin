Mike Wallace’s Summary

Almost 20-years experience delivering strategic advisory services to corporations, government agencies and non-governmental organizations (NGOs) on sustainability strategies.

A specialist in creating business opportunities from the sustainability trends that are emerging in the global financial markets. Expertise in identifying environmental, social & governance risks and opportunities and providing strategic guidance on measuring, managing and reporting performance.

Speak and write regularly on the subject of corporate social responsibility and sustainability and is a contributing author on “Climate Change: A Guide to Carbon Law and Practices”.
