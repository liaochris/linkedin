Charlie Snyder's Summary

Originally from Houston Texas, I believe the southern values instilled in me from a very young age have helped make me a successful person and business professional. I am a Cartus relocation network preferred agent where I work with Military, Government and Corporate relocation clients. I place the needs of my client’s first and place high value in working with my team and business partners to provide you with the level of service and commitment to quality that you expect and deserve.

Additionally, you have my personal assurance that I will be with you every step of the way, removing obstacles and anticipating challenges that may arise within the complexities of a real estate transaction.

Prior to entering the Real Estate profession, I enjoyed a 10-year career in the management-consulting field where I worked closely with all levels of both government and commercial organizations to advise my clients in the best manner to execute and achieve their strategies and objectives. Additionally, I hold a BBA from Baylor University and an MBA from the University of Houston. With my diverse experience and education; I am able to quickly customize my approach based on the individual needs of my clients in order to provide them with Respect and Personal Service with a Difference!

Specialties

Educating and Advising clients looking to buy, sell or rent properties across the Northern VA marketplace. Our organization is part of a large network of relocation service providers under the CARTUS umbrella. CARTUS selected New Millennium to assist their clients partly because of our exceptional customer service reputation and in part because we are a large Century 21 company capable of reaching around the world to provide relocation assistance.
