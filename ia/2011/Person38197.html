Robert Treat, Ph.D.’s Summary

Senior educational evaluator and psychometrician with 10 years experience in medical education.

o Provide descriptive, inferential, and psychometric analyses for medical student and graduate medical education-related performance-based learner assessments, 360° ratings, and standardized patients to simulations. Analyze and interpret data, present assessment/evaluation results to committees.

o Consult and develop learner assessment, ACGME competency-related instruments and evaluation checklists, logs, surveys.

o Strong background in the use of analytical tools in education and the natural sciences. Experience as a researcher, evaluator, consultant, and teacher help provide faculty and staff clear and concise methods to acquire, measure, analyze and interpret data to inform their educational practice(s).
