Bombet Stephane’s Summary

Stephane Bombet is today’s Renaissance man. International nightclub owner, business developer, music publisher, restaurateur, music manager, marketing and PR executive - Stephane’s varied background is backed up with a chain of successful businesses and projects. He has international appeal, with vast networks in many countries. Stephane also extends his expertise to non-profit projects and is a firm believer in the green initiative.

Currently, Stephane serves as Co-Founder and GM for Anticucho Restaurant & Tapas Bar. This is a brand new restaurant concept due to open in September 2010. Stephane has put together a great team of professional in the FOH and a world acclaimed chef behind the grill:). More info will be revealed soon!

Stephane has opened his own music management company called VO Management in 2009. He is representing singer-songwriters and producers.

For seven years, Stephane Bombet served as the Executive Vice President of Right Bank Music and Right Bank Management. The company directed operations in Paris, London, and Berlin. As head of Right Bank Music and Management worldwide, Stephane directed all music publishing operations, ran the management company, formulated and implemented company goals, policies and procedures, directed artist and writer development, managed the company catalogs and promoted the international expansion of the company.

During Stephane’s tenure at Right Bank Music, he had 11 recordings on #1 records in six different countries, cuts on multi platinum albums, high placement across Billboard magazine charts and a Song of the Year award at the 2007 ASCAP Pop Awards.

Prior to moving to the U.S. in 2001, Stephane owned SB Communications, a public relations, marketing and branding company in Paris. He was also a successful restaurateur and eventually became a partner of the VIP Room on the Champs Elysees and in St. Tropez.
