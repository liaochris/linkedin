Partha Chakraborty, Ph.D., CFA's Summary

Dr. Partha Chakraborty is a Founding Partner at Angle Light. Partha has over a decade of investment experience with leadership roles covering all parts of investment value chain, across multiple asset classes, geographies and client bases. Most recently Partha was a senior investment risk professional at Western Asset Management Company, a top global fixed income asset manager based in California. Previously Partha headed investments for a Bermuda based hedge fund turned NYSE listed reinsurance company Flagstone Reinsurance; was a Director of Asset Allocation at Bank of America; led US Financials equity research and investments for institutional accounts at Nomura Asset Management as a Vice President; and researched balance sheet restructuring deals for global life insurance companies at Lehman Brothers. Partha attended Harvard Business School Executive Program for Senior Investment Managers, has a Ph.D. in Economics from Cornell University, Ithaca, New York as well as degrees in Statistics and Economics from the Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata, India. During graduate school at Cornell, Partha co-founded an Internet startup called Bookwormbooks.com. Partha is a CFA Chart holder and is a member of CFA Society. Partha has already lived and worked in six different countries and currently lives in Pasadena, CA.

Specialties

Quantitative Investments, Hedge Funds, Risk Management, Asset Allocation, Investment Strategy, Equity Research, Equity Portfolio Management, Investment Analytics, Quantitative Research, ABS Research, Team Leadership, International work experience and Entrepreneurship.
