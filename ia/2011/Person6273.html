Brian Marantz's Summary

I am an 18 year industry veteran in software QA. I have successfully tested and shipped multiple retail software products during this time--v1 products, major releases, and service pack updates used by millions of customers. I have been an integral part of teams building and testing hardware and software on embedded devices using Linux.

Specialties

Test Processes, Test Development, Cellular GSM Telephony, 802.11 WiFi, Bluetooth, UI testing, and management of QA relationships with 3rd parties.
