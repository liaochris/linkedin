Wrenda Goodwyn’s Summary

Southwest Florida-based certified, professional interior decorator who transforms homes to fit the client's lifestyle and budget. Her column, Home Inspirations, appears in the Fort Myers News-Press.

With a career that began as a journalist and led to senior-level marketing positions with Walt Disney World, major hotel corporations and an art museum, Wrenda followed her passion for interior decorating and opened Spectacular Spaces in 2001.

An A.S.I.D. Industry Partner, she is also a member of the Interior Design Society. She trained and was certified in New York with "Use What You Have" founder, Lauri Ward, well-known Manhattan designer and best-selling author. In 2010, she received the Gold Member award from the Interior Redecorator's and also earned her IRN Green Certification.

For the past ten years, she has been helping her clients lead a more balanced life by transforming their spaces with practical decorating solutions and design ideas that are easy and affordable to implement. She is a popular speaker and frequent guest on FOX 4 Rising, Thrive in Balance radio and has been featured in the Fort Myers News-Press and the Naples Daily News. Her monthly column, "Home Inspirations," appears in the News-Press.
