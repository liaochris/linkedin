Jesse Slome’s Summary

The American Association for Long-Term Care Insurance and the American Association for Critical Illness Insurance are the national professional organizations for insurance and financial professionals. Our primary role is educating consumers to the risk and options available. We also support insurance and financial professionals with education and marketing support to help them serve their clients and prospects. Jesse Slome is founder and director of both organizations. He is an award-winning marketing and public relations executive having authored numerous articles and books on various insurance and financial topics.
