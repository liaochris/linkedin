Threta Davis, MBA’s Summary

Threta Davis is a Pharmaceutical Professional with extensive background and knowledge in global regulatory affairs publishing/submission support, global contract management, technical systems consultant, and domestic and international training/facilitator.

Threta's most recent position is Senior Manager of Regulatory Affairs Publishing and Submission Support with Alcon Laboratories, Inc. where she led the team for the implementation of global eCTD submissions to health authorities and domestic electronic SPL labeling to the FDA.

Prior, Threta was the manager of the Global Legal Contract Management System and Department with Alcon Laboratories, Inc.
