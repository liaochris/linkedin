James Cullinan's Summary

James is a resourceful, energetic and insightful advisor with a sharp focus on the drivers of business performance. He has developed strategic growth platforms for large corporations such as GlaxoSmithKline and NatWest Bank, as well as commercial strategies several start-up ventures in the Arc Lémanique.

He uses his background in change management at KPMG Consulting, to take a holistic, cross-functional approach to the strategic initiatives he implements, and to compress the time taken to embed the desired results.

James has launched several business initiatives of his own, including BusNet, a local “network of networks” and Se-la-vie, an online stop-smoking programme.

James has an MA in Philosophy, Politics and Economics from Oxford and an MBA from INSEAD, where he was awarded the Sasakawa Young Leader Foundation Scholarship.

James speaks fluent English and French

Specialties

programme management, innovation process, strategic retreat, business model optimisation, value proposition development, financial modelling, scenario analysis, building capability, business development, network marketing, negotiation, outsourcing, performance metrics, business networking.
