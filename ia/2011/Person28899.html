Matt Davis's Summary

After graduating from NYU's Tisch School of the Arts, I began by cutting music videos for The Bee Gees, Selena, and Yo La Tengo, but the major arc of my career has been editing comedy TV shows such as MTV's "HUMAN GIANT" and "AUSTIN STORIES", "MAD-TV", John Leguizamo's "HOUSE OF BUGGIN'", and Robert Smigel's "TV FUNHOUSE."

I was one of two editors who cut the pilot for MAD-TV and also directed segments featuring Orlando Jones, David Herman, Phil LaMarr, Debra Wilson, and Nicole Sullivan.

I was supervising editor of Martin Short's improvisational series, "PRIMETIME GLICK," for Comedy Central (you might even see me appear in a couple of episodes), and of Short's feature film, "JIMINY GLICK IN LALAWOOD."

Since returning to New York, I have edited the SpikeTV show "MoCap, LLC," created by Chris DeLuca, "BACK FROM HELL," a documentary on the late Sam Kinison, the MTV series, "MEGADRIVE" starring Johnny Pemberton, and "The Onion Network News" for IFCTV.

Specialties

I am a member Director's Guild of America (Category: Director) and the Motion Picture Editors Guild (Category: Editor).

I can edit self-supervised on DGA signatory productions, under the category of Associate Director.
