Nigel Dunand's Summary

Tired of hearing “Sales is a numbers game!” “50% of marketing expenditure is wasted; we just don't know which 50%!" “Ask for the order" “Ask open ended questions!" "Be persistent!” ?

I was!

With a background in engineering and many years spent in Industrial Automation, I was frustrated with the lack of predictability of the sales and business development process.
Then I stumbled across the Sandler concept. Counter-intuitive and contrary to anything else I'd every heard, it provided the process for predictable success for which the engineer in me was looking .
My Sandler team and I now work with open minded,committed , and ambitious businesses to implement this proven methodology into their world.

How do prospective clients start the investigatory process? - By asking to attend a 2 1/2 - hour interactive seminar we call a Directors Briefing. Or just pick up the phone and call!

My Goals:
To restore dignity to the 'sales' process.
To be the person my 4 year old thinks I am.
