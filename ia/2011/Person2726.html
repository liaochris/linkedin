Andri Yadi's Summary

Andri Yadi is the founder and CEO of DyCode. Andri is a geek, an educator, and an entrepreneur in Information Technology field.

As a geek, Andri is a coder, system architect, business analyst, IT pro, and project manager for 10 years experiences and has been involved in more than 20 software development projects. He has become a coder since 15 years old, make coding has become a hobby and a job of living for him. He has expertise in multi platform technologies, started from open source technologies and currently focus to Microsoft .NET framework. He is a natural and fast learner, and always passionate in exploring and adopting latest technologies. Under his leadership, DyCode has been considered as early adopter of several .NET latest technologies, such as: ASP.NET 3.5, ASP.NET AJAX, LINQ, Silverlight, VSTO, and WCF. In software development, he likes to develop frameworks, libraries, and controls. He is the mastermind behind software architecture, framework, and best practices used in DyCode. In geek community, Andri is active in INDC mailing list and blogging some useful and sophisticated topics in INDC Geeks Blog.

As an educator, Andri is a lecturer of Q-College, a well-known IT college in Bandung. He has become a trainer of several trainings in .NET, Java, and PHP since 2002. Currently, he is often involved in .NET developer community as a frequent speaker at several events, such as: MSDN day, Expert Gym, and other Microsoft Indonesia’s events. He also often delivers speaking in several entrepreneurial talks.

As an entrepreneur, Andri has co-founded three IT companies prior to DyCode since 2003. In the beginning of 2007 he co-founded his fourth company, DyCode. Under his leadership, DyCode has been turned into a well known and strategic ISV within less than one year.

Andri received his undergraduate title in Physics from Bandung Institute of Technology (Institut Teknologi Bandung – ITB) and achieved Cum Laude.

Specialties

Objective-C, iOS SDK, Nokia Qt SDK, BlackBerry SDK, Visual Studio Tools for Office (VSTO), ASP.NET, SharePoint, Windows Communication Foundation (WCF), OData/WCF Data Services
