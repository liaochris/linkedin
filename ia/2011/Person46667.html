Software development veteran. Persistent, resourceful and getting things done. Taking ideas and turning them into products. I breath technology and excel in learning new areas. Over the past 20 years, I performed various roles in software developments. Vast experience in software architecture, design (mostly tech, but functional as well) and coding. Managing software development teams, working with large (tier-0 Telcos) international customers, pre-sales support (RFP handling, presentations, POCs) and professional services. The software is currently on sale and generates revenue. I formed partnerships, joined as a member in the Eclipse foundation and even negotiated with a potential buyer for the company.

I'm an advocate of Open Source technologies and an Apple fanboy. I don't work with Microsoft-related technologies, although I've done so in the past.
