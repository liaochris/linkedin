Charlotte Hagard’s Summary

Being a Career expert,consultant, entrepreneur, speaker and trainer for over 20 years, I have worked with thousands of people in transition to help them find an inspiring and fulfilling worklife.
I am also an author in the Career Development field with a number of popular books, ex CV-boken, Bli din egen coach, Ansökningsboken, Jobbsökaren Verktygslåda och Din karriär, vems ansvar?. My books are called in English;
"The Cover letter book - how to market yourself "; “Become your own Coach”; “The CV-book – the art of marketing your competence";"The Job Searchers Toolbox” and “Who is responsible for your Career?”

I also run my own publishing business, Newstart förlag.

Over the years I have developed an extensive expertise in developing career development products, services and systems. I have created numerous structured and creative tools, workshops and web-based career planning tools that have been implemented in hundreds of organizations throughout Scandinavia.

My professional focus has been developing integrative, user-friendly systems that serve the needs of both organizations and individuals. These include succession planning, mentoring, selection, coaching, career management and leadership.

Holding a BSc. from the University of Uppsala,
I have been very fortunate to get a lot of media coverage and have over the years been frequently quoted in daily papers and magazines throughout Sweden and published widely in a variety of journals. I have given numerous public speaches and workshops in Sweden and abroad on such topics as “Become your own coach”, “Internal Career and Change Management”, “Career Coaching for Managers and HR”, “Building your own brand”, “ Building a new industry and being an entrepreneur in Sweden” and much more.

I live in Sollentuna, a suburb of Stockholm with my husband Johan and my two sons Peter born 1995 and Martin, born 1991.
