Willem Maas’s Summary

Entrepreneurial executive with deep experience taking advantage of market opportunities to launch viable products, differentiate them, and evolve and grow with market response.

Most recently, Willem was founder and CEO of GreenHomeGuide.com (acquired by U.S. Green Building Council), the leading online resource for green home improvement, providing expert Q&A and local directories of green professionals.

Before GreenHomeGuide, Willem held product management and product marketing roles in the enterprise software industry, most recently as Vice President of Product Line Management at HNC Software (acquired by FICO). Previously, Willem led product marketing for Prime Response, Inc. and was Product Manager for the Likeminds personalization product at Andromedia.
