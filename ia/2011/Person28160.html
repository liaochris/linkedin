Mark Buchheim (mark@buchheim.com)’s Summary

Mark Buchheim has more than 18 years of experience with large scale, high performance Internet and video production infrastructure. As Director of Digital Media and Video Production Operations for AOL, Mark led a large team across two production studios Los Angeles and New York and is responsible for live studio production, post-production, motion graphics, application development, digital asset management, digitizing, compression, and archiving. 1,000 audio and video assets were created and delivered on a weekly basis to a diverse set of AOL web properties; amassing a media archive of more than 180,000 high quality digital files.

Previous to this position Mark held several others at AOL, including Digital Media Systems Architect and Senior Database Administrator. Before joining AOL, he was also helped launch the popular CalendarLive website for the Los Angeles Times as an Application Developer.

Mark also founded On The Net Yet, Inc., where he managed design and implementation of Internet sites, applications, video production, and backend server infrastructure for brands such as AOL Time Warner, MTV, Nickelodeon, Peterson Publishing, EMap USA, FHM Magazine, Teen Magazine, Surfer Magazine, Skateboard Magazine, Snowboard Magazine, Pittard Sullivan, Ironclad Performance Wear, EAgents (acquired by American Greetings), Leads360, and ReelPlay.
