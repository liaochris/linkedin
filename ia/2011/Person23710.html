  • Senior Closing Coordinator

    Transfer My Timeshare, LLC

    (Online Media industry)

    August 2007 — December 2008 (1 year 5 months)

    Combining my talent in customer service with my devotion to detail, I was able to become the first Closing Coordinator in the Dover, NH office at Transfer My Timeshare, LLC. I learned what needed to be done quickly, and taught myself the most efficient way of handling the job.
    The job needed someone with an acute sense of attention to detail, as new deeds needed to be created and contracts needed to be looked over thoroughly, so as not to put the buyer or seller at any risk. I was able to complete a timeshare closing in about two months. This is typically unheard of in the timeshare closing world, and I like to think my hard work had something to do with that.
    This position strengthened my organization skills, my ability to be cool under pressure, and the ability to complete my work on the deadline given.

  • Client Care Specialist

    SellmytimeshareNOW

    (Online Media industry)

    January 2006 — August 2007 (1 year 8 months)

    The client care specialist position in this company focuses on helping the clients to figure out the best pricing for their timeshares to sell. We also had a daily pile of "Seller's Que's" which needed to be completed each day. Each Seller Que had price changes, seller's complaints or questions, and directions on how they wanted their Client Care Specialist to help them. I would complete at least 80 of these daily.
    We needed to have incredible product knowledge and most of us had more knowledge on timeshares than others in the company. I successfully helped my clients over the phone and through email, in a fast, informative, and friendly manner.
    While working in the Client Care office, the company's BBB rating was at an all time high thanks, in large part, to the great team we had.
    Through working in this department, I gained incredible product knowledge, problem solving skills, and a unique sense of knowing exactly what needs to be said or done at any given time.

  • Business Development Coordinator

    Key Auto Group

    (Automotive industry)

    August 2004 — September 2006 (2 years 2 months)

    I worked for all 5 dealerships (Melrose Dodge, Portsmouth Chevrolet, Key Auto Center, Portsmouth Used Car superstore and Salem Ford/Hyundai) in an office in Portsmouth, NH. Each day, we received around 50 leads each from people looking for a specific vehicle. It was our job to get a certain amount of customers to come to the dealership, we would receive a commission for each show and a higher commission for each customer who purchased.

I also worked the 1-800 line for the company, which led me to use the multi-lined phone system and direct calls to the appropriate individuals. I learned great customer service skills there, and also honed my sales skills a great deal. It's a great skill to get a person not really that interested in buying a car, to come in and buy.
