Louis Olave’s Summary

Co-founder of Rapid Satellite, the largest online marketer and distributor of U.S. satellite media & entertainment services, generated over $40M in annual revenues within three years of inception.

Accomplished executive specializing in strategic media dissemination, customer acquisition and digital brand strategy for Fortune 100 companies. Successful in leading high grossing, interactive campaigns across evolving media platforms for clients in telecommunications, internet technologies and consumer services. Experienced marketing operations expert recognized for forging key relationships within the U.S. and Latin American media markets.

His career began on Wall Street in equities, options on futures, and ADR's, where he honed his skills in the dynamic environments of the trading floors of both the New York Stock Exchange and the New York Mercantile Exchange, and on the Cantor Fitzgerald Latin American Markets Desk. Transitioned to Hedge Fund manager of Upper Ridge Capital Management in early 1999 capitalizing on substantial institutional client relationships established on Wall Street. He successfully navigated through the boom and bust of volatile Internet stock IPO and secondary offerings before co-founding a series of successful start-up ventures focused on satellite services and the media industry.

Fluent in English, Spanish, and Portuguese.
