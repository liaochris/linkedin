Liz Hernández’s Summary

Born in New York City and educated through high school in San Juan, Puerto Rico, Liz Hernández moved to Tucson where she received a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration and later completed courses towards a Masters degree in Art History at the University of Arizona.

Liz Hernández has been actively involved as an art broker, art consultant, independent curator and gallery owner with contemporary Liz Hernández Gallery in Tucson and Hernández Contemporary Fine Art in Scottsdale.

As a bilingual, bicultural professional, who has lived, worked and volunteered in Arizona for over twenty-five years, Liz Hernández served on several community and professional boards including The Foundation for Creative Broadcasting, Girls Scouts of America, Tucson Museum of Art and Tucson Pima Arts Council. In Phoenix, she served on the board of the Scottsdale Gallery Association, as an ASID Industry Partner, a member of Contemporary Forum, and currently serves as President of the Latin American Art Alliance of the Phoenix Art Museum.

  • Owner

    Liz Hernández Gallery (Sole Proprietorship)

    (Sole Proprietorship; Public Relations and Communications industry)

    September 2003 — September 2006 (3 years 1 month)

    The Liz Hernández Gallery is an innovative art venue created to make it easy and enjoyable for emerging and established collectors to own original works of art. By selecting serious contemporary local and international artists who are committed to furthering their artistic careers, the Liz Hernández Gallery offers clients the opportunity to enhance the aesthetic quality of their living and work spaces with fine art. Our goal is to offer high-quality contemporary painting and sculpture at affordable prices.

    A unique idea of the Liz Hernández Gallery is to incorporate the use of a “virtual gallery,” which links a closed computer system to a 42-inch plasma screen which displays digital images of art work. Visible at all times through the front gallery window, the “virtual gallery” offers our clientele a way to view multiple works with the color, depth of field, image clarity and resolution rivaling the live viewing experience. By making the entire collection available on the plasma screen, Liz Hernández Gallery creates viewing and acquiring opportunities that extend beyond the gallery site. This innovative use of plasma technology distinguishes Liz Hernández Gallery from the rest of the gallery community.

    Liz Hernández believes that living with art is a personal and unique experience, yet it is also an integral part of interior design. She also believes that it is better to own one original piece of artwork than several reproductions because original artwork adds to the comfort, distinctive vision, expression and meaning to our lives.
