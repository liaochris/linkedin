    WRR Classical 101.1 FM

    (Government Agency; Performing Arts industry)

    May 2006 — Present (4 years 9 months)

    WRR Classical 101.1 FM operates under FCC License and is the only municipally-owned commercial station in the United States dedicated to classical music. WRR was the first licensed broadcast station in Texas. Today, the station's 100,000 watt signal brings the greatest classical music to a listening area spanning 100 square miles. In early 2009, WRR set up a satellite studio at One Arts Plaza in the Dallas Arts District. This studio, known as the "WRR Music Lounge" continues to host some of the biggest names in the arts world as well as providing a platform to expose aspiring young musical talent. In July, WRR welcomed nearly 100,000 of our nearest and dearest friends to the Cottonbowl in FairPark for A FairPark Fourth which featured the park, a live broadcast of the Dallas Wind Symphony and fireworks by the world famous Grucci Brothers. WRR's locally produced programming received 9 prestigious MarCom awards including 3 Platinum awards for radio and print advertising in the past year.
