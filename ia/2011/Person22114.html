Jonathan Seidenfeld’s Summary

Experienced game industry veteran. I have worked in a variety of positions, mostly in a business development capacity with major companies like Sony Computer Entertainment and Sega, as well as with smaller companies and start-ups. I work best when challenged to create partnerships that produce lasting value for all involved.

As one of the first employees at SCEA I was responsible for attracting Third Party support for the Sony PlayStation, then participated in the PlayStation launch. More recently I was with VestGame Entertainment in Shanghai to oversee the launch of their free-to-play online game in North America. I help companies to establish the right partnerships for successful business between China and the West.

In my present role, I have become a full fledged publisher of Asian online games in overseas markets, and am happy to discuss ideas for cooperation with all interested parties.
