Pushkar Sane's Summary

I help Marketers navigate through the digitized world & create ideas that help them to connect (meaningfully) with Human Beings and ultimately build brand value. I also enable them to learn more about new technologies & bring alive the implications for their brands & businesses.

All through my career I have been fascinated by observing & working in three Billion plus markets – China, India & the Internet. Starting in Account Management I have worked across all functions of modern communications business and as a result I know how different pieces fit to complete the brand jigsaw. I have gained valuable business understanding by virtue of working with Clients from diverse industry sectors (IT, Electronics, Auto, CPG, F&B, Travel, and Financial Services). Having worked with talented people from different nationalities I have come to respect and appreciate cultural differences. Experimentation has been an integral part of my life and as a result I have taken up challenges in uncharted territories. My passions include Reading, Travel, Sports, Photography, Music and Films.

I currently lead Digital Practice for Starcom MediaVest Group in North & South Asia and also provide Strategic Leadership for the Social Marketing Practice. I previously worked at Foote Cone & Belding (now DRAFTFCB) and Euro RSCG Worldwide.

Specialties

Understanding People, Business Development, Client Servicing, Strategic Planning, Digital Marketing, Data Driven Marketing, Brand Management, and Customer Relationship Management.
