Robin Rogers's Summary

I match real estate to people, both as homes and as good investments. I love working with prospective buyers, tenants, and investors to find them the right property for their needs and financial goals. I work with people relocating to the area in the military or transferring jobs, retirees or people looking for a vacation home, and investors searching for a high-quality rental property.

I know what home buyers and tenants like in a home. Looking at a whole bunch of houses helps. Listening to what my clients say (and don't say) they want does, too!

Specialties

My skills include research, writing, graphic and website design, digital photography, social psychology, residential real estate investment, leasing, and property management.

I've worked on several remodeling/rehabbing projects, both on my own properties and for my clients. I consult with sellers to recommend updates and additions that will net them highest return.
