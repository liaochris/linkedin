Sujai.G Pillai's Summary

Sujai.G.Pillai founded 2tion.com, which is being funded by Chennai funds and later acquired by EdServ Softsystems Ltd. Now he serves as Head of Mobile Division in EdServ.

Sujai has over 10 years of experience in the field of education. He was working as an educational consultant for World Book and Britannica in Oman and UAE. He specializes in netpreneurship and is the co-author of the books "Browse and Grow Rich" and "Money From Internet". He has an MBA in Marketing and Systems from Madurai Kamaraj University. He is co-founder for websites like areapartner.com, humthum.com , mandapam.com
