Amy DeBerg-Ferwerda's Summary

Executive Search Professional - Since 1996

Together with client companies, the team at R&P Group locates, recruits, and helps hire the best people in the Cards and Payments Worlds. This includes C-level jobs as well as jobs in Sales, Business Development, Relationship Management, Marketing, Product Development, Product Management, Risk, Commpliance, Operations, and Technology.

Our Clients include: issuers, processors, consumer and corporate credit companies, and merchant processors, card printers and fulfillment as well as the companies who provide additional products and services to our industry.

Because your hiring decisions are your single most important responsibility as a manager, your choice of a recruiting firm is critical. The quality of your recruiting firm determines if you will even get to review and talk with the best people for your jobs. And most importantly, an outstanding recruiting firm helps to ensure that you will be able to construct an offer that will be accepted and seen as fair by your first choice candidate.
