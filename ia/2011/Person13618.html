Emily Lautenbach's Summary

Emily is a retail business management professional with a passion for the consumer and the retail process.

Emily brings a breadth of experience to the table and has a solid understanding of the many facets of a retail business. She catches on to new systems quickly and is constantly working to make things better and more efficient. She is also called on frequently by her peers for advice and knowledge and by her superiors for special committees, projects and advice.

Emily has been a member of a number of cross-functional teams and prides herself on her ability to understand and work well with other parts of the business.

Emily has also has a passion for marketing and has served as a marketing consultant for various organizations including Gerald R. Ford Job Corps Center, GFT, Hudsonville Ice Cream, Godfrey-Lee Public Schools and GVSU iTunes University. She likes to spend her free time reading about, observing and understanding consumers and their behavior.

Emily is familiar with a variety of Merchandising tools including JDA Space Planning, Manugistics, BI Query, Enterprise Planning, Arthur Allocation, Discoverer and Microstrategy.

Specialties

competitive analysis, project coordination, allocation, demand forecasting, marketing, product performance analysis, assortment planning, relationship building, leadership skills
