Andrew Wiseman's Summary

Andrew leads the Telecoms, Media and Technology team for ICM Research. In addition, he is responsible for advanced analytics for the research division.

Prior to ICM, Andrew held the role of Partner at Verve, the specialist in customer advisory panels & online brand communities.

Andrew joined Verve in 2009 having previously held Chief Executive roles at Optimisa Research and Quaestor. Prior to this, Andrew spent a successful 12 year period working in senior roles at Nunwood Consulting, and in ACNielsen’s global analytics business.

With a strong track record in delivering business focused solutions to clients in sectors as diverse as retail, FMCG and telecommunications, Andrew’s key interest is in the development of commercial, rather than academic solutions to today’s business problems. More recently, Andrew has increased his focus on developing links between research data and actual business performance, building on his not insignificant skills in the area of marketing mix evaluation, including the development of models aimed at better understanding of the longer term financial benefits of investment in advertising.

Sectors worked in:
Modelling experience focused in the FMCG space – working globally with brands such as Coca-Cola, Kraft and Procter & Gamble. More recent experience in understanding the marketing mix for retail, health and financial services clients. A track record in customer experience across a number of sectors – notably telecoms, retail and financial services, with an emphasis on linking satisfaction/loyalty measures to the bottom line. Also broad experience in the travel and transportation sector – working with major UK train operating companies and international tour operators.
