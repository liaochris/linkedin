Jacki Easlick's Summary

Highly motivated creative design professional with significant and progressive experience in branding strategies, communication and sales strategies, budget management, and design identity serving three Fortune 500 companies as well as entrepreneurial interests. Background includes fashion accessories design and 3-dimensional package design. Also art directing photoshoots, video shoots, and designing literature, videos, showroom environmental graphics, multimedia, web sites, and mobile showrooms. Strong knowledge and experience of the selling process, promotions and fashion accessories.

Specialties

Designing, sketching, color trends, key silhouettes, shopping, travel to Tokyo, Hong Kong, China, and Taiwan for manufacturing, print patterns, hardware development.
