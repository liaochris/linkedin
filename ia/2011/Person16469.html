Rob Mayer's Summary

I am the founder and owner of Graphic Technologies, Inc., a commercial printing company located Omaha, NE.

We provide commercial printing services to a widely varied client base. We specialize in printing for corporations, advertising agencies, schools and non-profit organizations.

Our Mission is to give you beautifully printed materials that meet your deadlines, work within your budget and present your company or organization in ways that will impress your clients, employees, audience and customers.

Specialties

We are best known for banners, booklets, bookmarks, brochures, business cards, cd/dvd sleeves, calendars, door hangers, envelopes, greeting cards, labels, letterhead, magazines, note pads, postcards, posters, presentation folders, rack cards, statement stuffers, table tents.
