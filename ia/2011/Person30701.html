Résumé de Michael Kamp

Lindus Advisors, Inc. is a private money management firm which specializes in developing and managing sophisticated investment strategies for high net-worth individuals, families, and institutional investors.

Lindus also provides sub-account and separate account management services for investment professionals and their clients.

In addition to our core investment management services, Lindus Advisors provides wealth management services for individuals and families.

Our wealth management services take into account the complex financial issues facing modern day families including:

* Retirement Planning
* Trust and Estate Planning
* Tax Strategy
* Asset Protection
* Unique Family needs

We design each portfolio on the basis of two beliefs:
1. Each client should have an investment strategy tailored to their individual financial circumstances and unique preferences.
2. Each portfolio should be designed to mitigate downside risk.
