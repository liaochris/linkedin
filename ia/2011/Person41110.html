Stacey Shipman’s Summary

Drawing from a decade as a market research professional and more than 15 as a wellness educator I speak, consult and write on topics related to self-esteem/confidence, stress management and general health and wellness.

Consult with health, wellness and other people-focused organizations who want to confidently connect to their customers through market research, web video and networking.

Creator/Publisher/Host - Feel Good Living Blog, TV, Podcast

Video Spokesperson, working with organizations who need a friendly, credible face for to represent their company or brand via web video/audio.

Creator of the audio CD Let it Flow: Short Guided Meditations, the book Experience Less Stress, More Success: Networking for Business.

Educators, students, healthcare providers, business owners and service professionals have experienced improved health, productivity, effectiveness, presence and confidence by participating in these programs.

Stacey Shipman’s Specialties:

Stress management & relaxation techniques, self-esteem/confidence, body image, personal wellness, market research, spokesperson/web video, community builder
