Jim Ginn's Summary

Experienced Software Development Professional

I have more than 20 years of software development and system integration experience. During that time, my focus has been science oriented, delivering software that analyzed satellite imagery, simulated wind tunnel conditions over wire frame models, collected and analyzed clinical pathology data, supported high throughput screening, analyzed chemical properties to predict and select candidates for screening assays, and managed and automated testing in laboratories.

This science bent means I thrive on finding solutions to difficult and challenging problems. I once spent a weekend developing an algorithm to detect and record the audio frame offsets for an audio file with missing header information because it was interesting and no one else had found a solution.

I am technology agnostic. Programming languages, IDEs, frameworks, test suites, version control and others are tools to assist me in developing a solution and should be selected based on their merits to support that endeavor. Likewise, process and methodology are meant to support a team’s effort to deliver a solution and must be chosen to fit the development team.

I’ve had the good fortune of working with many excellent technologists during my career, benefitting from their guidance and advice. Much like the African proverb, “it takes a village to raise a child,” I believe it is important to share our experience with younger developers and accelerate their growth into strong team contributors.
