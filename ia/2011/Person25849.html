Eric Jones's Summary

http://www.targetcenteredgolf.com Eric Jones is a World Long Drive Champion as well as a PGA Pro who also has a Masters Degree in Sport Psychology. Eric founded the Seaver Golf Academy with the idea of fusing his unique talents as a Long Driver, PGA pro, and Sport Psychology to help golfers of all abilities hit the ball farther, score better, and have more fun. Eric is the author of the widely acclaimed eBook "The 5 Keys to Distance."
