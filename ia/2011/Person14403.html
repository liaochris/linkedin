Eric Renz-Whitmore’s Summary

Through various organizations and activities, my overall goal is cultivating an environment that encourages satisfying creative work, whether through writing, performance or digital media production.

My current active involvement is with the following groups:

NMTC (New Mexico Technology Council)
The New Mexico Technology Council provides focused, value-added services and initiatives to both industry and associate members. While programming is targeted to commercial enterprises that operate in the software, computer hardware, internet-related and telecommunications sectors, New Mexico's entire business community stands to significantly benefit from NMTC's presence.
More info at: http://www.nmtechcouncil.org

NM Post Alliance
New Mexico's professional post-production community is banding together to build our local industry. For more information, please join us at http://www.nmpostalliance.org

IGDA
One of our latest endeavors is building New Mexico's game development community. Are you a game developer in New Mexico -- please get in touch!

NM Media Industries / MISP
An informal partnership between New Mexico's colleges, government and media business, our Media Industries Strategy Project (MISP) has created the conditions for great success in building sustainable film and digital media industry. Through listservs, websites and events like the annual Media Industries Conference, I help connect and promote growth of our creative media community.
http://www.altmedianm.org
