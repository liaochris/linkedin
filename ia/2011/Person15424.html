Gail Z. Martin’s Summary

I help business owners, coaches, consultants, solo professionals and authors improve their marketing results in 30 days. To do this, we connect their marketing directly to their business plan in order to save money, save time and get results. DreamSpinner Communications provides one-on-one and group coaching programs, keynote speeches, seminars, workshops and teleseminars, as well as custom consulting.

My passion? Helping business owners and solo professionals achieve their dreams through effective, affordable marketing.

As a speaker and author, I help companies and professionals at every stage of their growth to improve their marketing results and build their business.

Since 1986, I have been helping companies, nonprofit organizations and entrepreneurs achieve their marketing goals regardless of the size of their budget. I hold an MBA from The Pennsylvania State University in Marketing and Management Information Systems.

Speaking live for groups has taken me coast to coast across the U.S. and Canada, as well as to Europe. Keynotes, seminars, workshops and panels are a great fit for me.

I'm a frequent teleseminar presenter, and have taught for MarketingProfs.com, PR Newswire, Microsoft Partner Edge Network, SmartWomensCoaching.com and eWomenNetwork (and others), and am an Expert On Call for 911MarketingHelp.com. My Shared Dreams Marketing Podcast shares tips from top business and publishing experts(http://gzmartin.audioacrobat.com/rss/shareddreams.xml ).

In addition to my nonfiction books, my articles have been featured in more than 40 different print publications as well as in numerous ezines, newsletters and blogs.

On the fiction side, I write the Chronicles of the Necromancer series (Solaris Books) and The Fallen Kings Cycle (Orbit Books) www.ChroniclesOfTheNecromancer.com and I host the Ghost in the Machine fantasy podcast (http://gzmartin.audioacrobat.com/rss/gailzmartinpodcast.xml).
