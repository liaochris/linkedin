Mark S. Fitzgerald, AIA, LEED AP, CDT, MAD's Summary

Mark has over 15 years of experience as a licensed architect and project manager to offer the savy business owner who is searching for an architect with a diverse market portfolio. Here are just a few of his strengths and attributes that he can offer to add VALUE into your business:

++ Ability to successfully manage multiple projects from start to finish while maintaining Quality Control.

++ Effective collaboration with project team members by building strong, trusting business relationships with staff, clients, consultants, and contractors.

++ Proven expertise in reviewing and evaluating project documentation and technical inquiries.

++ Quick grasp of technical, building code, specification, and sustainable design issues.

++ Proven ability to quickly read into projects at any stage of development from sales and marketing through construction administration.

++ Reviewer of construction documentation for completeness of systems, constructability, and communication between designer, client, and contractor. Mark's goal is to assist in carrying out design intent and in reducing the likelihood of errors and omissions claims.

Value Offered

Forward-thinking professional offering a unique combination of creativity and analytical skill with the ability to assess both vantage points simultaneously for an effective balance of visual nuance and sound business decisions. Results can be easily transferrable into a variety of positions that will establish business growth and profitability for your company.

Currently working as a Rainmaker to help bring revenue into organizations, be it profit or non-profit.

A member of Expressers Toastmasters, Mark is diligently practicing his oratory and presentation skills.

Would consider relocation for the right opportunity. Contact Mark at mbfitzgerald91@insightbb.com with leads!

Specialties

AutoCAD 2009 + ISO 9001 Simulated Training + Construction & Green Desgin Advisor + Interpreting and Recording Data + Reviewing and Editing Documents + Maintaining Records + Mediating between Business Owners and Contractors that have design issues
