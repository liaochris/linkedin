Robert Cooksey's Summary

Curriculum and course development for Game Art + Design program. Teach game design, concept development for games and film, world building, scripting, and game studies.

PhD candidate in the area of communications theory and philosophy of media. Philosophical work concerns human experience modeling and reflective design of artificial life (that's us).

Advised/consulted a number of academic and entrepreneurial leadership teams in information technology design and development.

Design of interactive and information systems to varying operational specifications for specific audiences and cultures.

Managed small, efficient technology smart teams organized to adapt to varying operational demands --mostly involving people and technology interacting within learning and research environments.

Game design and development for continuing medical education.

Current professional projects involve concept and application design/development for web, mobile, and game applications.

Currently interested in iterative model design for intensification of human experience and performance. This involves method design of techniques and principles engaging physiological, psychological, and technological aparatii in service of desired goal states or operational conditions.
