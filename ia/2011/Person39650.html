Frans Dijksman's Summary

As a skilled multimedia producer with a strong marketing background i am experienced in building websites optimized for speed and Search Engines on a daily basis since 2001.
I develop programs and websites using the Python programming language, PHP, frameworks, HTML, CSS, XML or Javascript and additional SEO copywriting for the Dutch (mother tongue), English and German languages.
I am also experienced in developing applications in Drupal, Typo3, Joomla, Magento, Xoops and some other web building software packages and Google App Engine.

My goal is to build high perfoming, fast, tableless and liquid websites with a minimum of code and a maximum of keyword/visitor range within the SEO/SEM balance, which is the key factor to develop and sustain business on the internet.

Within the multimedia environment I'm experienced in 3D and 2D designing. From 1999 till now I have done projects on architectural-, product- and natural 3D modelling, as well as animations.

What all started with piano lessons in my youth, now in my spare time I love composing music using synthesizers.
