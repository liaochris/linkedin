Mekayla Ichneumon Beaver's Summary

Mekayla Beaver is a design researcher and human factors specialist. Her expertise lies in understanding and uncovering the factors that determine how people adopt and use products, services, and environments. She guides teams from initial fieldwork (in-context interviews, observations, analogous experiences), user insight discovery, through the translation and communication of these insights into design opportunities and requirements.

Sustainable design is one of Mekayla's key areas of focus. She uses design thinking and stakeholder research to help solve difficult challenges in improving social and environmental impact. She has worked on a number of different initiatives for sustainable development in the US, New Zealand, and developing countries.

Specialties

design for social and environmental impact, stakeholder research / engagement, qualitative research methods, participatory design, persona development, storyboarding, design strategy, presentation, mentoring / teaching, organizational change, life cycle thinking, systems thinking, prototyping, wireframing
