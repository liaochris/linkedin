Rui de Sousa Neves, PMP's Summary

I seek new challenges every day and always try to surpass myself in every situation.

I started my career as a software engineer but soon evolved to Product and Project Management (in IT projects), an area where I can use both my technical background and management skills.
I like coaching and mentoring but I also love to keep learning, so that I can transmit my know-how to others and improve my own, to be better every day.

Nowadays, I'm working on a BPA, BPM and SOA project.
I'm also an internal consultant on the company's CMMi certification process.

Also, I am currently vice-president in AlumniEI-FEUP (an alumni association) and have, in the past, been involved in several associations, such as JuniFEUP (a junior enterprise) and Junior Chamber International (JCI) - Porto delegation (an international organization focused on training leadership and management skills), among others.

Mostly, I like to think of myself as a person that people can rely on, both personal and professionally.

Specialties

Very experienced on Project Management and Product Management, Oracle RDBMS and PL/SQL, Java and JavaEE technologies, PHP, as well as other Web frameworks.

Also knowledgeble on development methodologies such as Scrum, XP and collaborative software.

BPMN, UML, SEO and CMMi are also strong skills of mine.

In general my know-how is mostly on the following areas: Software Development, Project Management, Requirements Engineering, Quality Assurance, HR, Marketing, Training, Problem Solving
