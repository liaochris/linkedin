Steve Thorson’s Summary

Private Money Manager specializing in managing investment portfolios of high and ultra-high net worth individuals, foundations and institutions.

Our long term track record is impressive and spans 13 years of top-tier performance.

Emerald Capital does not operate as a hedge fund but does manage money in a similar fashion. All accounts are held separately and are not commingled. This allows investors complete transparency and disclosure of holdings and transactions.

Our management discipline is Multi-Style, which allows us to invest in any market capitalization, sector or growth/value as our research determines where there are profit opportunities "presently" in the domestic stock market.

We craft the portfolios using quantitative analysis which lends to a very objective "numbers based" stock selection process and removes emotion from the equation. The portfolios are optimized for minimum risk while reaching for maximum gain. Strict sell disciplines are employed to keep the portfolios always ahead of the market curve.
