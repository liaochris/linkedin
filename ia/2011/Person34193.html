Paul Judge's Summary

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:
Mr Judge is an experienced technology executive with strong background in Financial Services, Straight Through Processing (STP), Electronic Connectivity, Business Process Modeling and Technology Architecture. He has 20+ years of experience in wide ranging information technology roles in the financial industry. Currently he is a Director in the Financial Services Solutions practice at Paragon Computer professionas.

Mr Judge has held various technology management positions at financial services firms such as State Street Bank, TradingLinx, Lexit Capital as well as Merrill Lynch. Prior to re-joining Merrill Lynch in 2002, he was the Chief Technology Officer with Lexit Capital LLC, an introducing and executing broker servicing European institutional customers. Prior to that he was the Chief Technology Officer with TradingLinx Inc., a startup firm that attempted to provide universal electronic trade confirmation services over the internet.
