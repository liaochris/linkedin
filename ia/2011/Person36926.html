Rich Rosenthal's Summary

Marketing and sales management professional with broad experience in the medical device arena. For the last 20 years I have led the marketing and sales efforts for companies manufacturing pulmonary function diagnostic equipment for both the professional and consumer health marketplaces. Upstream product development efforts have yielded many successful products. Downstream focus includes a depth of experience in global distribution planning and management. Sales leadership includes development of distributor networks domestically and internationally and management of a direct sales contingent.

Specialties

Upstream product planning; OEM negotiations; distribution planning; downstreeam promotional and budgetary planning; international regulatory distribution needs.
