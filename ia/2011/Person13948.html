Eric McGill's Summary

I'm an experienced management consultant covering a range of industries and functions, and I'm now focusing on applying unique insights to traditional industries. I'm currently building an investment firm around converting proven behavioral economics theories into viable financial strategies.

Our first strategy is based on exploiting medium-term behavioral biases in how investors perceive data trends. We launched the Ashbury Heights Capital US Large Cap Fund in early 2009, and will expand the core theories to other investment areas over the next few years.

Our company will grow stably over time, adding strategies and funds as we break new ground in behavioral finance with the right combination of directors, advisors, analysts, and insights. Please drop me a line if you're interested in learning more, otherwise wish me luck!

Thanks,
Eric
