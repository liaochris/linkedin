Richard Wroten's Summary

Goal: To bring value to owners of middle-market companies, whether they:

* Want to TRANSITION their business through a sale to the right buyer or partner,

OR

* Want to TRANSCEND their current operating performance to bring them to the next level.

Richard brings over 34 years of business experience ranging from top executive in two large public companies; partner in an international consulting firm; to on-the-ground practical consulting to small and medium-sized entrepreneurial companies. He has substantial experience both as a principal and as a consulting intermediary in mergers & acquisitions.

Specialties

Mergers and Acquisitions; Financing; Team Building; Business Development; Consulting to the C-suite, Succession Planning; Executive Coaching; and, Motivational Speaking/Seminars
