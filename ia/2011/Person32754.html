Nicole Murray's Summary

Hello there, and thank you for viewing my profile.

I am a business development coach and help business owners get clear on the direction their business is headed, develop a plan, and then help them implement that plan to ensure its success.

I predominantly work with solo-preneurs and small business owners from a variety of industries. My personal experience has been in industries such as Education, Health & Fitness, Law, Building/Manufacturing, Real Estate and Hospitality.

Over the years I have heard so many business owners say how challenging it is to know where to turn for the right help in developing a business, with so much information to sort through out there in the marketplace. So, to make it easier, I have also created a website that: -

- connects you to the many wonderful coaches I have met from all my travels here and overseas, listed in their category of expertise;

- connects you to my favourite business books, audios, and videos that have helped both me and my clients; and

- this year, I will be interviewing business owners so they can share their expertise on various topics with you too.

So whether you are a business owner, solopreneur (actors, authors & sports professionals included), budding entrepreneur or a small business start up, please be guided by the people, products, experiences and free resources on www.hotchoc.com.au.

Please also join me at www.facebook.com/hotchocolatecoaching

May your business develop and grow just the way you want it to!

Thank you again for viewing my profile.
