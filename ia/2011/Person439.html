Josh Slavitt’s Summary

Thought Leader and Certified Business Strategist.

I am actively involved in expanding the development of both Corporate Social Responsibility and Special Situations Planning. I am involved in a wide array of organizations that seek to move the ball forward in these two important areas. I have more than 25 years of public policy experience working for a Global 100 corporation, for individual clients and for public policy organizations and think tanks.

As a business strategist and advisor, I work to help my clients:

1. Make More Money;
2. Free Up More Time; and
3. Have The Lifestyle That They Want.

I am certified by ActionCOACH, which is the world's largest coaching firm with 1200 coaches in 27 countries. I can help you grow your business, establish sound financial metrics, improve your marketing strategies and develop a high-powered team of employees.

Do you have a long-term plan for your business? Start today. It's never too late to get your plan on paper where you can actually see it, analyze it and establish goals, strategies and tactics for implementation. A business without a plan, is like a person in the desert without a compass. With my executive management, public affairs and business development experience I can help you realize your business and personal goals, while working smarter and not harder.

Josh Slavitt’s Specialties:

Let me help you to learn how to spend time working on your business so you don't always have to spend all your time working in your business.
