Arman Atoyan's Summary

Some Goals and Tasks of ARiSO (Armenian Youth Association of Developing Information Society)

• Realize broad educational and cognitive programs, the purpose of which is building and developing information society in possession of up-to-date technology and modern digital world knowledge, promote IT use in everyday life and in work,
• develop and implement such educational programs as interactive education, information and interactive television (Projet iTv)
• organize meetings between IT and other sphere specialists, the purpose of which is to introduce the latters the mechanisms of deployment of online management systems and IT use in their spheres,
• facilitate the implementation of IT in the regions by providing rural zones with computers and internet accessibility, using Association’s funds as well as sponsors’ and helpers’,
• assist state programs which are aimed at the development of IT in the country,
• promote the raising of qualification of IT specialists in the Republic of Armenia by implementing other countries’ experience,
• promote the creation of necessary mechanisms for mutually beneficial cooperation and exchange of experience between RA and foreign IT specialists,
• form voluntary groups of IT specialists who will occasionally visit different educational institutions and hold cognitive courses,
• promote IT students’ participation in the Association’s educational programs, arrange re-qualification courses,
• promote IT students’ and specialists’ research works, provide the best students with professional courses in organizations of appropriate fields,
• represent educational programs of development and promotion of the sphere and reports on implemented programs to Government regularly,
• support Association members’ activity as well as any IT sphere representative’s independent professional activity, promote the enlargement of opportunities of raising their qualification.
