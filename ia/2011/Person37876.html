    Awesome Foundation

    (Non-Profit; Philanthropy industry)

    October 2010 — Present (4 months)

    The idea is simple: create a monthly $1,000 grant awarded to a person doing something Awesome – whether it’s music, film, art, technology, design, food, publishing or anything else. The money will be spent on a project, activity or research – we don’t even really care if it’s for fun or profit. We never claim your intellectual property or anything like that, and anyone in the world is eligible. So long as this can have a material impact on what you are doing and the idea is awesome, you will receive the money no strings attached. If you have an awesome idea (or even want to point out somebody else), let us know.
