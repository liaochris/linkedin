Law Practice industry

I practice law in those areas that affect people in their most important relationships -- their family relationships. Among the issues I help people resolve are divorce (property division, custody and visitation, support), clarifying rights and responsibilities in relationships (pre-nuptial agreements, living together agreements), and estate planning for people with modest estates. I am experienced and skilled in working in three models: collaborative law, mediation, and litigation.
