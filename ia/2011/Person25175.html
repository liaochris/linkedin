Katherine Tryjankowski’s Summary

I have experience with various publications. I have done work for Pittsburgh Magazine (regular and special editions), The High School Graduate Series, and The Expectant Mother's Guide Series. I have also used my design skills to freelance for several companies in the field of both identity and web page design. I also have a knack for social media and offer my excellent services to companies to help bring them into the social media world. My goals are to someday have my own company where I can offer my services to everyone and build a team of excellent creatives. I would also like to teach, as I have always enjoyed people and sharing what I learn with others. My goal overall is to just be happy, and right now, it seems I am as close as I ever have been.
