Oliver Urpi's Summary

►I am Director of an Independent review and feedback site aimed at cleaning up the Recruitment Industry called Recruitment Reviews◄ – www.recruitmentreviews.com

Recruitment Reviews provides candidates, recruiters, clients and employers the opportunity to leave constructive, anonymous feedback on the service they receive. Our aim is to promote good practice and improve standards across an industry that has suffered from a poor perception over the years.

Recruitment Reviews will be going live in early 2010. Visit www.recruitmentreviews.com now to sign up or contact me at oliver@recruitmentreviews.com if you too are passionate about improving standards and would like to get involved.

On a personal level, my background is in Legal Recruitment. I have over 5 years experience and have also had experience of setting up my own recruitment business.
