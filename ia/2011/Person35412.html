Priyanga de Zoysa's Summary

SUMMERY
Sixteen years of clinical experience in sports medicine .– Eleven years as medical officer to the Sri Lanka national football team, nine years as chairmen of the medical committee of the Sri Lanka football federation and six years as a medical officer to FIFA and AFC.

SPECIAL INTEREST
Non surgical management of musculoskeletal disorders integrating manipulative medicine,physical medicine and mind /body medicine

SPORTS MEDICINE PROFILE
• Head of the Police Sports Medicine Unit –Sri Lanka Police Department (1999 – to date)
• Sports Physician – Sri Lanka Police Hospital (1994 – to date)
• Club Physician – Police Sports Club (1994 – to date). Duties and responsibilities include, treatment and rehabilitation of injuries – providing medical services at matches and tournaments – education on injury prevention, mental training and nutrition for male and female police sports teams participating in division one national tournaments in 21 sports (football, rugby, cricket, athletics, basketball, volleyball, netball, karate, judo, taekwondo, tennis, table tennis, badminton, squash, swimming, water polo, boxing, archery, shooting, kabbadi and cycling).
• Chairmen Medical Committee – Football Federation of Sri Lanka (1998 – 2007)
• Team Physician – National Football Team of Sri Lanka (1995 – 2006). Gained vast experience working with two challenging Brazilian coaches during this period (I also was born in Brazil)
• Doping Control Coordinator and Medical Officer – FIFA and AFC (Asian football confederation). Conducted doping control tests in UAE, Syria, Singapore, Malaysia, Maldives and Sri Lanka (2000 – 2006)
• Visiting Sports Physician – Institute of Sports Medicine, Ministry of Sports and Youth Affaires (1995 – 2000)

Specialties

Sports Medicine, Manipulative Medicine, Mind/Body Medicine, NLP, Public Speaking, Authoring
