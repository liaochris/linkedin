Nancy James's Summary

For over thirty years I have been a versatile performer in the Tri-State area on stage, on camera and on the air waves. I began my television career on the Bob Braun Show singing, interviewing, and creating live commercials. I continue to perform as a singer/ entertainer/ emcee, an on-camera spokesperson, and voice-over talent for radio and television. I also am enjoying "live-TV" once again as Host of Northern Kentucky Magazine on ICN6- the only live talk show in the Tri-State area.

Specialties

On Camera: Naration, Host, Video Presentations, Commercial venues.
Voice Over: for Television and Radio, Narrations, Audio Books, Commercial venues
Entertainer: Big Band singer (with Musical Charts for 8- 16 piece orchestras) Trio charts, Corporate Shows, Wedding Ceremony Specialist, National Anthem Singer
Emcee: Any type of Corporate, Church, School, or Community event.
