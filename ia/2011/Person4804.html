Benjamin Chute, PMP’s Summary

The key to a successful software project is to listen to the business goals of the client and the needs of the users - both spoken and unspoken - to recommend a solution that meets and exceeds the short-term target by looking to the long-term benefit.

• Ten+ years of client-facing software industry experience, primarily web-based technologies with demanding n-tier development requirements.

• Proven leader in IT business strategy with experience executing key accounts.

• Critical thinker with a positive attitude who understands that communication and team collaboration are vital to a successful project.

• Demonstrated value across services such as business process analysis, project plan creation, scope definition, requirements gathering, wireframes, use cases, setting budgets and timelines, earned value analysis, return on investment analysis, profitability projections, mentoring, and team-consensus-building.

• Substantial experience engaging new business opportunities, responding to RFP’s, writing proposals, contracts, SLAs, and giving presentations.

• Project and hands-on experience with technologies, tools, and other acronyms: SOA, ESB, JIS, Java/J2EE/JEE, PHP, .NET, ASP, SQL, MySQL, Oracle, DB2, WebSphere, JBoss, Tomcat, CVS, SVN, CSS/HTML, JavaScript/AJAX, W3C/WCAG/508 compliance, Unix, Mac client-server development (Cocoa/Objective-C, WebObjects), iPhone development, Xcode, OmniPlan, Microsoft Project, dotProject, Basecamp, OmniGraffle, Visio, Mantis, JIRA, Serena, Selenium, Liferay, SharePoint, advanced Excel, Mac OS X, Windows.

• Influenced by the Project Management Institute (PMI), Rational Unified Process (RUP), and Agile schools of thought.

• Appreciation for realistic, iterative, and value-focused development.
