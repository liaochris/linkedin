Sarah McArthur's Summary

I am a writer and editor with more than 15 years of experience in publishing. My fields of expertise are management, leadership, executive and business coaching, and human resources. I have just recently published The AMA Handbook of Leadership, co-edited with Marshall Goldsmith and John Baldoni.

With influential clients such as Marshall Goldsmith and Anthony Robbins, I have played significant roles in many book projects including the recently published New York Times best-seller MOJO, as well as the best-selling management classic Coaching for Leadership, and the Amazon.com, USA Today, and Wall Street Journal #1 best-seller, What Got You Here Won’t Get You There.

Specialties

Contract writer, editor, and project manager of works in business, business and executive coaching, human resources, and leadership.
