Ann Zeise’s Summary

As part of my philosophy education at the universities, I studied about mass movements and their philosophies and leaders. I have used that knowledge to apply to marketing and civic and organizational networking.

Currently I am world famous in my niche of homeschooling. I help to network families who have chosen this educational alternative, to help them do so legally, and to find resources to help them educate their own children successfully.

I am also notorious in my own city of Milpitas, CA, for having started and continuing to expand our community website here. The purpose of this site is to provide a model democracy for the citizens of our community, and those who work and visit Milpitas.

Ann Zeise’s Specialties:

I've become fairly good at SEO, and at building communities that last, and are influential in their own rights.
