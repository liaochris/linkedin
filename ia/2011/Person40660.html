Steve Martin's Summary

Creative executive with over 20 years experience in the design and advertising industry, managing large and small design groups with miniscule to multi-million dollar creative budgets. Packaging, print advertising, POP, direct mail, sales promotions, marketing programs, corporate identity, PR, event marketing - creative solutions provided for a wide variety of industries, companies and mediums. Innovative creative grounded in solid marketing strategies. Inspirational creative direction without the attitude.

Specialties

Creative direction and inspiration, innovative creative solutions, Design and workflow management, expertise in all creative mediums, excellent client relations, team leadership, result oriented, marketing grounded creative.
