Kim Smith’s Summary

Director of Operations at Evolve Technologies in Boulder, CO. We have been in business since 1995 and we offer many products and services to businesses throughout Colorado. Evolve Technologies’ customer base is rich in various markets and sizes, and they span across many locations across the country and the globe.

Evolve Technologies is a value-added partner for ShoreTel, Enterasys, Extreme Networks, LifeSize, Juniper and Aruba Networks. Evolve offers our customers more than just product offerings. We provide traditional services too, like voice and network cabling at your premise. We work with local exchange carriers like Qwest, Level 3, Time Warner, XO Communications, Cbeyond and Integra to provide our customers with competitive pricing and services for their voice and internet circuits. To keep their data networks fully up to speed, we also offer WAN and LAN services featuring products from several industry leading network partners.
