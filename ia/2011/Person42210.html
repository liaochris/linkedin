Suzanne Pletcher's Summary

Suzanne Pletcher is a communications consultant distinguished by her project management, speaking and facilitation skills and experience in business and environmental issues. She has superior written and interpersonal communications skills. Suzanne is a respected meeting facilitator, executive director, and project manager. Her career has focused upon writing, public relations, leadership development, and sustainable communities. She has received several significant awards for her work. Over the course of her career, Suzanne has managed small businesses, public and investor relations campaigns, innovative corporate and public sector environmental programs, and collaborative teams. Her professional goal is to help build healthy communities.

Suzanne received a Bachelor of Science degree in Journalism from Boston University.

She currently resides in Boulder, Colorado.

Specialties

Communications, speaker, meeting facilitator, business community relations, environment and sustainability, consulting, investor and public relations, leadership development.
