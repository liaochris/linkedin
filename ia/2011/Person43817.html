Tom Graunke's Summary

I’m a product of an entrepreneurial family. Six months out of college, I realized I’m not someone who could be told to go sit in a cube. I need the big picture.

I make it my goal to always deliver what I promise..and then some. Creating ‘something out of nothing’ - and making money from it - is what gets me going.

Additionally, I enjoy helping grow people’s careers. People are usually much more experienced, and at a higher position level, after they’ve spend several years in my business.

I’m most excited about watching the ‘new Web’ develop and grow. The internet, in many ways, is still basic. It can go much farther. Rich media is on the way to making everything more entertaining.

I'm all about setting an outrageous goal and finding a way to hit it...make the impossible possible.
