Marco Grieco 葛英豪, MBA's Summary

MBA graduate from SDA Bocconi (Milan, Italy)/ CEIBS Business School (Shanghai, China) with strong international exposure towards Asian countries, experience in start up, and business development and sales growth in dynamic and competitive environments and in consulting in several areas.

Experienced professional in running a start-up in the UK involved in retail trading (300 k€ turnaround after 2 years) on the .com channel, as an economic ICT Junior Controller in ENEL (Italian incumbent in the EU electrical/energetic market), and in management consulting both in The Boston Consulting Group and in Ernst & Young, mainly in FMCG, Industrial Markets, TLC and Public Companies in several sectors including: Strategic Planning, Financial Analysis, Management and Financial Accounting, M&A and Debt restructuring.

Result driven professional with strong focus on top line growth, obtains results through efficient cooperative negotiation with customers and business partners committing continuously on looking for win-win opportunities and long term partnerships. Leadership and general coordination abilities enable effective involvement and performance speed up of team members with a higher effectiveness of actions assigned.

Interest and expertise in the Chinese and APAC markets, culture and business environment developed through Business Studies at CEIBS, internationalization projects with Ernst & Young in the Chinese market, CasaModa startup in Shanghai and daily exposure to Chinese business environment; exposure to Chinese business networks through active participation at CEIBS network and business community (more than 16,000 ALUMNI in China) and daily relationships with entrepreneurs, investors and managers in the Greater China region.
