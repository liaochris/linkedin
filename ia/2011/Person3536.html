Arden Clise’s Summary

Certified business etiquette consultant, speaker and business etiquette columnist for the Puget Sound Business Journal. Founder of Clise Etiquette, focused on helping companies increase their profitability and improve their company image by giving employees the skills they need to be confident, courteous and successful.

Over 25 years of business experience in marketing, management, event planning and public relations.
