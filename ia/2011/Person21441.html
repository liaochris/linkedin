Joel Comm’s Summary

A serial CEO—starting, building & selling many notable companies—I am known as the “New Media Marketing Strategist.” A New York Times bestselling author & technology innovator, I consistently harness the power of social media & mobile apps to expand brand reach & engage in active relationship marketing.

In 1995 WorldVillage.com was one of the 1st 25,000 sites in the world. I've created popular family-friendly sites including familyfirst.com, dealofday.com & toponereport.com.

In 1997, I sold ClassicGames.com to Yahoo—Yahoo! Games. Look closely at the avatars for a guy with a red cap, glasses & goatee. That would be me!

I traversed the dotcom boom & subsequent bust. In 2004, I discovered Google AdSense, making a substantial passive income. The resulting “What Google Never Told You About Making Money with AdSense” (also known as AdSense Secrets) is the definitive guide on the topic. In 2006, I wrote New York Times Best-Seller “The AdSense Code.”

I created a number of technology companies supported by amazing teams & personally leveraged success in software applications, writing & public speaking.

♠ 2007 – Executive Producer/Host of the first competitive Internet reality show “The Next Internet Millionaire”
♠ 2008 – “Click Here to Order: Stories of the World’s Most Successful Internet Marketing Entrepreneurs.” Hit #1 in iPhone application development with world-famous iFart Mobile (That was me!)
♠ 2009 – “Twitter Power: How to Dominate Your Market One Tweet at a Time” the most popular Twitter book in the world.
♠ 2010 – Second edition, “Twitter Power 2.0” is translated into Japanese, Chinese, Portuguese, Korean, Italian, and more coming.
♠ 2010 – KaChing: How to Run an Online Business that Pays and Pays, written to demonstrate the opportunity for anyone to create a sustainable online business.

As an international public speaker I love to inspire & entertain audiences, arming them with strategic tools to create new media campaigns to explode their business.
