Roberta Budvietas's Summary

I am a keynote speaker who can help people and organisations to focus their dreams and do the ABC's to make their dreams real.
Having worked over 30 years in marketing and education, I have experienced the diversity of people and know that the greatest need of every person is Acknowledgement, Acceptance and Appreciation. Customer service, Human Resource Management and Relationships are all built on this principle. Helping individuals and business people provide these 3As helps the business achieve the action, belief and commitment necessary to make their Vision (dream) come true.

Specialties

Keynote speaking, Business consulting, coaching, process engineering, systems improvement, managing know how and understanding what is important for people in businesses, specialising in the SME markets where hobbies and trades have become viable businesses
