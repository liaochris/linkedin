Christopher Bracher’s Summary

Chris Bracher has worked at the corporate level at companies ranging from manufacturing to consumer goods within the plastics, pet, software, luxury goods and entertainment industries. He has supported the advancement of Adobe Systems, Time-Warner, Turner Broadcasting and Hanna Barbera Studios.

Previously Chris has been involved with technology integration and marketing consultation with experience ranging from Fortune 500 companies with national and international campaigns to small businesses with local and regional promotions. He has also worked in graphic design, marketing and product development, business planning and financial analysis, public relations, advertising, sourcing and purchasing, and high tech equipment needs evaluation and sourcing.

Chris is friendly, methodical and naturally creative which provides him with the innate ability to conceive and drive highly recognizable brand and marketing concepts. Chris can easily translate from "techie" to "creative" and back again.

Chris earned a B.S. from the University of Southern California and an MBA from Santa Clara University. He is also a graduate of the renowned USC Entrepreneur Program.

He participates weekly in a local jazz band and enjoys volunteer work with groups such as the Landmark Education and Junior Achievement and was an active volunteer manager in the three year running of the San Jose Grand Prix, 2005-2007.

Civic mined and always willing to lend a hand as a volunteer, he regularly works to raise money for various Bay Area cancer related charities.
