Donald & CherylLynne Rubbo's Summary

Donald and Cheryl Lynne Rubbo are leading experts in mind-body medicine.

An extraordinary husband and wife team, the Rubbos have written two books, Extraordinary Breath, Making the Power of Deep Breathing Work for You (Xlibris, 2008), and Primordial Qigong, a digital eBook, and have been featured in The Empty Vessel, A Journal of Contemporary Taoism.

The Rubbos teach worldwide, and their work focuses on transformational learning, by getting beyond gaining factual knowledge alone to instead become changed by what one learns in some meaningful way - questioning assumptions, beliefs and values, and considering multiple points of view.

Spiritual Warriors
The Rubbos honor all spiritual traditions, and have been immersed for almost four decades in Taoist and Buddhist mysticism, and for two decades in Tibetan tantric mysticism and Tibetan tantric Buddhism. It is from these traditions that they acquired the wisdom and skillful means that allows them to benefit themselves and countless beings.

Donald Rubbo has a thriving private practice, offering appointments for Bio-Energy Healing, Consultations, Instruction, Instructor/Healing Training, Distant Healing, Telephone Consultation.

The Rubbos blog is http://rubbos2.wordpress.com. Their websites: www.CultivateChi.com and www.ExtraordinaryBreath.com.

The Rubbos can be reached by telephone at 415.459.9098, and email at Fivetigrs@gmail.com

Donald and Cheryl Lynne have spent over 30 years of their lives immersed in the martial, internal and healing arts, including Karate, Traditional Shao Lin Kung Fu, Nei Gung, Guang Ping Yang Tai Chi Chuan, I Chuan, Hsing Yi, Bagua, many systems of Qigong and profound meditative methods. The Rubbos honor their many teachers and the blessings of the traditional teachings.

Author

Primordial Qigong - Rubbo Art of Energy

Non-Profit; Health, Wellness and Fitness industry

January 1980 – Present (31 years 3 months)

In this popular, lovingly crafted digital eBook, Donald and Cheryl Lynne Rubbo present their take on Primordial Qigong, a gem from the treasure chest of Taoist mystical Qigong, emphasizing the role the mind (intention) plays in the alchemical process inherent in this form, to help you find the Fountain of Youth.

The Primordial Qigong eBook gives a fascinating detailed description of how to incorporate within yourself all the components that encompass the primary aspects of Taoist philosophy and cosmology: yin/yang concept, the trinity of heaven, earth and man, the Five Element theory of Traditional Chinese Medicine, the I Ching, the Bagua and the mystical aspects of numbers.

“With this book the Rubbo's have done a wonderful job of presenting this magical, mystical, life-altering form, which has been passed down through the ages from the great Dao master Zhang San Feng. It is indeed a pathway back to the Primordial, back to the beginning of ourselves in form and without. I have practiced Wuji Qigong for ten years now and still find it exciting and fulfilling. This is the first time this wonderful teaching has been presented in book form in English. Xiexie ni to Donald and Cheryl Lynne for presenting us this great gift!” Solala Towler, editor/publisher of The Empty Vessel: A Journal of the Daoist Arts, author of eight books on Daoism and qigong, past president of the National Qigong Association.
