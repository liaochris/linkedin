Patrick Nelson's Summary

Professional web architect and website designer working in Information Technology since 1985 when leaving college as a COBOL programmer.

Was setting up Bulletin Board Systems (BBS’s) in the mid 90’s when the Internet suddenly (seemingly overnight) became mainstream. Sensing a massive opportunity, retrained as a website designer and over 13 years has become proficient at a range of SQL databases including MySQL, SQL Server and Access as well as programming languages and scripts including PHP, HTML, Python, Javascript, Perl and CGI scripting.

Has grown UnderDesign Ltd from a sole trader in 2006 to become one of Nottingham’s leading web and online marketing agencies with clients such as Experience Notts, Marsh Property Investments, GamePlan, Express Embroidery, Peak Conference Centre, What If Coaching, Mortgage and Property Centre, Springwater Golf Club, Artsbowl, PPS, Frank Key, JumpStart, Tax Partnership, Goodbrooks, Mansfield Clinic, AWEBB, RADS Storage, Worksop College, MXG Skiphire, Integrity Psychology, SurveyGuru, Rootsmap, LAMP, Racetech Harnessing, Petit Paris, Rushcliffe Borough Council, Yes! And..., Rushcliffe Business Partnership, Ten Wealth Management, Develop to Grow, Radius Shopfitting, EXP, Creative Bassetlaw, The Dressing Room, Mistry's Pharmacy, Hazelford Notts, SLAA UK, Farndon Marina, Piccolo Restaurants, NEBA, 16 Pilcher Gate and Notts County Football Club.

Has also written a number of articles on Internet marketing that have been published in Fresh Business Thinking, IFA Life, and Observer Newspaper amongst others and his monthly UnderDesign newsletter attracts over 4,000 subscribers.

Has gained recognition in a number of networks including Twitter, Facebook, Ecademy and LinkedIn and offline, local groups such as Gedling Business Partnership where he is Chair and Rushcliffe Business Partnership. In 2008, had the honour of being invited into the Observer’s Courvoisier Future 500, an exclusive club for exceptional entrepreneurs.
