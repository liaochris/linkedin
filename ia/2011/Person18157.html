Denise bradley's Summary

- My name is Denise Bradley and I am the owner of InnerAllure. My background is in Interior Design. I received a diploma in Interior design and started my own business called Luxe Interior Design where I was sub-contracted for many firms across Ontario and also got hired in Grand Cayman where I stayed for 1 year working in the Interior Design field.

- When asked what my passion was, it was the easiest question I could have been asked, as I have always loved Interior Decorating. In my career I was always shopping for customers at every Home Décor store imaginable. So it only makes sense that I followed my passion and now I can offer my favorite pieces to my customers through starting up InnerAllure. I am constantly on the look out for fabulous new pieces that are of great quality and that are affordable.

- By having my products online, customers are able to shop conveniently in the comfort of their own homes. I am always adding new products. Currently I offer shelves, vases, bowls, furniture, storage units, clocks, and many other products for your living room, kitchen, and bedroom or for a gift!

I pride InnerAllure on having impeccable customer service. If you have any questions please contact me at 647-866-5893 or at info@innerallure.ca where a staff member or I will call you back within a couple hours.
