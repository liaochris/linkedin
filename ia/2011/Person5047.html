Beth (Emerson) Aldrich’s Summary

As a Healthy Lifestyle-Green Living Expert, I represent eco-friendly brands and corporations through media interviews and appearances. I write about healthy living and green lifestyle products on my blog, RealMomsLovetoEat.com.

My integrative health and nutrition company, Restoring Essence and my media company, For Her Information Media (FHI), is dedicated to empowering, inspiring and informing conscious-minded women through one-on-one and corporate wellness counseling and public speaking as well as through print and radio. My slogan, "Savor your Life" brings awareness on all aspects of a women's life, from health and wellness to home, garden, career and family. My professional website is www.BethAldrich.com

My life's experiences have come full-circle enabling me to operate my integrative nutrition and media companies through networking, cooperative support and referrals. My recent television series (For Her Information) aired nationally on PBS (2006) in 60 markets and in is syndicated in Israel and Turkey. My radio shows, A Balanced Life and Conversations with Beth and Dan can be heard through podcasts on my blog.

I am able to share my expert advice in regular columns of my blog and numerous other media outlets (Twittermoms.com, hotmomsclub.com and diet.com) and on-air with our radio guests. My goal is to offer experts in various fields, authors and inspiring speakers the opportunity to promote their message through my media network and to encourage everyone I meet to live a healthy and happy life!

My intention is to launch my book tour in early 2012 and continue writing, producing television and radio projects that inspire and entertain and educate the public about the importance of living a healthy, nutritious and greener lifestyle !

Beth (Emerson) Aldrich’s Specialties:

I write about delicious, nutritious and sumptuous food; I represent eco-brands and corporations in the media and produce television and radio programming that entertains and inspires healthy living. As an Integrative Nutrition & Healthy Lifestyle Coach, I'm also available for keynote speaking, health presentations or media interviews; online website advertising opportunities available, for-credit Internships available.
