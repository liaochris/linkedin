Ian Burns's Summary

Ian Burns has more than a decade of experience in interactive, with work ranging from high profile advertising campaigns, to business-critical web applications. Ian has lead numerous successful projects from beginning to completion for some of the biggest, most demanding clients, delivering award-winning work that spans every major vertical. From Entertainment to Consumer Products, from Automotive to Corporate, from Real Estate to Banking, to Application Design, he was worked tirelessly to innovate and provide both clients and users with experiences that entertain, engage, and just plain work.

While at Blitz, Ian worked with dozens of high-profile clients including Microsoft, General Electric, Sony Pictures, Naked Juice, EA, and Lucas Arts.

In 2005, he worked in the design department at CBS Digital Media, in an effort to bring the high-quality demands of an agency to the online presence of the Tiffany Network. While at CBS, he designed both a new look for CBS.com and the Innertube Broadband Channel.

Before working at Blitz, Ian was an Art Director at Media Revolution. There he worked with major studios to produce a myriad of movie websites. He was integral to the company being awarded a multi-million dollar account with Sony Pictures to develop a series of websites for the Spiderman franchise.

Specialties

Creative Direction, Design, Art Direction, Flash Animation, After Effects, Team Management, Presentations.
