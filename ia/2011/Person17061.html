Harry Drok's Summary

My formal studies include a BA in Marketing and a Masters in Small Business and Entrepreneurship from the University of Groningen. Currently, my focus is on driving forward new eCommerce properties around the luxury goods market online. As a catalyst in developing leading player, SunnyWatches, I have been building up the network and strategy of the company online as well as consulting on a number of other successful Internet-based projects at Moho Holding B.V.

With ample experience as an eCommerce analyst and innovator, I have been leading successful growth activities within rapidly growing businesses in this sector. From operations to, strategy, development and execution - it's been a number of years in building successful eCommerce properties from the ground up. Flexible and self-reliant with multilingual communication skills, I have wide experience in developing vertical markets in the Internet space.
