Warren Wilczewski’s Summary

Warren Wilczewski is an energy market specialist, providing advisory and analytical services in energy markets and policy. Currently with ICF International, Warren develops market assessments for the propane industry, and analyzes various aspects of the natural gas market.

Focusing on natural gas and LNG, Warren has written for the LNG Journal, the Journal of Energy Security, and Policy Innovations. His previous experience includes research work at the Carnegie Council, and an advisory position with the Palau Mission to the UN.

Warren holds a B.A. from the University of British Columbia, and an M.S. in Global Affairs from NYU
