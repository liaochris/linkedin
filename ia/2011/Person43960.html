Tony Casabat's Summary

Employee Benefits broker with over 20 years of experience in designing competitive healthcare programs for mid-market and large companies.

I am effective and experienced in working with your Human Resource, Finance and Operations team, discussing your needs, developing an approach and executing on your employee benefits strategy.

As a trusted member of your team, I work with you in all phases of your program:

- Planning, strategy, negotiation and ongoing management
- Identify relevant cost reduction strategies
- Develop alternative self-funding and financing arrangements
- Focus on employee wellness, health and productivity
- Design a competitive benefits program benchmarked to your industry, size and geography
- Develop Employee satisfaction and readiness surveys
- Produce employee communications that demonstrates the value of your program
- Consumer directed healthcare and value-based benefits design

Flexible in my approach, creative, results oriented and highly effective at developing a health plan and vendor selection strategy that meets your benefit and financial needs. I bring both project management and negotiation skills in my work with you.

If you are looking for a broker/consultant, someone you can trust, who is experienced, effective and seriously committed to your success, please send me an e-mail or call, and let's talk about your needs.

I am supported by an exceptional team of local and national employee benefit professionals dedicated to serving the needs of my clients and producing results. CBIZ brings national resources delivered locally through our network of over 40 benefits and insurance offices located throughout the US.
