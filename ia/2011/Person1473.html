Alexis Nascimento’s Summary

Alexis (Dias) Nascimento specializes in integrated marketing and communications, with expertise in strategic planning, branding, public relations, message development and deployment, social media and event planning, and currently works as the public relations and social media manager for Magnum Boots USA. Prior to joining Magnum USA, Alexis worked in marketing and PR for Doctors Medical Center, and a Merced-based communications agency, On Target Marketing. Alexis also aided in forming Central Valley Public Relations Organization (CVPRO), where she has served as the chairperson, publicity chair and secretary since its inception.

Alexis resides in Turlock, where she lives with her husband Steven, and dogs Mo and Ruby. In her free time, Alexis fosters dogs in need and advocates for animal rescues/adoptions. For marketing and PR insights, news and tips, you can follow her on Twitter at http://twitter.com/AlexisNasc.

Alexis Nascimento’s Specialties:

Strategic planning; branding; public relations; event planning; media relations; interactive marketing and social media; message development and dissemination; news and feature writing; and research.
