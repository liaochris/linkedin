Mike Symes's Summary

Mike Symes is a multi-award winning financial and B2B branding strategist, communications expert, author and professional speaker.

The founder of the Strand group of companies (Strand Branding Limited and Strand Financial Limited), Mike has a passion for B2B branding and communications, drawing on over 20 years top-level experience working with global financial services brands.

He is also Chairman of online publishing company, Financial Marketing Limited (financialmarketing.co.uk) and FIDES, the premier networking group of service providers to financial institutions.

Mike’s senior client-side roles have included Brown Shipley, Credit Lyonnais and Banque Paribas group and latterly, Head of Marketing of Bank of New York Financial.

In 1998, Mike Symes was granted the Freedom of the City of London, one the oldest surviving traditional ceremonies still in existence today.
