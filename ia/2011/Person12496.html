Doug Turnbull's Summary

After attending university in the 1970's - my aim within the mining industry was to undertake a wide range of roles/functions/jobs - many within the same company and some (back then) would suggest the roles were diametrically opposed - example 1 - when I swapped a Production Managers Hat (looking after Shiploading, Beneficiation Plant, Mining, Contract Tyre Maintenance/Management and Mining Rehabilitation) for a Maintenance Managers Hat (looking after maintenance of fixed and mobile equipment of all above plus the civil contractor, company town maintenance and services maintenance (sewerage, water, power). Why - aim was to become in the true sense of the word - a General Manager that not many could hoodwink in any given discipline.

Specialties

His technical expertise covers operational mining, middle management mining operations and maintenance, contract management, tyre and rim applications engineering, supply chain management, some direct sales experience, MARC contract reviews and global applications engineering for in-pit crushing and conveying
