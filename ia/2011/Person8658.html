Cindy Fruitman [LION]’s Summary

A superior relationship builder, Cindy successfully partners with corporate clients and individuals to deliver client-centric service which supports both employees & organizations and champions sound business decisions with a long-term view to growth and sustainability. She is a dynamic facilitator & coach and persuasive communicator with a reputation for delivering learning beyond the classroom by developing and implementing resourceful, innovative initiatives for both internal and external clients on career management issues and training development strategies.

Passionate about facilitating client insight, finding resolutions to complex issues and helping others to move toward meeting their goals and aspirations, Cindy deeply believes that a sense of humour is a vital accessory for meeting every day challenges in both work and life. She encourages her clients to look to the words of Charles Darwin for inspiration in times of change and transition: “It is not the strongest of the species that survives, nor the most intelligent, but the one most responsive to change.”
