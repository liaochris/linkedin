Rita Keller’s Summary

Nationally known consultant to the CPA profession. Author and Speaker on CPA firm management issues.

Named to the list of Top 50 Blogs for Accountants
Repeatedly named to Top 100 Most Influential People in Accounting by Accounting Today. Passion for excellence in CPA firm management. My goal is for ALL firms to be managed efficiently and profitably.

Rita Keller’s Specialties:

Developing firm cultures to attract and retain future leaders of the CPA profession, mentoring programs, individual coaching/mentoring of managing partners and firm administrators/COOs, facilitation of partner and leadership retreats, helping CPA firm managers manage, establishing and maintaining women's initiatives, managing generational differences,implementation of strategic plans, succession planning and establishing effective processes for serving clients effectively and profitably.
