Michael Perry’s Summary

Leading with passion and energy I have built a 20+-year career that spans a variety of industries, holding executive level positions with some of the worlds best-known brands such as Wyndham Worldwide, Cendant, JPMorgan Chase, and Citibank.

My experience ranges from the client to service side, running international businesses with revenue in excess of $500 million dollars, to division level responsibility, to advising and partnering with start-ups and established businesses.

With a reputation as a first-class marketing and operations professional I am called upon to speak at webinars and conferences, moderate digital forums, and provide articles and blog entries for industry platforms. My marketing and sales teams have won numerous awards as well as established new industry benchmarks for performance; one area of continued accolade is the cutting edge application of emerging technologies, quantitative methods, and research techniques. On the operations side I have helped to reengineer divisions and processes resulting in over $75 million in effectiveness and efficiency gains to my credit; I am a Six Sigma Black Belt, certified at the project level.

I am extremely passionate about what I do; I understand that the one common thread across all businesses and market segments is people. I pride myself on focusing on staff and organizational development. I understand that things don’t happen in a day, but I will appropriately lead an organization to gain cultural alignment and drive business results.

I invite you to join me on Twitter - @mlperryny, FaceBook – www.facebook.com/mlperryny or here on LinkedIn – www.MLPerryNY.com.
