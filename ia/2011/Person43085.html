Nikki Cooke's Summary

I am a professional freelance copywriter, specialised in helping small to medium sized organisations generate more business.

To me, business words must work as hard as any other employee. After all, they are the voice and face of your company and must pull their weight.

What makes me different? With a background in Education, interest in psychology and innate writing talent, I understand what makes buyers tick.

With a Copywriting Diploma and freelancing for several marketing agencies behind me, I've built up oodles of experience writing for - and supporting - a host of sectors from Healthcare to IT.

More recently, we've built up solid expertise in Internet marketing, as well as Social Media. Although we continue to support SMEs with their offline marketing, we specialise in giving businesses that all-important digital footprint. Just ask...

On a personal note, I value integrity. And people are important to me... As many clients discover, I do my upmost to ensure clients are thrilled with the service they receive.

In my spare time, I write a copywriting blog - Copy Break - through which I give advice on how to write effective sales copy. When I'm not working, I publish online articles, tutor students and (try to) escape to the sea.
