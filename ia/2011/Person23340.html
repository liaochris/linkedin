Kandace Hudspeth's Summary

Ms. Hudspeth is a passionate leader and coach, focused on cultivating a culture of innovation. She develops interactive marketing strategies that combine the attitudes and interests of consumers, clients and creative’s to help produce effective advertising. Acting as the voice of the consumer within the agency, Ms. Hudspeth synthesizes research data to identify ideal audiences and optimum methods of communication. She combines market data, qualitative research and product knowledge within a core proposition to enable the creative team to produce advertising ideas that resolve defined business problems. With increasing public awareness of marketing strategies, a key challenge is to develop innovative ways to reach consumers. Ms. Hudspeth brings the consumer to the forefront of the process and motivates the team to work with the consumer in mind.

Client history includes: IBM, U.S. Army, Intel, MasterCard, Verizon, Unilever, General Mills, Diageo, Coca Cola, DaimlerChrysler, General Motors, P&G, Alberto Culver, Fairmont Hotels, Genworth Financial, and more.

Specialties

Interactive marketing professional with experience in strategic planning and market research as well as project and client management. Disciplines include the following: Digital Marketing Strategy, Relationship Marketing, CRM and Funnel Strategy, Program Design, Channel Development, Metadata Strategy, SEO, SEM, Social Media, Mobile Marketing, Creative Strategy, Persona Development, Audience Segmentation, User and Interactive Experience Strategy, and Technical Program Design
