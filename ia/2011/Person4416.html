Barbara Ficarra's Summary

Barbara Ficarra, RN, BSN, MPA

•Award-winning journalist, media broadcaster, speaker, media spokesperson, consultant, media trainer and creator/executive producer/host of Health in 30® radio show, founder & editor-in-chief of Healthin30.com. Writer for Huffingtonpost. Editorial Advisory Board and Consumer Health Expert for Sharecare.com.

•Received Journalism Award of Excellence for Coverage of Emergency Medicine from the American College of Emergency Physicians.

•Admin head nurse at a level 2 trauma center, covers multiple med/surg units, critical care, the inpatient psychiatric unit, women and children’s units, peds ER and multifaceted in her clinical experience.

•Quoted as an expert in health care for multiple journals and magazines.

•Active medical professional, experienced public speaker, popular keynoter, moderator, panelist and workshop leader at conferences, workshops and professional meetings.

•Selected as faculty to teach at the American Medical Association’s Medical Communications Conference in 2007 through 2009.

•Elected to the Board of the National Association of Medical Communicators, a member of New York Women in Communications, Association of Health Care Journalists. Serves on the Academy of Judges for the International Health Medical Media Awards,selected to the advisory board- Medical Communications Conference.

•Lead workshops and served as a one-on-one trainer to prepare doctors, nurses and others to succeed in television, radio and other media.

•Contacted by nytimes.com/health as a consultant, met with Director, Sarah Greene and Mike Mason, Editor of nytimes.com/health at their New York City office. Offered insight into ideas for new content for The New York Times Health website. Subsequently nytimes.com/health has implemented a number of her suggestions.
