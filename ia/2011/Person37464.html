Rob Jacobs's Summary

I currently work in full time ministry for Saddleback Church's School of Church Leadership.

I am the author of three blogs. “Education Innovation”, which was nominated in 2009 for Best Individual Blog in the Edublogs Awards, "The Learning Church" and “PLC_Next.” I am also a contributing author to the education policy blog Edurati Review.

In my free time, I think about the intersection and blending of organization and personal learning, training, teaching, innovation, collaboration, creativity, networks, technology, and marketing, and crowdsourcing. I also am developing an organizational approach to idea development called Lateral Wisdom. I love being a catalyst. Helping groups or individuals get ideas off the ground and implemented and I am usually volunteering with a number of groups or on projects at any given time.
