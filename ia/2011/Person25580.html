Laura Wagner's Summary

I assist businesses in setting up & using social media sites such as Twitter, Linkedin, and Facebook. I can help your business wade through the confusion of social media so you can find & attract more customers.

I do individual and group training in social media for beginners as well as more advanced users. I welcome speaking opportunities so I can spread the word about the benefits of social media.

With 10 years experience in Web design & development, I create clean, concise, user friendly websites with minimal code so they are easy for people to use & easy for search engines to find.

I'd like to help every small business owner have a professional looking, up-to-date, user-friendly web site they can be proud of. See my portfolio of 50 websites and Facebook business pages I have created for varying businesses and organizations. http://websbywagner.com/portfolio

I am passionate about what I do & love to work with like-minded business owners.

My official bio reads --

Laura Wagner formed her web design business, Webs by Wagner in 2003 after earning a degree in Web Development from Moraine Park Technical College the previous year. She then continued her schooling, earning a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Lakeland College in 2005.

From 2006 through 2009, Wagner worked as a web developer in the Marketing Department at Kohler Co and later taught Web Developer and Microsoft Office courses at MPTC. She continues to perfect her web design and programming skills while building a strong business with loyal customers.

Wagner also currently teaches social media classes to local business owners and their employees, helping them expand their web presence and gain new customers.

The strong work ethic Laura learned growing up has been enhanced through 30+ years of helping her husband Joe on their family farm and raising four children. She brings that work ethic, along with great sense of responsibility, to each Web & Social Media project.

Specialties

I specialize in helping you, the small business owner or employee attract more customers by creating for you a user friendly, quality website and by teaching you how to use social media and blogging to your advantage. I've done the learning so you don't have to. Let me show you how to get the most out of the web so you can focus your valuable time on the things you do best - your business.
