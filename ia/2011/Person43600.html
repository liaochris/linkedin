Ted Marcus's Summary

15+ years experience as a project/program manager for complex global enterprises.

Currently, I am on an engagement with GfK, a global market research company. GfK is consolidating its subsidiaries globally to achieve economies of scale and information sharing. The core of the program is migrating all 120+ subsidiaries to a Global WAN, a single ADEX platform, and enabling corporate wide Shared Services. I am managing the transition of sites in Austria, BNLX, Greece, Italy, Portugal, Spain, and Russia.

Additionally, I am the Global WAN Liaison, managing the WAN connection process for all our subsidiaries.

Previously, I consulted to HP SW on a network security project. Additional prior experience in mobile telecom, IT multinational infrastructure, and US hospital IT.

Specialties

global project management, IT project management, program management, general management, health care, business process redesign, presentation skills, negotiation, business process engineering, marketing and sales,
