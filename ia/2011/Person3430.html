Anuradha Udunuwara's Summary

I have more than 10 years of networking and communications industry experience. For the past several years, I have been mostly involved in architecting, designing, planing, implementing and maintenance of Service Provider Networks. I have designed and implemented SDH and Metro Ethernet based networks with more than 40 Network Elements and 100 Network Elements respectively, to deliver Leased Line, E-Line and E-LAN services mainly to business customers. Alcatel-Lucent, Cisco, ECI, Huawei, MRV, Tellabs and ZTE are some of the vendors/products that I have worked with. In technology wise, I have been working with PDH, SDH, FR, IP/MPLS, Ethernet and GPON technologies.

Currently I am working on Carrier Ethernet Aggregation, Metro Ethernet Access, GPON, WiMAX backhauling, NGN/IMS core networks, VSAT networks and network transformation.

Specialties

Preparing technical specifications, evaluating products/solutions, conducting testing, designing and planing Service Provider Networks.
