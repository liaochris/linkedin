Susan Wood’s Summary

I have been involved in the emissions trading markets, including NOx, SO2, NSR, and GHG, for over 15 years. This includes the startup of a brokerage company (The AirBank), developing projects, and assisting with regulatory program development. I have also marketed and traded oil and natural gas commodities in both the OTC and Nymex markets.

I am currently involved with developing climate change and renewable energy projects for the rapidly developing US carbon markets. My present company funds, develops and monetizes the environmental and energy commodities that result from these projects.

Susan Wood’s Specialties:

Building new businesses in esoteric markets, creating value in illiquid markets, emission trading program regulatory development, significant industry contacts, commodities trading.
