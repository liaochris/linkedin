Danilo Campos's Summary

I'm nuts about user experience. Disappointing UX inspired me to learn programming so I could do better. I cut my teeth on event-driven C-style scripting in Second Life five years ago and now I've got five iOS apps and a teensy OS X app under my belt.

I love making things for other people to enjoy.

My career has two constants: ambition and idealism. I constantly work toward personal and professional growth, while doing my best to give back and help my colleagues succeed. I can't escape my ideals and I don't want to – it's deeply important to do the very best work possible.

I'm a passionate guy and I love working with people who share my energy.
