Giorgio Migliaccio's Summary

Giorgio has close to 20 years’ experience in sales management, and in leading, coaching, mentoring and motivating teams for organisations such as Thomson Learning, Macmillan and the Australian Stock Exchange. He brings with him international experience having worked in the United Kingdom in the niche recruitment field of interim management where he worked closely with board level executives of the BOC Group, The Abbey and De La Rue. Giorgio has a Certificate IV in Training & Assessment, Diploma of Management, Graduate Certificate in Careers Education from RMIT and a Masters in Career Development from Edith Cowan University.

As a success coach, Giorgio now helps executives and individuals in Melbourne (and all States of Australia) create career success and as a business coach helps SME's and small business in Melbourne Victoria, Western Australia, Tasmania and South Australia improve productivity and increase profits.
