Reuben Heller's Summary

A freelance digital planner and consultant with over a decade's digital experience.

I've worked for leading agencies like AKQA, Dare, JWT, Poke, Publicis and Sapient. And on accounts as diverse as Orange, Shell and The Army. There have been a few awards along the way, including a Gold Cannes Lion.

I'm successful freelancer because I get to grips with projects, pitches and new teams very quickly. I get the job done with minimum fuss. My depth of experience means I am not phased by even the most fiendishly complex briefs. And I am very comfortable in front of senior clients.

I'm proud to have earned a number of recommendations from colleagues and clients. These are listed below.

My two areas of expertise in more detail:

1. Digital planning and strategy

I can handle all aspects of digital planning and strategy. From managing research through to writing creative briefs, reviewing work and presenting to clients at any level. I am comfortable with every digital channel and I have lots of experience in creating integrated campaigns. As you would expect, social media plays an increasing role in my work.

2. Digital consultancy for agencies

I help agencies to build a digital capability from scratch or improve their existing offering. This might involve recommending a suitable agency model, advising on what types of staff and processes are required or helping to select appropriate digital partners.

Reuben Heller's Experience

Digital Planner & Consultant

Reuben Heller Consulting Ltd

Internet industry

May 2005 – Present (5 years 11 months)

I've worked with a number of leading agencies on a freelance or consulting basis.

A key project was the multi award winning One Army Recruiting campaign. The central element of this project was a complete overhaul of the Army Jobs website, to make it more relevant and appealing to potential recruits. The associated TV ads comprised of first person vignettes from serving Army personnel. Each ad finishes on a cliffhanger. Viewers go online to find out what happens next.

More recently, I've developed a social media strategy for a leading telco and a three year digital strategy for a large public sector employer.

As well as my planning work, I also consult with advertising agencies looking to improve their digital capabilities.
