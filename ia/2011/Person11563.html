"Factotem" may be considered a facetious term, as I wear many hats, and am not above operating a copy machine at midnight, when the case requires. Although I am still mainly booked with existing clients, we are expanding, and prospective new clients are welcome to apply. Please email for details.

We are available for all legal support services, including service of process, record retrieval from all local courts and police stations, video and audio recording, interviewing, background checking, and legal research.
Last-minute deadlines are fine. I often work far into the night, should your case require it, to ensure your documents--and your presentation--are court-ready by morning.

I've been fortunate to have studied under several prominent investigators, and am a graduate of "The Private Investigators Academy Of The Rockies." I was honored with having my completed homework assignments designated "classified" off the record. Never know what us fresh eager PIs might discover!

I am fairly active politically; I spoke before the House regarding Proposed Bill 07-1083, which would have required the licensing of Private Investigators in Colorado. I am in favor of licensing, and believe that well-drafted and specific licensing requirements will benefit Colorado Investigators. Currently, as there are no licensing requirements here, I include legislative work among my current projects, and goals.
