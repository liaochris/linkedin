Tim Windsor's Summary

I am Editorial Director, South at Patch.com. My focus here is on helping to build a new approach to local news and information.

In 2010, I launched and managed more than 170 community news sites in Pennsylvania, Maryland, Washington, D.C., Virginia, Georgia and Florida. I oversee the work of 19 regional editors and as many as 300 local editors.

Patch is a network of community-specific news and information sites dedicated to providing comprehensive and trusted local coverage for individual towns and communities. Editorial Directors together form the basis of a high-level editorial committee, working closely with the editor in chief to determine and execute the editorial strategy of the company.

Previously, as the Director of Digital Strategy for The Johns Hopkins University and as VP, interactive for The Baltimore Sun Media Group, I led the expansions of two of the most powerful names in higher education and local news deeper into the digital space, reaching our online audiences wherever they were -- at their desks, on the go, and on their phones.

Specialties

Strategic planning, team-building, leadership, vision, revenue generation, industry knowledge, futurism, user interface, site architecture, usability, advertising creative, video, audience development
