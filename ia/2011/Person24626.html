Kim Bishop's Summary

20+ years in Clinical R&D gained in industry and academia working in global project teams in Clinical Research, Clin Ops and Drug Safety/PV, have finance/budget planning experience and an in-depth understanding of the drug development process, GCPs, and clinical trials conduct. Experience in psychiatry, neurology, ophthalmology, cardiology, dermatology, infectious disease, transplantation, and immunology.

Educated in pharmaceutical medicine, cognitive psychopharmacology and experimental psychology. My passion is "cognitive and behavioral drug research" and "my business". I founded Global Pharma Consultancy, LLC (GPC) in Basel Switzerland in 1999 and PA, USA in 2005.

GPC is a company of specialists providing R&D services across the healthcare and educational sector throughout North & South America, Europe, Middle East, Africa and Asia Pacific.

GPC Services: CNS & Cognitive Testing, Clinical Operations, QA & Compliance, Drug Safety & Pharmacovigilance, Training & Education, E-Learning, Audio/Video Production, and Business Initiatives.

In addition, GPC specializes in the provision of all the necessary expertise and resources needed for companies to develop new medicinces for CNS conditions with cognitive impairment and/or to assess the cognitive effects of CNS-active substances. We offer independent advice re selection of cognitive tests, psychiatric rating scales and clinical assessments (e.g., study-specific Cognitive Test Batteries, including "Executive Function Test Batteries"), customization of testing materials (stimulus booklets, etc.), prep of training materials (manuals, etc.), operational activities, training to report writing and publications.

We also offer best in class online training solutions - hosting and global network, campus set up and management, online tutoring, content development, audio/video production, online course building modules. Why not get your own campus?

Liaison offices in USA, Europe and Latin America.

Specialties

Cognitive Testing, Clinical Rating Scales, online GCP training in Spanish, webinar on inspection readiness, Bilingual Auditors in USA, Latin America & Europe, Drug Safety, Clinical Trial Monitoring & Management, Network of Medical Writers, Online Training Platform, Content Development & Video Production, Business Initiatives
