Shawn Melton’s Summary

I have worked in Information Technology since the September of 2002. My focus has been in hardware support starting out with desktop support and moving up to server support. For six years I worked with four different agencies for State of Alabama. I love getting involved in all aspects of design, maintenance, and implementation of a network system. I left State employment for the private industry and began working as a contractor for the government in October of 2008.

I began working with SQL Server more around 2006 and in 2008 I began expanding my knowledge in SQL Server. I started taking a a few certification test around summer of 2009. Before I knew it I earned the MCITP DBA for SQL Server 2005. Then around the fall of 2010 I took the MCTS test for SQL Server 2008 and passed it fairly easily.

My ambitions now are to continue learning SQL Server and continue the certification path toward earning a Microsoft Certified Masters in SQL Server 2008. I still have a few years to get some experience under by belt and also a ton of knowledge.
