Rika Susan's Summary

I am privileged to have the opportunity to introduce myself to you. My name is Rika Susan and I am writing this from my home-office in South Africa's Western Cape.

I specialize in the health, nutrition and fitness fields, as you can see in the jazzy recipes for juicers and vegetable juicing at www.best-juicing.com. I also offer healthy weight loss tips and advice.

My interest in and research about healthy nutrition began in 1979 when I was diagnosed with Chronic Fatigue And Immune Dysfunction Syndrome (CFIDS) or ME. I have been living with the illness for 31 years.

Although I am housebound to a large extent as a result of the CFIDS, I feel privileged to be able to reach beyond my physical boundaries through the Internet. This is such an amazing tool, expanding the world of people with physical limitations and challenges! It is possible to make friends all over the world, without moving from the comfort of an easy chair!

Although it isn't easy to live with a chronic illness, the CFIDS has been a teacher in many ways. I believe that, while we can't always control what happens to us, we can always choose how we are going to react to a given circumstance. And I choose to learn from the CFIDS. It has made me a better person.

For me, my vegetable juicer is one way of ensuring that I get all the nutrients my body needs. Juice is also super easy to digest, if my energy is low.

Incorporating using a juice extractor into your daily diet and nutrition program, can be really helpful, especially if you are struggling with your weight. Juicing for weight loss has a whole host of benefits compared to a water fast!
