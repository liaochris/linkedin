Monica Wright's Summary

Monica Wright is a veteran online marketer specializing SEO and social media, and has worked with national and regional clients in a wide variety of industries including hi-tech, hospitality & tourism, and higher education.

Monica has over 10 years of media and content marketing experience, with a strong focus on driving online audience to media companies. Prior to joining agency life, she worked as the Audience Development Manager for MaineToday.com, recognized nationally as one of the leading interactive programs in the newspaper industry.

• 13 years of proven marketing, promotions, branding, creative direction, project management and product development experience.
• Excellent grasp of building and growing a successful website using search engine optimization (SEO), social media, Internet marketing, and traditional marketing and advertising programs.
• Demonstrated strategic thinking, leadership skills, and relationship management experience, including proven ability to lead multi-functional teams to drive concrete actions and results.
• Expertise in social media marketing/optimization (SMM/SMO), including developing viral marketing campaigns.
• Deep understanding of search engine technology, functionality and optimization tactics.
• Knowledge of multiple analytics packages and employing strategies based on data collection.
• Involved with SEO community on and offline, the surrounding blogosphere and via social media.
