Deb Reiser’s Summary

I have over 15 years of experience in user interface design and information architecture. Working with cross-functional teams and project stakeholders, I have led the definition and design of information-rich Web sites, Web-based applications, medical equipment interfaces, and Windows-based software applications. Using research methods such as focus-groups, field studies, heuristic evaluations, usability studies, and in-depth interviews, I've worked to ensure that user needs, expectations and goals, as well as business objectives, are translated into engaging and learnable interactive experiences.

Deb Reiser’s Specialties:

user experience definition and design, information architecture, information design, persona development, project coordination, strategy definition, usability evaluations
