Brian Smith's Summary

Brian Smith is a Senior Sales Executive with Wells Fargo Insurance Services. With over 20 years of experience in the insurance industry, Brian has worked in the areas of Risk Control and Commercial Insurance sales. Prior to joining Wells Fargo he served in various positions that included risk control consultant, and risk control practice leader, and finally producer/business development.

"I create an environment of mutual trust, technical competence, and sound judgement. Clients view my work as needed, prompt and thorough".

Professional designations include: Commercial Insurance Counselor (CIC), Associate in Risk Management, (ARM), and as a Certified Safety Professional, (CSP).

InsuranceEdge℠, an American Express OPEN® division, has recently added Brian Smith as an Insurance Advisor.

Specialties

Developer and creator of The Risk Reconnaissance™ Program, Insurance blogging, Emerging and Middle Market companies, Commercial Risk Management, Cyber Liability, Government Contractors, and Safety & Risk Control Practices.
