Nathalie Nahai's Summary

I'm the Founder of We Make Them Click, a leading consultancy and design agency that specialises in the psychology behind successful website design and communication.

Since effective communication involves more than online exchanges, I also deliver seminars on how to use applied psychology and body language to achieve success in your business.

I am on several knowledge and innovation boards, including THECUBE’s Idea Engineering and Brain Bank, and have contributed to publications including Mashable and the Skeptic Magazine.

As well as writing articles on website design and web design psychology, I also collaborate with leading agencies to deliver workshops, and I am currently working as the Communications Director for the Third Sector Social Media convention.
