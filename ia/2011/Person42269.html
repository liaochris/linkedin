John Swisshelm’s Summary

I'm a gameplay and combat designer at EA's Visceral games studio, where I'm wrapping up Dante's Inferno PDLC and stoked about creating awesome new action experiences in the future!

Before Visceral, I had internships as a Technical Designer on Steven Spielberg's "LMNO" at EA Los Angeles and as an Assistant Producer with EA Tiburon's Central Online team.

At Georgia Tech, I was Design Lead on 12 student and indie game projects.

I love bringing ideas to the screen, and putting my best into a game is always triumphantly rewarding when I get to watch people at play.

Check out my personal games and art at www.swisshelm.com!
