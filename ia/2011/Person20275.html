Jennifer Sotelo's Summary

Through my entrepreneurial spirit and drive, I was led to the start-up, "Ego Miami" magazine, where I had the opportunity to build a sales department from scratch. I moved to Los Angeles in 2005 to launch "Ego LA" magazine and gained first-hand experience in running an office of employees and strategically developed a brand in a city unfamiliar with the magazine. Since then I have joined "Los Angeles" magazine, a nationally recognized and award-winning publication, where I am constantly strengthening my sales and leadership skills.

Specialties

Specialize in the southwest region of the U.S. working along side some of the most visible brands in the country including Mc Donald's, Walt Disney World, Ritz-Carlton Hotels, and Amtrak. Industries of focus are travel, real estate, fashion, packaged goods, liquor, beer, and non-profits.
