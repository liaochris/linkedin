Kathryn Jones's Summary

Who I am: A creative, versatile, efficient, adaptable, flexible and dependable graphic designer/production artist and photographer with broad knowledge of the field and excellent skills in the Adobe Creative Suite programs and in QuarkXpress. I have many years of experience in all facets of design, from conceptualization to layout, to coordinating with vendors and producing the final printed piece. I'm used to handling and organizing multiple design projects and keeping them all on track and within deadline. I have very good communication skills, both verbal and written. I copy edited, proofread and contributed to the writing of many pieces I’ve worked on.
I'm looking for opportunities in print design and photography. My goal is to build up my portrait photography business and to increase my web design skills to complement my print and photography expertise.

Specialties

Design and illustration for print and web, proofreading, copy editing, photography.
