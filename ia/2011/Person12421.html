Douglas Carroll's Summary

Emmy and Webby-nominated photojournalist, with over 20 years experience; specializing in environmental, sports and breaking news. Peabody Award for participation in CNN's coverage of the aftermath of Hurricane Katrina. DuPont Award.

Specialties

Northeast Photojournalist for CNN Sports Illustrated for 10 years, covering all major sporting events from the Olympic Games to the World Series. Producer, Shooter, Editor for 'The Business of Green', an Environmental-Business on-line series.

worked on the emmy-nominated web series, 'The Business of Green' for CNNMoney and Fortune.com, as producer, shooter, editor (complete one man band and the roadie)
