Sam Collyer's Summary

Based in Sydney, Sam is the chief media contact for Caltex, Australia's largest oil refiner and fuels marketer. As Caltex's Media Adviser, Sam is responsible for the daily engagement of Caltex in Australian news media, providing advice to the company on its media strategy and assisting journalists to better understand the refining and fuel retailing sectors.

Sam spent three years as Editor of Lloyd’s List Daily Commercial News, including extensive daily newswriting and editing experience. Born and educated in Newcastle NSW, with experience in print and radio newsrooms at Fairfax and the ABC, Sam has been a leading commentator on Australia's commercial transport market, writing and editing news and analysis of issues affecting freight across the sea, air, road and rail sectors. He has also previously contributed news, analysis and opinion pieces to Lloyd’s List in London as a Foreign Correspondent.

Specialties

News reporting, copy editing, newspaper production, online news, publishing, photography, news analysis, blog. Also, freight transport, logistics, bulk shipping, transport infrastructure, containerisation, break bulk, supply chain, freight rail, road transport, airfreight, aviation. Fuel refining, oil, Australian retail and convenience store operation. Liquid transport fuels and energy.
