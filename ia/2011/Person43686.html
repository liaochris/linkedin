J. Todd Greeno's Summary

27-plus years in Information Systems and Business Technology, providing technology-based consulting to key business areas of Pfizer Inc. Project areas include Finance, Strategic Planning, Legal, Civic Affairs, Human Resources, Marketing, Manufacturing, Executive Support and Informatics.

In 2005, Todd provided direct support to Pfizer's Tsunami Disaster Relief teams, and joined the Hurricane Katrina Disaster Relief team, working in the field for 5 weeks where the eye made landfall. We distributed more than $2 million of pharmaceuticals, and helped to reestablish the medical supply chain for the stricken areas.

Todd creates a competitive advantage for organizations and bottom line cost savings by leveraging information technology solutions while controlling and reducing IS expenses and has a reputation as an innovative and dynamic agent of change. Extensive experience with rapidly evolving organizations, and corporate restructurings.

Led global cost-reduction initiatives that included data-center "footprint reduction", applications retirement, and business process reengineering.

Prior to his experiences at Pfizer Inc, Todd flew jet Strike-Fighters for the US Navy, including two trans-Pacific deployments onboard USS Enterprise, CVN65, homeported in San Francisco, CA. Logged 238 carrier arrested landings in A-7 Corsair, A-4F Skyhawk and TA-4J/F Skyhawk aircraft. 2,250+ hours total jet time and 700+ hours civilian time. No accidents or flight violations. Flight Instructor; Squadron Legal Officer. FAA Certifications include Airline Transport Pilot, Commercial and Multi-Engine.

Todd transitioned from the US Navy to business via the University of Pennsylvania Wharton MBA program.

Specialties

Technology Governance & Standards Compliance; Competitive Analysis; Innovation; Legal Discovery; Website Design; XML, HTML, JavaScript, User Experience, CSS; Problem-Solving; Project Management Cost Reduction & Control.
