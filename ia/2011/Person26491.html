Lisa Esposito’s Summary

Cvent is the largest event management software company in the world with 600+ employees and 80,000 users in 34 countries. Cvent offers software to help meeting planners increase event attendance and decrease event costs. In addition, it has tools that help meeting planners find event venues, and marketers conduct web surveys and emarketing campaigns.

I have been working as a senior sales associate specializing in consultative selling and cold calling for 2 years. During this time I have honed my communication, presentation, lead generation, prospecting and relationship building skills. Currently I am working on a newly developed team (Mid Market Team) that focuses on companies from the $500 Million to $1 Billion dollar range. In this position I have worked with the marketing, sales operations, CRM and technology teams to define processes. I have also created marketing communication collateral, planned events, researched companies and used extensive social media and databases sources to generate leads. Despite all my individual responsibility I collaborate heavily with my colleagues and work directly with my Sales Executive to execute complete sales cycles.
