Erik Bowman’s Summary

Erik Bowman is an author, speaker, serial entrepreneur and business consultant. He founded and is current President of Guanzi Institute TM. Through the Guanzi Institute he has created the Certified Entrepreneur TM training program that empowers first-time entrepreneurs, existing business owners and corporate managers with the skills they need to succeed in any industry.

Based upon his 20 years of hands-on business startup experience, consulting and teaching experience he has been able to condense a wealth of knowledge into simple, understandable and actionable modules.

As a business and technology consultant, Erik has worked with companies of all sizes in both public and private sectors, from start-ups and small businesses to large corporations and major utility companies. Erik holds a double major from California State University in Political Science (summa cum laude) and Philosophy.

As a serial entrepreneur, Erik is a results-oriented, strategic thinker with proactive, creative and analytical abilities. He works successfully with all functions and levels of management.

Erik is an industry veteran, having founded and developed iMessenger.com, one of the first 'social networking' sites, in 1997.

Over the past 11 years he has gone on to found a number of multimedia and communications companies including Talent-Exchange.com a co-branded multimedia talent portfolio service launched in 2001, VerticalAnswer.com a Hosted PBX and Telephony service provider launched in 2003, ProviderOffice.com an appointment scheduling and call center solution for healthcare providers launched in 2003, PublicityEngine.com an XML/RSS based press release service that featured syndication in most popular feed formats and PeopleScreening.com video resume hosting service for individuals with private-labeled online career center for businesses and recruiters launched in 2006.
