Drew Sing’s Summary

In addition to graduating with my Bachelor’s in Finance this May, I have also had the opportunity to study abroad and increase my global awareness. Through Boston University, I studied in Sydney during the fall semester of 2010. My studies consisted of researching the economics and politics of the Pacific Rim while interning full time for a web-based financial startup. As a first generation born Chinese-American with experience abroad in China, Japan, Australia, and New Zealand, I have personally experienced the meshing of Asian and Western cultures and the unique dynamics and challenges it brings to today’s world of business and relationships.

Much of my college work experience revolved a startup I co-founded. This experience gave me a firm grasp on how tech startups operate and the unique work cultures that many of them foster. While we have ceased pursing our web application, it has taught me the importance of relationships and networking within an industry.

Professionally, I understand that it takes people of diverse talents, skills, and thoughts to make extraordinary progress. This is why I enjoy networking and building strong relationships.
