Ed Breaux’s Summary

Ed has assumed a wide range of roles in the software development industry, including management, marketing, business planning, quality assurance, programming, and consulting. Over the course of more than 25 years he repeatedly identified business needs, then proposed and implemented solutions designed for flexible, long-term growth. Ed has managed projects, systems, employees, products, and product lines in a variety of applications and systems environments: business, finance, engineering, education, marketing, retail, web applications, scheduling, software languages and tools, 3D computer graphics, and artificial intelligence. Ed has worked on mission-critical software systems for Ford (multiple divisions), Mellon Bank, Texas Instruments, and DEC.

Ed has productized his 32 years of professional experience by creating the Millennial Corporation model for business, a model which is engineered for 21st century growth. The model is energizing North Shore Technologies, where the highly leveraged system allows everyone, from senior management down to part-timers, to tap each other’s productized knowledge. Contributing to the design of Millennial Corporations, North Shore’s Chairman and COO, Bob Martin, has captured his 20+ years experience in technology staffing, creating two Millennial Corporation models, the Millennial Sales Force model and the Millennial Technology model. Ed is currently working with a broad range of industries to spread this model, which has shown itself to be robust, flexible, and resilient.

Ed Breaux’s Specialties:

The Millennial Corporation model for business, providing a proven structure for high-growth companies in the 21st century. Technology and technology staffing. Global supply network. Education.
