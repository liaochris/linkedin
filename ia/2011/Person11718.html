Dexter Valles's Summary

I am a Mumbaikar by residence and a Goan by old roots. Which explains the unusual name combo. Dexter is very English and Valles is very Spanish.

An MBA specializing in Marketing Management from the reputed Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies, Mumbai, batch of 1986. Across life this far, I have charted an interesting career over twenty one years.

My current occupation is CEO - Valmar International, which is a Management Consultancy in HR Management , Training and Research.

My core professional experiences are founded in International Business with an extensive career founded in the arena of International Marketing & Sales ( as DGM Exports with leading Indian Engineering firms ), later moving on Professor of Management Studies & Corporate Trainer in Behavioural Sciences Training -with leading Indian firms and also Education (as International Business School Head and Faculty - Mumbai campus) spanning across an absorbing 21 years of active engagement with the business of doing business.

Empanelled as Lead Trainer & Learning Facilitator, I estimate that I have facilitated the learning of over 12,000 participants across the national and international corporate horizon through several intensive training workshops across a fairly fulfilling range of learning, with key Indian Corporate Houses
