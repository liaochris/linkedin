Arvind Ashok's Summary

Trained and accomplished interaction designer. Finalist at international student design competition at CHI 2007.

Have managed off-shore development team and worked with CEO, CTO and marketing team to drive the design and development of a social media startup.

Conducting user research and understanding the business and functional requirements of the company leads to drawing up a initial design spec and wireframe. Then add detail to this by using scenarios and low-fi prototypes. These are validated by usability testing. Then develop high-fidelity prototypes using tools like InDesign (or Photoshop - or even code it in HTML if necessary). Conduct usability testing. Draw up final documentation and ensure that development proceeds according to specification. This is my typical process. With a strong technical background, I am equipped with various lenses required to empathize and understand various parts of the organization.

Specialties

Requirements gathering, Scenario/Storyboarding, Prototyping, Usability testing, workflow analysis, user research, design!
IHMC Cmap Tools, InDesign and most of the Adobe Creative Suite.
