Gabrielle DeTora’s Summary

Gabrielle DeTora has nearly two decades of healthcare strategic marketing experience and a proven track record in effective market research, strategic planning, campaign strategy, media planning, public relations, consumer/physician sales and marketing, and e-marketing. Gabrielle is a frequent presenter for healthcare conferences and forums such as The Healthcare Roundtable, the Snowmass Women’s Health Conference, and the Healthcare Association of New York State. Along with being on the editorial board for HealthLeadersMedia and The Ireland Report, Gabrielle has written feature articles for HealthLeadersFinance, Healthcare Advertising Review, Healthcare Marketing Report, and MHS (American Marketing Association’s health publication). She has been a quoted expert in many publications including HealthLeaders. Her white papers and presentations are used in healthcare business and marketing curriculums in MBA programs from the Haas School of Business (UC-Berkeley) to Princeton University.

At Princeton Partners, Gabrielle served as the VP of Health Strategy and oversaw all strategic business planning, branding, marketing and execution for New York City Health & Hospitals Corporation, North Shore-LIJ Health System, Penn State Hershey, Capital Health System, Westchester Medical Center, and the New Jersey Council of Teaching Hospitals and its thirteen health systems. As the Senior Director of Marketing and PR for St. Joseph’s Healthcare System and as the Director of Marketing and PR for Hunterdon Healthcare System, Gabrielle managed overall branding, strategic planning, marketing and sales efforts, promotions, public relations, and education. Her past work also includes serving as the National Director of Development and Marketing for the Cure for Lymphoma Foundation and as a Campaign Manager for the NYC Chapter of the Leukemia & Lymphoma Society. Gabrielle has an MBA from Kennedy-Western University and a BBA from the Zicklin School of Business (CUNY).
