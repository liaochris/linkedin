Krista Reiner Carnes's Summary

I have worked in a variety of publishing and book selling roles for 15 years ranging from bookstore management to editorial consulting to publicity. For the last 10 years I've focused my love of books on helping new and established authors market their books and promote their expertise through custom tailored speaking development and marketing campaigns.

I started Booking Authors to meet a need many non-fiction authors and experts have – implementing an effective speaking engagement strategy as an integral part of an overall marketing plan for their books and their businesses.

As with any worthwhile marketing endeavor, effective speaking engagement development takes time and consistent attention and action. We brings clients:

Marketing insight on positioning their expertise as a speaker
Development of engaging topic descriptions
Critical knowledge of event coordination
Insight into direct competition
In-depth research on the key issues facing ideal audiences, The organization, communication and negotiation skills necessary to generate interest and, most importantly, book those gigs!

Specialties

Book/author marketing strategy, speaking engagement generation, author representation, contract negotiation, and writing.
