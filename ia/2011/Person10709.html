David Cockbain's Summary

Managing Director - SAFE Group

Safety and Forensic Engineering (SAFE) is an Australian company specialising in all areas of Risk Engineering, HSE Systems, technical HSE auditing and high level Board development.

My working life has been heavily involved in the mining and construction industry, both nationally and internationally with experience in both undergound and Open Cut mining - both Coal and Metalliferous in operations and management roles.

In 2000, I commenced my own business (SAFE) with a business partner concentrating on incident investigations/determinations for legal counsel and specialist technical auditing and Occupational Health and Safety/Risk consulting for construction, engineering, mining, power generation and telecommunications firms.

SAFE has steadily grown, both in experience and numbers of professionals employed with up to 17 professionals engaged on Projects. Professional qualifications are in the Occupational Health and Safety, Engineering, Risk and technical auditing fields .

Work undertaken includes specialist technical auditing of Plant and Machinery for Mining organisations, technical risk engineering, specialist consulting on International Mining and Construction, Statutory Auditing of tunnelling and Mining operations, Corporate HSEC/HSE auditing, HPI/Specialist investigations on behalf of Regulators and Corporate Counsel and auditing of major projects for OFS as Federal Safety Officer,

The company investigates and reports on hundreds of major incidents per year (5000 to date), consults both Nationally and Internationally and completes research into personal damage prevention for the benefit of Industry and Regulators with a large percentage of Pro-bono systems development provided to not for profit organisations..

Specialties

Multipractice firm specialising in
* Risk engineering & HSE across wide range of industry sectors.
* Hazardous areas/Process auditing.
* Forensic Investigations of all types of incidents- Rail, Mining, Industrial/Commercial;
* Technical Auditing of Open Cut & Underground Mines, Industrial Operations, Smelters, Rail, Aviation and FMCG.
* Specialist training in Risk Management (RTO), Electrical Safety Systems, Plant Safety, Operation & Maintenance,
* Specialist support in Risk Engineering
