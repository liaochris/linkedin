Megan Libon’s Summary

Growing up, Megan Libon had a love towards graphic design the intricacies that were involved. Initially starting out as a hobby in her last year of high school, graphic design has blossomed into a passion that has changed her life, personally and academically. Constantly having the need to create beautiful works of art by the power of the computer, Megan has become the go-to designer for many of her friends. She has spent hours designing websites, advertisements, and flyers for many student groups, both within and outside of her college.

Initially, Photoshop was her main tool for design, but once she made the transition from biology major to graphic design, she soon was looking for different outlets. This brought her into the new realms of working with Adobe CS3, which included the highly skilled programs of: Photoshop, Illustratior, Flash, InDesign, and Dreamweaver. She is continually developing her knowledge of HTML and CSS for further web design.
