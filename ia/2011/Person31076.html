Mark Hurley's Summary

My name is Mark Hurley. I’m a management consultant looking to put my skills to use for you. I provide honest, reliable, and useful advice for business owners. I’m looking to partner with start-up, early stage, and lower-middle market businesses to help them succeed.

I founded a business advisory firm named Venture97, we’re a group of professionals endeavoring to provide unbiased advice and honest solutions for business owners. In this capacity, I’ve found success for my clients in three areas:

1. Collaborating closely with business owners and managers to craft smart strategies to structure appropriate equity and debt financing to fund investment and operations.

2. Creating effective solutions to revitalize businesses that have faced hard times, with a goal of returning already established businesses that might be down on their luck back to the days of profitability. Teamwork, restructuring, and recapitalizing are the name of the game.

3. Develop exits that maximize value for owners, preserving the value of your most important asset – your business.

I have a keen eye for assessing both strategy and day-in and day-out tactical execution. Every business is different and the path to success is different for every venture. That’s why I don’t offer boring, boiler-plate, one-size-fits all advice. Together, we can forge ahead with effective strategies for funding, ideas that grow market presence, and maximizing exit value. There’s no fast talk, no funny business. I’m here to offer results and exchange ideas. Your success is what matters.

I look forward to speaking with you about the next successful phase of your business. Send me a request to connect on LinkedIn, or you can email me directly at info@venture97.com. I’m here to help pave the way to your success. All you need to do is take that first step.

Specialties

Strategic Partnerships, Joint Ventures, Funding Strategies, Turn-Arounds, Recapitalization Strategies, Forecasting, Financial Planning, Contract Negotiation and Valuations for Exits.
