Bryce Norman's Summary

Bryce's work purpose is to empower people to break free from limitation, live in joy and fulfill their dreams.......To be "F"'n AWESOME!
Bryce Norman is a Certified Life Coach and has been on a path of personal and professional development since the summer of 1999.
After a friend and mentor asked him the following question, his life has never been the same: “If you keep on doing what you’ve been doing, where do you see yourself in five years?” That was the moment that catapulted Bryce into the understanding that self-awareness, coupled with a shift within an individual, leads to inspired responsibility and right action, and then ultimately to great success.
Clear on his goals for the present and future and, as an active participant in the world of business and the expansion of consciousness, Bryce practices a process of fully applying the profound truth behind Zig Ziglar’s famous quote: “You can have everything in life you want if you will just help enough other people get what they want.”
He achieved tremendous success in applying this motto during his time as a Sales Trainer with Peak Potentials Training at their Millionaire Mind Intensives across North America. His work supported thousands of people in shifting their negative beliefs about money, and inevitably themselves, to a more positive paradigm, one that is indeed necessary for the evolution of our race.
Bryce is passionate about the emerging and increasing need for such a shift in global consciousness. His goal is to make the world a better place for his two sons and for all children who share so immensely in the construction of the human fabric. The first place to begin is within!
