Len Keeler's Summary

Advertising sales professional with experience in business to business magazines and digital publishing.

My objective is to help publishers and content providers communicate the value of their target audience to potential advertisers.

I have lectured internationally, including at the Japan Marketing Association’s “JMA World Marketing Conference,” on improving ROI in the digital age, and was chosen by AMACOM, the publishing division of the American Management Association, to author one of AMA's first books on online marketing.

Specialties

advertising, marketing, sales, association, B2B, crm, email, planning, presentations, promotions, proposals, prospecting, research, web
