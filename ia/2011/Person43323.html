Tim Bush's Summary

A member of the Institute of Direct Marketing (The IDM), with 12 years of marketing experience, I have established a proven track record of consistently delivering successful marketing initiatives within both the technology and professional services industries.

With an entrepreneurial nature and a strong interest in community marketing, I specialise in the development of marketing strategy, lead generation and partnership management.

I am an active member of the tech and marketing community and have considerable experience of all aspects of the marketing mix, coupled with great communication and networking skills.

I have recently successfully graduated from the one-year certificate in digital marketing programme with the Institute of Direct Marketing and I am now studying for a Diploma in B2B Marketing. This is also with the IDM.

Additionally, in June 2009 I was appointed as education advisor to Huddle.net. This role involves me consulting on marketing and market development activity within the academic sector and is carried out in my spare time.

Marketing, Product Management, Strategy, NPD, Affiliate Partnership Management, Product Launches and Business Development, Digital Marketing, Online, Internet, Social Media, B2B Marketing, Podcasting
