Freelance Writer

Emily Maloney

Writing and Editing industry

January 2003 – Present (8 years 3 months)

Writing: Professional technical, copywriting, and web writer with over five years' experience for a variety of applications, including real estate, public health, print and web advertising, commercial and residential real estate development, and scientific, medical, and Olympic history, as well as contributing to one book currently in print. Additional experience in writing standardized test items and response sheet design. Current projects include two books in progress.

Editing: Edited five manuscripts, fiction and nonfiction, and additional experience in editing scientific or technical copy for web and print.

Business and Corporate Writing: Past work has included Pearson, Prairielands Addiction Technology Transfer Center at the University of Iowa College of Public Health as a technical writer and web editor, providing copy to Realtors(R) for web content and print advertising, and writing for commercial and residential real estate development. I have written white papers, drafted user manuals, and written business process documentation, as well as edited and assembled internal audit reports. I also have experience as a proposal writer.

Internet, HTML, and Desktop Publishing: Experience building and maintaining a number of websites, including those with Flash or Fireworks components. Comfortable with HTML, some Java and PHP. Facility with Adobe Creative Suite line, including Dreamweaver, Fireworks, Flash, Illustrator, Photoshop, Acrobat, MS Word, Works, Excel, Publisher, Visio, ProTools, Final Cut Pro, HarborFlex. Types 120 WPM. Familiar with Mac, Windows, and Linux platforms.

Creative Writing and Freelance Journalism: Poems, essays, and journalism have appeared in or are forthcoming from The North American Review, Ellipsis magazine, elimae, McSweeney’s, The Adirondack Review, and elsewhere.
