William Exeter's Summary

William L. Exeter is the President and Chief Executive Officer for Exeter 1031 Exchange Services, LLC, Exeter Fiduciary Services, LLC, Exeter IRA Services, LLC and their affiliate companies.

Mr. Exeter has been in the financial services industry since 1980 and entered the 1031 exchange and fiduciary services industries in 1986. He has written and lectured extensively on various tax deferred and tax exclusion strategies, including 1031 Exchanges, 1033 Exchanges, 1034 Exchanges, 721 Exchanges and 121 Exclusions, as well as Tenant-In-Common (TIC) Properties as like-kind replacement property solutions, Self-Directed IRAs, Title Holding Trusts (Land Trusts), and Deferred Sales Trusts.

Exeter 1031 Exchange Services, LLC is a national institutional Qualified Intermediary that administers all types of 1031 Exchanges in all 50 states as well as foreign property.

Exeter Fiduciary Services, LLC is a private, professional fiduciary services provider specializing in dispute, ressolution, settlement trust or escrow accounts.

Exeter IRA Services, LLC provides self-directed IRAs that allow taxpayers to invest in real estate related assets such as deeds of trust, mortgages, tax liens, real estate and more.

In addition, Mr. Exeter is the owner and host of The Exeter Group Real Estate Talk Radio Show™, is a frequent guest expert on "The Financial Advisors — Money Talk Radio Show” on San Diego News Radio AM 600 KOGO and on the "Inside Business Radio Show" on Business Talk Radio AM 1000 KCEO San Diego.

Exeter has offices in San Diego (HQ), Los Angeles, Fresno, San Francisco, California, as well as Chicago, Illinois, Kona, Hawaii, Springfield, New Jersey, and East Northpark, New York.

You can reach our national corporate headquarters office at (619) 239-3091, toll free at (866) 393-8370, or ask any 1031 Exchange related question via email at ask@exeterco.com.

Specialties

Our 1031 Exchange services include forward, reverse and improvement 1031 Exchanges for real estate and personal property. We administer all types of 1031 Exchanges in all 50 states as well as foreign property.

Our fiduciary services include the administration of dispute, resolution, or settlement trusts or escrows, Title Holding Trusts (Land Trusts) and other specialized trusts.

Our clients include individual, corporate and institutional investors/taxpayers.
