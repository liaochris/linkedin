Mostafa Kamel Attia's Summary

Mostafa Kamel has been mixing business with technology for over 11 years entirely focused on the web and internet industry, witnessing the rise of the internet in the region in the early 90’s Mostafa decided this was his career,

Joined LINKdotNET in 1999 and acclaimed multiple roles since then, ranging from Technical Manager of MSN Arabia In 2001, managing all technical operations of preparation, development and launch of the giant portal, Service Operations manager of all LINK’s Products, to consulting for Connect Ads (the MENA region largest Digital Agency) since its inception, he then developed a substantial experience in Content acquisitions deals and intuitive business models, which played a key role to also manage the Digital Media content acquisitions and licensing of all Audio and Video Content for VOD, IPTV, Online Retail for LINKonLINE, as well as his long active role in shaping the technology road-maps of LINK's products for years as General Manager of Technologies after the major acquisition of the largest Egyptian portals by LINKdotNET, and was in charge of all technical teams related to the product’s projects, with a special attention to R&D projects.
