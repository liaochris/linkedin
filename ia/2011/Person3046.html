Ankur Mathur's Summary

Ankur has sixteen years of elite work experience in the Real Estate, Architecture, Engineering and Construction industry. He is a pioneer and proven leader in the implementing Virtual Design and Construction technology, developing innovative global sourcing strategies for dynamic market needs, successfully handling international clients, initializing and solidifying profitable relationships and managing operations. Ankur has expert knowledge of technology-based business transformation and process management solutions.

He has seven years of elite industry experience in pioneering and deploying “Building Information Modeling” (BIM) as a BPM solution with Owners, Developers, Real Estate and AEC firms. Ankur specializes in deploying technology to achieve higher profitability, greater productivity and faster delivery of ‘better’ buildings. He is also an expert in implementing international design and construction best practices on Indian projects.

In the last two years, he has been focusing on implementing BIM on Indian projects, as the owners representative. Ankur is one of the first users of Revit in India. He co-authored the book “Autodesk Revit for Building Designers & Architects R7.0”, with Prof. Sham Tickoo, Purdue University Calumet, IN, USA, that was published in 2004 by CADCIM Technologies USA.

Specialties

Building Process Management, Building Information Modeling (BIM), global sourcing, architectural outsourcing, business strategy, operational excellence, integrated project delivery (IPD), design management, digital asset management, international design practices
