Sarah V. Bundy's Summary

Sarah loves the business she's in. As a passionate and high level thinking Internet Marketing Specialist, she has been a top performer for more than half a dozen corporations in her lifetime.

Over the last decade, she has managed affiliate programs, lead sales teams, written and implemented online marketing plans for several multi-million dollar corporations. Throughout her sales and marketing career, she has been recognized for ten sales achievement awards, four customer service awards, been nominated for employee of the year twice, won Marketing MVP twice, and had her company nominated for two different awards in 2009: Best New Start Up of the Year in Vancouver, BC and Best New Outsourced Program Management Company of the Year by A Best Web.

Sarah founded and now leads a team of highly skilled and dedicated Internet Marketing Specialists at AIM (All Inclusive Marketing Inc.) in Vancouver, which she created to deliver exceptional results through affordable and effective online marketing strategies.

This year, in addition to bring her beautiful son into the world, Sarah founded the Vancouver Chapter of 85 Broads, a global organization of over 19,000 members to bring passionate female entrepreneurs together for networking and professional growth opportunities.

Sarah's favorite pastimes are spending time with family and friends, eating delicious foods, and traveling the world.
