Ajasa Technologies’s Summary

Anthony Williamson, having worked in technology and computer systems industry since 1987 and as a consultant since 1992, he discovered a disservice that was re-occurring to both the Client and Consultant. Clients were overcharged by consulting firms and Consultants were underpaid by the consulting firms. In both cases, the consulting companies were the culprits. Shelleye and Anthony, a husband and wife team, decided to start a company that could operate efficiently and where the Client is charged a reasonable rate and the Consultant is paid a fair wage. This vision had to be built from the ground up and started in 1995, out of Shelleye and Anthony’s basement. The company name is from the five (5) members of the Williamson family. The name is pronounced as ahh-jaw -suh.
• A - Anthony IV
• J - Julius
• A - Aiyla
• S - Shelleye
• A - Anthony III
AJASA Technologies started its wheels in motion when it hired its first employee in 1995, currently the company has over 495 employees (375 contractors, 120 employees). Ajasa Technologies, Inc. was founded with $150 and now produces nearly $50 million a year. AJASA has expanded its client base to several major companies and continues to grow day by day. AJASA grew rapidly, primarily by offering management consulting, information systems and/or engineering support services.
Our client base is ever growing but at the same time AJASA Technologies has remained loyal to its clients and has built long standing relationships. Anthony and Shelleye have kept a humble mind set as their company has grown and grown. AJASA expanded from Golden Valley to Iowa in 2006 and first branched out to Arizona in 2003. Hard work and their commitment has kept AJASA Technologies and its employees ahead of there competition.

Smart People. Smart Solutions. Smart Business.
That’s Ajasa Technologies
