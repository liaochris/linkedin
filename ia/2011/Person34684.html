Peter L Green's Summary

Peter is an enthusiastic individual with a zest for life. His senior management roles cross functional and industry boundaries and he brings this wide-ranging experience to bear with a structured, yet innovative approach, to problem solving; combining strategic vision with a hands-on attitude. He combines gravitas with an approachable personality that engenders collaboration at all levels.
