Carl Wolfbrandt’s Summary

In partnership with the management team of the hotel and casino operating departments we identify and install method and procedural improvements, restructure and streamline work processes, perform work measurement, conduct time and motion studies, track statistical forecasting data, install business volume forecasting systems and develop variable and fixed labor standards. Historically, labor cost savings of two to five percent of total annual payroll costs are achieved.

Carl Wolfbrandt’s Specialties:

Labor standard development for valet parking, bell & door, front desk, concierge, room attendants, turndown attendants, laundry workers, public area attendants, housemen, room service servers, restaurant servers, buffet attendants, banquet housemen, bartenders, cocktail servers, cooks, stewards, dealers, cage attendants, slot attendants, slot technicians, security guards, and engineers.
