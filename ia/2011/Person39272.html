Scott L. Sells’s Summary

15+ year energy practice advising clients in all aspects of the electricity and natural gas industries. Domestic and international experience in connection with regulated utility (including rate, interconnection and merger proceedings); unregulated traditional (oil, natural gas, LNG, mining); and renewable/alternative (biomass, wind, solar, tidal) project development, finance and M&A.

Proficient in all aspects of privately held or publicly traded corporate entities including regulatory compliance, corporate governance under Sarbanes Oxley, securities and exchange regulation, capital markets, and corporate finance. Demonstrated ability to timely negotiate, structure and close complex project, M&A and corporate restructuring transactions.

Scott L. Sells’s Specialties:

Energy (regulated and unregulated), oil and natural gas (exploration, production, development, transportation, storage, retail and wholesale marketing and trading, LNG, midstream NGL gathering and processing, downstream refining), electricity (generation -all fuel sources fossil and renewable; transmission and distribution), mining, water rights, environmental issues, stakeholder relations; related project development and M&A.
