Eric J. Fleischman's Summary

Government and community relations professional and social services program director with exemplary career, including nine years supervisory experience. Experience working with elected officials, public agencies, advocacy groups, and community-organizations. Experience managing staff and operations, developing fund-raising and marketing strategies, preparing program budgets and submitting grant applications. Demonstrated ability and willingness to both take initiative and work collaboratively. Extensive experience and training with mental illness, HIV/AIDS, developmental disabilities, alcoholism, substance abuse, housing, homelessness and senior issues. Exceptional ability to build coalitions among groups with diverse interests. Excellent analytical, problem solving, organizational and communication skills.

Specialties

Government and community relations, program and budget development, office management, staff supervision and training, strategic planning, non-profit administration, special events, fund-raising, outreach, public relations, public speaking, speech writing, newsletter publications, research, social services, case management, advocacy, counseling, crisis intervention, senior issues, mental illness, housing policy, homelessness, HIV/AIDS, developmental disabilities, alcoholism, substance abuse.
