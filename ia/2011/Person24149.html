Kendi Kim’s Specialties:

What is 'LightHeart'? Okay, it's not quite the same meaning as "light-hearted", which is actually an adjective meaning "cheerful" or "optimistic". LightHeart is my "coined term" for what is a human heart, with the qualities of a diamond-- being both strong and beautiful at the same time (they're actually both made of carbon). I don't mind whether it refers to a person who seems to be a source of light, or is merely a prism. You know, I probably got the idea after watching that movie, "Powder."
