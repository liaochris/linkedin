Lic. Gabriel Simonet’s Summary

With a professional background in Advertising, Business and Technology, Gabriel focuses on Project and Personnel Management, Business Planning and Innovation, Problem Preventing and Solving (when is already too late to prevent), Traditional and Online Marketing, SEO, Art & IT Consultancy.

The Turner Broadcasting Systems Inc., Conviva, IamFootball.com, Panda Software, Bacardi, Wamba.com, Hispavista, Bingos.com, Bingos.co.uk, MediaGambling, ElGranBingo.com, Mambo.ca, HealtCare.com & many other are among the Companies, Sites and Projects he worked with as Crea's and SX Networks Chief Marketing Officer, Project Manager and finally as its Chief Operations Officer.

Although Spanish is his mother tongue, communication in English and German is very good (written) and could be fluent (spoken), if the chances to use those languages were more common (allowing him to practice and get rid of the rust in his mouth).
