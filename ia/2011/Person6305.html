Brian Powyszynski’s Summary

I am a concept artist and 3D modeler and texture artist with three years of industry experience. I have worked freelance and in-studio, independently and as part of a group, producing commercials, television shows, websites, and games. My work is daunting but enjoyable. I have worked on projects big and small, my larger client list has included companies such as Gamestop, Heelys, 1000 Ways to Die, Bell Helicopter, Samsung, and many more.

My true passion in conceptual design. I love to make people think, I love to deliver an experience and produce ideas that get the viewer not only involved visually, but mentally. Character design is my primary strength, but thanks to my father's architectural and structural influence I feel comfortable doing environmental and product design in both technical and non-technical settings.

When not buried beneath my work I am enjoying time with my friends and family, on the rare occasion that I get time to myself I enjoy playing really old or really new video games and reading books about the end of the world.
