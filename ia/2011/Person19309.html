Jason Jacobs’s Summary

I am a serial entrepreneur and high growth CEO who loves building great teams and companies. I formed The Verge Group as the vehicle to assist other Entrepreneurs to maximize the potential in their companies. The Verge Group draws on some of the best business minds in the world in areas of Strategy, Top Management Team Dynamics, Organizational Performance, Organization Network Analysis, Information Technology and Seed/Venture Finance.

I focus particularly on Strategy and Organization for exponential growth or restarting growth. The creation or destruction of value is purely a function of decision making. I help leaders and organizations become better decision makers, strategically, organizationally and operationally by employing two concepts, Emergent Strategy and Agile Organization.
