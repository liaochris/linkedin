Buxton Company’s Summary

Founded in 1994, Buxton is the industry leader in customer analytics providing strategic decision-making information for site selection and targeted marketing to major retailers, business services, consumer packaged goods entities, cities, economic development groups and healthcare organizations throughout the United States.

Buxton Company’s Specialties:

Customer insight, data analytics, delivering quality and timely solutions, database management, desktop real estate planning solutions,
