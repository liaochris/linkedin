GARY KRETCHMER’s Summary

GARY KRETCHMER is a direct response specialist, developing risk-free lead generation campaigns for business-to-consumer advertisers. Gary's marketing expertise spans across several media channels, with the goal of generating high quality, cost-effective sales leads for advertisers, and non-traditional revenue for broadcast and print publishers.

For over 20 years, Gary has been an owner and principal executive at Target + Response Inc., a direct marketing agency specializing in performance-based advertising. This includes per-inquiry, pay-per-lead, pay-per-call and cost-per-action programs.

Target + Response has maintained a leadership position in per-inquiry radio advertising since 1987, and has expanded its services to include yellow pages, email, print and other risk-free media opportunites.

Target + Response generates highly qualified sales leads for direct marketers at a fixed cost per lead. We work with thousands of quality media sources to generate cost-efficient and virtually risk-free results.

Through Target + Response, you can save 80% in media costs. Visit our website at www.target-response.com for more information.
