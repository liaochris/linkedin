Co-owner BusinessRoom-Hilverum
BusinessRoom - Hilversum is a platform for - new - talented artists who want to develop commercial, rendering it dependent on subsidies and other government schemes.

Business room - is located at the Havenstraat in Hilversum, an creative enterprise area. With an open window it distinguishes us from a gallery. This is not only to reduce the threshold, but also make it more attractive to various target groups to step inside, so visitors can meet the artist in person, with the benefit to encourge the purchase desire.

BusinessRoom - Hilversum finds that the quality is determined not only by works of art, but mostly by her/ his personality. The power of business room - Hilversum is to achieve the ultimate combination of personality, skills and commerciality of the artist.

BusinessRoom - Hilversum believes in: "Business is believe in emotion and passion of your activities and skills!"
