John Keating's Summary

Databroker specialise in B2B Data, B2C Data, Email Lists, Mailing Lists, Sales Leads and European Data. Our aim is to provide solid independent advice and source the best quality data for your marketing needs. As we do this, we have a tendency to save you money. We are nice like that. We provide a comprehensive service that allows you to relax and forego the hassle of locating a perfect list for your requirements.

Databroker has robust relationships with all DMA approved major data owners and collectors in the UK and beyond. This strong platform ensures fantastic rates for data can be passed on so you can be confident that your budget is being utilised to its full potential. Whether it is Consumer data or Business data that you need, let Databroker save you time, stress and money in advising and delivering the most suitable data.

Databroker doesn’t have an internal sales team or huge sales targets, we are not looking to monetise at every stage and we don’t have to cover data collection costs – we are an independent advisory driven by client satisfaction and your marketing results. Spend time on booking your next holiday, writing a business case to impress your boss or taking that important client out to lunch – don’t waste it on trawling databases from every Tom, Dick and Harry, that’s our job.

We really dislike jargon, spin and waffle. We prefer to leave that to data owners and we have spent over 6 years translating such waffle into tangible products and services to get you where you need to be, so you don’t have too. There are no ‘customer journeys’ here or blurb devoted to ‘blue sky management centric strategic magic wand solutions’. Waffle should be only seen with syrup - www.data-broker.co uk, syrup free.
