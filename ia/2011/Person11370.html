Dede Haskins’s Summary

Experienced, award winning senior executive with a successful 20+ year career history with six of Northern Virginia’s fastest growing companies (Cigital, Proxicom, Legent, MAXM, XMCO, and RCI). Experienced in both commercial and government services environments. A highly organized, competitive leader, capable of teaming with peers and building high-performing teams to drive revenue generation. Strong record of improving customer satisfaction. Experienced in developing strategic plans, meeting organizational objectives, managing budgets and staff, and developing and managing performance and compensation plans. An impressive list of “firsts” in establishing new departments and new lines of business; marketing, growing, and spinning off product/service lines; and building and running professional services units. A dynamic speaker, recognized in the technology community, with an influential network of community leaders.

Dede Haskins’s Specialties:

I've run virtually every function in a commercial software company or professional services consulting company, including professional services, software development, customer support, distribution, operations, information development, maintenance, knowledge management, product management, marketing (including thought leadership, advertising, public relations), direct mail, sales operations, and inside sales.
