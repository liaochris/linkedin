David Eisele is Founder and CEO of Davelle Clothiers, a premier fine tailored and custom clothing store for men and women located in Reston, Virginia. David is a recognized authority on custom menswear and personal image design.

For 20 years, the name Davelle has been synonymous in the DC Metro area with impeccable style, quality and service. Davelle Clothiers carries select designer clothing lines in addition to their own David’s Signature Collection custom line. They offer wardrobe consultations at their store, home or office, as well as executive style seminars for groups.

David attended the renowned Fashion Institute of Technology (FIT) in New York, and earned a degree from Oregon’s prestigious Trend School of Design. He holds the title of Certified Master Designer from the distinguished Custom Tailors & Designers Association (CTDA), where he also serves as President of the Board of Directors.

David spent twenty- three years in the jewelry business, first with Black, Star & Frost, then with Bailey, Banks & Biddle, and as owner of Charles Schwartz & Son Jewelers. In 1991, a desire to establish his own enterprise and return to his passion for fashion led him to open Timeless Ties and More at Union Station in Washington, DC. He launched his first clothing line, Davelle by David, in 1995.

In 2001, Timeless Ties and More became Davelle Clothiers, and in 2003 David launched his handcrafted wardrobe collection, David’s Signature Collection.

Davelle Clothiers carries select designer clothing lines in addition to their own David’s Signature Collection custom line. Wardrobe consultations are offered by appointment in the store, or at clients’ homes or offices. Executive style seminars are also available for groups.

Davelle Clothiers is the official clothier for the D.C. United professional soccer team.
