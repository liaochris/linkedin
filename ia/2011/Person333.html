Angela Bickford's Summary

My main job is as the Marketing Assistant for AWAI (www.awaionline.com). In this position, I do a lot of marketing, copywriting, social networking, etc. to help promote the company.

In addition, I have a side-business that involves creating stationery & gift products for people and businesses. This ranges from basic things such as business cards to other things such as decorated clipboards and scrapbooks, and now jewelry.
