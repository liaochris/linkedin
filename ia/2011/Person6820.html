Cameron Myler’s Summary

I am an associate in Frankfurt Kurnit’s Intellectual Property, Entertainment, and Litigation Groups, where I handle a wide variety of matters involving intellectual property, including trademark prosecution proceedings within the United States Patent and Trademark Office, as well as litigation involving trademark, copyright, and right of publicity issues. My work includes conducting trademark searches and protecting and enforcing clients’ rights in their trademarks, domain names, and copyrights. I also have represented a number of Olympic athletes in disputes with the U.S. Anti-Doping Agency, and counsel athletes and sports organizations on eligibility and other matters.
