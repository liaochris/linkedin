Holly Hight’s Summary

I serve as the California Field Organizer with Bread for the World -- a collective Christian voice urging our nation's leaders to end hunger. Over the past six years, I've strengthened advocacy networks throughout the state of California. I empower local advocates with tools to contact their elected officials about important hunger issues.

I am deeply committed to service in the nonprofit sector. I am currently pursing a master's degree in Public Administration from California State University Northridge.

Recognized for exceptional problem solving, volunteer & time management, creative leadership, team-building, social networking and interpersonal skills.

Mission Statement for 2011: Strengthen relationships with anti-hunger leaders and provide tools to empower advocacy efforts throughout the state of California.

Holly Hight’s Specialties:

Social networking, program development, campaign and grassroots organizing, community outreach, coalition building, public speaking, creating interactive educational opportunities for grassroots empowerment, leadership development, conference planning, organizational skills, relationship building
