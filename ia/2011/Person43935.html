Tony Abboud's Summary

Antony "Tony" Abboud is a partner and one of the proud founders of Gounaris Abboud, Co, LPA. Mr. Abboud has been a legal defense attorney in the Miami Valley since inception of his practice. Mr. Abboud earned his high school degree from Centerville High School then went on to the University of Dayton and earned a Bachelors Degree in Criminal Justice Studies with a minor in Political Science. Upon receiving a scholarship to the University Of Dayton School Of Law, he completed his Juris Doctor and commenced a successful law career as one of the Miami Valley's premier Dui and Criminal defense attorneys.

Mr. Abboud is licensed to practice law in the State of Ohio and the United States District Court for the Southern District of Ohio. He belongs to the Ohio State Bar Association, Dayton Bar Association, and Greene County Bar Association and the Dayton Bar Committee for Criminal Justice.

His experience as a former City Prosecutor has given him extensive knowledge on how DUI Offenses and Criminal offenses are prosecuted. He has taken this knowledge and created a formula to defend his clients in all DUI and Criminal matters.

In addition to his experience as a City Prosecutor, Mr. Abboud has been involved in several successful jury trials, including a Murder trial where the jury found his client not guilty of Murder in the 1st Degree, an offense that is punishable by life imprisonment. Mr. Abboud has also handled multiple successful criminal and civil cases in the United States District Court for the Southern District of Ohio.

Mr. Abboud is a Montgomery County DUI and Criminal lawyer who handles all aspects of Ohio DUI and Criminal Defense, including arrests, trial, hearings, and all issues that you may face during a DUI and Criminal charge. Mr. Abboud is also a highly qualified family law attorney and personal injury attorney within the State of Ohio.

Specialties

DUI/OVI, Criminal Defense, Family Law and Personal Injury
