Daniel Shaurette’s Summary

Phoenix, Arizona native goth-geek, byte banging digital anthropologist, self-published author, podcaster, father and fiancé.

As a software analyst, I see myself as not only a designer of software, but as someone who likes to dig into a project and learn as much as I can. I'm not satisfied fixing a defect unless I know what the code was meant to do and why it may not be working as expected.
