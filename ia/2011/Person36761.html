Suomen KännykkäRahaksi Oy

Environmental Services industry

January 2010 – Present (1 year 3 months)

Suomen KännykkäRahaksi.fi – Your reliable mobile phone recycling company

Suomen KännykkäRahaksi Oy is limited liability company owned by Finnish investors. The company specializes in mobile phone re-use, refurbishment and recycling. We recycle all types of mobile phones; old, new, used, unused, working or un-working, to be used once again. Suomen KännykkäRahaksi Oy buys mobile phones from consumers, as well as companies.

Our service is unique in Finland. Our aim is to become the leading mobile phone recycling company in northern Europe. Due to our large volumes and efficient logistics and processes, we are able to offer our customers a competitive price for their unwanted mobile phones.

Customers can via our online service KierrätäKännykkä.fi order a RecyclePackage™, with which customers can send to Suomen KännykkäRahaksi Oy their unwanted mobiles phones easily and for free. Once a customer has ordered a RecyclePackage™, they will receive a customer ID that they may use at KierrätäKännykkä.fi to login to their account, where they can follow the sales process in real-time. Our service if highly efficient and we pay our customers in 24 hours upon receiving their RecyclePackage™.

We work in cooperation with the authorities, like the police. We can offer the authorities a login ID to our database, where they can check have we received mobile phones that have been reported stolen. The technology we use increases transparency and aids the authorities to have an up to date record of transactions.

We also support several different charities and our customers can if they so please donate the money they receive or part of it, to the various charities that we support.
