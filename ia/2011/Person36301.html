RD Watkins Senior Project Manager | PMP | LION TopLink.com's Summary

My name is RD Watkins, a Senior Project Manager PMP (PMI Certified). I help companies or individuals to reach their project goals and succeed.

I do this by "Applying Common Sense Project Management Principles in a Virtual World." I bring people together and help them in clearly defining their strategic goals, building consensus on a plan and mentor them in achieving their desired results.

Unlike many other project managers, I am capable of applying my interpersonal, communication and project management skills in a context where people matter and they know it. This approach builds enthusiasm, commitment and relationship capital within the team. This is an environment where all parties involved know their personal brand depends on trust, integrity, accountability, commitment and follow-thru.

If you have a project where dispersed teams "just aren't getting it done", everyone is "on a different page" or where departments are "on different planets", contact me for an initial consultation and let me help you and your project succeed.
