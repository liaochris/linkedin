Dick O'Hara’s Summary

Dick O'Hara (info@dickohara.com) is the Director of Worldwide Alliances at Extreme and is responsible for forging relationships with key alliance partners as well as building the ecosystem in support of customers in all markets.

Dick is also currently a "Top 5%" Leader in Gerson Lehrman's expert network of technology consultants.

In addition to his 20+ years of experience in wireless, telecommunications, and networking (Motorola, BellSouth/RAM Mobile Data, Cisco/Aironet, Avaya, and Extreme), Dick also consulted for Securicor Wireless, founded 7 businesses while continuously employed, and was CEO of Hydro Electric Power Inc. (HEPI). His efforts at Aironet were instrumental in taking that company public and subsequently seeing it acquired by Cisco. Dick has extensive knowledge of the Wireless LAN industry, Data Networking, and the IP Telephony (VOIP) / Convergence market space.

Dick holds a BSEE from the University of Illinois (Urbana-Champaign) with post-graduate work in business administration completed at Keller Graduate School of Management.
