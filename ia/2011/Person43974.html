Tony Gaidhane, MBA, PMP, CISSP, CISM’s Summary

Tony Gaidhane has been with WellPoint since 2002, and is currently a Senior Manager of Information Security. Tony heads teams of security experts responsible for the design and maintenance of security systems which provide Enterprise Identity and Access Management services, including Enterprise User Provisioning, Security Access Reviews, Directory Engineering, Audit Management for all WellPoint identities. With over 13 years of IT and Management experience, Tony is also responsible for assessing the risks of WellPoint’s identity management initiatives and for setting the overall security strategy for IAM services across WellPoint.

Previously, as a Senior Manager of eBusiness Security, Tony managed teams and systems to protect the online identities of customers that use WellPoint’s websites and portals to access their personal health information, along with the information of partners that use online systems to transact with WellPoint.

Prior to working for WellPoint, Tony spent 4 years as a Principal Consultant at Aquent where he led teams of software developers, analysts and testers to implement complex Enterprise Architecture and eBusiness solutions for large Fortune 500 companies including Coventry Health (formerly First Health), Capital One, Citibank, IBM, Walt Disney and Verizon. His past positions also include working as a Senior Analyst for Circuit City, headquartered in Richmond, VA, leading the delivery of a number of enterprise IT solutions for the retail business.

Tony holds an MBA from Duke University’s Fuqua School of Business and also BS and MS degrees in Computer Science from Nagpur University (India) and Texas Tech University respectively. Tony also holds numerous certifications, such as the PMP, the CISSP, the CISM, the CGEIT and the CISA in the fields of Information Security, Audit and Project Management.
