Andres Guerra-Mondragon’s Summary

Freight Calculator.Com develops air and ocean instant online shipping calculators and quote generators for select carriers and shippers as well as Network Routing Tools, Rate Maintenance Tools and Supplier Request Tools.

APX is unique, as we are the only TSA approved (I.A.C.) Carrier and the only approved FMC N.V.O.C.C. / O.T.I. Ocean Common Carrier offering INSTANT ONLINE door-to-door quotes for Air and Ocean services from any city in the USA to worldwide using its proprietary Global MAX freight calculator.

Air Parcel Express, Inc. (APX) is able to give shippers the opportunity to produce copious amounts of quotes per day allowing users to query air and ocean pricing for a multitude of purposes thus leveraging the power of the Internet, increasing productivity and lowering overall operating costs. Combined with performance, customer service, attention to details, proven professionals, and a staff that cares, APX makes Global MAX an indispensable tool for shipping overseas.

We are licensed by the Federal Maritime Commission as a Non-Vessel Operating Common Carrier (N.V.O.C.C.) or O.T.I , and as a Freight Forwarder. In addition we are approved by the TSA and the FAA as an Indirect Air Carrier (I.A.C.) to engage in air cargo worldwide. APX is also the founding member of the Global MAX Network.
