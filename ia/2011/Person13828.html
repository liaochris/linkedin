Erica Nelson's Summary

Professional writer focusing on good news stories for philanthropic works, family foundations, corporate giving programs, nonprofit organizations and more.

Author of book "Happiness Quotations" due in March 2011 and "Prospect When You Are Happy" published in 2007 (available online at Amazon.com).

Former Sacramento Bee and Santa Barbara News-Press news reporter (12 years professional journalist). Community relations for Santa Clara based Intel Corp (3 years). Program Director of Grove Foundation (7 years). First employee of Kenneth Rainin Foundation, assisted with strategic plan; online giving system; created five-year plan; plus built website working collaboratively with designer and Jennifer Rainin. UC Davis grad. Real estate investor.

Specialties

Internet marketing expertise, excellent on Facebook fan pages and creating an online presence, traffic generation. Former realtor. Strong communicator. Write and edit monthly publications. Enjoy work. Responsive and communicative, clear thinker.
