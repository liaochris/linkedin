Steve Jarvis’s Summary

I am Development Director for OhioDrugCard.com, Greg Lashutka, Program Director. We provide a Free discount prescription card to the multitudes of uninsured residents in Ohio.

Additionally, I have worked in the insurance and non-insurance benefits business since 1982. After 6 years working as a large NY-based carrier agent and registered representative, I transitioned to being an independent agent representing many top carriers to individuals, employer groups and associations.

In 2000, I started an online insurance business geared to individual consumers around the country...www.BenefitHouse.com. I am currently licensed in 28 states and also work with affiliated agents in all 50 states.

In 2005, I began to concentrate again on providing unique non-dues revenue opportunities to associations, unions and non-profit associations with my partner in Rhode Island, Ken Fransson.

We also provide a unique no cost fundraising program for non-profits, alumni associations, sports orgs & trade associations. This program is available in the U.S. and in apprx 15 non-English speaking countries.
