Max Macleod's Summary

My twenty year career in IT has seen one great wave that began in 1995. That was when everyone knew that the internet and the web were sure to be the next big thing. I had to be on that wave. There was no alternative. I ditched my mainframe job at the bank and started a consulting company developing web solutions using Perl, PHP, and Java.

That was twelve years ago.

Now, I can see the next big wave coming. This time it will be a revolution in usability that makes computers a pleasure to use for everyone. In software development we will see a shift from sequential to massively parallel GPUGPU power.

Again, there is no going back. Time to grab the board and start riding the wave...
