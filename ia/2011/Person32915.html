GrexIt

Privately Held; Computer Software industry

July 2010 – Present (9 months)

GrexIt helps Google Apps users easily create a knowledge base out of their day to day email communication. Its like, well, a Shared Dropbox for email.

View the quick (and probably entertaining) 2.5 minute video on http://grexit.com to know what our product does.

A bit more about GrexIt
----------------------------------
Email inboxes contain a lot of information that can be useful to people who were not a part of the email conversation, and also much later into the future after the conversation took place. With GrexIt, useful conversations and files from email inboxes can be easily added to a shared repository of information. This can be done in a rule-driven, automated and access controlled manner.

We're an absolutely lean start-up with just two people (both founders and programmers). And we're hungry!

May 2007 – December 2010 (3 years 8 months)

I co-founded Mobicules (http://www.mobicules.com) in May 2007, and after almost an year of exploration, we settled down into providing web application development services, building innovative and sophisticated products primarily for start-ups/new ventures in the US, Europe and India.

In a span of 20 months, we grew Mobicules to be a 35 people company from being just a three member team. I personally worked as a programmer and architect on some of the most complex projects at Mobicules, along with working on business development, hiring and operations.

I exited Mobicules in January 2010 to focus on GrexIt (http://grexit.com)
