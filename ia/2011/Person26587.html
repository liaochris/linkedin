Accomplishments

2010 - The “Dove Award.” winner for “Littluns”
2010 - WINNER in the Young Adult Fiction category of the 2010 National Indie Excellence Awards" for “Littluns.”
2009 - “Mom’s Choice Awards” Gold winner in two “Young Adult” categories “Fiction,” and also Gold for “Fantasy, Myths, and “Legends” for “Littluns.”

1998 - 1999 - Emmy Nominated for “Life with Louie” Director
1992 – Awarded a patent for invention SCOREGUIDE
1989 -1993, 1994 -1998, 1999 - 2003 - Elected to six terms as Animation Governor for the Academy of Television Arts & Sciences: Created the first Over one hour program category for animation and the first annual individual achievement awards’ categories (Emmy’s) for Animators / Background Artists / Background Stylists / Layout Artists / Production Designers / Storyboard Artists / and Voice Over talents. In 1996, with support from other ATAS leadership successfully brought the daytime Emmy awards structure for animation consistent with ATAS primetime. “Production Executive Committee” for “Cartoon All-Stars to the Rescue 1990. Appointed by the President as Vice-Chair for the ATAS Activities Committee 1991 – 1992; Served on the Budget, Awards, and Membership Committees.
