Regina Baker's Summary

A computer geek at heart, I love everything about computers, how they operate as they do and the limitless opportunities with software applications.

I love Internet Marketing. Online since 1996, I gotta tell you, there were times my family thought I was nuts! That was simply because they had no clue where the Internet was about to go… but thank God, I did ;-)

Experienced in computers and software made it easy to teach myself on how to write html in order to design websites — so when my manager came into my office one day and asked me could I design a website for the department… let’s just put it this way… I wasn’t in the least disappointed when the company merged and I was part of the lay-off.

Fast forward to today, I will tell you it hasn’t been all easy. There was a lot to study, research and learn about doing business online. Fortunately for you, I’m here to make sure you don’t have to follow that same path. Because of my 14 year online experience, I’ll give you simple, easy to follow instructions that are personalized to YOUR business, while ditching the hype, all the information how to products and helping you through the process to being on purpose, focused and becoming profitable.

Having various roles in computer technology led me to where I am today as the Co-Founder of Wahmcart.com, a full featured ecommerce software solution.

I have a strong passion for networking – both online and in the offline world – and my love for creating relationships have taken many forms in the 14 years I’ve been focused on online businesses. There are tons of forums, social networking sites, facebook events and teleseminars which can be quite mind boggling at times however, your main goal is to not get lost in the shuffle hence, the reason for…

‘keeping it real’

Make no mistakes, the Internet has powerful and actionable resources to provide you with the tools you need — you just need the directions — sort of like a road map.
