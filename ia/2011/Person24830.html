Kelle Coleman’s Summary

As a highly skilled marketing professional with proven experience developing successful marketing strategies, increasing sales and revenue, and building brand awareness, I believe my talents will enable me to excel at any company. I am particularly interested in a position that combines my passions for the fashion and entertainment industry with my desire to obtain a position that demands creativity, problem solving, and excellent communication skills.

I am an excellent candidate because I have acquired 5 years direct experience implementing strategic marketing programs and managing all aspects of both online and retail marketing efforts. As a marketing coordinator for the New Jersey Nets, I was primarily responsible for overseeing online and in-arena retail merchandising and I was in charge of all marketing efforts for an annual volleyball event. Additionally, during my tenure at Reebok International, my extensive exposure to consumers allowed me to gain a unique perspective on the real drivers behind purchase decisions.

In addition to my skills and experience, I am dedicated to continuously improving marketing strategy and achieving measurable results. As a result, I was able to increase the Nets online retail sales by 35% and the online store became one of the company’s most profitable divisions. Through my experiences with the Nets, Reebok, and Microsoft, I have learned how to marry strategy with seamless execution to successfully implement marketing campaigns for the nation’s top brands. I have drawn on this experience to achieve impressive results in the past and I am confident that I can translate this experience to achieve a similar result for any company.
