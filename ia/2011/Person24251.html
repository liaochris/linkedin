Kent Frese’s Summary

Kent is a nationally recognized expert in leadership development, strategic planning, business development and operations process improvement with over 24 years of practical experience in organizational leadership and development. Additionally, he specializes in the design of organizational continuity programs, succession planning and exit strategies for CEOs and business owners.

An entrepreneur by nature, Kent excels in the creative end of the business, posing compelling questions to clients and reaching strategic, visionary solutions to today’s workplace challenges. His particular strength is aligning employees, and key institutional and technological resources, with business goals to achieve improved, measurable results.

LMI’s clients have the advantage of Kent’s proven track record with clients across the U.S and in front-line management and executive positions at both local and national financial services firms. He has held positions at all levels of management, including Director, CIO, Vice President and President.

Kent holds a Bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering from Villanova University. He is also a Catalyst graduate; Certified Professional Behavioral Analyst; Certified Professional Values Analyst; a certified facilitator for both Leadership Management, Inc and Teleometrics International; and he is certified for high and low ropes training. He also served as a skiing instructor and coach for over 18 years.
