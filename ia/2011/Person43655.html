Todd Adams's Experience

Publisher

Home Tek Design

Public Company; Consumer Electronics industry

August 2010 – Present (8 months)

Home Tek Design is a digital journal dedicated to the enhancement and development of the home technology industry. The primary purpose is to provide an in-depth technical resource for electronic systems contractors and technical savvy DIY enthusiasts.

Our digital journal provides a steadily increasing base of knowledge for reference and education. In addition to in-depth technical tutorials and articles, it includes online tools to aid you in technology design. For example, charts providing commonly-used mathematical data, easy-to-use calculators for your design needs, and key standards and terms. Since the magazine is digital, it can be stored on the reader's computer for archive purposes.

Todd Adams's Publications

  • Cabling Best Practices

    • Home Tek Design
    • January 7, 2011
    Authors: Todd Adams

    When cabling a home or flat for technology, you must be aware of the limitations of the material you are working with. Save yourself a lot of time and headaches by following the industry's best practices for selecting and installing cable. This will help you avoid damaging the cable from a myriad of issues that can happen when cable is pulled too tight, pinched too tight, or run too far.

  • How a Forced Air HVAC System Works

    • Home TEK Design
    • February 8, 2011
    Authors: Todd Adams

    Electronic systems integrators are sometimes asked to incorporate a home's HVAC system into the house-wide electronic integration system. And although the HVAC contractor is responsible for installing the heating and cooling system in the home, it is the integrator that ties it into the whole-house integration system. Here, for the benefit of the integrator we review how a forced air HVAC system works.

  • Where in the Market are You?

    • HomeTEKDesign.com
    • February 23, 2011
    Authors: Todd Adams

    Where does your company place in the selling market? Do you understand what it means? How can your company use the market triangle? Here we look at the market triangle and explain how it can help you in planning for your company's future.

  • Building a Design Philosophy

    • March 1, 2011
    Authors: Todd Adams

    Home electronics integration products range from simple to complex. How can we be consistent and efficient in our efforts to satisfy and exceed customer expectations? In this series of articles, we guide you through the design philosophy building process.
