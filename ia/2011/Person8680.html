Cinthia Novick’s Summary

I'm a creative director and senior copywriter from Argentina.

I have worked at agencies like Savaglio/ TBWA, Agulla & Baccetti, J. Walter Thompson, and The Vidal Partnership for accounts like MasterCard, Heineken, Directv, Unicef, Cingular Wireless, etc.

Now I'm based in Buenos Aires, working along with planners, graphic designers, art directors, photographers, web designers and music composers. Together, we create creative campaigns and marketing strategies for the U.S. hispanic market, as well as for the Argentine market.
