Ty Abernethy's Summary

Founder of ZuzuHire.com, a multimedia interviewing tool incorporating video, voice, essay, & multiple choice questions. ZuzuHire is an extremely simple to use screening tool that allows you to create online interviews. You can email them to specific candidates or embed them in a job posting to send all applicants through the process. Visit the website at: http://www.zuzuhire.com

Branch Manager for the accounting division of CHASE Professionals. CHASE Accounting offers executive recruiting and contract support for corporate accounting & finance positions. Other divisions include CHASE Technology (IT) and CHASE Technical (manufacturing). We are headquarted in Atlanta with offices throughout the country.
