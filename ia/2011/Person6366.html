Brie Johnson’s Summary

Over 10 years of environmental experience in the realms of business, government and law. Experience designing and implementing general and specialized sustainability projects for Fortune 500 global corporations including Whole Foods Market, Williams-Sonoma, and organizations including the NRDC and the US EPA.

Skills include project management, business analysis, creative problem solving, financial modeling, quantitative/qualitative evaluation methods and research.

Accomplishments include coauthoring "California’s Solutions for Global Warming," an AB32 report reaching international audience with E2/NRDC on how the policy is driving economic growth and encouraging innovation in the clean technology industry. Presented findings at national Net Impact National Conference in Nashville, TN and at Heinrich Boll Foundation Conference in Berlin, Germany.

Brie Johnson’s Specialties:

Sustainable business, carbon market and policy analysis, corporate communications, employee engagement, CSR, environmental law, green building, international development, sustainable agriculture, international sustainable development, green IT, environmentally preferable purchasing, LCA methodologies and legal research.
