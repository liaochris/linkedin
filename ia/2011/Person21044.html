Jina Bolton's Summary

I’m a user experience and visual interaction designer, a web standards HTML/CSS developer, a published author, and public speaker currently living in San Francisco, California. With a strong focus on grid design and elegant typography, I enjoy creating beautiful, user-centered web sites and applications.

Specialties

typography, grid, layout, web standards, HTML, XHTML, CSS, Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, speaking, teaching, writing, usability, interaction, art direction, user experience
