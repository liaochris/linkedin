Jeremy Hight's Summary

I have shown work in locative media, new media, sound art, text and image art and text art at galleries and museums internationally.

Presented my ideas on the future of the internet and the possibilities of geo-spatial search engines and historical databases and writing forms in mapping software at Where 2.0

compared to Bruce Sterling's keynote in my latest interview

http://serialconsign.com/2010/03/thoughts-writing-within-map

I have been writing about augmented reality and working on information design in relation to future ar since 2002.

author of "Writing within the Map"
http://www.neme.org/main/1111/writing-within-the-map

author of "Modulated Mapping" (http://piim.newschool.edu/journal/issues/2009/02/pdfs/ParsonsJournalForInformationMapping_Hight-Jeremy.pdf)

essay on research and development of intuitive open source mapping/ web 3.0

author of "Immersive Event Time" (/piim.newschool.edu/journal/issues/2009/01/)

essay on new ways to measure events in time...and time itself.....looks at ar, immersive graphic visualizations and game interface recreations of historical events with ai

Author of "Immersive Sight in the Third Space" (neme.org/main/645/immersive-sight)
Essay on how to combine augmented reality,virtual reality, aspects of locative media in exhibition spaces to create architectural analysis integrated spatial graphic design and interactive augmentation

author of "Narrative Archaeology"

I have published 30+ essays and book chapters in various fields connected to art and technology, science and art and language,semiotics and creative writing. I am currently co-editing an issue of the leading journal in art, science and technology as well as a book. I curate on line exhibitions and advise festivals. I have been a professor (english, multimedia, design theory art history) for 10 years and love it.

Specialties

locative media,new media, augmented reality, immersive visualization, interface concepts,critical theory, interface design,photography,digital imaging,editing,analytical writing,creative writing,curating,grant writing, hybrid art making, sound art, electronic music composing
