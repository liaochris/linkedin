Vineet Agrawal's Summary

Vineet is instrumental in managing large and complex Products/ Projects using Open Source Technologies for more than 10 years. Vineet and OSSCube’s engineering team have developed model to “Assemble, build and develop” products with quick turnaround time and quality. This model draws from best practices of BPMN, Prototyping, Agile and works on leveraging existing components rather than trying to develop things from scratch.

Vineet regularly speaks at International conferences and actively contribute to build up unconference communities across the world. He has cofounded OSSCamp, a community focused on Open Source Knowledge Sharing.

Specialties

Organizing Communities, Operations, Open Source Promotion, Managing Strategic Business Units, Software Project Management
