Vito Botta’s Summary

I am a passionate and agile developer with 10+ years' commercial experience and 360° skills in the design, development and maintenance of modern, user centred as well as enterprise-scale web applications on both Unix and Microsoft platforms, with a strong interest in TDD, performance, scalability, security and search engine optimisation.

My responsibilities have encompassed the full project lifecycle from development to infrastructure, and I have always had an important role in technical decisions; I am an organised problem solver who can work either in team or independently, and also has relevant experience as team leader of small groups of developers. I can easily switch from one technology/platform to another, as required, and am always readily available to pick up new skills and challenges.

I am a self motivated, driven and focused individual, who can and does deliver through a strong passion for computing, the Internet and technology in general, and who loves researching into best practices and applying them to the actual projects.

My immediate aspirations are to continue to develop my own skills and experience from both a technical and business perspective, while also contributing to other smart people's growth plans.

I write a technical blog, and am actively involved in various tech communities.
