Ralph André’s Summary

Graphic Design is the art of visual communication and presentation. With that, my purpose as a graphic designer is to produce artwork that is creative, relevant and speaks to the viewer. My ability to produce big ideas has been further polished with over 4 years of experience and a solid clientele.
