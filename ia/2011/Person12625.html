Carl Sampson’s Summary

My professional experience started in government contract management when I staffed and oversaw 11 locations with approximately 300 indirect reports each. Of those 11 locations, I personally wrote the necessary RFPs to obtain 4 of them which generated the company 48 million dollars in new revenue. I gained strategic marketing experience at the Minka Group where I designed and implemented an easy to use Sales / Marketing Database system 66% faster than anticipated. The system reduced the company's operating costs by approximately 35%. At SYS Technology I started out as Sales Manager, and over time I was promoted to Marketing Liaison. I set up large distribution channels with Walmart, Target, Best Buy, and Fry's. The company grew to the point that it was sold to Gigabyte Technology. At Radio Shack I quickly progressed from sales associate to Manager In Training to Store Manager and then finally to Area Manager. I was able greatly improve the stores in my district and drastically increase sales. Most recently I managed a Verizon Home Services Kiosk, and through proper training and goal-setting, we surpassed goals and hit bonus every month I was manager.

I am searching for an opportunity in Sales, Management, Marketing or Recruiting where I can apply over 9 years of experience, my education, and the skills I have acquired in each position.
