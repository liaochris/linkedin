Chris Thomas's Summary

I'm a qualified Chartered Management Accountant and MBA.

I've worked in a number of large and small companies as a Financial Controller. I then set up my own practice prior to merging it in 2007. I've been a Director of One Accounting for the last 3 years.

My business goal is to build a great accounting firm, which turns the accounting profession on its head through the use of great technology and even greater client service.

I'm a big fan of relaxed business networking, so do get into touch if you'd like a coffee/beer and a chat.

I also like business blogs (especially Seth Godin!) and books (recommendations below), so tell me what else I should be reading!

Specialties

I help small business owners to grow their companies and ensure that they keep more of what they earn. My firm can also help Contractors and six figure earners pay less tax.

We are big fans of online accounting software - FreeAgent, Clearbooks and Xero.
