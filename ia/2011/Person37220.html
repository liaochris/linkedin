Robert "Buck" Buchholz’s Summary

I started my network marketing career in 1996, although I was very successful in my insurance career, I quickly found what worked for me in the regular business world did not necessarily work in this industry.
I began to attend the trainings given by the company and other trainings given by others that were already successful in the industry of network marketing. I immersed myself into the information, listened carefully, took notes and began to apply what I was learning into my network marketing business and shortly thereafter began to have the success I was looking for.
While I continue to work on myself development, I am now able to give back to those that are currently seeking the same thing I was when I started my home based business.
I have trained thousands of individuals the steps necessary to follow to become successful in this industry. There is no question people are searching for a better lifestyle than what the traditional corporate work place can offer. Many of you reading this are individuals working a job and I applaud you, do not get me wrong. BUT where will it lead you 5, 10, 15 or more years down the road? Today there is NO such thing as JOB security.
If you are searching for something more out of life and you are willing to put in the time and effort, I am willing to work with you and teach you the RIGHT way to build YOUR business. Being a PROFESSIONAL Network Marketer is a very rewarding and exciting career. The FREEDOM it creates is PRICELESS.
