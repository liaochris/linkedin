Yael Rozencwajg’s Summary

Hi there,

I'm Yael Rozencwajg the founder and the director of YOPPS Digital Media Agency (www.yopps.com).
Yopps provides web business development and collaboration-oriented solutions for professionals and individuals by creating effective and relevant customer awareness.
