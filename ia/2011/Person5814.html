Brad Habansky's Summary

Brad brings more than 15 years of sales and sales management experience—along with a well-developed sense of style—to Evolve. He has spent his career building sales teams, territories and sales strategy for companies like Cap Gemini Ernst & Young, Comdisco and Platinum Technology and has an impressive track record of driving revenue in new business development and new product and service offerings.

As much as Brad loved helping high-tech companies find the path to success, he also has a creative and entrepreneurial side that is perfectly aligned with the apparel industry. Brad was responsible for the concept development and rollout of two successful retail apparel concepts, Guise Style LLC and Guise Chic LLC, in the Lincoln Park area of Chicago. He owned and operated this business for six years, honing not only his love for and knowledge of the retail clothing market, but overseeing the e-commerce side of the business and acting as head buyer for men’s and women’s apparel. Brad has extensive experience developing marketing strategies and raising capital through private investment and commercial lending. Guise and Guise Chic were featured on “The Today Show,” as well as seen in The New York Times, Lucky magazine, GQ and Details.
