Linda Peters’s Summary

A strategic, global regulatory leader with over 20 years of pharmaceutical/ biopharmaceutical expertise in the area of drugs, biologics, vaccines and devices.

Linda J. Peters joined MedImmune as a corporate officer and senior vice president, regulatory affairs, in February 2005. In this role, she is responsible for defining and orchestrating the company’s global regulatory strategy and regulatory compliance for all biological and vaccine products.

Ms. Peters was also recognized by the Washington Business Journal and received the 2006 "Women Who Mean Business" award, which is given annually to the top 25 leading business women in the Washington DC area.

Prior to joining MedImmune, Ms. Peters was vice president of global regulatory affairs for Baxter Healthcare’s BioScience and Renal businesses, where she was responsible for implementing global regulatory strategies for biologics, vaccines, drugs, biosurgical products and devices. Also, she served as director of regulatory affairs at Takeda Pharmaceuticals North America and held positions of increasing responsibility at TAP Pharmaceuticals.

Ms. Peters is also a member of the Board of Directors of Sequoia Pharmaceuticals.
