Ali Al Khalil's Summary

Ali Al Khalil is the eMarketing Executive Manager at Mobily, the leading telecom operator in Saudi Arabia. Ali is responsible for Mobily brand's web presence, online marketing activities and communication. Mobily is the Middle East's fastest growing telecom and the biggest advertising spender in the region, with their web portal being a crucial touch point with their customers.
Ali's role as e-Marketing Manager is to introduce Mobily's different products and services online through a state-of-the-art experience, as well as creating specialized experience mini-portals and web pages to present a specific proposition or to reflect a certain campaign.
Also, it is Ali's responsibility to supervise the communication of different offerings, services and propositions online through devising online campaigns that will comprise of banner placements, search engine marketing activities, in-game advertising and direct activities on different web entities. The e-Marketing department is also the key driver for the company's viral marketing initiatives through planning, conceptualization and execution.
Before, Ali served as the Content Manager of Tejari.com, the region’s biggest e-Commerce network that offers various B2B and B2C online products. At Tejari, Ali was actively involved in launching new web entities, branding, researching, devising and implementing strategies, developing campaigns and preserving the company’s media value through PR activities.
Prior to Tejari, Ali held the position of Editor at The Star weekly English newspaper, as well serving as the managing partner of Media Arabia, which is a media consultancy and content development company, focusing on digital marketing and content development.
Before founding Media Arabia, Ali worked with Arabia.com in content products development and commercialization. During his Arabia.com days, Ali served in project management, online content development/management, products’ modeling, and marketing.

Specialties

Online Marketing; Digital Marketing; Social Media; Wireless Content development; Online Content development; Project Management; Business Development; Business Analysis; and end-to-end requirement setting for products development.
