Matthew Parker's Summary

Are you:
- fed up at all the jargon in print?
- frustrated that you may not be getting the right price from your printer?
- worried that you could be using your print budget much more effectively?

Do you just wish that buying print was so much easier and less time consuming?

Print & Procurement was founded by Matthew Parker, in the belief that there is not enough help for people who buy print. Unlike most other companies in the print sector, Print & Procurement focuses on practical advice that YOU can put into action (although we can do it for you as well). We are also totally independent:
- we don’t take commission from suppliers
- we offer unbiased advice in the marketplace
- we can work with your current suppliers without any problems
- we don’t do print broking

Print & Procurement works with all sorts of companies. Amongst other things we:
- train staff in all matter related to print and purchasing
- audit print buying at companies: what you buy, how you buy it and where you buy it from, and make recommendations from our findings
- run tenders for you
- write service level agreements for you
- resolve print problems from you

Matthew has had over twenty years experience in buying all sorts of print. He as worked at many different companies of all sizes and from many market sectors. Amongst other roles, Matthew headed up print purchasing at Future Publishing, one of the UK’s leading consumer magazine publishers, achieving significant reductions on a multi-million pound spend every year. As a result Matthew has experience of the variety of company cultures that exists for print buyers. From his experience working on projects in sectors other than print he is also able to bring relevant experience of different market sectors to your solutions. This means that Print & Procurement can often bring a different approach to a project.

Print & Procurement runs projects on behalf of print purchasers, printing companies and print management organisations.

Specialties

Purchasing, e-procurement, negotiation, procurement and print training, supplier management, process reviews, cost reduction.
