Jan Badosa's Summary

After four years working in the communication area, I quit my job to start a new adventure with two colleagues. Working in Tinkle Consultants was a great experience and an opportunity to get deep knowledge about offline media.

Now, in the new company I launched in October 2008 (Digital Seed), we are working to be a reference in online communication and social media. Digital Seed works as a social media agency, using all new communication platforms such as Facebook, Myspace, LinkedIn, Viadeo, Xing, Tuenti, Twitter, bloggers, forums and the rest of the Internet.

Specialties

Public Relations on and offline, communication strategy and consultants. Facebook marketing, Twitter strategy for business, social buzziness, "viral" marketing. Expert in b2f (business to friends).
