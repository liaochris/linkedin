Andy Woodruff's Summary

We operate film studios and have a strong and creative video production facility.

Our clients come to us to fix online communication issues. We can 'do' the corporate video but we work best with forward thinking companies from all sectors who place a strong focus on connecting with their customers online. For these customers the debate about web videos has been decided and they want to understand how to integrate video with their websites and online campaigns and objectives.

The videos we make are designed specifically for online viewing to fulfill a range of objectives from selling, to informational, to providing zero-touch online customer service.

We offer a rare combination of business, design, creative and IT implementation skills that enable us to work across all parts of a company regardless of their size. We have never had a dissatisfied customer.

Specialties

WebVideos core services are providing corporate video production, from creation to filming to editing to the end. We work from Camberwell Studios in south-east London.

Our focus has always been creating videos designed specifically for viewing on the Internet.

We also build websites and provides SEO services.
