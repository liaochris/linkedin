Mike Harvey's Summary

In my current role, I provide outplacement and transition counseling for individuals terminated by fortune 500 companies as a result of restructuring and re-organizing. Many of these individuals are mature workers at job levels ranging from administration to CEO with long term careers with one company and little to no job search skills. I employ a unique, one-on-one approach realizing that everyone requires non-cookie cutter, personalized support.
I have a distinguished career with senior positions in both HR and Sales & Marketing. My experience spans a wide range of industries and companies including Firestone, Reed Paper, Coles Bookstores, Wilson's Stationers, FeherGuard Products and Wilbar International.
I founded M. Harvey & Associates outsourcing HR solutions for small and medium companies (this was in 1994 when outsourcing was not yet developed).
