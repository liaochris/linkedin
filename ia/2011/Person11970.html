In Aug. 2007, (Now this is big for me, knocked me to my knees.)

One time, with one of my houses, instead of evicting this single Mother I helped her buy the house she was renting and I only made $12,000 on that one, but “that is another story”.

I can, on my own do Short Sales, I have done many as a mater of fact, but I prefer faster money, Hence, Wholesaling. “That is another story.”

I have been looking at the houses on the market today and I would have, before, flipped homes for $100-$125k and now they are going for $35K-$60K. I was making that kind of commission, “Almost”.

I must say, I was in denial for a Long While
