Sara Martin’s Summary

During my time with the DJC I have played the role of Account Manager and more recently the Inside Sales Manager. My main focus is to develop new concepts and create internal/external “buy-in” for special advertising sections frequently inserted into the DJC Newspaper. My goal is to help the DJC grow by building long lasting relationships with the Built Industry here in Oregon and SW Washington. I work with many organizations developing advertising campaigns for their directory's and resource guides that they provide to their members.

My background is well groomed from early experience in business development for an industry leading SEO consultancy; a $2B privately owned staffing firm, and more recently a dental practice management/patient education software company. I am excited about my role here at the DJC and the opportunity to serve as a strategic resource within the Portland business community.
