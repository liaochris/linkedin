Tyler Dixon's Summary

Tyler Dixon is Sales Training Manager at Cars.com, a subsidiary of Classified Ventures, owned by five large media partners Belo Corporation, Gannett Company, The McClatchy Company, Tribune Company and The Washington Post Company.

Born in Atlanta (GA), Dixon holds a bachelor’s degree in business administration from Mercer University. She has been in the print/advertising business since the age of 18 when she began her career as a writer for The Mercer Cluster and editor of the Mercer Yearbook.

After graduating from Mercer University, Dixon joined Cox as advertising assistant at the Atlanta (GA) Journal-Constitution, where she also worked as Recruitment Sales Executive, Retail Sales Executive, Real Estate Sales Executive, Automotive Sales Executive, and later Classified Advertising Supervisor.

In 1999, Dixon transferred to the Palm Beach (FL) Post Newspaper as the Classified Advertising Manager (CAM). She was promoted to Manager, Training and Customer Service three years later. In this position, she was responsible for contributing to the achievement of advertising division revenue goals by overseeing the provision of instruction in new employee training, consultative sales methodology, systems and skill training, core competencies for new and veteran sales executives, and development of new programs as business needs changed.

In 2007, Dixon relocated to Chicago (IL) to work for Cars.com.

Active in professional and community organizations, Dixon has been associated with during her career: Society of Classified Advertising Managers of America (SCAMA), Newspaper Association of America (NAA), American Press Institute (API), National Association of Minority Media Executives (NAMME), Chamber of Commerce, Toastmasters International, United Way, and Urban League.

Specialties

Tyler Dixon is a seasoned and effective Sales and Marketing generalist, with years of experience in Sales Management and Training in print and internet advertising. She is a proven leader, communicator, problem solver and strategic planner with demonstrated ability to develop and manage projects in a fast paced environment.
