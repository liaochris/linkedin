Wyatt Taylor’s Summary

Experienced business consultant with enterprise systems in a variety of industries. My emphasis is on improving profits and reducing costs. I can help you identify projects, define them and execute to successful completion. Maximizing your companies' profit is my goal - I can help you rational and prioritize your plans - saving you time and money.

IBM systems a speciality, but I also work with all flavors of technology from MS to Unix to PLC's - I don't do all the work myself, I have a wide network that supports me.

My strength is the ability to understand the business from a P&L perspective and apply IT technology to reduce costs and increase profits. Not a traditional IT staffer or manager - I thrive on helping my clients achieve business goals. My business and IT skills are both broad and deep - can develop solutions, implement, and train. Completing projects is what drives me.

Stronger than most skills with financials and manufacturing cost accounting. Strong manufacturing (high mix/low volume) and distribution. Experience with EDI, both automotive and retail.

I've had two segments of my career as a consultant installing and customizing and integrating ERP systems as a vendor rep, and then developing my own practice offering IT services for IBM i customers. Significant experience with other platforms. I work with packaged software as well as writing code from scratch, and repair other code, either commercial or written specifically for your business.

I've attached as chief architect, led teams of developers and operations staff, teaching at every step along the way. I can work with others and improve their performance and the performance of the organization.
