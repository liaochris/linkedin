Don Wright’s Summary

As a seasoned Creative Graphic Design Professional, I have a proven track record of creating and developing successful solutions for a wide variety of print, web and multimedia design needs. Armed with a keen understanding of the creative process, effective branding, and the follow-through of what it takes to bring projects from concept to fruition, I deliver high-end design solutions for any campaign or promotion, large or small.

• Project Management: Art Direction, Leadership, Fostering Creative Environment, Attention to Detail, Vendor/Client Relations, Trade Show Booth Design, Website Design
