Caitlin Irving's Summary

Caitlin has worked for some of the biggest names in the PR business including Markson Sparks, Maxted Thomas PR and Elise Garland Public Relations which has seen her work across a diverse range of fashion, beauty and lifestyle clients including Max Factor, Covergirl, Bonds Home, IMAX, Housewares International, Kayser Hosiery, Nudie Juices, Husk Boutiques and Marquise.

Branching out as a freelancer in late 2009, following a move to Perth, Caitlin has spent the last year and a half working with Detail, JPPR, Bang PR, Saunders&Co and Klick Communications on clients such as Zara Bryson Boutique, Melissa Harris Jewellery, FashionWA.com, Polo in the Valley, iphone 4, Havaianias, Kailis Jewellery, Red Bull International Air Race, MAMMA MIA! The Musical, Rougemag.com.au, Baci Chocolates and The Fashion Institute to name just a few.
