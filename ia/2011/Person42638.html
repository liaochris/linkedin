Ian Evans's Summary

An experienced projects manager with proven skills as a "rescuer" of troubled projects. Often placed on projects that require refocussing or re-alignment to achieve the original project objectives. An excellent manager capable of building strong, focused teams.

A highly experienced facilitator of large and extensive business Interventions or workshops, often events dealing with sensitive subjects, such as anti-racism interventions in South Africa, with large participant groups.

Ian has worked in South Africa and Southern Africa, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Abu Dhabi and the UK. He is an excellent communicator, experienced with (and enjoys) working in multi-cultural environments.

Ian has guest-lectured at universities on data analysis and modelling and also project management. He has also lectured at colleges and corporations on IT Skills, public speaking & presentation skills. He has taught literacy, numeracy and key skills at college-level. He was also involved with facilitating anti-racism sessions for large business corporations in South Africa.

In addition, Ian is a highly experienced IT designer and analyst and has worked as a developer and project leader in large corporate IT environments. He believes in excellent design discipline, followed by a rigorous testing cycle, results in a quality, right-first-time final product.
