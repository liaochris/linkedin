Ted Canaday’s Summary

I believe in the power of imagination, and its limitless ability to creatively address ANY challenge.

I’m an 8-time Detroit Music Award winner and member of Crain's 40 Under 40 Class of 2008 whose creative & business experience allows me to not only think outside of the box, but kick it to the curb & relegate it to the recycling bin where it belongs.

Creativity is a virtue. Let me help inspire your imagination.

Ted Canaday’s Specialties:

Marketing, communications, public relations, digital & social media, creative direction, strategic planning, organizational development, operational improvement, audio & video production & post, music composition & performance, art direction, copywriting, project management, culture & team building, facilitation, presentations, training, workshops

  • Producer/Songwriter/Guitarist

    Black Bottom Collective

    (Music industry)

    November 2001 — Present (9 years 4 months)

    Guitarist, songwriter and co-producer of Detroit hip hop & soul group Black Bottom Collective. Led by HBO Def Poetry Slam finalist Khary Kimani Turner, BBC has shared stages with Stevie Wonder and Mos Def; been featured in Rolling Stone and Vanity Fair; and are 8 time winners of the Detroit Music Awards, including the 2008 DMA for Outstanding Hip-Hop Recording in collaboration with Grammy award-winning producer Don Was.

  • Professional Musician

    Teduardo

    (Music industry)

    1989 — Present (22 years )

    Performing professionally since the age of 16, I received my BA in music from Wayne State University and have appeared at such venues as St. Andrew’s Hall, the Detroit Opera House, Orchestra Hall, across the country and overseas. I've worked as a guitarist, composer, producer, engineer, singer and multi-instrumentalist with some or all of the following artists and groups: Black Mali, Majic Dragon, Purplefly, Professors of Faith, Isabella, Teduardo, and Black Bottom Collective. As a composer, I've written for commercial clients such as Compuware, GM, Ford Motor Company & Kmart. I've logged over 500 live performances and appeared on close to 20 musical releases, and have had the honor and privilege of performing with artists like the Afghan Wigs, Angelique Kidjo, the Beastie Boys, Blue Dog, Cee-Lo, Chuck D, Darnell Kendricks, Don Was, Dwele, fIREHOSE, Joe Hunter, Mike-E, Nadir, Nappy Roots, Talib Kweli, Thornetta Davis, Zap Toro, and others for whom I count myself as first and foremost, a fan.
