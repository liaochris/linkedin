Vickie Elmer’s Summary

I assign, write and edit useful and creative career stories for several media outlets, notably the Washington Post. And I write high impact, thoughtful articles about business, careers, workplace and the economy for websites, magazines and newspapers. Foreclosure on Easy Street; pink-slipped twice, staging a career comeback and circus performers who stay with the big-top for decades are some of my recent topics.

Recently, I've started writing more about kindness and generosity - and how these beautiful attributes enrich your career and your world. I'm actively seeking more "kindness and careers" story ideas.

I also enjoy coaching writers and other creative types to bring out their best, advance their careers and shorten their job search.
