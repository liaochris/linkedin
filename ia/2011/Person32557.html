Nick De Mey’s Summary

I'm a young Belgian entrepreneur active in several innovation initiatives. My interests in trends, new technology and start-ups have lead to the foundation of the BoardOfInnovation.com, a consulting office focused on business model innovation. Beside that, I also started the successful Belgian marketing & innovation blog nickdemey.be where I share my insights on innovation, new products & services and entrepreneurship in general. Based upon my knowledge of innovative principles used in new media & internet start-ups, I currently assist organisations in making better strategic decisions in relation to new revenue streams and partnership models.
