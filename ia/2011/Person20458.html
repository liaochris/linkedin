Jeri Denniston, SMP’s Summary

You know how some leaders find it hard to get their staff doing the right things to move the organization forward? I help you find clarity in your personal and work life, develop targeted action plans, stay focused on your customers' needs, and keep your long term vision in sight.

I'm a certified strategic management professional, a marketing strategist, and a strategic thinker with strong people and team building skills who has launched new products, increased community involvement, and helped to increase revenues for previous employers.

A visionary idea person, I see connections others don't, often bringing people, ideas and seemingly disparate concepts together that enhance or move a project forward.

If you're looking for someone to chart new paths, develop new market and/or product opportunities, and implement change strategies, consider it done!

My more than 30 years of experience span all facets of marketing and management, including social media networking, cause-related marketing, product and market development, business and strategic planning, public relations, creative services, events marketing, and community relations.

Jeri Denniston, SMP’s Experience

  • Partner / Marketing & Social Media Strategist

    Denner Group International

    (Management Consulting industry)

    January 2006 — Present (5 years 2 months)

    The world of marketing has changed. People are connecting online through social media, using tools such as Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook, FriendFeed, and more. I help people navigate the seas of change using strategic thinking, marketing and social media strategies.

    I am passionate about helping business owners, individuals, and their teams integrate sound marketing strategies as part of their overall strategic planning process. If you look at your business as a whole system comprised of many components that work together to achieve a common goal, you’ll see that marketing is one of those systems.

    For the business to function successfully and smoothly, everyone must be on the same page in terms of how they support the organizational strategies -- marketing included.

  • Strategic Management Principal

    Haines Centre for Strategic Management

    (Privately Held; Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    October 2005 — Present (5 years 5 months)

    Strategic Thinking, Marketing, Leadership and Change Management using Systems Thinking. We are interpreters and translators of best practices research in strategic thinking, planning and management.

    "If you don't know where you're going, any road will get you there," said the Cheshire Cat to Dorothy in The Wizard of Oz.

    That's true for businesses, large and small. Without a destination in mind of what you're trying to create, any action taken within an organization will net something....it just may not be what the executive team had in mind. So what's your dream for your business and life?

    We help you achieve your dreams with a simple backwards thinking tool that gets everyone in the organization working together to achieve the desired results. Productivity increases, bottom line profits increase, employee morale goes up....because everyone understands how their work is contributing to the organization's success.
