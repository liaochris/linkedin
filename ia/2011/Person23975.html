Keith Graves’s Summary

12/1/2010 - Actively looking for a career as a Solar Energy Consultant. Do you know of someone that I should talk to?

9/10/2010 - Well, it appears that this Chapter in my career may be over. After 22 years of promoting Engineered Wood Products it’s time to say thanks and goodbye to my RedBuilt Family, Loyal Customers, supportive Structural Engineers and other friends in the Industry. I have learned so much from you all and will miss interacting with you on a regular basis.

22 years ago I was hired by Trus Joist in Thousand Oaks as a commercial draftsman; shortly after, we moved our office to Ventura. Our Commercial Business was thriving, our Residential program was just taking off, TJ Beam (software) had just been released, Parallam & Timberstrand had not yet been introduced; Ammonia blueprint machines, lead-holders and scum-bags were common office equipment.

I have had the pleasure to work for so many great companies including Trus Joist, Macmillan Bloedel (Tacoma), Weyerhaeuser, Terry (Stock) Lumber and Willamette Industries. The last eight years was a home-coming for me, having had the chance to return to my roots and finish where I first started (Trus Joist Commercial). In the past eight years, we've went by many names (Trus Joist, Trus Joist Commercial, iLevel and RedBuilt); A true testament that change is all around us.

All of you have felt the effects of our struggling economy and have had to scale down your operations in order to survive; Redbuilt is no different. My sales position has been eliminated (as of 9/10/2010) along with many other (Redbuilt) positions in sales, manufacturing and engineering over the last two years. The remaining RedBuilt staff is the Best of the Best and will weather the full extent of the economic storm. RedBUILT is BUILT of great individuals (and friends), with a long-standing culture that is fueled by Passion, Customer Service and Excellence.

Thanks again & best of Luck!

Keith Graves
