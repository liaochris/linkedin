Markos Psarros's Summary

Last 18 years in software sales and distribution. One of main goals is to establish Maple as the mathematical software of choice in all levels of greek education and at the same time convincing people to use legal software. One of our main efforts was to fully translate Maple software's menus, maplets and tutors in greek, a huge work that started in Maple10, completed successfully in Maple13 and continued to Maple14.
Before that, 6 years experience in Environmental project planning and evaluation and 10 years experience in Construction as Construction Manager on large projects.
In total more than 25 years in software development (as a programmer), evaluation and beta testing.
Writing reviews and comparisons of scientific software in computer magazines and blogs in Greece. Regular contributor in PC Magazine-Greek Edition and in foracamp.gr.
Always seaking for new business opportunities and opening new software markets in Greece and Cyprus.
Last but not least, interested in investing in Renewable Energy Sources. Has applied for a license to install a 100KW photovoltaic park in Sparta, which was expected to be approved in first half of 2010 but due to economic crisis in Greece has been stopped.
New ventures include the building and operation of a new facility in Sparta for packaging organic agricultural products (Bio-certified) like oil. olives, honey, marmelade etc and distributing then in Europe and North America.

Specialties

Extensive similar software product comparison and evaluation. Active beta tester of Maple, Maplesim, MathType, SigmaPlot, Minitab, Geometry Expressions, PhotoModeler, PhotoModeler Scanner and x-beta tester of Mathcad, Statgraphics and Tecplot. Localization of scientific software in greek (Maple and Geometry Expressions). Distributorship of scientific, technical, mathematical and engineering software in Greece and Cyprus and in SE Europe and Middle East in general.
