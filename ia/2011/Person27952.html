Maria Rapetskaya's Summary

Maria is equally versed in motion, web and web design. She has been in the industry for over 14 years (but she doesn’t look a day older than when she started.) She has worked for top design and post-production facilities, collecting Telly and BDA awards and international kudos. Her first independent venture was highly successful partnership as balance gfx in NYC. She has now transformed that experience into Undefined Creative, expanding the client and project base within a fresh business model. She merges her creative, technical and practical expertise to manage this innovative boutique design studio, dedicated to all things creative and digital.

In addition, Maria teaches motion graphics and digital design at NYU and at University of the Arts in Philadelphia, where she organized a collaborative project for the final thesis, partnering students with non-profit groups to produce pro-bono original motion graphics content for socially responsible and green causes.

If not at her desk, she can be spotted in offbeat places like Mongolia or Easter Island. Or powering up with a cup of coffee a block away.

Specialties

Motion graphics, broadcast design, creative development, branding, print design, web design, packaging/POS design, illustration.
