Louis DiBridge's Summary

My goal is to work with and help companies expand their business with my unique brand and printing knowledge. I believe in the philosophy of you are either growing your business or it is dying. I have over twenty years of running and marketing a commercial printing company delivering top-quality direction and vision in the custom manufacturing business of commercial printing. Being able to create or follow brand graphic standards and having complete knowledge of fine papers and the printing and color characteristics on different paper finishes has a special value to any client.

Specializing in understanding what businesses needs in terms of brand development and increasing sales, then being able to recommend solutions to help a business differentiate itself from the competition and help to continue to grow. My ability to consulate with business owners who want to analyze and strategize their current situation and then make positive changes in the direction of the business is uncanny, extremely beneficial and profitable.

Specialties

Marketing, commercial printing, digital printing, PURL based marketing and tracking, direct response advertising consultation, brand positioning, brand personality, brand identity, competitive media comparison, advertising, sales, sales management, printing, trade shows marketing, proficient in Monarch estimating system, complete understanding of print market pricing, gang printing, 4 color process, PMS color,and graphic softwares.
