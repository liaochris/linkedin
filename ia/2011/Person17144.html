Howard Blumenthal's Experience

CEO

MiND: Media Independence

Broadcast Media industry

September 2005 – Present (5 years 7 months)

Based in Philadelphia, Independence Media operates one of the nation's largest independent broadcast TV stations. We have transformed a local channel into MiND: Media Independence (www.mindtv.org), a multi-platform, community-activated channel serving local, national and international communities. MiND is now seen in 20 million homes nationwide.

Executive Director; President

NJN (New Jersey Network)

Non-Profit Organization Management industry

September 2009 – September 2010 (1 year 1 month)

(Interim one-year role.) Dual chief executive role included leadership of a state authority (NJPBA) and President, Foundation for New Jersey Public Broadcasting. NJN is known for news, public affairs, and a rich history of award-winning productions. In terms of households reached, NJN is the nation's largest local television broadcaster. Principal responsibility for strategic leadership, large-scale organizational planning, and business reinvention as a result of anticipated change from 40-year government entity to independent nonprofit.
