Justin Giardina's Summary

Originally from Rochester, New York, I escaped the cold gray skies in search of warmer weather and a brighter future. Through my experience I have learned a lot about myself and realized my true passions.

As a designer I am inspired by those who go above and beyond the call of duty to create something extraordinary and unexpected.

By growing up in an entrepreneurial family, I have learned what it takes to run a successful business and have always found an interest in learning what makes that business better.

I am continually looking to expand my talents and knowledge through creative problem solving. I thrive in a creative environment as it fuels my passion and drives me to do better. I look forward to the challenges in the future and am confident my experience has prepared me to meet them.
