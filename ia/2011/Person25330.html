Lachlan Sloan’s Summary

As a Director for a Private Equity Fund Lachlan spans across multiple investments. Currently he serves as a Director of three diverse investments.

As Group Director, Compass Offices Lachlan is reasponsible for the strategic group wide operations. This includes all new market development, leasing and larger Outsourced Workspace Solutions.

As a Director of Encore Professional Services Lachlan advises with the company on strategic direction, client engagement and service delivery models.

As Managing Director of Group Operations at International Recruitment Investments , Lachlan is responsible for the day-to-day operations as well as special projects that include mergers and acquisitions,and other board directed tasks.

Lachlan began his career in technology, starting his own dotcom business straight out of university. This experience at such a young age meant that he gained a solid understanding of sales and operations at an early point in his career, before taking these skills and knowledge to a boutique technology recruitment firm in his home town of Auckland, New Zealand.

Always interested in Asia, he joined ConnectedGroup in 2001, an IT recruitment firm based in Hong Kong. He was quickly promoted to head of the firm’s technology division. He later participated in a joint management buyout of the firm and this led to a new role as business development manager for the firm’s human resources outsourcing solutions business.

In 2003, Lachlan joined Hong Kong recruitment firm Wall Street Associates serving first as Chief Operating Officer and then as Chief Executive Officer. This firm was acquired by Talent2 in June 2005, after which Lachlan continued to manage the business in his capacity as Chief Executive Officer for another two years.

In July 2007 Lachlan then moved into the Managed Services business unit of Talent2 Asia. In 2008 he decided to take his skills in Recruitment, RPO, Management and Solution Selling and work in private equity.
