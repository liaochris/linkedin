Leslie Knowlton's Summary

I work with the high performing individual who is challenged by a new leadership role. Together we define the path to leverage their strengths and accelerate their learning curve. I also create a safe environment for working teams to have difficult conversations so they can enjoy their teamwork and reach their desired goals.

Twenty years experience in leadership and team coaching, professional development, organizational change, communications, training technologies, leadership, management for a Science and Technology Research Organization.

I enjoyed pursuing a Masters of Fine Arts early in my career where I learned to think critically and observe the world with fresh eyes. I am a student of Carl G. Jung and believe his theories can help us to understand what motivates us and how each of us deals with life's stresses and inevitable changes.
