David Allen Ibsen's Summary

For nearly 25 years, David Allen Ibsen has been brand and business strategist, working with some of the world's leading organizations.

David is the principal strategist at 5 Meetings Before Lunch, a seasoned business marketing consultancy, working with leaders who want to advance great ideas and catalyze change in the organizations.

He has led agency teams for Microsoft, Xerox and Novell, and was on the launch teams for Lexus, and Hyundai automobiles. David directed the global re-positioning of Autodesk and the revitalization of the marketing organization at Macromedia. He has created global event programs for Blurb and helped dozens of organizations re-position, re-brand and launch successful go-to-market plans.

He started his career in New York, Los Angeles and San Francisco with some of the largest advertising agencies in the world, including Team One/Saatchi & Saatchi, Ogilvy & Mather, Backer & Spielvogel and Young & Rubicam.

David has spent the last 13 years building a successful strategic consultancy, working with over 70 clients in a variety of industries to "solve problems and accelerate change."

In addition to his consulting practice, David writes an internationally recognized brand and marketing blog titled "5 Blogs Before Lunch" which has been acknowledged as the Best Business Blog by the prestigious Webby Awards in 2006 and 2009.

He is a member of the International Academy of Digital Arts and Sciences, and has participated as a judge in their Webby Awards and W3 competitions.

David is often asked to speak on the topics of brand and strategy, market building, advertising, social media marketing and blogging.

Specialties

Business strategy, brand strategy and messaging, corporate marketing and product marketing programs, advertising campaign development
