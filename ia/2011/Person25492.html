Larry Pizzi’s Summary

Appointed President and Chief Executive of Currie Technologies in May of 2005, Larry was former Executive Vice President in charge of Sales and Marketing at Currie. Joining the team in May of 2002, he acted quickly to secure a brand license agreement with Pacific Cycle, LLC for Schwinn, GT and Mongoose while developing a product, sales and marketing strategy around the distribution of these strong brands into the broader marketplace.

Prior to joining Currie, Larry was the Senior Vice President and General Manager at the Schwinn/GT Corporation. Hired by the private equity that owned this ailing business, Larry was recruited to help consolidate this business and package it for sale. A successful sale was completed late in 2001 with the sell price exceeding expectations in excess of twenty five million dollars.

Prior to joining Schwinn/GT, Larry was the General Manager at Fogdog Sports, a leading dot-com retailer of sporting goods. Fogdog is the largest web-based retailer in this category and has built a strong brand within the marketplace. He was successful in helping to transition this business to a new owner in December of 2000.

Prior to this short stint in the Internet world, Larry held sales and marketing management roles within the Brunswick Corporation’s bicycle division from May 1996 through December 1999. He began at Brunswick as Director of Specialty Sales and Marketing. While in this role, helped engineer a successful cross-channel strategy that took Brunswick’s newly acquired Mongoose brand of bicycles, into the mass market.

Larry has also held Sales and Sales Management roles at Bell Sports, Service Cycle and Derby Cycle Corporation. He has owned and operated a successful chain of Specialty Retail stores from 1978 to 1990 and has served on the board of the National Bicycle Dealers Association as a Director.
