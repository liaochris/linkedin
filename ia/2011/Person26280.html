Sheena D Whyatt MCT, MCASI, MIITT’s Summary

Sheena leads Lightning Training with a firm belief in the importance of using training as a valuable tool to bring benefits to clients, new and old.

Accredited by the Institute of IT Training as a Gold Accredited Training Provider, Lightning Training are also member of the Microsoft Partner Network, working towards Information Worker competencies, and a member of the TAP Partners Programme.

Our industry knowledge allows us to recommend the most appropriate solutions to our clients needs. By offering a blended, modular solution to learning, a wide range of training needs can be covered by our Director and her team.

She says ‘Whether our clients engage with us for specific training dates to meet specific needs, have a rolling programme of training in response to a detailed Training Needs Analysis, or take up one of our membership packages, our training helps to make our clients’ staff more efficient in their day to day roles, more confident when performing less routine tasks, and able to help themselves and others solve non-technical problems when using software applications.’

With many years experience in providing and delivering training, Sheena is superbly placed to understand the needs of our clients and of our training team. Her project management skills allow her to manage training projects both simple and complex, keeping targets and team focussed on the task in hand, and working together for success.

A highly qualified trainer herself, Sheena holds Microsoft Certified Trainer status, with instructional qualifications in all Office suites. She is also a full Member of the Institute of IT Training, which recognises trainers and those in a management or consultancy role who have a demonstrable track record of excellence with associated certification. In keeping with membership of the Institute, she has a rounded set of skills combined with validated experience and measurable achievement of business objectives.
