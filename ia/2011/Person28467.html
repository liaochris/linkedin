Mark Zmarzly’s Summary

GENERAL OVERVIEW

I am an accomplished marketing, business development, banking, and creative professional with demonstrated success solving customer acquisition, marketing, and profitability problems facing financial institutions. I have worked with banks from 1 branch up to 300+ branches in the areas of marketing, copywriting, account management, consulting, teaching, social media, and business development. My ideas and execution have been positive catalysts for both my companies and clients to grow profits and gain new customers.

You can find my insights on issues facing the financial industry here on LinkedIn and on Twitter @BankMarketing. I am also an open networker – so please connect with me at markz@actonfs.com.

PROFESSIONAL SKILLS

• Wide-ranging experience and education in all types of marketing and writing.
• Full sales cycle knowledge: marketing, prospecting, qualifying, presenting, follow up, and closing.
• Broad background in presenting to large groups, small groups, one-on-one, and team facilitation.
• Extensive network and knowledge in financial services, marketing, and higher education industries.

EDUCATION

I am a graduate of the University of Nebraska-Lincoln with a B.A. in both Organizational Communication Studies and English. I also have an M.A. in Creative Writing from UNL. In 2011, I graduated with honors from the ABA School of Bank Marketing and Management. I look forward to more learning and growth opportunities as my career evolves.
