Marilyn McSweeney’s Summary

I am a dedicated business partner and take immense pride in the achievement of positive results for my clients. I offer a comprehensive knowledge of our nuanced job market and a big picture mentality. As an accomplished business executive, I am heavily involved in local and national business communities as well as service organizations—this has allowed me to form authentic, meaningful connections. I feel that when others associate with me, trust and loyalty are the results of our time together.

If you are looking for a professional career coach and recruiter who brings understanding, connections and proven results to your challenges, I would love a chance to speak with you further.

My company, McSweeney-Miller Executive Career Management, excels in the arena of career transition services for companies and their outplaced staff members. We differ because of our refusal to adopt a one-size-fits-all strategy of pre-packaged services. Instead, we consult in order to provide boutique-styled outplacement programs, tailored to fit the needs of each client. We believe in our ability to be agile, robust and affordable. Our extensive knowledge base, research skills and vast North American networks allow us to assist others in the navigation of a tumultuous job market environment with results that are both genuine and focused.
