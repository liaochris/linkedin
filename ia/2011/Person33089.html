James Norquay's Summary

I have been involved with Internet Marketing since 1999, starting at a very young age, I taught myself how to build websites, I taught myself SEO by reading forums and networking with professionals from the USA, testing methods over many years, I taught myself Social Media Tactics the same way. To date I have built over 100 different personal websites which have brought millions of visitors over the last few years.

I did not limit myself to only websites, I have been involved with website network management, Domain name buying and selling, Affiliate marketing, Viral Marketing, Social Media Marketing and pretty much any style of internet marketing I can get my hands on.

I then moved into Internet Marketing Consulting working on the client side for large networks of websites and also small to medium sized business then now working on the agency side for the largest corporations in Australia. I have been taught the secrets and trained up by some of the top SEO's from US/Europe and Brazil over recent years to develop me into the SEO and Social Media Professional that I am today.

I now manage several big accounts for SEO and Social Media and love teaching others about SEO. I also pitch for new business regularly and love teaching others about internet marketing.

Some achievements to date:
* One of the first to launch a large scale social media campaign for personal injury lawyers in australia.
* Have had my SEO writings featured on SEO MOZ, Search Engine Land and many other internet marketing websites.
* Have increased many clients traffic.
* Have taught many people about the benefits of Social Media and SEO.
