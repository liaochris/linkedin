Expériences de Marlon-David Sias

  • Systems Administrator

    ACT For America

    (secteur Affaires étrangères )

    mars 2008 — septembre 2008 (7 mois)

    New York Times best selling author and terrorism survivor, Brigitte Gabriel recruited me to move an office from Virginia Beach, VA to an undisclosed location in Pensacola, Fl. This required me to move all the existing computer systems to the new location and set it up with the latest security technologies necessary for fighting terrorist's ideology. Confronting the threats which had repeatedly brought down Act For America's website, I worked the organization through a complete system transformation; from a new website and secure forum, to creating a Sales Force secure IP single signon and cloud solution. I preformed the extensive research necessary to develop a long term plan for preventing user and terrorist interruptions. Instead of just hiring a professional hacker to destroy our data from a stolen computer and audit our system, I began implementing LowJack for Laptops, use of biometric thumb drives, cloud computing, alternative operating systems and software and hardware firewalls. I went beyond training their long term staff in providing typical help desk solutions by working to ensure the organization had the ability to bring together numerous disorganized databases and deal with emerging threats.
