Sarah Rock's Summary

I'm a native Californian who is passionate about teaching and sharing my love for the fine and performing arts. I hold a bachelor's degree in Public Relations/Advertising and Art History from Chapman University. I interned with many companies in the Southern California area, including For Your Art in Los Angeles where I assisted in planning L.A. Art Weekend. I have also volunteered my PR and event planning experience with various charities including Barks of Love Animal Rescue. While in college, I worked as a teacher's aid at Blind Children's Learning Center. This was my first experience as a teacher and it did not take long for me to realize this was my true calling. I am currently pursuing my Multiple Subject Master's in Teaching while working as a gymnastics instructor and birthday party coordinator at Team OC in Costa Mesa. My goal is to teach third, fourth or fifth grade, but I would also love the opportunity to be an education coordinator at a museum or performing arts center. I hope to start coaching my own summer cheerleading classes for ages 6-10 as well as continuing my volunteer PR work in my spare time. In addition to teaching, I enjoy cooking and party planning!
