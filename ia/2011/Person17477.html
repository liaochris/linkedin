About RIA Connect

RIA Connect is a community initiative started by Himanshu Mody as an Adobe Community Champion in India.The objective of RIA Connect is to educate potential talent about opportunities to build a career in RIA and encourage organizations to adapt flash platform.

Under this initiative we visit institutions and organizations to conduct a free workshop on any of the adobe products related to flash platform


Steps to sign up for RIA Connect

1. Join the official RIA Connect group site on http://groups.adobe.com/join/riaconnect
