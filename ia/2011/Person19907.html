Jeff Hicks's Summary

Having worked for years providing full advertising/marketing agency services, I am currently focusing on interactive marketing and social media. Web 2.0 has changed the marketing landscape forever, and Optimize Interactive is leading the way with new online strategies that increase revenue while reducing marketing expenses. With our myMOSAIK project, we're using interactive and social media techniques to bring companies in direct, regular contact with highly targeted communities. We're turning social media into a channel that can be easily managed and tracked. And, we offer the industry's most advanced tool for managing brands and SEO performance in real time.
