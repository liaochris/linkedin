Sean Collins’s Summary

Sean Collins is a rising senior at Fordham University's Gabelli School of Business Administration. Currently, he is pursuing a Bachelors of Science in Business Administration with a dual concentration in human resource management and finance. As an aspiring human resources professional, Sean has capitalized on a variety of opportunities that have progressively strengthened his competencies in many HR disciplines. These opportunities include the completion of a hands-on, in-depth human resources internship with A|X Armani Exchange. Furthermore,as a Part Time Mac Specialist, Sean actively participates in hiring events for Apple, where he attracts and identifies talented individuals for the highly-selective retailer. Along with these hiring events, Sean also assists with Apple's daily zoning schedules, where he creates staffing solutions that optimally meet customer demand by analyzing sales data of previous years. Finally, Sean is a Career Peer Advisor at Fordham University, where he contributes to the training, development and success of fellow Fordham students' aspiring career goals, by conducting mock interviews and resume critiquing during his office hours.

Currently, Sean is actively networking to prepare for future employment opportunities upon graduation. Upon securing employment, Sean looks to work toward an MSHR degree. Please do not hesitate to reach out to Sean for any inquiries, networking or employment opportunities!
