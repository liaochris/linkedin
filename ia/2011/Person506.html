Adam H. Small’s Summary

Passionate networker and serial entrepreneur. I truly enjoy meeting new people and learning about their background, current endeavors and future plans.

Growing up in family business, I have discovered my passion resides in being a catalyst. There is no more exhilarating challenge than transforming an idea into an actual operating entity. Whether a company, organization or initiative, each day offers new challenges and I thrive on the chaos of the start up.

I welcome the opportunity to work alongside like-minded professionals and willing to explore and consider any new projects.
