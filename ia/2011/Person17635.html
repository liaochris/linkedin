William Provost's Summary

Expect More from your Online Marketing Campaign....


The travel leisure group travel industry is a dynamic marketplace. As projected by phoCusWright Research as a $90-billion industry, the leisure group travel market is anticipated to grow faster than the travel industry as a whole. The industry has evolved as a niche to usher in a new platform of rich group travel experiences, opening up a new customer base for travel marketers.

According to HeBus, in 2011, hoteliers continue to move funds to the online channel, and carefully employ ROI-centric and customer-engaging initiatives.

Hoteliers and Leisure Groups do not want just any online bookings. The focus of 2011 hotel and leisure group internet marketing budgets must be on driving bookings through the direct online channel, their company website.   The OTA channel not only is not “free,” but also is, on average, 10 times more expensive than the direct online channel.

Hoteliers and Leisure Groups need a robust direct online channel strategy accompanied by adequate marketing funds to be able to a) take advantage of the steady growth in the Internet channel and b) shift bookings from the indirect (OTA) to direct (hotel website) online channels.

Every tourism-related business needs a solid group marketing strategy.

That strategy begins with How2 Travel Marketing Group.
