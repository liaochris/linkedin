Roy Vella's Summary

Simply put, my passion is growing businesses in the mobile and web technology industries -specializing in businesses that enable the convergence between online and offline activities. My specific expertise has been in strategy & business development, sales & marketing, and, most importantly, being a strong customer experience advocate.

I create possibilities through partnerships, products and people, with a successful track record developing ideas into businesses and individuals into teams. Currently, I am advising a number of early stage, growing enterprises as I decide where best to focus my full attention next.

Most recently, I was recruited by RBS to lead their mobile efforts globally as an "intrapraneur". Previously, I was head of Mobile for PayPal Europe and helped jump-start multiple new businesses within PayPal during my time there. First, Merchant Services by building the team and driving large channel partnership deals. Next, PayPal's launch into the travel space and finally with mobile in Europe. Prior to PayPal, I have served in various startup/VC roles from Partner/Co-founder to VP of Sales & Marketing.

Also active in the non-profit, education and arts arena, I serve as a Board Member for the Stanford Business School Alumni Association and am an active alumni for the school in a number of regards. I have served as Board President for Variety Children's Charity of Northern California as well as Board Treasurer for Bay Area Theater Sports Improv. I hold a BA in Mathematics (with High Honors) from Holy Cross College, a JD from Stanford Law School, and an MBA (with certificates in Global & Public Management) from the Stanford Graduate School of Business.
