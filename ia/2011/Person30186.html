Michael Merhab’s Summary

20 years of successful experience in the marketing, sales, and licensing fields, at the senior management level, with over eight years experience in E-Commerce.

Expertise encompasses the domestic and international consumer products industry with a broad background in marketing, e-commerce, licensing, sales, creative, product & consumer research, manufacturing, and public relations.

Diverse experience in: traditional marketing, direct marketing, sports marketing, Internet & hi-tech marketing, entertainment marketing, and cause marketing.

Pioneer in creating ground-breaking marketing and promotional programs such as: The first sports licensed dual branded consumer product release, jointly executed with the Mead Company and Major League Baseball; The first interactive, on-site and live E-Commerce Internet auction, tied into a professional sports league and event with the NHL; The first promotional Internet auction rewards program, jointly executed with the Labatt Brewing Company.

Successful management and execution of several premium programs with clients such as McDonalds, Dominos, and Burger King.

Creation of successful product campaigns with retailers such as Wal Mart, Target and Kmart.

Possess strong relationships with every professional sports league and players association, major sports agencies, major video game manufacturers, major sports and entertainment memorabilia suppliers, University Athletic Departments, Ebay, and Collegiate Licensing Company.
