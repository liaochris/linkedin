Justin Miller’s Summary

Art director & designer with 10 years of experience helping brands and organizations innovate through design.

I have a history of building successful products and marketing experiences for a wide variety of clients in sports, entertainment, and consumer products.

My work and professional views have been published in a variety of publications ranging from Communication Arts to the Wall Street Journal. I've served the Advisory Board at the Savannah College of Art & Design.
