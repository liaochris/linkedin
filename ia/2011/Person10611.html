David A. Falter’s Summary

Dave is a forward-thinking and strategic executive providing more than 20 years of dynamic leadership success to start-up and established global businesses across broad spectrum of industries -- from online and offline travel services, textiles, and medical informatics to online consumer services, business consulting services, and enterprise software and computing. Dave possesses a record of achievement fueling multimillion-dollar revenues and dramatic margin increases as well as boosting productivity and efficiency, designing and launching lucrative product lines, and capitalizing on cost-saving measures. He is a collaborative agent of change who expertly builds and maintains high-performing organizations through top-down restructurings. An analytical and pragmatic problem solver, Dave confidently tackles and overcomes complex issues to maintain movement toward enterprise business objectives.
