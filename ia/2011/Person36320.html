Ian French's Summary

The Really Helpful Marketing Company exists to help marketeers improve their marketing ROI. Our core training in direct marketing and related disciplines across the full media spectrum means that we are highly results-motivated with an absolute focus on the bottom line. We work on a freelance or project basis with both agencies (offering planning and account handling services) and directly with our own clients.

We never forget that it is our job to provide best advice. We are independent and media neutral – and therefore entirely objective. So we can offer down to earth, practical help and support. We have no other agenda.

Through our network of highly experienced freelance marketing and creative professionals, we can call on a wide range of skills as and when appropriate, to deliver;

Marketing Communications Audits (Performance Metrics Analysis; Market Research Analysis & Insight Generation; Market Report Summaries & Analysis; Competitive Activity & Proposition Analysis; Creative Critiques)
Marketing Communications Strategy Development
Activity Planning & Forecasting
Customer Journey Mapping & Retention Strategies
Brand Positioning & Customer Proposition Development
Creative Development
Customer Proposition & Creative Briefing Training for both agency account handlers and client marketers
Workshop Development & Facilitation for ideas generation
Planning Process Assessment & Best Practice Facilitation
Continuous Improvement Programme Development & ongoing support

In short, we’ll help define where the issues and opportunities lie – and provide practical, actionable solutions. And because we keep our overheads low, we can deliver very competitive rates too.

We are friendly, co-operative, constructive and accommodating. We’re responsive to our client’s specific needs, we keep our promises, and we’re honest – if we don’t have the skills required to provide the right solution, then we’ll help identify an alternative.
