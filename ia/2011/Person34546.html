Perri Richman’s Summary

Business leader with 15 years of experience and emphasis on using communications to strategically deliver business outcomes.

Deep experience in all facets of communications including public relations, advocacy, community relations, crisis, marketing, employee and change communications. Interest and track record in environmental/green and safety-related communications.

Measurable success in executing positioning and thought leadership programs, product introductions, events, change, media training and relations, and leadership coaching for manufacturing, automotive, consumer and luxury goods, technology and financial services industries. Brands include Trane, American Standard, Citi, Ford, MasterCard, McDonalds, Sony, Unilever, and more.

Significant tenure with companies; currently employed by Ingersoll Rand, leading the communications function within Trane, a global provider of indoor comfort systems and services. Trane was acquired by Ingersoll Rand from American Standard in 2008.
