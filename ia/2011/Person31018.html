Mitch Friedman's Summary

A powerful creative resource offering twenty-two years of innovative design in Broadcast/Film Production & Post-production, Advertising & Promotion, Interactive Design, Global Branding and Search Engine Marketing. An award-winning Creative Director, Art Director, Design Director recognized for creating hundreds of network identities, main title designs, visual effects and broadcast promotion. Comfortable working in both visual design and development of high level detailed storyboards, architecture and prototypes to effectively communicate interactive design ideas. Primary focus on managing creative projects through multiple channels that integrate versatile styles with a strong emphasis on conceptual exploration and strategic branding. Goal: to deliver excellence and accuracy to key target audiences with a firm understanding of the client's expectations.

Specialties

Proficient In: Visual conceptualization & storyboarding, strong presentation abilities, aptitude to articulate and substantiate design direction, diverse design & art direction skills in interactive, flash, broadcast, film & video animation for implementation, creative team management, global branding and developing e-commerce strategies for search engine marketing.
