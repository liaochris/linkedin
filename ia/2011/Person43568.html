Tina Johnston's Summary

Tina is the Director of NewStarts Counseling and Education Services located in Keller, which provides counseling and educational support services. She holds credentials as a Licensed Professional Counselor, National Certified Counselor, Educational and Vocational Guidance Practitioner, and Global Career Development Facilitator. Her specialties as a therapist include ADHD, anxiety, and career counseling, but her passion is working with young people in helping them find direction in life and career. She developed CareerConstruction just for this purpose.

Tina is also the President of the Texas Career Development Association, founder of the Keller Counselors Association and is a member of the Career and Technology Taskforce for the Texas Workforce Investment Council.
