Raz Schiønning's Summary

With an internal staff of designers, developers, content experts, and Web marketing specialists, Raz Schionning manages American Apparel's global online Web presence, e-commerce, and logistics. In this role, Raz puts his expert knowledge of usability, marketing, commerce, and technology to good use. Since joining American Apparel in 2004, the company's Web-enabled business has grown from under $2 Million, to over $75 Million in 2009. The ever-increasing influence of the Internet makes for a challenging and fast-paced work environment that requires Raz's team to keep the company site fresh, functional, and engaging.

Prior to joining American Apparel, Schionning was Vice President of Production at the global interactive agency AKQA (previously Magnet Interactive). At AKQA Schionning was responsible for web sites for a broad range of clients including Discovery Channel, Häagen-Dazs, GlobalOne, AT&T, IBM, DuPont, and Harley-Davidson. Schionning holds a degree in arts from Dawson College and a B.F.A. from Concordia University with specialization in Human-Computer Interface Design.
