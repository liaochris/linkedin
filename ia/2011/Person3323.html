Anthony Ramirez’s Summary

Anthony Ramirez has been leading and developing recruiting teams since 2005. He is a Recruiting Manager with Freeport McMoran Copper and Gold in Phoenix, Arizona.

Anthony is very committed to the recruiting profession and views it as an opportunity to help change peoples lives. He is excellent at mentoring and developing new recruiters and building very strong relationships with his candidates and hiring managers.

Anthony is married to his wife Kerry and together they have five beautiful children, Nick, Sam, Jack and Isabel and Cooper.
