Cathie Heath’s Summary

Cathie is a life-long entrpreneur whose first venture, at the age of five, involved
cutting all her mother’s flowers and selling them door to door for a nickel apiece..three for a dime.

Since 1970 , she has worked in direct sales, network marketing, and has two successful homebased businesses. In June of 2009, Cathie began networking online and has developed expertise in social media, video marketing, blogging, etc.

She’s become an advocate for the “little guy”, standing against the “get rich quick” and “you have to have this high dollar system” offers seen on the Internet, reaching out to those single moms, retirees and unemployed parents who are handing over hundreds and THOUSANDS of dollars…because they have a dream…only to find themselves sitting alone in the dark, staring at a computer screen trying to figure it all out ALONE.

With over 40 years of success and failure, Cathie offers a real, honest alternative to her clients, teaching them the principles behind marketing, the skills needed to tie various marketing methods together, as well as the personal development and mindset concepts that foster both business and personal success.

Cathie’s passion is to help other home and small business owners with a dream in their heart avoid the hype, save time (and money!) by openly and honestly sharing her experience and expertise with them.
