Eddie Drake's Summary

Creative and entrepreneurial technology leader with focus on IP-based media delivery and consumer technologies. 10 year veteran of the emerging IPTV marketplace with a proven track record of leading geographically and culturally diverse development teams. Strong architectural, systems and software development leadership background. Strategic thinker with the ability to balance business objectives with market requirements, technical and resource reality. Strong experience in media design and production as well as technologies related to the digital home.

Specialties

Expert in IPTV Architecture and middleware systems. Strong background in consumer media platforms and distribution technology, content management systems, DRM/CA systems, video on demand systems. Background in networking protocols and routing. Experience with Agile software development methodology. Media production, animation, compositing and graphic design background.
