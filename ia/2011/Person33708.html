I am a writer, editor, and communications consultant because "It Matters How You Say It!". I help businesses and individuals to say what they do and say what they mean in the Write Way. I listen to my clients; help them to determine what they want and why they want it; and then help them to say what they want in a format that will get them what they want.

I work with individuals, graduate and post-graduate university students and businesses of all sizes throughout the United States, Canada, the U.K. and six other countries on a variety of individual, academic, and business copywriting, writing and editing projects. Non-profits and students receive a 15% discount. Ghostwriting and working with ESL (English as a Second Language) students are some of my specialties. I can be reached by phone, fax, Skype, Gtalk, Yahoo IM and email.

I am a full-time, mature and experienced researcher, writer and editor; my office is fully equipped with the latest hardware, high-speed internet, FTP and MS 2007 Office Suite. I am also proficient in Web 2.0 apps and social media. Minimum project amount is $150.00; free consultations and professional quotations/estimates are provided.
