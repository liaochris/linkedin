Kim Neff’s Summary

*Worked with business partners in UK, France and Japan as well as domestic partners to plan both top-line and bottom up financial plans for 11 channels across 3 different distribution models in 15 countries. Total retail business close to $200M. Extensive knowledge of wholesale, consignment and direct-to-consumer (QVC, web and infomercial) sales and revenue models. Global knowledge of trends and product preferences which helped drive different assortment plans and business emphases with decrease inventory ownership. Extensive knowledge of total supply chain (Sourcing, Production, Inventory Management, Inventory Control, Allocation, Distribution and Warehouse Management) because of critical cross-functional interdependencies and "start-up" feel of the business.
*From Feb 2009 to present, guided transition of business management to UK partners for UK channels with an emphasis on OTB for the back-end of the business. This project included: mapping the OTB process for the UK team, outline information pass-off between Channel Planning and Inventory Management in the UK and between UK and US; interviewing and training the managers of Inventory Management and Channel Planning in the UK office; learning Oracle R12 MRP system in order to trouble-shoot business parameters, outputs and how to incorporate that information into the domestic OTB data; help liaison between UK IM and domestic Wholesale Operations to ensure on-time delivery of shipments between US and UK DC's.
- March 2010: In addition, my responsibilities have expanded to managing International Wholesale Operations with a focus to develop the team and work on tools, process and communication with the function to improve on time delivery of products to external customers, increase visibility to revenue each month to internal partners, drive to earn every revenue dollar for the company and improve communication with our overseas partners at Bare Escentuals UK.
