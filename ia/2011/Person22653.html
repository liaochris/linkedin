Jonathan Peizer’s Summary

Currently working with a diverse set of clients on socially responsible strategic planning & implementation projects (see: http://www.internautconsulting.com/index-3.html)

Select (Foundations/Corporate/Govt/NGO clients) include:

Gates Foundation, Carnegie Corporation, Council on Foundations, Kauffman Foundation, Templeton Foundation, Doris Duke Foundation, Ford Foundation, Hewlett Foundation, Robert Sterling Clark Foundation, Microsoft, IDRC, US Govt, Eurasia Foundation, InsideNGO, CEE Academic Network, American Forum, Aspiration, iEarn-US, Telecentre.org, NPower

Previous experience:
As CIO/CTO, developing the Soros Foundation's operational IT strategy, and creating/executing its global Internet Program providing connectivity, training and content to over 1 million people globally. Raised $25 million in 3rd party partnerships. Set up ISP's that met social missions and then evolved into sustainable and profitable ventures.

Assisting the Media Development Loan Fund to create the Center for Advanced Media in Prague (http://www.mdlf-camp.net/).

Founding Aspiration (http://aspirationtech.org), a nonprofit dedicated to new approaches in nonprofit software development.

Creating Capaciteria.org, a comprehensive, searchable online directory of administrative resources. It promotes peer review allowing members to comment on, rate and add individual resource links.

Articles/Manuals on technology, capacity, philanthropy and social entrepreneurship. (http://internautconsulting.com/articles.shtm)

Board Positions:
Aspiration, IFN, MDLF-CAMP, DNT Technologies, ITC Technologies, Media Rights, Soros Medical Internet & Library, Markle Foundation Advisory

Jonathan Peizer’s Specialties:

Over two decades of extensive experience in strategic planning, development, management, and successful execution of projects employing a broad variety of technologies. His forte involves identifying needs and applying the appropriate strategy and implementation solutions to meet them. He has significant experience working in international, cross-cultural environments in over 75 countries.
