Molly Logsdon's Summary

In 2004 I became an entrepreneur overnight. I knew I was not going to suit up for a job and provide the income to sustain the lifestyle I had become accustomed to. I didn't have any work credentials nor the experience necessary. I purchased real estate and put myself to work as foreman/general contractor and now have assets working for me and providing me monthly income.

I am a huge believer of multiple streams of income and network marketing, the only real way to accumulate wealth.

There are countless network marketing companies and I have selected what suits me best. I have had my own journey of success with this in itself and can help you determine your own path of success.

In addition to being a professional network marketer, I find my creative brain does not quiet easily. I am manic to pursue many directions I have not touched.

With my journey of life It is my intention to help others find their own personal and financial success.
