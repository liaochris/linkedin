Steve Donnelly's Summary

Extensive HR and Recruiting experience within high-growth, dynamic and environments with a strong focus on consumer internet and leading technology brands.

Currently, I am the head of HR and Recruiting for Interspire / BigCommerce, the fastest growing SaaS ecommerce platform in the world. Prior to Interspire, I worked for over five years at Match.com, most recently as Sr. Manager, HR & Recruitment. Before Match.com, I spent over four years at Travelocity.com, both as a Technical Recruiter and a Sr. HR Generalist. In addition, I’ve also recruited for the software development/consulting (ObjectSpace) and staffing industries (Object Systems Group).

Interspire is a global leader in e-commerce, content management and email marketing software. The company's on-demand offering, BigCommerce launched in August 2009 and since then has experienced 5000+% growth. Our mission is to be the #1 e-commerce provider in the world by creating and delivering software that changes people's lives.

Based in Austin, TX and Sydney, Australia we offer a fun, success-driven, harmonious culture that provides outstanding growth opportunities for smart, motivated, passionate people. If you are ready to share in our success and take your career to the next level by joining the BigCommerce team, then I'd love to hear from you.

Specialties

Recruiting, compensation, applicant tracking systems, interviewing, sourcing, corporate career websites, job boards, hiring process improvement, employee referral programs, executive recruiting, international recruiting (Asia and Europe) and recruitment advertising, performance management, employee relations.
