I am an IT professional with over 10 years of experience in a management capacity. I recently graduated from DePaul University with a degree in finance and a minor in Economics. I am ready to take my career to the next level.

I am looking for a position in a management or consulting capacity and would consider a business analyst position to gain the right experience. I bring a wealth of experience to the table in the form of managing IT operations and infrastructure.

I am fluid in evaluating and identifying solutions to cut costs and increase productivity. I have a propensity to leverage systems already in place to achieve their maximum potential and greatest ROI to improve efficiency and output. I have the innate ability to evaluate the value chain and design systems to enhance profits and output.

Notable past experiences include:

Played a major role on a team of consultants working to reduce costs for the City of Chicago’s IT department. Resulted in a reduction in $8MM in IT spend annually.

Increased firm billable hours and output by constructing a system that automatically generated standard documents from disparate data to cut down on redundant typing.

Increased firm efficiency by selling the idea of a Document Management System to replace an antiquated failing file storage structure system that made monitoring workflow much easier.

Decreased costs by effectively negotiating vendor contracts to yield the greatest value.

Started a company and was able to market and sell IT services and solutions to various clients in the Southwest Suburbs of Chicago.

Recently left a position to return to school full time to complete a Bachelor degree. In doing so, I was able to leverage my network to market and sell skills in order to generate consulting income while finishing school.
