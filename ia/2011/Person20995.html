Jim Orrison's Summary

I have been blessed to truly exercise the formal education that I acquired in each step of my career. From simply solving engineering problems for Mississippi Power field engineers with remote access software programs in the early '80s through to refining business strategies for an IT services company in a shrinking economy over the last eight months.

 Computer Engineer for twenty-four years
 MBA for fourteen years
 Managed five, multi-million dollar projects to successful completion
 Authored / co-authored business-technical alignment strategies for two Fortune 500 corporations, one publicly traded company, a regional health-care provider, a health-care practitioner and an industrial manufacturer
 Grown three different geographically-based operations from under 12 employees to over $5 million in revenue each
 Established a Business Intelligence practice and three Project Management offices
 Business Development Executive for just over two years; top company producer on the second year


I yearn to solve business problems first and foremost. Technology has always been the weapon for making radical changes. For the last fifteen years, my focus has been on understanding the underlying business problem, abstracting the issues and options, gaining consensus on solutions and then delegating the execution to see teams celebrate the successes that can be achieved. Whether as a consultant or as an organization leader, I have enjoyed the breadth of solving many unique problems that at the end, improve organizational performance, customer satisfaction and product quality.
