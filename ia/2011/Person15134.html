Frank Anderson's Summary

I've been working on campaigns in Virginia since 2005, including Tim Kaine for Governor, Jim Webb for Senate, and the Democratic takeover of the State Senate. In 2008 I organized students at Northern Virginia Community College to get out the vote for Barack Obama. I worked for Brian Moran for Governor in the 2009 Democratic primary, and supported Creigh Deeds in the general election. I'm currently Co-President of the College Democrats at George Mason University.

I run PA systems for special events. I also play and teach the drums. If you're interested in a drum lesson, go to burkedrumlessons.com.
