Dustin Jacobsen's Summary

Dustin Jacobsen, Vice President, Social Media & Technology at Barkley, leads the agency on digital, mobile, social and emerging technology projects. He works on a variety of clients including Sonic Drive-In, 24 Hour Fitness, Blue Bunny Ice Cream, Lee Jeans, H&R Block, Weight Watchers, L'Oréal Paris & many others.

Before Barkley, Dustin worked at several start-up technology and marketing companies, leading cross-functional business, software engineering and marketing teams.

Dustin is a sought after speaker on social and emerging technology projects, with client projects featured in various publications such as The New York Times, Wall Street Journal, Advertising Age and technology related publications such as MediaPost, TechCrunch, eMarketer, ReadWriteWeb and Mobile Marketer. In addition to speaking at social media clubs and various business organizations, Dustin is a featured technology expert on various media programs. He has presented on a variety of topics at events including ad:tech New York, MobileBeat, Mobile Insider Summit, iMedia Breakthrough Summit and others.

Dustin holds a BS from Iowa State University, including program participation at London Metropolitan University and an MBA, magna cum laude, from Baker University.

He blogs about all things marketing and technology at shakegently.com and is on Twitter as @dustinj.
