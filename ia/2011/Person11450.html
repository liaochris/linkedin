Matt Gambino's Summary

Matt Gambino is a professional speaker, trainer and author who helps salespeople deliver jargon-free presentations and product demonstrations. Matt is also vice president of marketing at Catapult Learning, where he presents educational services to school superintendents and other educators.

Matt is the author of "50 Minutes to Better Software Demos" (Crisp Publications, 2010).

Specialties

Speaking, sales and marketing training, executive leadership.
