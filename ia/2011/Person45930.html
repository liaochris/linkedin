Willie Brake’s Summary

Self motivated with proven determination and a professional skills to satisfy various customer requirements. Over 20 years comprehensive experience with business processes, hardware, software, and peripherals. Excellent skills in written and verbal communication, problem solving, teamwork, and customer relations. An analytical thinker and change-oriented team player utilized positions to gain business experience in procurement and supply, finance, information technology management, and manufacturing while managing current assignments and responsibilities.

Willie Brake, a certified computer professional with advanced degrees in Technology Management, leads his enterprise, All About Technology, Inc. as it continues to be an operational force in the marketplace. Prior to the creation of All About Technology, Willie spent more than 12 years holding increasingly responsible positions in the automobile, consulting, education and manufacturing industries in locations around the world. He is currently the Executive Director for the Center For Creative Arts, Inc. and a faculty member at The University of Phoenix and Wayne State University as well as a speaker for various organizations. Willie is also a member of various civic, community and philanthropy groups that include United Way and Service Corps of Retired Executives (SCORE).
