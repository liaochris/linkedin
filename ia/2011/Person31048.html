Michael Jackson | Bradford, Yorkshire's Summary

With 30 years of experience in the signage industry, I set up Scriptus in order to make sure our clients look great, turning concepts into reality. We work with corporate companies, retail stores, hospitality and leisure sectors, universities, and hotels. Managing the design, manufacture and installation of signs in-house, we are able to design and manufacture single bespoke signs through to implementing strategic wayfinding strategies.

We frequently work in tandem with architects to strengthen their offering to clients through our wayfinding consultancy, brand identity and graphic design services. This is something I am working to further develop over the next year to provide end-to-end solutions for our clients.

Projects I've managed to date include creating the wayfinding system for King Abdullah's International Gardens in Saudi Arabia, designing, manufacturing and installing the signs across the whole of Bradford University and working with numerous retail clients and restaurants including Heal's, The Wolseley and The Grosvenor House Hotel in London's Mayfair. I've also implemented corporate signage programmes for BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Natwest, McDonald's and Ford Motor Company throughout Europe.

Based in Bradford but with international clients, Scriptus is a leading innovator in the signage industry and prides itself on delivering exceptional customer service. We've recently expanded so I have more time to be on site, meeting clients and ensuring everything runs smoothly from the concept of a project to its completion. This will allow me to maintain these relationships to the highest possible standard.

Specialties

signs, sign design, signage systems, sign strategies, sign manufacture, sign installation, wayfinding, wayfinding strategy, wayfinding consultancy, wayfinding masterplans, wayfinding audits, graphic design, brand identity, bespoke signs.
