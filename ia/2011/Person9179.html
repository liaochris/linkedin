Constanze Hoffmann's Summary

Constanze Hoffmann (born 1964) is a trained Dry cleaner, though their keen perception and good organisation- and coordination talent, she has worked since 1988 at the company Ahrens-Textil-Service ( a german professional leasing clothing company- http://www.dbl-ahrens.de).
By wanderlust and passion for fast driving have the activities of the Conny Hoffmann, as she is known by all, changed 2003 in the motor sport industry,
Nuerburgring (http://www.nuerburgring.de) and Shanghai /China company Matter Motorsport Consulting (http://www.matter-motorsport.com)
In 2009 it was conquered by the Internet, where she also met Arno Fischbacher (http://www.arno-fischbacher.com), for which she has been working since January 2010 as webassistent for the expert network Stimme.at.
