Heather Barnett's Summary

Your customers learn what to expect from you when they read your content, so make sure it shines! I believe your message should represent the way you do business. It should inspire people choose you over your competitors because they're confident you're the right choice – even if you're a little more expensive. Don't just tell them what you do and hope they pick up the phone. You have to show them who you are and why you're the best in your business. I can help you do that.

Saying the right thing in the right way at the right time will make you more money. If you're worried professional copywriting is too expensive, don't be. It doesn't cost you a dime to get a quote. What's expensive is losing customers because your content compels them to click the back button.

***

I have over 10 years experience in freelancing writing for advertising (broadcast and print), content (articles, blogs, websites) and marketing and sales materials, including extensive experience in financial services, legal, and medical writing. I'm also the DFW-area CityGuide writer for SheKnows.com. I have clients all over the United States and abroad.

Connect with me here to request a quote or you can contact me on Twitter (@HireHeather) or Facebook (also HireHeather). Or check out my blog, Writing in Red Ink (link above).
