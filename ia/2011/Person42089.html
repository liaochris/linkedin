J. Robinson Group is a business consulting company based in Orlando, Florida, specializing in management, marketing and social media consulting. To achieve successful and profitable outcomes, we focus deeply on a company’s brand, operating culture and capacity for change.  

Our company was formed to help businesses grow and prosper and develop more meaningful relationships within their local communities. Our culture is fun, focused and results-oriented. Our people are creative, intelligent and exceptionally resourceful.
