Raymond Dale's Summary

I have been working with clients using Self-Directed IRA's for 20 years. My vision is to work with Attorney's, CPA's, Financial Advisors, Bankers , Real Estate and Mortgage Professionals useing Self-Directed IRA's/ 401k's to enhance client portfolios and solve financing issues. My Company will set up the Self-Directed accounts and assist in negotiations. We will monitor the accounts for what the IRS calls "prohibited acts." We work with many real estate brokers and mortgage lenders to identify deep discounted properties and notes. Brokers would also handle purchase, sale and management of Real Property. At turnover they will handle the sale. We will perform compliance review on transactions. My company would service the notes and manage or co-manage the IRA/401k partnerships or LLC's as the case may be. Financial Advisors are needed to help update clients Asset Allocation to enhance their retirement funds (or other capital) with Real Estate, Mortgages or private business investments . CPA's and Attorney's will help to create LLC's and give tax advice and IRA Custodians will handle the funds at preferential rates. We also use developers and builders to complete projects. The current unrest in the banking and financial services industries, a business sector facing a general liquidity crisis unlike anything in the past three decades. This has created one of the best buying oppoutunities in many decades and has resulted in the proliferation of Private Investments. The majority of these private “ deals” are hard to read and understand, let alone analyze the risk. However we have many years of experience in analysis , negotiations and regulatory compliance reviews.

Specialties

Real Estate and Business consluting to Investors, Banks, Law Firms, Mortgage Lenders, Realtors. We identify problems and recommend solutions. We match investors with opportunities.
