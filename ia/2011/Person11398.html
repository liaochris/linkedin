Deepika Chowdhry's Summary

Deepika Chowdhry is a reputed professional from the services and concepts industry. She is the founder of Candid India - a Sales/Marketing & PR Consultation company based in New Delhi- India.

With more than 14 years' experience of National & International Marketing and PR under her belt, Deepika uses her business administration skills with a panache, towards the successful culmination of ventures undertaken. She is mainly responsible for the business developement & promotion of various projects handled by Candid India.

Candid India is involved in the Indian Travel & Tourism industry, marketing tourism products, selling exhibition space to South Asian travel trade for Internationally renowned exhibitions like- IMEX, ILTM & ALTM etc.

Specialties

Candid India is a multitalented company with expertise in the field of sales & marketing, promotions, event coordination, hosted buyer programme, and association representation etc. We offer complete professional infrastructure for representation and promotion of trade exhibitions, DMC's, Hotel properties, Association administration. We specialise in International Business Promotion, Management Consultancy, Marketing Management, Financial planning, sales promotion management, Representation etc
