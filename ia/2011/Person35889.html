Rakesh Sidana's Summary

An Information Technology and management graduate with over 13 yrs experience. Worked with renowned web development co. IndiaMart in India & international organization London Business School to develop & market web applications before launching MeriCAR.com
--DETAILED PROFILE --
He started his career in 1997 with VSNL associate partner, as a marketing executive for selling internet products. He contributed to media for spreading awareness about internet by writing articles on web technologies in the Times Of India and IT magazine when Internet was not popular in India.

He has held executive positions in various web software companies like IndiaMart, Panalinks, 1E and was heading technical team of dotcom company which shut down during dotcom bust in 2002. He started his own consultancy in 2004 to help international companies for development and online marketing of web portals. Later in 2008, he formed his company named, Amoeba Webware Pvt. Ltd. to provide similar services in India.

His desire for new technologies and futuristic approach allows him to create awareness about unique concepts in India. MeriCAR.com is a product of similar thoughts.

He is a founder of MeriCAR.com, first-in-India, a dedicated car servicing web portal. He is associated with experts from automotive industry in India to help car owners to solve their car servicing and repair problems. He has been short listed for unique concept business plan/idea in different business events and appeared in CNBC TV18, ET Now channels for showcase of his startup venture. Recently in 2010, he is one of the finalist of The Power of Ideas (Initiative by The Economic Times, IIM and Department of Science and Technology)

Recently he is recognized at IIM Ahmedabad with 10 days session on startup business and holds a post graduation course in information systems management from All India Management Association (AIMA-CME), New Delhi and BSc (Hons). Rakesh is CEO of Amoeba Webware Pvt. Ltd. He is living in Gurgaon.

Specialties

automotive, software, car servicing, car workshops, website development, web consultancy, web marketing, web portals
