Artha Doyle's Summary

I'm an entrepreneur. Always have been.

I travel the globe on the look out for potential technologies, franchises and licenses that are suitable for the Indonesian market. Why? To either help protect the business we are in, strengthen it, or accelerate the company by injecting my experience combined with the vision to see the possibilities in the business.

It is my experience that image and first impression is what is so important in getting yourself in bed with the biggest companies in Indonesia. Of course, you still need to be able to deliver what you promise and that's what I strive to do.

I'm responsible for setting up more than 15 companies from the ground up in the last 5 years alone. This is no minor accomplishment. My responsibility covers all fields from initial due diligence, quality control, business setup, market research, licensing to eventually marketing, sales and management.
