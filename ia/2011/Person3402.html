Antony Waldorf’s Summary

Entrepreneur in the Internet and mobile sector with the main role of CEO and founder. Inspired Labs is my third company in the Internet sector. I sold my previous company Inspire Infrastructure for $22.5million to a US corporation.

I enjoy leading roles that require strategic and creative thinking. My unique strengths include the ability to marry a companies business opportunities with technology solutions. I believe this role is key to the success of an Internet business.

Antony Waldorf’s Experience

  • CEO and Founder

    Inspired Labs

    (Internet industry)

    November 2006 — Present (4 years 3 months)

    Inspired Labs has developed a semantic search platform to provide companies with online business intelligence.

    The platform provides companies the tools to understand, monitor, measure and manage their online marketing presence, through real-time analysis of the Internet. A core strength of the platform is its ability to derive semantic information from all sources of content, both static and real-time; news, blogs, forums, twitter, etc. The results is the quickest access to the most valued business intelligence.

    The platform was engineered over 3 years of research and development to meet the real challenges of business intelligence for today and the future. It is built upon our own cloud computing architecture, with the main distributed components including search, crawl and content analysis. The platform is the very highest grade in terms of performance and scalability, but also in terms of autonomic computing.

  • CEO and Founder

    Inspire Infrastructure

    (Computer Software industry)

    December 2000 — March 2006 (5 years 4 months)

    Inspire developed a technology platform for local search that provided services via Internet, Mobile, SMS, Speech recognition and PDA.

    The platform was designed around a pioneering approach that used clustered search technology. This strategy created significant advantage over existing competitors, and was accomplished because we took a long-term technology perspective to meet long-term market opportunities.

    The technology was integrated and sold to leading European IYP companies, and was deployed across 9 European markets and the US.
