David Pinkley’s Summary

The power of dreams. Connecting people. The fusing together of personal strengths and professional pursuits. My passion is for anything related to empowering humans to realize their potential.

My passion is put into action daily through my role as a custom resume writer (The Resume Sage) and executive recruiter/job search strategist (Executive Insight.)

The Resume Sage will critique your current resume (you'll LOVE this!), overhaul your old resume or write your first one. Since 1995 I've seen over 40,000 resumes, interviewed over 3,500 job seekers and have negotiated over $20,000,000 in compensation (guaranteed bonuses, equity incentives, relocations, counter-offers, COBRA benefits, work visas, non-competes, etc.). I've even placed three women during their third trimester of pregnancy! In short, I know how to help people get hired.

The professionals who hire The Resume Sage gain:
• Perspective on their competitive advantage (vs. peers)
• Increased confidence about their accomplishments
• Renewed enthusiasm for job hunting
• Stronger compensation packages
• Shorter job searches

Executive Insight is my couture (boutique is so overused!) search and job search strategy firm. Yes, we do headhunting but we are quality-over quantity. High touch. Highly customized. Those we don't recruit for, we offer quality job searching advice. We work with experienced professionals who are not fully appreciated by their current management and empower them to go find what they want (with confidence!).

I have lived and worked as a recruiter in New York, Chicago, Hong Kong, Raleigh and Charlotte, NC. I've recruited on behalf of privately-held companies and Fortune 500s, placing hundreds of professionals. My hallmark has always been a low rate of turnover among the people I've placed into new jobs.

Whether or not we ever have the opportunity to work together may you find the power of your dreams - realized!
