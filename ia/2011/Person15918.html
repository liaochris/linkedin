Gerard Michaels's Summary

After working in the micro chip market with Texas Instruments I decided to try my hand at the booming real estate market in Santa Cruz/Monterey California. While on a vacation to show my wife the Adams/York area of Pennsylvania we realized how much we really loved the area. While enjoying a successful real estate career in California, we decided to take a chance and make the move to Gettysburg. We settled in Cashtown and haven't looked back. We have been married for 32 years, have two grown successful children, one living here in Adams county, and one still living in Califronia. I continue to enjoy a successful real estate practice here in the Adams/York area and will be opening my own brokerage in the near future. I look forward to meeting and working with what always turns out to be new friends.

Working in the real estate profession is not a job for me, yes of course it has it's "days" but all in all I enjoy what I do for a living. Yes for a living. I don't practice real estate part time or on the side, I am a full time realtor who honeslty enjoys the profession. I can't tell you how many freinds I have made from past clients. Meeting people is part of the fun of real estate. It is a profession that requres contant learning and training to stay on top of everything that goes on the world of real estate. A good agent knows what is going on in the mortgage world, with the economy, both local and national. A good agent keeps thier eye on growing, hot trends, local and national legislation that affects real estate, it is a never ending cycle of information.

I can honestly say that I enjoy and look forward each new day, and how that days real estate activities are going to affect me, as well as my clients.

Specialties

Historical Properties, Eco Smart renovations and restorations. Designations include, GRI, York Savay
I have 10 + years of experience and can provide you with a professional, knowledgeable transaction.
