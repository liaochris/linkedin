Rebecca Lamperski's Summary

Rebecca Lamperski is an award winning salesperson and sales leader with 20 years of experience. She is a high energy, competitive, and determined individual who has a proven track record of success. Rebecca Lamperski joined the Comcast Spotlight sales management team in 1999, and has guided local sales efforts for the past 11 years. She is responsible for sales direction in the Pittsburgh, Wheeling, Youngstown and Johnstown/Altoona/State College Markets. In 2009, Rebecca was honored as CableFAX Magazine's Sales Person of the Year. In 2007, Rebecca was nominated and accepted as one of 45 Nationwide participants in Comcast’s Executive Leadership Forum Program – the elite leadership development program at Comcast (45 of 85,000 selected).

Rebecca is currently participating in Duquesne University's Professional Coach Certification Program.

Rebecca has written a book to help herself and others achieve their Life Dream. Her book is titled: Full Bloom; Planting the Seeds for Your Future.

Rebecca has 2 girls, Jessica and Jackie, ages 10 and 7, and a wonderful husband, Jamie, of 11 years.
