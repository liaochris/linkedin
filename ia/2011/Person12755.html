John Dudley’s Summary

Dudley Productions is a Washington, DC area based visual communications firm that provides full service, development-to-delivery solutions for a variety of corporate, marketing, political, public relations and non-profit advocacy clients, worldwide.

Whether it’s an ad spot or a corporate project with a high-end cinematic look, a fast paced, run-and-gun documentary style production or a live multi-camera switched event, Dudley Productions develops customized strategies that meet client budgets and timelines… and then executes.
