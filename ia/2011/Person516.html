    3. Public Relations
    Achievements included the generation of favourable publicity for HWT brands in the media and marketing trade press; representing HWT as a thought-leader in the marketing space through my personal B&T columns and AMI involvement.

    4. Talent Management
    I managed a team of six direct reports. Achievements included high staff engagement and low staff turnover; delivery of successful Personal Development Plans for each direct report; and a successfully-actioned succession plan for my deputy.

    5. Financial Management
    I managed a seven-figure marketing budget. Achievements included the initiation of numerous cost savings after reviewing the effectiveness of marketing activities; managing the budget to within a 1% favourable variance every year of my tenure.
