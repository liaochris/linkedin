Rob Waldman’s Summary

SUMMARY:

I'm a skilled specialist in numerous areas of the nutritional supplement & functional foods and beverage industries.

My credentials and my areas of accomplishment and expertise -- earned and proven during nearly 15 years in the industry -- are multiple. I am:

(1) an expert CERTIFIED REGULATORY AFFAIRS SPECIALIST in the health-care-product sector;

(2) a NUTRITIONAL SUPPLEMENT SPECIALIST -- certified by both the American Nutraceutical Association and the American Botanical Council -- knowledgeable in nutritional supplement and functional food and beverage ingredient formulation and product development issues;

(3) an experienced CORPORATE AND REGULATORY COUNSEL, specializing in the regulatory, legal and business affairs that affect the nutritional supplement and functional food and beverage industries; and

(4) a PROFESSIONAL CERTIFIED MARKETER and experienced marketing professional.


My relevant supplement and functional food and beverage related industry legal and regulatory work has included:

(i) advertising and claims substantiation;
(ii) regulatory interactions involving the FDA, FTC, National Advertising Division (NAD), and state Attorneys General;
(iii) FDA compliance issues -- including DSHEA, CGMP, AER, and product labeling regulations;
(iv) California Propositon 65 compliance issues;
(v) false advertising and fair-business-practice related issues;
(vi) patent and trademark searches, applications, registrations, and infringement issues;
(vii) licensing, supplier, distributor and manufacturing agreements; and
(viii) breach of contract and commercial disputes.
