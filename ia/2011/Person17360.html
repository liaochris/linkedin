Past: Long industrial career, as President and CEO of various companies with up to 1000 employees, owned by "blue chip" groups like VELUX and Skandinavish Holding.

Present: As entreprenuer and seed investor successfully founded more than 6 companies within the mobile internet, the service sector and the recycling business. Specialties
