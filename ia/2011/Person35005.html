John (J.J.) Williams’s Summary

I have worked on many different titles in my career so far. All in which have been a great experience in there own way. I have roughly about 7 years of game development experience. I am working for Wideload Games currently as a Game Designer. At Wideload I'm doing game design documentation, level design, creating new game play mechanics, gameplay scripting, conceptualizing characters, and writing . My goals are to put the best quality work into a project and let it speak for itself. I love being a game designer and it is truly my calling.

It has been a long road to get where I am today, but its been a great journey. I wouldn't want to be in any other industry.

When I'm not at work I'm working on my side projects as an Independent Iphone developer. The game is coming along and should be available this summer.
