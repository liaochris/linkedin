Jim Bennett’s Summary

Passionate business professional committed to bringing out the best in people through individual and business team coaching. I guide Senior Executives, Business Owners, Division and Department Head's, Project Managers and their teams to successful execution and implementation of their objectives.

Having those you trust leading the teams is important, but preparing them with the tools and guidance to maximize their potential is PRICELESS!

All organizations are progressing today through the excellent execution and implementation of important initiatives in their company's. It is not an option to under perform. Preparing yourself and other leaders you trust to meet or exceed expectations is of paramount importance - so prepare them. In an Investor's Business Daily report a "study of nearly 500 business and staffing experts found that 78% view coaching as a credible and effective way to enhance an exec's ability to drive the performance of the organization." They went on to say that; "Among respondents who calculate ROI, 77% believe that executive coaching provides a solid return.
They estimate levels ranging from a minimum of 100% ROI to a maximum of 500%."

Jim Bennett’s Specialties:

Business team and Executive coaching, project management consulting, specifically coaching individual leaders. Sr. Executive in distribution and transportation operations. Business process re-engineering, and manufacturing management.
