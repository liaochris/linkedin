Doron Vermaat’s Summary

I'm a career-broker for people with superior talent in Media, Digital, Marketing and Advertising. Originally from the Netherlands I've been in Hong Kong since 2006 and currently working with ConnectedGroup, a cross regional recruitment business spanning Asia Pacific and the Middle East. Before my career in recruitment I co-founded and worked for some funky online businesses (tops and some flops since 2004).

I'm an early adopter of social media, mobile and everything that's tech related and have a strong personal network within Greater China's digital, tech and mobile community.

When not online, I enjoy hiking with my dog Pepper, squash, traveling, street art and Japanese pizza commercials.
