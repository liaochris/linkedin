Dusanka Badovinac's Summary

My art is me, everything I see or feel.
Creating balance in power and sensitivity in my art is my never ending challenge. Therefore I use knifes and fingers when working with oils, which I like mostly because of their smell, or hammer (no machine) when carving in stone.
Changing of medium and subject is good for my restless personality and gives me the feeling of no boundaries.
My goal is always to capture the feeling, to feel it intense and share it with others.
The joy and passion for art I am sharing nowadays with my students and that became my new source of inspiration.
