Allison Adams's Summary

I am a Senior Account Executive in the New York office of Fleishman-Hillard, where I work as part of the FHNY Healthcare team.

Before joining Fleishman-Hillard, I worked at Novartis Pharmaceuticals in Global Oncology Affairs.

I have a Bachelor of Science degree in Newspaper Journalism from The S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications at Syracuse University.

Specialties

Healthcare, Digital, Social Media, Online Editorial Outreach, Online and Social Media Metrics, Women's and gender issues
