liz wendling's Summary

Sell with confidence! We offer a unique approach into your selling style AND your customer’s buying style. Women learn to understand, analyze and adapt to the buying style of the customer and fearlessly step into the role of the salesperson for their business.

Specializing in women entrepreneurs, who started a business because they had a particular passion, talent, skill or ability; not because they like selling. Then discovered they had to sell to keep their business profitable. Like it or not, sales is the only way to generate revenue. Selling with anxiety, uncertainty and fear will sabotage efforts and keep women from reaching the goals, dreams and the success they deserve.

Specialties

Sales Behavior Specialist, Color Code Trainer and Sales Consultant. Working with sales people and organizations to increase sales by building meaningful relationships, developing individuals and teaching people how to exceed their sales expectations.
Now more than ever sales people have to sell to customers the way their customer buys.
