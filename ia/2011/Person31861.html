Nacie Carson’s Summary

Nacie is a Personal Development Specialist and writer who focuses in career transition, goal setting, productivity, authenticity and entrepreneurialism.

In 2008, she launched The Life Uncommon (http://www.thelifeuncommon.net), a personal development site geared to helping individuals achieve a career and lifestyle that is authentic and successful. Today, it is one of the most popular personal development websites online.

In 2010, Nacie launched The New Opportunists (http://www.newopportunists.com), a comprehensive resource to help young unemployed professionals, under-employed professionals, and upcoming graduates keep their careers and lives moving forward in spite of unexpected job market conditions. A companion book was launched May 1 in digital form.

Nacie also works as a curriculum architect for several executive leadership development companies based in Boston.

In addition to her personal development work, Nacie has experience as an online entrepreneur building and selling websites and content. Her work has been featured in two editions of Chicken Soup for the Soul, and she has been quoted in over a hundred online sources, including WalletPop.com. Nacie has also been interviewed by Gaebler Online for their entrepreneur feature and has appeared as a guest on Blog Talk Radio and other online radio programs.

Nacie received her BA in History from the College of the Holy Cross, where she graduated magna cum laude. She is a member of Phi Beta Kappa.
