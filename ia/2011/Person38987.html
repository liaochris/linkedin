Sascha Kronberg's Summary

A veteran in sales- and sales management, Sascha Kronberg currently serves   as Country Director responsible for Germany. Prior to joining ReachLocal, Sascha held several positions in the UK, Germany and Eastern Europe, including planning, opening and heading of new offices in Budapest, Leipzig and Frankfurt (Main), managing 50 sales professionals and -managers. His core responsibilities and specialties were locating new profitable markets, opening new business units and turning them into profitable operations from scratch. As a member of the board of the German Holding at Regus Business Centers, he later headed up the South region of Germany, responsible for 10 serviced offices in Munich, Nuremberg, Stuttgart & Heidelberg.   Sascha was co-founder and Managing Director of a Munich and Budapest based business event organization, specializing in niche industry events in Eastern Europe and sales trainings across entire EMEA.   Sascha started his sales career at BMW in Berlin, selling to large corporations, SMEs and private individuals. Sascha is known for cutting edge sales- and customer relationship training, specializing in buying motives as well as relationship sales. He is an expert in relationship building and sales by phone, as well as in face-to-face business meetings. On the side he is coaching and mentoring sales professionals in various industries.

Specialties

Sascha is known for his charismatic leadership capabilities, motivation and “do as I do“ mentality. His strengths are relationship sales by phone (cold call) and face-to-face sales, building strong and long lasting relationships. His network among the IT- and Telco industries reflect his personal interest in mobile computing.
