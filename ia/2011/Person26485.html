Lisa Dawson’s Summary

I began my technology career in 1994, dipping my toes into Internet application development before focusing on program management and team leadership. Nothing makes me happier professionally than identifying business challenges, finding the right balance of people, process and technology, and wrapping them up in solutions that stay true to an organization’s culture.

To fuel my passion for community and women’s health issues, I serve on the Advisory Board for the local affiliate of breast cancer powerhouse Susan G. Komen for the Cure (I tweet for them, too: @KomenPugetSound) and the Alumni Leadership Committee of Leadership Tomorrow Seattle.
