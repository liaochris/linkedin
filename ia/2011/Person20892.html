Jill Whalen's Summary

As CEO of High Rankings since 1995, I pretty much live, breathe and eat search engine optimization (SEO).

I most enjoy (and am successful at) working with start-ups or other companies who are planning their new website. Helping out at that stage so that all the technical aspects of SEO are baked into the design from the start is a huge piece of the SEO puzzle these days.

Jill Whalen's Experience

SEMNE

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry

November 2006 – Present (4 years 5 months)

SEMNE’s mission is to provide networking and educational opportunities for search marketers in Massachusetts, Connecticut, Rhode Island, New Hampshire, Vermont and Maine, while having a little fun along the way.

Unlike national search marketing organizations, SEMNE provides you with a chance to meet with your peers on a regular basis in person.

Members and non-members alike are invited to attend SEMNE events every other month at local venues around New England. Events have specific topics and speakers, as well as have a social networking aspect to them.
