Stefan Russwurm, MD/PhD’s Summary

Multi-functional life sciences executive with 18 years of combined experience in academia and industry and proven record of management leadership in developing CE compliant, PCR- & biochip based diagnostics in the field of clinical microbiology and immune monitoring. MD/PhD with special training in anesthesiology with focus on Critical Care Medicine.

-Biomarker development
