Paul McCaffrey's Summary

Professional Bio
Paul McCaffrey

Executive level leader/recruiter interested in building and executing global recruiting solutions. Expert twenty years of full lifecycle retained/contingent/temporary/corporate recruiting experience. Ten years of managing and training recruitment teams with average staff sizes of 6-10. Extensive US and international based recruitment experience. Collaborative/Influence relationship building experience across large population segments and functions. Highly effective employer of behavioral interviewing techniques, sourcing of hard to find skills, negotiating complete and sophisticated compensation packages. Excellent multi-tasking skills and highly customer focused. Known as a ‘can-do’ entrepreneurial spirit.

Paul joined Thomson Reuters from IQ Search Group, where he was Founder and Principal in charge of operations, recruiting and client relationship management. He has been providing staffing solutions and career guidance in the Financial Industry for both Technology and Business communities for the past 20 years. His primary focus has been in financial services markets, including buy and sell side financial firms as well as the vendors that service them.

Paul has recruited for established Wall Street firms and start-ups; including placement into investment banking, capital markets, commercial banking, financial information providers, portfolio management, IM related research positions, trading systems, fixed income, equities, and FX within both the buy-side and sell-side communities.

Paul’s experience and approach has been as a professional staffing consultant; focused on client requirements gathering and providing a highly engaged service, within a professional framework (in accord with the Association of Executive Search Consultant’s – AESC Code of Conduct). Paul is a graduate of the University of Buffalo with a BS in Finance and Marketing and a minor in Computer Science.
