Berk Kirca's Summary

Berk has gained valuable experience in the financial services and TMT sectors as a fellow intern at McKinsey & Co in due diligence and strategic review projects, as an intern at Europe's leading Private Equity Investor 3i in their German Venture Team, and as a part-time analyst for several M&A and fundraising projects at the corporate finance advisory firm Cartagena Capital, Munich. Previously he has had practical experience in high-tech research and telecommunications software development at the Fraunhofer Institute, Dresden and at Argela Technologies, Istanbul as an intern.

Berk holds a M.Sc. degree in Communications Engineering from Technische Universität München, Germany and a B.Sc. degree in Electrical & Electronics Engineering from Koc University, Turkey, both with distinction.

He is fluent in English, German and Turkish.

Specialties

- M&A, Financial Modeling, Business Valuation, Technology Assessment,
- Information Theory, Communication Theory, Probabilistics, Statistics
