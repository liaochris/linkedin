Marc Beacom's Summary

Marc Beacom, MCTS - SQL Server 2008 BI and Development, has over 15 years of technical and business experience in a wide array of industries. He is currently, Principal Architect and Founder of DawaBI where he focuses on Making Data-Driven Decisions Possible by delivering data warehouse and business intelligence solutions. Marc is and has been an active member in the SQL Server community and currently leads the Denver SQL Server UG. Marc also spent a year volunteering for PASS as a regional mentor.
