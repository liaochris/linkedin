Chris Gunther’s Summary

Having spent over 20 years in the professional film production industry I have built a wealth of contacts, business management, and technical knowledge. I have worked my way through the ranks from production assistant, to production manager, to producer and executive producer. I spent many years at the Partners Film Company, the largest commercial production company in the country. I have had huge success manging micro budgets to massive epoch productions.

My vast production knowledge allows my client base to achieve the highest production standard without compromise for the best value. Technology has changed things in amazing ways. One example of this is that clients no longer need to leave their office or home to view, supervise or approve a video production. It is now done all on line. What used to require a large team of people to produce a project, can now be done on a much smaller scale in less time with the highest level of production standards always provided.

Through Trailer Park my team builds valued client relationships by delivering the expected results. It is more than just providing a video that looks good. Expected results means we meet deadlines, there are no added costs, the client is able to make changes that won't break the bank because we always have a solution, we understand how to handle confidential material, the importance of our clients brand and how it is presented on everything. We have so many little tricks to make ordinary look spectacular, I couldn't list them here. To provide all of these things, and to maintain a valued client takes years and years of experience. You can never cut corners, but there are always ways to save money. That is the secret...years and years of know how.
