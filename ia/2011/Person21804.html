John Lundholm’s Summary

I originate and write home loans in all 50 states - purchase, refi, home equity, condo / co-op / single-family / multi-family / new construction, Fannie / Freddie / FHA / Jumbo / Super Jumbo loans. Specialty team giving excellent responsiveness, quick turnaround, and great rates, all at one of the largest financial institutions in the country.

I am based in Manhattan, so a large portion of my clients are tri-state (NY/NJ/CT), but I also do considerable amounts of lending throughout the rest of the country (CA, FL, GA, WA, MA, etc).
