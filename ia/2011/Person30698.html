Mike Mann’s Summary

I have over 13 years of experience in the entertainment industry, focusing primarily on the creative side of production in roles ranging from animator to lead artist to development director.

My biggest strength is my creativity, both on a concept level and in my team leadership. I don't believe in a strict, by the book approach in the development of interactive entertainment, and that the only way to make a game that is fun is to have fun making the game.
