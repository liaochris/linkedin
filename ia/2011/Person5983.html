Bravo Valenti's Summary

My global team of graphic and web designers, writers, animators, music producers and game developers have proven track records working with leading brands.

Over 25 years of business and media-related experience for you to tap into.

My goal for 2009-2010 and beyond:

Bravo'sWorld

A new online children's social network- where books books become games, games become a world of discovery and kids rule the world!

Designed and built by the world's leading online game developers, Bravo'sWorld is based on contemporary characters featured in hilarious stories with wild wordplay, brilliant graphics and animation. Offering kids of all ages countless multi-player and mini-games, quests, adventures, contests and more, Bravo'sWorld will touch kids' lives both online and off.

Bravo'sWorld is created for a generation that is smarter than we think...and deserves much more than "virtual pets."

Feel free to contact me to discover how you, your business, non-profit organization, charity or NGO, school or educational institution, family, friends and kids can all be a part of it.

Specialties

Creating books for children packed with wordplay, featuring contemporary kids in kooky situations and illustrated by cutting-edge artists.

Working with game developers, animators and social network designers to build a digital amusement park and online adventure for kids, unlike any other.
