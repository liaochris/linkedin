Adrian Bravo’s Summary

Adrian brings to Google over 16 years of industry experience, once providing technology and business consultancy to top Financial, Tech and Telco firms then leading digital/direct communications as CEO of top Marketing Agencies in Mexico. To date, he has more than seven years of executive experience creating digital/dialog consumer experiences for the Latin American region.

Prior joining Google, Adrian served for 7 years as CEO of top WPP digital/direct agencies in Mexico, working with regional clients such as: Movistar, Citibank, Ford, Nokia, Diageo, Coca Cola, Nestlé, American Express, Axtel among others. On the consulting side, he played different roles on Microsoft and Oracle providing business and technology knowledge mainly to the financial and telecommunications sector, between that he was in charge of setup and grow E.piphany subsidiary for the Mexican market offering CRM software solutions.

Adrian holds two master degree’s one MBA from ITAM and other from the Ecole nationale supérieure des Télécommunications de Bretagne on enterprise systems management.

Recently he was named mentor for Endeavor México, helping innovative entrepreneurial efforts to grow and position within the market as member of the board for some of its affiliates.

On the personal side he loves to be within family and motocross.
