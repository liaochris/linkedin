Davina K. Brewer's Summary

As a freelance marketing professional, Davina K. Brewer (that's me) is your communications partner.

I serve small business clients as a Public Relations Director or Marketing Communications Manager. With my experience in PR, marketing, design and events–and an eye on the bottom line–I help you integrate your campaigns to get the best return on your marketing investments.
