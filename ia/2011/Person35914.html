Ralph Poole’s Summary

Ralph Poole is a respected business leader, management consultant and accomplished executive. He has extensive experience leading Knowledge Management, Text Analysis, Business Intelligence, Content Management, Information Architecture, IT Strategy and Innovation consulting projects in global companies. He has pioneered the application of Lean Thinking to improvement of knowledge work. His approach is analytic and fact-based with a strong emphasis on enabling performance improvement.

Mr. Poole held senior executive positions in four of the world's premier management consulting and IT consulting firms: Boston Consulting Group, Bain & Company, Ernst &Young LLP, and Capgemini (a French IT consultancy). In this capacity, Ralph built multidisciplinary teams across several continents and was responsible for radically improving global learning and development, knowledge management, and software development methodologies.

Ralph is an expert at managing change in large geographically dispersed global organizations. When dealing with people in organizations, changing technology and work practices requires the same planning and problem solving discipline as technical and software engineering. Ralph has managed projects that required transformational change in the work practices thousands of people with this approach.

Ralph’s work has been documented in the Harvard Business Review, CIO and Fortune Magazine. He presents at conferences and frequently writes and comments on knowledge sharing technologies, text analysis, business intelligence, taxonomy and metadata strategies, the Semantic Web, Enterprise 2.0, and collaborative problem solving.

Ralph is an Engagement Director at Iknow, LLC where his work focuses on semantic enrichment of content, text mining, text analytics, business intelligence, business analytics and business intelligence.

Ralph Poole’s Specialties:

Knowledge Management, Text Analytics, Predictive Analytics, Sentiment Analysis, Taxonomy and Metadata Strategies, Business Intelligence, Innovation, Creativity, Executive Education, "Idea to Revenue", New Product Development
