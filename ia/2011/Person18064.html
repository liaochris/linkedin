Angela Hill’s Summary

Since 1992, I have worked in the fields of design, marketing and branding in St. Louis, San Francisco and San Diego. In 2004, I started my firm Incitrio and am the president and creative director. We work with innovative, growing companies looking to take their positioning and revenue to the next level.

Visit our home page at www.incitrio.com to see statistics regarding the revenue and ROI we have been able to generate for our clients. It all starts with our innovative holistic branding process of fully integrated print+online marketing solutions.

Contact startfresh@incitrio.com or send me a message via Linkedin to learn more.

Our services include:
BRANDING - strategy and analysis, personality, promise, positioning, performance, blue ocean competitive analysis, business/marketing plans, one-on-one coaching

GRAPHIC DESIGN - identity, collateral, catalogs, annual reports, tradeshow, packaging, print ads, direct mail, etc.

WEBSITE DESIGN - design & programming, e-commerce, intranet, customized wordpress

SEO - on-site SEO, ux design, site analysis for Acquisition, Conversion or Retention, competitor site analysis, keyword research, keyword strategy, on-site execution

SEM - off-site SEO, blogging, press release optimization, article distribution, link building

PPC - Adwords campaign creation and refinement, landing pages, product or vertical specific campaigns, keyword strategy, geo-targeting, behavioral targeting

EMAIL NEWSLETTERS - self serve and full serve complete back-end email technology/system, email newsletter design and programming, email marketing strategy, list scrubbing, dynamic segmentation, reporting

SOCIAL MEDIA - strategy and campaign building using integrated tactics such as: video, animation, imagery, contests, Linkedin, Facebook, Twitter, YouTube, Flickr and more
