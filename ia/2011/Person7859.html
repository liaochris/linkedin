Chaya (Anne) Cohen's Summary

A native speaker of both English and Hebrew (English mother tongue), with promotional writing and translation experience of several years, using my knowledge of Social and Community Work in the promotion of voluntary societies. In addition I am a graphic designer and DTP - I love designing book covers but also do business cards, logos and ad design. Being a creative person, I've always preferred work which demands creativity and change and above all, the need to think and achieve.
