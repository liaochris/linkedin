Kevin R. Johnston, CMP’s Summary

25 plus years has taught me much from my mistakes. 15 years in corporate marketing, 4 as an association exec and countless years as an entrepreneur designing & managing thousands of programs globally. Not many people in the industry I don't know or that don't know me.

- Founder and CEO of Advantage Event Group, an Atlanta based full service procurement | programming | production firm specializing in large scale meeting site selection/procurement and production.

- Managing Partner of Med Prof Ed, LLC, an international professional education firm delivering continuing medical education for Dermatological and Plastic Surgeons.

- Extensive experience in the development and design & redesign of new and existing conferences, trade shows & events that deliver stronger financial results.

- Have been heralded as a "Black belt Negotiator" due to the ability to call on an extensive network of hospitality and industry contacts to accomplish client goals and objectives.

- An active member of professional organizations including more than 20 years in Meeting Professionals International (MPI), Green Meeting Industry Council (GMIC), International Airlines Travel Agent Network (IATAN), Professional Convention Management Association (PCMA).

- Former International Board Member of Meetings Professional International (MPI).

- Have held and maintained the Certified Meeting Professional (CMP) designation since 1991.

- Awarded National Speakers Association (NSA) Planner Partner of the Year Award, MPI Planner of the Year Award.

- Featured | Quoted | Published in a variety of publications including the New York Times, Wall Street Journal, Atlanta Business Chronicle, Smart Meetings Magazine, PYM Magazine, Successful Meetings.
