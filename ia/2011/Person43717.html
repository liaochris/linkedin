Todd Williams’s Summary

Todd F. Williams, Esq. is the VP of Sales and Marketing for WNC Capital Corp., where he oversees product development, branding, marketing, retail sales and key accounts. WNC is the 17th largest owner of apartments in the United States according to The National Multi-Family Housing Counsel. We focus on Section 42 Low Income Housing Tax Credit (LIHTC) Investments and raise capital from both institutional and individual investors. WNC has been providing tax advantaged investment solutions since 1971 and since that time has syndicated over 190 funds and has acquired over $4.5 billion in real estate on behalf of more than 19,200 investors, including 21 Fortune 500 companies.

We have executed selling agreements with over 168 Independent Broker/Dealers and currently have 6 Reg. D Private Placements in the marketplace

Mr. Williams experience includes a position as the Chief Marketing Officer for Argus Realty Investors a leading Sponsor of Securitized Real Estate Investments. During his tenure at Argus the firm syndicated over $1.5 Billion in commercial real estate and raised over $550 Million in equity in over 40 seperate DPP offerings. Mr. Williams was responsible for all aspects of Sales & Marketing for the firm, including branding, graphic design, advertising, sales & marketing strategy, key accounts and product development.

Mr. Williams is an author, frequent speaker and panel expert on the subject of 1031 exchanges and DPP products and conducts continuing education seminars for Attorneys, CPAs and Real Estate Professionals. Mr. Williams has a diverse background, which includes being a founding partner in the San Diego Bankruptcy Law Firm (www.gobksandiego.com) and The Law Offices of Rojo, Williams, Schlegel and Moyers, where his practice included real estate, civil and criminal Litigation as well as corporate and business formation.
