Tito Melega, tito@titomelega.com's Summary

Tito is an award-wining advertising Creative Director, Art Director, Director and Creative Consultant.

In his position as Creative Director, Tito consistently helps brands connect with consumers and has a keen understanding of what’s relevant to today’s audiences. Utmost is Tito’s strong dedication to his clients by delivering online content that is entertaining, engaging and memorable.

Tito’s experience runs the gamut in almost every brand category. Throughout his career, he has worked on the world’s most recognizable brands some of which are: Lexus, Acura, Toyota, Pepsi, United Airlines, Cisco Systems, DirecTV, Miller Lite, Holiday Inn, Clairol, Kodak, and Beringer Wines.

Tito's innovative national advertising campaign for Toyota’s 3rd Generation 2010 Prius is one of the most talked about automotive campaigns of the year and continues to receive accolades from both the viewing public and trades.

Examples of Tito's work can be viewed at titomelega.com.

Tito currently lives in Los Angeles, California
