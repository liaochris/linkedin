Steve Mast’s Summary

Steve is fascinated by the way people interact with the world around them!
Keen eye for design, culture and understanding of technology stem from a uniquely diverse background. I have been referred to as the “Digital Architect” due to my education as an architect. However, I began my career as a Video Game Designer and Producer in the early 90’s.

In 2000, I joined Delvinia to build its interactive design and digital marketing practice. Our focus has been creating an environment where my team and clients can play, learn and grow together has been pivotal in creating Delvinia’s list of distinguished clientele. RBC Royal Bank, Scotiabank International, Manulife, Microsoft, Rogers, Bell, Random House, Richard Ivey School of Business to name a few.

I love BIG challenges and my philosophy is simple, success is measured by what you do and not what you say. When I have a minute away from work I enjoy volunteering my time by chairing councils and committees, speaking at various industry events but most of all I enjoy being active in my local community.

Steve Mast’s Specialties:

Passionate business builder; committed to helping individuals and businesses better understand the power and potential of interactive, digital marketing and new technologies. Strong management and interpersonal abilities provide solid leadership skills. Experienced in the following industries and disciplines: Financial Services, Government, Not-for-Profit, Media Communications, Technology, Advertising, Direct Response Marketing and Market Research.
