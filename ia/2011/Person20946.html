Jim Hyre’s Summary

Jim is the managing principal of Hyre & Associates Wealth Strategies, an independent firm of Raymond James Financial Services which he opened in 2005. Experienced at building teams in both the wirehouse and independent channels. 15 years of progressive track record of providing financial solutions to ultra high net worth clients utilizing a broad array of financial and investment products. High energy commitment to maintaining an advanced degree of technical knowledge along with an unwavering commitment to integrity and work ethic.

-CFP® CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™
-FINRA Licenses 7, 9, 10, 31, 63, 65
-Life, Accident, Health & Variable Annuity Licensed
