Ann Brine FCIM Chartered Marketer's Summary

With over 15 years of strategic and tactical sales and marketing experience, gained both from an agency and client-side perspective, I can offer an impartial view of communication across the whole marketing mix from experience with a broad spectrum of industries and sectors including IT/High-Tech, Aerospace, Defence and Travel.

As one the UK’s most talented and highly qualified marketers (a Fellow of the CIM), my key strengths include an excellent knowledge of through the line principles and practices, including advanced e-marketing skills, social media as well as new business development and relationship marketing experience.

Specialties

Travel, Aerospace, IT and Professional Services. Full Service Marketing, E-Marketing, Social Media Public Relations Specialist.

The Chartered Institute of Marketing is the leading international body for marketing training and professional development. As the world’s largest organisation for professional marketers we play a key role in training, developing and representing our profession.

We deliver accredited, practice-based qualifications through our UK and international study centre network. And we offer industry-proven marketing and sales training courses.

Increasingly recognised by the UK government as the voice of marketing, we set standards within the industry and are the first port of call for marketing information, knowledge and insight.

The Aerospace Resources marketing practice focuses on providing highly cost-effective solutions to a range of marketing challenges, concentrating on practical answers to clients' needs and employing the latest in technology driven, low cost marketing solutions.
