Gary Birk's Summary

My professional life is focused on engaging cutting-edge sustainable green technologies that help environmental consulting companies restore or remediate groundwater and soil at challenging sites associated with Fortune 500 companies, the U.S. Departments of Energy and Defense, and NASA. I work extensively in the field of bioremediation and environmental consulting on soil and groundwater assessments and cleanups. My experience includes two decades as an environmental contractor and consultant focused on project management of multiphase, multidisciplinary environmental design and construction projects, predominantly for industry and utilities. My current focus is on development and deployment of sustainable green chemistry for in situ bioremediation and have worked on over 200 enhanced reductive declorination projects in North America, Europe and Africa. I authored the EOS® Design Tools. This work has advanced the state-of-the-practice in estimating substrate application rates for in situ bioremediation.

I earned my bachelor’s degree in chemical engineering from North Carolina State University and hold registrations as a Professional Engineer in North Carolina, Virginia and Florida. I live in Wake Forest, NC with my wife Sally and our Maine Coon cat Gypsy Rose.
