Raj Lahoti’s Summary

I am a User Experience guy and a Creative Genius.

I take a blank sheet of paper approach to life and business. Why pigeon-hole yourself in past decisions that aren't serving you anymore. Start with a fresh paper, write down how you'd do things now if you could start over. You might realize you are already doing it, and that's always fun.

I love to sell, market, and Design businesses. The keyword is Design... - I'm really good in Microsoft Paint - forget visio and photoshop. I take things that I see online that I think are cool, and then cut, paste, and inno-vate. That's how I build my sites and companies, and it has served me well so far. Designing businesses from a true UX perspective has lead me into some of the most innovative sales strategies, marketing plans, and site/product features. It's truly fun, and you should try it some time.

I love meeting people with a shared perspective on life and business? What's that perspective? If you are passionate, enjoy what you do, and have a thirst and hunger for kicking ass and making things better, then you are my kind of people. And if you don't have any of those traits, but are searching for those out of life, then you are just honest... and thats even better.

Be your best and realizing that YOU are #1. If you want to help the world, start with yourself. Remember, you got to take care of yourself first if you want to be available for others. Thats my mantra at least, and who knows, may be it will work for you too.
