Travis Stoliker's Summary

Experienced Marketing Director with a skill for innovation and continual improvement.

I am the Sales & Marketing Director for Liquid Web Inc. My day to day activities include AdWords management, marketing strategy, sales management, brand/creative management, website writing, press release writing and new media advertising through Twitter, Facebook and other avenues. At Liquid Web, working with my team, I execute Trade Shows, manage all company websites, developed the customer newsletter, hired and trained the first sales department, established a Microsoft partnership & quadrupedal sales, hired over a hundred new employees and consistently break monthly revenue goals.

Liquid Web has been honored as an INC Magazine fastest growing company every year since I started at the company. I executed several corporate events which received television, radio and newspaper coverage. The Michigan Governor (Granholm), house representatives, local politicians and media attended these events.

In 2010 I helped Liquid Web design, research, market and launch their own proprietary cloud computing platform built on Perl called Storm On Demand.

In addition to my marketing activities, I am also a director and business manager focused on continual business improvement and strategic planning. I worked with our support managers and the whole company to reduce our support times (email response average from 2 hours to 10 minutes) and improve our overall support quality. I rewrote the employee handbook, formalized the employee review process and created specialized departments to improve efficiency and consistency.

While I was a Product Manager at TechSmith (Makers of Snagit, Jing, & Camtasia):
I was responsible for product feature requests, Marketing Requirements Documentation and working with a team of engineers to translate customer feedback into product innovations. To understand our customers, I traveled globally to meet directly with our customers and partners.
