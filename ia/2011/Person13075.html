Ed Roberson, MBA, BSME's Summary

Ed Roberson is a veteran sales and marketing professional with an on-going commitment to the principles of Kaizen (continuous incremental improvement in all aspects of his life).

He has extensive experience working with, in and for Chambers of Commerce ranging from small local and ethnic Chambers up to and including large Metro Area, State, National and International Chambers of Commerce. He has served in roles including Chairman of the Board of Directors, Vice President of various Committess and Ambassadors groups as well as consulting roles to numerous Chamber functions.

Ed is also a business and personal consultant with over 20 years of experience and literally hundreds of projects completed. He is also a seasoned networker seeking to expand his personal value and the value of others by constantly exercising his vast professional network. Ed Roberson is a trainer, coach, mentor, father, son, husband, friend and a lifelong student.

Ed Roberson's most current professional experiences (in addition to an on-going commitment to nurturing a world class business network) involve the succesful development of one of the top producing sales teams in the country.
