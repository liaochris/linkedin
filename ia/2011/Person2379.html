Andrea Duchon’s Summary

For the past year, I've been focused on building a successful freelance editorial career through clients such as AOL Travel, Orbitz Travel, Seattle Weekly and Thrillist.

From 2007 - 2010, I worked in NY as a Senior Mobile Strategist at MobileBehavior, a start-up company specializing in the strategy, development and execution of social media and mobile campaigns.

Before joining MobileBehavior, I worked as a Senior Account Executive at Fleishman-Hillard where I honed skills in the online/digital space through blogger and community outreach as well as B2C interactions.

My experience with both companies has allowed me to grow my professional network to include big name clients like AT&T and Ernst & Young as well as community and niche organizations like FADER Magazine.

As I plan my next move, I would love to combine my recent editorial experience with my passion for digital and mobile. I am looking for either a contract or full-time position.
