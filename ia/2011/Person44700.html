Valini Dsouza's Summary

I began my career path at the age of 18 by choosing to be a part of the then $ 712 billion BPO Industry, beginning with Aditya Birla Transworks and then moving to Intelenet Global Services. My role was an exciting one that went beyond mere operations. Besides being highly involved in preparing modules on Process & Voice & Accent Training, I was also part of employee relations heading various programmes on employee motivation in the organization.

In 2005, my passion for writing bagged me a prestigious project on copywriting and content development for eBay.com and that's when I chose to transition into Public Relations.
I started my PR career with Image Inc, part of Asia's largest PR Firm Perfect Relations, having handled Corporate PR for some of the largest names in the booming Infrastructure industry in India. Wanting to dabble in Brand PR, I then moved on to one of the most reputed and respected PR & Public Affairs Consulting Firm - Genesis Burson Marsteller.

Since May 2008, I have been with People Interactive (I) Pvt. Ltd. heading Corporate Communications for its flagship brand - Shaadi.com.

Having an agency experience of 3 years, I do understand that Corporate Communications is a different ball game altogether and an extremely challenging and exciting experience. I work closely with the business and functional heads of our organization in strategizing and executing our PR & Communication campaigns and ensuring that they are well-aligned with the broad objectives of the brand worldwide.

With a total work experience of 7 years, I have a better understanding of corporate culture and have much to offer in terms of innovative out-of-the-box ideas. It makes me proud to be associated with People Group - an organization that appreciates young upcoming talent and completely believes in the power of PR.

Specialties

Internal & External Communications, Newsletter Content Development, Website Management, Media Relations, Vendor Management, Budgeting & Strategic Planning.
