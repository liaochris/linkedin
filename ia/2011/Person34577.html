Pete Fellows’s Summary

I am the Managing Director of Fellows and Associates. We are a professional services sector recruitment and management consultancy focused on Intellectual Property worldwide.

I have a great deal of experience recruiting Patent Attorneys, Trade Mark Attorneys, IP Lawyers and IP Professionals both within the corporate environment and for private practices. I also have business strategy and recruitment advertising expertise particularly in the use of new media.

Throughout my career I have worked on large scale projects (such as the recruitment of 250 staff for a large consumer finance business in Melbourne Australia), smaller bespoke work (numerous placements of Partners and Senior Management positions) and complex deals such as business acquisitions.

I have recruited positions in Europe, Asia, the United States and Australia.

Feel free to contact me: pete.fellows@fellowsandassociates.com; +44 207 903 5019

Pete Fellows’s Specialties:

Patent Attorney recruitment, Trade Mark Attorney recruitment, interview & selection training, management consultancy, headhunting and advertised selection.
