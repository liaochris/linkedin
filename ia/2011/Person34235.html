Paul Cohen’s Summary

Paul M. Cohen, a sophomore cognitive science major (with a focus on consumer behavior) at Yale University, is the owner and manager of Trinston Diversified, LLC.

Paul has a serious passion for great design, exceeding expectations, and overjoyed clients. His other interests include (in no particular order) grilled cheese, entrepreneurship, comedy, cognitive science, technology, music, macaroni and cheese, economics, Roquefort cheese, psychology, women, philosophy, international relations, anti-genocide activism, parapraxes, neuroscience, and great company.

Founded in 2003, Trinston Diversified has provided unique technology solutions for over fifty clients. The internet investments and venture development side of Trinston Diversified's operations was started in August 2007 as a complement to the company's existing services.
