Jonathan Attwood's Summary

Having come from a Lloyd's and London Market background within Marine, Offshore Energy and General Liabilities, Jonathan provides a Contingency Search-Based recruitment service within the Broking, Claims and Underwriting division of Eames Consulting Group.

Specialising in Mid to Senior level Underwriters, Jonathan works across the class spectrum for remits between £50,000 and £150,000 per annum salaries.

Prior to joining Eames Consulting Group, Jonathan set up the Claims, Broking and Underwriting practice at another niche Insurance Recruitment firm.
