Alan Kravitz’s Summary

As a copywriter who specializes in helping socially conscious organizations (both non-profits and for-profits), I know that making the world a better place is a tall order. Many of the organizations I work with have writing/communications staffs, yet they still have more writing projects than they can handle. That's where I come in. I love to write - and I love the challenge of finding just the right words to tell audiences who you are, what you have to offer and what makes you stand out.

The biggest "headaches" I'm asked to solve include writing direct mail, as well as optimized website copy and web articles. Through my editing services, I also help clients gain a fresh perspective on their existing print and/or web copy.

My aim is to rid the world of boring "corporatespeak" and create copy that people actually want to read. I'm always looking to connect with additional clients who feel the same way.

Alan Kravitz’s Specialties:

The organizations I work with have a wide range of writing needs. My copywriting services include web writing, search engine optimization (SEO), direct mail campaigns, brochure copy, newsletters (both email and print), annual reports, case studies, white papers, articles for media publications and speechwriting.
