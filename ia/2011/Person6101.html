    Key Connections is a technology recruitment consultancy specialising in placing software and web development professionals (Java, .Net, and Open Source) throughout South Africa.

    We are not like every other agency that only sources candidates by placing adverts and searching for available candidates on Job Boards. Our experience tells us that the best candidates are not always on the open job market, and do not apply to adverts or register with online job boards! We proactively source these candidates and continually expand our network to ensure that our clients always have access to the best talent (On or off the market). This approach combined with our position as specialists in the software and web development field gives us access to an unparalleled network of contacts within these sectors.

    We pride ourselves on being able to provide our clients with those candidates that others simply CANNOT and DO NOT find!
