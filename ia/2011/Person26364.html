Linda Rey’s Summary

Linda Rey is a graduate of Pace University in Pleasantville, New York with a Business Administration degree. Linda has worked in the insurance profession since 1985 where she began her career working in the family business, The Rey Insurance Agency, Inc. located in Sleepy Hollow.

Among Linda's honors:
* 2010 Outstanding Member of the Year by the Sleepy Hollow / Tarrytown Chamber of Commerce.

* 2006 Emerging Leader by the American Association of University Women (AAUW) Westchester Branch,

* 2005 as Business Woman of the Year by the Westchester Hispanic Chamber of Commerce.

* Currently serving on the Board of Directors for the Westchester County Association.

* Past President and Vice President of the Sleepy Hollow Chamber of Commerce.

* Past Secretary on the Board of Directors for the Rotary Club of the Tarrytowns.

* Past Co-Events Chair for the National Association of Insurance and Financial Advisors Westchester (NAIFA-Westchester)

* Past Executive Board as Treasurer for the Westchester Association of Women Business Owners (WAWBO) prior to the merger with WEDC forming the Alliance of Hudson Valley Women Business Owners.

Rey Insurance Agency has been published in Fortune Small Business, Insurance Journal, Westchester County Business Journal, The Journal News, El Aguila as well the local publication The Hudson Independent. Rey Insurance is also the insurance blogger for www.Equifax.com.

Linda is licensed as a Property and Casualty Broker as well as a Life & Health Insurance Broker.
