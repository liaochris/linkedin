Noelia Fernández Arroyo's Summary

Noelia Fernández Arroyo was one of the founders of Yahoo! Spain and she is currently leading the Editorial & Content Group for Yahoo! EMEA, which ensures and overview editorial and content experience, production & programming operations.

Previous to that in Spain, and as director of Products and Services, she had the overall responsibility for the products and services offering, including the definition and coordination of the product strategy, development and maintenance.

She was also the Founding Partner and Director of NMP, a consulting company specialized in design, integration and publishing solutions on the Internet. From there she managed Internet communications solutions for leading Spanish Media companies such as Grupo Prisa or Vocento.

She has a Bachelor in Communications from the University of Navarra and a MBA with Honors from the Graduated School of Business of the University of Chicago. She is Associated Professor of the Media Business Department at the University of Navarra.

Also Member of the Academic Board of ISDI, and responsible of Content development Module.

She had been listed by the World Business Magazine, in association with INSEAD, as one of the most influential Business Women under 35.
