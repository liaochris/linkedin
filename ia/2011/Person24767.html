Katrina J. McBride's Summary

I get it.
Sales and marketing have a symbiotic relationship. If marketing creates marketing strategies, programs, and campaigns without including the input and collaboration of sales those campaigns are doomed to fail. That costs the company money without increasing revenue and worst of all does not offer the customer anything of value.

I get it.
I understand the dynamics between the functional departments, such as sales, marketing, operations, training, and manufacturing, that contribute an effective and efficient organization. To me there is a clear connection between managing customer relationships and healthy employee relationships. I have a passion for connecting those dots using clear, concise communications and building programs that work.
