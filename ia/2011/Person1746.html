Allen Qu's Summary

Allen Qu leads the Netconcepts China team to provide analysis, consultation and implementation of natural and paid search strategies for world’s leading companies in the US, Australia, New Zealand and China markets, including Expedia, eLong.com, The Planet, Taikang Life, Microsoft, Qunar.com, Zhaoping.com, New Oriental Group, eBay.cn, Gigabyte, Abang.com, Tuniu.com, Edeng.cn, EE-Media, UUSee.com, RealEstate.co.nz, Air New Zealand and many more. Allen's expertise includes search engine optimization, paid search management, social media optimization, permission/email marketing, blog & viral marketing, PR optimization and integrated marketing strategies. The SEO & SEM programs that Allen has been managing have achieved remarkable successes. Allen also has extensive experience in managing e-commerce operations before joining Netconcepts. In addition, he's the frequent speaker at search & online marketing conferences, including SMX, ad:tech and other internet related events, conferences and TV shows. Allen has also been providing SEO and online marketing trainings for Google, Baidu, government agencies and various organizations.

Allen graduated from The University of Auckland with marketing and IT degree. He is Google AdWords Qualified Individual, Yahoo! Search Marketing Ambassador, Certified eMarketer and Certified Direct Marketer. He also experienced in web development & multimedia, designing and building web 2.0 sites and applications.
