Pablo Calvo's Experience

Supply Chain Project Manager

Dia% - Carrefour

Privately Held; Retail industry

June 2006 – Present (4 years 10 months)

I work as Project Manager in the Supply Chain international Dept. of DIA% Supermarkets (Hard Discount of Groupe Carrefour).

My work is similar to an Internal Consultant, because my responsibility is to detect any improvement possibility in the logistic field, and then run a project (design, develop, manage teams, budget, train the people, start-up, follow-up, etc.) to change it.

Lately I’ve become a RFID expert because I run a huge project in which we apply this technology to our Supply Chain processes. But I am not only focused in technology; Bellow there are some other projects and responsibilities:

* Work-flow and processes optimization (change agent)
* In-field & office training (warehouse managers & logistics coordinators)
* Vehicle routing and dispatch: costs, fulfilment, scheduled times.
* Introduction of new logistics models, such as Backhauling or Cross-Docking.
* Introduction of different technologies such as RFID, Voice – Picking or Fleet management via GPS.
* Analysis of information systems for any process: identify main KPIs for the business.
* Design, test, first-run and training of logistics projects
