Sandy N. Finklin's Summary

Sandy holds several degrees specializing in Marketing, Psychology and Business/Social Impact Management. Sandy has also explored International Marketing in Malaysia during a study abroad at Kolej Damasa Utama. Furthermore, she’s touring the global with her new inspirational self-help book: Forgiven: Pathway to Recovery.

She is an active member of the American Marketing Association (AMA), Association of Fundraising Professionals (AFP), Miami-Dade Chamber of Commerce (MDCC), National Notary Association (NNA) and National Small Business Association (NSBA); Miami Christian Music Examiner for Examiner.com of Arts & Entertainment; Music Advisory Board Member of Positive Hits-Z88.3; Board Member and Executive Secretary of New Life Enrichment Center; Business Advisory Board Member of Chase Bank; Advisory Board Member of YMCA of Broward; Motivational Speaker/Trainer for Business Catalyst; Sunday School Teacher for the Awesome Warriors (6-10 years-old) of New Life World Outreach Ministries Church; Talk Show Co-Host as Dr. Butterfly and Vice President of Broward Gospel Guild Announcer’s Guild Chapter.

Additionally, she is an involved well-spoken speaker and judge not only to the National Student Organizations (NSO) Competitions but to thousands other national organizations.

Lastly, she is a 18 year veteran of fund-raising in the non-profit sector. I’ve had the opportunity to work in a number of roles in comprehensive fund-raising programs including grants, events/sponsorships, corporate and foundation support, and individual giving where I’ve been responsible for supervising development efforts, board management, and strategic planning.
