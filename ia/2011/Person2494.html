André Ribeirinho’s Summary

André Ribeirinho, 30, is the co-founder Adegga.com, a Social Wine Discovery service that helps people to choose wine and wine producers to promote their brand. He's also a co-founder at Filmz.pt and an advisor at Tarpipe.

In the past his roles have included User Experience Designer, Usability Specialist and Information Architect. He's a Web Developer with broad experience in large scale Web Applications using open source technologies. André performs his best while working between technology and business development.

André Ribeirinho is very customer oriented and has strong entrepreneurial and business skills. He's knowledgeable about web marketing and search engine optimization. André is fluent both in English and French and he has been writing a blog for over 5 years.
In his spare time, Andre is an avid crafter with a thing for lamps. He can be contacted at andre@adegga.com
