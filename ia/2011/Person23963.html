Keith Canning ~ Sales Director's Summary

We are the UK's acknowledged market leading Recruitment to Recruitment Consultancy; with the largest team of experienced Consultants and Support staff; the biggest advertiser and the largest database. Established in 2000 we have a long history of success and an impressive portfolio of prestigious Clients, the majority of which we have worked with since our early years.

Having won multiple awards over the years we are the current winners of the REC's 'Best Recruitment to Recruitment Firm' - as nominated by clients for service quality and results.

Due to our standing in the industry we are aware of all the worthwhile Vacancies for Recruiters at all levels throughout the UK, plus we have the most comprehensive information about thousands of Recruitment Companies

We have the largest database of Recruitment Candidates, with in-depth appraisals, billing history and motivations for each and an experienced team of Resourcers to help source the most elusive Recruiters and Managers.

Specialising only in Recruitment to Recruitment – we do not compete with our Clients

Dealing with all disciplines, sectors and markets, at all levels - throughout the UK

Innovative, honest, conscientious and talented – if a solution exists, we’ll deliver it; if it doesn’t exist, we’ll create it. We are the only Company in our sector offering a true Managed Agency solution.

Our approach is pure Consultancy - where quality & reliability are always paramount. True to our values, we are selective who we work with to ensure we are providing clients and candidates with options that match their specifications without compromising integrity.
