Jindrich Dinga's Summary

I am a senior software user experience architect at Oracle working with international teams on products for developers and consumers.

My responsibilities include working with external and internal customers. I also participate in the gathering of user input and transforming the input into requirements. I design user experiences and interfaces, conduct user studies, perform heuristic evaluations of various designs and build interactive prototypes.

In addition, I lead the design process for mobile controls, various mobile applications, and a brand new user interface for JavaFX Mobile. I also ensure that projects delivered by external vendors and all products meet the highest quality standards, having both an engaging user experience and user interface. Before I was assigned to JavaFX Mobile projects, I led all projects related to Sun's most visible web site, Java.com.

All these skills have enabled me to grow the site, be involved in a number of high priority and confidential projects that have generated a great deal of revenue. My efforts have been recognized by Sun in that I was awarded Sun’s internal award for Operational Excellence and Leadership in 2009.

I have also worked closely with the marketing, development, quality assurance, visual design, and brand teams, delivering high quality results in both the short and long term.

I spoke at JavaOne in 2007, 2008, 2009 and have been blogging at blogs.sun.com/designatsun.

Specialties

User Interaction Design, User Experience Design, Web Design, Information Architecture, User Centered Design, Human Computer Interaction, Usability Testing and Evaluation, Prototyping (paper, Flash, CSS, HTML, etc.), Visual Design, Branding, Designing for Mobile, Web-based Applications, Desktop Applications.
