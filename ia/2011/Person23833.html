Kaushal Thakkar PMP's Summary

Intrapreneur with 11 years of experience as Entrepreneur and Professional in building Internet Products, Internet Marketing, eCommerce development and Project Management.

I love exploring with new and unexplored markets and inventing ideas that can impact billions. I am passionate about technology, connected with the reality of business and enjoy ensuring quality execution of visions and actionable items that come out of brainstorming meets.

My goal is convert nascent ideas into profit generating, cash rich business ventures. My experience enables me to turn business units / brick & mortar business to Internet based profitable business ventures/ products.

My key strength is identifying talent and putting together teams that can master obstacles, and can deliver on the vision. This has helped me in effortlessly executing challenging projects & products that most people shy away from.

My current assignment with Times Group as a Product Head involves strategizing & conceptualizing Internet products - Job Site and AI based SaaS application for the recruitment industry.

Over the last decade I have managed several Product’s and Project’s involving User Experience Designs (UX), Technical Development, Instructional Designing and Search Engine Optimization of Internet Solutions in the domain of Banking, Insurance, Recruitment, Travel, eCommerce, eLearning, Media & Entertainment, Automobiles, etc

Specialties

IT Products & Services - Internet Product management, User Experience Design (UX), Project Management, eCommerce, Internet Marketing, Social media marketing, Business Analysis, Business Development, Portfolio & Engagement Management.
