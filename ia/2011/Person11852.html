Diane Putvin's Summary

I bring clarity and power to the chaos that many business owners and sales professionals experience. My mission is to help people be the Champion they were born to be by helping them manage (what Blair Singer calls ) their "Little Voice".

Working with my previous company, one of the most rewarding things I did was work and develop my staff as well as my clients to meet their goals. I ask the hard questions and are able to allow people to see the bigger person inside.

One of my goals is to work exclusively as a partner/coach with clients who are willing to "Stand in the Heat" and get to the other side to reach their goals. It's hard work sometimes and ALWAYS worth the ride.

Specialties

Building championship teams using Blair Singer's book, The ABC's of Building a Business Team That Wins.

Holding my clients accountable to the results they say they want.

Creating WIN, WIN, WIN scenarios for clients.
