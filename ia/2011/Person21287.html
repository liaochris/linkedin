Joann M. Ricci’s Summary

As an organizational development consultant grounded in a systems approach, I partner with foundations, education and other non-profit organizations to:

--Develop effective strategies and programs to meet mission driven needs
--Conduct needs assessment and develop new programs
--Design and facilitate learning opportunities which spread knowledge across the enterprise
--Lead strategic and operational planning efforts and guide you in clarifying issues, refining ideas and shaping responses
--Evaluate your impact
--Conduct research and analysis
--Facilitate high engagement meetings with small and large groups

As a coach and thought partner, I can assist leaders and others with:
--Addressing vexing issues, helping you think through options and solutions while building on your strengths
--Helping you accomplish what you want professionally and personally

Joann M. Ricci’s Experience

  • Leadership Coach

    RicciGroup

    (Professional Training & Coaching industry)

    July 2006 — Present (4 years 7 months)

    Noted by my clients for enthusiasm, creativity and insight, I combine energy and discernment to facilitate personal and organizational growth and learning.

    Grounded in systems principles, I approach coaching as a collaborative, working partnership between coach and client, with the intent to move them forward in their professional or personal development.

    By deeply listening with care and objectivity, I assist clients in creating the path needed to get from where they are to where they want and need to be.

    Tapping into a variety of approaches and evidence based methodologies, I help clients clarify the issues they and their organization face, identify options to address those, discern the steps needed for change, and hold them accountable to an action plan designed for success.

    My coaching practice is targeted to leaders and staffs of foundations, non profits, education institutions and other individuals, helping them navigate through the complexities of their day to day work. With expertise in leadership development, organizational change, and adult learning, I tap into the client’s individual and organizational potential for higher levels of problem solving, creativity, and learning.

    I hold a Master’s certificate in Evidence Based Coaching from Fielding Graduate University in California and an ACC certification credential by the International Coaching Federation.
