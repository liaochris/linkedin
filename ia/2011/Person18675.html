Jake Leith FCSD,FRSA's Summary

I'm very fortunate to have inherited a diverse cultural background. Born in Bushey Hertfordshire with English, Indian and Irish forbearers.

Exhibitions and museums featured large in my childhood. - as did an awareness of colour and the luxurious feel of velvet.

Attracted to textiles and their design I embarked on a career which would enable me to have a foot in both the academic forum and the business world - very useful.

I am design director and co-owner of of an interior design consultancy of 20 years standing. I have also lectured in business and professional practice in design for a number of years.

Love of quality in all aspects of design - very subjective - What is good design? What is kitch? I open my client's eyes to the new, to the different, enhancing their lives and surroundings to give relevance, meaning, and thought provoking interest.

I love to work on a fusion of styles with a client - we, the client and me, can be both daring and safe - there are no boundaries, only those we jointly choose to impose.

Specialties

Managerment of complex multi-disciplinary design projects
