Dan Cailler’s Summary

I am an experienced writer of many styles. I have had many articles published in The Syracuse Post Standard and the Syracuse City Eagle and the Columbia Daily Tribune. I also have good experience writing for the web and blogging.

My main strength is in the creative aspect of writing. I have a few completed manuscripts under my belt. I wrote, edited, formatted, designed the cover, and published 'Waking', a serious horror novel, in 2007.

My latest MS, a young adult fantasy/adventure, is currently being shopped around.

I have also worked as a Graphics Designer for three student magazines at Syracuse University. And coupled with my brief stint as a Web Developer for Untied Magazine, I have a workable range of tech-savvy skills.
