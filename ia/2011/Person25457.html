Larry Cervon's Summary

Over 15 years of software sales experience to both large and small organizations characterized by showing business value to senior executives. Comfortable with complex sales cycles.

Strong Solution Selling background. Self driven, creative, enthusiastic and proven “hunter” sales rep with an extensive relationship based network. Both start-up and large company experience provides versatile background for any organization.

Areas of focus include Analytics, Business Intelligence, Supply Chain, ERP, Master Data Management, Identity Resolution, Idea/Innovation Management and Market Research On-Line Communities (MROC’s).

Larry has an MBA from Babson and he maintains close ties with Babson, where he is on the Executive Committees for the Babson Alumni Technology Council and the Retail/Consumer Career Affinity Group.

Other affiliations include SIM (Society for Information Management), NETSEA (New England Technology Sales Executives Association and the Massachusetts Technology Leadership Council.

Specialties

Solution Selling; Pipeline Building; Account Management; Entrepreneur; Relationship selling; Establishing, building and managing successful client relationships; Creative problem solving and strong interpersonal, analytical, and business development skills; CRM; SaaS (software as a service); Salesforce.com; Analytics; Supply Chain (SCM); CRM; ERP- (Oracle, JDE, SAP, Infor); Innovation/Idea Management.
