Paul Leacock's Summary

At Blacksmith, we’re all about transformation and chose our company name as we see a multitude of similarities between the role of the traditional blacksmith and the
work we do forging successful, sustainable businesses with engaged cultures and awesome employer brands.

Most of my career has been about asking the right questions and then actively listening to answers. I believe that’s a key part of any sales or consulting role as it’s invariably within those answers that you find the solution to your client’s burning issues.

For the last 14 years I have been working with New Zealand business leaders across the country to support them in transforming their business results by leveraging the power of technology and talented people.

Having managed teams myself, I understand the frustration and isolation one can feel when there are limited resources and support for how you get the best out of your team and align them to the business strategy and brand promise. This is why I am so excited about TakeON! and the results I know it has delivered for clients both in New Zealand and globally.

I am passionate about giving people managers the talent, tools and resource they need to create high performing teams and sustainable business performance. We don’t turn your business upside down or inside out. We work with the best of what you have and build from there.
