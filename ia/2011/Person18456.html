Jack Tsang’s Summary

Jack Tsang is a compliance advisor, business coach and financial trainer in Hong Kong. In the compliance field, he has acquired substantial experiences with financial regulatory bodies and financial institutions over the past 15 years. In the coaching field, he is a Registered Corporate Coach providing services to both internal and external clients. In the training field, he has trained over 10,000 persons in the financial industry with more than 2,000 hours for both professional certification programs and CPD courses.
