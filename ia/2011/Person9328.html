Jane Cox's Summary

Jane Cox is a Founder and Director of Cause+Affect, one of Vancouver’s leading brand and design agencies. Her company has not only garnered a strong reputation in brand development and design, but as one of Vancouver's leading creators of innovative cultural happenings through events such as Pecha Kucha Vancouver.

Selected as Wallpaper magazine's "City Insider", Jane is recognized as a key member of the Vancouver design establishment and continues to act as a strong voice for local design and culture. Her new venture called KEYtoVan, supports and promote Vancouver’s cultural scene by building a community dedicated to celebrating culture and the uniqueness of our city.

Jane also co-founded Jorg&Olif, the sustainable lifestyle company. As Creative Director, Jane developed the company from a local start-up to an internationally recognized and trendsetting brand celebrated globally in publications such as Vanity Fair, I.D. and Vogue. Jorg&Olif was sold to an overseas investor in Spring 2009.

Specialties

Business Strategy, Brand Development, Marketing & Communications, Creative Direction, Multi-media Design (print, web, interior design, video), Exhibitions and Events

PechaKucha Night was devised in Tokyo in February 2003 as an event for young designers to meet, network, and show their work in public.
It has turned into a massive celebration, with events happening in hundreds of cities around the world, inspiring creatives worldwide. Drawing its name from the Japanese term for the sound of "chit chat", it rests on a presentation format that is based on a simple idea: 20 images x 20 seconds. It's a format that makes presentations concise, and keeps things moving at a rapid pace.
