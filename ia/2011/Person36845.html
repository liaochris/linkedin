Richard Siggs's Summary

Through my business, Network Presence, I provide quality & custom Australian Hosting (Cloud Computing, Virtual Private Servers, Co-location of your equipment or managed systems) sited at either Global Switch or Equinix colo facilities in Sydney.
See http://netpres.com.au

Being a Redhat Certified Examiner (RHCX, RHCE) and in coordination with Redhat, I am qualified to present various Redhat Training Courses & their Exams since November 2008. I'm also available for contract-based work or to consult on a range of Linux or Unix based Systems Operational & Administration tasks & roles, be it short-term troubleshooting, on-call maintenance or longer-term regular contracts.

In addition to the public offerings of Network Presence, I am available for ISP Startup or ISP Technology Deployment Services, anywhere, worldwide.

I have broad IT&C Operations and Infrastructure Management skillsets & capabilities and having run & managed technical & business teams in large ISP server deployments & networks, I'm across the wide range of operational & commercial issues associated with 99.99x% QoS & levels of service.

As an advocate of (& soon to be qualified in) ITIL, I believe that documentation & process/workflow management is critical to IT business operations & I'm productive from Machineroom to Boardroom.

Network Presence is also proud to host & sponsor the GamingAU (gamingau.net) online gaming network in Australia. GamingAU is a vibrant and active community of online game players & Admins, see http://forums.gamingau.net
