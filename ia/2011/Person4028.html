Marcio Gonçalves's Summary

Marcio Gonçalves works with Information Technology since 1999. He have strong experience in Partner Relationship Management focused in SAP products. Specializes in Utilities Industry, especially with SAP for Utilities (IS-U/CCS) which is currently present in companies such as CPFL, Neoenergia, AES Eletropaulo, Energias do Brazil, CEMIG, Light and Comgás.

Member of PMI (ID#1086639) on process of certification with experience in all knowledge areas of PMBOK: Integration, Scope, Time, Cost, Quality, Human Resources, Communication, Risk and Procurement. Areas of specialization: Resources Management, Procurement.

Experience with integration and development of portals specifics for consultancy services and e-recruitment of consultants for projects to implementation of SAP products. Huge experience in Excel (advanced) and MS Access. Creation and development of "Portal do Consultor", relationship tool between clients and professionals in ERP.

Experience in strategic planning, marketing, sales and service for electronic commerce.

Graduated in Computer Science with specialization in Management of Information Technology (CBT: Software Factory) and Mechanical Technology, working in the area of services and projects in electrical engineering and instrumentation.

Has passport and visa to the United States valid until 2013.
