Michelle Flowers, M.A.’s Summary

Michelle Flowers is an applied and visual anthropologist with ethnographic research experience in several cities across the US. Michelle holds an undergraduate degree in anthropology and communications from the University of Washington, and a master's degree in anthropology from Temple University. She currently works as an applied anthropologist with Alelo, TLT, a software development company that designs cutting edge products which promote a global community of learners through increasing awareness and appreciation of other cultures.
