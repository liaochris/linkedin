Colby Smith’s Summary

I love what I do and I make every effort to instill in my clients and potential clients my passion. Being passionate about what you do is essential in inspiring yourself to do the best you can. I take pride in every piece I create, no matter how large or small. I know that every piece represents my clients and their business image. If the smallest of items is not perfect it can have a large impact on your customer's view of you.

I strongly believe that functionality and aesthetics go hand in hand. Too many designers worry about the aesthetics but not the functionality. A piece that looks great but does not function well causes greater harm than a piece that is easy to use but lacks the 'wow' effect. I balance both to create pieces that are as aesthetically pleasing as they are functional.

I do not believe in meeting client expectations. I believe in exceeding them. I demand the best of myself to give to my clients. In a world that has become less and less about client satisfaction and more about making a buck, I give my clients more than they expect.
