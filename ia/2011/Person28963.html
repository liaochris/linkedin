Matt Ford's Summary

Hi I'm Matt:
I achieved a Bachelor of Arts degree in Geography with a concentration in environmental analysis and minor in Geology from Cal State Fullerton in May of 2005. I earned a second degree from the Los Angeles Film School in November of 2009 in film. I focused my thesis concentration in the areas of writing, directing and post-production both video and sound editing. I am self -motivated and dedicated to the craft of film and events, photography and visual story telling through the cinematic arts. I consider event/film operations and logistics to be an art form and science that is both challenging and rewarding.
Prior to entering film school in August of 2008, I worked full-time as a Special Event Specialist for both Disneyland Resort Marketing and Operations serving as a liaison between the Resort (including Disneyland Park, Disney’s California Adventure Park, Downtown Disney and the three resort hotels) and Walt Disney Company marketing initiatives such as red carpet press events, commercial filmings, company synergy events and filmings, radio remote broadcasts, publicity events and sales events. I planned and managed operational and logistical elements for external clients such as Google and Honda. Within the Walt Disney Company, I worked along side Walt Disney Parks and Resorts, Walt Disney Pictures, Buena Vista, ABC, Pixar, the Disney Channel, Radio Disney, ESPN, and Disney Sports, as well as, support for the Tournament of Roses on several occasions. I understand how to manage relationships throughout the planning and execution of all film/event elements. I have a vast amount of high profile VIP and celebrity experience.
Since graduating from film school in November of 2009, I have worked on thesis films at USC, the American Film Institute and Loyola Marymount University. Additionally, I have worked with models in the areas of fashion, editorial, swimsuit and lingerie to develop website content and portfolio work.

Specialties

Film and Event logistics and operational impact mitigation consulting, locations management and scouting, production management and coordinating, Directors assistant, Producing.
