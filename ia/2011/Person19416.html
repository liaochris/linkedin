Jason Rink's Summary

This is Who I Am:

I am passionate about liberty. I write, speak, and teach on the subjects of financial freedom, political freedom, personal freedom, vocational freedom, and spiritual freedom. In my professional career I have consulted political campaigns, launched grassroots organization, spoken at hundreds of events ranging 20 to 20,000 in attendance, started and pastored a non-denominational church, authored books, written articles, designed websites, and hosted an FM talk radio show.

This is What I Have Done:

Author:
I was a contributing author of “Ron Paul: A Life of Ideas,” the biography of Congressman Ron Paul. My work has also been featured on the anti-war, anti-state, pro-market website Lewrockwell.com.

I wrote a book called "Disciple of Liberty: Seven Priorities of a Christian Patriot" which was published in April of 2010.

Medi Appearances:
I have appeared as a guest on FOX Business Channel’s ”America’s Nightly Scoreboard” , the FOX News program ”On The Record with Greta Van Susteren,” and “Freedom Watch” with Judge Andrew Napolitano.

Speaker:
Over the course of the last ten years, I have been a pastor, a grassroots political activist, and a professional speaker. I have spoken over one-hundred times to audiences of all ages, ranging from 20 to 20,000 in attendance on a variety of topics such as State Sovereignty, the U.S. Constitution, Early American History, Political Activism, Money and Economics.

This is What I Do:
I help people and organizations communicate and execute the Philosophy of Liberty. Whether a political organization, a non-profit organization, or an individual, I consult people on issues of financial freedom, political freedom, personal freedom, vocational freedom, and spiritual freedom.

I do this through direct leadership as a Board member or Executive Director, as a Social Media Consultant, ad a Creative Director and Multimedia Production Specialist, and as a Personal Coach.

The Foundation for a Free Society

Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; Political Organization industry

May 2010 – Present (11 months)

The Foundation for a Free Society is an educational organization dedicated to promoting the principles of liberty, personal sovereignty, private property, and free markets in order to create a more free and prosperous society.

We believe that the key to a free society is individuals who understand their natural rights, and are empowered to exercise them. Free individuals are the literal foundation, the building blocks, of freedom.

The Foundation for a Free Society equips citizens and future leaders to understand and defend the Philosophy of Liberty for the next generation.

We want to help make freedom “click” in the minds of a new generation of people.

Our mission isn’t just to educate, but to equip people with the knowledge and tools they need to live as free individuals.

Our three-fold mission is to:

EDUCATE:
Provide education and training in the Philosophy of Liberty by distributing materials and organizing events.

ACTIVATE:
Enable individuals to more effectively communicate the Philosophy of Liberty in their community.

LIBERATE:
Promote individual sovereignty by providing information on alternatives in the areas of education, agorism, currency, health and wellness, self-defense, and entrepreneurship.

Co-Founder/Director of Education

Ohio Freedom Alliance

Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; Political Organization industry

March 2008 – November 2010 (2 years 9 months)

The Ohio Freedom Alliance has been in existence since March of 2008. We are a non-partisan political organization dedicated to education and action at the grassroots level in the state of Ohio. Our actions and agenda is founded in the following principles:

Our rights are granted to us by our Creator and are therefore and integral part of our humanity. Any attempt by any individual or group of individuals to take away these rights is immoral and contrary to the laws of nature.

It is only in an environment of freedom that man is capable of achieving his full potential. A society that maximizes freedom will also maximize prosperity, peace and justice.
Voluntary action is ethical and it is the growing state that is the negation of freedom and liberty.
We seek to put these principles into action through the following means:

To EDUCATE: The Ohio Freedom Alliance is dedicated to educating citizens on the principles of true freedom.
To ACTIVATE: The Ohio Freedom Alliance is committed to getting the grassroots organized and active in the fight for liberty.
To DELEGATE: The Ohio Freedom Alliance is actively working with our representatives in the Ohio Statehouse to introduce legislation that promotes liberty, and organizing a grassroots lobbying force to promote that legislation. In particular we focus on legislation that promotes decision making at the state and local level, such as state sovereignty legislation.
