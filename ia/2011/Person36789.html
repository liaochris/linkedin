Richard Jones's Summary

Ventura creates and improves telecoms operations – working for utilities, operators and industry bodies including the FTTH Council and GSM-A.

In parallel, we have also bootstrapped our own profitable operator in the ultra-competitive Swedish market and it is now ranked in the top 5 globally for broadband service value by Point Topic. It has grown to 120,000 subscribers with up to 100 meg symmetric services and 870,000 homes passed.

Using our experience, we are working to improve stalled operations where margins, revenues or subscribers have stalled - as well as commercial launch, due diligence and operational improvement.

Our strength in organisational change has led to a current assignment where we are leading the change programme in a telecom regulator on behalf of the UN.

I regularly chair, speak at, and hold workshops for events on FTTx, WiMAX and broadband issues in Europe and the Gulf (e.g. for the FTTH Council – Europe and GCC, FTTX MEGNA, WiMAX MEGNA). I also hold training courses for senior executives in strategy, finance and marketing.

I have previously held roles as Chief Commercial and Strategy Officer in a startup – working from pre-license through to the launch phase and covering all aspects of business and financial planning, commercial development and implementation. As part of this I planned and recommended the largest WiMAX deployment in EMEA.

I have worked closely with broadband and fixed wireless access companies - planning, modelling and implementing complex rollouts of wireless, fibre and xDSL based implementations across a number of countries in Europe and Africa.

I am an experienced project leader who knows how to turn around tough projects and struggling companies. My book on this "Project Management Survival" is published by Kogan Page and is also available in Chinese.

I have also led, founded and helped others build businesses – e.g. one now on Nasdaq (post-acquisition) and with 75x returns for shareholders.

Specialties

Telecoms modelling, telecoms license bid support, due diligence, business planning, managing complex teams and developments, project management, developing ventures, creating exciting product roadmaps, facilitation, managing major change.
