Linda Stanfield's Summary

Customer service is what is important to me; For the past 15 years, I have personally used plumbing to offer the best customer service I could. My team of plumbers and customer service rep's help many homeowners with their plumbing needs, wants, concerns, and problems to make their life much better 24/7. We want to customize water treatment and plumbing needs to each homeowner that contacts us.

I can help with both commercial and residential plumbing. Benjamin Franklin, The Punctual Plumber ROC 250620, my residential service company and Approved Plumbing ROC 247494,my commerical plumbing service company.

I love making homeowners lives better with proper plumbing service, sharing the new green and update technology available, changing to solar or tankelss water heating.

I also love business, its cycles, its success's and its challenges. They are all great.

My experience is successfully building a company from the ground up to a multimillion dollar company and helping other business's start up and move on to the next level, whatever that may be for them.

I enjoy connecting others, finding great resources that business's need, marketing business's to the right audience, building a great business culture,

I am a proud Graduate of APS AAAME Business Development Program.

I'm the past chair for the Gilbert Chamber of Commerce in Arizona, and continue to chair their Work Safety program. I have also chaired Phoenix NAWBO Ambassador and Business Mentoring Program, along with many other community needs for education, business or women needs.
I am currently donating my time to the board of The Gilbert Historical Society and Shop Gilbert program.

I’m a proud member of Referral Institute referrals for life program. I really like working with people and seeing them succeed in their dreams.

Specialties

24/7 Plumbing Service, Green Plumbing, Customer Service, Business Management, Solar and Tankless hot water heaters, Water Treatment Specialist
