Doug Jackson’s Summary

Mr. Jackson has a wealth of corporate leadership and business consulting experience, ranging from Fortune 50 Company to small/start-up company environments. He has led corporate turn around, high growth and start-up successes. He has operated in, and is familiar with, a range of industries including the consumer packaged goods, technology, medical research, and defense industries. He has a strong record of building/leading management teams that consistently exceed productivity, quality, revenue and profitability goals. He holds a BA degree in Economics from Harvard University and an MBA degree with an emphasis in Finance from Brigham Young University.

Throughout his career and in addition to his overall interests and skills in management, Mr. Jackson’s primary focus and core competency has consistently been in the discipline of financial management and “bottom line” results.
