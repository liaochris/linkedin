Patrick McKeough's Summary

Patrick McKeough operates a unique investment organization that aims to eliminate the conflicts of interest that plague investors elsewhere in the investment industry. Pat's organization, The Successful Investor, carries out investment research that it makes available to clients by subscription to its four investment newsletters, or by membership in Pat McKeough's Inner Circle. But investors can also hire the Successful Investor as their personal investment portfolio manager. A free daily update is also available from Pat's corporate website, www.TSINetwork.ca, to give you a free sample of our research and investment advice. Unlike other portfolio managers, the Successful Investor deals directly with investors and doesn't rely on the brokerage industry to find clients. This eliminates a dangerous but often overlooked conflict of interest, between brokers and their clients.

Specialties

Our unique three-part investment approach which uses classic proven investment methods to cash in during market booms and cut risk during market downturns.

Investment Management industry

December 1994 – December 1995 (1 year 1 month)

C.M. Oliver was a regional broker that was trying to become national. I joined after leaving Marpep Publishing, at the same time launching my first investment-advisory service, The Successful Investor. Conservative sort that I am, I wanted to bring in some steady income and avoid digging into capital while my own company was in its start-up phase.

The brokerage business hadn't changed much from my last involvement with it, more than two decades earlier. I left C.M. Oliver as soon as it became clear that The Successful Investor was going to survive and prosper as a business.

When I began managing investor portfolios, my first portfolio-management clients were people whom I had served as a broker at C.M. Oliver.
