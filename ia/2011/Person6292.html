Brian Nourse's Summary

Brian Nourse Design is a Bristol based design company which offers affordable, high quality websites for both larger established businesses and smaller start-up companies. Brian has over 15 years experience in web design which is reflected by the impressive standard of his work (www.briannourse.co.uk). However, despite this high quality of work, we still offer sites at competitive prices with some costing as little as £500.

Brian Nourse Design still remains a relatively small company, with all clients able to speak to Brian directly on the phone and via e-mail when they have a site under construction. In order to make things as clear and straight forward as possible we keep all customers updated during the development process and avoid using technical speak and jargon, leaving you to carry on with business as usual while we deal with the technicalities.

In conclusion, Brian Nourse Design offers a one-stop-shop for all your web needs, combined with friendly and professional service and affordable prices.

Specialties

Web Design, Flash Programming, CDROM & Kiosk Design, Print Design - Including Brochures, Signage, Press Advertising, & Creative Concepts.
