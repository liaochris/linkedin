Katie Stephensen Rogers’s Summary

With 5 years experience in Information Technology Recruiting in both corporate and agency settings, I excel at high requisition loads of a variety of roles and get results.Experienced in Sourcing, Recruiting, Talent Acquisition, Client Relationships, Sales, Networking and Offer Negotiations.

My recent experiences have branched into Human Resources functions. I have assisted in Policies and Procedures, Immigration, Employment Law, Benefits Administration, Orientation, Training and Performance Reviews. I completed my Masters Degree in Corporate and Organizational Communication with a specialization in Human Resources Management at Northeastern University in September 2010. This degree gave me further understanding of communication standards, business and marketing functions, and how Human Resources can add value to make an organization successful.

Prior to my career in Recruiting/Human Resources, I was an Interior Designer. I still run a small consulting business, Kate & Company. This is a side venture - please inquire for more information.
