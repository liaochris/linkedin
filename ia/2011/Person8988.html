Tanya Smith, MBA, PHR’s Summary

Tanya Smith is fiercely committed to guiding women solopreneur professionals to achieve genuine expert status with their personal brand and clarity of significance in their work so they can do the work of their dreams and enjoy a better quality of life.

If you are looking for a proven professional who can guide you to address things that get in the way of your success - limited promotional opportunities, inability to communicate your outstanding value, and lack-luster business relationships, you’ve got the right person in this Expert Brand Coach.

With more than 10 years of experience working with individual contributors and guiding them to achieve remarkable success, Tanya’s mission and commitment is to offer advanced, yet practical brand management strategies for working professionals and business owners to realize personal fulfillment in their daily grind.

What sets her apart from other service providers is the ability to use the most current technologies and to help you identify chunked down steps to creating a more personal plan of action that sticks. Tanya is an expert problem-solver, sharing tremendous practical insights with you that help you get momentum.

You can feel confident engaging Tanya to guide you to solve your career challenges. Over the years her expertise has been finely groomed through remarkable training and certifications such as the Certified Career Management Coach, Certified Leadership & Talent Management Coach, Professional in HR, Reach Certified Online Identity Strategist and she is currently working towards a certification as a Branding & Marketing Coach. Her perspectives have been featured in radio, print, and online references.
