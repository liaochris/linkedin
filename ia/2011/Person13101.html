Edward Adler’s Summary

Edward Adler is a Senior Partner at MediaLink, LLC, a New York/Los Angeles-based advisory firm that provides critical counsel and strategic direction to the media, advertising, entertainment and technology industries and to companies and institutional investors that interact with those sectors.

Ed leads the strategic communications practice within MediaLink, offering his expertise in corporate communications, media relations, investor relations, crisis management, event management and corporate marketing to MediaLink’s roster of well-known media, entertainment and tech industry clients.

He is one of the most respected professionals in the business, having deftly managed a host of issues, crises and opportunities for the media and entertainment company, Time Warner Inc., where he previously served as Executive Vice President, Corporate Communications. Reporting to Chairman and CEO Jeff Bewkes, Ed was a member of Time Warner's senior management team, with responsibility for the company's worldwide communications. In addition to serving as Time Warner's chief spokesman, Ed oversaw the development and implementation of Time Warner's communications strategy and policy. He also led the company’s branded public events program and was a pioneer in activating opportunities to support Time Warner’s corporate positions and brands in emerging media such as Facebook and Twitter.

In the ever-changing media landscape, Ed has gained the respect of business leaders throughout the national and international media arena. He has played a pivotal role in launching initiatives such as TV Everywhere, and has successfully worked across all facets of investor relations, public policy, and strategic planning.

During a decades-long career at Time Warner Inc. and its divisions, Adler was a reporter for Time magazine, a programming executive for Home Box Office and news editor for Time’s magazine development area before becoming a corporate communications executive.
