Nathan Aaron Place is an Actor & Visual Artist based in San Francisco, California. He works in a variety of formats and idioms but specifically enjoys the challenges of communicating through painting, mixed media, sculpture, installations, theater, and film.

His current work is divided into two areas of exploration and discovery.

First is the creation of art objects through manufacturing processes that are then in turn augmented with intuitive and chance elements. This provides variation and individuality to each piece in the series done. (They are often representational to him of the processes of memory in regard to details and time.)

The Second Area of exploration is the creation of paintings from reclaimed and aesthetically questionable hotel/motel canvases.

His past works have filtered influences from abstraction, realism, popular culture, interdisciplinary performance art, theater, film, and subculture iconography.
