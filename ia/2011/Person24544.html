Oliver Kharraz, MD's Summary

I am passionate about improving healthcare through technology.

My current calling, ZocDoc, may be the best example of this: a technology empowering patients, benefiting physicians, and with the potential to save up to $20bn in healthcare cost nationwide.

I have been prepared for this through prior experiences at McKinsey, where I worked extensively with governments, hospital chains and pharmaceutical clients to utilize technology more effectively. One of my most satisfying experiences here was maybe increasing throughput at a hospital in an under served area by 20% by developing advanced patient logistics software.

I've also learned a lot through my work as a Neuro-ICU doctor, which helps me understand the demands on doctors, my PhD, which gave me first-hand experience of research, my online startup (sold in 2006), which taught me the importance of timing, and my Masters degree in Philosophy (Ethics and Ontology), which taught me that I know little.

Specialties

healthcare logistics, healthcare policy, healthcare ethics, online marketing, motor learning, hospital operations
