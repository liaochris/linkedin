Rebecca Monforte's Summary

Rebecca holds a Bachelor of Business (Marketing) Degree from RMIT University and was President of 'RMIT BSA' (Business Students Association) during her tertiary studies, managing a host of social events and mentoring initiatives. She won a trip to Paris as the Australian National Finalist in the International Marketing Competition L'Oreal Brandstorm.

In a highly competitive job market, Rebecca secured a 12 month placement at GM Holden working in the Sales and Marketing team as Communications Coordinator.

Seeking a change in industry, Rebecca leaped into Financial Services and joined the Austock Group as Marketing Assistant where she crafted 13 Strategic Marketing Plans for each Business Unit in the company, in collaboration with Executive Management. During this time she managed several events and assisted in the production of various printed and online marketing material.

Rebecca joined Financial Synergy in Feb 2009 as Marketing Communications Coordinator, diving into a host of events, collateral production, integrated communicatons campaigns and web content management initiatives for both B2B & B2C audiences.

Promoted early 2010 to Marketing Manager of Financial Synergy, Rebecca currently heads up all end-to-end planning and campaigns for marketing, events and communications not only for Financial Synergy (B2B) but also multiple Superannaution Funds (B2C) including GrowSuper.

Specialties

Strategic Marketing Planning, Campaign Management, Intergrated Marketing Communications, Conference & Event Management, Website Content Management & Digital Marketing / Social Media
