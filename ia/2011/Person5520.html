Bruno Couto's Summary

Born in São Miguel (Azorean Island), moved to Lisbon at his 18 to study and later work. Went to Amsterdam to develop a project at TomTom and recently came back to Lisbon to lead a mobile and open standards department at VIEW. Write and speak fluently in Portuguese and English.

Motivated web-developer with expertise in (x)HTML, CSS and jQuery. Very passionate about javascript and it's understanding. Daily work with Webkit, HTML5, CSS3 and jQuery.

Always make that extra effort to accomplish the best user interfaces by innovating both in the creative and technical areas. Always hunting for knowledge and very self-taught.
Fully W3C standards-compliant. Turns any PSD file into an optimized web page for search engines and providing a great user experience with all the accessibility requirements and all the necessary cares with http requests, sprites, markup and compression to achieve the best performance possible.

Experience with SVG - Raphael JS library, and font replacement techniques such as Cufon, SIFR or Google Fonts API.
Also experienced with CMS's, social networks integration, developing for mobile devices and XML interfaces for Android.

Skilled user of version control systems such as SVN, GitHub and Perforce.
Also works with PHP and MySQL on freelance projects.

Very focused on his work and goals, participative within the team, very organized and fully motivated to each project.
Good relationship with clients, always fighting to fulfil their needs.
Never give up on a challenge, neither on a good laugh. Always ready to pack up and get his foot on a new culture.

Music lover and sushi addicted.

Specialties
