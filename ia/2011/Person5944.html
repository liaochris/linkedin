Brandon Stafford’s Summary

An embedded systems engineer by training, I've worked professionally on mechanical, electrical, and software projects. I've laid out PCBs with hundreds of components. I've released mechanical drawings for sheet metal, molded, and CNC'd parts. I've written software libraries used in hundreds of machines around the world. My primary interest is solving the energy problems that we're facing. Over the last few years, I've consulted for around 50 renewable energy companies, and I've advised various government agencies and investors about the technical feasibility of proposed technologies. At heart, I like solving problems.

Brandon Stafford’s Specialties:

Renewable energy, embedded systems, hard mechanical problems and system analysis.
