Manish Malhotra's Summary

Manish Malhotra has diverse experience ranging from wealth management, banking and IT in financial services.

He launched Fiducioso (maker of Income Discovery platform) to develop a new approach for solving retirement income planning problem. The framework and the platform helps advisors and retirees practice the art of discovering ideal retirement income.

While at Smith Barney, he evaluated all major financial planning products and contributed to modeling a new retirement income planning approach. He led the IT strategy to build a new Advisor Desktop to give unified access to all applications.

He has experience in all aspects of IT from software development to architecture and software product management, specially for computationally intensive finance systems.

He has been a successful banker at the largest private financial institution in India handling large loans for greenfield projects.

He has published two papers recently: one exposes flaws in making random walk assumption for the long term portfolio simulation and the other proposes a new framework for retirement income planning.
