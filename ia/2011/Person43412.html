Tim Moreland's Summary

Tim is President and CEO of Appraisal Guardian, a web-based solution that eliminates appraisal fraud by revolutionizing the way real estate appraisal reports are ordered and submitted in the appraisal and mortgage industires.

During 30 years in the real estate, mortgage and title industries at the local, regional and national levels, Tim Moreland was the leading franchise development director at Coldwell Banker Residential Affiliates. He also has served as Vice President of Affiliated Business Arrangements for Chicago Title and Vice President of Operations (Joint Venture & Marketing Alliances) at Wells Fargo. As an Executive Vice President at E-LOAN, Moreland developed business-to-business models involving equity real estate investments, strategic alliances and joint ventures for the real estate, development and lending communities.

Tim has worked with more than 300 joint ventures and more than 600 marketing alliances (with Chicago Title and Wells Fargo) and more than 500 franchisees (at Coldwell Banker). A popular speaker and educator, he serves on the Board of Directors of RESPRO National and is Founding President of the RESPRO West Coast chapter.
