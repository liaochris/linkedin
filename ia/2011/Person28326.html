Mark Nierras, REALTOR’s Summary

" To keep all parties (agents, loan officers, inspectors, etc) updated during each step of the transaction;
This is my personal commitment to you. "

Licensed Real Estate Agent since 2001.

Mark Nierras is not only a Realtor, but also a Certified Residential Specialist (CRS) and an Accredited Buyer Representative (ABR). (Less than 4% of all Realtors nationwide hold this CRS designation.)
In 2010, due to the growing need for qualified and competent "short sale" and foreclosure representation, Mark completed the Short Sales and Foreclosure Resource Certification (SFR).

He started his Real Estate profession with a Century 21 franchise, then joined one of the largest independent real estate companies in Nevada and is now with Prudential Americana Group, REALTORS.

"I have closed transactions with the following agents: Bryan Pellican, Laura Worthington, Thomas Blanchard, Adam Fenn, Jared Jones, Steve Cross, Valerie Hyams, Skipper Flippin, Thomas D’ Anna, James Petty, Oana Sterlacci, Tim McKenna, Melanie Speed, Bill Jorgensen, Samvel Alaljajian, John Hatch, Troy Kearns, Paul Sweeney, Bobby Tran, Kyle Kozlowski, Andre McCollough, Jim Hastings, Jason Ekus, Denny Seybert, Jason Ekus, Jason Abrams, Dan Woolston, Yonas Woldu, etc.

I make sure that the buyers I work with are fully informed on what to expect during the process of purchasing an REO or a "short sale" property. I hope to work with you on this transaction.
