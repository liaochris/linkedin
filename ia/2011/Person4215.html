Averi Thomas-Moore’s Summary

Averi Thomas Moore is an intern with the Albright Stonebridge Group’s Africa Regional Practice group. She has experience with issues facing private companies in emerging markets, especially in the African context, as a result of internships with the Millennium Challenge Corporation’s Private Sector Development Group and the Corporate Council on Africa. Ms. Thomas-Moore also has held multiple internships in the financial services industry including investment banking at private equity. She has also interacted with government officials concerning private sector issues from African countries at business conferences and delegations here in the U.S., Tanzania, and Senegal. Ms. Thomas-Moore received a BBA in International Business and with a concentration in Finance from Howard University where she was active in the African Business Club and the International Business Society.
