Dan LoBue’s Summary

President and founder of Competitive Energy Consulting, has more than 20 years of experience in the power production and energy business. Professional focus is in advising clients on RTO and market design, risk identification, position management, contract analysis and developing decision support tools for portfolio optimization. Advising clients in the Mid-Atlantic, North East, Mid-West and Southeast on asset management techniques energy market protocols. Providing advice and optimization strategies to IPP, Utilities & Hedge Fund owners of combined cycle power plants in both organized RTO and traditional Control Area markets. As a retained, Subject Matter Expert, represents clients and their positions at RTO committees and working group. Provided the functional design expertise in a joint venture to develop an application that provides an innovative and competitive approach in creating and managing three-part generation offers for an RTO markets. Advised a wind and thermal developer on integration and commercial management activities specific to RTO operations. Presently, Project Manager of team responsible for independent certification of a new ATC/Flow based calculation and OASIS management system required by the FERC Order 890 mandated compliance standards.

* 20 years as an experienced professional in Power Production, Plant Development, Power Marketing & Origination. This includes service as a Nuclear Reactor operator in the US Navy
* 16 years in power system and energy market operations in both regulated & deregulated environments.
* 15+ years of Management experience - includes both Manager & Director level positions
* 12 years in the NY energy Market – from original New York Power Pool operations through development, implementation and first three years of NYISO operations.
* 8 years participating in PJM – committees, working groups and in trading & asset management
* 3 years in the development, implementation and start up of the MISO Day 2 market
