Angus Dockrill’s Summary

I have a real passion for helping people enjoy happier lives by helping articulate their goals and values, and putting clear financial plans in place to achieve them. How much is enough? And how do you get there? (BTW once you're 'there' what next?)

All client referrals are respected and appreciated. If you have been referred to me please let me know who I should thank.

I focus on helping individuals optimise their financial affairs, putting them in control of their financial future and enabling them to focus on what is important in their life.

My client base tends to be people who are thinking about retirement or want to achieve financial independence. I attract clients in the legal, accounting and medical professions, business owners, and those in an established financial position. I also have a special interest in advising those going through divorce & the related money issues.
