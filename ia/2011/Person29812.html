Marissa Meyer's Summary

After working as an editor and typesetter for over five years in the museum publishing industry, I began my freelancing career in 2009. My experience with fine art tomes of the highest quality has given me an eye for typography and design that I strive to utilize in every project I work on.

Specialties

Typesetting, proofreading, editing, layout, and writing services. Background in art publishing, fiction (focus on young adult and children's literature), memoirs, and web writing.

Familiarity with Chicago Manual of Style and industry typesetting standards. Software: Adobe InDesign, Photoshop, and Illustrator; Microsoft Word, Excel, and PowerPoint; Mac OS and Windows. Internet savvy.
