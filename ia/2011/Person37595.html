Roger Corn's Summary

==========
Summary
==========
Roger is a results-driven general manager who brings a strong analytic orientation to building and growing businesses. He is an operating purist and passionate student of business. He believes that business skills should be practiced daily, continuously improved and mastered over a lifetime .

==========
Specialties
==========
-PRAGMATIC STRATEGY- skilled at developing the simplest action plan required to get the desired business outcome
-CHANNEL PARTNERSHIP EXPERT- deep understanding of how to build and manage a successful partnership program
-ONLINE ADVERTISING GURU- years of balancing revenue goals against the optimal end-user experience
-PERSUASIVE COPYWRITER- niche expert in applying advertising copywriting techniques to corporate communication

==========
Experience
==========
Roger’s expertise is derived from over 20 years of study and real world experience. He has been a senior-level strategy and business development executive at leading Internet companies like Yahoo! and Overture. Recently, Roger has worked as an in-demand private consultant to top technology companies like Comcast, Veoh and OpenX.

Roger is also the publisher at Three Hour Business Solutions, a professional education firm that strives to solve common business problems in three hours or less. He is the author and creator of the Three Hour Business Bootcamp, a step-by-step online workshop that helps small business owners hone their ideas into winning business concepts.

==========
Education
==========
He received his MBA from the University of Chicago, graduating Summa Cum Laude in Economics. Prior to that, Roger graduated with Phi Beta Kappa honors from UCLA with a BA in English and Economics.

business development, corporate development, channel marketing, corporate partnerships, online traffic generation, revenue production, online advertising, copywriting
