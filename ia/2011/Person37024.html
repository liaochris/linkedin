Ricky Rainbolt’s Summary

On the personal side of life, Ricky’s charm can be defined as backpacking on the Appalachian Trail or sitting on the beach with his wife Robin, sipping on a couple of cold margaritas and of course, listening to Buffett! Whether Ricky is on an early morning run or spending time with friends, his natural energy and passion shines through in all areas of his life.

In business, Ricky is an internationally known Digital BRAND Specialist, a.k.a. “The MEGA Brander”, for ambitious leaders. He is recognized as a leading intelligence expert for the execution of credible digital brands that make a difference, exceeds expectations and creates an unforgettable emotional experience.

His MEGA BRANDER™ program is a ‘Total Brand InFusion’ Blueprint that’s a result of the New Digital Brand Generation. This generation demands real and credible engagement along with rich interactive content the way they want it, when they want it and how they want it.

Throughout Ricky’s career he has focused on answering one question… "What makes the difference in the success of ambitious leaders brands and the quality of their lives?" This quest over the last 29 consecutive years have lead him to speak at more than two thousand seminars and workshops through his Hall-of-Fame career as a professional speaker, author and digital brand champion.

What are the results of his motivational keynote, half-day or full day presentations? His audience’s leave empowered to raise their standard of performance and armed with strategic tools to unleash the true brilliance of their brand, their business and themselves. They will have the blueprint to expand their brand’s reach with real intuitive engagement and a rich consumer experience.

If you have a business that is flat-lined, a new entrepreneurial start-up, or a business leader who wants to ignite your organization with ‘Total Brand InFusion’, start your MEGA Brander journey today.
