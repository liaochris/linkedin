Ghazali Ismail's Summary

Ghazali Ismail has over 12 years of experience in the field of Human Resources, Business Consulting, IT Project Management and SAP HR Consultancy.

As a certified SAP HR and payroll consultant, he has performed full software development lifecycle in project management – which included gathering business requirements, impact analysis, and implementation of HR modules, change management, configuration changes/enhancements, testing, user manuals, support and training.

He has worked and resided in large global projects in the Netherlands, United Kingdom, South Africa and Malaysia. These experiences have given him the exposure and opportunity to work with cross-culture and diverse workforce and have enhanced his communication and people-skills with the implementation of SAP HR modules for his various customers.

Ghazali was a Solution Architect (Human Capital Management) for Singapore Computer Systems Limited (SCS), which is a public-listed information and communications technology service provider based in Singapore. His responsibilities in SCS, among others, included defining SAP HR business solutions and assuming a leading role within SCS as a champion and spokesperson for the SAP HR solution set.

Other than projects, he was also involved in the pre-sales activities which assisted in the driving of new business, and ensure that the business requirements of customers are clearly understood and translated into SAP HR and NGA solutions that meet the customer’s business requirements.
