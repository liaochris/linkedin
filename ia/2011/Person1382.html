Alex Badalic's Summary

...Im NOT an advertising agency specializing in creative communications, communication strategies, multilingual copywriting, SEO copywriting and localization of advertising campaigns. I'm a strategic copywriter and communication strategies planner in advertising since 1969, and freelancing from 1983 as a consultant for advertising and web agencies, but occasionally I also accept direct clients.

I write my copy in English, French, Italian and German, off-line as much as for the Web and for site contents.

To me David Ogilvy's principle, "Not rules, fools, tools" still is capital. And I don't believe in pure creativity, but only in the one suitable to objectives.

Specialties

Above- and below-the-line advertising, soft diret marketing, marketing information, communication strategies planning.

NON CONVENTIONAL MARKETING!
