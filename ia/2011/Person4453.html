Barbara Yang's Summary

I have completed my MBA at ESSEC Business School in France where I focused my studies in Sports and Luxury marketing. I am very passionate about the Sports and Luxury sector, especially from a marketing and branding standpoint and have completed a specialization in the International Sports Marketing Chair at ESSEC.

I received my undergraduate degree from McGill University in Montreal where I majored in Marketing and minored in International Business and International Relations. I have worked in both the Finance field and the Marketing field.

I am very involved with community work in all parts of the world. I volunteered for 4 weeks in South Africa working with AIDS orphans and have spent many years involved with the BC Children's Hospital Foundation. During my time at McGill, I held the position of President, Communications Director and Social Director with the McGill Chinese Students' Society.

Specialties
