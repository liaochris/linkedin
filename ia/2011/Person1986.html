Gabriel A. Romero's Summary

My name is Gabriel A. Romero and I am a Video Editor, Animator and Graphic Artist.

I have a Bachelor of Fine Arts with a focus on Graphic Design from the School of Visual Arts in New York. In addition to the skills and critical thinking that I gained from my studies, I have a few years of practical experience, both full-time and freelance, designing motion, interactive and print graphics.

I have handled pre- and post-production work (shooting, editing, motion graphics and audio enhancement) on several music videos, tv shows, films and documentaries shorts.

I am extremely proficient in the following video and audio programs: Final Cut Pro 7, After Effects CS5, DVD Studio Pro, Pro Tools 8, Reason 5 and entire Adobe CS5.
