Corey Ehmke's Summary

I am a technology leader with over sixteen years of professional web development experience. My focus is on Web application architecture, information security, and enterprise-class software development, particularly in the online marketing and advertising industry.

I have extensive experience working directly with clients, stakeholders and industry partners in driving technology strategies. My clients have included Starbucks, US Army, Morgan Stanley, Wieden+Kennedy, Publicis Groupe, Altrea (Philip Morris), Thomson Learning (Cengage), Ketchum Directory Advertising, Emeritus Senior Living, and Penske Truck Leasing.

I bring a collaborative, problem-solving mindset and a vendor-neutral skill set to each project, staying focused on meeting and exceeding business objectives with high-quality technology solutions delivered using Agile development and project management methodologies.

In my leadership capacity I have a proven track record in breaking down barriers between technology professionals and business stakeholders, building strong development teams and maintaining focus and morale in high-pressure environments.

Specialties

Architect, developer, and leader with 16 years of experience developing for the Web.

Technology expertise includes Ruby on Rails, AJAX, HTML, CSS, Object-Oriented JavaScript, XML, SOA, SOAP, and REST.

Expert in application of design patterns for OO development, enterprise architecture, and user experience. Specialization in search engine optimization (SEO), search engine marketing (SEM), usability, and findability. Active contributor to a variety of high-visibility Open Source projects.
