Barbara Segal’s Summary

A thought leader on the Internet since 1995. Public Relations, Media Relations, PR campaign creation and implementation, and out of the box PR consulting. Experienced in Affiliate Communication and Management, building Social Network Platforms for new or existing brands helping brand equity and to gain consumer confidence.

Among America’s most prominent interior designers, heads one of the region’s most successful and versatile full service-design firms.

Experienced in creating on-site presence for event sponsors and promotional strategies. Over 20 years of Marketing, Award Winning Sales, and Buying with a focus on luxury brands and International business. A hands on adviser in fashion, home furnishings, housewares, and Interior Design. Skilled in customer relations, styling, merchandising, and luxury in specialty store environments. Proven results expanding a major consumer brand.
