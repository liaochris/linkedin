Gray Writer's Summary

StablesOnline.com is a professional management company. Its business and service is to form a limited liability partnership to acquire seven Thoroughbred horses and subsequently to manage the racing careers of these racehorses for the benefit of the partners.

StablesOnline.com and its principal continue to be involved for over thirty-five years nationally and internationally in virtually every aspect of the Thoroughbred industry and involved for over forty years in business partnerships.

StablesOnline.com strengths come from its many years of experience in the industry and from John Fulton, John Fulton Bloodstock Agent a long time friend, assistant, consultant and representative who is currently involved for over thirty-five years in the racing industry as a trainer, owner, breeder and bloodstock agent; who has trained and purchased Grade I winners and is a representative at all major domestic and international sales.

Team StablesOnline.com will consist of professional agents and veterinarians who shall be involved in the selection and purchasing process of each horse and include experienced professional management in arranging, coordinating, monitoring and evaluating all of the operations in the horses' careers. Further, StablesOnline.com will retain the services of appropriate leading trainers for horses determined to be best suited for national and/or international racing.

StablesOnline.com vision (short term and long term goals) is for the partners to enjoy racing these colts at premier racetracks nationally and/or internationally for the purpose of creating major stallion prospects. In today's stallion market a top major stakes winning colt can be worth USD20m to USD25m, or more.

Specialties

Thoroughbred investments involve risk and the partnership format inherently spreads that risk amongst its investors. The investors pooling together their assets will be able to participate at a higher level of quality than an individual for his/her own account. The management then has the capability to assess markets for value, select outstanding quality assets for the partnership and engage the services of top agents, farms, trainers and veterinarians for the partnership and its horses.
