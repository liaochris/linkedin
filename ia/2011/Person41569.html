Steve Kwan's Summary

Steve Kwan is a user experience designer from Vancouver, BC. He is currently the Director of User Experience for Zen Point Design Ltd.

Steve has scoped, designed and constructed solutions for major clients such as: Nokia, Alliance Atlantis, the State of California, the Province of Alberta, the Province of British Columbia, the Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory, and many others. Steve now brings his wealth of experience and knowledge to Zen Point.

Steve is an honours graduate from the British Columbia Institute of Technology, where he received an honours degree in human-computer interaction (HCI).

Specialties

User experience design, usability, user interface, web design, human-computer interaction, information architecture, web design, accessibility, usability.
