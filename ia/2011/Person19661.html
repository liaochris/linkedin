Jenna Pere's Summary

J Carter Marketing News Channel has the expertise to place, execute and manage online campaigns for hundreds of advertisers. This can be done directly with the client or through many of the fine agencies J. Carter Marketing works with.

The all encompassing News Channel is comprised of the largest and smallest news organizations (websites) in the USA. The channel is fragmented into unique verticals; business, sports, health, entertainment, and of course local and national news.

J. Carter Marketing creates innovative and effective advertisement campaigns with precise deployment. The standard online advertising metrics are utilized.

This includes: Pre-Roll Video, In-Banner Video, Targeting, Re-Targeting, Local Geographical Targeting (city/state/zip code), Behavioral Targeting and Rich Media.

All of J Carter Marketing's clients receive an overwhelming commitment to customer service.

Specialties

Creating & Implementing Innovative & Effective AD Campaigns with Precise Deployment via our Online News Verticals.
