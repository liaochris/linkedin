Ann Pallo’s Summary

My skillset ranges the full spectrum of web design and development. My current focus is on backend development, using languages such as PHP to develop fully functional web applications, especially social media apps for Facebook. I am also placing heavy focus on iPhone, iPad and Android development. This skillset is complemented by my proficency in database design and development, server administration, and of course front end development using HTML5, XHTML, CSS and Javascript/JQuery.

My career has been focused primarily towards working in advertising. I immensely enjoy the fast paced, creative environment of advertising, and enjoy the ability to always work with new technologies. I work very well with clients, and have succeeded in not only development, but also promoting the work with the client.

I love my career and it is a hobby for me as well. I created the Atlanta Web Programmers Association to create an environment for programmers to learn and network. I also enjoy graphic design, creating music, caving, road biking, and video games.

I'm a member of the Atlanta Interactive Marketing Association, the Atlanta Ad Club, the National Speleological Society, and of course, the Atlanta Web Programmers Association, as well as various development and advertising related user groups.

I am pleased to announce the results of the 2007 Addy Awards, and being awarded a Silver Addys for my development work on Buffalo Wild Wings with 22Squared/WestWayne.
