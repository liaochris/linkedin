Alana Joy's Summary

Alana Joy is a digital strategist with a passion for creative brands and innovative startups. She also provides web/graphic design services with a flair for WordPress customizations. She was the mastermind behind Facebook Protest, which gained national media attention raising awareness about the importance of privacy and transparency online. She was featured in the CNBC documentary "The Facebook Obsession". [clip from segment: http://bit.ly/FBcnbc]

Alana was co-Host of This Week In Twitter and is a former co-Managing Director of Girls In Tech‘s Los Angeles chapter. She was on the advisory board for Social Media Week LA and has spoken at Wordcamp LA.

When she’s not writing lyrics for her self-produced music projects, she’s blogging on any one of her numerous websites, and does the occasional tech post for LA Weekly. She won an LA Web Award for being “LA’s Best Most Shameless Self-Promoter”: and she finds that just as amusing as you do.
