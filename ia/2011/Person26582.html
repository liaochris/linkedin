Lissa Steffen's Summary

Strong understanding of the roles quality, professionalism and integrity play for organizations today, with previous management, recruiting, and human resources experience, Solid experience in recruitment with progressive, fast paced companies.

In addition to recruiting and development responsibilities, I have worked closely with my teams to manage the firm recruitment program from inception to offer. Closing capabilities are well-developed from my experience in client contract negotiation and corporate recruiting. I have negotiated contracts from $23,000 to $435,000 with over 50 different clients at industry high closing rates. I have participated in and managed a number of large, successful events in my career, ranging from events with Diversity Best Practices, the Anti-Defamation League, and the NAACP. Considerable experience in training, management, and teamwork development.

I have a history of strong regional staff management, working closely with teams on individual development plans, and understanding my role to assist and promote organizational success. Actively seek upward and peer feedback and recognize its importance in organization success.

My background includes Global Recruitment, Talent Acquisition experience in the US, Canada, EU, Japan and Mexico. I have worked in all sides of recruitment including sales and new business development through experience in field, corporate and agency environments.


Certifications: AIRS Elite Certified Recruitment Expert (ECRE), Certified Social Sourcing Certification (CSSR), Certified Internet Recruiter (CIR), Certified Diversity Recruiter (CDR), DDI Targeted Selection Administrator and Trainer
