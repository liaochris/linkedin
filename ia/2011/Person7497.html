Caterina Fake's Summary

Caterina is co-founder of Hunch.com, a decision-making site that gets better the more people use it.

Caterina also co-founded Flickr (www.flickr.com), built at Ludicorp R & D Ltd. Flickr is almost certainly the best photo sharing and management application in the world.

Previously, Caterina was at Interval Research as a Member of the Research Staff, and served as Art Director at Salon.com.

Her primary area of interest is social software.
