John Morris's Summary

At the end of 2010 I joined the global IT Hosting company, Rackspace as VP Customer Experience for their international business.

So back to corporate-life (well almost – although it has a $4bn market cap – it still feels like a new business) after 10 years in ecommerce/ start-ups!

During 2010 I spent time consulting at Move With Us (www.movewithus.co.uk), a b2b property services company, helping them develop their direct to consumer strategy - and also as COO at E-Trader Group ( a VC-backed start-up that operates a b2b marketplace, trading clearance/overstock goods between retailers and traders - www.stockshifters.com. )

Prior to that I was COO at Viagogo Ltd (www.viagogo.co.uk) for 4 years – Europe’s leading secondary ticket exchange. I joined the business pre-launch and had great fun building the out the people and processes.

I have considerable experience in taking business through change and rapid growth, both in a corporate and early-stage business environments. The early part of my career was spent in the travel industry helping establish the direct-sell and ecommerce divisions for Thomas Cook. I then joined lastminute.com post-IPO, as their lead operations expert, helping them scale effectively across Europe and then streamlining their UK operations prior to the acquisition by Travelocity.

I thoroughly enjoy working in a dynamic environment where I can utilise my broad general management skill set. I am also a qualified life coach and co-founded the work-life balance consultancy The Well Room. (www.thewellroom.com)

Specialties

general management, online operations, contact centres, outsoucing, people development
