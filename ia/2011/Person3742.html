Arthur Charles Van Wyk’s Summary

I am a Brand Evangelist, Speaker, Blogger, New Media Marketing Navigator and general Agent of Change.
I head up Brand Marketing at an importer/distributor to the creative industries, I am the Creative Head of an internet marketing consultancy and I am the Founder & Principal of a school of blogging & social media.

I studied BCom (Industrial Psychology) at the University of the Western Cape in the mid 90s.
In 1998 I joined a ticketing company as the Creative Head where I was - in addition to revolutionising the ticketing industry using internet-based technology - part of pioneering contact centre assistance via Instant Messaging.
I simultaneously launched Urbantainment - the first social network (we called them virtual communities back then) for the South African entertainment industry.

After relocating to Durban, I joined MTN where I conceptualised and developed the biggest internal company portal in the South African mobile telephony industry.

In 2006 I launched a design & branding agency that developed identities and branding strategies for countless startup companies in the city of Durban.
