Susan Guarneri's Summary

"Empowering best-fit job and career transitions with smart insights, courage, and heartfelt support"

My approach to career and job transition springs from the notion that you do not have to be miserable in your job! In fact, you can even enjoy it, grow and learn, and make a good living. I use proven personal branding and career assessment tools (tapping into right-brain and left-brain content), which includes both formal career tests and more creative assignments, to help you establish your benchmarks for a best-fit career.

What sets me apart from most career counselors is that I take a holistic approach AND I incorporate the 360Reach personal branding assessment and coaching. It's important that you understand your authentic personal brand and the compelling differentiators that will grab attention with your selected target employers.

Career assessments are not just for career changers. If you are interested in improving your management and leadership abilities in areas such as coaching, employee motivation, team building and improved communications, then let me assist you with insightful and relevant career assessments and executive coaching.

My qualifications: 25 years' experience in career consulting, outplacement, job search coaching, resume writing, corporate training, and management development for individuals and groups, including Fortune 500 companies.

Key credentials: ONLY National Certified Career Counselor in the world also certified in Online Identity Management, Personal Branding, Career Coaching, Resume Writing, and Distance Counseling.

Co-author of Job Search Bloopers: Every Mistake You Can Make on the Road to Career Suicide and How to Avoid Them (CareerPress 2008).
