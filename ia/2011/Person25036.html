Kristen Stein's Summary

I have been a professional contemporary fine artist since 2000. I currently have artwork online through my website http://www.StudioArtworks.com. My artwork is also available in several galleries and boutiques throughout the United States.If you are a business owner interested in carrying my products, please visit my website for more information about wholesale orders.

My goal is to continue to create beautiful works of art fueled by passion and imagination. I'd like to further expand my artwork into galleries, boutiques, retail catalogs, and set design for television and motion pictures. I'd also like to broaden the reach of my artwork through licensing and through the help of an artist representative or agent.

In addition to LinkedIn, you can stay up to date on Twitter: http://twitter.com/kristensteinart or facebook: http://artist.to/kristensteinfineart

Specialties

Contemporary Artist specializing in a wide-range of art and styles:: Series include art deco cubism, modern minimalist atmospheric landscapes, whimsical enchanted gardens and custom whimsical pet portraits. Kristen also creates handcrafted jewelry and handbags featuring her original work.

Kristen's work is available through her website, or you can request a custom commissioned painting through her site. http://www.StudioArtworks.com .
