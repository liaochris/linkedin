Allison Grayce Nold's Summary

As a professional graphic and web designer, I bring a range of art and design disciplines to every project I work on. From brand strategy and user interface design to creative writing and front-end development, my work exemplifies an appreciation for complete brand experiences.

I utilize my strengths from concept and strategy through final artwork and production – having done so for brands such as Outback Steakhouse, Wild Wing Café, Tribridge, Tampa Bay Technology Forum (TBTF), and a number of emerging Tampa Bay companies – one of which happens to be my own!

“You’re the Youth: The Future of Democracy” is an organization I founded in early 2010 that aims to assist in bridging the generation gap between the American youth and politics. It has been established with the goal to educate, engage and mobilize the youth by leveraging the potential of new media. With consistent visitors from over 150 countries and all 50 states, these efforts are proving to be on its way to success.

As a recent graduate from the Art Institute of Tampa with my BFA in Web Design and Interactive Media, I have been awarded both a Gold and Silver ADDY award and featured on over a dozen web design showcase galleries. As a member of the American Advertising Federation (AAF), Ad2 Tampa Bay and American Institute of Graphic Arts (AIGA), chances are if I’m not behind a computer or with my nose in a sketchbook – I’ll be participating as an active member in the design community.

Specialties

Brand Strategy, User Interface Design, Graphic Design, HTML & CSS and Creative Writing
