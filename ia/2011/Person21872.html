John Farrell's Summary

Accomplished business consultant with expertise in market trend research, analysis, forecasting, target market optimization, and competitive positioning. Over twenty years experience deciphering technology and consulting market trends with specialty in IT services and business consulting markets. Now committed to ending corporate missteps in risk management by identifying and communicating the best practices, products, and consulting services that support corporate strategic decision making in relation to risks.

Specialties
