David Westhof's Experience

Business Unit Manager

BCA Europe

Privately Held; Automotive industry

January 2011 – Present (3 months)

As Business Unit Manager I am responsible for the Belgian subsidiary.

Thanks to years of experience and expertise, both local and international, BCA is the reference on the second-hand car market.

BCA Belgium began its activities in Belgium in 1992 as a national car auction. These vehicles are mostly end of contract cars from leasing companies, manufacturers and short-term rental companies.

BCA is the only company in Belgium to offer a complete remarketing service, through a unique combination of three concepts. The physical auction, BCA Online (an electronic sales platform) and the BCA Live Online (the physical auction in Wavre which can be followed on the Internet via live streaming).

BCA Belgium offers both the buyer and the seller the advantage of only working with one partner. BCA offers the seller the advantage that as a financially strong group, it can guarantee payment. With the buyer, BCA works according to the principle “buy, collect & go”: the vehicle can be picked up immediately after payment. In addition, BCA Car Auction deals with the entire VAT administration and document flow process and can organise transport throughout Europe at the customer’s request.
