Arlene Hauben's Summary

I have been a marketing writer and public relations professional for more than 30 years in New York City and South Florida. Many of my clients have been in real estate, engineering and construction, and technical disciplines so I am accustomed to writing about complex subjects for industry publications. I have always been involved in getting publicity in consumer magazines and newspapers and get a thrill from the accomplishment.

For the past 9 years, I am a writer and contributing editor at The Prepaid Press, a monthly trade publication covering prepaid payment methods, including debit cards and gift cards. My freelance practice, Scribe Tribe, Inc., concentrates on writing marketing communications for small and mid-sized businesses, and I work with design professionals to produce all types of presentation materials.

My new blog is about creating news to get publicity, providing helpful advice and pointers to entrepreneurs and professionals. It's all about creating persuasive communications to highlight and reinforce expertise. I believe in combining traditional public relations with web strategies and social media marketing. The goal is always to help businesses grow and make them more profitable and attractive to stakeholders and customers.

Specialties

I turn technical information into clear and simple language for the general public. Business to business public relations campaigns for professional services firms.
