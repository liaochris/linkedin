Mark Stasiuk’s Summary

CG Supervisor/Co-Founder -- Fusion CI Studios.

Mark's studio specializes exclusively in fluid & particle fx, providing fluid assets, rendered fluid fx elements, pipeline development, fluid & particle fx consultation and bid support for vfx studios, production companies and creative agencies. Typically, Fusion collaborates with other vfx studios, creating the fluid assets for them. The studios then light, texture, render and comp to retain creative control of their project, atho Fusion also provides rendered images if clients prefer. Rather than competing with other vfx studios, Fusion offers a nich service in a complex area of vfx. It's a great working model!

As fluid fx are still one of the most difficult areas in vfx to achieve realistic looks and behaviors, Mark's experience, his proprietary methods & technologies, his custom scripting abilities, and of course his agility on molten rock, have been a great resource to his clients.

With a PhD in Fluid Dynamics, Mark began his career investigating active volcanoes. Now using his skills for evil instead of good, Mark has created the fluid assets for a number of films, including GI Joe, The Curious Case of Benjamin Button, National Treasure: Book of Secrets, Nim's Island (trailer), The Guardian, and Poseidon. His studio provides fluid fx for commercials and television as well.

See www.fusioncis.com for full details and reel.

Mark Stasiuk’s Specialties:

CG Fluid FX Specialist. RealFlow Expert/RealFlow Artist. Specialist in physically-based animation: particles, rigid body, soft body and fluid dynamics. Main tools are RealFlow, Maya, XSI and Cinema4D. High-level expertise in Python scripting and C++.
