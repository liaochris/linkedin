Joanne Turner’s Summary

I was a civil servant for 20 years in various Government departments and roles, with particular specialism in water and building related policy areas. I am a qualified manager and regard my areas of expertise as policy analysis and development, strategy, communication and people management. Latterly I have worked in the construction sector, as Sustainability Manager at the Concrete Centre, liaising with the rest of the sector to develop a sustainable concrete strategy, and as a Senior Consultant for BRE Global Ltd. My current post is a new departure, in local government - project managing a nationwide project called Tell Us Once for Surrey County Council.
My key specialism is getting things done - the ability to deliver, even in difficult circumstances. I also have extensive experience of working in and with government and in a variety of organisations both public and private.
