★Brett Woudenberg’s Summary

Brett Woudenberg is currently COO for Gaggle, a rapidly growing, safe online learning environment for K-12 schools headquartered in Bloomington, Illinois. His current focus is international market development to expand Gaggle's global footprint.

Previously, Brett was CEO at Trailblazer (aka Campus K12), a professional development management solution for educators. As the strategic and operational leader of Trailblazer, Brett oversaw all aspects of sales and channel management, content partnerships, product management, marketing, operations and customer service until its sale in 2009.

Prior to his ventures in the education sector, Brett successfully applied his strategic leadership abilities in C-level roles for private ventures including a Top 100 regional commercial brokerage and a boutique private equity group.

In the early stages of his career, Brett forged his analytical and business development skills as a commercial loan officer at a large regional bank in the Midwest.
