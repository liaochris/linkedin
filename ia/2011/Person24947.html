Kevin Bolen's Summary

Kevin R. Bolen
Partner
Innosight, LLC

Kevin is a Partner with Innosight, where he consults with Fortune 500 firms in the technology, consumer and healthcare sectors.

Kevin has balanced his career among management consulting, strategic planning, and marketing leadership roles in a variety of high growth environments. He has worked for and consulted with Fortune 500, mid-sized, and start-up enterprises in the IT software and services sectors, mobile devices, consumer, retail, travel, and transportation industries.

Prior to joining Innosight in 2007, Kevin served as the Chief Marketing Officer of Lionbridge Technologies (NASDAQ: LIOX), a $450M global outsourcing company. Before Lionbridge, Kevin led product management and marketing efforts for several venture-backed technology firms. He began his career with IBM Global Services, spending six years in a variety of consulting and strategic planning roles.

Kevin holds an MBA in Marketing and Management from the Stern School of Business at New York University and a BBA Summa Cum Laude in International Business from the Lubin School of Business at Pace University.

His commentary and/or bylined articles have appeared in Forbes, The New York Times, BusinessWeek, Harvard Business Online, TIME, Pharmaceutical Executive, CNET and InfoWorld. He is a regular speaker at corporate, analyst and industry trade events.
