Sonny Caberwal's Summary

Professionally, I began my career as a corporate attorney in NYC, before leaving to pursue the entrepreneurial path. I've been fortunate to experience the startup world across a broad variety of industries, from the world of consumer products (tea), to mobile and social media. I'm currently co-founder and COO of Exclusively.In, which is a multi-category e-commerce company backed by Accel Partners and Helion Venture Partners, and which provides Indian fashion, home decor, jewelry, and travel services to customers around the world.

Throughout it all, I've tried to do as many things as possible to broaden my personal experience. I'm currently serving as a visiting lecturer at Munich Business School each semester, and have continued a lifelong interest in music as a percussionist with a variety of groups, including Thievery Corporation. I've also been featured as one of the first turban-wearing Sikh models for brands like Kenneth Cole and magazines such as GQ.
