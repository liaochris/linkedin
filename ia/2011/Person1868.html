Alvah Parker’s Summary

Alvah is a career and business coach who also volunteers as a SCORE counselor. Her professional experience includes that of sales executive, sales manager,quality manager, and career coach.

At Parker Associates Alvah works with attorneys who want to develop a practice that is consistent with their own values and needs. Clients discover what works best for them in the areas of client development, time management, cash flow and staffing.

For those who want to move on to another career Alvah works with lawyers and others using a program developed by Laura Berman Fortgang called the "Now What?" Program. Alvah is an Authorized Facilitator of this program.

Alvah also works with people in transition who aren't sure how to do a job search. She helps them to use the latest job search tools and techniques so that they will find suitable work more quickly.

As a SCORE Business Counselor Alvah works with new business owners and business owners who want to grow their businesses and make them more profitable.
