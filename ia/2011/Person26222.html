Larry Posner’s Summary

Senior executive with a successful record in consulting and operating roles, with a proven record developing innovative growth strategies that have improved sales and profits for a broad range of business-to-business services companies. Exceptional talent for quickly identifying and correcting key revenue drivers impacting underperformance, and maximizing new market opportunities to significantly accelerate revenue growth up to $1B.

As a transformational leader, I have created organizational energy for change that has turned around underperforming and down-trending organizations, increasing new customer acquisition 200% and net growth up to 300%.

I have a strong ability to evaluate unmet market requirements and devise actionable solutions that consistently improved business results, including outsourcing and product initiatives that led to $175M+ increase in net income.

My successful consulting experiences with Andersen Consulting/Accenture, Coopers & Lybrand, Ernst & Whinney, and The Alexander Group have given me the consultative skills to select the best customers and channel partners, while aligning limited marketing, sales, and service resources, to attract, penetrate, and retain profitable business. My areas of specialty include the healthcare, insurance, financial services, telecommunications, and other B2B sectors.
