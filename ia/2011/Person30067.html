Michael Grollman’s Summary

Renaissance throwback, in sneakers and a tie. Disruptive technologies a specialty. Strategic builder of successful engineering-driven businesses units and startups. Results-oriented, management professional with over 20+ years experience driving strategy, sales and service within highly competitive technical markets in information systems. Creative and unique thinker. Deep diver into a number of critical technologies, especially on the software and network architecture fronts, from the embedded sensors to the enterprise network. Oversaw successfully design/proposals/sales/project/executive management of numerous client IT infrastructure efforts, including a 5000 site network, a multimillion dollar new product launch, service programs for over 700 engineers and developers under direction, and a variety of international infrastructure projects involving tens of thousands of users. Capital formation, raised over $2M in new equity via PIPEs at last position. Strong presentation skills and experienced public speaker with groups sized anywhere from 1 to 1,000. Excellent group facilitator and team leader. 10+ years of globe trotting managing domestic and international teams.

Key clients have included: ARCO, HP, Tektronix, Wells Fargo, US Bank, BP, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Kaiser Permanente, US Army, US Army Corps of Engineers, US Navy, Bank of America, Intel, Boeing, PACCAR, Freightliner, States of Alaska / Oregon / Washington / Arizona, including state universities, dozens of county and municipal governments and K-12 schools, and many others.
