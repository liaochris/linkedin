Joseph Rusty Bishop, PhD’s Summary

Rusty is an entrepreneur at heart who excels at strategic business development and creating new products to increase your sales and marketing efforts through mobile devices and training.

In the spring of 2010, Rusty and business partner, Mark Walker, conceived and developed FatStax, a sales and opportunity App for the iPad. The App combines the team's insights into field sales, user interface design, and databases to take Sales Forces where they have never been able to go.

Instant Product Search at the Point of Sale.

During the process, Rusty has become a student of Sales Techniques with iPad and enjoys teaching others successful tricks with the iPad.

Rusty also has a deep understanding of Web 2.0 and how consumers interact with the web including product search, seeking help, and manuscript search.
