Michael Rodriguez's Summary

Michael has been in the banking industry since 2000. He has worked his way up from an entry level position to running an entire mortgage operation.

Michael has become a sought after resource for the local real estate community. Michael routinely teaches classes for local real estate agents to help them understand the many dynamic pieces that make up a market place. He has also appeared on the local CBS affiliate to help explain the foreclosure crisis.

Michael ultimately wants to leverage his knowledge and understanding of the financial markets to help his network of friends, clients, and partners find and maximize the opportunities they have to build wealth.
