Anthony Weight's Summary

I began my business to business sales career working within the hospitality industry as an Account Manager, growing and developing new business for the group.

Following a successful time with Hayley Conference Centres I moved in to recruitment with WYSIWYG, I started out in a junior level role within the company and after a successful period I was subsequently promoted in to Account Manager / Business Development Manager position and was responsible for winning and maintaining new business for the company, my focus was on the New Media and IT industries.

I then moved on to work for IdealPeople where I continued to specialise in New Media.

I am now working for Amasco Associates again working in the IT and New Media sectors coverving the whole of the UK.

I believe networking is key to success within modern business and would welcome hearing from likeminded professionals that either work within the New Media Industry or would be interested in using my services either now or in the future.
