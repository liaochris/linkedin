John Mancini’s Summary

Over 25 years of progressively expanding responsibilities with three major non-profit organizations. Two of these -- the Association for Information and Image Management (AIIM) and the American Electronics Association (AEA), are among the largest technology-focused trade associations in the U.S. The Public Affairs Council is a professional association focused on government and public affairs executives. Currently President and CEO of AIIM, International, an association with 40 staff members and annual revenues of $7 million, focused on the document and content management technologies that are key enablers of process change at leading companies.

John Mancini’s Specialties:

Extensive public speaking and public relations work
Background in a variety of chapter models and implementation strategies.
Conversant and experienced in major technology issues and trends.
Experienced in both trade and professional non-profit models
Experienced in non-profit mergers and acquisitions.
