Tara Deck’s Summary

I believe the best way to truly educate yourself is by living the life you were given, one mistake at a time, and then proudly carrying those experiences with you to wherever life carries you next.

While happily raising my children for the last 13 years, I have had the opportunity to volunteer and work for many different schools, churches, businesses and individuals. At 37 years old, I can absolutely tell you what I am NOT good at.

I have worked for love, I have worked for money, I have worked for compassion, and admittedly - maybe even sometimes just for greed, obsession, or power. But one thing I can honestly tell you, is that I have never worked toward something I didn't have a passion for.

My main goal in all things, is simply to enjoy the journey we call life. We only get one chance to be who we are called to be, so why not risk it all and just love the way we live and what we do?
