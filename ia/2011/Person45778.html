Whitney Taylor's Summary

I also complete freelance for other clients across the U.S. through my freelance design company, XCentric Design LLC. I have previously completed internships at Nickelodeon, E! Entertainment Television, The Electric Quilt Software Company, and Velocity Sports Publishing.

I seek to obtain a position in user experience, web, or interactive design where I have a wide range of responsibilities and tasks. I love learning new programming and design techniques and enjoy the entire design process from start to finish. I look forward to being a part of a creative and dynamic team as I work hand and hand with talented individuals as we create, design, and implement the new cutting edge ideas and solutions of tomorrow.
