Todd Enghauser’s Summary

Marketing executive with broad experience in within the sales, operations, technology, project and product management areas of an organization, with particular expertise in business start-up, brand awareness and performance improvement initiatives. I have been involved in internet marketing and building internet businesses since 1999.

My current goal is to create a platform where small and mid-size businesses can compete and succeed within their industries by providing solutions for marketing while also adapting their practices for management and procurement to ensure success. As the heartbeat of America remains to be small businesses I look at it as building a better world one small business at a time.
