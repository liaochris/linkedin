Skip Blankley’s Summary

I am a creative entrepreneur with a passion for music, art and business. I have a background in sales, management and music. I am currently building 3 companies from the ground up and assisting in the development of another. I am perfecting my business skills with the intention of building and selling many businesses over the next couple of decades. Ultimately I will use the skills I develop starting and building businesses to empower the next generation of creative entrepreneurs to do the same through workshops and seminars aimed at empowering a creative economy that is a self sustaining, forward thinking, envelope pushing, revolutionary power house that restructures our economic foundation based on ideas that make a difference.
