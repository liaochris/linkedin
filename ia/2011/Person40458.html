Simon Kerr's Experience

Senior Creative Designer

Eclipse Creative Consultants

Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry

November 2004 – February 2011 (6 years 4 months)

In November 2004 I joined Eclipse Creative Consultants; the Chester based full service advertising, design and marketing agency as a senior creative designer.

My role comprised of unsupervised creative direction for all major clients, working both reactively and proactively on highly demanding B2B and B2C accounts.

Primary day-to-day responsibilities included devising creative concepts (personally preferred through divergent production), art direction, graphic design and layout solutions for print and digital media, usually to extreme deadlines.

As well as personal daily tasks, I maintained an influence to assist through creative guidance and shared production expertise.

Directly accountable for all design and production aspects of each project that fell within my remit.

Senior Creative Designer

RB&Co

Design industry

March 1997 – November 2004 (7 years 9 months)

In March 1997 I joined Richard Barber & Company; the Manchester based full service advertising agency. As a senior creative designer, my role involved design and production direction for an array of media, primarily operating across most industrial, technological, professional and public sectors.

Tailoring towards specific commercial property and allied ‘Business to Business’ accounts, day-to-day responsibilities included full colour brochure design and production for local and national property developments across the country, comprehensive integrated marketing campaigns inclusive of national corporate advertising concepts, as well as on-site promotions and web authoring.

Directly accountable for all design and production aspects of each project.

Credits:
Introduction of interactive design and production methods for web design/publishing. Full creative design and production of Manchester Eastlands ‘Sportcity’ promotional literature that supported retail and leisure opportunities surrounding the Commonwealth Games site in New East Manchester.
