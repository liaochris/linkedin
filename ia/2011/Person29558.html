Meg Forsberg’s Summary

While earning a Mass Communications-PR degree from VCU, worked as Classified Ad Manager for Landmark publication, STYLE Weekly. After an internship in Press & Publicity at WRC-TV, in DC and one at The Martin Agency, became the Sales Manager for Lamar Advertising’s VA Logos, Inc. for 3 yrs. World Investor Link was next as Bus.Development Manager + then Channel Partner Manager. In 2000, joined Trader Publishing Co. now known as Dominion Enterprises. There she was Product Manager of Yacht Trader Magazine, Marketing Manager of For Rent Magazines + National Marketing Director for Harmon Media Group. In February 2005, she joined Chartway Federal Credit Union as Marketing Manager for VA, NC & RI. She spearheaded the group of financial literacy ambassadors at the credit union organizing opportunities for these Chartway volunteers to teach financial literacy for free in the community. In 2007, Meg became the Director of Bus. Development leading a team of regional managers to grow new + existing corp relationships in the 10 states where Chartway has branches. She concentrates on generating new product sales to existing corporate clients through relationship building and developing effective marketing campaigns. Creates RFPs + gains approval from the National Credit Union Administration to expand the field of membership for their credit union. In 09, she had a record breaking achievement of developing over 50 new Select Employer Group relationships, creating over 85,000 potential new members for the credit union. VP of Marketing for the Hampton Roads American Marketing Association in May '04. Led the association to new levels of success as their President from 2007-2009. She remains on their board as a Member at Large. Other community organizations that she is involved in: The Downtown Norfolk Council, SMEI of HR (Sales and Marketing Excellence International of Hampton Roads), Hampton Roads Money$mart & the VA Credit Union League’s Financial Education Committee.
