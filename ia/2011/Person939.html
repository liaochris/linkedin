Aisha I. Jefferson's Summary

I am an award-winning media professional with eight years experience delivering compelling news and feature stories for print, broadcast and online media outlets. A multi-skilled self-starter, I am able to edit and shoot video, write a good headline and am comfortable researching, writing and editing on a wide range of topics. I am a naturally inquisitive person who asks good questions and is known for quickly gaining the trust of sources. These sources include community leaders, crime victims, politicians, Fortune 500 executives and A-list celebrities.

Aisha I. Jefferson's Education

Northern Illinois University

BA, Journalism, Africana Studies

Aisha majored in journalism at Northern Illinois University where she worked as a reporter and editor for its award-winning campus newspaper, The Northern Star. Her college experiences include receiving several honors: two-time recipient of the Scripps League Journalism Scholarship, a recipient of the Northern Illinois Newspaper Association Scholarship, and recipient of the James Copley Foundation Journalism Scholarship. She was a member of the Northern Illinois University Honors Club and received a Northern Illinois University Institutional Tuition Waiver based on her academic performance, journalistic achievements, and contributions to the enhancement of the quality of life at the university.
