Alison Cummings’s Summary

Creative, versatile, bilingual Web strategist experienced in management of business-to-business Web sites, employee intranets and related projects. Entrepreneurial, analytical thinker. Accomplished writer and editor, with Master’s degree in Journalism. Social media savvy.

Led successful launch of major Web site, managing internal team and vendors. Revolutionized the way the organization viewed and used the Web. Responsible for $1.5 million budget.
