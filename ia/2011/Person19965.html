Jeff Perreca's Summary

Throughout my career, I've worked on every type of medium possible: Digital, Print, Outdoor, Radio, Local and National TV and Primetime Television. I've also worked for clients in the Auto, Banking, Healthcare, Retail, and Wireless industries.

So what does that all mean? Nothing really. I believe you have to constantly prove yourself in not just our industry, but today's world. I love challenges and hate doing things that have been done before. I'm always looking for new ways I can push myself creatively, profesionally and personally.
