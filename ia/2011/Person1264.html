A'Lelia Bundles’s Summary

Author of award-winning historical nonfiction and biographies. Engaging motivational speaker. 30 year career in network television news as an Emmy-award winning producer with NBC News and ABC News and an executive with ABC News. Member of several boards including Columbia University Board of Trustees, Foundation for the National Archives, Madam Walker Theatre Center and Radcliffe Institute Advisory Board.
