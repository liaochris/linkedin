Fuzzy is the Founder & CEO of PTS Treatment, which is an international alternative clinician/therapy practice where he brings balance and normalcy into the lives of female and male military veterans, and their families with Post Traumatic Stress (PTS) and Chronic Disease.

PTS Treatment incorporates a 12,000 year old alternative process that identifies the causes of the trauma symptoms, provides individualized therapy to prevent reoccurrence, and reintegrates a balanced mind and a healthy body.

He has studied in over twenty-eight countries and practiced Ayurvedic, Chinese, and Aboriginal Medicine for more than thirty years. In his travels, he became a Shiatsu Master, Medical Intuitive, and an Urban Shaman.
