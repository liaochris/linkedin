Adviser for Communication Manager

Junior Observatory of Sustainability

Non-Profit Organization Management industry

October 2010 – Present (6 months)

The JOS will allow young people from the most diverse cultural backgrounds to work together in order to publish each semester a report. That report will show what is being done
in matters of action and cooperation for the ecological, social and cultural sustainability of the world.

In that report, there will be articles that expose the successful practices implemented in four types of institutions:
1) Governments (local and national realm)
2) Inter-Governmental (International Organizations)
3) Academical (educative and research centers)
4) NGO’s (including business sector)

Mission.
1) To promote international cooperation for the Sustainable Development by bringing to the youth useful information on the existent initiatives of Governments, International Organizations and NGO’s.
2) To strengthen the channels of communication among Civil Society and Governments by encouraging young people to design strategies to develop that dialogue.
3) To inform on successful sustainability policies implemented in the local realm, in order to promote the cooperation among local institutions.
