David Guzman’s Summary

Formal Expertise:

I am a strategic communicator, versatile writer, social marketer, methodical researcher, visual storyteller, and well-versed organizational positioner. My vision and in-depth planning provide the means of which I use to assist individuals and organizations in their effort to effectively convey their story to: employees, customers, policymakers, the media, and various other stake holders.

Recent work has drawn me into the field of campaign development alongside seed-stage startups and non-profits including: Innovate Wireless Health, EvolveSix.com, and Positive-Footprint.org in an effort to execute successful, content-driven, marketing strategies, that generate results.

Community Outreach:

Actively engaged in the community and mentoring youth since the age of 14, I have coached a youth soccer team throughout the course of the past 9 years and successfully managed a team of volunteers that impact the lives of hundreds of youth residing in Central Texas on an ongoing basis.

Recently, my efforts have led to the establishment of Positive-Footprint, an organization that serves to empower youth through volunteerism, professional mentorship, and access to resources, with the end objective of cultivating successful, high-impact, youth-led, service projects.
