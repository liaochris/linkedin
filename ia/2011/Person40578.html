Kevin Skarritt’s Summary

GOALS:
To act as a Sherpa to our clients ...Guiding them along the circuitous and confusing new path of business development and marketing called "Social Media." By teaching business owners how to embrace these new technologies and helping them to understand the new psychology of their customers, we can help businesses not only survive but to thrive and grow beyond their wildest dreams.

EXPERIENCE:
Between 1997 and 2009 I owned and operated a boutique design and web development firm in NH and Northern CA. Acorn Creative's primary goal was to guide businesses along a path of power and success. In the late 90's, the services needed were primarily web development, as businesses embraced and adapted to their new web marketing strategies. Later, as these same businesses realized that competing in a global environment required they present themselves more powerfully (via better brand strategies), Acorn Creative adapted its own suite of world class services to continue supporting their customer base. As we passed a handful of years into the new millennium, businesses were finding the world was changing yet again ... this time to a world of social communication, networking and instantaneous online collaboration. At the front edge of this wave, we found ourselves again helping our customers as a guide along this technology path.

Since 2009, I have been assisting a small Education non-profit in Lansing to develop a robust, social network and online mentoring community.

Kevin Skarritt’s Specialties:

Brand identity development, award-winning website design, logo development, powerful branding strategies, social media and social network development, web widget development and new media marketing strategies.
