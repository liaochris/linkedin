Tracy Benson’s Summary

Senior Marketing Executive for Best Buy US Marketing including consumer brand cross-channel & digital marketing - responsible for all retail marketing and promotional strategies, vendor co-marketing & media integration strategies, $7B Vertical product marketing (including management of more than 20 key product vendor partners such as Sony, Samsung, Canon, Nikon, Beats, Bose, etc) for the portable product portfolio and cross-channel customer experience in-stores, online, on mobile.
