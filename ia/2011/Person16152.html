Glen Hellman’s Summary

Glen is a Principal at Driven Forward LLC, a firm that supports entrepreneurs and venture investors by providing strategic guidance, interim executive leadership services, and executive coaching. In addition he is a Chair for Vistage International where he serves as a professional facilitator for CEO advisory boards.

Mr. Hellman is frequently quoted on corporate governance and entrepreneurship issues in print and broadcast media. In print he’s been quoted in the Wall Street Journal, Financial Week, The Washington Post and USA Today. He has been featured on radio programs including NPR Marketplace and Voice of America. Television appearances include NBC and FOX news.

In his over-30-year career he has been the President and COO of Proxy Governance, a corporate governance watchdog; CEO of Intellipark, a manufacturer of next-generation parking meters; and CEO of Ikimbo, a developer of network communications software.

He is currently Chairman of the Board of Driven Forward and on the Board of Advisors for the University of Maryland, RH Smith School’s, Dingman Center for Entrepreneurship. He is an active member of the Capital Access Network (CAN) where he is responsible for deal flow, deal selection and coaching corporate executives seeking capital.

Mr. Hellman has experience running operations in the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, Japan, South Korea and Australia.

Glen Hellman’s Specialties:

Executive Coaching, Strategic Relations, Start-up Strategic Planning, Go-To-Market Strategies, M&A, Geographic experience in North America, Asian, and European Markets.
