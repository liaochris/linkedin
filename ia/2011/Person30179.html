Michael McDermott's Summary

Our company goal is the creation of 'sustainable excellence' in our clients through personal coaching, team coaching, leadership development and culture alignment. We focus on top individuals and teams in organizations and enable them to perform at their highest level. This is done using the engagements above to build skills and knowledge, increase self-awareness and shift sub-optimal mindsets and beliefs that may prevent peak performance. We also empower our clients with tools and techniques that they will use on their own, creating 'sustainable excellence'.

We have worked with major global investment banks on projects such as the alignment/coaching of management in the global equity division, team optimization/coaching in wealth management and a number of personal coaching initiatives.

Work with hedge funds has included development and delivery of leadership programs for top leaders, coaching for top leaders, coaching and leadership consulting for head traders and general advisory work.

Within the health care industry we have done work with head administrators and practitioners with a focus on performance and strategic development and execution.

We have developed a program called 'The Program in Sustainable Living' in conjunction with the Weill Cornell Medical Center. This program is for senior executives seeking to maintain or improve current levels of performance while significantly reducing stress levels and significantly increasing their level of fulfillment across all aspect of their lives.

Specialties

Leader development, coaching, management, strategy, talent management, organizational development, integration of development systems, performance management, talent reviews
