Chesley Ruffin’s Summary

Chesley Ruffin is a Certified Mortgage Planning Specialist- a designation held by less than 1% of all mortgage professionals that emphasizes more than qualifying a customer for a loan. The CMPS Designation ensures that the client is working with someone who understands the totality of their decisions in terms of taxation, cash flow, and long- and short-term financial planning strategies. The mortgage planning process ensures that the loan is suitable for the client

He also has been in the business of real estate for over 25 years as a property manager, investor, and advisor. He has helped many people find their dream home. As an advisor he has helped many investors who are having difficulty with their properties. His passion is helping people and seeing them succeed. Through real estate investing & mortgage planning his unique and sometimes counter intuitive strategies is what makes him successful. His good or bad credit no closing cost program to home ownership makes it easier for those facing financial difficulty to own a home even if they are going through foreclosure, have been foreclosed, or in bankruptcy.

Chesley also teaches seminars on credit repair & management , equity & debt management, and fiscal literacy. These seminars are approved by the Financial Planning Association for continue education credit. He is working on having it approved for continue education for the National Association of Realtors.
