Taryn Pisaneschi (@TarynP) ★'s Summary

In the new relationship economy, it's important to have a human bio brimming with personality... That being said, I'm a master brainstormer on creative marketing utilizing new and old marketing and media technologies as well as relationships and networking. I have a passion for public speaking and teaching others... and sometimes I'll even improv or rap my presentation and teaching points.

Here is my "Traditional" Bio:

Ms. Pisaneschi is an accomplished public speaker, a trainer on business networking, referral marketing and social media marketing. She is a true social media pioneer, having been immersed in social media + internet marketing since 1990.

In 2000, she received a B.S. degree in Public Relations from the Boston University School of Communications. Upon graduation, she worked with the United Way of Asheville, NC to increase donations at a time when they were experiencing a significant drop in funding. During her successful tenure, donations from her company base tripled as result of her innovative use of public speaking + new media marketing. She later moved to Atlanta + served as a legislative assistant at the GA State Assembly in the State Senate for 2 terms.

Ms. Pisaneschi joined Lenox Chem-Dry in 2002 with the goal of increasing market share + brand awareness. Using her non-traditional marketing methods, the company quadrupled its market share in 18 months. She helped build a client database of approx. 4,000 clients prior to its sale in 2008. During her tenure, Lenox Chem-Dry received the Consumer's Choice Award in Atlanta for 5 consecutive years.

In March of 2007, Ms. Pisaneschi established Women Intelligently Networking (WIN), a business women's organization that supports the success of professional entrepreneurial women through events, luncheons, keynote speakers + professional development + business skills workshops. WIN! has gained regional + national attention and continues to thrive with both offline and online events.

Specialties

Workshop facilitator on business growth, social media and relationship marketing strategies for small businesses and entrepreneurs. She educates on how to leverage both networking and online social media, Social networking educator providing workshops and public speaking, branding/identity and marketing thinktank, extreme "out of the box" thinker, extremely resourceful and detailed researcher...and yes, an expert networker.

Professional Training & Coaching industry

March 2007 – Present (4 years)

I founded Women Intelligently Networking in March of 2007 because, quite honestly, I got sick of getting hit on by older men and wanted more camaraderie and education from the organizations I was in. I became apparent that a need existed for business women and ladypreneurs to have a safe but professional space to develop professionally while cultivating powerful and productive relationships. Focusing on helping business women better understand the power of effective networking and leveraging relationships, WIN! has evolved from networking events to structured weekly luncheons, business development seminars, after-hours mixers, holiday socials, online resources and much more.
