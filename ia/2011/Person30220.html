Michael Perrault, P.E.'s Summary

I am the founder / owner of P. M. P. Associates, LLC, a privately owned New England based Civil Engineering and Land Planning consultant located at 200 North Bedford Street (Route 18) in East Bridgewater, MA.

P.M.P. Associates, LLC was started in June 1992 and provides the following:

Civil Engineering Services:

Large and small residential land development subdivision designs
Stormwater Management design & drainage analysis
Roadway design
Water distribution system design & analysis
Commercial site plan & special permit plan designs
Condominium plans
Building permit plans
Affordable housing project (Chapter 40B & 40R) design
Soil evaluation & percolation testing
Single family residential & commercial septic system designs
Environmental permitting (Conservation Commissions, MEPA and EPA )
Construction observation services & cost estimating

Land Planning & Consulting Services:

Residential & commercial land development
Conceptual design
Preliminary subdivision design
Definitive subdivision design
Due diligence land development studies and reports
Wetland resource delineation & mapping
Zoning compliance & variance services
Municipal consulting & peer review services for
Board of Health
Planning Board
Conservation Commissions
Zoning Board of Appeals
Housing Authority
Water Department
Highway Department
Department of Public Works

Over the last 17 years , P. M. P. Associates, LLC , has been an instrumental part of the residential and commercial growth in New England.

Our clients value our customized professional engineering services which are specifically organized to meet their diverse needs. Our staff knows the importance of working diligently for our clients to complete their projects on-time and within budget.

Specialties

Land development civil engineering, stormwater management & drainage, stormwater recharge systems, commercial site planning, on-site wastewater disposal systems (septic systems) for residential & commercial uses, 55+ housing development design, affordable housing project design, municipal consulting.
