Karl Ossentjuk's Summary

I am a Marketing, Sales and Product Development Executive with proven experience leading the development and implementation of strategic and tactical initiatives within highly dynamic environments. I am known for my strategic leadership, team development skills, results orientation and ability to effectively foster cross-functional partnerships to deliver business results.

My professional experience is comprised of a variety of leadership roles within the telecommunications industry. Following the completion of my MBA from the Haas School at U.C. Berkeley, I worked for a small telecom management consulting firm in San Francisco. After 9 great years in the Bay Area, my wife and I decided to move back to Colorado to start our family. Over the past 15 years, I have successfully held marketing, sales and product management roles for various cable/telecommunications companies.

I am currently leveraging my prior experience to provide consulting services to various clients as I seek out the next move in my career. I am focused on high-impact marketing or product-related leadership opportunities in the Bay Area or Denver.

Specialties

Product Management, Product Development, Pricing Analysis, Small Business Sales Leadership
