Christophe Dissaux's Summary

Christophe Dissaux is an accomplished senior sales and marketing executive with a proven track record of achievement and leadership skills in highly competitive markets.
In his career, Christophe has executive roles in international business development in Europe, the Americas and Oceania.
He has dual citizenship French and American.
