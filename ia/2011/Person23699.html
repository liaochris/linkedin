Kathleen Rickert Nelson’s Summary

I am a Fine Arts Illustrator who captures the essence of homes, businesses, and historic structures.

These images help clients appreciate where they live and work, and they create memories for the future.

Kathleen Rickert Nelson’s Specialties:

Specializes in architectural details in each illustration, bringing the buildings to life.

Listed in 2010-Preservation Trades Resource List - McHenry County Council of Historic Groups.

Created porcelain ornament design for The Field of Dreams, a Horse Rescue & Adoption, a Non-Profit 501(c)3 Organization.
