Thad Miller's Experience

President/Owner

ddsmatch.com

Financial Services industry

May 2009 – Present (1 year 11 months)

Thad Miller is a dental industry leader with fourteen years experience. His circles of influence span across the United States and include practicing dentists, dental schools, manufacturers, suppliers and beyond. Over the years he saw firsthand the difficulty dentists had when it came time to buy and sell a practice or find a partner for the practice. He wondered...“Could there be a better way?” What if the process could be professional, streamlined and efficient? Utilizing technology, yet never losing the human touch? These questions led him to found ddsmatch.com in 2009.

Miller brings to ddsmatch.com vast expertise in management, serving as Operations Manager for a national dental financial advisory firm. Responsible for marketing, partnerships and assisting dentists with practice transitions, he gained an intimate knowledge of the specific financial considerations dentists must take into account.

Prior to that, Miller served as Branch Manager for Patterson Dental Indianapolis Branch, where he was responsible for $31 million in sales, operations, budgeting and marketing the dental distribution business to more than 2,000 clients. He is an active Board Member of the National Foundation of Dentistry for the Handicapped and is on the board of directors of the Indiana Golf Foundation. He is a graduate of Indiana University.
