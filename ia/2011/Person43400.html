Timothy Lorang's Summary

Video and Social Media Consultant

I help organizations, business, and individuals develop and execute Social Media and on-line Marketing Strategies with an emphasis in video. This includes integrating video into your Social Media strategy; developing online video products; using Social Media to distribute video; and including video as part of your overall Social Media Marketing and Outreach plan. Visit www.imagemediapartners.com for more information.

Social Media Marketing

Video is just one part of a larger Social Media Marketing Strategy that includes effective use of on-line social media tools such as Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, YouTube (and other video distribution tools) Foresquare, blogging, e-mail newsletters, and many others. I help develop and implement an over-all Social Media Marketing strategy. Send me a message at tim@imagemediapartners.com to find out more.

Digital On-line and Streaming Video

I have spent over 33 years in television, 24 of those years were at the University of Washington. There I was Production Manager during the time we pioneered many aspect of digital on-line and streaming video including the first time HDTV was streamed live over the internet. My business partner Nate McQueen and I started Image Media Partners to help deal with using digital video on the internet. Visit our web site at www.imagemediapartners.com.

Video Producer, Writer and Director

I have done all aspects of video production and I am still active as a writer, producer and director. Most recently I won the 2009 Lowell Thomas Travel Journalism Gold Award for Broadcast for the Public Television program: “Richard Bangs’ Adventures with Purpose/Norway: Quest for the Viking Spirit.” For more examples of my work at www.timlorang.com

Public Speaking and Workshops

I speak at conferences and workshops on video production, on-line media and streaming video. If you would like to know more please contact me at tim@imagemediapartners.com
