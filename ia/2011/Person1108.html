Alan Parker's Summary

I'm a social media and digital communications specialist with experience across PR, WOM and social advertising. I have worked on UK, Australian, European, Asia-pacific and global campaigns across a variety of industry sectors including: gaming, technology, consumer technology, digital, sports, sponsorship, alcohol, FMCG and automotive.

Throughout my career, I've had the chance to work for some of the world's most respected brands, including: Nokia, HP, Heineken, Vodafone, Unilever, Microsoft

As a digital communications professional, my goals are focused on helping brands engage with the most relevant and rewarding audiences for them. To help them tell their brand stories in the most interesting and compelling way. Ultimately, my goal is to develop solid and robust online campaigns that demonstrate real and tangible results.
