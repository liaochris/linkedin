Bill Gibson's Summary

I've been recording, producing, performing and writing music for most of my life. I entered the book and video publishing business in 1991 as an author and course developer. To this point I've written 30 books and produced countless records. I'm also on staff at The Art Institute of Seattle, acting as the liaison between the professional audio community and the college.

Specialties

Recording engineering, producing, arranging, song writing, live sound, teaching, authoring books, and people skils
