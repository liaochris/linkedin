Jenet Ahn’s Summary

Teens and women of all ages want to look and feel pretty, radiant, unique and even sexy! Of course, it is mandatory to look our absolute best at a formal event. And don’t we love a special occasion as an opportunity to pull out the stops and look divine. Now, there is a premier resource in the Washington DC Metropolitan area for formal dresses, wedding gowns, tuxedos and accessories – Annalee’s!

The array of styles found at Annalee’s ranges from demure but elegant to flashy and fun. Annalee’s carries beautiful, trendy, fashionable, fancy, sexy, and modest long and short dresses. Dresses that will be noticed and remembered! We have the largest and best selection in Northern Virginia in stock – in sizes 00 to 32 – to fit the personality, shape and taste of every woman.

Annalee’s is the place to shop for prom, pageant, ball, gala, holiday party and wedding. The boutique is a one-stop-shopping resource for the bride, bridesmaids, mother of the bride, flower girl, groom and best man.

We have complete lines of accessories, including jewelry, shoes, evening bags, SPANX® products and much more. Our professionally-trained, friendly consultants are ready to assist you to find that perfect dress or gown. An on-site seamstress can tailor it to fit and flatter.

Annalee’s, which opened February 2008, is located in the Brambleton Town Center in Ashburn, Va., in eastern Loudoun County. People who want the best formal attire and personalized service are smart to drive the extra few miles from Maryland or Washington, DC, as well as from all over Northern Virginia.

Annalee’s is the headquarters for all of your special occasion formal wear. If we don’t have a dress, we can get it by special ordering it just for you. And we register your dress to avoid duplication at an event.

Owner Jenet Ahn comes from a family who has been in the formal wear industry for more than 25 years.

Remember, shop early for the best selection for your prom.
