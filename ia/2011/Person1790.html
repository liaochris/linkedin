Michael Antonio Canganelli's Summary

I am interested in representing clients, including lawyers, law firms, and businesses in contract positions, preferably legal (including covering hearings in Chicago, and the federal courts in Illinois, Indiana and Wisconsin, other litigation and general law), and although I currently concentrate my law practice in representing consumers in chapter seven or thirteen bankruptcies, I have also represented chapter seven, thirteen and eleven creditors, and would be happy to discuss other opportunities, especially legal, as well.

I have been licensed to practice law before the Following Courts: November 1, 1982, Supreme Court of Illinois, #6183126, Cook County, IL #52835, DuPage County, IL #10575; December 6, 1982, U.S. Court of Appeals for the Seventh Circuit, and U.S. District Courts for the: December 16, 1982; General bar, Northern District of Illinois , February 1, 1990, Trial bar, January 6, 1986, Eastern District of Wisconsin, February 14, 1986, Northern District of Indiana, May 18, 1994, Southern District of Indiana and May 10, 1995, Central District of Illinois. At various times I have been a member of the American Bar Association and the American Trial Lawyers Association, and a quite active member of other national, Illinois and local bar and professional and union organizations.

I have had a consumer bankruptcy practice for about 28 years now.
