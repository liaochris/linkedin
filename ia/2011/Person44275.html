Trix Vertogen's Summary

I've been a copywriter since 1994 and I'm a professional in Public Relations since '99. Since 2002 I'm an entrepreneur and have founded Newzone Communications and PR. In this company I work for both small businesses and larger corporations, who want to get more credibility and brand awareness in the media. Through editorial articles, interviews and online presence but also by outstanding events that attract many people and clients.

Part of the PR activities are e.g.: web copy production, copywriting for sales letters, ezines, press releases, article writing, media relations, social media strategies, PR coaching for individuals and marketing consultancy.

My goals: to help other people with their PR, so that they get noticed with their great companies, products and services.
Part of my company is development of information products about PR, events and copywriting, with which we can help more people than ever before and maybe you as well!

Specialties

PR coaching, training and workshops for entrepreneurs, communications marketing strategies, copywriting, press releases,publicity, publiciteit, public relations packages
