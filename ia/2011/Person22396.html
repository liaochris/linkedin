Josephine Raneses's Summary

A young creative with a passionate interest in all things visual and keen to experience new techniques. I believe in strong ideas delivered simply, appreciate good visual wit and enjoy collaborating within a team to come up with effective and lateral solutions to briefs. Through working with companies in various fields upon the completion of my degree, I am beginning to understand the unique needs of each client or situation and am willing to rise to the challenge to find innovative yet workable solutions in every case.

Throughout this interaction, I was able to gain a wider perspective of the industry and realise the importance of translating a client’s ideas into a well-rounded result. Looking forward to pursuing a career in the creative, branding and advertising industries and would approach any opportunity with enthusiasm, patience and energy.
