Joel Elad's Summary

Internet and eBay guru with a software development background and a yearn for Entrepreneurship. Fourteen years experience selling on eBay, six years experience with software project management as well as software development in languages like C, C++, and Java. Author of LinkedIn for Dummies & Web Stores Do It Yourself for Dummies. Co-author of 5 books, including eBay Your Business, Facebook Advertising for Dummies, Starting an iPhone Application Business for Dummies, How to Sell Anything on Amazon.com, and Starting an Online Business All-in-One Desk Reference for Dummies. Excellent public speaker and an Educational Specialist Trained by eBay. Former Lead instructor for eBay at the Learning Annex in New York City, Los Angeles, and San Diego. Consultant to small businesses and entrepreneurs on matters of eBay and e-commerce.

Specialties

Selling on eBay, building websites, Selling on Amazon.com, giving lectures, teaching seminars, writing books, software development, software testing, software design, software maintenance, e-commerce, WebSphere, SQL, Java.
