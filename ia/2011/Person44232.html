Trinaddie|Transactions was co-founded by Trina Hunt and Addie Grilli in 2002. Trina got her start in 1997, training in Corporate Relocation, Residential & Commercial Property Management, Real Estate Finance, & Real Estate Sales. Both Trina & Addie have Real Estate licenses & currently working on their Brokers & notaries.
Trinaddie|Transactions provides a wide variety of real estate support services. From your everyday administrative assistance, to super powered up escrow coordination. We help coordinate your real estate transaction to ensure that everything runs smoothly from acceptance to close of escrow. The coordinating process is designed to keep you organized and informed throughout the entire transaction and to provide you a complete and well-documented file at the close of escrow. Best of all, our service allows you the support of a full time assistant; one-transaction-at-a-time. We're as involved as you want us to be, this service is completely customizable.
