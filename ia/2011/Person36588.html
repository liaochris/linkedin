Stephanie Pelaprat's Summary

I founded Restopolitan in 2007 with the idea to satisfy one of the areas of tourism that had been left out by the web, i.e. restaurants.
Indeed back then you could easily find static data with restaurants list and at best email reservations but no systems existed where you could get real time access to availabilities as you could in the US and also as you could for planes and hotels.

The idea of Restopolitan emerged from the combination of my restaurant background, as a daughter and grand daughther of a traditional Paris Brasseries family, as well as my experience in the US, working in New York and experiencing back then the efficiency of a real time web booking restaurant system.

From there, with the help of 2 dedicated IT, we developed from scratch a software custom made for the specifics of the French restaurant community which was key in order to provide real time access on the other hand to web users. Since the official commercial launch in 2008, Restopolitan has aggregated the support of leading Chefs and Restopolitan.com offers a selection of over 3500 restaurants accessible from anywhere in the world and where a table is just a few clicks away.

Moreover, we have found the restaurant industry to be a little bit the “poor child” in terms of communication and so on which has given us the opportunity to become much more than a simple software company. We are solutions providers to the restaurant community in the widest sense of solutions possible and one of the most important barometer of our success is the actual trust our restaurant clients put in us every day in order to increase their client traffic and loyalty.
