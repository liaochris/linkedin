Make safer choices with clearKarma.org!
“clear” stands for ‘transparent’ and “Karma” for ‘responsible action’.

300 million food-allergies patients worldwide and millions of people concerned with consequences of food scandals on their health need access to food safetyknowledge. Reading the small print on each product is too difficult and too time consuming. ClearKarma.org is a social start-up enterprise. We will offer food safety knowledgethrough a multilingual mobile application and an information website. People with special dietary needs see immediately, based on personal filters, the impact of their buyingdecisions on their personal health. You will see whereto buy healthy alternatives with eco-friendly and fairproducts. We make information available (transparency) about the food production anddistribution process. Therefore we createvalue for producers, consumers anddistribution partners andencourage you to join us!

Our website offers user-generated information, search, marketplace and social media communication around health and sustainability. All our profits will be reinvested to serve the purpose & aim of ClearKarma.
