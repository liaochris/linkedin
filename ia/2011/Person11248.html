Dean Praetorius's Summary

I'm a digital media devotee who believes the future of journalism lies in the internet.

Content is the future of the internet, and finding that fine line between driving traffic and creating meaningful content is my specialty. While I'm a self-sufficient worker, I feel nothing is more effective than a well thought out idea and the opinions of as many people as necessary. It's better to do it right than to do it quick, but if both are possible, all the better.

My experience varies from editorial creation to graphic design to long-term planning, and I'm always looking for ways to turn the internet on its head with good vision and better design.
