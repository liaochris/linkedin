Amy Wood’s Specialties:

In February 2008 Amy launched the first LIVE CHAT in Upstate South Carolina and Western North Carolina.

Amy remains one of the few anchors in the nation to chat live with her audience.

And Amy was incorporating Twitter Facebook and MySpace comments 6 months before the networks thought about it.

She continues to blaze an innovative social media trail in television news.
