Professional Training & Coaching industry

January 2001 – Present (10 years 3 months)

I coached hundreds of people to step out of their comfort zone to focus on & realise their ambitions.

From Intellect to Intuition Retreat
Result: New global children's charity created. New online magazine created.

Case study 1:
Owner of manufacturing business wanted to reduce huge overdraft of €450,000.
Issue: He personally knew his suppliers & customers. Felt awkward asking for money.

Result: He built up his confidence and overdraft substantially reduced. One wealthier, more confident man
.
Case Study 2: Ambitious lady with big job and no life; rarely saw her partner, work was her life and all she could talk about.

Issue: She was at the mercy of her negative thoughts, was emotionally involved in staff issues with no way to detach and prioritise.

Result: She said no to overworking, to office gossip, exceeded her targets at work & got her personal life into full swing. She and her partner moved to new location to be together.

Case study 3:
Very ambitious guy in senior position. His boss was a bully and making his life miserable. He had an idea for a new business for himself and was working away on it.

Issue: He hated confrontation and was so compromised he couldn’t decide whether to go or stay.
Result: He found his courage and took a stand. He handled the bully, got a good package and left to start his own business, which is now very established.

Case Study 4:
White Space 5-Day Retreat for people at a turning point, about to make a big decision, wondering "what now?”
E.g.Break up with partner. Children have flown the nest. Significant other has died. Want career change or to create something new

Issues: Frustration. No time or space to gain perspective. Too many distractions. No clarity on next step. Wanting to make the right decision.

Results: One person decided not to become a writer, another decided to proceed with a divorce, someone else gave up her job & moved to Spain to live permanently.
