Ramon Lopez-Vives, MBA, PMP, CSM’s Summary

Ramon’s has managed his 20+ years career to multiple and ever increasing responsibility roles, recently he has specialized as a Business Analyst & Project Manager Professional with experience in:
• Software implementation across large enterprises.
• Project and Product Development Life Cycle.
• Data analysis and strategic directions.
• Change management and process improvement.
• Telecommunication infrastructure deployment.
• Manufacturing operations, departmental budget management and human resources leadership.
• Supplier and business management in the Americas, Europe and Asia.

Recently Ramon has been part of Nortel’s Supply Chain Project and Process Management group, multimillion dollar Projects have been entrusted to his Project Management and Business Analysis abilities.
Projects like MatrixOne Software implementation, Product Rationalization and implementation of the European Environmental directives (ROHS, REACH, WEEE).

Ramon initiated his career as a Test Engineer in 1989 with Delphi, shortly he was one of the founding members of the Sourcing team created to develop local suppliers achieving better cost and quality.

Hired by Nortel in 1993 as technical lead in the start-up of Mexico's Operations, Ramon quickly pulled ahead to several managing positions: Wireless Engineering Manager, Test and development Manager and Engineering Operations Manager.

Ramon was a factor of change in the Nortel-Axtel project in Monterrey, which delivered a Fixed Wireless Access (FWA) turnkey network, with initially 10+ cell sites and 2 DMS100 Nortel Switches.

Fluent in Spanish, he holds a BScEE, an MBA and Industry recognized certifications such as Project Manager Professional (PMP), Certified ScrumMaster (CSM), ITIL V3 and a Master Certificate in Business Analysis.
