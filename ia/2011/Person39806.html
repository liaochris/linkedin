Shaene Siders’s Summary

I have a BFA in creative writing, which shapes my work in all areas, not just entertainment writing. I love writing movie and television scripts, and I am a video game and comic book fanatic, so I am always seeking work involving screenplays, teleplays, documentaries, comics, and video game script writing. But I also like to bring fun, creative themes to everything I write, even technology writing (like my "Mad Scientist" courseware series, writing for Web pages, Web programming, ClearCase consulting, and technology training).

I have placed in finals and quarterfinals in national screenwriting competitions (including Scriptapalooza), and I am a published writer and fantastic editor.

Please feel free to contact me about rewriting and editing opportunities.
