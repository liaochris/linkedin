Christian J. Farber's Summary

I was fortunate to be the 6th employee of Albridge Solutions as the company was being founded back in 2000 having raised $16.5 in venture capital. It was a great experience building a company from $0 in revenue to $60 million in recurring revenue during my tenure. We would have been content to remain a stand alone entity but we were VC backed and the funds supporting us needed to be liquidated. When we sold the company to PNC bank for $305 million in late 2007 I decided to take some time off to be with my family. For me I then became involved in the fight of my life as a family member battled cancer for a year eventually losing the battle in 2009. The experience taught me much about life and how you spend your time. I recently decided it was time to return to work but the economy was crumbling which has made my search that much more of a challenge. I am up to the task so below you will find a summary of my qualifications and what I know I can do for a small or start up company.

I AM
* A Proven leader and manager of people, process and
technology.
* Results-driven with a track record of devising and executing
creative growth strategies.
* An executive that has a talent for hiring, training and motivating
world class teams.
* Someone who wants to move the needle every day.
* Passionate about the Golden Rule and as a result have built
exceptional relationships with clients, peers and employees.

I SEEK
* A small or funded start up technology company seeking
management talent.
* Other executives who are passionate about what they do and
who see what their company will be, not what it is.
* Executives, VC’s, Angel’s who want to build a company with real
profits.
* An opportunity to utilize my strategy and execution skills.
* To be affiliated with A players.

Specialties

Strategic Planning, Consultative Sales, Start-up & Rapid Growth, Relationship Management, Marketing & Branding, Strategic Alliance Building, Pricing Models, Entrepreneurial Leadership, Value-based Management, Training & Development, Recruitment & Selection, New Market Penetration, Miller Heiman - Large Account Management Process (LAMP), Miller Heiman - Strategic Selling, General Manager responsibilities.
