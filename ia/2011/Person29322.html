Marcos Ballestero's Summary

A Creative Director with over 15 years of hands-on experience, Marcos has helped manage and grow the Creative departments for many organizations. His diverse background with traditional and interactive marketing has given him a full range of expertise in the areas of brand development, advertising, web design, information architecture, multimedia, mobile applications, social media, usability and more. He currently serves as Associate Creative Director for Ketchum Worldwide Public Relations where he helps manage the Creative work and department for the Washington, DC office. While his focus is primarily Digital, he supports projects across all media channels.

Prior to this experience, Marcos has acted as the Creative Lead for several full-service marketing agencies in the Washington, DC area, such as AgencyQ, Mindshare Interactive, Proxicom and more. He has also gained extensive experience in Government communications working as the Interactive Creative Director for The National Cancer Institute of NIH. He coordinated directly with all internal offices and outside partners to help unify their identity and led a full-scale redesign of their main public website Cancer.gov, winning over 12 awards including a Webby for Best Government Website.

Creating effective design has always been a passion. He has helped numerous organizations turn their business goals into successful marketing concepts that connect with audiences. Included in his client list are Adidas, Nike, AT&T, Kraft, Marriott, American Express, Microsoft, Timberland, ExxonMobil and many others.
