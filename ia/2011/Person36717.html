Richard Balcon's Summary

A highly creative and experienced leader with 15+ years in high-tech and consulting, steeped in a variety of product development areas and IT projects, delivering the exceptional, not merely what is expected. I am equally comfortable in companies ranging from small start-ups to larger corporations. My focus is bringing customer-focused value and delivering results through the translation of deep technology knowledge to implement pragmatic scalable strategies and solutions.

Highlights:

• Developing Strategies - Working with key executives and business leaders, understand how their mission translates into the required technology and resources infrastructure. Make plans and execute.

• Understanding Customers – Teaming-up to capture, prioritize, and scope their needs for technology and support resources, and position IT, more as an Information provider, not just as an Infrastructure conduit.

• Inspiring Teams - Organizing crack teams to deliver mission-critical projects and instill client-focused mindset in all their projects. Empower teams by connecting them with clients and providing hands-on leadership.

• Exploiting Technology - Translate deep technology knowledge into providing best-in-class infrastructure and architectures and work with clients to fully leverage its capability through training, support systems, and improvements.

• Delivering Results - Working with the customers and other executives translating the technology agenda into actionable, milestone-driven projects. Creating visible dashboards and keeping all stakeholders on the same page.

• Recovering Derailed projects – Working with stakeholders understand project goals, strategy, and plan remediation for meeting immediate and longer term results. Mentor teams to obviate project derailments and delays.

In short, a driven, creative, execution orientated development leader with deep knowledge of a variety of technologies and an entrepreneurial spirit.
