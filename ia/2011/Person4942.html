Ben Ziegler's Summary

I am a mediator and collaboration consultant, based in Victoria, BC.

As a certified mediator since 2001, I’ve successfully mediated hundreds of civil/commercial disputes, in- and out-of-court, face-to-face and online (including 3 years mediating eBay disputes). Currently I work as a mediator and mentor in the British Columbia Court Mediation Program, mediate privately, and consult to organizations seeking online dispute resolution solutions.

My goal as a mediator is to make it easy for disputants to come together and resolve their differences. Behind this is my belief that good things happen when people find ways to respect, understand, and appreciate each other.

My consulting practice serves clients in the government, private, and not-for-profit sectors. I have extensive experience planning and implementing large business information systems.

In a volunteer capacity, I work as an Advisor with the Canadian Executive Service Organization, a Canadian NGO that works with Aboriginal communities in Canada, and developing nations abroad.

Specialties

Appropriate Dispute Resolution, online dispute resolution, collaboration best practices, community development, information systems management
