Gina Macchiarella’s Summary

I have 2 years of pharmaceutical marketing experience and 3 years of advertising experience. I have covered a large territory of production and design work including media kits, websites, power point presentations, corporate identity, brochures and packaging.

My career is just blossoming and I would like a creative director to be my mentor so that we can move forward as a team. In the future, I would like to take on a leading creative role as an art director and provide a company with award winning design and success.

Currently, I am developing 3 different web businesses of my own; “Green” baby diapers, cupcakes and a neat scrapbooking web site. Reaching these goals has given me a great sense of accomplishment and I believe they make me a very attractive candidate because I am a proven self starter and I know how to follow through with my goals.
