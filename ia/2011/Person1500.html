  • Co-Founder and Director

    LinkCycle

    (Services pour l'environnement industry)

    novembre 2010 — Present (4 months)

    LinkCycle, LLC is a start-up that hopes to be the solution to current expensive, cumbersome, black-box LCA software that dominate the market in this space today. While LCA is the future of environmental analysis, we have identified several techniques to overcome its current shortcomings through an online, collaborative platform that makes it easy for businesses to conduct and verify an LCA, yielding results that are more reliable and usable. Our idea and strategy has gained significant attention and support, encouraging us to grow our team and develop a beta product.
