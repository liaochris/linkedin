Leah Fotis’s Summary

Based in Dublin, Leah Fotis focuses on Ashoka's efforts to scale social impact and build a thriving marketplace for social innovation. Leah works with Ashoka's Globalizer program to strengthen the supply of scalable solutions and the Localizer program to build local demand to import innovation. Leah has also worked on Ashoka's Venture and Fellowship programs in Mumbai and Washington, D.C.. Prior to Ashoka, Leah worked with early-stage entrepreneurs in the Middle East through AMIDEAST, global student leaders in AIESEC and start-up businesses in Europe. Leah is passionate about entrepreneurial solutions to today's pressing social and environmental issues.
