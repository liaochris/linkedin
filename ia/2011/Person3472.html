Richard Appleyard's Summary

Richard is a Senior IT manager (CIO), proponent of Holistic Technology Management with a strong focus on the User Experience. His expertise includes Web Technology & Site Management, Health & Consumer Informatics, and Web User Experience (including Web accessibility and Section 508 compliance). He is passionate about helping companies to maximize the impact and effectiveness of their technology projects.

Specialties

Web Accessibility, Web Usability, Web Program/Project Management, Web/IT Management, Business / Systems / Application Analysis, Large Web site Management, IT Continuity of Operations Planning, Technology Ethics, Information Privacy / Security, User-Centered Web Design, User Experience Design (UxD), Human-Computer Interaction
