Scott Winer's Summary

With more than 10 years of experience in broadcast and online media, I've most recently worked as a camera operator for network coverage of Big 12 football and basketball, as a videotape logger for CBS Sports and as a spotter and production assistant for Westwood One.

I've worked in various roles for CBS Sports, Fox Sports, ESPN, FSN, FCS, Westwood One Radio Sports, St. Louis Rams Radio, KUJH-TV (Lawrence, KS) and DomeTV (Syracuse, NY), including broadcasts of the 2008 AFC Championship, 2009-10 and 2005 NCAA Men's Basketball Tournaments, 2008 Orange Bowl, and 2002 SEC Football Championship. I've directed more than 200 live sports, news and music shows since 2000.

In 2008, I graduated from The University of Kansas with a B.A. in Geography, using Geographic Information Systems to analyze the NFL's broadcasting rules in U.S. markets during my senior year.

Specialties

Remote sports production, Remote and studio directing, Handheld and hard camera, Visual storytelling, Video & audio editing, Broadcast technologies
