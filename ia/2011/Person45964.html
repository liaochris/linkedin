Dr. Wilson Chew's Education

University of South Australia

Doctorate in Business Administration, Influence of Trust & Perception in the Buying Behaviour of B2B Services

2006 – 2010

I was awarded the Doctor of Business Administration degree from The University of South Australia. My thesis reads, "Trust in the Corporate Brand - Its Antecedents and Consequences in the B2B Services Context". My research literature review extends into the sciences of organisational strategy, brand strategy formulation, marketing, corporate reputation, trust and buying behaviour; all of which were studied within the B2B services context.

The findings of my study confirmed that long term organisational strategy and brand strategy are NOT mutually exclusive. The formulation of both serves to build trust.

This further confirms existing literature in both academic and applied sciences. My doctoral study is research based; 150 companies were studied.
