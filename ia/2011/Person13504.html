Elon Gasper’s Summary

Lead teams to translate customer needs into winning products and market them with flair. Driven development, business and marketing strategies for a wide variety of projects. Experience includes founding, financing and managing a venture-funded consumer software firm from inception through acquisition by a public company. Awards include a Traditional Game of the Year, a Best Genealogy Product of the Year, a COMDEX Envision, a Best Practices for Product Innovation, and a Codie, the retail software ‘Oscar’. 9 US patents for innovations in addressing software consumer needs. Finalist for the Fleugelman, the only personal achievement award given by the Software Publishers Association.

Software product design, development, marketing & business affairs. Internet marketing systems, consumer applications, multimedia, intellectual property, life sciences. Analysis, strategy, management and hands-on tactics.
