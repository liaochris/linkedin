Therese Tappouni's Summary

Therese Tapouni and Lance Ware have multiple degrees and certifications between them. Their experience is in teaching, counseling, (both one on one and in groups), training, research and publishing. Their books and CDs teach clients to create a personal and business life that is satisfying, connected and pleasurable. Both have led groups, created programs, and taught in areas as diverse as primary programs, universitites, corporations and specialty groups such as the Associated Builders and Contractors Women's Council and the National Organization of Women Legislators.

Specialties

Training and certification in Medical and Clinical Hypnotherapy, Somatic Intuitive Training, (with concentrations in Stress Reduction),Time Dimension Therapy, NLP and various certificates in other modalities and areas of study with teachers such as Jean Houston and Deepak Chopra.
