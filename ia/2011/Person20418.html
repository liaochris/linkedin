Jeremy J Lewis's Summary

Jeremy is a qualified subject matter expert in all aspects of organisation effectiveness, including talent and performance management, leadership development and colleague engagement.

He has extensive experience of translating HR strategy into business results through balancing structural and behavioural change to optimise productivity and performance during change programmes and within business as usual.

Jeremy has over 20 years experience, with strong delivery at executive level in various functions within financial services including leadership roles in HR, finance, strategy and operations, and as an internal/external consultant. He is currently working on developing leadership behaviours within local government.

In 2010 he graduated top of his class at Sheffield Business School, attaining a Masters degree in Organisation Development & Consultancy, and was awarded the Torres prize for outstanding achievement.

Jeremy is a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants in England and Wales and is certificated (to level B) by the British Psychological Society for psychometric profiling.
