Eric Cerda’s Summary

Animator with a solid traditional skill set in 2D Cel/ Flash character and effects animation. Firm understanding of the animation production pipeline for broadcast, web and film animation production, having had active roles in pre-production, character and background design, storyboarding, lead key character and effects animation, and post production processes. Skilled in Flash animation and art style design for online social gaming applications for Facebook.

Past experiences include lead animation and designer roles for clients such as The Walt Disney Company, Playdom Inc, Cartoon Network, Comedy Central, MTV, Adult Swim, and PBS.

These projects include: commercials for Cartoon Network USA and Latin America, broadcast shows for Adult Swim including Aquateen Hunger Force, Perfect Hair Forever, and Squidbillies seasons 1-3. Animated on the "Aqua Teen Hunger Force Colon Movie Film for Theaters" full-length animated feature film. Was lead animator and character designer for Comedy Central's animated series, "FreakShow". Lead Animator for "Busted", a short Film by director Matt Maiellaro. Animated on various pilots episodes, and have lead teams as big as 15+ artists. Freelance work on the National Liberty Museum's 2009 Heroes of Character interactive exhibit, various Hi5.com website animation, as well as animating on a select episodes of PBS Kids Emmy Award winning series, "Word Girl". Currently, Art lead and Flash animator for an ESPN social game title TBA, with Playdom, Inc.

Eric Cerda’s Specialties:

2D Cel, Flash animation, art direction, character design, background design, storyboarding and layout, painting, graphic design, web design, isometric and 2D game art design
