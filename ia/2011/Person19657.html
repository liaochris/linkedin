Judy Cuffaro Arnold’s Summary

I've been driving direct marketing strategies throughout SEI's various business units -- to help generate revenue -- for the past few years as a Marketing Director on the Corporate Marketing team.

Prior to joining SEI in 2000, as Director of Lead Generation for the Institutional group, I spent almost half of my 25-year-career in affinity marketing with Seabury & Smith, a third-party administrator of insurance and a subsidiary of Marsh, Inc. in the Direct Response Services division. Along with financial services expertise, the early part of my career was in retail sales promotion and direct marketing. Notable clients that I handled included AARP and IEEE (the largest international engineering association), at Seabury & Smith, and Rent-A-Center while at Harte-Hanks Direct Marketing.

My reality has been balanced with a Master’s Degree in Management and Marketing from the University of Maryland and a B.A. degree in Communications Journalism from Shippensburg University. I'm the recent past President of the Philadelphia Direct Marketing Association (PDMA) and the current Chairperson. I'm a member of the Direct Marketing Association (DMA) and have judged the DMA International Echo Awards annually since 2006.
