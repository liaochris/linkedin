Sunshine Lehmann's Summary

During my 16 year tenure at Leo Burnett/Starcom MediaVest Group I developed a solid media background with an early focus on broadcast investment. More recently as Media Operations Supervisor, I helped manage a $220M budget spanning across all mediums, trained and worked individually with members of 70+ client team, managed a local radio trafficking team, was heavily involved in two major media system transitions, served as liaison between Media and Finance, oversaw local bill/pay, coordinated SOX audits and worked on countless internal and client projects.

Moving forward, I would like to integrate some of my personal interests into my day to day. I enjoy exploring technology, digital advertising and social media. Career opportunities with a digital focus do have an appeal to me although I also do still have strong ties to my broadcast investment/operations management roots.
