Jeremy Hall's Summary

After studying Accountancy and Financial Control at Sheffield Hallum University, I started my career as a field Sales Executive at Tetra, a leading supplier of accounting software. I spent two years on the road meeting computer resellers in London and the Home Counties before setting up my first company, Wyse Leasing, at the age of 24. This business grew organically and via acquisition to a £65m turnover company employing 75 people based out of six regional offices. My business partner and I sold of the Northern business in 2006 and in July 2009, I exited completely with the sale of the Southern operation. I am still involved in the UK leasing business via my new company, WestWon Capital. (www.westwon.co.uk). However, this is a much smaller operation. I have also been writing for Leasing Life, the UK’s leading magazine for the leasing sector and intend to keep close to this market.
My main focus now is to develop Who’s Who in the UK. The publishing side, the development of biographical books noting eminent business leaders and entrepreneurs was the main focus of this company that I purchased in 2004. Our intention will be and move the business into business training DVD’s and other related products aimed at the business sector.
Having bought and sold numerous companies, not to mention the ones I have founded, I have gained some invaluable experience that will be used in the future.
