Julie Reiher’s Summary

PROFESSIONAL SUMMARY
Accomplished director with over 15 years experience in business analysis and design, with emphasis on program/project management, process improvement and system implementation. Respected leader, with the demonstrated ability to build highly motivated teams focused on defining strategic objectives and achieving business results.

Currently, a Director at McDonald's Corporation in the U.S. SpendSmart Procurement Group focused on strategic sourcing and savings capture for our indirect spend categories. Prior to my current position, I was in the U.S. Controller Group focused on leading various special projects related to optimizing the U.S. McOpCo portfolio and enhancing financial policies, processes and controls and also in NASS establishing and implementing Business Intelligence capabilities aligned with Oracle Financials.

Prior to joining McDonald's, as a consultant with both a "Big 4" audit and advisory service firm and "Big 5" consulting firm my client base included telecommunications providers, utilities provider, multiple financial institutions, multiple global consumer and industrial products companies, and a technology consulting provider.
