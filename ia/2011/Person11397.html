Dee Copeland Patience, PMP’s Summary

* PMP Certified Project Manager with 7+ Years advanced project/program management and process improvement experience in a results-oriented team environments for public and private sector. Currently Division Manager of the $22 million Texas affordable Housing Trust Fund.

* Experienced in public/government contract management and performance monitoring, compliance, procurement, stakeholder and risk management, and financial oversight. Adept at using PMI/ASQ methodologies and IIBA business process analysis to streamline and optimize operations, lead global and state-wide projects, implement IT solutions and develop Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs).

* 12+ Years in Management, Operations, Leadership, Negotiation and Influence, Training, Employee Development, Team Building, Public Speaking, Writing, Strategic Marketing and Communications.

* 6+ Years Real Estate Development, Investments, Brokerage. Researched, analyzed and evaluated markets, labor statistics and demographics data to lead clients to an investment decision. Specializations included land and income property acquisition, infill development, green building, sustainable development, repositioning, rehabilitation, and disposition. Prior appointments include the city advisory commission for the 711-acre Mueller Austin mixed-use, mixed-income redevelopment project.
