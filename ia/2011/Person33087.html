Norman Tan's Experience

Assistant Director (Digital/New Media)

Singapore Tourism Board

Government Agency; Leisure, Travel & Tourism industry

December 2008 – Present (2 years 3 months)

Responsible for setting up and leading the Digital/New Media team within the Singapore Tourism Board (STB). This team spearheads global digital marketing initiatives for the YourSingapore brand. Areas of responsibilities/achievements since assuming this role include:

1) Architecting the Singapore destination brand - YourSingapore (an evolvement of Uniquely Singapore), ensuring that it cuts through the clutter currently existing in the destination communication space, and launching it globally.

2) Developing and executing STB's digital marketing strategy (inclusive of portals, search, social, customer relationship marketing and mobile), and ensuring that digital communications integrates with offline communications.

3) Revamp destination portals for Travelers (www.yoursingapore.com) and Business Events (mice.yoursingapore.com).

4) Planning and execution of STB's year-round global marketing campaigns together with STB's global offices, ensuring that the YourSingapore brand and key events (Great Singapore Sale, Singapore Grand Prix, Christmas in the Tropics etc) have top of mind recall among the target audience in key markets;

5) Deriving key matrices to track success of STB's global digital marketing initiatives and digital assets, keeping a keen eye on the return on marketing investment;

6) Evolving operating processes within STB and growing digital expertise within the marketing group so that the organization is better able to shift and adapt to the strong focus on digital communications.
