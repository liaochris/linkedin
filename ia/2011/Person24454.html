Kevin Popović’s Summary

Welcome to my company, Ideahaus®. Since 1990, We've been helping clients figure out what to say and how to say it.

I work directly with CEOs, C-Level Communicators and Entrepreneurs to help shape their companies and build their brands. I appreciate the life of a creative professional and know how to deliver the results that positively change companies forever.

The role I serve is as a Communications Director who helps develop communications plans, including schedules, budgets and metrics for everything a company uses to communicate. I work with existing marketing teams to lead the development of most types of creative and communications resources. I’m also responsible for managing the accounts, as needed, as an adjunct to a client's leadership.

Over the last few years, I've added social media to our list of communications channels. I've found it to be the most interesting medium I've experienced, and it has become the focus of my forthcoming book, "Satellite Marketing: Using Social Media to Sell," which details my trademarked strategy on using social media to create a marketing environment and sell products / services.

My clients tell me they appreciate working directly with me, and the personal service I bring to these relationships. After more than 25 years of professional experience, I help them understand the various types of communications, their opportunities and how we can utilize all of the resources available to build truly remarkable brands.

  • Speaker, Author

    Satellite Marketing

    (Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry)

    January 2008 — Present (3 years 1 month)

    Satellite Marketing is a communications strategy designed to connect an organization with its target markets via social media sites and services. These social media sites and services act as marketing sub-stations, or “Satellites”.

    Satellites are used in addition to, or in place of, traditional media, such as print, radio, television, direct mail and outdoor advertising. The goal is to expand the reach of the organization and enhance the results by creating relationships.

    Satellites offer smaller, faster, dynamic communications opportunities, engaging prospects where they already exist. Social media is among the fastest growing forms of communication today (site reference), with users connecting online for both personal and professional purposes.

    “The term “Satellite Marketing™” is a trade name developed by Kevin Popovic and used by him and Ideahaus, LLC, to describe the business process described herein. All rights to the term are reserved, and you may use the term only with the permission of, or proper accreditation to Kevin Popovic and Ideahaus, LLC.”
