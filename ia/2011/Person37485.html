Rob McGregor's Summary

Adroite is a full service communications and public relations company based in Wellington providing our clients with specialist communications expertise and advice.

Our strengths include strategic counsel, media and government relations, public affairs, and reputation and issues management. Organisations experiencing crisis management issues, both externally and in-house, seek our commonsense and practical advice. Adroite’s writing skills ensure our clients’ communications are current, accurate, timely and well-targeted to their intended audience. Our clients have said they are impressed at the range of skills Adroite can offer them; we work closely with them to help provide both strategic direction as well as giving hands-on tactical support with strong attention to detail. We not only work closely with and report to our clients’ key decision makers, but we also use our strong teamwork skills to work alongside an organisation’s communications professionals.

Our clients come from public and private companies, the public sector, and the not-for-profit sector. We are experienced in communicating with a wide range of audiences; we understand government processes and have a sound understanding of financial marketing, ideas and concepts.

With over 20 years in public relations, Rob’s experience includes strategic communications planning and programme development and implementation, crisis preparedness and issues management, stakeholder management, public affairs and government relations, financial communications and investor relations, public information campaigns and business mentoring.
He has also been responsible for integrating client communications, ensuring advertising, direct marketing and public relations work together to fulfill an organisation’s business and marketing objectives. Rob is an accomplished speechwriter. His work on the Electoral Referendum and Firefighters campaigns has won international recognition from the International Association of Business Communicators.
Rob is a trustee of Te Omanga Hospice, chairs the board’s finance committee, and writes the Wellington Business Blog on wotzon.com.
