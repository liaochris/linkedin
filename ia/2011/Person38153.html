Ruth Ann Barrett’s Summary

I have been influenced by what Nicholas Carr refers to as those “in the counterculture who were enthusiastic about computers as personal tools that would liberate us by giving more power to create and communicate with others.”

This idea has been central to my career in three sectors of the economy: non-profit associations, corporate high tech, and entrepreneurial endeavors of owning a direct marketing agency (1994+), and launching www.earthsayers.tv, the voices of sustainability (2008).

"Thanks to hyper-competition, organizations now have two separate tasks - to get today's business done and to create the future. The former calls for good management; the latter for leadership." McCrimmon, Leadersdirect.com

While I have a long history of getting things done and enjoy a reputation for being a highly skilled and innovative direct marketer meeting as I do Gladwell’s “ten-thousand-hour-rule for success," what I have taken to heart is Bob Edgar’s suggestion that “the leaders we are all waiting for is us.” Bob is Common Cause CEO.

I write two blogs, earthsayers.tv/sustainabilityadvocate.com and digitalsavvy.blogspot.com. My vision statement for EarthSayers.tv is a video on YouTube, just search on "Sustainability and EarthSayers.tv.

Websites I have produced include the 1st short story site with audio streaming (2001), a precursor to podcasts and EarthSayers.tv (2008) which aggregates video and audio programs based on a cause.

I am a leader in bringing Web 2.0 technologies, especially collaboration networks, to firms who embrace the relationship marketing model. An early adopter, my considerable communication skills as a producer-writer, are focused on converting the pragmatists of the world to the idea of adopting sustainability as a business strategy.

As noted by CEO and Chief Inspired Protagonist, Jeffrey Hollender of Seventh Generation, “it can be no longer business as usual."

Ruth Ann Barrett’s Specialties:

Response marketing, interactive marketing, online marketing, cause marketing, technical marketing, software for marketing applications, writing, producer/writer, executive communications, strategic, strategy, sustainability, innovation.

Extensive experience integrating Web environments with advertising programs to include personalization and customization techniques such as PURLs (personalized URLs) and on-demand software tools.
