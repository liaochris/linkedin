Sarah Epstein Bowman, Esq.’s Summary

I am a licensed attorney with 13+ years of attorney recruiting experience. In the past few years, my lateral associate, counsel, and partner placements have included Pillsbury Winthrop, Milbank Tweed, Arnold & Porter, Crowell & Moring, Howrey, Duane Morris, DLA Piper, Kilpatrick Stockton, Tydings & Rosenberg, Ober Kaler, Dewey & LeBoeuf, Ogletree Deakins, Herbert Smith, Shulman Rogers, Stanzione & Kim, Oliff & Berridge, Lindquist & Vennum, Bulman Dunie, Roetzel & Andress, Zuber & Taillieu, LexisNexis, Hughes Network Systems, Vornado/Charles E. Smith, Elysium Digital, & Sodexho.

My work entails partnering directly with a candidate to find a firm that would be a suitable match. I do not work based on job postings but, rather, based on my network in order to uncover unique opportunities that are often created and customized exclusively for my candidates. In any market, let alone a tough market, such as now, it is important to conduct a job search based not on advertised openings but, rather, based on contacts.

I am based in the Washington, DC area, but I have an expansive network, which extends well past my home base. So, I am able to work with candidates who are interested in relocating just as well as those who wish to move across the street. Locations of my recent placements have included Washington, DC, Maryland, Virginia, New York, California, North Carolina, Minnesota, Colorado, and Hong Kong.

I have numerous references who can attest to my discreet, efficient, and effective work.
