Jessica Henderson's Summary

Careers Adviser experienced in a range of higher education careers services, including 'new' and Russell Group universities.
Experienced in 1:1 guidance, designing and delivering careers education material and workshops, teaching and assessing on career development modules and writing career-related resources. Substantial experience of initiating and developing strong working relationships with external organisations as well as with internal colleagues.
I am particularly interested in coaching and self-confidence/ lack of it, how this impacts on an individual's career decision making, and helping clients overcome this.
