Max Donzella's Summary

Immigrate with your family to Canada in less than 2 years with the Immigrant Investor Program of Quebec ( approved in 4 to 6 months)

The mandatory requirements for this program are :

1- Net assets of a minimum of 1,600,000$CAD
( only to be demonstrated , no need to sell anything)

2- Management experience or Business Ownership required of minimum of 2 years

3- Making a passive investment of 800,000$ CAD for five years without receiving any interest payment OR paying a 1 time financing fee of 220,000$ CAD to the financial institution authorized by the program.

Special waivers : No formal education required and no language tests for this type of immigration and there is NO AGE LIMIT.
