Gregory Nichols's Experience

January 2000 – Present (11 years 3 months)

Interactive Marketing & Advertising, Online Promotion, Brand Strategy, Creative & Media Strategy, Team/Project/Account Management

Experience: As a founding member of RED's management team, Greg successfully produced hundreds of Rich Media ad campaigns before moving into the role of V.P. for Red's Interactive advertising division. These campaigns include dozens of award winning creative executions. Greg oversees RED's interactive advertising team and is responsible for a diverse client list that includes Warner Bros, Sony Pictures, Paramount, National Geographic Channel, ESPN, MTV, UFC, GE, Ford, Honda, Toyota, and California Pizza Kitchen. He works closely with producers and project teams to oversee the development of creative executions that achieve client goals and meet RED's high standards of creative excellence. During his ten years at RED, Greg has helped to build a creative and producer team that is widely recognized to be one of the best in the business.
