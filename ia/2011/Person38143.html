Rusti-Ann Blanke's Summary

Background: Lawyer, HR subject matter expert, Occupational Health & Safety. 25 yrs experience in the public, private and non-profit sector, gov't and academic environment)

Training: With a background in private law practice and a side trip into human resources people/performance management, I have dedicated the past 11 years exclusively to workplace issues on the conflict continuum: performance issues & workplace relationship rescue, disrespectful & inappropriate conduct, destructive & disruptive conflict, bullying, harassment, discrimination and workplace violence. In addition to countless hours of one-on-one coaching, I have designed hundreds of training, educational and skill-building workshops, delivering customized educational and training solutions to university staff, faculty and students and to employers/employees in the public, private and non-profit sectors.

Services: Complaint investigation (misconduct, harassment, discrimination, workplace violence). Policy and program development and implementation. Review and assessment of anti-harassment policies, including review for OHS compliance.

"On call" harassment Advisor/Coach services for small/med businesses (custom contract to meet your organization's needs and resources).

Restorative services, coaching and/or training to address the aftermath of workplace issues and restore the health of the workplace.

Goals: Through RST Training & Consulting, I bring a wealth of knowledge, experience and passion to partnering with organizations to build and sustain or restore safe, healthy, respectful workplaces, free from bullying, destructive conflict, harassment and violence. The goal of RST Training and Consulting is to partner with your organization to deliver customized solutions that suit your workplace culture and meet your organization's specific needs.
