Mitchell Ignatoff’s Summary

Certified by the Supreme Court of New Jersey as a Criminal Trial Attorney. I have been practicing criminal defense for over 20 years. I represent clients charged with drunken driving, theft, misappropraition, drug possession, rape, child pronography, assault, murder among others. I have represented clients in state and federal courts. J.D., University of Tulsa, 1978; LL.M, Trial Advocacy, Temple Law School, 1999. Admitted New Jersey, New York, Pennsylvania, Federal Courts of Appeal for the Second and Third Circuits, District of New Jersey, Eastern Districts of New York and Pa., Southern Distict of N.Y.
