Scott Kaplan’s Summary

Over the past years, I have been able to channel my energy, success and growth behind my core values of integrity, determination, and responsibility. These values focus my management style that results in effective communication, team member growth and achievement, and successful completion of strategic goals.

Since the mid '90s, I have worked in the technology, real estate, and advertising industries where I have consistently excelled in key positions that required leadership, persistence, and a calm sense of urgency to create positive results. Throughout my career, I have creatively maximized the opportunities for my company to grow in the areas of new sales development, revenue retention, and customer expansion. I have been responsible for global development and execution of sales best practices, new customer acquisition campaigns, customer support, technical implementations, training, recruitment, project management, financial trends and analysis, and marketing programs for B2C and B2B clients.

I also have a deep passion for helping others succeed. In 2007, I launched www.BecomeYourVeryBest.com to create an online community that focuses on personal and professional development. The site focuses on topics like leadership, sales, training, networking, and other key business topics to ensure success in the 21st century.

In late 2008, I joined Idearc Media to lead their staffing and training departments. Idearc is an industry leader in local advertising for small and medium businesses with over 700,000 clients in the US and over $2.5 Billion in annual sales. There main solutions include the Verizon Yellow Pages, direct mail, www.SuperPages.com, direct mail and created the "SuperGuarantee" to ensure consumers get the service job done right or Idearc will step in and make it right.
