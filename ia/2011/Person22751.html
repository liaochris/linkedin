Jack Skates’s Summary

I have been designing and building digital products and services since 1994. I started off in web design and development and have migrated into more digital products and services including web interfaces. I have worked with over 120 clients on over 150 projects. I started off as a generalist doing the design work and implementation myself including code development and database architecture. I then migrated to information architecture and now my work is heavily influenced by user centered design methods and techniques. I have significant experience in web application, mobile application, TV set top box experience and proprietary devices.

I am currently working as an independent contractor, but open to discussing a senior position with an agency, firm, product or service company.

Jack Skates’s Specialties:

Research, strategy and design for digital devices and services. User Experience, Information Architecture, Interaction Design, User Researcher, Testing Facilitator, Web Design, Software interface design, Mobile Device interface design, Set top box, Interactive Television
