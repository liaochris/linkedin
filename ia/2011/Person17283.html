Helen Adrienne’s Summary

I am a Licensed Certified Social Worker and General Therapist that specializes in infertility counseling, mind/body stress reduction for infertility and gynecological and reproductive issues.

My approach is based on the mind/body connection involving mental, physical and spiritual well-being. I strongly believe believes that the mind influences the body and the body influences the mind. This approach and thought processes combine psychological techniques with a deep clinical and spiritual understanding of the mind, heart and body. This understanding empowers me to help my clients in a targeted way to reduce mind/body stress.

My typical treatment model aims to enter the mind/body unity so you can respond rather than react to challenges, whether for infertility or anything else. This will enhance self-esteem, build confidence, create new behaviors, coping mechanisms and thought processes leading to major breakthroughs.

I have helped hundreds of people reach their goals and navigate the emotional component of complex medical issues, particularly infertility counseling, throughout my psychotherapy and counseling career that began in 1979. My primary practice is in New York City, Rockland County, New York and, Bergen County, New Jersey but it's not uncommon for me to draw patients from the entire NY metropolitan area. I understand how you feel and with you to reduce mind/body stress relating to the mental, physical and circumstantial factors.

Often, people need a fresh point of view, new perspectives, skills and support to rebalance a relationship, get clarity on a complex or stressful situation or build more self-confidence and self-esteem. I provide support, guidance, compassion and encouragement. And above all, solutions that bring relief.

Helen Adrienne’s Specialties:

I am a psychotherapist in general practice BUT, I have a specialty in working with infertility patients. Check out my web site, www.helenadrienne.com. I have just finished writing a book on infertility. When it is published, I will post a notice.
