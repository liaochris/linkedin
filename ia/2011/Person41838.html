Rick Lite’s Summary

Helping authors,(published or self-published) recording artists and entrepreneurs sell more titles and build a business by teaching current marketing and distribution strategies for the book and CD markets.

Rick Lite’s Specialties:

As a daily marketer I have the expertise in getting book & CD titles into the hands of a client’s targeted markets. This can be done through distributors, resellers, organizations, websites and social media sites. Other strengths include digital content development and distribution, eBook conversion and distribution, video creation and editing, social media marketing, website design and marketing. I treat all titles as if they were my own and all clients the way I like to be treated.
