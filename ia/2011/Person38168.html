Ruth Trojan’s Summary

A recognized industry leader, Ruth Trojan is a strong, vocal advocate for reliable and actionable travel and tourism research. She is the President and CEO of Nadler & Associates, a solutions-oriented tourism research, planning and performance reporting organization. Ms. Trojan has over 25 years experience in market research with the last twenty years focused on travel & tourism research.

Ruth Trojan’s Specialties:

destination marketing research, travel industry research, performance reporting, technical editor
