Norman W. Davies, Director, Davies International's Summary

Davies International provides sales consulting, sales recruiting and sales training to businesses that find their sales have plateaued.

I believe that sales is a process that can be developed and managed! Get the right process, the right people with the right tools and motivation and success will happen!

We are The Sales Experts!

Norman is an experienced sales & marketing professional with broad international experience. Norman enjoys working with businesses to bring focus and direction to the sales process, to make certain the right people are in place and motivated to achieve the plan!

Norman has, personally, made over 180 business trips to Asia, traveling to China since 1986. He has also traveled extensively worldwide. Complete fluency in Chinese and knowledge of several other Asian and European languages have given Norman a competitive edge and made him a global expert in this new global century. Whether involved in market development or product sourcing Norman understands the subtleties of diverse business cultures.

As a widely published writer Norman has carefully honed his opinions and philosophy about selling and marketing. Norman is a regular contributor to industry journals and has authored several publications.

As an award-winning speaker Mr. Davies has developed the skills to effectively communicate his success strategies to individuals and groups. Mr. Davies has lectured to university groups, taught college courses and addressed trade show attendees.

Persistence is one of his fundamental traits and is key to his personal and corporate success. As an avid marathon runner, triathlete and long distance open water swimmer, he has the energy and determination to keep on keeping on when others have left the field. A daily yoga practice has provided focus, calm and discipline. Understanding and defining the fundamental attributes that create success for individuals and businesses is his lifelong quest.
