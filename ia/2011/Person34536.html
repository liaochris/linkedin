Per Junel’s Summary

Innovative and problem solving application developer with 10+ years of experience in software design using OOM/OOD (UML), C++, Java, VB and SQL in a broad field, ranging from components (hooks, dynamic linked libraries, controls), over stand alone applications to client-server systems. Industries have also varied; manufacturing plants, power plants, warehouse management, and payment solutions for restaurants and the retail industry to name a few. Today I work in the field of Rich Internet Applications (RIA), or web 2.0 as it is sometimes called, with modern technologies like JavaEE, Groovy, Grails and Griffon.
