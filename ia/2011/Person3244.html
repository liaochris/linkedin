ANOOP A.K’s Summary

I have been in this industry for more that three years and during that time, was successful in contributing to the Character Rigging process for various International projects.

I would like to describe myself as an experienced 3D Technical Artist, but I would benefit the team best in areas of Rigging, Technical Animation, and Tool Development for all departments in a CG Production Pipeline.

Coming from an Engineering background, my passion for art and movies urged me to pursue a career in animation. Having started my career as a Rigging Artist, I have extended my skill set in areas including Task Automation and Tool Development in scripting languages like MEL and Python.

My objective is to become a Lead Pipeline TD for feature film projects with specialization in Character Setup and Technical Animation where I could combine my technical experience and skills with a more artistic focus.
