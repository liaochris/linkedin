Nicolai Wadstrom’s Summary

I am an serial Entrepreneur, I am 37 years of age, and have been starting companies since 1995, ranging from consulting companies, software companies, media companies, computer games and internet companies. I have a fair bit of Telco experience, and have done some management consultancy to help other startups, and larger organizations in between, I have been a CEO, a CTO and have had few different hats since 1995.

For my startups I have raised capital from Angels, Private Equity, Investment Banks and VC's.

My current primary focus is creating and building disruptive companies with world-class entrepreneurs, mainly through my BootstrapLabs business lab, working with startup's or major corporations to help build new disruptive products/services.
