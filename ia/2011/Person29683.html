Melissa Jo Powell, MS, RN’s Summary

Old to healthcare but new to education and development. Open to new paradigms that challenge old ways of viewing complex quality and training issues. With a clear focus on research, seeking to educate using evidenced based strategies to train and develop nurses and ancillary clinical staff. Focused on using the latest innovation to incorporate simulation and serious games to train and develop clinicians. A huge professional fan of Atul Gawande and Peter Senge. Excited by health care reform and the challenges of moving from intervention to prevention as a focus. Seeking to find answers in new ways, through social media and old ways, methodical research. Thoroughly surprised that I get paid to do what I love.
