Scott Shapiro's Summary

Scott Shapiro is a composer/sound designer and audio engineer and based in New York, is active in all phases of original music composition and audio design in a variety of performance venues and media formats.

Scott's unique musical style has made its imprint in numerous original compositions airing on network and cable TV, radio and recorded media, both in the US and internationally. Recent credits include: NBC Nightly News, Today Show, Dateline, The Apprentice, Medium, Discovery Channel, Sony Music, TV Land's Pity The Fool and Martha Stewart Daytime show.

In addition to composing and creating his own music, Scott is the President and Owner of SonicEmulations, Inc.

About SonicEmulations

SonicEmulations, Inc. is a digital audio and content solutions provider for companies in the music business with an emphasis on multimedia production and produces world-class sample libraries for music professionals and enthusiasts. BandmateLoops.com is a web destination offering a complete line of sample libraries and other audio content products.

Specialties

Technical Experience Integrating Computer Technology into creative music making enviroments.
