Rich Gee's Summary

I do three things (I call them the 3 P's):

I PUSH you.
Everyone procrastinates in one way or another. That's what's holding you back.

My job as your coach is to get you to realize that you are not moving forward, step out of your comfort zone, and take action. Not a huge jump, but a small step. Most people will find this first step is all they need to start accelerating towards their dream.

I PARTNER with you.
I'm there as a partner to help you celebrate your successes and brainstorm alternatives when you hit obstacles.

It's hard to be out there without someone to bounce ideas off of - someone to help you take the emotion out of a decision and clearly define all the options available to you. In addition, I'm there to help you with your blind spots - you don't know they're their, but I see them.

I PLAN with you.
Most people want to reach for the sky (good for you!) but they don't have a plan to get them there.

It's like taking a trip and leaving the map or GPS home. Where will you go? Will you make mistakes? Will it slow your traveling speed? Yes. That's why you need an action plan.

Not a HUGE business plan that you put into the drawer and forget about. An action plan that does what it says: ACTION. I usually keep it to 90 days - to ensure that it is actionable, you reach your goal quickly, and you enjoy the ride.

That's what life is ALL about.

P.S. If you’ve gotten this far, you’re probably a likely candidate for the Rich Gee Group.

I can offer you a complimentary session. What is it?

It’s an actual coaching call — you can talk about ANY subject that you choose.
And there are only two openings a week – so act fast. They fill up quickly.

I GUARANTEE two things will happen:
1. You will experience a significant breakthrough for your business or career.
2. You will have the experience of being coached by a professional.

Bottom line, the only way to really know how coaching works is to experience it firsthand. Call me.

Rich Gee's Experience

CEO

Rich Gee Group

Privately Held; Professional Training & Coaching industry

2000 – Present (11 years)

I run one of the premier executive advisory and coaching firms in the nation. How can I say that? Well . . . I bring 20 years of corporate management knowledge, 10 years executive coaching experience, a practice of 20+ clients, 25+ corporate workshops yearly, and frequently tapped by The New York Times, The Wall Street Journal, and The Today Show for my views on business topics.

What’s the #1 problem you are best at solving for your clients?
I help my clients THINK BIG. We all tend to constrain ourselves in one way or another — we are our worst critics. I get my clients to focus on what they can really do and help them realize that the obstacles that hold them back are pretty easy to eliminate. On calls, I sometimes yell out, “Big Smile!” or “Think Bigger!”. Clients then get the message.

What are you most passionate about professionally?
I love helping people. I love to get people motivated, engaged, and energized. But more importantly, that we have fun during the process.

Look, I did this for 20 years in corporate, but I had to deal with all the corporate BS that trundles alongside. As a coach, I can directly impact my clients immediately — and they get it. I don’t spend my time critiquing them – we look for opportunities for them to shine and acknowledge their successes. That excites me.

Anything else you’d like to tell people about yourself?
I like to have fun. And I want all of my clients to have fun too.
