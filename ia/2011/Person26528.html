Lisandro Colantonio, MD MSc(c)'s Summary

Physician, graduated from the School of Medicine, National University of La Plata (SM-NULP), Argentina (2002). In 2006 completed his residence in Internal Medicine at the National Hospital “Profesor Alejandro Posadas”, El Palomar, Argentina. Internal Medicine Specialist, graduated from the School of Medicine, University of Buenos Aires (SM-UBA), Argentina (2007). Graduated with honors from the Clinical Effectiveness Master Program, SM-UBA (2007). Magister candidate in Clinical Effectiveness, SM-UBA. In 2009 he was selected as Fellow of the Fogarty International Clinical Research Program in Global Health (Institute for Global Health, Vanderbilt University, Nashville, TN).

Currently he is Fellow of the Research Training Program on Tobacco Control in Argentina (CICTA) and Reviewer at the Argentinean Center of the Cochrane Collaboration. Since 2006 he is Lecturer at the Department of Public Health, SM-UBA.

From 2005 to 2010 he has worked at the Institute for Clinical Effectiveness and Health Policy (IECS), Buenos Aires, Argentina, where he has conducted several Health Economic Evaluations, Systematic Reviews and Burden of Disease analysis, mainly on cardiovascular diseases. From 1999 to 2003 he was Research Assistant at the Department of Pharmacology, SM-NULP, where he collaborated in several studies on Pharmacoepidemiology.

Specialties

Design and conduction of Health Economic Evaluations and Systematic Reviews.
