Chris Pinto, CPC’s Summary

Pinto Employment Search is a nationwide recruiting firm specializing in the Transportation Industry. The Pinto Team has over 25 years working experience in the shipping industry and has recruited and placed transportation professionals in successful positions for many years. Pinto’s expertise is drawn directly from being employed in the same jobs that we recruit. We know exactly what transportation job seekers and employers want and get the results they need.

Chris Pinto, CPC’s Specialties:

Successfully placing qualified candidates in all levels of the transportation industry from entry level up to senior management.
