Adam Karon’s Summary

Adam Karon is the Senior Director of Akamai’s Global Services and Support organization. He leads Professional Services, Sales Engineering, and Account Management focusing on Akamai’s Digital Media customers. He has been at Akamai Technologies for more than 6 years, where he has been a key contributor to Akamai's Professional Services Organization’s growth from its early beginnings to its current position as a leading Global Services Leader working with the Internet’s most influential companies.

Adam joined Akamai from Leftbrain Solutions, where he served as Client Director for General Electric’s Power Systems division and the lead for their “Procure to Pay” digitalization effort. He brings more than 17 years of organizational management, business operations and technical experience to lead Akamai in addressing the rapidly changing online technology space.
