Shawn Morton’s Summary

I have over 14 years of web product development and digital marketing experience in both startup and Fortune 100 companies.

I am currently the Director of Mobile, Social Media and Emerging Media as part of Nationwide Insurance's digital marketing team. I lead an award-winning digital marketing team responsible for our mobile marketing (including our Nationwide Mobile iPhone & Android apps), social media strategy & execution (Facebook, Twitter, YouTube), and emerging media (gaming, connected TV, etc.).

The Nationwide Mobile app won a 2010 DMA ECHO award and was AdAge's #4 Coolest Branded App for 2009. Our digital integration for our The World's Greatest Spokesperson in the World campaign won a 2010 Bronze iab MIXX award.

I spent the previous 9 years at CNET Networks where I lead web product development and large-scale community/social media initiatives.

In my spare time from 2006-2008, I also built one of the first social media aggregators at Profilactic.com. Like FriendFeed which followed it, Profilactic brought together all of your and your friends' social media content into a single stream.

I have also led user experience and usability consulting projects for companies including Office Depot, Yahoo! Shopping, Sony, HCA Healthcare and Kemper Insurance.

In addition to my work on the web, I am also a professional writer, blogger and speaker. I am currently a contributing author for CNET's TechRepublic covering smartphones and mobile technology.

I regularly speak at a variety of local and national digital marketing events including BlogWorld Expo, Bazaarvoice Social Commerce Summit and ad:tech. I have also been interviewed for articles and research reports for Forrester and eMarketer.

I have written articles on web development & personal technology for CNET and was the editor of TechRepublic's consumer electronics blog Practical Gadgetry.

I have maintained a personal blog at http://www.smorty71.com since 2004. I am sMoRTy71 on Twitter.
