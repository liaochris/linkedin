Nigel Merrick’s Summary

I started my professional photography career in 2000 when I left the world of IT to open an underwater photography and video center in the Egyptian Red Sea, which was a wonderful experience.

After spending several years there and more time in Grand Cayman, I moved to the US in 2004 to start up a professional photography studio, concentrating on weddings, portraits and commercial photography.

I truly love photography and, more than that, I devote my life to helping my clients capture the meaningful moments of their lives. This is one reason why my chosen tagline is "Images Inspired By You..."

My goal for the future is to grow the studio into a prestige photography practice, based on traditional family values and excellent client relationships.
