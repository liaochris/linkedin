Michael Apfelberg's Summary

After 15 years in the printing & publishing field (Training, QA, Customer Service, TQM, Managment), it was time to step out of my box and "go entrepreneurial." In 2006 we (my wife and I) founded Educational Outfitters of Nashua, and have never looked back. Today we serve customers throughout Southern NH and Northern MA with school uniforms, embroidery, logo apparel, screenprinting, promotional products, Girls Scouts, Cub Scouts, and Boy Scouts Uniforms and Merchandise.

You can check us out at http://www.eo-ne.com

Specialties

printing and prepress; direct mail; purchasing; retail; embroidery; graphics; customer service
