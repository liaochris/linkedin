Andrew Steele’s Summary

As an executive that is both personally and professionally passionate about technology and its capacity to influence and improve consumer experiences, I have focused my career around building sustainable businesses to achieve that goal.

Throughout my 20 year career, I have led business development, strategy, sales and marketing teams in both high-growth startup and turn-around environments, contributing to over $100MM in venture and strategic startup capital raised, over $2B in strategic investments at Microsoft into the cable and telecom sectors, and four M&A transactions with aggregate value approaching $500MM.

Key areas of expertise include:

• Experienced dealmaker with proven track record developing partnership strategy and negotiating & closing complex deals
• Energetic and natural leader with demonstrated ability to lead, mentor and grow successful teams
• Domain expertise spanning P&L management, Sales, Marketing, Strategy & Business Development and Financial Analysis
• Sector DNA in consumer web, communications, mobile, digital media & advertising

Andrew Steele’s Specialties:

Leadership and team building; raising startup capital; developing, negotiating and closing complex licensing agreements; partnership strategy, development and growth; success in dynamic environments

    Truedomain, Inc

    (Privately Held; Computer & Network Security industry)

    April 2009 — Present (1 year 10 months)

    Truedomain is bringing trust back to email through our end-to-end authentication solution, which connects email receivers with domain owners through a standards based technology framework and services layer to stop email-borne threats before they can reach their intended targets. In addition, Truedomain provides rich set of data and analytics about the mail stream including authentication performance, delivery results, spoofing and phishing activity and a wide range of key forensic data to assist in identifying attack vectors and pursuing back-end mitigation and enforcement efforts.
