Selim Eren, PE, LEED AP's Summary

Selim Eren is a Registered Professional Civil Engineer and Construction Project Management Professional with more than 7 years of construction experience ranging from small construction projects as an Engineer Officer to $30+M construction projects as a Project Manager.

His professional experience includes institutional facilities, commercial buildings, new airport terminal, infrastructure transportation and public works projects.

He worked on the construction, modernization, seismic retrofit and infrastructure of K-12 institutional facilities under the jurisdiction of Division of the State Architect (DSA).
