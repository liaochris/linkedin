Raymond Frazier's Summary

I founded Frazier Wealth Management for one purpose....to help people live more abundant lives. The uber-wealthy can afford to pay big bucks to wealth strategists. Famous people always seem to attract wealth strategists who want to get in on the glamour. But....what about the average person who has worked hard, perhaps managed to accumulate a modest amount? Even if you are not as rich as Ted Turner or as famous as Oprah Winfrey...aren't you worthy of having a wealth strategist? Frazier Wealth Management believes that you are. We specialize in working with people who have $120,000 to $5 million in investable assets. We offer a complete advisory program designed to help clients work toward achieving their goals...not just financial goals....but LIFE goals. Our philosophy is that it is about more than money....it's about life....YOUR LIFE. Many people settle for far too little out of life. People deserve to live abundant lives.....and in America....it can happen !!! We help them identify goals...develop a plan to work toward them....and then we help them implement the plan and monitor it. We treat each person as an individual. We do not provide cookie cutter planning. We customize for each client. Our goal is not to attract thousands of clients. We accept only a limited number of new clients each year. This allows us to provide only the finest attention and service to each client.
