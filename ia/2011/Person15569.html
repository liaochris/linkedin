Gary Richman’s Summary

I am a Product Marketing Executive, Engineer and Wharton MBA with an entrepreneurial spirit and a record of success driving consumer technologies in both small and large companies. I have a wide breadth of marketing management experiences including product marketing, product management, brand marketing, channel marketing, business development and sales. I approach business as a strategy developer and an analytical problem solver with a strong results orientation. I also have a history of increasing revenue and profit and driving staffs to greater productivity. I have a passion for, and experience in various consumer software & technology initiatives. I have championed, drove requirements, and marketed consumer technologies including wireless streaming media, PC ‘instant-on’ functionality, Personal Area Networks, smart phones, HD and Blu-Ray, Wi-Fi, WiMax, Flash memory, an education web site, and GPS mapping software.

My career is focused on leveraging my experience and leadership in consumer software and hardware to deliver compelling technology solutions that help people be more productive and enjoy themselves.
