Darrin Hyrup’s Summary

A 30-odd year veteran of the computer games industry, with the last 24 years or so focused in online games and massively multiplayer online games in particular.

Over the years I have worn nearly every hat in the game industry, many of them simultaneously. I have also contributed to the development of more than 30 game titles (7 of which are MMO's or expansions) and built 3 successful online game platforms.

As a founder at Mythic Entertainment, I learned a lot about building and operating a successful game development studio, and about the game business in general... both good and bad.

Darrin Hyrup’s Specialties:

Strategic planning, workforce management, game design, software systems architecture, IT infrastructure architecture, technology guidance, and implementation & operations of massively multiplayer online games.
