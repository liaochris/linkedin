Rob Bennett’s Summary

A highly skilled, reputable, and goal driven Senior Business Development and Carrier Relations Executive, with over 12 years experience primarily in International Wholesale Voice Termination with a proven track record of building and leading successful partner relationships with all levels of Carriers Worldwide.

Mr. Bennett has held prior elite positions such as Director of Purchasing with major PTTs, Swisscom North America and Sonera. Applied entrepreneurial skills by successfully establishing, funding and operating Mantis Telecom, Inc., as a profitable International 214 licensed Carrier.

Area of Expertise

Identifying and Representing Carriers which have true value. Expediting interconnections with other reputable and trusted relationships and companies, achieving a new successful and long term business arrangement.
