Larry Arth [LION]'s Summary

We have been tracking the fundamentals of Real Estate Investing. Specifically as we say in our Tagline "Knowing where to invest."
We have learned that there are many niches to Real Estate investing that work such as short sales, singly family versus multi-family investments, etc, etc. What we have done is identified the fundamentals of what makes a Real Estate market rise and fall and put together a forecast based on Job Growth, Local government incentives to attract jobs, population growth, the rise and the fall of building permits being pulled, the absorption rate, the days on market, the affordability index and the list goes on and on. Very clear pictures emerge about markets. We then work with local builders, re-devlopers, wholesalers, etc to create great cash flowing properties in markets that are poised for rapid and sustained growth.'
You Can learn more about this by Calling Larry Arth at the Equity builders Group of Sun Castle Realty (727-808-8735 and visit us at www.theequitybuildersgroup.com
