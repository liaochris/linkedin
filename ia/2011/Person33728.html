Suman Pandey’s Summary

President and founder of Explore Himalaya Travel & Adventure.

Actively involved in the Travel & Tourism business for almost 20 years.

President of TAAN (Trekking Agent's Association of Nepal) from 2001-2003.

Served as an Executive member of the Nepal Tourism Board from 2004-06 and is now in the advisory panel of the same.

Avid promoter of the entire Himalayan region of Nepal, Tibet, India and Bhutan in the international tourism arena.

Out of box activities and events such as World's highest Skydiving in Everest, Coordinating world's highest cabinet meeting.

Lifetime Achievement Award in 2008 by the American Biographical Institute in recognition of contribution and achievement in tourism.
