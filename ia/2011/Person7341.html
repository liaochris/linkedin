catherine kirkpatrick's Summary

I am a graphic artist and fine art photographer who has won awards from the Connecticut Academy of Fine Arts, the Foundry Art Centre, the Museum of Anthropology, the Center for Visual Arts, the Bloomingdale Museum and others. I am on the board of Professional Women Photographers, and am launching the PWP History Project, that includes an oral history and blog devoted to the experience of women photographers from the 1970's to the present day. It will touch on the social feel of the times, NYC in the bad old days, and the change of photography from film to digital. I am also working on a series of photographs about women looking back on their lives, and another on Long Island City, an area of New York that is rapidly changing..
