Leonard Sparks’s Summary

I have five years experience as a journalist and graduated in May 2009 with a master's in journalism. My peregrinations include an embed assignment in Iraq and stints covering the Baltimore City Council, the Maryland General Assembly and Capitol Hill. I have also covered school board and commission hearings, and trials in state and federal courts. I know how to work a beat, including cultivating sources and mining public records. I am adept at using Excel and Access to "interview" data and know the basics of SPSS. I can also shoot and edit digital photos and video, and am a genuine enthusiast for new media.

Leonard Sparks’s Specialties:

Beat reporting, copy editing, digital photography and video, photo and video editing, computer-assisted reporting (Excel, Access and SPSS)
