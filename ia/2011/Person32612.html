Nick Morgan's Summary

Recently launched Caddis Partners in partnership with MSI. MSI owns, operates, and manages over 60 Apartment Complexes throughout the USA. We offer investors/partners preferred returns of 7% per year with additional participation in the back-end profits. We aim to return 12-14% per year. We do this by purchasing small preferred equity positions in multiple apartment complexes with incredible cash-flow. Our Investors do not have the stress of the stock market causing them to wonder if the will be able to retire. We send monthly checks to our investors.

Spent multiple years in the Financial Planning industry with Northwestern Mutual Financial Network and left there in 2003 to begin a career as a consultant for Real Estate Development and Software.
