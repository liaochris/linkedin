Stuart Norrie’s Summary

My name is Stuart Norrie and my entire reason for going to college and obtaining degrees of higher learning is to be able to work for a strong, successful and inspiring company. I am a current graduate student of web design and new media at the Academy of Art University in San Francisco and I am a graduate of Brooks Institute of Photography with a degree in digital imaging; I have been honing my design skills in print and digital mediums ever since I entered college and I want nothing more than to work for a company that is on the forefront of of their industry. My skills, strong work ethic and enthusiastic persona make me an ideal candidate for any graphic design or photography position. As my graduate program draws to a close I am doing everything I can to improve my skills and move into the professional arena ready to create beautiful and compelling designs.
