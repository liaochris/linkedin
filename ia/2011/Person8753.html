Claire Mason's Summary

I have been working in international accountancy recruitment for over 5 years. My career has been entirely devoted to this sector with a particular focus towards Public Practice firms. I have worked with accountants at all levels, from Audit Seniors to Partners, and successfully helped them to attain their dream job overseas.

I have travelled and worked internationally, visiting countries such as the USA, Canada, Caribbean, South Africa, South East Asia and many countries in Europe. I can fully appreciate the benefits that these experiences can bring and highly recommend it to everyone.

As a recruiter, I believe in delivering a high quality service to all. I am dedicated to my candidates and clients needs and strive to exceed expectations. As a result I have achieved a lot of new business from referrals and am proud to have
received excellent testimonials from both candidates and clients.

Specialties

Locations I recruit into include (but not limited to): UK, Ireland, Europe (Luxembourg, France, Germany, Switzerland etc), Middle East (UAE, Saudi Arabia), Far East (China, Hong Kong, Singapore), Australia, New Zealand, Bermuda, Caribbean, USA and Canada.

Companies I work with include: KPMG, PwC, Ernst & Young, Deloitte, BDO, PKF, Grant Thornton, Goldman Sachs, JP Morgan, Barclays, Procter and Gamble, Coca Cola, Sara Lee, Macquarie, TNT, Hertz, Adidas, and more...
