Steven Lewicky’s Summary

I represent companies and individuals in Maryland and the District of Columbia in litigation matters and in business transactions. Prior to joining Davis, Agnor, Rapaport & Skalny I served as Vice President and General Counsel for venture-backed companies in San Francisco, and I also worked for Intel Corporation in California. Earlier in my career I was engaged in litigation practice in Chevy Chase, Maryland. In addition to a law degree, I hold an MBA from the University of Maryland.
