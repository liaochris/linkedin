Darren Pierson’s Summary

Professional technical communicator with experience in technical, promotional, and procedural writing to targeted audiences. I currently work at Starkey Labs, Inc. writing test procedures and conducting usability tests for new product development. Starkey is a world leader in the design, development and distribution of comprehensive hearing solutions. We believe in using superior hearing technology as a means to something even greater - creating meaningful connections between people and their worlds.

Prior to Starkey, I worked at Emerson Process Management as a Technical Writer. I created, edited, and updated all technical documentation and marketing collateral for the Rosemount Flow Division and Rosemount Nuclear Instruments.

Managed the communication efforts for eleven different candidates seeking government office in Minnesota; writing speeches, producing targeted campaign literature and increasing exposure.

Interned at Boston Scientific assisting in writing clean room descriptions, corporate giving initiatives, and corporate sponsorship programs. Boston Scientific's strategy is to lead global markets for less-invasive medical devices by developing and marketing innovative products, services and therapies that address unmet patient needs, provide superior clinical outcomes and demonstrate proven economic value.

Teaching assistant at the University of Minnesota in a high level corporate communications course (Comm3441) advising students on a simulation project, real-world corporate scenario.

Wherever I have worked, I brought three key strengths to the table: a passion for writing and communicating technical information, dedication to the art of gathering information from SME’s while being concise and accurate, and a strong work ethic. At this time, my future plans are to continue my work in technical communications to drive the development of documentation that promotes products, technologies and processes.

Darren Pierson’s Specialties:

Gathering and deciphering information, writing logical test procedures, target marketing, writing to specific audiences, process improvement, promotional and technical writing, information development, and public relations.
