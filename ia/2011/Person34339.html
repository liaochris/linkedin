Paul Lorenz's Summary

My background in architecture (15 years) has given me the confidence and perspective to follow numerous creative paths: interior architecture, fine art education and film festival management. I identify myself as an artist (independent since 1999), first and foremost. With commissioned work in New York, numerous group and solo exhibitions under my belt, I keep my eyes open for creative opportunities all over the world.

Specialties

Oil painting on canvas, panel or paper. Large and small scale projects. National or international scope.

STUDIO mars

Fine Art industry

2005 – Present (6 years)

STUDIO mars is my private art studio in Paducah, Kentucky where I explore abstraction through oil paintings, water based media and Bauhaus inspired drawings.

I am currently an MFA instructor with the Academy of Art University, San Francisco, California, since 2001 working through their cyber-campus.

The River's Edge International Film Festival is a project I helped start in Paducah, Kentucky in 2005. From 2005-08 I have been chairman of film selection, and from 2007-08, co-director of the festival. I will resume being co-director and chairman of film selection for the 2010 festival.
