Paul Vassiliou’s Summary

Paul has extensive experience in engineering, sales and market development with various multinational corporations spanning 23 years on two continents. His career includes business sectors ranging from oil and natural gas to distribution and in progressively senior positions in engineering, sales, energy utilization, and market development.

For the past several years, Paul has leveraged his corporate experience into the Human Capital marketplace as a specialist in recruiting sr. level engineering, environmental, energy, and sales professionals from executive to mid-level management. Paul is a Managing Partner with Hire Results Ltd. a leading search company that has developed the “next generation” Hiring Simulation and On-Demand 360° Referencing™ assessment tools having positively impacted organizations throughout North America. He speaks regularly at industry events on topics such as:

• Managing Hiring Risk through unique and innovative techniques
• Use of Hiring Simulation to dramatically impact your hiring success
• Managing Sales Force Risk through innovative hiring processes

Paul holds a Bachelor of Technology degree in Civil Engineering and Bachelor of Science degree in Industrial Technology. He is also registered as a Chartered Industrial Energy Consultant.
