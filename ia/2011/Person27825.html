Marc Villacorta's Summary

I have an advanced knowledge of Linux systems. Most of my experience has been under RedHat-like and Debian-like distributions. I feel confident setting up systems and I am not afraid of kernel patching, source code customizations and system tuning and hardening.

I worked closely with development teams and designed and maintained critical production environments. I have developed Linux daemons in C, automated tasks in BASH, deployed many LAMP scenarios, packaged software in RPMs, virtualized servers in EC2, replicated data with DRBD, enabled high availability with PaceMaker, established VPNs with OpenSWAN and OpenVPN, configured DNS with bind, mangled packets with netfilter / iptables, fixed routing tables in Linux and IOS, shaped network traffic with Traffic Control and many more.

After many years of using Windows systems, I have become an advanced user with skills to successfully track down complex problems. I also have developed basic kernel drivers (ring 0) and services (ring 1) and therefore I have a basic idea of windows system internals. In my early years as a helpdesk assistant I acquired skills to deal with customer situations.

I am an intermediate but still learning programmer, most confident in C but also skilled in Bash and JavaScript. I have a base of knowledge about networking protocols such as Ethernet, IP, TCP, UDP, RTP, HTTP, SNMP, SIP and SDP. I have experience capturing network traffic and analyzing complex NAT and routing scenarios.

Specialties

LINUX, IP, IPSEC, TCP, UDP, RTP, HTTP, SIP, SDP, NTP, SNMP, SSH, C, HTML, JAVASCRIPT, IPTABLES, EC2, TCPDUMP, VPN, VLAN, DHCP, SAMBA, APACHE, MYSQL, OPENSIPS, ASTERISK, DNS, TCP/IP, ROUTING
