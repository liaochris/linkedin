Natalie Sutton's Summary

I do PR because I love it.

I boast more than 17 years experience in PR and work to an ethos that balances creativity with good old fashioned hard graft. I try to make it fun for my clients, and always exceed their expectations.

I'm a senior consultant that has worked in-house as well as agency side. Able to consult strategically as well as write first class copy.

I believe passionately in delivering a balance of short-term results and holistic, objective-driven campaigns. In the modern PR enviroment, this translates to establishing a strong content engine and driving co-ordinated and consistent communications across traditional press channels, social media and right through the marketing mix.

Cost-effective media relations, strategic consultancy, product launches, press releases, case studies, feature articles, headline-driven research, SEO, social media relations, influencer relations.
