Dan Tran’s Summary

Had more than 19 years of experience in Information Technology management and software development, with the last 8 years focusing on data warehouse solutions. Managed a team of 50 developers and system analysts to eliminate spreadsheets by delivering analytical, global Web applications to support all Motorola's Broadband Mobility Sector and saving Motorola millions in automating manual efforts. Led a team to implement eMART, which became the de facto standard for Networks' Supply Chain business information. Led a team to implement the Networks Data Warehouse, using Informatica PowerCenter ETL tool, to support Supply Chain, Finance, and Services businesses. Led a team to implement ESP, which saved Motorola millions in quality cost. Led a team to developed GENESYS as the standard MES solution for Networks. Implemented HA, cluster environment for eMART, GENESYS, and the Networks DW.

Led a team to globalize eMART, which became the source of shipment, on-time delivery, and various operation information for the Account, Distribution, and Manufacturing teams within the Integrated Supply Chain. Led a team to implement BrioQuery as a standard, ad hoc reporting tool for Networks to report data from various manufacturing and ERP databases.

    Led a team to implement ESP, a quality application using build data from manufacturing systems, shipment data from the Oracle ERP and return data from warranty systems, to help detect and predict product failures. The project was a big success, is actively being used by the engineering and manufacturing communities, and has saved Motorola millions of dollars in quality and development costs.
