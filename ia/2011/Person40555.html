Sjaak Hendriks’s Summary

Mr Hendriks is a highly experienced project manager, trainer and entrepreneur with 30 years experience, 25 of which were with Blue Chip Multi-Nationals. His key assignments have focused on project management of ICT solutions implementation, process redesign, improvement and implementation, and PABX manufacturing start-up.
Sjaak started training in 1988 within Philips, where he developed material and delivered training to Manufacturing, Testing and Quality Assurance staff in the Netherlands and China.
Sjaak has also developed material and lectured at Post graduate level in Business Telecommunications including Project Management.
Sjaak has started up several companies in the Netherlands, Thailand and Singapore, delivering SAP implementation, project management training, telecom installation & consultancy and consumer products manufacturing.
Sjaak’s international experience covers most West-European countries, as well as China, Hong Kong, Taiwan, Malaysia, Thailand, Singapore, Philippines, South Africa and Nigeria.
Mr. Hendriks has a post-graduate degree from The Hague Highschool in Business Telecommunication, and a degree from Leeuwarden High School in Information and Computer Technology.
In addition to being an accredited PRINCE2 trainer, Mr. Hendriks has also studied APMG’s Program Management standard to Practitioner level.
