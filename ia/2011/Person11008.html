David Schmidt’s Summary

David is a landscape architect analyst who brings exceptional skills and a balanced approach to site design and community planning. He is a graduate of the University of Arkansas with a Bachelor in Landscape Architecture and a member of the American Society of Landscape Architects as well as the U.S. Green Building Council. His background includes coordination of multidisciplinary design teams, working efficiently to integrate the client’s needs, site characteristics, project economics and environmental sustainability. David’s work advances the innovative use of materials to create sustainable and green environments. He is dedicated to creating places that are artful, contextually engaged, dynamic and ecologically sound.

David Schmidt’s Specialties:

David expresses his passion for healthy communities not only through professional practice, but also through community involvement. David is an active member of the City of Sarasota Habitat for Humanity where he facilitates teams to create local, sustainable homes. David has committed additional efforts into becoming a LEED-GA(leadership in energy and environmental design Green Associate) and plans to be registered soon.
