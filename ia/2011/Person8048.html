Chris Bunney's Summary

Creative entrepreneur with over eighteen years of media experience working in film, video, interactive and event media. Advertising, marketing, communication, entertainment and design experience on initiatives for Apple, BNP Paribas, Dreamworks, Ducati Motor, Fox Interactive Media, General Motors, HP, Intel, Microsoft, NBC/Universal, SanDisk, Sprint, Walt Disney, Wildlife Conservation Network and Yahoo!. Productions for these brand names include Websites, product launch videos, rich-media demos, live events and broadcast commercial spots.
