Naghi Prasad’s Summary

I am currently VP of Engineering at Tapjoy, the leader in distribution and monetization of Social and Mobile games and applications.

Previously, I was the VP of Engineering for Turn, a novel contextual advertising network. Prior to that, I was the VP of Engineering at Verity for Enterprise Search products that accounted for $100 million dollars of Verity’s annual revenues.

At PeopleSoft, I was the director of development for Enterprise Data Warehousing and business intelligence products group. As a director of engineering at VerticalNet, my team created the award-winning suite of Spend Analytics products. I was a key member of the Open Services marketplace initiative, one of the earliest Web Services applications.

As a senior manager at the Center for Strategic Technology research of Accenture, I was the lead on projects involving simulation, modeling, text search and smart environments. My job also ensured smooth transfer of promising technologies to Accenture’s practice areas and present technology assessments to clients.

I have a Ph.D. in Artificial Intelligence and Agent-based Systems from the University of Massachusetts, MS from Massachusetts Institute of Technology, and MS in Computer Science and B. Tech in Electrical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology, Chennai.
