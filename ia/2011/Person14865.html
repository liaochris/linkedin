Flavia Paganelli's Summary

Passionate about building high quality software while solving real-world users' problems, technology is my means instead of my goal.
The highest satisfaction in my work is to find out that what I built is useful to (many!) people. Technology and software engineering give me the tools to achieve these goals in the most efficient way and having fun in the process.

With a background in Computer Science, I've been building software in diverse industries, languages and teams for 14 years. And I keep enjoying every new challenge, and looking for a new one every time.

I'm currently building the very well-known Augmented Reality application, Layar, discovering how to engage millions of people with this new technology.
My other recent passion is Decaf, a mobile application with which users can check on their Amazon cloud. Android technology and 'the cloud' allows us to manage servers and fix eventualities with the phone from anywhere in the world.

Specialties

augmented reality, android, the cloud, object oriented design, web applications, user interfaces
