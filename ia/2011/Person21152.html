Johan Strydom's Summary

Johan is an executive manager with more than 10 years in senior positions with particularly strong experience in building Internet based businesses. Johan has good managerial skills combined with substantial understanding of all the key business disciplines including finance, sales, marketing and operations. Johan has managed both small entrepreneurial companies from start-up, through to participation in global strategic planning for multi-national corporations.

Specialties

Deal negotiation, business structuring, business plans / models, B2C Internet business, B2B Internet business. Professional business management and enhancement, from start-up through turn around.

Johan Strydom's Experience

Managing Executive

PropertyGenie

Internet industry

December 2006 – Present (4 years 3 months)

PropertyGenie is South Africa's leading real estate portal bringing together all the best properties for sale from South Africa's top real estate agencies.

Johan is the head of this business and as such is responsible for setting and implementing strategy, managing P&L, negotiating key partnerships, business development, product strategy, recruitment, key account management and all other general business management.

Johan has led the growth of the portal to become one of the largest property portals in South Africa with well over 120,000 properties for sale offered by over a 1,000 real estate offices across the country.

Business Development

Brand Knew

Publishing industry

February 2005 – November 2006 (1 year 10 months)

Brand Knew is a Cape Town based media company specialising in the production and publishing of children's entertainment and educational content.
It employs a unique concept of icon-recognition to teach literacy and language skills through the use of brand logos. Brands pay annually to participate in a program which typically includes weekly publication in large circulation magazines / newspapers and free content distribution to schools.
Johan held the rights to exploit the Brand Knew concept in territories outside of South Africa. This involved identifying and meeting with various potential international media partners in India, Italy, Brazil and the UK to assist with international expansion. In addition, Johan was instrumental in commissioning and overseeing the production of a 3-D animated television pilot for the Brand Knew children's characters, Bluster & Tikolo.

General Manager - icanonline

M-Web

Publishing industry

1998 – 2005 (7 years)

Johan joined M-Web with the brief to devise and execute an online financial services portal strategy for the Naspers media group.
Stage one was the creation of Moneymax, an investment website which combined the groups financial data assets such as news, co. information and share price information and packaged them for the online retail consumer. Despite facing significant competition at launch from established sites, Moneymax quickly became the no.1 private investor's website in South Africa and remained so until its merger with Finance24 in 2005.
Stage two of the strategy was the launch of an online transactional platform. This involved negotiating a joint venture with BoE Bank and BoE Stockbrokers to form icanonline. In addition to offering online banking and online share trading, icanonline also developed and patented a unique online payment mechanism which became the largest non-credit card online payment mechanism in the country within 2 years of launch.
