Joel Warady’s Summary

I am currently the Chief Marketing Officer for Enjoy Life Foods, the #1 brand in the Allergy Friendly, Gluten Free marketplace. Having spent a number of years on the agency and consulting side, I bring my expertise to the brand side, looking forward to being able to utilize my knowledge gained from numerous client campaigns, and focus on building a single brand.

For the past eight years, I have specialized in Marketing, Business Growth, & Social Media Consultant focused on companies with a desire to increase their customer engagement. We specialized in working with entrepreneurial small to mid-sized companies, as well as Fortune 500 companies. We also provided consulting on trends in marketing, assisting companies in their integration of Web 2.0 and social networking strategies into their overall marketing plan. We have worked on numerous Word-of-Mouth campaigns for our clients, both in the US and internationally.
