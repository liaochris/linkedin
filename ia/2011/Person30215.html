Michael Crane's Summary

I love ideas. I love the process of discovering ideas, evaluating them and even take a certain pleasure in shooting some down as well. Mostly, I love helping others unleash their creativity and venture into a place of unconsidered possibilities.

I've created, designed, developed and marketed products.

Having a curiosity for the workings of both the natural world and the business world, I earned undergrad degrees from Le Moyne College in both Biology and Business, and also hold an MBA in Sustainable Management from Presidio Graduate School.

Where I find myself today is playing in the nexus of design, natural systems, and the fast-changing landscape of business, helping companies develop business models and solutions that create positive social impact.
