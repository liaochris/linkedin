Millie Tiffany's Summary

The litigation firm at which I worked for 22 years recently closed due to the retirement of the senior partner. I have decided to put my experience to work as a freelance paralegal, providing virtual support to attorneys throughout the USA and personally to attorneys in northeastern Pennsylvania counties for attendance at court proceedings and on-site.

Although I loved my job in which my primary concentration was Plaintiff Personal Injury, Medical Malpractice and Wrongful Death and Survival actions, along with Family Law, I am looking forward to this new and exciting chapter of my life!

Specialties

Drafting of detailed and creative Recapitulations for Plaintiff personal injury demand pkgs, Arbitration and Mediation Memoranda, designed to humanize the Plaintiff, making his/her losses acutely felt & understood by defense counsel or third party/UIM adjuster, thereby increasing the claim's value, stimulating settlement & obviating the necessity of trial---the objective of every Plaintiffs' attorney!

Also highly skilled in all aspects of PA Family Law.
