2004 – 2010 (6 years)

Visible Assets provides systems based on IEEE 1902.1 "RuBee" wireless communications standard (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rubee). We work with market leaders who license RuBee for targeted markets including military, energy exploration, and tools. RuBee works in industrial environments (metal, water, people) where RFID does not. RuBee is suitable for deployment in high-security environments (no tempest/ target/ eavesdropping risks) and in high-explosive environments where RF technologies are not suitable.
