Nancy Rollins’s Summary

Nancy Rollins, M.S., LCPC, NCC is a Licensed Professional Counselor at the Center for Christian Life Enrichment who has worked for nearly 35 years in the Mental Health field. Special areas of interest for Nancy include assisting people who are dealing with life changes, loss, transitions, marital and relational challenges, and parenting concerns. Nancy has a passion for empowering women to live grounded, balanced and nourishing lives. She leads CLE's Moms Group and Sage Sisters, a group for women over 40. Nancy is also an accomplished marathon runner. Nancy started the CLE Running & Walking Club in 2002 where clients learn how to use exercise and fitness as a metaphor for their personal growth. Nancy and her husband have been married for over 25 years and have two sons.
