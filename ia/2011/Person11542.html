Dennis Snyder’s Summary

We creatively package and ship just about anything, anywhere. We have shipped everything from live pirannas to 34' box folding machines. Some of our biggest revenue producers have been specialized packaging and crating for delicate electronics equipment that has been shipped all over the world. With Dennis' technical expertise, we delight in handling the unusual to the outlandish. From 16th century original oils going to Sothebys for auction, Jar Jar, the 7 ft lizard creature in Star Wars which we sent ot Spain. At one time, we built and shipped specialized, fitted crates for a $1M exhibit for the Money Museum. We love doing the unusual that hones our creativity as well as packing and shipping the more mundane things such as a 2 bed room apartment to Hawaii.

Oh yes, in my prior career, I was an innovative system and integrated circuit design engineer, architect and marketer with 164 new products to my credit and 22 technical articles, book chapter and papers internationally published.
