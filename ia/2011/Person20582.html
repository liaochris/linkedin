JESSE CRIM, MSIA CISSP’s Summary

I am a Security Engineer who has been working in the Networking / Security Field for 14 Years and have earned a Master's Degree in Information Assurance. My experience and education has brought me the following: 8+ years extensive experience working in a large Enterprise environment to provide technical support with Network Security Equipment ranging from commercial to open source tools: vulnerability scanners, network monitoring, IPS/IDS, and endpoint enforcement devices such as Clean Access NAC. During the last 8+ years, I have supported Cisco IPS (4255,4260's), CNAC Admission Control (CAM/CAS), CSMARS 100/200, Firewall ASA 5500s, ACS Radius Server (AAA), Arbor NetFlow, and CiscoWork LMS. Beyond being technical, I continually work with Upper-Management to assist in security policies creation, technical documentation, and security awareness training. Over the last two years, I have had the honor to teach many students (old and young) my passion in the Technology Field.
