Kevin Young’s Summary

Kevin's career started at PC Connection in Keene, New Hampshire where he cut his technical teeth by manning the tech support lines.

After living a year in Japan teaching English, Kevin expressed his talents in design, marketing and operations management roles with companies in Boston and Los Angeles. Kevin edited two books on management published by Wiley (Integrated Management Systems) and Productivity Press (Four Practical Revolutions in Management).

While studying new media design at Harvard, Kevin conceptualized and launched a popular online magazine, 'Bay Review' which featured works by artists, photographers, poets, short fiction writers and reviewers from around the country.

For three years, Kevin managed his own successful online business selling new and used audio gear.

By 2006, Kevin was again caught in the Monadnock Region's magnetic pull. Since returning to New Hampshire, he has worked as a graphic designer and web administrator for New Chapter in Brattleboro, and as Information Director for Executive Search Information Services in Harrisville. Kevin is presently works in Procurement at C&S Wholesale Grocers in Keene.

In 2008, a dozen of Kevin's short stories were published in the anthology "Writer's Block: Short Fiction by Three New England Authors".

Kevin lives in New Hampshire with his wife and four children.
