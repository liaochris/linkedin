Sandra Iris Eilenstein's Summary

With 22 years of marketing and sales experience in diverse industries such as banking and publishing, I founded Information-Travels Public Relations in 2000. I’ve spent the last 13 years in PR agencies, having personally represented over 70 technology and electronics companies in the German-speaking marketplace and throughout Europe. In recent years our reach has extended into the areas of legal PR, representing Fish & Richardson - the world's largest patent law firm in Germany and the UK and fashion clients in Germany, the UK, Italy & France.

Our ultimate goal and permanent drive is long-lasting client relationships ... being an integral part of a client's communications team and working together with them to develop strategies to achieve our common goal. Our motto is "Quality not Quantity" and that describes our underlying mentality and motivation.

Specialties

Local market know-how and longstanding media relationships, US and German background and bi-linguality, deep understanding of the media landscape in Europe, constant motivation to deliver results that are far above expectations.
