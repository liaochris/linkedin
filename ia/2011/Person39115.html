VideoWalkIn (www.VideoWalkIn.com) provides easy and affordable way to support video conferencing with your potential customers on your website using just a webcam. Skype is for people you know. VideoWalkIn is for getting face to face with your potential customers who you do not know but want to connect with them.

This pay-as-you-go service includes:
- Video conferencing with up to 10 participants
- Full screen mode for video conferencing
- Screen sharing
- Guest queue management
- White board
- File transfer
- Audio chat
- Text Chat
- and more
