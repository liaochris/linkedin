Sean McCabe’s Summary

Sean McCabe has been in the entertainment industry since the tender age of 6, when he received his first guitar as a birthday present. With guitar in tow, Sean attended Ithaca and Siena College in New York from 1984-1989, pursuing his career in Music and Communications as both an entertainer and a producer. It was through his formative band years that Sean would befriend many of the musicians, producers and engineers, followed by other various industry professionals that now comprise one of the most comprehensive talent databases in the country. A dedicated, ethical, hard-working professional in the special events industry; an international celebrity performer*; a nationally recognized speaker at trade shows, conventions and expos*; a writer for syndicated magazines*; a Master of Ceremonies for major trade affiliations and showcase events; and a true expert in the entertainment arena, Sean has been in the biz as a producer, performer and a connoisseur of elite talent for his entire adult life. With appearances on network television (MTV, Entertainment Tonight, Extra, The Learning Channel, E, Jerry Springer); performances as a Master of Ceremonies, an accomplished guitarist; and one of The World's Largest Theme Park's most highly requested party hosts and emcees, Sean has a solid track record of incredibly successful events that has helped EC earn a reputation for being one of the most reliable, respected Talent Agencies in the entertainment industry.

Sean McCabe’s Specialties:

Founder, President and Owner of Entertainment Caterers, Inc., Sean has over 20 years of experience as an event producer, stage manager, professional disc jockey, master of ceremonies, interactive party host and guitarist. His talents have translated into incredibly successful celebrations across the country, including Disney Fairy Tale Weddings, Spectacular Bar and Bat Mitzvahs and Full Production Stage Shows.
