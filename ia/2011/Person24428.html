Safety ForLife Corp (Battery ForLife)

Wholesale industry

January 2009 – December 2009 (1 year)

It’s a known fact: smoke detector batteries should be replaced twice a year in order to maximize their efficiency. However, this practice is often enough neglected or simply forgotten. In order to answer this need, our company has developed an innovative project: Battery ForLifeMC.

Operating under the brand labels Battery ForLifeTM and Pile PourlaVieMC, our company, incorporated in Canada, proposes a fire prevention program, which aims at helping save lives and reducing material losses. To attain this mission, we supply 9 Volt quality batteries for smoke detectors in each Canadian household.

This mission is doubled by developing partnerships with numerous organisations (schools, sports associations, foundations, non-profit organisations, and others) that share our objectives. By partnering with Battery ForLifeTM, these organisations give life to the program while ensuring themselves revenue that is generated from Battery ForLifeTM fundraising campaign.

By participating in the Battery ForLifeTM program, clients are guaranteed to receive new batteries, designed especially for smoke detectors, every 6 months. Users that have subscribed to the program will henceforth preserve their family and belongings’ safety and enjoy peace of mind, while partner organisations will have guaranteed immediate and recurring funding through a fees system.
