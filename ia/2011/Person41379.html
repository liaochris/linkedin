Stephen Britton's Summary

From 2002 to 2010, I was Director of Technology at Bevmax Office Centers. In this position, I have set up and maintained data and voice networks at five office centers in Manhattan. I also provide computer and network troubleshooting for Bevmax's 350+ clients. I have a lot of experience with Windows XP, Vista, Windows 7, Macs and know how to remove viruses, malware, as well as build and upgrade systems. I am a certified CompTIA A+ computer tech, and a CompTIA Network+. I am currently studying for the Cisco CCNA exam. In addition to Windows, I also like to use Linux and Macintosh.

Prior to joining Bevmax, I worked as a web developer/designer at Franklin Health/Personal Path Systems from 1999 until 2001. In this position, I worked with several developers and designed the interface for Complex Case Management, an online health care management system used by field nurses at Blue Cross Blue Shield of South Carolina and Michigan.

Specialties

Network troubleshooting, fixing Windows XP and Vista issues, removing viruses and malware, and web developement using PHP, MySQL, CCS, and WordPress.

In addition to knowing technology, I am experienced newspaper reporter and have excellent writing skills.
