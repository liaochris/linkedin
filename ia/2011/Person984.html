Ajithaa Edirimane's Summary

Ajithaa Edirimane has over 20 years of experience in the dual roles of corporate lawyer cum company secretary of commercial establishments dealing in merchant banking, shipping and manufacturing to property development having headed the Legal Departments of the companies in which she served in Sri Lanka. She also taught at the Sri Lanka Law College on conveyancing and drafting of commercial contracts.

Her advice is sought in legal work relating to condominium / multi-storey properties. A book authored by her titled “Understanding the Concepts of Condominiums” is a comprehensive literature on the application of the law relating to condominium properties in Sri Lanka and it is referred by both students and practitioners.

She is a life member of the Bar Association of Sri Lanka (BASL) and a member of the Editorial Committee of the BASL Law Journal. She is a founder member of the Sri Lankan Alumni Association of the International Development Law Organization (IDLO) based in Rome, Italy and currently functions as an Intellectual Property (IP) Resource Person of IDLO in Sri Lanka. She also functioned as a member of the Committee on Title Registration of property appointed by the Minister of Justice & Law Reforms of Sri Lanka.

Specialties

property law, competition law, joint ventures, drafting commercial contracts, commercial arbitration, sale of goods, data governance, corporate law and corporate governance, Intellectual Property and International trade law are some of her areas of interest.
