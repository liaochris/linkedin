Stephen Palmer’s Summary

KGaps stands for Knowledge Gaps. Our company was formed when we realized how few businesses knew how to make the transition from old Industrial Age rules to new Information Age rules. We’re on a mission to bridge the critical gaps that limit the potential of so many businesses.

We help you bridge the gaps between old habits and new realities, between strategy and execution, between old and new media.
