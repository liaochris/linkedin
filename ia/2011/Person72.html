Avi Cohen’s Summary

As a philanthropist & social entrepreneur, I hate 2 bear bad news, but, $ is draining from your home & business.

I teach people how to get completely out of debt—including your mortgage—in under 10 years with the money they now make.

You can learn to become “un-vulnerable” to credit and mortgage companies, and even the economy just by making the best possible use of ever penny you now make. By following the easy- to-understand, effective strategies, that I teach you’ll wipe out all of your debt (including your mortgage) in under 10 years. Regardless of your personal lifestyle, you’ll lower your cost of living and increase the quality of your life.

You’ll learn:
➤ A simple, livable financial system that is based on the cold facts of mathematics and probability.
➤ How to eliminate ALL of your debts (including your mortgage) and how to avoid any new ones.
➤ How to get a return on your money of more than 25% that is guaranteed, no-risk because there is no investment.
➤ How to have enough cash to cover your normal expenses and handle emergencies so that you never need credit again.
➤ How you can still enjoy life as you’re getting out of debt.
➤ How to calculate when you will be out of debt and when you will be able to retire. Payments on debt now account for 92% of family income after taxes.
➤ How to thumb your nose at the credit bureaus, their prying questions and their impersonal rating because you’ll never need credit again.

At a time when delinquencies are at an all time high, personal bankruptcies are at a record high, exceeding 1.5 million in 2003, one-third of all senior citizens live below the poverty level and A mere 1% of retirees enjoy the same living standard as when they worked. Don’t you want to learn how to avoid the mess?

I don't sell investments or insurance. I don't teach or sell debt-consolidation or bi-monthly mortgage programs.
