Subhabrata Ghosh (SG)'s Summary

Imagine being the only one in a huge category. Loved and respected by everyone. A dream?
Jeans for young women, J21. Peel of the label and you can't tell the difference. Now differentiate. Hmmm... the secret was in the trial room. Hips don't lie. Unlocked the secret. If waists fit hips don't and vice-versa. Aha! Idea!*! Three hip sizes for every waist. Now the idea is embedded in the product. Triple digit growth.
Studylite. The manufacturer wanted to sell inverters!?. Where is the idea? Wait. Let's see if we can find one. Who suffers most in your home when there is no power. Wife and kids. Lets focus on kids. Darkness robs them of studytime. So anything will do. Hmmm. Secret locked away in their table lamp. Idea: How about a study light for kids with 6 hour back up power. Should be good for the eyes and look really hot. So lets get the eye doctors, engineers, a crack designer together. So now we have a Studylite that has a built in UPS with a six hour back up and simply eye candy. Check out www.studylite.in or google.

So that's what we do at Celsius100. Help people. To think differently. We live in a surplus society. Which has a surplus of similar companies, employing similar people. With similar social and educational backgrounds. Who come up with similar ideas, producing similar things of similar quality sold at similar prices. Yet the their purpose is to differentiate their brands and businesses.
Business value chains are built starting with a capability to create value. That capability is built on technology. But, technology is completely democratic. Sooner or later two scores will have it. It's too late for the marketing guys to make any difference. Turn the business value chain on it's head. Start with the consumer and their life. Think of an idea that makes a real difference. Then embed the idea in a product. Spend money to own the idea, not just the product.

That's the goal. Think upside down!

Specialties

People. Technology. Design. Ideas. Innovative thinking.
