Peter Backman’s Summary

User Experience (UX) and Usability professional with 15 years of track record in defining, designing and developing approachable, intuitive and engaging user experiences for the web and other platforms with a specialty in enterprise e-Business and consumer retail e-Commerce.

Strong background in applying analytically refined methods and practices to create web-based user experiences that are optimized for engagement, conversion and 'stickiness' in the case of retail e-Commerce, or optimized for efficiency, simplicity and intuitive adoption in the case of enterprise e-Business.

Solid experience in leading teams of design and development professionals to produce both best-practice as well as innovative approaches to solve complex shopping, buying or task-driven outcomes on the web and other platforms.

Long experience in the worlds of usability design, graphic design and application development for the web, allowing for a unique vantage from which to view and achieve excellent, satisfying and memorable experiences and applications.
