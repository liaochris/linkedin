Shimpei Okumura’s Summary

My name is Shimpei Okumura, a London based Interactive Designer, with a specific focus on user-centered design and graphic user interface. I graduated from a Masters Degree in Interactive Media from the University of the Arts London, London College of Communication. I am currently working in John Brown Media as a multimedia designer, dealing with UI design, Flash animation/interaction, wireframing, AfterEffects animation, FCP editing as well as filming.

I am interested in web2.0 based multi-platform communication. I have a strong insight into sophisticated graphical user interface design as well as professional motion graphic skills. These skills combined are essential, I believe, when creating mock-ups and animated user scenarios/story boards. I gained in-depth experience in creating interactive design whilst working on the IPTV menu interface. I also have a deep understanding of the user centered design process and have gained experience in a form of usability evaluation, called Heuristic evaluation, by testing a web application for a health care product in Japan. I have acquired a passion in identifying user needs and strongly believe that my methodology of the user centered process will enhance the quality of interactivity.

I always work extensively within a team environment and as an individual to create appropriate and aesthetically pleasing conceptual and digital imagery.
