Ahmed Hamdan's Summary

Since joining Optimal Technology Solutions (OTS) on 2008 as the CEO and member of BoD, Mr. Ahmed A. Hamdan has been leading the transformation of OTS from a local SMS reseller based in Saudi Arabia to one of the fastest growing regional ICT service providers, operating seven offices in Saudi Arabia, Egypt, Bahrain, Qatar, Sudan, and soon in Kuwait and Libya with further expansion plans across the MENA region.

In the last two years, OTS introduced an array of new services and products for consumer and commercial applications that lead to 140% increase in revenues during 2009. Those include, among others, SMS alerting solutions, bulk SMS services, online SMS Portals, as well as several digital and online marketing solutions, web based business solutions and CRM customization.

The result is a new OTS -- a company where online products account for 40 percent of revenue and commercial businesses account for 60 percent of revenue. Today, with adjusted gross-margins and pricing strategies, OTS offers a portfolio of profit-generating ICT businesses.

Mr. Hamdan brings to the task his experience from a 6-year-career at Saudi ICT companies, where he worked in different technical and business consultancy roles.
