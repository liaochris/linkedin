KPMRS ~ Rank Checker Tool's Summary

KPMRS is a free website ranking monitoring SEO tool. It tracks search engine positions for keywords or phrases on Google, Yahoo, Bing and provides a nice report of your site's rankings in real time.


KPMRS is a perfect SEO tool for internet marketers, SEO consultants and website owners. We understand how important it is to keep an eye on your search engine rankings.


We support rankings for up to 12 countries - UK, US, Australia, Brazil, France, Italy, India, Denmark, Norway, Sweden, Canada, and Germany.
