Guy Martin’s Summary

2011 will mark a defining point in my life as I leave my birth country of Australia, and move to Denmark to be with my fiancée. Not satisfied that the challenge of moving to a new culture and new climate was enough, I am also starting a technical services consultancy, working closely with various VingCard business units and business partners.

This will mean I continue to use the skills in IT (software/networking/ZigBee), mechanics (locks/encoders/safes/HVAC), electronics (lock control units/encoders/safes/energy management), and communication (hotel staff/guests/suppliers) that I have been cultivating at my previous position as the Service Manager for Vingcard Elsafe servicing hotels across Victoria, South Australia and Tasmania. I will also be working on some social media projects with VC Marketing.

I'm actively connecting with other service and sales people within the VingCard realm, to foster an open and collaborative global environment where knowledge is shared between business partners and business units in order to better serve the customer.

In 2004 I submitted a design for an international logo competition for Wikipedia, a Top 10 Global website. While my design wasn't chosen for the Wikipedia, the members of what was to become the Wikimedia Foundation asked if they could use that design to represent the Foundation as they felt it was a perfect fit. My design has now become the basis of the Wikimedia Visual Identity Guidelines, and is seen by millions of people around the world. I'm pretty happy with that.
