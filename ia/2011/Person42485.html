Tanya Gulliver's Summary

My main focus right now is as a PhD student in Environmental Studies at York University.

In the past I have worked in a variety of jobs; mostly related to social justice.

As a consultant I am available for strategic planning, goal setting, action planning and training on community development, advocacy, media relations, poverty and homelessness.

As a writer, I have experience with news, reporting, features, in-depth research pieces and much more.

My background of almost 8 years as a university instructor has provided me with great connections, and skills in training and curriculum development.
