Deanna Graves's Summary

With a degree in Marketing, I set out to conquer the world! I spent years working hard, gaining diverse skills, and filling my professional tool kit with a variety of experiences. Through it all, I felt the pull of my creative side, urging me to make a change. And so Daisy's Petals was born...

I entered the handcrafted arena by launching my own shop on Etsy, an online marketplace for handcrafted and vintage goods. For the past year I have gathered information on the ins and outs of Etsy, tweaked my shop when needed, actively participated in social networking sites, such as twitter, facebook, and flickr to gain exposure for my business and my products, as well as organized and run several "open house" sale nights. I believe there is always something new to learn, and I constantly search for ideas on how to improve my business and increase sales.

My marketing and business background have been extremely helpful in operating Daisy's Petals. They have given me a foundation on which to build and grow. Through various marketing efforts, I have achieved my monthly goals each month in 2009. I will strive to continue on this route for the remainder of the year, and to assess and reset these goals for 2010. I believe the sky is the limit!
