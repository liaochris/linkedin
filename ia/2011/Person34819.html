I am an experienced software engineer, with in depth knowledge of web application development, desktop application development and user interface design.

I enjoy getting my hands dirty at all levels of a project's process, from setting out initial infrastructure requirements, through the design, development and implementation stages, right up to client liasion, training and documentation.
