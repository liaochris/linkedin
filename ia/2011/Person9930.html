Danford Grant's Summary

Dan is an experienced litigation shareholder (partner) and Chair of the Employment Practice Group at Stafford Frey Cooper in Seattle. He represents both plaintiffs and defendants in complex business, employment, and torts litigation, focusing in particular on claims to restore lost revenue. He has successfully tried over a dozen cases and has obtained favorable settlements or pre-trial judgments in countless others.

Dan represents public and private companies, business owners, executives, and prominent individuals in a variety of industries, including healthcare, education, petroleum, and technology. Among other major clients, he has represented the Olympic Pipe Line Company, MEDNAX Services, Pediatrix Medical Group, American Anesthesiology, Columbia Physical Therapy, Green River Community College, the State of Washington, the City of Seattle, Hill Companies, Primus Knowledge Solutions, Air Associates, and numerous university professors, scientists, entertainers, and athletes.

Specialties

Business torts, contract disputes, trade secrets, workplace privacy, disability discrimination, and catastrophic accidents.
