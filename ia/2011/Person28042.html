Marine Chartier-Fruchard's Summary

Born and raised in France, I graduated from a mastercourse in Institutional Communication at Sciences Po Lille in 2009 and I worked for a year as an account planner in Isobar Paris, a full service digital advertising agency.

I decided to go live in Australia and flew in early 2010 to Sydney to undertake a mastercourse in Digital Communication and Culture at the University of Sydney.

I am now working as a Digital Strategist at Clemenger BBDO Sydney.
