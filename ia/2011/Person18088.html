Indranil Chandra’s Summary

I am a global trade management automation expert with more than 16 years’ experience in the IT industry, the last 10 years of which have been in streamlining global trade operations through setting up, implementing, and managing highly effective infrastructure using vendors’ trade compliance platforms at Fortune-500 leaders. My expertise lies in understanding the business requirements and architecting a tailored solution within the framework of the US export and import regulations and then leveraging that expertise in providing highly effective, streamlined solutions to enable the export/import channel for fast growth in global markets.

Highlights:

Leadership – As a creative leader, I’m expert at assembling skilled global development teams and leading them to deliver effective, innovative, value-added quality solutions. I constantly audit Global Trade business performance and ID improvements and then work with business leaders, internal teams, and vendors to mobilize solutions.

Domain Knowledge – I have deep understanding of how global trade compliance drive business processes, revenues, and global business and can translate that insight into company-wide initiatives. I have worked in multiple areas of global trade management such as ERP order licensing, restricted party screening, product classification (including encryption classification), importer security filing and shipper's export declarations filing. As the expert I know how trade compliance affects business and I leverage that to propose management actions. I translate my ERP platform expertise into specific projects to streamline trade practices for rapid global business expansion.

Customer focus – As a customer-centric solutions architect, I focus on delivery commitments. I work closely with the business leaders and stakeholders, understanding the requirements and current pain points, to devise customized system solutions that significantly improve/streamlined many department processes.
