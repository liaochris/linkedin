John Owens's Experience

Proprietor

John Owens Associates

Information Technology and Services industry

March 2011 – Present (1 month)

I work with a range of clients, both public sector and SME, in the areas of web consultancy, online /email marketing and training.

I focus on working with businesses to help them improve the performance of their websites – maybe by getting more visitors to their site, or turning more of those visitors into customers.

This might e.g. include developing SEO and other online marketing activities such as email, structuring the web site process to get the visitor to do the desired action, whether “contact us” or “buy”. I can then help my clients retain the customer by using good customer relationship systems and effective email contact.

After working with literally hundreds of clients over the last 20+ years, I'm expert in the developing of materials, processes and staff training to get website visitors, take them through a journey onsite, leading to the desired action for the website owner - more customers!

I'm very clear about working firmly within the context of “what are your business aims” – what do you want the web and online marketing to achieve for your business? I can help your business use the technology that will enable that - but it's just that, an enabler.

I firmly believe in being honest, clear and open with my clients - I speak English, not jargon, and don't hide behind the technology. Your business aim is foremost!

I don't design websites, (but I know some great people who do) and am very happy to help you develop the site in conjunction with them or your own web designer - so you get it right, right from the start.

Additionally, I also provide both commercial and public-sector training on a range of web related topics. For example, I'm a training deliverer on behalf of the emda-funded East Midlands Chamber of Commerce eBusinessclub programme across the East Midlands.

Full time as Director and Consultant with 360e Ltd based in Lincoln. (The business existed part time from 2004 to 2007).

Training deliverer on behalf of the emda-funded East Midlands Chamber of Commerce Ebusinessclub programme across the East Midlands (www.ebusinessclub.biz)

This role also involved extensive selection and liaison with a broad range of business ICT /web suppliers to address client needs.

During 2009, delivered contract with the East Midlands Chamber of Commerce (funded by emda) as an eBusiness Consultant to provide a specialist diagnostic service for SME’s in the East Midlands area. The aim was to provide eligible businesses with a fully subsidised independent and impartial service, specifically targeted at addressing a technology based issue within the business that prevents their growth.

The service resulted in the development of a project specific Action Plan that will support the interaction between the business and the Business Link Adviser in order to support a possible grant application.
