Third String Productions

Music industry

February 2004 – Present (7 years 2 months)

THIRD STRING PRODUCTIONS happened by accident. When he was only 18 years old, owner Mike Ziemer wanted to help out his friends. There were no all ages clubs in the suburbs, it was near impossible to get on good shows in Deep Ellum as an all-ages band, and they needed a place to play shows. He asked a friend's mom to lend him the $500 it cost back then to rent the small room at The Plano Centre and from there, it all began.

Over the years Third String Productions has established a name for itself by putting on multi-stage festivals with as many as 40 bands providing a mix of genres as well as giving unsigned and smaller signed bands a chance to gain exposure with the crowds of the larger acts. Festivals done by Third String Productions have reached attendance as high as 5,000 people!

In 2010, Third String Productions is "reinventing" itself and expanding all over Texas with shows currently in Austin, Dallas, Houston, and Wichita Falls. The goal of Third String Productions remains: bringing music to kids in places that don't have a big music scene. Although some of our events do take place in Dallas, we stick to the suburbs as much as possible.



Our niche market in the suburbs brings in 3,000 - 6,000 kids for all day festivals feature small to medium touring acts.
