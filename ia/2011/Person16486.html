Host

The Great Unknowns Presents

Broadcast Media industry

March 2009 – Present (2 years 1 month)

The Great Unknowns is a music/talk show dedicated to spotlighting new music artists in the unsigned and independent industry. Each artist is interviewed for the spoken and text material to give you exclusive information about each music artist, because the audience deserves to know who they are listening too.

If you would like to be considered for playlisting, submit your music through Music X-ray http://submit.musicxray.com/profiles/595

If you are a band member or music producer, I definitey want to talk with you about a possible interview spot for the online music magazine, Great Unknowns Music Exposé.
