Stephen Dill's Summary

A social media strategist is someone who understands the many tools of social media (social networks such as LinkedIn and Facebook, content aggregation sites such as Flickr and YouTube, bookmarking sites such as StumbleUpon and Delicious, and blogs such as Wordpress, Tumblr and Posterous, location-based services such as Foursquare, Gowalla and Scvngr, and many, many more) and can integrate them to guide a company in its desire to use this new channel to hear the voice of the customer.

The merger of my personality, skills and experience are represented in a role as Director, Social Media for a large international firm. There I am responsible for:
• Leading the development and implementation of a holistic social media strategy that fully addresses the opportunity and risk presented by social media for the company and its brands integrating corporate and customer communications
• Leading efforts in identifying new on-line methods for communicating with critical stakeholders: customers, shareholders, media, bloggers, employees, etc.
• Attending and speaking at key seminars, conferences and trade shows as the social media face of the company to meet and build relationships with members of the company's communities
• Identifying and maintaining relationships with agencies and online influencers on various digital platforms
• Partnering with business units and functions to develop and implement digital/social media plans that drive greater sales, customer service, product development and brand awareness
• Providing direction to and be responsible for a team of brand community managers
• Leveraging employee communities to generate innovative ideas and promote interactive communication 

I would entertain relocation or long-distance commute for the right company and working with and for the right people.

Specialties

Leveraging Web 2.0 social networking platforms and tools with old-school face-to-face speaking, panels, presentations and interviews to effectively promote a brand image, communicate messages, and support sales, customer support, and product development. Demonstrated skills in collaboration, team development, project management, knowledge management, and creative strategy and planning. See recommendations for testimonies.
