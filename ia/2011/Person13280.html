Elazar Cohen's Summary

I have been in the IT industry since 1999. The majority of my IT knowledge comes from self-learning from books, conferences, trade shows, learning from experienced people etc...

Starting out as basic HTML class in high school, I got interested in building my web designer skills. Couple years later, after dozens of books, trial and errors and many websites in my portfolio, I started to offer web design as a side business. My hunger for knowledge did not stop there. I continued on with learning programming for web (mainly PHP, CSS and MySQL certifications) and added it to my services list.

By that time, I realized that getting traffic to my customer's website will have to come from search engines and so my quest to discovering the world of SEO began. Of course, in the beginning it was far less complex.

I quickly bonded with the SEO industry and the drive to rank customers and bring them traffic was priceless. I have been following all the ins and outs of optimization for the past 5 years. I attended many seminars, webinars and conferences. I also had the pleasure to sit down and talk about strategies with the biggest names in the industry.

Since, I've been a big SEO fan and I continue to follow and experiment new techniques, technologies and strategies.

Come and visit our MetaSEO Blog for great information and insights on the SEO industry. http://www.metaseo.com/blog
