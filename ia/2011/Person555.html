Adam Barry's Summary

Having been brought up with a transport related family and being an HGV driver for some 17 years prior to coming into Recruitment I have the experience, expertise and determination to make things work. Our plan over the next few years is to increase to 4 offices strategically placed to service the 4 corners of the UK.

Specialties

(Polaris Logistics Recruitment) Supply of HGV drivers and operational staff for all areas of the Logistics infrastructure. (Polaris Distribution Services) Sale and distribution of Ad-Blue, Logistical infrastructure consultancy and the allocation of goods movement to third party hauliers.

Adam Barry's Experience

Operations Director

Polaris Logistics Recruitment & Polaris Distribution Services Ltd

Staffing and Recruiting industry

February 2008 – Present (3 years 2 months)

One year after joining Best Connection I posted my CV on a number of networking sites and as a result I gained the opportunity of starting a brand new Logistics Division with Brighter Prospects based at their Head Office in Manchester.

From the inception I quickly built an admiral client base and was invited to take over the existing Logistics Branch staff with total responsibility for training, motivation and growth of all four branches.

Manchester soon outperformed all the other branches and was completely on track to break all previous records as achieved in the North West. Brighter Prospects, like many other companies suffered in the economical downturn, leaving the Shareholders and Board no other choice but to cease trading in April 2009.

I, along with a number of like-minded colleagues, seized the opportunity to carry our good name and relationships forward. This was done under the now trading name of Polaris.

We are now coming up to our second full years trading and I am really pleased to state, we have doubled our output and are actively expanding all our operations as others are downsizing.

I believe this is down to hard work, customer service and tenacity. I will continue with this ethos of effort and hard work which will keep us moving onwards and upwards.
