Michael Auger's Summary

Michael Auger (last name pronounced: O-'zhA / rhymes with okay) is a full-time freelance artist based in the Washington DC area.

Graphic design services include ads, books, catalogs, logos, mailers, postcards, posters, photo editing, and website design. Illustration services include album covers, apparel (t-shirt), books, cartoons, comics, cards, banners, and editorial art. Even custom animation and fine art projects are no problem at all.

If you can dream it, I can draw it.
