Bonnie Kavanagh RN-BC, BSN, CMC’s Summary

The Founder and President of Kavanagh Consultants, Bonnie Kavanagh RN, is a Certified Care Manager and Nurse Consultant. We are a team of health care specialists that serve to assist and meet the challenges families face in caring for their family members. Our team provides assessment, care management, transitioning assistance and oversight to optimize the clients well being.

Our long standing commitment in the community of Grand Junction, Colorado, and Mesa County affords our company with a unique ability to provide top quality interventions for our clients via the private sector. Medical and cost benefit analysis is professionally provided at the review level for: crisis intervention, private matters, and litigation.

Allow us to consult with your family, business, or firm so we may better serve you in meeting community needs as a Care Manager, Mediator, Advocate, and Nurse Consultant.Before needs become a crisis, please allow us to assist and discuss those issues that may concern you. If a crisis is inevitable or in process, know our company specializes in those interventions with a trusted and strong commitment to privacy, while we optimize the client’s health and social status.

Our expertise and extensive background in health care is extended in volunteerism to the public and health care mentoring programs nationwide. Guest speaking at your group or association would be our pleasure. We are the experts in assisting your need to understand our crisis in the challenges the elderly face in today's managed care environment, health care reform, and medical litigation for your business or firm. Our company's combined expertise gives us an edge through Lexus Nexus support, social media communications, and Health IT.
