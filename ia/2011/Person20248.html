Jennifer Perez's Summary

In my current position as Account Supervisor at REVOLUCION, I work on a variety of accounts - from beer to entertainment to digital cameras and more. It is the ability to work and manage above and below the line advertising campaigns for these accounts that continually hones and sharpens my management, strategic and creative concepting skills.

Before becoming part of the REVOLUCION family, I worked my way up to Account Executive at US Concepts, focusing on all aspects of event marketing, from field activation to internal client services. I was named Associate Producer of the Cuervotón tour spearheading various facets of the program during its first two years and built positive partnerships with such entities such as BMI, Def Jam, Gibson and LIFEbeat. Throughout this time I also honed my skills with artists' relations and PR. I transitioned from the Jose Cuervo brand to Tanqueray where I worked on the T&T Style Sessions Tour as well as new programming for Tanqueray Rangpur, the newest addition to the brand’s portfolio. I transitioned to REVOLUCION where my skill set has come in very handy in the advertising world.

During my years as a freelance event coordinator, I worked at the SBS radio stations Amor 93.1 and La Mega 97.9 as well as with Daimler Chrysler's Jeep brand.

I am an Andover alumnus who continued my studies at William Smith College, Geneva, NY and then John Jay College of Criminal Justice, NYC where I went on to study Forensic Psychology before delving into the arena of Television and Radio post-production and freelance marketing projects.
