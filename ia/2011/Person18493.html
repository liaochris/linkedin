Jack Turkel’s Summary

Jack Jay Turkel has been fascinated with photography since he was four years old. A native of Brooklyn, NY. As his high school yearbook photographer, Jack knew where his future career would be going. He continued his interest of photography in college as a telecommunications major. He also studied design and video production. After graduating with honors, Jack found employment in his field as a lab technician and soon advanced to managing a full professional studio in Princeton, NJ.

After more than a decade of management, Jack was hired by a Fortune 500 pharmaceutical company to and went on to create and manage their photographic studio. For the next eleven years Mr. Turkel produced professional media for their internal and external use. From unique animated PowerPoint presentations for the executive staff to product and portrait photography and graphic design for the marketing divisions across the country--every department in the corporation sought Jack’s award winning expertise.

Now with his own company--jackjay Digital--He now offers a wide range of professional services in photography, graphic design and multi-media presentations. As a creative problem solver, Jack is energized by the opportunity to think outside the box.

Jack Turkel’s Specialties:

Studio and commercial photography (Specializing in food). High-end custom PowerPoint. Corporate graphic design, Production Management/Live Events, Retouching, Creative problem solving.
