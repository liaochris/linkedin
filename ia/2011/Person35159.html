Paulo Franca - PLAFstudies.Com's Summary

PLAFstudies.com is a student travel agency. We have signed contracts with several schools worldwide. We act on their behalf and as their representatives to recruit and assist prospective students with enrollment at educational institutions in several English-speaking countries: Australia, Barbados, Canada, Ireland, Malaysia, Malta, New Zealand, Singapore, South Africa, UK, and USA. We are located on Brazilian soil in Bahia State, but we welcome international students from the whole wide world.

Study abroad is an excellent way to gain experience, learn a new culture and practice a foreign language. Large companies are always seeking candidates that have international experience. Students that participate in a study abroad program are usually more communicative, independent and better prepared to face challenges. These are all characteristics that an employer consider when hiring a new employee. Many study abroad programs combine formal instruction in a classroom with traveling and visits to businesses, museums, and other historical places.

Language Travel Agents are important partners for language schools. They play an important role in providing advice and information to help students choose which language training provider and program is best for them. They give advice to students about the different programs and course options and about studying in different areas. They are knowledgeable about program fees, living expenses, visa requirements and are able to make necessary arrangements for program registration, accommodation, travel and often additional services. Although almost all language programs have very detailed websites, well-informed agents are able to help students make the best decision for their specific needs.
