Ritz Steytler's Summary

Founder and Managing Director of Abacus Partners - a privately held investment firm that seeks to acquire and actively manage established private companies in the United Kingdom.

Our typical investment targets are mature, profitable IT-related companies with £5m to £20m in revenue,with predictable cash flows and sustainable competitive positions in growing markets.

We actively consider both standalone companies and divisions being divested by larger corporations. Our preference is to take outright ownership or a controlling stake in our acquisitions. We are unlikely to consider turnaround or startup opportunities.

If you are thinking about selling all or part of your business, we invite you to contact us, in order to learn more about our unique blend of skills and experience, and our strategy for success.
