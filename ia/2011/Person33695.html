Steve Paltridge’s Summary

In my current position as Technology Officer and Encompass Engineer I've been able to accomplish the following:

I added most of the departmental roles to Encompass from Department Management and Secondary Marketing to Personas for Underwriting to Post Closing, and integrated them into our workflow seamlessly.

I designed and created a completely new and substantially more efficient origination and processing workflow by simplifying and reducing or replacing data entry forms.

I created most of the new data entry forms that Catalyst now runs on and helped to troubleshoot the majority of the others.

I’ve integrated a number of third party solutions, including a new imaging software, that are now used daily to shorten workloads and move us closer to a paperless operation.

In my 20+ years in this industry, I’ve had the incredible fortune to have seen and worked on Loan Origination programs since their inception. I’ve been the customer, the sales person, the implementer, and a company, as well as customer advocate. I know what’s been done right and what’s still missing.

If you are interested in really making a difference, and want to do it right, give me a call. I have some great ideas and I’m ready to help.
