Peter T. Combs, Ph.D.'s Summary

Peter is a technical consultant in energy efficiency in metropolitan Phoenix, AZ. His short & mid-term goals are Certified Energy Manager (CEM) and a Certificate in Construction Management. He is a member of Association of Energy Engineers, American Meteorological Society and Phi Kappa Phi.

Combs' technical expertise is underpinned by several decades experience in home remodeling, climate control, climate dynamics and statistical analyses for application in energy efficiency management. He received his degree in Climatology in 1995 from Arizona State University in Tempe, Arizona.

He has conducted in-depth, statistical-thermodynamic research of cloud-radiative properties of the southwest North American monsoon, changes in thunderstorm activity as a result of climactic warming, and the climate dynamics of the MTA New York City subway. Field experience includes cold weather home design and construction in southwest Montana; modular home remodeling in central Texas, and supermarket & commercial refrigeration installation, service & repair in central Arizona.

Specialties

Solutions for energy; Systems analysis; Enviro-climate control; Statistical atmospheric thermodynamics;
