François Barbut's Summary

CHAPATIZ.COM is a funny online virtual world that has been endorsed
by hundreds of thousands of pre-teens.
Chapatiz has found a sweet spot between online games and communities: it combines the best of these two worlds, to provide a rich multimedia experience to preteenagers.

The site is easily accessible, provides numerous forms of interaction and a real
multimedia experience, delivers user generated content and serves as a hub for social networking.
The access to this virtual world is free and immediate - since it does not require any
download.
Chapatiz is not a game with a fixed goal. It is instead a dynamic and entertaining world in which pre-teens can play and socialize with other people of their age.
