Christian Svane's Summary

Management consulting has given me international work experience and improved my ability to handle complex business situations. I have learned to work with all levels of an organization and to create impact through focus on real issues combined with clear communication

My work as entrepreneur gave me unparalleled experience fresh out of university to deal with an organization in growth while keeping business focus. I later deployed my business start-up insights at other similar companies in need of business development

Specialties

Growth strategy, operations strategy/ turnarounds with extensive cost reduction experience. Experience in healtcare (pharma and medtech) but also experience from other industrial goods and consumer goods. Upstart experience within business development and high-tech
