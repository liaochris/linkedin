Darla Campbell's Summary

Trainer and Master Practitioner of Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP) focused on providing individual coaching for high performance leaders. Specializing in work related stress, resolving issues that hold leaders back from achieving their results and creating confidence in leadership style. Interested in: 1) coaching leaders who need better or different results; 2) advising corporations on communication strategies to achieve their vision; and 3) aligning corporate values to achieve peak performance for teams. Vision is to make the world a better place through advanced leadership and communication. Get on the fast track for results.
