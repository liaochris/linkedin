James Coy Driscoll's Summary

I am an attorney who has specialized in representing tenants since 1992. Many tenants are not aware of their rights when their landlords attempt to raise their rent, evict them, or violate their rights. My law firm helps tenants learn about and make use of their legal rights.

Specialties

Tenants rights eviction defense, fighting eviciton, lease disputes, rent control and landlord-tenant litigation.
