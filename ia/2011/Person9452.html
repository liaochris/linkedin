Valeri A. James's Summary

I love my job! Hardly anyone gets to say that these days, but I really do. I think that I am very fortunate and lucky to love what I do. I have 3 jobs: Certified Credit Consultant/CEO, Master Trainer and Public Speaker for Simple Solutions Credit Consulting and Training, Inc. I have been helping people improve their lives by helping/educating them about credit, debt and money management. With 24 years of experience, I am extremely good at what I do. Yes, a lot of confidence! With using State and Federal laws to protect and help consumers, we can actually help them delete: collections, charge-offs, repos, bankruptcies, foreclosure, tax liens, child support, and even late payments. Most consumers don't know how to do this or where to begin, so we get to educate them and get their lives back on track. This helps the average consumer save money on: insurance, lower payments and interest rates, and even helps to obtain some jobs! Call us if you need help or know someone who does! We will take care of them like their our own family!
