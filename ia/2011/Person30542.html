Miguel Barbosa's Summary

Casual Bio:

I am best described as an investigative journalist or detective. I was born in Spain and raised in Florida. I recently moved to Chicago. I am a renaissance man passionate about finance, psychology, decision making, forensic analysis, accounting, economics, and ethics. I am also a dj and a financial analyst.

I have a passion for connecting with exceptional individuals and interviewing them on my blog SimoleonSense.com (which is dedicated to sophisticated financial decision makers and responsible citizens). I cover many topics such as behavioral finance, psychology and complexity theory.

Formal Bio:

Miguel Barbosa received his Bachelors of Liberal Arts and Sciences from the University of Florida. He also holds a Masters of Science in Management and a Masters of Arts in International Business. Currently, he is working on obtaining his CFA designation.

He has worked with angel investors & venture capital. Currently he is founder of SimoleonSense.com and an analyst at an international value fund in Chicago
