Nilesh Bhandari’s Summary

I am a Product Manager for Ironport Systems, the leading email and web security appliance company. I am responsible for both inbound and outbound product management for our email security product lines.

Prior, I helped execute a turnaround of the Image Sensor Division at Avago Technologies, the former semiconductor division of Agilent. Previously, I had invested in and worked closely with technology services and services firms while at Commonwealth Capital Ventures from 2000 to 2003. Prior to that experience, I had cofounded a boutique consultancy called Investor Group Services, which conducted business diligence for private equity firms, as well as worked with a management consultancy called the Parthenon Group. I have a passion for investing in and helping to build world-class companies, as that is how I have spent the majority of my professional career.

My education consists of an MBA, MSE and 2 BSE's from University of Pennsylvania. As my friends say, I bleed Red and Blue.
