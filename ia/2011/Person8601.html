Chuck Fishman’s Summary

Manager and Analyst, Media and Entertainment Partnerships
Cisco Media Solutions Group
Cisco Systems, Inc

Chuck manages Cisco relationships with media and entertainment partners, reporting to the Senior Director of Media and Entertainment Partnerships at the Cisco Media Solutions Group.

His work at Cisco includes keeping tabs on the digital entertainment landscape to inform strategic decisions in the Cisco Media Solutions Group (CMSG). CMSG manages a SaaS platform for media and entertainment companies, Cisco Eos(tm), to launch unique branded and social entertainment experiences. Chuck project manages both Cisco Eos and other Cisco digital media initiatives, media and entertainment analysis delivered in internal communications, and some external communications (via Cisco Media blog - http://blogs.cisco.com/media).

As part of his work at Cisco, Chuck participates in several tech media oriented consortiums and committees including DECE, the IAB Digital Video Committee, the IAB UGC and Social Networking Committee, and the Entertainment and Technology Center at USC. Chuck also manages the CMSG Graduate Intern program.

Chuck has broad media and entertainment experience - from 1997 to 2007, he created and produced new radio ventures for Clear Channel, Bloomberg, CNET Networks and the Wall Street Journal. Chuck was the first ever producer of both The Wall Street Journal This Morning radio show, and the Morning Show with Brian Cooley on CNET Radio, joining them at their creation point.

Chuck holds a B.A. in Geography / Cartography from the University of Colorado and practiced in the GIS / digital mapping space before he started his passionate media and technology career.

Chuck is also an independent musician who toured with the funk band George Clinton & The P-Funk All Stars in the mid nineties. In his spare time, he continues to collaborate with that group and he utilizes digital media platforms promote his own music made with a band known as fONKSQUISh.

Chuck Fishman’s Specialties:

Media and Entertainment Industry Research and Analysis, Extensive Knowledge of Major Media co's (CBS, Comcast / NBC U, Disney, Sony, Time Warner, Viacom), Digital Distribution Business Models, Emerging Content Formats and Digital Media Production Techniques, Social Media, University Relations / Recruiting, Communications (Internal and External), Blogging and Content Production, Project Management, Partner Relations, Digital Music, Music Production, and Media and Brand Marketing
