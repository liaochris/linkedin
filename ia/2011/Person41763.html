Steve Wilhite's Summary

15+ years in the learning field have fueled my passion for the design of high-quality, effective performance interventions that make organizational and individual goals attainable. I view my profession as both a science and a craft, informing my practice with thorough, accurate assessment and maintaining flexibility and a spirit of innovation in my design approach. Instructional technology lies at the core of my design process, always as a means to an end, and never as a bolt-on component. Quality and attention to the learner's needs are the foundations upon which my work has its footing.

Specialties

ISD, Flash/Web design, e-learning development, technical training, documentation, task analysis, online collaboration, systems thinking/modeling, simulation design, software visualization, SharePoint site design and utilization, multimedia development, sales training, management development, communication skills, fitness education, writing/editing
