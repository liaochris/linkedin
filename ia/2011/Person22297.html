Jorge Canal’s Summary

Jorge Canal is a Senior IT Executive since 1993, when he founded Techno Global Inc. in Florida to partner with large U.S. companies. With a team of savvy professionals, Techno Global Inc. builds and run global IT factories, builds its own IT products and provides consultancy in Process Management, (CMMI/PSP/TSP), Development, and QA for multiple industries worldwide.

Techno Global Inc.´s latest product is Wortix, a global healthcare solution, which features thousand of Patients and Health Issues for the last 3 years since its launch. Techno Global Inc. has serviced multiple large Healthcare Organizations.

Jorge Canal's vast experience on being a Senior Consultant with a 20 year background where he's acted as a VP of Technology, Senior Consultant, IT Director, Director of CRM, IT Manager, CRM-SQL-Pivotal Customization Master, Lead Developer, Lead Analyst and Project Manager. Jorge is a Pivotal Customization Master and has implemented 20+ CRM/Web systems nationwide, with each project lasting from 6 to 18 months. Largest sites included companies like Universal Orlando, Symantec, Epiphany, Chordiant, Blue Pumpkin, Remedy Inc, I-Pro, Manage.com, Immuno Laboratories, Data Warehouse Mortgage, Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport, and Amira Medical.

Techno Global Inc. has extensive expertise in interpreting user requests, re-engineering enterprise workflows, designing integration layers, building new schemas, performing large migrations and configuring and optimizing third-party applications.

As a VP of Technology, Jorge Canal also leads Moving Interactive's technology division and he is also the Managing Director of the Peru IT Factory (60 developers/CMMI/PSP/TSP/Scrum).
