Bob Chambless's Summary

Over 12 years experience in Full-Time Worship Ministry leadership. Proven ability to help ministry teams improve overall worship excellence in areas of vocal, choral, instrumental, technical, promotional and relational arts for intimate worship settings to mega-church gatherings.

Have led and produced worship events using most every style of music including traditional/blend, contemporary blend, Modern Contemporary and Hillsong Contemporary.

Highly skilled at developing message series packaging for weekend services which includes theme, graphics, video, props, drama, all promotions, print, web, and music. For a video documentary example, visit www.bobchambless.com and view "Creative Worship."

Conceived, produced, promoted and directed many bridge events involving choir, drama, orchestra and technical arts. "God and Country-- A Patriotic Celebration” evolved to be an annual event drawing thousands of attenders to our church.

Produced multiple city-wide Christian concert venues including Chris Tomlin, Matt Redman, Jeremy Camp, Louie Giglio, Dennis Jernigan and Mark Lowery.

Co-wrote and produced 6 original Christmas dramatic musicals for the local church.

Produced and conducted 3 choral CD recordings.

Co-presented a break-out session at the 2008 Echo Conference, a media and technology leaders conference in Dallas, Texas.
