Joe Denner's Summary

Leaders face significant challenges to their ability to gain clarity about and stay focused on what is most important to them. Rarely do they get an opportunity to carve out solid blocks of time to think and talk about what they really want because of the constant interruptions and endless to-do lists. When they do schedule those self-appointments they are often challenged to follow through because “real” appointments with real people come along and vie for that time. As an executive coach I partner with leaders to help them get more of what they want and less of what they don’t by providing those critical spaces of time to think about and process through their true priorities.

Before becoming a professional coach I spent over 15 years working in leadership positions within companies as well as in the church. My extensive experience in finance, accounting, operations & human resources brings a wealth of knowledge relating to the development of human capital for positive, profitable and sustainable growth.

In my most recent position with Microdynamics I was the VP of Operations. I was responsible for overseeing all of the operational functions, including Production, Quality, Fulfillment, Materials Management, Mailing, Project Management, Maintenance and Safety. Our plant produced over 100 million first class mail pieces each year primarily for financial service organizations such as banks, credit unions, brokerage firms, utilities and collection agencies. We were proud of our 99% quality and 99% on-time delivery record.

As a professional coach I provide executive coaching, training, assessment and organizational development services to leaders who are looking to produce sustainable, profitable growth. I am a certified Myers-Briggs administrator and have extensive experience with the Gallup StrengthsFinder 2.0 instrument.

Specialties

Executive Coaching, Leadership Training, Team Building, Strategic Planning
