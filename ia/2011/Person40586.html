Renee Skiba's Summary

Free lance photographer and painter.
Co-Owner of Skiba Photography LLC & Skiba Fine Art.
Along with photography, I specialize in creating digital paintings & portraiture. My subjects include landscapes, architecture and portraits.

We are now accepting bookings for new painted portraits. Please contact us at 414-465-9448 or 970-631-3931.

Our list of art shows and daily activities can be followed on our blog at http://skibafineartandphotography.blogspot.com/.

Look for us at several of the art shows in the Milwaukee area. We will soon be extending our art shows to Colorado and Wyoming.

We are also pleased to announce the publication of our photo book: Milwaukee, WI, a Photographic Portrait. Locally it can be purchased through us or at Barnes and Noble bookstores.

Specialties

Specializing in creating fine works of art from photos.
Use of digital technology to create painted images.
