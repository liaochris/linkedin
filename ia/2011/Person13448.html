Elizabeth Vida's Summary

As a professional geoscientist (Project Manager/Sr Exploration Geologist/Consulting Geologist), I have 25 years experience in mineral exploration.

My field of expertise is exploration geology (Au, Cu, Pb, Zn, Ni, Co, U) and project management.

Phases of project involvement include:
• managing projects in mineral exploration,
• project/ property assessment and evaluation,
• planning and researching projects,
• Diamond Drill Management & interpretations (exploration – Diamond, RC, Auger & Roto-sonic; open pit mining – exploration using Diamond, Auger and Roto-sonic; U/G – data compilations),
• QA/QC,
• safety preparedness,
• data base management,
• project quotes, tenders and budgets.
• land acquisition,
• interpretation of data,
• liaison with upper company officials and report writing.
• fieldwork, mapping, soil/ humus/water/ lake sediment/ stream sediment surveys, geochemical surveys.
• report writing.

I am fairly fluent in Hungarian, low-moderately fluent in French and I am currently learning Spanish.
