Evelyn Lee, AIA, LEED AP [BD+C]’s Summary

A licensed architect, Evelyn received her BArch w/honors from Drury University and her MArch from SCI-Arc.

Active in leadership at all levels of the AIA, Evelyn is a recipient of the Nat'l Institute’s Assoc of the Year award. She has the distinct honor of being the youngest member to serve as the Assoc Rep to the AIA Nat'l ExCom, the first Assoc to serve on the AIA National’s Secretary Advisory Committee, & the first Assoc to serve on AIACC’s ExCom.

Evelyn is currently working to build her sustainable consulting practice, focusing on clients in the hospitality and design industries, & is constantly on the hunt for the latest in sustainable luxury for her new venture, EcoLuxLife. Evelyn is also a senior editor for Inhabitat, a contributing editor for Design Future Council’s, Design Intelligence, & is a frequent contributor to a number of online & print design publications.
