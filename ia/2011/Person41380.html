Stephen B. Starr’s Summary

I design and produce a variety of printed materials and digital assets as an independent business consultant. Specialties include design services for branding (logo creation and implementation), websites, annual reports, e-newsletters, custom email solutions, marketing materials and advertising. Responsible for managing strategic relationships with subcontractors such as web content developers, photographers, website programmers, illustrators, writers and printers. Design is treated as the overarching strategy that creates cohesive organizational communication.

Stephen B. Starr’s Specialties:
