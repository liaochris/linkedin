Jeffery Palumbo’s Summary

Entrepreneur-
Mr. Palumbo is an entrepreneur with 13 years of experience in charity management, Executive leadership, consumer internet, (internet) marketing and business development. Jeff helped to start and build Wantsa Inc where he was instrumental in the growth of the company as an executive. In his role he was responsible for major partnerships and business development.

Currently Mr. Palumbo is the founder and CEO of FreePickles.com a professional sales organization and “daily deal” company. FreePickles focuses on crafting the highest quality deals in the industry by addressing the 4 points. The four points represent the key areas of a quality deal; commissions, “The business”, consumer discount and Terms & conditions.

Additionally Mr. Palumbo is the CEO of GivingPal (.com) Inc. where they are in the early stages of developing technology innovations to help nonprofits.

Mr. Palumbo was a successful leader with Primedia, MCI Worldcom and @Link Networks. As well as the founder of Printorweb Inc.

Non-Profit-
Mr. Palumbo confounded the Believe for Boey Foundation in 2007 a nonprofit to aid families suffering from pediatric cancer. In 2009 he founded the nonprofit KIDlanthropy, created to assist teachers, leaders and parents in raising philanthropic children.

Family-
Jeff is a proud family man with a highly creative and assertive lead by example management style, combined with a passion for helping people through business and charitable initiatives. He is a family man who understands the need for love, respect and commitment.
