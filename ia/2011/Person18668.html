Jake Aull’s Summary

Award Winning Marketing Creative Director, with 16 years’ experience collaborating with and galvanizing teams and individuals to produce fresh, successful brands. Enjoys interacting in a creative team environment and advancing knowledge. Inspired to use creative experience and strategic marketing knowledge to grow others (my personal mission statement). Masters of Science in Marketing, Promotions and Communications focus, GSU, April 2009, BFA in Graphic Design, SCAD, 1994. Co-owned a successful design firm.

Jake Aull’s Specialties:

Years of demonstrated leadership and great ideas from conept to execution. Award-winning art direction, as well as UI design, copywriting, strategic marketing, brand development, theme/tagline creation, CRM/ROI measurment, technology marketing, information graphics and presentations. I love collaborating with, developing and learning from others. My thesis final project (completed April 2009) was on "Social Media and Green Promotions for the IT Industry."
