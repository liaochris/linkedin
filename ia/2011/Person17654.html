Thomas Howe's Summary

Independent technology developer, analyst and consultant to service providers, enterprises and financial institutions with over twenty years of experience in the design and development of next generation communications equipment, services and applications. Recognized expert in Voice Over IP, member of several technical advisory boards and frequent speaker at industry events. Winner of multiple industry awards, honors and development contests. Seasoned manager with experience in leading executive, development and cross functional teams. Currently doing basic research in integration of real time communications technologies into web services architectures.

Specialties

Cloud Communications, Voice Application Development, technology and marketing strategy, large scale software development, VoIP, Web 2.0, cross functional management, international business
