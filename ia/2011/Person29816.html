Résumé de Matt Farineau

Although my professional experience stretches across several industries, I feel that I’ve always been anchored firmly in graphic design. I hold a Bachelor of Science degree in computer graphics and have always had a passion for art. Throughout my life I have learned and maintained a working knowledge of industry standard programs in graphic design as well as been active in online design communities such as CGSociety.org and similar groups even when my full time job did not involve graphics work. In addition to my full time job, I am currently taking classes for a Master's degree from the Harvard Extension School in Digital Media and Instructional Design.
