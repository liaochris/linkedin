Carrie Edgeworth's Summary

My job requires me to work with various stakeholders including injured workers, employers, union representatives, employer and worker representatives (lawyers), case managers, return to work specialists, etc. during my on-site assessments of the work.

Specialties

My specialty is application of ergonomics to workplaces (large range of industrial and office environments) with the goal of getting injured workers back to work.
