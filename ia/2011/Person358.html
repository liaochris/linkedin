Personal

Art -- My passion is art. I've painted with my grandmother since I was little and can lose myself in my drawings for hours. I minored in Fine Arts while getting my BA and fell absolutely in love with pastels and vellum. I believe that art can educate and help people and that art should be for a purpose, not just to hang over a couch or in a stuffy gallery. That is why I adore street art and graphic novels so much.

Running -- A couple of years ago I ruptured my achilles tendon while playing basketball. I'm all healed up now and it's time to get back to being the athlete that I've always been.

The Outdoors -- I grew up in the country with animals and land to roam around on. I live in a rural area outside Boston and love walking my dog by the stables and farms nearby. I hike and camp every chance I get and really crave a campfire by a stream when I'm under stress.
