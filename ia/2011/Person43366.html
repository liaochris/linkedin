Tim Hackbardt's Summary

A senior executive, and entrepreneur, with an impressive track record for consistently leading organizations to exceed their own vision and definition of success. A broad category scope of experience includes restaurant, theatrical, music, banking, fashion, action sports, personal care, home improvement, sporting goods, non-profit, online entertainment, satellite communications and medical devices.

As a brand leader, and evangelist, results feature positive same store sales every year over a 14 year period. This includes a string of 96 consecutive months of increased same store sales.
