Eder Holguin’s Summary

Founded by Eder Holguin in March 2009, OnDemand Research is an industry leader in market intelligence. We help our clients find, develop, and manage meaningful consumer relationships by providing data solutions and processing services. Leveraging proprietary online tools, we assist businesses in any vertical market to seek, target, and convert users for their products and services. OnDemand Research provides the tools and support needed to drive success to your business.

Prior to OnDemand Research, Eder co-founded Iron Traffic in March 2003. Iron Traffic started with three employees and quickly grew into one of the fastest-growing privately-held companies in the United States. With annual revenues surpassing $8M, the company was featured in INC500.

Previously, Eder served as a Director of Sales for Media Whiz, an online marketing company specializing in lead generation and affiliate marketing services. He spent five years as Executive Manager for Equifax Marketing Services where he specialized in working with market research companies. Eder also served as a Director of Sales for Sweepsclub.com, Naviant.com, Bay9.com and R.H. Donnelley.

Eder has extensive experience in new product development and marketing, particularly in the online industry. He has built and executed numerous online panel development platforms, including projects for internetsurveypanel.com, thenetpanel.com and ECNResearch.com.

A significant player in the online industry for the past eight years, Eder has a combined twelve years of experience in sales and marketing. He is currently active in the online industry speaking circuit and serves as an executive advisor to several privately-held companies. He is also an active investor in small new technology and real estate ventures.
