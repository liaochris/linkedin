Dragos Dehelean's Experience

CEO

ResponsabilitateSociala.ro

Privately Held; Public Relations and Communications industry

April 2006 – Present (5 years)

ResponsabilitateSociala.ro, founded in April 2006, is the first Romanian CSR portal. Its goal is to promote corporate social responsibility principles and best practices, in order to encourage companies to contribute to a Romanian economy capable of creating a more sustainable future for Romanian society and local communities.

At this time, ResponsabilitateSociala.ro is the leading Romanian source of news, case studies and know-how for all professionals involved in the CSR field. Its audience reaches over 3.500 corporate professionals, communication consultants, NGO representatives, journalists or other key opinion leaders.

CEO

Selenis - Online Public Relations

Privately Held; Public Relations and Communications industry

December 2005 – Present (5 years 4 months)

Selenis manages ResponsabilitateSociala.ro (“Social Responsibility”), the first Romanian internet portal on CSR.

Selenis has ongoing consultancy programs for big companies such as: BRD Groupe Societe Generale, Carpatcement Holding, Tuborg Romania.

Romanian Public Relations Association (ARRP)

Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; Public Relations and Communications industry

March 2004 – Present (7 years 1 month)

Executive President

Ethos Communication Association

Non-Profit; 1-10 employees; Public Relations and Communications industry

September 2000 – December 2005 (5 years 4 months)

Ethos has the ownership of the multi-awarded, annual program – Olimpiadele Comunicarii (“Communication Olympics”). Olimpiadele Comunicarii is the most important series of events that aim to help the development of young Romanian communication specialists.

In the past, Ethos has helped, as coordinating partener, the organisation of another two big annual programs: Internetics Festival (2004-2005) and Gala Societatii Civile (2004-2005). It has also helped, as external communication partener, the organization of the 2004 edition of Adprint Festival.
