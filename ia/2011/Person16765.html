The CEMIP is the French Committee of Ethics and Mediation for the Pharmaceutical Industry. It was set up in 2000 by the Leem (the Professional Association of the Pharmaceutical Industry in France) who, as major players in Public Health, see it as their duty to promote professional ethics not only among their members but also with their partners.
To regulate the professional practices of its members,
To mediate between its members,
To constitute an advisory body for professional practices,
To provide recommendations in promotional or professional matters.
