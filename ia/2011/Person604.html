AdaPia d'Errico's Summary

I work with artists and creators to successfully brand themselves, their art, and their characters and properties.

I help artists and creators develop a proactive, confident attitude - the 'brand mindset' - necessary for successfully taking their art to market, developing a loyal following, and achieving their creative and career goals.

I have built on the experience of working with my sister, and fantastic artist, Camilla d'Errico. I manage her career, while at the same time building her artist brand - Camilla d'Errico - as well as her art and character brands, Helmetgirls & Tanpopo.

Specialties

artist management, promotion, licensing, merchandising, social media strategies, branding, consulting,

AdaPia d'Errico's Experience

Managing Director

d'Errico Studios Ltd

Fine Art industry

January 2008 – Present (3 years 3 months)

Working with my talented sister, artist Camilla d'Errico, I manage her career that spans from fine art to comic books, and disseminate her artwork through promotion, merchandising, licensing, cobranding and brand extension opportunities.

Together with Camilla, we have established d'Errico Studios Limited, where we create and manage projects for clients in publishing (comics/graphic novels), character creation, product development (toys, merchandise), design and illustration, packaging and promotion, and fashion. We work with other talented creative professionals in the fulfillment of these projects.

We are developing our own brands, Tanpopo and Helmetgirls for the publishing & entertainment industries with a focus on expanding the brands through licensing.
