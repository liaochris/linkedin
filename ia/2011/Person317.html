Abhilash Nair’s Summary

Technical convergence within media was inevitable since the advent of the Internet. Since the Internet came into existence, the world has seen several drastic changes that challenged the traditional way of working within media.

With a vast technical, management and business background, a strong passion for media and having worked closely within the UK ethnic media for various productions and understanding the complex fragmentation of the ethnic audience in the UK, Abhilash co-founded Talnet Productions in 2004 with Rajesh to address the need for companies to reach out to the ethnic audience.

Our Goal is to be the leading full service creative advertising, media and marketing services agency that targets the Ethnic audience through strategic PR and media campaigns (TV / Print / Radio / Online / Mobile) for customers seeking to market their brands, products, companies and events to these communities. In pursuing this mission, we aim deliver value by executing campaigns in a professional manner using a structured and a result-oriented approach yielding optimum return on investment.
