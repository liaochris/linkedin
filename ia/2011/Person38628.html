Samta Chowdhary's Summary

- Senior instructional designer with over 16 years of experience in curriculum design, corporate learning, strategic planning, and project management.
- Versatile in meeting diverse client requirements and developing appropriate instructional solutions for multicultural audience. Have worked with clients from varied industry sectors in the USA, U.K., Ireland, Australia, and India.
- Experience in developing training solutions for technical and end users, certifications, and soft skill courses for diverse industries including IT, avionics, banking and finance, manufacturing, retail, telecom, healthcare, and education.
- Experience in editing storyboards as per style guidelines (Chicago and Microsoft Manual), standards, and 508 guidelines and review of Web-based trainings.
- Software proficiency includes MS Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint); MS Project, Visio; authoring tools like Articulate, Adobe Presenter, Lectora, Robohelp, Multimedia Toolbook, and Icon Author; screen and audio/video capturing tools like Captivate, Camtasia, Audacity, Snagit, and SoundForge, LCMSs like Saba and Evolution, and online presentation tools like Brainshark.
- Additional experience includes creation of Web content, training manuals, user manuals, job aids, press releases, proposals, and marketing collaterals.
- Recognized as the ‘Best Instructional Designer’ by Gartner Group.
