Résumé de Thomas McKay, MSM

I work with individuals and businesses to design a personal and/or business financial plan based on life goals, needs and long-term objectives.

I am an independent advisor, which means I have access to a broad array of financial tools and products. This means I’m not limited to or required to promote a particular line of products. Being independent enables me to focus on solutions to a client’s needs without bias.

I make a point to be accessible and client-centered in a way that brokerage houses and their representatives cannot. I strive to develop genuine relationships with my clients so that when a unique need arises, I can help them adjust to the life changes occurring. To me, helping my clients with their personal or business goals doesn’t stop at insurance or investments.

To that end, I am community-centered and a great networking resource. My involvement with our area chambers, professional financial organizations, and other networking groups help me connect my clients with other individuals or businesses that can help them with supporting solutions.
