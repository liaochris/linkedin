Bonnie Ruffin, NCCP’s Summary

Senior Litigation Paralegal/Entrepreneur providing professional services to attorneys, insurance companies, colleges, business owners, and individuals. We provide transcription services, deposition summaries and medical summaries, interpreting, and translation.

Our state-of-the-art website portals allow clients to upload digital audio directly to us. Analog tapes are mailed to us for transcription including micro cassettes, VHS tapes, and regular cassettes. Turn around time is excellent.
We also have online scheduling for interpreting and online uploading of documents that need to be translated directly to us through our website. Final documents are password protected and submitted to clients. See our website at
www.RuffinConsultinginc.com or www.PhoneASecretary.com.

Other services include deposition and medical summaries, coding and indexing. If you need a paralegal task completed call us!
