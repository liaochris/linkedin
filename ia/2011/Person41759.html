Steve Wehner’s Summary

I am an accomplished professional with over fifteen years of experience in creative management, advertising design, interactive media, and marketing communications.

I excel at creating design driven communications that help companies present their brand, products, and services with a unified and elegant voice. I add value beyond design basics by generating ideas that take a concept from good to great.

I’m the Creative Director at Butler/Till Media, and serve as an Adjunct Faculty Member at Nazareth College of Rochester, where I teach Advanced Graphic Design and Advertising Design courses.

I’m also an elected member of the International Academy of Visual Arts, where I judge the W3, Davy, and Communicator online marketing and design awards.
