Kevin Dowling’s Summary

Technology executive with broad and deep experience in technology implementation and the management of fast-moving teams in both entreprenuerial and established companies. Florish in fast-moving environments and in fast changing situations.

Strong track record of building teams and delivering products and systems.

Experienced in guiding and leading technology including government programs, research in lighting and robot systems, semiconductor-based lighting and more.

Sought after communicator. Experienced as public speaker and writer with many hundreds of presentations, papers and articles. Established leader in establishing industry forums, committees, goverment relations and bringing diverse interests together to create legislation and standards.

Strong experience in technology integration, intellectual property issues, competitive benchmarking and product innovation.
