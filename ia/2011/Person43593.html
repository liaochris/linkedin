Tina Quach’s Summary

San Franciscan native T.L. Quach moved to New York City for a Cinematography workshop and to pursue a film production career. She has already made about a dozen short films ranging from documentary to narrative with genres including drama, action, comedy, and horror.

She currently specializes in being an Assistant Director, Camera Assistant, Camera Operator, and a Screenwriter
