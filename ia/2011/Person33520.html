Osman Perksoy’s Summary

Entrepreneurial spirited Product Manager with experience in Electronic Payment Processing and Mobile/Wireline Telecommunication Technologies.

Develops requirement specs, project plans, business case analysis, relese content documents, white papers, etc. Leads cross functional teams (CFT) and champions new/existing product development activities through the product life cycle (PLC) process. Experienced in working with diverse team of individuals spread globally. Interfaces with Engineering, Sales, Marketing and customers. Provides responses to RFI, RFP and other customer queries.

Areas of technical expertise include Electronic Payment Systems for Card-Not-Present and e-Commerce applications, Payment Industry Standards, Voice over IP (VoIP), Next Gen Networks (NGN), 2G/3G GSM/UMTS technologies, 3GPP network evolution, LTE/SAE, Mobile Broadband Convergence with Femto/Picocells, Fixed Mobile Convergence (FMC) with VCC, IMS, SIP, H.248/MEGACO, ISUP and ISDN.
