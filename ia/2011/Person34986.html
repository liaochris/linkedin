Adam Scull’s Summary

Moving from the still camera to microphone to video, I am returning to my roots of video broadcasting.   I will be reporting for PHOTOlink.TV, (a wholly owned subsidiary of PHOTOlink) specializing in red carpet celebrity event audio coverage.  PHOTOlink.TV was started in September of 2010 as an outgrowth of my radio interviews, as many of my subscribers asked for video coverage. An example is Broadwayworld.com, which has used quite a bit of my coverage, and now gets constant feeds of PHOTOlink.TV


My product is distinctive. There are dozens of entertainment writers, bloggers and still photographers, but there are few radio or video reporters that specialize in celebrity red carpet coverage with over thirty years of experience on the red carpet.   PHOTOlink.TV can be used as bonus content on websites, mobile apps and broadcast to enhance your current entertainment coverage.   

PHOTOlink.TV's red carpet coverage is real; it can be fun, noisy, chaotic, flash-popping bedlam with dozens of photographers calling out to the celebrities. PHOTOlink.TV captures the red carpet experience in all it's chaotic splendor, bringing the viewer right onto the red carpet, up close and personal.

Adam Scull’s Specialties:

One of my strongest skills is the ability to talk to anyone, anytime and anywhere, making them feel like they’ve know me all along, and feel at ease and comfortable on "mic". I quickly establish a rapport and develop a more fulfilling video interview soundbite.   With these skills I can make a website or mobile app stand out from the crowd in the highly competitive entertainment field.
