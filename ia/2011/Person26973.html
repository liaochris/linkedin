Lu-Ann Fuentes's Summary

As Editorial and Communication Services Director at Ogilvy Public Relations Worldwide Manila, Lu-Ann develops communication strategies that drive digital and traditional media campaigns to achieve clients' objectives.

She doubles as PR Account-Services lead for client Thomson Reuters Manila, as well as directs a pool of writers and media relations team who service the editorial needs of accounts managers and their clients—which include Yahoo, LG Electronics, Ford, Nestle, American Express, Nike, Fitness First, Quatar Airways, Avon, Jollibee, WWF, Special Olympics and more. She has been providing editorial services (involving marketing support, account management, internal and external communication) for two decades—spanning different environments from corporate communication to advocacy/non-profit, from journalism to PR.

Before joining Ogilvy, her last two professional engagements were: as Editorial Director generating print and online content for a U.S.-based publishing company, and as Corporate Communication Officer at San Miguel Corporation (SMC), the largest publicly listed food, beverage and packaging company in the Philippines.

For SMC, she logged in seven years of providing publicity support and account servicing for its various business units—San Miguel Beer, San Miguel PurefoodsCorporation, San Miguel Packaging Products, and Ginebra San Miguel, Inc.—and communication support for the San Miguel Customer Care Center, SMITS, Inc. (info tech arm), San Miguel Properties, and San Miguel Logistics Asia Corporation. This included producing SMC’s monthly corporate publication (which won three Anvil Awards of Excellence during her term as editor-in-chief) as a group-wide communication tool, extending the reach of corporate and brand image-building.

She holds a Bachelor of Arts in Communication, major in Journalism, from the University of the Philippines, Diliman.

Specialties

Editorial direction/services: print and online content management, public relations, corporate/employee communication, account/project management
