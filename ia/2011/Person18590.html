TIVA-DC, the Television, Internet & Video Assn. of DC, is dedicated to promoting education and excellence in media production for professionals, pre-professionals and students in the DC metro area. I was President during the transition from ITVA-DC, a chapter of MCA-I (Media Communications Assn.-International), to its current status as an independent nonprofit. President/Producer

Tonal Vision LLC

Media Production industry

1990 – Present (21 years)

From its inception in 1990, I have managed Tonal Vision's business affairs, designed and maintained its web site and helped promote Kraig's music. In 2002, Procter & Gamble offered me an early retirement package, and I join my husband of 25 years in the family business. I helped expand the business from music and audio into video and multimedia, in the process earning a second Masters in Producing Film & Video from American University in Washington, DC. I continue to manage the usiness, accounting & financial affairs; as well as serving as producer for the company's video projects.

Section Head, Regulatory Services

Procter & Gamble

Public Company; PG; Consumer Goods industry

December 1980 – January 2002 (21 years 2 months)

During my 21 years in R&D management with Procter & Gamble. I helped develop Liquid Tide, provided technical support and issues analysis of the phosphate detergent issue, helped obtain FDA approval of the fat substitute Olestra, managed the client side of the development and roll-out of a global IT application "Clearance Tracking" and earned my law degree nights during my time as Beauty Care Regulatory Affairs Section Head.
