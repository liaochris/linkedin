Mike Boehmer, APR’s Summary

On Jan. 10, 2011, I began a new phase in my career. I joined Catholic Health Partners, which operates 34 hospitals in five states, as Media Manager. I am excited about the opportunity to serve this top quality organization. My responsibilities will include proactive media relations, social media strategy and web/Intranet content.

I'm a public relations and marketing professional with a proven track record helping organizations achieve strategic goals. I have been recognized in Cincinnati, the state of Ohio and elsewhere as an innovator and leader who cares about helping others.

I possess the ability to see the big picture, yet ensure the details get carried out. I'm always learning (and striving to implement) the latest in public relations and marketing. I want to use my talents and skills to make a positive difference in this world.

Recently, I have learned a great deal about social media marketing (and marketing in general) while helping my wife with her book From Incurable to Incredible: Cancer Survivors Who Beat the Odds.
