Steve Owens's Summary

Steve draws on over fifteen years of web-based business experience as an entrepreneur and innovator. His pursuit of creative opportunities has lead to leadership throughout the sports industry. Currently a commercial real estate professional with Jones Lang LaSalle's Project & Development Services team in Washington, DC, Steve excels in marketing and business development initiatives while taking on various project management roles.


Prior to JLL, Steve lent his out-of-the-box thinking to the United States and Guatemalan Olympic Committees as an exercise physiologist, and began one of the first online coaching companies in the world in 1999. He implemented social media strategies for this and other businesses, and consistantly stay ahead of these related trends.


As an innovator, Steve brought together one of the brightest, most forward thinking ideas to the sports industry to date, as project manager to the most state of the art wind tunnel testing apparatus in the world for this type of work. It's through these innovations, Steve was afforded the opportunity to work with many of the top professional cyclists, triathletes and skiers in the world.


Steve is skilled in a wide variety of software and web based tools, including Microsoft Office, Google Analytics and AdWords, SEO, Constant Contact, Adobe Photoshop, Microsoft CRM, HTML and others. He is very versatile with functioning and 'manipulating' a variety of computer platforms, including PC, Mac, iPhone, iPad & Andriod.
