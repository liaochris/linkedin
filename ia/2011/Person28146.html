Mark Barner's Summary

My inspiration comes from challenging the norm. I enjoy taking a different approach to every-day dilemmas. I combine ingenuity with functional ideas, in the pursuit of finding the best solution, satisfying the client as well as staying true to my personal style.

I strive to create trendy websites that standout, with features that are innovative. User friendliness is one of my absolute focus points. Lack of user friendliness, can be the downfall of even the most impressive websites!

My goal is to evolve continuously: in my designs and expertise to benefit my work and therefore my company. Being an independant company it allows me the freedom to pursue new ideas and layouts. I am flexible to work with and committed to tailoring the perfect product to suit my clients.

Let me know if you have a new challenge for me!
