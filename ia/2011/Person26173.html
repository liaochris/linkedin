Les Wadeson’s Summary

Les is the European Managing Director for the Extended Warranty and Retail Services division of NEWAsurion Europe. NEWAsurion is a $4bn revenue organisation with 10,000+ employees worldwide managing in excess of 200m active consumer contracts for extended warranties & handset protection.

Value Creator - He has a proven relationship sales ability both as an individual and managing a sales focused team; He strikes trusted relationships with clients at all levels from the ‘C’ suite to customer facing employees; He has a successful entrepreneurial track record launching Geek Squad and the associated customer propositions.

Business Operator - He has a proven track record of programme delivery and operational management of the Geek Squad launch & on-going operation; Successfully managed the cost base of the full Geek Squad enterprise across 200FTE Contact Centre Agents; 200 FTE Field Agents and 500 FTE Repairs & Reverse Logistics Engineers.

People Developer - He is a proven people leader through inspiring the Geek Squad Agents to be passionate about the operation; Continually scored upper-quartile in 360 degree leadership appraisals in a highly driven consulting practice from both personal counselees and the wider Practice; Managed skills & resourcing for a consulting practice of 400+ consultants in line with market demands.

Industry - He has built and managed a niche service brand in the UK Consumer Electronics Industry and Retail. Networked with leading clients and eco-system suppliers in the European Communications & High Tech (CHT) industry; Advised major clients in the UK, Europe and the Far East.

Brand & Culture - He has a proven ability to lead businesses and operations under a strong consumer brand and culture. Recognised by the Founder of Geek Squad (renown for it extremely passionate brand culture amongst the agent), as the brand ambassador for Europe.
