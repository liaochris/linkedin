Jean-Stephane Gourevitch's Summary

I am an expert in regulatory affairs, public policy, economic regulation, competition policy, public affairs, external, government and stakeholder's relations, corporate strategy/competitive intelligence in the field of the digital communications ecosystem (telecoms, media, broadcasting, content delivery and production, internet), convergence and cloud networking/services. I have over 19 years of experience and held senior management positions at the International, European and National levels. I have worked for blue-chip wireline and wireless telecoms operators, top-tier consulting and Communications/PR firms, as an entrepreneurial International consultant and finally for public authorities. I was a lecturer in European Competition Law at the University of Paris 11 for 3 years as well as a guest speaker in European Telecoms Policy at Telecom Bretagne/Telecom Paris schools for 4 years.

Specialties

First pillar: regulatory strategy, public policy, competition regulation, strategic communications & Government relations. Includes: policy development, issues management, support to product/business developments; strategic mapping, planning/implementation of internal and external relationship management/influencer communications.
- Second pillar: corporate strategy/planning, strategic negotiations, competitive,market, political & technological intelligence; strategic communications roadmaps.
