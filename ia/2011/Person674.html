Will Roffé's Summary

Will has over 20 years advertising and marketing experience.

He has worked client-side in roles such as Sales Manager, Sales and Marketing Manager and Marketing Manager, before jumping the fence and enjoying ad agency life; first as a Media Buyer, then Account Manager and Account Director, before training as a copywriter in the industry's prestigious Axis Ad School in 1992. (12 students were accepted that year.)

Will has worked across many industry sectors and for the following ad agencies, in a freelance or permanent capacity:

Saatchi & Saatchi :: Saatchi & Saatchi Direct :: Colenso :: Ogilvy & Mather :: McCann-Erickson :: DMB&B :: Bettle :: FCB :: Bates :: Mindspace :: Ogilvy :: Lavender :: Frank :: Robbins Brandt Richter :: Mojo :: .99.

Will founded Genr8 in 2002 to take on special assignments for direct clients and agencies, then relaunched as Adland, a hybrid digital ad agency in October 2008. Today he has a team of 15 who are spread around the globe. Adland uses a wider net of trusted colleagues and partner companies when needed.

Adland exists to solve problems and to sell. Services include business and marketing strategies, marketing and brand audits, concept development, creative direction, advertising, relationship marketing, copywriting, design, social media marketing, website development, mobile development and more.

Specialties

Advertising, Copywriting, Art Direction, Strategy, Concept development, Direct Marketing, Social Media Marketing, Design, product development, Creative Direction, web development and more.
