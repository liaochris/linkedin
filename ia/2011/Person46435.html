Yuri Gitahy de Oliveira's Summary

I founded and manage Aceleradora, a startup accelerator, to help tech startups build scalable businesses.

In my early career, I've worked for IBM, taught for Sun Microsystems, founded and buried a software startup, did better on the other two, plus bootstrapped BOL - the largest Brazilian freemail service. Later I managed the systems engineering team for UOL, was CTO and partner at Vetta - a CMMI-3 offshore outsourcing provider. Over the recent years, I founded an IT consulting company, joined Confrapar as an investing partner, and self-funded Aceleradora.
