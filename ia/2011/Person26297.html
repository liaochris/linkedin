Lillian Wu's Summary

I am an award-winning marketing professional with over 10 years of extensive experience in product management, product development, and business development. I hold a MBA and a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science.

For the past 6 years, I have been a product manager and a business development manager in Canada’s telecommunication industry. I’m best known for strategic planning, problem-solving, and interpersonal skills demonstrated by leading cross-functional team to translate market needs and technological concepts to business solutions to achieve strategic goals. In my recent role as a product manager at TELUS Business Solutions, I’ve been a core team member managing a $4M “Business One” portfolio for TELUS. In 2009, the team achieved 30% growth in revenue and 17% growth in subscriber counts. I made critical contribution by leading the ‘Economic Transformation’ project, which aimed to standardize business case forecasting and financial reporting models and processes. The initiative achieved outstanding results by recovering over $600k missing revenue each month, and I was awarded ‘Passion for Growth’ for the year.

My career goal is to obtain a key product management or business development position using my skills and professional experience to help a progressive organization meet and exceed its objectives.
