Phil Streatfield’s Summary

I am a results oriented commercially focused supply chain profesional who has delivered significant cost and cash efficiencies coupled with significant service improvements in complex global supply chains. My experience spans end to end supply chain, IT and orgnisational change in Retail, FMCG and Pharmaceutical arenas. I am a resilient, process oriented, people development driven individual capable of building effective teams able to cope with highly ambiguous business environments.

Goals: to be a valued friend and colleague; to stay fit and healthy and to make a positive contribution to society. To support my wife and children as we all grow and develop together.
