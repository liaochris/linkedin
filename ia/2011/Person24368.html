  • Senior Vice President of Product Strategy

    Click Forensics

    (Privately Held; Internet industry)

    September 2006 — August 2007 (1 year )

    Click Forensics is the industry leader in online ad verification, traffic quality improvement and ad analytics for the online advertising community. For over 5 years the online advertising industry has relied on Click Forensics as the independent authority on traffic quality and click fraud. The company provides audience verification solutions for search and display advertisers, agencies, publishers and ad networks. The team leverages their deep expertise in online marketing, Internet security, and click fraud detection techniques to now find other sources of low-quality traffic, and even score the relative merits of high-quality traffic, in an effort to provide robust traffic quality management solutions.

    Deployed as hosted SaaS solutions, Click Forensics' offerings help to optimize online advertising campaigns by identifying sources of unwanted or underperforming traffic (such as click fraud) and scoring valid traffic based on its value to advertisers and ad networks. The Click Forensics' products are based on machine-learning and data mining technologies trained on a massive data set of clicks and impressions collected from hundreds of advertisers and ad networks since 2006. Click Forensics is the author of the quarterly Click Fraud Index, the industry benchmark for traffic quality.

    Click Forensics is headquartered in Austin, Texas, and is privately held with funding from Sierra Ventures, Austin Ventures, Shasta Ventures and Stanford University.
