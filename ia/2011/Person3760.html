Artie Bernaducci’s Summary

I know this is a professional site and I could go about explaining the last 19 years saying “I’m a financial advisor” and "I specialize in retirement planning."

However, that just doesn’t cut it and quite frankly it's boring, so let me say it this way...

Have you ever looked at how complicated the directions are for assembling “DO-IT-YOURSELF” furniture?

Well, we have and know that finances can often be just as complicated, if not more!

So we’ve developed ways to put together people’s “financial furniture” that helps take out potential “stumbling blocks” and complication, and what you get back is something easier to understand that is complete and customized just for you.

    (Financial Services industry)

    July 2010 — Present (8 months)

    My goal is always to provide real value to anyone I see...

    So to start, I ask 2 questions:

    1. What do you buy in your house that you 'have to have' but don't understand?

    2. Of those products, why do you continue to buy them where you do?

    I have found well over 9 out of 10 times people put "insurance and investments" at the top of their list.

    So it boils down to this...

    If I could help educate you about one of those products, would you sit down with me?

    If you do, I do not expect you to trust me or anything you've ever heard about me. And after a little while together you can make the decision about whether you want to hire me as your advisor or get rid of me.

    If you do, I will help you to be as prepared as possible -financially- for life's unforseen events and strive for a more confident future knowing you have a comprehensive plan in place.
