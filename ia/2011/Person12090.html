Dori Gilbert’s Summary

Dori (aka Dorothy) is an accomplished project, marketing, and communications manager. She is known for her ability to execute, her enthusiasm, and her experience driving performance and brand value. Her resourcefulness and broad knowledge of to-market activities brings cost effective, timely execution to product line business objectives. Her strong interpersonal and communication skills lead to collaboration with technicians and executives alike. Dori has a strong desire to succeed and relies on her creativity, work ethic, business acumen and intelligence for driving projects to completion that meet market, business and stakeholder requirements.

Her experience includes individual contributor and management roles in software product development, product documentation, product releases, training, vendor management, market research, product marketing, product collateral, messaging, positioning, writing, editing, planning and strategy.

Dori is also a speaker with experience presenting to business leaders on branding, communications strategies, networking strategies and LinkedIn.

Technologies include data storage systems, NAS, storage management software, computer networking equipment, document storage, cloud/SAAS software, and other software and systems.

Tools include SalesForce.com, Central Desktop, BaseCamp, Adobe Illustrator, Corel Draw, Creative Briefs, MS Office and LinkedIn.
