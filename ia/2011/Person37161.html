Robb Braun’s Summary

As Founder and President of The Leadership Source™, I bring over 20 years experience as a training and personal development professional. I have spent many years as a business owner and workshop presenter. I have participated in executive leadership trainings, mastery communications courses and am an active member of Toastmasters International. Most recently, I created, developed and implemented two largely impactful projects at a resort casino. I developed and led an executive training program geared to teach over 600 managers how to better
lead, develop and inspire their personnel. I also created and implemented a 'guide program' that led ultimately to increased communications between floor personnel and management and to greater leadership effectiveness of the supervisory staff. Overall, both team-building projects led to increased guest service and business efficiency. I am a certified leader with the ManKind Project and have led and developed over 3000 men in weekend trainings throughout the United States and overseas. I am an extremely gifted speaker and workshop presenter who, with great ease, can deliver to any size group. My core strength lies in my ability to lead others to self-empowerment by way of my strong communication skills. My greatest passion is to enthusiastically lead people through disciplined training and preparation resulting in their ability to lead themselves toward higher aspirations.

"I Train and Develop Personnel!"
"I Build and Develop Teams!"
"I Develop and Inspire Leaders!"

Robb Braun’s Specialties:

I guide and support individuals to take responsibility for their failures and successes - leadership and responsibility.

I guide teams to develop their identity and know their mission and purpose - individual roles and responsibilities.

I coach and support leaders and managers of people. I help them to identify their strengths and the areas they will want to make changes. I guide leaders of people to identify their style of leadership and how to motivate, inspire and lead effectively.
