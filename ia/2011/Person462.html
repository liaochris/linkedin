Adam Akarsoy's Summary

Global/Strategic Management educated and trained professional who currently recruits sales/marketing & technical professionals in the food and beverages industry

Upon graduation from Penn State University with a BS in Global/Strategic Management, I joined the Retail Industry in a managerial position. This opportunity gave me a very clear focus on what a good sales and customer service professional has to do to be effective and successful. I appreciated the contact I had with both my management and the customers that came to do business with our company.

The recruiting business for me has been a terrific experience because it allows me to match the right candidate with the right company. I believe in listening and probing with regard to my client’s needs in order to make sure that the candidates I present see the potential position as a real opportunity.

Industries I serve are the Food, Beverage and Consumer Packaged Goods. The primary positions I place are in the Retail and Food Service segments of the above industries.
