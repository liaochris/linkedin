Michelle Riordan's Summary

Started Photography By Exposure in 1994 in Atlantic County, NJ. Clients: Trump Properties, Caesars Atlantic City, AC Hilton, The Borgata. Photographic expertise in: special events and conventions, public relations, advertising, aerial photography and portfolios. I opened a studio in 1996 and added wedding photography, family and children's portraits. Built up a fabulous following for on location family beach sessions.

Opened a second location in northern Virginia in 2008.

My goal is to build up my business in northern Virginia while maintaining & servicing all of my great clients in southern New Jersey.

Specialties

Corporate/convention photography, Special Events, Golf Tournaments, Portfolios, Family & Children's Portraits.
