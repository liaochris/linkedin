Ken Eisenberg’s Summary

15+ years of qualified lead generation, sales process facilitation, custom market research, direct marketing, and database consulting in manufacturing and distribution.
Over 8+ years of successes in implementing and leveraging Salesforce.com CRM. Created Industrial Marketing Associates, LLC to provide specialized services to improve the efficiency of clients' sales processes. Ken and associates have completed over 100 custom-designed "Strategic Marketing" programs in manufacturing, including the bakery, beverage, biomedical, chemical, cosmetics, dairy, environmental, flavors, foodservice, health-care, industrial machinery, information technology, medical devices, metals, pharmaceutical, plastics, renewable and solar energy, and wholesale distribution industries.

Pioneered the concept and implementation of strategically integrating telemarketing, advertising, direct mail, publicity, and database marketing to realize the benefits of their combined synergism in, "Strategic Marketing". Applications have been implemented at various stages in the sales process of developing and selling a new account. These have included everything from primary market research to dormant account reactivation, inquiry qualification, quote follow-up, marginal account handling, field sales support, new product introduction, profiling, prospecting, internet/database marketing, and Customer Relationship Marketing. Clients are both small firms, and large manufacturing and service companies, which produce a wide range of products and services.

Ken Eisenberg’s Specialties:

Increases the reach and effectiveness of your salespeople to have more qualified sales appointments in a specific geography. Specializes in utilization of Cloud computing with Salesforce.com CRM and the needs of industrial marketers to identify the changing numbers of buying influences and buying specifiers in your niche market segments, familiarize them with your products and services, communicate the benefits offered, and provide a method for them to buy from you.
