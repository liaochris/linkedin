Mike Machak’s Summary

Veteran TV news guy here - telling stories with pictures at stations in New Mexico, Texas, Florida and Tennessee… who has transitioned into being the news guy in the corporate setting as an ‘all purpose’ business communicator, writer and multi-media producer.

Turn on a dime… heart stopping… never a dull moment 24/7 TV News experiences have helped me make the leap into the fast pace of social media. Having a blast because it’s still all about getting the story on first with great pictures.

Thrilled for the recognition of working well with journalists with a GOLD AWARD in Media Relations in 2009 from the International Association of Business Communicators (IABC) Nashville, Tennessee Chapter 'Gold Pen Awards'

Mike Machak’s Specialties:

On a daily basis... creating, managing and delivering news and information directly to the public, journalists and all available media channels.
