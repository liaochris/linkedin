Malcolm McKee’s Summary

Malcolm is an MBA qualified business consultant and strategy manager with ten years experience in a global professional services firm, working extensively with clients in the energy and utilities sector, and leading strategic initiatives. He excels at translating strategic vision into workable business plans and implementing them to deliver measurable business improvement.

His proven business transformation expertise includes engaging the leadership team, making the business case for change, chartering and facilitating business improvement teams, and delivering tangible results that become embedded in "business as usual". An accomplished author and presenter effective across a wide range of media, he has extensive expertise of managing internal communications, and is a regular guest lecturer on undergraduate degree programmes.

As a chartered engineer Malcolm is known for his analytical approach, personal commitment, and his ability to reduce complexity and find creative solutions. He has a reputation for delivering against tight timescales in high pressure environments. As a manager he derives great satisfaction from inspiring his staff to develop themselves and achieve their career goals. He is a trained business coach, and mentors others both informally and as part of organised schemes.
