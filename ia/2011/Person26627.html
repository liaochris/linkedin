Liz Moise’s Summary

Liz is a marketer with a design background and a love for words. She helps clients identify the most affordable way to market their products or services, or connect with their customers.

She is passionate about helping people and giving back. In her free time, she is a contributing blogger and pro-bono marketing consultant with Affinity Interactive Group, an interactive agency focused on amplifying the impact of non-profit and social enterprise organizations.

--------------------------

In her role as Account Director at Springbox Liz managed a team of account service professionals, providing interactive strategy and planning to a range of clients including Central Market, H-E-B, Michael J Fox Foundation and LIVESTRONG. In her time at Springbox she advanced from Supervisor to Director. She managed the restructuring of the Account and Productions teams, creating training and orientation programs to help ensure team growth.

As Manager of Account Services at BluePoint, Liz delivered strategies to her clients that included both traditional and social media marketing and PR tactics.

As Marketing Communications Manager at InnoCentive, a global crowd-sourcing company, Liz and her team drove high-profile coverage through relationships with national media and industry bloggers, and helped build a broad community of "Solvers" to tackle global design, manufacturing and engineering challenges.

As Art Director and Account Manager at Nokia Interactive Advertising (formerly Enpocket) Liz built and managed a team of in-house designers and freelancers who delivered customized mobile web sites and interactive campaigns for clients including Sprint, Ford, Pepsi, MTV and CondeNast.

Liz Moise’s Specialties:

Marketing and PR specialties: Account Management, Social Media strategy, Community building, online marketing, mobile strategy, email campaigns, non profit, not for profit marketing
Graphic Design Expertise: Web (PS, DW), Print Collateral (all things Adobe), Mobile (MMS and WAP), Corporate Branding, Exhibit Design
Other areas of Expertise: Team Management, Art Direction, Copy Writing, Fashion Design, Illustration, Photography
