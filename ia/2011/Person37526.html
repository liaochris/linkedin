Robyn Simpson's Summary

With 20 years marketing experience in a range of industries, I've learn all the 'tricks of the trade'. I know how to manage a marketing budget (no matter how small) to make sure it delivers the best tangible results effectively.

I love teaching people. I teach about attitude and personal responsibility. I find high performance mentoring extremely rewarding for both myself and the client.

I also teach business people effective low or no cost marketing techniques to help them build their businesses.

One of my goals this year is to secure professional speaking engagements and workshops so I can help people develop professionally and personally.

One of the least utilised aspects of branding and marketing is the power of a company's internal culture. The combination of attitude, personal responsibility and marketing is unique and incredibly powerful. If you want to know 'how' to do this, connect with me on Linked In—I'm happy to help.
