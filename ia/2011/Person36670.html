Rian Rathwick’s Summary

Experienced in developing and delivering solutions in start-up, turnaround, and growth situations. Business experience includes public corporations and public financing. Adept at creating business and opportunities from small investments.

As an officer and director of public companies, an entrepreneur, and a consultant, I have raised millions of dollars of public and private funds; managed hundreds of employees; prepared business plans and prospectuses; generated numerous feasibility studies; been involved with multiple mergers and acquisitions; given oral presentations to groups of hundreds of people, boards of directors, government agencies, corporate executives, and television interviewers; and personally handled the development, organization, and coordination of various sales and marketing programs.

Here are a few of the successes I have achieved:

Maritime Resorts International – Business development of a company from a startup to a multi-million dollar publicly traded corporation in 5 years. My successes included raising over $60,000,000 in public funding.

Casino Resource Corporation – Negotiated and established two major acquisitions in 2 years. My project management skills were instrumental in the acquisition of a major resort project from bankruptcy as well as the establishment of a brand new resort project in Tunisia, North Africa.

Lanlinx Corporation (Wireless LAN, Video, VoIP) – From technology start-up to a viable ongoing entity for over 11 years. As owner/operator, I have successfully packaged, promoted, and operated Lanlinx, resulting in profitability for every year in existence. This experience has honed my skills for sales and marketing, independence, self-motivation, creativity, and delivering on time and within budget.

In addition to my extensive business background, I have very strong technology skills that I regularly leverage to benefit the companies that I work with in order to multiply profits, reduce expenses, and improve performance.
