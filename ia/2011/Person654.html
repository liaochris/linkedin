Andre' DiMino's Experience

Co-Founder & Director - Security Research & Analysis

The Shadowserver Foundation

Computer & Network Security industry

2005 – Present (6 years)

The Shadowserver Foundation is an all volunteer watchdog group of security professionals that gather, track, and report on malware, botnet activity, and electronic fraud. It is the mission of the Shadowserver Foundation to improve the security of the Internet by raising awareness of the presence of compromised servers, malicious attackers, and the spread of malware.

* Lead all divisions in the various phases of the group's process and operation. This includes malware analysis, tracking botnet and other malicious activity, network flow and traffic analysis, honeypot analysis and deployment, disseminating cyber threat information, and coordinating incident response.

* Perform detailed studies of malware infections, botnet analysis, and network traffic analysis. Test various detection and defense systems. Utilize various network security analysis techniques to analyze botnet and malware behavior.

* Perform static and behavioral analysis of malware specimens. Study various Web and network based attacks and correlate to detected botnet activity. Monitor and report on malicious attackers.

* Develop strategic alliances with outside groups and organizations to better coordinate data exchange, incident observation and research, detection and defense strategies, and process and system integration.
