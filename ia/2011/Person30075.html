Michael Hardenbrook's Summary

Already a $60 billion industry in 2006 and predicted to double by 2012, MEDICAL & DENTAL TOURISM provides patients with the opportunity to receive top quality medical/dental services in foreign countries at substantially lower prices than in their home country

It is Dental Destinations goal to provide access to the best quality dental care available to those Americans who choose to combine their treatment experience with an excursion outside of the United States. In order to meet that goal, Dental Destinations has created a Quality Assurance Program to screen the dentists that are associated with our business. This program allows Dental Destinations to provide our clients with the best information possible so they can make an informed decision when selecting a dentist.

The program consists of an evaluation and credentials review of the practitioners, their facilities, staff, protocols, treatment procedures, and outcomes. The results of these reviews and site visits are available to you on this web site. Please carefully evaluate the information we provide. You will also be able to personally communicate with the office and dentist directly, so feel free to ask for additional information.

Dental Destinations wants our clients to feel safe and confident with the dentists they select.
