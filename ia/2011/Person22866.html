Judie Stanford's Summary

I am a gadget enthusiast responsible for almost 500 individual and co-written consumer reviews at the popular website The Gadgeteer. Writing for my own site since 2006, I have continued to cover the gadgets and other types of gear that we all love to collect and carry. I also make occasional guest review appearances on other respected gadget and PDA sites.

I am currently the Android App editor for T-Mobile's 'MyTouch Insider's Guide' magazine.

My time is divided between running my family's ranching operation and the never-ending pursuit of the ultimate gadgets.

Specialties

My specialty is writing in-depth reviews that include lots of touchy-feely observations which can only be made after using the heck out of a particular product.
