GMSHRM is proud to be a local chapter affiliate of the Society for Human Resource Management (SHRM), the world's largest HR organization.

GMSHRM continually strives to serve HR professionals and to advance the Human Resources profession by providing a variety of programs and services designed to meet the professional needs of HR practitioners in Miami-Dade County.

GMSHRM is committed to developing a strong Workforce Readiness initiative to strengthen and promote HR leadership in this area. Workforce Readiness (WR) helps HR Professionals to successfully address the workplace challenges that arise when the current and future workforce is not ready to meet the changing skills and knowledge needs.
