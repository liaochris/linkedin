Gina Alagata’s Summary

Business woman with entrepreneurial experience for over 15 years in organizing and facilitating women's groups. Direct experience in helping women in business achieve their goals in life and business through business groups and personal settings.

Wink, Women Inkorporated launched in San Diego in April of 2010. Wink is the Link to Women's Business Networking. Our vision is to open other chapters in San Diego through 2010 then reaching out to other locations starting in 2011.

Currently the groups are open to all women professionals, business owners, entrepreneurs or those new to business. The events offer high energy, fun, interactive, professional networking that is always done with integrity.

Gina Alagata’s Specialties:

Specializing in business connections, negotiations, collaborations, women's business events, business socials and strategies, women's personal and professional growth. Wink is all about women in business networking - always done with integrity!
