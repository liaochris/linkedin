Ron A. Smith, MBA’s Summary

Ron has extensive team leadership and process improvement experience working for companies such as Intel, Microsoft and Advanced Technology Labs (Philips Medical). Ron has led many successful teams in the development and implementation of process and automation enhancements and solutions resulting in significant improvements to the bottom line.

Ron's process improvement work at Intel, resulted in an approximate 35% increase in platform validation efficiency, the Chipset QA team completed the i965 Northbridge project 15% ahead of schedule, while taking on an addition 30% increase in workload. Ron received a Division Recognition Award and several Division Team Awards for his efforts that were instrumental in the team’s success.

As an Intel representative at Microsoft, Ron developed many new strategic relationships, acting as a Liaison to a number of campus wide Windows development and Quality Assurance teams to promote Intel systems and processors as the premier development and software test platform.

Ron earned his MBA in Technology Management from the W.P. Carey School of Business at Arizona State University, a Master of Engineering certification in Software Program Management from the Fulton School of Engineering at Arizona State and his BS in Computer Science from City University of Seattle.
