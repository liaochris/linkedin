Jim Conner’s Summary

Jim Conner is a Network and Systems Professional and Technical Project Manager. He has designed, installed and managed IT projects for over twenty years. His experiences range from banking and trading to financial and healthcare. Jim is an excellent resource in the Information Technology Infrastructure. He is adept with review, development, documentation and implementation of Information Technologies. Jim demonstrates high personal ethics and a strong professional work ethic. He has a proven track record of productive and cost saving management performance. During his years of professional experience he has established and managed operational and capital project budgets exceeding several million dollars annually. He has effectively managed staff and customer IT structures. These comprised of administrators, software and hardware analysts, systems and network engineers. He is an effective communicator with executive and technical staff.

My goal for over 20 years has been to help people live a better life through technology. I have done this since 1994 at Erlanger Health System in Chattanooga, TN. I have helped Erlanger touch thousands of people through our enterprise network and server technologies using the state-of-the-art ATM, Gig and Wi-Fi connectivity. Bed-side monitor networks, IV pump wireless networks, and many others. I designed Erlanger’s Wi-Fi network. It is one of the largest “hot-spots” and Wi-Fi networks in Hamilton County TN.
