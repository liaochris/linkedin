Alison Barkman, MS, RD’s Summary

I am a nutrition writer and communications consultant. Currently I provide public relations support for Jill Jayne, MS, RD, and her rock ‘n roll nutrition show for kids, Jump with Jill.

I dissect the latest food & nutrition news on my blog, Food Chat. I also write for Examiner.com as the Long Island Diet and Exercise Examiner. I am also the current co-editor for the Greater New Yorker Dietetic Association's member newsletter.

My background:
My nutrition experience includes private counseling and clinical nutrition.

Prior to becoming and RD, I was an account supervisor for pharmaceutical/consumer healthcare clients at GCI Group (division of Grey Worldwide). I have also freelanced for several other agencies including Cohn & Wolfe, Hill & Knowlton and Edelman.
