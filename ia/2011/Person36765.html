ANS Group is an ISO9001/14001/27001 ITIL technology solutions company specialising in the provision of hardware, software and 24/7 managed services.

Operating in a diverse range of business areas within the public and private sectors, our technology solutions help simplify business challenges. Our Infrastructure 3.0 architecture (www.infrastructure3.co.uk) is set to transform the future of IT networks, using next generation technology to streamline and unify the infrastructure. As one of the only companies to hold the necessary specialisations with Cisco, VMware and NetApp, ANS is uniquely positioned to deliver this unified infrastructure, helping clients achieve a higher level of performance, at a much lower cost.

ANS Group are Cisco Gold Partner (UCS ATP), VMware Enterprise Partner (VMWare Partner of the Year), NetApp Star Partner (APSP + ASP) and Microsoft Gold Partner, placing us in a unique position as no other IT organisation in Europe has achieved this level of accreditation. As an award winning business and with our impressive portfolio there comes huge reassurance that you will be working with people who fully understand the growing range of technology solutions, giving you the confidence that your IT network and your business will be in the safe hands of infrastructure and technology solutions experts.

ANS Group has been awarded a framework agreement by Buying Solutions for Commoditised IT Hardware and Software, Lot 2 - IT Infrastructure Hardware. We are pre-approved for Public Sector purchasing within the scope of this Lot. ANS was selected as one of thirteen organisations who can provide this under the Framework Agreement. There is no need to run a full EU tender process as the Framework has already been competed through a full OJEU procurement by Buying Solutions, reducing public sector procurement costs.
