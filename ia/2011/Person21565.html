Johan Brand’s Summary

I am curious, passionate, full of energy, drive and excitement. I have several years of managment experience and a strong and diverse experience within the creative industry spanning a multitude of high profile projects & brands.

I am strategic, quick-thinking, proactive, and a hands on creative problem-solver with a unique blend of skills within design, technology & business.

I believe in the concept of play and its ability to unlock creativity and stimulate innovation. My ambition is to improve the world we live in through the strategic use of design.

I lead talented teams of strategists, researchers, designers and developers in areas such as brand & business innovation, communication, production of campaign work, games, microsites, web portals, intranets and software applications.

I run workshops, brainstorms, innovation processes and develop methodologies to support the strategic and creative process.

My involvement and guidance is critical in all phases of a project from inception to completion; in client relations as well as encouraging the team to deliver quality at all times. The result is long lasting client relationships and industry award winning work including Peoples Choice Webby, Design Effectiveness Award, several FWA SOTD's and numerous Gold Horizon Awards.
