Medini Pradhan’s Summary

I am chef by profession and entrepreneur by trade. Love to cook and I love to talk about Indian Cuisine & Culture and do it through CuisineCuisine.com. I also run a successful gift basket business, called Elegantly Expressed Gift Baskets. I am currently working towards publishing my books.

I enjoy pleasurable gluttoning and exploring new and unique foods. Although I don't think I can venture to even try -"monkey brain". I love reading a well written piece of literature, be it a book, magazine or blog. As much as I like to cherish a well made dish, I delight in creating one too. I also, like the very mundane task - to many - of putting together a daily meal for my family.

On a professional level, I like to carry a passionate dialogue with anyone about food. I indulge in digital photography for my business as well as to capture the fleeting moments of my life. I love to engage in creative discussions with entrepreneurial and giving people across the globe.
