TECNiA iT

Information Technology and Services industry

June 2002 – Present (8 years 9 months)

I established TECNiA iT in 2002, a company based on the concepts of keeping technology simple, functional, and supporting excellent customer service. We provide local businesses with management consultancy, IT procurement and technology support services.

Our services include:
* SaaS Solution Providers
* Business ICT Advice & Consultation
* Project Management
* ICT Planning & Strategy Support
* Specification & Supply of IT Hardware & Software
* ICT System Security
* Data Network Design & Implementation + VPN
* Business System Support
* Web Design & Hosting

Along with providing ICT Services, I use my experience to work with clients who have little or no experience of running their own business. I have helped a number of small businesses startup, offer them advice to make best use of funds available: financial systems, Internet, marketing, email, telephone systems and back up procedures - providing clients with peace of mind, disaster recovery methodology and resilience against failure.
