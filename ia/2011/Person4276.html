Steve Floyd’s Summary

Hello, my name is Steve Floyd. Since day one, I've been a self motivated entrepreneur. It hasn't always been easy, but it's definitely been interesting. Between being awesome, I design and develop interactive marketing solutions for small to medium size businesses.

I have a web consulting business called AXZM based in Dallas, TX that provides affordable Interactive Design, Web Development and SEO / SEM services.

Steve Floyd’s Specialties:

In addition to designing for web, mobile & print, I specialize in Free Open Source Software (FOSS) Web Development with platforms ranging from light content management systems to robust J2EE ERP solutions.

I have worked on sites using Joomla, Wordpress, Drupal, Magento, Expression Engine, Plone, Typo3, Xoops, Pligg, Elgg, VTiger, OpenCRX and Sugar CRM primarily running on a LAMP stack.

  • Founder / Operation Manager

    Nice People

    (Information Technology and Services industry)

    November 1998 — January 2008 (9 years 3 months)

    Originally founded in 1998 by Dallas entrepreneur and tech evangelist Steve "Deadicated" Floyd, Nic "Enoch" Hernandez and Bryan "Figment" Daniels, Nice People was created to address the lack of underground hip-hop shows supported in the North Texas area. At one point in 1999, local hip-hop shows were 2 or 3 months apart. The 3 friends conspired to organize and promote a series of events that later developed into "Knowledge Wednesdays" (founded November 2001 at the Sandbar in Deep Ellum), one of the longest running live hip-hop weeklies for local artists to date.

    Hosting over 300 live hip-hop events with such underground artists as Little Brother, J-Live, El Da Sensei, Myka Nyne, CunningLynguists, Dj Swamp, Awol One, Circus (Shapeshifters) 2Mex, Busdriver, CVE, Josh Martinez, Oldominion (Sleep, Smoke) Galapagos 4 (Qwel/Typical Cats), The C.O.R.E., Bavu Blakes, VG Skillz (V-Zilla), Deadicated, Cannibal Ox, C-Rayz Walz, Blueprint (Rhymesayers), Johnny Polygon, The Literates, Deep Space 5, Bucc of the Narcissits, Astronautalis, Ground Opening, Ekzile, Arcane, Pikahsso, Dos Noun, Keynote, Al Lyric, Assasyn Dynasty, Headkrack, The Listener, Sivion, Moon Criket, Gutterball, Haps, Zeale 32, Hydroponic Sound System, Media Darling, Kin Fok Kru, Vortexas and many many more... Nice People inspired and supported many of the more recognized artists from the region long before many people even knew who they were.

    Nice People eventually evolved into the Dallas based web consulting boutique AXZM, but the foundation that was built over their 10 years in production would no doubt influence the three friends' professional careers.
