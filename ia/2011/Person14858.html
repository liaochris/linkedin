Frederic Kerrest's Summary

I am passionate about enterprise technology and have spent the last 12 years helping companies improve their operations by embracing software solutions.

In early 2009, I teamed up with my friend Todd McKinnon (ex-SVP Engineering at Salesforce.com 2003-9) to start Okta with the vision of helping companies more easily adopt business web applications.

Previously I spent my time
- Evaluating early stage enterprise software companies at Hummer Winblad Venture Partners
- In Business Development and Sales at Salesforce.com (2002-2007)
- As co-founder and director of technology at an award-winning high-tech consulting firm
- As a software engineer at Moai Technologies, Sun Microsystems and Applied Materials

I received a BS degree in Computer Science from Stanford University and an MBA from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology's Sloan School of Management.

Specialties

SaaS (Software-as-a-Service), on-demand computing, enterprise software, software startups, operations management, enterprise sales and sales engineering, software product development and marketing, business and channel development, venture capital and private investing.

Okta Inc.

Privately Held; Computer Software industry

April 2009 – Present (2 years)

Okta is the market leading on-demand identity and access management service that enables enterprises to accelerate the secure adoption of their web based applications, both in the cloud and behind the firewall. Okta delivers a complete solution addressing the needs of IT, end users, and business leaders; no customization required.

With a catalog of pre-integrated applications and deep integration with Active Directory, Okta provides IT centralized user provisioning, access management, and reporting. Every end user gets a personalized, single sign-on home page for all of their applications, and business leaders get the insight they need to maximize ROI and track compliance.

Okta’s initial service is built on a secure, reliable and extensible on-demand platform that will serve as the foundation upon which a core set of services will be delivered to eliminate cloud adoption barriers and unlock the potential of the cloud for enterprises everywhere.

The Okta team has built, deployed, and supported market leading, on-demand and enterprise software solutions from companies including Salesforce.com, SuccessFactors, PeopleSoft, Microsoft, Sun, and HP. Okta is backed by premiere angel and venture investors including Andreessen Horowitz, FLOODGATE, and Ron Conway.
