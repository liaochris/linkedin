George Kassabgi’s Summary

My aim is to contribute to entrepreneurial energy -- the optimistic courage that is the seed of all great endeavors, the essential nature of the explorer, the inventor. Some time ago I decided that what truly mattered professionally was to contribute to the journeys of new business ventures and to have the privilege to mentor a few.
