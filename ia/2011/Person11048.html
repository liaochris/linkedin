David Spinks’s Summary

I'm a NYC based social media entrepreneur who has always been fascinated by technology and it's impact on the way we interact.

I share my thoughts and ask a lot of questions on my blog, http://www.whatspinksthinks.com. I also occasionally write for Mashable and other social media publications.

I am the co-founder of BlogDash, a blogger outreach tool. We're working to change the way businesses and bloggers can work together.

I also co-founded u30pro, a community and twitter chat that helps young professionals find answers to the tough questions they face at the beginning of our careers.
