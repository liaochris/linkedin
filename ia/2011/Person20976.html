Jimmie Cannon's Summary

A creative and hardworking young entrepreneur focused on growing in various fields of small business. Spent the past 10+ years working and living in southern California, analyzing and growing within a volatile, challenging markets, through multiple market cycles.

Known for taking a small business start-up or re-engineering situation from "zero to ten" in record time and developing lean operations that deliver efficiency, quality, and sustainable profits.

* Pioneered a new Bail Bond company in 2001 "Abierto 24/7
Bail Bonds" that caters to the rapidly growing Hispanic
market. http://www.abiertobailbonds.com.

* Drove Abierto 24/7 Bail Bonds business into competitive
markets from Ventura to San Diego while growing over 300%
in last two years using aggressive white hat organic search
strategies and strong gorilla marketing tactics to capture
very competitive market share in a down economy.

* Started a profitable flooring store "Carpeteria" in 2006 in the
midst of the housing boom and was able to successfully
penetrate the market and sell the business in 2008 for a
800% profit from my original investment.

* Started Eco Maintenance Solutions in 2010 and will be
applying to be included in the very coveted 8a SBA program
in 2011. The company will be bidding on class A & B
Government buildings for cleaning and maintenance
through out the country.


I am a proponent of using the principles of creative capitalism, marrying the principles of capitalism and business with social development. I blog about topics related to small business leadership and the role creative capitalism as a pathway for small business profit and world progress at http://www.jimmiecannon.com.
