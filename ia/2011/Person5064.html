Beth Gibbons's Summary

After years of working on features desks, I decided to go freelance and now write for a number of magazines and websites including You, Fabulous, Stylist, Grazia, Woman & Home, Top Santé, Zest and Pregnancy & Birth.

I'm happy to write on pretty much anything (from heart-wrenching real-lifes and laugh out-loud first-person pieces, to controversial health reports and thought-provoking lifestyle features). I have a knack for knowing what editors want (even when they don't) and pride myself on filing sub-friendly copy (no really, I used to be one!). In other words, if you're after a talented, engaging writer, please do give me a shout.

Specialties

Health, pregnancy and birth, fitness, wellbeing, relationships, psychology, thoughtful first-persons, dramatic real life, light-hearted humour... anything really!
