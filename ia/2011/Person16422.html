Graham Eades's Summary

Following early career roles in housing I secured a variety of leadership roles at business unit and corporate levels. I achieved success, across the start-up, growth and maturity phases of organisations. I have significant experience of steering organisations through merger, group structure and acquisition.

I provide coaching/mentoring, interim management, and business improvement consultancy services. I am experienced in supporting leaders through facilitating action learning sets, through individual coaching and through leadership development programmes.

My particular interest is in collaborative working and cybernetic whole systems interventions and I believe strongly in the strength of integrated teams. I have built on my early involvement with customer engagement, individual, team and process performance improvement and have assisted a number of organisations with a flexible approach to collaborative leadership and continuous improvement.

Specialties

Individual and team leadership and mental toughness coaching and development. Organisational change through whole systems approaches. Communication and influencing.
