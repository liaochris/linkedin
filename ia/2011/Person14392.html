Evonne Brown's Summary

Since 2001 I was an award winning obesity counsellor, helping several hundred clients to lose weight and manage their new healthy weight.
In 2010 I set up AllChange in order to provide SMEs with a cost effective stress management service. This service helps protect employers' reputation and guards against costly stress related absenteeism, 'presenteeism' and employee tribunals.
