James Polanco’s Summary

I am Web Application Architect specializing in application development using the Adobe Flash Platform (Flash, Flex and AIR). I provided system architecture and code development on projects such as Scion.com, Toyota.com, Adobe kuler Desktop and other high profile Flex and Flash RIAs.

Prior to starting DevelopmentArc™ LLC, James was an Interactive Architect at DHAP Digital, Inc. where he led the development teams on Scion.com 2007 redesign and Build Your Toyota on Toyota.com and was a lead developer for sections of Lexus.com, ILM's "The Show" and other large projects.

I was also a Senior QE Team Lead on the Adobe Flex Builder team focusing on ActionScript 3.0 development harnesses and automated testing systems. James has spoken at multiple Adobe MAX conferences on topics such as ActionScript 2.0 Design Patterns, demonstrating the pre-release of Flex Builder 2, and Best Practices in Designing and Developing Flex applications. He has written multiple articles for the Adobe Developer Centers and is an avid blogger on Web Application technologies for his personal Vivisecting Media website.

As a side company I founded Fake Science LLC with Chris Walcott, Josh Kebble-Wyen, Maer Ben-Yisrael and Christopher Morin which focused on the digital music industry. I lead the architecture and development of Fake Science's PHP driven eCommerce digital media service site. I helped design and develop an online business model for selling and distributing commercially viable music via an online eCommece site.

I have also have an extensive history in audio and music production, including pre/post production. Extensive experience in developing targeted podcasts that includes; planning, recording, and editing interviews, the overall podcast editing process, creating custom recorded content and publishing and distribution management of the podcasts.
