Teresa Saxton, Executive Coach’s Summary

I'm passionate about helping people discover themselves in ways they never thought possible. Developing skills they never knew they had and fulfilling dreams that have faded away. Anything is possible...do what you love & you'll never work a day in your life.

I spent over twenty years of my career working as a Senior Finance Executive. I focus on clients who want to maximize their potential by helping define career goals, develop work/life balance and maximize their overall personal/professional purpose.

I also work with organizations that want to improve productivity, communication and morale. The McAloon Group focuses on helping businesses overcome perceived obstacles that hinder growth, as well as developing internal talent by grooming high potential employees.
