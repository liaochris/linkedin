Rich Blank’s Summary

I assist clients with technology strategy & transformation, solutions architecture, project & change management, governance and optimization. In some cases I’m focused on social media, collaboration, or knowledge management. In other cases I’m helping to surface analytics, develop taxonomies & information architecture, and optimize project, process and information management. My passion is simply changing the way people connect and manage information & knowledge through technology as I thrive on the impact it has on people and organizational culture.

Published articles on social media, collaboration, knowledge management & information technology in Greater Charlotte Biz Magazine, SocialMediaToday.com, regular contributor to CMSWire.com, and featured on AIIM.org E2.0 and SharePoint Communities. My personal blog is also featured on http://sharepoint.alltop.com I've been invited as a guest speaker at the 2008 IT & Business Alignment Conference, 2010 SharePoint Summit and SharePoint User Groups. I've also conducted formal SharePoint Administration and Governance Training classes.

You can follow me on Twitter @pmpinsights or find more insights on my blog: http://www.pmpinsights.com

Industry experience includes professional services, pharmaceutical, energy/utilities, financial services, telecomm, high-tech, media/entertainment, & government.
