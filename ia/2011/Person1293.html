Alessio Mantegna's Summary

In April 2010 I obtained my International Ph.D. in Public Relations at Palermo University (Italy) in association with Tel Aviv University and Manouba University.

My PhD research included a social research which analyzed the innovation degree of agro-industrial firms. Moreover, one of the most important research’s objectives was the design of an accessible and usable web portal on scientific communication concerning agro industrial sector.

From 2008 Feb to 2010 Jun, I worked as a researcher, UI designer, web accessibility expert and front end web developer focused on usable & accessible interfaces at LAU (Web Usability and Web Accessibility laboratory), CSI-Piemonte (Turin, Italy). During this job experience, I improved my research experience concerning Web Design, Human-Computer Interaction, web Usability, web Accessibility and User Centred Design techniques. Moreover, I improved my skills about development of usable and accessible websites with emerging standards.

In 2006 July, I got a degree in Media Studies and Marketing with Experimental and cross-disciplinary dissertation with collaboration of ENEA (Italian Agency for New Technologies), Engineering Department and Social Science Department of Palermo University. The dissertation was focused on restyling of ENEA website, observing Web Usability and Web Accessibility rules.

During my academic studies, I created a multidisciplinary study plane which included computer science subjects, Human Computer – Interaction, Web Usability, Web Accessibility, Web Design.

According to me, multidisciplinarity and imagination can contribute to creation of innovative, simple and efficient web services.

Specialties

Html/Xhtml, Css2 development, UI design, web accessibility, web usability, national and international accessibility law and guidelines (WCAG, Section 508, Legge Stanca 4/2004), web marketing, SEO, media planning.
Reasearcher concerning web usability and web accessibility projects.
Use of Screen Reader: JAWS, NVDA and VoiceOver.
