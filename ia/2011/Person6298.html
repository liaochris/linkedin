Brian DeSousa’s Summary

Process Engineer with over 19 years of design and commissioning/startup experience with various facilities, domestic and abroad. Lead Engineer in the conceptual, front end, and detailed engineering phases for various energy industry projects.

Brian DeSousa’s Specialties:

Vast experience on a wide range of onsite and offsite units including Crude/Vacuum, Dehexanizer, Hydrogen Plant, Integrated Gasification Combined Cycle (IGCC), Coal Gasification, Coke Gasification, COS Hydrolysis, Hydrotreater, Propylene-Propane Splitter, Flare Gas Compression/Recovery, Gas Turbine, Oil Movements, In-line Product Blending, Shipping Facilities, Utilities, and Offsites.

Brian DeSousa’s Experience

  • Principal Process Engineer

    Fluor

    (Public Company; FLR; Construction industry)

    1991 — Present (20 years )

    Lead Process Engineer for the gasification, slurry preparation, and solids handling areas for the front end engineering phase of a large coke gasification project. Supervised a team of ten engineers to develop the various front end engineering deliverables. Worked with the client to incorporate extensive input from prior plant operating experience into the process design.

    Lead Process Engineer for revamp of the existing utilities and offsites systems to support the addition of a new hydrocracker. Supervised a team of three in the front-end and detailed engineering phases to complete the following: detailed balances for the major utility systems; a comprehensive tankage, blending, and shipping study; design of a fuel gas compression system to supply the third-party hydrogen plant; and other modifications to existing utility and offsite systems.
