João Duarte's Summary

I am a Portuguese native, but since 2004 I have been living in Prague, in the Czech Republic. I am a graduate in Informatics, from the Faculty of Sciences of the University of Lisbon and earned a PhD in Computer Science and Engineering from the University of West Bohemia, in Pilsen.

Professionally for the past few years I have been working as a recruitment specialist with a core focus on the ICT and Finance sectors. If you are an employer looking for additional staff or a job seeker looking to take the next step in your career, please feel free to contact me.

Specialties

Recruitment, headhunting, executive search, staffing, IT / ICT, Computer Science, software development, distributed systems and simulations.
