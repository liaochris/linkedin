Eliah Gilfenbaum’s Summary

Regulatory analyst with significant business development experience and knowledge of policy-driven markets. Currently building my knowledge of the US electric transmission system, wholesale electricity markets, and the regulatory bodies that govern them.

My career goals are framed by a passion for addressing climate change from a pragmatist's perspective. I'm particularly interested in policy developments around renewable energy and electric vehicle integration. I look forward to a career where, as a non-engineer with a semi-technical background, I can supplement/complement technological innovation with
enabling innovations in business models and regulatory frameworks.
