Sports industry

January 2009 – Present (2 years 3 months)

I started up a local promotions company (UMDBeerPong.com) back in May 2008 to bring the sport of beer pong in an organized fashion into local bars and clubs. My company grew so fast that it caught the eyes of the founders of MDBEERPONG.com and a new partnership grew from that, merging into one larger company in January 2009. Our main goal is to make beer pong known as a sport and not a drinking game. We use strictly water in the cups, do not require drinking, have an established set of rules and run a statewide ranking system with league, tournament, and overall stats. Our website is the most advanced tournament and league website of its kind in the world with an advanced backend scoring system, electronic brackets, news feeds, forums, user profiles, and photo albums.
