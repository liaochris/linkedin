Joe McManus’s Summary

Joe McManus has a strong background in business development and international business.

Joe founded the Pro Squash Tour hosting its first tournament in September 2009. The tour had a very strong first year and is growing. In year two the tour will have over a dozen tour stops as well as a tv show on NESN (New England Sports Network).

Previoulsy, he built WelcomeNeighbor.com, a start-up which provides relevant, local information for your neighborhood . . . or your next neighborhood.

Prior to founding his own firm, Joe was an executive for a national not-for-profit health organization where he oversaw business development, program implementation, and media relations in the nation's 4th largest market. In addition to dramatically improving financial performance, he led the company's first successful effort to build a public, private partnership to improve a state workforce's health and fitness.

He spent most of 2006 and 2007 in China consulting with small to mid-size firms. Joe speaks, reads and writes Chinese.

Joe studied classical music performance in college and began his career as a prep school teacher at one of the country's elite secondary schools.

Additionally, he has served as a paid consultant to political candidates running for state and federal office. He has a strong understanding of message development, fundraising, and media relations.
