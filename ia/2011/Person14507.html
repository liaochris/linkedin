Fabio Viola's Summary

Fabio Viola is a young founder of the two digital entertainment companies based in Milan and Pisa. DigitalFun is focused on cross platform digital experience releasing games broader accessibility via ipTV, iPhone, Android, Facebook and mobile web. DigitalFun was named “Tech StartUp of the year” and hosted in the Ericsson Campus in Rome.
The company reached over 1.5 million users across its applications leading the digital gaming field
In his ten years carreer, he served several international gaming companies: Electronic Arts Mobile, Vivendi Games Mobile, Digital Chocolate, Namco both sales and marketing side lanching AAA titles like Pac-Man, Crash Bandicoot and Fifa Soccer.
In addition to his roles, Fabio Viola is serving in the “Digital Entertainment” MBO at IULM University of Milan.
