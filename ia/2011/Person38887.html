Sarah Lord's Summary

Sarah specialises in working with clients to design individual bespoke financial planning strategies aligned to their particular goals, her expertise covers, retirement planning, education planning, estate planning as well as advising on personal and business protection requirements.

Sarah is the highest qualified female financial planner in the Gulf Region.

Sarah was recently commended in the Best Financial Planner 2009 category at the FT High Net Worth awards in London.

Experience

• providing strategic wealth planning advice to high net worth individuals to align finances with life-time goals

• advising on personal and business protection requirements including key person insurance, shareholder/partnership protection issues

• extensive knowledge of all investment instruments and solutions

• extensive knowledge of individual wealth and financial planning tax wrapper solutions both in the UK and offshore
