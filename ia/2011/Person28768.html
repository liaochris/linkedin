Mark A Smith's Summary

I have extensive experience in principal safe retirement and family investment planning. I am highly trained in risk free, tax deferred and tax free savings strategies. I work with Business owners; pre-retirement and retired individuals to help them preserve their principle maximize their gains and reduce or eliminate their tax exposure. I construct IRA’s to maximize returns for the individual and then develop a strategy to roll the income and growth benefits to their airs and even their grandchildren. When doing this I work to keep it out of probate and make the transfer seamless and tax efficient. I also work with Estate Planners, CPAs, Financial Advisors, Attorneys, HR Companies and Reverse Mortgage Companies to help them as a value added resource to protect their client’s future.
My goal is to educate everyone I can that there is a safe way to grow your retirement nest egg without ever risking their principal.

Specialties

I am an expert in designing strategies for risk free retirement investing, life time income, college planning and capital growth with no risk of principal. Businesses are now facing more challenges than ever. I work with business owners to maximize their tax savings, build value in their benefit plans, accelerate their executive bonus plans, and minimize their risk exposure while at the same time lowering their cost basis.
