Louis Lafrado's Summary

Professional experience includes 20+ years as a biomedical researcher specializing in retroviral vaccines and retroviral-induced pathogenesis in feline and simian animal models of AIDS and HIV-1 infection.

Additional experience includes private sector function as a business owner of L&D Associates Consulting Group, Inc. The firm started as a sole proprietorship in 1999 and is now an S-Corporation with 7 employees and offices in New Mexico, New Jersey, Ohio, and Washington.

The company provides grant writing services and has been successful in attracting $10.6 million in support for its clients since 2000. We have successfully competed for $0.67 of every dollar applied.

Our ability to add value to our clients; efforts is in the recognition of the clents' 'voice" and translating that "voice" to the grant applcation. It s the clients' work that must be funded not ours.

The company also provides process and outcomes program evaluation services to its clients.

Specialties

Grant writing, grant reviews and evaluation
