Tara Orchard's Summary

Experienced Career Development Professional, Organizational Development Trainer and Social Media Strategist. Highly skilled Trainer and Facilitator with a large repertoire of Career and Organizational Training Deliverables.

My focus is on facilitating individual and organizational insights and identifying strategies to promote positive change. My strength includes my ability to see and make connections between insights and possibilities.

With over 16 years experience as a trainer and career development strategist I have worked with 1000's of clients in government, business, sports, industry and not for profit organizations, assisting both students and working professionals (including senior-Level professionals and clients of diversity and disability).

I have extensive knowledge of the effective application of career and learning theory, psychology and organizational development in program and services development and in practice with individuals.

As an experienced trainer and speaker I know how to build fun and effective training sessions which incorporate multi-media and video content.

I have demonstrated an outstanding capacity to work with clients to build professional brands, develop self-marketing strategies and leverage social media for networking and career building.
