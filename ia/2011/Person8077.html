Christopher Dessi's Summary

Chris Dessi is Managing Partner at Drive Action Digital. Dessi is often called upon as an expert social media commentator in television and print. He's a regular contributor on Fox News covering such diverse social media topics as people losing their jobs because of Facebook posts, to finding social media deals on Black Friday. He has led social media programs for the NHL, Washington Redskins, Michael Kors, TJX, Rand McNally, Signature Styles, Saks Fifth Avenue, Geico, Bealls Department Stores, Childfund International, Stila Cosmetics, Redbox and Travelzoo to name a few. His social media blog Christopherdessi.com is a trusted resource for social media enthusiasts focused on leveraging the power of social media for brands. A Twitter enthusiast, and expert in migrating brands into the social media landscape, Dessi's Drive Action Digital focuses on Social Media, Search Marketing, and Brand Strategy.
