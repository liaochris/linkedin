Amol Mehra’s Summary

Coordinator, International Corporate Accountability Roundtable at EarthRights International.

Co-Founder and Director of the Right Respect, a not-for-profit organization dedicated to fostering a dialogue on business and human rights.

Board Member of Human Rights Advocates, an international NGO.

Coordinating Member and Thematic Specialist on Business and Human Rights at Amnesty International USA.

Bar Memberships:
California, New York (pending)

Publications:
"Federalism and International Trade: The WTO Government Procurement Act and State 'Buy Local' Legislation" in the International Law and Management Review;
"Corporate Accountability over Non-State Actors" with the Nuremberg Human Rights Center;
"Bridging Accountability Gaps – The Proliferation of Private Military and Security Companies and Ensuring Accountability for Human Rights Violations" with the Pacific McGeorge Global Business and Development Law Journal.

United Nations Submissions:
