Catherine Cleare's Summary

Catherine Cleare’s residential projects reflect her passion for design, her insistence on perfection, her reverence for history, and her dedication to artistic excellence.

Catherine defines lifestyles one client at a time; she pays homage to architectural style, period design and individual preferences – at all times evoking an intimate appreciation of her client’s wishes through each and every thoughtful detail.
