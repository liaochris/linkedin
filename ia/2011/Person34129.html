Paul Dua’s Summary

Why approach marketing with a shotgun when today's web and mobile technologies enable us to use a sniper rifle?

Ok... while there is certainly more politically correct ways to communicate that, the point is still the same. Marketing initiatives that are non-branding in nature need to answer to the bottom line / return on investment (ROI). Simply running a print, TV, radio, or web campaign does not make sense without setting specific goals and monitoring the performance / ROI data behind it.

To that end, while many separate sales and marketing into two different roles, I consider myself to be an established sales and marketing executive. Within my experiences, I have successfully:

Led the development of sales teams.

Led an in-house marketing team credited for generating a seven-figure monthly return on investment which was 5 times the industry standard.

Trained salespeople, managers, executives, and national retail clients on methods of utilizing interactive platforms.

Won sales awards & recognition based on interactive sales and leadership.

Lectured and served on a panel at AD:TECH.
