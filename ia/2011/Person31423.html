Tammy Davis’s Summary

Now writing for the Taos News, Choice Health Mag and Natural Awakenings in NM.....

I have been creating custom essential oil blends promoting health and wellness since 1989. As it's true botanical essences can be used for specific reasons, I have developed a method for customizing my client's blends which takes the physical, mental & emotional wellbeing into consideration & assists him or her w/ pain resolution / management, hormonal issues, fatigue as well as end of life transitions.

In addition to the Aromatherapy, another one of my pleasures is writing; especially about healthcare. My desire is to raise the level of awareness and assist people with making effective choices. I'm an avid belieiver in combining modaliaties for optimal results; in other words, Integrative Medicine.

The outcome of 30 plus years in the industry is this consulting and advocacy program designed to assist anyone looking to improve their practice, health and wellbeing. It's a safety net for questions and concerns in order to obtain all the necessary information for consumer and doctor.

Tammy Davis’s Specialties:
