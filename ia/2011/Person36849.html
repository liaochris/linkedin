Richard Spooner's Summary

I am a geographer and town planner by training, and I have fortunate to pursue my interests in geospatial information both in academia and business.

I have 8 years experience as university lecturer and researcher in urban planning, followed by 19 years experience of researching, building and taking to market new busines propositions with GIS software vendors and with systems integrators.

Most of my business career has been in strategic marketing and business development roles based out of the European headquarters of multinational information services companies.

I have a track record of generating profitable business by working internationally with both direct sales and with partners, and I continue to relish the challenges of doing business across cultural, geographic, and business boundaries.

Specialties

GIS applications and technology; familiarity with fixed and mobile telecommunications, utilities, and government sectors; product marketing; market research and analysis; marketing communications; business strategy; consulting; partner recruitment and management; consultative selling; bid management; communication in English, German, French and Italian.
