Vincent Mann’s Summary

MANN MEDICAL SALES RECRUITERS, Inc.

Mann Medical Sales Recruiters, Inc. is an Executive Search Firm established in 1992 with disciplines exclusively in Health Care Sales. This includes the Medical, Pharmaceutical, Biotechnology, and Managed Health Care communities. We place individuals at all levels of sales and sales management. Mann Medical is committed to isolating, recruiting, developing and placing the finest sales talent available to Corporate America today.

Based in Long Island, NY, Mann Medical recruits candidates both locally & nationally, specializing in the U.S. North East, East Coast & the NY / NJ / CT Metropolitan Area.

We are committed to establishing and maintaining the finest standards of excellence in our industry. As an industry leader in Health Care Sales Recruiting, you will find our expertise to be uniquely qualified and our judgment of talent and character to be second to none.
