Marion Gamel's Summary

Result-driven and award-winning Management Executive benefiting from 15 years of multi-faced experience in marketing and media across Europe, Asia, Africa and the Middle East.
Strong experience in market research, strategic planning, customer acquisition and retention, budget management, relationship with agencies, partnership creation, team building and mentoring, product launches.

I have a data-driven and customer-focused approach that translates into strong ROI and scalable projects and I have demonstrated during my career the ability to adapt marketing excellence to any industry, environment and market.

Thanks to my entrepreneurial spirit and creativity, several of the marketing strategies and programs that I created have pushed boundaries, won awards and become global benchmarks.

I believe that marketing is a though-leadership function within a company. Its role is not only to promote externally but also to influence decisions of the Board with thoughts-provoking research findings and an in-depth understanding of all consumer segments and trends.
