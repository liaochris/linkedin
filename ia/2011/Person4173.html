Austin Lee’s Summary

Austin is a Social Media evangelist and organizational storyteller for eyespeak, LLC. He is responsible for working with clients to implement social media marketing campaigns, help clients define their social media objectives/strategies, and helps develop content for websites.

Prior to coming on board with eyespeak, Austin worked as an accountant with PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP and CapinCrouse LLP.

In his five years as an accountant he was always more well known for his creative writing and marketing ideas than his accounting acumen and found it a natural shift to become a marketing professional as his main occupation.

Austin has a BS in Business Administration with a focus in Accounting from Mars Hill College and a Masters in Accounting with additional courses in Managerial Finance and International Negotiation from North Carolina State University.
