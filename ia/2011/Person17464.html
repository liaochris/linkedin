Hillary Davis's Summary

Hillary Davis has over 15 years public relations experience as the owner and founder of a public relations company. Previous to this she worked as an investment banker in London at Barings Asset Management. She is the author of three critically acclaimed books, including A Million A Minute, proclaimed by the Financial Times in London as "an excellent book!" She is a motivational speaker, and has appeared on television and radio including Bloomberg Business News, Chronicle TV (WMUR-TV), B Sky B Television News (London), CNBC Moneywheel (European), and BBC Radio. She has been featured in over fifty international publications, including Real Business Magazine and Investor's Business Daily. She is also an active community volunteer and serves on the Board of Directors of Connecticut Women in Healthcare Management.

She is the co-author of Pain Is Not A Disease with Dr. Agostino Villani ---a book about chronic pain in America and its healthcare policy implications.

She is also the CEO of tapunited, a software company that specializes in creating applications for the iPhone.

Ms. Davis has an M.Phil from Cambridge University in England in International Relations and a BA from Columbia University in New York in Economics. She has lived over 20 years abroad, both in England and France, and is fluent in French.
