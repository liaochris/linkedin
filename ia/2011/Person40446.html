Simon Fitzgerald's Summary

I have been working in PR, in an agency environment, since 2004 and have worked across and managed a variety of campaigns, spanning B2B, corporate and B2C markets.

This includes experience working in a number of markets: Dublin, London, Sydney and Adelaide.

I am enjoy creating, managing and delivering strategic campaigns for my clients.

My experience includes consulting to brands including IBM, Lenovo, Amazon, VMware, Macquarie Telecom, Optus, MTV, Citrix, LG and Novell.

I also have a strong passion for sport and have helped brands, such as LG and Lenovo, leverage major sporting sponsorships such as F1 McLaren, The Winter and Beijing Olympics and the Irish Rugby Team.
