Chad Collier's Summary

Chad Collier has accomplished more in the retail automotive business than most could ever dream to in a lifetime. With a desire to enter into the automobile business as a teen Collier started as a lot attendant and began selling cars to pay his way through college. Collier quickly ascended to sales management and general management positions.
In 2004, when Collier was only 26 years old he achieved his first major goal when he became the youngest non-succession Ford dealer in Ford Motor Company history. Quickly Chad began to accumulate multiple dealerships in the central southern states.
In 2008, Chad was one of only eight recipients of the Automotive News 2008 Innovative Dealers of the Year Award with his development of the sales tool I’M A LOOKER. I’M A LOOKER is the future in Technology Assisted Selling in the automotive industry.
In addition to President and Founder of Collier Automotive, in which his responsibilities include running multiple dealerships, Chad is also President of Consumer Driven Solutions.C.D.S. was the first company to bring mobile selling to the auto industry. C.D.S is an emerging technology, mobile and media company that develops and markets iphone apps for the automotive industry.
Chad is also C.E.O. of Collier Consulting that provides services to various branches of the United States government and multi-national corporations and is a leader in solar consulting.
Chad is regarded as one of the thought leaders in the automotive marketing space. Chad is an accomplished international keynote speaker and panelist. Some of his speaking appearances include the Thought Leadership Summit, and the A.A.D.A. conference. Collier also has served on numerous corporate boards, community service organizations and the Indiana Auto Dealers Association Board of Directors. In his spare time (?) Chad is a pilot. He is married with two beautiful children.
