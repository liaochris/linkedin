==> Maestroangels! (www.maestroangels.be)

I had the opportunity to be closely involved in this first campaign where we Interacted with the consumer, and gave them a chance to be the ambassadors for our brand.
Risky business you would say. but we managed to keep some level of control. and got amazing results with a very small budget.

The campaign: be part of an unforgettable road trip, and help Belgians on their trip to the south

Phase 1: was the recruitment of teams that would be helping Belgians on their trip to the south (autoroute du soleil). 58 teams participated and we interviewed the top 10 and picked the 4 best teams.

Phase 2: the road trip, here the MaestroAngels were given daily missions they had execute whilst being followed by a camera. and on these videos the public could vote. 10 days on the road.

result: more than 120 000 votes in 10 days with 58000 votes for the top team.

I am honoured to have been co-leading this campaign.

Djeeni Audio Visual Productions

Marketing and Advertising industry

2009 – January 2010 (1 year)

For Djeeni we had to create a strategy, which had to fit "Video meets online" & "Video meets 360°". This Marketing approach was needed to cultivate the through potential of this great company.

Starting with an extensive study in video meets online (trend, websites of 500 top companies, technology,...) showed us the potential was even bigger than we ever expected.

With this research we build the new site and worked on a go to market plan/relaunch.

At the end of the project it resulted in good contacts with companies where we did not go to sell, but explain the implementation of video in the 360° approach.

I would like to thank Maaike, Antoon & Tom for all the hard work and look forward the following this project further. It was a true pleasure to work with them.
