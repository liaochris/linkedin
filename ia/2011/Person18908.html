James Stayton’s Summary

I am a 3rd generation entrepreneur. I believe that the hands on approach is the best way to build any business model. Having been involved in many verticals including the Semiconductor and Automotive retail industry I have learned to be competitive, creative and cost effective in my solutions. Having been fortunate enough to do consulting with firms from Japan, Taiwan, Singapore and Mexico I have found the value of multiple approaches to business that each culture brings. There is no single way to run a business, a company’s success or failure can be as much about the owner’s personality as it is about viability of his industry.

I have found that in many way’s SEO / SEM has become a buzz word. While the idea of search engine placement has become extremely important in obtaining a sales edge, it is more dimensional than site optimization through keywords and PPC budgets. Today’s business needs a strong placement as well as top reviews (we have created a proprietary tool that dramatically solves this issue), blog posting and back links to build cross traffic. I greatly believe in the use of landing pages for different aspects of the business. I believe that it can help obtain the best possible results. Many of my clients have found my work in mobile campaigns to be particularly profitable given its open opportunity for growth. The video search space has become particularly interesting as it grows and forms its own industry techniques. For me it is going way beyond a simple you tube update.
