Kristen Hess's Summary

Kristen is a well rounded advertising and design professional with 20 years of work experience as a Graphic Designer/Art Director, Creative Producer, Account/Project Manager, and most recently has gained experience in Food Writing, Food Styling and Photography. Her industry experience spans across Food/Beverage/Hospitality, Retail, Financial Services, CPG, Health and Beauty, Telecommunications, Technology, Real Estate, Professional Services, Entertainment, and Government. She holds a Master of Arts in Advertising and a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Graphic Design. Kristen also has extensive culinary and food media training from the Institute of Culinary Education, Gotham Writer’s Workshop and The New York Times, and the International Center of Photography in New York City. Kristen has international work experience in Melbourne and Sydney, Australia while studying her Masters Degree in 2004-2005.

Specialties

advertising, branding, marketing communications, design, interactive media, integrated communications, food writing, food styling, food photography, blogging/vlogging
