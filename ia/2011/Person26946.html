LeeAnn Reed's Summary

LReed Consulting is a premier technology consulting firm specializing in QuickBooks company setup, training, and general technology support for creative entrepreneurs. LeeAnn Reed, principal and founder of LReed Consulting, is a nationally recognized speaker and author, with an extensive background in business management, accounting software, and interior design.

LeeAnn understands your issues and she is here to assist you.
Having been a small business owner herself, she understands the day-to-day challenges you are facing. LeeAnn knows that you would rather work with your clients than update your QuickBooks files. LeeAnn has taught hundreds of design professionals how to quickly improve their profit margin by harnessing the power of QuickBooks reporting.

Visit my website at www.LReedConsulting.com for your complimentary 30 minute remote consultation.

Both Onsite (within 50 mile radius of the greater Phila area) and Remote (via GotoMeeting) QuickBooks consulting is available, by the hour or via annual Support Packages. See my website (www.LReedConsulting.com) or call me at 610-316-8553 for more details.
