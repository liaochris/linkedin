Solomon Rothman's Summary

Solomon Rothman is the CEO and founder of MoviePals Entertainment, a technologically innovative media company with diverse brands that include an online collaborative filmmaking studio, an idea social network, and a cutting edge web development shop.

Business Achievements:

• Developed and executed a revenue plan for MoviePals Entertainment which enables the creative team to sustain its filmmaking ventures by providing profitable commercial services in video production, online advertising, and web development.

• Pioneered a revenue sharing system to provide and organize artist contributions for collaborative projects.

• After building over a hundred websites, Solomon wrote “The Rothman Guide to Building Successful Websites” to help people understand more about the complexity of creating an evolving web presence for fun or profit.

Filmmaking Achievements:

• Currently leading the production of the crowd-driven open source series Mob Mentality

• Authored the Open Cinema Manifesto and launched a grass roots movement to enable audience participation and international collaboration in film making.

• Created web commercials for companies ranging in size from cottage industries to larger corporations.

• Wrote and directed “Jathia’s Wager” in 2009 a collaborative open source sci-fi film that was creation with an international collaboration with artists online.

• Wrote, directed and starred in “Boy Who Never Slept”, a full length open source movie released in 2006, which after its release online went on to garnish millions of views and international attention from both the mainstream and independent media.
