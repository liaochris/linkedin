Andrew Battisti’s Summary

THE ISSUE

Business-critical projects finishing behind schedule, running over budget or failing altogether is a major challenge impeding companies from being strong and viable in today’s economy.

THE SOLUTION

The optimal way to overcome this risk is to utilize knowledgeable, innovative and customer-focused project managers who are not only well-experienced in the day-to-day oversight of the project schedule, budget and resources, but also are business-focused and mindful of how projects fit into overall company strategy.

HOW I CAN HELP

I am a senior-level PMP-certified project manager who consistently provides high-quality, in-scope project deliverables on time and within budget. My specialty is IT projects, including server consolidations, application development, data warehousing and system conversions. I also have extensive experience in leading direct marketing projects to support a variety of retail and service-based companies, which has helped me to focus on key business drivers for projects.

WHY ME?

My project management philosophy is: “90% of project success is related to communication.” For every project I lead, the majority of my time is spent creating, collecting, organizing, reporting, disseminating and archiving project information using a plan that lays out the specific frequency, delivery channel and level of detail for each stakeholder's communications. Moreover, I always strive to maintain a positive attitude and high ethical standards when working with project resources to develop and keep a strong level of trust within the team, which goes hand-in-hand with effective and productive communications.

NEXT STEPS:

I am currently searching for a challenging senior-level project management position that would be a mutually good fit for both my skills and experience and a company’s needs and requirements. I would welcome any leads or potential opportunities and look forward to communicating with you!
