Maik Beekmans’s Summary

Throughout my career I have been involved (in one way or another) with HRMI systems and improving the performance of HR departments. At this stage I dare to say that I truely understand the manner in which the design and implementation of HRMI systems affects the performance and maturity of HR professionals and departments.

I am driven by the notion that everyone should be able to contribute to the succes of the company. If the systems and processes used don't support this notion, you should change the systems and processes rather than the people involved. I have seen this happen all but too often.

Maik Beekmans’s Specialties:

Selection, design and implementation of HR processes and systems, union negotiation, compensation and benefits, project management, general management, HR management, organisational development, change management, sociotechnical redesign and restructuring of businesses, business mentoring and management coaching
