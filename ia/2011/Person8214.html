Chris Murray's Summary

I am presently Managing Director of epiGenesys, an innovative social enterprise and a wholly owned subsidiary of The University of Sheffield. epiGenesys combines excellence in bespoke software development, specialising in the delivery of information systems for the medical and health research sectors, with a passion for enterprise education to enhance the learning experience of more than 250 students each year.

My role is divided between business and educational activities. Business activities primarily involve collaborating with public sector organisations including the National Health Service, the Medical Research Council and the Medical Schools Council Assessment Alliance, and operating a successful partnership with the Clinical Trials Research Unit at The University of Sheffield. Educational activities include facilitating training and work experience within the curriculum for students who are studying degrees in computer science, enterprise computing and software engineering, mainly by operating Genesys Solutions, the student-run division of epiGenesys.

I am also a Trustee and Treasurer of The GIST Foundation, a not-for-profit charitable company whose goal is to be a sustainable resource that encourages and supports community-driven grassroots innovation. The Foundation provides the long-term structure necessary to build on the hugely successful activities in Sheffield and the surrounding region, offering the grassroots creative and technology community a cohesive programme of events, a network of special interest groups, and providing open access to both physical and virtual community collaboration environments.

Specialties

Agile software development, automated testing, behaviour driven development, enterprise education, extreme programming, grassroots innovation, learning experience, medical and health research sectors, social enterprise, test driven development, web development, ASP.NET, C#, Ruby.
