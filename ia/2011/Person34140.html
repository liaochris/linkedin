PAULETTE CARROLL's Summary

As a full-time, full-service real estate agent I am passionate about helping people at all levels make positive changes in their lives and believe that both home buying and/or selling should be an enjoyable, exciting and worry free experience. Whether you are in the market for a new home and/or seeking a buyer for our current home - I am ready to assist you in the process!

I am dedicated to providing care, answers, vision and peace of mind to motivated sellers and buyers seeking to make a change in their lives and who thrive on a collaborative relationship. I value family, community, kindness, integrity, humor, and promise to carefully listen to your objectives, work diligently to achieve your goals and never lose sight of your dreams!

Buying a home means more than just putting a roof over your head. I believe it offers a lifestyle for you and those you love. It may signal a new beginning, a marriage, a move up, a practical downsize, a job shift, an educational need, an expanding family, or an empty nest. Whatever your motivation for buying or selling your home, rest assured that you can hire me with confidence and trust!

With commitment and passion about changing the lives of people living in extreme poverty I have partnered with Jeff Scherber and his team at Prime Mortgage/The Scherber Elite. By putting our heads, hearts and hands together we are making a difference not only in the lives of our clients, but also in the lives to those who need our help the most! With a portion of our income - from each and every sale, and on behalf of our clients, we are building homes in Haiti to improve the lives of those living in the poorest country in the western hemisphere.

I continually strive to make a difference in the lives of others - both here in Minnesota and in Haiti by ~ Moving Hearts and Homes ~ One Home at a Time!

For more information on how you can help log on to paulettecarroll.com, movingheartsandhomes.com, healinghaiti.org or printforchange.com.

Specialties

Buyers and sellers agent, first-time and move-up buyers, investors, staging.

Interests:

My passions run deep! God first, family second, friends third and last but certainly not least business! I love creating lasting and meaningful relationships both personally and professionally! Making family memories is of utmost importantance - time at the cabin, bike riding, walking, puzzle making and playing games. With commitment and passion I strive to enhance lives both in Minnesota and in Haiti. They are the same faith-filled, loving people that I had the opportunity to meet in January - just two days before the earthquake. My life mission statement is to live my life for a greater purpose than my own!!! Between family, clients and Haiti I intend to do just that!!!
