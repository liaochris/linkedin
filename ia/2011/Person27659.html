As Global Marketing Manager I look after all the aspects of the advertising section for BidRivals LTD worldwide.

My duties don’t only include finding new deals, but also to make sure all tracking is working, the creative material is ready and the ROI of my campaigns is over 200% on a 60 days break-even limit, target that I set to myself as an extra challenge.

One of my main objectives is to bring this company to be the top of the market, out-shining the competition and bringing new ideas to this field.

The way I see it, if you don't work to be the best, you don't understand what marketing is. And that's the truth.
