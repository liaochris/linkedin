David Chow’s Summary

After serving for 12 years as a full-time pastor, I made the transition to coaching and consulting over 6 years ago working primarily with non-profits organizations. My mission is to be a catalyst for leaders transforming organizations through authenticity, emotional intelligence, and organizational excellence. This happens through helping individuals and groups increase their self & other awareness, leverage their strengths, and maximize their service to others.
