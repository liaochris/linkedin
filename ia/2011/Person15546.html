Gary Parker's Summary

I offer a full range of consulting and training services for all types of transportation companies from legacy and low-fare to niche carriers. I have over 26 years in airline passenger marketing and revenue management, with over 10 years teaching and consulting at airlines around the world. I bring practical experience and well researched expertise, allowing me to introduce your company to the best practices that drive improved results and profitability.

I can perform pricing and revenue management (RM) business audits and then recommend processes and practices as well as deliver practical commercial training at companies of all sizes around the world.

Contact me at: gparker@rmexpertise.com

Specialties

My expertise covers all the areas associated with airline passenger marketing, including product management, revenue management, pricing, scheduling, network planning and product distribution. In particular I focus on business processes and the unique requirements of my customers, so that I can assist them in implementing new business models and systems.
