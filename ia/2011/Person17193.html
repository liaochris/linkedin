Heather Frey's Summary

Owner and President of an innovative business concept matching Personal Trainers to their ideal clients and clients to their ideal trainer. Not only do potential clients get to match to their ideal trainer, but the Trainers, Coaches, Instructors, and Fitness Professionals *also* match to their ideal clients... a match both ways.

Using my experience competing in Figure Competitions, along with 15 years in the graphic design and marketing field has allowed me to use my knowledge of health and fitness to create what people need...the *right* information and motivation. And not just any information, but the information they need specifically for them.

The goal is to make SmashFit.com the go-to spot to find a fitness professional to help whoever needs it and to fill in the site with every specialty and every special need.

I am also the fitness specialist for NBC6 Miami's new show, Mom's of Miami, covering all topics that relating to Mom's.
