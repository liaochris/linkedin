Mark Evans's Summary

I am an accomplished and progressive executive focused in the areas of social media, online media and entertainment sales, business development, ecommerce and marketing as well as payments.

I am current Vice President of Sales for Fan Appz. Fan Appz is a social media platform designed to help brands and business engage, grow and monitize their fans base on Facebook and other social media services.

My background includes highly respected leading edge solution providers and companies whose vision has helped to transform markets. I have had a Sales focus with such leading firms as Advertising.com/ AOL and Revolution Money, a new credit card backed by Steve Case of AOL. My duties with media and entertainment firms like CBS SportsLine.com and ICM (International Creative Management) include business development, marketing and operational experience. I am a fiercely loyal employee in a very turbulent industry and am proud of my long tenures with each firm.

I excel at strategic sales and relationship building with C-level executives. I have presented these executives with leading solutions including search engine marketing optimization, display media (both for branding and performance), direct marketing solutions (including email and postal data programs), contests and sweepstakes and most recently payments as a marketing solution (including promotions and loyalty).

I value my relationships and network extensively with many groups and associations. I am a past President of the South Florida Interactive Marketing Association (SFIMA) and am proud of my tenure and what this group has been able to accomplish since its creation. This the premier networking group for online marketers and sales executives in South Florida and throughout the Southeast and provides a leading resource for educating executives about new and leading online solutions and their experiences in the industry.
