Peter Kowalski's Summary

Specialist in communications research with an emphasis on the tactical and evaluative use of data, especially demonstrating the value of public relations in the achievement of organizational goals and objectives. Currently focused on alignment of data-driven news with traditional and new media routines.

Past work includes metric development for social media analysis, the application of social network analysis to public relations research, the documentation of intermedia influence between traditional and social media, collection methods to protect ecological validity in the measurement of online news media and the integration of organizational performance indicators into public relations measurement programs.

Theoretical competencies in agenda setting, organization-public relationships and corporate reputation.

Specialties

Public relations research, data-driven news, global consumer sentiment measurement, content analysis as a methodology, effective visual and written presentations of data, Russian media analysis, computer-aided text analysis, non-profit, higher education, consumer technology, business to business.
