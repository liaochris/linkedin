Internet industry

October 2002 – March 2005 (2 years 6 months)

jbcar.ca is a company with a mission and a vision: affordable vehicle advertising for fellow Canadians. With rich experience in marketing, technology, and automotive sales online and off, jbcar.ca believes that all Canadian buyers and sellers can save and benefit thanks to the company's expertise in harnessing the capabilities of the Internet to sell cars.

Compared to newspaper advertising, where the average cost for a 3-line/3-day ad is $60, jbcar.ca is a much more affordable option…and more effective, too, thanks to its targeted and unique "location search." One of the first sites of its kind to offer this option in North America, jbcar.ca has a unique application that allows visitors to plug in a city and receive seller's results within a 50KM radius of their location.

Additionally, unlike the lag time that occurs between buying and running a print ad in a newspaper or "penny saver," jbcar.ca advertisements allow sellers to offer their cars…and make a sale online (through PayPal). Another mark of jbcar.ca's superiority over print ads is its highly desirable "AUTO Alert!" function that informs potential buyers when the car of their dreams becomes available.

Subscribers on jbcar.ca enjoy a variety of benefits, including the ability to update inventory instantly and a one-click option that displays their latest inventory to consumers. They also receive live and email support, as well as guidance in setting up an effective, irresistible sales page.

John Lui, an experienced professional with a strong background in sales/marketing, IT technology, and car sales, founded jbcar.ca in 2002. The company's mission is to provide a comprehensive database of vehicles, with advanced search capabilities and user benefits aimed exclusively at Canadian automotive buyers and sellers. The site even offers a choice of English and Chinese interfaces to ensure that jbcar.ca is user-friendly for a broad spectrum of users -- another "first-and-only" jbcar.ca has achieved.
