Integrated with IT and Editorial departments for new product development or re-engineering of existing products.

Interested in Social Networking as a tool for business building. Available for project work: Internet research, HTML, Blog contributer, article writing, or anything you can think of that won't get me in trouble. :-D

I have a real interest in helping people grow and excel. I will always be intensely interested in people/project management, and examining why this so often fails and how it can succeed.

My sites are totally different from one another in subject matter, but they are all based on keeping a sense of integrity and ethics while monetizing an internet-based business.

Favorite business book: The Speed of Trust by Stephen M.R. Covey.
