Danilo Venturi’s Experience

  • Marketing Manager

    La Perla

    (Privately Held; Apparel & Fashion industry)

    January 2002 — June 2008 (6 years 6 months)

    In charge of different stimulating tasks.

    First, the management of the “five senses project” aiming to develop an emotional bound with the clients, including the special merchandising for pampering, the Private Show cd series, the exclusive events in Milan, Paris, Geneva, Athens, New York and Hong Kong, and the co-branding actions (among the others, with Fashion TV, Bang & Olufsen, Radio Monte Carlo).

    Second, the involvement in the image building process for worldwide advertising at the Communication Department.

    Finally, the management position in the Web Department, including image & communication, web media planning, and business management responsibilities.

  • Communication Manager

    Jato Spa

    (Luxury Goods & Jewelry industry)

    January 2001 — December 2004 (4 years )

    First and seminal work experience was at Jato Group, a multidisciplinary enterprise, including embroidery production for Haute Couture and Pret-a-Porter, two concept stores, and a creative pole called FashionFM, one of the first in the world to organically match music, underground cultures, publishing and fashion.

    During the communication management of this Group he was involved in many special events as "Fashion & Art: John Galliano Collection", "Michel Haillard Collection" and "Kibosh by Terry Richardson". The first catwalk experiences were for Marchese Coccapani, Max Mara, McKenzie, Blumarine and La Perla.
