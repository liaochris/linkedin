Robert Woodgate's Summary

Robert Woodgate welcomes you to Familclub, the professional network on LinkedIn for business event industry professionals.

The Familclub now in its 9th year continues to grow with over 39,000 double opt confirmed subscribers because it fills a niche as the communication between meeting and event venues and services and their customers the meeting and event planners.

Familclub is a professional website for the business events industry, where you'll find networking opportunities to meet thousands of industry professionals, buyers, planners, organisers, bookers and venue referrers.

We distribute Invitations to view speakers, entertainers, venues, destinations, sample food and beverages plus be treated like a VIP.

For the buyer Familclub arrange and promote showcase events, marketing promotions and familiarization tours attracting qualified meeting and event planners both in Australia and offshore destinations.

We offer a free subscription to the Invitations magazine or a paid membership for $99 per year to receive public and private invitations to complimentary networking events, functions and FAMIL iarisation tours.

Advertisers i.e. Meeting and function venues, accommodation, speakers, entertainers, services and suppliers can list their business details with an online enquiry button on the website for $99 per year in the Market Place Business Events Directory to attract 12 months of sales leads, enquiries and attendees to events and site inspections.

They can advertise or supply editorial in the Invitations Digital Magazine special conference a function offers, product launches trade events and invite buyers to their showcase events.

We also distribute business event invitations to our subscribers by direct email blast at competitive rates.
