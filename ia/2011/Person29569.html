Résumé de Meg Handlin

Recognized for her resourcefulness and strategic execution, Meg has worked with home furnishing and building material manufacturers, healthcare practitioners, and start-up businesses to generate sales leads, promote new products, and strengthen brand identity.

Meg has over fifteen years of public relations, marketing and event management experience. She got her start coordinating and promoting successful fundraising events and educational workshops for a non-profit organization. Then honed her skills at Diversified Business Communications where she directed marketing for three international trade shows and three industry publications.

Compétences de Meg Handlin

marketing direction, public relations, market research, copywriting, media relations, social networking, search engine optimization, and project management.
