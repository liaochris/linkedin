Bryan Tobiason’s Summary

I am a Realtor who comes from a customer service background, so my style is a little different and frankly welcomed by my clients. You are able to reach me on the phone, by email, text message, Facebook or any other means that is convenient for you. Almost always I'm able to reply within 30 minutes if not immediately. This accessibility is just one of many things that separate me from other agents out there.

I spend at least 30 minutes per day reading real estate news and keeping up to date on new laws/regulations, coming changes, tactics and other items that may effect my clients. While I'm required by the state to take continuing education, I have more than tripled my required hours for continuing education because knowing more allows me to perform at a higher level for my clients.

Integrity Driving Results is more than a just a slogan for me. This is the way I live my life. I always operate with a high level of integrity. This has been to my detriment from time to time, but I've realized being able to look back on life with no regrets is better than sacrificing my integrity for short term gain. So when you work with me you'll see my integrity coming through front and center, I'm honest to a fault and make no apologies.

My low average days on the market put me in top 2.5% of all agents in Kansas City and show my marketing approach works quite well. Also in a survey conducted of all my past clients each one was completely satisfied with me as their Realtor. Many have also referred friends and family which is the greatest compliment I can receive.

I look forward to continuing my success in the Real Estate industry, gaining more raving fans and life long clients. If you have any questions relating to real estate at all, I would certainly appreciate the opportunity to speak with you. I'm not the pushy type and would enjoy being your resource for Kansas City Real Estate.

  • Realtor

    City Wide Realty Corp.

    (Real Estate industry)

    April 2008 — August 2009 (1 year 5 months)

    In my one year with City Wide Realty Corporation I learned a great deal about the world of real estate. I found that this industry fits my personality very well. I work extensively with Bank Owned homes and greatly enjoy helping people find the perfect home.

    My customer service skills pay dividends in this industry. All my clients compliment me on my communication and timely responses to questions or other needs.

    I have found that I am somewhat of an odd Realtor because I actually enjoy looking at homes. Talking to some others in this business I have found this to be a rarity. Maybe after some more time that will fade, but for now I enjoy looking at homes with my clients.

    I scrutinize homes that may be a fit for my clients to make sure that any home we go see will meet their basic requirements so the client can save time and only look at homes that fit what they want.

    I sold my last listing in 13 days, I know how to price a home correctly so that buyers are attracted to the home that is priced right and staged well. I throw all my marketing muscle into every listing to ensure that the buying public finds your home. I am a Trulia Pro which allows me to feature your listing and get more exposure on the fasting growing Real Estate Search site out right now.

    I am also a Realty Trac preferred Realtor which allows me access to one of the best resources for foreclosures and pre-foreclosed homes. Working with bank homes takes experience, and I have that. I understand that most banks have their own contracts that are necessary before an offer can be accepted. Missing this step can allow a property you are interested in to slip away from you. This is the worst thing that happens when working with an inexperienced agent.

    If there is anything I can do to help you in the Real Estate World it would be my pleasure. I have a very flexible schedule and truly enjoy helping people in this complex and sometimes frustrating industry.
