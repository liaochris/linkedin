Jose Freyre, MBA, AIF®, CFP®’s Summary

I lead a wealth planning and investment management firm specializing in the needs of entrepreneurs and affluent individuals/families.

We act as our client's "Personal CFO" and work closely with our client's other advisors (cpa, attorneys, etc...) on complex financial, business, estate and investment needs.

A key area of expertise is working with owners of privately held companies to help plan and execute the transfer/exit of their business.

Jose Freyre, MBA, AIF®, CFP®’s Specialties:
