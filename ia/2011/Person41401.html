Stephen Murphy’s Summary

Digital marketing and media strategist now SVP at IQ Solutions a health communications and health IT company in Rockville MD. Oversee interactive marketing, data analytics and communications groups. Marketing and digital lead for SAMHSA information services contract.

Founder and owner of The Media Network employing twenty professionals with billings of almost $5 million. Achieved annual average growth of 30% each year. Multi sector experience in government, corporate and non-profit arenas in paid and unpaid media, advertising, market research, social media, and multicultural outreach. Expert in applying technology solutions to meet productivity needs and drive new communications solutions for clients. Have recruited, hired, trained, and evaluated over two dozen agency staff from designers, copywriters, traffic managers, research staff and account managers. Have contracted dozens of vendors from producers, writers, studios, designers, media buyers, media distribution vendors, programmers, printers and web developers.

Experience with national social marketing campaigns in healthcare, the environment and traffic safety for CDC, EPA, DHHS, NIH, NHTSA involving advertising, media relations, as well as message development, message testing and evaluation. Presently responsible for business development. Over a 10 year period, we have now grown the firm into one that has in-house capabilities in research, design and production, multicultural outreach, media relations and advertising.
