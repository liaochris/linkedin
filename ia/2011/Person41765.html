Steve Patterson's Summary

Served as a successful executive in the NFL, NBA and professional hockey and baseball for a quarter century. I've built championship teams and designed, financed, built and run major league stadiums and arenas.

Specialties

I have a broad range of sports related experience including major and minor league team business and NBA basketball operations,stadium, ballpark and arena design, finance, construction and operation. As an attorney, I've conducted extensive and complex negotiations for media, facility and and player contracts. I've overseen all legal, branding, marketing and sales operations for teams, facilities, radio stations, a food service company, political relations, startups and turnarounds.
