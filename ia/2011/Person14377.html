Evgeny Voutchkov's Summary

Master Planner/Urban Designer/Project Manager with over 15 years of professional experience in urban design, urban planning and project management from both public and private sectors - of them 8 years in Canada and 3 years in the GCC. LEED Accredited Professional. Certified Project Management Professional (PMP). Member of the Canadian Institute of Planners (MCIP) and Register Professional Planner (RPP) in the Province of Ontario. Evgeny has over 20 completed masterplanning projects internationally in the past 10 years. Canadian nationality.

Evgeny is specializing in the conception, design delivery and supervision of large-scale master-planned communities, including Feasibility Studies, Concept and Detailed Master Plans, Design Guidelines and Development Control Regulations. He has also experience with coordinating design disciplines complementing the master plan, such as Landcape and Public Real, Environment & Sustainability, Safety and Security, Community Facilities, Traffic Impact Studies, etc.

Specialties

Large-scale master planning, urban planning, urban design, project management, development management, design guidelines, development control regulations.
