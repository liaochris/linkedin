Jesse de Agustin’s Summary

I exceed expectations while delivering progressive suggestions and improvements to a diverse set of organizations. At a young age, I’ve gained exposure in positions requiring high levels of responsibility, independence, and collaboration.

There is a strong parallel between studying philosophy and understanding the importance of crafting and executing impeccable brand experiences. I coined the term “Brand Metaphysics” after recognizing a philosophically trained mind is apt for holistic thought by asking broad and targeted questions - both essential for advancing any organization forward.

These interests coincide in my senior philosophy major thesis where I apply various brand identity conditions to inquire, "what makes a brand the same brand over time?" Or phrased colloquially, "what constitutes a "re-branding" effort?"

By drawing upon abstract philosophical work in metaphysics, I position my thesis for a practical and potent application to the branding literature for a truly interdisciplinary analysis.

Through my blog articles, I intertwine branches of philosophy with topics in branding. I invite you to delve into the arguments and comment.

My live Twitter feed includes thought provoking articles, blog updates, and interactions with other inspiring individuals on the twittersphere.
