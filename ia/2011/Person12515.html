Diana Plasberg’s Summary

As an experienced graphic designer, I have created logos and handled both print and electronic design and production. Because I’ve worked with so many clients in government, advertising, education and healthcare, I am comfortable with tight budgets and deadlines. And because I believe that effective design must yield tangible results, my work has been recognized by organizations such as the International Business Communicators and the Public Relations Society of America. From brochures and displays to web graphics and illustration, I always aim for the design solution that inspires a call to action.

Diana Plasberg’s Specialties:

Print and web design. Art and creative direction. Account and project management.
