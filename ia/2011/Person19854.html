Mr. Marketology | Jeff Beale’s Summary

Jeff Beale is passionate about Internet marketing and the different techniques involved in bringing customers and businesses together through interactive and informative means. By first focusing on the needs of the client, Jeff puts his MBA from AIU as well as his 12 years of marketing experience to work.

Jeff began his career as a web designer for the United States Air Force providing them with a registration portal for their annual symposium thus saving them over $35K in the first year alone. He then migrated to the corporate sector working for a small digital agency providing web design, search engine optimization and rich media marketing for them and their clients.

With ability for strategic planning and capacity to generate results, he developed great part of his career in the digital marketing space earning him a position as a SEO Manager with 360i, an award-winning digital agency that helps Fortune 500 marketers reach their target audiences. This providing him the opportunity to work with major retailers such as Saks, The Children’s Place and Audible as well as major firms in the financial space such as E*TRADE and TrustedID. From there he provided marketing consultation on the client side guiding in the areas of social search optimization, SEO, link building and social media marketing for Fortune companies such as The Home Depot, Mint, and FleetCor.

Jeff Beale, known as "Mr. Marketology", is author of "The Marketology Series: A Practical Guide To Marketing In The Social Search Space"
