Stiles Bennet’s Summary

A seasoned executive and marketing professional with 15 years of consumer products marketing, business development and management experience. Recent experience includes 4 years in high-growth direct marketing businesses and subscription-based ecommerce start-up ventures, and 4 years in the direct to consumer luxury travel services industry. Led acquisition and integration of a leading social network for travel. Currently a senior executive at a luxury villa rental and travel services company with operations in St Barts, the Caribbean, Europe, Hawaii and Mexico.

A General Manager with specific expertise in building online-driven B2C businesses. Track record of successfully developing business models for new products and services, building and managing teams, lowering the cost of acquiring new clients, cost effectively driving revenue growth, and tightly managing costs. Experience in presenting to corporate boards and investors, and with acquisitions. Earned MBA from Harvard in 1994.

Stiles Bennet’s Specialties:

Consumer products marketing, P/L modeling and management, search engine marketing, search engine optimization, social network marketing, direct marketing, development and optimization of direct marketing metrics, online subscriber and customer acquisition marketing and strategy, online lead generation, food & wine marketing, luxury travel and leisure marketing.
