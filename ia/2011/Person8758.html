Cyril Lapostolle’s Experience

  • President of EPSA

    Ecurie Piston Sport Auto (EPSA)

    (Automotive industry)

    September 2009 — September 2010 (1 year 1 month)

    Ecurie Piston Sport Auto is a french non-profit organization founded by some students from Centrale Lyon in 2002.
    The organization's main goal is the design and the construction of an environment-friendly but competitive véhicle each year. This achievement is only possible through the existence of strong partnership (educative & industrial).

    As the President of the EPSA, I managed a 50 persons organization with a 130 k€ budget, having a weekly meeting, entrusting responsabilities to fellows and receiving reports concerning the progress and the troubles encountered.

    Moreover, I was especially in charge of the consolidation of existing partnership, the seeking of new ones. I teamed up with four persons, all of them working under my orders. Gain coming from this work were :
    - the most important budget ever collected
    - the multiplication by ten of the enterprise's data base.
    - the elaboration of process' sheet
    - the creation of a new partnership with Renault Trucks
