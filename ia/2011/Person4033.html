Astrid Wehling’s Summary

With 20+ years in design and advertising, I now run a well-established design business and enjoy working with my national and international clientele.
I focus on high-quality corporate and print design, with a special emphasis on conceptual, strategic graphic design. I draw my clientele from a broad range of industries, including automotive firms, financial services, biotechnology companies, lawyers, tourism and publishing organizations. From logo creation to a complete identity makeover or advertising campaign, my hands-on approach ensures that I am able to deliver design elements reflecting a business’s immediate needs and longterm goals.
