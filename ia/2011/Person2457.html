Andrea Vollf, LEED AP ID+C’s Summary

Graduated from Escola Superior de Propaganda & Marketing, one of the top schools for Advertising and Marketing in South America, Andrea Vollf became a seasoned creative director with over 10 years of experience in the advertising and marketing industry. Very knowledgeable of capabilities of different platforms and browsers, and their design constraints on the Web, Andrea has created and developed concepts for magazines, newspapers, brochures and direct mail. Previous ad agency experience allows her to work under very tight deadlines. Andrea is also very experienced managing others, creating budgets, and developing strategic planning.

A second degree in Interior Design along with over 8 years in the interior design & decorating industry makes her a very unique professioanl. She is a LEED Accredited Professional specialized in Interior Design and Construction, with in-depth knowledge of sustainable design for both commercial and residential spaces.

Andrea has succesfully passsed the NCIDQ exam and she is a registered interior designer in Illinois and Nevada. Her educational background in interior design includes Parsons School of Design in NYC and the Illinois Institute of Art-Schaumburg.

Fluent in English and Portuguese, Ms. Volff has presented a seminar on "Advertising and Marketing for Interior Designers" at the 2008 ASID IL Design Summit. She has been quoted several times on articles across US regarding Color Trends for 2009 and 2010.

Andrea is currently working on books and continuing education classes for the design industry.
