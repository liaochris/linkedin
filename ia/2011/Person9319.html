Vincent Rosalia's Summary

CourtReporterDepot.com is a full service and technologically advanced court reporting and document management company. We are dedicated to the accuracy and timely delivery of deposition transcripts as well as the production of miscellaneous case management documents.

CourtReporterDepot.com is a member of the The National Court Reporting Association as well as many other national and regional associations including the NFPA, NALA and IPMA. It is our goal to not only provide our clients with top quality cost effective services, but also to simplify the way they arrange for the many other services they will require during the litigation process. Whether you require court reporting services, copy/scanning services, videoconferencing services or any of our other services we offer, we are certain you will find our competitive pricing, unique service offerings and personal attention a "welcomed addition" to your selection of resources. We hope to work with you on your next case!
