Christine Young's Summary

Christine is mommy to six children, all of whom are 8 and under.... Five of them are boys, and the oldest two are twins. When she's not teaching lessons, changing diapers, wiping noses, or sweeping up cheerios and raisins, Christine is looking for ways to make family life FUN. She keeps her eyes peeled for ways to save on gas and groceries, and trying to find ways to make cleaning up after six kiddos easier (even just a bit) has become just short of an obsession.

Christine has been given the honor of being named as one of Nielson's Top 50 Power Moms, is a Lifetime Mom, is one of Walmart's ElevenMoms, and loves to create buzz around products and services that are helpful for large families.
