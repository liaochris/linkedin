Lauren Lazzara's Summary

Marquette University graduate with a Bachelor of Arts degree in public relations and minor in marketing. Throughout my years at Marquette I was actively involved in Marquette Student Government, Special Events Committee and PRSSA.

My professional interests include emerging media, writing, event planning and video editing. I have been able to be a part of a diverse sample of projects including and not limited to theater, restaurants, motorcycles, and kitchen cabinets.

Currently, I work in the PR department at Hoffman York, a fully integrated marketing communications firm. Here, my responsibilities include conducting research, drafting press releases, media list development, monitoring media coverage and assisting in the coordination of various strategies and tactics.

I am also a member of Milwaukee Special Olympics Young Professionals Board, where I help to plan and implement various events in order to raise awareness and fund Milwaukee Special Olympics initiatives.

I am a firm believer of loving what you do and doing what you love. Personally, that is public relations!

Specialties

I have an intense passion for social media and I enjoy keeping up with current trends.
