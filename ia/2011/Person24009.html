Keith Vera’s Summary

Keith is a digital marketing executive who has designed and optimized successful online marketing initiatives across a multitude of clients and industries. Currently, he manages digital marketing program development and execution at EyeTraffic Media. Keith has particular expertise in search engine marketing (SEM), including paid search (PPC), search engine optimization (SEO) as well as web analytics implementation and analysis with a focus on online business intelligence. Keith also has a very strong background building and managing supplementary digital marketing programs and strategies in areas such as social media marketing, email, media buying, viral marketing and mobile marketing. Keith enjoys working to develop holistic marketing strategies that blend tactical online program execution with offline marketing initiatives to exceed campaign goals and objectives.

Keith received a BS in Business Management with concentrations in Marketing and Entrepreneurship from Babson College, where he currently lectures on interactive marketing subjects.
