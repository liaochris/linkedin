Adrienne Sundquist’s Summary

Intern at SWOCC Studios. Head writer of The ShoW, a student-run sitcom through MSU Telecasters. Creator/Producer/Writer/Director/Editor of TheGiraffeHouse.com, a student-run web-series through MSU Telecasters.
I am interested in writing in the entertainment business; television and screenwriting. I have written two full-feature screenplays, several "shorts," and various sitcom episodes and webisodes. As creator and producer of an award-winning and nationally screened web-series, I understand the demand for web-based media and its advantages in the growing global entertainment business.
My primary interest is comedic writing, but I am capable of incorporating this into the professional writing world, as well. I have taught creative writing to middle school/high school aged students at Brewster Academy in Wolfeboro, New Hampshire, and am interested in pushing young adults to discover their creative talents through writing short stories, poetry, screenplays, plays, songs, etc.
