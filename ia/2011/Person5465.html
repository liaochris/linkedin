Rose Miller’s Summary

Accomplished advocate and negotiator with expertise in the design and application of early dispute resolution processes to control risk and significantly reduce litigation and related costs for businesses. Proven track record of expense control for clients through litigation avoidance and expedited matter resolution. Seasoned litigation and claims manager for a global retailer. Highly skilled expert in mediation. Successful trial attorney. Provides results-oriented negotiation services using mediation for clients, either directly and/or through their claims administrator, insurer or broker, to reduce claims/litigation exposure and protect their brand.

Rose Miller’s Specialties:

Litigation/ claims management of personal injury, products liability, transportation, employment, environmental, regulatory/ compliance, security/loss prevention, commercial, medical malpractice/healthcare and mass tort/class action matters. Management/resolution of catastrophic losses and high volume exposures. Innovative design/execution of alternative dispute resolution processes. Risk/insurance management, claims administration, reserve management, indemnification/tender and bad faith.
