Martin Silcock's Summary

Martin is a strategic thinker with an enthusiasm for creating innovative, actionable ways to produce new value for brands and colleagues.

His hybrid combination of commercial marketing experience, and practical understanding of research and insights applied to marketing problems, is what makes him different and interested in roles that involve broad visions and creative thinking in teams.

Outcome driven, generous with his knowledge he possesses a constant drive to learn, make a difference.

▶Focus: Developing deep understanding of people and brands and their possible future evolution, then placing this at the centre of decision-making. Making insights believable, actionable and the driver behind business strategy.

Constant curiosity and appetite to know and foresee “what’s next”, and experimenting with new ideas, thinking tools and methods (e.g. mind map software, online communities)

▶ Background:
UK and global product marketing, brand management, brand development consultancy and internal process improvement roles for International consumer companies. Projects and roles in various market research (Qual and Quant and strategy oriented) consultancies and client-side insight deployment environments.

Specialties

▶ Skills: Stakeholder/client management, strategy creation workshop and client co-creation facilitation, virtual team management/ leadership, qualitative and quantitative analysis and insight synthesis, creative and strategic thinking, entrepreneurial and planner mindset.
