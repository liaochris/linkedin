Peter Kentie's Summary

The connecting element in my career is a strong love for design and technology and a strong will to share and publish my knowledge and experiences.

I work as marketing and media manager for PSV Eindhoven. With a strong emphasis on value creation through broad (cross)media reach and emphasis on conversion of PSV related products and services. Innovative new media development is a key focus area, especially in the domain of mobile services and TV & Internet conversion. In 2008 the official PSV website was awarded Best Sports Site and Most Popular Sports Site in The Netherlands.

I'm really proud of being awarded Dutch Sport marketeer of the year 2008. And for PSV's social media strategy I received the social marketeer of 2009 award in the spring.

Public speeching and giving presentations are a great way to promote my innovative personal marketing and crossmedia beliefs. You can always contact me for a speaking engagement at peter@kentie.com.

My publishing efforts lead to two books about Desktop Publishing and Web Design that I have written, designed and published for Addison Wesley, Pearson Education geared towards the Dutch market. The books have also been translated in English, German, Spanish and Korean and over 65.000 copies were printed and sold worldwide.

Culturally I'm active as co-founder of the STRP art & technology festival (www.strp.nl) and with board positions at the Dutch Design Week (www.ddw.nl) and the Baltan medialab (www.baltan.org) for research on media, science and art.
For Brainport Development I initiated the Wireless Eindhoven project together with Joep Brouwer.

To learn to know me well and to check on my activities follow me on www.kentie.com.

Peter Kentie's Experience

Chair of the board

Baltan Laboratories

Research industry

March 2008 – Present (3 years 1 month)

BALTAN Laboratories initiates, supports and disseminates innovative research and development activities in the field of art, technology and culture. A two-year pilot initiative located in the SWA building in Strijp S in Eindhoven, BALTAN Laboratories is a first step towards a broader Art Science Lab in the former NatLab (Philips physics laboratory). BALTAN Laboratories actively pursues new collaborations between disciplines and acts as point of intersection for artists working with technology in Eindhoven and beyond.

Public Company; Sports industry

Responsible for marketing and commercial media exploitation of Dutch football club PSV Eindhoven. Marketing responsibilities include commercial media activities for PSV and PSV related brands. Focus on innovation and creative development of new media and social media products and services.

Co-founder

STRP Festival

Entertainment industry

2003 – Present (8 years)

Together with Frens Frijns and Ton van Gool we founded STRP art & technology festival in Eindhoven at Strijp-S. Third edition was in april 2009 with Laurie Anderson, Aphex Twin + Hecker, Laurent Garnier and Squarepusher in the line up. And 20.000 attendees!

Supervisory board member

Dutch Design Week

Non-Profit; Design industry

2005 – September 2010 (5 years)

During Dutch Design Week 2010, the city of Eindhoven will for nine full days completely be dedicated to design. The largest design event in the Netherlands offers a platform to established design bureaus, well-known designers, talented upcoming designers and recently graduated designers. All possible aspects of contemporary Dutch design will be represented in a great many large and small venues all over the city: from process to product, from industrial design to applied art.

DDW 2010 targets the public at large alongside designers, companies and educational institutes. The broad scope and high degree of accessibility ensure a lasting relationship between the professionals themselves and between professionals and a wide audience.

The Dutch Design Week is now part of Design Corporation Brainport, so my role as member of the supervisory board has ended for Design Platform Eindhoven.

Peter Kentie's Publications

  • DTP in de Praktijk

    • Addison Wesley Longman
    • March 1, 1993
    Authors: Peter Kentie

    DTP in de Praktijk, translated real-world desktop publishing, is a book teaching designers the ins and outs of Desktop Publishing in 500 pages. The publication was a sell out with 5000 copies. I wrote the text, designed the layout with the special two-column grid and asked lot's of friends and colleagues from Kothuis, my employer, to cooperate in the project with their illustrations and design practices. To emphasize the real-world practise the book aimed to radiate. I also did the desktop publishing myself in QuarkXPress.

  • Webdesign in de Praktijk

    • Pearson Education
    • June 1, 2003
    Authors: Peter Kentie

    In Webdesign in de Praktijk, expert webpage designer Peter Kentie provides a profusely illustrated, full- color guide to the development and utilization of HTML tags to make a web site functional and aesthetically pleasing. Additionally, Kentie offers a wealth of clearly demonstrated, practical, user friendly information on advanced all the current techniques that an HTML programmer must master and a starting webdesigner must.accomplish.
