Hedi Hoka Ph.D.'s Summary

Dr. Hedi Hoka is a practitioner and an academic in coaching psychology, as well as board executive director. HH focuses on providing coaching services to organizations and individuals in a variety of settings. With her experience in both the practice of coaching and the development of coaching as a field of research, HH brings a unique approach of knowledge and theory with business and personal application. She uses her self-awareness, insight, strengths-based approach to help people develop their capabilities to think bigger, act more effectively, care about their quality of life, achieve desired success and be the person they want to be.
HH blends the need for personal insight with the need for goal and action-oriented development so that clients see clearer decision-making leading to effective action. She is on the faculty of Applied Psychology Department at Babes-Bolyai University, in the Work Psychology and Organizational Development program, and she teaches in the Evidence-Based Coaching framework. Evidence-based coaching links theories and research from behavioral sciences like psychology.

Hedi has presented and published her science-based work in a wide variety of international professional venues such as Romania, the Netherlands, Finland, Sweden, the Czech Republic, Portugal, Estonia, Hungary, Bulgaria, Austria, and the UK. In her presentations and training sessions, she focused on bringing neuroscientific knowledge into the areas of leadership development, management training, change management, education, consulting and coaching.
Hedi is also the current Board’s Executive Director at Bethania Foundation – a CSR project for children’s education.
Hedi’s professional and educational background is as a licensed work/organizational psychologist. In 2001, she received her Ph.D. in work and organizational psychology from Babes-Bolyai University, and completed her internship at the VU Netherlands and ELTE Budapest.

Specialties

Sustainability education, Leadership Coaching, Research Evidence based Coaching, Business Coaching, Business Psychology, CSR.
