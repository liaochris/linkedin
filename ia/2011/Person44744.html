International Ambassador for Trust

entente pty ltd

Philanthropy industry

May 2000 – Present (10 years 10 months)

entente and entente foundation are uniquely focused on building and restoring TRUST in businesses, homes and communities around the world.

entente, which means Understanding and Good Relationships in French, is an International Movement of Trust, a social enterprise made up of both a not for profit foundation and a for profit business.

We have developed a strategy that allows us, through corporate sponsorship, to rebuild and restore trust around the world, with programs in schools, and in communities, to bring the truth about trust where it is most needed, and at the same time, giving benefits back in the form of training, books and other resources to those organisations who sponsor our work. It's a win win for all!

Our goal is to bring about a new awareness of the power and fragility of trust in every relationship on this planet. We have forgotten how to trust, and it's time for us to remember!

I guess you could say I'm the creative brain behind what entente does, as the author and international speaker on trust. I like to think of myself as merely a messenger with a distinct purpose which I am trusting wholeheartedly!

People describe me as passionate and on a mission! I love what I do, but most of all, I love what it does for others.

Head of Product Compliance

MLC

Public Company; 1001-5000 employees; NAB; Banking industry

2004 – 2005 (1 year)

I was responsible for a team Australia wide, ensuring that all products produced and offered by MLC, and there were lots of them, were all in compliance with legislation, regulations, industry standards, and so on.

Considered boring by most people, what I found fascinating was the fact that the reason there was sooo many laws and rules was because business and industry had broken the trust of consumers, shareholders and employees, and the only way we knew how to respond to that as a society was to put more rules in place - not the best way to build trust. In fact, it actually breaks down trust even further!

Head of Compliance

Barclays Global Investors

Public Company; 5001-10,000 employees; BLK; Investment Management industry

1996 – 2000 (4 years)

I was responsible for all products and services Barlcays produced and promoted, ensuring their compliance with laws, industry standards and so on - basically making sure that if you invested with Barclays, you could trust what they said and did.

I reported directly to the Compliance Committee and Board, and spent time in London and San Francisco sharing ideas and processes with Barclays counterparts.

I left Barclays to set up my consultancy business, Entente, initially providing Compliance, Risk and Governance services to Financial Services Companies, then moving to providing Trust education and advice (during this time, I also worked for MLC for a year).
