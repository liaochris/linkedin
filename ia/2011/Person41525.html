Steve Goldstein, PMP’s Summary

Steve Goldstein, a Project Management Institute PMP Certified Project Manager with over 26 years of corporate and solution provider achievement, clearly grasps both the art and the science of ERP/business solutions project management. Through intelligently-scaled practice, never losing his focus on the project's economic justification, client satisfaction exceeds his reliable delivery of "on-time, on-budget and with full in-scope function" performance.

He brings a broad-base of industry and business process expertise honed in sixteen years of prior senior and executive financial management achievement. Keen analytic insight and well-balanced perspective have long been hallmarks.

Whether his current mission is project management, business process analysis or solution practice management Mr. Goldstein delivers a unique depth and scope of experience, and an unwaivering commitment to quality and satisfaction. Having over one hundred successful project involvements will always be secondary to the satisfaction of having respected the individual needs of each client.

A well-established veteran of the solution provider realm, he has worked within and managed many of the mid-market industry and regional leaders.

Project Management, Project Best Practices/PMO, Business Process Analysis, Microsoft Dynamics Practice/PSO Development & Management, Microsoft Dynamics NAV Solution Design.
