Cynthia Goldbarg's Summary

Education management, leadership and training specialist with 20 years of experience in NGOs, government, public and private organizations (for and non profits) in Argentina and the United States. University professor, trainer, advisor and coach for different levels of the private and public school system in both countries. CDA trainer and advisor. Particularly interested in total quality management, education excellence and innovation.

Specialties

Project design, implementation and evaluation. Teacher mentoring and training. Coaching for school administrators. Accreditation for ECE, schools and training programs. Professional development. Leadership development.
