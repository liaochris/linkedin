Andrew Wiech’s Summary

Andrew joined Comet as a Social Media + PR Coordinator in January 2010 after graduating with a B.A. in Business and Communications from Wisconsin Lutheran College in Milwaukee.

In college, Andrew interned at local television stations CW18 & My 24, creating promotional campaigns and video spots. Andrew also worked as a social media marketing intern at ThirdCoast Digest where he doubled the network of fans/followers and established social media as one of the largest drivers of traffic to the publication’s website.

In addition to Comet, Andrew contributes to the Student Branding Blog and co-chairs the Young Professionals Committee for the PRSA Southeastern Wisconsin Chapter.
