Zia Wesley’s Summary

Zia is currently seeking a position as a spokesperson with an established cosmetics/skin care company. She brings thirty years of expertise to the table, along with her naturally flawless complexion that defies her years. Women who meet her immediately ask two questions: “What’s your secret, and what do you use on your skin?”

For the last decade, Zia has personally focused on “youthing” as a viable option to aging and will divulge all of her secrets in the book she is currently writing, The New Forty: Why You Never Need to Look Your Age.

Zia’s latest tips and articles may be found on her blog at www.ziawesley.com. She is also the
“Fountain of Youth Examiner” at Examiner.com.

Zia Wesley’s Experience

  • Author/Consultant

    Youthing Institute

    (Partnership; Cosmetics industry)

    January 1983 — Present (28 years 2 months)

    Zia Wesley has been a pioneer in the field of natural skin care for thirty years. Known as “the inspiration of a generation,” Zia is the best-selling author of six books on beauty, health, and well-being. As a cosmetics consumer advocate who created the original cosmetic ingredient dictionary in her first book in 1983, Zia recommended hundreds of products while warning readers away from others that were harmful or simply a waste of money.

    A licensed cosmetologist trained by Vidal Sassoon, and one of the first certified aromatherapists in the United States, Zia founded Zia Cosmetics, Inc. in 1983, with seven innovative products that carried groundbreaking money-back guarantees. In 1991, Zia Cosmetics was listed for the first of three times, in Inc. Magazine’s 500 fastest-growing companies in the U.S. When Zia sold her interest in the company in 1996, the line contained 136 products and was the best-selling line in the natural foods industry. The Hain Celestial Group bought Zia Cosmetics in 2007.

    Zia was featured as a resident beauty expert on San Francisco Bay Area radio and television throughout the eighties and nineties, including a quarterly spot on the top-rated Ronn Owens radio show. She has regularly appeared on television shows in Los Angeles, Seattle and Portland and toured nationally with each of her books. Zia’s articles have been published extensively in magazines and newspapers including The Los Angeles Times, San Francisco Chronicle, Chicago Tribune, Chicago Herald, Yoga Journal, East-West Journal, American Health, Natural Living, Elle, Glamour, and Delicious, among others. Zia is included in: The World of Aromatherapy by Jeanne Rose and Susan Earle, 1996.
