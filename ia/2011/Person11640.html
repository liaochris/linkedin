Gert Van Duinen's Experience

Brand Identity Designer & Creative Director

Cresk Design

Design industry

January 2002 – Present (9 years 2 months)

my name is Gert van Duinen and I’m a Dutch based illustrative logo & identity designer and founder of cresk, a logo design company primarily focussing on logo design and brand identity creation. Officially I’ve been into graphic design since the early 90′s, after studying graphic design and receiving a handful of diplomas and certificates. But according to mom I’ve been scribbling signs & symbols before I could even walk. If mom’s right, it’s no surprise that I’ve always been intrigued and passionate about iconic design, trademarks and anything distinguishable by either brandmark, symbol or impression.

As a senior designer, creative director as well as a signmaker and packaging designer, I have worked for a number of national leading creative agencies, advertising firms and modern consultancies. Meanwhile I have worked with a number of globally recognized clients, including Hallmark, Dish Networks, MTV, TMF, Coca Cola, ING, Philips, to name but a few.

“I worked with globally recognized clients such as Hallmark, Dish, MTV, TMF, Coca Cola, ING, Philips, …”

Throughout the years some of my work has gained international exposure in a wide spectrum of the media, ranging from creative magazines to viral campaigns and television broadcasting. My work has been published and featured in logo design books, design related blogs, articles, advertorials, and several logo galleries.

As a self-employed brand identity designer for a couple of years now, I thrive with the most important characteristics for a successful logo and identity, such as originality, distinctiveness, relevance, memorability and maybe even pushing a few boundaries as well.
