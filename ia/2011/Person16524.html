Greg Bybee's Summary

Greg Bybee is a Product Marketing Manager for VMware’s enterprise cloud computing initiatives and the global business lead for vCloud Director. Working in the Palo Alto headquarters, Greg has had responsibilities spanning product and solution marketing to corporate strategy.

Before VMware, Greg was a management consultant at McKinsey & Company, where he advised executives on major strategic and operational initiatives. His work experience also includes Microsoft Corporation, Credit Suisse Group AG, and the United States Senate.

He graduated with honors from Harvard University with a degree in Applied Mathematics and Economics. Living in San Francisco, Greg enjoys travel, fitness, photography, public policy, international development, and education reform.
