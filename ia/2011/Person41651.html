Steve Hogan’s Summary

My passion is early-stage and startup companies, particularly those that can have a significant positive impact on the way people lead their lives and do business. I am a product and strategy guy at heart, but also a proven, hands-on manager who understands what it takes to build a startup into a profitable, well-run, highly-respected company. My background includes diverse industries -- telecom, wireless, Internet, payment processing, GPS, and electronics manufacturing.

My Career Snapshot ---

>> Unwavering passion for cutting-edge technology products and markets. Developed new products that received patents (see below) and won awards from prestigious industry publications.

>> Serial entrepreneur. Builder of unique companies. Accustomed to leading new business ventures.

>> Uncanny ability to identify the hidden value in a company, architect a compelling vision embraced by major stakeholders, drive team performance, and modify a company’s value proposition to deliver explosive growth.

>> Regarded as a specialist in revitalizing companies that are floundering and experiencing extremely challenging difficulties. Considered an outstanding business reconstructionist and turnaround artist.

>> Successfully collaborated with companies, venture capital firms, angel investors, and professionals across the globe. Strong understanding of global business practices and challenges.
