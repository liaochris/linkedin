Todd McDonagh’s Summary

Having had the opportunity to teach at the collegiate level for the last three years, I have realized the importance of knowledge. The rules of the road for marketing are being altered on a daily basis, yet many businesses aren't riding this dynamic curve.

The Mad Dog Group is an internet marketing and social media consultancy that is dedicated to spreading the knowledge of the digital age. We have a niche within the home design industry(furniture, bath, kitchen & plumbing and appliance) but we work with any business or industry that is willing to throw out the old marketing manual and rewrite the script. We also believe that there is great opportunity to help local businesses harness these new tools.

I am an internet veteran, having helped acquire and operate HomePortfolio back from near bankruptcy. During my first six years at HomePortfolio, I managed strategic planning with Founder/CEO and handled the day-to-day operations. My role shifted to business development after the company was acquired. I have cut my entrepreneurial teeth and understand every aspect of a business.

The Mad Dog Group gives me the opportunity to build a business founded on the principle that knowledge leads to understanding and understanding leads to trust. Social media is built on trust. Social media is slowly inserting itself into the fabric of business. The Mad Dog Group can help businesses strategize, implement, monitor and adjust their social media and internet marketing plans.
