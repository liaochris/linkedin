Jeff Hunter’s Summary

We are a national search firm. With office locations in Atlanta, Chicago, Tampa Bay, Kansas City, New York, & Salt Lake City.

Our clients are some of the top IT firms such as Big 4 firms, Top 5 Systems Integrators, major IT Svc bureaus, and "household" name vendors of S/W & H/W. We also work with several mid-tier and ‘boutique’ firms.

We have significant depth of experience in several "inside" industry verticals such as Energy & Power, Financial Svcs (Retail & Commercial banking, Brokerage, Insurance, & Transaction Processing Svcs), Healthcare & Life Sciences, Hi-Tech, Retail, & T-comm/D-comm.

The specific functional areas which we cover are BI/DW, CRM, ERP, Enterprise Architecture & ITSM, IAM/Infosec (Traditional Infosec & IT Audit), SOA-ESB/EAI, & S/W Sales/Pre-sales & Biz Dev / Practice Leadership for Professional Services.

The primary common denominator of our client's is that they possess or provide products and/or services for large, heterogeneous, widely networked, highly complex environments which are a core part of the client’s business as opposed to merely being viewed as a ‘cost center’.

We place people in all roles related to these functional disciplines. This covers everything from internal roles to client-facing roles, strategic advisory consulting to tactical execution, product development to post-sales implementation. The common criterion is that the role be related to one of these functional areas.

Many of us joined the firm ‘from the trenches’ so to speak. We have over 100 yrs of IT recruiting experience between us, and roughly the same amount of actual ‘hands-on’ IT experience managing accts, engagements, implementations, and personnel management/hiring while with some of the major players in the IT arena such as IBM, Oracle, Fujitsu, & KPMG/Bearing Point. We believe this gives us a good perspective on things from all sides of the equation.

We are exclusively a permanent hire search firm. Our contact information is below.
