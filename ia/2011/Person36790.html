Rick Ward’s Summary

Rick is a Director of Business Development at MedAvante, Inc., a Hamilton, NJ based company dedicated to reducing risk of failed trials in CNS (Depression, Anxiety, Schizophrenia, Alzheimer's, etc.) by reducing the variability and bias inherent to traditional methods of conducting patient assessments.

Prior to joining MedAvante, Rick was a Director of Business Development with PAREXEL International - directly responsible for Japanese pharma companies with clinical operations in the USA. Rick has over 20 years of sales/business development experience including 5 years with Veritas Medicine where he was Director of Sales and later promoted to the post of Vice President, Sales & Marketing.

Rick is an active member of the Drug Information Association (DIA) and has spoken on the topic of Patient Recruitment in Clinical Trials at industry conferences such as IIR’s Clinical Trials Congress, the Philadelphia Area ACRP, and the DIA Outsourcing Summit as well as serving in an advisory role for various other industry conferences.

Rick graduated with a B.S. in Marketing from the Pennsylvania State University. He is currently pursuing an MBA in the Biotechnology & Healthcare Industries from the Penn State Great Valley Campus. Rick has also served on Penn State’s Smeal College of Business Alumni Society Board of Directors since 1999 and is one of the founding members and former President of the Smeal Business Club of Philadelphia.
