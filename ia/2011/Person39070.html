Saygin Yalcin's Summary

Saygin Yalcin is a budding entrepreneur and is known in Dubai as Founder and President of the first and largest online private shopping club in the Middle East. Sukar.com brings the best deals home to 500,000 private shoppers, offering them the privilege of purchasing top lifestyle brands at up to 90% off retail. In less than one year, the company has become the most successful e-commerce startup in the Middle East and, with Saygin at its helm, has grown into the multi-million dollar business it is today. “In this business, it is all about working together to overcome the challenges of an enterprise that spans 9 markets and 9 cultures. At Sukar.com, we are proud of what we have achieved and flattered by our members’ reception of this business model. We look forward to an even wider presence in the coming year,” says this dynamic and energetic entrepreneur.
Saygin’s achievements are the result of a distinct passion for turning opportunities into reality. Holding an international Master's degree in Business Administration and Economics from top business schools in Germany, USA and Mexico. He is fluent in German, English, Spanish and Turkish.
