Brian Moorhead's Summary

Brian Moorhead brings over 16 years of sales, business development & marketing leadership to the Ygnition team.  The large majority of those years have been spent within the multifamily real estate industry with a focus on video, voice and data services. Prior to joining Ygnition in 2004, Brian worked in various capacities for several leading private cable operators serving the multifamily industry. 
Brian was the Manager of Property Owner Relations & Business Development for TVMAX, a private cable operator serving the multifamily industry.  Prior to TVMAX, he served as a Marketing Director for OpTel and its largest system in Houston, TX where he was accountable for all aspects of consumer marketing and managing the growth of customers and revenue.  Brian began his career in the multifamily industry in 1995 as Regional Account Manager for GE Capital-Rescom in Los Angeles.  He later accepted the role of National Portfolio Manager where he was tasked with the daily management of over 130,000 apartment units for the company’s largest customer, Insignia Financial.
Brian is a graduate of Southeastern Louisiana University in Hammond, Louisiana.
