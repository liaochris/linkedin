Lester Bostic's Summary

I have a very diverse back ground in Software Development, Automation and Quality Assurance.

To date my most outstanding accomplishment was to run my own Systems Integration Company for 4 years (Logical Technologies Inc.) which included international work in Germany. As CEO I got to use all of my talents gained over the years to help the business progress.

I have led/managed many QA projects over the years as well. I look forward to using my skills with the team here at Blue Cross to develop "Lights Out Testing".

Goal #1 -Is to be on the beach with my team w/laptop/s via BB connection creating libraries for our "lights -out" automated tests which kick off by themselves after the test environment has verified itself by checking all the logins, pinging services, servers and when the build completes and auto installs. Then the system sends detailed End-Of-Day reports to our SharePoint site which includes; troubleshooting assists reports for developers so that they can fix the defects, project managers will get a report detailing which of the requirements are failing for the release, which defects belong to which requirements and who is responsible for each defect, the managers will receive a progress report that summarizes where we are in the testing cycle and lists any issues derived from posted defects and last but not least a High level report to Business stakeholders with a summary that covers cycle status and project impact information. Oh and BTW...All test cases written by SME's that have no idea what a "For...Loop" is.

Goal #2 – Write a book showing you how to do it too.
