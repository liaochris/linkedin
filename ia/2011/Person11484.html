Denise Magill's Summary

“Collaboration + Communication = Unmatched Innovation”.

So, what are wikis? Simply put, a wiki is a tool that allows you to bring tangibility to your ideas, enabling you to implement them through others. If you can’t explain to somebody what your thought process is, then you’re limited to how much you can delegate, and that limits how fast your organization can grow.

Wikis are also an essential tool when it comes to collaborating with your clients effectively. You can engage your clients earlier in the creative process resulting in greater client satisfaction. Virtual RoundTable will help you create a single point of contact for your client, providing them a direct view of the status of their project increasing accountability within your team and the client.

Capture your organization’s best practices of approaching certain types of projects by utilizing Best Practice Workflow Templates. Virtual RoundTable will show you how to capture this and utilize it again and again saving your organization time and money while increasing the quality of your product and projects.

Wikis are created from scratch! Therefore, whenever you consider purchasing a product or service you have to ask yourself how this is going to grow your profits. Virtual RoundTable will demonstrate and consult with you how wikis can not only increase your sales but also decrease and eliminate some expenses. Before any work is started, we will:

• Determine how we will demonstrate a return on your investment and then some
• Help you identify the best uses of wikis by department
• Develop a comprehensive implementation and adoption plan
• Provide initial and on-going training
• Communicate on-going and value-added updates of the latest wiki uses and practices
