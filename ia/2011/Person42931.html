Michael Drake's Summary

Our goals at FitzDrake Search are:
1)To provide a service that "makes sense and seems fair." We developed this practice from the perspective of a client and strive to keep things simple, provide great service and deliver outstanding results. Our model is based on a Professional Services model and therefore we do not work under the traditional fee based retainer model.
2)To build strong relationships with talented individuals and offer them exceptional career opportunities and career advice.
3)To always act with Intergity, Honesty and Candor. Our approach to recruiting, with both clients and candidates is very direct. We make no attempt to deceive and ask the same in return. We will not waste your time so please don't waste ours.

We have deep search expertise in the following areas:
• Finance/Accounting/Risk Management/Compliance
• Marketing/Sales/Business Development
• Internet/New Media/Advertising
• Senior Level IT/Operations
• HR/OD/T&D
• The Not-for-Profit world – Foundations, Associations, etc.

Specialties

Executive Search, Senior level recruitment, Mid/Upper Level management recruitment, design, implementation and execution of corporate wide sourcing and recruiting strategies for executive and non-executive talent acquisition, competitive intelligence...
