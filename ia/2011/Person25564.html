Laura Darrell’s Summary

Check out my website for updates, including just becoming part of the cast of Rocket Boys!

Laura just performed in the 2010 CAP21/Tisch Showcase after graduating as a Phi Beta Kappa scholar and Magna Cum Laude from USC's School of Theatre. Before that she attended CAP21/Tisch, NYU for two years.

A conservatory trained actress in both LA and NY, she performed in the main stage productions, as well as thesis films at both USC and NYU. She just finished filming her first indie feature in LA, called City of Angers, with a leading role, as well as several other professional shorts!

She has worked at several professional regional theatres including Hudson Theatre in LA, Portland Stage, Maine Stage Music Theatre, Good Theatre, Biddeford Regional Theatre, and has sung with the Portland Symphony Orchestra and LA Rock Opera Company on multiple occasions.

In addition to theater and film, she has helped raise money for the Maine Children's Cancer Program with a Christmas CD and Maine PBS special, for which she was nominated for a New England Emmy Award. She has also recorded with internationally recognized composer, Tim Janis on some of his albums, sold nationally on the QVC network.

In addition to her studies at NYU and USC, Laura takes voice lessons with Victoria Clark (A Light in the Piazza) and Marni Nixon (voice of Natalie Wood in West Side Story, and Audrey Hepburn in My Fair Lady) in NYC. In LA she studies with Edward Sayegh.
