Tara Campasano Malia’s Summary

An experienced teacher of fourteen years who began my career at Lawrence High School, I have since cultivated my skills and gained invaluable experience at The Pennington School, a private preparatory school in southern New Jersey. My certifications in Special Education and in English, an advanced degree, and professional experience as an educator, administrator, and Assistant Director of College Guidance have afforded me with incredible opportunities. Throughout my career, I worked with a myriad of students of all ages who displayed a variety of skill levels. One of my greatest achievements has been to motivate and celebrate many different students. Finally and most importantly, my students like to learn and I love teaching. I bring that same enthusiasm to my college advising business.
