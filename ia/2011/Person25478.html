Larry Kutcher’s Summary

My company, theAdvantage, is a business consulting firm providing strategic, operational and technology services to uncover profit and growth opportunities for emerging-growth, high-growth and early stage companies. Bridging strategy and execution, we work with owners and management to ensure that operations and technologies are aligned with business goals, delivering greater value. We do this is by building, strengthening and managing effective business infrastructures, processes and technologies that scale and drive profitability. Our expertise enables companies to increase efficiencies, expand into new areas and leverage existing capabilities.

With 25+ years experience in finance, operations, technology and consulting, we drive businesses to the next level of growth. Our results include:
• Enhanced profit through increased transparency of business metrics, better information and sophisticated analysis of key business drivers.
• Improved decision-making capabilities by providing thorough and accurate information.
• Greater competitive advantage through more efficient targeted processes, sophisticated business modeling, strategic vision and business acumen.
• Sustained growth through scalable operational infrastructures and the strategic use of technology.
• Opportunistic investment guidance in start-up companies and real estate, including due diligence, modeling and strategic advice.

We offer our clients:
• Consulting - strategic analysis and roadmaps.
• Project management.
• Negotiation and contracting.
• Project implementation and training.
• Information Technology infrastructure.
