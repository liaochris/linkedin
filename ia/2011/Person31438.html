Morgan O'Hara’s Summary

Morgan O’Hara is completing his Master’s in Business Administration with an emphasis in Management from the California Polytechnic State University at San Luis Obispo. He previously worked in New York as a news producer for RAI, Italy’s public service broadcaster. He has coordinated shoots with influential figures such as John Grisham, Gary Hart, and the celebrated architect Renzo Piano. Morgan has himself conducted interviews that aired nationwide on Italian prime-time television. Morgan also traveled extensively for work, and coordinated live news specials on a number of issues, including the 2008 political primaries.

Morgan is at the top of his class at Cal Poly. He is a member of honor societies and a recipient of academic awards. This past summer he went with his MBA class on an international business tour to both India and the People's Republic of China. The trip was a three-week assessment of the global business practices of India and the PRC, with visits to small, medium, and large companies in key cities (New Delhi, Mumbai, Gurgaon, Beijing, Shanghai, and Suzhou). After the business tour, he traveled across China to improve his Chinese and immerse himself in the culture.

Morgan has lived and worked in Mexico, where he taught English as a second language at La Universidad Tecnológica del Valle del Mezquital. He also lived in Rome, where he had the opportunity to intern for the Glocal Forum, an NGO emphasizing the central role of cities in international relations. Morgan was responsible for supporting and coordinating the organization’s youth delegates in South America and Western Europe.

Morgan has traveled extensively though Eastern and Western Europe. Morgan’s experiences abroad have given him more than fluency in other languages: he has gotten to know the ins and outs of working in foreign countries, is able to adapt and work outside of his comfort zone, and can communicate with, understand and engage people from different cultural backgrounds.
