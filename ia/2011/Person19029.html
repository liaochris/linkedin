Jane Gehlhausen's Summary

Senior executive and proven performer with 15 years of successful experience driving international business initiatives. Recognized as a strategically-focused leader and tactical implementer with superior interpersonal communication skills, impeccable work ethic, and particular expertise in Global Operations, Alliance Management, International Partnerships and Strategic Implementation.

Professional experience encompasses business and client relationship management including development of strategic partnerships in over 30 countries, collaborating with C-level executives, developing/negotiating international contracts, as well as leading and energizing global teams in complex project management initiatives.

Strong community advocate as evidenced by serving as Chair of the Board of Directors for the International Center of Indianapolis, as well as Alumni Chair for the Fishers Rotary Foundation, past Board President of the World Trade Club of Indiana and the American Lung Association, as well as serving on the Board of Sister Cities Association of Fishers and Partners of the Americas.

Passionate advocate for creating global awareness by strengthening Indiana’s global connections and promoting Indianapolis as a culturally diverse city.
