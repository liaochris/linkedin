Christina Sloan’s Summary

National REO Relief provides solutions to lending institutions and other holders of REO properties. With foreclosures expected to top as many as 3.5 million across the United States in 2010 alone, National REO Relief has the ability to purchase, manage and/or sell foreclosed real estate, relieving the current owners from these non-performing and often costly holdings.

Christina Sloan, Director of Acquisitions for NRR in the West Palm area, has over seven (7) years of experience in Property Management, Development, Residential, and Commercial Real Estate combined.
Ms. Sloan began her full-time career leasing and selling homes in Manhattan in 2004. From '05 on, her focus to Residential Development, first as on-site Sales, then focusing on the Operations end of converting Luxury Rental properties into Condominiums. Christina worked in both Residential & Commercial Leasing & Sales and Property Management in the State of Florida prior to joining the NRR team this Fall. Ms. Sloan is the Managing Broker for the State of Florida for both BankREOs.com, LLC and OwnACondo.com, LLC, or REO/Auctions and Traditional Brokerages respectively. She is a licensed Realtor® and maintains her sales license in the State of New York.
