Educator in Greater Philadelphia Area

School Lane Charter School

Education Management industry

August 1998 – February 2008 (9 years 7 months)

Carol Adams was a middle school administrator. Throughout her 33-year career, she championed authentic and mindful approaches to learning and leading. She has led hundreds of workshops on authentic/holistic teaching and assessment, visibility of the student, and genuine and collaborative leadership. Her insights come from her varied perspectives as middle school teacher, supervisor of literacy, curriculum coordinator and administrator of a middle school. When working with educators, she prompts them to explore the question that lies at the heart of their work. Key themes in her career: visibility of the learner, authentic assessment, shared decision-making, literacy and genuine leadership. Carol brings the full presence of acceptance and hope to all her encounters.
