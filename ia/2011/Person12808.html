Dustin Massey's Summary

Founded in early 2009, GreenPLUG Staffing (formerly Masey Search Group) merges more than 35 years of global recruitment experience in Energy and EPC industries.

At GreenPLUG, our value proposition is predicated on our comprehensive understanding of your company, your competition, and the industries you serve. We recognize the importance of retaining industry-experienced, prospering talent; that’s why our detail-oriented recruitment teams are trained to implement a mix of the most innovative, targeted search strategies to both identify and qualify the ideal candidates to meet our clients’ critical staffing needs.

GreenPLUG offers clients a suite of temporary, contingency and retained search solutions, all backed by an industry leading guarantee, for positions around the world. Our Temporary and Contingency Search Teams are available for critical project support, technical and management staffing needs, while more complex (and often confidential) executive and senior management searches are facilitated by our Executive Search Team on either a retained or contingency basis.
