Juan Camilo Hoyos M.'s Summary

Juan Camilo is a Political Scientist of the Pontificia Universidad Javeriana with an emphasis in Public Opinion and Political Marketing. He is currently taking part in a specialization in the same area of this University. Juan Camilo has worked at El Tiempo (Colombia´s most important newspaper) as a Journalist, in special projects related with Internet, thanks to a program offered by the organization for youth talents. Later, he did his internship at Bogotá town council and worked in political campaigns running for congress and major office in the political party of the former major and member of the group Urban Age of the London School of Economics, Enrique Peñalosa. Juan Camilo developed the first strategy of web 2.0 for a Colombian politic with Peñalosa and has worked as a consultant in that topic with companies and politics of the country, and joined the team of FD Gravitas in April 2009 to design strategies 2.0 for the customers of the company.
