Rick Kranz’s Summary

Video marketing specialist focused on helping business owners learn how to use online video as a marketing tool for growing their sales. My expertise includes online video technology and Internet marketing strategy.

I am currently President of OverGo Studio, Inc. a video marketing firm that specializes in interactive sales videos.

For the prior 15 years I spent my time as an entrepreneur building and selling various companies including a display manufacturer, a graphics company, a hair salon, an online retailer, and a video marketing firm.

In my past life I was a manager of a Fortune 500 construction firm for 13 years. I have an extensive background in financial management and marketing.

I have been a member of Vistage since 2000.

My goal is to use my experience to help other business owners grow their business.
