Mark McKee’s Summary

I work for the best company in the world ... FedEx. I'm the sole recruiter for a division of FedEx Services. I'm a recipient of the FedEx Customer Information Services Partnership Award and so far have received 3 Bravo Zulu awards for excellent customer service in dealing with internal and external customers.

Co-founded a career transition group in 2006.

I have 10+ years of national accounts sales management experience in selling cooking oils, olive oils, confections, pan sprays and non-dairy creamers to:

* grocery chains
* mass merchandisers
* club stores
* military.

Won Kraft General Foods' Merit Award for outstanding national accounts management. Won multiple sales contests throughout sales career.

Also have five years of industrial ingredients sales experience, with:

* value-added rice
* roasted peanuts and peanut butter
* protein pieces and color pieces

Sold ingredients to Pillsbury / General Mills, Nestle, M&M / Mars / MasterFoods and Hershey.

Project manager for development of first value-added rice products for Wal Mart. Generated 3 million pounds / $4.5MM sale of product in a year and a half.

Little known fact about me: I have been an extra in over 18 movies since 1978. The first movie I appeared in was
"Coalminer's Daughter" starring Sissy Spacek and Tommy Lee Jones. I was also in "The Firm" and "The People Vs. Larry Flynt".

One of my main passions, outside of my family and work, is photography. I had my first exhibition in July 2009 called "Lucidity" at the Memphis Botanic Gardens. Also recently had a photo placed into the "Capture Memphis" coffee table book along with several of my photos added to the accompanying DVD. Three of my photos were chosen for "Jones Soda" labels. One other note: I had a photo exhibited in a digital display at the Louvre in Paris.

Started a ministry in 2004 dedicated to teaching inner-city children the fine art of digital photography.
