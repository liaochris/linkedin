Malcolm MacGregor’s Summary

I love new beginnings, so much so that I’m starting my 3rd business in Seattle. My move from Amsterdam to Seattle kicked off what I like to call the “Seattle chapter”, which started with Razorfish, followed by a move to Microsoft Corp, working with fantastic agency partners on national and international projects spanning from Ferrari to McDonalds.

The journey started with my first business that paid for University. My second business taught me a more valuable lesson - I have lots to learn. My wife then told me a about this thing called the web. I was immediately inspired by the possibilities and new our lives were about to change.

I then joined my first web company, Quadravision. Cool name! I became an expert in financial services by building out relationship marketing strategies for Bankers Trust and CIBC. I need to learn more about technology, enter in Sapient and a move to CA. Sapient’s success extended beyond great design and into functional and integrated digital systems. I was very fortunate to receive the Chairman’s Award for my contribution and founding of a Healthcare practice in London.

Next came a move to Amsterdam with Blast Radius to be the European Union GM. Life was exciting, as we developed everything from an ATG eCommerce platform for Vodafone’s EU roll-out, to in-gaming commerce for Sony, to branding for Virgin.com.

The entrepreneurial journey that started at university places me with my third business, a wonderful family, great friends, residence in 5 world-class cities across the globe and an endless sea of marketing ideas. Born now, is Blab. As an agency we have designed a platform to enable businesses to connect with consumers in real-time through trends and the placement of contextual content. Seattle is now mission control for Blab and the beginning of an exciting new journey.
