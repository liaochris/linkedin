Greg Derkowski’s Summary

My mission as a strategic marketing and brand specialist is to uncover opportunities for clients to clearly differentiate their unique products and services. But being different is only the beginning; I help create and drive strategies that build real brand relevance, understanding and loyalty among targeted consumers. I am naturally focused on the big-picture, and take smart risks that build brand equity.

My experience and skills include:

> Strategic brand planning and implementation
> PR and media relations
> Quantitative and qualitative research
> Digital media
> Partnership development
> Corporate communications
> Creative writing, producing and directing
> Hands on team leader and coach
