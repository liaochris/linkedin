Robert McLaughlin's Summary

Successful CEO of multi channel brands in the international import and environmental eco responsible industries. Strong operational skills in start-ups, turnarounds and growth stage mission based businesses. Strong participant of certified floral production and fair labor initiatives, helped convert over 1,000 acres of traditional production to certified sustainable under several standards.

Over 25 years experience in international business and working in Central and South America maintaining relationships with large US retailers like Walmart, Sams, Kroger and Winn Dixie.

Current focus and success in E-Commerce models of both B2B and B2C

Specialties

Sustainable and Organic Agriculture, E-Commerce, Operations and ERP solutions, Accounting Solutions, National Branding and managing cross cultural relationships in developing nations.

Managed Organic Agriculture project in La Paz, Mexico for sustainable investment fund
