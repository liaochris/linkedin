Art Hoffman’s Summary

I have a proven record of experience in the establishment and auditing of ISO 9001 Quality Systems and Failure Mode and Effects Analyses (FMEAs). These, coupled with my manufacturing experience, enable me to identify opportunities and implement cost-effective measures to improve existing systems as well as develop new ones.

I have implemented cost-effective systems to achieve:
* an annual customer return rate, regardless of fault, of less than 0.02%.
* an Approved Supplier List whereby all suppliers have achieved a material acceptance rating of at least 99.7%.
* Annual Customer Satisfaction Ratings for Product Quality, Service and Technical Support of >95% from our top 35 customers and 35 newest customers.

Additionally, the Quality System I initially established and manage has not received any audit findings by any independent auditor in the past seven years. In fact, I have received praise during these audits for exceptional Quality Training, Quality Records, Design and Development, Contract Review, Document Control and Metric Measurement systems.

My extensive experience effectively dealing with suppliers and customers on technical and quality issues enables me to gain their confidence and for my employer to secure more of their business. This experience includes establishing high-quality, low cost off-shore manufacturing capabilities in the Caribbean, the Far East and India.

My extensive travel, both domestically and internationally, to meet with potential customers and review my employer's capabilities and the customer's needs has always resulted in substantial new business for my employer as well as satisfied customers.
