Paulo Ferreira's Publications

  • The emergence of strategic elites: a systemic analysis of the topic.

    • Lusophone University of Humanities and Technologies.
    • June 5, 2006
    Authors: Paulo Ferreira

    This essay aims to present some concepts, definitions and scope and influence of the so-called strategic elites. Passing Pareto until Mills we observed the formation of new strategic elites and their importance to the formation of modern capitalist societies.

  • The symbols of power: a scientific debate.

    • Lusophone University of Humanities and Technologies
    • September 18, 2007
    Authors: Paulo Ferreira

    This critical essay of the work of Pierre Bordier aims to present a methodology able to understand the symbolism associete to the power presented by one of the greatest thinkers and sociologists of the twentieth century.

  • The presence of China in Africa: a geo-strategic position for the XXI century.

    • Lusophone University of Humanities and Technologies
    • November 18, 2007
    Authors: Paulo Ferreira

    This study analyzes China's already significant presence in Africa, as well as economic diplomacy used by the State and chinese companies in their economic and cultural relations with African countries.The presence of China in Africa is not and never will be, of an elephant in a china shop, and the purpose of this study is to demonstrate this fact.

  • Economic Liberalism versus Socialism economic protectionism: what economic model for the twenty-first century?

    • Lusophone University of Humanities and Technology
    • June 22, 2008
    Authors: Paulo Ferreira

    This prospecting work as the premised of examine what economic model that will endure as model that prevails in most developed economies and even in emerging countries (e.g. BRIC `S), after the economic reformulation generated by the global economic crisis of 2007 so-call sub-prime.

  • Socioeconomic Policy of the European Union.

    • Lusophone University of Humanities and Technologies
    • October 12, 2008
    Authors: Paulo Ferreira

    This analysis has as main objective to understand which way the European Union has follow since its formation in terms of political economy in order to assess what is the path that the current 27 Member-States will choose to fight the growing influence of emerging economic countries like China, India or Brazil.

  • The Portuguese economy: 20 years of European integration.

    • Higher Institute for Labour and employment (ISCTE)
    • September 10, 2009
    Authors: Paulo Ferreira

    This work aims to understand the behavior of the Portuguese economy after twenty years of EU integration. By formalizing three empirical hypotheses, it is possible to see if Portugal has converged with the EU average in the key economic indicators analyzed, or whether it has diverged in respect to the mean of the EU.

  • CIMPOR - Surround Market.

    • Higher Institute for Labour and employment (ISCTE)
    • November 4, 2009
    Authors: Paulo Ferreira

    This analysis surrounding the market's mission is to figure out which is the core business of cement Cimpor, their behavior in the economic market, strategic management chosen for its internationalization and SWOT analysis of the company by analyzing its key assets and weaknesses, of one of the largest Portuguese companies.

  • Portugal Science or Portugal Innovation? Study of convergence with the EU (15) (2000-2008)

    • Higher Institute for Labour and employment (ISCTE)
    • December 10, 2009
    Authors: Paulo Ferreira

    This work aims to understand whether the Portuguese economy has kept pace with the so-called knowledge based economy (KBE), comparing it to our behavior in this field with 15 other Member-States which are part of the European Union within the time space between 2000 to 2008.

  • Prospective Scenarios of the Portuguese Economy: 2030 goal.

    • Higher Institute for Labour and (ISCTE)
    • June 10, 2010
    Authors: Paulo Ferreira

    This prospective study is based on the methodology of building scenarios from Global Business Network, for a time period 2010 - 2030, called the Florida scenario, which seeks to understand the behavior of the Portuguese and european economy in the time period defined, on the basis of economic, political, military and social leadership of the United States at global scale.
