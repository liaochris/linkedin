Mark van der Meijden's Summary

Mark obtained his MSc in Business Informatics in 2009 from the Utrecht University. Some keywords that describe Mark as a person: ambitious, analytical, process oriented, social and he has a strong focus on results.

Knowledge management is one of Mark's interests. Therefore he encourages his colleagues to share knowledge both internally and externally. He also explains how Social Media & Social Networking can help them with knowledge sharing.

Mark's interests also focus on Business Process Management. It is always a challenge to translate primary processes to technical solutions. Certified Be Informed practitioner.

Specialties

Knowledge Management, Web 2.0, Social Media, Social Networking, Business Intelligence, "The New World of Work" ("Het Nieuwe Werken"), Business Process Management, Be Informed
