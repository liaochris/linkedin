VIA American Language & Culture, or ALC1, brings 70 Asian university students from Japan, Taiwan, and Korea to participate in a challenging, educational, and fun program at Stanford University. The students attend rigorous English courses in the morning, and use the rest of their time to learn about American culture through activities such as company visits, community service projects, and recreational trips. As one of six Hosts for the ALC1 program, I lived in the dorms with the students as a Resident Assistant, helping with homework, and answering questions about Stanford, the United States, or my personal life. I was also Activity Coordinator, a position that involved using spreadsheets to organize information about every activity, being responsible for activity sign-ups and the selection process for each one, and the distribution and taking down of activity flyers throughout the dorms. This was an incredibly rewarding experience for me, and I plan to continue working with VIA.
