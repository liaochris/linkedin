Lena Benjamin MBA's Summary

Through the Six Step CRM Framework your organisation will achieve increased profitability, better internal collaboration, satisfied and loyal customers and continuous business improvement. A business whose strategic aim does not involve developing stronger and longer customer relationships is not realising the true lifetime value of their customer relationships. The Six Step Approach has been developed by Lena Benjamin of Ones Business Advice, get in touch for a free one hour consultation session.

Lena Benjamin has over 12 years experience developing and delivering client relationship management (CRM), business development and marketing communications initiatives for a variety of small, medium and large businesses.

Lena is a business mentor and trainer for organisations that wish to develop stronger and longer relationships with their customers and works with associate trainers and strategic alliances.

Lena is also an advocate of the advancement of women both personally and professionally and offers mentoring and inspirational events for women through onescompany.

We specifically target those clients that would like to develop stronger relationships with their customers and in turn maximise profitability. Our existing clients are in the professional services, finance and banking sectors.

Behind a successful organisation is a strong, sustainable customer base. Managing that asset is a key task, from the boardroom down.

To help in that task, Ones Business Advice has developed a Strategic CRM Framework which can be used to identify gaps in customer-facing processes and systems.
Once those gaps have been identified, they can be addressed to build stronger and longer customer relationships.
