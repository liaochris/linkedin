Greg Timpany’s Summary

As a Customer Intelligence Professional I am experienced at the fine art of measuring marketing efforts and relating them back to stated goals to see what works and what doesn't. I am comfortable in working the space between Marketing and IT.

Possess specific skills in marketing research (primary and secondary), data mining, marketing analytics (including developing LTV models) and strategy development for both acquisition and retention.

Passionate about developing and managing customer and prospect panels for voice of customer (VOC) feedback initiatives.

Recent Projects:
- Redesign and launch of a broad-based IT industry research panel
- Strategic communications surveys (for the University of Redlands)
- IT Salary and Skills Survey (for Global Knowledge)
- Brand tracking study for regional grocery chain (through FGI Research)
- Alternative media usage (through Lattitude research)
