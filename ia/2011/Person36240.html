Raz Greenberg's Summary

I have worked as a freelance and fulltime writer and editor for a variety of English and Hebrew publications, in-print and on-line. Texts handled by me include news items, promotional and advertising material, book, film and television reviews, and research reports.

Specialties

Writing and editing material related to news and entertainment, content management for events.
