Kim Luu’s Summary

Moneyandrisk.com focus on managing risk as a critical component of business success and personal success.

Our goal is to inspire and give real world actions that can be used immediately.

Financial security brings freedom of choices not just for the individual but also for their family.

Our mission is to help women and minorities understand the depth of resources available to them and how to use it wisely.

email us info@moneyandrisk with questions.

Our editor, Kim Luu, has been working with business owners and executives for over 25 years. She is a first generation immigrant and worked in the family business at 12 yrs old until the business was sold.

She continued with business consulting and worked for the California Employment Development Department. She later joined the financial industry and set up guidelines for HR, Credit, and Commercial Lending Departments for a bank.

Kim has worked closely for years with government agencies such as SBA and corporate procurement executives to help small businesses maximize their opportunities.

With her background, Kim brings a unique perspective from all sides of the table that is unmatched. She understands the struggles that business owners, government employees, corporation and individuals have to deal with because she has lived and worked through the same issues. With that experience, she is able to help people navigate through complex situations.
