John Philip Green’s Summary

John Philip Green is the CTO of CommunityLend Inc., an innovative company in Canadian financial services. CommunityLend.com is an online loan marketplace where people can borrow money directly from investors, making a new asset class of consumer credit available to the capital markets. CommunityLend has raised over $5M in venture financing.

Previously, John founded Savvica, an online social media company focused on international education. Savvica operates LearnHub.com & JumboTests.com, two higher education, test prep, and career websites for Indian students. In 2010, the company acquired its Kleiner Perkins backed rival, StudyPlaces. Savvica is funded by Educomp Solutions Ltd. (EDSO.BO), India's largest education company, and has 75 employees based Toronto and New Delhi.

LearnHub was used by over 1 million students in the month of May 2010, making it the 30th most visited website in India (QuantCast), and the #1 education site in the world's largest student market.

Before that, John was the Director of Engineering at Affinity Labs, an online communities company in San Francisco. Affinity Labs was funded by the Mayfield Fund and Trinity Ventures. It was acquired after 14 months by Monster Worldwide (Nasdaq:MNST) for $61MM after remarkable user and revenue growth.

John is an advisor to several companies on technology, marketing, and strategy, including TasteKid, a media recommendation system; SmartHippo, a community-driven comparison shopping engine for financial services; and Ampush Media, an online education marketing company.

John created and regularly hosts the FoundersLunch.com, private functions that welcome technology startup entrepreneurs to meet—free from investors, media, and employees. He is also a frequent public speaker at technology, education, and entrepreneurship conferences.

John holds a B.Math (Honours Computer Science) from the University of Waterloo.
