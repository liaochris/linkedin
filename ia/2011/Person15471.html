Garin Toren’s Summary

Chief Operating Officer and part owner of Striata.

Head up the USA, Canadian, Central and South American Striata operations based out of NYC.

With more than 15 years dedicated internet and email experience, I specialize in 6 key areas: paperless adoption strategies, electronic document delivery, ebilling, email marketing, social media and general internet marketing strategy and execution.

Bachelors Degree in Business Administration from Pace University in NY, as well as the Nexus Post Graduate Program from the Gordon Institute of Business Science in Johannesburg, South Africa (SA). Certified SAP Functional Consultant specializing in SAP CRM and CIC.

Striata is a global eDocuments, eBilling and eMarketing software and services specialist.

Striata’s Secure eDocument Delivery and Email Bill Presentment & Payment (EBPP) are solution sets that deliver a rapid reduction in operational costs, quicker payments and an enhanced customer experience.

Striata revolutionizes the way bills, statements, policies, collection notices, letters, paystubs and other high volume system-generated documents are delivered and paid. Registration requirements are eliminated by emailing feature rich, interactive, encrypted documents directly to the inbox and enabling innovative one-click electronic payment from within the document itself.

Direct email delivery of bills and statements dramatically increases customer adoption of electronic documents, paper turn off and ePayments. This enables Striata’s clients to achieve rapid ROI; complement their existing self-service and e-communication strategies; significantly reduce paper output and to meet their carbon footprint / environmental impact targets.

As a leading international provider of electronic messaging since 1999 with more than 250 blue chip customers, Striata has operations in New York, London, Sydney, Johannesburg, Hong Kong and partners in North, Central & South America, Europe and Asia Pacific.
