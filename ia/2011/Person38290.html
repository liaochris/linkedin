Ryan Nobrega’s Experience

  • VP Product

    SendMe, Inc.

    (Privately Held; Online Media industry)

    September 2007 — Present (3 years 5 months)

    SendMe operates a variety of media entertainment properties including SendMeMobile.com (premium mobile content), SoLow.com (sweepstakes), and mbuzzy.com (mobile social network). With over $100MM in annual sales SendMe is among the largest and most successful in the US market.

    I joined after selling mbuzzy to SendMe and have continued to manage all aspects of the business. mbuzzy is now a profitable business unit, generating consistent double-digit growth quarter over quarter with a 10x increase in registered users and a 50x increase in traffic to hundreds of millions of monthly pageviews.

    mbuzzy is a mobile social network developed around a virtual goods offering and is currently the 8th most trafficked mobile web property in the entire US market, one position ahead of Yahoo!.

  • Founder / CEO

    mbuzzy inc.

    (Consumer Services industry)

    June 2003 — September 2007 (4 years 4 months)

    Raised financing, grew team, struck partnerships with major media companies, grew usage and engagement to be among top mobile properties in the US market - all while continuing to lead the technical architecture and software development of the product.
