Donna DeCosta’s Summary

At Seven Step Recruiting, we're in the business of solving large-scale recruiting problems.

The rules change when you've got to hire a lot of people in a short amount of time. Skills that work for traditional hiring no longer apply. And sometimes, the task is simply too big for the staff you've got now.

Seven Step Recruiting can help. When the need is particularly large, or time is really pressing, we have the outsourced recruitment services to assume some (or all) of the load ourselves. From 50 to 500 hires per month, at Seven Step Recruiting, we know how to hire a lot of good people quickly.

Specialties:
recruitment process outsourcing, internet recruiting, passive candidate search and pipline generation, job board management and making web 2.0 work for your company, contract negotiation, relationship management, sales incentive programs, sales management, sales best practices, compensation negotiation, sourcing, training and development of recruiters, mentoring, change managment
