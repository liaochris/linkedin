Nick Disabato’s Summary

I'm an interaction designer. I love creating products that are simple, humane, beautiful, and useful. I want to work on usability analysis, product design, or information architecture.

I have a master's degree in human-computer interaction and five years of professional experience in the field. I'm knowledgeable in writing use cases, rapidly wireframing and prototyping, conducting user testing, and working on strategy. I'm expert in OmniGraffle and Visio. I know Photoshop, Illustrator, Acrobat, and InDesign.

I love writing. I've taken photos since I was thirteen years old. I have an ardent interest in typography.
