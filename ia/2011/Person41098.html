Stacey Boguslavskaya's Summary

Stacey Boguslavskaya specializes in Social Media, start-ups and strategic planning.

After graduating from the University of Toronto Culture, Communication, Information Technology program and Sheridan’s Digital Media certification program in 2007, Stacey went on to work with such well known companies as French Connection Canada, easy-share.com, wildapriciot.com and currently holds the position of social media coordinator at the Toronto Real Estate Board. An entrepreneur, trendsetter and social addict Stacey has acquired a large network of entrepreneurs, young professionals and innovators both interested and involved in social media. Ever since she was young, Stacey has applied her interest for social media and networking to develop ventures all over the world. She previously spent six months working in Moscow with one of the largest internet holdings companies, where she was brainstorming new and innovative strategies for social media in the Russian market.

Specialties

Social Media, Digital Media, Marketing, Communications, SM Start Up Investment, Social Media Strategy development and implementation.
