Paolo Cacciato's Summary

With a bachelor degree in Japanese and Chinese Languages and Cultures and after post degree specialization trainings about International Languages, Cultures and Communication at University of Milan, I have taken part to several study experiences among Europe, Japan and China and I have worked there as teacher, interpreter and journalist.

Now I manage a multidisciplinary association focused on cultural and business mediation among Italy, China & Japan called Asian Studies Group. The Head Office is located in Milan but I cooperate directly with companies / business partners in Shanghai and Tokyo. Among our members and clients we’re proud of good collaboration with governamental institutions, research centers, universities and multinational companies operating on different fields.

I’m also working as chief editor for Corriere Asia, magazine focused on business opportunities, business culture and market’s conditions on eastern Asia.

As special consultant for Caleffi S.p.A I managed the opening of the representative office in Tokyo, hiring japanese staff and providing technical information on company law, business and social system in Japan.

In Tokyo about several business issues for small and medium enterprises I cooperate directy with Japan External Trade Organization, the governamental office for import and export’s relations on japanese market with headquarter in Akasaka district.

Specialties

Business Negotiation, Market Research, Japanese / Chinese / English / Italian language support, Investiment Research
