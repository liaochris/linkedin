David Nathanael Beck’s Summary

I am an experienced digital artist holding a bachelors in Visual Effects and presently perusing a masters in the same.

I have ten years experience as a freelance graphic designer and nearly four years experience as a visual effects artist in film having worked on two CG shorts and two feature films.

My goal is to advance my career in the 3D film industry, first as a matchmove artist, something I very much enjoy doing, but ultimately a CG Supervisor and eventually a film Director.

My life goal is to share or help share quality artistic stories with the world that will have an impact on peoples lives.

David Nathanael Beck’s Specialties:

3D matchmove and match animation, 3D animation, 3D modeling, 3D painting, Composting, Rendering.

Flash action scripting, flash animation, website design, vector art, Illustration, Graphic Design, Photography, Videography, Video Editing.

Skilled in the following programs:
Photoshop, Illustrator, Flash, Maya, MentalRay, RenderMan, Boujou, PFTrack, Matchmover, After Effects, Nuke, Unfold 3D, Deep Paint 3D, Corel Painter, Premier, Final Cut Pro.
