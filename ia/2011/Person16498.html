  • CEO

    Greener Product LLC

    (Public Company; Building Materials industry)

    January 2008 — Present (3 years 1 month)

    Greener Product LLC.,(GreenerProduct.com) Charleston, SC. Tel: 843 743 9180
    The company has developed a web based platform for architects, builders and the public that allows users to search, evaluate and document qualified green building products according to the standards established by the United States Green Build Council’s LEED Rating system and the National Association of Home Builders NAHB Green Building Standards.

    This free service is the only platform that creates a marketplace for the analysis of green building materials. The search for the most appropriate green building materials is costly, time consuming and difficult. Currently there isn’t any marketplace or software available that effectively solves this problem. Green Product and its proprietary software solve the problem

  • Founder/President

    Vencon USA LLC

    (Construction industry)

    January 1989 — February 2007 (18 years 2 months)

    Vencon USA LLC is part of an International company founded in 1989 and is recognized as a global leader in the sale of glueless or self installing hardwood flooring products with a emphasis on eco-friendly products produced from sustainable resources.

    The company sells a wide variety of products including engineered wood, cork, laminate and other prominent flooring products from quality European manufacturers. Whether its Red Oak strip hardwood flooring from the forests of Pennsylvania, Hard Maple from the forests of Canada, Brazilian Cherry exotics from the Amazon Jungle, high quality laminate flooring from the presses of Germany, or hand scraped flooring.

    In dealing with Vencon our customers have the security of buying product from a large International corporation with the ability to purchase products from the biggest and the best suppliers of quality products worldwide. Through our International focus, commitment to our customers and continual search for the best products Vencon is recognized as a world leader in the supply of globally produced flooring products.

    Vencon USA LLC has their head office in Charleston, S.C. and has distribution centers located throughout the United States.
