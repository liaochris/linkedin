Yuko Nakanishi, Ph.D., MBA's Summary

Dr. Yuko J. Nakanishi is Founder and Principal of NAKANISHI RESEARCH AND CONSULTING LLC and is Vice President of ITS-NY. For the last 15 years, she has been actively involved in ITS projects as a lead investigator, researcher and program manager. In addition to ITS, her expertise includes transportation security, public transportation, performance metrics, and customer satisfaction. She is Chair of TRB’s Subcommittee on Training, Education, and Technology Transfer; former Chair of the New York Area Data Council; former Instructor at RPI; and has authored numerous publications including three books. Dr. Nakanishi, a former Eno fellow and a recipient of the Eisenhower Fellowship, holds a Ph.D. in Transportation Planning and Engineering from the Polytechnic Institute of New York University, an M.S. in Civil Engineering from the City College of New York, an MBA in Management from Columbia University, and a B.A. in English Literature from Harvard University.
