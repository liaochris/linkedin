Chuck Garrett’s Summary

Co-founder & managing partner of Hawaiian Island Properties, Ltd. in 1988, Chuck spent ten years learning all facets of residential real estate during the record breaking Honolulu market of the late 1980’s and early 1990’s.

In 1998 an opportunity in real estate technology put Chuck on the road to Dallas, TX where for eight years he directed the implementation services group at RealPage, Inc., the recognized leader and pioneer in multifamily property management software.

Returning to Hawaii in 2006, a strong foundation in real estate sales, management and technology serves Chuck well as a Broker-Owner and Vice President of Kahala Associates where he manages the company’s global business development programs and online business applications.

Kahala Associates is a luxury focused, agent owned real estate firm, offering the ultimate in client-centered service; a full-service and independent residential brokerage.

Chuck received his Hawaii Real Estate Broker’s license in 1990, his Graduate REALTOR® Institute (GRI) designation in 1995 and the Certified Residential Broker (CRB) designation in 2008.
