Ernst Kasteleijn's Summary

I've had an interesting journey ... from 'PC gamer' to 'young & promising IT geek' to 'Industrial Automation professional' to 'IT e-commerce Entrepreneur & OpenSource developer' to 'IT Support Teamleader' to 'IT Retailer' to 'senior Forex Trader' to 'online managed investments guru' to 'Senior Linux/Unix Administrator' to 'Top Telemarketing Sales Agent' ... to ... owning my entire life & doing the things I'm passionate about thanks to the wonderful world of MLM.

I can still hear the laughter and the ridicule. And don't get me wrong, it has been no fairytale the past 7 years: working full-time on an income (as everybody does every day to be able to pay the bills), and then to MAKE TIME; to work part-time on my fortune. The price I paid / the sacrifices I had to make / starting over several times ... It has all been worth the prize! I'm no millionaire, yet I enjoy it's lifestyle benefits; travel, party, make a lot of new friends, have a lot of fun, no stress, contribute, real life international networking ... and IT remains to be a passion!

The difference being: I do what I want, when I want to do it, with the people I want to do it with.
