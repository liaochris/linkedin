Sunit Bansal's Summary

Static Timing Analysis
Placement, Clock Tree Synthesis, Routing, Crosstalk (Delay and Glitch)

1. Doing the static timing analysis & timing signoff of 1-2 SoC designs at a time..
2. Scheduling and executing the Place & Route activities on complete SoC.
3. Mentor the junior team-members to prepare them as Physical design experts.
4. Team responsibility includes:
a. Management of Design Collateral (.lib models) & doing Quality checks on them.
b. Deciding upon Block level & Full chip constraints (Timing Margins. Clock Latency targets, global constraints etc)
c. Creation of clock & reset diagram for full chip.
d. Chip level timing sign-off.
e. Chip level crosstalk delay implementation & analysis.
f. SDF generation & resolving timing issues during gate level simulations, and also during post-silicon debug.
g. Help the implementation team in resolving timing closure challenges encountered during synthesis/placement/clock tree synthesis/postroute/crosstalk optimization.
5. Part of STA, Placement, CTS, Routing and Crosstalk methodology activities.
6. Worked on flat & hierarchical designs with Gate counts till 2 million

Tapeouts across various technology nodes, 32/250nm
