Lina Barbieri's Summary

Will begin a new chapter in my communications career as a fundraiser for my alma mater. I believe in the DeSales University mission and all the good works they do on behalf of their students and hope to build and extend a philosophy of giving among the alumni base and all those who also believe in the DSU mission.

Has been in public relations and communications for more than two decades. During that time, served as senior public relations officer at several private liberal arts institutions in the Northeast. Earlier in career, provided public and media relations support for Blockbuster Video and Binney & Smith, now Crayola. For five years, I was a private public relations consultant specializing in public and media relations, strategic marketing and social media for a small group of diverse customers.

Specialties

Public and Media Relations, Publications, General and Crisis Media Training, Feature Writing, Copywriting including ghost blogging, web content, features, Social Media Training, Project Management on MarCom Efforts for Small Business, Long-time higher education public relations professional
