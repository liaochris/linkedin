Dan Henderson’s Summary

Executive, Leader and Advisor for Business Improvement;
“Focus for Clarity, Energize for Action, and Execute for Results”

A President/COO who leads turnaround profit and growth in Business Services, Technology Services, Outsourcing, and Shared Services businesses. Diverse experience leading restructuring and transformation at some of the largest global financial services, industrial, and technology companies. Applies experience to advise C-level executives, and to engage frontline service providers. With background as CFO and CIO, analyzes operations, transforms complex business problems into clear improvement plans, and executes for measurable results. Attracts and energizes top notch teams. Successful improvement initiatives include; global customer service operations, acquisition integrations, outsourcing/shared services implementation, six sigma process reengineering, information technology/ERP investments, organization restructuring, and new business development.
-Led and grew global P&Ls of $80M to $2B, Allied Signal and EDS.
-Pioneered the P&L model for global multifunctional shared business services at Allied Signal. Grew business services revenues ten fold and reduced operating costs by 25%, highest employee satisfaction in the company.
-Led marketing and sales BPO strategy to grow revenues by 15% for EDS.
-As CFO of Galileo/Apollo Services, led restructuring and increased profits $50M.
-Integrated acquisition and restructured technology services, saved $70M for JP Morgan.
-Ten former direct reports are CFO, CIO, HRO, or President in major companies.
