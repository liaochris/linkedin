Marco PIERANI's Summary

Marco is Chief of Public Affairs Office at Altroconsumo, the Italian consumers’ organization and Responsible for Lombardy Region. He joined Altroconsumo in 2002, after working as a lawyer mainly on Internet law, e-commerce, and telecommunication.
Marco holds an LL.M. Degree in European Business Law

Specialties

He specialises now in Telecommunication, Internet and Intellectual Property related issues.
