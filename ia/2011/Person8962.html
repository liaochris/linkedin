Carmine Nardis's Summary

Before establishing AvantisIT LLC he spent 3 decades in business management as a Global CIO- Chief Information Officer / Senior VP of Information Technology at top global Media and PR firms. The enterprising and visionary leader has guided multi-national IT teams in building world-class IT business solutions promoting innovation, while extenuating risks & ensuring a sustainable IT-Business alignment. In addition to contributing millions of dollars to the bottom-line of companies, he has developed sustainable, cost-effective strategies which led to business operations optimization, an increase in the systems security and performance, and hence achieved considerable savings.

As President of AvantisIT LLC, he seeks to provide CIO Expertise to your organization as well as access to a coherent IT strategy and a portfolio of services and solutions that are cost-effective, tangible and pragmatic.

A speaker of English, Italian & Spanish he has served in various Business Boards, Engineering organizations, Technology Executive advisory forums and the Mentor program at Columbia University.

Key achievements:
Leading innovation & ensuring a sustainable IT-Business alignment

Implementing effective IT governance based on triage of Applications, Infrastructure Architecture, Project Mgt (through strict portfolio mgt, clear technology road mapping & meaningful business engagement)

Transforming IT from an order-taking product provider to an indispensable business service solutions partner

Demystifying IT role, value and ROI to management

Ensuring data protection and compliance with regulatory requirements

Developing sustainable operational cost efficiency strategies

Building a high-competence IT organization -balancing technical & business expertise. Managing & measuring staff performance, developing & empowering, motivating it through cross-function/geographical programs & stimulating career path & retention initiatives
