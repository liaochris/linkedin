Brian Tedeschi's Summary

Bachelor's Degree in Journalism from the E.W. Scripps School of Journalism at Ohio University. Launched career in Columbus, Ohio, at one of the region's largest advertising and pubic relations agencies. Continued career at Burson-Marsteller in Pittsburgh and also served as vice president, marketing, for the Steel Recycling Institute - an operating unit of the American Iron and Steel Institute.

In 1998, co-founded Think Communications, Inc., a fully integrated brand consultancy agency in Pittsburgh, providing comprehensive advertising, public relations and marketing solutions for local, regional and national clients.

Specialties

Brand consultancy, public relations, advertising, marketing, word-of-mouth communication.
