Thomas Bosilevac’s Summary

For over a 14 years Thomas has been involved in the sales, management, marketing or analysis of Fortune 500 interactive projects. Currently Thomas manages the Intranet Traffic Analysis service at Microsoft; which extracts insights from internal websites and applications to: decrease technology and resource costs, increase documentation efficiency and increase knowledge across over 150K employees and vendors.

Not accepting the role of "Report Monkey", Thomas specializes in mapping business process's with actionable insights to assure data becomes an active part of the rhythm of business (RoB).

As if this wasn't enough, Thomas has extended many of his analytics extraction automation techniques (a secret ingrediant to success) at YourAnalyticsSite.com, a development and consultancy shop specializing in performance based reporting (or web analytics).

Formerly at Webtrends, Thomas was in charge of assuring the satisfaction of WebTrends most strategic clients such as: Mattel, MetLife, Orbitz.com, NBC Olympics, Realogy, Microsoft, and Monster.com. Thomas achieved a 100% client renewal rate by assuring that clients are enabled with the insights to make data driven decisions. Thomas does not stop at assuring the monitoring of data but builds the process to assure opportunities are discovered and acted upon. Thomas has particular strengths in offline lead capture conversion models, online brand awareness monitoring and SEO/SEM.

Before WebTrends, Thomas managed the in-house implementation of WebTrends Analytics at Caterpillar, while increasing the adoption of web analytics for all public facing websites.

Thomas managed the product development for WHN 2.0, the ecommerce platform standard within the network and cable television industry. Clients included NBC, ABC, FOX, MTV, Comedy Central and Playboy. From 2001- 2004, Thomas worked independently managing project development teams for companies such as Avid, The Black Dog, Virtumundo and more.
