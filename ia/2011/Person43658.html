Todd Berger, MBA’s Summary

Todd Berger, MBA has over ten years of solid marketing communications and program management experience, and thrives in highly matrix and cross-functionally demanding corporate environments. I have a proven track record of success at SAP Americas, Indigenous Designs, Cisco Systems, Autodesk Software, Oracle, Truereq, Informix, and Informatica where I've worked with both, internal sales and resellers. Moreover, I have a rich background in global customer programs, lead generation, events management with heavy end-to-end seminar development, logistics and execution, customer technology-training, as well as content and presentation development and delivery.

As a customer reference manager at Oracle and Autodesk, I frequently reviewed customer presentations and coordinated the content review and approval process. I am also a very persuasive writer and adept at message development, content management, editing, and ensuring consistency across communications as demonstrated at Cisco Systems where I authored the Cisco at Your Service Newsletter with a circulation of over 100,000 recipients, including internal sales and partners.
