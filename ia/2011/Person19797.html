Band Trackr is an innovative mobile app that mixes two components of our daily lives that we deem very important; mobile communication and music. Most of us can't go a day without either of those, we need to keep in touch and we need our music to keep our sanity.

Band Trackr takes both aspects of our lives and puts them together in a surprisingly useful, easy and fun way. Did you ever hear that your favorite artist has come to town and you missed it? That dilemma is now in the past! Notifications are now sent to you via your mobile device when your favorite artist is in town and gives you the ability to get tickets to the show right from your phone! We believe that everyone should be able to get tickets to their favorite bands even if they are not on a computer or at the ticket line.

We are currently working on the closed beta release of the application which is being developed for the Android platform.
