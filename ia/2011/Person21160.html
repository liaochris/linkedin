Recruitment Advisor - QCLNG

QGC

Public Company; BG; Oil & Energy industry

May 2010 – Present (11 months)

QGC is a leading Australian coal seam gas explorer and producer focused on developing its world-class reserves for domestic and international supply.

Australia’s energy industry is at the beginning of an era of momentous change, with multibillion-dollar investments aimed at supporting domestic consumers and pioneering the development of coal seam gas into liquefied natural gas (LNG).

Much of the attention is focused on Queensland as it emerges as one of the most abundant regions for coal seam gas in the Pacific basin. Exploration and development is concentrated in the Surat and Bowen basins, where QGC’s reserves and resources have reached more than 13 trillion cubic feet.

Our heartland is Queensland, although we have acreage in other parts of Australia. In Queensland the business holds interests in more than 40,000 square kilometres of acreage. To date, only a fraction of the total ground under lease has been explored or developed.
