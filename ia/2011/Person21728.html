John Gourdin’s Summary

I'm a Financial Services Professional with over 20 years of experience. I work with individuals and small businesses in conjunction with their legal and tax advisors to create tax-advantaged, wealth building and protection plans.

I specialize in simplicity. My goal is to empower you with the fundamental keys needed to meet your financial goals.

Ask yourself the following:

Individuals and families:
-If I died tomorrow, would my family be able to recover financially?
-How much money might I need at retirement?
-Am I saving enough?

Business Owners:
-Are you getting all of your available tax-savings?
-What happens to my business if I die? My employees?
-How can I retain my key employees?
-How do I save for retirement?

CPA's:
-Do you understand that added-value is key element to differentiate your practice for your clients?
-Are your clients optimizing the tax-advantaged plans available to them?
-Do you have a partner to handle the "leg-work" in communicating these opportunities to your clients?

Are you confident that you are doing all you can to meet your goals?

If so, congratulations.
