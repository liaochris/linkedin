Laurie Sherman’s Summary

Dr. Laurie S. Sherman was a successful entrepreneur both in PR/PA and in Childrens Play Programs, a USAID/CDC visionary strategest on AIDS and medication logistics from the PR/PA communications POV, VP of 3ed largest Arts Center in US and Co-Executive Director of the Australian National Environment Centre. Sherman has two PhDs and three Masters level degrees. She is a Member, Board of Directors of Opus Arts, a holistic health institute to support people who are newly disabled working through the arts. Sherman works closely with her extraordinary husband who was a medical nurse anesthesiologist and is a 6th level black belt Master Instructor. They live in Northern VA. Dr. Sherman is grateful for having received many honors and awards for poetry to political strategy to political-scientific theory from cities, countries and industries. She is proud to have been inducted into the PRSA College of Fellows, a lifetime achievement award, and of her spiritual ordination from an Australian Aboriginal nomadic clan of tribes which honored her with cave painting and 10 day sacred Corroboree in 1980. Dr. Sherman is also has a wonderful husband, one perfect son and two sets of A+ twin daughters, 3 siblings and her venerated father, Robert B. Sherman (Mary Poppins, Chitty Chitty Bang Bang, Its a Small World, etc.), plus her wise cat. Now, Sherman is focused on getting healthy, and enjoys reading, photography, writing poetry, painting, sculpting and making gemstone jewelry. Her goal as always is to be ready to be a channel for good works and beautiful words.

Laurie Sherman’s Specialties:

For the work she lives now, She is a poet and artist. She is a Crystal and Satya Rising Healer, She writes and sings for and with several new/old world instruments such as Quartz Crystal Bowls, Tibetan Singing Bowls, Digereedoos, Tamburas, acoustic and electric piano. She has been an expert in Public Relations. She has many skills in directing, acting and staging events and dramatic theatre.
