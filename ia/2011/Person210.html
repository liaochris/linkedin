Aaron Rom's Summary

I'm a musician, songwriter and audio engineer currently based in Fort Wayne, Indiana. I'm a graduate of Full Sail University, having completed a Bachelor's Degree in Music Business and an Associate's Degree in Recording Arts. I've played drums and percussion for over 20 years, playing everything from Beethoven to Coltrane to Meshuggah. My main passion is recording and performing music, but I'm interested in all aspects of the music industry. I currently work at Sweetwater Sound as a Sales Engineer, and it was definitely the right decision for me at this point in time!

Specialties

Session Recording, Live Performance, Songwriting, Remixing, Keynote Presentations, Public Speaking
