Scott Burton Williams's Summary

Scott Burton Williams is the General Manager for ATS Tech Solutions, a Microsoft Gold Partner located in Gainesville. Georgia. ATS specializes in help business owners manage their IT so they can manage their business by providing Microsoft technology experts focused on uncompromising service. Scott is responsible for sales and marketing; managing the current day-to-day operations; developing and implementing the long-term goals of ATS; and assisting customers with their information technology needs.

ATS Tech Solutions helps businesses with:

* Network and business computer systems design.

* Implementation of computer networks and software.

* Maintaining their computer networks.

* Information technology consulting.

* Project management.

A South Carolina native, Scott graduated from North Georgia College, the Senior Military College of Georgia, where he earned a bachelor's degree in Business Management. He also earned a master's degree in International Business Management from Georgia State University. Scott joined the ATS Tech Solutions executive team in 2004. Prior to ATS Tech Solutions, Scott served as an Information Technology Manager, Senior Business Technology Consultant, and Network Engineer. Previous to his career in information technology, Mr. Williams served 15 years in the United States Army as a logistics officer with combat tours in Iraq, Saudi Arabia, and Kuwait. Scott is an active member of Pi Kappa Phi Fraternity and is a current or past board member of several community organizations. He and his family reside in Hoschton, Georgia.
