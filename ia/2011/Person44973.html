Victoria Lynn Childress’s Summary

In every sense of the word, I'm a writer. My passion, education and experience has allowed me to develop an expertise in the craft of writing. My interest is not and will never be in a specific field. My philosophy of writing, is that in order to master the art, you must be able to adapt and fully understand any subject matter well enough to put into, not your own words, but someone else's. I must be able to understand a subject as if it were my own in order to effectively write about it. Therefore, while time and practice must be spent in areas of diction, word-crafting and grammar, an equal amount of time must be spent listening, imagining and grasping, fully, the meaning of my subject.

My professional experiences include writing for a wide variety of mediums with various objectives -expanding my writing ability. *Editorial*Creative* Web Copy *Advertising Copy*Direct Marketing*Broadcast copy. I've written for music artists, doctors, boating club owners, realtors, entertainers and promoters, business owners, corporations and non profit organizations.

My goals are to advance into professional projects that include children's literature, film/screen writing, music and fictional.

Victoria Lynn Childress’s Specialties:

Adept at translating faint ideas into simple yet powerful and smooth prose that educate and inform readers; contemporary approach at translating pictorial and conceptual branding into comprehensive literature/ copy, industry specific wordsmithing and competitive in approach, theory, skill and nature - cohesively merging conceptual ideas into objective concepts.
