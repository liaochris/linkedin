As a Customer Service Consultant I help companies improve support offerings by:
- Working with key customers to ensure satisfaction and help drive new business
- Re-defining customer support policies, procedures and methodologies
- Conveying a passion for enhancing the customer’s experience to all support staff
- Implementing customer focused best practices and tools

Example Client:

Client was expanding internationally and was opening support/development offices in Taiwan and Tokyo. The existing tools, policies, procedures and methodologies were antiquated at best and wanted to take support to the next level.

- Changed from existing CRM to SugarCRM with JIRA plugin, this was an economical and scale-able decision that allowed customers to open their own support issues and have them automatically routed to the appropriate group and track the progress as the issue was worked
- To keep customers informed we implemented Twitter to provide regular updates on new products and ventures to our customers, clients, and partners
- To help distribute information we implemented Confluence (Wiki) to distribute different levels of information internally and externally.
- Client needed a global focus, we created a knowledge base that allowed support engineers from all locations to share information on problem resolution as opposed to the prior practice which was each engineer re-worked the problem from scratch
- Delivered customer satisfaction training, with an emphasis on enhancing the customer’s experience in dealing with the customer (i.e. Setting proper expectations, then consistently meeting or exceeding those expectations)
- Defined appropriate metrics to ensure the customer is always getting the best level of service possible
- Created a rewards and recognition program regularly rewarding support engineers for achieving defined metrics and customer satisfaction goals
