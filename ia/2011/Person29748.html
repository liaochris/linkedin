Meredith Murphy's Summary

I knew pretty early that I wanted to be myself.
As such, I've trusted my inner compass and as a result have had a wonderfully expressive work life and met many cool people. {thanks all of you!}

Philosophically, I am inspired by the drive within us to individuate, differentiate and self-express. I believe in our innate value and relevance; to be ourselves; to make the universe evolve by contributing what only we could--this is our purpose. I believe in desire. I trust it and think furthermore, it’s directional! I believe in doing what makes us happy and gives us more peace.

I believe we're living in an unprecedented time and on an ascending energy arc. The multi-dimensional nature of our experience is becoming conscious for many of us. I notice a trend of people living from their inner guidance and in a heart-felt way. I am curious about how our lifestyles and culture will evolve to align with this emergent experience.

I created Expect Wonderful as a means of investigating and participating in this leading edge life. I function mainly as a practical explainer; a translator of experience. I create and facilitate events, channel messages, write, teach (via live classes and self-paced experiences), and provide a limited amount of personalized services. I also host a lively Facebook gathering place!

I am always interested in conversations with authentic partners in this great task of evolutionary transformation.

If you'd like to get in touch, please do so.
It's time to expect more... ExpectWonderful.com
May your day be just what you want.

Specialties

Giving myself permission to do what feels right and good.
Enjoying food and music such as to rival any hedonist.
Feel one with everything--nature, the universe, you...
Almost always happy and in a good mood...why not?
