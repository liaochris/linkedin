This project was completed on time and within budget, which the client was more than pleased about.

    I have also worked on large multi million pound construction projects over the years and recently I have been involved with measuring a 200 000sq ft central London hotel that is to be refurbished into luxury apartments. It is a revival scheme to create 100 prime apartments with underground parking overlooking Hyde Park to be completed by 2010.

    This programme is concerned with mapping of the earth's surface and the management of information gathered. Combining the skills of physics, mathematics, environmental science and IT, you learn how to map, measure and analyse spatial measurements for a variety of uses.
