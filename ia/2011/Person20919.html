Jim Derrick’s Summary

I have spent over 20 years selling pressure sensitive tape and related products to end users and distributors. I have held four different jobs, Technical Sales Rep, Territory Manager, Product Manager and National Sales Manager for Commercial Products.
My goal is to find a manufacturer or distributor who will value my strengths as a hard worker who is dedicated to understanding my product and the company I work for inside and out. I take those abilities to create value with end users and distributors. My strength is creating a product mix at distributors which enables me to grow the "P" in the P&L.

Jim Derrick’s Specialties:

I approach sales with more understanding of the total picture of a company because of my background in Product Management, Territory Sales and National Sales. I have extensive experience managing to a P&L and can use my knowledge of manufacturing, logistics and distribution to add value to my sales by creating greater profit.
