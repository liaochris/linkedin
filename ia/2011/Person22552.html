Joshua Skolnick's Summary

Natural Resources Professional with 16 years of professional experience in restoration and management of ecosystems in the Upper Midwest and Great Lakes region. Specializing in sustainable landscaping and land use analysis, management planning, invasive species management, stormwater management with native vegetation, wetland mitigation, and monitoring and management of native plant communities. I am working towards increased public awareness of the importance of sustainable landscaping and native vegetation for clean water, clean air, cost savings and climate change mitigation.

Specialties

Site analysis and natural resource planning, wetland delineation/determination, US Army Corps Section 404 and local wetland and environmental permitting, plan and site review, identification and characterization of native plant species and communities, invasive species biology and control. Lake County Certified Wetland Scientist since 2007, Kane County Wetland Review Specialist, Certified Illinois Pesticide Applicator in the following categories: Aquatic, Rights of Way, Ornamentals and Turf.
