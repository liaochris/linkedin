Life Cycles Film

Entertainment industry

June 2010 – October 2010 (5 months)

www.lifecyclesfilm.com

Did the sound design and 5.1 mix for this incredible movie.
Lifecycles is a movie about the experience of riding bicycles. The creation of filmmakers Ryan Gibb and Derek Frankwoski it is now touring the world and out of DVD, Blu-ray and iTunes.

Ryan and Derek were looking for a composer early on in the project and I was able to put them in contact with Ajay Bhattacharyya, of the amazing Vancouver duo Data Romance and drummer for the Zolas, who composed the majority of the music for the film and worked directly with us.

Visit it at www.lifecyclesfilm.com
and dataromance.ca to sample the EPICNESS

Sound Designer

The Escape Movie

Entertainment industry

May 2010 – July 2010 (3 months)

www.theescapemovie.com

Had the opportunity to do sound design and a 5.1 mix for the short movie The Escape.

Filmed in Los Angeles, The Escape is the Master's thesis of American Film Institute darlings James Connelly, Paul Michael Merryman, Justin LaForge and Cinematographer Wes Cardino.

The movie allowed me to work with Academy Award Nominee Craig Berkey and Emmy Award Winner Supervising Sound Editor Robert Grieve who were consultants on the project. As usual I created custom sound effects and did all the foley for the film as well.

Sound Design Teaching and Production Assistant

Vancouver Film School

Educational Institution; Higher Education industry

March 2009 – June 2010 (1 year 4 months)

Curriculum development. Instruction in Foley, ADR, Microphones, Pro Tools, DVD Authoring. Mix Studio calibration and use. Tutoring in Post Production, SFX Recording, Editing, Mastering, Signal Flow. Game Audio, implementation Tools, Wwise, FMOD, Max MSP, Pure Data. Sound Design for Short Films and Animations.

That job allowed me to expand my knowledge and experience in audio like no other studio in Vancouver would have allowed me to. I routinely did Post Production editorials and mixes for VFS films. I interacted with actors and young directors when recording them for dialogue replacement.

I have installed and wired a dual operator Avid D-Control Icon system including Pro Tools HD3 rigs as well as a Pro Tools digital dubber. I have a strong understanding of signal flow which allowed me to create custom patch bays and wiring for the VFS sound facility.

I was also involved in curriculum creation and teaching of classes including Synchronization, Foley and ADR, Microphones, Pro Tools, DVD Authoring and media creation in Final Cut Suite and Adobe Complete Suite.
