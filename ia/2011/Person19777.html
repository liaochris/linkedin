Jeannene Edwards's Summary

Hi there . . . Over the past several years I have been successfully merchandising model homes for national builders. These models were each designed to target a specific market. My goal was to create emotional connections, WOW factors and great first impressions for the prospective buyer so they could easily visualize themself living there, entertaining there, and ultimately calling it "home"!

Because of our current sluggish real estate market I want to share these same Home Staging strategies and services with real estate professionals, investors and individual sellers who want to experience quicker more profitable sales. You can contact me for a list of my top staging tips and other great info designed to put your listing ahead of the competition!

Specialties

I specialize in all aspects of residential interior design, commercial design, home staging, model merchandising, and staging for special needs and events. I am also CAPS and HSR certified. I consult with real estate professionals, investors, builders, event coordinators, and individuals who are looking for that all important edge in a competitive market.
