Elementary School Teacher

Itamarati Educational Center

Primary/Secondary Education industry

2005 – 2008 (3 years)

Thereza assumed a leading role in developing new pedagogical actions by applying her knowledge of Constructivist Teaching, which suggests that learning is more effective when a student is actively engaged in the learning process rather than attempting to receive knowledge passively. Educating students from 6-10 years old at a school with social inclusion practices, Thereza has developed and implemented educational activities for students with Down Syndrome, disseminating values of equality and access to knowledge in a democratic way amongst her students.

She stood out in teaching Math, developing a project on The History of the Numbers, emphasizing the social role of this science. Her students demonstrated superior performance after being presented to the functions of mathematical calculations in human daily relations. When leaving this position in search for new opportunities, Thereza received a great job review and special thanks for services rendered to the Educational Institution and still maintains strong ties with former colleagues and students.
