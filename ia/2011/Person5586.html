Bob Jones's Summary

I have over 40 years of experience working with business information systems.

My business focus targets business owners and managers of $2M-$50M businesses in Northwest Washington and southern BC.

I help businesses tune their "information engines" so that information flows smoothly into the system, from one system to another, and from the system into the minds of business decision makers.

The Socrates Group provides a range of services from evaluation of business needs and recommendation of appropriate software solutions, to installation, integration and customization of accounting, shipping, distribution and warehouse management systems and even custom development of entire systems. Please visit our web site at thesocratesgroup.com for more information about our business.

Specialties

We are experienced in upgrading old accounting systems to a newer solutions, integrating accounting, shipping, warehousing and other systems and developing custom reports and solutions.
I currently specialize in Microsoft-based solutions using SQL Server and ASP or .NET and I have considerable experience in PHP, mySQL and Visual Basic. Dick (my partner) is experienced with many accounting packages and expert with Crystal Reports and with ClipperShip, a multi-carrier shipping platform.
