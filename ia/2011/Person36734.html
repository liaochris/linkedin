Richard Bunkiewicz's Summary

27 years experience in the electronic alarm monitoring industry.

20 years at Affiliated Central, a national wholesale alarm monitoring center responding to burglar, fire, and life safety signals from over 100,000 subscribers. Managed an operational team consisting of Shift Supervisors, Dispatchers, Customer Service Representatives, Data Entry and technical personnel.

7 years experience at Dictograph Security Systems as both Central Station and Service Manager.

Lifetime (and ongoing) experience as a professional musician (bassist) and recording artist.

Specialties

Experience in proprietary alarm automation systems such as MAStermind and legacy systems. Have managed people and technology in 24 hour customer service environments. Responsibilities have run the gamut from ensuring a high level of customer service at all times to a customer base of over 100,000 customers to making sure there was enough toilet paper in the bathroom. Fluent in most Microsoft Office products.
