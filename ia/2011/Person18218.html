Specialist Engineering, Construction, and Real Estate headhunter focusing on difficult to fill Infrastructure roles in the mid to senior levels across the Middle East, Asia, and Australia.

We are currently focusing on major Infrastructure (Airports, Rail, Marine, Build) projects across the Middle East, and Asia, and Australia. All these roles are live, and are suitable for Regional and Western Expatriates.



Now part of Jackson-Scott, a Technical Executive Search business that provide a number of Recruitment services to clients on an International basis. We focus on Mid to Senior level opportunities across the Technical markets, working within the Build & Infrastructure, Rail, Power, Utilities, and Gas & Oil sectors in the Middle East, Asia, Australia and Europe.

Previous experience of working within the Real Estate/Construction industry for the past 15 years, with the last 10 years recruiting Senior Professionals across a number of sectors, predominantly Real Estate and Construction, within a variety of disciplines.

Personally, I have also provided a consultancy service to a number of clients in terms of staff retention, salary reviews, and interviewing skills.

I am always very interested to talk to Senior professionals in relation to new opportunties, their experiences and the market in general.
