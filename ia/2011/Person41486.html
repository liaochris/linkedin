Steve Bathe’s Summary

Steve Bathe has been leading high performing teams at world class companies for over twelve years. He presents a unique blend of proven business acumen, technical ability, strong leadership and interpersonal skills. His career is noted for significant accomplishments in strategic management, operations management, project management, quality, engineering, training and team building.

Steve’s style emphasizes a culture of discipline, urgency, continuous improvement and teamwork. He employs his knowledge of Lean principles and tools, problem solving, and project management skills to target significant cost savings and process improvements at all levels of an organization. Steve has saved companies millions in reduced material usage, labor, scrap, downtime, and capital costs.

In 2010 Steve founded Strategic Partners of the Carolinas, a consulting firm that offers assistance to businesses in strategic planning, business process improvement, and project management. His company has a core mission to bring excellent and effective services while observing the highest standards of business ethics. His company offers these services to both the for-profit and non-profit sectors. The company collaborates directly with a network of strategic partners to offer a wide range of options to the business community.

Steve’s interest in the business community led him to join three partners as a principal at the Entrepreneur Forum, a 1,600 member organization dedicated to helping diverse business professionals to plan, associate and grow by facilitating communication, professional development and best practices within a strong and vital association of entrepreneurial communities. He is also the leader of Anderson Business Strategy and Networking Meetup group in Anderson, SC. This knowledge based association of over 60 members is dedicated to growing each others businesses in an environment that emphasizes the open exchange of ideas and assistance.
