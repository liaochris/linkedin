David Koopmans’s Summary

Marketing Manager with Aconex, the largest global provider of online document management and collaboration services. My focus and experience are particularly in B2B marketing, with a passion for the use of technology in marketing.

Before this role, I had a B2B marketing consulting business (Mokum Marketing) for seven years, with customers particularly in the the technology and services markets.

I've held senior marketing positions in technology companies, developing new products, evaluating markets, developing brands,driving lead generation as well as Business Development. Before that, I managed the operations of a Direct Marketing bureau, at a time when people just started to see the importance of one-to-one communication.

I've grown up with technology and in particular the internet as a marketing and communications tool.

I write a marketing blog called "Business of Marketing and Branding" (www.mokummarketing.com/blog) and have contributed to two collaborative books, "The Age of Conversation".

For what it is worth, I also have a Graduate Diploma in Marketing from Swinburne University in Melbourne.
