Mark Anthony's Summary

A quick learning generalist, I see both the forest *and* the trees, with a balanced grasp of details and focus on results.

First and foremost, I am a communicator, at my best when analyzing complex needs and problems and transforming them into clear solutions and communications.

In other words, I'm pretty damned good at making things easy to understand and at turning chaos into order.

Over 20 years experience and a rare combination of technical, business, communication, media, and people skills lie behind my capacity to identify and align business and technology needs.

My success often lies in my ability to help people see things in new ways and from new perspectives. I am trained in both quantitative and qualitative research methods, and I am driven by a passion for user-centered design and usability.

I possess a unique intuitive ability to get to the root of business pain, organizational roadblocks, system failures, process breakdowns and communication impasses. .

In addition to holding a CSSA (Certified SonicWALL Security Administrator), multiple IBM and HP certifications, and a long history of IT experience, I also have a wealth of practical business and management experience.

I love all things geeky, but I'm more interested in the big picture and strategy aspects than being the guy who sits and codes for days on end (though I do that, too). I prefer to work on the analysis, design, writing, and usability ends of the project, and I thrive when I can experience a variety of new challenges and projects. I prefer to work with people than to hide from them.

My passion for technology goes beyond an appreciation for gadgets and apps to a deep curiosity about human/technology interaction and its effects on popular culture and individual and social identity.
