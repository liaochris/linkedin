Vivek Jayaram's Experience

Founder

Jayaram Law Group

Law Practice industry

August 2009 – Present (1 year 7 months)

President/Founder of Jayaram Law Group, a law firm providing legal services to small and medium-sized businesses around the United States, with an emphasis on commercial collections, copyright and trademark registration and litigation, and corporate law.

Vivek assists a wide variety of clients in handling critical litigation matters in both State and Federal Courts, from shareholder disputes and breach of business agreements to trade secret and copyright litigation. He also advises clients on creative and effective strategies to collect on accounts receivables from customers around the world. Vivek also acts as local counsel in the Northern District of Illinois for attorneys around the country.

Vivek also represents app developers and gamers around the country, assisting them in registering the source code for their products. Visit www.copyrightyourapp.com for more concerning these services.

Vivek also offers a range of corporate and business services to medium-sized corporations and private businesses. In many cases, he serves as general counsel for corporations and represent them in all aspects of their corporate life – from incorporation to formation and operation. His corporate business practice intersects with closely related practices, and has been involved in many facets of corporate governance, including director and officer liability. He routinely counsels businesses in connection with their ongoing contractual relationships with domestic and international customers, suppliers, and distributors.
