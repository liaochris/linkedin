Michael Gough's Summary

Entrapreneurial executive with significant experience of the IT consulting and services industry, and not-for-profit membership organisations. Recent leadership experience in Information Security & Assurance and government/industry relations.

Well rounded experience in general management, strategy and marketing, mergers/acquisitions, integration and product/service re-alignment, and corporate governance associated with 3rd sector businesses.

Computer Scientist, Chartered Engineer (CEng), Chartered IT Professional (CITP), and Fellow of both the Institute for Engineering & Technology (IET) and the British Computer Society (BCS).

IT Industry commentator - computing, skills, outsourcing, information technology.

Specialties
