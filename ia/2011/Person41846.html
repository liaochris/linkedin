Richard Stroupe's Summary

As SVP, General Manager, Mr. Stroupe is responsible for all business operations, finance and business development activities for NCI’s National Security Group.

Before joining NCI, Mr. Stroupe was the founder and CEO of TRS Consulting Inc., which was acquired by NCI in July 2009 (Link: http://bit.ly/R4Lcl).

Mr. Stroupe is a visionary leader with more than 12 years of diverse experience in supporting mission-critical applications for the U.S. Intelligence Community. Before TRS, Mr. Stroupe delivered software development and database administration support at companies, such as Oracle Corporation, BAE Systems, and SAIC.

Along with his consulting experience, Mr. Stroupe is a published author and has served on several national journal editorial boards supporting Oracle Database technologies. Mr. Stroupe has also served as a lecturer at various Washington, DC, area universities and corporations. Mr. Stroupe is the 2010 recipient of the SmartCEO Smart 100 Award for top executives in the Washington, DC, area and currently sits on the advisory board for Appalachian State University’s Walker College of Business.

Mr. Stroupe received a Bachelor of Science degree from Appalachian State University and a Master of Science degree from The George Washington University. In addition, he is a graduate of numerous executive education programs, including The Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania and The Owner/President Management (OPM) program from Harvard Business School.
