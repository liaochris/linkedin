Liz Thompson's Summary

I am a full-time mom, a part-time freelance writer and virtual assistant.

ThisFullHouse.com is where I enjoy writing about the trials and tribulations of raising 4 children (ages 17, 15, 12 and 9) a rambunctious lab (a.k.a. Doofus-Dawg) a delinquent cat and killer dust bunnies...since 2003. I am a charter member of the BlogHerAds Network, listed on Alltop.com, as well as Top 100 Mom Blogs and chosen as one of the inaugural Babble's 50 Best Mommybloggers.

This Full House Reviews is where I enjoy writing family-friendly, informational and (hopefully) entertaining posts about products and services that perhaps help make life for families (like mine) a little easier. My shopping blog is listed on the Top 10 Product Review Mom Blogs on Blogs.com.

I love sharing information on products and services that perhaps help make life for families (like mine) a little easier, or give back to the community, by partnering with brands, networking with writers and connecting with REAL families (ditto) online and...yes...I do this AND more, ALL from my kitchen table, mostly.

Feel free to visit my portfolio found at LizThompson.info
