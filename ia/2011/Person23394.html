Karen Armstrong's Summary

Karen Armstrong, founder of Inside Out, has had a life long interest in human behaviour and has explored many avenues in her efforts to discover tools and techniques that will support her journey of self development and improvement.

While in the corporate world, Karen successfully filled positions in customer service, sales and human resources but noticed that many people were struggling with balance and quality of life issues. In 1997, her quest to understand why we do what we do found expression in the powerful personality system known as the Enneagram. Karen founded Inside Out in 2000.

Karen would like to introduce the Enneagram system to 500,000 people in the next 5 years. She is also committed to bringing forth an environmentally sustainable, spiritually fulfilling and socially just human presence on this planet as the guiding principle of our times.
