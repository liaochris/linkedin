Jeffrey Cooper's Summary

I have built over 5 years expertise in Marketing Communications and Advertising. Coupled with my professional day-to-day schedule as General Manager for Step Change Marketing, I spend the time I really enjoy advising the not-for-profit sector pro bono on a host of different projects (www.goodreturn.org/, www.dayofdifference.org.au/, http://heapsdecent.com/), tapping the final key-strokes to the most powerful Marketing Strategy book to hit the scene in years (well okay, maybe just this month - stay tuned!) and lecturing at a number of Sydney universities (Sydney University, Nortre Dame, University Of NSW). The rest of the time I'm producing music and seeking out opportunities for my own business's growth and the growth I drive for clients.

I started my professional career in 2004, ambitiously starting my own business in events management. 13 months later, with meager money made, I decided to seek out the best in the Marketing Communications Industry to learn how to hone my skills.

My next position was with Onepartners, a full-service brand experience agency, offering an end-to-end service; including; strategy, creative, design, production and 360 degree project management.

I joined thebrandshop as a Business Innovations Strategist in September 2008, a full service agency with a focus on communications and digital. Here I learnt to deliver "Outstanding Results, by Always Standing Out."

It wasn't long though until 'Standing Out' became 'Stepping Out' with a team of highly talented and driven Directors to form what is now the strategic powerhouse of - Step Change Marketing.

We craft strategic solutions that help our clients to unlock the growth and hidden potential that was previously invisible - we get a real kick out of doing so! We've won a client a week since we started and we're not slowing down.

That's the short on me, look forward to meeting you,

Cheers,
