Brian Anthony Hernandez's Summary

Most people call me "BAH" - as in the sound sheep make - because of my initials.

I recently joined Mashable, where I edit news, features and promotional content as the online publication's copy editor. I also occasionally write stories and fill in as an assistant news editor.

As a military child, I had lived in three countries before the age of 5: United States, Japan and Philippines. After my family and I left the beaches of Japan, we settled down in Scottsbluff, a small town in western Nebraska, where I eventually graduated from high school in 2004. I studied journalism at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln before leaving early to accept a job in Phoenix in 2009.

A long way from the cornfields of the Midwest, I now live in New York City, where I work hard and play hard. I'm a fan of change, Hot Cheetos, optimists, fried chicken, dance clubs, smiles and, of course, grammar.

Specialties

Editing | Reporting | Online producing | Using social media, SEO, HTML, Wordpress and other CMS programs | Paying it forward
