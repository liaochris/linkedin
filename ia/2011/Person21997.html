Jo Jinks MCIPD’s Summary

My use of LinkedIn is mainly to provide information on services that my Company can offer as well as the work that I have carried out either as a HR Contractor or the work that have been provided via my Virtual Assistant business.

HRVA is a part of Do You Need My Help Ltd and specialises in offering Virtual HR Services to start up companies and established SME businesses within the West Midlands and surrounding areas.

I pride myself on the flexibility and professionalism of my service, and always aim to work in partnership with clients, to ensure the brief is fully met. I believe it is this collaborative approach that sets me apart from my competitors. In addition, I aim to provide a specialist, personalised service that meets your exact requirements and exceeds expectations in terms of value and accountability.

I have over 15 years experience in HR, Recruitment, Employee Relations, Absence Management, Employment Law, Policy Writing, Administration and Management Information (see attached flyer for more information) and I have experience within a number of different industries including; IT, Logistics, Utilities and Financial Services.

If you have employee issues and/or are looking for assistance in improving your HR processes and practices, then please contact me now on 0121 288 5251 or email joanne.jinks@hrva.co.uk for an informal discussion, where I would be happy to work with you in achieving your goals.
