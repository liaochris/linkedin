Faisal Azam’s Summary

Faisal Azam approaches filmmaking with the eye of an artist and the instincts of a natural storyteller. Born in Karachi, Pakistan, Faisal grew up in the UAE and Saudi Arabia and came to the United States at age 15 to complete his education. Graduating magna cum laude from UC Berkeley with a B.A. in Rhetoric, Faisal began his professional life as an advertising communications analyst in Los Angeles where he evaluated television commercials and marketing campaigns for blue chip clients like Ralston Purina, Procter & Gamble, Campbell Soup, Vanguard, Hennessy, Nestle and Oral B.

With little more than an instinctive love of cinema, strong aesthetic sensibilities and a hunch to follow in the footsteps of his exemplar, French filmmaker Alain Resnais, Faisal moved to New York City in 2004 to become an editor. Discovering he had an innate feel for rhythm, pacing and emotion, Faisal developed a keen sense of narrative structure and a deep understanding of storytelling as a precise and exacting art. Versatile and adept at his craft, Faisal has worked with fine artists, independent filmmakers and high profile clients like Nike, Rolling Stone, LVMH, Illy, Consumer Reports magazine, and Academy Award winning director Zana Briski. Films he has edited, produced or directed have screened at numerous domestic and international film festivals including: Sundance, Comic-Con International, Clermont-Ferrand, Santa Fe, Ars Electronica, SITGES International, New Filmmakers New York and Digital Narrative Arts.

Faisal Azam’s Specialties:

Film editing, editorial consulting, title design, art direction (movie posters and websites), film direction and production, story consulting, script analysis, advertising strategy, branding and identity, creative communications.
