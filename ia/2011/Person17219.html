Heather Ramirez's Summary

Five years of experience in public relations, program development, office management, and customer service. Dynamic, goal-oriented, and resourceful individual committed to quality, accountability, and professional growth. Driven to tackle new challenges and learn new concepts.

I currently serve as the Youth Programs Coordinator at The Volunteer Center, South Bay-Harbor-Long Beach. The Volunteer Center's Youth Programs lead our youth in promoting positive social change in the community through meaningful volunteerism and active citizenship.

Volunteer Center Youth Coalition
VCYC is a program that brings together high school students from the South Bay area to create and implement region-wide community service projects. VCYC members develop leadership skills, advocate for youth volunteerism, and serve the community.

Summer Service-Learning Program
Volunteer.Involvement.Values.Action (VIVA) is a program that allows youth (ages 13-18) to have a meaningful and fun volunteering experience while developing useful skills that will help achieve future goals. VIVA participants learn about community needs and become agents of change.

If you are 18 years old or younger and would like to get more involved in the community through volunteering, visit our youth programs website at www.volctr-sobay.org/youth_prog.htm. If you are a nonprofit organization with volunteering opportunities for youth, please email me at youthprogs@volctr-sobay.org.
