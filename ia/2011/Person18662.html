MANOJ JAIN’s Summary

I am Manoj Jain, a 36 year old I.T. professional & entrepreneur from Kolkata, India. I have been working in the I.T. services field for the past 14 years. I founded my first I.T. business in 1993. I have a special interest in the internet services field including internet consulting & marketing, Web solutions and BPO.

I am the Founder, Managing Director & CEO of Destiny Infotek Ltd. and Ishika Technologies Pvt Ltd, Kolkata – a leading Web design & Web development, and internet marketing company.

I love reading & writing blogs, and I do a lot of blogging. I own and manage a number of blogs on a wide-range of topics like Bollywood, health care, yoga, homeopathy, recipes, honeymoon tips, gadgets, & information on India travel and tourism. You can find a list of my blogs on my site, www.manoj.co.in. I also own India's leading information portal site - Hindustanlink.com - founded & launched in the year 2000.

My Journey:

After losing my father when I was just 5 years old, I started my schooling in Imphal (Manipur), located in the North East part of India, where I graduated with a concentration in Science. As a teenager, I started to see the power and influence of the computer, & that is when I started to acquire my diverse set of technical I.T. skills. After launching my business career in Imphal, in 1993 in the education & training side of I.T., I diversified into the computer hardware and software business in 1994. My love for Bengal compelled me to close my Imphal-based business in 1998 & moved to Kolkata where I continued to work in the I.T. industry. I have served as the COO of 3rd Millennium Group’s Computer Training Division. I oversaw the operations of 3rd Millennium's 20+ computer training centers in Eastern India. With my technical skills in computers & my interest in the Internet, I entered the dot.com world in August, 2000 when I launched the informative and unique portal – Hindustanlink.com. Presently, I manage Ishika Technologies Pvt. Ltd.
