Diana Corso’s Summary

My name is Diana Corso and I am the Executive Director of disABLEDperson Inc. a 501 (c) 3 public charity whose mission is to reduce the unemployment rate amongst individuals and veterans with disabilities.

As a founder of our organization, I have guided it to where it is today a branded name in the disability community. Our www.disABLEDperson.com job board was named the U.S. Government Website of the week for the week of October 26, 2009. Our Job Opportunities for Disabled American Veterans (JOFDAV.com) site is a non profit Ally of the U.S. DOD in their "America Supports You" initiative.

We have forstered relationships with many federal agencies as well as private companies in helping our community in their quest for sustainable employment.

disABLEDperson, Inc. as a Microsoft IT Academy is in partnership with the State of California, Department of Vocational Rehabilitation as we offer DOR consumers as a public service training in Microsoft Office and Dynamics, giving them marketable job skils.

Ours is just to serve.




Our organizational goal simply is to serve.
