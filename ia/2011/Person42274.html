Stephan Saaltink's Summary

Stephan Saaltink (1953) has an outstanding career as a typographic and editorial designer. He started Typography & Other Serious Matters in Rotterdam, and worked at Total Design, the famous Corporate Identity agency in Amsterdam.
As an artdirector he joined a leading Dutch newspaper (NRC Handelsblad) and
an opinion magazine (Vrij Nederland). He introduced two typefaces, Lexicon (Bram den Does) and Capitolium (Gerard Unger), to a bigger audience.
Saaltink is dedicated to design education. Most recently he was the director of the Willem de Kooning Academy Rotterdam University.

Specialties

Ordening. Structuring. PROGRAMMING. Simplifying complexity.
