C. Sunny Martin’s Summary

Most recently Mr. Martin was the founder and CEO of Who's Who Publishing Company, (www.whoswhopublishing.com). Under his twenty-plus years of leadership, the firm expanded its unique brand of directories into (26) twenty-six cities and markets. As a result, the company became recognized as the largest African-American directory publisher in the country. Additionally, Sunny led the company to publish similar directories geared towards the Latino/Hispanic market.

In February of 2009, Mr. Martin sold Who's Who Publishing Company to an out of state firm located in Detroit, Michigan, in a multimillion-dollar deal. Under his reign and tenure at Who's Who, he led the company by successfully publishing a (59) fifty-nine books. Additionally, Sunny is credited with building one of the largest networks of influential and affluent African-American achievers in the nation.

Mr. Martin is has now focused his energy and talents in completing a self-publishing project titled, "Shake a Hand, Make a Friend, and Get a Check." which he intends to release later this year.
