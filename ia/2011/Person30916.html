Melinda Roberts's Summary

Melinda (Mindy) Roberts is the personality behind TheMommyBlog.net. She is a divorced, single mom to three young children, survived the ups and downs of Silicon Valley’s tech bubble, and worked in the nonprofit sector for twelve years before focusing mostly on her writing.

She is a panelist on Momversation.com and has been featured on CNN Live Prime Time, Oprah, and most of the major networks, and also consults with companies such as Johnson & Johnson, Pampers, Proctor & Gamble, Clorox, Kellogg, and Google for her parenting and blogging expertise.

Mindy Roberts is known as a key influence in the area of moms and the internet. A mommy blogger since 2002, Mindy's TheMommyBlog.com is an online diary which journeys the ups-and-downs of motherhood. She is also the author of Mommy Confidential: Adventures from the Wonderbelly of Motherhood.

She is also a member of Federated Media's Parenting and Entertainment Federations.
