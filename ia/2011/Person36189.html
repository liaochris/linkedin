Ray Flores’s Summary

In his role as Director of KW Commercial, Ray Flores oversees the operation of the various commercial departments related to real estate investment, leasing, consulting, representation, and development. Ray brings more than 12 years in real estate expertise. He is experienced in the areas of retail and development. Ray is a licensed Arizona real estate sales person.

From the start of his real estate career, Ray took a, then, small retailer to new heights. Stating with 17 units in the southwest, Ray assisted Cold Stone Creamery's expansion plans to the east. Ultimately placing Cold Stone in Times Square, NY.

From there, Ray has held leadership positions with retailers like Jamba Juice, Bank of America (CBRE/GCSG), and others. The growth that Ray accomplished with those retailers lead him to begin his own book of business, and land at KW Commercial.
