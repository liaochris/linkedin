Maryann Rivers-Moore's Summary

Maryann is a photographer, writer and photojournalist in conservation and the environment, lifestyle, travel and tourism, social issues and history.

Maryann is based in Hilton near Pietermaritzburg in KwaZulu Natal, South Africa. She grew up and lived in Cape Town until 2008,and completed a Fine Art Degree and Journalism Studies at Rhodes University, graduating in 1995.

She has been doing journalism and photography work, much of it in the environmental and conservation sector, since going freelance again in May 2009.

With fifteen years’ experience in the South African media industry, Maryann has worked in the stock library sector for many years.She has also freelanced for magazines, newspapers, NGOs and corporates doing photography and writing, both commercially and doing editorial work.

Specialties

Photography, photojournalism, writing and editing,picture research, stock images
