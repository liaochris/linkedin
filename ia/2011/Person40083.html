Shayan Sanyal's Experience

Chief Marketing Officer, Head of Strategy

BluWan

Privately Held; Telecommunications industry

February 2010 – Present (1 year 2 months)

Bluwan enables the world’s leading service providers to supply high capacity, profitable, fixed & mobile broadband with Fibre Through The Air™. Bluwan spinned off from Thales Communications in 2005, leveraging disruptive military technology to deliver multi-gigabit capacity mobile backhaul & super fast broadband (up to 100 Mbps) to premises in areas where it is economically unfavorable to deploy fibre. We bridge the digital divide, not just with killer technology, but with game changing operator business models.

Our solutions are 20x faster than existing wireless solutions, carry 10x more capacity than traditional PTP microwave links, with 99.99% availability, even in difficult climactic conditions. And this, at a fraction of the cost of deploying optical fibre. Operators using FTTA can expect to be cash flow positive in 1.5 years for 50,000 subscribers deployed.

Bluwan is backed by Responsible Technology & Thales Ventures and recently received 2.3 million euros in funding, and launched this February at Mobile World Congress 2011. We are engaged in several pilots with governments & carriers.

I am currently responsible for Bluwan's go-to-market strategy and all aspects of execution: Core Strategic Vision, Product Marketing & Management, Communications (AR/PR), Regulatory & Government Affairs, as well as Business / Trial / Reference Development.

Built the marketing, sales and product management organisation from the ground up. Led the recruitment of key GTM partners around product market insight (Gartner), media and analyst relations (Ballou PR), as well as established a network of key business development partners (Strategic Alliances). Developed a pre-launch pipeline of > $50 million from scratch.

To set up a meeting and discuss how we are changing the economics of fixed and mobile broadband, contact us at sales@bluwan.com.
