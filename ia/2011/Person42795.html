Terri Ritchie's Summary

With more than 15 years of corporate communications experience, I manage marketing communications and write for print, websites and social media. When my son was an infant, I left the corporate world for a more flexible schedule so that I could raise him while doing freelance communications work and volunteering.

As a freelance consultant, I support the communications efforts of a variety of organizations — ranging from retail and financial services to manufacturing and non-profits. I write/edit marketing collateral, publications, website and social media content, press releases, annual reports, postcards, training materials, articles, trade publication ads and media kits.

With expertise in all aspects of marketing, advertising, public relations, event planning and management, I have demonstrated the ability to manage multiple projects concurrently with strong attention to detail, problem solving and follow-through capabilities.

I am now looking for a full- or part-time opportunity with a corporation or non-profit where I can use my skills, experience and enthusiasm to assist the organization with meeting its communications goals.
