Niall Flynn's Summary

My name is Niall Flynn I am an Online Developer based in Dublin, Ireland.

I began my online career in 2003 studying what was then called Multimedia, in the Dun Laoghaire Institute of Art, Design and Technology.

I graduated in 2007 after completing a 3 year BSC in Multimedia Systems Programming and a final year, finishing with a BSc (Hons) in Computing in Multimedia Programming.

Since graduation I have worked as an Online Developer specialising in SEO and Online Advertising, my career to date has been focused around services provided to the advertising industry, during this time I have had the great privilege to work with some excellent people, brands and agencies.

I am currently working with Ground4D here in Dublin as a Digital Strategist.

I am available for freelance projects and my most recent work is showcased at www.niallflynn.com.
