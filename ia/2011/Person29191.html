Maureen Hall's Summary

I’m an award-winning interactive marketing executive who drives e-health solutions that empower patients and transform customer relationships.

I believe in supporting patients, not selling to them. I believe better-coordination and communication among all healthcare stakeholders will improve care and outcomes. And I believe new and emerging digital technologies will play a pivotal role in opening minds and changing behaviors to increase compliance, adherence and effective self-care.

I have spent my entire career on the agency/service side of the business, starting with consumer packaged goods, where I led the charge to build exceptional share growth for Alpo and Beech-Nut, earning a slot in Who’s Who in Advertising.

I moved into corporate communications, successfully aligned 35,000 pharmaceutical employees with the company brand and vision, and launching a new Rx therapy to #2 in its class within 2 yrs.

Most recently, as founder and president of digital marketing company Hall Media Productions, I forged lasting client relationships and led digital strategy and innovation for Rx brands, medical devices and diagnostics, healthcare providers and advocacy groups. Our innovative web and interactive initiatives won 30+ awards… delivered exceptional results for our clients... and spurred our own successful growth.

Since selling Hall Media a few months ago, I’ve been expanding my knowledge of new mhealth and telehealth technologies, immersing myself in the changing dynamics of our healthcare industry, and exploring best practices to truly engage and partner with all healthcare stakeholders.

I’ve got lots of great ideas and I’d like to put them to work for a truly innovative organization that is as patient-centric and as passionate about improving care, value and outcomes as I am. Contact me at me.hall@verizon.net
