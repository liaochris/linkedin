Al Duplisea’s Summary

Party Rentals Plus is a family owned and operated business; therefore, you can be assured that we will do everything we can to help make your event a happy occasion. Since we are a family business, you can be assured that one or more family members will have some part in seeing that you are served to the best of our ability. We believe that our pricing is competitive with any other company with equipment of equal quality and value.

We attend the national rental, and special event shows and in this way we are able to keep abreast of new equipment and trends and are continually adding new inventory that is in demand.

We are a member of the national American Rental Association.

Al Duplisea’s Specialties:

Our services and products are used by brides, family birthdays, corporate events, school events as well as church events. We rent tents, table, chairs, linens, dinnerware, candelabra, archways and inflatable bounce rides.
