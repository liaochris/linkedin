Hometel Travel, LLC (private beta stage) is a budget conscious traveler peer to peer network consisting of local accommodations all over the world. The Hometel Travel global marketplace is for like-minded travelers looking to take advantage of the multitude of benefits to renting an apartment or home versus staying in a hotel. Apartments are cheaper, larger, and more private than most hotels.

Hometel Travel allows its users to connect with prospective leasers or hosts through whatever channel best suits their individual preference. Whether it be through an alumni network, shared lifestyle preferences, or even similar interests, Hometel Travel makes networking through any of these means as easy as possible.
