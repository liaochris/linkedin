Kevin Elliott's Summary

From Air Traffic Controller to Financial Advisor

As an air traffic controller in the United States Air Force from 1980-2003, I was entrusted to calmly control thousands of multi-million dollar aircraft to safely takeoff and land in all types of conditions --- regardless of bad weather, wartime conditions, emergency situations, and severe traffic pattern saturation.

As a financial advisor, I'm applying these unique skillsets and experiences to help my clients safely reach their financial goals --- regardless of the circumstances such as bad markets, job loss, and other risks.

Specialties

- Making money for you when markets go up
- Protecting your money when the markets goes down

I deliver results through a disciplined 4-Step Investment Process that helps protect your portfolio just like I've assisted thousands of pilots land their aircraft safely.
