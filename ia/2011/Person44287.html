Troy Anderson's Summary

An award-winning journalist at the Los Angeles Daily News and other newspapers for 19 years, I freelance for Reuters and 20 magazines and Web sites, including National Wildlife (650,000 circ.), Inland Empire (190,000 circ.), Christianity Today (155,000 circ.), NBIZ Magazine (30,000 circ.) and the Pacific Research Institute's CalWatchdog.com. I also write and ghostwrite books, book proposals, speeches and fundraising letters. In addition, I offer media relations services, including press releases, backgrounders, press kits and strategic public relations counsel.

Specialties

Politics, government, business, faith, Hollywood, entertainment, health and nature.

City and county government and law enforcement reporter at this 75,000-circulation daily newspaper.
