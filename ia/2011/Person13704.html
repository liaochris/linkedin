ENEM Technologies's Summary

ENEM Technologies Pvt. Ltd. established in 2006, is a pioneer in providing world leading offshore software development services, web development company services, offering an array of IT related services to its clients across the globe.

The services offered cover Custom Software Development, Software Outsourcing, Ecommerce Portals, Application Development, Website Design and Software Maintenance With sound domain knowledge and out of the box thinking, we keep on delivering value to our customers through our pioneering software solutions and services.

ENEM is a privately owned company with marketing, interfacing office and development center in India is well established in Noida. Our role varies from one-source solution vendor to an extended development partner to a key on-site development group. We have successfully delivered large projects such as scalable JAVA service platform, Financial Applications, CRM solutions, ERP Application, RFID plug-in, CMS based on Drupal and Joomla, Networking Portal and many more; the complexity of the projects varies significantly from one project to the other; the resource involvement also varies from one man month to more than 100 man months.

Since our inception with a strong team having vast experience in specialized software development, we have steadily grown into a reputed provider of high-quality and cost-effective software development services. We are devoted to provide robust, lucrative and long term services, enabling our customers to get high returns on their IT investments coupled with corporate authority designed for greater agility and market focus in this globally competitive environment.
