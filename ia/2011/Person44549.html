I have installed Solaris Operating System on SPARC platform, and I am familiar with system layout including listing directories, file security, disk format, syslog, and system boot sequence. Familiar with LDAP, NIS and jumpstart/kickstart technology. Familiar with VI/vim editor and system files including vfstab/fstab, inetd.conf, syslog.conf and /var/adm/messages or var/log/messages.
