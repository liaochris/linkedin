Myles Davidson's Experience

Managing Director

i-KOS Ltd

Privately Held; Internet industry

September 2000 – Present (10 years 6 months)

I started i-KOS on the back of findmedical.com (1997-2001) an online directory of medical companies and products. Ultimately it was the result of owning this website that has defined i-KOS as a creative marketing agency.

i-KOS today comprises 10 talented individuals; collectively we are a digital agency with a proven track record for originating, managing and implementing unique marketing strategies and campaigns. Our clients are typically SME’s, who want to advance their business without the hefty overheads of a London agency.

We are fortunate enough to work with numerous forward thinking clients who have allowed us to develop into a multi-disciplined agency through; Brand Development, Website Design, Search Engine Marketing, Graphic Design, Publishing,

Leading a small business is a wonderfully complex, ever-changing and challenging role that requires you to adapt your business to suit the next opportunity.
