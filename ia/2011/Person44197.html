Trevor Byrne's Summary

After many years of experience in running global IT Software Development teams, I became an entrepreneur in 2009, starting up a mobile custom framing business.

In early 2010, I bought one of the oldest custom framing and galleries in Denver and now spend my time running both businesses.

As a small business owner I've become a marketer, accountant, CEO and COO and I'm enjoying every minute.
My goal is to make AUM the most well known frame store in Metro Denver - it's one of the oldest and most respected but it isn't that well known (yet).

As the first step in achieveing this goal, I'm partnering with Deluxe Corporation and SCORE as one of the 9 small businesses selected to participate in the brand new Project Rev. Take a look at http://projectrev.com/about/ for the basics. You'll also be seeing much more about Project Rev in the news and on social media.

Trevor Byrne's Experience

Owner

AUM Framing & Gallery

Retail industry

February 2010 – Present (1 year 2 months)

AUM, celebrating its 25th anniversary this year, is one of the oldest custom frame stores in Denver. Our four professional framers have over 70 years of combined experience.

AUM has a great reputation with retail, wholesale, and corporate clients for unique designs and superior craftsmanship. We carry one of the largest selections of frames in the Denver Metro area. Our newest additions are finished corner frames and leather frames from Peru, and we're about to start working with stone (yes, stone) frames. Over the years the staff have framed a multitude of unusual items.

In addition to custom framing, the store offers custom mirrors, leather furniture and accessories for the home, and has a small gallery where we often showcase up and coming local artists.

We also work closely with a number of trusted partners allowing us to offer additional services, such as giclee printing; printing on metal, acrylic, tapestries, fabric, etc.; mounting of images on a number of different substrates; photo restoration; art sourcing, valuation and restoration; and installation and storage.

Owner

Frames on Wheels

Retail industry

August 2009 – Present (1 year 8 months)

Frames on Wheels is a new, much-needed concept in custom framing - a mobile framing service. So that our clients do not have to take the extra time to visit a frame shop, we go to them, help them design a piece, and then deliver the finished product.

Our design sessions take advantage of the latest in visualization software technology. We import photographs of the art and the intended location into a program that contains images of moulding and mats. We combine all of these elements to show our client multiple design possibilities, eliminating guesswork about the best moulding and mats for the art and location. Our clients can be confident that they are making the best choices for their homes and offices.
Frames on Wheels caters to both retail and corporate clients.
