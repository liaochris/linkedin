Kathi Browne's Summary

Kathi has a BFA in Creative Writing and an extensive background in computer techologies. She has over 10 years technical communications experience and is also familiar with social media strategy. Kathi speaks on topics related to social media and provides staff training on the powers and dangers of social media. She recently published the book TAKING ON TWITTER: STRATEGIC TWEETING FOR SMALL BUSINESS. Feel free to send an invitation to connect at kathi(at)wingspouse(dot)com

Kathi also owns Wingspouse Consulting, which helps companies improve their leadership, succession plans, recruitment and more by focusing on relationships. Kathi specializes in improving a company's bottom line by focusing on the relationships that can affect leadership. Executive spouses are a key component of her approach, and she understands the role well, Kathi is married to a physician executive and is familiar with the executive lifesyle and all of the challenges and opportunities that go along with it.

Specialties

social media, writing, executive topics, employee recruitment/retention, work life balance, twitter, linkedin, facebook, foursquare
