Triple S Group's Summary

The successful story of Triple S Learning, Triple S Recruitment and Triple S Speakers goes on…

Our successful growth, the fast development and the professional work principles, inevitably introduced the need for creating Triple S Group – the group that will unite the clear visions of these three subjects and will continue to exist in the society as unique and innovative in the modern manners of working.

The vision of Triple S Group is to initiate changes and development in the people, their companies and the quality of life by designing contemporary training programmes, conveying the technologies of work of the world’s most successful companies and individuals. We have created a brand that has helped many professionals arrive at the place they are now at and we have succeeded in helping them realize their professional careers.

We provide real solutions and advanced skills designed according to the specific needs of the client:

• Triple S Learning – the first and exclusive specialized training centre in Macedonian in the areas of: Customer Service, Sales Skills, Guerilla Marketing™, Marketing, Management, Finance, Effective Communication and Human Resources.
• Triple S Recruitment – specialized agency for recruitment, selection and support of the human resources that will active the human capital in your organization.
• Triple S Speakers – the only representative office of CSA Celebrity Speakers Associates in this part of the world that will enable you access to the knowledge of the world’s most important business gurus, academics and politicians.

Let us help you in realizing your business plans. Let Triple S Group become part of your success story via our intensive training programmes and the professional services we offer.

The professional team of Triple S Group abides the principles implemented in our programmes.
