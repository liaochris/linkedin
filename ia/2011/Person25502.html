Larry Weil’s Summary

At Branded Retail Energy We are affinity marketing specialists. We have now launched Aggie Energy, Mustang (SMU) and Texas Longhorns Energy in conjunction with those Universities and their athletic programs, with more Universities in the pipeline. Our specialty is customer acquisition. We know that people want a reason beyond price to choose their provider of electricity. Our programs connect with consumers in a meaningful way. We are experts in the deregulated electricity industry. We have the ability to pull together retail electric providers and properties in order to increase revenue for both parties.

My primary background is in Sports and Event Sponsorship. I like to say that I have done everything from the Super Bowl Half Time Sponsorship to Monster Truck Rally’s. Throughout my primary focus has been on creating sponsorships that have measurable activation elements. Specifically, I have focused on customer acquisition and lead generation. I have worked with all of the major sports leagues including the NFL, NBA, MLB, MLS, NHL and NHRA.

My avocation is identifying and creating opportunities for products and services in the "Green" sector to grow their business through sponsorships that take their message "mainstream". Working primarily with sports teams and venues, I assist companies in identifying the "right" match that blends the tremendous exposure with the right platform and values of the target partner. I can also assist teams and venues in developing the correct platforms, values and comprehension of the environmental sustainability "culture". Our mission is to educate and inform the vast majority of consumers and businesses that are confused, unaware, or reluctant to embrace "green" values, products and services through the teams and venues that inspire their loyalty.
