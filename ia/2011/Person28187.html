Mark Daugherty's Summary

Mark C. Daugherty is Founder and CEO of AllTalk™, the nation's first "unlimited minutes" international calling service.

Prior to his appointment at AllTalk™, Mark was CEO of Sonic Capital, Inc., which he founded in Jan 2005 after parting from GoodBrand Lending Corp. (www.gblending.com), a large CA-based mortgage broker/banker, which he co-founded. While at GoodBrand, Mark served as COO and was responsible for day to day operations of the firm. During Mark’s tenure at GoodBrand, the firm’s gross monthly loan volume grew from $2 million per month to over $12 million per month and $150 million in funded loans for the 2004 fiscal year.

Prior to his experience in mortgage banking, Mark spent nearly a decade in investment banking and corporate communications. He has counseled dozens of public companies ranging in size of market capitalization of $20 million to $5 billion in investor relations issues, strategic positioning, sales and distribution channel development and implementation, media relations and crisis communications, equity financings, and venture consulting. He was most recently Senior Partner and Chief Operating Officer of Investor Relations International (www.irintl.com), an investor relations and corporate communications firm recently recognized as one of the 500 fastest growing companies in America by PriceWaterhouse.

Prior to IRI, Mark was a Principal at Typhoon Capital Consultants, LLC (www.typcap.com), where he served as head of the firm’s Think Tank consulting division, with particular emphasis on Typhoon's publicly-traded technology companies. Prior to joining Typhoon Capital, Mark served as Managing Partner and Chief Operating Officer of Los Angeles-based Metropolitan Capital Partners, Inc. (MCP), a regional investor relations firm serving small to mid cap public companies. While at MCP, Mark managed the investor relations programs for over 15 companies and also served as a board advisor on several start-ups.
