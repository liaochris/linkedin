Lemmy-Boy Hoogendoorn’s Summary

Since I finished primary school I've always been busy in the Design industry. I went straight to the Grafisch Lyceum in Rotterdam and studied there for 8 years. In my last 4 years at the Grafisch Lyceum I came in contact with Multimedia Design where I graduated in 2004. Shortly after that I decided to go to CMD (Communication & Multimedia Design) at the HRO in Rotterdam where I still study User Experience Design.

Lemmy-Boy Hoogendoorn’s Specialties:

Conceptualizing, Information architecture, Motion graphics, Interaction design, User centred design, Graphic design, Innovative problem solving.
