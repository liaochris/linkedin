    Saskia is Partner of AML Services International, LLC, a consulting and training company in money laundering and terrorist financing. The company has developed training manuals for the Association of Certified Anti-Money Laundering Specialists (ACAMS), and she conducted training for regulators and financial institutions around the world. She co-authored “The Guide to Anti-Money Laundering: A self - study course” for Informa, a U.K. financial crime services company. She has conducted Bank Secrecy Act and money laundering compliance reviews for a money remitters and banks in the U.S and the Caribbean. She is Financial Crime Advisor to NetEconomy, a leading firm in real-time enterprise risk monitoring solutions. She spearheaded ACAMS as the Executive Director from September 2001 to December 2005 and was responsible for the growth of the overall organization from its inception in 2001 to 3500 members in 101 countries in 2005.
