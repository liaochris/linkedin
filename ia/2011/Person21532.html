Joe Tomasic’s Summary

Joe Tomasic specializes in selling businesses in virtually every industry in the Western States Region and abroad and brings with him years of experience and knowledge.

Joe has owned, operated and sold many successful businesses. He provides strategic, personalized counsel to individuals and companies in areas of business sales and acquisitions, private debt and equity financing, and succession planning.

Recently a 2008 nominee for the Vail Valley Partnerships "Entrepreneur of the Year" and a 2007 Runner up for the same award Joe knows what it takes to run, buy and sell businesses.

As a seller it is wise to consider a Business Broker not only to find a buyer for your business, but also to ensure that the deal actually closes. Fewer than half of the small businesses offered on the market – whether they are retail stores, restaurants, distribution companies, manufacturing enterprises or service firms - are ever sold.

This is greatly attributed to buyers and sellers having a difficult time coming to a mutually beneficial agreement. A business broker with experience in these matters can greatly increase the probability of successfully closing the deal.
