Tim Mancuso's Summary

Tim Mancuso is a musician, public speaker and the former VP Sales & Marketing for Social Media startup ViewMyLife. He is focused on training and coaching business owners and entrepreneurs on leveraging traditional and new media to Stay in Touch with their clients, prospects, and key influencers. Follow On Twitter @TimMancuso or connect on Facebook: **www.facebook.com/RelationshipMarketing**

I am now available for Speaking engagements, Seminars, & Training sessions throughout Colorado and the US. ( for Real Estate Brokerages, Mortgage Co., Banks, Sales Organizations, Insurance Companies, Title Companies, Auto Dealerships, Sales Organizations, Local Business, Chamber of Commerce, Non-Profits, Ministries)

*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*

Tim Mancuso specializes in addressing complex business development roadblocks and issues. These include: sales team implementation, training, coaching, deployment, M&A Change Management, compensation structure, motivation, Tracking and Business Intelligence, CRM implementation and rapid adoption, and market penetration / turn-around practices (flip).

Tim Mancuso has been a driving force with the International Expansion of existing North American MLS / Real Estate and Mortgage models in advertising, marketing, aggregation and integrated consumer services.

Current Real Estate focus is on the globalization of diverse industry models including:
Property search, homes for rent, homes for sale, realty, advertising, lead capture, lead incubation, lead distribution, online marketing, drip marketing systems, contact management, data distribution, data aggregation and normalization, data syndication, sales, marketing, training, motivation and recruiting.

Additionally, Tim has been working within the non-profit sector discovering innovative partnership strategies to drive corporate and private donations and support local foundations.
