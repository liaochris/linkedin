Francisco Martinez Roman's Summary

Francisco Martínez Román founded ARQ.I.TEC Corp., which serves as the parent company to Revista arq.i.tec since 2005. Mr. Martínez also founded im.pac Graphics & Design in 2006 where he has gained extensive experience in graphic design, project management, administration, coordinating project budget and schedule, among other things. Mr. Martínez hold a Bachelor/’s Degree in Environmental Design from the University of Puerto Rico and a Master’s Degree in Architecture from that same institution.

Always looking for ways to expand creatively and professionally, Francisco, along with his brother Fernando, co-founded Constructora Martínez to respond to the always-increasing need of construction, remodelation, and project management in Puerto Rico.

Specialties

Brand management, logo design and corporate image, magazines & pubications, architecture design, space redistribution, marketing

Director & Editor-in-Chief

Revista arq.i.tec

Architecture & Planning industry

October 2005 – Present (5 years 5 months)

Revista arq.i.tec is Puerto Rico’s leading magazine in architecture and design. Founded in 2005, it has successfully reported on the latest tendencies in architecture and design while becoming a valuable reference for architects all over the island in terms of projects, materials, suppliers, and ideas. It was designed to give its readers a broader look at architecture and design and how they translate and affect our daily lives. Revista arq.i.tec is a past winner of the 2007 Honor Award given by the Puerto Rico chapter of the American Institute of Architects [AIAPR].
