Ethel Gondwe's Summary

As a child I was fortunate enough to have a great Entrepreneur for a Father. As the proud owner of the first Travel Agency in Zambia, that introduced a luxury coach service to and from South Africa via Zimbabwe, he also owns various other businesses, that have not only proved to be lucrative but successful. As a result this put me in a position of ambition, wit, and determination. By the age of 16, I worked as the assistant secretary for Global Travel and Tours taking care of the administration and customer service within the business.

Upon completing my A levels I assisted with the running of one of my fathers fast food restaurants to which I later managed.

Due to the successful running of the restaurant, I was later transferred to work for Global Travel and Tours as a travel Clerk, where I was later promoted to office manager and on occasion ran the entire business in the absence of the CEO.

Whilst horning my entrepreneurial skills, my ambition was to pursue a career within the entertainment industry. After years of improving on and acquiring my entertainment and music knowledge/skills, and already armed with a business background, it was only feasible that I took the next step in becoming the entrepreneur I was set out to be. At present, I work in several aspects of the entertainment industry; I have experience in styling, make-up, singing, song-writing, A&R, branding, marketing, artist management, administration and management.

My goal is to build Effycom Productions into not only one of the U.K’s largest entertainment concierge services, but the world’s. The aim for the business is to provide services as minor as advertising banners to services on a larger scale such as great artists for labels, enabling a fusion of cultures and ideas for the world to share all through entertainment.

Artist negotiations, brand Identity, Artist grooming, Artist Representation, Multimedia, Image and Styling
