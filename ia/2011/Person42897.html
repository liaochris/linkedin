Beverly Jackson’s Summary

PR Pro and a former television news professional with a wealth of major market experience in the fast-paced television news environment and body of "first hand" knowledge about what makes news and how to sell it.

An award winning former Television Writer/Producer/Director with credits on hundreds of shows on major local, regional and national television networks in Philadelphia, New Jersey and New York—including the nationally syndicated Joan Rivers Show and daily programming for Comcast SportsNet, MSNBC, MTV and VH-1.

Since I transitioned into a second career as a Media and Public Relations Consultant , my specialized experience and expertise in television programming, production and news operations have been coveted contributions in the development and implementation of successful public relations strategies; strategic partnerships; and image building campaigns that achieved a high level of positive media exposure for leading local, national and international advocacy organizations; celebrated national conferences and major special events.

An imaginative outreach strategist who has conceived innovative story placements; orchestrated award winning media campaigns; leveraged noteworthy special partnerships; and successfully orchestrated the involvement of celebrities and news personalities in numerous non-profit causes and activities.

Solid relationships with an established network of local, regional and national media professionals—from high-level program and production executives to news anchors and field reporters, in addition to major contacts in all areas of the entertainment industry.

Sharp pitching and presentation skills, and a command of state-of-the-art resources, techniques and technologies for local, regional, national and global media and public outreach in today’s technology driven world.

Advanced knowledge, skills and abilities in computer and broadcast technology, online resources and website content development.
