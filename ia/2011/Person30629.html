Mike Cooper's Summary

Highly motivated and enthusiastic Internal Sales Manager with 15 years sales & marketing experience in companies such as Unica, Sage (CRM), JD Edwards/Oracle & most recently The Fizzback Group.

Fizzback are widely recognised as the leader in Customer Engagement programs, helping organisations all around the globe capture customer comments along different stages of the customer lifecycle. We help you get closer to the Voice of Customer and Fizzback’s artificial Intelligence engine helps you to easily identify at-risk customers, capture customer satisfaction scores & highlight current topics that are key to your customers heart.

I get a huge kick out of helping small-mid sized companies grow revenue, successfully launch in new regions & significantly increase the number of opportunities for the sales team to work with.

I have over 1350 connections through LinkedIn which enables me & my network to reach out to over 15 Million contacts around the world.
