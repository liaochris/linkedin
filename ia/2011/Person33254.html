Oge Okosieme's Summary

I am a personal and business development coach with a special interest in helping women who are stuck and frustrated working for someone else to develop skills that will allow them to own and run their own business.

What that means is that I help people get clear and confident about who they are so they can start doing the things they love.

I work with ambitious individuals who are looking for fulfillment and direction in their lives and want to use business as a vehicle to do that.

A typical question my clients are asking at the point they make contact with me is 'I want to understand what is happening in my life'
'I want a sense of direction', I want to start living my life on my terms

My typical client feels lost, but also has a sense that all is not lost
