Myra Thomas’s Summary

Sixteen years as a business/finance editor and writer working freelance and in-house for print publications, websites, universities, companies, governmental agencies, consulting firms, and trade organizations. Produced newspaper and magazine stories, white papers, e-blast content, press releases, online content, business research reports, a recurring stock to watch feature and two antiques columns. Served as a ghostwriter/editor for two finance book projects, including one for McGraw-Hill titled The Five Keys to Value Investing. Acted as a regular commentator for radio, discussing business, environmental, social and political issues. Served as a moderator and panelist at top business industry events. Oversaw the revamping of two media websites. Supervised web personnel and designers.

Myra Thomas’s Specialties:

Business journalism, finance reporting, financial analysis and research, creative nonfiction, book reviews (narrative and finance), and editorial work.
