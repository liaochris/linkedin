Ellen Long’s Summary

"I create world-class products from genius ideas."

Working directly with authors and entrepreneurs, Ellen directs the creative and production teams who develop products. She has an extensive background in art direction, branding, editing, content development, and print and video production.

Because of her experience in developing leadership products, Ellen has a track record of bringing diverse teams to agreement on complicated projects. Recognized as "an inspirational leader" with a talent for getting things done and fostering loyalty, Ellen is consistently able to motivate and inspire others up and down an organization, and to create a sense of shared vision and values.

People who work with her know that among her top values are creating world class quality productions, maintaining a sense of humor, respect for the creative process, and professionalism.

Ellen Long’s Specialties:

Executive leadership of creative teams, including audio, video, web, print campaigns and productions, Product Development, Brand Management, Art Direction, Instructional Design, Writing, Management Consulting and Leadership Development, Personal and Business Coaching
