  • Executive Director-Cenergy, a Division of CSU Ventures

    Colorado State University

    (Educational Institution; Higher Education industry)

    January 2009 — Present (2 years 1 month)

    Cenergy is the business arm of Colorado State University’s Clean Energy Commercialization arm and works to directly enhance Colorado’s leadership in building a new energy economy as well as improving quality of life for people around the globe.
