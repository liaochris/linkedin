James Fleet’s Summary

My passion is large scale e-commerce strategy where I have ten years experience working with & for leading Australasian companies both pure-play online businesses as well as 'bricks & clicks' multi channel organisations.

My online marketing strategy skills derive from hands on experience in key e-commerce marketing tactics like search engine marketing, affiliate marketing, web analytics, comparison shopping site feeds, social media and email marketing.

I also have a strong external and internal sales management background which combined with my experience in online marketing strategy drives my ability to maintain focus on bottom line business results & profit.

My long term goal is to remain at the forefront of online marketing and e-commerce strategy internationally. Ideally in a leadership role where my experience in business development, management and implementing performance based online marketing strategies can be best put to use.
