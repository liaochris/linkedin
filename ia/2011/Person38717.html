Sandy Harper's Summary

I change lives by teaching people how to incorporate The 12 Gratitude Habits into their daily life. My book, Gratitude Habit, will be released soon. It was recently featured as one of the top 25 (out of 2800) entries in The Next Top Author Competition. My children's book about gratitude is coming soon, as well as the Gratitude Boot Camp audio/workbook program. It's important to make gratitude a habit because your habits create your life.

I'm a Contributing Author in the book Visionaries With Guts.

As a professional speaker, I inspire and empower people to stay positive and count their blessings, no matter what circumstances arise.

As a real estate broker, I've helped people to turn their dreams of home-ownership into celebrations for over 20 years. I'm a Broker-Associate at Century 21 Premium Properties. I specialize in helping luxury home buyers in Celebration, Florida (the town developed by Disney) and in Disney's new, upscale community, Golden Oak.

I'm passionate about helping people become and stay healthy and am grateful for the opportunity to introduce them to Mila (by Lifemax). Mila is a raw, whole food.

It's a pleasure and honor to connect with and learn from positive, inspirational, and successful individuals.

Specialties

Coaching about Gratitude, Writing, Speaking, Luxury real estate sales throughout Orlando, Florida (with a specialty in new construction and homes near Disney)
