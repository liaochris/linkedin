Naresh Singh, CISSP’s Summary

Naresh Singh is Chief Information Officer for Pali Capital. He provides strategic planning, development, evaluation, and coordination of the information and technology systems for Pali Capital.

Prior to joining Pali in 2003, Naresh was with ABN AMRO's Prime Brokerage division (formerly ING Barings / Furman Selz) where he supported their email infrastructure and data centers. Earlier in his career at ING Barings, he planned and implemented technology solutions for their hedge fund clients. Naresh began his IT career at New York University's Courant Institute for Mathematical Sciences.

Naresh earned his B.A. from New York University. He holds MCP and CISSP certifications and is also an affiliate of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE), Computer Society Division and a member of the ISACA.
