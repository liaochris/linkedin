Serge Fenenko’s Summary

Serge Fenenko started his career by setting up an own business in marketing of cultural events. Then, he moved to the financial services industry and joined the Dutch Rabobank. In his latest position, he managed the Business Development department of Rabobank Corporate Clients, and was responsible for marketing of online services. In 2005, he moved to the interactive marketing agency Novocortex.

Novocortex is a Dutch company that works internationally - in Western and Central & Eastern Europe. Novocortex manages market introductions of innovative products, with the focus on (online & mobile) interaction with your clients. Novocortex develops internet marketing strategy, and integrates online and offline marketing to increase results of your marketing efforts.

Novocortex executed internet strategy development projects and interactive campaigns for, among others, Rabobank, Mitsubishi Europe & Turkey, Yamaha Europe & Russia, Interpolis KV, Intouch Insurance, Sanoma International, Ebbinge, InterExcellent, Bank of Moscow, Alstom Power.

Novocortex won about 20 awards of international advertising festivals, such as Webby, Creativity and Horizon Interactive Awards in the US, Golden Award of Montreux in Switzerland, Golden Drum in Slovenia, Red Apple in Russia, the Baltic States Ad Festival Golden Hammer, KIAF in Ukraine, Ad Stars in South Korea. All of them in the Interactive Advertising category. See our award-winning campaigns, games, sites, iPhone apps and virals on http://portfolio.novocortex.com.

Serge Fenenko studied History in the former Soviet Union and Business Administration in the Netherlands, and holds an MBA of Rotterdam School of Management, the Erasmus University's business school.

Serge Fenenko’s Specialties:
