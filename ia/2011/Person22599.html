Jo Levy's Summary

I have been a national make up artist and trainer for BECCA Cosmetics for the past two and a half years. I have contributed to online beauty columns in Glamour.com, Glow.msn.com, Examiner.com and Prevention Magazine. I am the spokesperson for BECCA cosmetics on ShopNBC and I also travel all across the U.S. making appearances and working events with high end boutique retailers and spas for BECCA. I have eight years experience working in the cosmetic sales industry and four years experience with commercial based clients. Some of my previous clients include: Wella hair care, The Oprah Winfrey Show, NBC Chicago, August Jackson, United Auto Insurance Chicago, Ford, and Factor Agency in Chicago. What I love about my role is that I am able to travel all across the U.S. teaching women how to feel and look their best while giving them the tools to be their own make up artist. My goals are to continue my role as a make up artist and educator in cosmetics and to be constantly challenging myself as an artist. I believe that it is important to exceed expectations, strive to deliver the best results, and most importantly, be passionate about what you do. This is definitely the recipe for success.

Specialties

Make up artistry and product education, airbrush make up application, extensive background in commercial and bridal make up.
