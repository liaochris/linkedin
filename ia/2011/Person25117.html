Kristy Vetter's Summary

Directly Speaking Seminars and Coaching helps individuals and companies find the right words to motivate, educate, inform and inspire others while increasing the "volume" of their business.

Kristy Vetter, president of Directly Speaking Seminars and Coaching, is a writer, speaker and consultant specializing in interactive and engaging seminars and presentation coaching. She has more than 20 years experience in financial services, animal health and not-for-profit communications. Her focus has been on helping companies and individuals find their voice by building their presence and credibility through marketing and communications, exceptional customer service and presentations that prompt action.

Previously Kristy worked 13 years for American Century Service, Inc. helping investors understand and take action on their investments. She acted as a company spokesperson for several businesses including American Century Services, Greenies, Inc. and the Lil’ Red Foundation. Kristy understands and appreciates the importance words and the effect they have on everyday work and personal life.

Kristy fulfilled one of her life goals when she returned to college in 2002 and completed her bachelor’s degree in Speech Communications. Her daughter, Ellen, holds the same degree from Kristy’s alma mater. Her son, Mathew is currently attending college studying interactive digital media. Kristy has been married to her college sweetheart, Mike, for 27 years.

Specialties

I use my inspirational speaking skills and talents to help others increase the volume of their voice through words that motivate, educate, inform, and inspire.
