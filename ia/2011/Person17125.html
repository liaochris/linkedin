Haydn James's Summary

My early work career, I have been fortunate enough to have experienced a number of work disciplines and developed skills in various industries and areas of expertise.

My career lead me to Absa Bank where I believe I gained a significant amount of experience in product development and commercialisation in the digital space. The experienced also allowed me to apply my innovative and entrepeneurial skills which lead me to be an integral part of the Channel Strategy development process. I developed a passion for stratgeic planning and enjoyed being in the forefront of technology and innovative concepts to deliver a service or benefit to our internal and external customers alike. My speciality is building on ideas and developing them to products, services and in the case of recent times, companies.

Following a successful 5 years at Absa, I wanted to move out of the corporate space and developed something on my own. So I started a small Mortgage Origination company to service the mortgage needs of a partner real estate company. This exposed me up to a whole new range of experiences and new skill sets, from company startup through to management consulting to company merger and thereafter franchise development, IT operations and franchise management consulting at Leapfrog Property Group.

Following Leapfrog, I joined Aqua Online as Client Service Director, looking after the Digital Marketing activities and website development of a leading South African Hotel Group. My tenure at Aqua while short was significant to my skills development, which has resulted in a move to Striata to head up the eMarketing division.

My goals are to develop the local SA business in other key digital areas and continually drive innovative ideas and services within our customers communication programmes. My experiences over the last 10 years has given me the confidence to be an asset to any company out there.

Product Manager: Digital Channel

Absa Bank

Public Company; Banking industry

November 2001 – April 2006 (4 years 6 months)

Within the Digital Channel environment at Absa I was involved with a number of key areas primarily in Channel and product strategy development.

On the product development and management side, our core focus revolved around Internet Banking for both Retail and Business Segments. I was responsible for 3 key products / services that complemented the Internet Banking services – MyAbsa (single view of customer), Electronic Statement Delivery, eContact (inbound and outbound email communication for the entire Bank).

The role encompassed total end-to-end responsibility from product research, planning and forecasting – new product and existing product enhancement to business case development & funding acquisition from finance committee through to business requirement specification documentation (BRD’s), use case development with Business Analyst assistance, and overseeing the IT development process.

Following development and testing I was responsible for product roll out to business and customers and driving of product usage. Other responsibilities included IT operational infrastructure performance measurement and management with Absa Group IT, interfacing with support staff, call centre frontline for product feedback to channel back into product enhancement plan, SLA management with key stakeholders in the product support value chain, product performance measurement and reporting to channel management and supplier liaison for product planning and strategy input.

Channel and product governance was also a key function that I performed and was responsible for development of the product Governance framework for the channel.
