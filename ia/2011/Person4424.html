Barbara Cooke’s Summary

Counselor with over 20 years experience in career and college counseling. National Certified Counselor (NCC) and Global Career Development Facilitator (GCDF). Author of Parent’s Guide to College and Careers, How to Help, Not Hover (2010) JIST Publishing, now available on amazon.com and in all major bookstores.

Lead counselor at MCC-Maple Woods, coordinating all counseling, career planning, academic advising and student employment services provided to students and community members. Member of the Board of Directors of the Missouri Community College Association (MCCA). Educational consultant and speaker on a variety of career issues, including how to support young people in achieving career/life goals. Freelance writer of articles related to career and educational topics.

About the book:
Parent's Guide is an upbeat, easy-to-use “how to” book for parents concerned about the cost of college and the career happiness of their children. Topics covered include: evaluating college readiness, minimizing student loan debt, making the most of high school courses, helping new college graduates get focused, and identifying career options for students who don’t want a four-year college degree.
