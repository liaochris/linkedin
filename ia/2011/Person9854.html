Dana Lambert's Summary

LIFE TRANSITION RESOURCES-Professional Services for Seniors and Families
Life Transition Resources promotes family harmony by simplifying the aging process for seniors.
This is accomplished through transition planning which empowers the individual to direct the course of their future by creating a personalized plan which encompasses their interests and values. When conflict arises, elder mediation provides a collaborative avenue for resolution and healing. Geriatric care management focuses on enriching the well-being of the loved one in need while providing peace of mind to the family.

Specialties

Services
-protect independent decision-making and self-sufficiency through transition planning
-preserve relationships through mediation
-provide solutions for living through geriatric care management
