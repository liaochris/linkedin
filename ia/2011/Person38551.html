Sam Coburn's Summary

Sam is the National Risk Management Practice Leader at Frank Crystal & Co., Inc. Frank Crystal & Co. is one of the 20 largest insurance brokerage firms in the United States. The firm is headquartered in New York with offices in Houston, Los Angeles, Miami, Palm Beach, Philadelphia, Portland, San Francisco, and Washington D.C. As an alternative in the insurance brokerage industry, Frank Crystal & Co. strives to provide industry-leading services, solutions and counsel to its clients.

As the National Risk Management Practice Leader, Sam has overall management responsibility for the corresponding local practices in each office in North America. These responsibilities include the development and management of client service protocols, cost of risk strategies, and enterprise risk management initiatives. In addition, he has responsibility for growing the risk management service platform in North America.
