How do I know it will work?

We utilize an already proven methodology that delivers results for your business. However, if that is not enough for you, see our money-back guarantee below.

Do I have the time for business coaching?

Yes! You will be amazed what you can accomplish in your business with a very minimal time investment. Besides, when we start working together one of the first things we focus on is improving your time management skills and your productivity.

Can I afford business coaching?

Yes! Over time, the Return on Investment with business coaching is anywhere from $7 to $10 for every $1 spent. In other words, business coaching becomes free as I will find my fee (and then some) through improvements in your business.

Will I be locked into a long-term business coaching commitment?

No! With FocalPoint Business Coaching, there is no contract. You can stop using the service at any time with no penalty.

We guarantee that you will be delighted with our business coaching and the return on your investment. We believe this so much that if after 30 days of business coaching you are not satisfied, we will give you a full refund of your fee. This money-back guarantee continues for as long as you use the service.
