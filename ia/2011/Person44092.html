Tracey Zeckhausen's Summary

I have over 20 years of experience, primarily in the areas of higher education and non-profits, in all aspects of public relations and media relations. I am a writer, editor, and desktop publisher with strong organizational skills and an ability to pitch positive stories to the press as well as remaining calm and objective in the face of potentially negative press attention. I currently serve as spokesperson for the largest state agency in Rhode Island. I have conceived and carried out special events and programs which have brought positive attention to the organizations for which I have worked.

Specialties

desktop publishing, photography, editing, writing, media relations, crisis communications, special events planning and promotion
