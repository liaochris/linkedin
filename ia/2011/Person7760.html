Charles J. Lord, PE’s Summary

Over 25 years of design in embedded systems, including telecom, medical devices, pharmaceutical, military, and industrial controls. Recognized expert in ZigBee and IEEE 802.15.4, particularly with Freescale processors and silicon. Also design partner with Microchip and Renesas.

My company has shifted its focus to technical training and is now providing technical seminars across the US. For 2010 am traveling N America teaching 1-day classes in USB, RTOS, TCP/IP, IEEE 802.15.4 / ZigBee, and PCB layout for embedded engineers (http://www.tadatraining.com/) as well as the original 2-3 day classes.
