Aleksandar Stevanovic's Summary

Aleksandar Stevanovic has been Chairman Director and Head of Investment Products and Wealth Management Services of Ascot Capital Group Investment Holding Corporation since 2003. From 1997 he held various specialist and managerial positions in the areas of investment management, wealth advisory, financial and investment banking services. He has more than 10 years of experience in merger and acquisitions, private placement deals, and distressed and special situation transactions. From 2004, he has responsibility for the Ascot Capital Management offshore investment management for high net worth investors, as well as for external asset managers. For the last eight years he has worked as CEO at Ascot Principal Investments and as a Member of the Executive Board of Ascot Capital Partners and STEBRO Management. Since 2006, Aleksandar Stevanovic has also acted as Head of International Fund Business Development in FCS Capital Ltd.
