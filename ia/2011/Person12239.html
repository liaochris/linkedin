Dom Sawchuk - CPIM, Jonah’s Summary

Business Transformation Specialist seeking opportunities to assist companies with exceptional Lean Manufacturing, Six Sigma and Theory of Constraints skills. For over twenty years, I have successfully led product and process improvement teams in a wide range of industries, markets and cultures, with a strong bias for action and an eye on the bottom line.

During my career, I’ve been very fortunate to have been directly influenced, guided and critiqued by well known thought leaders, including; Chirhiro Nakao (President of Shingijutsu), Bill Smith (Six Sigma leader at Motorola), Dr. Eli Goldratt (Theory of Constraints originator) and others. I am highly experienced in leading Business Transformations from the floor level to the corner office and all points in between.

Some examples include:

• Cut the response time from quote to on-dock for engineered-to-order, In-Ground Pool Liners from the industry standard of 10 days to same-day service, increasing sales by 40%.

• Reduced the throughput time (production order release to warehouse acceptance) for Industrial Valve manufacturer from 4 weeks to 20 minutes, cutting operating costs, improving service levels and quality, and slashing inventory by more than 90%.

• Re-engineered the quoting and product development process for engineered-to-order Oil and Gas Pipeline Distribution Systems, reducing the number of decision points by 35% and overall process steps by 44%.

There are many more examples that I can share with you. However, examples don’t easily express the experience and skill that it takes to lead and motivate teams to not only improve, but to take ownership of process and product improvements. This is an area where you will find that I am deeply experienced and exceptionally well positioned.
