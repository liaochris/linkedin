Jack Zufelt ~ "Mentor to Millions"'s Summary

Jack M. Zufelt is one of the most successful speakers and business consultants on the national and international scene. He is very popular keynote speaker. He conducts seminars and customized training programs as well as life-changing weekend retreats all over the world.

He is a very successful businessman with a vast amount of experience in many different corporate arenas. His client list includes companies of all sizes including many Fortune 500 companies.
