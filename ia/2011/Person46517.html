Zaid Fredericks's Summary

Advertising,Media and Branding Recruitment consultant in the GCC.
My career in media account management and business development spans over 8 yrs where I have worked in various dynamic teams and across numerous brands to create effective and lasting relationships between clients and their consumers. An opportunity then presented itself to take my knowledge of the advertising and media industry along with this passion for working with people and developing relations to my current position at Morgan McKinley in Dubai.

My job as a recruitment consultant now entails developing a network of contacts to ensure that top talent is spread through the various advertising,media and branding agencies that I deal with on a daily basis. I deal with all account management positions in advertising,branding and media from entry level account executives through to account managers,account directors,account directors,business directors,MDs,junior to senior strategists and junior to senior digital account management staff.

It is hard work but building long lasting relationships and helping people make the right career choices is very satisfying!

Get in touch if you are looking to experience working in the advertising, media and branding fields in the Middle Eastern region.

Specialties

I deal with all client servicing Advertising/Branding candidates up to GM level such as Advertising/Branding/Media Account Executives, Advertising/Branding/Media Account Managers, Advertising/Branding/Media Account Directors,Strategic/Media planners at all levels as well as digital platforms within the MENA region .I also look after Project Management and Events Management staff within the region.
