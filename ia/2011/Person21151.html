Joseph Trahan's Summary

Angel Investor & Entrepreneur; working with portfolio of companies in the information and communications technology market sector. Mr. Trahan is a founder and past director of the Houston Angel Network (www.houstonangelnetwork.org). After acquiring several tech service companies, Trahan launched 1-Service LLC (www.1-service.com) in 2003 providing Data Center, Software Development Labs, and Hosted and Managed Services. As President/CEO of 1-Service, Mr. Trahan is consolidating outsourced Information Service providers.

Prior, Mr. Trahan organized and helped launch Houston based Optical Entertainment Networks. OEN provided fiber optic to the home in West Houston, Texas with high speed Internet (10-25mb), 470+ channels of high quality digital(70 HD), IP phone service, Home Security, Internet Gaming and more. Mr. Trahan brought the company from "concept" to production ready acquiring metropolitan fiber networks, strategic vendor partnering and the completion of the broadcast IPTV head end until its acquisition by Mesh Networks. Prior, Mr. Trahan founded DNScorp, the technology consulting firm that designed the network for NASA’s International Space Station and was a startup team member of Houston’s very successful Paranet Corporation which sold to Sprint for 486m in 1996.
