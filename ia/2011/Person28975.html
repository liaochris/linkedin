Matthew Garbero's Summary

Effectively Putting Recognition back into Recognition Programs. To make Recognition Programs Positive Experiences. Reemphasising the need to show Appreciation by Reconstituting the Value of the Recognition Moment that most on-line Award Programs diminish.

Consulting with clients regarding Service, Safety, Attendance and Performance programs. Providing the expertise so their programs meet their targeted objectives.

Service Awards - Our goal is to assist our client in keeping the management and the employee engaged. To provide opportunities to recognize and say "Thank You" for a job well done. The let the employee know they are appreciated and needed.

Safety Awards - Our goal is to promote corporate health and safety initiatives. Our focus is on The 10 Essential Elements of Safety Awareness: 1) Simple and Well Defined Rules, 2) Accummulation Design, 3) Short-Term Recognition Periods, 4) Individual Recognition, 5) Human Interaction and Peer Recognition, 6) Useful and Desirable Awards, 7) Family Involvement, 8) Immediate Gratification, 9) Positive Reinforcement and Continuous Communication, 10) Management Support.

Attendance Awards - Our goal is to provide a mechanism in which management can reduce the costly affects of Absenteeism, Tardiness, and Turnover. We understand the consequences of a unengaged employee. Productivity losses effect the bottom line directly. Our Programs engage management and the employee and provide an incentive for the employee and the family. Management is given the opportunity to re-engage the employee through positive reinforcement and recognition.

Performance Programs - Our goal is to affect productivity by using positive reinforcement to affect behavior. Simply stated, if we are recognized for doing something well, we keep doing it to satisfy our need to be recognized. Our programs are designed to engage management by engaging the employee, the team, their peers and the family.

Specialties

Consulting with New Clients. Guiding them through the process effectively building or upgrading incentive and recognition programs.

Offering tested programs which have proven to accomplish their goals while reduce costs.

We offer simple turnkey programs that require little administration.

Our consultative approach allow our clients upgrade their programs and refocus on Employee Recognition..
