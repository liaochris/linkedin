Gary Pontifex -- "The Networking Guy"'s Summary

Are You Ready For a Flood of New Business Through Referrals?

Reallogic is not only the “Logical Choice” for Business Development and Referral Networking, but your opportunity to build quality relationships with extraordinary like minded people.

Reallogic provides a friendly and safe environment where businesses can come together and grow as they have done in small communities for thousands of years.

Family helping family, neighbor helping neighbor and business helping business. This is community spirit, built on relationships and trust, this is Reallogic.

Relationships are the foundation stones of networking. Without these relationships, you will not establish the trust and sense of community required to succeed in networking. True networking and quality referrals only occur once this trust is built between one another.

Reallogic was founded in 2009 in Perth, Western Australia, and currently has 10 clubs with over 150 members. We build our clubs based on one business, one category, one club. Our members come from over 90 categories of Business and Service providers on the list.

We are now expanding to New Zealand and wish to announce that Reallogic New Zealand will commence on March 31st in Auckland. Already we have had a great response from people in WA with branches or head offices in Auckland and look forward to our first meeting there.

Reallogic “Creates Great Opportunities” and we want to be able to show you how we can assist you and your business in 2011. Check out our website for a club close to you.Start building quality relationships through “People with Passion” in the Reallogic Community.

Email us now: admin@reallogic.com.au for more details.
