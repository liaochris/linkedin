Jeff Lloyd's Summary

Jeffrey Lloyd is an engineering manager with over 26 years’ experience in engineering for mining, industrial and utilities applications. He has a degree in electrical engineering and specialises in application of systems and information technology to engineering, industrial and business processes.

His objective is to develop professional engineering services business through knowledge and process management and continual improvement.

Specialties

Jeff has a background in automation engineering, complex systems project planning and development of technology-based engineering services. He also has experience in process analysis and software tools development.
