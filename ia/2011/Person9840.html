Dana Cataldi's Summary

Dana Cataldi, Partner of the Company, began her career in the entertainment industry with MJI Broadcasting and Clear Channel Communications focusing on event marketing and entertainment sales for over 15 years. Her position with both companies afforded her the opportunity to call on a prestigious list of executives and radio personalities and collaborate on the production of large industry events such as the Country Music Awards and Grammy Awards. Dana has lived in New York, Connecticut, Los Angeles and Italy and her hardworking approach has always been applauded and has helped launch her successful career in real estate. Dana's vision has always been to help her clients by creating relationships that are based on integrity, caring and trust. With her highly ambitious attitude and forward thinking, Dana has been able to provide the utmost in service, care and professionalism to both her past and present clientele. Dana brings both a loyal dedication and a passion for excellence to all of her real estate transactions and is continually looking for ways to help her clients achieve their goals. Dana works with buyers and sellers throughout the entire Westside of Los Angeles including communities such as Santa Monica, Pacific Palisades, Brentwood, Beverly Hills, Westwood, Bel Air, Holmby Hills, Beverylwood and beyond. Dana is continually referred by her clients and is extremely knowledgeable about location, private and public schools and the non-profit and social aspects of Los Angeles. Dana is a graduate of Smith College in Northhampton, Massachusetts and lives in Los Angeles with her husband Don and four wonderful children who attend Crossroads School and Westwood Charter School. Dana is a member of the National Association of Realtors (NAR) and The Beverly Hills Greater Los Angeles Board of Realtors (BHGLAAR). She is also very involved in her community and One Voice, a non-profit organization helping families living at poverty level in Los Angeles.
