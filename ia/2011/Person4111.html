Aubrey Eblin’s Summary

I’ve been blessed to have amazing professional experiences in a variety of sports business settings.

Leading a team of Sales Associates and Account Managers in a thriving Sports Technology business has taught me that perseverance really does pay off and that its the little things you do that make the big things happen.

Working at a fast-paced Digital Marketing Agency provided me with the understanding that you must have an organized plan of action combined with a flexible attitude to handle constantly changing landscapes. Over-prepare for everything...then go with the flow and make brilliant adjustments on the fly.

My background in the business of NCAA Division I intercollegiate athletics presented me the opportunity to lead multiple initiatives in an exciting atmosphere. And of course, coaching basketball at the Division I level offered me the priceless experiences of team building, mentoring, motivating, and caring for a group of people that are giving their all towards a common goal while maintaining a highly competitive culture.

Wow…what a ride it has been!

My primary goal is to continue living life with integrity and approaching each day with a positive attitude, motivating leadership, and attention to detail. All the while, making health and fitness a priority in my life and those around me. I am also a firm believer in setting goals, writing them down, and consistently checking in on yourself in order to achieve greatness and authentic happiness.
