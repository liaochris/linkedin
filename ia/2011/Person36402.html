Heather A. Wright’s Summary

With 13+ years experience in sales and marketing, we are frequently approached by companies seeking change, increased efficiency and restabilization.

OUR IDEAS ARE OFTEN UNHEARD OF, AND CAN OUTDO "OUT OF THE BOX"

Being all-in-one "Marketing Artists", we bring a variety of ideas and skills to the table that can do wonders for positively adjusting the bottom line. With economic downturns businesses have often been forced to cut talented staff from their teams. We step in to fill in the gaps where it is needed, making outsourcing a more desired option. We can even provide marketing assistance for those stuck in the cycle of hiring processes, so that progress and promotion does not come to a halt.

We'll adapt to all styles of marketing in various industries, with experience in working with clients in Oil & Gas to Automotive to Weddings.

It is important for us to build a loyal customer base by helping their businesses grow, because we listen to their concerns and simply because we make an investment in their success.

HEATHER'S SUPERPOWERS:
Diversity-Creativity-Adaptability

I am confident, yet unassuming and continue to produce results and ideas beyond the expectations of those around me. I am most often sought out by peers and clients alike to cure creative blocks, create a new company direction, promote events and products, revamp websites, marketing collateral and often assist with a general company "rebirth". Hence, "Red Phoenix".

I pride myself in being lighthearted, intuitive, aggressive, creative and forward-thinking all at one time.
