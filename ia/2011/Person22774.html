Joseph Stubblebine's Summary

Joseph possesses 19 years in Internet development, design, business development, and management experience. Joseph holds the title of President, Co-Founder of Human Capital Solutions, LLC, a privately- held recruitment products and services company founded in 1998 and located in West Chester, Pennsylvania. Joe is also President and Co-Founder of JobNet.com, a career-centric social network startup, also located in West Chester, Pennsylvania.

As President and owner of Human Capital Solutions, LLC, (HCS), the parent company of JobCircle.com, Joe's entrepreneurial & technology experience has been used to invent new products and services in the recruiting and social media space. Joseph oversees all marketing, sales, finance, and technology efforts within the company. HCS has produced many of the region's largest job fair events, and JobCircle.com currently has over 1.2 million registered members, receives over 200,000 unique visitors weekly, and offers over 100,000 open and active job listings.

In May, 2010, Human Capital Solutions, LLC was responsible for the full scale production of SocialMediaPlus (SocialMediaPlus.com), the Mid-Atlantic region's largest Social Media conference; this conference featured over 28 speakers and 20 exhibitors, and attracted over 650 paid attendees.

Joseph is also President and Co-Founder of JobNet.com, a startup he launched in may 2009, which is a national career-centric social community that is open, free, and allows jobseekers and recruiters to connect and communicate with each other in new and different ways.

Joseph can be reached directly at joe@jobcircle.com. You can also connect and message Joe by visiting his JobNet profile at http://JobNet.com/JoeStubblebine.
