SMITH AND LAUE SEARCH
chuck@smithlaue.com or phone: (503) 460-9181


History:

Since 1990 we have successfully placed senior and mid-level individuals in virtually all managerial positions. Smith and Laue is a retained executive search firm, exclusively focused on providing candidates, who are in the “Top 5%” of achievers, to our clients, both in the United States and globally.

Specialty:

For the past seventeen years our assignments have been focused in the Food Processing and the Animal Pharmaceutical industries. Our rate of success has been over 90%, with the retention rate for the first three years equally as high. This success ratio is significant, especially compared with an advisory quote from Peter Drucker, “Chances are good that 66% of your company’s hiring decisions will prove to be mistakes in the first twelve months.”

Advantages:

We are fast but thorough! Mr. Smith, with a designated team of at least two researchers, comprises the core team on each search, and all finalists are personally interviewed and selected by Mr. Smith before they are presented.
