Stan Louw (LION)'s Summary

Upon completion of my undergraduate degree at a leading university in South Africa, I teamed up with a fellow Marketing Management student where we were involved in creating and running a very successful business on the back of an Entrepreneurship Project in our final year of study. It was presented in a competition format and we subsequently won the whole thing for that year. Our business enabled us to end up the only two students who could walk out of university straight into our newly created company. After 2 years I sold my shares to a partner and started my new career in recruitment.

At the first company I was introduced to the entire concept of recruitment and learnt what it takes to be successful at it. I was focusing most of my time on ERP and Information security placements which proved to be a great enjoyable challenge. The next company was part of a very large group where I moved into a very specialised sector and focused only on recruiting Chartered Accountants - This high level recruitment gave me acccess to some of the top CEO's and CFO/FD's in business, many of which I have been able to build solid relationships as a result of my efforts to alleviate their senior staffing problems.

Apart from the high level engineering and telecoms placement projects I worked on, I later also returned to recruiting CA(SA)'s locally and internationally. In finance I focused exclusively on the CA(SA) niche and now also own the most successful CA(SA) group on Linkedin.

In my last position as BDM for a boutique headhunting firm I enjoyed the daily challenges of covering Business Development and Marketing with a specific focus on Social Media Strategy and International Business Development.

The in depth experience gained prompted me into full time social media training, and marketing consulting which is what I enjoy most. I'm currently busy launching and growing VIRA Consulting into the leading provider of social media training solutions in South Africa.

Specialties

Social Media Strategist & SMM, Scenario Planning, Global Search & Selection, International Business Development, Innovative Marketing Solutions, Intrapreneurship, Linkedin Expert, Branding & Advertising, Marketing Communication Planning and Management, Sales Management, Competition Analysis, Process Improvement, Training and Presentations.
