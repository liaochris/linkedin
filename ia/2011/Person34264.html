Paul Parsons’s Summary

Immigration law practice in Austin since 1978. Certified in this field by the Texas Board of Legal Specialization. Served as a Director on the Board of Governors of the American Immigration Lawyers Association (AILA). Past Chairman of the Texas/Oklahoma/New Mexico Chapter of AILA. Appointed by Governor Ann Richards as Chairman of he Governor's Advisory Committee on Immigration. Appointed annually from 1990 to 2005 as Chairman of the State Bar of Texas Committee on Laws Relating to Immigration & Nationality. Served ten years
as Chairman of the Examinations Committee of the Immigration & Nationality
Law Advisory Committee for the Texas Board of Legal Specialization.
Listed in The Best Lawyers in America (Woodward/White) since 1996;
the International Who's Who of Corporate Immigration Lawyers; the International
Who's Who of Business Lawyers; and as a Texas Super Lawyer in immigration
law by Texas Monthly.

Our law firm's goal is to provide high quality legal services with personal attention to individual applicants for benefits as well as to their employers and
families.

Paul Parsons’s Specialties:

Naturalization & citizenship; employment sponsored immigration; family sponsored immigration; nonimmigrant employment status (H-1B, L-1, O-1, and TN status); K fiancee and spouse visas.
