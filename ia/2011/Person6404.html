Brandon Brittingham’s Summary

Brandon Brittingham, of Moore-Brittingham Properties/Long and Foster Real Estate - Winner of the Long and Foster Rising Star Salesperson of the Year award, Rising Star Listor of the Year award, Honor Society Winner, Overall Rising Star winner in 2008 and 1st place finisher in the Salisbury office for both units and volume in 2009/ and received top regional producer awards for production for the entire eastern shore region. Over 10.8 million in sales for 2009. Certified short sale, foreclosure, and investment specialist with over 200 plus completed short sales. Travels locally and nationally to train and teach agents on short sales and foreclosure. One of the only active agents in the country that can train and certify agents to be foreclosure and short sale specialist. Currently the top producer at Long and Foster in the Salisbury area, and averages the lowest days on market in the entire coastal region. Innovator of some of the top foreclosure and short sale technology and the first agent to get a foreclosure application on the iphone(www.thinkreal.com). Being an investor Brandon provides clients with insight to real estate market other agents can't. On average Brandon averages his clients $50,000 in instant equity on purchases.If you are interested in Short sale, REO or investment education contact Brandon, as he has classes monthly via live webinars. Also check out the free educational blog at www.easternshorehomesolutions.blogspot.com

As President of Moore-Brittingham properties Brandon aquires real estate for immediate rehab and liqudation as well as to also create revenue streams through the purchase of long term residential and commercial properties. MB properties purchase single to multi-unit properties. Brandon oversees all facets from purchase, rehab, disposition, and new construction.
