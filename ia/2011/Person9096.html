Kathryn Delany’s Summary

Kathryn is the founder and President of ColorSplashes. She has a love of color, design and texture that has helped place her as one of the top artisans in the Oregon and Southern Washington area. Her murals are beautifully rendered and can range from impressionist in style to trompe l'oeile, abstract to whimsical.

Her ability to match the requests and dreams of clients to her art is unsurpassed. Her style in decorative finishes is unique and reflects her love of layered finishes. She uses a lot of metallic in her decorataive work. The metallics reflect light and creates a sense of luxury.

Kathryn's subject matter includes as many diverse aspects of life as she can keep up with. She challenges her horizons and her viewpoint of the world around her. Her passion for art helps her to depict her interpretations of that world as energetically and colorfully as she is capable of.

With over 25 years experience as an impressionist pastel artist and running a studio/gallery, Kathryn has successfully run her business and won many top awards for her artwork, throughout North America. Including places such as Connecticut, The Netherlands, Pennsylvania, Oregon and Dallas, Texas and Portland, Oregon.
