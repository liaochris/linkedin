Lauren J. Essex-SantaMaria's Summary

I've been on the Advertising and Marketing side of IT for about 20 years. Started on the ad agency side and then jumped over to the client side. Spent some time in
the VAR reseller channel as a Regional Marketing Manager and have been at AT&T in a variety of roles for the last 11 years. At AT&T I have supported all of the Enterprise Business portfolios from an advertising and marketing perspective as well as a stint over in AT&T WorldNet doing online advertising and cobranding work. Currently I am working on strategic messaging intiatives around Mobility, Convergence, Unified Communications, Business Continuity & Emerging Technologies.

Very early on in my career I worked in PR at the Chamber of Commerce in Datyon, OH and sold advertising for a group of local newspapers owned by The Princeton Packet, also in Princeton, NJ.

Specialties

Content development, print, online and broadcast campaign development and creative execution, agency and vendor selection and management, conference and event planning, media planning and buying, media sales, strategic messaging architecture development
