Aleksandra Ilic's Summary

While studying management at Faculty of Organizational Sciences, Aleksandra has participated in creating and organizing many projects and attended a lot of seminars and training courses which was quite knowledgeable in marketing, PR and HR field. She leaded establishment and consolidation the Local group of European student organization ESTIEM at Belgrade University. As a member of its board, she worked on organizing many International conferences, seminars and student competitions. Also, Aleksandra has been visiting speaker at the Faculty of Organizational Sciences in area of team work, project working, business communication, CV and Motivation Letter writing.

Aleksandra experienced work in International Trade field. In Farmakom MB Concern, she was managing foreign trade preparation. In Binvex (Belgrade Investment and Export Company) she was communicating with key costumers (Plieger BV-Netherland, Soteco-Italy, Al-Hyat Damascus-Syria, Ducasa-Spain, EuroFlex-Italia).

In September 2009, she founded „Hold 4 Aim“, business development consultant agency, the director and partner of which she is today. She covers business and HR planning and managing, communication with partners and clients and leads projects. Also, she covers marketing and HR consulting. Her main clients are: Renault Trucks Romania Slr, Concern Farmakom MB, Industrijski kombinat Guca, Serbia, Sat Trakt NS Serbia, Isomat Greece.

She is currently Master program student with major in Behavioral economics at NLP and coaching academy.

Specialties

Team and Conflict Management, Organizational and Project Management.
