Mick Davis's Summary

Mick Davis serves as the Chief Security, Operating & Business Development Officer for a small Colorado based R&D Company.

Prior to this, Mr. Davis worked as a business and start-up consultant, venture capital technical advisor and political appointee.

Mick also worked several years as a subject matter expert for a variety of defense contractors in the Naval Aviation Research, Test and Evaluation community.

Mr. Davis was educated at the University of Maine in Liberal Arts and Cognitive Psychology. He started his professional career as a Naval Electronic Warfare and Intelligence Operator accumulating nearly 4,000 flight hours as a United States Naval Combat Aircrewman serving for more than 20 years as a Selected Active Naval Reservist.
