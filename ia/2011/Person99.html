Paul OCallaghan's Summary

CAREER OVERVIEW Paul has gained over 20 years of management experience working for a number of large companies including national and multinational organisations which has meant relocating to different countries.
Paul has developed a sound working knowledge of the processes and requirements associated with the establishment of utilities and infrastructure management. He is currently 2IC for the Northern Territory business and SPM for the Darwin projects.
Paul’s experience in utilities, Oil & Gas and infrastructure projects along with financial reporting and documentation control has equipped him to effectively apply sound contract administration processes to his Projects. Paul has strong full project life-cycle management experience from concept through design management, implementation and project close-out.
Paul has demonstrated a record of achievement in all the roles and projects he has managed and is a genuine team-player and relationship builder. He has well developed skills in stakeholder and community communications and management. Paul has experience of dual-contractor alliance contracting.
