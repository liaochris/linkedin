    CoffeehouseForWriters.com

    (E-Learning industry)

    July 1998 — January 2004 (5 years 7 months)

    I founded Coffeehouse for Writers after operating a successful online writing group called The Novel Workshop. As sole proprietor, I was truly a one-woman show, and my responsibilities were myriad. The highlights include:

    - Editing and publishing the site's monthly newsletter, Fiction Fix.
    - Recruiting, training and advising contract writing instructors, who operated online writing courses through our community.
    - Managing and moderating multiple mailing list discussion groups.
    - Promoting the site and forging relationships with other writing communities and writers.
    - Facilitating 4-week online writing workshops.
    - Day-to-day business operations, from bookkeeping to site maintenance.

    I finally realized that I could not run the site on my own, and that I was burned out, so I sold it in 2004. But during my tenure it was recognized (twice!) as one of Writer's Digest's 101 Best Web Sites for Writers. Coffeehouse for Writers was also written up in features in The Wall Street Journal and USA Today.
