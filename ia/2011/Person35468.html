Przemyslaw Duchniewicz’s Summary

Member of the Personnel and Organisation managers’ team in a manufacturing company (CE region18 countries). Experienced both in ‘soft’ and ‘hard’ HR and Sales. Broad perspective from different areas: training, sales and health care. Currently resposible for developing and implementing a regional health, productivity and wellness project for Central Europe that was recognized by DG SANCO two years in a row as the best among 160 other similar projects, as well it is published by EU OSHA as a 'Good Practice' case study on mental health promotion at the workplace. Also finalist of Make the Difference award with finals in Washington D.C.

Since 1998 developed and delivered hundreds of workshops using MBTI: Teambuilding, Trust building, Life Energy Management, Innovation, Team development sessions ... as well as one to one coaching.
Educational background: Medical Doctor, MBA, post graduate studies: Health Care Systems Management and Policy, labour law, certified in several psychometric tools (MBTI step II, Firo B, EQ-i ect.)
