Greg Feezel's Summary

More than 20 years in computer industry with 16 of them managing both people and technology. I take pride in the fact that I have both technical and people management skills. Background is in systems administration, networking, information security management, incident response, project management, people management, and process improvement. Perform extensive research in various areas with a specialization in Internet intelligence, malware analysis, and threat assessments. Currently serve as an information security & IT risk management officer of a computer software company.

Co-founded local user group of security professionals called The Northeast Ohio Information Security Forum (NEOISF). Sit on Executive Council of northern Ohio InfraGard chapter. Member of organizing board of northeast Ohio security conference called the Information Security Summit.

Speaks regularly at regional and national security conferences and serves as a subject matter expert in incident response and malware analysis. Researches and analyzes Internet domain intelligence relating to malicious activities.

Specialties

Enterprise Risk Management, Vulnerability Assessments, Incident Response, Network Security, Log Analysis and Correlation, Malware Analysis, Forensics, Internet intelligence, People Process & Technology Management, System/Network Admin/Design, Data Center Management
