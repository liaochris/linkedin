Robin Colner’s Summary

Robin is a digital marketing strategist specializing in the integration of social media with mobile applications and SEO programs to drive customer acquisition and retention. As a social media marketing consultant, Robin advises businesses on ways to leverage social commerce and geo-targeted (location based) promotional platforms.

Robin is a recognized expert in creating web solutions for advertisers to reach the lucrative 50-Plus demographic. An experienced direct marketer, Robin has managed large-scale, multi-channel direct response campaigns for marquis clients including, American Express and Citicorp.

Her industry experience also includes marketing and product development functions within the financial services, advertising, retail and non-profit sectors. She has a fifteen year track record of developing innovative consumer financial products, and being the first to bring them to market.

A passionate savings sleuth, Robin launched her blog, SaveNotSkimp to share her insights and tips on ways to get the best things in life for less. Her readers learn how to live a 5 star life on a 3 star budget.
