Clive Gott’s Summary

Clive Gott is an adventurer who has completed some amazing and physically gruelling challenges and an internationally renowned Professional Speaker. Although pigeon holed as a ‘Motivational Speaker he prefers to be known as an Inspirational Humourist. Clive’s message is generic; it does not recognise age, race, religion, gender or job titles and is regularly rated as the highlight of the day at conferences and training events as these testimonials will concur...

“You made your talk funny which engaged us and, although we were sat for quite a long time, we never got bored.”
(Emma, year nine, Brayton College)

“When Clive is on form (and he always is) he is the best there is at what he does.”
(Etta Cohen, MD, Forward ladies)

“The feedback we have received from delegates has been nothing short of sensational and I would be happy to recommend Clive for any other similar events.”
(Maggie Shannon, Great Places Housing Group)

Clive Gott is a down to earth Yorkshire man with a style that proves he is passionate about what he does. His purpose is to be a permanent inspiration to anyone and everyone who seeks it. His passion is to encourage and inspire everyone, be they suits in the city, students at college, sales professionals or world class athletes to take life by the throat and say “I’m not done yet.” When Clive speaks he has a unique ability to involve his audience making it real for everyone there. His goal is to have his audience saying “me too” within minutes of starting to speak.

Most of all though Clive Gott is authentic, what you see is what you get, he IS his story and most importantly of all he walks his talk. Clive Gott is just a bloke who enjoys life, he is compassionate, courageous, inspirational and apparently quite funny. He has a unique ability to make the complex seem simple to understand, the challenging seem achievable and the impossible to seem possible. And one of his greatest skills is to be able to pass that on to others.

Clive Gott’s Specialties:

I specialise in assisting my audiences, be they one-to-one or in their thousands to discover their purpose and personal values and then to creat a vision congruent with both. Testimonials are available from many sectors of business and education.
