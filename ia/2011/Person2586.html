Andy Ford's Summary

Trusted & respected in the industry. A proven track record of delivery & innovation. Passionate about people, clients & service. A hands on & results oriented leader with Director & senior management experience in Telemedia, Service Provision, Mobile content services, Mass Participation iTV and Retail. Managing and growing business of all sizes, from start up, through rapid growth and to exit.

InteracTV are the global leaders in live interactive TV game shows, producing over 140 hours per day of live TV in over 40 countries. Passionate about service delivery, committed to long term sustainable revenues and compliant, fair shows. Long standing relationships with broadcasters over 6 years. www.interactv.com

Previously Global Head iTV Buongiorno (B!) with budget responsibility of $180m

Prior to the acquisition of iTouch by B!, Group Head of B2B (Business to Broadcast services) iTouch.

Joined iTouch in December 2002, following its acquisition of m4 Group, (for a consideration of up to $20m) where I was one of the two founding directors, with responsibility for Sales & Operations.

From creation of m4 in 1999, I was the driving force behind many mobile global industry first solutions for blue chip clients including Tesco, Sky TV and o2.

Prior to founding m4, I held senior management positions with retail and wireless companies, including Vodafone, Colorvision PLC & Pointgold Group.

At the age of 21 I first showed my entrepreneurial flair, passion for innovation & customer service, opening my first franchised electrical retail business, within 5 years growing this business to include multiple outlets and a state of the art service centre, with revenues in excess of $60m

I have homes in NW England & Florida USA. Married to Alison with 2 wonderful children, Kasia 18 (and off to college, where did all the time go?) & Finlay 7. In my spare time, I support 2 "lost causes" Burnley FC & The Oakland Raiders.

Specialties

Strategic Alliances & Partnerships; Integrated Web, Mobile & Fixed Line Solutions; Contract Negotiation; Telecommunications Management; Product creation & evolution; Operational Management; Company acquisition, integration & change management; Next Generation 3G, UGC & community, participation TV formats; Leadership
