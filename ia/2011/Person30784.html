Mike Stack's Summary

Software developer with a focus on object-oriented software development. Design experience under various heavy and lightweight methodologies, such as the Unified Process and Extreme Programming and with heavy use of UML modeling, Design Patterns, Use Cases and test-driven development. Extensive programming experience in numerous languages, with a recent focus on C++ (including STL and Boost).

My career has led me in and out of embedded systems-- primarily I have worked with system services, whether it is OS-layer/BSP development or utilities for application development.

Specialties

Object-Oriented Design (OOD), UML, Design Patterns, Real-Time Operating Systems (VxWorks), Protocols (TCP/IP, SNMP, MIL-STD-1553b, ARINC-429), languages (C, C++, Java, Ada95, Assembly).
