Vanessa White’s Summary

I am a quick-learning go-getter, ready to “wear any hat” needed to support the needs of my company.

I began by building my foundation at Tufts University on an ambitious three-major course load, with an extra-curricular focus on student government and event planning. As the head of the primacy event planning organization at Tufts, I helped carry out some of the largest events on campus.

I kicked off post-grad life as a Marketing intern, but within two months, moved to lead product release activities for the Global Marketing department. And after just a year at the company, I have become the principal project Manager for the Global Marketing team. Now I oversee production for global campaigns, product releases, and corporate marketing requests, and track and prioritize all Marketing translations. I work with the Global Campaign director and Regional Marketing to sync their promotional calendars so that we can deliver successful campaigns not only in North America but in all of our regions.

This role positions me as a Global Marketing liaison to every department in the company - from Product Marketing to Support to Engineering - and exposes me to strategic campaign development, web, collateral, and creative development, site optimization efforts, SEO, SEM, product messaging, sales enablement, engineering and IT processes, and program management. It also puts me in touch with outside vendors.

To top it all off, I manage the Global Marketing budget, tracking spend and communicating with directors and executive management to ensure we meet our budgetary goals for the month, quarter, and fiscal year.
