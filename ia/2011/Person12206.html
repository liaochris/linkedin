Dom Barra's Summary

Domenico Barra, better known as Dom, is an Italian artist,art promoter. He was born on the 9th September 1982 in the ancient Greek town of Neapolis know today as Napoli.
It was a sunny morning the day his extraordinary life began, perhaps one of the few in his life because of course for most extraordinary beings.. everyday is stormy.
After roaming the streets of Napoli for many years, he eventually moved to England at the age of 19. Since then he has been conducing a bohemian life-style studying art, reading philosophy and psychology, writing poetry and joined in 2006 the band Echoing Green, of which he was frontman and songwriter.
Barra is back to live in Italy since a year ago.

"My work as artist express my personal interpretation of emotive physical pains, the persecutions of the soul, mental instabilities, the fragility and the anger of human beign in the obstinate and folly run up to catch and posses what he desires. Deformed bodies, storms of colours, liberating scratches, flatterers caresses. Arms waving between the bars of my memories prison cage, and the present which I anxiously try to overtake. A mediocre painting of self destruction"...D. Barra

I speak Italian (mother tongue) - English and Spanish

Specialties

Contemporary art and creative writing.

My Art expresses my personal interpretation of emotional and physical pain, the persecutions of the soul, the fragility of the mind and the anger of human beign in the obstinate and folly run up to catch and posses what he/she desires. The perception which passes through my eyes to my mind and there further to my arms to find his new context in a never seen place. Deformed bodies, storms of colours as skin, liberating scratches and liquid caresses, blured and shining forms will appear leading me to achivement of the divine maestry of creation. The future which I anxiously try to overtake, to shape at my taste, pleasure, desire and sufference drives me to find a balance try good and bad, ugly and beautiful, possible and incredible, letting nature assist me once my work is almost done. I am the eye on the uknown, the door of perception, I give life to color through motion and observation, I am food for your mind, a soft meat for your mouth to chew and spit it out once the flavour has reached your unconciousness. I defeat mortality. My art is a mediocre peinture of self destruction and redemption, obscured and bright search for abstraction create with great modesty to either hurt or pleasure your eyes....
