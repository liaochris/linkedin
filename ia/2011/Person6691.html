    Anderson Advisory Services is a boutique commercial real estate consulting firm. Our focus is on corporate real estate management and bank advisory services. Our corporate practice has the ability to assist with all facets of real estate, including organizational design, planning, transactions, programming, construction, portfolio management, facilities management, financing, lease administration and sustainability. Anderson Advisory Services has experience managing numerous internal corporate real estate functions, including, selling to senior managers, dealing with multiple constituencies, and collaborating with internal groups.
