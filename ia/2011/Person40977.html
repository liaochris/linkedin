Spidey De Montrond's Summary

I started out as a sponsored amateur skateboarder and turning pro at The Capitola Street Style contest in 1985. The companies and corporations I represented were top companies in the action sports field which was a term that had yet to be coined. I also consulted these companies on advertising,marketing,PR, talent acquisition, product development, opening new markets nationally and internationally. The companies were : Quicksilver, N.H.S. inc ( Santa Cruz Skateboards,Speed Wheels,Rip Grip,Pro-Motion magazine),Rector safety equipment,Converse inc. I was also a contributing writer to Thrasher magazine, Freestylin BMX. Doing interviews,music reviews, concert reviews,personality profiles and contributed photos as well. I left it all behind in 1989 and went to collage. While I was in collage I was signed to Capitol Records as singer songwriter. Through all these experiences I learned what it takes to get a project from idea to in your hands and all the steps along the way. I am now partnered with Bulit Entertainment an LA based marketing/management/production/legal firm doing sales, talent brokering,talent development,script development and act as a consultant based on first hand experience from both action sports and music / entertainment industries. So let know how I can be of help :)
