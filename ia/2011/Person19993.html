Jeffrey Hartmann's Summary

As a website developer with over 10 years of experience, my database-driven Intranet websites improve communication, enable quick information searches, reduce data-entry errors and otherwise optimize employees’ performance.

Most of my work has been on Active Server Pages, whether in “Classic ASP” or ASP.NET. I have also quite easily adapted to PHP/MySQL, as I often do with most technologies new to me.

Overall my web and database work has generally been solo, performing front-end design and back-end development while creating and managing database queries. Nevertheless I would welcome being part of a team.

In college I studied film, television, journalism and critical and creative writing. I have a Bachelor of Arts degree in media studies (communications) and won an award as a film critic for the school paper.

My own website, www.jephmann.com, is a live, personal ASP.NET workshop where I test ideas, solve coding problems, indulge in my love of classic and cult film, and otherwise stay in practice. Currently (May 2011) I am upgrading the site to include HTML5 and CSS3 technology.

While not a "LION", I am grateful for invitations from Open Networkers in the Greater Chicago Area and perhaps beyond. That said, please ensure that we share Groups or other categories before adding me as "Friend".

I look forward to networking with positive, engaging people who can show us how networking is done. Thanks!
