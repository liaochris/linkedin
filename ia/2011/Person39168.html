Scott Allen's Summary

After eight years on the bleeding edge of what we now call social media, I've finally landed my dream job as VP of Marketing for OneCoach Business Growth Coaching & Consulting. It combines two of my great passions: entrepreneurship and social media.

I'm especially interested in meeting and talking with:
- Small business service providers (we're always looking for quality professionals to whom we can refer our clients)
- Business experts with a particular niche area of expertise of interest to small business owners (we conduct frequent Expert Interview calls for our clients)
- Affiliates interested in offering OneCoach products and services (we have a generous affiliate program and several big-ticket opportunities).
- Content partners (guest bloggers and guest blogging opportunities)
- Business media outlets
- Business owners looking to grow their business (are there any business owners looking to shrink their business?)

Specialties

Social media, entrepreneurship, business growth, marketing, branding, personal branding
