David Toy’s Summary

David has experience of internet services, manufacturing consulting, electrical design and the movement of people within buildings.

He has a Masters in Manufacturing from Cambridge, a Masters of Engineering from Strathclyde and an award winning internet start-up to his name.

David’s next ambition is to start an internet business of global scale.

His free time is spent with his family and friends or travelling to encounter new people and new experiences.
