Barbara Drummond’s Summary

When I left school I had big dreams about how I wanted my future life to be. At 17 years old I didn’t quite know how I was going to get there, living in a small country with few opportunities. However, it seemed to me that a good start was to further my education and I pushed myself through 6 years of evening classes while working full-time. At the end of that time I found myself working in an office doing accounting for a living. It was not what I wanted.

I soon realized that I wasn’t going to get ahead in life if I continued to work for someone else. To achieve my dreams there had to be another way. I began to look at alternatives but they all seemed like years of hard work for very little reward.

When I finally came across the perfect business my husband had recently passed away and I was at an all time low in my life. It was certainly not the best time to be starting a business of my own, but I'd already faced one of the biggest challenges of my life and felt that another challenge was just the tonic I needed. That’s when I started on a journey I never thought I’d experience.

It is as though this business was made just for me. I have always enjoyed helping others to achieve their goals and be successful. Now I can make a living doing just that and the best part is, I have found it is so easy to operate that little experience was required to achieve success.

I have come a long way since then and along the way have discovered that this business truly can take me wherever I want to go. With financial security and a great lifestyle what more could a person want. The desire to go after it and the determination to see it through was all that I needed.
