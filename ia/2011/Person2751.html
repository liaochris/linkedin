Andy Corgan's Summary

I am a Senior Treaty Officer (similar to Client Manager), in a client facing role, dealing with all aspects of operational risk relating to the smooth running of (reinsurance) contracts.

A main focus is in providing the best service to clients by understanding their business and requirements. I will lead regular meetings at Senior Manager and Director level for existing business including product developments, treaty negotiations and all other operational aspects of the business.

I am heavily involved both internally and with clients in the procurement of new business and once a new treaty has been won will lead the take-on process to ensure the smooth and timely transition of business to Munich Re.

I lead and facilitate the communication between both internal departments and Munich Re and the client in all aspects of the business including the collation and production of Management Information (MI) data and reports to aid analysis of treaty performance and business knowledge both externally and internally.

Alongside functional experts I will aid clients in analysing and refining processes and procedures to increase efficiency and accuracy of underwriting, claims management, accounting and data production.

As well as the external responsibilities, the role includes developing and improving internal, cross-departmental processes and procedures using Business Project Management (BPM) and Six Sigma techniques and methodology. I am Green Belt trained in Lean Six Sigma.

I am Dip CII qualified and the Senior Examiner for the Chartered Insurance Institute (CII) for the Group Risk examination. This involves heading and organising a team of fellow experts writing, editing and setting the questions that candidates will be required to answer and ensuring all is achieved to the required timetable.
