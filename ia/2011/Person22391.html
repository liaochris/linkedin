Joe Grier's Summary

Blah, Blah, Blah - over 25 years of this, over 25 years of that - "in my best English accent", Chairman of the Board of this, developed turnkey soluuuutions for thahht. Hummm - how many of us are caught up in the stringent controls of a "job" or the ultra sharp demands of a "career"? I would love to liken us to the gerble on the treadmill just running in place - but he/she (don't wanna leave out women - could be bad for business and politics) (back to the gerble who is) actually getting healthier because of it's workout - those of us (eh-hem') those of YOU - working 70-80 hours a week, including the "tee time" at 7am - who are missing out on the fortunes and the fun of family and raising wonderful functional children - let's just say, I certainly don't envy you.

I am living my dream - my passion - and having fun doing it. I take time for my children when I WANT and need to. I am personally held hostage to the desires of achievement, but whoa! If you could just taste the balance of life that I deliciously pour myself each day? And the dose comes without religious shackles and professional mandates. The desire is there because my mother, father and family instilled the competitive spirit in me........but my flavor of BALANCE comes out of my personal rebellion to let a 70-80 hour a week "job" turn me into someone who drinks the coolade and believes that "THE RAT RACE IS NECESSARY!"

My Professional Experience? A successful business career in making the priority my people - never losing the ability to convey to those that work for me or with me - that no matter how technically sound we might find ourselves - HUMANITY must be at the core of what we do.

My Goals? To be the best human being I can be. Nothing more - and certainly - nothing less. To hold fast to this lifestyle and thinking - with the creative waters of calm to capture me and the faith in the Universe that the money will come - but only if I build it (my business) from this foundation.
