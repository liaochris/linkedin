Ruth Mattson's Summary

Ruth Mattson’s law practice emphasizes estate and trust planning and administration; estate, gift and generation-skipping tax planning including planning for international and multinational individuals and couples; business tax planning and individual income tax planning. Ms. Mattson is also experienced in the areas of probate litigation, guardianships, conservatorships and the administration of contentious estates.

Ruth Mattson practices in the Worcester, Framingham and Boston offices of the law firm of Bowditch & Dewey.
