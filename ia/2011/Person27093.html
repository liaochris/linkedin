Luis Aponte’s Summary

Luis is an experienced professional in the field of Human Resource and Organizational Development. He has helped companies and entrepreneurs formulate and implement strategy, develop leaders, and implement processes to accelerate growth.

Clients include TV/entertainment, city governments, warehousing and distribution, financial institutions, insurance companies, IT groups, and consumer services.

He is a skilled speaker, presenter, trainer, and facilitator.
He has received certifications in human resources and leadership development. He was nominated for the South Florida Business Journal Excellence in Human Resources award and in 2010 received the Pinnacle award from the Miramar/Pembroke Pines Chamber of Commerce.

Education: Bachelors Degree in Communication and Organizational Leadership.

Community Service: Volunteer Treasurer for a nonprofit, Chairperson for the Miramar/Pembroke Pines Human Resource Consortium and volunteer at Have a Heart Charities.

Luis Aponte’s Specialties:

Strategic and Business Planning, Executive Coaching, Organizational Assessments, Change Management, Recruitment and Retention, Performance Management, Training and Development, Call Center Training, Instructional Design, Leadership Development, Corporate Culture Assessments, Sales Development, Customer Loyalty, and Time Strategies.
