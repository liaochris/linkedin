Susan Sharma's Summary

I believe India's biggest wealth, apart from her people, is her natural bounty. I also believe nature can or should pay for itself; through eco-tourism, photography and knowledge dissemination.

IndianWildlifeClub.com and Wildscapes.net are independent ventures, partly financed by proceeds of educational videos on nature, sale of photographs and advertisment income. The portal uses the latest available online technologies to maintain a web based community for life long education about nature and environment on the web.

Trailers of our nature videos can be viewed at

http://www.youtube.com/group/wildbytes

Bringing naturescapes to everyone's drawing room, be it through photographs or videos -that is my vision.

Awareness through knowledge
Conservation through awareness
