Marianne Kulka's Summary

Experienced in all areas of Recruitment Marketing including strategic sourcing solutions, employment branding, internet based solutions, jobs optimization, SEM, recruiting videos, employee referral programs---ways to drive qualified and right fit candidates to your recruiting microsites.

Interested in speaking at professional associations on how to leverage the internet for recruiting.
