Laura Crawford’s Summary

I am a Creative Director and Media Strategist with more than 15 years of experience in media design and interaction. With a focus on creating meaningful and engaging experiences in both the physical and virtual space, I drive business and media strategies through the application of trans-media and interaction design solutions.

In 2005, I received my Master of Fine Arts degree in Media Design from Art Center College of Design in Pasadena. Prior to my graduate studies I had an extensive career in broadcast design, media production, motion graphic and interaction design.

As an Entrepreneur, an educator, Creative Director, and strategist, my experience in cross-platform design disciplines and unique creative passions allow me to push the new evolution of technology, social media and interaction design to find new incarnations of engagement.
