Peter Lamb’s Summary

I enjoy applying my Electrical Engineering background to the realm of National Instruments products. NI has a unique computer-based hardware and software approach to test, design, and automation, and it's exciting to be a part of such an industrial "revolution."

Outside of being an engineer, my interests are many and varied. Family life comes first, but I also enjoy a wide variety of sports and hobbies.
