Danielle Baker's Summary

Welcome one and all!

A bit about myself: I started reading when I was 4 years old and I haven't put down a book since. English and language have been passions of mine all my life. So much so, that I've decided to dedicate my life to it! I started by tutoring kids one on one and working within the LA School district. Although I loved helping kids learn, something was missing in my job-life. That's when a colleague introduced me to the world of Copywriting. I was fascinated. I found that utilizing my understanding of language, I could get others excited about markets and services they hadn't known about before!

After following some of the great copywriters of our time, I set up my own freelance copywriting business.

I currently manage blogs, create white-paper material, sales pages, and mailers. I'm always up for any writing job, so if you're a business, a one-footed dragon, or just a friendly person who wants to say hello, feel free to connect and send me a message!

Cheers!
