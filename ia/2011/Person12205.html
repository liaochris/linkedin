Privately Held; Internet industry

July 2010 – Present (9 months)

It enables content creators to generate micro payments and make incremental money from their creations. It simplifies the access to unique quality content by offering a one-click, pay as you go solution to online consumers. It works on web-enabled devices and for any kind of online content and avoids the hassle of multiple subscriptions.

Cleeng is backed up by top industry influencers and gathers a team of international online experts.
