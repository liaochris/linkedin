Arko Brakel, van's Summary

My mission in life is to support entrepreneurship and innovation.

Entrepreneurship is my passion. More than that, entrepreneurship is a lifestyle. In my vision, the entrepreneurial way of life will become extremely important for us to deal with the challenges the communication age is offering us. There are less and less certainties in life. The only guarantee we have is in ourselves, in our passion, in our dreams and above all in our capability to follow our dreams and passion.

My goal is to help people and organizations become or remain successful in the communication age. I am specialized in Online marketing, E-Business (web2.0), innovation and entrepreneurship. Training, advising, counseling, power sessions, motivation and inspiration sessions, workshops, events and projects are examples of the activities I like to perform. On a regular basis and at several events I pick up the moderator role quite often.

Interaction with people, trying to help them getting the best out of themselves is my passion. Mt approach is very powerful and energetic. Last but not least I have to mention my great network of brilliant friends and colleagues. They are all people I can always rely on: entrepreneurs, consultants, project managers, creatives, ICT-specialists, professors, etc. etc. I love them all!

Specialties

Inspiration, motivation, lots of energy, web2.0, online marketing, entrepreneurship, innovation, trainer, speaker, moderator, coach of entrepreneurs, discussion leader, a brilliant network of talented people
