Gary Phillips's Summary

HSEQ Corporate is an independent, specialised Health, Safety, Environment and Quality Recruitment Company formed in 2008

We are a privately owned and operated recruitment service company with offices in Perth & Singapore.

* We operate exclusively in the Mining, Oil and Gas, Construction and Engineering sectors and our clients range from engineering consultancies, major owner operator oil companies, international EPCM’s to the largest diversified mining companies in the world.

* We provide Health Safety, Environment and Quality professionals of all levels including those experienced in Risk Management, Emergency Response and Training.

* At HSEQ Corporate we relish the fact that we are helping to make the work place a safer environment for all, and we are proud to be aligned in a corporate partnership with the Safety Institute of Australia.

* We have access to a total of 38,500 HSE&Q practitioners located in Australia and New Zealand and a worldwide database of over 300,000 candidates.
