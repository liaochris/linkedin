Lu Ann Reeb's Summary

Emmy award-winning Television Executive Producer turned entrepreneur. Launched Boston Media Group after two other new media marketing companies, Skyways Communications in 1999, and Legal Talk Network in 2005. Messaging and marketing to a target audience is the key to communications and the foundation of our services. Our clients hire us for the the high quality of our creative development and our expertise reaching the right audience! I'm also a faculty member of the Marketing/Communications Department at Emerson College in Boston.

Specialties

New Media Marketing Strategy. Public Relations. Creative media production: online video, podcasting, webcasting, project management & speaking engagements.
