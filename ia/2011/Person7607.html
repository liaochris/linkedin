Chad Brocker's Summary

Senior level management for Creative teams designing media for on-line Education and Corporate Training. I work with the other team leads to deliver content utilizing 3D/ VFX, Flash, Video, Instructional Design, Photography and Gaming. We work with client's Subject Matter Experts (SMEs) and/or faculty to create fully interactive learning modules.

Art direction and design management have been goal since college. I was fortunate to begin my career just a few months after graduating. I have always been a 'working manager'. I like to get my hands dirty and learn new applications and technologies. Building my skill set is an ongoing goal of mine.

I am currently working with Gaming and Virtual Worlds. However I plan to move on to Mobile Apps next since I will be able to utilize all my skills with Interface Design, Graphical Content, User Experience and Aesthetic Consistency.

Specialties

3D Modeling, Animation Director, Graphic Design, Product Display Design and Retail Advertisement.
