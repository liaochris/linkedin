Derrick W. Rivers, MBA’s Summary

Marketing strategist who knows how to win new customers and keep them coming back. Expertise in delivering robust bottom line results through strong creative capability, inspirational leadership of creative teams, and a proven ability to make connections with consumers.

More than 12 years of agency and Fortune 500 corporate experience translating customer insights into revenue-generating marketing campaigns using integrated communications platforms. Grounded in latest new media strategies that propel brand awareness.
