Rob Casey's Summary

Christ changed my entire life when He saved me in the midst of my first full time ministry position. I was a youth pastor and worship leader for a church in Anna, Texas. In my heart, I was on a secret mission to climb the unseen corporate church ladder, to really be somebody that everyone would recognize by name.

The Lord has radically altered my life. Now, my greatest aim is to lead people into a deep and meaningful, interpersonal relationship with the Lord Jesus Christ by coming alongside the lives the Lord allows me to lead, doing life together, learning from one another, and growing simultaneously into the ever unfolding image of our Lord, making disciples that make disciples that make disciples.

I no longer seek for ministry to take place just from behind the pulpits and podiums He allows me to preach and teach from, but in homes, back alleys, workplaces, front porches, coffee shops and the dinner table as well.

I am deeply burdened over the discipleship problem that is the large elephant in our sanctuaries that the modern church seems to be stretching their necks to look around week after week. The cost of this apathy toward the call of Christ is generational and eternally catastrophic.

My desire is to lead the church out of death and into life, out of apathy and complacency into vibrancy and real ministry.
