Chris Ihrig’s Summary

Chris Ihrig, Talent Management Executive
With over twenty-five years of corporate leadership experience, including fifteen years as an executive, Chris has a proven success philosophy that advances people and establishes corporate vision around the creation of dynamic Talent Management cultures. Chris provides visionary leadership with action principles based on creativity, imagination, faith, love, insight, passion, and boldness.

As an industry leading talent management consultant, Chris partners with the Sr. Leadership in the development and implementation of Talent Management platforms. Areas include Strategic Planning, Business and People Alignment, and organization wide change initiatives that invest in people including Training & Development Programs, Mentoring, Coaching, Leadership Development and Succession Planning.

Chris' commitment to excellence in service provides a focused and intentional investment in the people and systems of these organizations ultimately leading to their ability to provide greater community impact and social change.

Chris has coordinated extensive employee Talent Management programs from conception to implementation. Through his timely and supportive consultation to organizations in the areas of strategic planning, talent management, organizational change and leadership development, Chris has partnered with leadership of teams representing all shapes and sizes to successfully work through powerful change management processes that create healthy Talent Management cultures.

As a passionate team facilitator who brings energy, fun, focus and strategy to team development events for group of 5 - 350+, Chris has successfully led over 500 team building sessions throughout the country.

Talent Management Systems & Philosophy Development & Implementation, Succession Planning, 360 Appraisals, Performance Systems, Cultural change programs, Organizational Assessments & Surveys, Executive Coaching, Leadership Development
