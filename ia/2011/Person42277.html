Associate Director

Ironstone Funds Management

Real Estate industry

July 2009 – Present (1 year 9 months)

ABOUT IRONSTONE

Part of the Ironstone Group of companies, Ironstone Funds is a ASIC regulated boutique funds management business specialising in the residential property asset class.

INVESTMENT OBJECTIVE

To outperform the major residential markets of Australia over the long term.

The Fund is suitable for long-term investors - at least five years - and who are looking for capital growth and the potential for income over the longer term.


INVESTMENT STRATEGY

The Fund invests primarily in quality established residential property for lease in Australia, and seeks to outperform the Australian Residential Property Market through:

Selecting assets with scarcity value
Securing assets below market value where possible
Adding value to properties through additional amenities, renovations, etc.
Managing portfolio intensively to optimise overall net return to investors
Geographic diversification, which is expected to enable the Fund to take advantage of the differing property cycles across Australia

RECENT PERFORMANCE

Ironstone's flagship fund, 'Ironstone Residential Fund' was the #1 performing unlisted property fund of 2009 (Morningstar performance rankings). The first of its kind in the world, the Ironstone Residential Fund invests solely in Australian residential property and has delivered 13.0% returns (net of all fees and costs) to its investors since inception vs. benchmark market growth of 4.4% (according to the Australian Bureau of Statistics). This was as at February 2010.

Ironstone is committed to delivering innovative managed fund solutions for investors worldwide who wish to gain access to Australian residential property within their investment portfolio.


HOW TO ACCESS?

For as little as a $10,000 investment, Investors can gain access to a portfolio of high quality, professionally managed Australian residential properties, providing valuable diversification within the asset class.
