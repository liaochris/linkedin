Jeff Boddy’s Summary

Hailing from the Ringling College of Art + Design's world-renowned Computer Animation program, Jeff Boddy began his career at Ozone Studios in 2000. He was quickly sought after for his art direction and presentation skills, enabling him to experience all aspects of film & video production. In 2004, Jeff joined the creative department at Blue Diesel where he met fellow Ringling alum Robb Dierken. He left the ad agency world for Dierken's ultra-hip motion graphics studio, Spacejunk Media, in 2005. Two years later, Jeff "took the whole tribe cross-country in the family truckster" and relocated to Los Angeles, where he freelances as a motion designer and 3d generalist.

Jeff's work has been seen at several film and animation festivals including SIGGRAPH, The New York Digital Salon, Anima Mundi (Brazil), Puchon (South Korea), ReelHeArt (Toronto), and on the covers of Millimeter and Production Reality magazines. He has also been a guest speaker at the Columbus College of Art & Design and Ohio's 24 Hour Sci-Fi Marathon.

Jeff Boddy’s Specialties:

Various animation techniques, compositing, styleframe design. Preferred software: Autodesk Maya, Adobe After Effects, Photoshop
