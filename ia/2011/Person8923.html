Sheila Galligan www.organize6figureoffice.com’s Summary

Sheila Galligan is known as the Office Organizer with vision. This vision allows her to see the positive possibilities the moment she enters her client's workspace. Sheila's humor, enthusiasm, and no judgment attitude add to her genuine approach to the task at hand. As a reformed paper-piler and email over-loader she can relate first-hand to the challenges her clients face.

Ever since leaving corporate America in 2008 as a meeting and special events planner and project manager for FORTUNE 500 companies her organizing skills slipped. During this time Sheila was not only taking care and dealing with the death of her mother from terminal cancer but was also struggling to keep her new business afloat solo. Sheila became the unorganized organizer. It was not until Sheila started working with a life coach in 2010 that she was able to identify her disorganization issues, live in the present, and focus herself and business on the future with no judgments. These emotional experiences and determination helps Sheila partner with entrepreneurs and small business owners, to reach their 6-figure sales goals, by setting up the simple 5-step ZONES organizing process.

Sheila has studied with Anne Blumer, whom is among the inaugural 200 Professional Organizers in the world to receive the Certified Professional Organizer CPO®. Since joining the National Association of Professional Organizers (NAPO-MN) in 2008 Sheila has built a successful account base in the Twin Cities. She is also an active member in the Edina and Eden Prairie Chamber of Commerce's where she has presented numerous times to networking and entrepreneur groups coaching them to be organized and productive. Since starting OrganizeThis (formally Clutter Control MN) in 2008 Sheila has touched 500+ disorganized, cluttered, and stressed out people through her 5-step ZONES 6-figure office organizing system.
