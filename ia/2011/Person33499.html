Oscar G. Hormigos's Summary

Oscar Hormigos was born in 1973 in Madrid.

He too graduated from the Complutense University, with a Bachelor’s Degree in Communication. He hasn’t shut up since…


After a year as Art Director, Oscar became a partner of WYSIWYG in 1997 and also the managing director. From 1996 -2001, the agency won numerous awards for creativity (Cannes, San Sebastian, New York, FIAP, AMPE) and Oscar never missed a gala dinner.


In summer 2001, he created interactive communication agency Zentropy for McCann-Erickson WorldGroup. Among his satisfied clients are companies like Barclays, Coca-Cola, Dell, Loewe, Lois, Microsoft, Nokia, Telefonica, and Volkswagen…


Oscar spent 2003 – 2005 traveling throughout Asia (the seeker…) and began to collect the stories that became the origin of Mola Noguru a fun new series for children, inspired by the universal heritage of storytelling. Since 2005 to 2008, he has been telling long and involved stories at Zinkia  Entertainment.


In April 2008 Oscar returns to Spain after travelling for 5 months in South America. The following day he receives a call from David Cantolla who asks him to join as founding partner of Planet Idea. From 2008 to late 2009, Oscar has combined setting up Planet Idea with other activities including strategic development for the launch of Faster, a new brand created for the Formula 1 racing driver Fernando Alonso. He has also served as curator of the festival Art Futura.


In 2010 Oscar creates a new television format called "IDEAS TO GO" and begins the financing stage and pre-production. On the side he has been working on co-creation projects with Coca-Cola, DNX and Yorokobu...

In July 2010 Oscar Hormigos with his friend David Cantolla founded Wake app!  A company that designs, develops and publishes applications for mobile, television and computers from a different perspective.
