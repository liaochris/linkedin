Nola Hennessy's Experience

Founder and Managing Director

Serenidad Consulting Pty Ltd

Management Consulting industry

January 2010 – Present (1 year 3 months)

Nola Anne Hennessy was born in Sydney Australia in 1957. She is renowned for her people skills, her love of life and fun, and her astounding level of energy and passion. Recognised widely for her business acumen, with no exceptions Nola successfully delivers up what's considered "too hard" by others.

Nola became a senior manager at 16 (in hospitality) and has three times risen to the top of her chosen profession, first at 19 (in hospitality) then at 31 (in finance and human resources) and again at 52. Described by Price Waterhouse (1992) as a "polished professional" who will "continue to bring credit to herself and the organisations with which she is associated" and by Myer (1981) as someone whose "integrity is undoubted", Nola is most proud of the positive contribution she makes, and the change and improvement she can initiate and embed.

Nola is founder and managing director of Serenidad Consulting Pty Ltd, an Australian company that provides global consultancy services in excellence, great leadership and positive personal power.

Nola's literary works are a foundation block for her work as an international consultant - Nola does what she teaches, promoting peace, calmness and compassion in every context.
