Tara Garkowski's Summary

A licensed Realtor who has been living in Central Florida area for the last 24 years, I'm a Certified REO Specialist and New Home Construction Specialist with over 8 years of Real Estate Industry Experience, 4 of which were working with a Fortune 500 Home Builder placing within the TOP 10 in the Nation. I have a vast knowledge of alternative financing for first-time home owners and credit challenged buyers; including, but not limited to, down payment assistance, 100% financing and credit repair. With an extensive network of New Home Builders, Realtors, Mortgage Professionals, Home Inspectors, Distressed Property Preservationist and Interior Designers; along with being a hardworking and dedicated master negotiator who is always working towards the best interest of her clients, I have the necessary tools to make the Real Estate process smooth and as stress free as possible.
