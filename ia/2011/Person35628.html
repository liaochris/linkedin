Fawad Asrar Qureshi's Publications

  • Telecom Network Capacity Planning

    • Teradata Magazine
    • November 25, 2010
    Authors: Fawad Asrar Qureshi

    Network Capacity Planning is tedious exercise in a telecom, making the right investment becomes very critical in these economic downtimes; in order to make the right decision for your Telecom investment you need to have an integrated view of your whole network not just from a technical performance perspective but also from CapEx, Opex, Revenue, ARPU, Churn at each element within your network.

    Traditionally there are several network performance applications (most of the times of different vendors) which are available in different data sources. Reconciling the data to generate a consolidated view of the network is a cumbersome task; tracking the impacts of different events such as site upgrades, change of network element software/hardware is nearly impossible.

    What if you get a single picture of your network on a map; with all the stimulating components relevant to the network element displayed on it using an interactive dashboard and thus enabling you to make well informed decisions!
