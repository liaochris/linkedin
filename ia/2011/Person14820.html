Fiona Cherbak's Summary

Partner/owner of ThemePark Studios, a marketing and media production company offering comprehensive marketing strategy and communications services, innovative public relations, event production, graphic design and web design.

Clients have included Activision, Comedy Central, Entertainment Merchants Association, Fantastic Fest and Fantastic Arcade, Fox Broadcasting Corporation, GameRecruiter, GSD&M Idea Entertainment, HBO's U.S. Comedy Arts Festival, Hypernia Hosting Corp., IEEE, Intelligent Transportation Systems, IGS Software, Jose Cuervo, KMFA Classical, Kinesoft Development, Motion Capture Society, Nickelodeon, Origin Systems, RC Cola, Technical Business Network, Texas Book Festival and numerous other companies and non-profit organizations.

Most recently served as VP of Business Development for GameX (Games & Media Expo), a national game consumer show and game industry conference sponsored by NBC and affiliated brands SyFy Channel, NBC Home Entertainment, Universal Pictures and IGA Worldwide. Responsible for building international marketing and PR strategy, developing key strategic, media and synergy relationships and driving overall business development.

Formerly VP of Marketing for GameRecruiter, premier search firm in the games industry, responsible for developing global marketing strategy and facilitating high-visibility marketing campaigns and initiatives, with focus on promotional partnerships, publicity and trade show participation.

Previously Director of Client Relations and Sr. Account Manager for L.A.-based recruiting firm Enterprise Talent, Director of Business Development and Sr. Account Manager for Mary-Margaret, and Senior Recruiting Specialist for Disney Feature Animation in Artist Development and CG Programming.

Specialties

Executive recruiting for interactive entertainment industry; marketing strategy and long-range planning; publicity and media relations; business development; account management; corporate partnerships, sales and events; non-profit fundraising; investor relations and investor presentations.
