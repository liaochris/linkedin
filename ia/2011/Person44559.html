Benjamin Umpleby’s Summary

I have enjoyed the opportunity to work with both internationally recognized agencies and local boutique search firms within the recruitment industry for over a decade.

While "headhunting" and recruiting mid to senior level professionals across various disciplines and industries, my focus has always been simply the building and maintaining of lasting relationships. As a result, I have been fortunate over my career to work with some amazing people as clients, as candidates, and sometimes both.

We built RedBranch Executive Search & Recruitment on the foundation of these relationships and we look forward to another decade of servicing them while continuing to build even more.

Get to know us at www.RedBranch.ca
