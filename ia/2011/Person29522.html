Megan Crosbie’s Summary

Megan Crosbie, a graduate of Eastern Michigan University, discovered her passion for all things digital during her internship with the Ann Arbor Area Chamber of Commerce. Recently, Megan Crosbie landed a job with the Charles Reinhart Company as Marketing Coordinator. The Charles Reinhart Company is the definitive market leader in the greater Ann Arbor area with annual sales of more than $380 million.

As Marketing Coordinator for the Charles Reinhart Company, Megan is primarily responsible for their online presence, including the social media strategy, website and blog. Megan assists with marketing training classes for agents as well as event and activity planning. Megan also works with agents on their website and new media strategies.

In addition to her full-time marketing position with Reinhart, Megan is also an age-group swim coach for the Saline Stingrays.

Megan is on the Steering Committee and Co-Chair of the Marketing Committee for the Women’s Exchange of Washtenaw as well as an active member and Secretary of Lunch Ann Arbor Marketing. Megan is very passionate about helping non-profits including: Ele’s Place, the Leukemia & Lymphoma Society and the American Red Cross.
