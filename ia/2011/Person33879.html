Pat Ramsey's Summary

With over a decade of experience in website creation and development, I've seen several stages of how we use the Web and how we build for the Web. My goal is to educate, through both my work and my teaching, developers and clients of the success that can be achieved through good code, organization and design.

Over a period of several years I became the self-appointed Web Standards and Accessibility go-to guy at Southwestern University. In April of 2007, I left Southwestern to strike out on my own. I am active in Refresh Austin, a group of Web professionals interested in the exciting growth and advances in Web technologies, and I serve as both an adviser and trainer for Knowbility's Accessible Internet Rallies. I've also conducted presentations and professional training in areas of accessibility, captioning multimedia for the Web, and Cascading Style Sheets (CSS).

Specialties

Web Standards, Universal Design, Accessibility, Website inception and management, Information Architecture, online video, digital imaging & photography, Microformats, Wordpress, XHTML, CSS, PHP, MySQL, jQuery
