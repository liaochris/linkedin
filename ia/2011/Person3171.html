Anne Robinson's Summary

Anne is an accomplished and gifted staffing professional who, over the last 20 plus years, has successfully and cost effectively recruited superb candidates across many industries including healthcare, high technology, banking, retail, academia, utilities, and manufacturing etc.

Anne’s primary focus over the past 10 years has been Healthcare Recruiting. She has successfully placed hundreds of healthcare professionals for hospitals, in/out patient clinics, physical therapy clinics, orthopedic clinics, long term care facilities, hospice facilities and assisted living facilities.

Anne began her successful and progressive career as a human resource generalist providing staffing, employee relations, benefits administration and compliance, and human resources administration and reporting for a range of organizations headquartered in the Northeast.

Realizing that her talent and love is in the sub-specialty field of recruiting, Anne moved to performing contract assignments while building her own human resources staffing firm, AWHR Consulting, which she founded and served as President & Principal Consultant, until relocating to Scottsdale, AZ in December 2006.
