  • Owner

    America's First State Capital Group, Inc.

    (Financial Services industry)

    July 2000 — Present (10 years 7 months)

    Established in July, 2000, America’s First State Capital Group, Inc. is a financial consulting and mortgage loan origination company and represents the logical outgrowth of the business activities of the principals of the company in combination with the trends in economic development in real estate in the state of Delaware, focusing on Lewes, Georgetown, Millsboro, Rehoboth, Milton, and Dover. Recently, in response to client requests, we have expanded our services to include income tax reporting.

    The owners have long-term careers in the financial arena. Both principals possess wide-ranging backgrounds of over twenty years in financial services professions, including insurance, securities, estate and financial planning, real estate investment and management. The company focus is on providing for the needs of individuals and businesses in the central and lower areas of Delaware through a high integrity, high service avenue to the capital and mortgage loans necessary to keep the growth in these areas moving, using the skills, knowledge, and business and personal contacts of the principals.
