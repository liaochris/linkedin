Tom Schulte’s Summary

Tom Schulte is an author, speaker, executive coach, and leadership trainer located in Atlanta, GA.

He is also Executive Director of Linked 2 Leadership, a global group of over 14,000+ members dedicated to Leadership Development, Organizational Health and Personal & Professional Growth.

:: Leadership Content ::

He is editor, publisher, author, and blogger trainer at the popular blog called The Leadership Collaboratory™. With 40+ Contributing Authors, The L2L Blogazine (Blog + Magazine = Blogazine) is building the central spot on the web where leaders can learn, grow and build other leaders at www.Linked2Leadership.com.

He also leads The RockStar Leader blog at www.RockStarLeader.wordpress.com for people interested in the intersection of Music & Leadership.

:: Leadership Resources ::

His firm Recalibrate Professional Development creates and delivers short-format "High-Impact Leadership Training fit for the Blackberry-Attention-Span". These licensed programs called Leadership PowerLabs are perfectly designed for breakout sessions, executive meetings, interactive keynote addresses and private training sessions. These can last from 50-minutes up to a half-day. Also suitable for sales training and motivational sessions.

Recalibrate Professional Development can assist corporate leaders and their teams with "Values-Based" leadership principles to show them:

~ Who they are as a leader

~ Where they are on their leadership journey

~ How to get better results through values-based relationships
