Firoz Khan's Summary

Being a Psychology Graduate always felt my people management skills were far better than others and hence pursued my career in Operations & Supply Chain which is full of People & Processes. Later pursued my Marketing Management from University of Mumbai.

Right out of college wanting to know how things work on the media front, took up an offer with then Mumbai's second best eveninger "Afternoon" the Busy Bee’s newspaper.

With the hunger to learn more on the internet front I grabbed the opportunity to be a part of the e-commerce revolution that Rediff.com was planning.

Spent almost 6 years building and re-building the e-commerce operations for rediff.com, kept working closely with different breeds in technology, operations, finance, sales & marketing and editorial and it only helped me understand them better so as to manage them better.

It was time to move on with the next upbeat thing Online Gaming, MMORPG - Massively Multiplayer Online Role Playing Games

Level Up Games! Philippines most successful online gaming company was trying to get a grip on the Indian market with their most popular MMORPG, Ragnarok and I took the opportunity to be a part of this start-up as well. A lot of unlearning had to happen before I took the responsibility as Business Head for Mumbai. Youth Marketing was the key to this business and the learning from this start-up was fruitful.

Then came along this opportunity knocking at my door, yet again another online start-up, but this time with a Retailer "Future Group's" online arm, FutureBazaar.com

Started FutureBazaar in 2005 and still continuing to build it. Setting up a world class Supply Chain operations for e-retailing first of its kind in the Indian environment.

Retail is at its peak and what else; I am being part of yet another boom learning & unlearning business once again.

Specialties

Supply Chain Operations, Order Management, CRM, Payment Gateway Management
