Mark Cohen's Summary

Mark is a technology professional with significant experience in the web and online media industries, and in IT leadership and management capacities in general. His most recent experience is in his current role as the CIO at Deals Direct, Australia’s largest online retailer. This is both an operational and strategic role, with current focus on engaging vendors to secure key technologies as logical platform extensions, introducing industry best-practices such as Agile development, Continuous Integration, and a lightweight project management methodology to provide transparency and daily guidance to the technology team and the broader business.

Prior to this role, Mark was the Technology Director for the Classifieds Division at Fairfax Digital, providing both guidance and general IT management to the Classifieds division, and to the four technology teams within it. Mark was also part of the technology council, formed by the CEO to assess internal technology practices and existing assets and to deliver a roadmap for technology within the organization going forward. Mark reported directly to the CIO, and was also part of the Classifieds Executive, reporting to the Managing Director of the Classifieds division.

Mark is currently involved in strategically significant technology projects that will potentially help change the face of online retail in Australia

Specialties

Technology management of large technology teams
Managing technical projects from start to finish using cross-functional teams
Vendor management - including geographically remote vendors
Solid industry knowledge of online media
Advocate of agile technical project management
Managing business, IT and vendor staff in a matrix management structure
