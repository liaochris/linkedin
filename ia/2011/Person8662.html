Cindy Kubica's Summary

Are you focused on what gives you an Energetic Edge or is your edge dull from managing stress? According to the law of attraction you draw to you what you think about most. When you focus on managing stress, your focus is on stress – guess what you attract? It's time to move beyond stress management to energy management. I will show you how to embrace your strengths, insist on a healthier approach to life, and sharpen your Energetic Edge.

I’m a high energy person and use that energy in my presentations. In fact, it was my audience members who first started calling me ‘The Energy Coach.’ I’m an active member of the National Speakers Association (NSA) and co-owner of a commercial photography studio with photographer Farris Poole. My current audio books are: In Search of Normal: Moving Life from Chaos to Clarity; and Fit to Communicate/Fit to Lead. My bound books include Career Compass for Women and I Want a Love Story to Happen to Me.

Specialties

Behavior, attitude, human energy, power of attraction, stress management, health and wellness, creativity, innovative thinking
