Cindy L. Herb’s Summary

Cindy L. Herb is a healed rape victim and author of the book that has been endorsed by Maria Shriver, Awakening the Spirit: The Open Wide Like a Floozy Chronicles. She passionately advocates a way to overcome obstacles and understand adverse events that may have occurred in people's lives.

She has over 25 years in training, team building and communication skills with a leading global consulting firm. In that role, she developed associates to perform at their highest potential through instruction, motivation, and encouragement.

Cindy L. Herb is always open to speak to any group, including conferences, national conventions, and women's groups. As a public speaker and author, she is an engaging and entertaining speaker, whose love of life and joy is very uplifting and contagious. Virtually anyone can benefit from her story and message, especially those individuals who suffered a painful past or feel unsure of their future. She inspires others to lead a more constructive life.

Cindy L. Herb can help anyone who has endured adversity heal and find joy through a simple, proven process! To download Cindy's FREE guide, 'Some Simple Steps to Healing,' visit her website at http://www.cherbchronicles.com.

    Cindy L. Herb is the author of 'Awakening the Spirit: The Open Wide Like a Floozy Chronicles,' endorsed by Maria Shriver. Her expertise is supported by many first-hand, life-changing experiences.

    On July 31, 1966 at the tender age of nine, while throwing papers for a local newspaper, a stranger raped Cindy L. Herb. Because of that trauma, and the reactions of shame and guilt by others, Cindy L. Herb felt isolated and alone. She now, as a healed rape survivor, is an inspirational author and speaker who passionately advocates a way to overcome obstacles and understand adverse events that may have occurred in people's lives.

    The author writes as a result of her personal discoveries, in the hopes of inspiring others and to show that, despite adversity, there is always a way to overcome life’s tribulations along the path to finding true happiness and joy.

    Cindy L. Herb’s approach is unique in that it offers a different spin on spirituality—one that enhances western religious philosophies while offering an alternative way to look at healing.

    Upon listening to her speak or reading her book, you will come to realize that guilt, shame and anger are a function of the mind, not the spirit, and simple adjustments in thinking can dissolve these emotions. Tragic events occur as an individual challenge to allow the spirit of God to flourish in each and every one of us. She provides a plan on how to end pain, and a deep understanding that you are never alone.
