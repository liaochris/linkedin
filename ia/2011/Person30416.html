Michelle Cowan's Summary

I am an English and Law graduate with several years of experience in web technologies, marketing and team management.

My most recent permanent position was that of Senior Marketing Manager: Sport for the University of Johannesburg, South Africa. This role involved significant responsibility for the sport unit's strategic direction and the corresponding marketing and communications plan.

The role also involved using the university's website as an important marketing tool as the university is widely recognised as one of the top sporting institutions in South Africa.

I have strong experience in managing web deliverables and in assessing web effectiveness.

My previous roles were largely web-oriented and I have a wealth of experience and understanding in web development and technology.

I left the university in 2008 when my husband was transferred to Norway for business purposes before being transferred to the UK in July 2009, where we shall now be based indefinitely.

I am currently engaged in freelance web work, so my web skills are up to date. I am entirely familiar with all the latest technology, including web analytics, search engine optimisation, social media and social networking.

My people skills have been refined over the years through delivering staff training (an early position in my career) and managing teams of one to six people. I thoroughly enjoy working with people and have a good track record in the management of both people and projects.

I have always leaned towards community involvement and have served on several committees in my life. The most notable of these was my role as Head of the Women’s Commission for Rowing South Africa in 2007 and 2008. I gain great satisfaction from knowing that my work benefits the community.

My key skills lie strongly in communication and management. I appreciate the value of strong relationships with clients and am an accomplished relationship builder in this regard.

Specialties

Strategic planning in marketing, communications and web development; project management in a web marketing environment; client interaction; broad understanding of IT concepts (databasing; programming; multimedia; web development, Office products).
