If you're a legal professional or legal marketer, I'm very interested in your feedback. I've posted a short introduction video here http:///www.legaladpro.com/intro.html.

Product Description: Legal Ad Pro gives Lawyers the capability to produce high-end advertising and marketing materials in-house at low-cost. The software includes a comprehensive royalty-free legal stock photography library and an in-depth template library created by professional photographers and cutting edge graphic designers. Whether you have a large law firm or you're a solo practitioner, new clients often choose to hire you based upon your marketing materials such as a yellow page ad, website, brochure, etc. Shouldn't your image and marketing materials look their absolute best? You now have a resource to make sure they do! Legal Ad Pro is designed to make your public image more attractive by making your marketing materials look their absolute very best.
