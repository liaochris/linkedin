Blake Madden’s Summary

I have over 20 years experience as a full-time professional photographer. Located in Black Mountain just outside of Asheville, NC, I've been fortunate to combine my love for the western North Carolina Mountains with my work.

My career began as a photojournalist and photo editor. Capturing the "decisive moment" was the goal then, and I continue to rely on that skill today in my commercial work. It is my belief that recording an expression or catching a moment, beyond the necessary and critical technical requirements of the shoot, is what produces a great photograph.

My work has won over 40 professional awards and has been published in Time, The Wall Street Journal, US, Runner's World, Trail Runner, Links Letter, Luxury Golf & Travel, Dow Jones Investment Advisor, The Pfizer Journal, Civil War Times Illustrated, Out Here, NC Today, Our State, Zen, numerous foreign magazines including Men's Health (Germany), and The Best of Photojournalism: 1995 book.

Blake Madden’s Specialties:

Specializing in location photography, I take pride in my versatility photographing a wide variety of subjects including portraits, business and education, health and medicine, architecture, adventure/sports, scenics, event coverage, the arts, and my own personal visions.
