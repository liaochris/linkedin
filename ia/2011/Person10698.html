David Calkins's Summary

Finding and following your passion is the ultimate key to success. My professional passion is to approach the marketing and advertising process like an underdog; always seeking and mining actionable insights to create competitive advantages that result in business growth.

Throughout my 10+ year career I have built a strong knowledge base and long list of success from being a leader in the development of marketing communication strategy for auto, CPG, telecom, retail and non-profit brands. Utilizing actionable insights balanced with econometrics, I’ve developed integrated and multi-cultural strategy through various outlets including digital, social, mobile, TV, print, outdoor, radio and other unique channels targeted at young adults. Working with senior level management regularly, I've influenced, inspired and created solutions to produce sales and market share success for high and low involved consumer products.
