Erik Pace Birkholz’s Summary

ERIK PACE BIRKHOLZ, CISSP

Seasoned industry leader and renowned author Erik Pace Birkholz, CISSP is the author of the best-selling book, SPECIAL OPS: Host and Network Security for Microsoft, UNIX and Oracle, and a contributing author of five international best-selling books for Osborne/McGraw-Hill including SQL Server Security, Hacking Windows 2000 Exposed and Hacking Exposed: Network Security Secrets and Solutions. Erik is an award-winning speaker who has presented to audiences worldwide, including NATO, the Pentagon, the Federal Financial Institutions Examination Council (FFIEC), the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI), the National Security Agency (NSA), and most branches of the Department of Defense (DoD). Additionally, Erik has presented at 5 of the Black Hat Briefings as well as the RSA Conference, SANS Institute, ISSA, ISACA, IANS, TISC, WCSF, to 3000+ attendees at the Microsoft Global Briefings, to 600+ members of Microsoft’s Windows Development Team, as a VIP for the Microsoft Exchange Conference and delivered the keynote speeches at the 2006 ITEC Portland & the 2008 ISSA Secure San Diego conferences.

Birkholz has a B.S. in Computer Science and has been an information security professional since 1995. He has worked for both KPMG LLP and Ernst & Young LLP as part of their National Attack and Penetration Testing teams, Internet Security Systems (ISS) as their West Coast Security Assessment Lead, at Foundstone as a Principal Consultant and charter member of their Professional Services division, and as the President of Special Ops Security. In late 2006, Birkholz became the Manager of Global Information Security Assurance for Dell. In 2010, Erik worked as a Director for WhiteHat Security.

Erik can be reached at erikpacebirkholz@gmail.com and of course via the same mobile phone at (323) 252 5916. I hope to hear from you.

Erik Pace Birkholz’s Specialties:

Birkholz has performed hundreds of vulnerability assessments, network and web application penetration tests and security infrastructure reviews for many of the largest corporations in the world. Additionally, he has delivered security trainings, compliance audits and security awareness programs. His integrity driven approach has resulted in a successful 15-year career in Information Security that has yielded a wealth of strong trust based relationships throughout the industry.
