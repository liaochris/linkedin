Antti Vilpponen's Experience

CEO, Co-Editor, Co-Founder

ArcticStartup

Privately Held; Internet industry

December 2009 – Present (1 year 4 months)

As the CEO from December 2009, my main task has been to organise and position ArcticStartup in a market supported way. This means, we organised a select few processes to get the editorial team working in a streamlined fashion as well as creating the basis of revenues that has helped the company grow.

ArcticStartup today continues growth through the best form of investment there is, revenues. In the future, we will be creating new ways of engaging the ecosystem and supporting the creation of new growth companies.

Our goal is to grow the company to better facilitate and help in reporting about growth companies in the Nordics and Baltics. While doing so, creating a radically optimistic entrepreneurship culture.

Founder, Chairman of the Board

ArcticStartup

Privately Held; Internet industry

September 2007 – December 2009 (2 years 4 months)

I created the concept for ArcticStartup in the summer of 2007.

Later that year, I set up the site and began blogging with Miikka Kukkosuo on growth companies in Finland. We slowly grew the concept over the years as more people joined (Ville Vesterinen and Karri Saarinen) our team.

From 2008 we have organised numerous events and continue to be the number one news source for startup related news from the Nordics and Baltics globally.

I was the Chairman of the Board until December 2009 when I left one of my other companies, Gyllene Skor, to work on ArcticStartup. I took over as the CEO.
