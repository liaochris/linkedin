Leo Jennings’s Summary

Leo has special expertise in matching intellectual property strategy to business goals. His legal practice includes obtaining, evaluating, and enforcing patents and other intellectual property in the U.S. and internationally. He counsels clients on patentability, infringement, validity and enforcement issues, and on the valuation and management of intellectual property assets for all types of technology and innovations.

Leo also has experience teaching graduate courses on intellectual property in both graduate business school and law school.

A chapter written by Leo will be published in spring 2011 in the reference book "Great Patents."

He has also been a frequent presenter to visiting delegations at the U.S. Patent and Trademark Office Global Intellectual Propery Academy (GIPA).
