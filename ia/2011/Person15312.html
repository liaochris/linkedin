Fulter Hong’s Summary

Fulter is on Google's Global Advertising Operations management team. Prior to Google he spent five years with General Electric's Lighting Division as a Product Service Manager leading their quality group for Incandescent, Halogen, CFL, Automotive, and Specialty Products. Prior to GE, Fulter launched E-Tours Systems, a multimedia graphics and computing services company.

Fulter graduated from Case Western Reserve University with a degree in Electrical Engineering/Applied Physics and a Masters degree in Engineering & Management.
