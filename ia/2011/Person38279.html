Head of Investment Origination

Five Elms Capital

Venture Capital & Private Equity industry

2010 – Present (1 year)

Five Elms is a growth equity firm focused on providing capital to businesses in the financial services, business services, healthcare services, and media/information sectors.

The firm is an entrepreneur-focused investor looking to partner with management teams who have taken a concept, proven their business model, and now see exciting growth potential. Despite strong management teams and proven business models, companies are being overlooked by traditional investors because of their smaller size and smaller capital needs.

Five Elms has carved a niche in providing growth capital of $1-5 million, offering a valuable source of funding that was otherwise unavailable. We are not only a funding partner; our portfolio companies benefit from strategic guidance, thought partnership, extensive industry networks, and capital markets experience.

CDW

Privately Held; Information Technology and Services industry

2008 – 2010 (2 years)

CDW is a leading provider of technology solutions for business, government and education.

Ranked No. 41 on Forbes’ list of America’s Largest Private Companies, CDW features dedicated account managers who help customers choose the right technology products and services to best meet their unique needs.

The company’s technology specialists offer expertise in designing customized solutions, while its advanced technology engineers assist customers with the implementation and long-term management of those solutions. Areas of focus include notebooks, desktops, printers, servers and storage, unified communications, security, wireless, power and cooling, networking, software licensing and mobility solutions.

A just-in-time inventory model and two state-of-the-art distribution centers allows CDW to provide the latest technology with fast and accurate delivery. Approximately 2,000 custom-configured systems are shipped daily. CDW holds ISO 9001 and 14001 certifications.
