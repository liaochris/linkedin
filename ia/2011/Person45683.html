Wendy Ellin's Summary

As founder and president of Atlanta, GA-based Momentum, Wendy Ellin is dedicated to helping people 'get more life out of life' through a variety of organizational systems, tools and techniques. Wendy came to the organizing industry with over 20 years of corporate business, sales and entrepreneurial experience. Her roles in radio sales, radio sales management and executive administration give her a broad perspective on what it takes to be organized and successful in business.

She developed the Momentum Method to meet the needs of businesses, organizations and individuals who can benefit from having a foolproof system for running their personal and professional lives.

The Momentum Method gives you a process for making quick, simple decisions that yield sustainable results in every aspect of your life. It's these simple techniques that help jump start momentum in everything you do.

Wendy’s approach to training is engaging, interactive, and with a "let's make a difference in your life" attitude. She is 100% passionate about helping clients develop solutions to a wide variety of problems related to lack of organization. Her goal is to not change who you are, but to change the way you address your challenges for the sole purpose of having Momentum in your life.

Specialties

Helping people reduce clutter, increase ability to focus and be productive, manage their workload on a daily basis, decrease stress related to disorganization, create balance in their life.
