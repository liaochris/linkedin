Matthew Pritchard's Summary

My partner and I launched AltoBasso Photography in 2008. We meet a wide variety of photographic objectives for companies ranging from small charities to multi-national banks. Our clients include Nomura Int. plc., Teenage Cancer Trust, ACS plc., Bancroft, HMS Nelson and Breast Cancer Awareness. In the last year we covered the CNBC broadcast from Nomura's trading floor and provided the Jan 2010 cover portrait for a 'Profit & Loss' magazine. In 2009 we started branching out into video for Cambridge University’s Judge business school drawing on my experience from the TV and film industry.

I have been self-employed since 2004 as a photographer and video editor. I trained under an experienced corporate photographer in London before gaining my own clients. As an editor I worked in Italy editing documentaries for a design conference and short films which are currently doing well on the Italian festival circuits, including winning best short at the ViaGrande festival. In 2009 I worked on an MBA video insert for Cambridge university's Judge business school.

My experience prior to this was in television as a camera trainee working mainly for Sky Drama and for the BBC in Commercial Rights as well as a stint in the arts as a Programme administrator.

Specialties

Corporate portraits, promos, advertising, editorial, commercial photography, corporate events, website photography, corporate video, virals.

I mainly use Canon dSLRs and occasionally Hasselblad medium format film cameras. I also have extensive knowledge of Adobe Photoshop, Premiere and Apple's FCP and HTML / CSS.
