Anthony Armendariz's Summary

I am the Co-Founder and Partner of 39Argyle, LLC., a mobile user experience design agency based in New York and Texas. I am Co-Owner of The Mobile Armada an independent mobile and software company that creates productive and fun mobile applications.

I'm a veteran and recovering agency Creative Director and User Experience Designer with over 12 years of dedicated focus in information architecture, interaction design, visual design, and interface design of; web sites, web-based software, and mobile applications.

Specialties

Coffee Drinking, Strategic Planning, User Experience, Information Architecture, Interaction Design, Creative Direction, User Interface Design, Visual Design, Web Design, Mobile Design, Product Development, Software Project Management, and Photoshop Awesomeness.
