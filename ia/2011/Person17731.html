Hunington Sachs’s Summary

Accomplished senior executive and dealmaker with over 18 years experience advising and managing entertainment companies in game development, film, television, sports, music, digital media and technology. Experienced intellectual property, business and finance lawyer for domestic and international companies, including the Japanese venture capital company Transcosmos Inc. Certified securities arbitrator and panel chair for FINRA (formerly NASD Regulation Inc.). Prior to law and business, Hunington led troops as a Captain in US Army-Europe. He’s a creative problem solver, dealmaker, and leader. His personal motto: “Make it happen.”
