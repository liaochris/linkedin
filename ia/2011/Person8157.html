Chris Jones's Summary

I have spent my life in movies. Watching, making and helping others make them. My aim is always the same. Create a story, movie, book or seminar that connects powerfully with the individual who is experiencing it in the moment. For me, that is where true success lies.

I was raised in the North of England where I started making horror movies as a teenager. This quickly led to series of commercial features in the nineties, followed by a string of Guerilla Film Makers Handbooks and training courses in the naughties. Most recently I made a short film ‘Gone Fishing’ to relaunch my film making career, after my successful segway into writing.

For my sins, I also host the TV show for film makers, ‘The Production Office LIVE!’ Check it out.
