Philippe Nussbaumer's Summary

With a thorough scientific training, Fundamental Physics graduate, Philippe develops throughout its career a pronounced taste for the relation, a capacity to see differently, to pin-point the weak signals and to face the unexpected one.
Master degree in SME management, President of the non-profit organization Mucoviscidose-Innovation, Philippe was award-winning of the national competition of assistance to the creation of companies of Innovating Technologies 2007 (French Ministry for Research),Well-known and active in the network of cystic fibrosis he has a good expertise on information around this disease.


Philippe is in charge of external relationships, his mission is:
-To establish the link with associations of patients
-To develop partnership relations with the researchers, research organizations, and non-profit financial to support and develop research on related topics in Meveol
-To make known the results and the new therapeutic way followed by Meveol.
The ultimate goal of these actions is to place at the disposal of the patients, as soon and in best conditions as possible, this new promising treatment.
See more about Meveol program : http://www.alaxia-pharma.eu
