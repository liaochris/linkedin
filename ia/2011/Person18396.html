Ivan Leon's Summary

Ivan Leon is an accomplished multicultural communications strategist specialized in the Hispanic market. A former publicist, television producer and new media executive, he is equipped and driven to assist businesses and organizations overcome cultural communications barriers and gain a credible influence with Latino audiences throughout the Americas.

After spending 15 years at top mainstream and religious media organizations, he founded Kerux Communications Group, a multicultural consulting firm based in Dallas, Texas, with core capabilities in Hispanic market research, brand identity, media relations, multimedia production, media buying and interactive marketing. His client base extends across several industries, with a focus on retail, healthcare, education, performing arts, non-profit, and faith-based organizations.

In his previous position, he oversaw the launch of GodTube.com en Español, which attracted half a million Spanish-speaking registered users and 80 content partners, contributing to the site’s success in being named the fastest growing web property in 2007 and current leading online destination for faith-based, family friendly content.

Prior to GodTube, he worked in the news department at KXTX Telemundo 39 and KXAS NBC 5 in the Dallas-Forth Worth area. His responsibilities included collaborating with assignment desk and senior producers in identifying breaking stories and gathering related media assets, editing news packages, and managing the studio operations during live broadcasts.

A native of Santa Fe, Argentina, he moved to the United States to pursue a BA in Communications from Liberty University, where he graduated Magna Cum Laude. He then continued his education at the Dallas Theological Seminary in the Masters of Theology program. He has also received a certificate of Entrepreneurship from the University of Texas.

Ivan Leon can be reached via email at ileon [at] keruxgroup [dot] com or telephone at 214-417-5641.
