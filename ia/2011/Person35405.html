Prithvijit Sarkar's Summary

I am a 3D artist, specializing in modeling and texturing of environments, characters, vehicles, products etc.
The various aspects involved in creating a visual for a movie or game or film is what primarily attracts me to this field.

I have worked for around 5 years in this area, and have also acquired a working knowledge of shaders and preliminary lighting of environments.

I am also available for freelance projects.

Whether individual or team assignment, I look forward to serve the requirements to the best of my ability.

Specialties

Modeling of organic or inorganic assets.
UV layout using Maya and/or third-party tools.
Texture painting (photo-sourced and/or hand-painted)
Detailing in ZBrush for Normal/Displacement map creation in the Maya-ZBrush-Maya pipeline.
Optimizing of normal and displacement maps for output.
Basic environment lighting setup (software render or Mental Ray).
