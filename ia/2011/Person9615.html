Curt Ewald’s Summary

Below is Curt's last update to Linked In. His battle with cancer came to an end on Sept. 5, 2010, less than two months after diagnosis. A memorial event is planned for 10/10/10 at his home in Los Angeles. For more details visit http://cancerfreecurt.ning.com.

Garrett Ewald

Taking a professional break to deal with the serious cancer I found out about this July. (Overcoming this will be proof of my "extraordinary result" abilities). Those of you who know me personally will soon be invited to a website where there is more info (it's August 2010). Feel free to ask questions here too. I like communicating about it, but don't want to assume you do...

My broad industry knowledge, international leadership experience and powerful networks add value to any team.

From culinary apprentice at The Greenbrier to Senior VP of Starwood Hotels & Resorts, I've worked in smaller luxury properties, upscale convention giants and senior corporate roles for Hyatt, Hilton, Regent and Westin. Eleven years in Asia Pacific included sales & marketing leadership for 70 hotels and 6 Starwood brands across 15 countries.

I have trained & coached hotel professionals on sales fundamentals, revenue management, customer service and team leadership. Working with an innovative online DMC start-up connected me with the meetings & events community and constantly renewed my understanding of the customer perspective.

Social media networking creates new possibilities every day - both for myself and any team of which I am a member. I enable extraordinary results.

Curt Ewald’s Specialties:

Authentic Listening, Transformational Leadership, Effective Coaching, Dynamic Workshop Facilitation, Making Social Media Productive for Front-Line Sales Professionals

  • Senior VP

  • Senior VP

    Starwood Hotels & Resorts

    (Public Company; HOT; Hospitality industry)

    August 1996 — July 2000 (4 years )

    Multi-unit senior corporate leadership experience at its best...

    Our portfolio grew from 15 to 70 hotels (6 brands) and included a very successful corporate merger and cultural transformation. Working directly with Ted Teng and the myriad of hotel owners, GM's, DOSM's and others from so many different cultures was the greatest learning experience of my professional life.

    There are just too many interesting stories to tell them all here...
