Dan Ryan’s Summary

I provide Retained Executive Search, Leadership Consulting, and Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO) services. I have held roles in the areas of Quality, Operations and Human Resources.

My work currently focuses on Leadership in the Engineering, Architecture, Construction, Manufacturing, Healthcare, and Life Science industries. Many of my current clients are working in the Healthcare, Manufacturing, Federal Design and Construction Markets, especially with USACE, GSA, VA, DOD and DOE. My Manufacturing experience is wide ranging and includes the domestic appliance market as well as the international automotive supplier world.

I work with organizations and associations such as ACEC, SAME, NSPE, SHRM and ASAE in addition to both public and private sector organizations.

I am just as comfortable talking one-on-one or in speaking to a group of hundreds on topics that range from Workforce Planning, Succession Planning, Talent Identification, Technology in Sourcing and Recruiting and Self-Awareness.

I have led Leadership experiences with both domestic and international clients.

You can reach me at Dan@Ryansc.net for more information.

I can also be found on Facebook and on Twitter (@RyanSearch or @That_DanRyan).
