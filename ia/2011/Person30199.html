Michael Moon’s Summary

Tens of thousands of executives worldwide recognize Michael Moon, CEO, GISTICS, dynamic lecturer, prolific writer of white papers, and masterclass innovation leader.

He and his team advises brand-driven corporations on customer engagement, marketing operations, and digital asset management. Clients include Amway, Boeing, Disney, Frank Russell, Gap, Hallmark, Hasbro, Leo Burnett, Mattel, Nokia, SanomaWSOY, TeleSonera, Thomson Corp, VF Corp, and Warner Bros as well as enhance customer engagement sytems and processes of major high-tech businesses such as Adobe, Alterian, Apple, EMC-Documentum, Ericsson HP, IBM, Infinium, OpenText, Oracle, SAP, and Wamnet.

He has delivered more than 400 keynote presentations, seminars, workshops, and Webinars around the world. Chief Executive Magazine featured him in the “Technology Gurus” article in January 1999. Amazon reviewers gave his book, Firebrands: Building Brand Loyalty in the Internet Age, four-and-one-half stars; published 13 languages, including Chinese (simplified and complex), Korean, and all major European languages.

Michael Moon’s Specialties:

GISTICS constitutes an innovation think tank for customer engagement and content monentization. We provide clients with catalytic insights and bold solutions: breakthrough strategies that harness customer evangelism in the social networks of early stage-markets.

Currently, GISTICS specializes in assisting legacy MRM customers in building an internal business-case for switching to next-generation integrated marketing platforms as well as speeding the transition with a minimum of disruption.
