Dan Wallace’s Summary

I work with leaders to increase sales and profitability by conceiving and implementing marketing strategies. My process is collaborative. The focus is on results.

People call on me to position brands and develop integrated online/offline marketing strategies. To implement I frequently recruit, organize, and lead a team that is drawn from an established network of talented specialists — or I can work with your team under your direction.

I work by the project, by the hour, or on retainer. Some clients (e.g., Honeywell, Star Tribune, Martin Williams) have hired me to work on their site to provide strategic support and creative output.

Representative Clients: Apple; BioMedix; BuildAnApp.com; Churchill Financial; City of Minneapolis; Design Ahead; e-fortunecookie.com; Fredrikson & Byron; Experience Engineering; Hamline University; IBM; Leonard, Street & Deinard; Murray's; Palomino; PDI Ninth House; Regis; Research International; Snap Fitness; Summit Brewing; Target; Tuesday Morning; Twill by Scott Dayton: The Hill Library; The Prouty Project; United Way; Wealth Enhancement Group; What Really Works; Wilson Learning; Wize.com; Yoplait.

To find out if I am a good fit for your challenge, text or call my cell at 612-701-9005. Or send an e-mail to dan@ideafood.com. I will happily point you in the right direction if your needs are outside of my capabilities.

Dan Wallace’s Experience

  • Owner

    Idea Food, Inc.

    (Marketing and Advertising industry)

    June 1986 — Present (24 years 8 months)

    My company started as "Dan Wallace, Copywriter" and I wrote everything from direct response TV ads to brochures to fortune cookie messages. During these years I worked on more than 500 brands and collaborated with dozens of talented designers, art directors, illustrators, photographers and videographers.

    In 1994 Messerli & Kramer asked me to represent them as an ad agency, and "Dan Wallace, Copywriter" became the virtual agency "Dan Wallace, et al." Other agency clients included The Bureau and Palomino Euro Bistro.

    I started Idea Food in 2001 to provide marketing strategy and marketing communications for companies, agencies, design firms and consultants. Idea Food is also the brand name for two product lines in the marketplace — including SKUs at retail and intellectual property licenses to Fortune 500 companies.
