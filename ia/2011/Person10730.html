David DiStefano's Summary

I am a seasoned executive with nearly three decades of successful senior management experience with both early-stage and global organizations. I have spent the last 18 years in the sales training and performance industry, leading Richardson to its place as a premier global sales performance organization. In 2006 I became President and CEO of the Company and have since led our talented team to achieve significant year over year growth – being named as an “Inc. 5000 Fastest Growing Company” for 3 consecutive years. I frequently speak at industry conferences and serve as a strategic advisor to our clients. Prior to joining Richardson, I spent 10 years with PwC.

Specialties

strategic vision setting, sales performance improvement, sales training, sales coaching, innovation, negotiation, and business acumen.

In 2009, David led the launch of the RichardsonConnect™ webinar series with his whitepaper and webinar titled "Return on Engagement”.

David works closely Richardson clients in the capacity of executive sponsor, where he brings a strategic vision and guidance, sales performance expertise and 18 years of best practice insights. In 2010 David has presented to senior executive teams and asked to participate on a client's senior level Task Force for Sales Leadership Development

David has been the driving force in Richardson's expansion of its executive team and its sales performance solution. In 1999, he led the Richardson's efforts in raising substantial equity to build an eLearning curriculum, Richardson QuickSkills™ and expand the Company’s global footprint. In 2006, David led the successful sale of Richardson to ClearLight Partners (www.clearlightpartners.com) and became President and CEO immediately thereafter.
