Anupam Saraph's Summary

Anupam Saraph works with today's most imaginative, entrepreneurial leaders to mentor organizations in leadership, innovation and strategy in order to accomplish missions and achieve business goals. Together they pioneer breakthrough practices to address complex problems, design innovative strategies and create enduring value.

Dr Saraph works to align the purpose, people and processes to alter the pace and outreach in diverse industries including energy, engineering, environment, IT, governance, consulting, education, NGO’s and governments in diverse geographies including the Americas, Europe and Asia. He is a frequent speaker at national and international forums on the future of various sectors and strategies to address them.

Dr Saraph has served on the board of Public and Private Sector companies and NGO’s. He has also held ministerial level positions, where he was responsible for facilitating the shaping of policies and programs relating to governance and IT. He has also been a member various international and national professional and business associations for many years.

Specialties

* Mentoring innovation, leadership and strategy.
* Strong market-futures research, communication and positioning.
* Designing processes, services and products for multi-stakeholder environments.
* Designing and deploying technologies and processes for diverse audiences and learning needs.
* Designing collaborative, real-time and mission critical information systems.
* Environment, resource management, economy and development policy, simulation and modeling.
* e-governance and education domains.
