Raj Rajagopalan's Summary

Raj Rajagopalan is a Senior IT Architect in the Enterprise Architecture & Technology space. He has over 25 years experience in the IT industry with the last fifteen focused on Enterprise Systems with emphasis on integrating systems and the application of software engineering methodologies to solve real world problems.

His interests are in understanding how people can ubiquitously connect to each other securely through SOA, Web 2.0 and Mobile technologies. He has worked for clients in a variety of industries including Public Sector, Financial Services, Internet Startups, Aerospace, Utilities and Banking.

Specialties

SOA, Governance, Security, Technical Strategy, Mobile, Modernizing Legacy Systems, ITIL Certified
