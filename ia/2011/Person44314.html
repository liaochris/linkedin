Troy McCasland's Summary

I'm an experienced software executive, entrepreneur, and real-estate investor. I succeed by focusing on creating value for customers, keeping their best interest at heart. My love of technology, selling and helping people is what drives me and makes me good at marketing and business development.

I'm passionate about all things related to web hosting, online marketing, direct marketing and channel marketing. I have a knack for identifying market needs, seeing opportunities and filling those needs with new products and services.

I have built and sold several companies, done consulting, been a board member of SaaS (software as a service) companies, and worked on the executive management team at a number of Corporations.

My goal is to make the world a better place.

Specialties

Sales and Sales Management, Marketing, Business Development, Channel Marketing, Direct Marketing including Telemarketing and Direct Mail, Product Marketing, Product Management, Contract Negotiation, PR, Expertise in all types of online marketing: Search Engine Optimization, Landing Page Optimization, Paid Search Marketing, E-mail Marketing.

Troy McCasland's Experience

VP Business Development

Attracta.com

Privately Held; Internet industry

September 2009 – Present (1 year 7 months)

I have been at the company since it's inception working on the executive management team, building the business. I created our channel sales programs: web content, contracts, business model, and signed up our first sales partners Hostway, BlueHost, Hostgater SEO, Inc., and others. I manage a team of channel sales reps training and mentoring them for success.

I am also active in business development, marketing, and product management helping to define the product road map based on market needs. I established and manage our relationship with cPanel and spearheaded our effort to build a cPanel plug-in utilizing an new category in cPanel called Attracta SEO Tools.

One of the cool things I have done is create a unique way of licensing our technology, using an opt out bundle strategy where by Hosting companies deploy our SEO technology across their entire network enabling every singe customer to get more traffic to their site though higher search rankings without even clicking a mouse. This licensing model helps everyone in the value chain (Host, Website Owner and Attracta) to get more customers.

Founder and Board of Director

Rental Avenue

Internet industry

January 2004 – Present (7 years 3 months)

Rental Avenue provides an online booking, property management and marketing system for vacation rental owners and manager. About 2,000 companies use our technology to automate their business, achieve higher occupancy and get more bookings.

Partner

Vacation Rentals On Maui

Partnership; Hospitality industry

January 2004 – Present (7 years 3 months)

My wife Susan and I own 8 rental properties on Maui in Kihei and Lahaina. Susan runs the company, managing our properties and does bookings for other vacation home owners. Vacation Rentals On Maui offers luxury villas, vacation homes and affordable condos for rent daily, weekly or monthly. At one point this company was a licensed real-estate brokerage firm employing real-estate brokers and agents, selling homes and managing vacation rental properties for off island owners. With the decline in real-estate values, economy and tourism, the company has downsized to just managing the properties we own and providing reservation services for other owners. The company utilizes the online reservation system developed by Rental Avenue, Inc., one of our other ventures.
