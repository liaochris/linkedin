Kostas Polyzos's Summary

Mr. Polyzos is an SAP Project Manager. He originates from the MM area, with more than 10 years of experience in the field of Materials Management. Throughout these years, he has been involved in the implementation of 18 full lifecycle projects . He has gained experience and global knowledge of the logistics processes, order fulfillment business cycle (material requirement planning, purchasing, inventory management, logistic invoice verification) and in Greek Localization (Material Valuation – Warehouse books - Analytical Ledger). His experience has gained in several industry fields like: industry of refinery / petroleum products, industry of medical consumer goods, industry of telecommunications systems, production of envelopes and common typographical materials, optical media industry, media channel etc. Moreover, Mr. Polyzos has development skills. He is very creative and he uses his experience in convincing the client of the proposed solution.
Since July 2008 Mr. Polyzos pursues a carrier path as Free lancer. In collaboration with the SingularLogic SA he is performing his duty as Project manager and MM principal solution consultant. Now is taking part in a project located in Greece in client Olympic Air SA (SAP ECC6) while he is also responsible in MM module for a rollout project of Chipita Russia.
Since September 2010 he also collaborates with the Accenture SA in a international project located mainly in Greece in client Diageo Greece
