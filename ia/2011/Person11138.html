Dayananda Raju, PMP's Summary

Dayananda Raju Chief Manager, Global Procurement Shared Services at Sutherland Global Services has over 18 years of experience in Practicing and Consulting in the areas of eProcurement, Strategic Sourcing, Procurement, Contract Management, Spend Analysis, Project Management and SCM. His broad set of experiences from Industry through his stints in Kirloskar, GEA, Satyam, Tata Consultancy and AIG has given him deep understanding of the interrelationships between business processes and technology.
