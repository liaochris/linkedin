Gary Peeples's Summary

Personally, I’m a runner, triathlete and a musician, but not at the same time. The audience logistics would never work. I have done 5 marathons, a 140.6 mile Ironman, and I run nearly every day; it never gets any easier.

I’ve fronted 3 bands as guitarist & singer, all pretty big [in small geographies]. I once started a DJ entertainment business and ended up not sleeping for 2 years; I was awake for like, 17,520 straight hours.

I show the world how creative I am by wearing tee shirts with witty sayings like: “I listen to bands that don’t even exist yet” and I love sarcasm & movie quotes. Also I'm known for coming up with 37.5 new ideas every day.

I’m very passionate about working hard and having a good time doing it, but my favorite thing to do is breathe; without that I wouldn't be able to blow up balloons.

Professionally, I have been in sales and marketing (including 2 global brands) for over 12 years, with much of it focused on balancing online, offline, and social marketing.

I have launched fully integrated online/offline programs, sports and entertainment property activations, web sites, traditional & internet advertising, SEM strategies, online promotions, tested emerging media, and have experience with media planning and buying for TV, web, radio, out-of-home, print, cinema, and PR.

What I value most is my friends, many of whom are marketing and digital experts. I stay connected through all social channels; particularly blogging and twitter.

In my spare time I am an entrepreneur, having launched my own consulting business in 2007. I've helped small businesses optimize their business plan and assist them in leveraging social media.

In general, I like to think I have proven success in many different facets of the business world. With my most recent roles -- running an aquatic center & developing business for an athlete in the sports / fitness / health industry -- there is almost not a element of today's business world I'm not touching on.

Specialties

General business management [P&L], people leadership, licensing, brand / product strategy & development, internet marketing, digital / interactive advertising, channel marketing, new media strategy, creative strategy (TV, digital, radio, print, out-of-home, point of sale), consumer promotion development, A.C. Nielsen scan panel and general consumer research tactics.
