Ana Lydia Monaco's Summary

In 2006, I launched padma media & marketing to service the communications, marketing and social-media needs of consumer-products, entertainment, fashion and beauty companies. Padma has worked with a broad range of clients, including:

Macy*s
American Liver Foundation
Long Beach Transit Authority
UNAM (Universidad Autonoma de Mexico-Los Angeles)
Bienvenidos Children's Charities
Corona Extra
Time Warner Cable
NDM Bollywood Dance Productions/Nakul Dev Mahajan
La Alameda (a Primestor property)
Esther Ting, Chinese doctor, acupuncturist and author, "Total Health the Chinese Way"
Jose Gutierrez Jr., writer/director "The Last Mermaid"
Mervyn's
Latin Sphere PR/Social Media partner

Previously, I served as Account Supervisor at RLPR + Marketing/Sportivo. In this post, I managed the national media campaigns for Telemundo and Sears. I also led the regional Verizon Wireless account and provided strategic counsel to Nike, the National Honey Board, The California Milk Processors Board.

Prior to joining RLPR, I managed the Public Relations Department at Anita Santiago Advertising (ASA), spearheading the successful launch of IKEA North America’s Hispanic initiative, managing the regional spokesperson campaign for Foster Farms, the west-coasts largest chicken supplier, and overseeing the Firms external public relations efforts.

I joined Anita Santiago Advertising after managing the national Hispanic consumer and fashion public relations campaigns for Mervyn’s, a (then) Target-owned retailer at Valencia, Pérez & Echeveste Public Relations (VPE PR). While at VPE PR, I also managed and worked on varied consumer, fashion and entertainment clients including AT&T, McDonald’s Lo McXimo de la Musica, Washington Mutual Bank, La Opinion, General Mills Trix Circo Mundial, Jafra Cosmetics International and City of Hope.

Specialties

Branding and identity, product launches, staff development, ethnic media outreach, South Asian publicity, Hispanic publicity, Latino publicity, special events, social media, new media, Urban marketing, brand launches
