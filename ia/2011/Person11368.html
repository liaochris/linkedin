Declan Barnett's Summary

Declan Barnett is Founder & CEO of Leisuretime Media, a Leading Social Media Consulting & Marketing Firm. Individuals partner with Leisuretime Media to solve one or more of these problems:

- To identify real ROI driven Social Media solutions for professionals & business owners

- To find a "Done for You" Social Media service to create an additional substantial reproductive residual income

- To bring together the best of e-commerce and social media to exploit opportunities in the global market place without having to become an internet marketing guru.


If you had a chance to be part of Amazon when it first opened for business and created a few threads of business which grew through "word of mouth" like a Twitter... what would that be worth to you today?

To gain an unfair advantage in business today one must embrace the latest technology and concepts and follow those who know how to use it. Then watch them, learn from them and then follow their every move until you’ve perfected it for yourself. And then you’ll have a market share in an industry that is set to change the way we do things online forever.

What Declan will show you about online shopping will revolutionise the way you brand yourself globally and have targeted leads seeking you out as the only logical person to do business with.

Once mastered, this will allow you to separate yourself from the pack and distinguish you as the expert in your field. Despite being easy to do there are tricks and techniques that Declan has learned the hard way, through trial and error, that will allow anyone to fast track their results.
