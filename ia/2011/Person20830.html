Jason Hittleman's Summary

Jason has spent nearly his entire career in the downstream petroleum industry. In 1998, he joined RKA Petroleum Companies where he spent nearly thirteen years as the Director of Information Systems. Jason worked diligently to improve the company's business processes through logical applications of technology. During Jason's tenure, RKA enjoyed the highest growth surge in its history. Revenue increased by over 500%, market reach expanded from two states to 28, and gallons increased by over 400%. Jason helped take RKA from a local distributor to a national player in the downstream sector.

Now, as MARS Advertising's Vice President of Information Systems, Jason is focused on the diverse technology and business process needs of the agency internationally while working to support the growth and build out of its customer-facing systems.

Jason is currently involved in the Midwest Technology Leaders CIO Mentorship Program called Elevate, is a member of the Automation Alley Innovative Tech Committee, and serves on the YAD Board of Directors of Jewish Federation of Metropolitan Detroit.
