Bob LaCour's Summary

My life experience has bought me to this point at this time for one purpose, to serve. Having worked in the hardware / home center business since I was 13, learning everything about every product to repair anything around the home. I held every position in the retail stores and then moved on to the wholesale side of the business where I learned from manufactures of home improvement products all aspects of the industry. I started as a sales rep with small territory, later moved into management where I eventually led a large sales force who serviced hardware stores and home centers. My knowledge of homes and their problem areas grew and was later used to start a home repair business of my own where I put to practical use 30+ years in the home center business. Now I am in the business of helping people sell their home or find their new home, where I am privileged to use all those years of knowledge to serve as a consultant to wonderful people as they make one of the largest decisions of their lives. At your service, .......Bob knows houses !

Specialties

I specialize in representing sellers to get maximum value. With my past experience, I can look at most any home and give expert advice on repairs needed or general condition of the home. For buyers my knowledge of homes and just how serious that repair might be, proves invaluable, as they say, "Bob Knows Houses".

ePRO

Louisiana REALTORS

SRS, Seller Representative Specialist

Regardless of how long you have been in the business, seller agency remains the primary focus for agents across the country. However, since the advent of the Buyer Agency and the emphasis on training how to be an effective buyer representative, it has become apparent we can no longer assume seller agents have the same level of effectiveness. This course is designed to address the challenges Seller Agents face on a day to day basis with a particular focus on the practical side rather than theoretical, covering the basics of agency responsibilities and learn cutting edge approaches to enhance how the selling public is represented.

ABR, Accredited Buyer Representative

The overall goals of the Accredited Buyer Representative (ABR) Designation course are to educate and prepare buyer's reps to provide the kind of service and fidelity to buyers that sellers have always enjoyed, and to offer methods for building your buyer representation business. In each Course Module, you examine a different topic, and together they create a comprehensive guide to help you become an effective, efficient and profitable buyer's representative.
