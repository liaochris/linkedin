February 2010 – Present (1 year 2 months)

Generation Next Summit was birthed from an inspired idea that came to summit Founder and Host Rev. Dewi Maile Lim in Meditation one day early in 2010. Nearly nine months later GNXT launched featuring interviews with 10 Visionary Leader-Entrepreneurs under 40 committed to positive change and awakening consciousness.

These conversations partnered with mini-masterminds served to encourage Summit participants to Be their Brilliance and explore the question: How may I be of greater service to what’s emerging during this crucial time on our planet?

More than 250 people joined the 2010 Virtual Summit with participants hailing from every continent and 10 countries represented. Our tribe continues to grow as more like-hearted folks discover GNXT and are called to align with our ‘ohana (family).

Art Unites Us

Arts and Crafts industry

April 2003 – June 2006 (3 years 3 months)

[Umbrella organization under Fort Greene S.N.A.P. 501(c)3]

Co-Founded a non-profit multi-disciplinary arts and culture organization serving children in the Fort Greene, Brooklyn neighborhood with free art sessions in Fort Greene Park and other NYC green spaces.

Taught visual art, music, creative movement, hula and Hawaiiana in collaboration with Co-Founder and fellow Hawaii-native Kaina Quenga.

Created and implemented innovative summertime programming for children focusing on the expression of Universal Values - ALOHA (Love), LOKAHI (Unity), KAULIKE (Equality) and MALAMA 'AINA (Caring for the earth) through a variety of art forms.

Successfully applied for and received funding from Independence Bank, City Parks Foundation and Brooklyn Arts Council.

Shared the ALOHA Spirit with children via fairs, festivals, school assemblies, guest artist spots and art camps.
