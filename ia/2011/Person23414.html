Karen Frey’s Summary

My 20+ years of experience in executive search and corporate recruiting has convinced me that having the right people in the right positions can move mountains. I work with leaders who are looking to find the best talent for their organizations. I’ve witnessed the power the right individual can bring to an organization creating focus, engaging and aligning employees and driving performance to the next level. Our typical searches are within all disciplines of human resources and all areas of expertise of corporate attorneys. Every client deserves and is delivered exceptional talent with an operations mentality and superior interpersonal skills.
