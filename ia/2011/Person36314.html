Molly Whalen's Summary

My LOVE for education, partnered with my interests in negotiating Real Estate Transactions in Southwest Florida area, including Fort Myers, Naples, Cape Coral, Sanibel, Captiva, Bonita Springs, Estero, Golden Gate, Lehigh Acres, Punta Gorda, Port Charlotte, and Marco Island Real Estate Market, led me to form my own Real Estate Team “The Real Estate Leaders” in Southwest Florida.

My goal is to provide First Time Home Buyers, Second Home Buyers, and Vacation and Investment Buyers with the knowledge to make an informed decision in the Fort Myers, Naples, Cape Coral, Sanibel, Captiva, Bonita Springs, Estero, Golden Gate, Lehigh Acres, Punta Gorda, Port Charlotte, and Marco Island, Real Estate Market; whether you consider buying:

* For Sale by Owner
* Foreclosure
* Bank Owned Property
* Condos in Florida for Sale

I invite you to join me and “Search like a REALTOR” and “Follow The Real Estate Leader at Sun Realty” to find the home of your dreams! Call 888-655-1325 for more information.
