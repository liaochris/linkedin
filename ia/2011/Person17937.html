Igor Vasiliev's Summary

Goal: To elaborate potential ways of co-operating in Switzerland by booking clients and managing their accounts in the most traditional way in Switzerland.
Clearly central to the independent asset manager is the customer and his/her individual requirements. This is absolutely guaranteed, because the commercial success of the independent asset manager is direct linked to the success he/she brings to the client. An independent can manage the client's capital according to his/her own convictions without having to consider the business interests of third parties, such as a bank.
The independent asset manager values a long-term personal relationship and takes time for tailored consultation. The expertise and wealth of experience of the independent asset manager enable him/her to ideally match the investment strategy to the client's requirements. The client's satisfaction is always the asset management's prime concern.

Specialties

As independent asset manager we look after all wealth management matters for ours clients. That means that we go beyond banking-type asset management and offer additional services. Such activities can include: all types of investment business, tax advising, consolidation of capital, development of real-estate portfolios, successor and inheritance planning, setting of inheritance issues, legal advising, financial advising, cash management, commercial deals and much more.
