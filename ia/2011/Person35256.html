Positive Signs

Translation and Localization industry

2003 – Present (8 years)

Formed in 2003, Positive Signs Ltd has 25 years experience in the Sign Language Training and Interpreting line of work, and provides 1:1 teaching or booking services for BSL Interpreters at any time to clients throughout the local area. The company is situated in East London.

Positive Signs Ltd can provide individuals and businesses with a professional Training and Sign Language Interpreting booking service and has a wide database. The experienced staff ensure customers are dealt with promptly and professionally. The service is intended to be cost effective every time.

With a large team of very experienced employees, Positive Signs Ltd has a professional team, qualified or working towards qualifications to a high standard. The company is managed by Sally Paull who has been involved in the BSL Training and Interpreting Services business for many years. Sally Paull is well trained to oversee the company, having previously worked for RNID and a broad range of other companies.

Positive Signs Ltd provides a comprehensive range of BSL ( British Sign Language ) Training and Interpreting Services, including a choice of programmes to suit your requirements. Tailored to specific requests, the services are appropriate for professionals and anyone else, and can be offered at times, days to suit. The range of services are available from the website.

Positive Signs Ltd has an expanding customer base, located throughout London and the Home Counties, including several large Deaf Organisations and a number of private customers.
