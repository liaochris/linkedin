Józefa Fawcett’s Summary

I have what can be described as a portfolio career which means that sometimes I am never sure which 'hat' I am wearing in certain situations :-) That said, my areas of work are complimentary and interdependent of each other:

As a Learning Specialist at LearningVoice Ltd, I offer instructional design and delivery of training, interim management (mainly in field of HRD), voice-over for website enhancement and online tutorials and technical Advice on European Projects.

As Managing Director at OFQT Ltd, a small Learning Improvement Company, I provide a range of products and services to improve the WHY, WHAT and WAY people and businesses learn using quality improvement, evaluation, sensemaking, cognitive mapping and professional supervision.

And as a Senior Lecturer at the University of Westminster I am module leader and tutor on 'Designing & Delivering Training' - 'Learning & Development' - 'Knowledge Management' and 'Health & Care Management'

From over 34 years of working in the private retail and the employment agency sector, public healthcare sector, voluntary charity sector and a range of other industries and organisations across UK and Europe, I bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to every learning, training or quality project.
