Rich Greenberg’s Summary

Mr. Greenberg's specialty is working with Sole Proprietor, and small Corporate, LLC and Nonprofit entrepreneurs in the earliest stages of startup. He is the Founder and CEO of SeedPort. He places special emphasis on openness, clarity and integrity.

Literally hundreds of startup enterprises have been organized, formed and registered in California with Rich's assistance, between 2000 and 2009, most being Nonprofit organizations and single-owner Corporations and LLCs. These companies have varied from IT-based consultants to animal rescue organizations, philanthropic foundations, cycling teams, post-production cinema studios and many more.

From the very beginnings, through re-organization, mergers, to dissolution, Rich is able to assist and advise.

Rich Greenberg’s Specialties:

- Registration and permits to establish a Sole Proprietor business
- Earliest stage of business for Corporations, Nonprofits and LLC's in California
- Registration in CA of an out-of-state corp/LLC
- Conversions of LLC to Corp or vice versa
- Mergers of Corp/LLC into other Corp/LLC or from/to out-of-state companies
- Registration of TradeMarks, ServiceMarks on State level
- Filing of DBA/Fictitious Business Trade names
- Closure of entities, including filing with City/County, State, Federal
