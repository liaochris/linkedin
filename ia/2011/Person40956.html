Spencer Maus's Summary

SpencerConnect facilitates, manages and participates in communications "Talent Teams" of senior-level professionals in media and public relations, investor relations; marketing, branding, SEO, website design, graphic design, video creation to provide the best team for the best results within our clients' budgets.

The sole goal is to "Increase Revenues of Corporations and Small Businesses by Raising Executive Profiles and Building Corporate Awareness."

Spencer Maus has over 16 years of public, investor and media relations experience representing public and private corporations, and entrepreneurs/start-ups. He has been actively providing social media guidance since 1996, and became a member of LinkedIn in the 4th quarter of 2003.

Spencer has gained hundreds of media placements including the Wall Street Journal, New York Times, CNN, AP, the Times of London, Chicago Tribune, Houston Chronicle, CNBC, CNN-fn, Crain’s Chicago Business, the Financial Times, Success Magazine, Business Week, AP, Reuters, Boston Globe, Boston Tribune, MarketWatch, Minneapolis Star-Tribune, Milwaukee Journal Sentinel, local Chicago radio and TV network affiliates and a variety of national trade publications.
