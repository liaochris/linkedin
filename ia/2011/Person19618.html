Jonathan Bradley Augustine’s Summary

Gamespeed Skills (GSS) LLC is a skill development company based out Tallahassee, Florida. The mission of Gamespeed is to put fundamentally sound athletes back into sports. We set the standard for skills training with a superior training program, a staff of experienced coaches, and a commitment to developing the total athlete. Our athletes demonstrate not only the highest levels of skill and ability, but also stand out in character, leadership, and personal responsibility. We provide custom training experiences that allow young athletes the opportunity to reach their full potential.We also aspire to create a community where all who are involved from players, parents, and coaches can see their dreams realized.
