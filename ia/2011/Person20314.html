Jenny Garrett's Summary

www.reflexion-uk.co.uk
http://virtualdialogue.blogspot.com/


Jenny specialises in executive coaching, facilitation and research in the area of leadership and change.
 
Over the last 10 years, Jenny has coached and mentored formally and informally in various roles at the centre of organisational change. Jenny has held senior marketing roles in organisations such as Schroder Leasing Ltd, Hamptons
International and Ashridge Business School, gaining considerable insight into organisational life from her experience of working in complex organisations, where lines of communication and accountability are ambiguous and pressure for results is paramount. In addition Jenny facilitates practical, context specific, and illuminating workshops on coaching skills, influencing and leadership skills, she also facilitates bespoke development programmes for women.

Jenny holds Level A and Level B qualifications with the British Psychological Society which qualifies her to administer and provide feedback on a number of psychometric instruments/personality questionnaires, including MBTI,
16 PF, Implicitly, WAVE and the Leadership Judgement Indicator. She also works with Insights, OPQ, Firo B and a range 360° feedback tools.

Jenny completed a Masters in Management Learning and Leadership at Lancaster University in 2009.

“My style is incisive, pragmatic, and supportive... My aim is to enable you to penetrate to the heart of the matter in order to move forward in a focused and expeditious way, that you will hopefully enjoy!” Jenny Garrett
