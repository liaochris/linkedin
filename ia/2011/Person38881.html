Sarah Lam's Summary

In 2008, I tried freelance writing for a few months and it wasn't rewarding at all. In fact, I started to feel guilty for leaving my full time job in a reputable office.

However, I have always wanted to have a career in writing and so I have decided to keep going until one day in 2009, I finally got the idea to start my own business.

I have been writing for almost 2 years now, professionally. I do wish to expand my business into other areas in the future.
