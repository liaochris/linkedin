Richard Sedley’s Summary

20+ years experience in the creative/customer experience field, the last 16 in online/digital. In recent years I've focused on psychology and persuasion, in the context of creating employee and customer engagement.

As Commercial Director at Foviance I over see the company's Client Relationship, New Business and Marketing teams. Our clients include: Barclays, National Lottery, Dell, Sky, Nokia, BBC, Aviva, Mulberry, William Hill, Dunhill and many more.
http://bit.ly/client-list

Foviance is one of the UK's leading customer experience consultancies. We combine customer behavioural research (analytics, surveying, user testing inc. eye-tracking, accessibility testing, ethnography etc.) with user experience planning to advise our clients how to develop business strategies that optimise the effectiveness of their customer interactions.

Prior to taking my position at Foviance I spent 11 years at the digital agency cScape where I set up the cScape Customer Engagement Unit (CEU) in 2006 and developed a team of specialist consultants that delivered on all aspects of online marketing and communications. The CEU's Annual Online Customer Engagement Survey and accompanying report is now in its 5th year. http://bit.ly/Engage2010

In 2008 I established the Customer Engagement Network
http://customer-engagement-network.com

March 2008 also saw the publication of 'Winners and Losers in a Troubled Economy: how to engage customers online to gain competitive advantage' which looks at the importance of customer engagement during harder economic conditions.
http://www.winners-and-losers-in-a-troubled-economy.com

Previous clients at cScape included: Sony Europe, Aviva, Barclays, National Police Improvement Agency, Chartered Institute of Personal and Development, British Council, Toshiba, AkzoNobel, NHS Employers, ...
