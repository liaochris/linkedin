Sam Lawson's Summary

Sam Lawson is an Australian based recruitment executive with a background in board, executive & staff recruitment.

Sam has a strong interest in recruitment methodologies, emerging technologies in the industry and recruitment industry developments in general.

Sam is Managing Director of two recruitment businesses, ‘Regent Recruitment’ and ‘Wallage Executive’ each with offices in Sydney, Melbourne & Canberra. He is a practicing Migration Agent & Real Estate Agent enabling him to provide advice to organisations looking to relocate executives to Australia

Specialties

Board Appointments, Strategic Planning, Recruitment Technologies.
