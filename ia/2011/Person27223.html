Lylan Masterman’s Summary

I have been in Product since 2001. I enjoy running Product - both Management and Marketing - at promising startups.

I build effective teams and I create environments where people work on the right things, at the right times, for the right audiences. At the same time, I lead by example and I roll up my sleeves to get things done.

I'm sector-agnostic: I've worked in Enterprise Products, Advertising, Developer Tools, Academic Tools, and Semi-Conductor.

I was once in Venture Capital and my VC network is relatively strong. I'm a Kauffman Finalist, class 15.
