anina .net's Summary

Anina is an international model with a passion for technology. She has been featured in L'OFFICIEL, ELLE, AMICA, MARIE CLAIRE, IO DONNA, HARPER'S BAZAR, and worked with world renowned fashion photographers such as Javier Valhonrath, Fabrizio Ferri, Toni Meneguzzo, and Walter Chin. She has participated in runway shows in Paris, London, New York, and Milan: Salvatore Ferragamo, GFF, DRES VAN NOTEN, SONIA RYKIEL and more.

She is the one of the first MOBILE artists, producing multimedia content with cutting edge cellular technology. Anina has completed 6 art exhibitions in Paris, France, two of which were titled, A NEW KIND OF SUPERMODEL and EVERY HUMAN IS A WORK OF ART.

Anina is the founder of the 360Fashion Network (http://360fashion.net). 360Fashion has been in motion since it's launch in Paris, France OCT. 3rd, 2005. 360Fashion was created to give a global visibility to local fashion economies and provide networking opportunities among high level fashion professionals. Anina is the first woman ever interviewed by Robert Cringly on NERDTV, and featured in PBS TV documentary Transformational Age. She has been written about in WIRED magazine and spoken at LES BLOGS, LEWEB3, LIFT, DIGITAL LIFESTYLE DAY, HFF UNIVERSITY, and CHINA COMMUNICATIONS UNIVERSITY in Beijing, China.

Anina wishes to use her image and voice to encourage all women to embrace technology so they will have the possibility to compete in the new digital markets that are emerging globally. Please review the following:
