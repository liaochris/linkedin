Merrily Orsini, MSSW’s Summary

Internet marketing since 1998, corecubed designs websites with search engines in mind. Specialists in website design that integrates the traditional with the interactive, a special niche is home care, hospice and private duty. Home Care Agency Marketplace has innovative products to start and grow home care businesses at MarketHomeCare.com. Special educational consultative marketing program at www.MOST4YourMarketing.com.

Founder, Merrily Orsini, MSSW, is an award-winning entrepreneur with a background that combines skills in technology and people. She was a pioneer in the geriatric care managed home care business model starting in 1981, and now is again ahead of the curve with her internet marketing firm providing website design, marketing services, graphic design and PR.

Read about Merrily's speaking engagements and her home care blog at www.merrilyorsini.com.
