Jim Hennington’s Summary

I am a fully qualified Actuary specialising in the retirement industry and its multi-trillion pound problems.

My background and training was within the top-tier actuarial consulting sector (Watson Wyatt, now TowersWatson). I then moved on to freelance actuarial work and then in 2002 I co-founded a boutique retirement consulting firm helping wealthy expatriates manage their global retirement assets when moving country. I successfully sold my half of this business in 2009.

I am now looking at ways to leverage this skill set further in order to help a much wider market. My concern is that the UK is 'sleepwalking into the next crisis' whereby, as employers and the state withdraw retirement funding, middle England is not yet aware of the terrifying gap this leaves them. And hence they are not reacting accordingly.

I also have a specific interest is in the design, pricing and investment of products that provide sustainable lifetime incomes for baby boomers (eg. annuities, structured products and draw down product options).
