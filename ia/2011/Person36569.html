Clients include tv, online and radio.

Founder Splash Agency: Renske van Kollenburg has a wealth of experience working behind the scenes in Dutch television. She has worked at Endemol International, in advertisement and PR. Her active involvement in the electronic music scene never diminished, however, therefore she decided to make steps to develop her career in this scene.

Today, Renske is a renowned music journalist. She has consistently delivered interviews with many key-players of the music scene, from electronic artists, to pop and hip-hop artists.

Her interview skills go beyond the music or profession a person has; Renske has a gentle approach when behind the mic or in front of the camera, always very much intrigued by every individual and the lives of the artists she interviews. Her disarming manner allows her to get more out of the person she interviews.
She calls it "emphasizing the purity and positivity in people and music to contribute in bringing consciousness."

Renske is currently producing and presenting her own formats and documentaries and works internationally as a freelance journalist and host for other companies.
Furthermore she hosts a live radio show for tech house label 90watts on XT3 techno radio and Ibiza Global Radio!
