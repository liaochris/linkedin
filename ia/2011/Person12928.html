Sandra Frank, Ed.D., RD, LDN’s Summary

Registered Dietitian and Licensed Nutritionist with experience in journalism, education, web design, recipe modifications and analysis, menu planning and grant writing.

Goals.
1. Provide current news, events and resources in Nutrition, Food, Health, Food Recalls, Safety, Disability Rights and Environmental Issues.
2. Encourage awareness and inspire ideas for Journalists, Educators, Consumers and Health Professionals.
3. Provide employment opportunities to idividuals with special needs, such as Cerebral Palsy, Autism, Down syndrome, Muscular Dystrophy and other disabilities.

Dietitians-Online Goals:
Dietitians-Online was created to acknowledge the contributions of the Dietitian (RD and APD) on the Internet.
1. Provide and network with the media, consumer, health professional and educator reliable on-line food and nutrition articles and materials created by the online dietitians.
2. Dietitians-Online submits articles with national or worldwide appeal to major networks and government health agencies in the USA and worldwide. (All submissions are subject to approval. You will receive full acknowledgement with your submission.)
3. Create dialogue with dietitians all over the world.
4. Construct an on-line resource of Dietitians' Blogs, News and Services.
5. To assist the Dietitian in planning and creating an on-line presence. Web and Blog Design services are available.

Sandra Frank, Ed.D., RD, LDN’s Specialties:

Web and Blog Designs; Consumerism and Education in the following areas: nutrition, food, health, weight control, food recalls, food safety, food savings, disability rights and environmental issues.
