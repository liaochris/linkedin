Jean Rognetta's Summary

A financial journalist with a strong technology background (or the reverse :-), with an experience in managing teams of journalists and launching innovative editorial projects.
Founder and president of PME Finance, an independent, non-profit think tank bringing together leading entrepreneurs, VCs and politicians in France.
Also very active on the French VC/tech conference circuit, academic and professional.

Specialties

Venture capital lato sensu, ranging from innovation finance to IPO via expansion capital.
Innovation and technology with a non-exclusive Internet/IT/telecoms bias and a deep interest in biotech, nanotech, and just about anything new.
