Dennis Stauffer's Experience

Dennis Stauffer founded Insight Fusion in 2003 to help companies overcome the barriers to innovation; offering speaking, training, coaching and consulting services including, keynotes, workshops, retreats and seminars focusing on improving leadership, culture and employee engagement...leading the behaviors that drive innovation.

He applies a unique and powerful approach aimed at “Releasing the energy of ideas…through innovative thinking and leadership” and outlined in his award-winning book "Thinking Clockwise, A Field Guide for the Innovative Leader."
