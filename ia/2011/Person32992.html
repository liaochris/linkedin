Neil Mody's Summary

Attorney licenced in the State and Federal courts of New York and New Jersey.

Focused practice includes complex insurance coverage litigation and counseling.

Primarily represents domestic and internationally-based insurers in a wide variety of insurance coverage matters, dealing with general liability coverage, excess liability claims, long-tail claims (environmental contamination, asbestos, silica, mold and other toxic torts), construction defect, bad faith/extra-contractual claims, reinsurance matters, product liability, employment claims and catastrophic loss.

Regularly coordinates with federal, state and local environmental agencies, including the USEPA, NYDEC and NJDEP, and private environmental consultants, to assess, investigate and facilitate remediation of environmental contamination at commercial and private sites throughout the United States.
