Michele Perejda's Summary

Michele has been an industry leader for over eleven years. She began her real estate career with Hunneman Coldwell Banker and was a consistent Gold Circle producer in Cohasset, MA.
Her love for skiing and her commitment to her alpine ski-racing son, Matthew, lead to a focus and devotion to resort real estate and the mountain living lifestyle.
Michele lived and worked in North Conway, NH for 8years, where she contributed to the community, volunteering and serving on various boards, while consistently producing outstanding real estate results, as a sales agent for Badger Realty, then as an independent contractor for RE/MAX Presidential.
Most recently Michele was Designated Broker for Sunday River Ski Resort. Her experiences there have honed her unwavering commitment to helping others find their mountain lifestyle! Michele’s real estate philosophy is to provide the best service possible; her energy and enthusiasm for the business is contagious!
Michele lives in Newry, Maine with her son, who attends Gould Academy, and her two golden retrievers.
