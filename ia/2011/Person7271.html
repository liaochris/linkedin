Chris Shaw's Summary

Established for over twelve years, Pink Elephant PR boasts a wide range of national and international clients in various sectors, ranging from local authorities to IT, software and digital media companies, as well as SMEs, high acceleration organisations, professional organisations and local industries.

Familiar markets include the legal & professional sectors, software & IT, digital & creative, financial services, property, education, healthcare, general consultancy, industrial & service sector businesses, automotive, telecoms and the leisure sector.

Pink Elephant PR has represented each key sector in the national, broadcast, trade & technical and sector specific media, as well as the consumer press.

Specialties

Media relations, including local, regional, national and sector specific press, broadcast media, TV, radio and Web.

Drafting articles, case studies, feature contributions, expert opinions, Q&A sessions, press & news releases, brochure, direct mail and website text.

Strategic marketing tools that include event & seminar organisation, interactive seminars & workshops and other consultancy services.

Pink Elephant PR also has wide ranging experience with internal PR matters.
