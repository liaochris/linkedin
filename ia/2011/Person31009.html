Mitchel Groter’s Experience

  • Principal/ Founder

    (Management Consulting industry)

    January 2005 — Present (6 years 1 month)

    Mitchel has over 15 years of experience in all facets of high performance sales, business leadership, strategic assessment and business development consulting with variety of firms from large complex organizations to small businesses. . His work with these organizations resulted in strategies and implementation plans that increased sales revenue, reduced costs and helped remove constraints and obstacles to sustainable improvement and more profitable relationships with their customers.
