Linda Joseph's Summary

I am a stay-at-home mom who has loved teaching her children. As my oldest displayed an aptitude toward website design and graphics I spent several months learning all I could about the Internet in order to direct her education. Other moms were relieved to have a trusted person who could help their children as well as educate them on Internet opportunities.

I found I loved being an entrepreneur and teaching my children to be entrepreneurs. I worked for several years for an Internet entrepreneur and then started my own businesses. I love working with newbies and have a membership site which guides them step by step for setting up their own business. When I found out how much I could help local businesses drive customers to their doors, I started another business which focuses on maximizing their Internet presence.

My daughter now has her own website/graphics business and I outsource all my design work to her. My son is developing his video skills and will soon be doing all my videos.
