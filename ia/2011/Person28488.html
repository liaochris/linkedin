Marlon Montgomery's Summary

I joined Lucasfilm in 2008 as the Project Manager of Technology (Singapore), which is comprised of the Information Systems, Production Engineering, Information Technology, and Resource Management departments.

The team and I work closely in conjunction with our technology divisions based in the US (R&D, Global Pipeline, IS, Production Engineering, and IT) in supporting the technology needs across all of the Lucasfilm production divisions (ILM, LucasArts, and Lucasfilm Animation) and Administrative departments (Finance, HR, Recruiting, Training, Admin, Operations, etc...).

My 18 years of experience in the media and entertainment industry combines extensive management skills with superior industry and technological knowledge.

Prior to joining Lucasfilm, I served as Production Manager at Laika Entertainment (formerly Vinton Studios), before leaving to co-found Mind The Gap, Inc. a consulting and software development firm that helps animation studios apply pipeline and infrastructure technologies to their own specific needs.

Previous experience includes production and technology related management positions at studios such as Vanguard Animation, PDI/Dreamworks, and Fox Animation.

I have international work experience, dual citizenship (EU and USA), and fluency in English and German and am willing to relocate/travel if necessary.

Specialties

A wide range of experience in organizational and process development, collaborative team management, business development, professional speaking, and consulting.

Expertise in full project cycle management across various technical fields (IT, Web Development, Software Development, etc...) using various methodologies (PMP, Scrum, Agile, Waterfall, etc...)
