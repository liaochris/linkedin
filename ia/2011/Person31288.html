Mollie Coyne’s Summary

I have a J.D. and an LL.M. with almost 4 years PQE in International Trade and Customs law, as well as corporate law in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. I am a member of the New York State Bar. I speak English, French and Spanish.

I have a specialized LL.M. degree in International Trade law with practice experience at a trade boutique firm in Manhattan concentrating on customs law. I have participated on two NAFTA dispute panels (White Goods and Softwood Lumber) resolving disputes between the U.S. and Canada.

I am currently practicing corporate law in Saudi Arabia, representing a variety of Saudi companies and foreign entities by drafting shareholders' agreements, joint venture agreements, asset purchase agreements, as well as non-disclosure agreements.

Mollie Coyne’s Specialties:

International Trade law, U.S. Customs law, NAFTA law and dispute resolution, HTSUS categorization, Import and Export compliance for EU and NAFTA countries, assistance in U.S. Customs audits, Country of Origin issues.
