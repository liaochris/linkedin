Daniel Pinkney's Experience

Owner

Bytheway Stores Ltd

Design industry

May 2007 – Present (3 years 11 months)

Purchased a freehold convenience store two years ago in North Yorkshire. In that time developed and incorporated various systems and procedures to enable the store to run smoothly and efficiently under independent management operation. Gone from having one part-time staff upon acquisition to seven at present.

Boosted store turnover from £14k to £21k per week through incorporating additional services and a shop refit. Adopted the Booker 'Premier' Fascia and STILL trying to get the National Lottery service instore which should increase turnover dramatically.

Owner

Boro Takeaways Online Ltd

Public Company; Food & Beverages industry

2009 – January 2011 (2 years)

Borotakeaways.com is an online takeaway ordering service covering Teesside, Darlington and North Yorkshire

The website enables customers to place an order with local registered takeaways for delivery or collection and also to pay by card or Paypal through our secure checkout system (or cash on delivery if so desired). The site also provides up to date news and features on our takeaway and restaurant partners and well as a whole host of additional features.

Our restaurant offering includes a whole host of advertising and marketing services for our registered takeaways from social media and online promotions through to PR and offline promotion (print/press) of the Boro Takeaway Service. So as well as taking our local takeaways and restaurants online we also reduce their advertising, marketing and printing costs.

Owner and Creative Director

Ellahi & Pinkney Ltd

Design industry

2000 – 2006 (6 years)

Founding partner of a creative design agency based on the outskirts of North Yorkshire. Managed and worked alongside the in-house design and production teams as well as installing and maintaining the IT network/backup systems. Also worked alongside MD Tanvir Ellahi in developing new business openings.

The company saw turnover increase to £8.2million in 2004/5 thanks in most part to becoming the National design agency for NTL. Unfortunately E&P lost the account in 2006 due to the Virgin takeover.

Established in 2000 E&P has produced work for many bluechip clients such as Tiscali UK, NTL, Electrolux, Husqvarna, McCulloch, Cleveland College of Art and Design, Business Link, Northumberland College, Odyssey Systems, Middlesbrough College, Ringtons, Flymo and Forest Holidays.

Owner and Creative Director
