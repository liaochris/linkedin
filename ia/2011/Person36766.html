Richard S. Gendler, JD, LLM, JSD (cand)’s Summary

RIchard Gendler is the managing shareholder of a ten lawyer firm with offices in Key West, Miami Gardens, Plantation and Boca Raton, Florida.

During law school, and thereafter, Mr. Gendler worked at the Miami-Dade County Public Defenders office advocating for indigent individuals charged with a myriad of crimes. Additionally, during this period Richard Gendler worked as a Guardian Ad Litem representing abused and neglected children. Subsequently, Mr. Gendler worked as an associate at Robbins, Tunkey, Ross, Amsel, Raben & Waxman, P.A. representing criminal defendants in State and Federal cases. Thereafter, Richard Gendler worked as an associate attorney for Marcia T. Dunn & Associates, P.A. where his practice expanded to consumer, corporate, and trustee bankruptcy matters.

In 1998 Mr. Gendler established his present practice and has since expanded his firm to a multidisciplinary office employing lawyers with extensive knowledge of not only criminal and bankruptcy matters, but real estate, tax, estate planning, probate, and civil matters as well.

Over the years Richard Gendler has appeared on all of the local major television networks, including ABC Channel 10, NBC Channel 6, CBS Channel 4, Fox Channel 7, Telemundo Channel 51 and Telemiami Channel 22. His commentaries have also been cited in the Miami Herald, EL Nuevo Herald, Fort Lauderdale Sun Sentinel, New Times, and the St. Augustine Record.

Richard hosts three weekly radio shows that discuss legal topical issues in South Florida on WSUA Caracol 1260 AM (Fridays 11:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m), on WFTL 850 AM (Saturdays 10:00 to 11:00 AM), and on WLVJ 1040 AM (Mondays through Friday from 9:00 to 9:30 a.m).

Richard is a member of the Florida Bar, the United States District Court for the Southern District of Florida, the United States Bankruptcy Court for the Southern District of Florida, the Eleventh Circuit Court of Appeals, and the United States Tax Court.
