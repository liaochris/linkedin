Corey Rollins's Summary

I have a wide range of experience in media backed by a solid foundation of business and marketing. My goal is to successfully combine these skills with my passion for graphic design, film and audio to improve and innovate the Canadian gaming sector.

Having worked in the gaming industry for several years I've had the privilege to work in a variety of positions ranging from QA to Producer, and most recently Owner and Managing Editor of ShogunGamer.com.
