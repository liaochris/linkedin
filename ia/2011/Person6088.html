Brett Arp’s Summary

Brett Arp is a numbers guy who understands sales inside and out. He spent a decade in a direct sales environment where his ability to understand the total business put him in charge of $275 million in sales compensation. Understanding how every variable within the company impacts the others allowed him to ask the right questions within the organization to achieve the desired goals. Brett's true passion is international business, which inspired his time abroad as well as his international MBA from Thunderbird. He is a practical visionary who can run the numbers with the best of them.
