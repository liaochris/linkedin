Brian Kirk’s Summary

LC KIRK is a diversified search and placement firm focused on sales and marketing executives within the financial services industry. We offer you and your firm a complete recruiting strategy through competitive intel, industry relationships, in-depth research, strategic planning and partnership. Think of us not as a service provider, but a results provider - results that lead to better candidates and ultimately a superior individual that improves your bottom line.

With over 25 years of experience, LC KIRK is a true leader in the industry. While many recruiters focus solely on your short term needs, we offer strategic solutions that not only provide top personnel for your organization, but also plan for long term needs and goals.
