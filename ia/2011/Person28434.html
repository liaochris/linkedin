October 2009 – Present (1 year 5 months)

Mark Vanneste is an associate with the firm. He focuses his practice on the defense of hospitals and health care providers in malpractice litigation.

Mr. Vanneste earned a B.S. in Human Biology and a B.A. in Economics at Michigan State University before earning his J.D. from the University of Detroit Mercy School of Law. As a law student, he was senior editor of the Law Review, a member of the Moot Court Board of Advocates and won the 2008 Gallagher Professional Responsibility Moot Court Competition.

Mr. Vanneste is licensed to practice law in Michigan.
