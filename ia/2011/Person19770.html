Jean-Michel Pailhon's Summary

As the Execution and Admission Manager for the NYSE Euronext Listing Department in Paris, I deal with the whole admission and execution processes relating to new listings of companies (through an IPO, pursuant to a Private Placement, or through a Direct/Dual/Cross Listing) that take place on the three markets of NYSE Euronext: NYSE Euronext (the regulated market), NYSE Alternext (the exchange-regulated market) and the Marché Libre (the organized yet unregulated market).

The IPO process is a highly regulated and technically complex process with many issues to be addressed (legal, financial, regulatory, trading, investor relations). I advise and assist companies on all phases of the admission process with among others:

(i) Provide companies senior management (CEO, CFO), listing intermediaries (law firms, bankers) and majority shareholders (PE firms, National State Agencies) with advisory services on the listing, prospectus drafting, capital raising processes and investors relations management;

(ii) Lead project management with a variety of highly specialised service providers involved (lawyers, banks, regulators and clearing and settlement organisations);

(iii) Instruct the filing, performing of the strategic and financial analysis of the new listing candidate, and dealing with the admission to listing decision (including the request for the admission approval toward the Euronext Paris' Board of Directors);

(iv) Finalize the listing execution process through the design of the corporate event official notice, involvement in the organization of the IPO ceremony and the Press communication.

Prior to this position I held various positions within the NYSE Euronext Group: mainly in the Finance department (treasury and financing, corporate finance), and then within the Paris Listing department as Head of Research & Analysis. In the latter I have notably gained a thorough understanding in various innovating industrial sectors such as Biotech and Cleantech.

Specialties
