Cecellia Telkes, AICI FLC's Summary

Cecellia Telkes has lived and worked in Australia, Singapore, Malaysia and Philippines for over 17 years, contributing to her extensive experience in cross cultural diversity and international etiquette. Her 13 years of sales and marketing experience in Australia includes work with telecommunications giant Vodafone Australia, and in managing corporate marketing with SingTel Optus.

Among her prior overseas customers are some of Australia's top multi-national companies and high profile individuals including Commonwealth Bank, American Express, ANZ Bank, Ansett, Qantas and the Australian Rugby Union and its players

Cecellia obtained her professional image consulting qualifications through ImageAsia, an AICI-approved certification jointly delivered by top industry masters, Lynne Marks, AICI CIM of the London Image Institute and Christina Ong, AICI
CIM, Imageworks Asia. She also trained under Peter Post from the world renowned Emily Post Institute and is licensed as an EPI Business Etiquette trainer. Being experienced in media presentation and trained as a professional makeup artist, adds
to her well-rounded service offering to her clients

Engaging and lively, Cecellia has trained front-line officers in telemarketing, sales, customer service, presentation, professional image and presence, business and social etiquette. Her previous experiences also include facilitating and hosting various international functions for multi-national companies, foreign dignitaries and high profile celebrities.
Some organizations that she has worked with and existing
clients include the Nanyang Business School of NTU, OCBC, Singapore Police Force, HSBC, Civil Service College, Citibank and Temasek Holdings. She is regularly consulted by the media in Singapore on image and etiquette matters.
