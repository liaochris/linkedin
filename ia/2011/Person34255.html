Paul Oppenheim's Summary

Mr. Oppenheim is the President and General Manager for Cobham Composite Products Operation (formerly SPARTA Composite Products which was acquired by Cobham in June 2008) in San Diego, CA and with a second operation opened in May 2010 in Suffolk, VA. He joined SPARTA in April 2007. He has a degree in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering from Arizona State University from 1984, and since that time has been in the high technology, composite structures industry.

Prior to joining SPARTA, Mr. Oppenheim was the Executive Vice President, V System Composites (Anaheim, CA) and Corporate Vice President, DR Technologies (San Diego, CA); Director, Marketing and Sales, AAR Composites (Clearwater, Florida); Business Development Manager, STW Composites, Inc. (Montrose, Colorado); and Manager of Composite Structures Programs at ATK Composites (Magna, Utah).

Mr. Oppenheim has worked primarily in the DoD structures arena for his entire career, in program management or senior management positions for both advanced development and high-volume production programs. Primary industries included military aircraft structures, missile and munitions components, missile defense programs and space structural applications of composite products.
