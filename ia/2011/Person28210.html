Mark E. Randall’s Summary

My life's calling is to be a minister and I'm an entrepreneur at heart.

Having not grown up in the church, I accepted Christ as my Savior and Lord on March 11th, 1987 at Daytona Beach, FL during spring break of my 1st year at The University of Virginia after some Duke students with Campus Crusade For Christ "randomly" met me on the beach and explained what Christianity is all about.

From that point on, my life has never been the same. I've been married to the most awesome lady in the world, Kristin, since 1993. We have two sons.

In Rochester, we want to reach every student on every campus every year with the Good News of Jesus Christ. We want to build spiritual movements everywhere so everyone knows someone that truly follows Jesus Christ. We want to develop lifelong leaders for Christ.
