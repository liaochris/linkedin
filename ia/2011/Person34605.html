Peter Cobley’s Summary

I have been working in advertising since 1994 and moved into new media in 1998. I have worked for a number of major media owners including Reed Business Information and Carlton Television.

My background is advertising sales and business development. I have specialised in digital advertising, e.g. display, search engine, and mobile.

I am knowledgeable in all forms of new media advertising, whether driven by CPM, CPC, or CPA cost models. I am also versed in technology that revolves around digital media.

I am legally qualified, holding a law degree and professional qualifications.

I enjoy working in advertising, and specifically new media. I recently worked in search engine and display advertising for nearly six years for Yahoo!, and latterly joined home james, part of home group in Leeds as Commercial Director.
