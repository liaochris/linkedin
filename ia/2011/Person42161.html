Susan Luke’s Summary

Help lead O&B's Cultivate group, which tells the stories of major ag organizations to multiple audiences in multiple ways, mixing traditional and online experience with deep history in diverse news reporting. Expert-level writer, with passion for identifying potential stories to share, and innovative ways to share them. Also extensive experience in journalism and writing/production leadership, including print (Associated Press, Dow Jones correspondent), television news (writer and associate producer at CNN Headline News, local stations in Boston, Chicago), and other outlets.
