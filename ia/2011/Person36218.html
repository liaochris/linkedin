Ray Ruga’s Summary

Ray Ruga is the Founding Partner of the CVOX Group, a PR, Communications & Marketing firm specializing in the Latin America region. He has over 18 years of diverse domestic and international corporate, government and political experience advising C-Suite leaders at the highest levels, including two Fortune 10 companies, The White House, The Pentagon, a U.S. development agency and five presidential campaigns.

Ray Ruga’s Specialties:

Business Strategy and Development, Government Relations, Corporate Communications, Political Communications
