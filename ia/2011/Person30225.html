Michael Pilip's Summary

Casmul, my company, provides a white-label social gaming network solutions. With Casmul MultiNet platform you can quickly extend all your games on iOS, Android, Flash and HTML5 with all the social and multiplayer features and add vCurrency and vItems support.

Your users will be able to join your games with their social network accounts, bring their profile information and friends to your network and spread a word about your great games.

You will be able to open your network to 3rd party developers giving them easy to use self-serve SDKs to add their games to your new network. Casmul provides you with a flexible and fully customizable social gaming platform. We do all the heavy lifting and let you concentrate on driving your business.
