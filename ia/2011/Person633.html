Adele Ephron's Summary

I am a recruiter to recruiter who prides myself on finding the best fit for both candidates and clients- I don't massage square pegs into round holes. Although I am always proactive in sourcing talent from the inactive market for my clients, I definitely welcome approaches from experienced recruitment professionals seeking market guidance.

We all know that the success and dynamism of any business is dependant on it's people

I aim to be the recruiter who finds those great people and dynamic companies for my candidates.

I specialise in placing recruiters across Asia Pacific in all verticals and all levels of experience .
