Deirdre Skolfield, CFA's Summary

Deirdre has 15 years of Investor Relations experience focused on high-level financial analysis and communications for global NYSE and NASDAQ listed technology and business services companies. Her ability to combine cutting edge financial experience with strong communication skills provides an optimum interface between companies and Wall Street.

With the focus on maximizing shareholder value, Deirdre interprets the drivers of value and investor perception for the executive team and the board, in order to optimize executive communications with the analyst and investment community.

Specialties

Extensive experience in global institutional targeting, capital-markets transactions including large scale secondary offerings and mergers/acquisitions, relative valuation analysis, corporate communications and governance. Innovative approaches to the use of new media to effectively target investors.
