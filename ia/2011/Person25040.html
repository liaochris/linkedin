Kristen White's Summary

Kristen White is a native Phoenician who has an immense passion for the Valley of the Sun and its real estate market. For over a decade, she has applied this passion to providing exemplary client care to home sellers and buyers. Kristen is committed to maintaining four fundamental standards: integrity, market knowledge, work ethic, and understanding client needs.

Kristen listens to her clients and maintains an open line of communication. Her direct but personable approach builds long-lasting relationships. Mark and Amy Malatesta, who chose Kristen as their Realtor in a recent sale and purchase stated, "Kristen´s marketing and real estate knowledge were fantastic. She was diligent, patient, and responsive to our needs. All of her real estate skills aside, the bottom line is that we can truly trust Kristen to look out for our best interest."

Attention to detail is one of the strong suits that allows Kristen to provide seamless transactions to her clients. "I understand that a home is one of the largest and most personal financial investments a person makes. I am dedicated to providing the highest level of personal attention and industry expertise so my clients feel confident when they buy or sell a property," she stated.

As the youngest of 10 children and aunt to 19 nieces and nephews, Kristen has developed a vivacious personality. That, coupled with her dedication to the industry she loves, enables her to achieve the best results for her clients.
