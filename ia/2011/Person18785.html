James Forrest's Summary

We are a fixed-rate alternative to Private Equity, Venture Capital and commercial bank financing for start-up energy or job creation projects.

We lend term debt to fast-growth businesses needing more than $10 million, with no upside limit, if they have Moody's or S&P Investment Grade corporate or governmental sponsors committed to buying from them at a fair price.

We offer a true 3, 5 or 7-year "bullet" term loan structures, with no interim interest payments, to facilitate project construction or initial ramp-up periods. We also offer mezzanine and senior secured commercial term loans at 9.95% fixed rate for M&A transactions from institutional sources.

We are cash flow focused and rarely bother with collateral assignments. Our financing can be delivered in less than 30 days and is dramatically less expensive than conventional mezzanine and hedge-funded finance companies. Our lack of amortization, personal guarantees, plus our flexible "payment skips" for both amortized and bullet loan structures are features most banks would not care to match. We can get accept extremely irregular cash flows in order to facilitate the success of a financing, while providing the option of being subordinated to a conventional lender at any time.

We are paid by our institutional investors to provide Term Debt for projects with investment grade irrevocable guarantors. Also, we will pay all of the lender's closing costs when the transaction funds, so there are no "points" or surprise annual fees for you to learn about at the closing table.

We also finance:

1. Municipalities or government bodies with economic development projects;

2. Project financing with investment grade sponsors (energy creation, etc.);

3. Structured settlements of lawsuits (we provide present value now);

4. Charities receiving multi-year funding commitments, but need cash for immediate needs.

Specialties

• Project Financing for Energy Projects with customers
• Financing Lawsuit settlements for contingency attorneys
• Debt and Bridge Loans for Management Buy Outs (MBO)
• Not for profits needing financing to grow their physical space and equipment at tax-exempt rates;
• Commercial real estate Industrial Revenue Bonds at low tax-exempt rates.
