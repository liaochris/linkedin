Philip Martin, FIOR's Summary

In addition to my role as Founder at Enterprise Risk Advisors I am also the immediate past Chairman of the Institute of Operational Risk.

Enterprise Risk Advisors is an independent consultancy working with a variety of different companies across the financial services sector. We provide operational risk management and enterprise risk management solutions working within Basel II, Basel III and Solvency II frameworks.

The Institute of Operational Risk is an organisation that has as its primary aim the promotion of skills and standards associated with Operational Risk Management.

I was elected as a Fellow of the Institute in November 2005 and in January 2009 I was elected as one of the Global Top 50 Faces in Operational Risk by Incisive Media's OpRisk & Compliance magazine.

Specialties

I am an experienced risk management expert and an experienced business manager. I am regarded as a subject matter expert in providing solutions for operational risk management and enterprise wide risk management. I have broad experience working with different companies within the financial services sector where I have been providing solutions to my clients for over 30 years.
