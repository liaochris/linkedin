Lorenzo Toscano's Summary

Software engineer with over 10 years experience in reputed IT companies.

Experienced in IP Contact Centre, Video and Voice Over IP, Unified Communications and Collaboration, innovative software developments.

Extensive competence in the full life cycle of the software design process including requirement, definition, prototyping, proof of concept, design, interface implementation, testing and maintenance. Strong in design and integration problem solving skills.

Management skills, demonstrated proficiency in leading and mentoring individuals to maximize level of productivity, while forming cohesive team environments.

Analytical thinker that consistently resolves ongoing issues or defects, often called upon to consult on problems that have eluded resolution by others.

From 1998, starting with an experimental search engine development project, Lorenzo has consolidated knowledges and significative experience in Information Retrieval, Search Engine Optimization (SEO), pay-per-click advertising, Social Media Marketing, Web Site Design.
