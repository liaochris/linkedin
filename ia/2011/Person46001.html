Christopher Winton-Stahle’s Summary

I am the owner of Winton-Stahle Photography, L.L.C. which is a husband & wife photographic production team located in Richmond, Virginia that specializes in producing and executing still photography for advertising agencies, design firms, and magazines both regionally and nationally. My work has ranged from Editorial features for National music, entertainment, business, and trade magazines to working with advertising agencies to produce photography for a variety of ad campaigns, point-of-purchase, and product packaging.

I am an award-winning photographer with my Cultural lifestyle and portrait work and was recognized by the Virginia Museum of Fine Art in 2006 after receiving the Professional Fellowship Grant for excellence with my personal photographic documentary, " Folk of the Blue Ridge". I also launched my first major one-man exhibition with the Virginia Museum of Fine Art in October of 2007 with "China Now" which is a traveling statewide exhibition that examines the cultural shift and technological progressions of China in the last few years. You can view more about this exhibition at:

http://www.vmfa.museum/china_now.html
