Gaurang Desai's Summary

Gaurang is one of those rare leaders who is an innovator yet an effective manager due to his people skills and organizing-cum-execution capabilities. He has successfully worked with organizations of different sizes and scale. His adaptive nature and high nervous energy has made him a success with startups as well as established organizations.

In working with organizations like National Stock Exchange, Reliance group, NCDEX India and DGCX/DCCC in the last 15+ years, Gaurang has succeeded largely due to his ‘Ethics, integrity and hard work have no alternative’ motto.

Gaurang is a person who provides the vital spark. He is imaginative, original and produces lots of ideas. He is independent, unorthodox and forthright. Due to this he was instrumental in the success of the projects and organizations he worked for.
