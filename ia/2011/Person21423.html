Joe Izzo’s Summary

Currently a senior consultant in Sirius Solutions energy consulting group, Joe specializes in the design and implementation of trading and risk management capabilities for energy supply and trading clients. Joe has years of experience implementing and managing several ETRM platforms at various oil\gas clients within the crude and refined products commodity sectors. With vast role experience with multiple software vendors, clients, and project demographics Joe is a valuable and versatile asset to any client. Joe has been commended for his ability to get in, be proactive, and start delivering quality work quickly.The following demonstrates Joe’s strong points:

•Experience – Joe has functional and technical experience across all modules of a Trading and Risk Management system, specializing in the front office, deal capture, and contracts generation modules. Additionally, experience in many aspects of the trading & risk management business.

•Versatility – Having experience in everything from writing SQL reports to management of teams Joe is a versatile asset and is willing to use his experience to help and offer advice, improvement to even the smallest tasks or processes.

•Quality – Joe understands how powerful a person’s name and reputation is in any business. Every single action of a consultant has to be guided with quality, reputation, and doing what’s best for the client.

•Creativity – Joe never shies away from questioning current processes, and offering ideas and advice on how these processes can be improved.

•Communicator – Both in written and oral, Joe understands that communication has to be razor sharp to avoid assumptions, mistakes, and to sell important messages and bright ideas.

•Team Player – From a 1 person team to a 500+ team with several contracting companies with differing agendas Joe has been able to look past the bureaucracy and politics to get things done and at the end of the day deliver what’s best for the client.
