Bilal Jaffery’s Summary

All about Web, digital interactive, & social 'experience' media marketing. 14+ years of strong experience in competitive intelligence, brand identity, communication strategies, sales, business development, commerce, design, SEO, SEM, interface design & communities. Led teams working on multi-million dollar campaigns.

Organizations of the future require an open, social, agile collaborative structure. You don't need followers, you need innovators.

Recent: Keynote at the Toronto CIO Forum on value of social software - "The Social Organization".

Panel Speaker at PLUS Conference on Risk Management and Social Media.

Leading the digital media efforts for IBM Software Group.

- Led IBM's latest Lotus Knows campaign.
- Launched IBM's first C2B online jam, IdeaJam.
- Recognized as IBM Expert on Social Software: http://bit.ly/ibmsocial

- Referenced and quoted in ComputerWorld, LinuxWorld, CIO, InfoWorld, LinuxToday, Windowsrelated.com, & various other IT magazines. ie, http://bit.ly/3dvP1S

- Originated SweetTweets concept, an award winning lead gen campaign. Received Best Use of Social Media Award at Web 2.0 Expo 2009.

I've been involved with Web since the early 90s, when I founded my first startup which offered live streaming, email, community forums and syndicated web services for a targeted niche segment. By 1996, I transformed that into a 46K strong paid portal additionally offering file storage and 'community discussions'. The portal's mail services were offered by my partner firm at that time, Outblaze, now IBM (2009).

My first experimented with online communities in 1987 with my own BBS system (PCBoard, WildCat, FIDONET).

Board member of Jobtec, MarketingMinds, ThinkPanda & DesiVibe.com.

Along with web, my other passion is my co-founded non-for-profit charity project, Our Foundation, with the deeply held and shared belief that we all are capable of giving back to our communities, not only financially, but also through our time and talents.
