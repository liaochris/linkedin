Elizabeth Stollings, J.D., LL.M.'s Summary

I am experienced and educated in areas of intellectual property law and related international law. I enjoy the complexities of intellectual property and often times those complexities overlap into international law. My work experience is extremely diverse. As such that diversity allows me to bring a creative and innovative perspective to any work setting.

At present, I hold a Master of Laws in Global Law and Technology with specializations in Intellectual Property and Information Technology Law as well as International Law and Business. My professional goal is to locate placement (domestic or international) in a challenging legal related IP position on that will permit me to use my educational background and professional experiences to develop solutions and benefit any employer.

Admittedly, I enjoy intellectual property so much that I sometimes consider naming my next two cats 'patent' and 'trademark.'
