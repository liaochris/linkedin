Just as every person has a story, so does every business. As former journalists and award-winning PR pros, we are seeking opportunities to help businesses and non-profits tell their stories.

We expertly pitch stories to the media, but there’s so much more. We help clarify and express an organization’s story so that it defines, brands and creates a public image consistent with a company’s vision.

We buzz your story in every way that make sense. We’ll publicize it with the traditional media, write killer web copy, design an online marketing campaign, create a social media strategy and teach you how to keep it updated – all part of a custom communications plan.

The truth is there are a lot of people out there claiming to be social media gurus and experts on the latest whiz-bang technology. They may know how to use all the latest tools, but do they know how to create and manage the message? Do they have the expertise to handle a crisis? Do they share their knowledge or only flaunt it?

Yes, we can use the tools, too. But that's backed up by more than three decades of experience and success. And we’ll teach you to use social media and online marketing tools so that you can promote your business yourself.

We provide a full range of marketing and public relations services focused on helping your organization reach its potential. I've earned the accredited business communicator designation (ABC) from the International Association of Business Communicators and won more than 350 local, state and national awards for my communications work.

But we don't work for awards; we work for our clients. We’ll help you tell your story to buzz your brand.
