Evan Fowler-Guzzardo’s Summary

Evan is a Human Resource professional at Aetna in the company's HR Rotational Development Program where he is currently working in Executive Compensation. He holds a Master's degree from Cornell University's School of Industrial and Labor Relations.

Last summer, Evan interned at Aetna's corporate headquarters in Hartford, Connecticut as one of only eight 2009 Graduate Summer Associates where he worked to develop the company's international HR operations in a summer position known for its challenging projects and direct access to company executives.

Evan has experience in international HR, training, and business analytics. He has passed the PHR (Professional in Human Resources) exam and will earn the PHR certification after gaining two consecutive years of exempt level full time experience. One day, he would like to become an HR executive for a Fortune 500 corporation.
