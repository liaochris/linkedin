Eric Jacobson’s Summary

At my current company, Ascend Media (Overland Park, KS), we partner with nonprofit organizations, associations and businesses throughout the U.S. to create custom electronic and print media and content products that help our partner clients:

* grow membership
* boost event, conference and/or show attendance
* engage their communities and audiences more effectively and frequently
* drive more revenue-generating fundraising campaigns
* improve their marketing and communications activities

At Ascend Media, we help create the content and can deliver a turnkey project, often with a revenue-share business model for our partner clients.

Ascend Media specializes in creating electronic Newsletters, directories, annual reports, web sites, fundraising campaigns, custom branded magazines, eBlasts, and educational/instructional guides.
