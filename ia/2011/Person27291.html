Aaron Maass’s Summary

Aaron is a senior e-marketing executive with 13 years of web analytics and website operations experience in positions of leadership for Comcast, DuPont, KPMG and EF Education. His areas of expertise include SEO, SEM, lead generation, email marketing, e-commerce, web analytics and building a customer-centric mentality throughout an organization. As managing director of MaassMedia, Aaron helps companies maximize their web channel marketing through intelligent use of enterprise web analytics. Aaron's career also includes starting an ad-serving and web analytics software company, selling ultimately to KKR, and working for Fidelity Investments, Odwalla, International Paper and Kirshenbaum Bond & Partners in New York. Aaron completed his undergraduate studies at Cornell University where he concentrated in History and Philosophy.

Aaron currently resides in Philadelphia, PA.

Aaron Maass’s Specialties:

E-marketing strategy and planning, e-commerce, online/offline integrated campaign management, demand generation, SEO, SEM, lead management, website development, content management, email marketing, web analytics and reporting
