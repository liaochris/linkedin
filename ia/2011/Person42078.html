Surjendu Kuila's Summary

Surjendu is a 10 year international veteran in strategic marketing and sales with expertise in building companies from ground up. He is an avid technology enthusiast and passionate about ecommerce in general and etailing in particular.

After graduating from the prestigious IIT, Roorkee in 2001, Surjendu started working in the USA. While in US, he worked for big names such as Apple Computers and RSA Securities and also built his career in startups such as Ciber and eTouch Systems. His first stint as an entrepreneur did not see the light of the day, however, in 2006 he founded codearmory.com - a code repository portal for the developers around the world - which was a runaway hit.

In 2009, despite receiving admission offers from top US B-Schools, Surjendu returned to India to do his MBA from IIM-Calcutta. After graduating from IIM-Cal, Surjendu worked for Rediff.com as Associate Director - Ecommerce Programs.

After a very brief stint with Rediff, Surjendu moved on to his first love - working for startups. He played a very crucial role in building Luna Ergonomics - a promising new startup in the mobile domain.

Currently he is spearheading his new venture.

Specialties

Strategy, marketing and building companies from scratch. A thought leader in the area of ecommerce, mcommerce, social media, mobile social, search marketing, social networking and SEO.

Software architect and technology evangelist with specialties in web security, web architecture, SOA, SaaS and open source software.
