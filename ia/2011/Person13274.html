Elaine Rubin's Summary

I am an ecommerce pioneer who’s spent the past 18 years building, managing, and advising direct-to-consumer businesses on their ecommerce and digital marketing initiatives. I am immersed in the digital space and have experience in all aspects of ecommerce, including strategy, planning, implementation, and management. Having worked as a manager, executive and consultant inside and outside of many leading brands and retailers, I can help create and formulate the strategy and plan but also understand the details and skill sets required to create, manage and grow successful multi channel businesses.

After operating ekrubin inc, my ecommerce consulting business since 1998, and realizing the demand in the marketplace for qualified, experienced talent was exceeding the supply, I created Digital Prophets Network, a community of experts and thought leaders, who are available for client projects and help educate & grow the knowledge base in ecommerce. While still in our first year of operation, we have 60+ experts in our network and have successfully satisfied every client project request with the right talent at the right time.
