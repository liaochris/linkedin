Jake Mohan's Summary

Freelance writer and editor with substantial experience in creative writing and writing instruction. Focused on teaching writing at the secondary and college level; always interested in diversifying freelance work in all media and subjects.

Completed creative writing MFA with nonfiction manuscript. Taught seven semesters of creative writing at the University of Minnesota; currently in my second year as an adjunct liberal arts faculty member at McNally Smith College of Music.

Specialties

Writing pedagogy; online media, especially blogging; music writing/journalism; alternative media; creative writing and literature
