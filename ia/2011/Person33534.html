Olivier Taupin’s Summary

A Social Media Marketing & Technology executive, founder of the largest LinkedIn Group Worldwide (Linked:HR), Editor of the Software Engineering Productivity Roundtable, Vice-President of Social Media Marketing for Xoriant, in charge of developing new B2B social networking solutions.

Olivier is the #1 Group Owner on Linkedin. His LinkedIn® Groups, managed by the Social Media Marketing & Technology of Xoriant, include:

- Linked:HR (http://tinyURL.com/linkedhr), the largest Human Resources Group on LinkedIn. The Group has also become the largest LinkedIn Group worldwide.
- Star:Jobs (http://tinyurl.com/starjobs) one of the largest career centers on LinkedIn
- Linked:Energy (http://tinyURL.com/linkedenergy), the largest LinkedIn Group for Energy professionals.
- Linked:Seattle (http://tinyURL.com/linkedseattle), the largest LinkedIn Group in the Pacific Northwest.
- Linked:Golfers (http://tinyURL.com/linkedgolfers), the largest Golf Group on LinkedIn.

Olivier is also the Editor of the SEP Roundtable, the eZine of the Software Engineering Productivity think tank. The Software Engineering Productivity Group on LinkedIn includes top technology executives (CTOs, Chief Architects, and VPs) of many Fortune 500 companies, such as IBM, HP, Cisco, Sun Microsystems, EMC, Honeywell, Computer Associates, Northrop Grumman, and more. The level of the Group Discussions is exceptional. The objective of the SEP Roundtable eZine is to identify the global leaders in software engineering productivity and publish their success stories in their particular fields of expertise.

Olivier is dedicated to nurturing the continued development of new and innovative concepts for growing and managing high-quality online communities, such as the Linked:HR and Software Engineering Productivity communities.
