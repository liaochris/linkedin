Lisa Napieralaki’s Summary

Many people are not aware of what they want to do once they graduate with a bachelor’s degree. The million-dollar question of “what do you want to do with the rest of your life” sends many recent graduates into a full-blown panic attack. This is not the case for me. I am comfortable belonging to a small, yet unique, group of creatives. Each project I complete provides me with the affirmation that internet-based design is my chosen career path. I know that being creative is not a personality trait; rather it is who I am. I eat, sleep, and breathe in code. Development is my life.

I wish to develop and design internet media that works for the individual, and not for the recognition received after its creation. I want to create new online movements in design, and change how we interact, see, and use the internet. I am fascinated by what has already been done with online technology, and I am excited to take part in the online revolution. The advances being made every day in the industry inspires me to keep moving forward.
