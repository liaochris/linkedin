Gerald Adler's Summary

Senior Interactive Advertising Specialist for Key and National Accounts at Scripps - KNS Media Group. I work with and coach managers & senior advertising executives to increase company revenue - while best fulfilling the digital media needs of agency, national, and major account clients.

Strategic business development, consultative sales & staff management, relationship development, contract negotiation, and increasing market share are strengths.
