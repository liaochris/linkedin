Bill Boorman's Summary

I work in 8 key areas:

* Recruiter Training - In House, Coaching or Training (You can find my list of current courses at: http://recruitingunblog.wordpress.com/course-outlines/

* Management Development - All industries. Involved in identifying new managers, managers induction, developing and coaching managers from team leader to director

* Performance Management - Design and consultancy in all aspects including process design, automation, review, appraisal and implementation/training.

* Providing consulting services to recruitment firms - Growth, survival, market development, corporate accounts, structure, and recruiting recruiters. Many bespoke projects completed.

* Recruitment Audit and Diagnostic - I use a bespoke 145 point diagnostic tool to complete a full S.W.O.T. analysis of the business and recommend improvement.

* Social Media and Social Recruiting strategy and implementation - Projects completed have included Social Orientation, Strategy, Social Intern Programmes, Recruiter Training and On - Line Mentoring.

* Key-note Speaking - Over the last 12 months I have spoken at many events across the world on recruiting and social-media subjects. Give me a topic you need covering, I will let you know how I would deliver it.

* Organizing, marketing and hosting #tru events around the world - Since inception in November 2009, we have hosted 5 unconferences and continue to grow.
