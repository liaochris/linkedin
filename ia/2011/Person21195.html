Jennifer Miller’s Summary

I have over 20 years experience in the communications industry including radio and television broadcasting and public relations. Jennifer Miller Media provides personal, product and event promotion; media outreach; ALL TYPES OF WRITING including: Ad copy, articles, bios, blogs, brochures, columns, letters to the editor, news releases,newsletters, op/eds, PowerPoint, speeches, and web content. I use innovative message delivery including social media like Facebook and Twitter, as well as blast e-mails. I'm constantly in search of new ways to get your message to the people who need it. Plus, I do media training to help you and your team communicate effectively, build relationships with reporters, use new technologies to spread your message, and learn ways to become known as the expert in your field.

I work with individuals, non-profit organizations and small businesses in the Madison, WI area.
