Dana Bernard’s Summary

My mission in life is to educate others. My current position as President of Boston Reed College allows me to lead a team of talented professionals who will guide and develop others as health professionals. I am a registered nurse by trade and a teacher by passion.
I founded The Learning Oasis to provide women in business, nurses and adult educators with continuing education and professional development in an easy and affordable way.
