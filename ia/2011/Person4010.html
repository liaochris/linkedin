Dave Asprey’s Summary

I'm a technology, strategy, and marketing executive with a track record of picking disruptive technologies that win. Think of a CTO type who knows how customers think and likes to negotiate deals, backed by a Wharton MBA.

My 17 year career has built skills in strategic planning, product management, and corporate & business development, from startups to F500, at places like Trend Micro, Citrix, Blue Coat, Speedera (Akamai), Exodus (Savvis), & Zeus.

My core industries are cloud, virtualization, SaaS, software, networking & hosting, with a dash of "medtech meets cloud."

I'm an accomplished public speaker and evangelist with thousands of hours on stage at conferences, in classrooms, and in press conferences globally. The NY Times, Fortune, Salon.com, & GigaOm published my writing on the cloud.

My keen eye for disruption has me paying attention to 2 spaces: cloud security and cloud-based health monitoring.

►In 1993 I was 1 of the 1st people to sell anything over the internet; covered by about 75 publications

►Yogi, expert nutritionist, meditator, brain hacker

I blog about personal performance for business at www.bulletproofexecutive.com.

I am open to startup board seats or advisory roles focused on strategy, PM, and fundraising.
