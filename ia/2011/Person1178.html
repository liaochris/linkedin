Albertino Sant'Ana, PMP's Summary

Mechanical Engineer, Project Manager certified by PMI (Project Management Institute) as PMP (Project Management Professional), with great skills in industrial and infra structure project management, green field and brown field. I have been worked in the following segments: mining, steel and chemical industries.
Post Graduated as Quality Management and Quality Award’s Volunteer Examiner where have been worked in the awarding cycles of 2005 and 2006 years.

Administrative and financial controls based on earned value analysis, contract management,management and development of work teams, .
Large technical experience in equipment specifications, sizing and calculations of processes and structures.
