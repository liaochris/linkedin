UAE Exchange's Summary

UAE Exchange is the brain-child of visionaries H.E. Abdulla Humaid Ali Al Mazroei, Chairman and Dr. B.R.Shetty, MD and CEO. Established in 1980, UAE Exchange has today grown to become the leading global remittance and foreign exchange brand, spread across five continents with close to 500 direct offices in 22 countries.

Based in Abu Dhabi with Mr.Y Sudhir Kumar Shetty, COO – Global Operations, at the helm, UAE Exchange has been setting new standards in global remittance industry with diversified product and service offerings supported by deploying latest technologies and excellent customer service.

Quality-driven approach and customer-centric perspective have won the global remittance major, various awards including ISO 9001:2008 certification, Dubai Quality Award and Sheikh Khalifa Excellence Award (SKEA). Apart from that UAE Exchange won the
UAE Emiratisation Award from the HRD committee in the Banking & Financial Sector and the DHDA Award under the patronage of H.H. Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum for achievements in the development of UAE Nationals. UAE Exchange also earned the ‘Superbrand’ recognition for 2009, 2010 and 2011, consecutively.

A vital link in the prestigious NMC Group, UAE Exchange has over 6,500 multi-cultural personnel representing more than 40 nationalities, striving to ensure customer delight. So each and every customer, in any part of the globe, gets the same quality of service. These relentless efforts to add more value to customers worldwide for 30 years, have earned UAE Exchange the acclaim of World’s Trusted Money Transferrer.
