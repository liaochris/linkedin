Deal origination, deal screening, marketing and technical due diligence, discounted cash flow valuations, deal structuring, shareholder agreement negotiations and post investment management.

Peter van der Zee's Experience

Senior Account Manager

Industrial Development Corporation of South Africa (Venture Capital)

Government Agency; Financial Services industry

January 2008 – Present (3 years 3 months)

Through the IDC venture capital business unit, I have either led or been part of a team that has invested over R220m into 20+ businesses. Our investments typically range between R5m and R30m and for this investment we acquire a meaningful equity position. Our target companies show strong competitive positioning, excellent management capabilities and have great potential for rapid growth. In this role complex commercial propositions with potential for significant returns must be identified from within a myriad of similar but less attractive opportunities. Thorough marketing, technical, managerial and financial due diligence is undertaken to drive a DCF forecast indicating the magnitude of the likely returns. Once the decision to invest has been made, shareholder agreements are negotiated and agreed upon. Thereafter funds flow, introducing a period of intense investment management where good corporate governance is instilled, a strong board is put in place, value is created and returns are realised. I bring with me strong relationships with state and private funders as well a network of capable entrepreneurs and corporate relationships.
