Linda Press's Summary

Linda Press heads CorpCommunicators, a boutique public relations firm specializing in corporate, financial and marketing communications. She and her team develop innovative communications strategies that utilize advanced social media techniques, savvy media relations and thought leadership platforms. She has established a network of independent consultants and business partners to enable clients to benefit from the breadth and expertise of the network, while enjoying the attentiveness and value of a boutique.

Ms. Press combines the best practices from the management consulting, marketing and IR/PR worlds to deliver smart strategies and real results. Clients include law firms, turnaround managers, private equity firms and their portfolio companies, high-growth, emerging organizations or companies undergoing a transformation across a broad spectrum of industries.

Prior to establishing CorpCommunicators, she led the LA office of Financial Dynamics (FD), Strategic Communications unit of global management consulting firm FTI Consulting. Her background includes spearheadiing corporate communications, investor relations, issues management, merger and acquisitions as well as marketing communications initiatives for numerous companies from young organizations to the Fortune 500. She began her career at Salomon Brothers, and then parlayed her Wall Street experience into the communications consulting and business advisory worlds.

Her experience also includes serving as a vice president of Investor Relations at a technology company undertaking an initial public offering.

Specialties

corporate and financial communications, marketing communications and branding, social media, executive visibility, media relations, mergers and acquisitions, litigation communications, investor relations, issues management, crisis communications
