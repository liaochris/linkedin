Heather Kaye’s Summary

I am a leadership coach and organizational consultant and leadership coach. As CEO of InVision (www.InVisionLLC.com), a leadership consulting firm, I work with socially responsible organizations in developing their leadership. I guide leaders in exploiting what is paramount to them in order that they make their contributions count and experience the satisfaction of success.

My goal is for clients to get the heart of what matters to them.
Over the past 15 years clients repeatedly report they are passionate, engaged and flourishing as a result of our work. I am also the co-creator of www.TheLeadershipSanctuary.org , a yearlong program for nonprofit leaders. The program offers a very safe space to deeply explore how to be a more effective leader, strategically blending personal satisfaction and organization success while offering breathing space for each leader in these demanding times.

InVision has an unwavering commitment to the advancement of leadership ensuring that leaders have the inner strength, presence and vision to take a commanding stand for the causes that make our communities and our world stronger and healthier.
