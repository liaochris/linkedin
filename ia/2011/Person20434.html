Jeremy Pitcock's Experience

Owner

The Pitcock Law Group

Legal Services industry

March 2008 – Present (3 years 1 month)

My current practice involves counseling clients in all areas of intellectual property, with a particular emphasis on patent litigation. Under my current platform, I can offer services on a risk-sharing or greatly discounted basis over traditional legal models.
I have been involved in all aspects of trial and appellate practice before Federal courts, and have successfully argued at various summary judgment and Markman hearings. I have litigated patent matters in several diverse fields, including fiber optic networks and various optical components, network and microprocessor architecture, software used for various Internet applications and database synchronization, Ethernet routing and communications, semiconductor manufacturing and fabrication, videoconferencing equipment and protocols as well as pharmaceutical inventions and business methods. I also have extensive experience in copyright (including computer-related matters), trademark and trade secret litigation.
