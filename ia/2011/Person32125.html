Nathalie Rondot's Summary

Multitasks, social, I love working in Team. The domain of Web projects is always evolving that's why it's interesting, there is always something new to learn and to discover.

After a fist experience as project manager in Australia, 2 years in agencies in Paris and 1 year in a publishing and digital company in Canada, I’m back to Sydney as Freelance digital producer.

My strengh is my technical knowledge. It helps to get easily the trust of the production team because they know that I understand exactly what they are doing.

Specialties

- Gathering the information allowing us to define the needs and the scope
- Creation of the project Sitemap, Wireframes, and specifications
- Creation of the workback schedule
- Planning of the process with the team
- Management of the project execution, from the creation phase to the development phase until the go live step.
- Close follow-up with clients and providers
- Quality Assurance
- Detailed statistics analysis
- Campaign monitoring
- Financial Monitoring
