Ash Banerjee's Summary

I currently serve as Chief Information Officer for WilmerHale based in Washington DC.

Prior to that I was appointed as Global Chief Information Officer for EMI Music based in New York, NY where I was responsible for Technology programs and Operational Management of All Technology Applications and Infrastructure Services worldwide.

Prior to that I served as VP Equant Global Application Systems for Equant, based in Reston, Virginia, reporting to the CIO. In this role, I was responsible for all Global application systems, with staff in the US, Europe and Asia. Post merger with Global One I delivered the merger plan involving conversion to common Global systems worldwide, bringing down costs by over 50%, headcount by over 40% while at the same time improving cost avoidance and enabling new capabilities within the business with increased automation. Equant is now known as Orange Business Services and is part of the France Telecom Group.

I joined Global One in 1996 as Director, Carrier Systems, based in Brussels. I relocated to the US in 1999 as VP Biling Systems and led the pre-merger planning work-streams for Systems while holding the position of Global CIO for Global One.

Earlier in my career, I worked for Executive Search Consultants Heidrick & Struggles International Inc as Director Information, based in Paris and was responsible for all information services and systems in Europe. Prior to this, I worked for Andersen Consulting (Accenture) in London. There, I led several multimillion-dollar projects, the last one covered a 2 year stint in Belgium in December 1994 leading a Pan European Systems Integration and Change project for a food manufacturer.

I bring a combination of international outlook with Global responsibilities - having worked in the US, UK, Belgium, France, and a few months in the Israel - and 25 years of experience in senior management in consultancy and line roles.
