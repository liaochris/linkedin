Debbie Miller's Summary

Debbie Miller is a dynamic and well-respected marketing veteran with over 20 years experience, consistently holding executive posts at several of the entertainment industry’s premiere companies. Her broad-based knowledge of the entertainment field has been instrumental in creating synergistic opportunities at both CBS and Warner Bros., culminating in fully integrated marketing campaigns. Known for being a straight shooter, a strategic thinker and a collaborative leader, Miller has developed and implemented highly creative and innovative campaigns, resulting in the successful launch of hundreds of major motion pictures.

Specialties

Equally adept at Left Brain/Right Brain Thinking; Fully Integrated Marketing Campaigns; Strategic Positioning; Cross-Divisonal Publicity & Promotions, Competitive Marketplace and Data Analysis; Exceptional Communication and Organizational Skills; Trusted, Bottom-Line Focused Executive.

Debbie Miller's Experience

Head of Worldwide Marketing

CBS Films

Entertainment industry

June 2008 – Present (2 years 9 months)

Created the studio's marketing department from the ground up, including the formation of organizational structure, recruitment and hiring of all positions, development of competitive media and research databases, establishment of and adherence to overhead budgets, implementation of all necessary business systems, corporate branding (including creation of the studios logos and websites), and the hiring of all outside support agencies.
Generates comprehensive marketing strategies for all films.

Supervises all International, Home Entertainment, VOD marketing plans with various third party distributors.

Executive Vice President Domestic Theatrical Marketing

Warner Bros. Pictures

Public Company; TWX; Entertainment industry

January 2001 – May 2008 (7 years 5 months)

Primary contact between Domestic Theatrical Marketing Department and all other divisions of studio including (but not limited to) Production, Distribution, International, Global Brand Management, Consumer Products, Corporate Communications, Music and TV.
