Paul Magliari’s Summary

I have been playing and writing music for the majority of my life. I have since been living in a world of drums and guitars - rhythms and melodies now joined by visual art graphic design / art direction and finally writing for stage plays. This is an awesome environment to where everything works off each other; one helps the other to create a well-rounded outlook and insight to solid and deep conceptual results - no matter what the assignment is. This is exactly what I want in my working life as well as to keep challenging myself, aiding me in in new and fresh approaches.

Paul Magliari’s Specialties:

My specialty is to conceptualize. To bring the clients intentions to fruition. Whatever medium I am dealing with I enjoy hooking the audience and keeping their attention through stimulating and layered concepts and designs.
