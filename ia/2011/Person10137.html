Daniel Purchas's Summary

Over the last 18 months, Daniel has been a founding member of the GradConnection team that has successfully designed and developed the 'GradMaker, the graduate jobs matchmaker' which is a unique marketing tool for graduates and the organisations who employ them to come together online.

The site has grown from relative obscurity in March 2009 to currently being the highest traffic ranking Graduate Employment site in Australia and from August 2009 has expanded into the New Zealand market.
