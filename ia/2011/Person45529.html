Wayde Andrews MAusIMM's Summary

Wayde is a highly experienced Geological Recruitment Consultant with over five years experience specialising in the sourcing, selection and end to end recruitment of Senior Executive, Managerial and Technical staff within Mining, EPCM and Oil & Gas industries. Wayde has recently joined the Hudson Team after forging a successful career with such companies as Talent2 and Hays Mining & Resources, where he was able to build a strong industry network of both candidates and clients.

Wayde's specialisation allows him to successfully recruit not only across Australia, but into Africa, Asia and the Indo - Pacific region. This allows him to attract and feed his talent pool with the best, highly qualified candidates, and hence providing his clients with the level of service they require.

Wayde sources his candidates through a variety of different methods, including ad search, referrals, industry related associations and through Hudson's extensive database of active candidates. Being a large multinational recruitment company, Hudson has access to tools and candidates across the globe and is able to draw upon these as required.

Wayde believes in building strong relationships with his clients, and this has seen him become a trusted business partner for both larger and smaller companies both here in Australia and internationally.

Wayde is a current member of AusIMM
