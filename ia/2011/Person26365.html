Linda Salazar's Summary

My desire to join Linkedin is simple:

I’m looking for partnerships with companies and organizations that have charitable arms where they’re working to serve women and children in need – emotionally, spiritually and financially.

I am heart driven to make a difference in the world one person at a time - connecting with individuals in associations, organizations, small businesses, companies and corporations who are interested in building relationships supporting change for the greater good and personal growth.

Change that builds a more powerful work environment. Change that deepens personal relationships. Change that builds bridges where a chasm now exists. Change that supports the drive and passion toward charitable work to help others who are truly in need.

If you are of the mindset for positive change personally and professionally, and this kind of connection resonates with you, then let’s begin our connection right here.

As we learn about each other on Linkedin perhaps we’ll be inspired to support one another through either a business venture, a charitable cause or a combination of both. From there we can take the next step and talk on the phone, get to know each other on a deeper level and exchange our desires and ideas that allow us to prosper financially, emotionally and spiritually.

My coaching business and book, Awaken The Genie Within; A Handbook to Help You Silence Your Gremlin, Manage Your Emotions and Bring Out the Best of Who You Are, was created to support me, and those I joint venture with, in achieving all of the above and more.

In my book I share common experiences, important teambuilding and behavioral skills, that teach: patience, acceptance, tolerance, listening, compassion, gratitude and the other critical responses that build happy, productive and engaged relationships at work and in life. If there were ever a time for being the best we can be in our business and personal life, it’s right now.
