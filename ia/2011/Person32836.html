Founder

Zapak Digital Entertainment Limited

Internet industry

September 2005 – July 2006 (11 months)

Starting up Zapak, was the first assignment that I handled full time at Reliance ADAG. I was one of the first people to come on board soon after Rajesh started setting up Reliance Big Entertainment.

Transitioning from a 'gamer' to setting up a 'gaming business' was hugely challenging, particularly so because there were no benchmarks, scant resources, very few people with experience and a floundering local industry

I contributed by scanning the global market, creating a fairly simple product roadmap and bringing on the best early stage team including - Saishree, Nirav, Neeraj etc. We were quickly able to scout out alliances and partners globally for the casual gaming business that became the initial launchpad for Zapak.

While I considered this to be a good start, what followed was a substantially greater achievement, which I completely credit to Rohit Sharma, Arun Mehra and Sudipto Majumdar.

Today Zapak, is the defacto leader in the gaming space, both as a brand as well as the market leader in terms of revenue, with a diversity of revenue streams well balanced across, retail reach, shared access Gameplex's and the online business. It has all the top brands as advertisers and sponsors for its various properties and now reaches a very significant number of the internet user base in India.

With the increasing reach of broadband, it is poised to grow into one of the best success stories from Indian digital media space.
