Melinda Green's Summary

I'm a senior-level copywriter who enjoys words as much as concepts. I'm a blogger. I'm lots of things. I think in search terms. I understand social media is more than talking about social media.

As a copywriter, you need to own the brand, the product, all of it. Become an expert to the best of your ability. Learn about and engage your audience. Believability and authenticity is what sells the message. And the brand. This is what I live by. Every day. Every time I sit down at my keyboard.

All messaging is visual. Copy and design are not separate entities. Understanding both processes and how they work together is what makes transforms any creative endeavor into something memorable. Collaboration is key to everything.

I also can hold my own in creating SEO-centric content based on search engine analytics and other web trends. The best part? I can do this without it looking like "SEO copy."

Specialties

Advertising & marketing copy--web, print, video, direct mail, email, product & instructional, Content strategy and SEO, Landing page optimization, Keyword analysis, UX, Social media (development of both campaigns and overall strategy) and inbound marketing, Blogging & reporting, Writing copy/content targeting international markets
