Adam Rice's Summary

My experience and record of accomplishment involves hands-on market research, customer support and high level B2B sales presentations. I have a strong foundation in interactive marketing, account management, performance analysis, and business development.

I have worked in marketing and product development for an Internet search company, as well as in business development at a web-based sourcing company in China. My current work, with Koda Media, involves partnering with executives from various industries to create web content and promote products and services through online campaigns.

My travels have taken me to over 40 different countries and have given me lots of experience in a world full of ideas, philosophies, culture, and diversity. I am currently drawing on my experiences to produce interactive web content, branding initiatives and/or targeted marketing campaigns for some of North America's leading organizations.

Internet industry

SeekSource Solutions creates an unprecedented single source to search all available housing listed by various real-estate agencies in China. It provides brokerages with key agent listing and industry statistics to ensure informed decisions and growth.

I envisioned and oversaw development of proprietary software that aggregates house listings from various websites to a single database, creating a new market solution. My research found that local real estate agencies estimate 20-25% of their sales force engage in illegitimate deals. I foresaw an online technology that could track agent listing trends to mitigate unlawful behaviors.

I presented technology to industry leaders and facilitated operational partnerships for development of a strong customer base. My team aggressively marketed SeekSource through some of China's premier newspapers, television stations, websites and search engines.
