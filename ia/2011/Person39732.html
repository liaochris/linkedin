Seth Cohen's Summary

In short, my daily tasks include: booking live performance engagements for my company's artist roster which includes, The Commodores, The Temptations Review, Omarion, Marques Houston, hip-hop violinist duo Nuttin' But Stringz and many more. In order to do this, I must build and maintain a client database of talent buyers for fairs/festivals/casinos/theatres/clubs/etc as well as corporate and special event planners through mass emails, cold calls, and existing relationships. I negotiate performance deals with these talent buyers on behalf of our artists. Once a deal is complete, I issue engagement contracts detailing exact guaranteed fees, the expenses covered including flights and hotels and all other relevant deal points, collect deposit payments, and deal with every conceivable detail involved in the engagement from making sure airline tickets are booked for the travelers to making sure the artist knows how much money they are supposed to be receiving on any given night. In addition to all of this, I assist the CEO of the company in following through on every detail of his deals. Our company is a boutique so I am also involved with the basic daily operations of the company.
