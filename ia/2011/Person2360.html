Andrea Aker's Summary

Aker Ink is a full-service PR firm specializing in strategic media relations, marketing communications, social media, branding, copywriting and online marketing. Areas of expertise include professional services, financial services, non-profits and health/wellness.

I founded Aker Ink in 2007, armed with an extensive background in public relations and multiple facets of journalism. My newsroom experience has provided an invaluable edge when working with reporters, and I leverage this savvy to secure prime placement in targeted media.

Before founding Aker Ink, I served as the PR director for a PR and marketing agency in Phoenix. I led successful campaigns at the local and national levels for B2B clients, garnering sought-after coverage in target markets nationwide. Prior to that, I was a writer/producer for KPHO-TV, Phoenix’s CBS affiliate, where I produced top-rated newscasts for the morning and noon shows. I have eight additional years of experience as a freelance reporter for newspapers and magazines throughout Arizona, including The Arizona Republic and the Arizona Daily Star.

I received my bachelor's in journalism from the University of Arizona and my master's at Arizona State University's Walter Cronkite School of Journalism, with a specialization in online media.

I currently sit on the board of directors for the Phoenix chapter of the Public Relations Society of America, where I chair the technology committee. I'm also a member of the American Association of Grant Professionals and the speaker’s bureau for Planned Parenthood of Arizona. In addition, I'm a past member of the marketing committees for the Arizona chapter of the American Diabetes Association and the Southwest Autism Research & Resource Center.

Specialties

Public relations, journalism, strategic media relations, integrated marketing communications, online/print copywriting, branding and identity, online media and marketing, speech writing, video/online production, scriptwriting, social media, internal/external communications, non-profit grant writing
