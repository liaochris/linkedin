Hunter Waters's Summary

Oversees political and other national coverage for "Larry King Live." Based in CNN's Washington, D.C. bureau and have been with the show since January 2000.

Specialties

Have field produced in Kuwait and Qatar during the 2003 war in Iraq, as well as produced shows with notable guests including former president George H.W. Bush, Rev. Billy Graham, former Vice President Al Gore, and all of the 2008 presidential candidates. In 2003, was the recipient of the American Women in Radio and Television's "Gracie Award" for an hour-long program that featured an interview with former first lady of Texas, Nellie Connally.
