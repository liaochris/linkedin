Scott Matics’s Summary

I manage the product marketing for the MediaFUSE and Ignite lines of automated production and content re-purposing products at Grass Valley's Live Production Solutions, in Nevada City, California. I am responsible for product evolvement, from market research to conception, and management of the product lifecycle to worldwide customers.

My past experience includes various marketing management, sales and development positions, spanning the past twenty-four years. Fourteen of those years I have been working on broadcast automation, robotic camera automation, and with cutting edge technologies for content multi-distribution for Internet and mobile initiatives.

The way content creators produce programming, and the way consumers view content is evolving quickly. My goal is to use my technical understanding of broadcast technologies, combined with my marketing experience in new media, to produce innovative products that make the processes involved in producing content more efficient, and the resultant content better engage and enrich the viewer.

Scott Matics’s Specialties:

Market Analysis, Product Development, Branding, Promotion, Bridging of Marcomm and Engineering Departments to initiate Market-focused product development
