Ana M Catafau's Summary

M.D., Ph.D. by University of Barcelona and European Board certified in Nuclear Medicine, with a high level of expertise in neuroimaging research with SPECT and PET both in academia (University Hospitals Clinic & Sant Pau in Barcelona, 16 yrs.) and industry (GlaxoSmithKline, 8yrs.). Internationally recognized expertise, having been Chair of the EANM Neuroimaging Committee, member of the Journal of Nuclear Medicine Editorial Board, and member of scientific committees of international congresses.
Key attributes: Consistent and successful career with outstanding productivity records, leadership and managerial skills, networking and coordination abilities, teamworking, pro-active, resilience, sense of urgency, passion.
Objective: To provide PET imaging expertise for research as well as scientific management of the studies, in partnership with healthcare industry, bio-tech companies and academia.

Specialties

Experience in the set up, management and scientific direction of a Neuroimaging Unit, management of the Imaging Portfolio for Central Nervous System drug development, clinical imaging studies from Phases I-IV, imaging biomarker validation, and translational approaches.
