Mike Fikuart's Summary

I have always been technically minded and have approached matters in a logical manner, but am passionate about all that I do. With a background in electronic engineering, I have been able to move into many industries and apply the same thought processes and analytical techniques.

Presently, I am working in the in fire and gas detection, mainly for the Oil & Gas industry, and see great opportunities for the development of new technologies, as well as the need for training and education. The latter will form the focus of a training and consulting venture in 2011.

Specialties

Problem solving, electronic engineering, financial strategy, learning/adopting new technology
