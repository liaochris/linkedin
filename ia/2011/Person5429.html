Blair Graham’s Summary

Blair began his commercial real estate career in 2006 in the Raleigh/Durham area as a Commercial Real Estate Attorney with a prominent regional law firm. Blair made the transition to the business side of the industry in late 2008. As a Commercial Real Estate Advisor at Grubb & Ellis|Thomas Linderman Graham, Blair works exclusively on the tenant representation and corporate services side of the business, working with a wide array of clients, ranging from publicly traded companies to start-up companies. Blair utilizes his experience as an attorney to help his clients acquire and dispose of property in a way that aligns with their corporate needs. Blair helps tenants understand their options in the market and can leverage his legal skills and background in finance to negotiate deals that can generate savings to the bottom line.
