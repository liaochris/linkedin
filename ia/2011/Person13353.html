Elisabet de los Pinos’s Experience

  • Founder & CEO

    Aura Biosciences

    (Biotechnology industry)

    January 2009 — Present (2 years 2 months)

    Elisabet (Eli) de los Pinos, PhD, is a biotech entrepreneur founding CEO of Aura Biosciences, a company in Boston with the potential to radically improve cancer treatment. Eli has created Aura Biosciences (www.aurabiosciences.com) from the ground up: from concept, through patents in her name, fundraising and incorporation, to today’s pre-clinical research and strategic partnerships. Currently, cancer therapies (i.e. chemotherapy) are released into the entire body, harming healthy tissue in the process and creating significant adverse side effects that often outweigh the benefit. Under Eli’s leadership, Aura Biosciences has found a way to release drugs directly into tumor cells without harming healthy tissue. Aura’s Nanosmart technology first encapsulates the drugs, then finds tumor cells, permeates them and efficiently releases the drug in the cytoplasm, where it is most critical. This is a giant step toward the improvement and efficacy of cancer treatment and has a direct impact in the survival and quality of life of cancer patients.
