Ingo Bousa's Summary

Ex recording artist, 3 albums released on major record labels between 1994 and 2001. Lived in Berlin for more than a decade. Got a diploma in Graphic Design & Media Marketing, worked in various Berlin creative agencies [Zum Goldenen Hirschen, Plantage] as a copy writer. Moved to the UK in 2005 to follow a dream and to broaden my horizon. Worked for Google as a Quality Evaluator on German rating projects and since Dec 2006 as a Senior Linking Strategist for GUAVA in Cornwall, UK where I pleased lots of old and won lots of new clients. Left Guava March 2010 to go freelance and moved to Bristol.

Specialties

Search engine optimisation, online marketing, linkbuilding, social media, copy writing, strategy & campaign planning, team building, common sense. Fluent German and English, a little bit Dutch, French and Spanish. Lots of country specific linking strategy knowledge in Germany, Spain, Netherlands, France and the UK.
