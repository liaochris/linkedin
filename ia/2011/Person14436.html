Eyal Kattan's Summary

Industry expert with over 14 years of professional software engineering and user experience, analyzing business needs and functional requirements, designing, developing and managing interactive web applications through the full life cycle of the project.

Established Media Nexus Inc in 2007 as part of the transition into digital convergence of computer based systems and the living room.

Media Nexus brings a whole new approach to the digital lifestyle. We call it a "connected life"

Our solutions make homes intelligent.

Simplicity and versatility give you flexible control over lighting, temperature, digital entertainment, security, and more from anywhere in your home—with the touch of a button. Personalize your home environment from any television, computer, mobile device, or touch panel with ease.

We design our systems to go with the flow of your life and your home. With its easy-to-use interface, routine household tasks turn into elegant pleasures that not only make your home more comfortable but also safer and more energy efficient.

Whatever stage your project is in, from design to installation, we'll provide the expertise, technology and integration support to help you make it a success.
