Daniel Trujillo Escomel's Summary

Industrial engineer with an MBA from the Melbourne Business School and Business Strategy specialization from the Bocconi University (Italy). My professional experience is in commercial and strategic planning roles, direct involvement in the implementation of new ventures and development of new markets on the printing, financial and energy industries as well as international working experience on South America, Europe and Australia. During my career my sustainability passion has taken me through exporting fair trade textiles, developing and commercialising micro insurance products and establishing an energy management business.

As a commercially minded and self starter person my career objective is to be part of a dynamic business where I can lead a team to success. My personal satisfaction comes from satisfying customers needs with innovative solutions generated through team effort.

Specialties

Strategy, product management, business development, business planning, project management, problem solving, market research, business startup.
