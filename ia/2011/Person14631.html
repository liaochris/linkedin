Francois Coen's Summary

A tenacious and pragmatic project management professional who strongly believes there is no negative or hostile project situation that a subtle blend of teamwork, customer focus and re-alignment on Project Management fundamentals can’t resolve.

A disciplined and experienced Project Manager with a solid track record of project delivery in a truly international environment (multiple references in Europe, the Middle East and the Asia/Pacific region).

Extensive experience of complex cross-national customer projects where multi-lingual skills (English, French and German) and sensitivity to cultural issues were critical to successful implementations.

Personal qualities include: project team building skills; an energetic and proactive approach to project management; a business- and results-oriented attitude; and a taste for challenges.

Specialties

• Implementation of projects in a variety of industry sectors (Banking, Security, Insurance, Retail, Telecommunications, Hospitality, Construction)
• International/multinational/multicultural project environments
• Turnaround of troubled projects
• Project team leadership
