Rūta Avulytė Moreira's Summary

My career goals focus on continuous learning and mobility by developing expertise through practical knowledge of locations and participation in relevant field operations. Over the total of eleven (11) years of professional experience as information analyst and researcher, development and electoral programs officer, TV reporter and producer, public information officer and outreach/ training specialist, programme manager and project coordinator, I have developed good information collection, filtering and analytical skills to provide detailed assessment and profiling of situation within broader country or region context; communication and negotiation skills to advocate ideas and conduct presentations (oral, visual or written); experience in developing and executing programs throughout its life circle; supervise and mentor international and local staff, taking into consideration cultural differences; prioritise and act under most confusing and stressful situations.
Extensive field experience in UN DPKO, UN Agencies, OSCE, EU missions (UNMIL, UNMIT, WFP, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Mozambique). Gained experience in operational planning, logistics, coordination, training; reception, inspection and distribution of items; management/ administration of storage and offices; supervision of national staff and monitoring implementation of activities; liaison with local authorities; drafting, script writing, editing, periodical reporting.
Lived and worked in USA, The Netherlands, Germany, Poland, Liberia, Timor-Leste. Learned basic of each language where resided; learning Portuguese lang. at the moment.
I am in search for the position with greater responsibility and challenging assignment to further develop my managerial and analytical capacity of expertise willing to contribute skills and knowledge to securer world thru development.
