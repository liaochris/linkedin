Adam Waldie's Summary

Since departing from the Royal Australian Navy in 2004 after a career in Submarines, Adam has remained passionate in his support for this unique Australia Industry and is the current Submarine Business Development Manager for THALES Australia.

Prior to this, Adam ran a successful Defence Consultancy - Defence Directions, that despite the GFC was listed in the BRW Fast 100 List for 2009. With the scope of major Submarine related projects forecast over the coming years however, working at THALES is definetly the place to be.


In his spare time, Adam was fortunate to have spent a good deal of his time in the Australian Navy travelling and surfing much of the Pacific, he wanted to create a vehicle to document these experiences and the similar experiences of other travellers and surfers - hence Surfing Atlas was born.

www.surfingatlas.com
