Johnny Malek's Summary

I have 17+ years of marketing communications experience gained on the world’s best brands at the best of the multinational Clients and agencies in the MENA region.
This spans automotive with Ford Motor Company, FMCG with Kodak, Shell, Braun AG, Unilever, Nestle, SC Johnson, Warner Lambert and Procter and Gamble and as MENA VP Marketing at Orbit Satellite TV for 6 years.
