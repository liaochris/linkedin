Matt Clark's Summary

A Freelance Conversion Consultant whose aim is to help clients better measure, understand and improve the online performance of their business.


Ever wondered: What you users are doing when on your site? Why they leave so quickly? Why so few of them turn into customers? I can help.


I consult with companies large and small globally to help them better understand their users before making improvements to the way they market to them - significantly increasing bottom line.


The Typical Process:

> Measure - Looking at the tools you currently setup and ensuring that everything is setup correctly to maximise every drop of obtainable insight.

> Understand - Analysis of your performance against your competitors drawing upon every piece of information available.

> Optimise - Once issues have been highlighted, changes to key areas of the site are systematically tested site using performance data to understand what works best. We only permanently deploy changes which positively effect conversion meaning improvements are virtually guaranteed.


>> If you want to understand more about conversion optimisation or are interested in deploying a conversion optimisation do not hesitate to get in touch.
