Simon Fox's Summary

CEO and founder of TBi.

TBi SaaS Solutions leverage existing corporate assets breathing new life into your business processes whilst giving you control and visibility to deliver great service and instil rapid, flexible process improvement.

We collaborate with our customers to understand all their needs and then deliver individually tailored solutions which maps exactly to their requirements in just a few weeks.

We also understand how our customers need and want control over their processes - so we give it to them. We put our customers in the driving seat with the technology so they can adapt and create new processes and rules as their businesses and products change.

We are specialists in providing secure SaaS solutions for:

Virtual Call Centres
Credit and Collections
Customer Portals & CRM
Bookings & Capacity Management
Billing and Transactions
Data Transformation
Dashboard KPI's
Holistic Management Information

All of our SaaS Solutions are underpinned by our innovative and award winning Cloud technology - TBi Flow. TBi Flow works in tandem with existing legacy systems such as SAP or Oracle. Alternatively TBi Flow can standalone, but is always delivered through the Cloud to ensure we have minimal impact on our customers IT.

We have an unrivalled track record and we are always proven to create cost savings, improve service levels and reduce administration and overheads.

TBi has market-leading customers in Healthcare, Food Services and Transport.
