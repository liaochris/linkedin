Berk Ott’s Summary

Customer Data Integration Architect specialized in creating Pharma specific Siperian MDM implementations. Main objective is to bring rigor and quality focus to each CDI implementation so that a quality architecture and quality infrastructure is delivered. The quality of the implementation is supported by understanding of the client’s vision, goals, limitations, data sources and time line. Performance of logical and physical data modeling is seen as the key activity in producing the foundation of the CDI architecture. Extensive Siperian internal knowledge is applied in the design of processes which wrap Siperian. Process automation, control and monitoring is thought about and designed from the outset. Similar architectural considerations, features and experiences are applied around the inbound and outbound ETL and SOA components.

Berk Ott’s Specialties:

Customer Data Integration: Siperian MRM & HM, SOA, custom data steward consoles, Customer Hub ETL, publication, and process automation. CRM: Siebel ePharma, Dendrite; project management, vendor evaluation and selection, planning, implementation, ETL, initial and ongoing data scrubbing, legacy system interfaces, roll-out, post roll out support, config control, realignments and sales force activity reporting. Territory Assignment and Realignment processes. Call activity data and its analysis.
