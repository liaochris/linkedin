L. Roxanne Evans’s Summary

Combining my experience in restoration landscaping, organic farming, ecological landscape design/construction and my passion for ecology and nature, I am able to bring both science and art together to create beautiful landscapes. I appreciate a hands-on, collaborative approach to design and want my clients to really take ownership of the gardens we design together, before, during and after the garden is installed. My role changes from designer, to teacher, to project manager, to biologist, to resource specialist and back again. I love the flexibility of working in this field and the life-long learning it brings.

I love giving talks and demonstrations to local groups and offer classes in a variety of topics.

L. Roxanne Evans’s Specialties:

CA Natives, Edible Gardens, Wild Edibles, Firescaping and Deer-Tolerant Landscapes, Habitat Gardens; Utilizing Permaculture and Ecological Design Principals; Teacher, Speaker and Writer on all things Ecological
