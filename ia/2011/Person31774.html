Michael Williams's Summary

Michael Williams is sought after to be one of the most talented young emarketing talents in Canada. A graduate of Seneca College School of Marketing and e-Business, Michael has spent several years mastering the art of e-marketing management. (SEO, PPC, SMO, etc)

Many companies have transformed their business development process by implementing Michael’s talent. His unique approach to internet marketing is creative and has a proven track record for success in many types of businesses. (B2B or B2C)

Michael currently holds a full time position as the National Marketing Manager for Corporate Telecom Services Inc., a telecommunications company located in Oakville where he is currently implementing some of his best practices in marketing and lead automation.

Outside of his full time position, Michael works as a consultant with some of Canada’s top brands, helping to transform the way they do business. Just to name drop a few: Rayovac, Remmington, St. Ives, Eska Water, Shoplogix, TimeTECH, HRWARE, WPS, and the list goes on.

Michael has worked collaboratively on various projects with some of the most well known marketing and web technology organizations such as TechWyse, Blackdot, Klick Communications, Crewinc, e10 and Integram Marketing.

Michael is a guest speaker and lecturer at the University of Toronto and Seneca College on the topics of emarketing strategies, sales automation, personal selling and emerging technologies in marketing.

Michael’s definition of marketing is “Converting wants into needs” – he truly believes in this and lives by it every day, online or offline.
