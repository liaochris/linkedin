This is who we help

We help businesses and individuals who are looking for courses and training providers in the UK by matching their training requirements to our vast growing network of course supplier partners.

So if you are looking for a course try our free course search service Call us 01702 535628 or email us at courses@trainingdirectoryuk.com

This is how we help businesses & Individuals to find training

1) Once an enquiry is received we carry out a course search for courses that match the requirements
2) We then email the search results for review
3) If a course matches your needs then let us know and we send a booking form (T&C)for completion and return
4) Once we receive the booking form we process the booking with the selected training provider
5) The training provider will then send all the relevant details to you direct
