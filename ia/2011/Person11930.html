Scott Seeley’s Summary

Engage with your online target audience!

At Digital Partners, we like to keep it simple. We design and develop websites. Our core team have been doing this for over 10 years now.

Our goals are mainly helping clients capitalise on the opportunities offered by digital, screen and print media. Specialising in brand and identity projects mainly online via dp, and working together to design highly usable and accessible online applications and websites which deliver strong results for businesses, organisations and charities.

Core Skills: Project Scope, Information Architecture, Strategy Design, Planning and Project Management and Graphic, Print and Web Design.

Clients exp and projects directed include: SECC, SnowsportScotland, Mister Ltd, Dominos Pizza, Aberdeen University, British Gas, FirstGroup PLC, Star Refrigeration PLC, AllMediaScotland & Scotcampus.
