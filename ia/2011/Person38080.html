ABOUT CO-OP
With offices in New York and Los Angeles, CO-OP is a branding agency recognized for its award-winning work in hospitality, entertainment, consumer products, and real estate development. CO-OP has quickly established itself as one of the most sought-after branding firms in North America.

A member of Havas Design+, CO-OP has a proven track record of maximizing clients' investments, even during the economic downturn. The firm works with a range of companies including Forest City, IMAX, Canyons, Screenvision, Johnson & Johnson, NY Observer, Maesa, Talisker Corporation, Chalk and Thimble, and Lowes Hotels.


ABOUT HAVAS DESIGN+
Havas Design+ is a global branding and design community within the Havas global agency network. Our community is made up of 400 specialists from 12 unique companies and 12 international cities - Buenos Aires, Casablanca, Dubai, Dusseldorf, Libson, London, Madrid, Mubai, New York, Paris, Prague, and Sao Paulo.

Our focus is to share and adapt ideas based on culture, trends, and local issues.

Our approach is a human, user, and local consumer-centric approach that connects differences across markets and borders.
