Beth Ann Hilton's Summary

Successful at both corporate and entrepreneurial efforts, I am primarily a Marketing Communications executive who welcomes the challenges of balancing strategic planning with hands-on project management. I enjoy public speaking, networking and relationship building in a Communications capacity. I have a busy client roster, so I don't spend a lot of time "working" this or other social networking scenes; please phone or email me directly with any immediate project opportunities or needs.

My career has included special focuses on music, DVD, theatrical distribution and fashion. The B Company focuses on entertainment marketing services for music labels as well as studios such as Warner Bros. A boutique agency, our strengths include handling publicity, marketing, promotional partnerships, and business development for an exclusive roster of clients. We are particularly skilled at launching the new and unknown, and welcome such challenges!

On a personal business note, I value opportunities to network with socially-conscious companies and individuals. Our intention is to add healthy food, beverage and product marketing to our range of clients. All referrals welcome and appreciated...we treat your people like they're our people.

Specialties

strategic planning, negotiating, niche marketing, promotional partnerships, branding, distribution, product extensions and bundling, relationship management, music law, website management, Internet marketing & social networking
