Exhibitions Sales Manager Earls Court and Olympia

As Exhibition Sales manager at EC&O Venues, I am responsible for the managing of major accounts across both Trade and Consumer Exhibitions.

These included names such as Brand Events (Top Gear Live, Who do you think you are?), BBC Haymarket (MasterChef Live), Centaur Exhibitions (Business Travel, Marketing Week Live), Vos Media (Metro Ski Show, Vitality Show) and William Reed (The Restaurant Show) to name but a few.

Dealing directly with the managing directors and Show directors, this position has enabled me to build a very strong profile within the exhibitions industry. This has also given me fantastic insight into the dealings of a huge variety of markets.

The role also encompasses the targeting of events from other venues. During my time at EC&O I have been very successful in securing the likes of the Clothes Show Live (Haymarket) Symbian Smartphone Show (Informa Exhibitions), and Railtex (Mack Brooks) from rival venues.

New business and launch events have also been a major focus. Dealing with new enquiries has further strengthened the opportunity to examine new markets.

I have often undertaken a more consultative role with new entrants to the exhibition world and offered my advice to experienced industry professionals thanks to my privileged position of visiting over 300 exhibitions a year.
