Aaron Cruikshank's Summary

I believe that technology in its many forms is the key to our future. The proper utilization of technology makes our lives better and in most cases, I believe that we already have the technical solutions to change the world at our fingertips – we just need to think of the best ways to apply them.

I am a huge advocate for appropriate technology – choosing a technical solution that meets well thought-out requirements and solves a tangible problem in your life without too much unnecessary clutter. I think the best technical solutions are those which go from “holy crap, that’s amazing” to ubiquity in record time.

The way to achieve this ubiquity is by engaging the people whose problems you need to solve early on – understand their context as fully as you can and then go back to the drawing board and think very carefully about what to put in front of them as a solution. Once you have that solution in hand, you have to present it to them in their own context, using their own words, so that they see the elegance of what you’ve brought together for them.

I am passionate about helping high tech companies realize their innovations in the market place. I help them by making connections between the non-technical wants and needs of their target market and the technical solution that they have come up with. I do this with words and pictures. I do it in person, over the phone, on the internet and through traditional media outlets.

I am also passionate about helping non-technical organizations like non-profits and individuals achieve their goals and solve their problems by leveraging technology in all of its forms. Knowledge is power and my biggest thrill in life is putting that power in the hands of individuals who feel powerless.

Specialties

Marketing and communications, market intelligence, research, public policy, business development, stakeholder engagement, market assessments, program evaluations and appropriate technology
