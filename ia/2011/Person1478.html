Alexis Sheldon's Summary

Entrepreneur, traveller, linguist, teacher, mentor, internet specialist (especially SEO and back-end systems) and appalling dancer – that’s a lot of different things! I love growing existing and creating new businesses, managing teams and constantly developing new ideas and techniques. I speak 6 foreign languages (Russian, Portuguese, French, Spanish, Italian and Hebrew) and have lived in Israel, Russia, Ukraine, Argentina and currently split my time between England (where I grew up) and Brazil where I am a permanent resident and my first baby, the smart and beautiful Lilli Sophia, is now 18 months old.

After growing up in London, studying at Cambridge and years of living abroad and teaching English to support my travelling, I took a job in Brighton (England) where I threw myself into all things internet and language industry related and developed a passion for online marketing, especially PPC, SEO and using web systems to automate processes and connect with people all over the world. I was then tasked with setting up a new corporate business unit from just a three-word concept 'business language training' and ended up servicing multinational companies with hundreds of teachers worldwide just 2 years later, primarily due to a site with 100s of number 1 Google rankings and an incredible instructor network.

I then left to set up my own businesses providing business language training worldwide, website translations and SEO to companies in different industry sectors. I concentrate now on growing the businesses, keeping my clients' and my own sites at the top of Google and looking to constantly improve the quality of our services.
