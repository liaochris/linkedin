Susan de Sousa’s Summary

Susan has a hugely varied business background, which encompasses areas such as investment banking, freelance journalism, technology / social media startups and of course global project management.

Project Management Experience

Susan has over 13 years experience in this area and has successfully managed some of the most complex, high profile cutting edge deliveries in Europe.

She has consulted at ITV, Carphone Warehouse, Camelot (The National Lottery), NHS, Reuters, BSkyB, O2, 3, Hallmark Digital, Energis, MCI Worldcom, BBC, MTV & Capita Mclarens amongst others.

Susan is also the Site Editor of My-Project-Management-Expert.com, the industry leading project management website.

Highlights Include:

- Relaunching EuroMillions and creating the Millionaire Raffle for Camelot
- Delivering Live TV to a Mobile Phone for BSkyB (1st time outside South Korea)
- Delivering the IT Services Platform for the launch of 3 (1st video conferencing on a mobile)
- Managing the global SciPlay JV between Playtech & Scientific Games
- Delivering the first global SMS Aggregator for O2
- Delivering Interactive TV for BBC
- Delivering the New Online Media Infrastructure Platform for BSkyB
- Launching the Hallmark HiYa cards globally for Hallmark Cards

Social Media / SEM Experience

Susan has provided highly successful Internet and Social Media strategies for blue chip companies such as LexisNexis, Nomensa, EMAP, ITV, Practicus and Guess Watches.

Speaking

Susan is a sought after international speaker and frequent contributor to both media & TV. She has spoken recently at The Insitute of Engineering and Technology, The Institute of Directors, City Business Library and the Minerva Network. She has also provided expert opinion for BBC Business Breakfast, Channel 4 News, NHK (Japanese TV), The Guardian, The Evening Standard,and Computer News ME.

Susan de Sousa’s Specialties:

Programme Management, Project Management, Legal Project Management, Project Director, Programme Director. E-Commerce Strategy, SEO, SEM, User Retention, Website Monetisation. Social Media, Twitter, Facebook and Mobile Apps. Due Dilligence of Technology Startups including Business Case scrutiny.
