C. Scott Wilson’s Summary

Scott Wilson has twenty years of management consulting experience providing strategy, business planning, and advisory services to energy utilities and markets. His industry and business knowledge includes regional wholesale energy markets, supply and T&D operations, customer-side operations and customer service, retail market and demand-side planning, and system infrastructure/asset planning. He is active in developing energy/sustainability strategies and plans for Smart Grid, distributed energy resources (including renewables), and other emerging customer-side energy management solutions that integrate these options into utility systems and markets.

Over his career, Scott has specialized in determining operational and business impacts associated with significant industry change (e.g., regional market restructuring; evolving energy supply, delivery, and service models; and increasing regulation), and helping clients to improve operational/market performance, manage internal acceptance and change, and guide business transformation efforts. In 1996, he founded NorthStar Concepts Inc., an independent management consulting practice serving energy markets, utilities, and other industry clients for ten years. Prior to NorthStar, Scott was a member of ScottMadden’s strategic planning practice for six years and served as a U.S. Army officer stationed in Europe with US nuclear weapons and US/West German Army combat engineer units. As a cadet at VMI, Scott earned the U.S. Army Airborne qualification.
