Imre Szenttornyay’s Summary

Imre Szenttornyay is an experienced entrepreneur, technologist, and business development executive in the global technology services industry with over 15 years of international experience throughout the United States, Mexico, South America, and Asia with a nearshore specialization in the LATAM market.

Imre has held leadership and global sales roles with responsibilities that include managing alliances, acquisitions, strategic planning, risk management, customer satisfaction and client relationship development.

Imre's expertise is in contract negotiations, nearshoring and offshore operations, and labor arbitrage specializing in systems integration, application outsourcing, infrastructure outsourcing and business transformation outsourcing services.

Imre has successfully negotiated key distribution, OEM, BOT and global sourcing contracts with various international manufacturers, independent software vendors, and systems integrators, leveraging economic market conditions and government programs for his clients.

Imre has been featured in Wired magazine, the Houston Business Journal, Biometrics Digest, KPRC Channel 2, WBTV Channel 39, Disney World Epcot, Global ID Magazine (Italy), Smart Computing, Archimag (France), Scientific America, and Electronic House for his entrepreneurial leadership in the field of Biometric Security.

Married, and the father of five (5) children, Imre volunteers his time to serve worthy causes, such as the Texas Children’s Hospital Family Advisory Board, the Building Security & Technology Planning Committee at the Houston Community College, the Boy Scouts of America as Scoutmaster, and coaching a Cy-Fair youth football team.
