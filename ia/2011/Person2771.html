Andy Hollands's Summary

I founded The UK Water Group Ltd and took it from start-up to £3 million turnover in less than 7 years. In 2001, we completed an exit strategy, selling the business to PHS Plc.

Working now as a Non-Executive Director to help business owners tackle the challenges and opportunities of running their business by chairing their monthly management/board meetings. I help them step back, take stock of their situation, focus on their priorities and create a plan that will help drive them forward.
My core skills concentrate on:-
Helping business owners work 'on' their business, ensuring monthly meetings are held to keep the business on track.
Scrutinise the performance of management in meeting agreed goals & objectives.
Ensure financial information is accurate & financial controls are in place.
Put in place a business development plan & simplify their sales & marketing process.
Constructively challenge & contribute to the development of strategy
Previous roles included:-

Managing Director - SCI Sales Group Ltd.

The UK's leading Sales Outsourcing Company. Working from the London office.
Using our 25 strong team of sales professionals to understand the clients value proposition and generate quality leads, appointment making and field sales support.

Founder / Sales Director - The UK Water Group Ltd.

I along with my two fellow directors pioneered a new plumbed in filtered and purified drinking water dispenser to the commercial market place.
We grew the business from start up to over £3 million turnover in less than 7 years, employing over 52 staff.

Bank Manager - TSB Bank.

I joined the bank upon leaving college and spent over 15 years with them. During my time I became a Commercial Manager's Assistant and reached the position of Bank Manager at the age of 29.

Specialties

Desire to help others achieve, understanding people, sales skills, consultative business development skills, financial planning, first hand knowledge on running a business and successfully implementing an 'exit strategy'.
