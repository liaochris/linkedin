Tanya McRae's Summary

I have 20 years of experience as a television producer, field director, writer, reporter, anchor, host, production coordinator... and just about every job in between!

I've worked in many genres of television: reality, news, lifestyle, travel, entertainment, newsmagazine, and talk shows. My credits include great shows on ABC, NBC, CBS, Fox, Travel Channel, Lifetime Television, Discovery Channel, MTV, VH-1, CMT, USA Network, Soapnet, TLC, A & E, Oxygen, National Geographic, E! Entertainment Television, ABC and NBC News, and a number of local stations.

After getting a degree in communications from the University of Pennsylvania, my television career began in New York as an assignment desk assistant at ABC World News and Nightline. I covered the Tiananmen Square Massacre, the fall of the Berlin Wall and the San Francisco earthquake.

In 1992 I moved to Los Angeles and for the next 10 years I worked on very memorable shows from "Hard Copy" to "Leeza" to "I Wanna Be A Soap Star", and dozens of other shows on almost every major cable channel.

In 2005 I became a reporter and fill-in anchor for the NBC affiliate in Palm Springs, CA. where I received two Emmy Award nominations. In 2008 I launched my own weekly lifestyle and entertainment TV show "Spotlight On The Desert", through my company, Amazonia Productions. It was a blast and I tapped into the innovative world of product integration and advertorial content. I then became the Business Development Director at a similar show "Style Boston" that airs across New England on NECN for 6 months. I also hosted segments for them about New England travel excursions and real estate.

I'm now back in Los Angeles developing my own reality and lifestyle shows, freelancing as a field producer, and working with casting director Jacqui Pitman on a number of shows.

Specialties

* Diverse background in all aspects of TV production from show conception to research, field producing, conducting interviews, writing half hour and one hour scripts, overseeing editing, live and general assignment reporting, lifestyle and entertainment hosting.
* Equally comfortable in front of the camera and behind it.
* An insightful, persistent and personable interviewer.
* Known for my positive take charge attitude. :)
* Love to travel.
