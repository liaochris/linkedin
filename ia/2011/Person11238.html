Deanna Fusco’s Summary

Started freelance graphic design studio, SoHo Southwest in 2006 with focus on identity systems, conceptual design and specialty design. Work includes designs for offset printing, digital printing, Web graphics as well as custom designs for 3-ring binders, folders, specializing in the restaurant and hospitality industries.

Deanna Fusco’s Specialties:

Identity and branding, conceptual design, 4 color printing, unique custom designs, metal designs (photos reproduced on metal, embossed metal plates), leather binders, menu covers, in-room guest folders, brochures, annual reports, invitations, plastic cards, Web graphics and design
