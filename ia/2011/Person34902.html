Philippe Roy's Summary

I scarcely remember a time when I did not have a camera in my hand. Granted, at the age of four, my paparazzi like stalking of family and friends was for the fun of squirting them with water and there must have been sighs of regret that I had been given a joke camera. Eventually, however, I was bought the real thing – albeit only a point-and-shoot – and I discovered the joys of creating pictures.

At the age of eighteen, now equipped with a Nikon FM10, I sold my first photograph and knew that this was my chosen path.

It is a path which has taken me across the world, managing, producing and executing photoshoots in North America, Latin America, India, Japan, Korea, Singapore, Vietnam, Australia. And which led me eventually to China.

Here in Shanghai my commercial photography studio caters to local businesses as well as multinationals – names such as Elle, L’Oreal, BASF, Pratt and Whitney, Rolls Royce amongst many others.

Photography, retouching, graphic design, print management – the simple photo-shoot to entire catalogue production – my team and I can take on any project. We pride ourselves on creativity, innovative approaches and quality photography. If those are the values you are looking for, contact me at: info@philippe-roy.com

Specialties

I specialise in creating stunning visuals for companies. Each image crafted to exactly each one of my clients needs. My work speaks for itself: www.philippe-roy.com
