Alex LaFleur’s Summary

Currently an account manager with Rohrback Cosasco Systems in Los Angeles, CA. RCS is the global leader in corrosion monitoring systems, focused on process control. My role is to support and grow accounts in the Western United States including Alaska, as well as aquire new business in our major industries such as oil & gas, water/wastewater, petrochemical, and others.
