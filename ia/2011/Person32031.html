Nasim Bavar, MFT's Summary

My passion is working with individuals and couples in the areas of relationship issues, parenting, addictions, and eating disorders. I am a Trained State provider for gambling addictions, helping individuals, family members, and affected others – treatment is free and paid for by the State of California. I have specialized training in EMDR for treating trauma and abuse. I am becoming trained as a Gottman couples therapist and I use the IMAGO principles whenever needed.

On a personal note, I am originally from the Middle East; I speak Farsi, grew up in London England, and have lived in San Diego for 27 years.

Specialties

Addictions, Eating Disorders, EMDR, Couples therapy, Parenting support, & Relationship Issues
