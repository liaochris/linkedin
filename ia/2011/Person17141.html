Hitesh Bhatia (hitesh@thinkpeople.in)’s Summary

This is Me!

I stepped into this world on March 28, 1980, have been born and bought up in the city of dreams, that never sleeps called ‘Mumbai’.

At heart, I am a chef & an entrepreneur with a Hotel Management degree and a MBA from Symbiosis. I have co-founded many ventures over the past few years, the successful ones of which are:
- Think People Solutions Pvt. Ltd. – www.thinkpeople.in
- Team Force Asia INC – www.teamforceasia.com
- Campus Yield Solutions Pvt. Ltd. – www.campusyield.com

I simultaneously manage all the above organizations and the focus of all of them is to deliver quality & value to fortune 500 organizations. The scope of the organizations I manage cover: Recruitment, Training, Staffing, Outsourcing/Off-shoring, Enrolments and Placements. My area of forte is Business Development & Strategy.

The establishment of these firms to me has been a journey of constant exploration, of knowledge through the emerging day-to-day challenges of stabilizing a start-up. More than anything else it has molded me as an individual who dreams to grow beyond any limitations and strive for all that one can be.

I have over 8 young years of deliberation and steadfast endeavors aided with the acumen for innovation and vision resulting in agonizing the constantly metamorphosing scenario of Sales, Business Development & Human Resources.

I believe in the conjoining of Intelligence and Knowledge to practice excellence, which is a continual process and not a singular achievement, demonstrated with my attributes – Sales, Marketing & people management skills, Strategic Planning, Revenue Growth strategies, Team building & Team Retention, Negotiation skills, Branding & promotions, Operational successes, Productizing strategies, Expertise in New age technologies – Software and Hardware, market updates to summarize.
