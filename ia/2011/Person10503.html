Dave Cottrill’s Summary

OBJECTIVE: To utilize my strengths in Quality, Industrial Engineering, Operations Management and Supply Chain. To help small and medium sized firms remain or become competitive.

CAREER SUMMARY: I have over 12 years of experience as an operations analyst, industrial engineer, buyer and material planner. I implemented lean concepts and process improvements and kept order to enable companies to continue competing in the economic conditions and competitiveness of recent decades.
