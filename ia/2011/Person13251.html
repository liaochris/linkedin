Joseph Ekloff's Summary

Joseph Ekloff is an ambitious student and active designer completing a BFA in Visual Communications and Marketing minor from the University of Arizona, in May of 2011. Joseph is prepared to explore a fruitful graphic design career, which a diverse background in print and web design has prepared him for. With experience in both marketing and graphic design, Joseph is seeking a career which utilizes his specialties such as brand identity, advertising, typography, and illustration.

He currently works for the College of Architecture and Landscape Architecture, developing fundraising collateral. He has also interned for Simpleview, Inc, where he provided an internet presence by designing user interfaces and information graphics for leading DMOs (destination marketing organizations) across the U.S. and internationally.

With an eye for innovation and a commitment to integrity, Joseph strives to deliver valuable design solutions.
