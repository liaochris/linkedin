Joe Spake's Summary

Licensed Realtor with 13 experience in representing buyers and sellers of residential real estate in Memphis, Tennessee, utilizing Web 2.0 marketing techniques, including a Memphis Real Estate Blog. I specialize in in-city properties in Midtown, Downtown, and East Memphis, historic homes, condos, and relocations.

I also consult with businesses and non-profits on integrating Social Media marketing techniques into their overall marketing strategies, providing needs assessment, training and coaching.
