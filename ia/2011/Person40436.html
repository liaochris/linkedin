Buzzcapture

Online Media industry

January 2009 – May 2009 (5 months)

Buzzcapture monitors & analyses blogs, forums and other places on the internet where people share their opinions. By doing this, Buzzcapture provides insight in the discussions that are taking place online about their brands, products, services and competition.

We feel the impact of consumer generated media (also called User Generated Content or social media) is becoming increasingly important for companies. By offering reporting and access to a web-based dashboard we provide companies with insight in the online discussions, thus making it easier to come up with an effective online strategy.

In addition to buzz-research, we offer data delivery services to companies that want to participate in online discussions in forums and blogs.
