Don Larson, CAPM MCTS’s Summary

I enjoy leading teams as a manager or director, helping the team members achieve their best effectiveness on the project or workflow process. I earned my PMI Certified Associate In Project Management (CAPM), Microsoft Certified Technology Specialist (MCTS), and passed two Six Sigma Total Quality certifications. I have experience in Fortune 100 global enterprises and smaller organizations. My projects finish on-time and on or under budget, but more importantly, deliver business value. While most of my career has been spent in technology related areas, I'm flexible to other opportunities as they arise. I've led teams of volunteers in a variety of local organizations, such experiences bring a wealth of knowledge and wisdom for all involved. It is my firm belief that if you can motivate and lead volunteers to accomplish goals, you can do the same for employees.
