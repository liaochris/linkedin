Sameer Peera’s Summary

An accomplished eCommerce executive with strong business and technical expertise in multi-channel and integrated web systems coupled with industry experience spanning various sectors including Retail and Life Sciences. Developed enterprise retail solutions comprised of the latest integrated web technologies that provide a rich user experience and seamless integration with legacy interfaces (supply chain, inventory, merchandise, warehouse, reporting, point of sale, customer management and financial systems) for several prominent retailers. Directly responsible for on-line operations, including infrastructure and high availability, for a specialty apparel retailer for over five years. Developed program and project level enterprise re-architecture roadmap / across solution spaces that include BI, MDM, Search, ECM, WCM, CRM, Commerce, and integrated ERP systems. Collaborated with key client business and technology stake holders in developing, scoping, and executing engagements that satisfy business objectives and support the streamlining of IT / operational processes to maximize efficiency. Provided architectural direction for strategic projects and initiatives for cross-functional groups and project teams.

Experienced in Global WebSphere Commerce delivery methodology and execution. Established and trained teams in Hangzhou, China to deliver B2B and B2C Commerce solutions. Architected and Designed assets around WebSphere Commerce including multi-channel extensions and integration framework.
