Gary H Elzweig, PE’s Experience

  • CEO

    Hob Knob, LLC

    (Partnership; Information Technology and Services industry)

    September 2010 — Present (6 months)

    Hob Knob, LLC (www. hob-knob.com). Hob-Knob has developed a process that will revolutionize social media, local social gaming, and business geolocation applications. Until now, geolocation applications have been dominated by companies that use the internet, GPS or cell phone technologies to identify and link users to each other and to businesses based on their close proximity. While connecting users based on nearby locations is a relatively new development made possible with the growth of Smartphones, the current market leaders in this sphere have relied entirely on traditional internet, GPS or cell phone based technologies for connections. On January 14, 2011, Hob Knob officially launched Hob-Knob’s iPhone App (version 1.0) for download at the Apple App Store and Google Marketplace. Hob-Knob’s iPhone app represents a new era in smartphones and social networking as it is the first application to combine social networking and local social gaming opportunities with a geo-location platform. While that may sound complicated, it is actually rather simple. What it means is that now people will be able to connect with each other just as they would on Facebook or other social networking sites, simply by being near each other on their Smartphone's over Hob-Knobs Wi-Fi direct virtual network. It is as simple as this, walk into a shopping mall with a Wi-Fi system and other Hob-Knobbers that are also in the mall simply pop-up on your iPhone.

  • Chairman and Managing Member

    Quality Built, LLC

    (Privately Held; Construction industry)

    March 2010 — Present (1 year )

    Quality Built®
    Two award winning engineering entrepreneurs, Gary H Elzweig and Brian C Kramer, team up to acquire substantially all of the assets of Quality Built®, pursuant to Section 363 of the US Bankruptcy Code. Quality Built®, is a 16 year old professional construction service leader and is best known for its work in Total Quality Management, servicing residential and commercial clients nationwide. The company provides third-party quality assurance services as well as a full spectrum of risk management solutions. Beth Michaelis and Stan Luhr the founders of Quality Built® will remain on with the newly restructured firm as President and Vice President, respectively. Elzweig will serve as the Chairman while Kramer will serve as its CEO", Michaelis says.

    Michaelis adds, “The acquisition is an extraordinary partnership and a unique opportunity to create more innovative risk management solutions for the construction industry. Mr. Elzweig’s experience and visionary leadership coupled with Mr. Kramer’s strategic plan for integrating resources for operational efficiency, positions Quality Built® to renew its place as the industry leader in third party quality assurance for the residential and commercial construction industry.”

    “Our goal is to create more cost effective risk management solutions for the construction industry utilizing Quality Built®'s comprehensive ISO 9001:2000 registered Quality Management Systems, as with our newest and most innovative product, QB Builder Link®. The QB Builder Link® is a powerful quality assurance data collection and reporting software solution via an interaction database system which allows builders to take control of their quality assurance efforts at a fraction of the cost while still having the benefit of independent third party oversight. As the industry makes a comeback, this type of product will make a lot of sense to builders, lenders, distressed debt turnaround specialists, and others", says Elzweig.

Gary H Elzweig, PE’s Groups:
