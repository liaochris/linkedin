Quentin Charlier's Summary

Passionate about digital communication and marketing I am Online Marketeer for Fortuneo, an online bank.

I have two diplomas in two very different fields: just graduated from the Université catholique de Louvain, I have a Master’s Degree in Information and Communication and I have a Bachelor’s degree in data processing applied to management from the Haute Ecole de la Province de Liège Rennequin Sualem (ex-Inpres). Both diplomas gave me the opportunity to combine technical knowledge with communicational expertise.

I have specialized myself in online communication thanks to my daily reading, my website (see above) and two internships. The first one took place at Euro RSCG 4D as an Account Manager and I was Community Manager at Emakina during the second one.

My website is a virtual community about cinema called Wollyhood (www.wollyhood.eu). This project allowed me to improve my skills by different tasks like community management (of course), SEO, project management, editorial content, partnerships management, Social Media management,…

Specialties
