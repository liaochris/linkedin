Matthew Baxter’s Summary

I am an experienced writer, designer and producer from the UK, currently living in Tokyo, Japan.

I have spent a large amount of time collaborating with others to design games and Web 2.0 applications. At both a for-profit and non-profit level I created and maintained documentation not just for colleagues but for partners such as scientists, project investors and off-site contractors. I have liaised countless times with these people and companies to resolve problems and plan for the future of the products we were making together. I have also been involved in writing and managing help text, educational information text and narrative scripts.

I have previous experience working at companies such as Electronic Arts, Sega, THQ, and Lionhead (Microsoft).

UnrealEd (Unreal Engine 2 and 3), Hammer (Source SDK), SG Creator, Torque Game Builder, Virtools, Word, Excel, TestTrack Pro, DevTrack, Photoshop and Fireworks. Intermediate Japanese.
