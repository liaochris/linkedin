Peter Gan's Summary

Advertising, Branding and Communication executive who has spent 24 years in both local as well as international agencies working alongside great industry talents such as Tan Sri Lim Kok Wing, Michael Ball, John Burbidge and Neil French building brands like Nestle', Castrol, Reckitts, Mercedes Benz, BAT and GE.
Co-founded GanForHire with wife Agnes in1997 doing brand positioning and brand identity work for Southern Unit Trust, Hong Leong Assurance (HLA),Genting, Awana Hotels & Resorts, Bukit Kiara Properties and Sunrise Bhd. We were awarded special projects at adidas and also help build the Taylormade Golf brand to No.1 in Malaysia in 2 years. Went on to handle the Nike golf brand in the region. Still delivering Joy for Auto Bavaria-BMW, promoting entertainment for City of Genting, being passionate about Life with Great Eastern, exploring new horizons with GreenPacket. Currently uncovering brand values for a medical centre and an automotive parts manufacturer.

Specialties

Having cut my teeth in the advertising-driven era of the 80's and 90's working in international/global agencies then to form our own Spider Network and later ganforhire gave me insights on advertising & brand management, the brand communications became more effective and relevant. These disciplines gave me further expertise in seeing what's next and what really matters- that building brands is not a luxury but a necessity. Marketing is the job of the present and the future.
