Diana Antholis’s Summary

I am an experienced, client-centered communications strategist serving major clients in top consumer industries. As of July 2010, I completed an accelerated MA degree in Organizational Management at The George Washington University to further my career goal in building productivity and leadership through organizational development interventions.

I am passionate about developing successful leadership and management techniques within organizations to enhance productivity, increase morale, and work/life management.

Currently, I do strategy consulting at Performance Advantage Inc. We build better workplaces by focusing on individual productivity and performance.

I also founded Enter: Adulthood, a blog dedicated to young adults who are transitioning into that big bad scary place out there that some like to call the "real world." It offers advice and tips on a wide range of topics to make the transition easier and help them find themselves.

My previous corporate experience in the advertising/media industry involves five years of consulting businesses on traditional and emerging communications strategies, including television, print, outdoor, radio, digital, and social media.
