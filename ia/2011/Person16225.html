Paul Lowey's Summary

Gloo Studios Inc. is an exciting production company owned and operated by four artists, each with at least a decade of experience in the film and television industry. Our team combines their personal and unique talents to create exciting video content and visual effects for clients around the world.

At Gloo Studios Inc. creativity is king. Starting with a great vision can make the difference between wowing the pants off your audience or putting them to sleep in their chairs. We pride ourselves on being able to find the most entertaining way to explain our clients’ message, no matter what it is.

While we may take our work very seriously, that doesn’t mean we always have to be serious. It’s important to love what you do, and that’s one of the reasons why we’re so fun to work with. We love making videos and we want our clients to enjoy the process as much as we do.

Simply put, we can deliver everything you require for your film and video needs. Not only do we have the equipment, but (more importantly) we also have the talent to write, shoot, and edit your high definition video. We are a full service production company you can rely on from start to finish.
