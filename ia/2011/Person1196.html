Alberto Labuto’s Summary

Creative director with 3 years experience. Web designer with 9 years experience and web developer with 4 years experience with extensive knowledge of Adobe tools like Flash, Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, After Effects Premiere, and others. Interface designer skilled in web applications. Art director with advanced knowledge in online media. Videomaker internationally awarded. Experience with editing video for web and video management in Adobe Flash. Motion designer and video editor with 3 years experience in Apple and Adobe solutions. Bachelor with large domain of aesthetic concepts relating to the use of color, having accumulated years of studying forms arquitecture in visual arts and design. I learned to draw by observing comic books during childhood, and always like to learn by myself about everything that interests me. Today, i just want to keep working with art and design.
