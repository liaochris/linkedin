  • Founder, Creative Director

    Pont:Productions

    (Events Services industry)

    June 2002 — Present (8 years 9 months)

    Created Pont:Productions [ "Bridge the Gap" ] in 2002, one year after launching independent Email listserve, entitled "Where I Need To Be - In Detroit This Week" aka "WINTB," for young Detroit area residents highlighting "underground" music, art & culture events in the city's core, as well as weekly activities otherwise hard to find such as affordable yoga, dance or film classes.

    With a mailing list steadily growing into 4-figure numbers, approached by Asya Shein, founder of the then brand new Fusicology.com, in late 2003 to partner in order to launch the official Detroit chapter of the forward web and guerrilla marketing company. Pre-launch media was posted in 2005 for the Detroit chapter, with the full launch in 2006.

    Pont:Productions began as an "underground" dance music and multi-media event production house in downtown Detroit, having created over 2 dozen independent parties featuring forward musicians, DJs, performing and visual artists. Eventually, Pont:Productions would be contracted to produce specific cultural events in the metro Detroit area, such as "Techno Couture" for the 2003 Detroit Electronic Music Festival (DEMF), now Movement Electronic Music Festival, as well as several benefit fundraisers for local non-profits and charities.

    With the move of the founder to New Orleans during late 2005 - 2006, Pont:Productions began producing select, smaller events in New Orleans, as well as partnering with local promoters to co-produce a humble variety of events. However, Pont:Productions still continues to produce events intermittently in Detroit, as well as other select cities, usually during festivals or conferences.

    Though Pont:Productions has also produced market research events for clients such as Ammo Marketing, Ogilvy PR and Boehringer-Ingelheim Pharmaceuticals, and can provide full-scale event production services, we specialize in intimate, creative, interactive music, art, film and fashion events of multiple scales.
