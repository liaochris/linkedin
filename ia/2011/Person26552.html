Lisa Salazar, Esq.’s Summary

Recently featured in a Houston Chronicle's Chron 100 article, "Lawyers Use Web to Stand Out", Lisa Salazar is responsible for managing the firm’s on-line marketing efforts and web 2.0 efforts. Managing a number of online advertising campaigns, monitor and measure through testing and analytics the effectiveness of banner advertising, landing pages and lead generation. Working with the firm's leadership, partners, marketing group, public relations and IT, she deals with high-level, deadline-driven, time-sensitive projects.
