Frank Stonehouse Lupo's Summary

I am interested in connecting with other ESL, EFL, ELF, TOEFL, TOEIC, ESOL, etc. professionals concerning teaching and language acquisition theory, methodology, and practice ... especially in Latin America. I am creator of META, Mexico English Teachers' Alliance, an online Web community and network for EFL teachers, http://metamexico.ning.com .. I am interested in developing and participating in more virtual and online teaching opportunities. I currently maintain a virtual training center in Second Life for EFL teacher training. I am keen on integrating technology into classrooms and working with teachers and students to promote 21st century learning.
