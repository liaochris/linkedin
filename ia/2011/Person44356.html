Tiago Veloso's Summary

After more than 15 years working in almost every kind of Tourism business, from off-shore catering in Angola to snow resorts in Austria and ecotourism in Brazil, 2010 was the perfect time to bring those people-skills into the Social Media Marketing world.

The outstanding growth of Social Media Networks and services such as Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, Foursquare and so many others opened a whole new universe of challenges and opportunities for the corporate world - specially the ones involving monitoring and managing the SMN presence.

Social Media is all about people. Knowing their expectations, and exceed them with creativity, respect and professionalism, trough a process of continuous market trends analysis and a profound understanding of the human condition in all its different dimensions.

As companies still struggle to find the best way and tools to engage with their costumers in this new world, trying to sell, answering questions and monitoring complaints isn't enough to gain visibility and achieve success. You must generate, consistently, new and useful content.

My work today is focused on Top Content generation for companies (Brazil and worldwide) that see the true power and reach of Social Media as a Marketing and Branding tool, and I’m looking to establish long-term business partnerships with companies that are interested in establishing a solid presence in the Web 2.0 World. For any additional information and a full set of services description, please, feel free to contact me.

Specialties

Social Media Marketing, Buzz Monitoring, Content Generation, Blogging, Marketing Strategy, Social Networking, Intelligence Marketing (including Market Research), Branding Solutions, Concept Management, CRM, ERP, BSC, BI, BPM and Workflow, business opportunities in Brazil, Angola and Portugal.
