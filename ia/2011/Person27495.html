Mallory Woodrow's Summary

I'm a digital media strategist and social media specialist for an internet marketing firm. Due to my passion for my fields of work, I am proactive in uncovering the newest techniques and latest technologies as they emerge and I am continually updating my skill sets to meet the demands of the ever-changing industry of new media, social media, and search engine optimization.

My educational focus in English and Economics and Management have provided me with a strong framework from which to develop my skill set. My experience authoring press and marketing materials for Beaumont Hospitals, GiftZip.com, and WJR’s Greening of the Great Lakes have further allowed me to hone my writing and communication skills. My role as Director of Social Media with the start-up GiftZip.com has equipped me with the confidence and adeptness to generate creative and compelling ad copy and content with minimal supervision and to foster working relationships with bloggers and industry players. My current position of Digital Strategist and Social Media Advisor at Netvantage Marketing has given me the opportunity and expertise necessary to work with a variety of clients from a range of industries.

I am proficient in all Microsoft-based applications, social media platforms, Google AdWords, Facebook Ads, and Google Analytics. I work with affiliate providers such as LinkShare, CJ, and Google Affiliate Network. I am efficient in using SEO tools such as SEOmoz, MajesticSEO, Ontolo.com, Yahoo Site Explorer, SEOquake, 404Checker, and more. In addition to my work with Online Marketing and e-Commerce, I have experience writing and editing website content, as well as authoring press releases, media advisories, and other marketing materials.

I am currently happily employed, but am always seeking to hear about new projects and to meet new people, so please feel to connect with me or contact me at Mlorenw@gmail.com. I invite you to view my digital portfolio: http://www.mallorywoodrow.weebly.com!
