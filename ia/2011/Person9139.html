Richard Swerdlow's Summary

Historically low interest rates and a shrinking supply of available land in major cities throughout the world has led to the unprecedented development and conversion to condominium apartments during the past 5 years. This transformation in the residential real estate market has created significant opportunities for all market participants.

To meet the challenges and opportunities in the rapidly evolving market we developed Condo.com. We anticipated an eventual global oversupply of condos and developed a transparent marketplace to create liquidity by connecting condo buyers and condo sellers from around the world.

Our rapidly growing inventory of over 500,000 condo units, valued at over $150 billion, creates a single destination for buyers to view all available for sale and for rent properties on the market. For sellers, we have developed a self-listing process that provides free access to floorplans, photos and videos from thousands of buildings around the world to help create rich, multi-media listings. A listing on Condo.com is automatically displayed on dozens of domestic and international real estate sites accounting for over 15 million viewers per month - providing global exposure for a low flat fee.

Our award-winning site features video listings, a dynamic social network, detailed neighborhood information, interactive maps and a selection of other helpful tools and resources. We hope our dedication to the condo market makes it easier for you to connect with buyers, sellers and other real estate professionals!

Welcome to Condo.com, the World's Largest Condo Marketplace.

Richard Swerdlow's Experience

CEO, founder

Condo.com

Privately Held; Real Estate industry

2005 – Present (6 years)

Richard Swerdlow, CEO, founded Condo.com in 2005 and is responsible for the Company's strategic direction, marketing and operations. The idea for a global marketplace for condominiums came to him in mid-2005 while driving to a development site his company acquired for a high-end condo project. At the time, the lifestyle magazines and billboards along the major highways had become filled with ads for pre-construction condos. It occurred to him that, although the projects were selling out as frenzied investors from all over the world plunked down deposits with the hopes of “flipping” the units for a profit before having to close, this activity would eventually end and there would be a global over-supply of condos on the market. With the intention to help builders and developers, brokers, individual owners and financial institutions sell their inventory when the market turned down, he developed Condo.com into a global online marketplace to create liquidity for all participants.

Swerdlow has since grown Condo.com into the #1 condo real estate brand in the world. The site has over 800,000 listings valued in excess of $250 billion. The site receives over 1 million visitors per month and generates qualified sales leads in excess of $500 million monthly.

Mr. Swerdlow founded Everything Wireless, the first consumer cataloger and Internet retailer that provided national distribution of wireless phones and accessories. The Company was founded in 1993 as one of the first e-commerce companies in the country. It was hailed as “the granddaddy of all cell phone websites” and grew to $100 million in revenues with over 750,000 active customers and was sold to Federated Department Stores in 2000.
