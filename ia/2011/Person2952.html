Angus M Robinson's Summary

Angus is the Managing Partner of Leisure Solutions® which offers a range of strategic services which includes product development of inbound/outbound specialised travel for the +45 yo market interested in eco and cultural ('experiential') tourism, and specialised knowledge of the geological characteristics of geotourism.

In 2000, Leisure Solutions® authored a publication for Tourism Queensland to assist tourism operators in ‘going online’.

Angus’ other interests include non-executive roles as Chairman of Future Materials, and as a Director (Chair, Marketing and Sales Committee) of the Foundation of National Parks and Wildlife. Also serves as a Member of the Chartered Professionals Board of Management of The AusIMM.

Leisure Solutions® is listed on the Lobbyists Register of the Australian, New South Wales, and Queensland Governments.

Latest Product

Country Charm™ Discovery Tour

The Country Charm™ ecotour is a discovery tour of the beautiful Scenic Rim region of Southeast Queensland.
http://www.leisuresolutions.com.au/country_charm/index.html

Encompassing four days, this truly unique, self-drive tour package introduces travelers to volcanic peaks which provided the substrate for the lush rainforests and open forests of the Lamington National Park and much of the other high country. These volcanic areas and the valleys between, collectively form the basis of the rural lands and national parks in the Rathdowney and Boonah area. The tour package offers an itinerary which features a variety of quality rural (including heritage) and ecotourism experiences as well as an introduction to the region’s geology and wildlife. Affordable quality accommodation is included in the package.

http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yKbUR_WL9RE

Angus has returned to the Rotary Club of Sydney to chair a new Electronic Communications Committee. Previously he was a member of Rotary eClub One, the world's first internet-based Rotary Club, and as President in 2008/2009.

Specialties

ecotourism & geotourism packaging, lobbying, commercial management, marketing, business development into North Asia (particularly Taiwan & Korea), information and communication technology diffusion.

Angus is a Fellow of the Australian Institute of Management, a Fellow and Chartered Professional (Management) of The AusIMM as well as a Member of the Geological Society of Australia and the Environment Institute of Australia and NZ. Leisure Solutions® is a business member of Ecotourism Australia.
