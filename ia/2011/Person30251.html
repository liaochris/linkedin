Michael Roberts’s Summary

I am a recruiting director and staffing consultant with a fiscally responsible approach to staffing, mixing proven methods with the most current techniques in talent acquisition. I am a “fixer” who quickly evaluates the current state and implements efficient, cost effective, cutting-edge changes to recruiting processes and systems.

With me, you get someone with extensive experience with numerous corporations, talent segments and staffing models. I have been part of management teams tasked with hiring close to 20,000 employees annually and have successfully met the challenge of hiring more than 7,000 CSRs annually for APAC, a company of 8,000.

I have been deeply involved in HR transformations at Bank One as well as establishing the first centralized Staffing COE at Wolters Kluwer. I had full responsibility for the selection, implementation and user training for the staffing systems at Bank One including all related vendor contracts. This included all contracts and training related to Internet sourcing.

I have been responsible for managing recruiting functions with great success. Throughout my career, I have challenged my teams to identify and utilize best practices while reaching for new sourcing channels.

I lead HR Recruiting Advisors, a niche consulting practice centered on building out best practice recruiting operations. This includes establishing leading-edge processes; engaging internal/external sourcing and recruiting teams; evaluating, selecting and implementing staffing systems; as well as evaluating and recommending other service providers related to sourcing, hiring and on-boarding great employees.

I am affiliated with HR Pilots LLC, a consulting and delivery group capable of delivering a broad range of HR services including project based and high volume recruiting.
