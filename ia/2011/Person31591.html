Owner - Director

THIRTY5 (Sole Proprietorship)

Sole Proprietorship; Design industry

January 2007 – Present (4 years 3 months)

THIRTY5 is a global integrated music industry consulting firm. It controls THIRTY5 design, a creative web design agency servicing the electronic music industry. It serves international top DJs like Erick Morillo, Avicii, Mauro Picotto, Thomas Gold, Paul van Dyk, Francesco Rossi, Swanky Tunes, Steve Angello, Sebastian Ingrosso, Swedish House Mafia and many others.
Next to the web design agency, THIRTY5 operates a DJ booking and management agency. It manages booking and remixes requests in Italy and the rest of the world of international DJs like Sebastien Drums, David Tort, Gregori Klosman, Syke'n'Sugarstarr and young, talented DJs like Landmark, Sound of Monte-Carlo. THIRTY5 Booking manages the exclusive booking partnership for Italy of the Swedish DJ Avicii, the star producer of Seek Bromance, My Feelings For You, Street Dancer etc.
The THIRTY5 blog (http://www.thirty5blog.tumblr.com) has news and updates about DJs, news releases, free download mix, and radio shows.
THIRTY5 Records is the music label directed by DJ Landmark. It releases house, tech house and minimal music.
Landmark personally produces the THIRTY5 Records label radio show called THIRTY5 Sessions that airs exclusively on RTL 102.5 Groove every Friday from 2 p.m. to 3 p.m. CET. The radio show features a 1-hour DJ mix by Landmark and friends of the THIRTY5 family.
