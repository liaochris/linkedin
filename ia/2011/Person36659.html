Rhonda Sher’s Summary

Rhonda L. Sher is an author, speaker and business networking expert. She is a nationally known keynote speaker and the owner of The Two MInute Networker Wealth Building System. Rhonda is an expert at creating 30 second elevator pitches for small business owners, entrepeneurs and sales professionals..

By integrating proven off-line and on-line networking strategies, Rhonda is a sought after trainer by insurance and financial institutions as well as direct sale companies.
In addition, she is a partner with a company that provides getaways and gifts as incentives for businesses through a monthly membership program.

Rhonda has created a program called "Think Outside the Booth" for businesses that put on trade shows which has doubled and tripled the return on investment for compaines. She works with business owners teaching them how to keep their pipelines full and find the fortune in the follow-up..

Rhonda is THE EXPERT at teaching business professionals how to make more money with less effort through the process of online and offline networking.
She is the expert at teaching others how to::

* Identify their target market (the smaller the niche, the more you get rich)
* Develop an upbeat, easy to repeat 30 second marketing message or elevator pitch that creates curiousity and opens doors
* How to use getaways and gifts to create customer loyalty, increase referrals and improve morale
* Create visibility both online and offline to be known as "The Expert" and dominate over the competition.
* Find the fortune in the follow-up.
* Systematize their networking efforts for maximum monetization
