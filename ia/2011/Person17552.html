Dan Holland's Summary

Dan is a specialist in organizational restructuring, staff development, and building effective teams. With over 25 years experience as a management consultant, Dan has consulted with many organizations including Sears, Avon, Wal-Mart, Izod Lacoste, V F Corporation, and the Human Resource Agencies of Tennessee.

Unlike many consultants who lack practical experience in their craft, Dan was a practitioner before he became a consultant. His experience includes practical hands-on application of the leadership skills now offered through his workshops and consulting engagements.
