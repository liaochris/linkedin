Eric Lancaster’s Summary

I am a co-owner and co-founder of TeraGanix,Inc., the exclusive distributor of EM Technology® -based products in the continental US. My experience with this technology dates back to 1996. The areas I focus on are business development and technical services in the area of agricultural and environmental services (wastewater, odor control, bioremediation). I also manage our extensive website, www.TeraGanix.com, that has nearly 600 pages of information.

In 2007, I was invited by Dr. Masuro Emoto to present on the connections between EM Technology® and Hado. Dr. Emoto mentions EM Technology® in his book, The Secret Life of Water, and his meeting Dr. Higa, the inventor of EM Technology®.
