Lauren Fix’s Summary

Inaugural Inductee to the National Transportation Women's Hall of Fame and 2008 Automotive Women of The Year.

Nationally recognized automotive expert in car care, repair, safety issues, buying and selling new and used cars and trucks, and driving skills.

Author: USA Today, Good Housekeeping, Redbook, Woman’s World, Esquire, First for Women, InTouch, Self, Motor Trend, Truck Trend, Hot Rod, and Car Craft.

Automotive Editor for YourLifeMagazine.com

Author of three books: Driving Ambitions: A Complete Guide to Amateur Auto Racing and The Performance Tire and Wheel Handbook and multiple award winning: “Lauren’s Guide To Loving Your Car” with St. Martin Press. She is working on a fourth book with her daughter Shelby.

On Air broadcast experience: Oprah, The View, TODAY, The Early Show, CNN, FOX News, MSNBC, Headline News, TBS Makeover and a Movie, Inside Edition, ESPN, TBS, Discovery, Speed, and NPR.

Host of Talk 2 DIY Automotive, on the Do-It-Yourself Network (DIY) - 4th seasons. News inserts nationwide: Car Smarts and His Turn - Her Turn.

Member: Society of Automotive Engineers (SAE) since 1982; ASE (Automotive Service Excellence) certified technician.

She inherited her love of all things automotive from her father who owned a brake re-manufacturing business and many cars. Lauren has been fixing, restoring and racing cars since the age of 10. She has been advising drivers for almost all her life.

Lauren Fix’s Specialties:

Automotive consulting, keynote speaker, Author, media personality, and business consulting.
