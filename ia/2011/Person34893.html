Philippe Denis's Summary

Philippe Denis helps clients design and manage Web Analytics programs to accurately measure and optimize the performance of their digital strategies.

Having started his career 1999 in marketing agencies (Desjardins Bibeau, and then Groupe Everest), Philippe benefits from many years of experience as a communication and marketing professional, and dedicated the last few years to eBusiness, Web Analytics and online marketing.

Philippe also benefits from various experiences in public relation and social communications acquired through different mandates in Montreal and overseas.

As a partner at Nemo Solutions Interactives, he held the account director’s position and worked at business development, before moving to Telefilm Canada as Web Specialist.

Prior to joining Bell Web Solutions, Philippe Denis was Analyst, Marketing and eCommerce at Nurun, a global interactive marketing agency.

A communication sciences graduate from Université de Montréal, Philippe is currently completing post-graduate studies in eBusiness at l’École des Hautes Études Commerciales (HEC) Montreal.
