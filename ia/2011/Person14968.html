Forrest W. Kobayashi’s Summary

Forrest Kobayashi is currently a Strategist for JESS3, a creative interactive agency based out of Washington, DC. JESS3 has worked with a wide variety of clients including Nike, Intel, Google, Samsung and NASA.

Prior to taking on a strategist role, Forrest contributed to two major news organizations—the USA TODAY and The Washington Post.

In June 2010, he graduated from The Ohio State University with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Journalism with a second major in Sociology.
