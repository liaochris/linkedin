Marloes van der Werf MSc CMC's Summary

Marloes is a networker and connector with infectious energy and drive to perform. She is analytic, sincere & involved and her actions are full of direction and context. A coveted no-nonsense project leader or interim-manager in various branches and sectors.

Marloes is relationship-driven and easily connects with people throughout the organization. Her enthusiasm and persuasiveness inspire and motivate all people she works with. By putting trust in the people she works with, these people in return confide in her. This personal connection leads to successful professional cooperation and a measurable result.

Her interventions are aimed at uniting people and creating awareness. By asking the unasked questions and breaking down barriers, she qualifies as confidant to both board members and ‘blue collars’.
