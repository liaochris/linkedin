Dana Trombley's Summary

I started my career with the goal of being an editor, but after a year with the duel role of publicity assistant and editorial assistant, I realized publicity and promotion was my passion. After working as a traditional book publicist at Henry Holt concentrating on serious non fiction, pop culture, and paperback original fiction, I moved into a unique role, that of a almost entirely digital-based senior publicist, meaning my media landscape is the web. As the media world becomes more and more integrated with the web, so must publicity become based in the digital world. Especially for books--themselves turning digital at a rapid pace--and the authors who pen them.

My goals are simply to make the written word smoothly transition into the digital world, and to take away the mythology that the internet is confusing or that social media is something just for "the kids." Nobody should be kept in the dark!

Specialties

SEO content, digital publicity, social media coordination, project management, blogging, communication with authors, agents, and anyone who falls under the umbrella of "new media." eBook promotion, social media tutoring for authors and publishing professionals
