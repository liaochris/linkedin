Jack Schwartz’s Summary

A seasoned hands-on senior technology executive who has served several tours in technology start-ups in C-level and VP level roles as technology innovator, strategist, architect, team builder, product manager, educator, mentor, and principal software developer, specializing in the design and development of web and mobile enterprise products.

Expert in:
* Mobile Field Force Enterprise Architecture and Solution Products
* ESB (Enterprise Service Bus) Architecture
* Web 2.0 e-commerce (especially Magento)
* Web 2.0 wired and wireless architectures, tools and products
* Mobile and and online video and rich media orchestration, targeted advertising technology and architecture
* Intelligent wireless and Web remote presentation architectures, tools and products
* Interactive forms and database management tools and platforms
* Web and wireless enterprise development architectures and environments
* Asynchronous messaging architectures, protocols and products
* Web-based automated data acquisition architectures and products
* Workflow and process flow system automation technology
* Computer automated software engineering methodologies and products

As an innovator, conceived of numerous new methods, tools, products and solutions for every company for which employed. These innovations were used as the basis for technology differentiation leading to industry visibility, accelerated customer adoption, and venture funding.

As a hands-on software product development leader, headed and motivated numerous teams to deliver innovative, adaptable, scalable, and efficient software product solutions on time and within budget.

As a recognized and published expert in enterprise wireless and Web Internet software technology, have worked with major industry analysts to develop technology white papers and have served as a speaker on technology conference panels.
