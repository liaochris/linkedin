Zaixian Pan's Experience

Director, Head - Financial Services & Legal

Robert Walters

Public Company; RWA.L; Staffing and Recruiting industry

2003 – Present (8 years)

Pan Zaixian comes from a Big 4 Accountany background in London where he trained with the Institute of Chartered Accountants in England and Wales and specialised in the financial services before joining RW in 2003.

At RW, he works with Global and Asian institutions in close collaboration, project managing hirings as well as select search assignments.

In 2007, Zaixian managed the next phase of change at RW by restructuring its traditional generalist model into a specialist domain structure. He now oversees a multi-award winning and the largest specialised financial services team in Singapore.

- Front Office:

Sales & Trading, Investment Banking, Wealth Management, SME, Corporate Banking / Sales

- Middle-Back Office:

Finance, Operations, Governance, Risk, Projects

- Information Technology:

Application, Infrastructure, Projects

His key duties include working closely with RW global offices to develop strong relationships in the financial services market, and partnering institutions in advising resource strategies.

During his time managing the business, his teams have garnered the following awards.

- Asiamoney Headhunters Poll - Ranked #1, Banking Middle/Back Office in Asia (2010, 2008)

- The Asian Banker Achievement Award – Executive Search to the Commercial Banking Industry (2010, 2009, 2008)

- Singapore Human Resources Vendors of the Year Award (Financial Services) - Ranked #1, High & Mid End Roles (2011)

In 2010, he is further tasked with expanding the Singapore Legal business across both private practice and in-house.

Zaixian graduated with double honours in Accounting and Finance from the University of Manchester, UK.

In his earlier days, Zaixian enjoyed a hectic curriculum working with various organisational bodies. He enjoys reading biographies of personalities past and present.
