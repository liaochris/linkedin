Shawn Eyestone's Summary

I focus my practice in the areas of estate planning, small business and real estate law. I have helped many families and business owners with both simple and complex estate planning, including wills, trusts, family limited liability companies, business and cottage succession planning, among others. I have assisted numerous small to mid-sized businesses, including many healthcare professionals and facilities, regarding entity selection, formation, admission of new owners, reorganization dissolution, and succession planning. I regularly advise my clients regarding general business operational matters, contract negotiations, real
estate and equipment transactions, litigation avoidance, and regulatory compliance.

I have also developed expertise in the areas of Stark, Anti-kickback, HIPAA, Medicare and Medicaid reimbursement and other healthcare regulatory compliance issues. Additionally, he has significant experience in liquor license transfers and compliance with Liquor Control Commission rules and regulations.

At Eyestone Law Offices, PLC, we help entrepreneurs reduce risk and maximize profits throughout the life cycle of their businesses. We take a similar businesslike approach to guiding families in their personal and financial planning. Our business, financial and estate planning services are designed to ensure that your life’s work continues to provide for you and your family even after you no longer can.

Each solution begins and ends with you. Every business and personal situation is unique, so before charging you a cent or suggesting any course of action, we make sure we understand you, your business and your family and what you really are looking to accomplish. With that information and our knowledge of the law, we help you design a plan to make your life’s business work for you. We then continue to help implement that plan and make the necessary changes along the way when, as they say in the insurance commercial, “life happens.”

We pride ourselves on charging only for the value we provide and not just for the time spent. We work closely with you and your other advisors to develop cost-conscious and scalable solutions designed to address your needs both now and into the future.
