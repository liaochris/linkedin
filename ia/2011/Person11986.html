dinny lansdowne’s Summary

I've been a creative, socially and environmentally responsible entrepreneur for over 20 years. I am also a designer and artist. And a life long spiritual and personal growth student.

While building an international eco-fashion company my media coverage and awards led to opportunities to do brand and business consulting for other companies. So, while pursuing my own ventures, I was the "behind the scenes" consultant for several exciting success stories and charity events.

I am passionate about working with heart-centered change agents to design a profitable life style business that both showcases their core gifts and contributes to positive change.

Having worked with 1000s of customers and entrepreneurs, I have consistently heard an authentic, deeply felt desire for guidance. A call for assistance in truly stepping into one's confidence, in knowing that it is possible to align spiritual and creative expression with making a fabulous living - and contributing to a positive future too!

How do we get to be aligned, living our values, happy and financially successful too? It takes many people...and as one of my favorite brand mavens says "we don't have to try to do it alone anymore."

I haven't...I've been fortunate to not only be curious and focused but also to be encouraged by, study with, be coached by and learn from amazing creative, spiritual and business mentors around the world. And some of the most impactful shifts have come from truly connecting with my peers.

Bringing it all together, it's my intention to co-create a community where conscious entrepreneurs can support, train, promote and inspire each other. Where people at all business stages can find genuine encouragement, masterminds, mentoring and deepen relationships. A place where collaborating will allow everyone to up level their influence and impact in the world. And have fun!

Do we share a vision for positive co-creation and change?
