Peg Marckworth's Summary

When you’re branded correctly, competition is irrelevant.

Most people leave their brand to chance — or think their logo is their brand.
Peg Marckworth, through her company Marckworth Associates, works with individuals & businesses who want to build a resonant, memorable brand that attracts attention, engages interest & drives sales.

To develop their brand, Peg works with clients from the inside—out by finding what makes them excited, passionate and happy instead of imposing a contrived identity on them (an all too-common approach to branding). That means their brand is authentic. It's a perfect match, so it won't fizzle out in a year or two. It's enduring --- like them and their business.

Peg knows that if you don’t brand yourself, someone will do it first — & they may not be right. A strong, understandable brand:
1) Sets you apart from your competition,
2) Gives you direction for growing your business faster and bigger,
3) Helps you advance in your career,
4) Makes you the obvious choice to your ideal customer,
5) Positions you for financial, personal, and professional success.

Peg has a 20+-year career in communications. She has worked with corporations, non-profit organizations and government agencies. She is a former principal and owner of Muir Public Relations, a company that won awards for its national public relations campaigns and its community relations work in neighborhoods. She is a former Washington State lobbyist for family and children’s services issues, social service manager and family therapist.

Peg serves on the Board of Directors for the Association for Creative Business Writing and The Dunn Gardens. She is Vice President of Northern Lights Northwest and is an Advisory Board member for Next Stage Dance Theatre and the Cascade Symphony Orchestra. She is the past Board Chair for Billings Middle School.

Specialties

Personal Brand Identity Development, Corporate and Product Branding, Marketing Consultation, Message Development
