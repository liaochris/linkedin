J.R. Massa's Summary

I am an experienced entrepreneur who has founded and developed several technology-based companies with successful exits.

I founded BARON HOLDINGS in 2006 to manage my private equity investments and to serve as a tool for infusing cash flow into my new business ventures.

Currently, I am overseeing the buildup of BARON's 3rd company, TRUSTED TECHNICAL ADVISORS (TTA). I founded TTA to put control of technology operations back into the hands of business leaders.

TTA's mission is to remove the pains associated with technology decisions to ensure our clients can concentrate their time, money and resources on what is most important to them - the core focus of their business.

BENEFITS:
• CIO/CTO Services: Critical CIO/CTO services for less money than a salaried employee.

• Business Perspective: Solutions driven by business needs, not technology.

• Proactive Philosophy: IT best practices and best of breed managed services to prevent costly “surprise” support issues.

• Predictability And Stability: Manage, forecast and stabilize technology expenditures.

• Agnostic: TTA does not resell services or hardware/software of any kind.
