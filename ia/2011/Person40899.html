Jason Hargrove’s Summary

==============================================

+ + What I've Recently Done

In 2007 I made a growth decision in which I would close my advertising company in Vancouver, BC to embark on a refinement of my career. The past three years were one part sabbatical, one part hard work, and one part incredible journey. I chose Toronto, ON as my base for this period.

My focus project in Toronto was a technology framework that evolves user interfacing in desktop and mobile browsers. In early 2011 I'll release a great portion of my work to the open development and design community, as well as web services that allow non-developers to work with it. The primary service also allows experienced designers and developers expedite creation faster.

+ + What My Focus is Today

Using my focus technologies I am working on a number of standalone applications that I have sole equity in. These apps are varied in nature; generally influenced by my history in brand development as well as my interest in the Internet as a medium for collaboration.

Meanwhile I'm digging in with projects that are bigger than myself. Through the end of January I'm in a heavy production rotation working on an application with a group of medical doctors based in Houston, TX. We're setting out to do our part to help push forward positive change in US Healthcare. Our work is based on, and extends, the focus technologies referenced above.

Beyond this I'm accepting small projects that make use of my freelance services. I am well suited to work with venture-oriented startups who have a market-based glass ceiling they need to push through. I am available in the roles of Creative Director, Art Director and Senior Designer with specialization in brand strategy. I also bring a body of experience as a product-oriented Internet developer and of course am highly motivated to introduce and integrate my focus technologies where it makes sense.

For more information visit: http://jasonhargrove.com
