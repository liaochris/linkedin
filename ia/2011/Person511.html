Adam Law's Summary

Here is my professional blurb -

Adam is a registered Australian patent and trade marks attorney. He has first class honours in both Mechanical Engineering and Physics. He is a Fellow of the Institute of Patent and Trade Mark Attorneys of Australia and a New Zealand Patent Attorney. His areas of expertise include preparing, and filing patent applications in Australia and abroad for mechanical, software, business method, electronic, optical and telecommunications inventions. His areas of expertise include patents, designs and trade marks. Adam is completing a law degree.

On a social side -

I enjoy playing golf, tennis and squash. I also enjoy developing the odd invention myself, although this usually involves my being locked away in my garage.
