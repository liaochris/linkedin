Tony Huang’s Summary

Tony Huang | Innovations Strategist
Tony@TechExecs.Net | 866.661.0258

As well recognized "Process and Technology Strategist", Tony clients, mainly CIOs and CEOs, have engaged him in assisting them in facilitating the discovery of new breakthroughs, formulating new possibilities & product offerings and using best practices & technologies to positively alter the ways they do business. Mr. Huang has a solid & proven track record for having started and sold four profitable business ventures including taking one of his ventures public. He also served as the "rented Strategic CIO" for two medical ventures.

His clientele has included numerous multi-billion dollar enterprises including National Semiconductor eAdvisor Project, Sysco Foods QA Specifications, Office Depot Tax Automation, Shell TechVentures BlueGraf, Morgan Stanley Managed Futures, Koch Industries’ ERM - Enterprise Relationships Management system across seven multi-billion dollar Business Units, Spectra Energy Data Warehouse and many others. Mr. Huang began his career with Apple Computer, BSG and Ernst & Young and was a recipient of the Entrepreneur of the Year Award in 1998 by The Greater Houston Partnership. He participated and served as an Energy Alliance Venture Advisor to Prospect Street Ventures New York, and has been a speaker in numerous conferences including InformationWeek, Red Herring, Gartner Midsize Enterprise Summit, TiE Venture Forum, TechExecs CIO Panel Forums, and JavaOne. Mr. Huang graduated from the University of Texas at Austin with a BBA in Management Information Systems and attended Executive Management Trainings for Technology Innovations & Management at MIT.
