Kim Howard’s Summary

I have over 19 years of association publishing experience.
Prior to my work with ACC, I established my own company, Write Communications, where I provided strategic consulting and editorial writing assistance for individuals and organizations. I have held leadership positions in the publishing industry, senior-level management positions for national and international trade associations. I have provided commentary and insight for industry trade magazines, consumer publications and online forums. Throughout my career, I have published at least three million words, covering industries and topics that include association publishing, multifamily housing; cleaning and restoration; and business credit. I served as the editor of three magazines before joining ACC.
