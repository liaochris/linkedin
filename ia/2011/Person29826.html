Michael Fortson's Summary

Product design, with a focus on radical simplicity and creating habitual usage, combined with a deep background in full-cycle development (web and mobile), from rapid prototyping through multi-million-user scalable deployments.

Recently helped develop and direct Qik's consumer product from initial concept stages through breakout success, providing simple personal video sharing with friends and family including phone to networks (Facebook, Twitter), phone to desktop, and phone to phone (live and asynchronous). The Qik service is now built-in to the HTC Sprint Evo and is a major focus of Sprint’s 4G ad campaign. It has also featured heavily in Verizon advertisements.

Other recent contracting work includes mobile product design and development (iPhone & Android HTML5), location-based service design, social network sharing, and the technical design and development of a Rails-based consumer service for a mobile coupon startup.

Other highlights:

• 2010: Placed and held an iPhone video application in the top 3 (paid, US) in the iTunes app store (product management, UI design, advertising, creative)
• 2007: Launch partner for Facebook Platform for 3 of Facebook's first apps
• 2006: "Inny" Award and long-term exhibit in the Tech Museum of Innovation in San Jose
• 2005: DEMOGod-winning voice communication service
• Multi-role veteran of several startups (founder or first hire)

Specialties

product design, prototyping, product management, startups, mobile, live video, audio, mobile advertising, location-based services, company- and team-building
