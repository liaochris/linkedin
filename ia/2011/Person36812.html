Rich Mayes's Summary

Rich has enjoyed 20 years of direct selling through retail establishments as well as in-home. He has also enjoyed 18 years of direct selling through distribution networks, a.k.a. network marketing. He attributes his network marketing experience with teaching him how to move people, not just products.

Over the last few years (due to life events) Rich has been drawn to learn how to focus on the relationship with one's self, as a basis for all other relationships. He continued his personal growth work with King Solutions, Inc., and now shares his discoveries with others.

Rich is an intense seeker, a born uplifter - and a true teacher, who enjoys: empowering people beyond the physical, offering outstanding products/services, and building teams of independent entrepreneurs.
