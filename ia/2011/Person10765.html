David Ganulin's Summary

Through an ongoing, always evolving curriculum of kettlebell instructor training courses, I help club owners see a quick ROI with the implementation of a KB training profit center at their facilities. KBC provides courses geared toward both group exercise instructors and personal trainers.

My amazing staff of Senior Instructors provide properly credentialed, highly trained fitness professionals with the continued education credits (CEC's) they need to not only to keep their certifications current, but get them started in learning the kettlebell modality safely and effectively so they can bring it to their clients (and/or group classes) when and if the time is appropriate. We also provide other opportunities to allow them to evolve as a fitness professional through webinars, follow up articles, newsletters, a digital magazine (pending) and more.

More than just the "moves" and the "techniques" we ensure that the professionals that attend our courses know the "why's" behind the "how's." My formal background is in education and doing this any other way is simply unacceptable. My senior staff have impeccable credentials and feels as passionate about high-level education as I do.

I am a seasoned marketing, sales and PR professional with a strong entrepreneurial spirit with extensive experience in the health and wellness/fitness arena. I am strong in media relations, a seasoned public speaker and have strong skills in email marketing, copy writing, and event planning as well. (KettleBell Concepts founded KBC Galvanized. http://www.kbcconvention.com)
