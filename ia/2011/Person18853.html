James Mathewson’s Summary

* I am a proven leader of large organizational content teams. My legacy in these positions is to improve systems and processes in order to ensure higher content quality and better search engine engagement.

* As the Search Strategy Lead for IBM Marketing and Communications, my job is to help the organization develop a vision for future content efforts that enables it to optimize its myriad Web content for search engines such as Google. The role also includes SEO training and enablement of IBM content teams globally.

* I published a book with co-authors Frank Donatone and Cynthia Fishel called "Audience, Relevance and Search: Targeting Web Audiences with Relevant Content" for Pearson's private label IBM Press. The book is available in stores and Amazon.com.

* My career goal at IBM is to continue to lead search, social media and content quality efforts for organizations engaged in high-volume Web publishing operations.
