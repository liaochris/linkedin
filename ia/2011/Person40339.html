Siddharth Raja's Summary

Siddharth has extensive mergers & acquisition experience (both cross border & domestic Indian) as well as expertise in corporate finance, investments and private equity and general commercial / corporate law. Chambers & Partners 2009 rates Siddharth in Band 1 in India for private equity lawyers.

Along with two other colleagues from Law School, who like him were homecoming Indian lawyers, Siddharth founded Narasappa, Doraswamy & Raja in late 2005.

The Firm’s founding partners have worked in various legal and financial capitals of the world including London, Boston, Mumbai, Hong Kong and New Delhi. The Firm's principals have experience of working with the some of the best corporations and financial institutions in the world in different jurisdictions on transactions that were path breaking and innovative.

The Firm’s lawyers count among their clients some of the best and largest Indian and multinational corporations. We have also advised Indian corporations on their activities outside India, including acquisitions and operations in the United States of America, Europe and China

Prior to starting the Firm, Siddharth was a partner at one of India’s leading law firms headquartered in Mumbai. Siddharth headed the Bangalore and Chennai offices of this pan-India law firm.

Before relocating back to India, Siddharth was an associate at the reputed international law firm, O’Melveny & Myers LLP based at that firm’s Hong Kong office. Siddharth specialized in cross-border M&A. Siddharth has also acquired specialized experience in advising leading Indian corporates with their business acquisitions and market-entry strategies in China.

Siddharth has been a visiting faculty member at the Department of Management Studies of the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore. He is currently one of the Consulting Editors for "Business Law Reports" published by Manupatra. Siddharth is admitted to practice law in India and in England & Wales.
