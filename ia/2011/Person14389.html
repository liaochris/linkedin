Ev Nucci's Summary

Ev Nucci has spent three decades building high performance organizations. For the last fifteen years she spent working with bulge bracket institutional asset management firms, pension funds, hedge funds and FOF.

Professional highlights:

* Worked closely with the founders of BlackRock for years.
* Founded a company which she sold five years later to the industry giant.
Worked on two start-ups for Johnson & Johnson, ran 15 operating companies.
* Started a division for Baxter where she was responsible for managing a team that generated $10 million in annualized revenue in less than 11 months.
