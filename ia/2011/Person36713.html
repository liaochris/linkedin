Richard Arghiris’s Summary

I have contributed to a diverse range of printed publications, including British newspapers like The Independent and The Observer, as well as numerous websites specialising in narrative travel stories, such as the award-winning Perceptive Travel.

I have also co-authored or otherwise contributed to several best-selling guidebooks, including Footprint Handbooks to Mexico (2nd ed), Nicaragua (3rd ed), Costa Rica, Nicaragua and Panama (1st ed) and Central America (17th, 18th, 19th ed). I have also worked with Rough Guides and DK.

Over 2010-2011, I will be revising two titles for which I already have co-author credit. As a minimum, two completely new books are also in the works - one is a commissioned travel guide, the other is a first novel. Additionally, I will continue developing my travel blog, Interamericana, which went on-line in 2009.
