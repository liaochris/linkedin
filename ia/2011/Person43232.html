Philip Illum Thonbo's Summary

I started using Photoshop back in 1999, while doing abit Flash on the side. the first few years was mainly personal/friends Flash/HTML sites including the on i have on thonbo.com now - later on while getting some real flash gigs I started coding alot of "self tought" actionscript, back then it was still in transition from actionscript 1 to 2. In the time between 2002 and 2005 i had more and more freelance work for some larger scale clients such as Deducta, Kath Art and Great Danes, and in late december 2005 I started at Emerge(now Wunderman) - the jump from working freelance to fulltime was a major boost for me, getting my hands on some best practise actionscript, but I also became aware that 100% coding was not my thing, so I saw my path in animation, designed and scripted animation and interactivity - the visual and creative part of flash coding quickly became my thing... around 2008 when i started at Molamil.com I became aware of the limitation of flash, my request for quality and performace in flash pushed me to aftereffects, wich gave me an eyeopener for a more visual way of designing animations. I started using AfterEffects to build video effects for flash - wich later on evolved into more complete video compositions - but my urge for more led my way into 3D and 3DS Max - this gave me the tool i needed to form anything from nothing, enriching my flash with 3D from either Papervision3D/Away3D or video sequences. i am now at a point where i feel the circle is complete, and i constantly work to improve both my flash, aftereffects and 3d skills.

the future for me will definently be somewhere in 3D - some amazing 3D engines comming just around the corner for browser experiences both with Flash Molehill, Unity3D and WebGL - but digging into some more heavy 3D modules such as VRAY, fumeFx, realflow and rayfire will hopefully open some doors to the Motion Graphics business
