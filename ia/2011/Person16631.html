Greg Snyder's Summary

Executive with broad management and business experience in small start-ups, publicly traded corporations, and non-profits. New media forms, social media, podcasting, niche advertising networks, sales and marketing team management, advertising sales, and business administration are central to my experience in the for-profit sector. In my non-profit roles, my experience has been in non-profit administration, human rights advocacy and research, international affairs, government engagement on human rights issues, conflict transformation and peacebuilding, and the organization of government training projects. I have also worked in the non-profit religious sector as Cofounder, Zen Buddhist Priest and Director of the Brooklyn Zen Center in Brooklyn, NY, and the Education Director of the Austin Zen Center in Austin, TX.
