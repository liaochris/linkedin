Neil Gale's Summary

Let me introduce myself, my name is Neil Gale. I am a Business and Internet Consultant and own the firm; DrGale.com. This is my third Business Consulting Firm since 1988. I moved from Chicago, Illinois to St. Louis, Missouri going on three years now.

One of our specialties is the setup or reengineering of Business Development Centers and/or Internet Department for retailers and car dealerships. We provide lead generation and lead management functions along with the training to help the client become self-supportive. We also specialize in website design, development and marketing from simple brochure-ware sites to very complex websites including eCommerce. We manage web projects from developing an eBusiness strategy through website implementation and beyond.

I personally have been involved with web development since the images first came out on the Internet in 1992. I possess expert skill with conventional and online marketing, Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Search Engine Marketing (SEM), pay-per-click (PPC), lead generation, Mobile Marketing, campaign management, corporate/business branding, and social media for B2C and B2B organizations.

Most of my clients are in a variety of industries and include businesses of all sizes. I assist a lot of startups (my favorite) and many Fortune 500 companies, including Monsanto, AT&T, Lucent Technologies, GE Aircraft Engines, Sun Microsystems, Montgomery Ward and dozens of other midsized organizations. I’ve been lucky in working with some of the biggest and best car dealer groups in the St Louis area like the Dave Sinclair Automotive Group, Frank Leta Group and Newbold Toyota BMW.

Additionally, I am a professional photographer. I started at 11 years old and won an Honorable Mention (400+ participants) that year in competition. Since then I have won International Photography Awards and have had numerous photographs published and on TV including CNN and NBC.

I’d like to learn about your business. Let's Connect.

Owner

Everything Internet

Internet industry

1996 – 1998 (2 years)

Everything Internet designed complex, interactive and e-commerce websites. We provided high-level sales and marketing services, both online and conventional, for Fortune 500 companies and numerous small to mid-sized organizations. Services included the development and execution of e-Business plans, the state-of-the-art website design and development of online shopping cart applications, live, online appointment scheduling applications and Search engine placement (now known as SEM).

Everything Internet also produced graphic art, images and photography services to complement our website designs, making us one of the few full-service Internet companies able to produce the client’s ideas from concept through fruition.

We taught many companies to become self-supportive by training employees to make text changes to their websites without any outside intervention. Some organizations were instructed in graphic art and imaging software allowing them to change photos and images on the website. This provided the client with the ability to market the “flavor of the week” on the fly.

I sold the company three months before the “Dot Com Crash” in 1998.

Founder & President

National Consumer Credit Consultants

Management Consulting industry

1988 – 1996 (8 years)

In January of 1988 I struck out on my own and I opened a Business Consulting firm; National Consumer Credit Consultants (NCCC) in Chicago.

National Consumer Credit Consultants provided professional assistance and business consulting services to the consumer finance, credit and collections industry. Our business emphasis was in automobile funding, “buy-here pay-here” financing and loan collections. Our client base consisted of banks, credit unions, mortgage brokers, direct and indirect lenders, automobile dealerships, retail merchants, sales finance companies, high-risk lenders and collection agencies. We also offered Mortgage services for loan origination, application processing, Mortgage closing, portfolio management and wholesale portfolio sales. Clientele included companies like Fidelity Funding, American Auto Funding Corp, Telecheck, World Book, First Horizon Money Center, Inc., and many other companies.

Assistance services were provided for organizational planning, lending structure and policy, lending compliance with Sates level regulations, new product development, capital risk management, branch level operations, funding sales, credit analysis, account collections (commercial and consumer) and skip tracing.

National Consumer Credit Consultants installed, managed and trained lending institutions effectively utilize computer systems such as LAN’s and WAN’s to mitigate capital risk via computerized scoring applications for loan approvals. For collections firms, we installed auto-dialer and predictive-dialer applications for collection call centers and attorney groups.

I sold the company in 1996 after opening a new Internet Consulting Firm; Everything Internet.

Middle & Upper Management Positions

Consumer Finance Career

Financial Services industry

1980 – 1988 (8 years)

My professional career began in 1980 in the Consumer Finance Industry as a Manager Trainee for General Finance Corporation and progressed rapidly to a certified Branch Manager within nine months from the two year training program.

In 1984 I joined the Midland Finance Company of Chicago as their Marketing Manager developing new business from retailers and car dealerships for secondary finance funding.

I served as the Director of Marketing and the Loan Committee Chairman for the Diamond Mortgage Corporation from 1985 to 1986.

In 1987 I accepted a Senior Financial Analyst position for McKay Mazda-Nissan in Evanston, Illinois, where I successfully opened and supervised the “Oxford Bond and Investment Company”, an off-site "buy-here pay-here" finance company for high-risk car loans.
