Susan Mangiero, Ph.D., CFA, FRM's Summary

I am a seasoned business development professional with experience in areas that include investment risk management, derivatives, fiduciary best practices, hedge funds, prudence, due diligence, valuation and asset-liability management.

You can read my insights about investment best practices and risk gaps by visiting my two blogs: www.pensionriskmatters.com and www.goodriskgovernancepays.com.

I currently provide expert witness testimony, behind-the-scenes forensic analysis and due diligence/compliance consulting on investment and risk management issues.

I founded and raised nearly $2 million for a web-enabled educational resource for pensions, endowments and foundations and their advisers and attorneys. I led the acquisition discussions with multinational publishing and business intelligence firms. I am the co-creator of a pension litigation database and the author of Risk Management for Pensions, Endowments and Foundations.

Earlier in my career, I worked on several bank trading desks (foreign exchange, futures, options, swaps, caps), provided risk management consulting to U.S. and international banks, insurance companies, asset managers and traders and created two separate client relationship groups with direct P&L responsibility. I have worked for two multinational corporate treasury departments and have helped to implement risk management systems, carry out pricing model reviews and analyze performance reports.

Along the way, I have developed and led in-person workshops and e-learning (online) courses for professionals learners such as financial attorneys, auditors, bankers, board members, treasurers, CFOs, regulators and pension, endowment and foundation decision-makers.

I strongly believe that significant business opportunities exist at this time of unprecedented financial market upheaval and welcome sharing my ideas with other solutions-oriented professionals.
