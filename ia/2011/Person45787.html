Sharon Ryan's Summary

Sharon has over 20 years experience working with organisations on a range of large, complex business and technology transformation programs. She is passionate about championing respect for individual and team values in order to transform to a new way of working. Her industry experience includes: not-for-profit, financial services, public sector, gaming and entertainment, utilities and mining.

Sharon’s pragmatic approach is complemented by post-graduate qualifications in Organisational Development, and current law studies (Juris Doctor) majoring in Negotiation and Mediation. She is also an alumnus of the Benevolent Society Social Leadership Program and the sole Australian certified in Trust methodology.

She is a board member of the Women and Girls Emergency Centre (WAGEC) in Sydney and an honorary board member of Oddfellows Playhouse (United States)

Specialties

Board Visioning Workshop (nfp), Leadership development; Talent Management, Capability Assessment, Cultural assessment, Change Strategy design and execution (culture analysis, change readiness analysis, business impact assessment); Communications Strategy design;Stakeholder analysis and stakeholder management; IT Operations; Program Director ($10m+); Project management, Change Management methods - applying and coaching; Program Auditor and Assurance ($43m+);
