Christian Gammill's Summary

My passion is helping people grow businesses. I have a unique breadth of experiences ranging from helping build a small pharmaceutical company to helping market a local burger joint to leading the brand management practice globally for the worlds second most valuable brand. I'm now focused on bringing all my experience together to help business grow with the aid of social, mobile and other technologies. At SocialGuides we are helping businesses participate in social media/networking with an eye on building strong customer relationships and competitive brands/reputations. The race is on to 'own' the mindshare of the social customer and we want to help businesses win!

I've spoken on topics like Customer Driven Innovation, Customer Experience Management, Marketing Analytics & Social Media for Business at MIT, Michigan, Harvard & UCLA events and at conferences from LA to SF to NY to Lisbon. I love leading and participating in meaningful dialogue as long as the goal is helping people grow their businesses...

Ping me on twitter (@gammill) if you want to chat…
