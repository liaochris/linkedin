Lisa Tweedie's Summary

I have 20 years experience in HCI in both interaction design and usability testing. My early work was in low level experimental psychology on icon design. I then moved into much higher level exploration of visualisation and how external representations work. I did a PhD and Post Doc at Imperial in this field and then decided to get some practical experience. I then had six years experience in the software industry. I acquired two visualisation patents during a brief spell as an interaction designer at Nortel Networks. I then worked at Oracle on their JDeveloper UML Modellers.

In recent years to achieve a more comfortable work/life balance I have decided to embark on the world of consulting. This has enabled me to put my many skills to work. I am particularly interested in social and educational technology. I have been lucky to work with some great clients - dunnhumby to review current practice in their visual business analysis tools, Summit Media - identifying requirements and specifying e-commerce portals, porge - public sector IT research, i-board - management of the development of open ended whiteboard literacy games and Phonicbooks - website development and technical advice.

Specialties

Business Intelligence, Visualisation, Educational Technology, Interaction Design, Usability testing, quick and dirty usability testing, Social Technology, Whiteboards, Market Analysis
