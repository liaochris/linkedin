John Hogie’s Summary

I have over twenty years of experience executing projects, large and small, including CRM, CSS, KM, ERP and Software Development for the Healthcare, Manufacturing, and Software industries. By actively participating in requirements, design and solutions delivery, I have always maintained a focus on developing business and technology strategies providing clients in North America, Europe and Asia with increased profitability and client satisfaction.

I have designed and implemented CRM and SFA solutions for mobile sales teams at Fortune 100 organizations. In the case of one custom manufacturing client, the time from the initial customer requirements gathering to generation of a proposal with full engineering review and specifications was reduced from 5 months to three hours.

One of my most recent projects was the design and development of an organizational performance management system for a nationally known consultancy. The application was designed to be used via a zero client or on mobile handsets; BlackBerry, Windows Mobile, Palm and Symbian operating systems.

I designed an integrated content management system for a specialty manufacturing company. The system provides better customer service and support and will facilitate international growth without increased headcount. The system allows full text searches of engineering, accounting, sales and marketing content.

I have designed and implemented customer loyalty and marketing effectiveness analytics systems for Global 500 organizations.
