Adam Blackie's Summary

I am a career Interim Manager who leads IT delivery teams through their change programmes. I work with CEO’s and their Boards in the UK to change the way technology is used by staff and customers; and the way internal services are delivered.

I work with a wide range of clients in the Legal, Social Housing, Charity and Local and Central Government sectors. These range in size from 50 to 5000 employees.

I add value to change projects by reducing costs, initiating projects, securing stakeholder buy-in, developing new ways of working and managing service transformation.

I am also a visiting lecturer on change management at University College London and am a guest speaker at The European Association for Banking and Financial History Summer School.

For a flavour of what I do see my Blog. – http://adamblackie.wordpress.com

I am an Interim Head of IT, CIO and Change Manager. If you have a project where I can add value call +44(0)7941 270 640 or email a.blackie@websmartgroup.co.uk
