Derek Whyte’s Summary

Derek Whyte is the VP of Operations at FCV. Derek formed and runs the company's Project Management Office (PMO) responsible for designing and implementing best practices in project management. With over 15 years of experience, Derek has led a number of large projects at FCV for Nike, TransLink, Rick Hansen Foundation, Save-On-Foods, TELUS, Tourism Yukon, Tourism Jasper, and the Canadian Tourism Commission. Previously, Derek worked as Senior Project Manager at Blast Radius responsible for successful project delivery for clients such as Regence, Intrawest, EA, Nike and Brand Jordan. Prior to joining Blast Radius, Derek spent eight years as Director of Development for Stockgroup responsible for product development and delivery of the company’s financial portal tools for clients including National Bank Financial, Credential Direct, Sun Life Financial, the Toronto Stock Exchange, and the Associated Press. Derek holds a Computer Information Systems degree from the University College of the Fraser Valley.
