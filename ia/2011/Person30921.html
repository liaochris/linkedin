Ming Chan’s Summary

As founder and CEO of The1stMovement, Ming understands digital media technology in a way rarely seen in the industry today. Named as one of the “Top 10 Asian Entrepreneurs” for 2010 by Inc. Magazine, Ming has led the agency to numerous accolades with his passion for innovation and hands-on style. These honors include the “Inc. 500 Fastest Growing Private Companies," "Best Places to Work in LA," the ADDY, the Webby, the Stevie, Communications Arts, Horizon Interactive and many more. As the recipient of the prestigious Emmy® award in Interactive Media, Ming's created innovative digital assignments for some of the world's most renowned brands: 20th Century Fox, Adobe, Apple, AMD, Cisco, Disney, Microsoft, Nike, HP, Intel, Warner Bros. and Universal Pictures, a client list that could be found only in the hopes and dreams of many aspiring entrepreneurs and large companies alike.

Starting his career as a software engineer in the Silicon Valley, Ming navigated his way through the broad landscape of software and web development after graduating from Occidental College. Prior to founding The1stMovement in 2006, he served as the Technical Director of Flash Platform for Design Reactor, a leading digital marketing agency. There he helped establish the agency as one of the premiere Flash design/development partners for Fortune 100 companies such as Apple, Cisco, Disney and HP while receiving a variety of industry awards including FWA, Omni, KeyArts and New York Festivals Advertising Awards.

An active member of the Los Angeles Chapter of the Entrepreneurs’ Organization, Ming serves as a proactive advocate for entrepreneurs everywhere. He’s active in both the local and national Interactive community and currently serves as a mentor in the AFI's Digital Content Lab and is an Interactive member for the National Academy of Television Arts & Science.
