Cheryl Rhody's Summary

Marketing, public relations and communication specialist with direct and diverse experience ranging from integrated marketing communication, branding and results-driven public relations to sales support and digital/Web marketing.

I began my career as an account executive at MediaReach Public Relations where I was responsible for the creation and execution of external communication programs for a diverse collection of client accounts in various sectors including, financial services, professional services, national non-profits and issues-awareness and education programs.

I currently works as a public relations and marketing specialist at strategic workforce solutions company SFN Group, Inc. and its family of specialty brands: Mergis Group, Todays Office Professionals, Spherion Staffing, Technisource and SourceRight Solutions. I have been instrumental in the development and implementation of comprehensive corporate and candidate media relations campaigns and messaging for top executives. I also serve as a media relations contact, company spokesperson and crisis communication liaison.

I am responsible for developing thought leadership platforms to increase brand awareness among the company’s business units and works to promote internal and external brand equity through practical, integrated programs that are strategically aligned with business objectives and impeccably executed.

I possess a keen understanding of the latest business, economic and workforce trends. I have superior writing, editing and communication skills. I am equipped with a strong ability to make sound judgment under deadline and the ability to prioritize between projects.
