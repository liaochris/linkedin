Tim Huebsch’s Summary

I am a community dot connector! I have been involved in numerous organizations and am constantly looking to help individuals and organizations connect with each other. My past experiences have varied widely from serving as Principal for the Day at Nellie Stone Johnson Elementary School, teaching personal finance at a university in California, to collectively raising over $500,000 for Twin Cities charities and participating in the FBI Citizen’s Academy. These experiences have given me a great perspective and allowed me the opportunity to meet amazing community leaders and countless individuals who I have helped connect with each other.

I strongly believe in helping others figure out how to be involved in the community and encouraging them to take that first step. Annual I choose one thing to do that is completely unrelated to my regular job and these are the experiences that help make me successful within the community. My regular job is an IS Department Manager for General Mills where my team is responsible for the public internet platforms around website search, online communities and recipe systems. I am a past Humphrey Policy Fellow and Leadership Twin Cities participant. I received my MBA from Carlson School of Management at the University of Minnesota and my undergraduate degree in Computer Science from Minnesota State University, Mankato. I am currently the President of the Minnesota State University, Mankato Alumni Association which serves the 105,000 alumni of MSU.
