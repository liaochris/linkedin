Paul Boucher’s Summary

I have been a professional, bilingual narrator in one capacity or another since 1977. Clients include Microsoft, Showtime, BMW, National Geographic, Mercedes, Dell, NASA, Pratt & Whitney, GE, Harley Davidson, Met Life, Government of Canada, Blackberry (RIM), Accenture, CBC, and many many more.

Paul Boucher’s Specialties:

I excel in making e-Learning and corporate narrations as natural sounding as possible, including difficult technical and medical material. I also interpret documentary narrations with skill.
