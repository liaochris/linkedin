Jen Ware's Summary

I help small to medium sized businesses secure commercial space that fits their needs. My focus on local, small and start up businesses make my real estate services unique in the Charlotte, NC and Charlotte metro area. My goal is to be an extension of your company who serves as your commercial real estate department allowing you to focus on your core business and generating revenue instead of spending your valuable time driving around Charlotte calling signs and getting car sick! I will take an active interest in understanding your business operation and real estate requirements and combine that with relevant, up to date market knowledge and construction/build out knowledge to help you obtain the best location and terms available.
In 2008, I joined Alliance Commercial Real Estate and have successfully helped a variety of small and start up businesses negotiate and obtain excellent terms on their commercial space. Also, I have helped local landlords and developers lease out their commercial space to qualified tenants.
Prior to joining Alliance, I worked with Lincoln Harris in property management and First Colony Corporation as a broker representing landlords and developers. This particular background gives me an excellent understanding of how and why landlords structure their leases, while my property management background gives me an in depth understanding of the day to day issues facing a tenant once they have signed a lease and moved into commercial space.
I look forward to the opportunity to have fun working with you during your commercial space search!
