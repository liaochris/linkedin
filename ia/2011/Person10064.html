Daniel Lally's Summary

Daniel Lally is vice president at Pinger PR at Powers Agency and a co-founder of Cincinnati Social Media, a group of social media developers and users from across Ohio, Kentucky and Indiana. He also is a blogger, social media consultant and speaker on topics including public relations, crisis communication and social media.

A PR and brand strategy professional with more than 20 years experience in national media, agency, corporate and government practice, he was senior public relations consultant for Gyro/HSR where he was spokesman for the agency’s second-largest client, Hobart. He also provided communication and strategic counsel for clients in financial services, telecom, and energy; led crisis communication for organizations from durable goods manufacturing to call center operators.

As director of PR/marketing at luxury home products marketer Frontgate, Lally led the repositioning of the brand from niche retailer to lifestyle innovator. His efforts earned hundreds of millions of print impressions and placed products on major television shows and in big-studio Hollywood films. He appeared as a guest or presenter on network TV programs and was quoted in hundreds of newspaper and magazine features.

While at Frontgate, Lally led business development efforts with other premium brands to bring affluent homeowners to the company’s buyer file. For the last three years of his tenure, he was charged with the marketing of a new category at Frontgate, growing the segment from $2.5 million in net sales in 2001, to more than $30 million in 2003.

Cincinnati Social Media presents opportunities for professionals to share strategies and best practices for using social media in a business context. The group hosts local and national technology and application leaders at events throughout the region including Cincy Social Media Breakfast.

Dan can be found online at PowersAgency.com and at DanielLally.com. His Twitter handle is @DanielLally.
