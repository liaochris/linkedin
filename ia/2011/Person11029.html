David Seror's Summary

During my studies at EDHEC Business School, I developed a project threw 3 internships, in the clothing business (Façonnable) and diamond business (Farhi Diamonds).
These experiences brought me at my last internship, with PHB Group, a clothing industrial production based in Lithuania.
- We concretised an ambitious project, to develop a wedding between "jeans haute couture" and jewellery. The name of the brand is Franck Seror. We have about 15 specialty stores clients all over the world (Russia, Ukrainia, France, Islands) and we are actually developing new area of distribution. Every season our clients order more quantities, and new clients join us!

- That’s brought me at Façonnable, to develop a distribution for a new line of high-end beachwear worldwide threw, Travel retail specialty stores and department stores.
