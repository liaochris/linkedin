Isadora Gabrielle Leidenfrost's Summary

I seek to encourage people and to empower women. Inspired by multicultural motifs, I create documentary films, video productions, websites, and other multimedia about embedded culture. I do this to promote the under studied traditions throughout the world and I believe that by using current technology we can enhance our cross-cultural knowledge.

Specialties

film, video, textile history, website design, graphic design, PR, marketing, social media, and business management
