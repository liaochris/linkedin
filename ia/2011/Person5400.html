Brian Koppe’s Summary

I have work experience in fundraising, recruiting, and management for both political and business needs. Through the years, I have continued to cultivate my interpersonal skills. As field sales leader with Vector marketing I met individually with customers to determine their needs, then as assistant district manager I trained groups of sales representatives and met individually to help them improve performance. As field organizer with Dan Seals for Congress, I gave guidance to volunteers so that they would make an effective use of their time for the campaign, and as finance director with Wheelan for Congress I worked directly with donors to organize numerous fundraisers. Most recently, my work as campaign manager with Friends of John Burros led me to organize and guide a small campaign staff while also overseeing all public communications.

I enjoy working with people, and I strive to do work that benefits society in some way, whether that is by working for a political campaign, non-profit organization, or even for a company selling products which I believe in.
