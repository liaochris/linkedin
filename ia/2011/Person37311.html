Robert N Slinn’s Summary

A Chartered Chemist (CChem) and Member of the Royal Society of Chemistry (MRSC). I have extensive experience in R & D, synthesis, analysis and analytical methods development, troubleshooting, consultancy, and teaching and training methods in industry and in academe. Currently, I am a Visiting Researcher (University of Liverpool) and 'Physical Methods' author for the Specialist Periodical Report series 'Organophosphorus Chemistry' (RSC, Cambridge, UK)
http://www.rsc.org/publishing/ebooks/series.asp?seriesid=69

KEY SKILLS AND ACHIEVEMENTS

33 years laboratory experience (R & D, synthesis, analysis, consultancy).
Extensive experience in the analysis of foods, water, pharmaceuticals, medical gases, polymers, industrial solvents and environmental samples.
Effective in R & D, analytical methods development, troubleshooting.
Effective in the commissioning of new equipment and teaching and training methods in both the chemical industry and further / higher education sectors.
Current interest in organic spectroscopy data prediction (cheminformatics and chemometrics).

Robert N Slinn’s Specialties:

Chromatography and spectroscopy, with expertise in GC and GC-MS methods development.
