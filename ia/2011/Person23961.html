Keith Bourne's Summary

Keith has more than a decade of experience in building and launching large-scale social media and online marketing campaigns that have collectively grown communities by millions of users. He has in-depth knowledge of the technical aspects of digital media, while also fully grasping the marketing implications of these technologies.

In 2009, Keith founded MemCatch, a social media technology company that simplified the process of social media management for small/midsized businesses. Keith is also the Co-founder of the Michigan chapter of Mobile Monday and helps to organize both the Ann Arbor and Detroit chapters. Mobile Monday is an international organization with more than 70 chapters.

Keith is an avid social media and mobile speaker. Keith has also been published through articles, papers, and interviews in several magazines, journals and other media outlets on practical uses of social media, marketing in general, and mobile technologies. His blog can be found at http://keithbourne.com and you can follow him on Twitter @keithbourne.

Keith holds a BSBA from the University of North Carolina at Charlotte and an MBA from Babson College, the most renowned entrepreneurship program in the world.
