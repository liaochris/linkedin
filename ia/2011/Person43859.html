Tom Murphy's Summary

Founder of Aptus Personnel Pty Ltd and continuing as a recruitment consultant within consulting engineering.

After studying an associate diploma in Civil engineering Tom worked in Civil / Structural Engineering for almost 10 years with Scott Wilson Irwin Johnson (Irwinconsult) in Melbourne and Darwin.

In 2003 Tom realised his potential as a consultant by joining Bayside Personnel where he progressed his career as team leader. In Feb 2007 Tom joined Kingfisher recruitment to start their Engineering Recruitment group. He enjoyed his time there forging key relationships with long term engineering clients and candidates.

In 2009 Tom worked in the UK with Spring Engineering based in the South West of England. Starting a consulting engineering recruitment business for Spring in the middle of a recession was challenging however an enjoyable experience.

Currently Tom is working with Aptus Personnel Pty Ltd specialising in both permanent and contract recruitment within consulting engineering. We are a young company with many years experience among us. We are enjoying what we do... so far so good.

Tom enjoys working in a professional recruitment environment where he can consult rather than sell.
