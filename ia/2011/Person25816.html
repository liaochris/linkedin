Tom Turner’s Summary

Tom Turner Co-Founded LawTech P.I. in 2007, with a vision of providing the Professional Investigator with resources, publications, products and training. For over three decades, LawTech P. I.’s parent company, the LawTech Goup has produced the highest quality law enforcement related and criminal justice field reference publications, promotional study guides, legal and code reference manuals, as well as college textbooks for the professional investigator, SIU Investigator, law enforcement, police training, probation, parole, corrections, security and private and corporate investigations.

Tom is an author, lecturer and California licensed private investigator. He has addressed thousands across the United States through corporate programs, educational institutions, national trade and professional associations on identity theft investigation and prevention, investigative techniques and privacy issues. During his thirty plus years as an investigator, he has worked in law enforcement, was the senior investigator for TRW Information Services (now Experian) and owned a large private investigation agency in Orange County, California. He has investigated major fraud cases across the country and has successfully brought cases to prosecution and conviction with local and federal prosecutors. Tom is also on the board of directors of the High Five Foundation.
