Teri Mason’s Summary

Technology; It’s a double-edge sword. Sit still and it can knock you out of the ring, never reaching your potential. Engage and your technological tools can culminate into a type of “equalizer” when competing with the larger corporations.

As An Online Business Owner Think:

■PERFORMANCE;

Your site has to perform well in many aspects, the visual layout, ease of use or usability, reader scannability, your message or content, even font style.

■CONNECTION;

It's not just your website, it's your total online resources that you have available to you as a business owner and how they work together to connect to the online masses. This does include your website, along with your social media sites, and other online tools that can work synergistically in your favor.

■EXECUTION;

This pertains to your goals and how they translate across those connections into calls of action, landing pages, promotions, and retention. It's about helping your online user learn what they need to know and arrive at the solutions within your niche, providing specific direction in the best ways possible.

■TRANSFORMATION;

It's seeing the results of using our system for performance, connection, and execution. It's also having a plan of ongoing evaluation and measurement. Forget the experience of what most online business owners do - shooting hoops in the dark - this isn't about "playing business."


It's about strategy, measurement, and seeing your business grow, seeing it transform, in objective terms.
