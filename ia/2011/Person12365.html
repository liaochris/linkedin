Dori O'Dell’s Summary

Totally Chocolate specializes in creating unique and intricate designs in chocolate with our patented molding process. We import pure Belgian chocolate to our factory in Blaine, Washington where our art department takes your ideas and translates them to chocolate creations. We are OUD Kosher certified and our delicious premium milk and dark chocolate is unsurpassed in taste.
