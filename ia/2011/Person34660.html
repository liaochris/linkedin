Peter Abraham's Summary

Currently I'm a Director at Econsultancy.com, where we serve 100,000 digital marketers and e-commerce professionals (both client and agency side), helping people improve the way they do business online and to be successful. We pride ourselves in developing Digital Marketing Excellence. We believe the internet is the world’s biggest sandbox, chemistry set and battleground. If you're a Marketer or e-commerce professional you should check out http://econsultancy.com or drop me a line with your question.

I started as a graphic designer/art director working in various ad agencies, then got involved with the web in '97 as Digital Services Director at a leading Digital Agency in London, running teams of website designers and project managers offering digital marketing strategy, design and deployment for some very big names.
I have a BA Hons in Graphic Design (Typography) and an MSc in Digital Marketing. I also run various Linkedin groups and Twitter profiles.
