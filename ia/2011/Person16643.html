  • Managing Director - Stone Carlie Corporate Finance, L.L.C.

    (Privately Held; Accounting industry)

    2010 — Present (1 year )

    Stone Carlie Corporate Finance, L.L.C. (SCCF) assists clients with recapitalizations, mergers and acquisitions and financial restructuring. SCCF is the investment banking subsidiary of Stone Carlie, a St. Louis based professional services firm which also provides accounting, business consulting, outsourced CFO services and wealth management to corporate and individual clients.

    My focus at SCCF includes:

    Optimizing our clients' Amount and Cost of Capital through the proper mix of, for example, traditional bank debt, asset based lenders, mezzanine lenders and equity investors - There are funding options which may free up cash flow and cost less than current capital providers.

    Negotiating Mergers, Acquisitions and Strategic Partnerships which grow our clients' top line, bottom line and equity value and/or provide shareholders with liquidity.

    Recapitalizing clients, whether healthy or distressed, where a replacement is desired for the current bank lender.

    Assisting clients with financial restructuring:
    - Negotiating with current bank lenders where a client is in default or in the bank's workout group;
    - Raising the capital needed to support the management team's operational restructuring and turnaround - I work closely with the CEO,COO and CFO;
    - Finding and closing with the right strategic investor to strengthen the balance sheet;
    - Negotiating and closing a sale to a strategic or financial buyer;
    - Working to limit the liability of exisiting shareholders and maximize the value of their equity now and in the future.
