Frank Meijer's Summary

Drs. Frank Meijer started as a PhD-student at University of Twente on the project "Synthetic Environments", after his graduation in Cognitive Psychology in 2005. This project is one of the Innovation-focused Research Programmes (IOP) of SenterNovem, which is financed by the ministry of Economic Affairs. In close cooperation with various other universities and industrial partners, Frank investigates the feasibility of applying Virtual Reality (VR) for product development. Within this project, his primary interests are with processes of human cognition and vision in VR environments. Frank also works within the EU Network of Excellence INTUITION on VR. Further, he has conducted research on the relation between attention and visual working memory. In general, Frank's research always starts with experimental research after which findings are transferred to applied or technological frameworks.
