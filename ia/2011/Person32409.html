Rajat Bhatia's Experience

Founder & CEO

NEURAL CAPITAL

Financial Services industry

November 2005 – Present (5 years 5 months)

Founder & CEO of an entrepreneurial firm that specializes in developing quantitative trading strategies for the global financial markets using artificial intelligence techniques like neural networks and statistical arbitrage.

NEURAL CAPITAL has developed the SILVER BOX - a stat arb strategy - that has been particularly successful in trading all types of markets - Bull, Bear and Sideways markets.

The SILVER BOX had a positive return of 124% in 2008 when during a year when major investment banks like Bear Stearns and Lehman went bankrupt and many large hedge funds had negative returns that will take years to come back to their high water mark.

Between August 2004 and March 2005, the NEURAL TRADER made an annualized return of 65% and a 35% return from 2005 to 2006. Sharpe Ratio > 2.00 in live trading through the Susquehanna International Group, Philadelphia.

Neural Capital also works with early stage and growth stage firms seeking to raise venture capital capital and private equity. We partner with the Harvard Capital Group in Los Angeles to work on deals that we originate.

Our clients have included firms in the aerospace, construction technologies, fashion modeling, agricultural and food, media, refinery, advanced solar technologies and power.

We are also active in investments in distressed assets. We partner with LW Partners of Philadelphia and with several people in California on structuring and placement of distressed assets. We are also beginning to work on asset management in the real estate industry in collaboration with A.B. Scharf & Company in Florida.

In addition, we are working on very large structured transactions in physical crude oil, jet fuel oil, Mazut 100 and other energy transactions in collaboration with LW Partners, Philadelphia and several large refinery companies such as Reliance Petrochemicals, Gazprom and Petro Nafta.

Our private investment banking business has grown as a result of referrals only.
