David Crow's Summary

I have held roles in product design and development and more recently in marketing and business development. I am focused on helping emerging technology companies engage customers, develop and refine technologies, get product feedback and find scaleable business models.

My recent experiences have been focused on enabling a strong community in Toronto and across Canada, so that there is a strong ecosystem of product/SaaS companies to create a strong entrepreneurial density. My efforts can be seen at:
