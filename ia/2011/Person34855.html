Philip Hollonds's Summary

I have worked in the following positions in order of present date:
HSE Advisor/ Supervisor; HSE Advisor; Plant Manager; HSEC Superintendent; HSE Consultant; Snr. Plant Supervisor; Plant Supervisor; Diesel and Heavy Earthmoving Equipment Fitter;
Most of my work has been involved with Mining; Construction and Pipelines - Onshore & Offshore, in Australia and working Overseas with different Countries and varied Cultures.
Whether working in foreign countries or in Australia I believe we should always maintain our Company Policies and morals, our Country's standards and our own personal standards.
(Always attempting to aim higher, rather than allowing ourselves to lower the standards because regulations may not be as strict or standards may not be as high in a particular Country)

Specialties

I enjoy challenges, coaching and mentoring work crews, being involved with implementing change and the evolution of change in on-site Safety cultures.
Through my past work experience and being able to relate and understand work processes, it isn't usual to be confronted with a brick walls of resistance from work crews, though this is sometimes challenging.
Work Safely because it's beneficial to yourself and others; not because you have to.
