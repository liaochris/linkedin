Ximena Faralla's Summary

After working years as a teacher of English since graduating from High School at Villa Devoto School in 1990, in 2002 I created and directed my own educational travelling theatre company. I've written and produced every single piece; directed the majority of them too; even acted in some of them as well. I've been on stage since I was 13 both in English and in Spanish for several theatre companies. I am an excellent teacher and a most original creator of stageplays for the little ones, in particular. I've co-created and conducted Norland Audio Productions, working for very important international educational publishers, producing their Audio CDs. I've written over 50 plays for all ages, all of them in English. I've written two books for children, still in the editing process. My main goal is to give young learners the possibility of acquiring the English Language using tools which appeal to all of their senses.

Specialties

Young learners, coordinating teams, risk management, writing, directing, executive production, writing lyrics, adapting classic storires for children into plays, handling large groups of any age.
