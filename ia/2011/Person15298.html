Francesca Tassistro's Summary

User Experience Architect at work. Photographer in my spare time. Two passions, one purpose. Build something starting by observing reality. I take pictures of the world to get emotions across. I study behaviour patterns and user reactions to design digital applications that give a satisfactory experience, because it is conceived around real habits and interaction styles.

Specialties

My professional focus is on designing the experience of digital applications following the fundamental tenets of user-centered design

My skills are:
• Studying, defining and designing interaction solutions for the front end of different kinds of digital applications endeavoring to balance users’ needs with business goals and technological capabilities
• Designing and running usability tests, in depth-interviews, focus groups, ethnographic researches
