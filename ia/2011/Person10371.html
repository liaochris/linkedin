Darin Manis’s Summary

Darin founded financial human capital consulting firm RJ & Makay and is considered an expert as a consultant in the financial sector. He has been quoted more than 70 times in such publications as the Wall Street Journal (12 times), CNBC, CNN Money, Reuters, Wall Street Letter, Registered Rep, and many others.

Darin Manis is the CEO and Founder of eCompliments, LLC. eCompliments is an online review site whose mission and purpose is to share and find the good about any person, place, thing, business, or product. Anything and everything.

Darin has an extensive business background, is a consummate entrepreneur, and has successfully run several national corporations.

Darin and his wife have a heart for orphans, and founded the Orphan Coalition, a nonprofit global coalition of people, businesses, and other nonprofits supporting tens of thousands of orphans worldwide.

On a personal note, Darin has been married for 15+ years. They have a son and daughter and reside in Colorado, splitting their time between their homes in Colorado Springs and Breckenridge. Traveling, winter sports, hiking, and community activities are all passions the family shares together.
