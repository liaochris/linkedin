Kevin Hague's Summary

Founding Director and MD of M8 Group incorporating consumer retail websites www.petplanet.co.uk and www.greenfingers.com.

Shareholder and non-exec director of Endura Ltd (a leading cycling apparel brand in the UK enjoying strong growth in the US and other international markets).

Both businesses appeared in the November 2010 Sunday Times Fast Track 100 as two of the fastest growing profitable private companies in the UK.

Founding partner of nao ltd and bettercaring.co.uk (since disposed).

Previously partner at OC&C Strategy consultants, leading a wide variety of strategy consulting assignments across many industries ... with a particular focus on consumer industries, retail, new media and m&a.

Specialties

Traditional Retail, Ecommerce and Internet Retail, New Media, M&A, Strategy
