gina biancardi’s Summary

In early 1989, I co-founded The CollegeBound Network; a multimedia student recruitment company that has helped millions of learners connect with their educational institutions. With over 20 years experience in marketing and student recruitment/retention, I continue to play an integral role in the CollegeBound Network's success story launching ReachStudentsOnline.org which seeks to educate colleges and universities, career schools, Fortune 1000 companies, military branches, and lifestyle companies on the benefits, protocol, and efficiencies of online student recruitment
As a first generation Italian- American, I'm very passionate about my heritage and culture and have been involved in community service since I was a teen. I'm the co-founder of FIERI, a national social networking organization for young Italian Americans.
Over the past 20 years, I've successfully juggled motherhood (mother of three daughters), starting and growing a successful business and being active in the community. However, no project has been as ambitious and monumental as my current project of converting an old, historic mansion, and its surrounding property, into an Italian Arts and Cultural CAMPUS, the first of its kind in the entire country…..
In 11/08, I purchased 2.75 acres of prime real estate, formed a 501 (C) (3), nonprofit organization and donated the estate’s mansion to the newly formed nonprofit organization:Casa Belvedere, The Italian Cultural Foundation.
Opening an Italian Arts and Cultural Campus, offering educational programs and special events which highlight the rich heritage and culture of Italy has been my lifelong dream come true. This privately owned historic mansion, turned publicly owned cultural center, offers Italian language classes, cooking classes, musical performances, art exhibits, cultural events, fashion shows, lectures, workshops and more.
The surrounding 3 acres of Casa Belvedere property will eventually be transformed into a multi-building campus!

gina biancardi’s Experience

  • Founder/President

    Casa Belvedere, The Italian Cultural Foundation

    (Civic & Social Organization industry)

    January 2009 — Present (2 years 2 months)

    Casa Belvedere is a non profit, 501(c)(3) organization located in a historic and breathtaking, renaissance-style mansion atop Grymes Hill, Staten Island. The mansion is surrounding by almost 3 acres of sprawling property with spectacular views of the Verrazano Bridge and New York Harbor.
    This privately owned historic mansion, turned publicly owned Arts and Cultural Center, offers Italian language classes, cooking classes, musical performances, art exhibits, cultural events, fashion shows, lectures, workshops and more.

    Casa Belvedere is a symbolic structure which pays tribute to the tens of thousands of Italian immigrants whose sacrifices contributed greatly to the very fabric of this great nation. The goal is not only for the mansion to become a widely recognized Italian Arts and Cultural hub, but for the entire three acres to become the Premier Italian Arts and Cultural Campus in the entire nation with several buildings on campus.

    Our dream of preserving the legacy of Italy and Italian Americans while enriching the future generations is here.
    We are initiating a capital campaign to help us maintain, update, and ultimately begin major renovation to the mansion. Once all the work on the mansion is complete, we’ll no longer be a partially functional Cultural Center but a fully functional Cultural Center. This will greatly enhance our capabilities to serve a larger population and offer more classes, programs and special events.

    For more info visit www.casa-belvedere.org or write me at gina@casa-belvedere.org

CBN specializes in recruitment lead generation solutions for over 900 educational institutions and 3,500 campuses, using content-rich portals and innovative online and offline marketing strategies.
