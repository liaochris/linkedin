Kunal Kripalani's Summary

While studying towards a Graduate Diploma in Business, Kunal hooked up with technology start-up Saturn Media where he helped manage the development of Eventy.jp, an online invitations service in Japan. He continued to work with them during an eye-opening one year exchange program at Kyoto University.

Kunal cut his teeth as head of marketing at Ecommerce retailer Fishpond.com.au, Kunal's leadership helped drive 1230% sales growth which won recognition at the Deloitte Technology Fast 50 and also The Westpac Manukau Business Awards.

After 2 1/2 years at Fishpond, Kunal decided it was time to pursue an opportunity in Silicon Valley, so he resigned and one month later found himself on a flight to San Francisco where he developed Clyckit.com a daily deals text message service. After several months of sustaining himself through consultancy work, he came to accept that the opportunity was missed, surpassed by group buying websites.

Kunal then pursued some digital marketing work and travel opportunities in Singapore, Malaysia and Indonesia, including market-entry assessments and social media marketing. after which he found himself back in New Zealand and working with digital agency, Reactive Media.

Kunal is currently in Melbourne, Australia.
