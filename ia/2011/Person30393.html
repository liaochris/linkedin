Michele Scism’s Summary

Michele is a third generation entrepreneur who is excited to be launching her newest business venture. Decisive Minds, LLC is an organization dedicated to helping women business owners grow their businesses. Michele is a successful business woman who has spent most of her life working with her families businesses. As those businesses grew so did Michele's knowledge of business.

After going to college and receiving her BS in Accounting she returned to the family businesses and took an active role in management giving her broad experience in issues such as finance, human resources, negotiation, sales, marketing and the list goes on and on. In the early 90's Michele spent 2 years working for the fast growing CompUSA at the same time that the company went public giving her valuable IPO experience. In 2007 Michele was running her families trucking company, an affiliate with Quality Carriers, which was averaging $17 million annually in sales with about 100 employees when they decided to sell it to Quality.

Along the way Michele spent five years as a Mary Kay Sales Director. The information and knowledge gained through this time was immeasurable. She learned a lot about recruiting, training, planning events, motivating and speaking in front of large groups. She met many amazing women and this time really opened her eyes to the needs of women for training in business but more importantly in self worth, self esteem and inner peace.

Throughout Michele's life she has been labeled many things, a "self-help" junkie, confident, inspirational, motivational, positive but most importantly mother, wife & daughter. Always interested in self improvement she takes many courses and seminars, lives a positive life and believes that she is responsible for what happens in her life. She is a christian and believes that through God all things are possible.
