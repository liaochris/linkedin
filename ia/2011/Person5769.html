Boyd Willis’s Summary

I have 29 yrs experience in process engineering, including 15 yrs operations experience in continuous process plants. My experience includes 14 yrs of engineering design & 3 yrs of plant commissioning. I have a METSIM license (metallurgical process simulation, heat & mass balance software) & I am a very experienced modeller.

I became a freelance consultant in July 2008, specialising in nickel laterite project development work. In this time I have been heavily involved in 3 nickel laterite studies, for a heap leach project, a HPAL project, & an atmospheric leach project. Two of these projects intend recovering scandium as a by-product. For the 12 yrs prior to becoming a consultant I worked for Aker Solutions (prev Davy McKee) in Brisbane. I have been involved in many base metals projects, & have extensive knowledge of nickel laterite flowsheets, contributing to more than 25 nickel laterite projects. My involvement has extended to all facets of project development covering flowsheet development, testwork planning & management, mining, ore preparation, metallurgical processing, services, utilities & infrastructure, enabling me to bring a whole of project perspective to the table. My nickel laterite experience includes high pressure, atmospheric & heap leaching, & a variety of downstream recovery processes.

PreviousIy I spent 6 yrs at Kwinana Nickel Refinery, where I developed expertise in aqueous hydrogen reduction of nickel in high pressure autoclaves. I played a key role in the nickel refinery expansion & was involved in the design and commissioning of a new high pressure / high temperature oxydrolysis circuit, 2 new reduction autoclaves, nickel powder handling, sintering furnace, & facilities for the thickening & high pressure filtration of copper sulphide & mixed Ni/Co sulphide by-products.

I am interested in METSIM modelling, project definition & process development work.
