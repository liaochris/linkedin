Joe Romersa’s Summary

A Talent Of Many Trades

Producer - Sound Engineer - Voice Actor - Musician - Composer - Singer - ADR Director

Experience as a Drummer and or Sound Engineer includes working with legends such as:
Bob Dylan, John Prine (1992 GRAMMY), Carlene Carter, Eden Ahbez, Tom Petty, Donald Byrd, Paul Rothchild ...

Additional experiences can be seen @:
http://www.shadowboxstudio.com/discography.htm

Styles of music include: Rock, Blues, Indie, Techno, Dance, Folk, Country, Trance, Experimental, Eclectic, Soundtrack Scoring.

Joe Romersa, Shadow Box Studio owner, has produced various album projects and videos with many Los Angeles based music artists.
