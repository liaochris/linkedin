Russell Conrad's Summary

My professional career has allowed me to participate in two industries. My first career lasted 8 years, working with the ServiceMaster corporation, the worldwide leader in Facility Outsourcing. ServiceMaster allowed me to pursue three of my passions; new business start up, global travel and speaking spanish. My time with ServiceMaster allowed me to participate in the franchise start ups in Chile, Venezuela, China, Guam and Thailand.

In 2004, after finishing my contract with ServiceMaster Thailand, I made the decision to change careers. I went back to get my MBA at the Kellogg School of Management Satellite School, Sasin University in Bangkok. It was then that I discovered the exciting and fast growing industry of digital media. I partnered with several other entreprenuers to start the Digital Media Holding Company, DMS. In my 5 year career with DMS, I had the opportunity to run both our digital agency business, New Media Co. Ltd, and our Ad Network business, Admax Network Co. Ltd.
