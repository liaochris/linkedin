Vicki Lloyd's Summary

In addition to my personal experience, I have "inherited experience" in real estate, growing up in the business where my mother, grandfather, and great-grandmother have been real estate agents or brokers in California!

My goal is to make each real estate transaction pleasant and trouble free for my clients. My formal education includes an MBA in Finance and my undergraduate degree in Marketing included additional education in business law, statistics, psychology, and management theory, which all contribute to the skills that I make available to my clients.

Specialties

I use more technology tools than most real estate agents, including automating most of the process between listing and closing sales. Taking photos and distributing them to potential buyers and their agents, writing the copy for ads and brochures, thinking "outside the box" to accomplish the goals that my clients have.
