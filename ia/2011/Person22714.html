Jerry Sinclair's Summary

My passion is ministry (vocational or lay-leader) in the field God has called me to. During our 40 years of marriage, my wife and I have had only three Pastors in two churches. We are very loyal to the congregation we worship with, but have a global view of spreading the Gospel.

My first professional ministry experience was as a youth-sports director in which I was responsible for all youth athletic events at our church. There were times when we were blessed with over 250 athletes and cheerleaders that were participating on the property. I also served two years with the Fellowship of Christian Athletes, a rewarding experience that I will never forget. My duties with FCA were multifunctional. Special events, board member development, office management and donor - volunteer recruitment were a few of my duties.
