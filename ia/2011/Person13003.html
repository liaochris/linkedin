Ed Buturla's Summary

Ed Buturla is a seasoned eCommerce professional with over eleven years of B2C experience, and six years managing direct reports. He loves working in exciting environments with passionate, team oriented people. Ed enjoys the constant evolution of the web, and the resulting challenges it creates for businesses to communicate their brands and serve their consumers.

He is currently employed at Icebreaker in the Global eCommerce division.

Prior to working at Icebreaker, Ed spent nearly fourteen years at Nike, Inc. in a variety of roles of progressively increased responsibility. While managing a $15 million budget, he developed and implemented strategies to evolve Nike's online global presence as an eCommerce market leader.

He worked on several large scale projects including site re-architectures and redesigns, multi-channel integrations, and multi-brand integrations for Nike and its subsidiaries.

In earlier roles at Nike, Ed mastered the daily operations of running the US eCommerce business. He was involved in all aspects of the consumer lifecycle from customer acquisition thru site experience to order management and fulfillment.

Prior to a career path in eCommerce, Ed worked on Nike's apparel supply chain where he was recognized for leveraging systems enhancements to streamline business processes.

Ed received his M.B.A. from Portland State University's School of Business, where he completed the Management of Innovation & Technology program while continuing to work full-time. He received his B.S. in Business Administration with a concentration in Marketing from the University of Vermont, where he was awarded a full, four-year academic scholarship.
