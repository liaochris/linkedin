Dan Nunley’s Summary

I've practiced consumer bankruptcy law since 1996 helping thousands of Oklahomans struggling with serious debt problems to regain their financial freedom and enjoy life again.

Please visit my Oklahoma Bankruptcy Lawyer Blog at: http://www.nunleylaw.com.

I also am an allied attorney with the Alliance Defense Fund and offer legal representation without charge to individuals, groups and churches concerning constitutional law issues that impact Christian religious freedoms guaranteed by the First Amendment.
