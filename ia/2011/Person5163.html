Bhooshan Pandya (Certified Usability Analyst)'s Summary

11+ years of experience in design and usability engineering. My current job provides me the creative room to work and take critical decisions. My ultimate aim is to institutionalize Usability within an organization that will enable the company to make user-friendly GUIs and improve upon the user's experience.

I also spend time in understanding the role of communication design for emarketing and the role that technology can play. As more and more organizations compete on-line to sell their ideas, there is a definite need to translate the leadership vision and values through deft and crisply designed documents. The proper use of technology ensures the unobtrusive delivery of the message to the end-user. However, I personally believe it can only be achieved if the user-experience exceeds the user's expectation from the website. Excessive use of eye-candy technology ultimately blinds the user from his task.

Going forward, I would like to initiate research and analysis in the field of Human Computer Interaction (HCI), a field where I have associated myself over the past few years. HCI is also closely knit to user experience design which is the prime motivator for on-line transactions and dissemination of information. I've been quoted in a leading publication on my thoughts about the state of Government websites.
