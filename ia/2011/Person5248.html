Bill Graziadei's Summary

As an educator, research scientist, program director and (e)learning consultant, application engineer, implementation specialist and trainer I use my project management and teaching-learning skills, LMS/LCMS, real time collaboration learning systems and Web 2.0 knowledge/skills to ensure the successful implementation of strategic enterprise learning management solutions. This includes over 40 years of teaching and research experience, a range of project management opportunities, pre- and post-sales consulting activities such as demonstrations (remote and classroom-based), implementation planning, project coordination, system design and go-live pilot projects.

Specialties

- Accomplished educator, distance learning director, e-learning consultant, online instructor/course developer and trainer in both academic and corporate environments
- Analyzed, designed, developed and implemented learning strategies to support a wide range of learning formats, including classroom based, e-learning and mobile learning with an emphasis on distributed learning environments
- Excellent communication, teamwork and customer management skills
