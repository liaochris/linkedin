Robert (Rob) Frazzini’s Summary

Over the last 20 years, Robert Frazzini has combined his career in business leadership with his extraordinary passion for building new business. Growth and innovation are Rob’s specialties. As a serial entrepreneur Rob has launched several companies across a wide variety of business sectors including technology, marketing, and entertainment.

Rob’s passion is utterly infectious and he is widely recognized for his ability to motivate individuals and teams; challenging them to reach new heights of achievement and excellence. Rob’s audiences find his speaking style to be a great source of motivation and entertainment. He has shared the stage with several prominent leaders including former President, Bill Clinton.

Currently Rob is a Principal at large global professional services firm. His primary direction as leader has been to help develop and sustain services designed for the technology, business process, strategy, and federal marketplace.
