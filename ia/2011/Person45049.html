Vikash Singh is an alumnus of Bangalore Institute of Technology, Karnataka (BIT). He has an experience of over 3 years in IT industry as a SAP Professional working for major clients.

He is currently pursuing his Post Graduation in Management in International Business & Marketing at SIMSR, Mumbai and is graduating by May 2011.
