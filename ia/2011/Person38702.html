Sandra Sinnott's Summary

I studied Business and Legal Studies via a Certificate, Diploma and Degree course. I then went on to study an LLB in Law. I was always very interested in the legal side of business and so after working in claims handling for just over a year, I joined the Compliance Team of Euro Insurances Limited. There I helped to create a culture of compliance for the organisation and also helped to develop and maintain a Compliance Database for the storage of legislation, contracts and regulatory correspondance.
I then moved onto my current role in Alico Life International where I now work in the areas of Anti Money Laundering, Complaints management, marketing documentation review and monthly compliance reporting for our branches in Germany, Italy and Spain.
Outside of my work in Compliance, I have also completed a Diploma in Proofreading and am a volunteer editor for www.bibliotastic.com

Specialties

Anti Money Laundering, Compliance Database Management, Business Continuity, Contract Management, Proofreading
