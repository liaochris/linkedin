Daniel Ward (dgward@hotmail.com)’s Summary

Daniel Ward has 30+ years of CEO-level leadership experience in commercial enterprises, non-profit organizations, healthcare facilities and community systems, schools, colleges, and government agencies. He has created profitable businesses, started successful non-profits, administered government programs, and directed successful organizational turnarounds in healthcare and human services.

Mr. Ward started and ran several community mental health treatment programs in three different states, managed the turnaround of two large but failing medical clinics, directed marketing for a multinational web software company, started and ran an inter-urban bus company, managed continuous quality improvement in another software company, wrote advertising for the Dow Chemical Company and other Fortune 100 companies, investigated felons as an adult probation officer, built the largest private dental clinic in Oregon, taught high school and college, wrote the pilot training program for the Boeing 777...and more.
