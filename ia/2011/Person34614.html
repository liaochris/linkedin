Peter Dickenson's Summary

I am currently Managing Director of the UK arm of AffiliateFuture a performance based advertising network. AffiliateFuture had been trading for 12 months when i joined the company. Over the years we have developed AffiliateFuture into one of the leading global affiliate networks, with local office in London, Madrid and New York. We cater for a range of clients from multi-national brands to sme businesses.

AffiliateFuture has been through two owners and is now part of Progressive Digital Media Group PLC.

Peter Dickenson's Experience

UK Managing Director

AffiliateFuture

Public Company; Marketing and Advertising industry

July 2009 – Present (1 year 9 months)

AffiliateFuture enables advertisers to reach customers through the powerful, performance marketing model of affiliate marketing.

Continual investment in research and technological developments has positioned AffiliateFuture as one of the most authoritative and innovative affiliate marketing networks. Its tracking technology is industry-leading thanks to VeraciTag™, a cookie-free tracking system that captures 7% more transactions than standard cookie based tracking. Its many travel-related technologies, including a proprietary searchable database of over 30 million holidays, means AffiliateFuture is the unrivalled leader in the travel sector for affiliate marketing.

Understanding the most effective way of representing brands on affiliate sites is a critical factor in the success of AffiliateFuture. To that end the network undertakes regular research, runs frequent training sessions, developed and now delivers the IDM course in affiliate marketing as well as hosting its own networking events. Collectively these initiatives bring merchants and affiliates together in a range of environments where knowledge and experiences can be shared.

The pedigree of the AffiliateFuture team is unrivalled; all of the senior players are online marketing experts, many of whom have experience of affiliate marketing as high level merchants or top-performing affiliates. The resulting culture is a passion for getting the most out of affiliate marketing through first class service, continual improvement and transparency.

With offices in London, New York and Madrid, AffiliateFuture is able to manage national and international affiliate marketing programs. With over £200m of sales generated in 2008 and over 60 million users visiting our advertisers, AffiliateFuture is one of the most innovative leaders in its field.
