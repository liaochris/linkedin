Stephen Dowling's Summary

When managed properly organisational change will deliver better results for your business and for the last 25 years Stephen has done just that. It’s for this reason executive teams within Australia and the world’s most respected companies turn to Stephen to guide them through the most complex and legislative intricate organisational change projects.

No matter what the change project, Stephen works to achieve a balanced outcome. Where stakeholder needs are met and the dignity of the individuals impacted remain intact.

Stephen has a comprehensive understanding of the various IR, HR, HSE legislative frameworks with experience in representing the corporation in legal matters with a high level of confidentiality, so that any sensitive issues are addressed in a highly professional manner.

Organisations are comprised of people and the behaviors of people make up the outputs of an organization. To effectively manage your people, Stephen believes an enterprise-wide Governance, Risk and Organisational Change Management Program is no longer a luxury - it is a necessity.

Utilising a range of proven methods, including Delivering Safe Production and Services ™ products Stephen has helped clients successfully resolve complex Governance, Risk, and Compliance challenges. Tackling major programs relating to people, risk, cost reduction, sustainable business improvement and change. These methods have been proven across many different industries and areas of government.

Specialising in the most complex, heavily unionised, and publicly scrutinised organizations, Stephen has delivered better business outcomes for: Oil & Gas; Mining (Soft and hard rock) Energy, Power and Utilities; Manufacturing; Print Web, Digital, Media, Publishing; Aviation; Banking & Finance; Engineering & Construction; FMCG; Shared Services; Wholesale Hardware, Government State & Local; Information Technology; Health Public & Private; Public Transport, Agriculture; Higher Education.
