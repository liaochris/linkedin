Christine Holzmann’s Summary

More than 16 years experience as a graphic artist, 11 years as a design editor/creative director. Fully proficient in digital/graphic design, layout, typesetting, color correction on both PC and Macintosh. Particular expertise in Photoshop (CS5 & earlier), Illustrator (CS5 & earlier), Painter, Coreldraw, InDesign (CS5 & earlier), Quark Express, Multi-Ad Creator, Microsoft Word, Powerpoint, Acrobat, Dreamweaver and Drupal. Also fully proficient in HTML & CSS. Recently started working with Flash. Extensive knowledge in hardware and software maintenance and support on Macintosh platforms. Working knowledge of journalistic writing, editing, graphics and photography. 11 years extensive design experience in the newspaper industry as design editor/creative director. Solely responsible for the redesign of The Citizen newspaper in 2003 & built complete stylebook. Built the design and styleguide for The McDuffie Mirror newspaper in McDuffie, Georgia. First place for Page Design in the 2004 Better Newspaper contest sponsored by the National Newspaper Association. First place for Best Non-Page One Layout in the 2007 Suburban Newspapers of America Editorial Contest. My portfolio of newspaper section fronts can be viewed online at:
http://www.crissi.com/portfolio/index.php?x=browse&category=5
