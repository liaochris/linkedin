Eric Jackson’s Summary

I am part of the executive team of a rapidly growing visual analytics software company, as well as leading a new venture developing tools for cash flow and risk management for small businesses. Over my career, I have played lead roles in product design and development as well as product management and marketing. I have been the primary architect and developer of world-class software applications in three separate fields: very large-scale, high performance computer file systems (1 patent issued); computer chip design and manufacturing (1 patent issued and 2 publications); turbulent flow simulation (15 publications). More generally, I have extensive experience in high-performance computing, parallel computing and distributed applications.

Eric Jackson’s Specialties:

Product strategy and management; fostering innovation; product definition, design and development; technology transfer; complex problem solving; project planning; business process development. Area expertise in file systems and network file protocols, storage networking, electronic design automation, computer intelligence, grid computing, distributed/parallel systems, computational science, performance optimization and a wide variety of computational mathematical methods.
