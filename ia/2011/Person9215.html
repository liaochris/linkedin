Cord Pereira's Summary

20+ years in professional sports, live entertainment, broadcast, brand programs and related media and social community technologies. Creator and executive producer of the first LIVE audience-judged sports event on prime time television in THE GREAT SKATE DEBATE in 1996/98 (CBS Prime Time | co-produced with IMG). Privately developed and managed stadiums and arenas, and led investor syndicates in minor league sports franchises in multiple local markets. Experienced live event promoter for concerts, family shows and special events.

Specialties

Now focusing on developing brand-driven (branded entertainment) sports and entertainment properties built around online and local communities and media syndication. Developing investor syndicate to capitalize on new trends in entertainment and media.
