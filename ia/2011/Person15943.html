George Hechtman's Summary

I have been fortunate to gain skills in wide range of disciplines within the retail and wholesale distribution industries, throughout the entire United States and Mexico. My career has been focused on public and private companies at critical inflection points: turn-arounds, high growth, and mergers.

My approach is to work from the front line, nearest to customers, on up through the organization, with an emphasis on measurable results. Everything must start and end with customers, external and internal alike; the fun is in helping make those customers' lives more productive and satisfying.

I want to continue to leverage my skills by helping companies to address difficult situations and reach new customers.

Specialties

Business development, customer service, work force and working capital productivity, strategy, real estate optimization, business modeling, communications.
