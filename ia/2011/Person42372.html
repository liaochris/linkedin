Tali Blood's Summary

Tali is a specialist recruiter with eight years experience in the mining recruitment industry. Tali's area of specialty is mining & resources, process and maintenance.

The best recruiters are those who are motivated by the challenge of finding the best fit, with candidate, job and employer. Tali has the tenacity and attention to detail to bring the right candidate together with the right job. Her considerable sourcing expertise and a firm understanding of quantative metrics ensures the top talent in the job market is within her reach. Tali is consistently growing her contacts to guarantee a pool of talented candidates.

Tali has successfully assisted in bringing over in excess of 30 electrical & mechanical tradespeople from UK, Zimbabwe & Philippines to the Australian market, along with recruiting senior mining professionals into Africa, PNG, SE Asia & Australia.

Specialties

Mining Maintenance (Fixed and Mobile Plant from trade level to Management) and processing (Hydro/Pyro Metallurgy, base and precious metals) roles with the Australian and overseas markets.
