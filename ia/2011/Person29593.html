Mehmet A. Vural's Summary

Mehmet Ali Vural is founder of The Spotlight Enterprise Knowledge Management Design Inc. since August 2008 and Senior IT Consultant at CIRPD since 2002, Mehmet has been responsible for oversight of CIRPD’s website architecture and design working with other contactors and volunteers. Mehmet is skilled in many computer languages including ASP.NET, C#, Sharepoint, Coldfusion, Web Services, ADO, ADO.NET, JS, HTML, WML, SQL, LINQ, XML, and VB Scripting. Mehmet has been instrumental in the creation of innovative webservices to webservices linkages with the National Library of Medicine to support CIRPD developing Sharepoint Portal project – The Health and Work Productivity Web-Portal. In addition to his work with CIRPD.

Our Mission:
The prevention and reduction of pain, pain-related suffering, and disability through the creation and dissemination of evidence-informed best practices.

Our Vision:
Our vision is to help people improve the quality of their life and find relief from ongoing muscle and joint pain by bringing health professionals, pain sufferers and researchers together to improve diagnosis and treatment.

In many ways, CIRPD represents the future of health care. Beyond keeping people alive through such dramatic techniques as transplant technology, there comes the issue of quality of life.

When you consider that half of all visits to family physicians are directly related to the work of CIRPD, you will understand how very relevant and urgent our mission really is. There are millions of people worldwide whose lives could be dramatically improved as a direct result of the innovative efforts of the Canadian Institute for the Relief of Pain and Disability.
