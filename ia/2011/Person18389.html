Ivan Davila's Summary

Bilingual public relations specialist responsible for developing, managing and maintaining community relations and marketing-communications strategies that elicit the commitment and advancement of El Buen Samaritano's mission.

El Buen Samaritano Episcopal Mission provides a safety net of integrated programs and services intended to help working-poor families leverage resources, enabling them to change the future trajectory of their lives for generations to come.

For more information, visit http://www.elbuen.org.

Ivan Davila's Experience

Communications Coordinator

El Buen Samaritano Episcopal Mission

Non-Profit; 11-50 employees; Health, Wellness and Fitness industry

August 2007 – Present (3 years 8 months)

At El Buen, Ivan specializes in both Hispanic and general marketing communications, executing both internal and external communication plans which incorporate the use of marketing, advertising and media relations not only to promote the programs and services offered by El Buen Samaritano, but also to effectively convey El Buen’s impact on the community to current and potential funders.

Ivan’s media outreach efforts at El Buen have resulted in numerous media hits, including a news story on the nationally and internationally aired Univisión show Despierta América, featuring El Buen’s pilot program in collaboration with the RunTex Carrozza Foundation designed to combat childhood obesity through exercise and nutrition—Summer Fun with Born to Run.

Hispanic Communications Director

Dávila Strategies

Public Relations and Communications industry

February 2006 – August 2007 (1 year 7 months)

As Hispanic Communications Director and owner of Dávila Strategies, Ivan specializes in media relations, social media management, translation services and marketing communications.

Dávila Strategies knows first-hand that bilingual-language skills are not enough to appeal to the Hispanic consumer. Knowledge of the diverse Hispanic subgroups, socioeconomic expertise and cultural dexterity all play a significant role in the development of solid, effective and results-driven Hispanic marketing communications. Based in Austin, Texas, the firm develops and implements plans for clients to achieve visibility and awareness that support growth, profitability and positioning in the Hispanic market.

For more information, visit www.davilastrategies.com

Associate Program Coordinator

The Sallie Mae Fund

Public Company; Financial Services industry

2005 – 2006 (1 year)

In partnership with hundreds of local and national community organizations in 78 cities across the country, working directly with two public relations agencies, and as a member of a team of four, Ivan managed, coordinated and acted as the Spanish spokesperson for The Sallie Mae Fund’s 10,000-mile 2005-2006 Paying for College Bus Tour. The campaign reached more than 45,000 low-income Hispanic individuals, fulfilling its goal to help more Latino students achieve their dreams of a higher education.

Intern

Fogarty Klein Monroe

Privately Held; Marketing and Advertising industry

January 2005 – May 2005 (5 months)

Responsible for conducting media planning, in collaboration with account managers, in the planning and execution of public relations campaigns for clients such as Pfizer, Kroger, Waste Management, the American Cancer Society and many others, Ivan established solid relationships with media contacts across the United States.

A noteworthy assignment involved conducting media relations for Jesús Hernández, a race car driver sponsored by Waste Management in NASCAR. Ivan's media outreach efforts for this particular project resulted in numerous media hits, including a program segment on the nationally aired Univisión show, Control, where the hostess of the show interviewed Hernández and test-drove his car on the air.
