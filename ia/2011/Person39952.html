Tibi Puiu's Summary

I like to think of myself as a problogger and as an avid social media connoisseur. I've always been fascinated by the human social psychology, how we act within our communities and how we, as humans, can't possibly survive without social interaction. As the internet boom went on, I exercised my passion for sociology on the web, thanks to social media. Thus, I've managed, over the years, to acquire a large amount of knowledge and skill in the social media department, as well as to form a pretty authoritative persona in the SM sphere.

I firmly believe the years of experience, that I have behind me, qualify me as worthy choice for those seeking counsel and training in the field of social media, most notably in blogging.

I'm also a highly ambitious entrepreneur, always on the look out for new business opportunities and startup ideas. Internet marketing is another passion of mine, which I hope to further develop with the future campaigns I plan to launch. Freelancing is something I've jungled with a lot in the past, but which now regrettably, is no longer possible for me, due to lack of time.
