Michael Roberts's Summary

At age of 18 I was hired on as the youngest 9-1-1 operator ever hired in my state.

During the late nights of dead time I read self help books on business, real estate, management, etc.

I realized that I wanted to own my own company and wound up quiting my stressful job.

At the age of 19 I opened a cleaning company that grew at a very quick rate. With in two years the company was making nearly a quarter million per year.

I went on to invest in a Pizza restaurant and owned that as well.

As those two companies closed I learned more about business than I ever have before.

After that I took a job at my Dads company working with him on designing new products. My time spent with him has taught me a lot about how to lay products out, manage engineers, traditional marketing and how to handle research and development.

From his company I got into internet marketing.

I stumbled upon great teachers online such as Tim Buchalka, Frank Kern, Ed Dale and Dan Raine....

Through their teachings and my own research I managed to build multiple websites that reached the top of Google.

With hundreds of dollars coming in with minimal amounts of investment I wanted to do this on a mass scale.

I learned how powerful web 2.0 techniques are but were too time consuming.

From there I laid out a software that would automate nearly all of the web 2.0 process for me.

After countless hours of planning and designing I hired a team of engineers to start working on building me my product.

And Traffic-Bug was born.

I am now the creator and CEO of Traffic-Bug an online software that automates web 2.0 tricks.

This software is one of the most powerful online softwares for SEO tactics that money can buy. You can check it out at http://www.Traffic-Bug.com.

Online Marketing is an amazing thing with countless opportunities. Now I can't ever see my self going back to the traditional brick and mortar business model.
