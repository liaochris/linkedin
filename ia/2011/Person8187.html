Chris Lipper’s Summary

An experienced, honest, and creative sales professional who leads with integrity, imagination, energy, and vision. From large institutions to entrepreneurial start-ups, I have excelled in the conceptualization, development, marketing and both domestic and international sales of products in consumer, business to business, and institutional markets. Whether selling media, consumer electronics, or products of my own invention, for which I have over a dozen patented products.

1998 Founded Lipper-Man Ltd. Invented Med-Tats™ and founded a company that filed both domestic and international patents for the use of removable tattoos as a transdermal delivery system for vitamins and medicines. Negotiated, financed and organized FDA testing to show safety and efficacy of Med-Tat™. Created a team of designers, manufactures and chemists, completed studies and gave 50 plus presentation and speeches to the pharmaceutical community.

1995 – Founded Chris Co. Inc. Invented dozens of products and founded a company that conceived, invented, developed and sells and distributes patented products. Handled the strategic planning, financial and operational aspects of the business that shipped over 100,000,000 removable tattoos world wide.

Nominated for Humanity Award by the Tech Museum of Innovation for Med-Tats™, medicated tattoos. Recipient of 2004 MVP award for contributions by The Alternative Board, a membership organization of New Jersey Entrepreneurs. Featured speaker at the 2/11/03 Licensing Executives Society meeting.
