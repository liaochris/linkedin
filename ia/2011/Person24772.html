Kelly Kampen's Summary

I have been involved in multiple companies in multiple industries. Call center in/outbound, Mobile Content, Staff Leasing, Business Offshoring, ISP and a Newspaper. I have lived all over Asia, and now call Bangkok, Thailand my home again.

I excel in finding the right people to do the job correctly & efficiently, and then I give them the freedom to do it. I am extremely familiar with E-Commerce & Web Programming and full life-cycle of internet projects.

I have taken two Technology companies in the past from 0 to nearly 250+ employees within 14 months.

I have now been in Asia for more than 9 years and have become very in tune with the market. I have watched the outsourcing and call center industries explode in the past 4 years while in Manila.

As far as I know, I was the only BPO (Business Process Outsourcing) Consultant of this kind in Manila. I assisted multiple companies in opening branch offices here, and other parts of Asia. This saving the parent companies substantial amounts of money and increasing their bottom-line. I can and have taken companies through the entire process of corporate formation, office space locating & securing, to finding key personnel. I've even acted as an interim COO in some cases, until locating a local management or in some cases helping one of their current employees relocate and acclimate to local living and management.

I excel in all aspects of setting up a western companies offshore, usually labor intensive outsourcing. This results in huge savings and a workforce that is easily managed from overseas or can be completely managed for you.

Specialties

I am a man of many hats; Internet, Marketing, IT Consulting, E-Commerce, Internet Marketing, Mobile Technologies, Human Resources & BPO Management.
