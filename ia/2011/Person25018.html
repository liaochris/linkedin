Kristen Heck’s Summary

Serving Montana 25 years, LC Staffing provides temp, and temp to hire staffing, and professional search services for a wide range of industries. We also provide qualified, reliable Virtual Assistants and Human Resource and Risk Management services.

Loyal Care In-Home Assistance offers an array of non-medical in home assistance services. A loved one will receive understanding, special care and helpful support tailored to meet their needs. We provide personal care assistance, companion care and homemaker services.

In-home respite allows family caregivers necessary relief that might otherwise require permanent placement of a loved one in a facility outside the home. Respite service can provide the necessary breaks from exhausting challenges faced by a primary caregiver.

Loyal Care clients are comfortable without having to be inconvenienced with interrupted daily routines and medication schedules, transportation or strange environments.
