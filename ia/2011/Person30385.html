Michele Anderson’s Summary

For the greatest part of my 9 to 5 professional career I served in Marketing capacities of rising responsibility, after going to school and working and working for seven career-formulating years.

In 1995 when I had my son, Max, I took my business home and created a freelance marketing consulting and services business. It was what's now commonly known as a home-based business.

Just a few years ago, about the time my son was in middle-school, the ambition I held at bay while he was little, welled-up and catapulted me into a new era. It turned out after much contemplation and study, that I was ready take entrepreneurship and sales marketing very seriously, for the first time in my 'marketing' career life.

The rest is becoming history, and not without challenge, listening, learning a whole lot, and stepping back to punt in many ways - all over again. But the journey is great and the future is bright today in numerous and genuine ways for my 'self' and my family, and a number of friends and associates.

I am above all else grateful for my connection to 'something' much greater than myself, the mentors I met along the way, and the many special friendships that have carried me for decades to this unique point in time where history and my life are finally making 'nearly' complete sense.

Life remains a big, sometimes confusing puzzle of sorts, but the process is now so much more understandable. My unique experiences seem to be making all the difference in the world. It was about the time I stopped worrying about what it amounted to that the experiences became more valuable than I every imagined.

I encourage everyone to follow their inner knowing, and to learn how to enable their personal gifts to soar and take life.
