Jason Castellani’s Summary

During the last 2 years I have made the transition from a corporate cubicle career to a digital location independent professional.

I am the co-creator and operator of TwoBackpackers.com, an independent budget travel blog. After backpacking Central and South America for a year with my wife Aracely, I became completely engulfed in travel blogging, social internet media and online marketing. Since we began 1.5 years ago we have created an independent travel blog attracting 13,000 unique visitors per month and ranking among the top 25 independent travel blogs in the world.

Our goals revolve around our HD Travel Videos and the entertainment we provide from the less traveled places around the world. We will continue to produce high quality, high entertainment value videos with the goal of becoming a partner or integrated with a main stream social media outlet with an audience in the millions.

The broadcast market is saturated with luxury travel to common places of interest around the world. Two Backpackers (Jason & Aracely) will break into a new market of budget, backpacking adventure that is off the beaten path.
