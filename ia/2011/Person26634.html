Liz Strauss's Summary

LIZ STRAUSS is a social web strategist and community builder who focuses on growth through relationships, communication, and performance. She works with corporations, associations, and small business to seamlessly integrate online and offline cultures and social media tools into their growth strategies.

According to EatonWeb, “Liz Strauss is perhaps the most influential relational blogger on the Internet.” Her blog –Liz Strauss at Successful Blog — has been called both a destination and an event. During her tenure as an international publisher, Liz was consistently hired to rebuild companies and reignite teams. Liz trained hundreds of employees to understand the needs of their market and the ways leaders move action through a company. She has worked with C-Suite executives and interdepartmental teams on four continents.

As principal at Inside-Out Thinking, Liz Strauss with her partner. Terry St. Marie, builds leadership networks and loyalty communities online and offline using the interactive think-tank models and masterminds formats first introduced at SOBCon, their yearly business strategy and social media event. The internal high-performance leadership networks connect employees, customers, partners, vendors around ivalues fully aligned within the business value proposition to establish a thriving organization..

Worth noting:

Ms. Strauss has established core strategies and nationally-recognized brands based on consistent quality, customer-centered experience, and appealing design. She has developed business models of over 20+% growth. (707% in six years, 132% in three years). Net EBITDA ranged 35–45%, with IRRs at 65%–145%. She is Web Publisher of Successful-Blog.com, LizStrauss.com, and one other. Contributor: MyEscapeVelocity.com and Suze Orman's MoneyMondedMoms.com
