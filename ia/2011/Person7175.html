Carol Wain's Summary

I have been an entrepreneur since my early-teens – selling my French class notes to my teacher, leftover pizza and old clothes to my sister (who won’t let me forget!), newspaper subscriptions and macramé hanging plant holders. My college years were great and although I studied business management and labour relations, I didn’t really have a plan.

Fast forward to the mid-90’s, where the opportunity to explore the world of self-employment, first as a travel agent and then as the proprietor of an incentive travel business presented itself.

Over the past 15 years Incentive Depot evolved from selling travel to selling all types of rewards; to where we’re at today, which is working with senior level executives to improve their performance by concentrating on changing behavior of the people behind the financial statement numbers. In 2003 I won Entrepreneur of the Year for Vancouver Island as President of Incentive Depot.

A few years ago I launched World Incentive Network (WIN), which is a network of individuals that provide results-based services to organizations. WIN's global network includes business consultants, coaches, trainers and B2B services sales people that produce measurable business improvement results and sell at the VP level and above.

I recently launched Marquee Marketing as a division that focuses primarily on mobile, social media and local marketing.

I have an extensive background in travel, tourism and performance improvement. In 1997 - 98 I wrote the travel page for the Comox Valley Record and facilitated consumer educational sessions on the topic of “ABSeas of Cruising”.

I am a subject expert on the topic of incentive program ROI, incentive travel and global recognition & rewards programs.

I am a member of both the IMA and RPI and I am currently serving as the President for the Incentive Travel Council. I previously represented western Canada as a Director for SITE Canada

Specialties

recognition, incentives, loyalty, incentive roi, incentive travel, business improvement, global incentive programs, performance improvement, cash flow and profit generator, travel, tourism, marketing, sales, business creation, business development, strategy, local marketing, social media marketing, mobile marketing.
