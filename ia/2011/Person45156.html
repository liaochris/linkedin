Vincent Tong’s Summary

My childhood dream was to create something really FUN for tens of millions of people from around the world to play. And this is the story of my journey so far.

The production of fun and games has been a big part of my life since I was born. I worked in a toy factory, opened a karaoke studio in California, invented 2 toy patents, delivered online shopping fun for 7M users, and have been driving some interesting online game development at EA since mid-2003.

A) I spent the first 15 years of my life in Hong Kong before coming to America. I was practically raised in a toy factory. It was a small family-run business. Whenever we had crunch time for ship date, I was usually tasked to work on the assembly line. I guess I've had a head-start in appreciating the hard work behind producing entertainment products at a young age.

B) In college, I co-founded a karaoke studio in California and was granted two toy patents. Despite the rock star status of a karaoke mogul on campus, I was a loyal puppy and married the love of my life whom I met at the karaoke studio. My patents include an interactive computer controlled doll (patent # 5636994) and a bouncing boot (patent # 5685807).

C) I started my professional career producing live software products and services in 1998. Please see details below.
