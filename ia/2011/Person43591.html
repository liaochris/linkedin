Teresa Luetjen-Keeler, MA’s Summary

I have almost 20 years of experience performing market analysis and relocation services in the corporate sector, and program development and community relations activities for higher education. Over time, my professional experiences guided my graduate studies: at first, examining government administration and its effects on local communities at the University of Pennsylvania; then, exploring uncertainty and decision making within the family in my dissertation at Rutgers University. But ultimately, my personal experiences with family challenges and estate settlement led me to start Orella Associates.

The goal at Orella Associates is to ensure that clients can live well at any age in any location. Orella Associates offers life planning, relocation analysis and conflict resolution services to adults in transition - those facing layoff, retirement or a life change - who are wondering where to live or what to do next. Orella Associates brings practical solutions to challenging situations so that clients and their families can move forward with less stress, less conflict, and more enjoyment.

Follow our blog at www.transitiontipster.com to read more about issues related to planning for retirement, surviving life transitions, and overcoming family conflict. Visit our website at www.orellaassociates.com for more information about our company.
