Helen Whelan’s Summary

Helen Whelan founded Success Television to empower individuals and organizations to achieve their goals by honing their leadership skills. Success Television offers speakers, coaches and video trainings featuring founders of companies, CEOs and executive coaches like Marshall Goldsmith who help people make smarter choices.

Executives who want to acquire top talent,reduce turnover and innovate or schools teaching leadership skills buy Success Television’s video trainings of world class successful leaders to add credibility to the points their teaching. In fact, “The Wisdom of Caring Leaders” video training, which features Jack Welch, Richard Branson, Anita Roddick and others is used by the American Management Association to teach leadership skills.

Helen Whelan has worked at the forefront of TV and digital media integration. In 1995,she convinced Ted Turner to create the first digital, computer-based television network, CNNfn. The network launched on time and on budget within six months. She quickly established key strategic relationships that created the first profitable news website, CNNfn.com (now called CNN Money) within 18 months of launch. Whelan also created $41 million in revenue from partnering with new advertisers to create TV and online programming.

In 1999, Whelan saw the opportunity to reach baby boomers online and on TV and launched MyPrimeTime. After producing two TV series for PBS stations as well as motivational broadband programs, Whelan worked with investment bankers to leverage MyPrimeTime's video assets and online web tools to launch a linear TV and online network focused on personal development in 2002. (You’ll be watching a similar TV network with Oprah, called OWN, launch this year.)

Beyond its motivational training videos and speakers, Success Television is a social media network where self-improvement experts offer viewers wisdom in career, wellness, relationship and life meaning through articles and short videos.

Helen Whelan’s Specialties:

- Expertise and close relationship with best-selling authors and experts who provide insight into living the life you imagine.
- Corporate training videos around the issues of team building, effective communication skills, leadership and change.
