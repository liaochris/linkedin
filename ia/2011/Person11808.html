Diana Legate’s Summary

MY VISION, MY VALUES, MY MISSION

Based on more than 20 years of experience, I believe that the home is one of the greatest investments in one's life. We all have likes and dislikes, wishes and needs, that make the 'Neighborhood' quality of life such an important issue in determining the perfect place to live.

My resourcefulness, knowledge and dedication to my clients ensure that they make the most of their real estate opportunities to make the right decision when it comes to the right home in the right neighborhood.

As a full-time Real Estate Broker, I am extremely dialed into the people, neighborhoods and services of our communities. Whether you're a seller, a first-time home buyer or looking to move up; I have the connections, edge, insight and local expertise to help you reach your goals.

I am a professional, an experienced negotiator, knowledgeable, enthusiastic. I am a hard worker with integrity and honesty, providing excellent service to all.

This deep commitment is why I, Diana Legate, have become one of the most respected real estate agents in Contra Costa County; more importantly, how I reflect the true spirit of the neighborhood.

When you're ready to make your next move, give Diana Legate a call, 'Diana Sells Neighborhoods'.
