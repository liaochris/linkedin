Dave White's Summary

David is a highly motivated, hands-on professional with over 30 years of experience in digital printing technology including IP definition and patent creation. This has been gained through roles involving electrostatic printing research and development, laboratory prototype fixturing design & build, screen printed ceramics development, and computer programming. His ideal working environment is one in which he is able to leverage his enthusiasm for developing business and product development strategies based on the development of alternative formats for fine art reproduction fired on ceramics. David enjoys and seeks opportunities that allow him to be part of the entire design cycle, from art transformation concepts and strategies right through to installation.

Specialties

Creation of new ideas for transforming fine art into novel ceramic forms.
Promoting collaboration and creativity between artists, designers, architects, builders, manufacturers and vendors.
Troubleshooting problems and creating inovative solutions.
