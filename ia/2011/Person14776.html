Celeste Warren celeste.warren@fleishman.ca's Summary

Why am I a Human Resources Professional? Because I have a genuine passion for finding, developing and retaining top talent for progressive and growth-oriented organizations.

Over the past 13 years, I have successfully managed the human resources function for five international organizations, providing human resources support within a variety of industries including professional services, public relations, retail and technology.

I have extensive experience in HR program development, full-cycle recruitment, organizational development, professional development and training, compensation and benefits administration and career coaching. I am deeply committed to providing exemplary customer service to my client groups, and serve as the primary point of contact for all human resources, employee relations and talent development initiatives.

I hold a Bachelor of Arts degree in business with a minor in psychology from the University of Waterloo (with distinction), in addition to a post-graduate diploma in Human Resources Management from Seneca College of Applied Arts and Technology. I am also a Certified Coach practitioner with the Certified Coaches Federation and Nutrition and Wellness Specialist with the Canadian Fitness Professionals Association.

Specialties

Domestic and international recruitment (including direct "head hunting"), training and development, employee relations, organizational development, change management, performance management and retention.
