Jay Piz’s Summary

JAY PIZ in THE UNIVERSAL MARKETER
Beyond The Tchotchkes

In this latest installment of the series, Piz, who claims no parallel to Jean-Claude Van Damme's Universal Soldier, receives a communiqué from his superior to return to action and confront the toughest competitors ever including the dominant first-mover from Europe. At stake is the company's brand and a sales pipeline worth millions. But support will be minimal. The tools authorized for the engagement would be his superior's reliance on tchotchkes (choch-keys) and other unimaginative means to compete. The Universal Marketer would be left to his own wit - and grit!

Expert in guerilla marketing, veteran of B2B software wars, survivor of countless skirmishes across industry borders, the no-fear, no-sweat, trench-hardened Piz knew he must once again call on his skills in applying the universal principles of marketing. While not running through alleys in Los Angeles or dodging missiles or parachuting from a skyscraper, Piz isn't willing to slow down the momentum for a moment.

Breaking through the high-falutin', unholy union of technobabble and corporate-speak, Piz analyzes the competitive landscape and swiftly devises an ambitious plan to re-align the company's strategic and tactical objectives for ensuring the competitive edge. Setting out on a rogue mission the team maintains a breathless pace, complete with ticking deadline, leaving little room for error. Armed with flawless know-how, superior presentation skills and an extensive portfolio of compelling marketing materials, a revitalized web site complete with relevant content, SEO and social marketing strategic support, they move quickly toward a gaining strategic advantage as Piz leads his troops Beyond The Tchotchkes in penetrating the market, defending the brand, and ultimate market dominance. A new textbook case for return on innovation!

  • President

    Interscholastic Hockey Federation (IHF)

    (Sports industry)

    2005 — Present (6 years )

    A veteran marketing professional, writer, long-time youth sports advocate and community activist, Jay Piz is also passionate about scholastic roller (inline) hockey. Piz presently serves as board president of the Interscholastic Hockey Federation (IHF) - a non-profit organization governing scholastic (High School) roller hockey competitions for over 500 student-athletes in Orange County and surrounding Southern California communities. Piz also co-chairs tournaments such as the California State Cup High School Roller Hockey Championship, organization events and charity fundraisers such as the annual Give Blood Play Hockey Inline Hockey Charity Tournament which aims to raise blood donations in the community and funds for CHOC in support of their new Oncology Patient Care Room to be named for this community event. Piz also created and produced the "Concrete Pond" theme and program for the Inline community which led to breaking ground for sponsorships and new Ice Hockey affiliations with the NHL's Anaheim Ducks contributing to a new model for hockey as a sport in the Southland and other NHL markets. Piz, who's motto is Hockey Helping Hockey, also began gear drives to help provide much-needed equipment for less fortunate children wanting to skate and play hockey such as the first to help benefit one neighborhood in the far-away of City of Chula Vista, CA. As also a co-chair on the Amatuer Athletic Union's (AAU) National Scholastic Committee, Piz's pro bono work for the sport ranges from his hands-on participation with day-to-day management of the IHF to lending his marketing expertise and sponsor negotiations. In October 2009, Piz's history with the sport and his passion were covered by writer Phillip Brents and published in California Rubber magazine [read: http://tinyurl.com/JayPiz-IHF-CaR ]
