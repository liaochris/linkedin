Carri Bugbee's Summary

I’m the owner of Big Deal PR, a virtual agency that does social media marketing, PR, influencer outreach, community management, content marketing, advertising, and special events (real or virtual). I have 20+ years in traditional public relations and advertising, but in recent years I’ve embraced tools and tactics such as Twitter, Facebook, community building, search marketing, blogging and YouTube to help businesses build better customer relationships and boost sales.

I've spoken about social media best practices at numerous conferences, including OMMA Global, Social Fresh, South by Southwest Interactive, the Cool Twitter Conference (Seattle keynote, Chicago and Los Angeles), The 140 Characters Conference, O’Reilly Twitter Bootcamp, OnHollywood, WebVisions, Innotech and more.

I'm a contributing author for "The Big Book of Social Media Case Studies, Stories, Perspectives" and I I teach a class in social media marketing at Portland State University. I’ve also provided social media training for many businesses and organizations such as the Portland Metro Government. I'm teaching in the first-ever social media masters program (created by Social Media Club) as part of the Online Marketing Summit in February 2011.

In 2009 I received an international Shorty Award in the advertising category for being an outstanding content producer on Twitter while tweeting as @PeggyOlson, a character from the AMC hit drama "Mad Men." I’ve also been a finalist in the Sammy Awards’ best twitter branding category and the SoMe Awards “wildcard” category.

I founded Portland Social Media Club in 2009, served as president for two years and remain on the board. In summer 2008, I helped produce and market CreateCamp (Cre8Camp.org), which was the world's first Bar Camp for creatives. In 2008 I also helped resuscitate the Portland Creative Conference (Cre8Con.com) after it had been dormant for seven years.

Specialties

Big Deal PR creates strategic marketing campaigns using social media, PR 2.0, advertising and SEM for businesses and organizations in hi-tech, entertainment, health/fitness, retail, education, travel/hospitality, pet products, electrical technologies and construction. Big Deal PR also partners with other creative firms to provide PR for them or their clients. These creative companies have included ad agencies, animation studios, graphic design firms, film/broadcast companies and Web developers.
