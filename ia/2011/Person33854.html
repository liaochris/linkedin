Patrick Bohr’s Summary

I have worked in various aspects of Software Quality Assurance for 15+ years through 3 companies with 9 company names via buyouts. I've worked with a lot of languages and compilers, language compiler SQA, System testing, Integration testing, Database SQA and application SQA.
Most recently I have added SaaS application testing experience to my list through vAtuo/AutoTrader.com/Cox Communications.
My current goals include options of Quality Assurance and System Testing in newer environments, with modern open languages, and standard tools and methodologies. I am recently interested in Java, Oracle,QTP, loadrunner, winrunner, etc. I am also interested in Agile or Lean methods but only developing knowledge and skills with respect to these. I have read a lot on a lot of related topics and have a long experience history.
I am also drawn into the entreprenuerial world through the startups I am involved with recently. I have been learning about all the aspects of business and how they interact, of course I put a QA perspective on it all.
