Kia Jarmon's Summary

Kia Jarmon is the Creative Director of The MEPR Agency where she leads client initiatives, new business development, and the company's community involvement.

Kia has received honors as the Top 40 Under 40 from the Nashville Business Journal, Top 30 Under 30 from the Cystic Fibrosis Foundation, and is a nominee for the Athena Young Professional Leadership Award and the Nashville Business Journal's Women of Influence Award.
