Leo van Schie's Summary

Effective business partnerships have inspired me throughout my career. I have been working with strategic partnerships, channel and communities in many roles during 20 years in the ICT industry.

In 2004 I've founded Partner Performance to bundle specific knowledge about partnerships. Partner Performance is providing services to build and optimize alliances, channels and communities. Our services comprise of management consultancy, interim solutions, specific tooling and programs and training.

We support companies with a market entrance (using partners) to Europe but also build communities (themed Social Networks) around common themes or passions using Social Networking and other Web.2.0 applications.
