Carol-Ann Simon, CA, CPA’s Summary

I'm a specialist in expatriate/foreign national taxation. I started my career in Canada, and now do cross border work for Canadian / US taxpayers, as well as all other countries. I assist individual expats/inpats with their US and foreign tax returns, international tax planning and minimization. I assist their employers with relocation compensation package design, tax cost projections, equalizations, policy design, and payroll/accounting issues related to having expatriate employees.

Carol-Ann Simon, CA, CPA’s Specialties:

Tax returns, equalization calculations and policy, program start ups, accounting for assignments, tax cost projections. Just got my Certifying Acceptance Agent status. I can now certify ITIN (Individual Taxpayer Identification Number) applications for foreign nationals.
