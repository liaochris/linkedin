Colijn Van Noort's Summary

Hands-on professional photographer with a very personal approach.
Specialized in commercial and product photography, as well as original wedding, portrait and model photography; My free work consist mainly of nature/landscape photography.

Colijn owns a professional photo studio "Fotostudio Krommerijn" (+ 145 m2) where he works on his studio photography, teaches lessons as well as offers fine-art large format printing, framing, etc. Colijn gives also lectures in workflow and color management for advanced photographers.

Related activities: graphical design, pre- and post processing; marketing & communication; web design and hosting.

Colijn was connected as a teacher to the professional photo school Statief in Utrecht, The Netherlands. Currently, he offers specialized courses to professional photographers

Follow me on twitter: @cvnoort

Specialties

commercial photography, reclamefotografie, bedrijfsfotografie, event photography, product photography, product fotografie, food photography, fashion and model photography, glamour & beauty photography, wedding photography, portrait photography

Advances courses in photography, color management and digital workflow
