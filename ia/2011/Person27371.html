Subject: Letter of recommendation for Robert Hodges

During the last year, I have worked with Robert in his position as Supplier Quality Engineer with VAC Magnetics:
Robert was instrumental during the last 12 months assuring quality excellence in the newly designed electronic polyphase base being assembled by VAC in their KY facility. He familiarized his team with all the GE ctq's for parts, subassemblies and final assemblies...thereby creating the "as left" quality plan that the GE Meter Quality team embraced as their "as received" audit discipline...for each of the (14) different meter bases in the catalogue index. His continued effort to make samples of varying failure modes, also led to the standards being used in the functional / hi-pot tester that now serves as the manufacturing firewall prior to pack and ship. Quality metrics for the initial 50k units sold in 2003 show a dramatic improvement over the existing product line of electronic polyphase meters, and has resulted in a strategy to cease assembling "old" style by year end...a more aggressive exit than originally anticipated. The product is now being moved to a Low Cost Asian Region, and the quality plans developed in KY will be the basis for the
continued success after the vertical integration in the VAC CHINA facility.

He showed great enthusiasm and work ethic. On many occasions worked with the GE team to increase capacity, while never compromising quality. He is very conscientious and creative, and takes great pride in his contributions. I would not hesitate, in fact would request the opportunity to work with him again on any assignment where effort and attitude enables the success of a Project...
