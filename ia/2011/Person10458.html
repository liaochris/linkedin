Daryl Rother’s Summary

I'm an HR guy that helps people sleep better at night. That could mean a comprehensive analysis of your business model, or helping you find the next step in your career, to ensuring that your company is appraised of the strengths, risks, & opportunities of your corporate culture. I push the needle to ensure you are driving in your direction of choice.

Want the buzz words? Specifically, my expertise includes the following qualities:

• Strategic professional with a background in talent acquisition, organizational development, employee mediation, performance management, total compensation, policy development, & legal compliance.

• Expert in aligning trajectories between executives, managers, & employees that drive corporate culture and foster trust & respect amongst our shared vision & objective.

• Catalyst as change agent utilizing technology, systems development, and metrics analysis to deliver results aligned with business strategy.

Daryl Rother’s Experience

  • Human Resources Consultant

    Verity Credit Union

    (Non-Profit; Banking industry)

    June 2004 — Present (6 years 9 months)

    Embodied role as a driver of business strategy while managing HR’s effectiveness. Worked with executive and senior management to create programs for recruitment and hiring, performance rewards management, benefit offerings, strategic and tactical training, and policy development. Championed system upgrades for HRIS, recruitment, and timekeeping/payroll management. Negotiated and administered benefits programs while improving awareness of total compensation. Navigated workplace issues to foster continual alignment to business objectives.

    Key Results

    • Developed a workforce planning model to proactively identify org development and training needs.
    • Led creation of performance profiles that benchmark expectations and promote continual learning.
    • Revamped performance appraisal program, focusing on measurable achievements and accountability.
    • Partnered with executives to develop a company-wide bonus program aligned to strategic objectives.
    • Negotiated medical plans keeping premiums lower than industry average, without reduction in plan design.
    • Implemented a new HRIS and payroll system with improved functionality and self-service delivery channels.
    • Facilitated creation of HR policies and procedures, providing transparency and sustainability in processes.
