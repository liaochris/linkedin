Glen Gilmore's Summary

Principal of Gilmore Business Network, a NJ-based social media consulting firm, Glen is also a practicing attorney. He is the senior social media marketing advisor to Memphis-based Howell Marketing and to Harrisburg-based Deeter Gallaher Group, public relations and marketing firms with clients ranging from Fortune 500 to small businesses and non-profits.

He is an Adjunct Professor at Rutgers where he teaches Digital Marketing and Social Media Law. He has developed and provided customized training for J&J.

Memphis Daily News wrote “Gilmore is well-versed in all things Web-related.” He serves as a social media consultant to the New Jersey Hall of Fame.

With over 100,000 followers on the social network Twitter, Gilmore shares social media information on his primary account @GlenGilmore, as well as through various niche accounts, covering topics from social media law to healthcare social media marketing.

Gilmore served eight years as mayor and police director of NJ’s 8th-largest city, Hamilton. He chaired NJ's League of Municipalities Economic Development Task Force, as well as the NJ National Brownfields Association. Glen led Hamilton to achieve its greatest period of economic growth, job creation, and open space preservation, as well as recognition by Money magazine as “one of the best places to live in America,” and by CNN as “America’s 10th safest city.”

Lauded a “national hero” by The New York Times and a “man of action by TIME magazine for his leadership during America’s anthrax crisis, he appeared on Good Morning America, CNN, Fox News, and BCC. An adjunct instructor with Texas A&M University, he has assisted in the development and delivery of crisis leadership training funded by the US Dept of Homeland Security.

A former prosecutor, he completed a judicial clerkship with the NJ Superior Court, where he was also a certified mediator. He also served as Communications Director to a NJ Legislator.

He served on the RWJ Hospital Board of Directors.
