Dimitrios Pitenis’s Summary

Company Overview:
A. Pitenis Bros SA is one of the largest production companies in the Greek food industry. The company was founded in 1973, as a delicatessen appetizer industry.

By December 1997, the company completed the construction of a new modern appetizer production plant, with the acquisition of 14.000m2 land area and building facilities of 3.000m2 in order to efficiently handle the demands of domestic and international competition. The long-term confidence that consumers have shown in the company΄s products, as well as its “family-business” characteristics, lead to the production of more than 100 products by the year 2002. Since day one, the company's business ethic have assured the high quality standard of all its products.

In March 2003, the company established A. Pitenis Bros Co., a subsidiary company in Tripoli, for the distribution of products in Peloponnesus. By 2010, the company further facilitated the distribution of goods in Central Macedonia and Attiki by setting up branches in Thessaloniki and Athens. Overall, with its independent distribution network in Western Macedonia and more than 50 dealers worldwide, Pitenis supplies over 10.000 outlets.

Aim:
The company's main aim is to preserve the traditional Greek taste creating high quality products using the best available raw materials from the Mediterranean market and the most modern production techniques. The continuous effort of the company is not to compromise quality for profit.

Certifications:
> System Assurance & Quality Management "ISO - 22000:2005", which includes the Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Points "HACCP",
> British Retail Consortium "BRC" Global Standards for safety and quality products,
> International Food Standards "IFS" Global food safety system.

The company has started the necessary process for two new certifications: "KOSHER" (Keep Our Souls Healthy & Eat Right) and "HALAL" Lawful Certification.
