Cheryl Smith’s Summary

I help businesses, non-profit organizations and individuals understand and utilize social media to engage customers, partners and colleagues. I blend more than twenty years of marketing experience in both private sector and non-profit organizations with the ability to translate how to use emerging technologies - in human terms. Engaging people.

I also facilitate projects, staff or board retreats, meetings and conversations. I'm passionate about engaging people in conversation to reach objectives, sometimes discovering new objectives along the way.

I speak to groups on a variety of people-potential topics including generational differences, customer service, communication, networking and life/work balance and faith-related topics. You can find out more at http://www.culturesmithconsulting.com/meeting-planners
