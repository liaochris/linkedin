Jon Gisby's Experience

Director, Future Media and Technology

Channel 4

Public Company; Broadcast Media industry

2007 – 2010 (3 years)

Member of the Exec team at Channel 4, and appointed to the Channel 4 main board.

Ran Channel 4's digital services and technical operations, including full P&L and operational responsibility for its online sites and pioneering VOD service (4oD).
Created a scalable and profitable business: doubled traffic, increased revenues by 50%, improved user satisfaction, and achieved industry leading engagement metrics. This was delivered while significantly reducing both operating costs and FTE's.
Delivered landmark syndication deals, maximising the reach and impact of our content, and securing our first mover advantage in VOD.
Attracted and retained key talent in the organisation, delivering projects which won the lion's share of industry awards.
Launched 4iP, an innovation fund to finance early stage investments and pilots, building relationships and projects with a new generation of creative entrepreneurs.
Helped secure a change in Channel 4's statutory remit to ensure it engages audiences on new platforms.

Member of the pre-incorporation boards of Project Kangaroo and YouView
