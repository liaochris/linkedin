Sharon Y Lopez’s Summary

Purple Giraffe Productions (PGP) offers individual, non profit and corporate clients a broad range of innovative planning, production and communications capabilities. We apply a strategically focused plan to assist you, your company or organization in conceptualizing and managing your event, creating excitement about your project, forming sponsorship partnerships, increasing your visibility and assisting in satisfying your goals. We measure our success by your ability to relax and enjoy your event, rather than stressing out over the details.
