Steve Thompson's Summary

Steve is an entrepreneur and marketing professional with experience in business development, product management, marketing strategy and start-up consulting. In 2008, Steve co-founded and led the mobile communications company Textfinity, competing against 215 start-ups to be selected as the 2008 and 2009 winner of the Edson Student Entrepreneur Initiative. In 2009, Steve joined Global Crossing as a Product Development Specialist, organizing the 2010 and 2011 strategic planning for the global marketing team; project managing 3 global initiatives valued at over $8 million; and facilitating the creation of the Global Portfolio Management Office. In 2011, Steve joined Tallwave as a Venture Services Associate, assisting in business planning, strategic marketing and product management efforts, providing strategy and execution to take new ventures to market.
