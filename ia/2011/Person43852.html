Tom McLean's Summary

A senior-level general manager of online products with nearly 20 years experience in web site, book, magazine, and newsletter publishing. With a background in direct response and integrated marketing strategy, I currently oversee the P&L and digital product development for online paid content products: ConsumerReports.org and Consumer Reports Auto Price Services. Experienced in customer acquisition and retention through: SEM/O, direct mail, print, display, radio, mobile and social media. Background in cars, electronics, health, and home/garden markets. Strengths include: creative and branding strategy, digital product development, organizational management, cross-functional team leadership, project management and multi-channel marketing.

Specialties

Product Management, digital product development, SEM/SEO, digital product strategy, mobile, branding, direct response, radio, print, vendor negotiation, team management.
