Cameron Shaw's Summary

Cameron has over 10 years of product management experience, focusing on consumer facing media and entertainment products.

Cameron built the first subscription music service at AOL-Time Warner and later worked with Apple to integrate the iTunes catalog into AOL Radio and to make it easy for AOL subscribers to pay for iTunes content using their AOL payment method. In fact, you can still sign into iTunes using your AOL credentials. Cameron also led AOL's Digital Media Platform effort, which included product ownership of the AOL Media Player. Cameron built the first user-generated video platform for AOL, which interfaced with the AOL Video Player. Later at KickApps, he wrote the product specification for that company’s WYSIWYG video player app studio and then headed up that company's ad operations.

At LimeLife, Cameron introduced social media tools like Facebook Connect, forums and profiles, image galleries and sharing and used game mechanics to boost engagement.

Specialties

Product and Project Management, Matrixed-Team Leadership, Ad Operations.

Specializing in user-generated content and virtual goods. Proven track record working with web technologies Javascript, Flash, XML, HTML, DHTML and ActiveX as well as PHP, Java, client development and content management.

Project Plans, Scope Document, Business Requirements Specification, Functional Specification, Technical Specification, Gap & Risk Analysis, Competitor Analysis, Partner Development & Education
