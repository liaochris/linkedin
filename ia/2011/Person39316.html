Scott Sampson, MS’s Summary

Scott D. Sampson is a Business Systems Analyst/Systems Tester at The Vanguard Group in Valley Forge, PA. Scott's experience includes prior roles in Human Resources and Retail Management. Scott holds a Bachelor's Degree in Business Administration from S.U.N.Y. Brockport, and a Master's Degree in Human Resources Administration from Temple University. At Temple, he was awarded the Dean's Certificate of Excellence for outstanding academic achievement. He is on the Board of Directors for the Philadelphia chapter of the International Institute of Business Analysis (IIBA) as the Vice President of Programs and is the Social Media Ambassador for Temple University's Fox School of Business and Management Alumni Association. He is also a member of the Network of Women in Computer Technology. In his spare time, Scott is an avid reader and volunteers to help the homeless.
