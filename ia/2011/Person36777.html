Richard Heaslip’s Summary

Rick Heaslip is President and Founder of Programmatic Sciences, a newly formed consultancy specializing in the improvement of organizational capabilities in program, project and portfolio management. In this capacity, Rick works with executive management to assess organizational capabilities, to define specific improvement goals, and to design and deliver focused change programs that address organizational needs. Through Programmatic Sciences, Rick provides a variety of services to improve any organization’s ability to successfully manage complex and dynamic programs, including:

• Executive workshops on the development of innovative and adaptive organizational cultures, and the distinct leadership skills required for their success
• Leadership training in program and project management, customized to organizational needs and goals
• Individualized coaching in leadership skills

Rick is also an Affiliated Faculty member at the University of Pennsylvania, where he is responsible for development of core curriculum in Program Management and Leadership, as part of Penn’s Executive Masters Degree Programs in Organizational Dynamics. In this capacity, Rick has worked closely with executives from diverse organizations to improve competencies in the management of complex programs. Rick is a frequent speaker in workshops and forums on leadership development and the improvement of organizational capabilities in program, project, portfolio, resource, and performance management.

Rick is recognized as a successful pharmaceutical industry executive, acknowledged for insight and innovation in the leadership of pharmaceutical development programs. He is known for his:

• History of success in pharmaceutical product development
• Success in leading initiatives related to innovation, value optimization and performance management
• Leadership in the design and use of enterprise project and portfolio management systems
