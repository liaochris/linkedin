Chia-Li Chien, CFP® CRPC PMP’s Summary

Chia-Li Chien, CFP®, CRPC, PMP; is CEO& chief strategist of Value Growth Institute: creating business value that transforms your world. She helps grow business in VALUE not just in revenue. She is a Midas Advisor of MidasNation, a community that is dedicated to helping private business owners increase the value of their firms. She is the author of Show Me The Money (Award-Winning Finalist of the “Best Books 2010” Awards by USA Book News) and columnist for WomenEntrepreneur.com & Fox Business online. Her interviews appear in New York Times “You’re the Boss” Blog, etc.

Chia-Li Chien is pronounced Jolly Jan.

She can be reached at www.chialichien.com or www.ShowMeTheMoneyBook.com or valuegrowthinstitute.com or jolly@chialichien.com.
