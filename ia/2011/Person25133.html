Kathi Kruse's Summary

Kathi Kruse is the Founder and President of Kruse Control Inc.

Kathi is the co-author of "Next Up - Essential Social Media Strategies for Auto Dealerships" Download free eBook here: www.krusecontrolinc.com

Born in the heart of Los Angeles to a family of ‘car people’, Kathi’s interest in cars blossomed on the freeways of Southern California. Her passion for cars and a superior aptitude for business catapulted Kathi into a career that combines her two loves.

Kathi started at the ground floor of a dealership in 1979 and methodically worked her way up through all levels of dealership operations. Kruse Control has won numerous awards for achievement working with dealerships to increase annual sales to over $100 million while winning #1 in sales, service, parts, and customer satisfaction.

Since 2001, Kruse Control has bridged the balance between 30 years of ‘best practices’ dealership management experience and unconventional thought to generate results for dealerships and manufacturers across Southern California and US.

We are passionate about the car business. Our goal is to be the best at identifying, qualifying and delivering innovative solutions that enhance our clients’ bottom line. At Kruse Control, we bridge the balance between time-tested methods and unconventional thought to provide a new perspective. We apply our 30 years of ‘best practices’ dealership management experience to deliver solutions that make sense for our clients.

Fresh eyes, Key experience….that’s Kruse Control.
