Kimmo Nurmisto's Experience

CMO, Co-founder

AudioDraft

Public Company; Music industry

August 2009 – February 2011 (1 year 7 months)

AudioDraft arranges crowdsourced sound design contests that attract thousands of talented sound designers. With AudioDraft, companies have access to a community of motivated and highly skilled designers, ready to compete with their audio work for monetary prizes, acclaim and visibility.

I was one of the four founders that created the internationally unique service concept. My daily tasks included product development (interface design, graphic design and frontend coding) and the sales and marketing of the service. AudioDraft was on of the winners of the business competition Venture Cup in 2010 and and took part in Aalto Venture Garage’s BootCamp program. AudioDraft has been featured international business media such as Reuters, Venture Beat and Springwise and in local media such as Kauppalehti and IT-Viikko, The service has attracted clients such as Marimekko, YLE, Salomon and Gamehouse.

The project gave me a unique view in to the challenges of creating and growing a global web service and hands-on experience for conducting business in Silicon Valley in the US.

Production Manager

Activeark Oy

Public Company; Marketing and Advertising industry

March 2009 – August 2009 (6 months)

Activeark is a full-service digital agency with offices in three locations around the world. As a production manager I was involved hands-on in project management and in the development of the internal production processes. I was one of the managers leading a team of 15 digital media professionals ranging from creative to tech people. I was involved with various kinds of digital projects varying from smaller Internet sites to larger cross media marketing campaigns. I was the project manager responsible for the Nokia account and I managed the production of the highly successful N900 product launch campaign.

Sales and Marketing Manager Finland

Spreadshirt

Privately Held; Internet industry

September 2008 – February 2009 (6 months)

I worked in Spreadshirt (www.spreadshirt.net) as the sales and marketing manager of Finland as a part of the Nordic sales team. I handled the shop partner and the direct to customer businesses for the Finnish market. In the shop partner business I identified, conceptualized and acquired new leads and shop partners and handled the project management of the implementation of the new partnerships. For the existing shop partners I did marketing and created campaigns. In direct to customer business I did search engine optimization, affiliate marketing and created and implemented new campaigns.
