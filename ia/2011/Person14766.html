Ferran Ferrer’s Summary

Dynamic leader, manager and team builder with over ten years of experience specialized in strategy management, process improvement and cost reduction in manufacturing and service companies with a strong analytical, high energy and persistent culture. Proven track record managing simultaneous projects with international and multicultural teams and managing negotiations with clients and suppliers. Well-versed in lean manufacturing, 5’s, 6sigma in order to achieve process effectiveness and reduce waste. Effective resource utilization, production scheduling, manufacturing management and new products development with wide experience with JustinTime, TOC and Kanvan production systems. Excellent communicator and leader of people from all levels of operations with a talent for training and empowering staff.

Experience in consulting services, cosmetic packaging and single-use medical device manufacturer companies. Fluent in several languages, including English, Spanish, Catalan and French.

I have an Executive MBA, a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering and a Masters in Engineering. In addition, I have taken several specialized courses in Project Management, Production Management (TOC) and Global Strategic Management.
