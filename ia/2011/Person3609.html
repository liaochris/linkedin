Arindam Pal's Summary

Arindam Pal is currently pursuing his PhD work at IIT Delhi. He is working in the areas of Algorithms and Machine Learning. He is interested in knowing the limits of efficient algorithms, and how they can be applied to network design problems. His other interest is to know what it means to learn, and how machines can be made to learn, especially in the online scenario.

He completed his Master of Engineering from Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore and Bachelor of Engineering from Jadavpur University, Kolkata, both in Computer Science and Engineering.

Specialties

Algorithms, Graph Theory, Combinatorial Optimization, Data Mining, Machine Learning.
