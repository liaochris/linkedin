Randy Snyder's Summary

Over 25 years of experience in Corporate Financial and Sales roles have given me solid fundamentals in those disciplines. My most recent role involved overseeing the development of financial plans for the Gas Distribution segment at NiSource. Other experience includes managing a telecommunications business-to-business sales team, and directing a regional market support group that included inside sales, product support specialists, contract/sales order processing, and finance decision support. I have managed or worked within most areas of the finance discipline. I have worked in both corporate environments and in field operations management roles. My goal is to improve the product, processes and people in every business opportunity.

Specialties

Employee development, business valuation, economic analysis, budget development, project team leadership, and vision casting.
