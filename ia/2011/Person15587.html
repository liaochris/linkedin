Gary S. Winterton's Summary

Gary is President of OrangeSoda, Inc., Gary joins OrangeSoda after having served as Executive Vice President of Logoworks a Hewlett-Packard Company. Prior to joining Logoworks, Gary Served as Vice President of Sales at Enhance Interactive, (formerly ah-ha.com). Gary was one of the primary executives that led and contributed to the company's rapid growth and its eventual sell to Marchex (NASDAQ: MCHX). He has held a variety of senior leadership positions throughout his career, including: Founder and President of Internet Marketing Concepts, Board of Director, Wiworks.com, Western Region Manager of First Things First sales, and Consultant for the Covey Leadership Center, as well as Vice President of Clear Image, a Utah based production company. In these capacities, Gary has innovated and delivered significant results in various sale and business development channels. He has developed strategic relationships with companies such as, Sun Microsystems, Snap-On Tools, Avon, Tupperware, Hewlett Packard, Amway International, Morinda, many others. Gary attended UVSC, University of New Orleans, and holds a BS degree from Brigham Young University
