Ron Walters’s Summary

25+ YEARS SALES/MANAGEMENT EXPERIENCE IN THE PAYROLL/HR MARKETSPACE!

My passion is to meet and help corporations and business professionals to eliminate non-productive accounting chores (and headaches), and empower them to spend time working on their business!

I have been blessed to have spend time with a few Fortune 100/500 Companies i.e. ADP, Ceridian, and Oracle. I have effectively marketed and sold outsourcing and in house HR/Payroll/Time and Labor centric solutions to thousands of corporations in Florida, New Jersey, Pennsylvania, and the Southeastern US. With my extensive sales training and experience, I developed an uncanny ability to gain access to “C” level decision makers, which always insured my success in all sectors of business.

I relocated from the Cherry Hill, NJ/Philadelphia area to South Florida in 1993. This relocation was part of a struggling blue chip corporation’s strategy and desire to bring the “New England” work ethic here, to insure an increase in sales, and elevate their bottom line.
