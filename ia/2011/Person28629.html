Martin Townshend’s Summary

Managing Director of Infrastructure UK, providing innovative IT and Communications solutions to SME’s in the South-East. We specialise in the design, build, install, support and optimisation of solutions that integrate core business applications, such as Customer Relationship Management, with Cisco Unified Communications.

Our experts in local and wide-area network design, wireless networks, network security, network storage and building surveillance ensure you are protecting your investment for the long-term. By complementing our Cisco expertise with SIP, VoIP, IPT, calls, Broadband, data lines, cabling, maintenance and support services, we are able to provide an all-encompassing communications package for our customers through a single supplier. The result is often a reduction of operating costs, increased staff productivity and improved customer satisfaction resulting in increased market share.

My professional experience has spanned nearly 20 years in ICT and has included the successful delivery of multi-million pound Unified Communications programmes in enterprise organisations. These have taken projects from initial concepts, production of business cases, negotiation of supplier contracts, procurement of the solution, forming project teams, delivery of solutions and gradual handover to ongoing support teams.

I am particularly interested in working with businesses to understand how they can make best use of these technologies, all aspects of developing Unified Communications and developing partnerships with local organisations that would like to offer these solutions but don’t want to setup their own Cisco expertise in-house.
