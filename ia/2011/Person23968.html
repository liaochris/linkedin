Keithen J. Akanni, CPC (kakanni@lucasgroup.com)'s Summary

I worked in "Big Four" public accounting for five years before going into recruiting. I joined Lucas Group to become an experienced financial recruiter and accounting/finance technical topics specialist.

Lucas Group is North America's premier executive search firm. Since 1970, our culture and methodologies have driven superior results. We assist mid-tier to Fortune 500 clients find transcendent, executive talent; candidates fully realize their career ambitions; and associates find professional success.

Combining local knowledge with national reach, Lucas Group has the geographic breadth and industry depth necessary to deliver results. We provide trusted, consultative service by truly listening and responding to the needs of our clients and candidates. We are professional, high-performance, and passionate industry veterans with both insight and integrity, making a very real difference in businesses and careers. We listen. We understand. We deliver.

Across all industries, Lucas Group provides superior executive search for a variety of practice areas, including: Accounting; Finance; Human Resources; Legal; Marketing; Manufacturing Operations; Military Transition, and Technology

We also deliver premier recruitment services for a variety of specialty areas, including: Advertising, Aerospace/Defense; Consumer Products; Construction; Energy, Hospitality; Medical/Bio Pharma; Oil and Gas; and Supply Chain/Logistics.

If you are looking for someone with experience in accounting, finance, treasury, credit, or someone who knows the ins-and-outs of Sarbanes-Oxley and corporate transactions? Let us look for you. We'll do it fast and well. Then, we'll follow up after the placement to insure you are completely satisfied. We guarantee your financial placement will be right on the money!
