Imani Laners's Summary

Imani Laners brings more than 10 combined years of solid online, broadcast and print sales experience to every endeavor she pursues. As the current Interactive Sales Manager at WPIX, she is responsible for providing turnkey digital marketing solutions that the station can offer to both agencies and directly to clients. A critical member of the Tribune Broadcasting team, she play a pivotal role in managing national campaigns as well.

Prior to WPIX, Laners joined Black Enterprise in February 2007 as their Interactive Sales Manager and was promoted in less than a year to Interactive Sales Director. In her role, she led the development of short and long-term online sales strategies for BlackEnterprise.com, working with clients such as Beyond Petroleum (BP), American Express, Xerox, and Microsoft. Over the course of her tenure, she increased online revenue 100% and identified a variety of new revenue streams.

Before leading the digital sales team at Black Enterprise, Laners was an Interactive Account Executive with 24/7 Real Media. Along with building successful online campaigns for top-tier clients, including Ford Motor Co., CarMax, Publishers Clearing House, and Consumer Reports, she was successful in securing new business that generated an additional $750K in revenue. Laners was personally selected by the CEO to break ground in the Detroit automobile market by creating an innovative behavioral targeting strategy aimed at prospective auto buyers.


Laners was born in New Orleans and raised 1 hour outside of Seattle. She is a devoted wife and mother and enjoys traveling with her family. Laners is a graduate of the University of Maryland University College earning a bachelor’s degree in communications. She currently resides in Northern New Jersey with her husband Jean and daughter Sophia.
