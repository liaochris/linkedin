Vince Chaplin's Summary

Vince came from a building industry background, before taking on the challenges of implementing an environmental management system in QANTAS, auditing its global operations and gaining it public recognition for its improvements.

A tumltuous stint with the NSW Govt, building sustainability into the Sydney 2000 Olympic Games (Homebush) venues was the springboard into a role in a startup metals and scrap recycler, which has grown through acquisitions and takeover to become the major steel and scrap company in Australia.

Vince is a skilled functional leader, auditor, ISO14001 EMS specialist, speaker and mentor to State EH&S managers, stakeholder relationship builder and passionate advocate for the scrap metal recycling industry having once been the President of the Australian Council of Recyclers.

Specialties

Corporate environmental strategy alignment, ISO14001, Govt and NGO liaison, audit, sustainability advice, green building, life cycle analysis, contaminated sites, resource recovery, stakeholder management
