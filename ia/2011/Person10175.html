Daniel T Santos’s Summary

I started working when he was 19 and I had acquired a very diverse experience: from Purchase Administrative Assistant, to Sound Designing, Cultural Animation and Interface Design, I work now for Ideavity, Lda, where I'm involved in designing GUIs (Graphical User Interface).

From 2003 to 2006, I worked in part-time as a Cultural Animator on the Association for the Museum of Transport and Communications [Associação para o Museu dos Transportes e Comunicações], where the main goal was to prepare participants for an active role in the Radio an TV workshop that I was leading.

In 2005, I had finished in Sound and Image, with a Extension Course in Digital Art at Portuguese Catholic University of Porto.
While graduating as a Digital Artist I had developed interests in Georeferenced Applications, Virtual, Mixed and Augmented Reality and it was with this kind of knowledge that I had co-created the first Portuguese Georeferenced Game (X-Fetch).

Due to the multidisciplinary character of my background and my actual job, I had focus my attention on designing Interfaces for RIA's [Rich Internet Application] and the strategic side of planing and managing design of Ideavity's organization.
