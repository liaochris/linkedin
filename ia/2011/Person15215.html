frederik de wilde's Summary

Frederik De Wilde studied fine arts (MA), audio-visual arts(MA) & followed a pre-education in architecture, studied one year of philosophy and concluded his studies with a post-graduate degree in new media, arts & design at TRANSMEDIA where he was initiated in software programming. Frederik is collaborating with LAb[au], Q-o2, The University Hasselt (BE), Carnegie Mellon University (USA), Rice University (USA), Karlsruhe University (DE), Rochester University (USA), the University Ghent (BE) and the VUB (BE).
