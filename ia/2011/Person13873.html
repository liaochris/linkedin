Eric Fowler’s Summary

Technology & Music Professional

President of Venice Technology Group a business technology consulting firm. Enterprise level experience leading corporate IT in multiple industries / across multiple disciplines

CIO/CTO level experience directing technology for firms such as RealD, 3Arts Entertainment, Beacon Street Studios, Pajamas Media, Actuarial Consultants Inc. Strategic Financial Group, Gilbert Krupin, New York Food Company, United Food Group, Altoon & Porter Architects. Steven Ehrlich Architects,

Specializing in business process and IT infrastructure analysis, and implementation, project management, network security, business continuity/disaster recovery, regulatory compliance, technology planning & budgeting.

Currently providing consulting services to DirecTV IT Finance Department overseeing budget reporting for information technology solutions delivery department project portfolio. Managing development of an integrated Clarity / IBM Cognos platform based P.O. level project budget tracking platform.

Managing partner of Bijou Groove a music production company specializing in custom music for film, television and web productions.

Composer, guitarist, singer, songwriter, audio engineer and producer featured on platinum recordings by artists such as Sting, UB40, Roseanne Cash, Taylor Dayne, General Public, Clint Black & Kelly Price. Currently a member of longtime Los Angeles band Boxing Gandhis.
