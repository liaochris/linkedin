Amilya Antonetti’s Summary

As Chairman and CEO of AMA Enterprises, Amilya Antonetti leads a team that Discovers, Develops, and Delivers consumer brands and products that provide better choices for today's consumer. AMA's team leverages decades of experience in Electronic Retail, Direct Retail, Traditional Retail and Distribution combined with media relationships and visibility to develop and execute on go-to-market strategies that place consumer brands in the RIGHT channel at the RIGHT time so that our portfolio companies and partners get the RIGHT results.

Amilya rose to the national stage in the early ‘90’s, when she discovered that the cleaning products she was using to clean her home every Tuesday were causing life-threatening ailments for her infant son. This led to the launch of Amilya’s Soapworks, a line of hypoallergenic, non-toxic, and biodegradable cleaning products. As one of the first Mommies to go “green”, she documented her son’s journey back to health in her first book, Why David Hated Tuesdays. Her Soapworks story has been featured on Oprah, CBS News as well as in Time, People, and Working Mother to name a few and as a regular guest on CNBC’s “The Big Idea with Donny Deutsch”.

A regular contributor on Fox “Strategy Room's Business Hour with Eric Bolling”. Via social and traditional media, as well as in live appearances, she reaches audiences in a wide spectrum ranging across the corporate and entrepreneurial sectors and into the Mommy Market and Green sector. With her humor and compassion, she gets it and she connects. Amilya has addressed audiences alongside some of the greatest speakers of our generation, including Tony Robbins, Zig Zigler, Oliver North, Katie Couric and Hillary Clinton.

In her second book "The Recipe: A fable for leaders and teams" (May 1, 2010), she shares her recipe for leadership and team building - two key ingredients in creating your best business AND your best life.

Amilya Antonetti’s Specialties:

Creating products and services for mass markets, Helping inventors/Business owners bring products from their mind to the "Right" market space, working closely with QVC/ShopNBC and other mass retail outlets to highlight these products & services. Speaker, author and media personality.
