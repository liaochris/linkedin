Aurelia Mitchell Durant, Esquire's Summary

Aurelia Mitchell Durant is an attorney specializing in Intellectual Property law. Ms. Durant specializes in brand protection for entrepreneurs and creative business people. Ms. Durant has represented clients in protecting, enforcing, and capitalizing intellectual property rights (trademark, copyright, and trade secret) including litigation, arbitration, prosecution (procurement), counseling, licensing, and acquisitions. She has represented solo-preneurs, business coaches, visual artist, musicians, songwriters, speakers and fashion designers. She has also represented clients in cases involving trademark infringement, domain names (cybersquatting,UDRP), false designation/advertising, unfair competition, breach of contract, copyright infringement, and trade secret misappropriation, among others.

Ms.Durant is also at sought after professional speaker and regularly conducts various webinars and seminars on Intellectual Property topics. She is also a regular speaks to on w various organization across the country about women’s’ topics and has particular niche in speaking to teenage girls about social and emotional development issues. Ms. Durant is a very business savvy entrepreneur and has started and maintained several successful businesses. She has also served on the board of directors for several non-profit corporations.

Ms. Durant current reinvention has focused her attention “brand protection” and the preservation and cultivation of Intellectual Property. Ms. Durant’s ever-expanding calendar of educational events can be found on her website, www.ismybrandprotected.com, where she helps her clients to answer YES to the question, “Is Your Brand Protected?”
