Becky Lang Staffieri’s Summary

Beginning my 10th year as a marketing professional.

As Marketing Specialist with The Rosen Group, I manage all of the marketing functions for the company, including creating the overall strategic plan for company’s marketing. I am responsible for promotional branding, direct mail campaigns, advertising, public relations, off-site promotions, website management and internet marketing.

Prior to The Rosen Group, I worked as a key member of the marketing department of the Greater Philadelphia Cultural Alliance. At the Cultural Alliance, I managed programs to help increase the marketing capacity of our non-profit arts and culture member organizations, including a cooperative advertising program; buying and planning awareness and promotional marketing; and coordinating and managing our yearly members’ event and marketing training series.

My career started at the New Jersey State Aquarium (now Adventure Aquarium) planning special events for general admission and group visitors; and purchased and bartered for advertising of programs and general awareness.

My experience in marketing and events management, coupled with my master’s degree in public relations, makes me a well-rounded marketing and communications professional.
