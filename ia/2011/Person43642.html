Tobias (Toby) Teeter's Summary

I'm the President and Co-Founder of Localbase, which helps local businesses generate qualified leads from the social web. Localbase powers multiple pay-per-lead programs resold through distribution partnerships.

I'm also operate Teeter Capital Partners, LLC, through which I dabble in venture capital and commercial real estate development. My private equity investment portfolio includes the Gryphon Building (http://joplinoffices.com) and Advanced Diamond Technologies, Inc. (http://thindiamond.com).

Previously, I lived in Kansas City where I founded, grew, and eventually sold the fastest growing company in the metro (per the Kansas City Business Journal). It composed of several large online ad distribution networks, the largest network of online city guides (at the time), a socially conscious search engine, various desktop applications, and a product comparison distribution platform.

The balance of my career includes stints as a lobbyist, litigation attorney, and a corporate attorney (Mergers & Acquisitions, Contracts, IP licenses, etc).

Specialties

Mergers & Acquisitions, Corporate Development, Licensing Contracts, Internet-related law, Social Media, Local Advertising, Social Marketing, Venture Capital, Private Equity, Intellectual Property, Licensing, Online Advertising, Mobile Application Development,
