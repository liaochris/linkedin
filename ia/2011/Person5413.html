John- Blackred Apparel's Summary

John Walsh is founder & designer for the online action sports apparel brand known as Blackred. John attended college at Suffolk University in Boston Ma and focused his studies around Communications- Marketing/Advertising/Public Relations.

Once graduated from college in 2006, John designed the Blackred logo which started as a small sketch on paper and quickly turned into a lifestyle for many.

About the Brand:

Blackred is a committed partner in the action sports industry with a sole mission to spread the word on living a positive and healthy lifestyle. We work very closely to establish positive environments for the growing youth of all ages to express both creativity & style though these sports

These same kids are the future of this industry and it's an amazing feeling to see them grow as people and athletes. We realize the negative influences in the world today and work exceptionally hard to get more people involved with action sports, staying active, and living with a positive outlook on life. Blackred is 100% dedicated to accomplish our goals as a brand to better this overall community.

Specialties

Public Relations, Advertising, Video/Photo, Social Media, Marketing, Creativity,event planning.

John- Blackred Apparel's Experience

Blackred- Action Sports Apparel (Self-employed)

Self-Employed; Apparel & Fashion industry

March 2009 – Present (2 years 1 month)

John Walsh, who developed the concept of Blackred, has enjoyed watching his brand grow from the ground up, but more than anything the relationships & friendships that have been formed along the way.

“It’s been a great ride so far and hopefully one that will continue for a long time; says John”. With so many years committed to the silk-screening industry as a younger guy gave John a sense of design and style that he intends to incorporate into his brand. John tied his past experiences in design with a lifelong passion for action sports; and after seeing the industry change & expand over the years really made him push to create his brand.

“With the launch of social media sites like Twitter and Facebook have made business very interactive and enjoyable. It's a really neat feeling to wake up each morning and get your customers excited for another solid year; says John”.

*Blackred was created to give the fans who like these sports as much as John a new wave a style and a brand that will stay committed to grow with the action sports world.
