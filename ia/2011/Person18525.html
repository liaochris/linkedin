Jack Sumner’s Summary

Jack Sumner is a recognized leader in the Customer Relationship Management (CRM) and Business Intelligence space with over eighteen years of professional experience. Jack has a proven track record of delivering business results through investments in technology with extensive experience in strategy development, account management, large-scale enterprise solution delivery, process reengineering, master data management and managing the human aspects of technology projects (i.e. cultural change management).

Jack possesses strong analytical, quantitative, and creative problem-solving skills developed through extensive experience across leading organizations including Lanier Worldwide, Blue Cross Blue Shield of North Carolina, American Cancer Society, American Heart Association, McKesson Corporation, Merck Medco, PROMINA Health Systems, State of Georgia, CARE, ADT Security Systems (TYCO), Jacksonville Electric Authority, Siemens Energy and Automation, Chaparral Steel, Georgia-Pacific, Overnight Transportation, and the United States Navy.

Jack has provided technology leadership to the manufacturing, distribution, insurance, financial, security, military, utility and non-profit industry verticals.

Jack earned a Bachelor of Science degree in software engineering from the Georgia Institute of Technology. Jack served as the President of the Customer Relationship Management Association from 2002-2005. Jack also sat on the Microsoft advisory board for their CRM product and is a contributor to Gantthead.com, a virtual community of over 75,000 IT Project Managers and
