Rick Ellis's Summary

As Director of Channel Partner Development with AIS Media, an award winning interactive agency, Rick is responsible for developing the expanding network of Partner companies that provide our interactive marketing and social media services worldwide. He works with entrepreneurs and companies to enable them to become part of this exciting industry.

Prior to joining AIS Media in 2004, he was founder and Managing Partner for an Atlanta-based mergers and acquisitions firm. Chase Business Services provided organizations and individuals with corporate finance services, acqusition and divestiture as well as business consulting. Rick was involved in assisting more than 1,100 companies and individuals in their entrepreneurial ventures throughout the southeast.

Previously, he had spent many years in radio advertising sales and broadcast management in Atlanta.
