Edward Jarvis’s Summary

Executive Profile:

General Electric-trained, professionally developed competencies include positions of President, COO and VP of Sales & Business Development.

I possess more than 25 years’ experience in Entrepreneurial pursuits, corporate turnarounds, manufacturing, international outsourcing and international business development for Fortune 500 companies,

From designing One Xcel, the only optically correct protective eye wear available for helmeted sports to bringing world-class rowing shells out of the college realm and into the mainstream for Alden Rowing Shells, LLC, I understand exactly what it takes for any company to realize its global business potential.

My day-to-day responsibilities include driving and enabling growth, differentiation and improvement for all EntrataChina clients. I oversee company programs and I am responsible for bringing innovation to the business enterprise.

I am an internationally renowned expert in the fields of business planning, venture capital raising and new venture development. My industry experience includes consumer products, application software, manufacturing, professional education, business consulting and food products.

I am a former Executive-in-Residence at Suffolk University. I have served on numerous advisory boards, including Newbury College, University of Maine Law School, Bridgewater State University, Suffolk University and appointed by two governors from The State Of Maine to sit on the Board of Five Business Development Incubators.

I have been profiled by CNN, CNBC, Business Week, Success Magazine, US News & World Report, American Business Network, US News & World Report, Sports Illustrated, Success Magazine and Hoover Institution Stanford University.
