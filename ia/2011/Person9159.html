Connie Roberts's Summary

I am product review writer who also writes about websites, services, and charitable organizations. I currently author three blogs:

-Brain Foggles, product reviews and giveawaways,information about blogging, social media and being a work at home mother.
-My Chronic Life, about chronic illnesses, being an advocate for people with disABILITIES of any kind, education on health and wellness, and medical supplies. Written with a sense of humor and positive attitude.
-Connie's View, family news, information on crafts, food, holiday celebrations, education, maintaining a happy and socially enriched family. Product reviews and giveaways are also included.

I also maintain a website for free and paid advertising regarding eBay listings and stores, as well as providing eBay selling techniques, providing a free link exchange and maintaining information on non-profit organizations. I offer a Disney Vacation Countdown Package, which provides "autographed" postcards and gifts.

I maintain Squidoo lenses about eBay, Disney and medical information, a non-profit (DOUA) lens and its group page. Also help to promote this group of which I am a member and mentor on websites, forums, blogs and social networking sites.

Assist members of Disabled Online Users Association (DOUA) with promotion of their online endeavors, especially blogging, and editing of their listings, stores and websites.

Am active on Twitter, Facebook and blogging forums to connect with others, maintain business relationships, lend a hand and enjoy the time online while working.

Specialties

Product review writer, professional writing and editing, online sales, business marketing, social networking, blogging, and promotion of non-profits.
