Liam Clarke's Summary

Versatile and creative earth scientist with eight years experience in the oil and gas industry working as a Logging Geologist, LWD/MWD Engineer and more recently as a Geotechnical Specialist for BHP Billiton Petroleum. A strong communicator and dedicated team player who enjoys the challenges that come with working across geographical boundaries with a wide range of clients.

A strong educational and professional background in Petroleum Geology combined with proficiency in a number of industry standard software programs including OW-SeisWorks, Petrosys and ArcGIS make me an ideal candidate for junior to mid level geoscientist roles.

Specialties

Sequence stratigraphy, basin analysis, seismic interpretation, petrophysical log interpretation and quality control, geological evaluation, production monitoring, data management, drilling optimisation, directional surveying and analysis, migration, transfer and manipulation of petrophysical and drilling data, business process modelling, information management and technical support of EDMS (Documentum) and GIS, geoscience software support (Open Works-SeisWorks, Petrel, OFM).
