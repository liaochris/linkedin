Yaron Samid’s Summary

Yaron Samid is the founder and CEO of BillGuard (formerly CrowdSpot), a personal finance security startup harnessing the "collective vigilance" of consumers to protect everyone from unwanted charges on their bills. He previously co-founded Pando, the web's leading P2P CDN with over 40 million users, and lead product management and marketing teams at Zend, BackWeb and Register.com.

Yaron is also the founder and chairman of the TechAviv Founders Club and NYVideo.org, two Internet entrepreneur organizations that help hundreds of startups succeed in New York, Silicon Valley, Boston and Israel.

Current board seats include Aprigo and Pond5.

Yaron Samid’s Specialties:

Consumer Internet startups, UX/UI, PM, BD, Marketing. Big data crowdsourcing.
