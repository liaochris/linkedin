John B. Carroll, CPA, CTP’s Summary

An experienced finance professional specializing in Corporate Finance, Treasury Management, Financial Planning & Analysis (FP&A) and Accounting. A proven ability to identify critical financial issues impacting performance and drive results. Strong problem solving, business planning, negotiating and leadership skills with the ability to execute and succeed under extraordinarily challenging conditions. Throughout my career, I have continuously sought to increase my skill set and apply it towards improving the financial condition of my employers, though always with the utmost integrity. I am best at identifying and solving financial problems, raising capital and reducing financial risk.

Led treasury teams on frequent capital market transactions, improving liquidity and company’s capital structure during periods of financial stress.
