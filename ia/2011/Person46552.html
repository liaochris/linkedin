Zacharias Beckman's Summary

During two-and-a-half decades in technology, I’ve directed multimillion-dollar, multinational efforts in entertainment, finance, energy, legal, and government contexts. I’ve built and directed global technology divisions for clients such as WongDoody, The LA Times, Siebel, SeeBeyond, Fios, and Xerox. With my dual technical and business skill I successfully build collaborative relationships throughout engineering and guide organizational strategy in the C-suite.

I developed United Future’s Enterprise media division and directed Yoostar™, delivering the 2-year multivendor project on time for worldwide launch with Best Buy and Harrods. At Practice Technologies, I built a smoothly executing multinational development team, turning out new legal work product tools on schedule every six months and cutting costs by nearly $1.8M per year. At Xerox, I developed the business strategy and directed ProCare (today known as Xerox Sentinel™) and lowered customer care costs by 70% worldwide. At NASA JPL, I developed the training programs used today on the International Space Station. Working closely with Steve Jobs, I led strategic programs for NeXT Computer in law and energy sectors. I have managed departments ranging from 25 to 350 personnel, have led cutting-edge multimedia projects, and coordinated global initiatives. My leadership consistently results in delivering on time and on budget.
