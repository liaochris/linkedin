Paul Rispens's Summary

My job is helping people away from poverty by reducing debts and setting up a solid financial structure with which the future gets a better outlook. I have founded the Stichting Nooit Meer Rood Nederland in 2003, which provides information, tips and stories via internet on www.nooitmeerrood.nl. In 2004 I started a small company in which the actual debt reducering activities are brought together. Per 2006 this company is a member of the cooperative association SVF of which I am co-founder. Some other activities are brought together in another small company, Rispens.Net.

Specialties

Negotiation with debtors, social interaction, being a point of stability for people.
