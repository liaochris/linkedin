Arnaud Collery’s Summary

My nickname is Monsieur Challenge. I am currently managing the top brand sponsored community French Tuesdays Tokyo. I am also seeking theatrical and tv distribution for my first feature film. My real love remains the stage as an actor or stand up comedian or lecturer (motivation based).

Business and Finance.
Stints/Internships in brokerage, offshore banking, financial advising and M&A. Paris, Gibraltar, Tokyo, HK, New York. Last finance position was in 2000-20003 when I lead the investor relations team for a European Stock Exchange s communication partner firm in Paris.

Politics and International affairs
Graduated as a military officer (EIREL) and in international law (American U of Paris) I worked in politics in Brussels for the European Union researching on financial crime under the leadership of the late Judge Thierry Jean Pierre. Political campaign volunteer while in Japan. Grandfather was a senator in Champagne, France. Father was a regional congressman.

Luxury Business
Worked on Champagne wineries and sales during college, stints in the cosmetic sector and as a communication executive for Cartier in Japan. Some Fashion modeling for Japanese and Chinese catalog.

Entertainment Field
Produced 24 local tv comedy shows, 15 comedy events, 2 shorts comedy movies, 1 feature. The two shorts were at the MONACO FILM FESTIVAL ( May 2007). Journalist/Mc/host at the LA film festival TV 2005, Comedy store LA 2006-2009, Beijing Film Festival 2008, omegatv.tv 2009, Broadway Comedy Club NY . Stints on MTV and E! as a comedian and semi finalist of Last Comic STANDING on NBC on June 2008.

Early 2010, after 18 months across the world, I completed LITTLE KLAUS BIG WORLD, an 82mn English speaking docu-comedy somewhere between Borat and Forrest Gump. Financed by Japanese private equity + grant from the Chinese Government. Now seeking distribution.

Member of SAG & FIND and LA marathon runners.
