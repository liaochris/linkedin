Tania Usher's Summary

Connecting with others is a passion and I do this through hosting events that unite high-profile experts to share their knowledge and wisdom. It's been an exciting journey being behind the scenes creating and managing festivals, seminars, road shows and workshops in the UK, USA, China, Hong Kong, Italy, Netherlands, Australia, South Africa, Belgium, German, Thailand and Czech Republic.

I launched Connecting Mumpreneurs™ and the International Women's Day - Whole Woman Festival to encourage women to embrace the empowerment of entrepreneurship.

My soon to be released book get NAKED...get noticed advocates saving advertising dollars and leveraging the power of free publicity. I've been featured in media around the world including most recently the Today Show, Grazia Magazine, Paradise Magazine, Gold Coast Bulletin and the Daily News.

I'm also a speaker and MC for events ranging from hundreds to several thousand and have presented on platforms with renowned entrepreneurs, comedians, celebrities, politicians, entertainers, best-selling authors and health care professionals.

I work together with both female and male entrepreneurs and business owners to enhance communication effectiveness, performance and business development.

It’s always rewarding to see a client take their first big leap into entrepreneurship. And as they commence on their journey to grow a successful and sustainable business it’s exciting to be their backstop, offering encouragement to take their next big and often frightening leap.

Whether you're just getting started or are a seasoned entrepreneur, we'd love to see how we can best support you with bringing your business goals to life.

Together we'll strip back the layers to reveal the core, authentic you. We'll uncovering fresh perspectives. And we'll support you with harnessing your inner strengths and self-belief to build a business and life that supports the very best of who you are.

To keep connected, visit www.GetNakedWithTania.com
