Janette Beetham's Summary

Qualified and experienced Social Enterprise Adviser/Consultant working regionally and nationally. Specialist areas include; Social Impact Measurement, Social Accounting and Reporting, Marketing for Social Enterprises, Exploring & Developing Trading Potential. Social Auditor (designate).

Associate - British Dyslexia Association. Dyslexia Specialist, Business Consultant, Trainer and Coach. Workplace Needs Assessor listed with Access to Work (Department of Work and Pensions) and Trainer of Workplace Needs Assessors for the British Dyslexia Association.

Passionate about working with individuals and businesses to maximise potential.

'Having overcome learning challenges myself I am passionate about helping people reach their full potential in the workplace and help them on their journey to really understanding the challenges and benefits of their learning difference'.

Interested in undertaking contracts nationally, regionally and local.

Janette has experience working with 'hard to reach' groups and those overcoming disadvantage. She uses plain english and multi-sensory delivery techniques whenever possible to facilitate full participation.

She has a cross section of training/coaching experience including delivering all Business Link Start-up workshops and personal development/ pre-pre start-up programmes entitled 'What Are You Waiting For?' which have been a real 'launch-pad' for budding entrepreneurs. She also designs and delivers bespoke Social Enterprise training for a cross section of organisations including Business Link Southeast and Busiliness Link Advisers. (Feedback from this is available on request). Other training examples available.
