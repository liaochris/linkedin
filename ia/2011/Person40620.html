February 1993 – Present (18 years 2 months)

This is my BBS. I started it February of 1993 using a shareware version of TriBBS 5.0. By the month of May I was introduced to the ProBoard BBS software. I loved it. After that I had a goal to register everything, all my doors and ProBoard within the next 5 years. Living from check to check made it hard, but by 1998, I was finally ready to register ProBoard. That's when I read on the internet it's future was unknown. I kept popping in from time to time to check on ProBoard at proboard.com, when finally one day it told I couldn't enter unless I had a user name and password. I continued to run ProBoard though through my dial-up/telnet setup. Eventually I dropped my landlines for just a straight telnet system. Currently I'm running a hacker release of ProBoard called ProBoard 2.17, and I'm working on cracking ProBoard 2.20I. I have cracked ProBoard 2.20I so it thinks it no longer expires, well it expires, but not until like June 1st, 32767. Besides ProBoard Innovations I try to run a great ProBoard support site.
