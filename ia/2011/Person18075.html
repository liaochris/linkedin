Jeff "Classic" Popka's Summary

I have an unique blend of corporate management experience and an event/management/promotions/publicity background.

I have the foundation of corporate training and structure along with leadership skills coupled with financial forecasting and P&L responsibility experience.

I have a strong public speaking background while coordinating various events including entertainment productions (music). I have produced and/or promoted over 500 various type events during my career.(Including over 20 Rock-n-Roll shows).

I currently host and produce 3 internet radio shows: Indie on Air!, Music in Action and The Indie Spirit w/ Kelly Ballard.

I am a new media pioneer according to some within the industry. I find that amusing. I love promoting & producing music events and acts.

I am interested in expanding my audience, platform & event reach. I have been called a dreamer and a visionary.

I am currently producing a 20+ city tour in the US called The Abolitionist Tour. This tour is a partnership presented by Indie on Air!, Planet L.A. Records & the Not For Sale Campaign. The goal is to present the hottest indie bands and bring awareness to the causes of Human Trafficking/ Modern Day Slavery/ & Forced Child Labor. I believe music is the bridge to bring people together to accomplish good things. This tour starts in early 2011 and culminates in Austin, TX the week of SXSW. Following this tour I am planning a West Coast Tour along I-5 from Vancouver to San Diego which I hope to launch in the summer of 2011.

I am always open to job offers and collaboration of a mutually beneficial kind. I will only work with those who share the same vision of doing good and helping others while building their own success.
