Luke B Renchan’s Summary

I am a professional disc jockey who has been in the business since 1993. Our company was originally founded in 1983 by my father, Luke T Renchan. He is also the owner of Luke's Record Exchange in Pawtucket.

Luke's Disc Jockey Service was more of a passion for me. When I started during high school, I was working full time for my father's music store and performing small events on the weekends. I never thought that being a disc jockey would be a career for me.

People have many different oppinions on Disc Jockey's. I feel that we are different because we trully care about who are clients are and what they are looking to achieve by having us perform at their event. It is not just about what music to play, but what can we do to help plan with the client to make their event the most talked about for years to come. Whether planning an immaculate wedding, to a corporate function that needs to really stand out, we have the experience to achieve that. We offer planning assistance and can provide additional resources from Karaoke, a video screen with the music videos, performing on a game show, or maybe some team building for those corporate trainings. Ultimately, it comes down to trust. We personalize each event down to the very last detail. So when planning your next event, contact us for a consultation.

Not only do I love working as a Disc Jockey for my own company, I enjoy working with other companies as well. I am the president of the Southern New England Association of Professional Disc Jockey's. I started this group back in October of 2006 and we are now up to 50 members. What an amazing group of Disc Jockey's. Many are competitors, but we now look at each other more like collegues, many even close friends. If you know any disc jockey's, send them information to me. We would love for them to visit us.
