Kim Daniels's Summary

I’ve worked in various aspects of technology for years, doing everything from hardware and server support to web design and development. I have a deep appreciation of and love for technology and information, and have never been happier than when working in news/new media.

I have worked with both newspaper and television broadcast companies to make their web sites attractive and easy to navigate - making sure that the layout highlighted the most important news elements first and that the proper SEO elements were in place.

In 2009, I decided to give a chance to the side of me that is not only a techie, but a writer and news junkie as well. I created YoLadies.com and along with a few fellow writers, created a great website geared toward women. In the year that we’ve existed, we’ve built up a loyal following – something I’m proud of having done with only the investment required for hosting costs and lots of hard work and devotion.

I am looking forward to continuing on this path of creating and curating great content, all contained in a beautiful package.

Specialties

HTML, CSS, Javascript, Various social media platforms, SEO techniques, Movable Type, Wordpress, familiarity with PHP, Perl, and JAVA, familiarity with Flash, actively work with Photoshop, Illustrator, and Fireworks, great sense for the stories that people want to hear/read, ability to keep a cool head in a fast paced, breaking news environment and to stay motivated in slow, dull times.
