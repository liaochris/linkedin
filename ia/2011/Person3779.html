Goran Paunovic's Summary

Goran Paunovic has spent the better part of his career helping companies focus their branding and marketing messages, and giving voice to their corporate identities. Goran founded the full-service graphic design firm ArtVersion in 1999, and ArtVersion has since provided numerous clients, including Fortune 500 companies, with web-design, multimedia, corporate identity, and marketing services.

Goran has a strong expertise in creating and re-developing corporate identities and brand strengthening. “Among all products equal in functionality, cost and quality, the one with the most appealing design will always stay on top.” Goran said. “It's impressive to see how executives and their teams are willing to put forth the effort to make their marketing messages really represent their company. Many of them depend on our creativity and forward thinking. From idea to completion, we work hard together, and that is the only way of creating ingenious and effective materials. I'm personally greatly impressed when my Fortune 500 clients show me that they really care about their customers, and don't mind going the extra mile or expanding their budgets just to make sure that the marketing material relay means something to the consumer."

Specialties

Goran received his professional training at the Polytechnic Academy of Belgrade, Serbia, and has received numerous awards for his creative work. Goran is an active member of the National Association of Photoshop® Professionals, and of the American Institute of Graphic Art and Art Directors Club. In 2004, Goran was nominated as a Leader of the Year.
