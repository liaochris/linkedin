David Moritz's Summary

I am a keen and ambitious account manager with a key focus on forging strong relationships with my clients and offering highly optimised and tailored campaigns and services that go beyond the traditional. I try to ensure I keep on top of the latest developments in the industry and that I am aware of my client's operating environment and their ever increasing demands.

I understand that ROI and client retention is key, and meeting expectations simply is not enough. Today's unstable and increasingly competitive climate demands out-of-the-box thinking coupled with attainable and measurable targets. Managing expectations is key.

My role at DSGi allowed me to develop the skills necessary to sell and promote products and services in a fiercely competitive online environment. At the most basic level, this involved ensuring all product data was accurate and current, total compliance with merchandising standards, and offering relevant cross-sells and up-sells to each given product.

I was also involved in manipulating baskets of goods to ensure that the optimum multi-buy and when-bought-with (wbw) offers were made available to customers to guarantee not only an attractive offering, but also an offering that maximises the gross margin for the company - critical for a company whose products generate low margins.

I have experience using numerous content management systems (CMS) as well as a self taught knowledge of enterprise networking solutions including Microsoft Windows Server, Microsoft Exchange Server, Microsoft SQL Server and Microsoft SharePoint.

My previous role at Caffé Nero allowed me to develop the skills necessary to manage large groups of staff in various locations, administer multiple budgets, and ensure the day-to-day running of several of the company’s flagship stores. Analysing purchasing habits and trends, allowed me to grow the area of business I was responsible for.
