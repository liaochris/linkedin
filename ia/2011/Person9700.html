Cynthia Steiner’s Summary

Cynthia Steiner is an award-winning writer and broadcast/video producer with over 15 years experience. Since graduating from the University of Georgia with a degree in Journalism, Ms. Steiner has worked with numerous advertising agencies, production companies and corporations providing concepts, copy, and pre- through post-production services for television and radio, and short- and long-formatted corporate video. She most recently developed and produced a proposed television pilot.

Her experience also includes copywriting for a variety of print media, including direct mail, brochures, ads, websites,and newsletters. She co-wrote a non-fiction book entitled “KIDS ON CAMERA: A Comprehensive Guide to Child Modeling and Acting,” published by Peachtree Publishers, LTD.

Cynthia Steiner’s Specialties:

Complete broadcast and video project management, such as developing concepts, writing, estimating/budgeting, talent casting/auditions, crew hiring, directing, shooting and supervising all audio and video post-production functions, such as 2D and 3D graphic development, off- and on-line editing, voice talent direction, music selection or original music creation, and audio mixing/sweetening. Also an experienced copywriter for brochures, direct mail, ads, newsletters and websites.
