Some highlights from my personal background:

I've managed the entire lifecycle of emerging tech start-ups to various outcomes, including M&A and going public. My expertise includes product definition and development, marketing and sales, in the software, hardware, life-sciences, telecommunications and process control sectors.

My public speaking engagements cover the software development process, and Inbound Marketing and Sales and Inbound Marketing Automation.

I am the author of two books: The Deft Way to Run a Software Project (1988), and a novel (October 2008), entitled Napoleon's Gambit: Sailing through history to commit the perfect crime.
