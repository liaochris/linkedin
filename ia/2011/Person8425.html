Christine Kurtz's Summary

Ms. Christine Kurtz has a B.S. in Chemical Engineering and a M.S. in Project Management. She has extensive project management and process engineering experience and her expertise areas include environmental compliance and permitting, chemical process safety management, environmental site assessments, and sustainability. Ms. Kurtz has over 17 years of experience in industrial operations and served as the Environmental and Safety Manager of a large industrial facility. In addition to manufacturing experience, Ms. Kurtz can provide valuable Leadership based on experience working with salaried and union personnel, regulatory agencies, public and community relations, and clients.

Comprehensive development and documentation of systems to achieve and maintain compliance with environmental regulations.

Liaison between management and legal staff, and local and state regulators for regulatory inquiries and enforcement actions. 

Managed and completed all EPCRA, RCRA, EMS reporting.

Successfully negotiated a Title V Air Operating Permit renewal and favorably settled a NPDES wastewater permit appeal.

Asset Leader for a treatment plant treating 35 MGD of industrial wastewater. Project Manager for a $1.5M Aerobic Selector upgrade.

Project Manager for a $2 million dollar, 100-acre contaminated site from Environmental Site Assessment through No Further Action designation.

Experienced in Process Safety Management (PSM), Risk Management Plan (RMP), Process Hazards Analyses (PHAs), Security Vulnerability Assessment (SVA).

Specialties

Leadership, Communication, Public Relations and Regulatory Liaison, Project Management, Environmental Management, Multi-media Environmental compliance and permitting, Auditing, Chemical process safety, Hazardous Materials emergency planning and response, Sustainability
