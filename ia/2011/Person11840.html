Diane Lim Rogers’s Summary

I'm a Ph.D. economist by training and after a few years in academia have spent most of my career working for or around the federal government. I analyze and write about (as well as speak about) the macroeconomic and distributional effects of federal tax and budget policy. My current goals are to effectively communicate with both the general public as well as policymakers about the importance of fiscal responsibility, to keep expanding my experience in terms of "new media" and "social networking" despite being middle aged and slightly technology-challenged, and to keep having fun with my "work."
