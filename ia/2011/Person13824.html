Erica Lockwood’s Summary

Since joining the Joseph Chris Partners Family of Companies in 2000, Erica has built a rich global network of contacts in the residential and commercial construction, engineering and real estate industries. With over 17 years in Professional and Executive Recruitment knowledge, Erica has established credibility and built trust with countless clients by effectively attracting and identifying top industry talent.
