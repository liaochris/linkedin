Tisha Oehmen’s Summary

Tisha is a marketing, operations, and business development leader with a decade of successful leadership across strategic and tactical planning, program and product development, and community outreach. Possessing expertise in both front- and back-office operations, she conceptualizes, develops, and seamlessly implements initiatives to foster organizational effectiveness, integrate and automate operations, and promote a competitive market position and advantage.

Tisha has spent more than 15 years in the financial services industry, where she utilized her strategic business planning, product development and management, branding, positioning, channel development, and member outreach expertise. She developed and implemented winning programs, specialty products, and services oriented toward member acquisition, satisfaction, and retention. She liaised with community and local business leaders to develop partnerships and affinities for the benefit of both organizations.

Holding an MBA and a BA in Political Science from the University of Oregon, Tisha enjoyed a distinguished academic career punctuated by enthusiastic and successful participation in competitive speaking events. She holds numerous awards for her skill in public speaking and is widely recognized for her ability to capture audiences’ attention with her straightforward, engaging speaking style.

She is extremely active within her community, serving as a member or officer of such organizations as United Way, the Eugene Chamber of Commerce, and Camp Fire USA, and dedicated to fostering and contributing to programs focused on charity, youth support and development, professional networking, and community cohesion and growth.
