www.gliaffidabili.it

Privately Held; 1-10 employees; Internet industry

February 2008 – Present (3 years 2 months)

Gli Affidabili’s main goal is becoming the referral online community for people offering and looking for professional services, without the need to refer to an intermediary. Gli Affidabili offers valuable tools to select the best opportunities such as personal reputation (based on the feedback to offered services) and word of mouth.
In fact, people offering for services can provide detailed information on their services, being always identifiable to anyone looking for the service, and can turn customer satisfaction into an effective promotional tool. On the other side, people looking for services can rely on accurate information on the broad variety of services and service providers profiles, and moreover on the reliability based on users feedbacks.
