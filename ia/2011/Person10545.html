Dave McClure's Summary

I'm a geek, startup investor, former software developer & entrepreneur, tech blogger, marketing nerd, and overall fan of entrepreneurship. I've lived in Silicon Valley for over 20 years and loved every minute of it. I currently run an internet seed fund and startup incubator program in Silicon Valley called http://500Startups.com which has made investments in more than 100 companies as of March 2011.

Previously I managed a $2M early-stage investment program for Founders Fund called FF Angel, and also ran the fbFund REV 2009 Incubator program which made micro-seed investments in Facebook & Facebook Connect-related startups. After leaving PayPal, i made 13 personal startup investments from 2004-2008, including Mint.com which was acquired by Intuit in 2009 for $170M.

I advise and invest in geeks and entrepreneurs creating new consumer & small business internet startups. My specialty is helping startups improve product and marketing efforts by emphasizing customer development, an iterative approach, and the collection and measurement of simple, actionable metrics.
