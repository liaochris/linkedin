Libby Gill’s Summary

After 15 years as head of corporate PR at Universal, Sony, Turner Broadcasting, as well as the branding/media consultant behind the launch of the Dr. Phil Show, I am now an executive coach, brand strategist and bestselling author. My latest book is You Unstuck: Mastering the New Rules of Risk-taking in Work & Life.

I am now an internationally respected executive coach, speaker and bestselling author. My latest book is "You Unstuck: Mastering the New Rules of Risk-taking in Work and Life." I have shared my success strategies on the Today Show, The Big Idea with Donny Deutsch, CNN, NPR, Oprah & Friends Radio Network, Fox News, CBS Early Show, and in Time Magazine, Business Week, the New York Times, the Wall Street Journal, O Magazine, Good Housekeeping, Self and many more.

My clients have included Microsoft, Nike, Pfizer, USAA, Cisco Systems, Lycos, Sprint, Capital One, Lockheed Martin, Deloitte & Touche, Hewlett-Packard, Natural Products Association, The Boeing Company, Triad Hospitals, Price Waterhouse Coopers, International Association of Administrative Professionals, Cadbury Schweppes, PitneyBowes, State Farm, National Association of Women Business Owners, Parsons Brinckerhoff, National Kitchen & Bath Association and more.
