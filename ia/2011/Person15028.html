Francesco Spadoni’s Summary

Francesco Spadoni is Head of Research and Development at Rigel Engineering S.r.l., an Italian ICT company focused on developing turn-key solutions for digital content and rights management, database applications and GIS systems. He has a proven track record in identifying business needs and developing and implementing innovative solutions that result in increased business performance. His expertise in the areas of business development, project management, system integration and networking technologies build on a top level engineering education as well as on international R&D experiences developed by partnering with the major European players in the relevant fields.

Francesco Spadoni’s Specialties:

Projects:
ITEA-DS (integrated emergency management and decision support for maritime applications), FOREMMS (web based GIS for forest management), WEDELMUSIC (design and development of a complete and secure solution for digital music rights management), MUSICNETWORK (a centre of excellence for multimedia and interactive digital content), VARIAZIONI (a content enrichment project for tagging of music content). Attending 2nd year Ph.D courses on Information Engineering.
