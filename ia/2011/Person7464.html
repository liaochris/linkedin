Cece Salomon-Lee’s Summary

Throughout my career, I have demonstrated the ability to adopt new media to achieve marketing and communications goals. In combination with my proven leadership and management skills, I have successfully implemented business strategies from conception to implementation, counseled C-level executives, mentored junior staff, and delivered concrete results on time and on budget. I am an accomplished individual offering diverse technology and Fortune 500 experience that encompasses: consumer electronics and entertainment (Cisco Systems, DreamWorks Animation, Hewlett-Packard); healthcare (GE Healthcare); enterprise software (Hitachi Data Systems, Business Objects, SAP); mobile (Openwave, Destinator); SaaS and online streaming (On24, InXpo); high definition optical storage (Blu-ray Disc Association); Internet and ecommerce (MapQuest, Yahoo!, Ancestry.com, CyberCash); and venture capitalists (Lightspeed Venture Partners, Technology Crossover Ventures, Sutter Hill Ventures).

I am also the author of PR Meets Marketing blog, which highlights the intersection of public relations with marketing and is listed on the Advertising Age top 500 blogs and several PR lists.

Cece Salomon-Lee’s Specialties:

Corporate communications, public relations, analyst relations, blogger and media relations, event and conference strategies, customer relations, social media and marketing strategy, virtual events, elearning, virtual learning & training, online learning.
