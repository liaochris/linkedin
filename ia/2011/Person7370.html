Cathy Colmenares Suarez’s Summary

Cathy Colmenares Suarez has worked to empower the Hispanic community in various capacities for more than 15 years. She has extensive experience in Spanish language advertising, marketing and media. She has also worked with several nonprofit community development, child advocacy and volunteer programs.

Cathy has held senior positions with organizations including Todobebé , a leading producer of Spanish-language pregnancy and parenting content; El Diario/La Prensa, New York’s oldest Spanish-language daily newspaper; StarMedia, Latin America’s first Internet portal; and One Economy, an international nonprofit dedicated to empowering low-income families with technology and the Internet.

Her non-profit work with children and families in low-income communities has been featured in the Miami Herald, Miami Today, on NBC and Univision. She has also been featured as a prominent community leader in Miami Monthly Magazine.

Most recently, Cathy lead a successful fundraising campaign for the Leukemia & Lymphoma Society's 2010 Man of the Year candidate, Fernando Suarez. Through the use of online marketing, social media, PR and events, the winning campaign exceeded its goal and secured over $81,000 in a 10 week period.

Cathy is now combining her love of community development with her multicultural experience to become a bilingual elementary education teacher. The journey continues!
