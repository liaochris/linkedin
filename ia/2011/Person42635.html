Sanjay Gupta's Summary

Founded first business in early 20s, an import/export management company, Connexions Trading International, Inc. First major client lined up within 3 months of company set up. Highlight included the closing of major retail for client with initial sale of $250k in less than 6 months. Other responsibilities included working with major buyers at Kmart, Walmart, True Value, Ace Hardware in the United States and Canada. Key role in managing trade show booths in Canada, US, and German.

Years of import/export management led to the formation of Tea Connexions, a business TM owned by Connexions Trading International Inc in 2001. Traveled to India to find suppliers. Negotiated exclusive importing deal and 180 day payment terms with highest volume Darjeeling tea distributor in Northern India. Used exclusivity and favorable payment terms to pre-sell product using unique business model, Tea Connexions Buying Group (TCBG). TCBG is a membership based tea buying group that was developed by offering entrepreneurs favorable below wholesale pricing, exclusive marketing tools, and e-commerce solutions. Grew corporate revenues 1000% within three years, managed staff of 10, trained more than 200 distributors, set up an office in Australia, set up Shangers Teaopolis Ltd. (JV to promote tea and entertainment) and worked with three warehouse partners in the United States. Tea Connexions nominated for Business and Exporter of the year because of our rapid growth in 2005.

Moved operations and head office to Kingston, ON in 2007. Sold Tea Connexions in 2009. Tea Connexions is now based in the United States, all operations controlled by the Missouri based management.

Today, I am seeking new challenges and would like to work for an organization that seeks out a strategic thinking executive who has significant experience in project management, sales, marketing, contract negotiation, and e-commerce.

Sanjay Gupta's Education

University of Saskatchewan

BA (Honours), History

1987 – 1993

My first two years of University I pursued pre-Med as it was a dream to be a Doctor. After studying physiology, chemistry, physics, and other scientific subjects, I realized that I do not have passion for this. Surprisingly, it was during my second year that I took a history class which I thoroughly enjoyed and excelled at. I really enjoyed the critical thinking, research, and writing aspect, skills essential to business.

I obtained my BA Honors in 1991 and then began a Masters degree in history, specializing in Central America. My specialization took me to Guatemala for 6 weeks. During my travels in Guatemala, I got interested in international business of import/export. This essentially gave me the entrepreneurial bug. After teaching and furthering my studies for a couple of years, I chose to end my studies and eventually started my first business, an import/export management company, Connexions Trading Int'l Inc. to further pursue my dreams of international trade.

Activities and Societies: Executive and active member of many organizations: Youth for Environmental Sanity (Y.E.S.); History Club; Graduate Students History Executive; Students Against Apartheid; Campus Liberals; and many other organizations.
