Street Handball - Takes outdoor handball seriously!

If you add information here, you´ll help.
- New teams / clubs to find each other.

- All videos and pictures provided on these webpages have been approved by the owner.

Back in 2007, when we established Street Handball it was our wish and mission to let people with an interest in handball see how cool it is to play handball outdoors and experience how much fun it can provide. By way of a simple concept. Also, we wanted to show others It´s okay to take handball outside.

- We hope to get more handball updates and news over time.
- Be the concept where people gather so we can become the quickest growing sports activity of our time.
- We also encourage professional handball players to volunteer and show their interest.
- If you are a fan of this page we believe you like to get involved in voluntary tasks. :o)
Because society will become a better place if young people are committed.
