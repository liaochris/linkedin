Scott W. Gray's Summary

Mr. Gray is an engaging and witty writer in a variety of formats, with professional experience providing marketing, advertising, fiction, humour / satire, and non-fiction journalism to a number of vehicles, both print and online.

He was Lead Editor for Xtranormal, Web Editor for Ascent Magazine, a columnist for Matrix Magazine, and Montreal arts and culture blog Midnight Poutine, and he currently maintains blogs about Multimedia Journalism, experimental music and sound design, and being a Boston Bruins fan living in Montreal.

Mr. Gray has been commissioned by Magazines Canada to produce training pieces for Niche Magazine Publishers, and co-authored a national handbook on Human Resource Best Practices for the Magazine Industry.

He is a former member of Magazines Canada’s Consumer Marketing Committee and the Small Magazines Committee, and delivered seminars at magazine conferences on best practices for Direct Marketing on a budget.

Finally, Mr. Gray has been an instructor in the Hip Hop Literacy Project, teaching inner city youth the principles of audio work and music production, and as a recording musician he has contributed scores for commercials, spoken word releases and theatre pieces, as well as performing live in solo and group formats.
