Donna Raney’s Summary

Hi I am Donna Raney, owner of Pet Attention. As an Army brat I grew up on a farm in Arkansas and always had pets and could relate well with animals. I worked in the corporate arena for 20 years (in Atlanta) with my pet sitting business on the side. My company has done well and I decided to part ways with corporate America and give my full attention to my true passion, my love of animals.

Pet Attention is a highly reputable, one-of-a-kind, responsible pet care business. With over 14 year's experience, my company reputation thrives on its motto: "Your pets are our extended family". I treasure, love, energize, enjoy and show special treatment to each and every pet in my care. I cater to you and your specific needs, but my primary concern is making sure your pets are happy and healthy, and your home is secure and safe under my watch.

My business ethics are simple. I live by "The Golden Rule", I treat others only in ways that I'm willing to be treated in the same exact situation.

I will be honored with the opportunity to take care of your pet(s) and your home in your absence and I look forward to meeting you!
