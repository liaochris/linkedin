Ron Lichty’s Summary

Software development executive of best-in-class applications. Software engineering management and product development consultant for companies of all sizes. Published technology author. Passionate communicator of product vision and champion of development best practices, internally and industry-wide.

VPE of Fix-It. Quickly assesses organizational issues to optimize requirements and agile/scrum processes, boost productivity, and strengthen partnerships. Creates product and technology roadmaps. Leads software organizations to deliver high quality products with exceptional customer experience. Mentor, coach and team-builder.

Trained and coached teams in Agile and Scrum at Check Point, m-Via and Merced Systems. Led Charles Schwab to reengineer and standardize its software platform company-wide. Directed SaaS development at Forensic Logic and Socialtext. Got difficult-to-get-started products underway at Schwab, Razorfish and Phoenix Technologies. Created product and technology roadmaps at Check Point, Forensic Logic, Schwab, and Berkeley Systems. Led geographically distributed teams at Apple, Check Point, Forensic Logic, Razorfish, Socialtext and Berkeley Systems. Brought engineering discipline to product delivery in teams up to 50 people at Check Point, Razorfish and Fujitsu. Managed development and delivery of Apple’s UI.

Co-founder, Software Architecture and Modeling SIG, SDForum. Co-chair, Emerging Technologies SIG, SDForum, and Software Development Best Practices SIG, eBig.
