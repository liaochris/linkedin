Vlad Kunko's Summary

For Hire – Meta-Designer / Educational Consultant / Creative Strategist – Product + Service Systems Optimization / Technical Writer

Design, Science, Technology and Business – Hands-on experiential and pragmatic systems-thinking strategist (big-picture realist) interested in research, and/or, project lead relationship with "on-the-bubble" start-ups and established organizations with concepts, new products/ programs/ services, updates, and/or identity: (re)branding/(re)invention in the mix.

Proven profit-maker, adding depth, diligence, and detail to design teams by offering intellectual integrity, insight, and feedback as a professional perspective – based on post-graduate level expertise, research ability, and well developed social, technical, cultural, and ecological dimensions.

I use a Whole Systems / Critical Thinking approach in designating Experience, Meaning and Service.

Client/consumer analysis-synthesis-translation skills cross-functional with: Marketing/Sales; Architects/ Engineers/ Planners; Researchers/ Human Factors specialists; prototypers/ vendors/ fabricators, cost and quality control, and technical staff ...as well as imaginative futurists, theorists, ... and dreamers. I understand and speak your concrete and virtual languages.

Pro-bono work with non-profits, especially on adversity trend issues.

Expert Witness: Product Liability (Design management, process, materials)
