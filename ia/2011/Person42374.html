Tali Tarlow's Summary

I write speeches for all occasions from life cycle celebrations to business presentations. My returning clients will verify that my speeches are well researched, moving, and motivational. Writing speeches that represent my client's authentic voice is my number one priority.

As a ghost writer, I write blogs, newsletters and articles that support my clients' profile and branding.

As I provide a confidential service, I cannot give direct references. However my clients include a CEO of a large organization in the USA, top executives in organizations of varying sizes in America, South Africa, Australia and Israel, as well as other professionals. I get great satisfaction from writing speeches for Jewish life cycle occasions (see http:///www.yourjewishspeech.com and please go to the testimonials page).

I am an experienced teacher and I write educational material for schools and businesses in the USA and Europe. I have developed curriculum,teacher guides, educational websites, experiential programs.

In addition to this I have experience in research, article writing, content development, SEO and editing.

Specialties

Speech writing, writing, research, development of educational programs and teaching aids.
