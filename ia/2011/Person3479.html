August 2010 – Present (8 months)

We are proud to announce our new company name - via680. The via680 name truly embodies our company promise to customers “via” standing for “by way of” and “680” numbers that stand for fluidity, prosperity in business, significant events – hence via680, technology solutions that produce fluidity and prosperity in business as evidenced by the significant events you, your employee teams and your customers achieve through the use of our technology.

via680’s flagship product is intelliSling, a software application that will re-define your paradigm on how to drive increased levels of; employee and customer engagement, participation, intelligence and information exchange.

Users of intelliSling construct and distribute interactive media Slings that consist of a variety of different types of social networking and/or web based communication tools – ranging from digital video, assessments and electronic documents to chat rooms and discussion threads. Typically a Slings target a specific subject and are designed to capture responses from the person viewing it. Captured data is available via the intelliFlow section of intelliSling and allows a business to view recipient responses, measure comprehension and engagement then take proactive actions to ensure expected outcomes are on the right track.

Today intelliSling is being used across a wide variety of applications and industries.
