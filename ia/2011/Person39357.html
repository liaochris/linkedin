Deborah Womack's Summary

A dynamic and passionate senior CRM professional with a long and successful track record of delivering multi-channel CRM strategies including Customer Life Cycle Marketing, Retention Program Strategies and more recently Social CRM programmes.

Drawing on skills and experience gained by working in leadership and consultancy roles across agency, client, consulting and creative, Deborah applies a unique perspective to CRM solution design by focusing on understanding the core business challenges faced by organisations today: building successful customer relationships in ever-changing, challenging landscapes. Through strong leadership, planning, implementation and organisational skills, Deborah delivers brand strategies and CRM programmes that achieve tangible business results.

Since joining LBi London in 2009, Deborah has established a strong track record in leading the development and delivery of large-scale CRM programmes and new business engagements for blue chip clients such as M&S, Electrolux in Europe, BT Consumer and BT Tradesapce.

Specialties

Targeted Digital Marketing Strategies and Online Marketing Transformation Strategies including: Customer and Prospect Email Programmes Development, Multi-channel Marketing Optimisation, as well as Team Development, Proposition Development and Campaign and Marketing Process Design (Six Sigma (uncertified)), dynamic public speaker and various literary contributions.
