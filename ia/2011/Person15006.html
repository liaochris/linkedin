Francesca Bennet's Summary

Structure-property relationships of polyacrylates and polymethacrylates, under conditions relevant to external surface coatings
Polymer degradation
ESI-MS (Electrospray Ionisation Mass Spectrometry) of polymers
Polymerisation behaviour of urethane-containing acrylate monomers
High-temperature self-directed polymerisation of acrylate monomers
Pulsed-laser polymerisation techniques
RAFT and MADIX polymerisation (including degradation of RAFT-terminated polymers)
Synthesis of novel environmentally friendly polymers
Application of fundamental research to industrial processes

Specialties

Free radical polymerisation techniques (especially CCTP and RAFT polymerisation), polymer synthesis, Size Exclusion Chromatography, UV/Vis spectrometry, IR spectrometry, 1H and 13C NMR, Pulsed Laser Polymerisation techniques, Electrospray Ionisation Mass Spectrometry, SEC-ESI-MS system, glovebox, Density meter, TGA, DSC, TGA-IR, design and construction of novel equipment, organic synthesis, mass spectrometry, rheology measurements, wet chemistry analysis, ESR, Instron tensile testing.
