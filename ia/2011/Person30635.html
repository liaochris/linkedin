Mike Costache's Summary

Mike is an entrepreneur with over 12 years experience in investment banking, sales and consulting.

In 2006 Mike established Leo & Leo in Romania, providing management, brand consulting, sales, event planning and PR services to clients such as Maserati, Segway, NetJets, PrivatSea, Sunseeker Yachts, Marsh, Orgil Greenhouses, Miss Universe, and Rockstar Energy Drink.

From 2000 – 2006, Mike served as CEO of Pioneer TeleCare, an e-commerce company distributing office products and other consumer goods. Annual revenues reached $2.5 million and the company employed 20 employees between its offices in Los Angeles and India.

From 1998 – 2003, Mike was an Associate at WestPark Capital, The Interlink Group, Millennium Capital Partners, all Los Angeles-based investment banking firms where his responsibilities included identifying and evaluating new investment opportunities and assisting clients with strategic partnerships.

Mike holds a degree in Economics from Pepperdine University (Malibu, California), a Certificate in Negotiations, Arbitration, and Mediation from Pepperdine’s School of Law and has completed the Program on Negotiations for Senior Executives at Harvard. Mike completed studies in business, history and law in the UK, France, Germany, Italy, Turkey, Greece, Israel and Switzerland.

Since 2003, Mike has served on the Board of Directors and as the Director of Fundraising & Strategy for Blue Heron Foundation, a charitable organization which has raised $700,000 for various projects benefiting Romanian orphans.

Born in Bucharest, Romania in 1977, Mike migrated to Israel in 1988 and later migrated to the United States in 1992. Mike is fluent in Romanian, Hebrew and English and has basic knowledge of French.

Mike was featured in USA Today, Wall Street Journal, Entrepreneur Magazine, Malibu Times, China Central TV (CCTV), and in 2008, Ziarul Financiar (Romania’s nr 1 financial newspaper) named Mike one of the Top 1000 business people.
