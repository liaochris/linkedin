Ronald Croen's Summary

Ronald A. Croen is an entrepreneur, business advisor, teacher, and private investor.

From 2009 thru December 2010 he was Entrepreneur-in-Residence and a Visiting Lecturer teaching Entrepreneurial Leadership at Tufts University in Boston, Massachusetts.

Mr. Croen was founder and CEO of Nuance Communications. Nuance is the global leader in voice automation technology and applications. Mr. Croen led Nuance from the creation of its original business plan and its spin out from research institute SRI International in 1994 through IPO and beyond.

Mr. Croen has deep operating experience in the following areas:

- spinning-out core technologies
- recruiting and developing management teams
- establishing and managing international operations
- financing of early stage ventures

Mr. Croen's accomplishments at Nuance exemplify his skill at working successfully with technical teams to navigate challenging business problems. In 1998 Business Week named Mr. Croen a "Trailblazer" of Speech Technology.

In addition, he has extensive experience in information technology, enterprise software, voice communications, internet video and Web 2.0 businesses. He has been involved with a broad range of companies, from very early stage start-ups, to angel and venture funded growth businesses, to executive management and board service for public companies. As a board member, he has been involved in strategy, compensation, and corporate governance.

Mr. Croen earned a J.D. degree from the University of Pennsylvania Law School and a B.A. from Tufts University, both with honors. He plays golf and tennis, sails, appreciates food and wine and maintains an active interest in current affairs and politics.
