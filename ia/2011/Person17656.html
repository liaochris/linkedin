Howie Isaacks’s Summary

I am currently working as an Apple Consultant in Dallas, Texas.

I provide the following services and support:

-Mac Support (Mac OS X, Mac OS X Server, Classic)
-Kerio Mail Server setup, support, and administration
-Technical Training
-Project Management
-Cross-Platform Integration (Mac, Windows)


In the Navy, I received training in Windows 3, Windows NT and, Windows 95 when I was given an assignment to install and setup several PCs and the U.S. Navy Submarine School in New London, CT, where I trained as an electronics technician.

Upon moving to Dallas in 2000, I began my I.T. career at Earthlink where I provided tech support for internet customers. Later, I began working at CompUSA corporate HQ where I provided support for 50 Mac users in the Advertising Dept.

After CompUSA, I worked for AMX in Richardson. I worked with Apple's retail stores to ensure that their presentation systems were deployed and working properly. In April, 2002 I joined the I.T. department at IntegraColor. I supported Macs, Windows PCs and helped deploy Windows servers, deploying new network infrastructure (running cables, setting up switches and routers, etc.) and built several PCs for the company.

I spent 4.5 years working for Apple. I worked as both a Mac Specialist and as a Mac Genius. I gained certifications in Apple hardware repair and software support. When I began my career as an Apple Consultant with Digital Criterion, I received training in Mac OS X Server.
