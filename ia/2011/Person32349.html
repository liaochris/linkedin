Neil Sequeira's Summary

I have been closely working with startups since 2000 and B2B demand generation and marketing since 2004 I've channeled these years of experience into founding BuzzFactory.net

BuzzFactory.net is a team of passionate writers, bloggers and online marketing professionals who use a blend of keyword rich content writing and social media marketing techniques to drive the right audiences towards a website and build online visibility for the brand on the web. We work closely with our customers to create an online buzz around their offering and cut through the 'noise' with carefully crafted content marketing solutions. .

Prior to founding BuzzFactory.net, I've been involved with several aspects of marketing and demand generation from delivering lead generation projects to technology companies, overlooking operations to creating demand for newly launched products and solutions through online / inbound marketing, SEO and social media.
