Paola Vee’s Summary

After a two years of experimental research on three Italian Tv programs, carried out in RAI, I graduated with honour from Professor Carlo Sartori.
In 1996, I began work as a consultant at Italmedia Consulting, one of the finest analysis labs in Telecommunications and Broadcasting, in which I assisted professor Augusto Preta. I co-authored many research works, cited by the most authoritative Italian and foreign press, including Il Sole 24 Ore and the Financial Times newspapers. Clients: RAI, Telecom Italia, APT (Associazione Produttori Televisivi), and many more.
Then, in my work as the press office and marketing manager at the European School of Economics, I dealt with Nobel Laureates and prominent personalities of the diplomatic world.
In 2001, I accepted the offer of Mecalux and Accenture to start all operations in Italy for the launch of the portal Logismarket. In 2003, I moved to Barcelona to work at the Logismarket headquarters. I worked closely with the Business Development Director, assisting her with the business development management for 5 countries.
From 2004 to 2006, I was the head of marketing for Continental Europe at Leeside.
In 2007, I founded Atovee. Since December 2008, I have been the Editor in Chief of Les Cahiers - Fashion Marketing.
