Pablo Lorenzo Herrera’s Summary

I am a software engineer with more than 15 years of experience in development of applications of all kinds, from client-server banking and insurance environments to Web 2.0 services or online multiplayer games.
I pay special attention to understanding the needs and expectations of the customer with a detailed functional analysis, identifying the organization of the data and the modular division of the application. If it comes to integrating a new development into an existing application, be it a user interface Ajax module for a web page or a complex server process, I always develop stable and fully tested components. I am proud to ensure reliability and quality in the final product.
