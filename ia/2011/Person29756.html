Meri Aaron Walker's Summary

Helping Baby Boomer thought leaders ignite powerful collaborative action has been my passion for over 40 years.

Since 2006, from my home in beautiful Ashland, Oregon, I’ve been having a ball helping my peers shine up their 21st Century Social Skills - and use them to share things that matter. Why? Well, it matters deeply to me that our generation makes our full lifetime contribution. And this means developing ease using social media and virtual meeting tools to pass on things we’ve learned the hard way to young leaders who are creating new, sustainable systems.

Social media tools - and new thinking routines - really do make significant sharing fast, easy and fun. I coach thought leaders individually - and also in small face-to-face and online groups. We use the tools together - kind of like driver’s ed - so people quickly build up confidence, fluency, and autonomy.

I also provide clients access to a statistically valid natural strengths measurement and coach them to realign their choices so they can achieve greater ease and efficiency in their work and the quality of life they've been longing for.

ARE YOU SOMEONE WHO NEEDS THIS KIND OF HELP?

WANT TO BE MY CLIENT?

If you'd like some support for making your lifetime contribution in ways that save your precious time and money for what's important to you, please give me a call.

The only thing you might stand to lose could be some expensive habits - for which my clients tell me they've enjoyed learning new, easy alternatives!

I offer an initial FREE 30-minute consultation and I'd love to learn more about your situation: http://beingsocial.us/pages/contact.

Specialties

Social media coaching and virtual meeting facilitation. 21st century business communication planning. Virtual team coaching. Instructional and interaction design for adult learning. Executive, life, and health coaching. Application of natural strengths measurements to self-management of chronic conditions.
