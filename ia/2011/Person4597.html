Patrick Baynes's Summary

My career began at LinkedIn pioneering customer operations. At the time, social media was just taking off. Now we can all agree, it's definitely here to stay.

Freesource was founded by former LinkedIn employees and built with the help of seasoned consultants from Yankee group and Deloitte & Touche. We are a Business-to-Business social media consulting company, heavily focused on ROI.

Generally, we work with the market two ways:

• Sales Training:
Always custom, we develop a road map for connecting your products & services with your target market in a meaningful, strategic way aligned with your brand and business. This opens a new channel for lead generation and ultimately sales.

• Consulting:
Custom scope projects to solve tough problems and/or explore new opportunities. We have more real experience in social media consulting and generating results than you'd believe. Common services range from strategy consulting and corporate policy development to virtualizing entire operations.

• As for me:
I deliver. Working one-on-one with clients, I hold myself accountable for the success the work; so far I'm doing pretty well. When I'm not working directly with Freesource clients, I serve as the co-founder of business. I'm responsible for very serious, strategic business decisions... and yes, it is awesome.

You're welcome to contact me at pbaynes@freesourceagency.com.

- Patrick
