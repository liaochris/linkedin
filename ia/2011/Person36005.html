Randeep Buttar (randeep.buttar@emico-sm.com)’s Summary

Randeep is a process expert with over 9 years commercial experience of which, the last 5 have been as an independant consultant. Clients have included some of the most reputable (FTSE 100) firms within the finacial services, oil & energy, and healthcare industries.

His key fortes include the development, deployment, management and maturity assessment of ‘best practise’ and business aligned process through the application of core business process analysis skills.

His primary goal is to continue delivering professional process advise and expertise.

His core skillset covers:

BUSINESS PROCESS ANALYSIS
Target Operating Model, requirements analysis, definition and engineering; workflow gap analysis (“as-is” vs. “to be”); business process modelling

PROJECT PLANNING AND MANAGEMENT
“Agile” project management; resource management; delivery and risk management (RAID); stakeholder engagement and management (across distributed environments)

COMMUNICATION AND PEOPLE MANAGEMENT
Excellent cross-cultural communication ability, good presentation, work-shop facilitation; influencing, working group coordination, communication planning and execution; organisation structuring, training and skills/competencies analysis

BUSINESS CHANGE MANAGEMENT AND PROCESS IMPROVEMENT
Excellent appreciation of the cycle of change (“unfreeze”, “change”, “refreeze”); learning and adaptation cycles; how to communicate and sustain change as well as the impact of change to staff (“concerns-based adoption model”)

IT SERVICES
Strategy definition; service definition and cost modelling, supplier contract definition and negotiation; industry best practice and standards

AUTHORING
Document analysis, iterative review and publishing; business processes, standards, policies, procedures, supplier contract RFP/schedule; role profiles

INDUSTRY BEST PRACTICE AND STANDARDS
ITIL Service Design and Service Operation; COBIT based governance and maturity; ISO/BS standards
