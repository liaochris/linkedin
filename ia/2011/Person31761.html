Matthew Ventre’s Summary

Matt is a user experience designer and information architect with experience in designing user interfaces and interactions for web sites and web-based applications.

He has experience in designing, prototyping, coding, and testing web sites and applications following current standards and using semantic XHTML and CSS. He is passionate about creating solutions that solve real problems in the most elegant and usable way possible.

Matthew Ventre’s Specialties:

User experience design, interaction design, information architecture, usability testing, low to high fidelity prototyping, mental models, wireframing, standard-compliant front-end coding (XHTML, CSS, JavaScript), certified Scrum Master.
