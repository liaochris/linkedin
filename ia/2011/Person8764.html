Clare English's Summary

I Joined BMA Communications (now Speed) six years ago as a specialist beauty & lifestyle PR consultant for the beauty category at Tesco, having previously worked with Momentum PR and Nexus PR, on fine fragrance, skincare, cosmetics and generic & OTC pharmaceutical accounts.

In March 2009 I became Speed's business development director; driving the agency's marketing and pipeline activity, working closely with the client service and talent teams and advising on B2C integrated strategy.

Specialties

Speed is a PR consultancy, formed in March 2009 from five existing agencies, to address the impact that media convergence and the growing importance of online and social media is having on PR. 

Our hybrid approach to communications arms our consultants with a leading strategic edge; aligning new and emerging digital tactics alongside traditional media approaches.
