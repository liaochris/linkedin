David Bernard-Stevens's Summary

Helping people develop their own authentic leadership skills that inspire others to achieve their dreams with the understandings that:

* No one can lead anyone until they can first lead
lead themselves.

* That leadership requires knowing one's core values
and inner spirit AND having the commitment to make
choices that are in alignment with those core values.

* That as individuals, we are always at choice and
accountable for our actions and impact.

* To help individuals distinguish the difference between
managing/controlling and leadership.

* Recent Presenter/speaker at the Vison 2010 Leadership Conference in Younde, Cameroon

* Previously selected as one of the top 5 educators in the United States.

* Former state senator for 9 years.

* Former Teacher of the Year for the State of Nebraska (USA)

Specialties

I was born with the gift of communicating ideas in ways that "locks in" learning for people everywhere.

For those open to expanded possibilities, I can guide each to discover or refine their own leadership values and purpose in life (self leadership) and show each the tools to transfer all of that power to those they encounter whether a family, organization, or business. From there, the results that will be generated by their leading WITH others will astound them.
