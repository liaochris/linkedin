Donald Bowman's Summary

Over 30 years experience working for public and private companies, domestic and foreign, as a Controller, CFO and CEO. My experience spans public accounting, real estate development, construction and building materials, retail and management consulting. I am President of Pretium Bay Consulting, co-owner of a retail store and Assistant Adjunct Professor at the University of Maryland University College.

My goal is to help business owners of any size company increase their financial success. I offer a wide range of services such as: bookkeeping, financial reporting, financial analysis, cash flow management, profit improvement, internal control review, growth and capital management, strategic planning, accounting research, coaching financial executives and performance measurement.

Specialties

Completing mergers, acquisitions and divestitures, leading business turnarounds, financing projects, benchmarking and implementing best practices, developing strategic and business plans, managing within changing organizational structures, selecting and developing key talent, building community and business partnerships, retail operations, consulting and teaching.
