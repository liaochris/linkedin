David Izatt's Summary

David Izatt is a Creative Director. His Skills include many aspects of Film and Video Production. Including (and not limited to): Directing, Producing, Writing, Editing (Final Cut Pro), Sound Engineering, Photography, Cinematography and Acting.

He started off teaching creative writing to an online comunity in 2003 before focusing his attention on acting. From 2007-09 he studied various acting forms with Bruce Alexander and then Richard Sarell in Melbourne, Australia. He also did a Master-class in acting with Nancy Bishop in London in 2009.

He is now working predominantly behind the camera and has completed such projects such as The Devil's Whispers and Big Guns and Handbags. Recently he travelled around South East Asia (Thailand, Cambodia, Vietnam, Malaysia & Singapore) with his wife while filming a short travel documentary, before ending up in New Zealand.

In June 2010 he auditioned for a major role in The Hobbit in Auckland, before finally returning to Scotland in August 2010.
