Charles Cefalu’s Summary

Charles Cefalu has been involved in the Real Estate Industry for the past 19 years. Intrigued by the Keller Williams Realty Business and Financial Model, Mr. Cefalu became an Operating Principal and Licensed Real Estate Broker for Keller Williams Realty so he could share the phenominal growth and success of Keller Williams Realty with other Sales Associates.

Currently, much of his time is focused on training and recruiting Real Estate Sales Associates for Keller Williams Realty. He highlights the lucrative commission plan, profit sharing pool as well as the extensive training and coaching programs available.

Mr. Cefalu also handles large commercial investment property transactions as a Director for KW Commercial and routinely performs a comprehensive financial analysis on each property to best serve his Clients.

In addition to his real estate backgound, Mr. Cefalu has over thirteen years experience in mortgage banking having underwritten, funded and traded individual residential, commercial and land mortgages and portfolios on the secondary market with institutional clients that included consumer finance companies, community banks, insurance companies, private equity funds, REIT’s and private investors.

Mr. Cefalu was also responsible for managing the collection and servicing of a large mortgage portfolio as well as sales and the disposition of foreclosed real estate (REO) on a national basis. During this same period of time, Mr. Cefalu was actively involved in the capital markets by promoting both private placement and public offerings of debt and equity securities.

For 12 years, Mr. Cefalu also owned a Title Insurance Agency that served the entire State of New York. Mr. Cefalu has developed and managed various real estate projects and multi-family apartment units.

Charles Cefalu’s Specialties:

My greatest asset is my ability to problem solve and this is a tremendous benefit to my Clients in any real estate transaction or development project. My finance, engineering, construction and real estate background allow me to quickly review a project, highlight critical issues and then put forth a plan to secure necessary approvals, capital and financing structure. My creative approach is necessary in this Challenging Economic Environment to achieve desired results for Owners and Investors.
