Kayne Burk’s Summary

I love the adventure of working with sharp, talented people to create new products and build businesses. I led the team behind ShopWell, IDEO's first spin-out from their new entrepreneurship group; we raised $2.9m in venture funding. At MindSwarms, I brought key talent on board and worked with the team to develop an initial strategy. My latest startup has a product in early beta testing.

I gravitate towards challenging problems and intellectually stimulating experiences, often requiring immersion in diverse, new fields. As an undergraduate at Texas I programmed for a local startup, taught a Cisco sponsored networking lab, interned with Accenture's strategy group, and launched my first business‚ all before graduating with dual degrees in liberal arts and business. I then honed my analytical abilities at Goldman Sachs, managing $1.2 billion in invested equity across 17 real estate portfolios in four countries. I left Goldman to attend the Stanford Graduate School of Business, where I focused on entrepreneurship while studying design methodology at the Hasso Plattner Institute.

I currently work as a consultant to startup companies while developing AddressBookUpdate.com with a cofounder in my spare time.
