Ali Naqi Shaheen's Summary

I am currently involved in Run a Shop GmbH - an eCommerce platform for the "Third Wave of Innovation". I'm managing product roadmap, sales, marketing, operations, and finances - though I am looking for people to join us in all those areas!

My team and I envision a platform where merchants have access to tools that enable them to promote their products easily and effectively. On the other hand, the publishers are enabled to monetize content and traffic in an optimized way. Lastly, and most important, consumers have an improved and optimized shopping experience.

I have set up companies in Asia and Europe; have unique experience in bootstrapping my company from South Asia to Europe and working on technologies ranging from embedded operating systems to highly scalable web portals. I have also consulted with startups and businesses on technology roadmap development, marketing and sales plan, operational optimizations and raising investment.

Specialties

I am a technology entrepreneur with experience in international business, tax regulations across three continents, marketing, sales, operations, business optimization, financial planning, team building, HR management, negotiation, & conflict resolution.

Deep knowledge of technology ranging from embedded system to cloud based solutions.
