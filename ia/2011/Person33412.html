Hein van der Wielen’s Summary

You just found Hein van der Wielen (1974) on LinkedIn. I'm an entrepreneur but above all an online marketing specialist. I have been active on the internet since the early days of the '90s. Having weaknesses for media, computers and new technologies you can imagine I was hooked the minute I explored the Internet for the first time.
I started my first company during my study Communications in 1998. In the beginning I was active in several fields: Media/music on one hand and the Internet, web/IT development on the other. Today the field of online advertising and online sales & marketing is my only business.
I geared up on my online advertising/web development activities since the beginning of this millennium. During these years I became a specialist in the field of online advertising, SEM, media buying, analytics and e-commerce.
In 2006 I temporarily joined the team at Typhone.nl as a free-lancer since they had plans to start a new online marketing agency. As a result we launched Blue Mango Interactive (BMI) in December 2006 with a relatively small team of about 5 (still as a free-lancer) and started working for Vodafone and Typhone.
In 2010 I was given the chance to start a 2nd agency named Fresh Fruit Digital (FFD) of which I became managing director. Fresh Fruit Digital is a sister company of Blue Mango.
Today over 50 online specialists work for the companies Blue Mango Interactive (40+), Fresh Fruit Digital (7) and Source Republic (4) combined. From our office in Eindhoven (NL) we are proud to say that we support the following brands with their daily online advertising, sales and brand campaigns: Vodafone, Ditzo, The Phone House/Typhone, Mars Nederland, ASR (former Fortis ASR), Kodak and Sanoma Uitgevers.
We are different; we offer measurable results, not just good campaigns.
