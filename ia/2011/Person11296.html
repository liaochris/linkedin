In every man there is a boy, a bad boy.
That’s the thought behind the underwear brand Muchachomalo*.
Woman at a certain age stop talking about dolls and Barbie’s but men will never stop talking about cars and looking to nice girls don’t matter the age of the man. Tell the man a childish joke and they will laugh and think back about their youth.

The concept of Muchachomalo* is based on a way of living:
Be yourself, enjoy life and don’t matter how busy you are, don’t ever forget to make fun!
Based on that thought Muchachomalo* designed their collection.

Muchachomalo* (meaning bad boy in Spanish) brought out their first underwear collection in November 2007 in Holland. It is a dream start for every new brand. In 11 months the brand is already sold in more than 400 shops all over Holland. The collection is sold to various dealers in lingerie, jeans, kids and fashions stores and the high level department stores. For the moment in 2010 we deliver in 13 countries.

Every quarter of the year Muchachomalo* brings out a new collection. This unique collection with though prints are made for men, women and junior.
Every year we have a theme. For 2010 it is ‘Better Times’

The summercollection is inspired by classic movies which express our thoughts to bring back the happy feeling from those days when everything was so simple then.
