Jean-Sébastien Lamoureux's Summary

Jean-Sébastien is a senior consultant in the Financial Communication and Investor Relations group at NATIONAL. He provides strategic counselling on corporate communications, investor relations and relations with financial intermediaries. He has over 13 years experience in consulting and providing strategic advice to both the public and private sector on various public relation issues.

Prior to joining National worked for Kirchner Private Capital Group, an M&A boutique. Jean-Sébastien was also Vice-President of Investissement Québec Subsidiaries for three and a half years. During his stay with Investissement Québec, Jean-Sébastien was in charge of venture capital initiatives of the Québec Government. Among those initiatives, he was actively involved in Teralys, a $700 million fund of funds and Cycle Capital, a cleantech fund, where he sat on the advisory committee. He also worked with numerous technology funds and companies.

Before joining Investissement Québec, Jean-Sébastien was the chief of staff for the Chair of the Conseil du trésor where, among other things, he oversaw Cabinet communications, was responsible for the administration of the office of the Chair of the Conseil du trésor and liaison with minister’s offices and with the Premier’s office. In that position, he was involved in the creation of the Quebec Public Private Partnership Agency and in collective bargaining with the public sector. Previous to that, he was a corporate lawyer with Osler, Hoskin & Harcourt and a Member of the National Assembly for the riding of Anjou (1998-2001). He started his career with Leduc, Leblanc, a law firm.

He holds an MBA from the École des Hautes Études Commerciales de Montréal (HEC), a graduate degree in Management from HEC and a Bachelor’s Degree in Law from the Université de Montréal. He is also a member of the Barreau du Québec.

Specialties

corporate communications, investor relations and relations with financial intermediaries, strategic advice to both the public and private sector on public affairs
