Carrie Eckersley's Summary

I am Video Games recuitment specialist at Amiqus, focused on managing key accounts with clients including Sony and Rocksteady Games.

I am experienced in sourcing technical, creative and executive staff within all disciplines withn the games industry community. I can also provide realistic and sound advice on how to help less experienced and junior staff on how to break into or advance within the industry.

If you are looking for your next move, or would simply like to learn more about the job market in the games industry at the moment, please contact me on 01925 255 914, or carrie.eckersley@amiqus.com

At Amiqus we know your world. We’re a specialist recruitment consultancy providing professional candidates to the interactive entertainment, digital and online technology sectors. We have been acknowledged as a key recruitment partner to our clients as the winner of the Develop Industry Awards for Recruitment Excellence 2009 and 2010. Staying ahead of and participating in the fast moving entertainment and technology sectors we work in isn't just part of our job, it's our passion, a passion we know we share with people like you.
