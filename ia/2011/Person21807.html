John Abel's Summary

Recruitment/placement specialist in the Greater Toronto Area since 1987. Kindly review our Client Testimonials document below showcasing our respected firm's passion and commitment to our clients and candidates over these years.

We offer a variety of recruitment services on a national basis to both large and mid-sized companies (both public & privately owned) in the manufacturing, wholesale, distribution & service industries.

Typically, our clients have contacted us because they do not have a fully fledged human resources department to recruit top quality, permanent staff on all levels. This not infrequently resulted in costly hiring mistakes because of a "hit and miss" approach to recuitment due to:
1. a limited network of skilled candidates to choose from
2. poor screening process or simply because
3. they do not have the time or expertise to recruit efficiently
Poor hiring mistakes can be enormously expensive leading to poor morale and lost productivity. We take the risk out of recruitment, helping find the right people who fit with your unique needs and culture.

If this resonates with you, we would love to talk further and we welcome working with you as either a client, a consulting partner or as an individual looking to leverage your career with any of our prestigious client organizations.
