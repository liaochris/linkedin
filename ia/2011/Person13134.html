Edward Reilly PA-C, MPAS’s Summary

I am a Physician Assistant practicing since 1995. My medical experience has been quite diverse working in Primary Care and Internal Medicine, Occupational Medicine, Orthopaedics, Sports Medicine, Spine Surgery and Pain Management. I also have experience in orthopaedic implant sales and marketing.

Along with my busy clinical responsibilities I have recently created a consulting practice. Reilly Medical Consulting, Inc., works with Chiropractors to incorporate into their current practice Physical Therapy, and standard medical modalities to treat acute and chronic neck and back pain without (or with very limited) use of narcotics. This new integrated medical practice allows billing of several new services previously unavailable to chiropractic offices, dramatically increasing billing and subsequently, collections. After integration of medical and physical therapy services to a chiropractic office, owner/operators can expect collections to increase 8 to 10 times, with new and existing patient being very receptive to this model of patient care

Previously, I have worked outside of the clinical field as a Sales Manager for Stryker Orthopaedics in their trauma implant division. While at Stryker, I received professional education in leadership, management and team building. Beyond managing statewide sales forces in New York and North Carolina, I facilitated sales and service of orthopaedic implants, was responsible for meeting planning, contract negotiation, surgeon training, and operating room consultation. In these areas, I excelled, meeting sales goals in two of three years of employment.

I am always interested in hearing about new and exciting professional opportunities as a Physician Assistant with 15 years of diverse experience.
