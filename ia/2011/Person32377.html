Seth David’s Summary

The first things you will get immediately when working with me is peace of mind. Many people tell me that they get this just after the first conversation - they know they have in me someone who can actually deliver results.

If you have accounting and bookkeeping headaches, I am the cure. When I have you caught up you will feel a sense of organization like you've never felt before. That feeling you get when you know you are on top of things and handling them like a champ because that's how I handle my business and my clients businesses are my business.

You will benefit tremendously from using one of my bookkeeper/account managers. They have ongoing training from me. If you hire an independent freelance bookkeeper they do not have a resource to lean on. I am that resource so the bookkeepers I employ are empowered to get the job done - no task too great.

I work with business owners, bookkeepers, other financial professionals and consultants on their bookkeeping, accounting and financial management. I clean up the mess and then keep it clean. I work remotely as well as in person - however you prefer.

I work with CPA's supporting their clients sometimes as a representative of their firm and sometimes direct - whatever the CPA prefers.

Typical clients for me are doing from $1MM to $10MM in gross revenues. A smaller group are doing $10MM to $20MM who hire me to consult on their financial modeling.

In the past few years while always thinking of more ways that I can be useful to you, I realized a great deal of what I do is teaching. So I developed my Learning Center to offer you and your staff an inexpensive way to acquire the skills you need to decrease the cost of organizing and maintaining your financial and other information. It's simple - we supply the resources to make your business run profitably and enable you to focus on what you do best. You'll sleep well with me on your team.
