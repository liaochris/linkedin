Robert Ancill's Summary

Internationally experienced Restaurant, Retail and Leisure Consultant with a solid background in restaurant/cafe and leisure operations, management & finance, concept & brand development, launching new businesses, and managing change.

Managed over 240 restaurant/café openings and the launching of 26 new restauarant concepts in the USA, UK, Europe, China, UAE, India, Saudi Arabia, & Nigeria.

Past Employers and Clients include: House of Fraser, Aroma Cafes, Safeway, El Gallo Giro, Albert Roux, Hauser Entertainment, Sushiitto, Planet Hollywood, HMS Host, and Panera Bread.

Sits on the boards of two privately held companies, and provides industry advice to a number of financial institutions regarding future restaurant trends and specific company diagnostics.
