Marc W. Halpert's Summary

I have 2 main business interests and yes, they are intertwined:

1. Faster, Smarter Payments Improve Your Cash Flow:
I can show your business (Your Best Interest LLC) or nonprofit organization (e-giving) how to improve cash flow and accelerate incoming payments by:
• credit card,
• debit card and/or
• electronic check debit
• via one-time and/or recurring pre-scheduled payments
• face-to-face at the point of sale
• phone
• fax,
• e-mail and/or
• internet

I eliminate the costly delays in paper-shuffling, snail mail, and at the bank.

The goal: faster cash flow into your checking account, in an optimal secure solution, customized to YOUR needs. See www.yourbestinterestllc.com.

2. Linkedin Coaching: Make LinkedIn a Powertool In Your Marketing Toolbox
I use LinkedIn as a social networking focal point in my payments businesses: marketing, publicity, education, communications: "connect to collaborate."

I can directly attribute new business to my robust LinkedIn profile and frequent updates on LinkedIn. Can you say that?

I will coach you how to jumpstart your use of LinkedIn to help keep your business pipeline fuller. See www.connect2collaborate.com for my offerings of
• educational materials
• upcoming live webinars and
• three-part series of prerecorded online courses.
One-on-one coaching can be arranged. My popular LinkedIn group seminars can be customized to the audience.

So, How Are My Business Interests Intertwined?
I am indeed fortunate to meet amazing professionals in my payments businesses, and they become my LinkedIn connections, and many of my LinkedIn connections use my payments services.

The common denominator: you, the professional, want to do better using LinkedIn and/or you want to improve your cash flow. In each case, I show you how:
• giving you the benefits of my experience
• stressing my dedication to your client service and
• offering my true commitment to coaching you towards greater success. This is my value proposition.

Specialties

1. Representing multiple nationwide electronic payments processors, we show businesses and non-profit organizations the many ways to leverage the new payment technologies, speed cash flow and reduce the expenses arising from banking, postage and administrative expenses.
2. Depending on your budget and desire to invest in your business future, we can custom-design a LinkedIn training program that addresses your needs:
one-on-one coaching, onsite seminars, webinars or interactive virtual courses.
