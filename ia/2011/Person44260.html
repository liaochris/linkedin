Trish Gant’s Summary

I am an award winning, versatile photographer and have developed an informal, lively approach to working with models of all ages, especially children. Sought after for my relaxed style, I can turn my hand to difficult subjects such as family issues and health. I am comfortable shooting in studio or on location, using flash or available light, I have the expertise to get the job done to a high standard.

Published works include:
* Herbs for Herbfresh Hersham and Morrisons Supermarkets.
* Roseneath Dental Surgery Website.
* Backdrops for RoseTheatre Kingston.
* Sam Stern's Cooking Up a Storm for Walker Books.
* Turnford and Athelstan House School Prospectuses.
* Bill Wyman's Rolling with the Stones and Treasure Islands.
* RHS Encyclopedia of Plants.
* Dr. Miriam Stoppard's Family Health Guide.
* Property Ladder Magazine
* BBC Gardener's World Magazine
* Alamy stock photography contributor.

Technical Knowledge:
* In depth knowledge of high end digital image making and post-production processes.
* Retouching techniques.
* Knowledge of ancient black and white processing and printing for archive.
* Studio and location photography.

In Summer 2008 I completed a City and Guilds PTTLS course with the aim of teaching photography.

I wish to connect with graphic designers, P.R. companies , publishers and creatives within advertising agencies and any company needing quality imagery for web or print.

Teaming up with a design or marketing company to pitch for 2012 work is also of interest.

Trish Gant’s Specialties:

* Lifestyle, family, children and health.
* Food, horticulture, products and catalogue.
* Corporate events and portraiture.
* Theatrical and Performance.
* Education Sector.
* Stock photography and contributor to www.alamy.com
