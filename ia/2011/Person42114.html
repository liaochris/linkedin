Susan Kastan's Summary

Kastan Consulting is an emerging Information Assurance business based in Rochester, NY. Partnering with PJKlein Consulting in Alexandria, VA, the two companies provide their clients over 40 years of experience in the Information Technology/Information Assurance business.

There are no cookie-cutter solutions at Kastan Consulting. Every plan is customized to meet the specific needs of our customers.

Kastan Consulting can help your business with
• Assessing your areas of risk
• Creating Business Continuity Plans
• Training and testing of emergency response efforts
• Developing information security policies and procedures

Specialties

Business Continuity and IT Contingecy Plans,
Tabletop Incident Response and Disaster Recovery Testing,
IT security policies and procedures (Incident Response, Access Control, Media and Data Protection, Configuration Management, Backup Plan),
Project Management
