Matthew Runeare’s Summary

I'm a recent graduate from the Food Culture and Communications Master’s at the Universitá delgi Studi di Scienze Gastronomiche (UNISG) in Colorno, Italy which focuses on food-based issues including politics, history and culture, as well as intensive week long trips to artisanal food production areas in Italy, France, Spain and Crete. On these visits I learned first hand about production methods, marketing functions and promotional strategies of these unique and varied food related producers.

Previous to my attaining my Master's degree, I was a brand and advertising Art Director managing a staff of full time and freelance creatives in the development of communication strategies for Fortune 500 companies. During that time I collaborated with writers, account executives, and researchers to create targeted, strategically relevant communications that increased sales and built brand awareness.

Currently I work with Live Culture Co. a food consultancy firm in Oakland, CA as a Strategy and Research Associate and at the Eat Real Festival, which is a national food festival as the Senior Marketing and Sponsorship Manager.
