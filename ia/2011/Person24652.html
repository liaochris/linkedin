Kim Kubsch, CAPS's Summary

Change HOW you live, not WHERE you live!

Aging in Place Solutions provides on-line webinars and on-site workshops, plus room-by-room assessments comprised of over 100 potential risks and hazards:

*Fall Prevention
*Safety Risks & Hazards
*Accessibility for daily activities
*Cost-effective tools for independent living

Be safe, independent and confident in your own home as long as possible. I would like to help you by checking the “Vital Signs of Your Home” to age-in-place.

Specialties

Certified Aging in Place Specialist, Certified Environmental Access Consultant, emplowering aging adults and physically-challenged to live safely and independently in their own homes as long as possible.
