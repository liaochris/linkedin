Craig Brandis’s Summary

Craig Brandis is an experienced product development / marketing manager with a CTO's ability to bridge between strategic business concerns, customer needs and new technologies. He has held senior engineering management and product management positions in mobile, medical device, healthcare IT and software companies. He understands what it takes develop new innovations, and can drive creative product and service solutions to move into new markets. He has been recruited to build successful engineering teams in profitable venture-backed startups, and as well as within larger companies. He has created development partnerships with Fortune 500 companies, and worked with research departments and government labs to co-develop technology and transfer innovations from research into products. He recently completed the Executive Certificate in Strategic Innovation at MIT/Sloan. His current interests are in bringing new classes of mobile applications to market and applying mobile technology to social business, micro-lending and mobile health.
