Martin Bueno's Summary

Martin Founded the first On-Line service in Latin America Los Pinos II (BBS) back in 1984 and has been involved in the comunication and the Digital advertisent industry for more than 25 Years.

In 1997, development and create Fiera.com , the first portal of content and e-commerce of Hispanic speech with operation in more than 7 countries in partnership with Grupo Carso,owned by mexican millonarie Carlos Slim.

Prior to Join Quorumm as a COO and Chief Digital Officer, Martin was Vice President of Digital for the JWT in Latin America, Director of the Digital Innovation Group at WPP Digital and the last 3 years (2008-2010) as a Mananing Director of EuroRSCG AR,in wich help to sharp the Social Media strategy for EuroRSCG Social,at global level.

Dictate more than 60 conferences on Technology and Digital Advertisment invited by international organizations, exhibitions and ONG, in Latin America, Europe and Asia , place in where work exhaustive ( Tokyo, Japan ) to integrate the existing technologies in the West, with the competitive Eastern market, for private companies.

It was awarded with the Entrepreneur of the Year , by Professional of Tomorow Magazine 1997, Tecnoemprendedor of the Year, French Bank 1998 and nominee Entrepreneur of the Year in the United States, by Ernst & Young 2000.

Martin has a master in Computer Science from the Universidad Tecnologica Nacional, and a BA from UTN from Buenos Aires.

He currently serves as a Co-Chairman in the Global Board of Directors at Quorumm Digital Venture Group LLP with Jack Hartmann.
