Mino Sullivan's Summary

I'm an executive coach specializing in career transition and job search. As a Certified Executive Coach I work with managers, executives and professionals from around the world who are in a career transition or a job search. We work together to help them to gain clarity on what they want to do next in their career and then land a job or build the business that will bring satisfaction and fulfillment.

Our process is divided into two phases:

Phase 1 - Using a variety of assessments, proprietary worksheets and meaningful conversations clients gain clarity on work that will stimulate and fulfill them as well as compensate them well.

Phase 2 - Once this clarity is gained, clients plan and execute a state-of-the-art comprehensive job search to land a job that is right for them or develop a business plan for their new venture.

For the last ten years it has been my life's work to guide, encourage, motivate and support clients to create stimulating, fulfilling and meaningful work lives.

If this is the type of program that you think might serve you, please feel free to contact me for a complimentary consultation.
