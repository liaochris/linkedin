Fergus Quinn's Summary

I am currently pursuing an MSc in Mathematical Finance t the University of York. I spent the early years of my career in financial management roles such as Financial Controller and Finance Director with emphasis on analytical and modelling roles in the latter years, in particular supporting sales and marketing teams.

In recent years I felt that I would benefit from increasing my analytical skillset and so embarked on 2 potgraduate degrees. The first was an MSc in Applied and Pure Mathematics at the OU and the second was the master's degree that I am now pursuing full-time at the University of York. This MSc in Mathematical Finance will enable me to take on analytical and modelling roles in the Financial Services sector, with particular emphasis on derivative pricing and risk management.

I will complete the taught part of this MSc in June 2011, with a dissertation to complete by January 2012 whilst working and so I will be keen to return to the workplace from July 2011 onwards.

Risk, Financial Management and Analysis, Financial Modelling, C++, Excel VBA.
