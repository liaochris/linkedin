Anne McCrossan's Summary

Anne develops human and social capital value for businesses and works with clients to build business and brand equity through social business design.

This covers user-centric business model generation, social influence marketing, brand management, as well as social media mentoring and community management.

Visceral Business, her company, creates and develops social strategies and gives organisations and brands the ability to generate spontaneous growth by making connections that move people. For many businesses this means taking them from organisations that use social media in some measure to being integrally social and designed as social businesses so they have effectively calibrated business models for the social age and are seamlessly connected to consumers.

Anne's highly experienced in social media, branding and organisational development. A founder member of www.triiibes.com, Seth Godin's private marketing think tank, Anne is a TED speaker on Reinventing Organisation and very involved in emergent thinking about networked organizations, brands and collaborative cultures. She's a co-creator of the Business Model Generation Handbook, author of the Change This manifesto ‘Cracking the Genetic Code, A Way Forward for Organizations’ and a regular speaker.

Anne has extensive experience in leading complex brand change, multi-channel marketing communications programmes for some of the world’s most successful companies in the world across both public and private sectors in retail, financial services, travel, leisure, B2B, pharma not for profit and luxury sectors.

She's a Trustee of the Child’s i Foundation, an innovative charity that is setting a benchmark for the next generation of non-profits as a social organisation.

Her blog, ‘A Bit Visceral', covers social business and organizational development, brand marketing, leadership, psychology, corporate social responsibility, ethics, sustainability, social influence marketing and corporate and consumer behaviour.

Specialties

Social business design, organizational change management, brand management, executive coaching, community organization.
