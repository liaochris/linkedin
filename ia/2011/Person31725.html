Murray Clarke's Summary

We established Workbrain Japan KK in order to do two things: 1) assist technology vendors into the Japan market by bringing them to the optimal distribution and integration partners and 2) to assist such startup operations at the time they need to establish an on-ground presence. Subsequently we established a second company (Virtatech) to take on the recruitment function.

My role at Workbrain Japan is in finding new technologies as requested by partners (integrators, resellers), building the project team and managing the on-going execution.

Virtatech is an HR/search consulting firm specifically focused on the Virtualization/Cloud vendor space and is contracted to work solely for VMware for the time being.
