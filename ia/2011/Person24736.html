Kirste Webb, AF.APMP’s Summary

More than 24 years experience providing support in all facets of marketing, business development, and proposal development. Provides business development and marketing support, including identifying and targeting opportunities, identifying potential team partners and competitors, developing strategies for new opportunities, managing conferences, and developing marketing materials. Prepares proposals in response to Requests for Proposals (RFPs) issued by commercial and federal government clients. Active member of the Association of Proposal Management Professionals (APMP).
