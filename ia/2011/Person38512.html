Salvatore Giammarresi’s Summary

Dynamic localization management expert with extensive international product experience. Successfully strategized, setup, planned, executed and managed localization projects of all sizes. Proficient in driving international product requirements across multiple groups balancing costumer and business needs taking into account technological/human resources.
