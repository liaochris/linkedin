Jeff Bartee's Experience

Vice President of Product

Realcom U.S., Inc.

Public Company; Computer Software industry

January 2010 – Present (1 year 2 months)

Realcom provides Social Business software that enables Enterprises to tap the knowledge in their employees through the creation of internal communities, locating experts and directing questions to the right people to get answers quickly. As VP of Product I am responsible for Product Management and Software Development. This starts with the Product Vision/Roadmap; which includes the strategic direction, the overall value proposition with supporting features where we factor in competitors, our core product strengths, and the overall market movement towards web 2.0 / social networking benefits.

In support of the Product Roadmap, I lead our engineering/software development group (Dev, QA, PM, Ops) which is comprised of 30 employees in Pune, India and the architectural design team in Bellevue, Washington. My goal for Software Development is to build the best off-shore product team possible. We've changed to a matrix org, assigned functional managers, strengthened the PM role, revised our processes and added open source development / QA tools. We produce quality code through a balance between process based discipline and personal growth and ownership.
