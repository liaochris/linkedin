    Hire Patriots

    (Individual & Family Services industry)

    September 2007 — Present (3 years 5 months)

    One night in 2004, an injured Marine,who had just returned from Iraq, knocked at our door. "Sir," he said, standing military erect while balancing with a cane from his injuries, "do you have any work that I can do for you to earn some extra money? I have a wife and two kids. Our electricity has been turned off, and I need to make some extra money." -- So began this journey...

    Patriotic Hearts is a 501C3 non-profit. It provides a 'One Day' job board for US troops. Whenever residents near any US military base need help around their homes, yards or businesses for a day, weekend or part time, they can post their jobs for free on www.HirePatriots.com. In San Diego, CA over 1000 Marines and sailors find work when off duty, every month. HirePatriots.com also has an extensive full time job board. -- Patriotic Hearts also strengthens and restores military marriages and families by providing free counseling and marriage retreats. We have just added a new outreach. We are giving homeless veterans with families temporary shelter in Rvs. -- Our services also extend to all US veterans and military spouses, military children and their patriotic supporters.

  • Why I Do What I DO

    Hire Patriots

    (Human Resources industry)

    January 1972 — February 2009 (37 years 2 months)

    My basic premise is this: After many years of counseling PTSD victims from the Vietnam War, I am concerned about the effects of these current wars upon the young men and women and their families who serve in the military.

    If we can keep a soldiers' family together, show sincere appreciation for the soldier's service to our country, and employ that soldier when they transition back to civilian life, we will greatly reduce the incidents of PTSD. -- Hence we provide off duty, day jobs for currently serving troops, full time jobs when they get out and welcome home parties and military marriage retreats. Patriotic Hearts is our 501c3. HirePatriots is a program of our non-profit. -- We have supported our outreach by holding job fairs on US military bases and charging companies to attend, and through selling ads on HirePatriots.com. -- HirePatriots.com's ad banners receive an average of 16,000 clicks per month!

    We have received some grant monies and a few donations. -- I could use two special kind of volunteers: A person who knows the ins and outs of getting a government contract to pay for HirePatriots continuing to provide its services; and, a grant writer to bring in more funds.

    Our current economy makes job fairs and advertising very difficult. -- I am now beginning new businesses to support my family and our vital outreaches.
