Katherine Bock's Summary

Katherine Bock is owner of Katherine Bock Consulting. She is a "recovering attorney" and professional certified coach who supports individuals to create more success, balance and meaning in their careers and lives. Clients tell her that she helps them achieve extraordinary results with less effort by clarifying what it is that they really want and then co-creating a strategy to achieve what they most value.

Katherine has helped over 1,000 individuals over the past eight years to identify and articulate their unique value proposition to sell themselves into the right job. In addition to coaching and consulting with individuals, she’s taught a career management course in the Executive Education program at the University of Texas at Dallas. As part of her volunteer activities, she teaches a job search process at the Dallas Christian Women’s Job Corps.

Katherine holds a graduate-level certificate of Executive and Professional Coaching from the University of Texas at Dallas and is certified in various assessment tools including the Birkman Method®, The Winslow Report® and Core Clarity® In-Powering Teams program and is a certified TOPs group facilitator and has attended the Sedona Method Coach Training Level I. She holds the ICF Professional Certified Coach designation from the ICF.

Katherine earned her law degree from the University of Houston and has an accounting degree as well. The majority of her career was spent in the financial services industry with the organization that is now JP Morgan/Chase and prior to that worked with Browning Ferris Industries, Delta Airlines and Southwestern Bell Telephone Company.

Katherine is a 2001 graduate of Leadership Texas and belongs to various executive-level and professional groups and is very active in her community, having served on a variety of non-profit boards.

Specialties

Executive and Leadership coaching, career management, career transition, Certified Birkman and Winslow consultant, Core Clarity certified facilitator, Forton Group Leadership Coaching facilitator, Strengthsfinder, Career Anchors, Values in Action, Career Reflections
