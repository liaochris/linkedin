Gilli Moon’s Summary

For the past 10 years, Gilli (pronounced as with a "J"), has been one of Australia's most influential and iconic figures, forging an independent path before anyone knew what "indie" was. She has produced and released music albums of her original music, to critical acclaim (including a spread in Newsweek magazine), winning songwriting and artist awards, performing across the world many times, and landing songs in film and television.

Inspired to make a difference in the music industry for songwriters, Gilli co-founded Songsalive!, in 1997, and is now the largest internationally based non-profit songwriters organization.

Beyond being the Artist, she is one of Hollywood's few female music producers, as well as an event producer (in 07 she co-founded and co-produced the inaugural Los Angeles Women's Music Festival, - over 60 artists - 3,000 visitors, raising funds for animal rescue; and in 2002 the Songsalive! Expo at UCLA attracting 1000 songwriters). Notwithstanding she has produced many corporate and rock events in her lifetime.

Since 2000, she ihas been the CEO of her own record company and production house, Warrior Girl Music, (which is home to several internationally based artists including over 130 female and male artists on her compilations "Females On Fire" and "Art Of Men"), and offers services in film music supervision, artist development and music production.

Gilli has written 2 motivational books for artists of all genres based on her experiences in the music and general arts businesses as an artist and entrepreneur, - "Just Get Out there (2010) and previously, "I AM A Professional Artist - the Key to Survival and Success in the World of the Arts" (2007 3rd Edition). When time permits, gilli offers artist coaching and speaks on panels about DIY, the indie movement and how to survive the business.

gilli currently lives in Southern California.
