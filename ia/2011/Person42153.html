Susan Richards’s Summary

I was a psychotherapist in private practice in Solana Beach, California for 30 years, with a special interest in dreams, the relationship of creativity and the unconscious, and the resolution of creative blocks. I retired in 2010 and moved to the Philadelphia area where I am a full-time artist. My deep appreciation of the human spirit, a gift of the years of practicing my other calling, infuses my work as an artist. I feel fortunate to have the opportunity to pursue both of my passions in one lifetime.

Susan Richards’s Specialties:

As an artist, I now have wonderful studio space not only for my own work, but that can also easily accommodate small groups as well as individuals. I am developing ways to work with people using creative play for personal growth, and am excited by the idea of integrating my skills in ways that are unique and fun!
