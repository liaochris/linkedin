Steve Thrasher’s Summary

Steven is an intellectual property specialist, who is registered to practice before the United States Patent and Trademark Office, as well as all courts in the State of Texas.

Steven is on the board for the MIT Enterprise Forum of Dallas--a non-profit organization affiliated with the Massachusetts Institute of Technology that is dedicated to the education of entrepreneurs and inventors. He is the past chairman of the Finance Committee for the board of directors of the United Inventors’ Association, which he lead to financial independence.

Steven received B.S. degrees in Electrical Engineering and Finance from Auburn University, and M.B.A. and J.D. degrees from Baylor University. Steven completed additional studies at: The Queens’ College, Oxford University, England, and Universidad Autonoma de Guadalajara.

Thrasher Associates, LLC assists and educates entrepreneurs to identify inventions, trade secrets, and trademarks, get patents, and pursue protection (including internationally--in Latin America, Europe, the Orient, and the Middle East), enforce rights, and earn revenue from ideas. Often, he finds that clients have patents on products they do not make or use, but that others want. Professionals affiliated with Thrasher Associates represent over 100 years of patent practice in the areas of intellectual property prosecution, litigation, and licensing.

Prior to Thrasher Associates, Steven’s practice emphasized patent drafting and prosecution, foreign patent prosecution, licensing, validity and infringement opinions, due diligence matters, and litigation. He has over 250 filings in the patent office to his credit. In addition to software, he has technical expertise in the areas of cellular and wire line communications, silicon wafer processing, sensors, aircraft communication systems and instrumentation, digital signal processing and consumer products. Before law school, he was an engineer with the McDonnell Douglas Aircraft Corporation.

Steve Thrasher’s Specialties:

Helps clients identify intellectual property, including patents, trademarks, and copyrights. Also assist with invention disclosure, open source and trade secret management programs. Licensing Expert, including international licensing. With associates, litigates to defend client's rights.
