In my work, I:

First and foremost, bring attention to issues, people and movements that matter;

I investigate trends on the edge;

I experiment;

I use social media to network weave, share information and educate;

I connect dots where dots can't be seen;

I collaborate with movements around the world who are doing amazing things;

I bring people together;

I explore the emerging complexity of our world and use these insights who help organizations and communities innovate;
