Aaron Kershaw's Summary

Aaron Kershaw is your NYMediaGuy. Since 1994, I have been on the forefront of Internet technologies. I was a Director of Technology at About.com, a developer for Citibank International, have built hundreds of web sites and published tens of thousands of pages online.

My 8 year radio career includes programs for Whitney Media’s AM1460 & FM93.5 stations conducting well over 700 hundred live interviews which included; Chelsey Handler, Judge Alex and artist Peter Max.

I founded West1Media Group in 2004 which owned Multiple Media Properties; Online, in Print, and Radio also provided Internet & Video Production, Graphic Design as well as Marketing services to Westchester and Putnam County businesses.
I have produced PSA's for The Arthritis Foundation and Documentaries for Habitat for Humanity, Training Videos for Guiding Eyes for the Blind and Parades/Special Events for The Village of Elmsford.


In 2005 I was a recipient of a “Rising Star”-40 under 40 award by the Business Council of Westchester, I have a Certificate of Merit from former NY State Senator Spano, and numerous Certificates from local Chamber of Commerce's.

I have been featured in the Westchester Business Journal, Westchester County Times and Entrepreneur Magazine.

A Desert Storm Veteran who served in Iraq, (24 MEU) while serving in The Marine Corps, honorably discharged with honors in 1992.
