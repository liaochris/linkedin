David Sternberg, LICSW's Summary

I've been in private practice since 2002, providing individual psychotherapy, couples/marriage counseling and group therapy to adults. The three main areas of my practice are depression, anxiety and relationship conflicts. I do relatively brief therapy, meaning that most of my patients see me for about 25 sessions.
I've written on a variety of mental health issues, all of which are available for free on my website: www.dctalktherapy.com. You can also read my blog on my site.
I offer day and evening appointments at my Washington, D.C. office (Woodley Park metro), and I'm currently accepting new patients.
I also offer help to psychotherapists, counselors and other mental health professionals who are interested in optimizing their website. Email me at david@dctalktherapy.com for a free 15-minute consultation.

Specialties

I specialize in cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT). Also, I have a special interest in working with young adults (20s and 30s).
