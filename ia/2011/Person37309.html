Robert Nelson's Summary

I began my career with India’s largest distributor of Blow Plast Ltd., selling molded chairs to institutions and the retail channel. Two years after having honed my sales skills, I moved on to media sales with the Indian Express Group.

Here, I was part of a young and vibrant sales team that was responsible for generating ad-hoc revenue through supplements, from existing and new advertisers. During my five years with the publication, I set new sales records, specialized in Features across the financial sector, launched a ‘Special Features’ team for The Financial Express and managed a national sales team for Features and Supplements.

I continued my ‘space selling’ profile into my next job with the India Today Group and less than a year later moved on to Sony Entertainment Television, where I sold air-time for CNBC News Channel. A year later, an interesting and challenging profile at Indiatimes.com came by which I eagerly took up.

I joined Indiatimes.com to manage their e-commerce business for the Mumbai region. Apart from driving online sales and acquiring vendors, I was part of many successful initiatives like the launch of the ‘Half Price Shop’, online ticketing with Air India, Mail Order Catalogues and Reader’s Offers’ in the Times Group publications.

In 2006, I joined Future Group - India’s largest retailer, which had set its foot in the online arena as a pure-play retail company - as head of the online merchandising team. Being a ‘Start-up’, I was able to experiment, innovate, learn and unlearn and… thrive too.

During the last 4 years I have been a part of many new initiatives at Futurebazaar.com, through its various lines of business including Online, Institutional, Mail Order Catalogues and Gift Vouchers. More recently however, with Futurebazaar’s integration with the Group’s three key formats - Ezone, Big Bazaar and Pantaloons, I am now also responsible for online sales of all the businesses.
