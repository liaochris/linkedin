Paul Zurek’s Summary

My interests include asset pricing; financial econometrics; numerical techniques and model solution methodologies; financial institutions and risk management; and valuation of complex financial instruments.

At Cornerstone Research, I am working on various financial institutions cases involving risk management, foreign exchange trading, hedge fund and private equity valuation and mark-to-market accounting.

I also teach risk management to banking professionals and serve as Academic Director for the Risk Management Association/Wharton Advanced Risk Management program.
