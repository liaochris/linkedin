Michele McGraw's Summary

Michele McGraw has worked with many major brands on social media campaigns or as a brand ambassador. Brands include AMD, EA SPORTS Active, Healthy Choice, Yahoo, Microsoft, Jockey, HP, Epson, Skechers, Ford, Hanes, Creative Memories and OfficeMax.

Michele's blog, Scraps of My Geek Life, shows families how to use technology to enhance their lives. She shares her love of gadgets, digital scrapbooking and her journey to live a healthier gadget-filled life.

Michele is the Gadget Editor for the popular online site, Mom Blog Magazine (sister site of Mom Bloggers Club). She also writes for MyBlogalicious (Technology Editor), Yahoo! Shine, BlogWorld Expo blog (bi-weekly Parent Blogging Column), Technorati and The Balancing Act show on Lifetime TV.

Michele shares how her passion for technology spreads through all aspects of her life including (but not limited to) fitness, crafts, reading, running and being a mom.

MIchele McGraw actively participates in the women and mom bloggers community online through Twitter, Facebook, forums and blogs.

For the past 14.5 years, Michele McGraw has been helping women find support online for their hobbies and lifestyles by sharing her experience and passions.
