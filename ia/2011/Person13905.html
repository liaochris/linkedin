Eric Paul’s Summary

Dynamic and versatile clean technology and corporate sustainability analyst with business development, marketing, research and sales experience. Mr. Paul is an analyst with AltaTerra Research's Renewable Energy Practice.

Previous to joining AltaTerra, Mr. Paul held a variety of positions working with businesses and local governments on energy efficiency and environmental programs. He was responsible for running and managing the Business Environmental Alliance of Sonoma County. The BEA was a group of businesses focused at promoting the financial benefits of sustainable business practices. Before joining the BEA, Mr. Paul worked for the City of Edina, MN to establish a greenhouse gas emissions baseline for all city buildings and operations.

Eric Paul’s Specialties:

Sustainable business strategy, commercial solar energy market, renewable energy policy, solar (PV), solar thermal and CPV.

Software: Access, Excel, eQuest, Constant Contact, Adobe Illustrator, Power Point, Salesforce, SPSS, and Word.
