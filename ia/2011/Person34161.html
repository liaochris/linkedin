Paul Harrison's Summary

Carve is a digital consulting practice specialising in Corporate Social Networks, Enterprise 2.0, Online PR, Social Media Monitoring, Online Reputation Management and Search. With offices in the UK and Australia, the practice has developed corporate social networking strategies, social media marketing and social recruiting programmes for a range of private, public and not-for-profit organisations, including VisitBritain, IKEA, Yell Adworks, The Audit Commission, Fairtrade, ANZ Bank, Wolseley PLC, Hot Tuna, Hemscott PLC, Hays PLC, Hudson Global and the NHS.

Carve offers strategy, advisory, research, training and managed services.

Carve regularly collaborates with creative / specialist agencies. Recently we have advised FMG, August Media, 33, Blackbridge. We have strategic partners in China and France.

Carve in Australia (http://www.CarveConsulting.com.au) advises organisations including Wine Australia and Banrock Station.

Paul has worked online for more than 12 years, having joined early start-up Taps.com in 1997. Paul began his career in publishing, having graduated with a degree in Mass Media Communications. Before launching Carve Consulting, Paul set up and developed the new media divisions of two leading UK advertising agencies. An avid skier and current affairs junkie, his dream social media campaign would have been Barack Obama’s grassroots election strategy. Unfortunately the call never came.

Paul Harrison, Managing Partner, welcomes opportunities to speak at events / conferences.
