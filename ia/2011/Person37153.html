I'm responsible for raising the profile of the project in the public, educational, scientific and the gas and oil exploration industrial sectors. I work with a global network of partners to support the environmental dimensions of the project and help to coordinate the outputs of academic partners in the Gulf of Mexico and in South-East Asia.

Updated the web site to current web standards and redesigned the look and feel to show off the images, video, publications and mission details available through the project.
