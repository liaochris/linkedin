Brenda Krueger Huffman’s Summary

Brenda Krueger Huffman is an internationally syndicated political commentary & business writer as well as a National Reporter with World City Press, Inc's AXcess News. She is a Co-Host on the popular blogtalkradio program Women on the Move....The Roundtable.

Her writing focus is national political commentary, business, technology, & social media with an occasional article in the animal welfare & family resource sector

Her articles & posts display observation & opinion from her highly successful executive business experience & intelligence incorporating substance, relativity & humor. Brenda is a thoughtful & easy to relate with business development & marketing social media content & collaboration writer. She also collaborates with clients to provide content for various blogs, websites, corporate newsletters, corporate annual reports, & by consulting agreement.

Brenda is a business development, marketing and operations executive & an entrepreneur specializing in the information technology & social media sectors as well as in online media. She has been an executive at some of the foremost national information technology corporations such as Transamerica Intellitech – a subsidiary of Transamerica Corp, Homestore.com, Realigent, REBIG, Virtual Information Systems, & Planning Research Corporation. Her consulting client list includes start-up regional & national companies as well as industry directing, predominant national corporations; a majority of the various state & local government agencies in the US; & association owned or member owned companies that are non-profit or for-profit.
