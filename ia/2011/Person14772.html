Faisal Galaria's Summary

Faisal Galaria is a member of the management team at Spotify and the Global Head of the Corporate and Business Development team. In this role he is responsible for new market entry, international growth and strategic partnerships. Previously, Faisal was the International Managing Director of Kayak.com, the online travel meta search engine. Here he was responsible for expanding the service to nine countries and propelled it to become the second largest travel search engine in the UK in less than one year.

Prior to Kayak Faisal had extensive experience of commercializing and scaling mobile and internet companies across Europe and the USA including building an online festival film company and as Director of Europe for Skype where he was responsible for managing growth and key partnerships across Europe and the Middle East.

Faisal has also been a member of the Policy Executive of Britain’s telecom with and media regulator Ofcom. At Ofcom, he played a senior role in the high-profile negotiation and valuation of distribution rights for Premier League Football and co-authored the Telecoms Strategy Review.

Faisal started his career in wireless and Internet consulting at Arthur Andersen and was a member of Cluster Consulting, which was acquired by Diamond Technology Partners (NASDAQ: DTPI) for $930 million in 2000.

Faisal has been educated across Europe and graduated with honors from UMIST (University of Manchester Institute of Science and Technology) with a Bachelor’s in Management Sciences in the UK. He also holds an MBA from IESE in Spain.
