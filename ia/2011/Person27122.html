Daniel Souza’s Summary

SAP Netweaver BW consultant, with over 5 years in the SAP consulting arena. I have helped prestigious companies in their BI projects in achieving their goals. My range of experience covers several sectors such as consumer goods, retail, foods, industrial engineering, mining, metals and others.

As part of my engineering studies I have acquired a great skill in solving problems, including business problems. I excel in making links between technical and business solution, always focused on delivering outstanding client results; adding value in their business.

Key Experiences:

- Accounting and Finance: I worked on the design and development of a financial closure solution for Unilever Latin America. We used different SAP technologies to obtain all financial measures according to the corporate reporting standards. The system is responsible for making the financial closure for 19 countries.

- Purchase process: I developed a solution using SAP BI Integrated Planning to generate purchase lists for Pernambucanas. This system evaluates the best products to buy in terms of suppliers and time. The results were a more intelligent buying process and higher profit. This purchase process involves 1.5 billion dollars per year and 80 users directly.
