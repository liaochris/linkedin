Arti Sexy's Summary

Carina Howell was born in Reno, NV in 1982, raised in Los Angeles, CA. She obtained a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from California State University, Northridge in 2009 and is currently pursuing her Masters degree in Human Factors Psychology. She is an emerging artist, an engineer, and an entrepreneur, currently residing in Los Angeles, CA.

Since her emergence as a painter in 2001, her works exude her obsession with sex, love, lust, seduction, and human emotion, often through the use of the symbolic female body. Her artworks each exemplify an emotional fantasy caught in time, embellished with color, permanently exposed on a canvas. Typically, she makes the female psychosis vulnerable to public eye of criticism. Her mathematical educational influences can be seen in her pieces in terms of patterns, arrangement, and logical placement of color and components.

Her media preference is acrylic on canvas, but she has explored the use of metal fusion with canvas, oils, watercolor, wood, and airbrushing techniques.

Carina began “Artisexy” based on her belief that art is sexy, life is sexy, sex is sexy, and everyone should own “something sexy”.

Specialties

Acrylic on Canvas painting, surfboard airbrushing, start-up company business development, creative thinking, and fun.
