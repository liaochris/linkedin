Paige Beal’s Summary

Associate Professor in Sport, Arts and Entertainment Management at the Point Park University School of Business teaching graduate and undergraduate courses in advertising, marketing and new media. Author in progress of undergraduate text "Entertainment Marketing and Media Essentials".

Teaching builds on 20 plus years successfully managing media properties including internet, cable, radio, and print. MBA from the University of Pittsburgh Katz Graduate School of Business. Graduate of Leadership Pittsburgh program, previous board member of the Pittsburgh Advertising Federation and the Pittsburgh Radio and TV Club (a past president as well as winner of “Best Media Professional” and “Best Internet Professional” and Art Stein Award recipient).

Email: pbeal@pointpark.edu

Paige Beal’s Specialties:

Advertising, marketing, marketing and advertising research, internet and emerging technology advertising and marketing, media marketing and management.
