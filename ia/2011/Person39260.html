Scott Kosak’s Summary

RacingIn.com is the ONLY Socially Active racing-themed website exclusively devoted to connecting EVERY Race Fan with ANY Racer, Anywhere and more than 1,600 race tracks across the United States!

Through RacingIn.com, thousands of race fans find local racing every week and hundreds of racers have found a place to, AT NO COST, promote themselves, their racing and their sponsors! You can read more at this link: http://bit.ly/bMwG7Q

For brands or businesses that see Fans of Fast as a marketing target group, RacingIn.com has developed the most innovative and exclusive sponsorship and activation model ever seen in motorsports. You can read more about it at this link: http://bit.ly/afZRZM

If you'd like a free consultation on how RacingIn.com can help connect your product, brand or your vendors with this target audience, please feel free to drop me a note at scott@racingin.com
