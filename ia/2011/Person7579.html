Christina Ciccarone Gettins's Summary

Christina is a senior technical specialist with the Pennsylvania Technical Assistance Program (PennTAP). As a part of PennTAP's Cluster Initiative, she focuses on health care companies and organizations in eastern Pennsylvania, providing them with direct, one-on-one advanced IT assistance. In addition, Christina coordinates technology summits which bring Fortune 5000 companies into contact with federally-funded technologies for the purpose of commercialization.

Christina has worked in various health care payor organizations, including the "blues." Her most recent position was at Coventry Health Care, where she specialized in business consulting, data analysis, and database programming. While there, she worked as a liaison between new-business-acquisition partners in the definition and implementation of application and business practice conversion. She also has experience with JCAHO, NCQA, HIPAA, and SOX regulations.

Christina is an excellent project manager and specializes in implementations and logistics coordination.

Specialties

Healthcare IT and Consulting; Customer Relationship Management; Business Process Improvement; Project Management; Research & Analysis; Marketing and Promotion.
