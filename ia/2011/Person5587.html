Bob Kaplow’s Summary

I am an innovative knowledgeable product support engineer, storage systems manager, and VMS cluster systems manager. I have extensive experience in solving complex technical problems. I have a bachelor of science engineering degree from Northwestern University. I am a volunteer leader for youth model rocket programs. Through my professional and volunteer efforts, I have developed the unique ability to teach complicated skills to a wide range of individuals. I work well in a team environment to increase customer satisfaction, system reliability & availability, and reduce operating costs.
