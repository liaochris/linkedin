American Association of Private Lenders's Summary

The American Association of Private Lenders (AAPL) works solely to foster the development of our industry and to serve our members and the public through networking and educational opportunities, communications, industry resources, partnership development, public relations, advocacy and ethical standards. The AAPL brings together hard money lenders, rediscount / warehouse lenders, individual note and mortgage pool investors, brokers, and providers of goods & services to facilitate operational improvements and successful growth.

Specialties

Priavate Lending, Hard Money Lending, Real Estate Investing, Mortgage Pools, Hedge Fund Managers, Warehouse Lenders, Rediscount Lenders, Commercial Real Estate, Bridge Loan Lenders.
