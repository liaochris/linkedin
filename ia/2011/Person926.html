Susan Young’s Summary

I am currently the owner of AimFire Marketing, an Indianapolis area marketing consultancy specializing in Web and Internet marketing. I work primarily with small- to mid-sized businesses who need a focused marketing effort for executing marketing tactics to support the sales effort.

I have worked 10+ years in various marketing communications roles, from corporate marketing to agencies. My experience comes from many high-tech industries such as software, financial services and life sciences.

I enjoy writing and learning more about Internet marketing tactics, such as search engine optimization and social media.

My educational background includes a Bachelor of Science degree in Advertising from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

My husband, Jason & I also own an online retail clothing clothing store called Wow Clothing Club, with girl's clothing sizes 12 months - 6x/7.
