David Deubelbeiss's Summary

* Educational technology specialist with interests in online education / elearning. I have consulted with many major websites, helping them to be designed to benefit teachers.

* Experience working with adults and their own education needs. Planning curriculum, instruction, motivation. Experience includes teacher training and EFL / LINC (Language Instruction for Newcomers to Canada) instruction

* Passionate educator who motivates thousands of teachers online. Public and transparent presence both online and through workshops/lectures.

Goal: to find the students/organization that needs me. Where I can "make a difference", this being in my opinion, the primary goal of public education - "service to others".

Specialties

technology, text to speech applications, disability/special needs, adult education, online learning, curriculum development, reading skills/extensive reading.
