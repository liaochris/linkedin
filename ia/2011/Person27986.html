Marieke Schoenmaker's Summary

INNOVATION is my oxygen
A habit that brought me from Borneo to Oman and many other places, experiences, ideas and people that made my life innovative. My goal is to connect, consult and train others to realise their ideas, too. I have been doing this for 20 years and helped more than 50 companies uncover their true co-creation potential.

I am a BUSINESS DEVELOPER
I've started my first business development successes when I was 21 and since then have started and grew businesses to truely clientcentric environments with double digit growth.

I am a ENTREPRENEUR
I've started my first company in october 2008, the day the recession broke through. Since then have started 2 other ones. Although truth to be told I managed to loose the latest initiative - an exciting lesson on partnerships and co-creation, which will serve me well in my focus to realise my dream to become a Business Angel.

I CAN HELP YOU
Thanks to my extensive professional experience, the countries I lived and worked in, the many different people I know, I have gained the skills and knowledge on how to create, develop and transform ideas into successfull business:

- I can advise you how to GROW your business from new products and services resulting in +5% extra TURNOVER.

- I can advise you how to co-create more MEANINGFULL CUSTOMER EXPERIENCES with a higher IMPACT in society and your TOP-LINE growth.

- I can teach you how to innovate on your BUSINESS MODEL for a more AGILE organisation to be.

- I can motivate, inspire and empower you and your people how to become creative co-creators to TAKE YOUR BUSINESS TO THE NEXT LEVEL.

I use the same strategies I advise to grow my companies, biking long distances and adapting my life to changing situations.
